Hi Tim.  I have the Yokohama Y370 tires on my Syncro.  They are a 6 
ply rated, 1765 pounds _at_65 psi, and are excellent.  They have an 
all-terrain/all-season tread pattern.  Generally very quiet, and very 
strong.  I run them at 43 psi front/53 psi rear, and am very pleased 
with the traction and performance they give.  They can handle these 
vans very easily, and have a very strong sidewall.  I am on my second 
set of the Y370's, and highly recommend them all Vanagon owners.  
They have near perfect balance (one tire has NO weights on it, and 
the other 3 have less than half ounce), and are nicely made.  They 
may be a little more $$$ but they are worth it if you push the van 
hard in corners, etc.  They are excellent in winter, summer, mud, and 
good for 4-wheeling.
