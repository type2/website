I think I see the root of the problem here.  In Canada, mileage is measured in 
litres per 100 kilometers (volume/distance) whereas in the US, mileage is 
measured in miles per gallon (distance/volume).  In the interest of 
international detente, I thought I should clear up the fact that lowering your 
mileage is not as bizarre as it might sound if you live north of the border.
