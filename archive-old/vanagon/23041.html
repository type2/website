One of the things Cage was exploring was just what *is* music anyway?  If you 
think about it, it's pretty hard to define.  For almost any definition you 
come up with, someone else can say "Well, yeah, but what about XXXX?".
  As I see it, Cage's 4'33" is a piece that asks you to stop for a few 
minutes, think about what music *is* and listen to the music of the *world*.

  But this has nothing to do with Vanagonia.  At least it's FFriday.  (That's 
Friday, but Fortissimo...)
