WARNING!!  Cheap plug for VW club to follow.  WARNING!!

I've called up NEATO, and am very interested in joining their club.  They 
concentrate on pre '68 buses, but welcomed me and my '70 Kombi to join.  
They also are interested in Vanagon/Eurovan club members, too.  They have 
a newsletter called "Old Bus Review," which contains interesting stories 
and a VW classifieds section.   

The cheap plug part is this:  they welcome new members, so any one 
individual who gets five other bus owners to join gets one years' free 
membership.  See below for more info (but it would be really cheap if you 
were to mention my name...)                           ^^^^^^^^^^^^
