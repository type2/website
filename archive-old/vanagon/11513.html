If you are not driving it frequently, put the job off until it warms up
enough to do the work yourself.  It could be that the passenger side brake
cable is rusted through, or broken.  There is no quick fix, just work.

  However, if you are really motivated:  bundle up and lay a big piece of
cardboard on the snow under the bus, and get to work.  Work to stay warm. 
With the added ground clearance of the Bus, and the easily-removable
composite rear drum of the '71, you will be able to at least assess the
situation fairly quickly.
