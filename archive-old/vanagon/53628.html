The only ones i've seen in the newer Transporter/Vanagon badge-style
are the 'Transporter', 'Volkswagen' and 'Carravelle' Badges. Most of 
them are still available through my local dealer though i had some 
trouble ordering a 'Carravelle' badge for a fellow listmember. Most 
of them are between 15 and 20 US dollars. Most of them are interchangable, 
but i think the 'Bus' badge will leave one of the holes exposed or 
requires different holes.

I've also seen chrome 'Transporter', 'Multivan' and 'Carravelle' badges 
for the T4 eurovans and also a 'Transporter' sticker of the same 
size/font but with silver letters, it is meant for T4 pickups.
