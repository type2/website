Nate,
I suppose the $850 might cover the parts, but a two head replacement at a VW
dealer runs $2,000-$2,300 including parts & labor.
A rebuilt wasserboxer from VW runs $2,380, for the part with exchange.  I'd
figure about $4,000-$4,200 total including labor.  The VW rebuilt has a 1
year warranty good at any VW dealer.

I recently replace the engine in my 87 with a low mileage used engine for
$2,600. including lots of new hoses & motor mounts.  I did the job myself.
