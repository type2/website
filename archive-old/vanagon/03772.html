Ayuh,

 I repaired the rust-hole (using $4.00 worth of epoxy putty) an spread
the rest of the putty over the top of the tank where water could collect.
I then sprayed undercoating all over it...It stopped THAT leak.

 When I reassembled the magilla, and got fuel, the *same* symptoms as
above happened. I had not done a good enough job pushing hoses back on.

 If my memory serves me, there are 3 holes on the top-front of the tank, two
on the passenger side and one on the driver side. You can reach in and feel the
"T" joints going into the tank. Next time you fill-up, go Full-serve and
bring a flashlight. Look behind the front wheels into the bowels of the
fuel tank asembly. As you fill the tank, you will *see* the leak on the right
of left side. You can then figger out what to do. I shoved my hand in and
pushed the vent-hose back on correctly.
