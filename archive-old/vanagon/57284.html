Go to the Tirerack and check out the Borbet Replica II wheels:

http://www.tirerack_dot_com/wheels/borbet/bo_rep2.htm

These "Mercedes only" wheels are supposed to be a direct match for the Westy.

Kyle Wade
  Keeper of the volks-motorsports page
  www.volks-motorsports_dot_com  home page
  Pengi_at_cruzio_dot_com

Has told me that he has installed these wheels on a Vanagon and that they are
strong and look great. I am considering them for my '91 Westy. 

They take a "low profile" tire, 205/65 R15 which is supposed to result in an
overall gain of .2 inches in total wheel/tire diameter. This supposedly 
provides an accurate Odometer reading.

Kyle recommends a reinforced tire on these. I am having a little trouble 
getting the straight scoop on appropriate tires.

Michelin MXV Energy tires is what Tirerack recommended. These are a 94 H rated
tire. That's 1477 lbs/wheel at 130 mph. They are a max 44 psi rated tire.

This is a summation of what I have found over the past 2 weeks.
