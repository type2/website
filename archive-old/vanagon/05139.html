My van's (82) screens pop in and out. Look at the bottom of the screens
where it fits into the window track You should see a couple of tabs. If
you CAREFULLY (the plastic seems to get brittle with age), push up on
these tabs it should release the screen from the track.
I cut new screen from real no-see-um netting and glued it in with
"Shoe-Goo", clamping around the perimeter with clothes pegs and some wood
strips (covered with wax paper so that it won't stick to net). The glue
oozes between the mesh and bond quite securely. The tricky part is
clamping it tight so that you don't have saggy screens.
