Couldn't help but think about this one due to my loss of my '91 Westy. If I
had had the old metal (heavy, non-burning metal) rather than the light
weight plastic one provided on the newer models, might the fire damage to the

Even the weight of the lid from my older '80 might have been worth the struggle
if it could have saved the interior of my Westy from destruction.
