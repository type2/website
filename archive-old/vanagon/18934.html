I had lunch with Jim Kirk (not James T.) yesterday. He showed me some photos
from his recent vacation to South Carolina. My favorite was a red bubble
window bus he spotted on Sullivan's Island. It was definately water cooled.

of the chrome parts were black instead. I liked it!!

motor?
