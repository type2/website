Refrigent lines, especially the high pressure ones will loose their 
resistance to diffusion of refrigerent through the rubber with age.  The 
oily appearance is a sure sign this is happening.  Eventually you will 
have to replace these lines.  No this is not a design feature, just a 
fact of deterioration with age.  Sort of like the human body with age.
