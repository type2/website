Was the CO in range?  If yes, you have what is called alean misfire.  Replace
the plugs, make sure cap, rotor, and wires are good.  After failuer, did they
perform a precondition warm-up and retry?  In NY, that is proceduere.
Basically, the engine is run at 2500 rpm for 3 minutes.  This "lights up the
Catalytic converter.  This usually resolves the HC problem on Vanagons.  Try
driving a few miles and havein emissions checked with engine hot from
driving.  Makes a big difference versus warming engine by idling.
