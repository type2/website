With a bad sliding door and steering box, this van is not worth $1000.00.
The rust you mentioned probably means more extensive rust elsewhere.  When
you go look at the van, lie underneath it and check out every square foot
of the undersides and wheel wells.  Poke around with a screwdriver, and
take a long careful time.

I got a 1970 camper (in NY state) with rusty rocker panels and battery
tray, leaky windshield, extra engine, extra tranny, 5 rims, and assorted
parts for $500.  Offer this guy $400, tops, with the "powerful" engine
included.  But when you get this bus, be prepared to make many repairs, and
experience many hassles.

>What parts from a '71 would be usable on a '70 ?

Many of them, but don't bother with this van.
