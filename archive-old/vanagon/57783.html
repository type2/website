They cannot sue you for copyright infringement unless you cause financial
damage to them. Since they are not offering this old document for sale, you
can safely copy and redistribute it at will since you are not causing harm
to a revenue stream that they are otherwise likely to get.

Copyright only applies when there is financial damage. In the absence of
demonstrated financial damage, they cannot collect. Everybody keep this in
mind, and copy stuff as much as you like, especially stuff that is no longer
for sale from the manufacturer. My guess is, it is probably safe to copy
everything but the Bentley manuals.

If I had the time (I don't) I'd back this up and copy this "Journeys under
difficult conditions" manual for anyone who wanted it.
