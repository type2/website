Hi Michael!  I'm going to take a stab at this.

The first line is the manufacturing date:

January 19, 1965

The second line has the M-codes for options.  The ones I can figure
out are:

080     Separate Front Seats for Panel or Kombi

108     With SO-42 Camper Kit

168     88 Ampere/Hour Battery

The third line has the point of entry:

U is for USA, H is for point of entry

231     is the model, Kombi

The next number is for color codes, can't figure out

The last number is the VIN number (add 23 for model)
(23)5091317

The paint codes should be on a silver sticker below the metal plate.
If yours was originally white it should be Pearl White (L87) or
Ivory (L567).
