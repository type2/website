This sounds like the symptoms of a bad idle stabilizer. When the idle 
stabilizer isn't doing its "stabilizing", the idle settles in around
2000 to 3000. 

Is your idle high only with the AC on? When my idle stablizer went, 
it didn't matter if the AC was on or not. The idle would always be over 
2000.

It is a simple repair, although I don't remember how much the part cost.
