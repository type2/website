Take a look at the holes on the pump pulley - one is slightly 
different (smaller I think) than the rest. This is the hole which 
Bentley describes for locking the pump at TDC. Find something as 
close in diameter (I use a 3/8" drive extension) as this hole, and 
insert it. Rotate pump until the hole in the mounting bracket lines 
up with the sprocket hole. If the item you use is a tight fit, it 
should be clear which sprocket mark is TDC. If it's a little loose, 
move the sprocket back & forth as far as it will go with the 'tool' 
inserted, then split the difference.

For it not to start at all, sounds like you're out at least one 
tooth. BTW, the mark on the sprocket can be a horiz. line, or a dot - 
I've seen both. Once you find it, out some white paint on it.
