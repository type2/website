NEVER NEVER NEVER  use synthetic oil in an engine that has less than 5,000
miles on it.  The rings will not seat and you run the risk of ruining the
bearings.
We always use Castrol 10W30 in every engine unless we are using a synthetic
oil and then our oil of choice is REDLINE.  Can't be beaten :)
