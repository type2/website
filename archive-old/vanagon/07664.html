I am in the process of having my tent canvas replaced right now.  The
tent comes from West Coast Metric.  I went and looked at it this weekend,
and to tell you the truth, the material doesn't seem as good a quality
as the original.  It is thinner, and very soft, almost like cotton instead
of canvas!  I'm going to call them and ask what the material actually is.
There is a place in Texas, called VW Canvas (see their ad in HVW), that
claims to use superior quality canvas (I have not actually seen their 
product).  What I would do is call and ask for a sample of material before
buying anything.
