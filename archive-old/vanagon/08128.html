> It looks like my '76 Westfalia is going to need to have the engine rebuilt.

> less maintanence or anything like that?  Or should I just go with the

I remember hearing that the hydraulic valve lifters require a different
camshaft.  A mechanic told me that the cam profile is less aggressive than
the one used in the solid lifter engines, and that you might lose a few
horsepower.  No big deal, IMO, as the advantages more than make up for this.
