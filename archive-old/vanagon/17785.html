  The combination of these two symptoms makes me think "vacuum leak"! First 
  place I'd check is those rubber boots where the steel part of the intake
  manifold connects to the cast aluminum part. Those puppies can crack and
  create a leak which will trash your idle and lean out your mixture enough
  to make it run hot. Loose connections, busted hoses elsewhere in the
  induction path are suspect too. I'm assuming that you have already 
  checked the easy stuff <dwell, timing, etc.>.

  Maybe someone with more experience with the upright fan motor can give 
  some better advice?
