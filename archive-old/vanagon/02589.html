Y'all  is singular. meaning 'you'.
                    however, it can be used to refer to a small group,
                    such as two or three people. can also be used to
                    refer to anyone in the general hearing vacinity, as in:
                    "Any o' Y'all know how to git to Bubba's??"

All Y'all  is plural.   meaning 'all of you'.
                    usually meant to refer to large groups of people ...
                    rioting mobs, theatre-goers, and beer-bellied gin joint
                    patrons. generally pronouced as 'Ahlllllll Yawl'.
