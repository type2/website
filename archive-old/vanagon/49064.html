had seating for 7, a fold-up table, a rear seat that fold into a bed, and a

while your sleeping.  I would suggest calling all ads for 1990 or 1990
Vanagon Campers or Vanagon Westfalias, since the owner or dealer might not
correctly describe this model as a MultiVan.
