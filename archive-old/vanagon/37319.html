Ummm... I think $750 would be a bare minimum to rebuild his engine.
Probably $9-1200, based on my experience, but I guess it depends on your
definition of "rebuilt".  Not counting labor costs, of course, which
would be "free" if you do it yourself.

There are only a couple of differences between the Porsche 1.7 and the
VW 1.7.  I believe the intake manifolds only have three studs per side,
the oil breather, oil filler tube (and the dipstick?) are in a different
location too.  Then there is the problem of your '79 exhaust which is
the "vanagon" style and will only mate to the heads for '79-on engines
with rectangular exhaust ports.  I don't think the 1.7L 914 engine has
those.
