I had the same problem with mine and after looking at the job, finally 
wnt to an upholstery shop that I had done some business with before. He 
said that in order to fix the screen, he would have to take the canvas

re-instal it.

"no-see-um" screen material and he installed it and had it back up and 
fixed in a day. About $100 - well worth it I think.
