VanListies,

I am curious to find out if anyone has a definitive answer as to whether or
not VW sold Vanagon Syncros in the United States for model year 1988.  

The '88 brochure has no reference at all to the Syncro, but a couple of VW
publications seem to hint that there may have been some 1988 Syncros.  In an

have a U.S. model 1988 Vanagon Syncro or any other definitive means of
answering this question?
