Jeff Carver had collected several previous entries and asked:
"All the VIN's I received started with 25A not 24A, what gives? I wonder."

In 81 to 91 Vanagons the 25 in the VIN indicates a van body style while 24
indicates a pick-up truck body style.  Appliying that to the 1980 Vanagons
would mean that all these entries were van bodies.
