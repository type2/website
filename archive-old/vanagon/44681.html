Subject: alternator/second battery problem



This weekend the alternator light came on in my 84, miles from home while
my friend was driving (in fact she drove a long way with it on).  
We stopped and turned the engine off.  This
was a bad move since it would not restart - flat battery.  I traced the 
problem to the alternator brushes which are at the wear limit.  
Fortunately I had a spare battery which was charged enough to get the van 
started - just.  I have been meaning to wire it in as an aux with a 
solenoid for 
a while now but this experience makes me think twice, since if I had done
that, both batteries would have been dead and I would have been stuck!  The
advantage of an islolator is that even if the alternator dies you still have 
some power left...
