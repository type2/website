with as much as 2mm worth of crack between the valve seats as you described.

reinstall the head, fully warm up the engine, and check compression. Kind of
a severe test, who wants to reinstall a bad head. If it ran well before you
took it apart, leaking coolant or was OK until the haed gasket went. Use the
head.
