For just a few $ you can get a gizmo that turns an old freon bottle into
an air storage tank.  Useful for road trips into the backwoods as
well as an easier way to get air to things if you have a compressor (or not).

Just hook the bottle up to the coolant overflow line, pressurize to bottle
level (preset when you fill bottle) close the valve to the bottle then
monitor pressure on the gauge attached to the gizmo.
