  Trailer parks are called caravan parks in Australia.  They can be found 
almost everywhere: standards differ, but basics incluse electrical 
hookups, hot and cold shower, toilets, and laundromats.  Fees range from 
$6 to $12 a day.
  "Campervans rent from $350 a week including 1,000 m. and 15 cents per

from $450 a week including 1,000 km.  .  . . "
