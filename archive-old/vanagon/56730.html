Kelly,

What you have is a potentially DANGERIOUS COMBINATION OF TIRES !!!

Because you are running conventional tires in the back of a rear engined vehicle, you are 
set-up for very excessive oversteer.  In other words, your bus will be VERY EASY to spin.

As a stop-gap, unil you can dispose of the Uniroyals, move the Uniroyals to the front and 
Contis to the back.  I suggest inflation pressures of 35 psi front and 43 psi rear.

Buy another pair of tires, same size and load range as the Contis, ASAP to replace the 
Uniroyals.
