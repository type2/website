Well now that we now there are a few of us up here that would like to =
pop the tops on the ol' westies and stoke the campfire (PLEASE, WOOD =
ONLY! :-} )
The type of campouts that I had in mind were based on a motorcycle club =
I belonged to.  More or less anybody wanted to camping they would post a =
notice and whom ever showed up did, no fuss no muss.  Certain people =
would throw a campout every year and they have become quite fun.=20
So, I would like to start the ball rolling with a favorite place of =
mine.  On the the 5th & 6th of April I will be camping on  Shaw Island =
in the San Juans.  There is a small county campground there that is =
pretty nice and have great views of Canoe Island & Lopez Island. The =
only commercal building is "The little Portion Store" run the a small =
group of Nunns that have a farm there also.  The island offers nothing =
but relaxation.  The campground offers a boat launch, playground , =
covered cooking shelter and outhouses.  This island doesnt have many =
hills and very little traffic so is a great place to take the family for =
a bike ride.  I'll be there rain or shine & hope some of you will too! =20
