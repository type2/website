Mike:
Been there; done that^6. The switch overheats and deforms. The contacts
get out of alignment. I heated them (the contacts) with a soldering iron
and forced them back into alignment. The source of the problem is :
A: Bearings in fan losing lubrication causing excessive current draw;
and/ or
B: The wire carrying current supply to the switch is underrated ,size
wise. 
I rectified both and have not had any more problems.
