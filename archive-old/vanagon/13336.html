>
>3) Does any one have any experience in installing "anchors" for rear (and

We put in an anchor high up in the rear corner, by removing the engine air 
grill then drilling through the tin. I bought a mounting kit, Canadian Tire, 
with LARGE backing washers 2" dia. The strap looks a bit oddball, at a 45 
degree angle from the seat back to wall, but the rear deck is clear. Best 
site would be into the rear engine deck, near the hatch sill/latch. I can't 
remember why I didn't do this, since luggage space is not as important as 
the kid, although there are days... You need to resist 10G, ie upto 400 lbs. 
but the seat belt and strap are sharing the loads. tim s.
