When an engine won't run

Check the spark the Check the fuel

The spark is easy, it must jump and crack loud enough to be heard.

In your case, your engine was nt previously running, so you must really 
carefully check the points and the timing. Do it a coulple and have 
someone step through it with you out loud.

The vw fuel pumps change the position of the fuel-in fuel-out pipes at 
random.  try switching the hoses if you didn't mark the fuel inlet with a 
scratch when you pulled the hoses.
