                               Busses on '66'
 
A LiMBO & GROOVE sponsored All VW Bus camping event to be held on June 
Bug Jamboree Weekend (June 14-16) in Springfield, Missouri, USA.  
Situated on Historic Route 66 at the national award winning KOA 
campground in the Queen City of the Ozarks.  Join us for world famous 
LiMBO camping fun on Friday through Sunday.  Participate in GROOVE's 
Route 66 Cruise (through the heart of Springfield) on Friday night, head 
for the June Bug Jamboree (GROOVE's 2nd Annual All-VW Car Show) on 
Saturday morning, tour any of the area's internationally know attractions 
Saturday afternoon (including Bass Pro Shops Outdoor World or nearby 
Branson, MO), then it's off to the drive-in movies Saturday evening.  
Awaken to a hearty pancake breakfast on Sunday and get started home by 
joining in either the East or West Route 66 Tour (approx. 75 miles).   
Scattered throughout the weekend will be camping activities, door 
prizes, part swapping, story telling and just plain VW fun!
