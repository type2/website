73-79 loaf's actaully had two different alternators.  Bosch AL 75X (55amp)

although it does not say what that difference is (could be that 10mm
difference).  Both alternators look identical otherwise and it could be easy
for a local independent rebuilder to get 'em mixed up.  Of course I'm not at
