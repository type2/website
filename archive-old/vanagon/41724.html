>The expansion tank is behind the license plate, and should have some coolant
>in it always.  Main tank, under the deck lid, was full?  Try revving it up

>bake-off pronto.  Another easy check is to turn on front heat full and fan
>on 2.  If the defroster air is way cooler than floor air you have a bubble.

>for a couple of weeks.  If it air-bubbles again fast, start saving your
