The trans oil is checked by removing the fill plug on the side of the trans.
 The oil level should be about 1/2 inch below the hole. It must be checked on
level ground.  I would recomend draining and refilling the gearbox with a
75-90 synthetic gear lube.  This wil ease your shifting problems and provide
improved transmission protection.
