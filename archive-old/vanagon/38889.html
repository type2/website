This is called 'indexing' of the spark plugs and is a final tweeking 
in blue-printing an engine.  However, depending upon combustion swirl 
in the combustion chamber, the front of gas/air mixture may actually 
approach the plug from the rear.  I think overall, we're talking Nth 
fractions of a horsepower increase.  To me it borders on anal 
retentive.  Or, wait, is it obsessive/compulsive?

avoid the controversy and use a tri-electrode plug.....    and best of
