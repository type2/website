I don't know about the "strange noises", but your problems are most likely with 
your cold start injector, or the wire or fuel line running to it. Looking at 
your motor from the top, its right about in the middle. This valve provides 
extra fuel only when the motor is cold. While your at it, check also that all 
the air hoses are hooked up and not cracked.

Good Luck,
