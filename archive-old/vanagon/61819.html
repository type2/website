The Blue lever on the doors opens the cab to the outside air, allowing 
fresh air ventilation from outside to occur. As our vanagons are so air 
tight, air would not flow easily (in theory) if there was not some 
release for the air pressure. The ventilation does flow easier if it is 
open.
