 I live in Ottawa, Canada and the temperatures up here can drop to -20 C (Duh,
don't know F temp, but it's damn cold) Anyhoo, a safer alternative to
the propane heater is inline volksie heaters. We used to go ski flying
in the winter and two inline volks heaters were the only thing that
would preheat the engine. I.E. one heats the fresh, cold air, while
the other heats the 'heated' air. One precaution though: don't try
sticking your hand in front of the second one... Unless of course you
LIKE the smell of burning flesh... Cheers.
