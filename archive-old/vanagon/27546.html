Your heater is just wired to a terminal that's "hot" whether the ignition 
is on or not.  Moving the connector would be simple, if you wanted the 
heater to shut off automatically when you stopped.  But that's how 
they're supposed to be wired.  The only time you should be able to run 
them w/o the ignition on is by the timer, since it bypasses the ignition 
wiring.  Check the schematics for the appropriate terminal location.
