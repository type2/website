Hello-
Had similar problems with my '80 Westi, rebuilt starter, rebuilt 
alternator, changed ignition switch, intermittent problem continued.  
Latest solution is to install solenoid as outlined in Muir's book.  
Still had problem, chucked that solenoid and got a good one.  No 
problems since.  Overheating of starter was suspected, but had 
circuitry checked with good results.
BTW, I attached the solenoid to the upper starter hold-it-together 
screw with the starter removed, convenient location.
