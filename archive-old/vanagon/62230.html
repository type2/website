couple of years. This year, VW bugs and busses are going to be central 
characters....and I thought VWs only came into existance in the mid-30s..


According to the local weekly: "This summer, the Westport Theatre Company is 
staging "A Midsummer Night's Dream" transported to a 60's mood and place.

Its a place of magical 12 foot flowers, psychedelic colors, VOLKSWAGEN BUGS 
AND BUSSES (my emphasis...), and music to touch the soul........"

The play runs from July 11-13 & 18,19 at the winery located in Westport,MA.
