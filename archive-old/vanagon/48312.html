Valve lifters??  What is the problem?  If the lifters are noisy, there is
probably only one or two that are bad.  The lifters can be repalced without
tearing down the engine.  A competant mechanic should be able to locate the
culprits and they can be changed by removing the pushrod tubes.  Collapsable
tubes are available to allow this repair without removing the heads.  Lifters
should only cost about $20.00 ea. They are the same as used in the aircooled
engines.
