Not really, it depends if you want to stay for a longer period or just a few 
weeks. If you come as a tourist you cann keep your US license plates. May be 
you need additional coverage for insurance.

If you import the car to here, you need to have a certificate of the KBA 
(something like DOT) that the car has never been registered as stolen and a 
certification from VW Kundendienst that according to the VIN the car fulfils 
the local regulations. Than it has to be examined by the TUV (see VANAGON_dot_com 
dictionary) and off you go. 

That's the whole show for germany - other countries maybe different of course.
