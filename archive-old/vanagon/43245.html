My understanding behind heated O2 sensors is that the electrical heating
element comes on immediatly upon startup and brings the temp of the sensor up
to operating temperature faster than w/o the electrical assist, depending only
on the heat from the exhasust.  This brings the ixture feedback system into
operation faster and reduces emissions during warmup.
