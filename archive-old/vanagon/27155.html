 Dave---

Saw your note---the box is the oil cooler.  Coolant does circulate through
it and hence could leak from it I suppose.  I don't know what they cost to
replace but it can be done...although it will require draining oil and
coolant to do so and it's a pain to get out [have done it].  

But BE CERTAIN that is the cause first.  On my 86 GL the oil cooler is right
under the waterpump which can drip onto it [and has].   My guess is
something else is more likely---waterpump, gasket, hose. The fact that the
drip is forming on the bottom suggests coolant is dripping onto it and
running to the bottom corner.  I'm certain waterpumps go far more often than
oil colers fail.  Someone told me that VW went to a different water pump
gasket a few years back.  I have replaced gaskets and waterpumps over the
years [leaks apppeared on the oil cooler as a symptom].
