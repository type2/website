"Around" has to be given a fairly wide interpretation I guess, and I
agree that finding the bus you want is difficult especially if it's
a camper and your very particular about what you want. Several listees
can attest to this. Tom F. and Shawn W. gave very detailed reports to the 
list from their Westy hunting experiences. See the archives.
Difficulty variries with area, and some people have travelled far
to get their busses. My approach is to be patient, and check all
the local small ad's magazines. Locally in Rochester we have
at least 10 different ones. Remember that the more unlikely place
an ad shows up in, the more chance that you are the first caller.
The more widely advertized (internet!) the more potential buyers.
