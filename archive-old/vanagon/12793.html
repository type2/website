I have electronic ignition on my honda 750.  The main reason for replacing 
the stock points was exactly what Dave said above.  I've set the timing once 
in 1-1/2 years and 13000 miles.  The other reason for changing from the

think about adjusting the points on your VW, think of that.

cooling system on the 1500 had anything to do with the valve consumption?
