Two things: I think the design of the pump is such that the fuel acts 
to cool it, which may explain the noise in hot conditions. Also, I've 
seen /heard this problem on two different cars with similar fuel 
pumps (77 Rabbit, 82 Scirocco) where the noise was caused by rust in 
the fuel tank making its way into the pump.
