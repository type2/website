Bentley and Haynes each have pretty good pictures of the tinware.  The
Bentley manual is the "official" one and you can order it through
numerous VW places, maybe even your local dealer.  The Haynes you can
find in your local FLAPS.  If they don't have the one for your bus,
they will order it.  Still, you might also want to take a trip to a
junkyard to see one put together.
