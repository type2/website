The GVWR refers to the total wieght of the vehicle when fully loaded.  it is
the maximum the vehicle can wiegh.  The other numbers on the sticker refer to
the maximum wieght for each axle.  GCVW refers to the total wieght of the
vehicle plus a trailer.  Note that VW does not rate the van with this number.
