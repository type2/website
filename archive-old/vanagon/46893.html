I am not sure what the big deal is with these engines.  I rebuilt one with
nothing but Muir.  It was my first rebuild.  I did find it heavy and
cumbersome for one person to handle.  I did the rebuild over a couple of
weeks.  I stopped when I came to a problem, and asked around about the
problem.  I remember that setting end play gave me much concern.  I also had
a machine shop rebuild the heads, and turn the cam.  It may be difficult to
rebuild these engines, but no one told me, so I didn't know.  Dumb beginners
luck, I guess.
