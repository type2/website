You need to remove the tranny mount from above the bell housing.  Then the
engne and trans can tilt down and then the engine can be pulled out from
under the van.  There are two bolts that hold this mount to the trans, they
are reached from underneath (15mm) and the nuts are welded so the bolts can
be re=moved easily.  I also disconect the shift linkage so it does not get
bent from the trans tilting dowm.
