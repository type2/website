I know this is not too much help for finding the source in
the engine compartment, but FWIW, under the dash, the wire
bundle behind and just to the right of the steering column
contains the wire that runs the tach.  In my '85, it is the
only solid green wire in the bundle.  I needed a tach signal
to drive my cruise control computer, and after much probing
around I found that wire to contain the tach signal.
