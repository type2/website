Hey Matt, I have the Bentley manual as well as the chilton book and the
gospel of St. Muir.  I have nevr looked at the Haynes manual at any length,
but I can say that they are far superior to the Chilton manual.  I worked
auto parts for a number of years before returning to Graduate school, and I
can say that I used to sell quite a few Haynes manuals from the different
part stores that I worked at.  Nevertheless, I still wouldn't pay almost
$100 for the Bentley, I know theyre expensive, but I have seen them in alot
of the mail order VW places for around $40.  The Bentley manuals do have the
wiring diagrams (in color) which none of the other manuals have.  That is a
definate plus.  If you were to purchase the Haynes manual I wouldnt pay more
than about $25 for it.
