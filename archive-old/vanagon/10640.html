Well, if you're gonna have that engine rebuilt anyway, you could also look
at earlier type IV models and then fit them with a 2l crankshaft and
bigger pistons, etc. Dave Kautz thinks that the 74 and the 75 models have
the best Westfalia arrangements. And, if I'm not mistaken, the 78 and
79'ers have a swiveling front passenger seat.

>1976 Westphalia

IMHO, I would start at around $3,000 and then substract the cost of the
rebuild and maybe the cost of body work. So, anywhere between $1000 and
$2000 would seem right. What you will pay will depend on your dickering
skills and how desperate the seller is. But the new tires, exhaust, and
heat exchangers should be worth about $300 + $100 + $400 = $800 to you. So
you shouldn't feel _too_ bad if you pay closer to $2000.
