YES

The long studs that hold your heads onto the barrels are subject to fatigue 
loading from the firing.  ANY bolted connection is much less susceptible to 
fatigue(cyclic) failure if it has a high preload on it.  This seems counter 
productive but... This raises the steady state stress level such that the 
cyclic stresses become a smaller percentage of total load.  This keeps 
fatigue away, not so much in the studs as in the thread roots in the case!
