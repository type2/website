> Has anyone seen/made/bought a locking latch for the engine compartment

> than the mechanism for the big rear lid into the passenger compartment, so a

I searched in the junkyard until I found a locking door.  Then I bought
the lock, and took it to a locksmith to have a key made for it.  They
sell these things at J.C. Whitney for $10, but they state that they are
only for bugs, so I don't know if they will work with bus engine hatches.
