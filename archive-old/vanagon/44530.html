Since you ran it out of gas, my first thought would be that you probably
got some crud in the injectors.  You can check this by looking at the
spray pattern of the injectors.  If they are spraying a nice fine mist
with no drips, then they're probably okay.  If they are dripping and not
a good spray then you should get the fuel-injectors cleaned.  Any good 
shop should be able to do this.  Or you can try pouring one of the off-
the-shelf fuel-injector cleaner brands into your tank and see if this 
alleviates the problem.  Of course, you should check the fuel pressure
etc. to make sure that you are getting the proper pressure to the injectors
first.
