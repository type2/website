Here is what Hayne's says: green>12.5 v; 12.5.>yellow; 12v>red. In other
words the red goes on if the voltage is at or below 12 volts; yellow
between 12.5 volts and 12 volts and green above 12.5 volts....

On the water pump -  if the red glows there is a auto shut off to the pump....
