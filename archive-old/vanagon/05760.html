Blue book, schmue book. They have a different book for each occasion. I
found 5 publications listing used car prices: the Edmunds, the Red book,
the blue book, the NADA (yellow) blue book, and another which I can't
remember. All give a wholesale and a retail price, yet even within these
categories, prices vary substantially. I just sold our (very nice) 87
Civic. Wholesale price in those books ranged from $1450 to $3500; retail
price went as high as $5025 (all prices after adjustment for miles and
options). The dealer offered $1700 (second bid) and I refused. A week
later, he advertised an 86 Civic for $3995!
