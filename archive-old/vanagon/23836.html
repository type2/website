Jeff,
Just in case no one has replied to you yet, it sounds like you have the 
dreaded VW ignition wiring/solenoid problem.  Pick up a generic Ford 
solenoid (~$5 _at_ Checker) and use it as a relay for the solenoid.  If 
you're not sure how to wire it (or anyone else wants the info), mail me 
direct.
