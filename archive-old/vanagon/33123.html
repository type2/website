> This sounds like a problem with your accelerator pump on your carb
> to me.  The accelerator pump squirts a little extra gas down the
> throat of the carb.  If it pumps too much, it will momentarily
> flood and "stall".  If it doesn't pump enough, your engine has to
> build momentum from the other components of the carb in conjuction
> with either the vacuum or centrifugal advance of the distributor.
> 
> >... This also could be (I hope) due to
> >some slightly stiff components on the carb (specifically, the throttle
> >valve lever, which doesn't always return completely).
> 
> There are basically two systems that affect your problem: the 
> ignition system and the fuel system.  You have pretty much
> eliminated the ignition problems.  If you have KNOWN problems
> with the fuel system, those are the problems to fix FIRST.
> 
> BTW the accelerator pump diaphram is included in the carb rebuild
> kit.
