>Why do you want to get 6.17s from Germany? Aren't they available in the US?

The term "6.17s" refers not to rims, but to final drive gear ratios in the
transmission. We would not, of course, purchase our rims in Germany. But the
6.17s are a Volkswagen factory part, and not sold in the USA. The only ring
and pinion final drive gears sold in the USA by Volkswagen are 4.86s.
