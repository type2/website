If you have not had a look beneath the rear seat to check for coolant 
leaks at the rear heater core, I encourage you to do it soon.  You just 
need a screwdriver and 10 minutes or so, four screws to remove the kick 
panel, 4 or 6 to remove the heater shroud, two clips on the outlet vent.  

I ingnored my suspicions until the carpet and soundproofing were 
SATURATED with antifreeze.  Heap big mess.  Look for corrosion on the 
heat exchanger fins, and feel around for moisture at all joints.  Good luck!
