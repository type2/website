Well, many of our beloved FI VWs came without O2 sensors.  All Type 3s,
All Type 4s, Beetles '75-'79 and Buses '75-'79 and Vanagons '80-'83 that
had fuel injection did NOT have O2 sensors, EXCEPT for some California
models.  

I believe the only air-cooled Vanagons with O2 sensors are California
models.  I know my '81 doesn't have one and it's not supposed to.  

I know the '79 CA Buses had O2 sensors, and even tho the '78 CA Buses
had catalytic converters, i don't think they had O2 sensors.  

The CA air-cooled Vanagons also have electronic ignition while the
federal models have standard points-and-condenser ignition.  

When they switched to the wasserboxer, I think they all got O2 sensors.
