>The cost for a clear windshield was $20 for the new seal at West Coast Metric
 which has a lifetime warr. and $115 for the glass with a lifetime warr.
 against leaking.  If you want to do it yourself here are some tips.

Lube & clean everything well. Slip seal onto glass. Insert 1/4 cord into
groove all the way around. Place glass in position with one guy outside 
pushing while one guy inside s l o w l y pulls the cord thus pulling the
inner lip up and over the metal.
