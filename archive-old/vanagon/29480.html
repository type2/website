I'm sure that somewhere, someone has jumpstarted their FI vehicle from
a 1962 oldsmobile with a mousenest in the alternator that was giving
off voltage spikes of 20+ volts, and they warmed up their ecu a little
bit much.  But if you think about what a great capacitance a dead battery
has, you shouldn't be worried.  One idea would be to take the jump from 
another FI vehicle, that way if it's running, it probably isn't doing
anything damaging electrically. Or if you're really paranoid, take a jump
from their battery without their engine running.
