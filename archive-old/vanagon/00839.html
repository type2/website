Does this "outfit in town" realize that your heads are not cast and 
may be a little difficult to arc weld.  If you are rebuilding your 
old heads or having them done be sure that you replace all of the 
valves with new ones.  If your head has a peice missing it would be 
better to get another used one to work with. Good luck.
