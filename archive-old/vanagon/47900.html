 The transmission ratio data I have <got it from Joel> shows the same
 ring and pinion ration for both the 4 spd. and 5 spd. used with the 
 diesel. However, the  ratio for fifth is 0.77:1 compared to .85:1 for top
 gear in the 4 spd. This will result in an overall final drive about 
 10% higher with the 5 speed transaxle. Incidentally, first gear is the 
 same <3.78:1> in both gearboxes. I believe it's only the Syncro that has
 the "stump-puller" low gear.
