>2. Can anyone recommend a good dye for the curtains.

If you are going the single color route use RIT dye in your favorite
color, in the wash(be sure to run the washer empty at least 1 time to
get the dye out so you do not stain your next load of wash). Available
at you local grocery store, instructions printed on box.  The stuff is
cheap so if it bleaches, reapply.
