Subject: Questions About Buying Diesel Westi

negatives:
	loud, underpowered. no A/C.  No hydraulic valve lifters --
	needs valve adjustments pretty often.

a quick note:
	gas mileage is not as great as it is said -- maybe a savings
        of 5 mpg.

what to look for:
	have someone drive it (hard), and follow behind.  Too much
	diesel smoke may mean at the very least neglected valve
	adjustments.
	Look for signs of excessive blow-by: too much engine oil
	dripping.
