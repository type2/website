Check with the Bus Depot for AFMs - they often have both new and used, and
will know the differences. Old Volks Home (Sherwood Automotive) may also have
at similar price - I haven't received their catalog yet.

However I suspect your O2 sensor may be the culprit - it can cause similar
symptoms - and a failed one often makes the engine rich no matter what else
you do. (primary feedback loop for steady-state operation.) Try just
disconnecting it and see if runs nearly normal - they often do. If so, replace
with a good Bosch.

The Platinum plugs should be fine - I've used them forever.
