Good idea.  Check your area for industrial surplus stores.  There are
several here in San Diego.  The one I frequent has boxes of such fans,
not just from computers.  They range from 1" square to 9", and lots of
them are 12V low-amp fans.  I bought one about 3" square that is rated
.17 amps and mounted it behind my fridge to blow air over the coils.  It
is almost completely silent and won't drain the batteries.  Now I just
have to wire it into the thermostat of the refrigerator so that it is
only on when the fridge is running.
