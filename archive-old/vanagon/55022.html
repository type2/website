>>Yes.
>>When I was getting a Peter Russek Manual for the watercooled Transporter
>>from VW Books, I asked the bookshop if they had anything else interesting
>>and they mentioned 'Volkswagen Bus-Camper-Van 1979-1989' by Brooklands 
>>Road Test Books.  It is a chronological set of articles about the 
>>Transporter from numerous publications around the world, compiled by R.M. 
>>Clarke. There are 100 A4-sized pages from 29 articles dating from 1979 to 
>>1989. Two or three by Road and Track I have seen in the archives. Other 
>>publications include Car, Autocar, Car & Driver and VW Motoring. I would 
>>recommend this book to all Transporter enthusiasts and upgraders. I got 
>>mine from VW Books in Leicester, UK, Tel: 0044 116 286 6686. 
>
