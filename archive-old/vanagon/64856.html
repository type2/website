>important.

This is the same type of oil cooler as on the 86 and later Golf GTIs and
Jetta GLIs....unless someone screws around with the oil cooler unit, these
nuts do not come undone....At 215k miles, my GTI has not leaked from the
cooler, and I have never heard of a GTI/GLI owner complain of a leak before.
