A common setup on baywindow busses is three gauges side by side under the
radio cutout.  VDO has lots to choose from, such as oil temperature, oil
pressure (I think there's a sender that replaces your current one, with a
connection for the guage AND the dash light), cylinder head temp. voltmeter,
clock, etc.  They also make a tach. that fits in the empty location to the
right of the speedometer.  Check the ads in Hot VWs and VW Trends.
