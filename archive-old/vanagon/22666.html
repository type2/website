I have been bugging (pun intended) everyone here with the tale
of woe that my '71 Bus is putting me through.

Story: '71 Bus. Muffler rusted out. I replaced it with a 
Bugpack single quiet-pack extractor set. I also have the
stock dist. and a Mexican (Brosal) 34-PICT-3.

gives me a little backfire. Sometimes it does this when I step

driving, but I wanna go on the highway.

individually anymore, or if they are the same for the Brosal brand

I am worried that the extractor is too big (I got it free) for a stock
engine, or that the Mexican carb will run too lean, melting down
the engine.

This is a pretty standard setup, from what I understand. Seems
like it should have been covered before.
