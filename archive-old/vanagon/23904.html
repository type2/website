I don't know if this also applies to Split screen buses (it probably does) 
but Beetles and Bay windows have a voltage regulator that supplies the fuel 
gauge with a constant 5V. So it shouldn't make any difference weather you 
run it on 6 or 12V. 
Is there a little rectangular metal can plugged into the back of the gauge? 
That's the regulator. Try swapping it or testing it. They are not meant to 
be taken apart but I've managed to revive a few ailing ones.
