This may be illegal!!  But the easiest way to do it is to get a British
listy to send you a bottle of the yellow headlight lacquer that is used

used it often.  Stuff paints on easily, cleans off with alcohol. 
Easiest/cheapest way to go. Maybe you can find similar at good artists
supply store?  Yellow beams are way way better in fog/snow.
