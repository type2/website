On my '84, the paint codes are on a sticker near the fuse
panel (above the clutch pedal).  Take the paint codes to the
VW dealer to get the correct paint.  As far as wax goes, I
just finished bringing back to life the paint on my '84,
which had been neglected by the PO for several years.  After
trying different compounds, I found that the best and
easiest approach was to use McGuiers (sp?) Body Scrub,
followed by their cleaner/wax formula.  I ended up with it
looking like new!  Also, to minimize the frustration, I did
only one or two panels of the van per day.  It took about a
week, but was worth it to be patient.
