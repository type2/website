Saturday was a nice day here in Utah, so I decided to wash and drive 
the Pioneer.  She was happy to have a bath, and have the various 
fragments of her sun-roof fabric tucked back inside her.

I decided that it was again time to get a CD player for her (my 
previous bus had one), so I was straightening out the current wiring 
under the seats to the rear of the bus, and I discovered something!

To add insanity to confustion, stamped in the wall that divides the 
front bench from the rest of the bus, was the number: 299416.

Is this the manufacturing number?  

Remember the weirdness I am experiencing with this thing.  The 
title says '57, but the little id plate screwed into the engine

So, is the ID number on the tag supposed to match the number on the 
bulk-head wall?

Help the Pioneer... She's fallen and she can't get up!
