All Vanagons were originaly equipped with ATF as the power steering fluid.
 The "Secret" formula fluid was a change over to improve cold weather
operation.  The system will often scream and howl on startup with ATF in cold
weather.  I use Mobil-1 synthetic ATF.  Howl lasts about 1 minute in 0 degree
weather.  I change the oil every 30K. 126K on FunBus with no hint of fluid
leaks.  Castrol GT power steering fluid will also work on tired systems that
need a little more push or that are starting to leak.  More seal softening
additives than ATF.
