     Where I stop depends upon where I am.  (profound, eh?)  When I am on 
     Interstate highways I look for the brown signs that denote parks.  
     There are often blue signs that have a tent on them.  These are 
     usually KOA's or similar family run campgrounds the serve for me.  I 
     like hot showers.
     
     I've done alot of 'roadside' camping while on kayaing trips.  In the 
     mountains I've be comfortable  at wayside picnick grounds (unless 
     posted), country stores/gas stations, churchs (always be respectful) 
     and such.  I also use dirt roads when I can find them.
     
     Asking nicely at local establishments can lead to interesting 
     alternatives also.
     
     The paranoia factor varies but I have never had a problem (outside of 
     my own imagination), though in dense population areas (or where things 
     look shady) I'll opt for a hotel/motel.
     
     happy holidays !
