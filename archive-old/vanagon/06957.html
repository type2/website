>1.  What do I have to do to get the pilot light to work?

something you might look at is the wire that runs from the igniter switch
on the top panel down to the combustion chamber. My Dometic
(non-Westphalia) had a similar problem.
You only have to lift the stove up to check it. After it failed the 2nd
time I judiciously applied duct tape to lock the wire down to the top of
the fridge. Works fine now.
