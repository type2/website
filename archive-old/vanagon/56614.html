Try an old trick.  At night, uncover engine, turn out lights, start engine
and let idle.  Take a spray bottle of water and spray the ignition wires.  If
there's a breakdown of your wires when wet, it will show up as a nice spark
(one of mine were sparking directly to an injector).  Look carefully all
around the wires and be carefull of turning belts & pulleys.
