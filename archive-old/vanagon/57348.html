Yes, a '72-74 Bus exhaust will fit an air-cooled Vanagon (and also will fit 
a '79 bus), other than the lack of the CAT.  
I have a complete aftermarket system (muffler and pipes) for $79 for the 
whole thing, and have sold several to Vanagon owners.  This seems to be the 
most popular option; it isn't as heavy duty as the German '72-74 exhaust, 
but for a fraction of  the price it does a fine job.
