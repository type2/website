>**by the way, did i mention that my bus 'uses' about 1/4 quart in 2500 miles?

>too low.  
Good point.  I too seem to 'lose' oil at that rate.  As an aside on
hydraulic lifters, if I top up the oil when it is about 1/2 a quart low, I
notice right away that the engine runs significantly quieter... there's much
less valve clatter.
