I wouldn't let any dealer anywhere near the brakes on a car of mine. In fact 
I wouldn't trust most of them to find thier own arse with a magnifying 
glass. Look at some of the stories on this list of incompetent mechanics

them talk ('Oh yeah, they all do that'). I take the view - If you want

learn and save money to boot). OK, so there are a lot of good, honest 
mechanics around, but there are an awful lot more thieving dickheads. If you 
don't already have a tame, friendly mechanic, why put your life in someone 
else's hands.
