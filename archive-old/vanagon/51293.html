With a 27" tire diameter, a difference of 1/8" in tread depth corresponds to
a difference of less than 1% in circumference:

(.250) / 27 = 0.93%

I can't find any specs in Bentley regarding tolerances for tire
circumference on syncro vehicles, but a 1% difference sure doesn't sound
like it would be enough to matter.
