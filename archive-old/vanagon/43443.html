here's the success story:  For those who don't remember, my 76 was leaking
like a mother, under the driver's side, around the oil filter.  So it turns
out to be the oil pressure sending switch.  $6.50 (expensive, another FLAPS
had it for 3.90, but they were closing before I could get there).  Had to
widen the hole in the cooling air duct a little to get the socket down in
there, but mission accomplished.

To those who suggested a leaking oil cooler:  you were right, but that's
not the majority of the oil.  And I am most grateful yall were wrong :).
