road. Up to that point it was fine motoring as I drove in along scenic 
gentle curves. The tow truck guy, with 150 feet of borrowed cables and 
chains pulled me out in a straight line, sunk to the pan all the way. Don't 
wait for spring, wait for summer to go boonie-bashing!
