Those little heaters are wonderful, but they run at 1500 Watts.
Your household electricity can put out that kind of power,
110 Volts times 13.6 amps = 1500 Watts.
13.6 amps is easy on a circuit.

Your Van is at 12 V... let's look at the math.
1500 Watts/12 V = 125 Amps
Sorry,  You're not gonna be able to get that outta your batt and alt
no matter how fast you're going.  Besides you'd have to have a pretty
impressive fuse system and some pretty high guage wires.

Answer:
Run an extension cord out to your van.
Plug in one of those heaters (don't burn anything)
Plug in your block heater (I don't have one either, but we both should)
