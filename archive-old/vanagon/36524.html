What a bunch of hooey.  Jacobs has a real strong reputation going over
on the Fordnatics list.  Several listmembers have used their systems and
reported no discernable increase in performance over good-condition
stock parts.  I believe one of the Mustang magazines even did an article
on them recently with similar conclusions.  The only good things I've
heard are that their parts are well-built and look pretty.  

Of course this is all heresay on my part, but if it were possible to
extract 14% more hp, 10% more torque, and 2-3 mpg from these engines
with better ignition systems don't you think VW would have done it?
VW engineers are not stupid.  Then there is the issue of peak HP and
torque vs area under the curve.  If a system increases my peak hp 14% at
5000 rpm but decreases hp at 2500 rpm, well, I wouldn't want it, most
likely.  I wonder how the Jacobs system affects the entire HP/Torque
curve?
