OK, here goes.  I have a 009 on my '70 Bus and have been running it for 7 
years
(about 90k miles) and have had very little trouble.  I also ran a 009 on my
'68 Bug for 10 years with very little trouble.  I have found that it takes a
little bit of time to get the 009 set up right(trial and error) but once it 
is set it
runs fine.  I also have found that the 009 does not run particularly well
with any of the stock Solexes above a 30 PICT-1.  I am currently running
my '70 Bus with a '70 case, single port heads,  30 PICT-1, stock (factory)
exhaust  and the 009 at about 6 Degrees BTDC static timing.  This is
a compromise on the timing. It would run a little bit better with more 
advance,
but I have experienced detonation with more advance at highway speeds
so I backed off.  I have good idle and no flat spot on transition.
Just my $.02.  Hope it helps.
