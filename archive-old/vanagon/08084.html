Subject: Water in gas tank

I have had some problems with my Vanagon choking up when I step on the gas, 
almost like it was running out of gas.  The electric fuel pump has been a 
little noisy the past few weeks, so I decided to check it out.  I ran the

not any damage has been done?  Besides changing the filter, is there
