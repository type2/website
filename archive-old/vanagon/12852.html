If I remember my ergononics lectures from all those years ago, pale grey 
with black markings was reckoned to be the most visible. I painted the 
gauges in my bus off white (matches the dash and steering wheel). They look 
great, especially at night when they glow an eery pale green. You can use 
Letteraset to re-do the numbers on the speedo, but I left mine blank with 
little dots to mark 30, 50 and 70 mph.
