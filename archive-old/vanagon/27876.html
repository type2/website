Construct a tester by using  a 0 to 100 psi water pressure guage
available at hardware stores, a 3/16" barbed hose connector with a
female 1/4" pipe thread that fits the guage, and a length of fuel
line to make a guage.

a fuel injection cleaner can be made from this set-up... search the 
'95 archives under 'houg and fuel and injection and cleaner'
