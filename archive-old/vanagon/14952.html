Yo Dudes. I used to be a cranky old crank on this list until I got the right
mail reader. (Remember the birth of the /f symbol back when I used AOL?)
Anyway, I discovered that the problem was the mail reader software in AOL
that made it time consuming to read or delete any messages. 

Now I use the fully liscenced version of Eudora and it is killer! That is,
it automatically sorts all my mail from 15 mailing lists into their own mail
boxes. Then, I can open any one mailbox (like my Vanagon mail) and tell
Eudora to first sort this mail by subject. Then I can delete all mail on
subjects I don't like and open all mail on subjects I do like. It takes just
seconds to read a day's worth of Vanagon mail in contrast to the old days,
when it took, like 30 mins. 

Using this software, it is actually faster for me to read the non-digest
version of the Vanagon list than to read the digest version.
