Had a great recirculation heater system on my '66 Micro Deluxe w/curio, 
though the heat was quickly lost through the ceiling. Buy a blower fan from 
a marine store and hook the intake to the "rear" heater box and the blower 
fan exhaust to the "front" heater box. This takes cold air from the floor in 
the rear, heats it twice and pumps it like crazy to the front vents. Nice 
heat.
