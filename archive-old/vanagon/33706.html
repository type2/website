 I've got seats out of a Rover, which are a little like well-stuffed
armchairs. I have no problems with the thick back rest but the thickness of
the squabs is a problem. In fact, most of the seats I have seen have squabs
that are thicker than the standard bus seats. My SO can't reach the pedals
properly but my 6'4" bod (most of it legs) fits quite nicely. A friend has a
pair of Recaros in his double cab and I am planning to get some similar
ones. They are very thin ( which gives more rome for adjustment) and hard
but much more comfortable than mine. Recaros are available here quite
cheaply second hand because a lot of European cars came with them as
standard. New ones are mega-expensive though.
