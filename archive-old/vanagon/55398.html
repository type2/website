It is probably the torque converter seal. Before you remove the
transmission check the differential fluid. If it is a normal brownish color
it is probably old and you should change it. If it is over full and the
fluid has a red tint to it the seals between the automatic portion and the
differential portions of the transmission are leaking and when the trans is
out you should replace those as well. I did remove the automatic portion of
a Vanagon trans without removing the differential. This was done by
professionals, do not try this at home. :) Replacing the torque converter
seal is a rather simple job. If the trans shifts good when it is full of
fluid it's probably OK. You may want to drop the trans pan and look for
chunks. HTH
