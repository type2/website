Allow the TGIF runner-up to announce that 'VW Motoring' is a 
superb read.  It's a British mag, covers all things VW (from 
air-cooled to ... anything beginning with Z.)  It's monthly 
with a useful problems page and, I'm delighted to say, covers 
Transporters and campers quite often.  I'm addicted to the 
writing style.  It is really well written.

Another one is 'Volkwagen Audi CAR' (capitals intentional) 
which covers much the same type of stuff.  I feel that it is 
more suited to the drivers of smaller Veedubs (Golfs, Jettas, 
Audis, etc.)

I can post the direct snail-mail addresses for these mags if 
you wish.  I'm sure that they would air-mail them directly to 
anywhere in the world.
