MXL's and MXL's with Reinforced Sidewall are not the same.  I did a lot
of "calling around"  and found many tire dealers to be clueless when I asked
about a "Reinforced Sidewall".  If you go by size and max weight, passenger
car tires (non-reinforced sidewall) will just make it against the gross
vehicle weight.  Clueless tire dealers will try to convince you that the
tire they have for you (not reinforced, of course) is fine.

In all my callings, I never heard of a MX4 with reinforced sidewall.
