i have seen buses get to the point that the sliding door will actually fall
right off, because the rocker is so rusty! So when you open the door make
sure that your feet are not underneath! I don't know how difficult it is to
replace that section of metal. My guess is that it takes a bit of work to
get the metal welded up and have the door slide properly. Other areas that
are typical for bay window busses to rust are

- behind front wheels (on the inside of the wheel well)
-at the bottom of the sliding door
-the battery tray
-at the bottom of the windshield
