rebuilt 12V with the necessary bushing.  I thought the starting troubles were 
over.  The starter turned the engine easily, but it took several minutes to 
start the engine (it was sitting all winter).  Here's the question/problem:

battery).

So, should I buy a new, more powerful battery to solve the problem or is it
