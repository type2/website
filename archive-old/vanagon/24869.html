Remember the copper-colored wire mesh that was on your mother's back
door screen? 1/8" squares or smaller, kept out flies and mosquitos,
but not your lawn dart? Mostly it's been replaced by plastic lookalike
stuff (unsuitable), but a few hardware stores still carry the metal
version. Call around, tell them what you want it for. Tape it in
place with the same duct tape. Mice will stat chewing, then get a big
surprise. For added excitement, that old 6V coil you never threw out...

the ladder frame members, up thru the holes where the seat belt anchors
