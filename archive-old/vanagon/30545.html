>B)  Is there any way to adjust the temperature?

If it is a stock installation, there should be a cable going from the
thermostat control box in the gas heater ductwork (to the left of the gas
tank) to below the front drivers seat (next to the push/pull button that
controls the valve that redirects heat from the main heat tube to the rear
of the bus).  If the cable is there and works, pulling the knob out will
mean more heat, pushing in will reduce temp.

>C)  What is the little orange switch/knob for?

It's a reset switch--works like a circuit breaker.  Use it if your heater
has shut itself down (can occur if the heater is on for awhile and fuel is
pumped, but there is no spark) or if the electrical circuit is shorted
(heater is exposed to some moisture from rain or snow). You use this switch
to reset it once it has been dried or after you find what caused the shutdown.
