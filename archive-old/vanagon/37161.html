Hi Rich.  For the rear heater there is a 3 speed fan switch next to 
the steering column on the right side.  This controls the fan speed 
only.  Under the rear seat there is a valve that you can adjust for 
warm, and warmer air.  It's quite efficient.  Move the lever to the 
front of the van for maximum heat.

Hope this help.
