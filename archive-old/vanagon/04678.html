I make it a habit to remove the lug nuts and tighten them myself at home so
that it would be easier to remove if on the side of the road.  Instead of a
lug wrench I carry a 1/2 inch breaker bar and a metric impact socket.
This works fine for me.  I have broken regular sockets trying to remove tight
lug nuts.  Also keep on hand some spare lug nuts and bolts.  I have broken
those when taking them off.  I came up with these ideas from autocrossing
where I change four tires with a windup jack in 20 minutes.
