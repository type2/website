Hello Griffin:

You may take my "advice and opinion" as just that, I'm not a mechanic but I
felt
I should at least offer.  I had an '84 that was drive several years without
a cat. converter.  Driving for a long time without the O-2 sensor or C.C.
means the engine ran rich  most of the time.  this shortens engine life by
among other things, alowing gas to dilulte the oil.  Have your dealer do a
compression test
while he is checking everything else.  I would say if you really like the
van and have the bucks, bite the bullet and do an engine rebuild now, maybe
the dealler will participate, given that he probably knew the engine was
bad all along.  If not, let him keep it and look for another in better
shape.
Good luck.
