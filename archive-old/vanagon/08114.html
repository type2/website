on a somewhat related topic, when i change the gear oil in my outboard
motor, the oil will look "milky" if water has mixed with the grease. It has
been my experience with gas and water that the water looks like droplets on
the bottom of a container. Gas and water mixed together kind of look like
what oil and vinegar salad dressing looks like before you shake it up.
