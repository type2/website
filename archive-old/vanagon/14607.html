Subject: The Tao of VW busses



Martha Rubin's Red Rock Trip Report reminded me of some reading I've
been doing about Taoism (specifically, Benjamin Hoff's books).  Driving a
VW bus is sort of an *attitude*.  (Gee, Bill Persig would be proud!)


To paraphrase something I read a while ago:


    The great teacher asked three of his students why they drive VW busses.

    The first replied "I drive a VW bus because it uses little gasoline."

    "Good", replied the teacher, "you have learned the virtue of economy."

    The second student said "I drive a VW bus because it carries everything
  I need."

    "Excellent" praised the teacher, "you have learned the lesson of form
  following function."

     The third student said simply, "I drive a VW bus to drive a VW bus."

     "Master, I am your student!" said the teacher.


 -Dyer
