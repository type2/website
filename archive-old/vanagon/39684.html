Phoenix Bug-o-Rama will be held  on Saturday April 13, 1996 (tomorrow) at
Firebird Raceway in Phoenix, AZ. Travel on  I-10 to the Maricopa Road exit.
Proceed about 1/2 mile to the west. Firebird Raceway and Maricopa Road is
located generally in the southeast portion of the Phoenix Metro Area. If
coming from Flagstaff or California take I-10 as if you are going to Tucson
(I-10 East bound). If coming from the south (I-10 Westbound) simply watch
for the exit as you approach the Phoenix metro area. $15.00 entry $5.00
parking  $30 swapspace wich includes one person's admission.
