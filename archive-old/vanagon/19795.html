If the tension adjustment fails (the stud pulls out of its mount), the stud may be replaced with a bolt by extending the stud hole through the top of the mirror mount.  The bolt may have to be bent slightly.  I have needed to do this on both of my mirrors.  The repair is fairly easy and it is permanent.
