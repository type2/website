     Yep. Sounds like you split the nose-cone mount on the tranny. I have
     never done one on the old split-case style trannies, so I don't know
     if you can cheat it in there without removing the engine. Maybe you

     mounts. The tranny does not have to be removed, just unbolted and
     shifted around. Also, if the engine is out, that is the second-easiest
     way to change the swingaxle boots, if nesc. (Easiest is when the
     tranny is on a bench!)
