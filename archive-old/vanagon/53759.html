a total loss, including most of my camping gear onboard. The vehicle was 
outfitted as follows:

	Fully loaded with all options (power everything, cruise, etc)
	Standard Transmission
	Alloy Wheels
	Custom Heidleburg Stereo with tape and security detection
	Flash Silver Metallic Paint

	Mint condition, never driven winters (garaged)
	25,025 miles - detailed records (every gallon of gas, maintenance, etc)
	Dealer maintained (at quite an expense!)
	about 50 miles on new VW battery ($107!)

	Add-ons included Custom Awning, Security system, VW/Vanagon "Bra"
