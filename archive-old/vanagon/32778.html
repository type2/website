You need to get the commercial version of Eudora which supports filters or
get the shareware Pegasus that does the same. All your list email will be
filtered into its own "Email In Box For Vanagon List" and your regular In
box will contain only mail addressed to you personally. That way, you can
quickly delete the 90% of Vanagon mail that does not interest you (based on
its subject) but be able to read all the mail addressed to you personally,
even if the subject line does not at first look interesting.
