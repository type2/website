Filling the water tank after being dry and NOT having running water 
is normal.  Let the water sit for a while and try it again.  The air 
in the pump has to be displaced by water (primed) for the pump to 
begin doing what it is supposed to do.  The pump should be start 
pumping water by the time you read this.  It only takes a few minutes 
for enough water to seep in there.

I've forgotten this more times than I care to admit!

Gunk in hoses that prevent water from getting to pump is also a 
potential cause.
