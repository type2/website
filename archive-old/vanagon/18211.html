What about cutting out the fibreglass bottom, but leave enough of a lip to 
fit  hardwood slats to? With the FG out of the way get a standard glass lid 
sunroof installed for <$300. Then put in the slats/wooden rack. Should be 
able to get enough height to open the sunroof one or two clicks. Add spacer 
blocks whatever. You'd get the inside 1.5" height for sure. High curvature 
in breadloafs makes generic sunroofs leak, so get a brand/model rec. from 
the list. You can't graft a sheet of plexi into the FG cutout unless you 
seal the entire rack watertight to the roof tin.  tim s.
