Subject: Puppy (Volvo)
Date: Wed, 4 Jun 97 13:30:03 PDT

Some time back I was rambling around one of my favorite desert haunts and 
came upon a truly interesting Swedish Military Vehicle being driven "around
the world" by two swedes.  It was a Volvo "puppy" they said.  ~20" rims
ragged and battered looking like a breadbox on wheels.  Most functional 
looking and with a 5x2Forward and 2sp reverse and articulated suspension
(read reduction gears) it probably could go whereever a unimog might but 
with much better fuel economy so they said.  Volvo turbo diesel power and
25mpg.  But man it was plug ugly!
