Subject: RE: Tan paste on inside of oil cap

Yes the oil light should come on with the ignition before the engine starts.  
The tan paste is a mixture of combustion gases from blowby and oil vapors 
mixing with water vapor.  Often called sludge.  Common in winter and older 
engines.
