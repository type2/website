Subject: RE: Temperature needle low: thermostat or sensor? 

Before I ordered a new sensor, I would pull the old one and check for corrosion.

Corrosion around the sensor where it screws into the engine will affect ground and can cause 
a change in gauge reading.

Simply clean it up, apply an antisieze compound, screw it back in and see what kind of 
readings you get.
