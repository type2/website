> What changed after '63?  Is that when they went to the larger-diameter 
> stub-axle sticking out of the xfer case?  As I see it, the rest didn't

In '63 the rear axle got bigger (nut uses 46mm socket instead of 36mm like
on Beetles.  Also, the inside diameter of the drums went from 230mm
diameter to something a little larger (240 or 250 or something).  Guess
that the bigger brakes had something to do with the 1-ton rating someone
mentioned.  But they all had stock 14" wheels since the mid-fifties, I
think.
