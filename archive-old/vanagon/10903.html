They show a picture of this quad, and it is SUPER NEATO!!!, although
it may take some time to get used to reading it.  It's 4 indicators
within one circle, white print, black background.  As on hour positioning
on a clock, the volts are at about 11-1, the oil temp about 2-4,
the cylinder head temp at about 5-7, and the oil press. at about
8-10.  I REALLY like the idea of having all that info in one such
small space.
