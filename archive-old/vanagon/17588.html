Try to get the power booster working, it could simply be that the hoses
aren't hooked up.  This is not really expertise, just hoses.  It's worth it,
regardless of whether you're stopping for 40 foot diesels or 3 foot goats.
Retro-fitting would be far more labor and money.  Most people are
retro-fitting to what you have already.

Bad situation sir.  Ball joints are not easy, but review your manuals and
see if it's do-able.  If it's really going that far outback, maybe give up
and remember to spray the front down with water every other trip.  I doubt
if it's easier to replace the entire front end, but, hey, I haven't done it
so I don't _really_ know.  With the right tools, anything is possible.
