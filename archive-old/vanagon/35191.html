Hey great! Welcome to the fold. Using this thing, you realize how much
larger inside it really is!

Now the bad news: one thing you might check is the weld joints at both the
top of the A pillar (windshield) and the bottom of the B pillar (behind the
front door). They sometimes have minor cracks in the paint which VW will
repair for free. (All EVs I've looked at have a crack in at least one of
these places.)

Also, if you have any money left, check out the Sidewinder II awnings they
sell for it. Seems to be one of the few awnings still available for it.
Pretty nice.
