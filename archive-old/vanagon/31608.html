There is a certain M-code for this option, it is called
something like "1.2 ton loadcapacity", this was mostly
ordered on pick-ups. If you have a cooperative VW-dealer
you can ask him to look for "M-code rearshocks", that's
what i did. For my '73 they were not available anymore,
(although you can probably still order them straight
from Boge, Bilstein or Fichtel & Sachs with the VW part#)
My dealer told me that the last known prices was around
750,- Dutch Guilders EACH.  750 : 1.65 makes us$454.55 !!!
