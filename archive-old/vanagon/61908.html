Loise,

Don't know what year you have, but I've noticed on our 87 that the wiring
around the relays (mounted on the left rear body at about eye height right
inside rear hatch) gets VERY hot when the AC blower and clutch are on, and
the insulation actually melted a little.   Looked to me that VW had
undersized the wiring.    On our van (a Wolfsburg), you have to remove the
covering panel, but since the panel is spaced out some from the wiring and
AC plumbing, there is probably some air circulation by convection (unless
the bottom is covered by clothes....)
I rerouted the wires to get more air around them and  cleaned one of the
relay socket connections that semeed somewhat dirty, but it still gets hot,
and I could believe that on a hot day with lots of insulation stuff around
that panel and the Ac turned on, things might overheat.      If this is
your problem, I'm not sure what the solution is, other than to replace any
burnt wires with heavier ones, and beef up the relays (they already look
pretty beefy...)

Dave
