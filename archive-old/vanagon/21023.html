I've installed piggyback's on my Rabbit and Van and have had no problems 
whatsoever with starting since the operations.  I used a small (fog lamp 
type) Bosch relay for the Rabbit and a large, generic Ford solenoid ($5 _at_ 
Checker) for the van.  Prior to installing the relays I had to jump both 
vehicles whenever they were hot.  YMMV but It's a fix I recommend to 
anyone having problems with getting current to a VW solenoid.
