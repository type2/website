			VW Fest c/o Rick Dennis

Hi VW Fans: 

We have the perfect place for your last campout of the summer. The 2nd
Annual VW Fest at Lazy Day campground in Mineola, MO - Oct. 20, 21, & 22nd
(3rd weekend). A real nice campground with heated restrooms, fishing and
all sites have electric and water. 

Octoberfest in Hermann, MO. On Saturday and / or Sunday we'll take a 17
mile scenic caravan to the Historic German Town of Hermann, MO where we
will partake in their world famous annual German festival, "Octoberfest",
so pack your sleeping bag, don on your lederhosen and hop in your VW for
an unforgettable time.

Directions. Mineola is between St.Louis and Columbia, MO on Hwy 70. Get
off at the Danville / Mineola exit at the mile marker 170. Go South on Hwy
J approximately 2 miles. It's on the Left side. You can't miss it! See ya
there!

For more information on the campout, you can call me at 314-623-7124.
