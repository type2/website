> i had a quick question about how i loose power on my 86 vanagon(terrorist
> van). and was told i loose power after warm up due to a bad O2 sensor. so i

> said for 70.00 they would show me how to wire a "generic O2 sensor" in place

BMW's use the 3 wire version of the O2 sensor.  What I discoverd is 
that the 87 Ford Mustang Bosch O2 sensor is the same one.  I buy one 
of those for about $55, splice it in, and everything is A-OK.  FWIW:  
many BMW owners on the BMW list do this w/ no problems.  I suspect 
that the Mustang sensor will work on the Vanagon too.
