Somebody asked privately...

"Fow Vay" is how a German pronounces VW.  But that would be its
spelling in English.  In German, I believe, if you spelled out the
letters, it would be "Vau Wee", or maybe it's "Vau We",but, understand,
still pronounced "Fow Vay".  (Classier than "vee dub", nicht wahr?)
