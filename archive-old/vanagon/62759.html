Kd,

Well, as suggested by folks on the listserv I got the Bentley manual and
was able to use it to fix the problem.  It ended up being a loose
connector which has the bundle of wires from the gauges from the tach/fuel
gauge side of the instrament panel.  It plugs into a receptacle about 4"'s
down.  After re-seating it, the gauges came back to life. 

Although the problem is fixed, it didn't seem to me that
the connectors snapped together the way I expected and I half-way expect
the problem to reoccur.  Anyway...
