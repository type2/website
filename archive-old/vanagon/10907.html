Deleted lots of good stuff about Red Rock and Vicinity.

I just checked the 1994 copy of Tom Steinststra's "California Camping".  He
says it is $7-9 a night to camp there.  Just thought you all might like to
know.

We are planning on being there!

>ABOUT THE CAMPGROUND:
>
>        The campground is large and flat, very well
>        groomed with lots of sites.  I doubt it ever fills up.  There are
>        lots of sites at the back up against the sandstone formations
>        that are really nice.  There are only pit toilets there, but
>        on my last visit there I was absolutely amazed that they did not
>        smell at all!!  Not even a little.  There is running water.  At
>        night, it is *very* dark and there seem to be lots of rabbits
>        running around.  Also lots of squirrels in the area that will
>        eat out of your hand if you let them.  I saw lots of tracks and
>        droppings from larger animals (fox? wolf? deer?).  There is also
>        a small ranger station there with a visitors center, but it seems
>        to be closed most of the time.  I don't remember exactly what
>        the campground fee was, but I'm sure it wasn't more than $6/night.
>
