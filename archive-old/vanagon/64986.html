The cheapest possible cause would be the valve cover
gasket(13%pobability).
Next would be the oil pressure sending unit.(27.2%)
Next is a pushrod tube cover.(22.3%)
Next  is front oil seal .(12%)
The most expensive would be rear oil seal or oil pump cover seal since
this reqiures the removal of a major item i.e transmision,crossbrace
etc..
