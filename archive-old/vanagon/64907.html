I've had my '95 out in high wind & rain storms in February along the Oregon
coast and it stayed quite dry inside. The canvas is coated with something
to make it more waterproof. Manual (Winnebago) recommends a vacuum or hose
down with a light brush. Dry before folding. And don't use detergents or
solvents that may remove the waterproofing.
