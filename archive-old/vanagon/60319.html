I think it is likely the Idle Stabilizer Control Unit.  It's located forward
of the right tail light and looks like a large black relay.  Remove the tail
light to get access to it.
This happend to my 87 last spring.  I opened the control unit and found a
burnt componenent on the circuit board.  Replaced it and another component (a
capacitor) near it and it functions flawlessly still.  Components cost me
about $2.00.  I don't have the details of what I did, but I described it
pretty carefully when I did the work.  My message(s) should be able to be
found in the 1996 archives.
