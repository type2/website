Anyway, was reading all the discussions on stoves and had to pipe in about
my recent purchase. $40 bought me a Coleman aluminum stovetop oven. It's a
cool heavy duty collapsing unit that folds up to fit just dandy under the
rear seat. With a center rack and an oven-door thermometer, it's definitely
large enough to bake a full size pie, lasagne, chicken, etc.

The only downside to the unit - for those of us with late model Westys - is
that to fit it on the stovetop, one must re-mount the stove cover stand arm
slightly so that the stove cover stands in a more vertical position when
locked open - a five minute job.
