I have seen 1972-1975 Buses with the Type I engine.  The important thing to
consider is that the transaxle be the same as for the 1968-1971 Buses. 
1976+ buses have a different transaxle.

You have to make new engine tin to seal off the engine compartment around
the engine.  I've seen people use ordinary sheet metal, cut to size, and
sealed with rubber roofing material.
