>  I downloaded the Service Bulletins and found
> two references to this coolant light problem so it must happen rather
> frequently.  I'll look into these bulletins further but I would like to ask 
> if anyone has experienced this problem and perhaps have a recommendation on 
> what I should try first.

Drain the overflow tank down to the max. line.  (You don't take the cap off)
Take the end of the overflow hose off of the expansion tank cap.
Put the end into a bottle, lower the bottle to below the coolant level...
when the coolant is at the appropriate level, raise the hose end again.
(however, if you've got the wrong coolant in you'll have to change it all
eventually.)
Pour the bottle into the expansion tank.

See how long it takes for the overflow tank to re-fill...
If you don't want to do this operation that often...
Sorry, you need to get your gaskets replaced and should probably get your
heads re-faced.
