the most compelling reason i've heard for changing the oil SOONER rather than
later was to get the contaminants (acids formed by combustion, dirt and crud,
all that stuff that makes the oil turn black) out of the engine. and this
was brought home by a couple who had a beetle convertible ... they bought it
for an 'investment' back in about 1978. and only drove it 3000 miles per
year, little short 3 mile trips to and from work. well ... they changed the
oil like the book said: every 7500 miles. :( and the engine rusted solid.
the acids and condensation and whatnot actually rusted the engine so bad
that the mechanics had to break the cylinders to get them off the engine
to rebuild it: the pistons/rings had rusted to the cylinders.

now, remember that's an extreme case ... :)
