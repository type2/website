Along with Drews comments I would grab some Autobahn coolant and
on one of those steep inclines I'd stop and bleed the coolant, 
topping off the overflow tank as I did so.  Be sure that you have
sufficient coolant in the expansion tank as well.  CAUTION: Don't
open the expansion tank with the engine still hot.  You might 
not like the surprise you get!  (Scalding Coolant)  Bleeding 
the system should help with over-heating, but as Drew said, 
check the thermostat and the water pump.  Also check for leaks
starting with the heads and then work your way forward.  If 
you have an aux heater, be sure to check that too.
