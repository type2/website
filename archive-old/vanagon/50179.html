The 2.1 liter cooling system will bleed quite well with the van level.  With
engine cold, open t-stat bypass.  rev engine to 2500 rpm with radiator
bleeder open.  Keep engine at that speed until air is out of system and
pressure cap is back on the bottle.  This should also be done with both
heater valves open so the heater cores will also bleed.  A second person to
close the radiator bleeder is helpfull.
