> That rubber piece is actually a check valve.  It plugs up with dirt that the
> A/C washes out of the air.  That piece should not be removed as it functions
> to keep air from flowing backwards through the tubes.  With the fan on high
> and the van traveling at highway speeds, enough air will flow through the
> tubes to keep the water from draining down.  A long trip with out stopping
> will make this evident.  Cutting a small notch in the flap will allow more
> dirt to pass and allow the water to drain faster but you should not remove
> the flap.  I often use my van for long trips and It took me two years to
> figure out why I still got rained on with the ruuber tips removed.  I now
> clean them twice a year.  Only takes a few minutes.
