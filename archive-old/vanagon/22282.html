All this talk about Arlo Guthrie and missing coolant has sent me into 
the old "way back machine"...I remember one night my very blue collar
dad took me to the local drive in for no special reason;  special for the
pop-corn and the company,  yes,  but a real weird double feature.

Imagine being around ten and watching "Vanishing Point"  followed
by "Alices' Resturant" with your dad at the local drive inn.  I do 
beleave that I had a profound change of life that night in the front
seat of the old Volvo 122,  and a strange new view of my dad.

Now for the coolant...'83 1.9 litre wasser boxer...great runner,  great
looker....no leakage of coolant any where....no "odor" of coolant from
the exhaust...plugs are a little grey {fuel additive}...coolant changed
yearly,  phosphate free.... VW says no leaks and exhaust analyzer shows
a healthy engine...why would I be loosing around 500ml of coolant
every 1500 miles or so???  It has never done this before in its
60K mile life.  Evaporation?  Bastard neighbors messing with my mind?
Am I worrying about nothing?  Help!
