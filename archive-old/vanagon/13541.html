I had a similiar problem with my 87' syncro at approximately 55,000 miles.
 The problem was with the left rear.  I changed it myself.  Changing the

interesting note about CVs...  after i replaced the rear one i started having

owner had four different ones on, the problem with the cv joints has not
occured in the past 15,000 miles.  Could having different tires in the front
and rear affect the read of the viscous coupling???
