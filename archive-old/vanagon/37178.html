to the epicenter of known VW knowledge (East Coast side) Henry's Garage where

"Henry, I said", I come on a quest, and have brought spices and ..... No, wait

champagne... "Shoot" he says, whereby I relate my sad predictiment about my

and proceeded to rip all the wiring/hoses/stuff out of the engine compartment
before I could stop him...
