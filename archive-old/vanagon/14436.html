The rear heater only recirculates, doesn't bring in fresh air. The rear fan 
motors in my 2 vanagons have been trouble free, unlike the fronts. The noise 
sounds like a pencil ot something sitting in the fan cage. Have a looksee. 
When the front motors start to seize they groan a lot. My panel van has a 
plastic box over the rear heater, comes off with screws, the other is 
exposed, but under the rear seat and behind a fibreboard cover.
        Prob # 2, You may have a loose piece of flexi-pipe in the dash or in 
the roof area by the door pillar . These join the air box bits to the 
distribution system. Remove the ashtray/glovebox and make sure all is in 
place. Is something blocking the tunnel in the passengers door?   tim s.
