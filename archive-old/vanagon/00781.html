Subject:      recalls/safety info 800 number

for those of you who want to find the National Highway Traffic Safety
Administration information about recalls on your buses/vanagons/eurovans,
or to report a safety problem (like the bushings, maybe?),

Auto Safety Hotline, 1-800-424-9393

you get an automated menu, 24 hours a day.  if you want to talk to someone,
you have to call back 0800-1600 EST, Monday-Friday.
