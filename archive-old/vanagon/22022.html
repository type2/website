after you take the screws for the door armrest off, the armrest will come
off. Then for the window crank it issort of encased in plastic;gently take a
bladed screwdriver and pry it back where the handle is connected to the
crank, it should pop off to expose a phillips screw. caution: it will not
come off completely so be careful not to bust it. then the door panel is held
on with little metal clips that "pop" into small holes in the door should be
about ten or so all around the circumferance of the door pahel. * these clips
are metal on my '70 Kombi, however, they could be made of plastic on your
late model, I am not sure. If they are made of plastic you will more than
likely break several of thyem, but they can be purchased at part stores or
salvage yards etc.  Peace.
