One thing.  Be sure that when you are trying to unjam it to force it in
the opposite direction you were originally trying to pull it with.  I'd
go to the hardware store and get one of those thin metal rods and push
it through the original pulling side and as deftly as possible, force the
blockage clear.  Be sure to put plenty of grease on the rod, but not too
much.  You want as much lubrication as possible, but you want manuvering
room too.  Boy this is getting to sound sexy. ;)  Anyway work at it with
that strategy and get a second person to help you at the other end to
snatch the wire as it comes out.
