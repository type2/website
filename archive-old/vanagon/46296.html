Dear List Members, 
 Due to the incredible traffic on this list and the fact that many people have
signed off because of the volume, and the time it takes to run it, I've
accepted an offer by Ken Hooper (bighouse_at_socomm_dot_net) to host an Aircooled Bus

will end up on the new list (called type2_at_primenet_dot_com), Watercooled owners
will stay on the Vanagon list.  If you have both, you'll be on both.  If you
are a WannaBus or Sniveling Unknown Lurker, then you will stay on the Vanagon
list.  This transformation will happen sometime in the next couple of days. 
We will be sharing more details soon. 

Please show Ken the respect and appreciation you have all shown me.
