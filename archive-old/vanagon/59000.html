Higher voltage charges battery faster and improves performance of lights, 
blowers, etc.  It also compensates for undersize wiring throughout vehicle.  
As for the voltage drop of those battery isolators, I use a relay to connect 
the auxiliary battery for charging.  BTW 13.8 volts is the ideal voltage to 
charge a 12 volt battery.
