Mike, This is a common problem with the Vanagon as I learned when I went
through the same thing. The seal may be in great shape, but in all liklihood
it is pulled away from the body of the van in at least one place. Look very
carefully all the way around the seal. On my Vanagon, it happened in the
upper, passenger-side corner. The fix is very simple. I simply ran a bead of
*clear* silicon caulk (basic household outdoor type) all the way around
(underneath) the seal. No puddles since. You may have to wait for a string
of successive dry days to allow the water that's already in there to dry.


Good luck & stay dry :)
