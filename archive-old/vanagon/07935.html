Paul,

I have a quantum syncro wagon and a vanagon syncro westfalia and I can 
tell you there is probably not one part that is interchangeable, except
perhaps for the bulbs and oil (and off course the cheesy vw doodads - 
lamp housings, ash trays, etc.)

The quantum syncro used the audi quattro style 4wd (in fact it really was 
just a poor man's audi quattro wagon - with an identical drivetrain), while 
the vanagon uses the viscous-coupled arrangement.
