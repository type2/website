Here's a little radio I tip I stumbled across a few weeks ago...

VW has a factory exchange policy on radios that are not covered under waranty. 
The FM reception on our '91 Jetta AM/FM cassette degraded gradually over a 6 
month period, and I determined the problem was not with the antennae. I was 
talking with one of the service guys at the local dealership (Bob Lewis VW in 
San Jose), and he says, "well for about 70 bucks you can get a new one". And 
that's what it cost, installed!

A possible avenue for you to check....
