On a carbed system, you have to step down once in order to get the 
linkage on the fast idle cam.  This will also shoot gas into the throat 
as a prime.  To get the carb out of fast idle, step down once after the 
engine's warmed up a bit.

At least that's how my '64 Chevy Impala worked...:)
