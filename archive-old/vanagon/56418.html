The door sticker on my '88 Westy says:

For 185r14 REINFORCED tires: 30/40 psi front/rear
For 195/75r14 6PR LT tires: 39/48 psi front/rear

So you ARE ok with 40 psi if they are REINFORCED. If they don't say 
it, they probably aren't (and should not be used).

btw: I ran my re-inf. tires at max pressure all round, as they were 
too soft. I now run LT load range C/D tires at 39-50 psi depending on 
conditions and loading.
