Sooooo, my oil light/warning buzzer started coming on at 2000rpm plus, when
the van was cold. It would go away within a minute or so, and wouldn't come
on at all if I warmed the car up for a few minutes (5 or so).

The oil light blinks, unlike the steady light someone posted about last
month. So it's registering an oil pressure drop as far as it knows.

I suspected the oil, which had gas in it at the time. So I changed the oil
(didn't change the filter). Still had the problem.

I suspected it was related to my power loss problem (which was causing my
gas in the oil). So I got that fixed. Still had the oil light problem.

I suspected a bad oil filter. So I changed the filter and the oil again.
Still have the problem as of this morning.

My next two or three areas of exploration, in order:
1. The oil pressure sensor plug.
