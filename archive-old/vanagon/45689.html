about 4 turns or 1/2 way in, and then seized. Not good enough.

JCW's response, not to my surprise, was exchange or refund? Rather than go
through this exercise for several more weeks, I pulled it off tonight to just
send back. I'll just try to find something locally. Too bad really since the
cooler itself is a slick item and fit the space perfectly.
