engine is based on the engine that I have in my car.  The EV engine is
bored out to about to 2.4 liters(from 2.1).   My car has been reliable,

years, head gasket at about 100k , timing belts every 60ketc.)  My car

too lazy to fix).  I bet the Eurovan is a nice rig, but check out the
flat rate hours for maintenance.  I'm not sure that I would want to
change the timing belt on a mid/front engine van.  My audi seems to get
anwhere from about 18-40 mpg, but I drive way too fast.(yes, it is
possible).
