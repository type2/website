My '87 Syncro has plastic pipes running from the engine area under the
belly to the radiator area.  One thing I noticed poking around under there
is that gravel tends to get caught between the body and the pipes, and
also between the pipes themselves (they're wired together on my van).

I try to clean off the gravel, so it doesn't get a chance to wear a hole
or weak spot in the plastic.  I'm pretty they were OEM on my van, unless
they were replaced in one of the recalls.
