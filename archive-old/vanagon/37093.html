Yesterday I replaced the grabbag of tires on the 81 camper with a set of
Continentals in the correct 185r14 with reinforced sidewall. Price was $350
out the door which was probably $100 more than I wanted to spend but about
$75 less than the comparable Michelins. BTW, one dealer told me that the
Michelin was available only as a "consumer replacement" meaning that they
didn't want to sell a set, only singles!

was inherent in a van was corrected by the Conti's. Big difference.]
