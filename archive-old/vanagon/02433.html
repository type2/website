well ... ha! i have ONE (1) unbuilt revell model (from mexico, no less) of
the vanagon left! there are four  completed versions on my mantle (a camper,
a panel van, a regular bus, and a double cab pickup) and one incomplete
version (a shorty ... well, i just never got around to finishing it). if
anyone finds these things, i'd like to get some more.

now, as to the 'only one', there is a company called Schabak that makes a
1/43 scale series of vanagons. they are all syncros (just means that the
gas cap is in the rear) and cost about $10-15. the vw dealer might be able
to still get the vanagon one. i've found them at a hobby store in atlanta.

and there are other companies (Herpa, Rocco, and Wiking) that make tiny
HO scale (1/87) vanagons of various types ... pickups, high roofs, panel vans,
but no campers!! at least, i've not found any. :(
