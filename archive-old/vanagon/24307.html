We have the Ford/Chrysler solenoid in both of our busses. Because of
the length, hence the increased resistance, of the wire that goes
from the ignition switch to the starter, the extra solenoid saves
crawling under the bus to use a nickle or a quarter to get it started
on the days when the starter is stubborn. We highly reccommend Muir
solenoid trick.
