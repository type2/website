   Mark,

   Sorry to hear about your engine!
   I was recently in the market for an engine for my '84 GL.  I did not find
any working engines in Portland, OR, auto recycling yards.  One place had
sold one recently for $550.  The Vanagon it came out of read 200,000 plus
miles. I don't think I saw a newly rebuilt engine for less than $1,300. And
the range of prices I found, went up steeply to $4,000.
   I decided to rebuild from 2 blown engines (with different areas "blown"),
and many new parts.  So far, 300 miles on rebuildt, so good.
