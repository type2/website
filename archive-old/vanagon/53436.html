Dave;
If it was me I would try to run up the temperature as much as possible.
Drive it at interstate speeds for about forty minutes, pull off
somewhere safe and let it continue to idle. Open engine access and watch
for bubbles in the expansion chamber. Look under neath for leaks. Try
front and  rear seat heater if it has one. Do you smell anti-freeze?
Check for white smoke. Check the oil dip stick for precence of a white
film. 
Good Luck
