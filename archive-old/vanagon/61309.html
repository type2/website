      It's really easy to put an input shaft in. Assuming it is similar
      to the 1986 and up. The bell housing comes off, there is a c-clip
      on the shaft next to a coupling, after you remove the clip, the
      spline coupler will slide on the shaft to allow the shaft to turn
      free from the trans input shaft. NO need to remove the rear end
      gears.
