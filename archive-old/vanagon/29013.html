i found that one of those kryptonite u shaped bicycle lock fit nicely
through the sliding door handle and i then could lock it to the passenger's
outside front door handle. the lock is all covered in rubber so it didn't
scratch the paint. you will need the extra long version of the solid metal
kryptonite lock for this to work.

thus to break into you bus via the sliding door or pass. door they will
need to bust that bike lock.
