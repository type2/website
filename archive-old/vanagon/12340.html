> The Blaupunkt speakers on vanagons are actually very good speakers. The only
> problem is that they are small and sound poorly on frequencies below 250 hz
> (no bass).
> 
> What I did was I bought a pair of 6 1/2 inch subwoofers. I flush mounted
> them below the original Blaupunkt speakers and changed a little bit of the
> wiring. The stereo output will drive the subwoofers directly and a capacitor
> in the range of 150 uf is connected in serial with the Blaupunkt then
> in parallel with the subwoofer. The capacitor is actually two 300 uf ones
> in serial with polarity opposite to each other.
> 
> The result is great. The Blaupunkt is really great when it is driven without
> bass. The subwoofer produces plenty of bass. The combination is great.
> In fact I can play music very loud. There is no distortion at very loud level.
> 
> This is how it looks:
>                                  300uf  300uf
>                                  +        +
>                  o-----------#----||----||----#
>                              |                |
>                              |                |
>          stereo output    Subwoofer       Blaupunkt
>                              |                |
>                              |                |
>                  o-----------#----------------#
>
