The AUDI Turbo rims are sized to   6.5 J 15 ET 45  and
I would love to put them onto my Camper. But the
German safety standards do not allow machining rims and
use them on public roads. Check the maximum allowed
load of these rims, they maybe different from the
Vanagon standard.

BTW: ET means 'Einpress Tiefe' which is the difference
between the vertical centre line of the rim and the
mounting surface in mm. The AUDI rims are going very
deep into the wing und you have to use 10 mm spacers
to get nearly the same fit as standard rims.
