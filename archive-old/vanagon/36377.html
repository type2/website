Repairing fuel system related problems on modern spark ignition 
automotive engines can be a nightmare. The manual is often not detailed
enough on all the sensors and electronics. A good source of information
is the emmission tuneup specifications. See for instance "Mitchells"
tuneup guide. You should be able to find this one in a good library
and copy the sections related to your EV (and your daughters Volvo).
