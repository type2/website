There is an ad in the october issue of Hot VWs on page 18. It's called
Instaheater and is basically an electric heater blower with a magnet so
you can attach it to any steel surface inside the car.It comes in 4
different versions: 360, 540, 720, and 960 watts and cost between $189 and
$259. Phone # is (718) 476-1723. I'm not sure this will be sufficient for
a van, though.
