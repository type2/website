While in Washington, try going up north of I-90 and try the State Route =
20, you can find this off of I-5 in Burlington (about 50 or so miles =
north of Seattle).  It is a part of the North Cascades Loop Hwy and is a =
GREAT road!  Lots of  views and lots of neat campgrounds.  Also I =
suggest NOT driving the interstates as much as possible.  I just got =
back from a trip to Nebraska from here in Mill Creek Washington and =
managed to only hit 25 miles of interstate on the way over there, and =
was the Best trip over to grand ma's that I ever had!  Over 1775 miles =
of two lane roads.  I saw every thing from buffalo to coyotes and a =
billion odd deer, elk, you name it.  Try picking up the Delome map'n'go =
program it is a great way to find little traveled roads worth seeing.   =
