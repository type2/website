I've sucessfully used a Thule roof rack system for carrying bikes & a canoe
(sometimes at the same time) for years.  I would reccommend them.  I carry
with me a nice aluminum step ladder that helps alot.  The ladder is bigger
than a kitchen step stool but much smaller than a real step ladder.  It folds
to less than 2" thick and I usually stow it under the cushion over the
engine.  For a Vanagon you would use what Thule "high foot" mounting bracket
and then a pair of 65" load bars.  Attach to the load bars whatever mounting
parts that are applicable to your load.  Thule makes adapters for bikes,
skis, snowboards, canoes, etc.
