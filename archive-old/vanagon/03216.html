conditions no matter how abusively I drive. I've only heard the electric fan 
kick-in a few times in summer-stationary-rush-hour, and that goes away fast

only takes about 15 seconds to put the needle past HOT-YOU-IDIOT and make 
the little red eye wink at me furiously. Partly plugged lines 
(corrosion-crud and metal-chips?) will push your temp up to a higher 
steady-state reading without terminal meltdown, soak/flushing helps then.
