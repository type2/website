There is NO timing belt on an 86 Wasserboxer.  It uses a 'Timing Gear' that
id driven driectly from the camshaft and keeps time for the distributer.  The
gear usually will outlast the life of the engine and requires no service.  It
is similar in design to a pinion gear in a standard american car
differential.
