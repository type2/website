Get yourself down to Sears and go to the camping section and pick out
the porta-potti that best fits your needs.  No bus/camper should be
without one.  It's real nice to not have to leave the camper and 
just go.  Open a window for some ventilation if needed.  Also, on
long road trips it's nice not to have to make a bathroom stop every
time someone has to go.  Just close the curtains and go.  If you
need more privace, just take a large cellophane garbage bag and make
it into a poncho and that'll hide everything.

Don't forget to by the green chemical stuff for the collection chamber.
