btu heater all night, plus all the dinner/coffee cooking for those 3 nights.

same location (there's room for it) so now I have enough capacity for about
a week, running the heater each night.

I feel I have adequate propane supply. However, note that: 

1. You can make up plumbing so on an emergency basis, after the two big
tanks are dry, you can use those small 1 lb throw away propane cylendars
they sell. Adapters are available for this.

2. Adventurewagon when they sold Vanagon Campers of their own making fitted
oversize horizontal tanks. Perhaps this is what you already have, or you
could call the dude (Fort Bragg, Ca) and ask him what size fits.
