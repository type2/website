The O2 sensor is not working.  CO should be maintained below 1% at all times.  
Full throttle should bring it up to about 1.5%.  The rang you show indicates 
no regulation, or the ignition timing is too far advance causing air 
starvation at idle to maintain low RPM. These measurements are before the CAT. 
 After the cat, if all is okay, CO and HC should barely be measurable.
