On a GEX rebuilt aircooled (82 Vanagon 2 l) with about 5000 miles on it 
we averaged about 22 mpg on the highway (from CA - Hoover Institution to 
Carleton University Canada). Mileage will improve over 
time, but a bigger concern is to make sure there are no manifold leaks 
and that the engine is "tuned" properly.  PS try either Fred's Garage in  
Redwood or Greg's VW in Menlo Park for superb and inexpensive VW service.
