>Also, has anyone ever
>seen a Bosch "019" distributor or know what it's for?

The 0231-129-019 is the Bosch factory replacement for the "010"
(0231-129-010) Distributor used originally on 56-59 Buses and was the basis
for the first "Screamer" performance distributors Bosch marketed.  It was
replaced by the "009" Distributor in the late 70's.  A nice find if it's in
good shape.
