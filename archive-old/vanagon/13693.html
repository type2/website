I pretty much skimmed the earlier discussion about these new Eurovan 
conversions because I am really not terribly interested in a Westy type 
vehicle these days.  This weekend I attended an auto show, and saw the 
Winnebago Westy type conversion.  It looked very similar to what has come 
before and had a sticker price of about $30,000.

However, what surprised me is that there was another Winnebago Eurovan 
conversion at the show as well.  This was a much larger vehicle that was 
truly a small motorhome, including a 110V generator etc..  Somehow, I can't 
imagine the 2.5 liter engine even making this thing move, since other 
vehicles of this size have hefty V8's.  And, it only came in an automatic!
