Ned (Rothstein, I think) was asking about EV tire options.  I recently
bought a set of reinforced sidewall Michelin MXL 205/65R15 tires from NTW

difference in ride noise level.  If you go with NTW, you'll have to convince 
them that a reinforced sidewall MXL exists, and also wait a few days for 
them to order them.
