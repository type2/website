Subject: Westy tent - setting up

I just got my '80 Westy this past winter, so over the past month or so I've 
been doing my first camping with it.  I love it!  Former owner threw in a 
Westy tent in the sale - he said it was in demand and worth $.  I thought 
"yea, sure" - little did I know.  This thing is great!  It looks like new 
(hangs from heavy metal frame with orange, blue & white striped door) and it 
doubles the usable enclosed space.  Anyone who camps should beg, borrow or 
steal on these things... but you can't have mine :-)  

The only problem I had was attaching the tent to the raingutter.  This was 
QUITE a chore.  Was I doing it wrong?  I attached after setting up tent - 
should I have attached this first?  Is there a trick to this or is it always 
a bear?  Maybe the rubber strip was just stiff from not using it.  Anyway, 
any advise appreciated.

BTW - On a sadder note, I've noticed some fluid stains on the floor under 
the pedals.  Looks like my clutch master cylinder is leaking.  Clutch still 
works but it looks like I'm gonna have to replace it.  Oh well, these small 
things are worth it - I LOVE THIS BABY!!
