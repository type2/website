near PA there is a nice store called Strictly Foreign that has them.  They
come in two variteies.  Drop in H4 Hella Config and Drill in for others.

German Plate and the "D" shield.  They draw very little current and can be
easily tapped into the parking lights.  Or a Relay and switch could be
used, just a lot of extra wiring.  They give off a dull white light that
looks really neat.  I only use mine as DRL's.  Any questions I will try and
