all-VW conversion" which understates the glory of the thing a good deal

ured very much like the VW four.

In other news, I passed NJ inspection this morning -- no problem except

jig was up when the inspector got his mirror-on-a-stick and looked at
the muffler.  Whatever he was looking for, he apparently found it, and
I'm street-legal for another year.  The original VW engine, sitting in
the garage in case of need, can wait at least 12 more months.
