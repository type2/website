Jim,

I've got an 82 Scirocco w/ CIS - it showed similar symptoms, and the 
PO had already replaced the warmup reg., so I tried the injector 
O-rings - it's been running great ever since! Cheapest fix ever - 
about $3 ! 
The injectors are hard to get out - I clamped carefully with pliers & 
pried *straight* out. The O rings become brittle from the heat after 
a while & no longer seal well.
