        Dear Pete:
        
        The "S" switch changes the shift points in your automatic 
        transmission.  The "S" stands for "sport" according to my owner's 
        manual. I have used it sparingly because it increases the 
        "windout" time and lowers my gas mileage.  There is some sort of 
        electric gizmo (Joel help me here) that the switch activates to 
        accomplish all of this.
        
        If your 93 has a tach, try driving with the light off first then 
        the light on and watch the difference in rpms.  It was especially 
        inefficent in local traffic (lots of rolling hills) so I rarely 
        use it.
        
        If I remember correctly, you have the Weekender package in your 
        93.  I got your address earlier but have not had time to ask for 
        your opinion about it.  E Vans are rare around here and that 
        package is practically nonexistent.
