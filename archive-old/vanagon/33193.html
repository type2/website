I recommend the Die Hard, but NOT the Die Hard Gold, get the standard
one which is in a black case with multi-colored lettering.  I'd get the
biggest one that will fit, but see below first...

> Before I buy the new battery, I'm looking for suggestions for keeping
> the snow off the top of my battery.

Sure!  Pick up a battery box.  I got mine from West Marine for about 10
bucks.  This will also keep your battery tray from rotting out.  I would
first buy the biggest battery box that will fit in that space (group 27
box may require remounting some things elsewhere) and then get the
biggest battery that will fit in the battery box.
