The price is excellent--I usually charge $125 for a BN 2, but it is not the
correct unit for your bus. The correct German heater is the early BN 4 with
the floatbowl fuel regulator (I have about 85% of one if anybody is
interested).  It is a larger unit with almost twice the heat output.

If you are not woried about authenticity, have converted to 12V, and do not
anticipate severe cold, the BN 2 will work just fine.
