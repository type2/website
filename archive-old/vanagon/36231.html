This is straight from the Robert Bentley Official Service Manual:

Fuel Injected (L-Jetronic) 1975-1979 Type IV Engines:

Idle speed: (through 1978): Manual trans: 900 RPM plus or minus 50
                            Auto Trans: 950 RPM plus or minus 50
Idle speed: (1979 and later) Manual trans: 875 RPM plus or minus 75
                             Auto trans: 925 RPM plus or minus 75

Points gap: .40 mm to .50 mm (.016 in. to .020 in.)

Dwell angle: new points - 44 to 50 degrees; used points - 42 to 58 degrees

Plug gap: .70 mm (.028 in.)

Firing Order: 1-4-3-2

Ignition Timing:
1975 and 1979 and later California: 5 degrees BTDC with vacuum hose connected
1976 and later non-California: 7.5 degrees BTDC with vacuum hose connected

CO volume % at idle:
1975, 1976: 0.2% to 2.0%
1977, 1978: 0.9% to 1.1%
1979 (except California): 0.5% to 1.5%
1979 California (w/ oxygen sensor disconnected): 0.2% to 1.2%
