Banging on the brakes drums with a hammer should work.  Similar 
problem with my '67 sqbk in the rain.  Rust formed between the 
bracket from the handbrake and the brake plate.  Cleaned the 
components and applied very light grease to contact areas.  Amatuer 
solution though.
