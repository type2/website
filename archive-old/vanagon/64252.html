derek,  not to question your expertise in this regard because you have
been around these cars longer then i have,  but is there something
different about vanagons that would make them prone to ignition switch
failure from these bulbs?  i have used these bulbs with no relays for
somewhere around 12 yrs without a single problem.  this includes 5

seperately fused.  does anyone have any data in this regard?
