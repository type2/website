Make sure this t-stat is an exact replacement.  Especially the small bypass 
disc on the bottom.  I've seen these slightly oversized causing them to get 
stuck in the housing.  By the way, if you are heating in traffic, a lower temp 
t-stat will do nothing for you.  The problem is lack of air flow.  I carry low 
temp fan switches that will turn the fan on earlier which is what you need.  
80C (172F), is a little on the cool side.  Especially for winter use.  I would 
stick with the stock stat.
