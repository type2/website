Final reminder:

There will be a pizza dinner on Thursday, December 22nd at 7 PM at Kapp's
(formerly known as Stuft Pizza), 191 Castro, Mountain View, California. I
asked about the name change, but they assured me that it's still the same
owner and there have been no other changes. Their phone number is (415)
961-1491. 

All Vanagon list members and their families and friends are invited to
join in the fun. This is your chance to show your 4-wheeled pride and joy,
the real thing or pictures. Bill Abbott will bring his camera, so give
that bus a high buff and be ready to smile:)

I made reservations for 10-15 people under "vanagon list," our company
name.  Kapp's informed me that there will be a minimum charge of $4 per
head. 15% gratuity will be added and they expect to give us only one bill.
(We'll have to decide who gets to charge it to their Shell/Amoco
MasterCard and earn all that free gasoline :-) .)

For directions, here are, once again, both Patrick Tower's and Dave
Kautz's explanations: 

>Get yourself onto 101 (hopefully people can figure out how to get there
>from their various home-bases), and take the Moffet Blvd. exit toward
>Mountain View (as opposed to toward Moffet Field).  This exit is just
>south of the 85 interchange, to give you a point of reference.
>Follow Moffet until it crosses Central Expressway.  You will now be
>magically transported to the 100 block of Castro (the street changes
>names at Central).  Stuft will be near the end of the block on the left.

>From 280, I think it would be best to get off at El Monte, head east down
>to El Camino, turn right and go south to Castro, where you would turn left
>and find Stuft on your right 4 or 5 blocks up.

Remember, though, it's now called KAPP's!

Looking forward to seeing you there!
