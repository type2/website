Mark -

The repair was very easy.  There is a metal post that attaches to the chair
with two bolts.  If there is no post you need to get one through VW or a
junkyard.  If the post is there you just slide the arm rest on and secure with
bolt.  I can look-up the bolt number if you don't have it.

10 minutes plus a trip to the hardware store.
