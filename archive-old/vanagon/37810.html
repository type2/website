I have an 85 GL and installed 5.25" Sony "mids" in the lower part of 
the door panel and Sony tweeters behind the factor grills.  This 
improved the sound up front incredibly.  They are only driven by a 4 
x 20 watt Blaupunkt amp too!  For the rear, I replaced the factory 
enclosures with Blaupunkt surface mount RL4500B's driven by a 2 x 40 
watt amp.  Then to finish it up, I made an enclosure with (2) 6" subs 
to fit underneath the middle bench driven with a 100 watt mono amp.  
I used PAC active crossovers from Crutchfield to "cross" the system 
because I could most easily fit them behind the dash.  
  
The sound is fabulous and actually better in many respects than my 
BMW with more sophisticated aftermarket equipment.  If you would like 
any details, let me know.  The speakers and amps were not high end 
components but they sure sound like it.
