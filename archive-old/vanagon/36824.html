	Chris,
	Before you do anything drastic check the oil pressure sensor on 
the left side of the engine (from the back) in front of the distributor. 
I had a dripping, leaking oil leak only when the engine was running. 
>From underneath it looked like it was dripping from around the oil 
filter. But it was actually bubbling out of the top of the oil pressure 
sensor. All I did was replace the sensor ($6.00) and reattach the wire 
to it and it was solved. I don't know if that is your problem, but check 
out the simple (and less expensive) solutions first..
