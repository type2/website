Aaron J. Wood writes:
> 
> The pilot bearing on my 1700cc crank is stuck in the end of the 
> crankshaft.  I need to remove it because it looks dirty and I want to 
> clean it.  How do you get it out?  The pilot lubricating felt is 
> missing.  Can you make one yourself, or must it be bought?

Rent or buy a puller and get that puppy outta there.  Then replace it
with a new one.  Only about $5.  The puller you want should have an
attachment for *inside* pulling, or inner circumference, or something
like that.  Usually two small metal "fingers" that go in behind the
bearing and expand.
