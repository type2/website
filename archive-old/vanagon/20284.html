Keep a close eye on those valves,if they tighten again you're due for a 
valve job or new heads. Your valves are stretching and will break soon 
causing all sorts of expensive motor work. The little circular things you're 
finding are valve seals and they don't do a heck of a lot.By the way they 
were soft rubber at one time.
