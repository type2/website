No, really.  The 74 tranny will work in the 77, but why would you want to
swap them?  The '74 has a lower final drive ratio, so you won't be able to
go as fast in each gear with it, but you will accelerate quicker.  Some
things to be careful of - you will have to swap the bellhousings on the
trannies because the 77 has a larger flywheel and will hit the '74
bellhousing, and the starter won't fit unless you do.  Also, the mount at
the front of the transmission may be different, so look at that also.
