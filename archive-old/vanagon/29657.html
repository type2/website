Agree whole-heartedly with the *clean the threads and lube
it good* concept.  Another hint is to hold the bolt in place
with the blade of a carpet knife while *finger starting* the
nut.  The blade is just the right size to fit the grooves of
the thread, and if too much pressure is needed to start the
nut, the blade breaks unstead of messing up your threads.
