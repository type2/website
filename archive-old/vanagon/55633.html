the quad round headlight / driving light grille is just that, - round.

that's why you need the metal headlight buckets off an '85 down Van, if yours is
'86-'91. 

The '80-'85 US single round headlights are dual Hi / Lo beam. 

the smaller driving light buckets are integrated into the plastic SA grille. 
 
All '80-'91 grilles mount with basically the same tabs.
