VDO used to make an adapter plate that replaced the 2 hole metal plate on the
bottom of the crankcase.  Another option is to replace the oil filter bypass
with a direct fit sensor that VDO also makes.  On Vanagons, I've driiled and
threaded the releif valve plug and screwed the sensor into it directly.  All
these options work great.
