Although the idle stabilizer can cause this problem, I would first check the
throttle valve switch located underneath the throttle body.  I had a idle
problem that occured recently in my 87' Syncro.  The problem ended up being a
defective throttle switch which could not be adjusted to get the correct
switching point gap.  The routine for checking and adjusting the switch can
be found in the Bently Manual.
