I used over sized washers on the inside and I used short bolts.  Long bolts
will catch on the tent when you open the top.  I _did_ use silicone.  I
spread it outside, inside, and in the hole before putting the bolts in.  I
even spread a bead around the edge of the mounting plate before pressing it
to the fiberglass.
