if you pull up slowly on the parking brake (from a fully DOWN position), and
you count the click-noises ... if it takes more than 3 or 4, you need (1)
the parking brake cables adjusted, or (2) new rear brake shoes.
now one really simple thing that everybody forgets to do is to adjust the
self-adjusting rear shoes. ;)  supposedly (i can't tell if it makes any
difference, if you want to know the truth!), you are supposed to put the car
in reverse, and back up at about 5-10 mph, then hit the brakes really hard.
do this about three times, and that's an "adjustment".

like i said, i can't tell if it makes any difference. i've replaced the rear
shoes at 80,000 miles, even though they still had a lot of material left,
just so i could get more parking brake (and cause i was kinda paranoid about
six-year old brake shoes ... i mean, they ought to be wearing out, don't you
think??? :)
