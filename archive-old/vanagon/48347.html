Extreme heat, cold, and chemicals (atmospheric pollutants) all conspire to 
destroy rubber and vinyl. UV in sunlight also contributes. The more you 
protect against these elements, the longer your rubber and vinyl will last. 
Periodic use of silicone-based conditioners will prolong the life of this 
stuff.
