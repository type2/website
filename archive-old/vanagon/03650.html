Ditto that. You should consider buying gauges in this order of importance:
oil pressure; oil temp; head temp. BUT in any case do not disable your
idiot lights as they serve a purpose which gauges cannot. Also if you feel
squeemish about gauges there is a head temp sensor which attaches to the
idiot oil light. Also there was DIY description for something similar in the
Vanagon gopher (but I think he's got the head temps way too low). if you
