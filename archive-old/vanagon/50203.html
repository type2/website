I know of a local fellow who converted his '86 Vanagon to a Westy. He 
installed all of the interior parts (they simply bolt on), and then cut a 
hole in the roof and put on the pop top and the top bed.  The factory 
design has one of the curved roof beams cut and replaced with a flat beam 
(which, I imagine, would be difficult to do yourself).  However, this owner 
simply left the factory roof beam there .  The bed sat behind it, and he 
said it worked fine that way.
