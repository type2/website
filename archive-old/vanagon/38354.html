I can relate somewhat to this issue - my brother's '69 bus burnt to 
the ground on a backroad near Horsefly, BC (yes, it exists!) a few 
years back, and with it most of his belongings. He had just returned 
from a 6 month trip to India, Tibet, Nepal, etc. and was on a tree 
planting trip when it happened. The bus was part of the family (heck,

never did see :(  

This may have taught me not to neglect the important items on a
