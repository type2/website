board in front pipes for the rear seat (I remember people saying you have to

scaling on the floor of the assembly. The whole job took about 15 minutes to
pull the thing off (excluding the 15 minutes I sat wondering if I could get
the cover off without removing the whole seat... I'm kinda lazy...).
