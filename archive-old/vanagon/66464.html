Subject:      Big time idle problems , Solved!

Hello all,

Just wanted to say thanks to all those who helped me with the idling
problems my van was experiencing.  I finally had to give in and bring the
van into my local repair shop.  After 6 hours of testing everything on the
van (_at_ $50 an hour), they found a small tear on the bottom of the intake
bellows.  They put a new one in and the van idles great.
The knocking sound turned out to be the exhaust pipe hitting the engine
support.  This is due to a combination of bad engine mounts and lack of
exhaust supports.  The question here is, can you change out the engine
mounts without dropping the engine?  If so how.
