Subject:      TGIF: Meet Me In St. Louis (song parody)

I'm a St. Louisan. I lived there for 21 years straight before moving to
Iowa for a few months and then to Florida (where I am now.) I regret that I
wasn't able to make it to BBTA -- it would have been great. Perhaps next year?

"By The Arch" is a misnomer, by the way -- the arch is 45-60 minutes from
the actual location of the event. But it's *not* to be missed if you're at
BBTA -- and a few other places that might be of interest are the Car
Museum, the Transport Museum, the Science Center (don't miss the IMAX
movies!), and Forest Park.

The St. Louis home page is located at http://www.st-louis.mo_dot_us/.
