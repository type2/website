	Yup, but not in '63.  The Type III offered VW's first genuine
automatic transmission in the '68 model year, along with the first
massed-produced electronic fuel injection (Bosch D-Jetronic).  Also,

the U.S., so they're not so easy to come by in these parts.  In fact,
Type IIIs didn't get their official intro here until '66.
