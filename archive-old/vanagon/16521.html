I wouldn't bother if I were you.  In the seven years I have been camping
in my 73, that space behind the passenger seat has become very dear to me,
and I just recently ripped out the stove-fridge unit that was there and

and tired of having the entrance to the sliding door blocked.  Of course,
if you are trying to restore the camper to original, then disregard all of
the above.  :)

I have seen lots of those units in the junkyards over the years - if you
check them often you will find one eventually.  Be persistant!
