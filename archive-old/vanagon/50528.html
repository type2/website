My '82 pulls but only (I believe) because I need an alignment, a good set of properly inflated 
tires and probably some bushings on the upper control arms. Does anyone have a reasonably 
priced source of these? VW dealers want about $70.00 ea (need 4 to do both sides).. pain! Napa 
Dealers carries them for about $40.00 ea. This is still too pricey for what they are! When 
these go you get clunking when you brake or go over rough terrain. After replacing these on my 
'84 and putting on some nice LT tires (properly inflated of course) I had the front end aligned 
and it goes quite straight (assuming I'm on a really flat road without a crosswind). It is 
sensitive... but it does go straight.
