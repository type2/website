I, too, just called GEICO.  I asked for a quote for my '83 Westfalia and
'88 Olds Regency 98.  Based upon the serial numbers they quoted for 6
months:
		Rated on Serial #		Rated as Motorhome
Westfalia		$373			$331
Olds		$355			$438

The quote on the Olds skyrocketed, because if the Westfalia is rated as a
motorhome, it causes the quote on the Olds not to qualify for a multiple
"car" discount.  

Go figure!!!!
