	Mercy...the Oregon Coast.....Heaven for WanagonCampers.

	The coast was my favorite weekend destination (I've recently sold my 
	'91 Camper).

	Yes, several OSP campgrounds are open year round. I'm mostly famaliar
	with the central coast (Lincoln City down to Coos).

	South of Lincoln City, Beverly Beach is the biggie.

	South of Florence, Honeyman is the biggie.

	South of Waldport, Beachside is a not-so-biggie.

	You can pickup an OSP directory at any park and that denotes parks
	open all year.

	I never had a problem getting a space after October.

	Where abouts on the coast will you be?
