Its best to check the simple things first:

1) is the fuse blown? (on my Westy the fuse is located at the bottom of
the column, behind and to the left side of the drivers seat)
2) is there voltage at the wire connected to the fuse?
3) trace the wire from the fuse to the fridge. Is there voltage there?
