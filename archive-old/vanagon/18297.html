I don't have a Westy pop top, but the 3/4 (starts right behind the 
driver cab) top designed by County Homes, CA, that is guided by crossbars 
on each side, and goes up straight. Everything is worn out a bit, and the 
front of the top started to vibrate and rattle on the roof at highway 
speed. My quick and dirty solution was to use two 1 inch wide straps with 
metal clamps that hook into the rain gutter and hold the front and back 
of the top down while driving. I am thinking on permanently installing 
roof racks, and might change the design and bold the racks to the top - 
anti-theft device and, when the rack is clamped onto the rain gutter, 
pop-top downholder in one....
