That was me. It was one of my Grandma's handy hints, like standing on your 
head and drinking a glass of water to cure hiccups or keeping one foot on 
the floor as a method of contraception. You mash them up with a little water 
to make a sort of polishing compound. It works great on alloy wheels (and 
its free) but I never tried it on anything else. I also used some stuff 
called Chemico, that is sold for cleaning bath taps and the like. I don't 
know if you can still get this, I haven't seen it for years, but it is great 
on chrome and brass and a fraction of the price of Autosol.
