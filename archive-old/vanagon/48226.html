To all those who responded.
If you had a voltmeter , a systematic check would have revealed the
alternator had gone bad. 
I took the short cut and went and bought a voltage regulator but the
problem was not it. Well got to pay for one's weakness sometimes.
Anyway for the benefit of members reading, when you observe that the
alternator light is at half brightness with ignition on(engine not
turning) its a bad alternator. Even when the engine is started, the 
light remains same without going out. The Bentley manual does not
describe this condition specifically.
On shopping for parts these were the prices:
