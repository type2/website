So Sean O'Neil says:
> Does plugging in to 120v disable the 12v system? The interior lights won't

No, it should not inhibit the rest of the 12V system.  Does this happen
when you turn on the 12V power to the fridge?  Or does it happen when
you plug in the 110V?  This should help you track down where the problem
is.
