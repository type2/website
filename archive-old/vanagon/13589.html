Permatex makes a product called Extend which neutralizes the rust with a 
tough but inert laler. This basically keeps air out preventing the rust 
from  "breathing."
I just finished doing a repair on the body seam that runs Horizontal on 
the driver's side below the hookups. I sealed the rusted area with Extend 
from the inside after removing the sink\stove\fridge. The water was 
leaking in through the two vertical body seams that are on the driver's 
side also. The putty cracked from age, so I went to Builders Square and 
got some Dap window and siding putty. It is siliconed based and can be 
painted to match after it cures. It solved my problem.
