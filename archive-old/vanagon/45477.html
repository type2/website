ont h-beam had no support left in the sides; I remember using sign-post mat
erial (the square tube with holes spaced all along it) bolted across the tu
bes to keep them together.   Tailites also rotted and I stuck on those

lites.  The spare tire well was gone- used rope back and forth between

inspection in upstate NY!  (If it STOPPED and all lights worked, it
passed).  This was used in Ithaca to Syracuse trips constantly in the worst
