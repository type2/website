HI all,
The vacuum assist is definitely needed.  There is a big difference.  The
bus manifold is supposed to have a larger 1/2" tube coming off of it just
under the carb.  On bug manifolds there is a very small tube there, and you
can use it, but stopping power is not as good.  I had a hard time finding
a manifold with the big tube on it.  There's no other way to tie into the
vacuum system.  Gooday
