Hi, I have a Pansonic CQE15ECU usual AM/FM Cassette detachable faceplate,
digital display.  I have not finished its installation yet as I have gotten
sidetracked by projects at the rear end of my 84 Westie, but I have test fit
it and it will fit.  From the back of the faceplate to the back of the
chassis is just a hair under 6 inches in depth.  The specs call it 5 7/8
inches.  This seemed to be one of the few DIN mount units I found that was
shallow enough to fit.  I got a whale of a deal on it, $99, but I think it
normally goes for more like $149.  In the store, Best Buy, and mocked up on
the bench, it seems to have a decent sound to me, not much different than the
Pioneer Supertuners I have liked in the past.  From the price range you can
probably draw some conclusion about how serious an audiophile I am (not).
