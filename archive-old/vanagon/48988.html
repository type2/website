Open the front grill and bleed the radiator of air bubbles. I normally do 
it while the engine is still hot. Just be careful not to open the bleed
screw entirely or you may injure yourself from scalding. When the hissing 
stops, and no more air is expelled, tighten up, Refill the coolant in the 
expansion tank. That should take care of the problem for at least several
of hundred miles until the air leaks into the system again.
