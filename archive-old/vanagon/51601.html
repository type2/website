Are they LT rated?
If they do not have a load rating of 1450lbs and inflation maximum of 
40psi, I would return them, especially on a Syncro.
Sears makes a LT load range C tire which will work, but avoid 
passenger tires - you will not be happy. (I am speaking from 
experience here.)

My favourite is the Yokohama Y370G load range D. They are pricey, but 
worth it.
