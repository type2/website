After following some suggestions from Joel, I think I have located the source
of the oil spray in my 1982 diesel vanagon. It seems that the head gasket is
bad in the area near cylinders #3 and 4.  The oil come out onto my engine
and then drips down my flywheel into my skid plate where the wind is kind
enough to blow it all over my vanagon's rear.  So, I need to replace the
head gasket.
Here's the first problem:

(1) a 2 grove or a 3 grove gasket.  Does anyone know which I should use?
(2) thickness: 1.4,1.5 or 1.6 mm.  Same question here.  I guess I can measure
the edge of the old gasket to get this.

covering repairs in this vanagon?
Thanks for your help and suggestions!
