VELCRO!!! (tm) (R) (C) (etc)...

my '67.  It was very conveninet, but I always wanted the origianl AM

Anyhow, I installed a deck on the shelf right below the AM.  A friend 
of mine had lots of velcro.  I stuck some on the shelf, and some on 
the radio, and the thing hasn't moved since!  And it is easily 
undo-able.
