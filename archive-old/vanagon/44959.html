If you take the reading from the bottom of the sump you'll get the oil at
it's coolest reading.  The dipstick gets the reading from higher up in the
sump, which makes it more representative.  Remember, heat rises.  Bill
Fischer (and/or Berg) say that the best place is after the oil leaves the
cooler.  But that isa problem in aircooled VWs.
