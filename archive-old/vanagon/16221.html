I'm in the habit of waving to all Splits, Busses, V'gons and EVs.

Here in RI, there aren't many of any of these. I almost always get waves
back from the busses and splits, 50% on the Vanagons and only once from an
EV. 

My wife initially thought that waving to other drivers was something "You
shouldn't do". (Who knows, New England Puritan ethics I guess :-). But
now she enjoys it as much as I do. When we are together in the Vanagon, 
we try to make it a game to see how many in the other vehicle will wave.

Friday she told me she waved to an EV and got a response. Its the first 
that has waved back to her. Granted, she has only seen probably less than 
10 but for her it was a moral victory.
