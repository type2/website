Although I wrote this before reading your detailed explanation of all 
the factors involved, I still think my basic statement holds true. If 
there is more than 3mm difference in loaded radius (or whatever the 
spec is) when the vehicle is stationary and level, this ratio is not 
likely to change significantly when, for instance, driving on a level 
highway at 100 km/h, which I would presume, is the type of conditons
that will lead to VC failure. In such a condition, the factors you 
detailed would affect each tire more or less equally, leaving the 
3mm+ difference to cause problems...
