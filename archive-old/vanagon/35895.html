Hey Folks, 
I recently installed the 3" in my bus.  The 4" won't fit.
A better option, if there are such things, is to get the 3" fan
with 2 or 3 speeds.  My fan only has one speed, and I think if there was 
high and low, the low would be great for improved heating, and the high
speed could be used for rapid defrosting.  It's probably worth it
to find a dual speed fan IMO.
