The nut behind the the knob is a single one.  If you can loosen it, the 
whole valve should come out.  Mine leaked here when I was first 
installing it, so I just tightened it until it was snug.  You can find 
out exactly where the leak is coming from by putting some soap solution 
(soap and water) on all parts wher it might be leaking and look for soap 
bubbles.  If you're unsure, take it to an RV place.  It's scary when it 
comes to playing around with a toxic, highly volitile gas =-0
