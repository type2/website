The disk brakes are originally sold by Oettinger. That are complete kits 
including all you may need.
The rotors for the front are inside aircooled. The disks for the rear 
supports also the emergency brake.

This kit is NOT compatible with 14 inch wheels, you have to mount 15 or 16 
inch ones.
