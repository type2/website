Some of you may remember,  about a month ago, I did some unexpected bodywork
on the front of my 85 with the rear bumper of a Pontiac.

I appreciate the sympathies that were e-mailed to me.  Thanks all.

I took it to the body shop, Laird Auto Body  in Toronto, over my X-mas
vacation.  It's back now, and really quite beautiful.
For an extra $800, I had them sand and paint everything from the raingutters
down. (It had a few rust colored highlights that I didn't like).
They did a great job aesthetically, (lets see if it's rusty in 6 months).
And except for the left headlight now having an intermittantly poor ground,
it's in good looking shape.  They put the sideview mirrors on backwards,
left on roght, right on left,  And I have discovered that the driver's side
of a Vanagon works much better with a right side mirror on it (Mounted
horizontally instead of vertically).  The left mirror on the right side
is completely useless however, So don't bother making the swap yourself.
I gotta switch em back. But if you ever smash your left mirror,  you may
want to replace it with a right one.
