the story i heard was that the air-cools had an 091 transmission, and the
water-cools had an 091/1. supposedly they are the same transmission, same
size, etc., but the water-cooled buses have different transmission mounts
and (because the water-cooled engine is shorter than the air-cooled engine)
the transmission is mounted in a different place (to make the cv-joint
angles "better").

so the first thing you'll need to check is whether the two have the same
mounting positions.
