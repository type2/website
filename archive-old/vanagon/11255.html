I have added a small one to my '86 - a little more complicated that to an 
older models that had LOTS of room in the engine compartment.
The 80s series of Vanagons has a second battery compartment under the 
driver's seat. It is meant for a special battery when there is an 
electric only refrigerator - the relay and other stuff is missing from 
the ones that have the 3 way unit. Don't try and get it from a dealer - I 
did that and it is really frustrating (expensive too :(

The second problem is to tap into the fuze block for the vanagon. It is a 
rather sealed printed circuit board thingy so you can't find a simple 
"gozinta" with a matching "gozouta" which would really be nice since 
everything you would want to get are all on Fuze #3. 
 
The second area for a large second battery is under the passenger seat. 
We have ours full of camping stuff though and don't want to give up the 
scarce storage space.

I have been told that the "best" second battery (read most inexpensive 
with greatest storage) is a pair of golf cart batteries. Shop around.

While you are at this, you might also want to look into solar as a 
charger. Neat!
