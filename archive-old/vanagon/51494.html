Do you access to a volt meter? 
The first thing I would do is check/clean all associated electrical
connections. With engine off disconnect the battery ground clamp.
Inspect the following:
1>Alternator/battery connection at starter.
2>Ground straps at transmission and engine.
3>connections at alternator.
4>All battery connections.

for a spare if not bad .
