Have you seen the "Jerque du Jour" Web page? It's at:

Some guy who is silly enough to live an Santa Cruz (on the CA coast) and
commute to the San Jose area (over one narrow 4-lane road that winds
thru the hills), complains endlessly about the shitty drivers he finds

camper). I blocked a lot of traffic in the right lane in that car in my
