while you're back there and tracing hoses, follow as many of the AC hoses
as you can and look for indications of oil leaks (with caked on road grime,
or otherwise).  This would be an indication of a leak in your system, which
would lead to the observation that joel noted above (about the bubbles).
Perhaps you'll be lucky and all you need is a recharge, but get another opinion
if someone tells you the condenser or receiver/drier needs to be replaced ($$).
