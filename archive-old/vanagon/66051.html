Eric,
I recently discovered that there are in fact two timing marks on the
pulley wheel:
----------^---->F
---u----------->R
The "u" mark is TDC for cylinder #1. The "^" mark is 5% ahead of TDC.
This is the mark that should be aligned with the block split line.
Is it possible that you accidentally set your timing 10% ahead of TDC?
