Subject: Vanagon heater controls - tip


Minor "tip-o-the-day"...

After spending hours trying to adjust the lever that directs air either to
feet or windscreen so that it would stay "full defrost", I gave up and
attached a coil spring to the offending arm, the other end to the rear
mount of the radio. The spring gives the lever enough assist to keep it
where I slide it.
