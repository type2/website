a Ute is short for "Utility Vehicle". The vehicle is a cross between a car
and a truck, sort of the precursor to Chevy S-10's and Ford Rangers. The
vehicle is basically a mid to full sized sedan, except behinf the front
seat, a pickup truck bed has been added, and the rear passenger sear
removed. Most "utes" would be very similar to an El Camino or a Ford
Ranchero. Remeber those beasts from the 70's?
