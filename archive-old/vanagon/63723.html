Just wanted to let everyone know I've taken a few steps back and sold the
87' syncro Westy and just purchased an 82' GTI Westy. Man is this a sweet
ride. I bought the van fron the owner of Streetsboro Foreign Auto in
Streetsboro Ohio. He built the van for his retired father, who gave it back
after he found he didn't use it all that much. The engine had only 25000
miles on it. The trans is from an 82' aircooled van and is well suited for
the GTI power. Elmar says he's done over 20 of these conversions over the
years and from the looks of the engine compartment it shows. The intake and
fuel system is from an 80 rabbit (CIS) and the exhaust is strictly diesel.
It passed EPA inspection after the conversion (it has a cat.), but I won't
need to get it checked because it qualifies as a motorhome, which we don't
need to check here in Ohio.
