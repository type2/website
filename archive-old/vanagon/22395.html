Oh yeah, I've had that problem before.  Mine turned out to be leaking
out of the auxiliary heater bleed screw.  I verified it by running the
engine and checking under the cover.  When Linda revved the engine, it
peed straight up like a baby.  If it's coming from the bleeder screw,
just get a new one with a new gasket and the leak be gone.  I've read
that others have leaks in the core itself.  Check the archives for 
how they fixed theirs.
