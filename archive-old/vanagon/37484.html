>'67 Westy Resto. Accidentily bought the 40mm length and they were too long.

>trying to get broken bolts out of the reduction box.  I guess that if you

In addition to the size of the bolts, the grade is important.  Almost all el
cheapo bolts are grade 8.8  These bolts should be grade 10.9 or better.  The
grade 8.8 will fail again.
