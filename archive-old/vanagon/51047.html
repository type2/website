Yes! It will work in a Rabbit or pickup, but you will need the 
downpipe from a turbo diesel Rabbit or Jetta, since the exhaust exits 
the turbo at a different angle than the regular diesel. The turbo was 
offered in the rabbits for a year or two around '83, but a downpipe 
from a '80-'84 Jetta Turbo will also work.
$400 is a very good price for a turbo in working order. Any idea of 
mileage? I paid $1300 CDN 2 years ago for mine, which had 88k km on 
it, and included the downpipe and full exhaust. Can you see it 
running first? Or at least a compression check?
The difference in power is enormous - I can now regularly pass many 
cars going up hills around here, often in 5th gear. Mileage has also 
improved - I now average >55mpg CDN, or about 1040 km on 50 litres 
of fuel. Be aware that factory recommends changing oil *twice* as 
often for the turbo compared to all other engines.
If I were closer, and ready for a Vanagon project, I might be 
interested, but I've got my hands full right now.

btw: I've now put 180K on my TD since getting it, with only problem 
being a head gasket oil leak. It was not major, just making a mess of 
my garage with oil.
