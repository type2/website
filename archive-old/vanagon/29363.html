fan all the time, on 3 if needed to keep it spinning. Quit if it starts
popping fuses. My theory is that it is the plastic fan and housing

away.  Working so far on my '87 Syncro.  On the '85 I let the fan stay off,
and ended up pulling the dash to fix it.  Now since I let the bugger grind,
it's silent all levels now, whereas it was stalling/squawking on 1 or 2 last
year, only 3 worked, under labour.  Very cold weather may kill it however
due to plastic warping more. We'll see.  Tim Smith.
