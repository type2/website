Tonya,
I beleive that you are experiencing the effects of old wires with
failing insulation. Our Westy is a 1986 vintage and has had similar
problems. A cheap band-aid fix is to spray WD-40 liberally over all
wires (engine compartment, specially the high tension wires and
fuse/relay box). WD-40 is a Water Dispersant and is non-conducting and
will do no harm.
It worked for me :>)
