I use biodegradeable, non-phosphate degreasers on my cars too.  You got to
work a little harder, but it gets your job nice and clean.  Get a stiff
brush, some eye goggles, and coveralls if your work is still on the van.  A
toothbrush helps in the knooks and crannys.  If it is really bad, I use a
generic brand of automatic dishwasher detergent (contains phosphates,
cheap) to clean it up.  I clean my engine case-halves this way.
