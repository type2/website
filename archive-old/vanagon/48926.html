What is failing?  The bearings or the seal?  Bearing failure is usually
caused by excessive belt tension.  Seal failure is caused by excessive
temparatures, pressures, or grit in the cooling system.  It is very important
the the proper anti-freeze water mix is used.  Repalacing a water pump is a
do it yourself job.  Figure 2 to 3 hours depending on skill and practice.
