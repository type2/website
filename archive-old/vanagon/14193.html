Hi John.  I don't know the Y356/376 line.  As far as I know the Y370 
is a different tire.  The Y370 is an LT (light truck) tire with 6 ply 
rating, 1765 lb _at_ 65 psi max load, and a load rating of  'D'.

I got them locally (BC) at a Big-O Tire store.  Quite a few tire 
shops carry the Yokohama around here.  Even Costco carry some Yoko 
lines.
