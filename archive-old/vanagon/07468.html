ok. this is what i've got. as soon as someone gets any more definite info,
i'll add it to this bunch:
-------------------------------------------------------------------
the u.s. revell model of the vanagon was issued as kit # 7328,
Magnum P.I. Vanagon, in 1/25 scale. the plastic is dark brown (as was
the vanagon in the tv series).  it's listed as copyrighted in 1982.

Revell/Lodela of mexico also issued the same kit, but without the
Magnum P.I. stuff, in 1/25 scale. the plastic is light green/white
and the kit number is RH-7382  <no, that is NOT a mistype: the u.s.
kit was 73-two-eight and the mexican kit is 73-eight-two>

the u.s. revell kit of the split window bus is kit number 7304, and
yes, it can be made into a fair approximation of a stock panel van,
but it is a LOT of work to undo what the idiots at revell did.

now, for the real modeling nut, there is a catalog called
danhausen's world modelcar book, and it's put out each year (or used
to be ... i haven't been able to get one since 1986). it's from
