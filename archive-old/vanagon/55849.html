	Thank you for the information about the ignition.  That was the
problem.  Due to work it was 11:00pm last night before I could utilize
your suggestions. The ignition proved to be the source of the problem.

it inside.  Once the the switch was opened, I found that the contact in

few modifications the switch now works well.  I suspect the stero
system, now move to a new power source, caused the problem.  I posted
