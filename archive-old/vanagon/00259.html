I've got two auxillary "marine/RV" type batteries in my 85 Westphalia. I 
put them under the rear bench. Have to be careful with wiring. Must also 
be enclosed to minimuize risks. I had an RV place to the install.

It's great.

You also benefit by using very heavy guage cable to maximize throughput 
of amps. Short runs of wire. And you should consider putting a variable 
control on the alternator so you can jack up the amps flowing in.

James Cook
