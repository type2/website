> Does the air flow to the oil cooler actually decrease at high temp?
> This is really bugging me. Somebody straighten me out please!

The left flap is sort of hinged in the middle, if I remember.  When
closed, it shuts off most of the air to the cylinders and oil cooler.
When warm, it divvies up the air between the cylinders and the oil
cooler by "slicing" the incoming air into two separate streams.  The
obvious one goes to the cylinders, and the less obvious one (underneath)
goes to the oil cooler.
