I had this problem with my '78 a few years ago. Idid 2 things- first,
temporarily, Iclamped the old one in place and gooed it up with
silicone. This worked until later, when I installed a pop-top off
a dead camper. It took about a day to completely put the top on, and
didn't require that much drilling. If you feel adventurous and want a
camper anyway, I would suggest finding a complete top assembly at a
junkyard and putting that on.
