sits ahead of the rear axle, which has diff lock and independent 
suspension. No terrain gear, 5speed straight gearbox. You cant lock the 
visc. coupling. I have never seen a EV syncro with autotranny so im not 
sure about it. I have a diesel and drive a lot on low gears, its not so
