The speedo needle is attached to a metal cup.  The end of the speedo 
cable has a magnet on it.  The rotating magnet attracts the metal cup 
and attempts to rotate it, but is resisted by a spring.  Assuming 
that the speedo cable is working, remove & disassemble the speedo and 
use a lightweight oil (sewing machine) and lube the center shaft.  
You can check the cable by disconnecting it and go for a drive.  
Reach over you dash, pick it up and it the shaft will be rotating if not 
broken.
