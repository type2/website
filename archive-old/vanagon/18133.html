Subject: Re: Westy Pop-Tops in the wind

I also have a '71 pop top.  It used to pop up on the freeway all the 
time, and i even had those aftermarket rubber hold-downs.  One day it 
really popped up high and i noticed that one of the hollow aluminum cross- 
poles on the front left hinge was broken (this could be the snap that you 
heard).  Anyway, i replaced the hinge from a junkyard ($25.00) and have 
had no problems since.  Check your hinges.

Good luck,
Ted
