Get a '71 engine (complete with tin shrouding and carb/manifold) to rebuild
if you can.  The '71 has the doghouse cooler shroud, which puts the oil
cooler out of the way of cylinder # 3's cooling air.  The '71 has dual-port
heads and produces a little more horsepower.  IMHO it's the best engine
setup of all the pre-72's.  And it bolts right in.
