Unless somthing is wrong, no adjustments should be needed to run the
platinums.  I have had some experience with defective ones.  Inspect them to
make the tips did not separate and short out the electrode.  I find the
engines with marginal HC emission problems do clean up with these plugs.
 They will not make you van noticeably faster though.
