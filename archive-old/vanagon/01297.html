There is probably a garage or business that specializes in aftermarket
A/C in your area.  I would look in the phone book for them in your area,
call them and see what they had to say.  I'd try to get several estimates
before I made a decision as to which one to go with.  Personally, I'd
visit the garages I was shopping so I could get a look at the various
systems they were offering.  Note, this is a fairly expensive venture.
Especially now with the advent of CFC free systems.
