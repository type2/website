Rhubarb leaves contain the chemical, oxalic acid.  This is the chemical
that makes them toxic, and the chemical that makes them useful for metal
finishing work.  Oxalic acid can be used (in dilute solution) as a
rust/tarnish remover.  It is commonly available in hardware stores as a
wood bleach.

Wolf

pieces of chromed or rusty metal before dunking your valuable metal parts!
