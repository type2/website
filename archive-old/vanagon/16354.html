The small square pop-tops were only used until '67. After that, from
'68-'73, the tops hinged from the front, and had a small cot up top.
>From '74 on, the top hinged from the rear, and had a folding double bed
on the roof. I would try for one of the later models if you can get one,
but any of those years will fit. (I have a '70 top on a '78 bus)
