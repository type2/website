Last summer while at the LiMBO Transporters By The Tunnel, I met a fellow 
EVer from Chicago. He took a set of the old bug/bus mud flaps (the black

mounted them in the wheel well a la T1 bus.

a little bit of drilling, the mounting bracket for them could be adapted to 
the EV. I bought the mud flaps from RMR for $22 last fall.
