True, only only the 86> have DOPs. But the technique I original described is
valid regardless. Why is the oil pressure light coming on? Without measuring
we are only guessing, get a pressure gauge, remove the oil pressure switch
and measure the pressure under the same conditions. 

You will find that you have one of three conditions.

a. Your switch is defective and turning on at an incorrect pressure.
b. You have the wrong switch. VW and others make these things with many
different setting.
c. The pressure is too low and you need to do something about. Perhaps the 
   pressure valve is stuck.

And no, cold/dampness shouldn't make any difference.
