whatever.

the Kelly Park Bug Show to give to a friend who has a Eurovan.


	Rich Smith wonders how to strap-up a tranny for a tow.
Well, if its a tow with all four wheels on the ground, I'd use a
couple of stout ropes tied into the framework above the tranny. Make
it WAY stronger than you think it needs to be. Chain or steel cable would
be good if you can work with it. I suppose gigantic nylon tie-wraps like 
the phone company uses would work too...
	I would NOT use bungee cords or coat-hanger wires.
