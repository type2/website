Muffler you got is the BOSAL. It is a good unit.  You only need to remove one
bracket to install.  I suggest you use Stainlees hardware to mount this.  The
cat you got sound like the universal replacement with swivel flanges and the
O2 sensor mounted in the pipe.  Be careful with the never sieze as any thing
that gets near the tip of the sensor will desteroy it.  Test sensor operation
with voltmeter to ensure proper operation.  Out of tolerance mixtuer will
destroy new cat in about 500 miles.  All Cat failures are due to something
else being wrong or engine severly over worked.
