For what it's worth, I bought one at Kmart about 1-1/2 yrs ago for roughly 
$17.00.  There were two there, a few bucks apart.  The one I bought was the 
sturdier looking of the two, brand name Interdynamics, 200 psi. (The 
cheaper one was labeled 250 psi, but just looked flimsier so I passed on 
it.)  I've used it many, many times since then.  A little slow (maybe 15 
minutes from flat to full, definately not 40 minutes!), but has proven 
extremely reliable.  Gets a little hot to the touch after prolonged use, 
but has never overheated or failed.  It's helped me out of a bind many, 
many times - best $17 I ever spent!  I keep it in my Vanagon at all times. 
If my experience with reliability is typical, I'd recommend this one.
