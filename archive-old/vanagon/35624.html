r75/185/14

14   -  14" wheel
185  -  tred width in mm
r75  -  ratio of tire sidewall height to tread width
        IE: r85/185/14 is a very tall 185mm wide tire on a 14" wheel 
            r55/205/16 is a very low profile 205mm wide tire on a 16" wheel
