I've used synthetic oil in 5 of my own personal vehicles and have never had
an oil leak.  Really, never.  
1. 1984 Rabbit GTi, switched at about 20,000 miles - no problems through sale
at 85,000 miles
2. 1986 Jetta GLI, switched at 5,000 miles (2nd oil change) - no problems
through totaling collision around 100,000 miles
3. 1982 VW Pickup-diesel (Rabbit style), switched at 60,000 miles - no
problems through sale at 120,000 miles.
4. 1988 Vanagon, switched at 5,000 miles (2nd oil change) - no problems
through today at around 120,000 miles
5. 1987 Vanagon Syncro, switched at 46,000 miles - no problrms through engine
replacement at 140,000 miles.  In used replacement engine - no problems
through today at around 9,000 miles since swap. Engine had around 30,000
miles when I installed it.
