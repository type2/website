I'd strongly recommend two things.  1. Get yourself one of those one man
bleed kits.  Works great, and then you aren't pulling dirty fluid back in 
from your bottle because it has a one-way valve on it.  2. First thing, 
pull the bleed screw all the way off and make sure it is free of gunk and 
the bleed hole is clear.
