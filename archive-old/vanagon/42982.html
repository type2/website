How true.  Last December when my brother and I took the engine out of our 
72 beetle to put on the new single pack, we took the opportunity to not 
only double hose clamp every fitting, but we took glavanized picture

thought of having to take off any hoses cause they are really anchored 
good, however, the nuisance is null compared to what could happen if the 
hoses weren't "aviated" on to their fittings...
