> I have recently replaced the handle on my sliding door with a used handle.  I
> can't figure out how to remove the cylinder so I can switch it with my "new"
> one.  Is changing the cylinders a difficult process, because if it is, I can
> live with it the way it is, but it would be nice, however, to be able to open
> the door using the key.

There is a small knock-out pin which holds it all together. Pre-soak the
pin area with penetrating oil. I used a nail with the end filed flat to
tap the pin through and out of the hole. The nail must be slightly less
than a snug fit. Remember to put your key into to lock cylinder before
removing cylinder.
