Alice,
You must have some pretty noisy gas where you live. I never can hear 
gas hitting my engine here in Aridzona.  

The first thing you should check out is the cold start injector and 
the thermo-time switch. The Bently manual covers this. With fuel 
injection, pumping the accelerator pedal doesn't do anything. The 
missing cooling flaps shouldn't have anything to do with cold start 
problems.

Other things to check would be the head temperature sensor and the 
injector spray pattern.
