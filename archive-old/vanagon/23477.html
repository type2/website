RESTORATION TIP, NOT - Joseph Aaron tried an innovative approach to make 
adaptions to his car at his home in Wesley Chapel Florida.  Aaron needed 
to bore a hole in the exhaust pipe of his car.  Lacking a drill, he tried 
to shoot a hole in it with his pistol.  He was hit in the leg when the 
bullet ricocheted.
