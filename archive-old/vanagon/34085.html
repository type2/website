the wipers in my GL (all EV's?), are "programmable" in effect.  Just your
basic intermittent switch, except you set it by briefly turning on the
wipers for one swipe, then waiting till the drips accumulate to the point
you'd like them gone, then put the lever on the intermittent setting.
Swipes come at the interval you chose...  I like this better than the dial
types on most cars, because you don't have to take your eyes off the road
to look at the switch, in fact you look at the road (throught the
windshield) to determine the setting.  Only thing about the VW wipers that
I don't like is that when turning on the washer to clean bugs and birdshit
off a dry windshield, the wipers start to move before the window is wet.
My 80 westy had that, too.  Oh well, here in So. Cal, the ozone will eat
the wipers before they can wear out anyway...
