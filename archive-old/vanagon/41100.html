We don't do polls here.  This isn't a democracy.  For those who prefer a
newsgroup format, there are already groups in existance that they can read:

rec.autos.makers.vw.aircooled

configure to sort your incoming mail, depending on which software you are
using to read your mail.  To me, the Vanagon list mail is interesting
enough that I am willing to manually sort through all of the messages each
day.  I delete the threads that aren't of interest to me, skim the messages
with subjects that I think might be interesting, and read the ones that
definately *are* of interest to me.  Takes me about half an hour, usually.
Not unreasonable, if you consider it a hobby as I do.  :)
