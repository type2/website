as i understand it, diesel #1 is much closer to kerosine than is diesel #2.
which is why the 18 wheelers smell funny in the winter time. #1 doesn't
have the 'waxing' problems that #2 does in very cold temperatures.

the best place to find all this out would be at some local big truck stop.
get a cb radio and listen/talk to the truckers. maybe go up and ask the
folks at the fuel desk (but be prepared for the possibility that there's
only a Bubba behind the desk! lots of dummies work around those places.)
