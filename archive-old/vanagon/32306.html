Ahhh.... The old "we aint got no steenkin' bearings....for that" response.  
I would start out at your local alternator rebuider...the guy that actually 
has a shop that REBUILDS alternators, you should be able to get your unit 
rebuilt for about what....$60 or so?  He should have bearings.  If not at 
that shop, somewhere nearby must be a "bearing" shop....try the yellow 
pages, take your fried bearings in to them, look at the numbers on the 
outside or shoulder, or even measure the shaft and race and find a 
replacement that way....
