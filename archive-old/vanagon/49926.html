Juli,
Had the same problem when I bought my '85 Westy (except that the lock was 
already locked onto the resevoir, with no key!).  The lock has no key code 
on it, so VW cannot make you a duplicate.  Nor can they sell you a new
lock and key.  You have to buy the complete water filler assembly (about 
$40, I think, from Delta Six) to get a new lock and key.  I went to a 
locksmith, who removed the lock for me but could not custom make a key 
because he didn't have a small enough blank.  However, someone I know did 
bring his key into another lock shop and they made him a copy, so I'm sure 
such a blank is available.  If I were you I'd try a locksmith or two before 
considering paying for a whole new assembly.

Unless anyone else knows of an option that I'm not aware of?
