Gee... sounds in "GOOD CONDITION" to me!!!   :^)

Haven't towed a splittie backwards before. Always had the opportunity to
tow forward, of course, wrapping the tow chain around the front torsion
bars, but obviously that's not possible here.

Not knowing anything else, I'd first clear out most of the topsoil with a
shovel and hoe, then slide underneath and pull a long chain or snatch
strap all the way to the front of the bus, and wrap it around the front
torsion bars anyway. Careful not to wrap it around the sway bar or tie
rods, tho! Watch the brake lines, too... This should let you get it out
of "The Pit", and up onto level ground where you can do it right.
Good luck!
