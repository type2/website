Jeff, 
I recommend staying with duals. I have run both single Webbers and dual
Dellortos (of course, the Dellortos provide me with much more power, due largely
to their largeness). One problem with the single setup is the distance the fuel
has to travel while suspended in the air between the carb and the head. The fuel
tends to precipate out and cling to the intake runner walls causing a lean mix
at low speeds and a suddenly rich one when you get up to speed and the air
velocity in the intake runner pulls all that precipitated gasoline into the
cylinders. That is why VW engineers used the dual carbs on the type IV's (before
FI).
