I have a 95 EV camper.  Generally we've liked it a lot - it's our sole
vehicle for day to day and travelling.

Do check all the camper components before taking possession - when we
bought ours we discovered that Winnebago had left a fuse out which caused
problems charging the coach battery.  The dealer you buy from should be
one with the  EV camper dealer status so they're licensed to deal with the
whole beast.  Here in Michigan there's only one such dealer - 90 miles from
where I live.  We've been able to have routine VW service done closer to
home but anything camper related has to go to that dealer.  Regular Winnie
dealers haven't got a clue about the EV in my experience.

Now that we're out of warranty I've been horrified at VW's price for
routine service - will be going elsewhere for it!  All that said, we like
our 95 and are sad to be selling it.

good luck!
