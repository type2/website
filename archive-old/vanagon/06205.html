Hi Folks.  I have ALWAYS changed the oil within the first 1500 km, and
I religiously change the oil every 6000 Km or 3 months, whatever comes
first. The filter gets changed EVERY time the oil does as well.

I believe the first oil change is the most critical, to remove any filings
from within the engine.  I also never fill the crankcase.  I usually run
the oil levels from one third to 2 thirds full as to leave lots of room
for fruid expansion from heat.

I have a '90 Syncro and an '87 Golf Diesel.
