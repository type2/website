Prior to '88, these were the place where air exited the van (it got sucked
out through a passage in the door to slots in the front edge of the door
and then out through the gap between the door and the frame).   This caused
lots of air  noise and allowed road noise to enter, so in '88, the air
exhaust was relocated to the rear windows, and the old exit was plugged
(the gap between door frame and door was filled by a new gasket too).
