IMHO, it sounds like your reduction gears.  The four bearings in them 
have behaved as per your description for me before.  They sing and grind 
the worst under a load.  I'd drain the lube out of the boxes and look for 
metallic goo.  Check for play also, but this may not clue you into the 
upper bearings' condition.

If the metallic goo is there, then R&R the reduction gear bearings.  I'd 
change the gearlube in the transaxle while you're at it.  The reduction 
gears work fine if properly maintained, but there are other solutions if 
you care to swap them out, although I wouldn't recommend it.  Good Luck
