You probably heard that from me, but the only Audi wheels that will fit on a
Vanagon are wheels from a 5000 / 200 turbo or turbo quattro. The wheels off your
'86 Audi coupe GT won't fit. (unless it's a GT turbo quattro, and I SERIOUSLY
DOUBT THAT..)
(for you non-Audi enthusiasts, that is a really RARE car...)

the turbo wheels have the same 5 lug pattern as the '71 up bus/van, but the
centers have to be machined out slightly to fit over the Vanagon hub/axle cap. 

If anyone's interested, I have a complete set of 15" Audi turbo wheels for sale
already machined, and come with the elongated studs/bolts. (you'll need the
elongated studs and lug bolts to mount them)
They look almost like the Vanagon "Carat" alloy wheels, but are 6 spoke, instead
of 5 spoke.
