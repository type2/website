Well, if I have to use a Fram, I'll only use it in the warmer months
because it doesn't have the OEM filters built in by-pass valve.  So
when it's cold, the valve will adjust for thicker cold oil.  In the
summer, it's not as big an issue, since the oil is typically thin
enough in the morning.  I've never experienced the flickering oil light
problem with a Fram filter on.  I change the oil at every ~2,500 miles. 
I've also not used the Fram filter for quite a while.
