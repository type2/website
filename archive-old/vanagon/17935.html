> My father and I replaced the ac compressor last year on our 85 vanagon.
> We replaced the compressor with a new one that was for a volvo.  Several years

> The compressor was put away in our collection of used compressors.  When the
> compressor died we went through the collection and found that it was basically
> the same.  The only difference was the hoses were connected on the side while
> the vanagon hoses were connected from the rear.
> 
> Anyway he got the compressor from a truck refrigeration (reefer) place and

> we saved from the compressor went into the making of new ac hoses because

> place on the ac mount without any problems.
> 
> I took apart the old compressor just for fun.  It has five pistons/cylinders
