I can't help with a "bolt in" installation, but I just got done putting
some seats from a Porsche 924 into my 73, and lemme tell ya, they are the
cat's meow.  I had to cut some boards to mount the seats to, then bolted
the boards to the floor of the bus.  If you need details, search the
gopher archives for "seats".  As for the metal behind the seat, your best
bet would be to rent one of those power cutting disc things, but you could
probably use frameless hacksaw also, if you have, oh, 8 hours to do it.  :)
