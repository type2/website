A Rabbit heater core can be used in a pinch.  Requires some clearancing of
the shrouding around the heat exchanger to make it fit.  You lose the ability
to turn it off in the summer, unless you add in a Rabbit heater valve in one
of the water lines.  On the plus side, you get to keep most of the $140 that
the dealer wants for a new one and the $70 that most junkyards are asking for
used ones.
