Hmmm... have to admit ignorance here. I've never seen/heard of a rev
limiter on a VW engine, excpet for an ignition-cutout plunger on a
special rotor for certain bus distributor.

I *have* seen an odd EGR valve, that was fitted to a spacer plate between

single-QP exhaust that didn't have the special EGR fitting from the bug
engine. Left the spacer in place, and covered the port in it with a piece
of aluminum from a hobby shop. A dab of Aviation Form-a-gasket helped.

Any chance this is what's on your engine? Or am I simply showing my
ignorance (wouldn't be the first time!)
