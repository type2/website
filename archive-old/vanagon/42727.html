Is the radiator fan working properly?  If outside temp affects running temp,
radiator could be suspect.  Warm air coming through vents sign of water valve
not closing completely. Air flaps never seal that well.  Water pump belt
tension will not effect cooling capacity much.  Will effect alternator
charging as belt slipps.  Belt will burn up.
