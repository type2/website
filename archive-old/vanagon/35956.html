A key concept in the successful operation of fridge in ballast.  If there is
large cold mass in fridge, cooling system can keep up with hotter outside
temps, frequent openings etc.  If no mass, the fridge is working to cool air
only.  And then you let it out.

Keep a bunch of cold drinks (preferably beer) in there and it stays cold
well.

Put drinks *in* cold.

My preference is household current for stationary, propane for camping, and
DC operation for driving (in summer for more than an hour).  Propane
sometimes works while driving.

Also- almost time to inspect and repaint (if necessary) propane tank.  Be
mindful of that thing.
