as stupid as this sounds, it sounds like you are describing an Eberspacher
gas heater. Noticeable characteristics of this beast are an exhaust pipe
down through the floor, and a metal tag w/ Eberspacher written on it. Are
you familiar w/ Eberspacher gas heaters? That green knob on your dashboard
w/ the white cross controls your gas heater. if you turn it, you will hear
this whirring sound that is the timer
