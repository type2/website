I've come across a source for replacement (or additional) 12VDC flourescent
light fixtures that are the ones that VW used in 87-89 Wolfsburg and
1989-1991 Carat model Vanagons (perhaps others as well).

The light fixture is:
Thin Lite Model 171
Made in USA, 8W
17-5/8" long x 2-3/8" wide x 1-1/8" thick
The bulb is 11-1/4" long, 8W and is included.

Price: $29.95

These are very nice units compared to those at JCWhitney (I have one of those
in my 87 Syncro) and I can find no visible differences to the ones VW used.
