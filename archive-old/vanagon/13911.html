>I too, have this under-window rust problem.  Would anyone care to point 
>me to the 'easy fix'?> 

1) remove the window
2) with a drill and a wire brush attachment, strip off all of the rust and
   paint in the affected area.
3) use you favorite chemical on the area (naval jelly, extend, etc)
4) if needed, use a body filler (like Bondo) to fill in any pits.
5) prime with a metal primer
6) paint
7) install window
