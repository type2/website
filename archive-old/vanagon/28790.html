My wife and I stayed at a beautiful campground in Southwest Harbor ME (about 
5mi from the park) over the Forth of July weekend(make reservations early),

for around $20/nite and rode for two days on the rail trail.  Cape Cod MA is 
a must for any list Camper folks that are in the area.
