Your sources are wrong, the line from the fuel filter to the injection oump
on a diesel is not pressurized. Unless you are considering the suction from
the injection pump as a negative pressure. You can use any good quality fuel
line. Make sure the ends are tight, if the connections let in any air it
could mean trouble. The clear line is helpful in that respect, you can see
air bubbles in the line.
