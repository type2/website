BTW,  Happy birthday to me!

The '57 Update:

I have now christened the bus, "The Pioneer."  The name seems to fit 
it as it resembles a covered wagon, is about as noisy, and goes 
almost as fast as one.  Also living in Utah seems to fit it as well.  
Maybe Joel will put together a list of bus names!

The tranny nose mount is split as I found out after being led to it 
by list members.  For now, a bolt will hold it together until this 
winter when I drop the engine, do the main rear seal, and replace all 
of the tranny mounts.

I fiddled with the timing.  Probably not too wise to do w/o strobe, 
but at least the bus starts now.  Will take it to buddy to have him 
time it properly.

_Another_ bolt fell out of the right swing-arm.  Can't keep the 
things in there.  Probably all that grease in there.  Must get some 
carb cleaner and loc-tite and get that unit to stay in!

That's all from this end!
