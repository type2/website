I have done this.  In fact, I have *two* deep cycle group 27 batteries to
the left of my motor.  They are turned sideways compared to the third
(original) batteries.  The best batteries I could find are the Die-Hard
deep cycles, which are over 200 mins reserve capacity at 25 amps.  I
have one of those, and a Motocraft Deep cycle with about 150 mins reserve
capacity.  They run the fridge and lights for two days without
recharging - I really love this setup!!
