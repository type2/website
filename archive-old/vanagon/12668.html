A notce from VW dated March 31, 1991 reads: 
=====================================================

Attaching Pressure Plate or Flywheel to Crankshaft

Attaching bolt, part No. N 902 061.01 is no longer available. 

The replacement bolt, Part No. N 902 061.03 will not endure the current
torque specification in the Repair Manual Microfiche. It will cause the
threads to strip. 

The correct torque applied is now 30 Nm (22Ft. lb) + 1/4 turn. 

=======================================================
