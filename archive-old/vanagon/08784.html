I'll bet it's your valves that need adjusting.  Be sure to tow it.
I've heard too many times in the past that just after an engine
rebuild that people have blown up their engine because no-one was
checking the valve clearances during the break-in period or the
heads loosened enough to screw up the clearance.  Get that baby
home or to your rebuild mechanic and get the heads re-torqued and
the valves adjusted.  And yes, this can happen within 50 miles of
the rebuild.  I've seen it before.  If no damage was done, be sure
to keep a sharp eye on those valves until they wear in properly.

Hope this helps,
