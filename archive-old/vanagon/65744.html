Hi all, I just wanted to point out to those of you who looked at the photo
of the S&S headers at http://members.aol_dot_com/rubatoguy/vanmods.htm, that
the header is actually 3 pieces. If you look very carefully at the rear
header pipes you can see they are two-piece and have a press fit into the
main header section.  So it is easier to install that you might have
thought if you believed it was a one piece header.
