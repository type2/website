Cool idea!  Things I'd be careful of are: dust, heat, water and vibrations.
Make sure your electronics get enough air but not too much of these other 
bad things.  If you COULD mount a computer somewhere inside a wall it would
sure cut down on the space requirements.  Are you going to mount a telephone
jack somewhere on the outside so you can plug into a phone line and connect to
the outside world?

phone connection.  I know this makes me sound like a computer nerd,
