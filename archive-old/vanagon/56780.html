Subject: Re: Crazy Oil leak-86 2.1 L Syncro

Check the seal around the oil heat exchanger.    It's at the real drivers
side, near the water pump, right under the oil filter.   I had the seal
(square-section  o-ring) blow out after a very cold night with too-thick
oil.    Best case, the heat exchanger body is only loose.     If that's the
leaking area, first try taking the oil filter off and tightening the nut
inside that holds the filter seat and heat exchanger on.

Dave
