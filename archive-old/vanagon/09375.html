The listserv is set up so that replies by default go to the list.  There 
are a fair number of people who don't like that, but it makes the list 
what it is, and that's the way it's going to be.  However, what that 
means is that if you reply to the list, you need to be conscious that the 
message you just got was ALREADY seen by the list members within the last 
24 hours.  Most people can remember basically what was said, so what I'm 
asking is that if you reply to a list message that you DON'T include the 
ENTIRE text of the message you are replying to.  Please.

Tip of the day.  You come into work on a weekday and horror of horrors, 
there are only 72 messages in your inbox and only 70 of them are from the 
vanagon list!!! What's going on? Is the list down?  Your first instinct 
is to dash a message off to Gerry, but you pause and remember this.
On the vanagon gopher 
URL gopher://halfdan.med.umn_dot_edu/11/Miscellaneous/Vanagon

then the list is up and you can conclude that someone actually spent some

so they can post them in the evening).
