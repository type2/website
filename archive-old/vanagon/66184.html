Subject:      Subaru engine swaps

I got info from Kennedy engineering (805) 272-1147 and they have a nice
table and information on the various Subaru engines you could place in a
Vanagon. Since I have not seen the info on the group - I thought I'd
duplicate it here for those interested:

Engine                          Displacement        HP               Torque
         Comment
Vanagon Water              2109                  90_at_4800        117 _at_3200

'90-94 Subaru Legacy     2216                 130_at_5600        137_at_4400
    a
'95-97 Subaru Legacy     2216                 135_at_5400        140_at_4400
    a
'91 Subaru Legacy Turbo 2216                 160_at_5600        181_at_2800
    b
'96-97 Subaru Outback  2500                 155_at_5600        155_at_2800
   c
'92-96 Subaru SVX (6)   3324                230_at_5400        228_at_4400
   d

a: reccomended
b: rare
c: due to milder cam and longer stroke may not be as suitable with Vanagon
gearing
d: Rare, takes the same basic adapter kit as the 4-cyl. Avoid the '88-'89
6-cyl as it
    makes only 145hp from 2672cc, does not have a counterbalanced crank and
requires
    different adapters.

Kennedy kit prices range from $860 to $1793 depending on what model Vanagon
you have and what transmission.
