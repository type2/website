     You should switch the fridge off when you turn the van off, otherwise 
     you will drain your battery.  It's okay for a few hours, but it 
     doesn't take long to strand you, and it's way too easy to forget that 
     you have it on.  I know, I've ruined a battery by repeated discharging 
     cause I keep forgetting the thing is on.
     
     I have no problems running the propane while driving.  In fact I 
     prefer this method to running on 12V.
     
     Just remember to shut off the pilot when stopping for gas.
