Ken, my Vanagon makes a sound just like this when I have my canoe on
top. I found the source of the sound to be the wind moving across the
front hold down strap on the canoe. It too starts at about 40 mph and
grows louder with speed until at 55 you and the passenger can't even
carry on a conversation without shouting. In my case, the cure was
moving the strap back about 6 inches.

You might look for something that is flat and catching the wind. On a
VW van, this should be easy.  ;-)
