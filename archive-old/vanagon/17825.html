My Aftermarket injection system, and I think most others, are 'multipoint'.
This means they all fire at once.  I wondered about how it would affect the
engine as well, however, I think the injection timing pulses are so much
faster than the engine, it just doesn't matter, the fuel is ready and
waiting at the inlet port and it doesn't have to shoot right down through
the open valve.
