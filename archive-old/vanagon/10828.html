Subject: Coolant leak fixed

Joel was right - cracked expansion tank.  A series of cracks between the
fill cap and the level sensor.  At temperature it was probably spraying out
of there quite vigorously.

Pinched off the 1" (approx) hose (running to the front that comes off the
water pump) over the left axle, with a C-clamp.  Captured the spillage and
flushed it down the toilet (that seemed like the most practical advice -
that water is treated, somehow... somewhere... rather than just dumped raw
into the environment).  Replaced the tank, the o-ring on the level sensor
and the o-ring on the left injector-temperature-sender.

Our driveway is steep enough that I didn't have to jack up the front for
the ridiculous filling-and-bleeding ritual.  Certainly an entertaining
procedure.
