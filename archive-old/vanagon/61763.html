Hi Everyone.  I've been having problems with proper led indication of 
water level on my '90 Westy, and I've been doing some diagnostics to 
determine what has failed.  

I used a digital Ohm-meter to measure the resistance of the float at 
the three points along the rod, and I have recorded the following 
values:

green 0.3 ohms
yellow 0.6 megaohms
red      1.0 megaohms

My display panel only shows green and yellow... *sometimes*.. all 
with an empty tank!  The '90 model has a long rod that has a float 
that goes up and down indicating the water level by some magnetic 
type switches inside the rod (my theory).  

>From my diags, it would seem the float assembly is OK.  If someone 
could verify these settings for me, that would be great.  It *does* 
require some work by emptying your stuff out, and removing the float 
assembly, and unhooking the wires (use long needle nose pliers to 
unhook the wires).  

Personally, I think there is something wrong with the display panel, 
but I need to know if this is OK first.  Any other ideas are welcome. 
 The Bentley diagnostics for the display panel are NOT for the '90
