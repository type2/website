WARNING!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
before you put your engine's life into the hands of one of those, check
it CAREFULLY first.
A friend of mine who works on cars bought 5 of those. Just for kicks, he
tested them out, in a pot of cooking oil on the stove.
Using a highly accurate scientific thermometer, he found only 1 to be
accurate. 1 came on about 15 degrees too low, and the other THREE came
on about 10 degrees AFTER your engine has been damaged.
Soooooooooo, be very careful about trusting your car to one of those.
