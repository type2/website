I installed some junkyard (but excellent condition) Vanagon seats in my 
'76 Westfalia, and they are infinitely more comfortable, and work fine. 
They are complete with the fold-down armrests, but did not come in orange 
and green plaid, so don't fit the interior color scheme (was VW on drugs 
in '76?).

It took a bit of machine-work to get the runners to fit.  I used the old 
runners and attached them to the new seats, so that the turn-around passenger 
seat would work, too.  Some work, but DEFINITELY worth the effort!
