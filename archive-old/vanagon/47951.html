It's scary.  I just got back from a long drive, Raleigh to Atlanta
and back.  The damn thing could of come apart at any time. 
I "fixed" it by grinding the surface flush then slitting a piece of big 
radiator hose and wrapping the tube.  This and two hose clamps plus
a plug in the heater hose got me back on the road.  

I assume this thing is expensive.  So between now and late fall I need 
to fugure out if I'm going to replace it or find another way/place
to junction in the heater hose.  I'm leaning toward buying a piece 
of steel pipe, taping in a 1/4" nipple and putting the pipe inline 
with the big hose on the bottom of the unit.  Probably easier than 
replacing the dist. unit and a whole lot cheaper.
