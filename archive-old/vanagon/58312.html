Earl:
Test for stuck injector as follows:
1>Remove fuel injectors(two 10m bolts). Do not loose dougnuts on injector
tips!!
2>Unplug coil wire and ground.
3>Unplug all injectors but one.Point that one into a jar.
4>Have someone crank engine.
5>A fine cone of fuel should be pulsed.
6>Repeat 3 thru 5 for other injectors.
