I postponed fixing my oil leak many months, Although it did not 
adversely affect operation it did take MANY HOURS of cleaning to even 
find the motor. It also got on the motor mounts and ruined the rubber 
and found its way in the fan shroud and caused an oil smell in the 
cab. The oil cooler has a gasket just like a Bug motor, and there is 
info in the archives about other likely places. In my motor it was a 
combination of several things including the filler tube and dip-stick 
O-Ring.
On the reverse lights check the switch on the trans. and be sure its 
getting power (maybe from the coil?)
