the North Carolina Triangle Area Busses. (That's the Research Triangle area, as

potluck dinner and bus talk/comparing/showing off session.

the lot.
I live in Raleigh, about 2 miles inside the Beltline off Hillsborough St.

>From the I-440 beltline, take the Hillsborough Street exit. Head in towards
