Sounds like the EMU isn't getting the proper signal from the temperature
sensor(s).  There's a sensor for the intake temperature and the water
temperature that tell the EMU to activate the cold start valve (5th
injector).  This is activated when the engine is cold and supplies the
requisite extra fuel to feed the motor until it warms up.
