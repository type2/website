Subject: TGIF: The Bus Bug's Still Biting Me Now (song parody)

This one was performed by Celine Dion and written by Meatloaf (of "Bat Out
Of Hell 1 & 2" fame.) Since the copyright date on it is so much earlier
than the other songs in the Celine album, I have to wonder if it was
originally meant to go on a Meatloaf album. Anyone got any idea? Anyway,
enjoy. :) It's long and good, just like the rest of Steinman's work.

- J
