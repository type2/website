	If it is coming from under the front seat, you might want to go check
out your brake calipers. Give it a wiggle near both the top bolt and the
bottom. I found that my caliper wiggled a little. When I pulled the thing up,
I found the top guide bolt was rusty and was preventing the top of the caliper
from sliding along the bolt. I stuck in some WD40 and then some penetrating
oil and it moved much more easily. The squeak returned after about a week, but
disappears when I step on the brake because the caliper is being supported with
tension on both guide bolts instead of just the upper bolt.
