I find that fogging in my '73 is mostly related to internal moisture, I just 
keep a spare diaper in front to wipe the inside glass off.  On dry days I 
leave the sunroof open a crack.
Has anyone tried a product called Clean-Sweep?  Its two clear strips of 
plastic with little bumps imbedded in it.  They glue onto the windshield right 
above where the wiper rest.  The result is the wipers get cleaned on each 
swipe.  I got a sample from the developer a couple of years ago, and have had 
pretty good luck with them, they eliminate the usual streaking from dirt and 
such.  (Do not use with brand new wiper blades, creates this ugly gray smear 
as they take off the initial coating)  I was just wondering whether they made 
it to the marketplace or not.
