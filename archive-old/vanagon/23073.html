I have tried Castol 40 following the recommendation of a VW mechanic and
in terms of pressure and oil temperature there was no difference between
it and 20W50. The only noticeable difference was at cold startup - the 40 took
much longer to "run through the system". I was also told that 30W should
be used at engine break-in (500 miles)...which I followed, then switched to
10w30 (for 1000 miles) then 20w50/5W50 (for 15000+ miles).

With today's quality multigrades why would anyone want to use a single weight
oil?
