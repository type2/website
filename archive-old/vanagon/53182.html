A 2 footer?  Boy, you haven't even begun to get serious.  Get a 4 foot
piece of metal pipe to fit over the breaker bar (about a foots worth).  Now
you've got torque.  Mulitply your wieght by 5 and you know the torque
potential.  That nut will jump off from fright alone!

This is what they'll do in the shop anyway.  You won't believe how easy the
nut will turn with that kind of power applied to it.  It not only will get
the nut off but it will supply you with a feeling of victoy and power that
one rarely experiences!
