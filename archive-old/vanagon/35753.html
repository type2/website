You posted to the list.  I assume you were responding to my post.

If you found a good set of 195s for a good price, go for it.  I would choose
the 205s only if I had a second chance.  Keep in mind the difference is only
10mm in width.  I really wouldn't condsider paying more for 205s than I would
195s.  The difference is almost nil.  Its just the theory of more is better.
 To do over again, I would go with the 205s, but I wouldn't pay more and
wouldn't sacrifice quality or brand for the extra 10mm.

I live in Springfield, VA. How about you?  If you live in the area, I would
recommend NTW on Newington road just off of I-95.  They have mechanics (well,
tire changers anyway) that stick around and know their product.  The service
managers are fair and resonable.  If you give them a price comparison on a
tire they will beat it.  Then ask them for a 5% good customer discount.  I
have never been more satisfied with a local automotive place in the DC metro
area than I have with the folks at NTW.  Other NTW stores are catch as catch
can.  Some better than others.
