Your problem is more than likely an electrical sensor that is not installed
correctly or has made a poor contact.  I just went through this with my 85
(albeit watercooled).  The problem was the temperture sensor.  One of the
contacts inside the connector had slipped back into the connector housing.
 When the computer couldn't sense the engine temp, it sent way too much fuel
to the injectors.
