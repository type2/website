I would be concerned if my door popped open "whenever". Perhaps the latch is
worn out. More likely it has a broken spring, or most likely it is dirty/and
or/dry. Consider taking it out and cleaning it thoroughly, inspect it for
worn/broken/missing parts. Look in the bottom of the door for mystery pieces
<g>. If it all looks perfect then relube with a GOOD light weight oil. NEVER
EVER use "3in1 household oil" 10 weight motor oil works fine for most of
these things but there are wonder oils available for thoes with a few extra
bucks, or free trade samples <g>.

You may want to file a complaint with VW and NHTSA, latches that open by
themselves are a no-no.
