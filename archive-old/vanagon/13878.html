> J.C. Whitney sells tire chains by mail order, including the heavier duty
  ones with that extra little bar to extend the life and improve the grip in
  ice. I have an old set of those and they will shake your fillings loose if
  you have to drive on pavement with them. In snow, they work great. I also
  have a set of those cable things - much more pleasant as regards vibration
  and noise, but less grip when the going gets rough. Under some really 
  stormy conditions on some steep curvy roads at Sequoia Nat'l park a couple 
  years ago I ended up with the cables on the front and the gnarly chains on 
  the  back and could pretty much go <and stop> where I pleased. This was 
  weather severe enough that even four wheel drives were chained up. 

  If you want me to bring you a set of something from the local FLAPS, I'm 
  happy to but they would be more expensive than from J.C Whitney I think.
