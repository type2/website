Me! Me! And probably everyone else who has ever played
with VWs for any length of time.

On mine, it was the long wire from the ignition switch to the
starter solenoid. It was loose at the solenoid end-- just a
push-on connection. Clean it up a little, tighten a little (careful,
it breaks easily), push it back on, and give it a try.
