My Westy had passenger tires on it when I 'inherited' it.  What a disaster.
The van had a terrible understeer, it swayed, and generally felt really
shaky.  I put a set of Michelin LTX on it, and it now steers well, and turns
easily in parking lots.  The swaying has decreased so much I no longer worry
about the shocks.  I haven't noticed any change in the road noise.  I'll
never use passenger tires again.
