Remember, on a 4-cycle engine the distributor rotates once for each TWO
rotations of the crankshaft. When aligned with the notch #1 cyl is a TDC with
valves CLOSED; with the dist. 180 deg away #1 is at TDC with valves OPEN.
Rotate the crank 1 turn and the distributor will line up like the manual says.
