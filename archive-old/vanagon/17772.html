We were discussing this one over on the corrado list and after
a few calls to Crutchfield and one guy who bought it, the concensus
seemed to be you just can't get sufficient bass from such a small
driver, though it is a real slick idea for a small hatch, but since
you still got to mount the amp somewhere and the displaced jack in
a Corrado, didn't seem much better than getting a small (6.5") bazooka
with a built in amp, plus on a Bus who cares anyway? Plenty o room
for anything.
