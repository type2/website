I've had this happen twice-- once on a Ford van, once on a VW bug.
Everyone told me it was king pins on the Ford, ball joints on the VW.

Turns out it was bad shocks on both! On the Ford, the rubber bushings
were so old they were disintegrating, and the shock was rattling around
loose on its mount. On the VW, one shock had broken off its bottom mounting
ring. New shocks fixed them up, good as new!

Hope yours is this easy.
