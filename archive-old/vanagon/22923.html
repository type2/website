I've been doing this for years (and so, I suspect, have been MANY
old-bus owners). It's made easier by the fact that the bus floor
is perfectly flat, perfectly rectangular, and with a nice hidden space
under the rear seat to ack carpeting down so it doesn't slide around.

Putting two thicknesses of 1/2" carpet padding (the super-dense,
multicolored kind, available at any carpet store or large hardware
store), makes it very nice to sit/kneel/whatever on.

Plus, you get your choice of colors, textures, etc! And it's a fair
soundproofer.

Try it, you'll like it!
