Subject:      sliding door handle (interior)

one thing i did to remove some "stress" from the interior door handle ...
was to install another "grab handle" on the door, so the middle seat people
wouldn't have to use the latch-handle to close the door. most non-bus folks
don't seem to know how to close the sliding door, and tend to pull the
wrong way (inward) on the damn handle ... it's like they never saw a sliding
door before!!

anyway, i got an extra one of the overhead Ohmygod-bars, and screwed it onto
the sliding door, about level with the bottom of the windows. you have to
be a little careful about where exactly you place it ... otherwise you'll hit
your hand on the grab handle that's already there on the body pillar.

it's also a little surprising how many folks see the new handle first and
use it, rather than going for the latch handle.
