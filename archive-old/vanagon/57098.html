Subject: Re: Tan paste on inside of oil cap

The "tan paste" is from moisture in the crankcase. My guess is that you do
allot of short distance driving, anything less than twenty miles hardly
warms up the oil enough to boil off the moisture. Be sur the crankcase
breather hoses are all connected. The oil light should come on any time the
oil pressure is below about 8-10 PSI. Which of course is true when the
engine is not running. HTH
