If you have a diesel engine you need a few things from a diesel
vanagon (check your junk yards):

  Bell housing
  Oil pan, oil pump (or at least the lower part of the oil pump 
					with the pickup tube)
  The motor mounts and beams.
  Transmission (it has different gearing)

I think the easiest swap is to buy the whole engine+driveline
from a crashed Diesel vanagon and swap it as a whole package.

Fixing your gas motor would not be very expensive either if
it's just the head gasket and you do it yourself.
