Another approach worthy of consideration:

1.  Give the van a complete tune-up and compression test.
2.  Change the oil (with 20W-50W) and filter.
3.  Air up the tires (and the spare).
4.  Fill the gas tank.
5.  Grab your fins, mask, snorkel, swimming suit, inflatable boat, etc.
6.  Pack up the van with travelin' stuff.
7.  Quit your job or take a long sabbatical.
7.  Start the bus and head for the Caribbean or Pacific coast of Mexico
and/or Central America (or Baja).
8.  Head north about mid-April.
