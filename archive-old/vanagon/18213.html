Primer is not waterproof/impermeable, if it has been on the bus and exposed 
to the weather for some time you might consider a thorough resand and reprimer.

I've only painted using a DIY garage set up, fun, lots of work, worked well. 
On the actual painting of your bus: Do the rain gutter regions (side/roof) 
before you paint the nearby flat surfaces. This way you don't overspray/run 
that paint while trying to stick enough into/under the rain gutters. BTDT. 
Bend/squat down to spray the rockers, else the paint just blows by, and 
doesn't land on the lowest regions. BTDT.  (I wouldn't pay me to paint a bus 
BTW!, professional advice is worth having!)
