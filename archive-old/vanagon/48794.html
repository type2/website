> Well... from what you've said, it sounds like you're getting gas okay.
> 
> First, Check that your throttle valve is adjusted correctly.  Have someone
> press the pedal and make sure it is opening.  
> 
> Next, check to see that your getting spark from the plugs and go backwards
> from there.  Check the wires, distributor, rotor, cap, coil.
> 
> If you're getting spark, then check that you're getting enough air by
> removing the air snorkel from the air distributor and see if it will
> start.  If it starts, then you know you have a problem with air delivery,
> so check all of those components out.
> 
> If it still doesn't start then I would check the oxygen sensor, coolant
> temp sensor, idle stabilizer and all the electrical connections inbetween.
> 
> Another thing to check is the catalytic converter.  See if it is clogged.
> This may not allow the system to breath properly.
>
