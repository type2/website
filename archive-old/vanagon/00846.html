Where have you been ? ;-)  VW Beetles have always had their batteries under
the back seat.  Ever heard of someone riding in the back seat, and shorting
out the battery?  Happened all the time.  Regular vehicle maintenance should

should have been a dead giveaway that there was a battery problem, so now
you'll know for next time.
