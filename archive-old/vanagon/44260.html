To which I add:

I have seen a FAQ, unfortunately I cannot remember from whence it
came, that described how to overcome the voltage drop.  It was a one
liner so it is not too difficult to do.  The procedure entailed
putting the voltage regulator after the relay, or what ever you use,
rather than before.  That way the alternator would "see" the actual
voltage being passed to the coach battery.
