My ex-girlfriend's '78 blew one of those plugs they install in the ends of
the oil passages, what might be called a freeze plug.  It was behind the
flywheel and when she took it to the local repair shop they claimed all they
could do was seal it back in with JB Weld or similar.  It held okay until
several months later when the engine dropped a valve seat and I rebuilt it.
While I had the engine out and apart I had a local machine shop remove the
plug and thread the case for a pipe plug, which gave me a bit more piece of
mind than the JB Weld.  :-)
