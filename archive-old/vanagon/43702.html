Also, check the wire from the fuse box to the starter, and the connection
at the starter.  Also check the starter itself: short across the solenoid
(the two big connections) to see if the starter spins, then short the
right-most(pass side) big terminal to the little terminal to see if it
tries to start the bus.  This isolates the problem to either starter (1st
test fails),  solenoid (second test fails) or wire twixt switch and
solenoid (neither test fails).
