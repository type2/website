I was goofing around in my '62 bus last night at the apartment
complex, and a couple with a 3- or 4-year-old boy came out
to get in their Jeep Cherokee.  The kid kept saying, "Mom,
what's THAT?"  "Mom, what's THAT?"  over and over, until mom
finally answered, "A car."  This is when I realized the kid
was staring at my lovely green and white bus, so I 
popped my head out to say hi.  Then he said, with that
voice of assuredness only little kids can have,

"It's a RACE CAR!!!!"

All I could do was agree.
