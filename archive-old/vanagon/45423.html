The brake fluid reservoir (not just for the front brakes) is located under
the instrument cover behind your speedometer.  Sit in the driver's seat and
put bothe hands on the back of the cover, grasping the little depressions on
each side meant for your fingers.  Pull up and back and the cover will snap
off.  To access the reservoir, disconnect the little electircial connection &
unscrew the cap.
