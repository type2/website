Yeah, I've seen this done in a few westies (westy's?) and I
think it's fine as long as you have a good wood supply.  I've
seen little pot belly type stoves.  The one I remember was 
up behind the drivers seat in a 'brick' type camper and had
a stack that went out out where the window used to be.  

I think this would be useful if you were actually living in
the vehicle.  Kind of messy.  I wouldn't do it otherwise.
Seems like it would be a bit of a hassle to keep feeding the
stove though IMHO.
