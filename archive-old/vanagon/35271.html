David Goldberg mentioned that the owners manual for his '79 states the
height to be  80 1/8".  I had previously measured the '83 to be 6'8" (80")
running siimilar tires.  I prefer to park mine outside of the scary parking
garages. Keep in mind variations in the flooring and the over hanging
sprinkler pipes and light fixtures might lend a few surprises!  Also the
length of your bus as it starts up the up ramp might have some "trig"
effects on the height depending on where the roof of the garage changes to
the up ramp roof. After damaging the canopy of the single cab in a garage
with the "proper" clearance I keep the tall buses outside.
