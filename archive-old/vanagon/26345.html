>  My 86 Vanagon GL developed a tiny head leak 2 years ago.  I talked with
my local mechanic, he called a dealer mechanic, then decided he didn't want
to get into messing with the heads.  He also recommended trying a very small
amount of a stop leak product before doing anything else.  I put a tiny
pinch of Alumaseal (literally the amount I could get between my thumb and
forefinger) in and the leak has not recurred for two years, nor have I
noticed other problems...overheating or the like.  So I guess my experience
is it seems OK--at least in very small quantities for very small leaks.  Of
course maybe it has created something I don't know about yet.
