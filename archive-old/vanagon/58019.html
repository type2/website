It is very easy to replace the electrical switch.  
1. remove the plastic covers.
2. Remove the plug to the ignition,
3. If I remenber correctly there is only one screw holding the switch in
place.
4. Slide the switch out of the metal housing. 
5. reinstall.

I have disassembled a switch and cleaned the contacts.  A new one only
costs $8.  The steering, lock bolt is more difficult to remove. I think
it requires drilling at a particular point to release a holding pin.
Good luck
