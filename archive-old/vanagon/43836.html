Sounds like you may have a viscosity and or wear issue.  What oil are you
using and how hot is your engine getting?  The whole idea of that "Dynamic"
system is to give you some warning before it's too late.  Check actuall oil
pressure with a aguge and the engine "HOT"  A healthy engine with 20-50 will
hold 10 to 15 psi at idle and 35-50 psi at 3000 rpm.
