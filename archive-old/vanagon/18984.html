weather. Over the long haul they _might_ cost more due to the mileage thing.

Worked fine. They (Kellys) sound great for winter, get some spare rims and 
mount'em up, save wear on the real good tires, and on changeover costs. I 
noticed that the 8pr Yokos had a soft wall, not half as stiff as a set of 4 
ply Pirelli M+S Winter-190's I had on the '85.
