  Careful here, I think that while both engines would "fit", the '79 will
  have the "square" exhaust ports that use a normal kind of exhaust gasket
  and the '78 will have "roundish" exhaust ports that use that little copper
  ring up inside the port (like the '76). If getting an engine from a '79,
  make sure to get the entire exhaust system with it.
