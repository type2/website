Ron,

Are you sure the sound's coming from the front? I have a similar sound 
and I'm pretty sure it's coming from a sticky emergency brake cable and 
the resulting sticky right rear brake...of course, I'm open to exploring 
other possibilities.

After driving, check (careful not to hurt fingers) to see if any of your 
wheels are hot. That could indicate a sticky brake or maladjusted pads.
