Be careful here.  The rear seat used in the 87,88,89 Wolfsburg editions, 89,
90, 91 Carat, and 90, 91 Multivan is narrower (about 4") than the standard
vanagon seat to make room for that driver's side table panel and the table
itself.  You can't just buy the table/panel and install it in you van, unless
you change the rear seat also.
