 The fall meander through the Southwest in search of aspens and fine beers
 gave 3762 miles/200.3 gallons_at_$257US  for 18.7mpg and ~$0.07 per mile for 
 gasoline.  Prices ranged from 99c/gal to 178c/gal.  

 Auburn CA->Austin NV->GBNP NV->Delta UT-> Moab UT-> Gr. Jct CO-> DG0 CO->
 Navajo Dam NM-> Chama NM-> Taos NM -> Antonito CO-> DGO CO-> Moab UT->
 Meadow UT-> GBNP NV-> Austin NV-> Tonopah NV-> Lee Vining CA -> YNP CA
 Bridgeport CA-> Markleeville CA -> Auburn CA.

 Nice ride.  Good soaks at Austin, Meadow, Bridgeport, Lee Vining, Markleevl.

 Fine Beers Eske's (Taos), Eddie McStiffs (Moab), Carvers (DGO), Steamworks
 (DGO), Mesa Cerveza (Cortez NM).
