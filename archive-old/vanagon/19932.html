Something I encountered after installing a rebuilt in my 70:  the carb had
been sitting for so long that the float bowl dried out.  Check with a mirror
to see that gas is getting in the carb throat.  Before you pour gas down the
throat, take off the 5 screws that hold the top of the carb on and make sure
the bowl is full of gas.  If not, fill it up.  Are the points/condensor good?
