I know a nice spot near Ensenada called the Bufadora, which is a place on
the ocean where the surf shoots up through a hole in the rocks about 40
feet high.  Nice for a stop, at least.

There is also a little town between TJ and Ensenada that is famous for
Lobster.  I think it is called Puerto Nuevo.  We went there and got a
complete 1-lb lobster dinner with a margarita for $8.  

These places are not even 100 miles into Mexico, not true "Baja", but they
are nice places to stop by.  Lots of people here like to go to San Felipe,
which is about 200 miles inside Mexico on the Eastern side of the Baja
Penninsula.  I haven't been there, though.
