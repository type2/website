Having gone through a similar experience, after a rebuild (engine caught
fire - so  perhaps count yourself lucky) here is what I think may be your
problem: the valves were/are too too tight/ timing is too far advanced. It is
not
uncommon for the valves to tighten up after a rebuild. It is therefore a
good idea to readjust them after an initial 20 minute break-in. Tight
valves will cause an engine to overheat and cause a loss of power as you
describe. 
 Your best bet would be to go for the tow - check the compression and
timing and the valves on all four cylinders. Let us know how it turns out
----DC
