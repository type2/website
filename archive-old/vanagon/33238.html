my solution:  go to the carwash and take the
high pressure hose to 'em.

for the front drains, weep hole at bottom of
post but blow the drains out from above (corners
of the sunroof frame)

for the rear drains, weep hole is a tube just
visible through the engine air louvers high
on the side of the bus.  these you will have to
blow out in reverse, which means that this will
probably be temporary.

for me, twice a year does it.
