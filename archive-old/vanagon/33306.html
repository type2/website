Alex,

The bus clutch disk might fit a diesel Vanagon if it is 215mm.  My 82 diesel 
came with a 068 141 032A clutch disk (Fichtel & Sachs). 

There is no way a Rabbit pressure plate will work in a diesel Vanagon.  
They are completely different.  My old pressure plate says "068 101 F&S" 
on it, which is kind of odd for a VW part number, so I'm not sure if this 
is the factory part no.
