Ack!  I ran Semi-synthetic in my 1776 and it went from a mildly warm 
running engine to a cooker.  I quickly went back to dino and solved 
my problem.

Apparently synthetic sheds the heat into the case, and in our little 
air (really oil) cooled engines, you need the heat to stay in the oil 
until it goes through the air-cooler.
