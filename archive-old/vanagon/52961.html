    Setting Valves
     Open up the distrubuter. Point the rotor to anyone of the 
     Cylinder. Now watch the crank pulley and turn to line up the 
     timing mark. The Cylinder that the rotor points to is ready
     to be set. Otherwise known as Top dead Center (TDC). Do the
     Same for the others. Can't make a mistake.
