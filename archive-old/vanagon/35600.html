I get mine at professional autobody supply stores, usually PPG Finishes
stores. It comes in a gallon plastic bottle, enough for several very rusty
cars. Rebottle the leftover and sell it to your friends down at the
autocross. An autobody repair shop should be able to tell you where to find
a supply shop. They sell paint, paint guns, finishes, polishes, machines,
sandpaper, body fasteners, airhoses and connectors, as well as chemicals.
Some Newark-area numbers I got through a Yellow Pages Directory search on
the WWW follow (I haven't called them):
