The pertronix is not the problem unless it is defective or installed
incorrectly.  Check the ignition timing and make sure it is not too far
advanced.  If you are using a 009 distributer and have the timing advanced at
idle, that is probably the problem.  Set near TDC and turn up idle speed with
carb.  More air will decrease the HC. Also make sure that the engine does is
not leaking through the heads.  Compression and leak down tests.  New engine
means new, not always good.  Hate to sound pesimistic, but been there too
often.  Also make sure there are no vacuum leaks.  Especially at the manifold
to head gaskets.  A small leak ther will cuase one cylinder to runn lean.
 This lean mis-fire will make a lot of HC problems.
