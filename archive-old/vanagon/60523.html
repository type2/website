After plenty of searching, I've solved the "no-credit-cards" dilemma.  As
you may recall, I'd been reluctant to start taking credit cards because of
the roughly 5% cost of taking them.  Since my goal has always been
rock-bottom pricing, I went looking for a way to offer "charge-by-phone"
convenience while maintaining my minimal-markup policy.

My solution - Bank Drafts By Phone (or E-mail).  Basically, this is similar
to using your ATM card to make a purchase.  You may have done that before
at a supermarket or gas station.  It works just like paying with your
credit card over the phone, except that the amount will be debited directly
from your Checking Account instead of from your credit card.  What's more,
you do not need to actually have an ATM card or credit card to use this
service, just a checking account.

I have negotiated a really good rate for this service, so it won't raise my
prices.  Now I can keep my deep-discount pricing (and the Listmember
discounts!), plus offer you the convenience of ordering by phone.   

By the way, I'm told that this option can be used by Canadian list members
as well.  The amount will simply be paid in US dollars based on the current
exchange rate .

Of course, I will continue to accept checks or money orders by mail, or
ship C.O.D..  This just presents you with one more convenient option. 
Anyone interested in ordering this way, just call or e-mail me.
