 What you need is a friend who'll help. Virtually any job is easier if
there are two of you to do it. Another handy confidence builder is to 
dismantle a scrap bus- either a junker bought for parts or to get the bits
you want off a junkyard bus. Once you've taken one to bits it's a lot easier
to do your own- and since it's already a junker it doesn't matter if you
screw it up.
