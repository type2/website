Selecting the PCI button makes the radio look for a transmitted 
identity code from the radio stations and, if found, it displays the 
ident instead of the frequency.

To adjust the front/rear balance, try pushing the volume control in 
and turning it.  Similarly, pulling the volume control out should let 
you adjuct the left/right balance.
