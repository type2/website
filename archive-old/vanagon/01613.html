 One of my cv joints started banging so loudly, it sounded like someone was
hitting the side of my Bus with a baseball bat!  I removed them, cleaned
and inspected them thoroughly, repacked them with grease, reinstalled them
in the same orientation, and so far they've lasted almost 10,000 miles.
When I inspected them, a few of the balls had started to turn slightly
blue (hmmm....taken out of context, this would sound like an entirely
different subject!), but, given the price of new ones, I decided to go for
it anyway.  So far, so good.
