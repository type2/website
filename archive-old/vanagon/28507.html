Golfs and Jettas produced between 1985 and 1990 or 91 contained defective
heater cores that could and often did rupture as described in Digest 685.
It was the core itself that was defective, as I understand it. Their was
an official recall on it and I had the core replaced on my '85 Golf under
this plan.
