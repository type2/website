DW>They sound like the heat shields for the manifolds.  As the exhaust gas
DW>exits the heads into the exhaust manifold, it is as hot as it ever will
DW>be.  To keep it from radiating into the area around the cylinders the
DW>manifolds have a sheet metal heat shield spaced off them ~1/4 in.  If they
DW>are not there, the pipes add their heat to the air that is cooling your
DW>cylinders, which makes it less able to cool.  Not great, but probably not
DW>immediatley fatal.  Steve sez get 'em back, put 'em on.
