contraption that I knew of a few years back that was sorta like that. It
had a M/C engine, & standard M/C front & back tires. The body was much like
a fighter jet-pointy front, with a military style canopy; seated 2, driver
in front like an M/C. Had 2 stubby outcropings where wings would be on an
aircraft, a small tire on each. Only 3 wheels ever hit the ground at the
same time-had to have a M/C license to drive it. Controls were automotive
style, except steering wheel was like a jet aircraft. The thing got
incredible gas mileage due to the areodynamic styling and light weight. The
figure I remember was somwere in the neighborhood of 60mpg. Cost was around
$15+ k. I wanted one but couldn't hang with the price tag. Neat little
things, all the advantages of each of the genres it ripped off-except the
off-road capabilities of jet aircraft...
