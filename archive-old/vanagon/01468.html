The heads on VWs the 914 are reversible, ie. fit left or right side, so what 
points forward backwards on a 914 can be swapped to point backwards on a VW, 
IF it bolts in at all!. The side to side width may not fit ANY recognizable 
914 or VW muffler when installed in a VW, the flanges will line up nicely 
though. BUT (humble apologies) I have NOT done this, that's why the 
suggestion to phone Automotion for advice. It was bolt-to-bolt centre 
distance problems, rear valence clearances and heater junction box 
position/orientation that would really stymie this process. I can't tell 
about the heater air flow directions (in/out) in the small photo in the 
catalogue, if they're both on the same end this would be awkward to say the 
least. I've been desperate/frustrated enough to consider any potential 
stainless steel option for bus exchangers, since the prices are comparable. 
They rust out in <5 years here, even though still exhaust tight through to 
the tailpipe. Sorry for any confusion about direct bolt-in possibility!
