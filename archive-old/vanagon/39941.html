I believe that would be Electronic Differential Lock, which, I guess,
is the nerdic equivalent of Traction Control System...  Anyway, if
you have ABS, and your name happens to be Ric and you're teaching
your son Erik how to late brake for an offramp, but he overcooks 
the apex and gets a wheel or two onto the loose stuff, the ABS/EDL
system is supposed to apply braking to the wheel(s) w/ less traction
and divert more power to the wheels that still have traction in an
effort to make one look like J M Fangio at his best...   ;-)
