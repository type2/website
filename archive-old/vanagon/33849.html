WD-40 works well for bottom fishing with bait.  The attraction is the oily
film that lures the fish, like a natural bait would produce.  WD-40 is
outlawed in the State of California for fishing, as well as a lot of other
effective methods of catching fish.  I always carry a can in the Westie
weekend fishinwagon, strictly for maintenance. ;)
