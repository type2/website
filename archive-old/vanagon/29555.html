 I had a 73 Westy with a 77 2 litre motor with AFC.   Yes, I did get jump
starts.  It didn't fry the brain,  but eventually I did have to replace both
the dual relay and the air box.    They were expensive then, and I'm sure
more now.    There was no direct correlation,   but those warnings led me to
believe some connection was there.  But after I replaced the alternator  it
always started up.
