matter) to say anything other than maybe.

Electrically speaking:
        If the power the fridge draws is constant, then halving the
voltage would double the current.

If this is the case, a voltage splitting circuit would be simple to
design and likely cost well under $10.00 to build. Find out if this is
the case, and I'll throw together a quick schematic for you with parts
readily available at Radio Trash er Shack.

Power=Current multiplied by voltage

        If the power the fridge draws is NOT constant, then it would
work but the power would be halved.
