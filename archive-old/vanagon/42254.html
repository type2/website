I suppose that sanding and priming or putting in new metal depending on the
extent of the rust would probably be the recommended fix.  There's also stuff
called Extend (brand name) that is a rust sealant/converter.  You scrape off
the excess rust and then spray this on.  It forms a bond with the rust (or
maybe reacts with it), and leaves a hard black finish that can be painted.
