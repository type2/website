Subject:      Re: '81 Westy excessive clutch pedal play

Almost certainly a leak of some sort.  Crawl under the van and look at
and feel the clutch slave cylinder for signs of wetness.  Search down
around the footwell for signs of a leak also.  You'll probably find that
one or the other (master or slave) has a leak.

These tend to go at about the same time in my experience, so you might as
well replace or repair both.
