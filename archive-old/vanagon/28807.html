I think you're right about a ground problem, but it's more likely 
between the engine and the frame - on Rabbits it usually a braided 
copper cable on the back of the head, which gets oil soaked with 
age. Cleaning in solvent & replacing may cure the problem. I've heard 
that a secondary ground strap can also help here.
