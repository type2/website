Hello Chris:  I am still trying to import an 94 EV Westfalia from Canada.
You need to make sure from the owner that the vehicle meets EPA standards
(look in engine compartment; sometimes on drivers door or door post) and
Department of Transportation safety standards (usually on the drivers door
post).  In my case this requires a letter from VW certifying that it does
meet USA DOT standards.  The letter has been long in coming and may require
alterations - light; speedometer changed from km/hr to mph.

The DOT has a helpful web page specifically on vehicle importing from
Canada.  It is "http://www.nhtsa.dot_dot_gov/cars/rules/import/can0797.html".

Once I have more answers I will post to the vanagan mailing list.

Good luck.
