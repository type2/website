On Thu, 28 Sep 1995, Paul Smiley wrote:

> Does anyone know the criteria that a vehicle must meet before it can be 
> registered as an antique?  Is it just 25 years old??

Depends on where you live.  In B.C., 25 years gets you "vintage".  I 
_think_ 35 years get you "antique."  But the criteria for qualifying are 
pretty rigorous: must be totally rust-free, totally stock, etc.

A good thing about vintage or antique designation is that it exempts the 
vehicle from emissions testing.
