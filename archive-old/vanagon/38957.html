You either have a bad valve or there is a problem eith the idle speed
controller.  Unplug the valve, if the idles does not drop, the valve is bad.
If the valve checks good, check the idle speed controller. It is mounted in
front of the reght rear tailight.  Make sure the Oxygen sensor circuit is
working correctly also.  A slightly rich mixture will cause a fast idle that
the idle controller may not be able to controll.
