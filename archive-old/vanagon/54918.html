For some reason, they did this around '89-90 or so. Earlier diesels 
used a different final drive and sometimes different gears (5th 
especially). My '85 is about perfect for a diesel - at 100km/h in 
5th, it turns only ~2600 rpm. Using a gas trans gives about ~3000-
3300 rpm depending on the model.

The only Vanagon diesel I've driven was similar (too noisy at 55mph), 
VW should've made the 5speed a standard item to fix this problem.
