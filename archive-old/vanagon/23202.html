In my '69 Bus many years ago, its CV bolts were hex socket bolts,
needing an allen wrench to get them off. Don't know if yours are
the same.

I did the WD-40 thing overnight, then a sharp tap, then more WD-40,
then put my allen wrench on, and a 10" crescent wrench on the allen
wrench. This got them all off. I didn't break the allen wrench (it
was a Sears Craftsman), but I probably came pretty close a few times.
I _did_ bang my share of knuckles when the crescent slipped off the
Allen wrench a few times. Maybe there's a better way, but this worked.

'Course, if your hex-heads are all stripped out, or if you don't
have hex-heads, then  *** Never Mind *** .
