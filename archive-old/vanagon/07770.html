I respect the opinions of those who think that the air flaps/thermostat are
better left on and functioning.  I think that for *my* situation it was
better to leave them off because the cooling system is underpowered and
needs all the help it can get, plus I always warm it up for a couple of
minutes before I begin driving.  I don't expect people to agree with me and
I don't claim that my way is the best, or even that it is a good idea for
anyone else, but it is working for me.  I'm happy to discuss my reasoning
with everyone, but frankly, I don't think I am going to change my mind about
this unless someone runs two identical engines in a laboratory, etc.
