>I had a similar problem on the same tail light/turn signal/headlight
 circuit.  After searching for the grounded power line I found the 
 culprit...a bad flasher unit.  Pulled that puppy out from the bottom of
 the fuse buss (you could see it was burned) and replaced it...everything
 was back to normal.

 If that's not it...I would disconnect major lines like the power line
 that feeds the entire tail light section or the entire headlight section
 so you can get an idea as to where (front or rear) you need to look for
 the illusive grounding hot wire.
