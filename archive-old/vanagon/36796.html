Something very sad happened to VW in the end of the 70's.

Based on the experiences shared by members with Vanagon's and EV's, I 
could never be corageous enough to consider one of these as a primary 
driver.

with the wasserboxer, which would make anyone moving from an air-

perfect either.  No heat, no cool are problems that a water-cooled

Admittedly my '57 is more of a toy than a real driver, but I drove

other than standard mainainence.

I'll again silently remain an air-cooled only Vdubber...
