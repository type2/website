> RUN don't walk back to the seller and get rid of that stuff!!!!.    Syncros in
> a tranny require a certain optimal amount of friction,  and PTFE isn't the
> answer.  For a straight transmission, Redline Synthetic Oil corp. makes a
> Manual Transmission Lubricant, (MTL) that is specifically designed to retain
> the right amout of friction for the syncros, while providing proper
> lubrication.  It is especially good in cold weather to relieve stiff shifting
> before the gearbox warms up.   I THINK that they make another version for
> transaxels like VWs use, or that MTL is compatible.  You can check with them
> at:  Redline Synthetic Oil Corp., 3450 Pacheco Blvd, Martilnez, Ca. 94553. 
> (510) 228-7576.
> 
> The standard disclaimers apply, I have no connection, get no benefit (other
> than the fact that with  MTL in my 136Kmile  5 sp tranny on a BMW the tranny
> sure is a lot happier with MTL than wihout it)  etc  . . .
