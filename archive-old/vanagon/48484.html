> 2.0, Ignition Timing 10 deg BTDC. 

a couple more questions:  what fuel octane are you using?  also, the 
ignition timing must be checked and set with a specific set of 
conditions as noted in the BGBB (Big Green Bentley Book).  if the 
timing was set without adhering to the procedure, it could be off.

also, although your CO level is normal, you *may* have a lean 
condition caused by a variety of things, including a bad fuel 
pressure regulator.  if it is convienient, have the NOx checked and 
if this is sky high, start looking for items to cause a lean 
condition.  this will cause pinking and generally poorer performance.
