Last night, I got the rubber around the door installed.  A lot harder
than it looks, if you do it the way I did.

to cut it.

it will all fit if you scrunch it up a little.  It manages to even itself
out before the glue dries.

I'm using DAP Weldwood contact cement.  Sticky, yet forgiving.
