Jim,

Sometimes rust freezes the center of the drum where it slides over the axle
nut.  I just use a long screwdriver poking through one of the inspection
holes in the backing plate-- hammer the screwdriver so that it pushes the
drum off, rotating the drum so that you can work it off gradually.  Before
you reinstall the drum, clean off the rust where the drum contacts the stub
axle and apply a thin coat of anti-sieze.   Should work fine!
