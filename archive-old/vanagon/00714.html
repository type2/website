buses by the arch is a get-together put on by LiMBO (Late Model Buses Owners,
international) and NEATO (North-East Association of Transporter Owners).
LiMBO is for 1968-1979 Buses, 1980-1991 Vanagons, and 1992-? Eurovans. they
put out a newsletter every other month with helpful hints and pictures and
stuff (ads for t-shirts, parts, buses for sale, all that sort of stuff).

NEATO is for the 1949-1967 split-windshield buses (the north-east version of
SOTO).

anyhoo, twice a year, these yahoos get together near St. Louis, MO, to mix
and mingle with other bus types. it's in O'Fallon, MO ... Ft. Zumwalt?? some
sort of park. and the next exciting Gathering of the Buses is due on 27-29
May, 1994 ... just three weeks off. <i haven't gotten my SASE back yet!! :( >
