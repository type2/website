Locked?  What is locked?  Is the '56 that different from my '61?  The doors

the inside handle (that's how I work the passenger-side door on The Bozobus

anyone else park with their right-side wheels on the curb to keep gas from
flowing out of the filler?).  At one time I made a sliding window lock out
of some fancy plywood that was tabbed to fit behind the slidey one and keep
it from moving...
