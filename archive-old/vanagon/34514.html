Regarding the above,  It sounds to me that you have a clutch or main shaft
pilot bearing problem.  Get fixed before you do transmission damage.  All
gears including reverse are synchronized.  Clutch not properly disengaing or
pilot bearing dragging will cause all above sysmptons.  While trans it out,
carefully inspect input shaft for wear where it sits in pilot bearing.  This
is a needle bearing in the crankshaft.  Flywhhel also has to be removed to
service it.  The felt washer/seal must be installed to keep clutch disc dust
from destroying this bearing.  Many people do no repace this as it is only
available from the dealer.
Hope this helps,
