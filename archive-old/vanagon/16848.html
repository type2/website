I do what Muir says on my 71'  I clean the valve covers and glue the gaskets 
to them.  I also use a bit on grease in the other side (the one which faces 
the head.)
Using this method, you can reuse your gaskets for around 4-5 valve 
adjustments.  This method doesn't leak on my 71.
Hope this helps.
