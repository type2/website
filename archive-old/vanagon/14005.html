This is probably not a good idea. Such converter is not a converter. It 
eats same amount of power as the 6V wiper does. If the wiper takes 50 watts
to operate the described "converter" will also consumes 50 watts. 

A wiper moter could take 10 amps to operate. This will put a 10 amps current
through the power transistor. You would need to have a fan to blow on the
heatsink of the transistor.
