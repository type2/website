Chris,
With a recently installed engine, I would suspect that you have a
leaking flange or other bolted connection.  Chances are that you can
resolve this leak by simply checking and tightening all of your exhaust
system hardware.  This is a good practice with a new engine as the
thermal cycles do tend to allow the cylinder head flange gaskets to
relinquish some of their installed torque.  The more you drive it with
the leak, the less of a chance you will have of not requiring a new
gasket as the exhaust gas causes quick erosion past any leaks.
