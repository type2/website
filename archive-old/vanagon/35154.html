Do you mean valve _guides_ or valve _seats_?  Type IV heads, as they come
from the factory, are (in)famous for having their valve seats come loose.
See June 1995 _DB&HWs_ magazine for an article on Type IV head rebuilding,
featuring Mark Stephens High Performance.  It shows the painstaking process
Stephens performs on Type IV heads to keep the valve seats from ever coming
loose.
