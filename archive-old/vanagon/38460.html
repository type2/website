I put an aftermarket sunroof into the front 1/4 of the raisable shell.
The sunroof has a crank which pops up the back portion and then slides
the glass 2/3 of the way back.  The crank can be reached from the
driver's seat so it's convenient to operate while underway.  Helps
ventilation a lot and you can get to stuff that may be on the roof
racks.  For rainy climates, this solution is not as good as the stock VW
vent because you can't leave it open more than a bit without getting
water inside.
