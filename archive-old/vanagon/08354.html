Rear end bus shafts can be removed, since the stub axles remain in position, 
but the front axles on a Synchro will suffer from the above problem. You can 
disconnect at the differential end and hang them in thick 1" hemp rope 
slathered in grease. Don't use poly rope!!!  I've had to do this for a 180 
mile tow of a salvaged Citroen chassis/pan. No fun stopping to check every 
hour or so.  tim s.
