as you may know, '82 was the final (full) year of the air-cooled
engine -- there may or may not have been any sold as '83s; thereafter,
vw went into a disastrous flirtation with the water-cooled engine and
didn't come up with a good version of water-cooled until late '85 or
'86.  i bought an '82 vanagon (not westy) for exactly that reason,
air-cooled (traditional), the last of a great breed, and cheap -- i paid
$2,400 for a fresh rebuild at 102,000 and am very happy with it so far
altho i don't use it much except to kick around town (san francisco). 
$600 is an amazing price if it's running well, and maybe even if it
isn't running well (depending on local price for a rebuilt or whatever's
needed).  what you do want to avoid is a watercooled per above.  the
good watercooleds, 86 or so or later, are very expensive.  ultimately,
of course, it all comes down to whether the one you are looking at is
any good per se and/or worth the 600 (or more, or less).  price is
suspiciously low, in my view, so get a trusted mechanic to check it out,
and pay him for his expertise, i'd suggest.  cheers...
