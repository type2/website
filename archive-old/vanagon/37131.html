I replaced the original carriage bolts with eye bolts that I bought at home
depot.  To add a finishing touch, I found a rubber seal and matching
stainless steel disk with a hole for the eye bolt to pass through so that it
looks tidy. The eye bolts can come in handy when you're camping and you want
to clip a tarp to them to make an awning.  You can also use them to secure
light cargo on your roof.
