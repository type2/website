Yes, there is a problem with corrosion along the head gaskets causing water
leaks.  It generally takes several years and 60,000 to 130,000 miles for the
problem to occur.  When the leak occurs the heads need to be replaced
(possibly repaired) at a cost of up to $2,300 complete at a dealer.  Doing it
yourself  is much cheaper, but not a real simple job.  I like my Vanagons so
much, that I've just resigned myself to the fact that I'll have to live with
it.  Even $2,300 is only 2.3 cents per mile if the heads last 100,000 miles.
 Some have gotten well over 150,000 miles from the original heads, but this
is not common.  Changing coolant every 2 years or sooner seems to help.
