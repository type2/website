Victoria is about 200000 people.  I live about 25 miles away.  Westys are a 
popular item; within three blocks of my home there are at least two other
Westys.  They are an extremely popular recreation vehicle in this area.  When
I go grocery shopping, I usually see one or two other Westys in the parking
lot.  

I think that Victoria has always been partial to Volkswagens;  until five
years ago, there were two dealerships, and that was before the population hit
170,000.  Now there is only one (large) VW dealership.  They sell Porsche too,
so every time I go for parts, I check out the Roadsters.
