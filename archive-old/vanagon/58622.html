yesterday on the chat server.  I would be very uneasy using a child's 
seat in any of the stock Westy seats on an air cooled Bus or Westy

Vanagon with a regular center bench installed is about your only safe 
choice IMHO.  Yes it would be a pain in the ass, but at least the 
Vanagon seat is free standing (why the hell couldn't the Bus one be) 
so when you do remove it you can use it around the campground.
