> Consider leaving some room for Redline Water Wetter. This will greatly
> reduce corrosion and allow the coolant to transfer heat better. This
> product breaks down the surface tension of water, allowing it to hug the
> cylinder head walls much better, causing less eddys and turbulence and
> therefore pitting and corrosion. I just bought some and will install
> soon. Type Water Wetter in at Alta Vista and there are web pages on
> this. As well there have been discussions on the list about this.
