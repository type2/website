There are two brands that I know of, both made in Germany - Rienz (sp?) and
Elring.  The Rienz comes with sealant, while I have seen the Elring kits
packaged both with and without sealant. I have sold plenty of both brands
(and we have used both in our repair shop), and have not noticed any
discernable difference in quality between the two brands.
