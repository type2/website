The exhaust is the major difference.  The CA model has the cat in a different
place and uses an O2 sensor.  If you delete the CA exhuast, you will need to
replace the computer.  Some of the tin is different around the exhaust.
 Scavenge and reuse the EGR parts if possible.  These engines need the EGR to
keep them from melting down.
