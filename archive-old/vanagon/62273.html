Folks - the Popular Mechanics CD is actually made by Alldata. I was the
product manager for Alldata's biggest competitor, Mitchell
International's On-Demand for many years so have some inside info on
this stuff. Alldata arranged for a license to use the PM name and
marketing power. A tidy arrengement, which becomes more clear if you
know that Alldata got much of their service information for their

Hearst company. Now you see even more evidence of this trend to buy
familiar names, such as the "Car & Driver" brand air cleaners now at
Target, "Mad" TV, etc.
