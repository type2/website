Jeez, a proliferation of posts on the carbs today.

1.  I couldn't get them to run right with the holes simply plugged.  I'm
	told you need a metering device of some kind in there.

2.  Most people seem to think the Webers aren't that good.  My
	experience bears that out, but YMMV.  

If I were you (and I have had the same problem) I would get a carb body
that isn't stripped out and transfer all of your parts onto it.  Come to
think of it, that's what I did.  You could also put a helicoil in there,
but I HATE helicoils.  They make me as mad as a baby with a new tooth.
