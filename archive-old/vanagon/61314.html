That sounds like a vacuum leak. Have you tried listenening with a
"stethescope" made of about 4 feet of garden hose? run the motor at idle and
put the hose VERY near the vacuum fittings.

OR

With an extinguisher nearby, with the motor running, spray carb cleaner or
(non-flammable-type) Liquid wrench on each of the fittings individually, and
seqentially. When you hit the one that is leaking, the pace of the motor will
change.
