In late 87, VW changed over to a donut on the inlet side os the CAT.  Play
havoc when changing the CAT or that inlet pipe.  Your lcal FLAPS should be
able to get you a donut that is flat with a guide ring on one side.  This
will allow you to match up the parts needing a donut on one side and a flnge
on the other.  BTW, the Walker CAT.(#15612) will accept either a donut or the
gasket on the inlet side.
