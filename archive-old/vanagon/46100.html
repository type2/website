- Check your O2 sensor. If it hasn't been replaced in awhile, you
should replace it. ( < $50 I think). A bad O2 sensor will drop your
mileage in half. My '84 Vanagon went from 10mpg to 19pmg when I
replaced the O2 sensor this spring. The 02 sensor is that cylindrical
thing screwed into the exhaust pipe. It should have a wire coming out
of it.

(p.s. the O2 sensor light only does not come on when the O2 goes bad.)
