I have recently put a 30 pict carb on my dual port ghia engine in my 71 bus,
with a 009  distributor. I used an adaptor plate and have had some
difficulties (gas oozing around the gasket), but I have not had any luck
with 34 pict carbs so I thought I would try the 30. I also installed a
header and a quiet pack muffler, which has worked nicely.  Richard is right
the adaptor appears to be inefficient, but anything is better than the old
peice o' crap I previously had on my intake.

I use the force when I time my engine and adjust the idle, I have tried with
the static and stobocopic to get it right, but I alway do it better by ear.
