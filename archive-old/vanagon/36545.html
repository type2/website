   Someone I'm sure has already wrote you and posted their comments to 'the
List' But here is my 2 cents:  Mexican buse use 1.8L rabbit motors instead
of the flat-4 watercooled motor we have here in the states.  I've been the
Cancun and rented a bus for the day, what a nice bus.  The old style bubble
window mated with watercooling, what a concept!  A few of the options on
the mexican buses come form the Vanagon, IE: steering wheel, mirrors, seats
and radiator. I would love to have one.  The Salesman at the VW dearlership
in Cancun said the bugs go for about 7K and the bues were about 10k.  That
was almost 6 years ago.  Hope this answers your question. Take care.
