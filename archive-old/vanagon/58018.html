wires.  I am intimidated by adjusting points, so I switched to a Pertronix
Ignitor. Saw an immediate improvement in drivability (like new?).  PO was
original owner & had only routine maintenance done (oil changes, ect) but the
bus was missing the egr bits.  Other than a noisy lifter on startup after

has suprised a few motor heads at stoplights who see the bus as an easy
target to pass & cut off. (some NOx would really hurt their egos... naaaah;)
  According to conventional wisdom, I'm living on borrowed time.   You can't
prove it by me.
