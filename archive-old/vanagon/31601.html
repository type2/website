needed a new pop top seal. ie: once it starts to come apart, it 
rapidly falls apart.

I've priced new seal kits at $600 (dealer) or $150-200 (aftermarket), 
but I was wondering how tough they are to install. It appears they 
thin side section is glued to the wider front section at the two 
front corners - does it come this way, or is special glue needed?

seller to replace it first, or talk him down on price and do it
