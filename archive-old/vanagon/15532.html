Ok, the response has been profound about Bodega Bay! I'm talking like
maybe 9 vehicles!  Way to go, you geeks!  Now, the verdict is not yet
in on the date, but I wanted to call attention to something at least
one of you pointed out, and that is that Memorial weekend is THE
weekend everyone goes out camping/picnicing, etc., and that everbody
and their brother will be out then (sorry, Brad, that's an "expression"
that we "old" people used to use in days of yore  ;)>  Anyway,
keeping that in mind, I rather wonder whether the first weekend 
in June might be preferable to avoid as many crowds, and to get a
better site.
