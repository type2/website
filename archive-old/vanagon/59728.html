Hello Drew,

Going through same with my 100,000mi. '86 Syncro GL.  Here are my
considerations:

1.  Per mile cost has been very low.
2.  I can fix most things.
3.  In spite of some very nasty winters and rugged terrain here in
Colorado, it will still run when others won't get out of the driveway.
4.  I, personally, love the handling characteristics.
5.  I am the original owner.
6.  Its unique abilities have created a real niche in my overall
transportation needs and I'm not very sure I can replace it with a
single vehicle.
7.  ITS PAID FOR!

Just a few thoughts.
