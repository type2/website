Yes, it works, with this caveat;  works great at small signal levels, ie

air flow meter, and the harness connectors throughout the engine
compartment.  And a *little* smear goes a long way - it can also be diluted
with alcohol to seep into things like switches that are difficult to
disassemble, push buttons on older radios, etc.
