I had similiar experiences.  My solution was to disassemble it down to 
the swing tube, take it to a machine shop and have them put thread 
inserts into the soft metal inner casing of the reduction boxes.  It is a 
good idea not to use any 'screwed' up bolts when you put it all back 
together.  8-)    Good luck.
