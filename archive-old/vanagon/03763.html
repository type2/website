Joel mentioned in his FAQ that Beverly Hills Motoring Accessories was the
only place he could find to get Wheelskins leather steering wheel covers.
I have the pleasure to announce that I found them *right here in Missouri*
for only $22 at Imparts Ltd. (1-800-325-9043). To order, you need to know
the outside diameter *and* the grip size of your steering wheel. If they
don't have them, they will custom make them for you *for the same price*.
