I'm assuming you have an Eberspacher model BN4 heater.  The heater is 
mounted on the floor of the engine compartment to the right of the spare tire
well. Installation will require cutting holes for exhaust and intake air and 
cutting through the left side of the panel covering the gas tank to route 
the ducting to where it emerges below the rear .  Did y buy just thust the 
heater or all the du, cable, switch etc. that comprise the wholewhole 
system?
