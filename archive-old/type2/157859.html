<<Does anyone here have an effective, low labor trick for re-using their
blast media?>>

First Dan,you have to be blasting on a clean concrete floor.

Then you need a super sized funnel, and some fine window screen.

Shovel the silica sand back into the funnel with the screen in it, and
back into the blaster. 

You'll only get maybe 2 reloads from the used sand.
The cuttng edges of the sand will round off and be usless after the 2nd
re-load.
