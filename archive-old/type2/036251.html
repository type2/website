To do a leakdown test you have to have the piston at top dead center to
fire. To do this you need to screw the hose in the cylinder you are going to
test, then rotate the engine in the direction of rotation until you get
compression coming out the hose. Turn the crank to TDC on that piston. Apply
air. Does the air push the piston down? If yes: you are not on exact TDC.
Bring the piston back and try again. Apply air. Crank stays put? Now read
you % of leakage. Should be under 20, preferably 7-10.
