These heaters have a timer switch that kicks off after like 10minutes....if
you are running off an aux battery, I doubt you could fire it more than a
few times without draining the battery..................

Point is really can't use it for hours on end.....if you could you would
burn yourself up with heat.

Those propane heaters put out CO as well, and when you fall asleep with one
of those on you might not wake up.

I fire my BA-6 right before bedtime, it gets it hot in the van, and then I
fall asleep.  If I wake up and am frigid, I will fire it again, and that
will make it until morning.  My sleeping bag is a zero degree model, though.
