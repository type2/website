Hello Markus.

Without a regulator, the voltage output from a generator will continue
to climb until the unit burns up :>(  The regulator keeps the voltage at
*around* 13.5 to 14 volts. It must do this to put *juice* back into the
battery.

If you are getting 13.3V, your OK.

Yes, radios and stuff can handle this change.:>)
