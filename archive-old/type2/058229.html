This is a very important part, and due to its location it's one of those 
gotchas that will bite you in the ass if you don't check it out every 
once in a while.  I had a coupling disc fail on my old '75 Beetle.  The 
symptom was that when turning the steering wheel, the wheel would not 
return to center on its own -- it would just stick there wherever I 
turned it.  When I got the front wheels off to see what was going on I 
saw the rubber coupling disc torn all the way through and literally 
being held together by a couple threads.  I was *this close* to losing 
all steering ability.  Nothing sucks worse than losing your steering :-(

So get out there and check that sucker out, it may save your ass.
