Michael,

It sounds like an extra air leak someplace.  It could
even be the air bypass valve.  These make a tremendous
difference in fast idle speed, and of course if
they're not working right, on hot idle speed as well. 
The one on my 1980 Westfalia was adjustable, although
I had to take it off the van and disassemble it to
adjust.  They heat up electrically to taper off the
flow of bypass air as the engine warms up.
