Being the owner of multiple types of VWs I can't seem to picture the exact
setup of the Bus right now.  But if you can get to the back of the unit from
the inside of the engine bay you can just push down the spring clip (while
the bulb is out) to give it more tension.  You should also scrape off some
of the buildup of crud from where the little buttons on the bulb make
contact.  If you can't get to it from inside, use the 90 degree angle side
of a scribe to catch the spring clip and pull on it and use the same tool to
scrape off the contact points of the socket.
