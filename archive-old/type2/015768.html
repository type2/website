Blake, Harmon and all,

From my point of wiew VW wasn't that strict with the transition between
model years, at least not in the European markets. In the "border years"
like 1967-1968, 1970-1971 and 1971-1972 there are always some "bastard
busses" with mixes of both versions. Here in Finland we could always get
NOS Busses from last year. In the registration documents a 1971 bus could
become a 1972. My 1974 German made Bug have swing axels and the old style
front suspension. It could be confused for a 1969 if it wasn't for the
modern engine hood.

Let us not upset ourselves with these differences, let us enjoy them!

Vive la difference!
