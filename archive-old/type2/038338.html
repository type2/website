Check to make sure you have both caliper mounting bolts in place on both 
sides This could be a reason why you are hearing your grinding noise. With 
only one caliper mounting bolt your caliper will kind of float around and 
when you brake the caliper will be forced to hit the inside of your rim. 
Leave a nice scrape around the inside of the rim and the outside of the 
cailper.
