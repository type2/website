Well as far as I know the last year for the removeable apron was 71. The 
new type 4 motors required a change in the engine bay due to its larger 
size, so I beleive 72 was the first year it was not removable. I would 
not cut it out and make it a removable type because the strenght of the 
bus maybe involed. It is part of the structure of the bus unlike the 
earlier ones. It is not resonable to put an early type apron on a late 
bus, way too different. By the time you cut and welded to make it work 
you could have fabed someting better for less money and effort. If you 
were to weld in some strong brackets and reenforce stuff, it is possible 
to make a removable one but I would really look into the structure part 
of it first. Anybody else done this? Or seen it done?
