I found that the lock mechanism for the back hatch will
fit into the engine door.  Yes dont put an ugly padlock
on it!

it into the engine door lock.  I honestly dont remember
how I put it in step by step, but I remember thinking
it was almost made for the assembly.  Try it you'll
like it.

The only "issue" is that the key turns under instead of
over.
