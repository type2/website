>After I parked it, I got out and checked the oil
>level, it was right on the low line (not the bottom of the stick, but the
>lower of the two level lines).
>
>Could the oil light come on at that level?

That level would not be responsible for the oil light coming on.  The light
comes on when the pressure drops to about 8 psi.  This can happen if the
oil is very hot and the engine is idling slowly.  Tapping the gas to rev
the engine should make the light turn off in this case - if not then you
have a problem.

>I need to drive it tomorrow over
>100 miles. I plan on going out and adding a quart of oil, but even if that
>fixes it, do you think it would be safe to drive tommorrow.

DO NOT add a quart of oil!  That is way too much to add if you're at the
lower mark.  Add 1/2 quart max.
