..and you left it there? ;-)  Makes me think you mean "poptop canvas", not "tent"

  TENT: not permanently attached, often freestanding, has bags, poles, stakes.

  POPTOP CANVAS: the flexible part of an elevating roof

frustrate me, Mr. Language Nazi, even more than the VW-only usage of "ragtop" to mean a cloth-sunroof car (*everyone* else uses "ragtop" to mean "convertible" --
