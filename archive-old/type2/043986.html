> Can you use the front tin from a 72 to 74 Beetle?
[for a doghouse cooler installation in a type 1 engined bus]

Nope, because the bus tin extends higher and wider than the bug tin.
However, the bug tin can be a starting point.  Simply fabricate a plate
from sheet metal that matches the old non-doghouse bus tin and then weld
or braze it to the bug tin.  This seems to be exactly what VW did on the
'71 bus.
