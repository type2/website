One of the first things about learning the part numbering, VIN and other
serial number/letter sequences with Volkswagen is they NEVER use the letter
I or O because it is too easily confused with the numbers 1 and 0.

I'm sure this is a misprint in Tom Wilson's book (again mistaking a "1" for
an "I").

"D" Engines were 40HP 1200's originally installed in European Beetles (and
I'm sure elsewhere in the world) from 1966 on and replacement 40HP cases
also came with "D" prefixes as well, while they were in production.
