Hi Isiah,

On the 1968 - 1971 -- I would consider an engine pull if you are cleaning up
tin, replacing heat exchangers with J-tubes, and muffler.  Pulling the
engine is pretty easy...

However, if you want to do it in... the muffler can be more easily removed
by putting a jack under the oil drain plate and supporting the engine.  Then
you can remove the three center bolts and two outer bolts for your support
bar -- taking it off.

I presume that was the information you were looking for?
