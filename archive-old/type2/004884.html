Dear Chad & Jen, & List -

I would guess you have DC going through the winding in the coil.
How this could happen, I don't know. One possibility is that
points are closed and the ignition was left on.  There could also
be a short somewhere. If the coil was grounding internally, I
doubt if it would run the engine.

Check for possible grounds on the primary side of the coil, from
both terminals to the case of the coil.
