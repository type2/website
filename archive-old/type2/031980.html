> can someone define "pinging" for me? I read this in the archives/list
> all the time.... and could possibly be hearing it.... if i knew what it
> was...checked the muir sounds page, with no luck.

Pinging is pre-detonation of the air/fuel mixture, i.e., as the mixture 
is compressed it explodes on its own, without waiting for the sparkplug 
to fire.  On older cars with lots of internal carbon buildup the 
compression ratio is effectively raised to the point that your regular 
gas doesn't have a high enough octane rating to prevent this from happening.

If your car is pinging you'll typically hear it when the engine is 
working hard, e.g., accelerating rapidly, driving up a hill, etc.  Sounds 
vaguely like marbles being shaken up in a coffee can.
