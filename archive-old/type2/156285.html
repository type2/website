when my harness burnt up i had to go to radio shack and buy the right gauge wire... same gauge as the vw harness uses... cant think what that is without looking...
  should be in the haynes or bentley .. i think i found it in one of those two...gauge size~!~
   
  my harness plugs were fried..melted ka put.. so i had to buy ones from radio shack that are kinda like a L shape so that i could hook them back onto the plug(males of alternator and voltage regulator...

  hint here dont cut your self short with wire....
  wire the alternator and then you will know how much to pull through then after installing alternator back in... finish up ,up top....
