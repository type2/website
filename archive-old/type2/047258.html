GRRRRR!!!!!  NO!  I would recommend that you Don't do it and please don't
recommend that anyone else do it either, at least not until you have tried
it personally.

I read a post just like this a couple of years ago and decided to give it a
try.  I spent a lot of time tracking down the stuff and finally bought a
roll of it while I was on vacation up in Canada.  I was VERY DISAPPOINTED
with it.  It has a strong TAR odor and if the sun is out and the
temperature is above 70 degrees, the stuff softens up and RUNS.  It's also
very time-consuming and difficult to remove.

I have *almost* an entire roll left - it's been up in my attic ever since.
If anyone thinks they want to try this stuff out, they can have it for $8
(to buy me lunch) plus the cost of UPS shipping.
