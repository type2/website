 I'd suggest Just Kampers in the UK, as an alternative. I havn't heard 
anyone slagging them on the list, so they can't be doing too badly.
 They have a decent website (http://www.justkampers.co_dot_uk, strangely
enough) and a (UK-biased) chat site. They also ship overseas...and 
while the dollar/sterling exchange rate may not be doing you any 
favours you can take 17.5% VAT off the UK prices in the catalogue.
 They are happy to take credit cards, speak English etc.
 They have a new catalogue ("katalogue", in JK speak :-) which isn't
yet online... shouldn't be too long.. but you can still search for 
parts.
 I get most of my VW parts from them, but have no stake in their 
business.
