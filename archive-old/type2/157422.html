Subject: gas theft

Well, I was perfectly happy with my non-locking original gas cap -  until last night.  I went to dinner kind of late with a professor  friend of mine.  Parked across the lot from the place. But there  was still enough in it for it to run.  They didn't get much but  they also
 didn't put the gas cap back on.  That ticks me  off.  So I guess I'm going to be ordering a locking gas cap from  BD.  Grrr....KR
