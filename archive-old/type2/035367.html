Long ago, a grizzled mechanic taught me not to plug unused vacuum lines
with screws.  The threads can provide an opportunity for a vacuum leak
just large enough to make idle adjustment difficult.

His solution, which I still use, is to push a small ball bearing (a bit
larger than the ID of the line) into the line.  It seals great, and
allows you to cut the line shorter than a line with a screw.
