> Fuel Filter.

--> If it's not the fuel filter, make sure the cylinder head temp sensor
is tight in the head (but not *too* tight -- it strips easily!) and has
the proper resistance.  If it fails open or gets disconnected the engine
will load up on fuel and quit once it warms up, due to running rich.  If
it's loose, the engine will run rich erratically and misfire.
