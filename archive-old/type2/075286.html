>What BTW is the correct pronunciation?  I usually hear
>west FAYL ya but have also heard west FALL ya. 

Germans would probably pronounce it "vest-FAH-leea" or "vest-FAH-lya".  IIRC, the German vowel names/sounds are "ah", "ay", "ee", "oh", "oo".  One of the 
things I always liked about German back when I was learning it in HS was its isographic (?) nature -- a far more rigid relation between the letter and its sound than in 
English.  Usually no doubt about the pronunciation of a word when you see it in print.*

Within the U.S., few people will flinch if you call it a Volks-wagun West-fail-ya, even though most Germans would say "Folksvahgen Vestfahlia".

<sigh> Every now and then I get a yearning for worldwide implementation of the International Phonetic Alphabet....
