A Type 3 engine is almost identical to a Type 1 engine.  The difference is
that most of them came stock with dual carbs (or fuel injection for the
later years) and all of them have different engine tin/fan housing because
the fan is direct drive (attached to the engine pulley) instead of being
driven off of the generator.
