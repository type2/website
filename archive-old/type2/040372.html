Subject: Re: Help on touch up painting

Remember when using spray cans for touch-up to do the following:
Instead of taping newsprint flush to the area immediately outside of 
your painting area,
tape the paper on "backwards" (so to speak) and fold it back over onto 
itself so as to eliminate a sharp "stop" edge from your spraying work 
area.
In other words, fold the paper back over itself so as to create a 
"feathered" stopping to the paint  applied area, as opposed to an 
immediate stop of the application.
