As Karl pointed out, the C suffix in 185R14C stands for
commercial/reinforced sidwall for a stiffer ride.  Load Range in the US is
also called "number of plies" as in load range C = 6 ply and load range D =
8 ply.  load index is the total rated weight, and these have a load index of
102 which is close to 1900 lbs.

For a better explanation, see:
http://www.dualport_dot_com/bustech/load_tires.html
