You have either a bad vacuum unit or a frozen breaker plate.
It should move in either direction with only 5 to 10 inches of vacuum. You can
see it move by sucking on each nipple with not that much trouble. Remove the 2
screws holding the can to the body. Pull the can away and it should come out
about a 1/4" as the plate turns. [leave the E clip on] If the plate turns
easily, you have a bad vacuum unit. It should have 853 on the arm.....good
luck finding another one.
