This is a follow-up to my own message. I figured that there might be a
lot of folks running stock dual-port 1600 engines that might want to
know how they can revive their worn Solex 34 PICT-3 carburetors.

I took some of the info from folks on the list (thanks!) and asked
around Seattle. The bushings are available, but kind of hard to find.
The VW dealers here say they haven't seen them in a few years. I found
that the mostly-VW parts place called Bow Wow (206/523-9200) have the
bushings on order and charge only a few bucks for them. They recommended
Lewis Foreign Car (206/524-3366) as the machine shop to use, because
they had done the bushing job on a lot of these Solex 34 PICT-3s. The
installation will cost about $25. Cool.

Thanks for all your help, and I apologize for not knowing how to spell
"carburetor" in my original post.
