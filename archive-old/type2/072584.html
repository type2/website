Mark:  When I first started my 1776 after a rebuild,  the dual Kadrons
popped.  I put ethanol in a squirt bottle and dripped it over the place
where the throttle shafts emerge from the carb body.  Sure enough, the rpm
increased noticably when the liquid touched the shafts.  Evidently there
were air leaks where the bushings have worn and this led to unregulated air
flow and therefore a lean mixture. I had to send mine to the west coast for
rebusing.  When I reinstalled them,  no more pop.

Hope this helps.
