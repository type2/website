OK, now the rest of the story..  I was registering a vehicle at Ft Belvoir, 
and someone asked at the vehicle registration office where could he weigh 
his vehicle.  Well, it seems he was doing a self help  PCS move and he 
wanted to get the empty weight and later, the loaded weight.

I just followed him, and I put my '78 Westy on the scale after he got off, 
and I got off the scale, and they gave me a printout with my VW's weight and 
date and signed it to verify...

loaded weight...
