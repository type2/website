 Hi Craig,
Have you thought about going a mildly worked engine with say a mild cam, standard crank, machining the heads, ratio rockers & changing the standard bores out to 1640 or 1641cc(same difference).
All these things keep your engine almost standard but give you more horses without having to clearance the case & they won't suck that much more fuel either.
And it shouldn't cost you much more than a standard engine either.
What do other people think?
It's my belief that the 1600 is actually the most reliable & strong engine per it's horse power that VW ever built & these little tweaks only maximise it's efficiency.
Plus sticking with a single carb puts less strain on the engine long term as your not pumping all that extra fuel into the cylinders all the time & it also keeps the fuel efficiency of 1600's.
But definitely go a dual port.
