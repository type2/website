	I have a '77 Bus, but two -020 ('78-79) AFMs.
	I have tested these. I came up with these values:

	AFM #A,  temp sensor
	2900 ohms	at 65 F
	13k ohms at 5 F
	17k ohms at -8 F

	AFM #B, temp sensor
	2700 ohms	at 65 F
	11k ohms at 5 F
	16k ohms at -8 F

	Both of these sensors seem to work fine.

	I am looking for a more detailed explanation of how these AFMs work.
A week or so ago, I posted to	the list about the flap resistance 
(as it goes through its opening motion) being cyclic between 
	100 and 400 ohms (it would look like a sine wave on a graph), and
how this does not seem consistent with the common explanation 
of the flap resistance going up through its range of
	motion (more air, more resistance). If anyone knows of a more
thorough technical guide to AFC-F.I. I would like to see it.
