Toe-in (wheels not pressed) 0 to 30 minutes or 0-1/8 inch

Toe-in (wheels pressed together with 33 pounds of force) 4.75 - 5.25
degrees

Camber (wheels straight ahead)  20-60 minutes, no more than 30 minutes
difference between sides

Caster 3.0 degrees plus or minus 40 minutes
