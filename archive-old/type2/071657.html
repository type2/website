Here are the best stats on the bus that I can provide right now.  I live in
Puyallup, Washington (that is about an hour away from Seattle).  When I got

wrong with it, because the bus just wouldn't start, and after putting a new

it breaking down when we go up hills.  We try and start it up again and after
awhile it starts, but then breaks down again, and again even if we are on flat
hill.  as for the miles I think there are 300,000 on the engine.  I hope this
answers all your questions on it.  If you have any more dont hesitate to ask,
