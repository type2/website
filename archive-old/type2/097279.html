Yes there should be a gasket for the breather.  Get a can of carb
cleaner and spray lightly on all your vacuum areas.  Increase in rpm
means you have a leak where you applied carb cleaner.  Don't forget the

throttle body gasket where in attaches to the intake air manifold.  Good
luck.
