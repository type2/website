nice to me and sat and talked for a few.

Tell him I said hi but I am sure he will not remember me.  To remind him
tell him that I was the big guy from Georgia that stopped in.  He showed me
a couple of the MUGS Root Beer Bugs he was building.

He is a fine gentlman.
