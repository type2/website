> Check the oil. Make sure it's between the two notches, preferably closer to
> the top one. spray some wd-40 on the battery straps, remove battery and put
> on a couple of phone books and trickle charge it overnight. In the morning,
> put battery in, make sure the fan belt isn't super loose. start it up and let
> it run for a half hour. If the charge battery still doesn't turn it over,
> then remove the spark plugs and squirt a little bit of castrol oil into the
> spark plug holes there in from an old oil can that squirts -not too much-
> then be spinning the motor with a wrench on the generator nut until it
> loosens up. fire it up!
