Dave,
i too have the trusty 72 dormobile. The cupboards once removed do indeed
reveal a nice rusty hole. The problem is that the rust you can see is only a
small part of what is a REAL nightmare. I thought about it, and squirted
underseal and then expanding foam into the the hole. This will keep out the
draft. You are looking at an expensive welding job. Do you have the two
floor arrangement? If so, look at the belly pan directly beneath the hole
you have found. Prod it. Has your finger gone right through? Thought so. Now
if I'm right this belly pan will crumble away and you will be able to feel
inside the sill. There is an outrigger there which is probably rusted away.
We are now looking at several hundred pounds worth of welding.
My advice - put the cupboards back. If the belly pan is shagged replace
that. An MOT can't fail what it can't see. Your van is old and how long do
you want it to last?
