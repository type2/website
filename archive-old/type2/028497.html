Hi Robert,

I'm so glad you decided to agree with all the highly trained engineers and
VW who spent millions of dollars on developing and manufacturing thermostats
for the air-cooled engine ;-)

Since most engine wear occurs at oil temps below 180 F, your engine needs a
thermostat. The increased exhaust emissions that occur during warm up
combined with more strict pollution standards ('75 I think) have changed the
temp rating.  Make sure you adjust the t-stat so that by full warm up the
flaps are fully open. Your engine will last much longer.

Steve Trembley
