Do you know how to check the CVs? They should be tight and move through
their full orbit. Make sure they're good! Clean and repack them. Make sure
there's NO dirt or sand in them. My guess it they're out as part of the
intended repair for the tranny, which probably minimizes the chance that
it's just linkage. Still, for $400 in the bus, you can afford to replace the
tranny if you have to.
