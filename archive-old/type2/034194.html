>the transmission need to be taken apart to fix it?

The transmission can stay in the car while the worn bushing
is replaced.  Remove the tranny's "nose cone" and get a new
bushing fit into it.  The nose cone is the front part of the tranny
that has the shift lever shaft going into it.  I think the later model
bus trannys have a bearing in there; '73 has a bushing.
