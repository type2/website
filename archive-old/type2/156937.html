Stop.
Correct the vacuum lines 1st.

Manifold vacuum: comes from the plenum between 1 and 2
runners. Vacuum at idle. Feeds the fuel pressure
regulator and the decel valve.

Ported vacuum: comes from the front of the throttle
housing [front is front]. No vacuum at idle, appears
when you open the throttle. Feeds the vacuum advance
and the EEC valve on the air cleaner.

Are you using the 78 or the 79 throttle housing? If
the 79, it has a dedicated vauum nipple JUST for the
vacuum retard on the distributor. That is the one that
goes to the 75/76 dual dist you are using. Mid 76-78
throttle housings do not have it. 

You have me confused when you say you are using the
75/76 SVDA..... the 75/early 76 dist is a DVDA dist.
It has a dual vacuum unit. 

If you are using the 78 throttle housing, I would not
connect the retard side to anything. Leave it open and
ime it to 7.5 BTDC.
