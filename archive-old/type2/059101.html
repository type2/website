> I am planning to lower my camper, using the tried & tested method of
> fitting a Foksy adjuster.

Not such a good idea.  This method will result in a rough ride,
possible damage to the ball joints, and strange wheel alignment and
tire wear problems.  

If you must lower your bus, use lowering spindles as this is the only
method that preserves the suspension travel and steering geometry.
