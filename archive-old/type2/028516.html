Subject: RE: [T2] Torquing Heads

Years ago in conversations with folks at Rimco, I asked about their case
savers, 10mm studs and torque.  I have followed their suggestions and use 30
ft lbs of torque on T1 engines with their case savers only. The heads on my
Bus haven't ever lost torque and I almost never need to adjust the
valves...I'm talking 18,000 miles and maybe a couple valves need a .001"
adjustment.  I still check out of habit anyway at 6K intervals.  It's almost
spooky.

I also use 5/76 and earlier Ford Courier rocker buttons, Part # is
D27Z-6549-C...great modification for more stability.  Use shims and make
sure you have parallel stem/adjuster geometry at half lift.
