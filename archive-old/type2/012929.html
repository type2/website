I have the electric frige in my 74, only used on AC at present.  I plug
it in overnight before we leave to get it good and cold, then I take a
gallon milk jug, fill it with water and freeze it.  this is used to keep
things cold untill it is plugged in again.  The block of ice in the jug
will last the entire weekend, and you dont have the problem with draining
water as it melts, with the added plus of an available gallon of water if
needed.
