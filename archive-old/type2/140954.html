You still have the bench seat/bed, correct?

If yes, look at the base for the silver/black plate. Probably on the right.
Along the bottom it should have a string of letters and numbers. Should start with Campmobile 68 and then the 
number. After that will be a single
stamped in number, like 9 and then a period, then 5 or 6 numbers.
The single number is the year. 9 for 1969
