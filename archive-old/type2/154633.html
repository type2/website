Before I yanked the motor for a rebuild (in process), I ran a pair each of
Hella fogs and driving lamps with Silverstar bulbs.  These were wired
through relays and controlled by two additional VW switches I added on the
left side of the instrument panel, using the holes provided behind the dash
plastic cover.

I love them!

I also want to note that the stock 7" lenses, with Silverstar bulbs, are
quite bright on their own when properly aimed.  Honestly, they approach the
amount of light from my Touareg's HIDs.  Not as much "lumen overkill"  --
but the illuminated area is roughly equivalent.

Note:  keep an eye on voltage drop from 30 year old wires.  Run a new, heavy
gauge wire to the dash or at least replace the connectors on the existing
12ga red supply line.
