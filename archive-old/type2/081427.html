Did you use a clutch alignment tool when you put the pressure plate and
clutch disc on? If the clutch disc is even off 1mm to one side, it will not
mate up. Check that the clutch disc is absolutely centred in the pressure
plate. The alignment tools are about 2 bucks from most sources.
