Steve are you sure it's a battery drain or a non  charging generator?
I've found that the regulator has been replaced with a bug one instead
of a 38 amp bus one and that shortened its life.
Another culprit is the generator brushes. Since generators use
commutators instead of slip ring like alternators, brushes do not last
as long. I've never had a long running type 1 motor, but I'd guess in
the 30k to 50k mile range is the life of generator brushes.
