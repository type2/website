been a while since I had a clutch, but it was on a 71.  first thing I
would check is the adjustment, it sounds to me like the clutch wasn't
being fully engaged and slipping a little,.

so check

.  the "droop" in the clutch cable.  this is very important
.  the free play in the clutch, should be just a tad (precise measurment)
when fully released
.  check the springs in the presure plate.  

so what I would do.  
put in a new clutch, presure plate and throw out bearing.

check the "Flex or droop" in the clutch cable.  
then carefully set the "Free play"

if that doesn't work, check out an automatic tranny ;8*!
