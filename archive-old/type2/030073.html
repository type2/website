After I separated two passenger tires in the space of 100 miles, I saw the
light and started to insist upon the correct tires.  Try finding a 185R14
load range D tire in Bishop, California on a Sunday!  That experience cost
me lots of hassle and $25 for another worn out passenger tire i discarded
after 200 more miles.

If you do _nothing_ but drive around town, you will get by fine with
passenger tires, but the danger zone is when you take that first highway
trip after the weather warms up.
