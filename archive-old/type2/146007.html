021-905-205P is the VW number for Bosch 0 231 168 005. It's the stock distributor

>Does anyone have experience using this Bosch SVDA distributor (VW # 043 905
>205) on a Type 4 engine?

No issues except for the fact that your are installing a T1 dizzy. All this means is that you'll have to reorient to rotor from where you think it should be because the vacuum can on beetle is in different location. The end result is that the mark on the dizzy body for #1 won't be point to #1 (it will point to another plug wire).

>What is the timing setting in degrees BTDC at 3600+
>RPM? Will it advance far enough?
