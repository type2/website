I had promised to have the Sermons CD ready November 1 but I didn't make
it, I badly misjudged the amount of work it would require. My own fault.

I want this to be the best possible resource, so I went back to the
beginning and read everything Hoover ever posted to the vanagon, type2 and
vintagebus lists and grabbed everything that was rich but never made it
into the now-defunct Sermons web archive. This swelled the size of the
thing by ~30%.

Then there is whipping it into shape as a good PDF file, using Pagemaker
6.5, a buggy, clumsy, slow program about which I have nothing good to say.
=^ )

To give you some idea, the Sermons file stands at 630 pages and I figure
I'm around 80% finished. Yeah. Bob has posted a lot of good stuff over the
years.
