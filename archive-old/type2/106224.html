If it did I'd suggest that your generator/regulator is a tad bit
broken...it's attempting to run the generator as a motor, which it's able
to do just after the engine shuts off since it still has momentum.

solinoid on your carb/carbs to be sure that it's working/connected?  This
solinoid cuts off fuel flow from the idle circuit when you turn off the
key.
