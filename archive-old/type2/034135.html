I think that the sizes are correct, but I've never had any luck at
finding SAE-sized replacements for the metric-size fuel lines. 
Everything I've tried is either loose or too tight.  Luckily, you can
easily get the correct metric line through friendly mailorder suppliers
if you can't get it locally.  Even some local US FLAPS are carrying a
better selection as even US cars are going metric.

The one exception to this is the big vacuum line that goes to the brake
booster on '71 and up buses.  There is an SAE-sized hose that is within
.002 inches of the correct ID, which is close enough if you have priced
the metric hose in this size!

You should replace the looped hose with a straight one.  Hoses don't
take kindly to being bent too tightly.  I think that the rule of thumb
is that the hose should not be bent in a radius tighter than 5 times
its outside diameter.
