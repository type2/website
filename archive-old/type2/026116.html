I like these sweeping generalisations :-)
There was nothing wrong with the German fuel lines that VW put on in the
factory.  Any fuel line will go brittle with age.
The hose that you get now is internally reinforced (the cloth is
sandwiched inside instead of being wrapped round the outside).  One
advantage of this I can see is that you can tell if the hose is cracked.
I think the correct size is 5.5mm ID, they should be a good tight fit
before you put the clamps on.
One other thing, next time you disconnect your battery, take the
NEGATIVE wire off.  (And the positive as well if you're really paranoid,
but just the negative is fine.)
