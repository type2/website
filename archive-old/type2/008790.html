Not quite.  Some of what you pour on the ground will soak in before it
burns, which eventually gets to groundwater.

If you're going to burn flammable liquids it's better to do it in a
metal container open at the top, such as a coffee can.  Be sure to put
the can in the open, a long way from flammables, and light it from afar.

I don't generally like to do this, but sometimes the hazardous waste
disposal regs are a Catch-22.
