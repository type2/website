Do you have a wire from the harness hooked up to temp sensor II, the
cylinder head temp sender next to front (front being front) of the intake on
cylinder number 3? The connector which comes from the harness should have a
clearish plastic sleave over a male spade lug close to the area where the
double relay would be mounted. It should be connected to pin 13 of the ECU
though. Maybe someone moved the pin if they replaced the end, or miscount?
This would explain a lot...

The only other 'free' wire might be the one that goes to pin 1 of the
ignition coil, but it should go to pin 1 of the ECU harness as well. This
wire gets trigger information from the coil/distributor to fire the
injectors. Solid Light--don't flicker??? Don't see a way to get continuity
on pin 9 though.
