It was explained to me that the curtains in late busses are made of
polyester and the color is molded into the filament rather than dyed.  I
have dyed bus curtains before using the Rit dye like you find in the laundry
department of Wal-Mart.  It works, but how well depends on what you think.
I dyed a set of brown and a set of green curtains.  Both looked better than
before so I doubt you have anything to lose.  Be careful because the dye
works quite well on anything it touches.
