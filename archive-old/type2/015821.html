>I have a 1.8L, will the heads work on a 1.7L or 2.0L.

The heads will work fine on a 2 liter.  On a 1.7 you should upgrade it to
1.8 with 93mm pistons and liners anyway, but if you don't, the heads will
be a little loose on top of the cylinders.  This might not be a problem,
but I wouldn't do it on my engine.  It's the kind of thing I would expect a
sleazy mechanic to do without telling me.
