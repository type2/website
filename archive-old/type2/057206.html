With all due respect to those who's opinions are dear to them.  I just read
an article in an import repair trade magazine, written about some tests
Mercedes did on oils and viscosities of various ranges.  It appears that
there research indicated that the 10 weight variety oils are relatively
worthless in the normal operating environment.  They found the best overall
performer was good 'ole
30 weight.......yep.  I was reading this article and shaking my
head...remembering all the info by Muir in his book....I thought darn this
cat was really right. And now Mercedes engineers have proved it.  The did
say that 20 weight is much better than a 10 weight mix, but the very best
was a straight 30 weight......So,  In my humble opinion......(Only allowed a
Mechanic to touch my bus twice in 10 years) and never have any
problems.......Listen to Muir.  Mercedes says it's true.
