That is true.  I lived in Alabama a few years back.  There (AL) the law is
>= 25 years old = no title.  In GA the same applies.  The problem is when
you sell the car to someone in a state that questions why the car has no
title issued from the state in which the registration is...Trust me, I just
went through this.

The Alabama DMV was involved in the previously mentioned example, and they
were VERY cooperative.  In fact, it seems that they don't give a hoot about
an old VW.  They cooperated without question.

In my situation, I sold a car with a TN title, AL registration, and GA Bill
of Sale...What a mess.
