Fel-Pro used to make some really nice neoprene valve cover gaskets.  They
had one smooth side, and the other side had a series of parallel ridges and
valleys.  Much like the valve/cam cover gaskets for typical FWD econoboxes.

I had a bunch of them stashed for years, and finally used them all up.  I
haven't found any more for several years, so they may have discontinued
them.  They sealed exceptionally well, and could be re-used many times.
They had enough "give" and "squish" to tolerate even warped valve covers.

If anyone has knowledge of a source for these gaskets, or information as to
their production status, I'd appreciate an update.
