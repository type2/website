Jeff,
I'd be inclined to question your temp reading. My own experience tells me
that you're not likely to maintain that high a pressure with 30w oil at 250*.
Every situation is probably different but I would bet that you aren't as hot
as you think. In similar temps this weekend my 81 Westy with 20/50 synthetic
at 55 to 60mph managed oil temps of about 210 to 220 and pressure of about 35
to 40psi. In warmer weather 230* to 250* and 30 psi is more like it.
