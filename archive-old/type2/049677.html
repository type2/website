In regards to your note below, the best place I've found for good, really 
cheap, nice and alternative campgrounds are in the AAA Camper Guides.  Become 
a AAA member, and you can get these guides for free for all over the USA.  
Typically, I can find campgrounds for between $5 and $12 in many areas.  They 
are usually in rural settings, and tend to have pit toilets, sometimes 
showers, sometimes not, but are the most beautful and rustic places to go 
camping that one can find.  They are typically State Parks, which is why I 
think their prices are lower and their facilities less saphisticated.

Also out west, in really outback rural areas, you can typically find 
campgrounds that are for FREE, believe it or not, in the same guide.
