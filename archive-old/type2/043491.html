to convert from inhouse to doghouse,   you need:

doghouse fan (wider)
DH fan shroud
sort of triangular piece that vents the air from DH
rectangularish tube that vents the DH heated air out of engine compartment
seals for these vent bits
front tin  with hole for the DH rectangular vent
rectangular piece aka Hoover bit
oil cooler stand
oil cooler
4 oil cooler seals
the DH thermostat linkage to replace the non DH horizontal thermostat linkage

myself I would stay with SP for reliability, but it's your motor.  have fun!!
