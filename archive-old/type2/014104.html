Sounds like the shift coupler is bad.. either the rubbe
bushing or the shaft is broke loose where it mates with the
cage. Make sure you clean it off a bit before trying to see
if the coupler is following the shifter. Mine broke at the
shaft/cage juncture. So the rubber looked good and the slop
wasnt real noticable untill I cleaned the coupler off. Then
it was obvious that the shaft was not following the cage in
rotation.
