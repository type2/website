SMALL VACUUM LINES:

there are six places where these connect:

-deceleration enrichment valve, mounted on the firewall
-airfilter box, seems to run a diaphram for the carbon canister system
-fule pressure relief valve,  near #3, sends gas back to the tank
-air distributor box, between pipes supplying intake air to #3&#4
-distrubtor vacuum chamber, adjusting spark timing.
-back of throttle plate housing

I have two splitters (3-way connections), which makes it seem likely that
either there are two sets of 3 or one set of 4 and one set of two, but I
don't really know...

LARGE HOSES FOR CARBON CANISTER SYSTEM

this is easy, fan shroud upper left to carbon box left, and carbon box
right to air filter box.  small hose right goes to weirdo 'separator'
things...

VACUUM LINE FROM AUTO TRANNY

this seems to go to the left side of the air distributor box, it requires
an attachment, since both tubes are the same diameter.  I have a T

the decel valve...

DECEL VALVE.

bottom large pipe might go to the left side of the air distributor box,
using the T connection?  but I don't know.

AIR BIPASS VALVE

Electrically controled extra air valve, this has a large diameter elbo,
what does it connect to?  I think to the huge precase main air intake pipe

HUGE AIR INTAKE PIPE BETWEEN AFM and throttle
