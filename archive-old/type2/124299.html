The 1st thing I would do is adjust more play in the actuating rod between the pedal and the
master cylinder. Numbers 80 and 81 in this pic:
http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/Microfiche/t205110.gif

Brake fluid expands in the heat and without enough free play, the master cylinder will apply the brakes.
