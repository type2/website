OK let me see if I can explain, now that I have my FI manual in front of
me.

term 88z should have power
     86c should not have power with ignition switch on engine not running

at the injector the light should flicker. if not check term 1 on the coil
as you should be getting a trigger impulse 

put test light between pin 1 on the control unit plug and ground for the
following test, then crank engine


       if the light does not flicker check
. break in wire between term1 on distributor and term 1 on Control unit
multi pin conector (not the ECU itself)
.  points not adjusted correctly
.  condenser shorting to ground
.  defective coil


hope this gets you started.
if not we need to get into a step by step trouble shooting series.  or if
you can find it the VW "Troubleshooting Guide, Electronic Fuel Injection
AFC"  VW # 0.00.530.410.23 (don't know if it is in the library as I have a
copy and never looked)
