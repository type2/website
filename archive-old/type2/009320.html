I just talked to a mechanic friend, and he said that the wheels don't need
replacing, just the tires. So the stock wheels will stay on. I thought that
with all the rust the wheels would crumble, but he assured me that wheels
are a lot tougher than that, and I just need to scrape off the rust and
repaint and they'll be fine. 

So now I just need to know what tires to get. I'm getting really confused
about this. A read in the archives about someone who put passenger tires on
their westy and had a major blowout. The moral of the story was to never put
passenger tires on a westy, as they're not strong enough. Okay, so no
passenger tires. So I guess I go with light truck tires. I just know I'm
going to get in there and the tire guys are going to see right through me,
I'll be standing there saying, "Light truck tires please", and there will be
a million choices and I won't know where to go from there. :)
