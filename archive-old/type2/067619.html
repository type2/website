I hope that it is as simple and easy as one of your tires has lost 
it's balance weight.  Tire balance behavior seems to be related to 
speed.  If you have had the tires on for awhile, look closely on the 
edge of the rim for where a balance weight used to be by looking for 
a clean spot.  Check both inside and outside rims.
If so, get the tire balanced!
