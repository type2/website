 No hammering or prying etc is required.

 OTOH there are different size sucker tubes (whatever) for diff. years
of engines.

 My guess is that you have a late model engine and they are giving you a '67
screen.

 Get the engine number and have them equate that to the year.

 If they can't, get back to us. We can.

 One item, be real gentle tightening those nuts back on.
The number is 5 ft-lb. try to equate that to five pounds on a 1ft wrench. If
you screw that up you have real problems for a beginner.
