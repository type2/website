Anything you can do to flush it while on the bench is good. I have
also seen recommended that you fill it with straight Automatic
Transmission Fluid and run it briefly to flush out the motor. I
haven't heard any feedback on the other suggestion to actually use a

the motor to drive with straight ATF, but one quart in the oil might
not hurt for a short while.
