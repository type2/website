Read Bentley, use factory gaskets, seals and sealants. Follow procedures.

Why does 574 work so well for case halves? Because is was designed by 
Locktite, Germany, under contract to Porsche for that specific application,
just like all German gaskets, seals, sealants, ect. Experiment with after-
market stuff at your own risk or do it right the first time so you don't
have to do it over. The beauty of German machines is that if you follow
specification you get excellant value. I service 200 or so European vehicle
in my shop every year and I haven't used a single tube of RTV yet and I
have't had have any comebacks for leaks. Clean, straight flanges and surfaces
are the key. Don't overtorque.
