OK, try this. The voltmeter is a little deceiving.
Get a lightbulb and put it between the negative post
and the negative battery cable. A fog light bulb works
very well. If the light lights up bright, you have a
short. If it is dim, then replace it with your
voltmeter if it has amps/milleamp capability. See how
many milleamps are being drawn. 1000 ma is 1 amp. 500
ma is 1/2 amp. A 10w interior light bulb will draw
around 350ma.

Amps drain the battery, volts are misleading.

On that wiring diagram, fuses 1 to 12 are S1 to S12.
If you look along the bottom, you will see each part
of the fuse box below where the S? fuse is.
