There are nice small antennas for CB, but they don't work very well.  A
quarter wave on 27MHz is a bit over 100 inches long.  that's the antenna

length you want.  You can get a long whip, or you can try to fool mother

nature by wrapping the antenna into a coil.  You can fool the
transmitter,
but your radiated signal is pretty much a function of the antenna
length.
The best compromise is a 4-foot firestik antenna, commonly available at
truckers outlets.  It works reasonably well but isn't all that long.

George Lyle
