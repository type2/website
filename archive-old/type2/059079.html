You don't gain anything structurally, but you do gain a flat surface to
lay your flooring on and some isolation from road noise.
I have a sheet of 1/4" ply on my floor, on top of expanded polyethylene
sheeting.  This stops the ply from drumming against the metal and also
provides some extra insulation and a vapour barrier.
