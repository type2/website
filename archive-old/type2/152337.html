Subject: Re: Bench to bucket seat conversion?

You actually do have 1 bucket seat - the driver's side. The passenger side
of the 2/3 seat mounts the same way as the passenger side bucket seat.
There's 2 mounts where the back of the seat swivels and you will probably
need to weld in the inner mounting point because your existing (inner) one
is way over next to the driver's seat. Then you need to look at where the
top of the seat back hooks in. I don't remember whether the 2/3 seat uses
the latch in the same place but it might because I think there's 2 on your
seat so maybe the outer one is in the right place already.
