The nine pin box, part number 211 953 227B [says 211 953 227 on it] was used on 1968 models up to vin 2x8 098
285.

Your 211 953 215C was used from vin 2x8 098 286 to the end of the 1970 model year.

Look at the 1970 wiring diagrams on vintagebus_dot_com. #1 on either non USA or USA. J2 is the 4 pin relay.
