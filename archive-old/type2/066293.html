Tip: The previous radio was the old type bolted just to the face of the
dashboard. The new one fitted in slot but was not secured to the dashboard.
So I used a bent metal strip, bolted one end to the back of the radio and
the other end to a couple of holes conveniently pre-drilled in the top
centre part of the dashboard.

Question: The new radio is longer than the old one and impinges behind on
the heating/ventilation control cables which are now under strain and a bit
bent - will this do much harm in the long run?
