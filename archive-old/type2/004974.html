The weld-nut is accessable if you remove the gas tank panel. There is a
little room at the end of the gas tank, but not much. Yes, if you get the
bolt out, you could cut the head off a long bolt and weld it in FROM the
outside. Then slide the shock on and use a nut. I would not try to weld it
from the gas tank side.
