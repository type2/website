Subject: Re: [T2] got the old Cv/axle off, step 2?

Clean off all the old grease on all interior surfaces, face plates, inside
of boots, etc.  If the old grease was contaminated in some way not cleaning
it off will carry that contamination over to the new grease, and, as a
general proposition, it's best not to mingle different greases.  There's no
need to apply grease to the mounting plates; push it all into the CV.
