I believe the tumbler is fine. Your problem is in the lock assembly itself.
Spraying any penetrant like Liquid Wrench in it/on it to free it up is hit and
miss at best with the lock assembly in the door. If you have tools, your best
fix is to remove the lock assembly and give it a thorough cleaning and then
lube it to get all the pieces in it moving easily. Makes the world of
difference and saves your key!
