Yes, there is a 'Westy' handle..... if you have a 68 to 73 Westy. Because the sink/cooler unit sits so close
to the door, VW made a special double-ended handle. No one that I know of repops this handle. Very very scarce
to find unbroken.

There were 2 different passenger bus handles. The early bay handle has the screw hole off center. I have only
seen this handle on 68's and early 69's. Then VW made the handle with the screw centered. The repro handles
are also center-holed.
