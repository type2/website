Ben,

I am in the position of having to replace my exhaust as well.  In your
case if a Catalytic converter is not required, try RMMW. They have an
extractor, and single quiet pack for less than $100 together, something
like $35 for the extractor, and $40 for a single quiet pack(I think that
is what they are called). There is a dual quiet pack(no extractor) for
about $136 or so. These prices are not exact, but close. 
Bug-e-warehouse in San Antonio Tx(look in VWT&A for the number) also has
the same setups for a few $$$ less.

The air moving under your engine could be coming from the fan.  Do you
have your thermostat hooked up and working? If so, when it is cold, it
directs all of the air down over the oil cooler, and out of the engine.
Then as the engine warms, less air is directed over the oil cooler, and
towards the cylinders, though there is still a significant amount of air
directed over the oil cooler.

There is a possibility that the blue your are seeing, is a crack in the
distributor.  When I was having problems starting my 79, someone
suggested that I check the dist at night to see if there is a crack. 
This is a possibility to look towards.

Hope this helps
