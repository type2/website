I assume we are talking manual?  CG is just on the engine side of the where
the 2 meet (with exhaust, alternator and starter installed) for me.  Unhook
starter, remove tranny nose mount going to torsion bar housing, remove bolts
holding tranny to it's mount, remove axles.  Rest stays the same except it's
easier!  Once I get the nose of tranny behind the torsion bar housing I
lower it and roll it on out.  A few 2x4 under the heater boxes and I'm
happy.  Do jack the bus somewhat so I can leave my carb on.

Have fun!
