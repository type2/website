A couple things. First, when that leaking sender was
in there, oil was getting sprayed all over the place
in the cooling air area. It takes a few thousand miles
for that to "go away". If it is truly worse, then
either you buggered the threads putting it in or the
new one didn't last very long (common to my
experience) or you have another problem that maybe you
made worse when you were poking around. How about the
oil dipstick bellows? That's a common one and if it is
shot, just going up a steep hill will blow a gusher
out of there! My experience with OPS's (lots!) is that
you can look down from above while the engine is
running and see the oil seeping out. To see if the
threads are messed up, pull that rubber shroud out and
run without it while you watch for oil accumulation
and flow. -BaH
