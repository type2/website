This method is used fairly frequently in Europe. It's most valuable on new
and popular 'hot' cars. It will make them less attractive to some thieves
(but would it help if the car is shipped off to Africa or Russia?  Yes, that
happens). If the intention of the thief is parting out it will not help
much. In order to make your car entirely 'theft-proof' you need to take
several measures. This can be one of them and it's not very expensive. But
if they really want to get your car all you can do is keep it locked up in a
vault.
