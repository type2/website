Subject: Re: [Re: [T2] Rust advice]

Or........you can use a universal battery tray (I got mine from West marine).
Clean up the rusted area well using Naval Jell. Paint with appropriate stuff to
inhibit more rust and then install trays. The trays can be setup to hold battery
boil over or drain to ground with out getting to the steel. These trays are
plastic and seem impervious to acid. They are also cheap, have built in battery
hold downs, and can be installed with the motor in. This is not a solution for a
purist but it is extremely effective and cost efficient.
