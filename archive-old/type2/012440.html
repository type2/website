Steve:

You probably want what's called a "Super Turbo" muffler.  I've had real good luck in non-VW applicatins with a brand called Dyn-O-Max.  They're actually pretty quiet, yet flow much better than stock mufflers.  I recall seeing super-turbo mufflers for VW engines for sale at Kymco's website --
  
http://www.kymcovw_dot_com/Pages/specials.html

They call it the "Fat Boy" and is made by Dyn-O-Max.  Otherwise, look in the VW magazines for the ads.
