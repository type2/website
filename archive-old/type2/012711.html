given all the recent hoopla over the archives, i thought i would post
this little bit of info:

if you are using a mail program similar to outlook, you can create your
owm simplified version of the archives.  just save all of the digests
in a folder & use the mail programs search tool, just like the archives on
the web. of course, the search tool in the mail program will not be
as functional or flexible as the web search tool, but you will at least 
have a simpler version of the archives at your disposal.
