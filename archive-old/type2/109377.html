The Bentley and the Muir manuals are a must for the first timer. Although
the Muir manual is somewhat dated it gives a very easy to understand intro
into VW's. The type2 library is also a good place to browse through for good

good luck with BusDepot, GoWesty, and CIP1 for starters. There are a ton of
sites out there once you start looking.
