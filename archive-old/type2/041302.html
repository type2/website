First step is always a compression check.  If compression is low, check
valve lash.  If still low, it's time for major engine repairs.  If
compression is okay, it is spark or fuel related.  Spark - check everything
from the cap outward (cap, wire, plug).  Fuel - not much to check here -
possibly a vacuum leak where the manifold meets the head?
