> Last year I replaced my AFM with a rebuilt unit from Bus Depot.  I've
> noticed that my Bosch Platinum plugs foul very quickly.  I've read the
> threads in the Archive about these plugs fouling, but was wondering if I

The Platinum plugs might not be the best choice for a bus with points
ignition.  The smaller tip encourages spark discharge, but discharge
occurs before voltage builds to an optimum level.  This is fine with
"modern" CD ignitions, but our Kettering ignitions don't have as much
energy to deliver.

Anyway, that's my theory.  I found that my bus ran better on standard 
plugs.
