Dave:
I think there should be 2 large hoses hooked to the carbon cannister: 1
in and 1 out! In comes from the fan housing, out goes to the air
cleaner. In operation this air flow sweeps the vapors from the carbon
pellets into the air cleaner where they are mixed with air and swept
into the manifold.
They get into the carbon cannister via the smaller tubing from the tank
vents.
