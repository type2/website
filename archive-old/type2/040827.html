On my '72 Westy I had almost exactly what Shelby describes - rubber hold 
downs that they use for Jeep hoods. They are shaped like a "T" with a round 
bulge just before the T handle. My hardware looks original and judging from 
some pics in Bentley's the hold downs were VERY similar to the Jeep hold 
downs. I replaced my possibly original ones with Jeep hood hold downs (for 
lack of the correct terminlogy) from my FLAPS and have never had a problem.  
I do believe that the new ones from the FLAPS are a bit "beefier" than the 
ones that I have seen pics of - could be why I don't have a problem. The only 
modification was to make a hole in the "bulge" just before the "T" for the 
rear latch in order to install the little metal piece that cams in to place 
on the roof. Other than that, I used the hardware I already had in there. I 
would probably go for installing another clip instead of relying on the cam 
latch. Before I forget... all of this is **internal** hardware, nothing 
latching on the outside. And, of course my disclaimer being that this was 
four years ago when I was able to DRIVE my Wetsy (soon, people, soon... :-)
