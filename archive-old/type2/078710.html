Two problems with 2" PVC pipe.

1. too small.
2. PVC was not made to handle the temperatures that the heat exchangers
are capable of producing.  Outgassing problems might rear their ugly
heads.

Better to use a metal duct, close to the same size as the original.
Insulate with fiberglass.
