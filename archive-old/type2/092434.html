What does it mean for the points to bounce?  I've read this several place but I'm not sure what it refers to.

I'm starting to understand the ignition system better.  With new points and the bus running well, a stroboscopic timing light shows that the engine sometimes doesn't always fire at 7.5 BTDC. A lot of gas pumps in and there is a big stink when the engine finally starts.

As I understand it, electronic ignition smooths out both of these conditions: starting and idle.
