I've seen original, heavy OEM German CV boots that've lasted 15 years or more
on the axles. It seems that the boots made in India, Israel, Brazil or
wherever don't last nearly as long. However, one VW shop owner told me that
you've got to use the cheap boots while they're fresh and haven't been sitting
around on the shelf for a long time, or they'll crack and split shortly after
they've been installed. It seems also that constant contact with the moly
grease keeps the boots from cracking.

Try to locate some OEM German boots. Cleaning and repacking CV joints and
replacing CV boots is a messy job I'd rather not do anymore often than every
7-10 years or so.
