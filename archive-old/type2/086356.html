Bill,

I know that we have crossed this path in the past. Yet, I have just
completed this job on a friends '78. The short hose from the tank to the
metal filler pipe is a 2 1/4 hose and has a slight bend. I used a straight
section of pipe from Napa, 2 1/4 in inside diameter, and cut it long so that
I was able to bend it. I would also recommend cutting the tank side of the
hose at an angle so as to not bunch it up on the rear side. All this done
with the engine in.

What is happening with the transmission?
