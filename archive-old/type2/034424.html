Green diesel is for over the road use. (Fed & State Hikghway taxes were
paid) Low sulfer content
Red diesel is for off road use (no taxes paid) -- High sulfer content.
bad for newer vehicles, causes havoc with emissions system.  It will do
in an emergency-eg small town that doesnt have diesel fuel, but has
graders and other construction equiment--from first hand experience <g>.
I could have sworn I saw blue diesel once upon a time, but cant remember
(maybe for marine applications if it is).

You can also use double filtered #2 heating oil in a pinch, albeit its
the most illegal way to go.
