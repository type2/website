engine was acquired when business was down after 9/11,

send them my core first (included in the delivery
fee), cuz they didn't have one handy.  But it was nice
to get my fairly low-time case back.

if needed, for a small extra charge.

IIRC, AVP reuses certain parts if serviceable (like
intake valves).

The engine had no problems, and is running fine after
10,00 miles of short trips, with long hills.
Definately better than a mexi-engine.
