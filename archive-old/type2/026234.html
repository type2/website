There's more to it than just a good book.  Last night I scanned 12 pages of
tools for the project.  The book you need is the VW workshop manual, Section
H.  Granted, some of the tools are repeated on multiple pages because they
are used for multiple steps.  Start saving your pennies because, when you
can find them, tranny tools are expensive.  The first, and most important
tool for getting it back together correctly, is 289d -- mandrel and dial
indicator.
