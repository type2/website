Mike,

	You have established that your fuel pump is working correctly,
and all the other components appear to be working fine, aside from the
injectors not getting signal from what I understand. My concern would be
the series resistors, and the wiring harness at this point. Your
injectors should have power from the time you start cranking til you
shut the engine down (if the engine starts) or stop cranking (if it
doesn't). That is my best guess as to your problem. I would check the
series resistors for proper resistance first (I am unsure of the value,
im sure someone on the list knows). Then proceed to check and see if
there is a fault somewhere in the harness. I tend to lean toward the
resistors being bad instead of the harness.

Jonathan
