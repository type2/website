Hi Beth,
Have you tried heating up the lug nuts a bit?  Taking a torch to them and
then soaking them with various lubricants seems to work for me...that or even
tapping on them with a hammer sometimes works..._dot_dead blow hammer even. Are
they rusted on...or did someone use an air wrench on them?  Maybe just try
using a bigger 4-way on them?
As for the brake spring tool....no, you really don't *need* one.  I've never
used one, although I'm sure it would probably be easier with one.  As long as
you have a tight grip and a pair of long needle nosed pliers you should be
ok.  Thats the way that I always do it anyways....
