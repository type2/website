>We have cc'd the combustion chambers and measured the deck volume and end up

>1= +2.8cc

>I know that I could skim pistons and/or grind out the chambers, but I don't

>the 2.0L engine, nor do I want to unbalance the piston weights. So, how much

If you want a "beater" engine, don't bother with specifications. That, in
essence, is the definition of a "beater". If you balance the cc'c, they
will still be balanced if you install them later on another specification
headspaced balanced cylinders/pistons set.
