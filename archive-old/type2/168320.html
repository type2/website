Subject: Hawaii is breaking my heart...

So I am out here on O'ahu on business for a few days, and living at my
mothers house in Wai'anae - the town I grew up in and went to school at.

I was bored stiff today since my Zoey is currently 4k miles away or so and
decided to do a little driving around just to see how the place has changed
and revisit some familiar places. Bare in mind that the Wai'anae Coast is a
poor area in Hawaii and it is not uncommon to find shytloads of salvage cars
in peoples yards - including city buses.

As I drove around the areas of Makaha, Ma'ili, and Nanakuli, I counted no
lest than 10 buses (mostly Bays) that were sitting in weeds, rusted and
basically looking very unhappy. I honestly felt like a kid in a candy store
with not a dime to my name! I kept thinking about what Chris at Busted Bus
does and just saw all these buses wishing I could get my hands on them and
part them out for folks.

What can I say, it just broke my heart.
