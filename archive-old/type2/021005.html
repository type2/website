Hi Tom!

I have Beetle heat exchangers on my '68.  They are different.  The lever
to open and close the exchanger is on the opposite side so the cable
from the dashboard won't line up correctly.  You won't be able to
control the heat from the driver's seat.  This is a hassle.  I wire mine
open in the fall and close them in the spring.  You might be able to
swap the mechanism out of your old ones.  I haven't done it.  When I got
the '68 it did not have the correct heat exchangers.
