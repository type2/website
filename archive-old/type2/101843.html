Nose cone bushing is the name for the internal bushing, takes 2


Nose cone seal carrier is the name for the part that sticks out of the nose
cone that holds the seal.

These are made of silicon bronze and the easiest way to get them out is to
use a die grinder and carefully grind them out, I have a special tool I had
made to install them.

The seal carrier can be just tapped out and replaced. 

All the above is easier with the nose cone removed
