I acquired a boxed factory cruise control for my 77 Safare when a  local
VW agency went out of business back in the 80's. It stayed in the box for
a couple of years before I got around to installing it, which was after I
retired in 1987.

Installation was fairly complex (for me,) requiring removal of the
steering wheel and a couple of layers of switches in the column to install
the new control stalk and wiring. It has been working fine for the last 12
years. The only problem is that the cutout switch for the clutch pedal is
practically impossible to keep lined up and working. I just unplugged it
on mine. The instructions that came with the kit were quite complete.

It is certainly worth installing if you make any long trips. It does a
reasonable job of regulating the speed. As I remember, there was also a
cable connection to the speedometer box.
