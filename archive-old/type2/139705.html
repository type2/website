Csaba,

Ok, this one might be very easy.

There is a coupler between the shift rod and the transmission.
The coupler is made up of two metal "cage" parts with a molded piece of 
rubber in between them.  Frequently the rubber deteriorates and will cause 
the problem you describe.

It looks like this

http://www2.cip1_dot_com/ProductDetails.asp?ProductCode=ACC%2DC10%2D3595

and is just at the front of the transmission.

Have a friend try to shift and watch this part from underneath...if this 
is the problem, it should be obvious.

speed.<grin>
