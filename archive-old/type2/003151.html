coulda sworn i just went over this... check the archives for my post...
specific to your application, you just hook all the neg (not ground, they
are called pos and neg in stereo apps.) speaker wires to the one neg coming
out of the deck. it is a common negative connection and as long as you have
all the pos. from the speakers hooked up correctly it will work. happy 8
tracking.
