Ceramic heaters thread, questions about gas heaters:

Gas heaters only draw a few amps. The only noise is the fan, and
a quite roar of the fire which you won't hear over the engine.
They use about 1 litre of fuel per hour at -25F. Much less in
warmer temperatures. 
They have separate combustion chambers with an exhaust pipe 
like a furnace(they are a furnace) so there is no chance of 
poisonous gases unless the thing has rusted out just like 
your home furnace.

Electric heaters won't work. You only have a few hundred watts to spare. 
Try heating your bus standing still at -20 with a 100 watt light bulb. 
Now imagine 60MPH of extra windchill, and air leakage.
