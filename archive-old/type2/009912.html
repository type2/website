Shims range from 0.24mm to 0.36mm (.0095 to .0150 in).

Be careful in setting the end play on a worn engine, as the movement
might not the be the crankshaft in the bearing but rather the bearing
in the case.

If this is an "emergency" engine that will be itself be replaced soon,
it would be better to leave the endplay alone.  If you set it too
tight (very possible if the front bearing is a bit loose) you will
likely spin the bearing.
