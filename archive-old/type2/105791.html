Well...... not really the final word!

I have 2 68-79 Bentleys [V279]. Both are copyright 1979. One has color diagrams. Except the 79 diagram. It is
not but it is printed on the other side of the page of the color 77/78 diagram. Both are 3 page foldouts.
The other V279 is all non-color.
They both start the diagrams after page 41.
The one with color diagrams has them in the order of newest model 1st. 68 is last. And most are fold out 3 to
5 pages long. [3 are the max but 76 is 5, 2 on the left and 3 on the right]
The one with black and white diagrams has them in the order of oldest 1st, starting at 68. And they suck
because they are printed on separate pages. To follow a wire you need to flip the pages. 76 is really bad, 5
separate pages!

The only difference I see on the copyright page between them:
The non-color one has a large box that has a warning across the top:
CAUTION- Important Safety Notice
And then a lot of mumbo-jumbo.
All the ISBN numbers are the same except for the string of numbers printed below the Bentley stock number.
Color diagrams:
92 91 90 89 10 9 8 7 6 5
Black and white:
94 93 92 91 11 10 9 8 7 6
