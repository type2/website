I record every single fill up ... I record odometer reading, # of litres of 
gas, and $. Every single fill up. On both my 60 and my 78.

type 4 motor. I never bothered to calculate cents or dollars per mile with

isn't as much as it seems given you travel further on a litre of 91 than you 
do on 87.
