Torry,

Ummm...*only* 165-170F??  Lots of folks in the southern u.s. would think 
that was a great temperature as some of them apparently get up to 
220-250F.

Looks like your temps today are going to be about 84F.

My general rule of thumb here(for hot weather) is ambient temp + about 
100-120 degrees F.  If it is 100F here then I expect to get oil temps 
about 100-120f over that after a hard run at 70mph.

I would always check out my current setup and make it work right before 
installing any sort of aftermarket cooling.  

I have seen a simple valve adjustment lower temps as much as 15-20 
degrees.  On a type 1 stock engine, the addition of a little richer main 
jet and adjusting the valves dropped my temps at least 20F.

Unless I missed something, I really don't think you have a problem.
