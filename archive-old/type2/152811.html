Subject: Re: [T2] FI or carb

Not only is the FI superior, especially with the 2.0 T4, a single carb or
even dual carbs wont give you the power and reliability that FI does, it
will give you many more headaches than the FI. The L-jet does have some
common issues, sometimes even expensive, but overall, if you only have a
small issue with it, fix it!

also, just in case you don't have it, this troubleshooting guide is the best
for working with the l-jet systems. I suggest printing it out and putting it
in a 3-ring binder and keeping it in the bus at all times :) I also used
this guide to help me convert my '73 westy (installed a '81 2.0 with the
l-jet) and after i installed it I didn't even touch it until 10k miles later
when i rolled the bus on the drivers side, something went wrong after that,
and never figured it out before i sold it :(

http://www.conservatory_dot_com/vw/manuals_nyx/L-Jetronic.html

You will also find some good info on this site:
http://ratwell_dot_com/
