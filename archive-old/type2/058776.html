The adapter won't work, as it occupies the same space as the existing
bosses.

Best bet is to try and remove the studs.  If conventional weapons
don't work, find a shop that does EDM.  (look in yellow pages under
"Electrical Discharge Machining")  A good EDM man can remove the
broken studs and leave the threads unscathed.  The machinist can best
advise you about the helicoil.
