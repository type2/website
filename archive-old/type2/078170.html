Aerostars, and most other modern vans, have a completely different mounting
system that uses a claw that clasps onto a bar mounted below surface level
of the floor.  Its pretty cool in that it makes the seats easy to remove and
allow boxes and such to be slid in without bothersome mounting hardware
being in the way.  But getting a system like that to work in a VW Bus would
seem to be more trouble than its worth.  You'd have to figure out a way to
get rid of the claw feet entirely or cut holes in your floor and weld in the
recessed bars to accept the claws.
