Subject: Installing a BN2 heater in a Westy

I've pondered this for some time, and I think I have it figured out.

A BN2 (made to go in the trunk of a VW sedan) can be installed in the 
back closet of an early bay Westy.

relocated, but the exhaust from the heater can be routed  down through 
the engine compartment (and around the battery) to the outside.

The fuel supply line can tee at the gas tank outlet (and the fuel pump

fender, and then out underneath the closet door.  The switch can be 
located next to the warm air register.
