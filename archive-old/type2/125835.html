   Just get a hacksaw or cut off wheel and cut equal amounts off of each sode of the hex bar. I like to have at least 
2mm of thread showing on the ball bolt? It is not un common to have to do this on a set of webers using hex bar or 
tyco linkage.  Remember "rarely will an aftermarket part just fit with ease".  Be very careful when setting up the 
linkage also. Make sure that both carbs open evenly and stop at the same time. This is done by moving the arms on 
the hex bar in or out
