Yes there were.  The "cooker" was built on the back of the beetle and
looked a bit like a 19th century locomotive stack.  Wood was heated in a
chamber and the combustible gases stored in another chamber for use by the
engine.  Heaven help you if the apparatus sprung a leak and managed to get
air into the gas storage chamber!

> Is anyone aware of a bus being set up to run on alcohol?  Back in the '70s

Brazil had quite a few VWs that ran on straight ethanol.  This isn't
terribly practical in the US, as the agricultural production needed to
produce alcohol for a significant percentage of our cars would require all
our arable land and more.

OTOH, if you have a source of surplus grain and the knowlege of how to
turn it into Everclear, you could run your bus on it.  You would also be a
hit at bus gatherings, but remember to save enough fuel to get home! :)
