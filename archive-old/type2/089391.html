I've seen various late Baywindow buses with either Girling or ATE calipers. It
seems that VW used whichever brand of calipers that they happened to have in
stock at the moment. My '79 had Girling calipers when I rebuilt the brake
system. I couldn't locate rebuilt Girling calipers at the time, but ATE
calipers were plentiful, so I used ATE instead. I had to replace the short
pieces of steel brake line that screw into the calipers with some new ones I'd
bent to fit, but otherwise it went pretty smoothly.
