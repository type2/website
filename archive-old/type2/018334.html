Hi Volks,

I'm on the quest for pop music that somehow relates to
the VW Bus.  This is out of interest, but also for use
in the VW Bus Museum I'm a member of.

There are four "categories" into which such music might
fall:

1. A VW Bus is featured in both lyrics and video.

Naturally the most desirable category.  One example is
Men at Work's "Down Under", which starts with the line
"Travelling in a fired-out Kombi", and features the
Kombi in question in the video clip too.

2. A VW Bus is featured in the video.

I know no examples... yet.  But these would be very
interesting to know.

3. A VW Bus is featured in the lyrics, unambiguously.

This would mean that the lyrics contain "VW Bus",
"Splitty", "Westy" or any other of those VW Bus 
specific terms.

4. The lyrics can be understood as referring to a 
   VW Bus, even if they really don't.

An example would be Paul Simon's "Ace in the Hole",
containing the following: "Riding on this rolling Bus /
Beneath a stony sky"; even though we know from the
"One-trick Pony" movie that the "Bus" referred to was 
a Detroit product, the song doesn't say so.

Please reply via p-mail, unless you have a vague
recollection that other listees might be able to 
clarify.  But even then, let's keep it limited to
Friday!

I'll post a results digest next week on Friday.
