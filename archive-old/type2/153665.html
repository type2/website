in the driver's side rear corner next to the rear hatch.  That gets cut out
and some kind of screen plate with a right angle down into the engine

problem I had was the four inch thick padding of the older style westy beds
covers the hole and restricts airflow.  I wedged an old oil bottle or
something similar to hold the pad about three inches away.
