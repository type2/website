I love The Bus Depot!

Ron Salmon's Bus Depot has left me a very satisfied customer 3 out of 3 
times now. His prices on a wide range of VW parts are SUBSTANTIALLY 
cheaper than anyone else I have found. His turnaround on orders is 
typically quite speedy. His people have been quite helpful and 
informative when I needed information about country of origin and 
manufacturers of parts. As his business has grown he has developed a 
pretty slick online catalog and order system that I really like using. 

It's true that e-mail inquiries have gotten lost before, and that the
phone number is often busy when I call, but I've never seen any successful
VW business that didn't get really really busy. (For comparison, I'm STILL
waiting for that Bus Boys catalog I paid for 4 years ago.) But on my last
Bus Depot order I asked questions in the comment window of the order form.
I received answers within 24 hours and my replies were processed within
another 36 hours. 

I will vouch for Ron's good character as manifested in several years of 
type2 list posts. The integrity of his business speaks for itself.

Another satisfied customer,
