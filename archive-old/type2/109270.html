Drew,
Your symptoms pretty much match those that I had a few years ago that turned
out to be gunk (rusty internal pieces, most likely) in my fuel tank clogging
the outlets where fuel exits the tank.  I was advised to remove the tank and
either replace it or clean it out.  My second option was to try and not let
the fuel level get below half-empty in hopes of the particles not getting
low enough to clog the outlets again.  Although sometimes a PITA, that 2nd
approach has worked for me ever since.
