You will always get water coming past the scrapers, even new ones.  They
just don't seal the window properly.  That's what the drain holes are
for 8-)
If your vapour barrier is intact you shouldn't have too much water
coming in.  Are you sure it's not the door seals that are letting water
through?  I have some crappy seals that might as well not be there, they
will get replaced soon (moral - don't spend less than about 25 pounds
each on door seals).
