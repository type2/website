Markus:  There is a whole lot of stuff in your system which is no longer
available.

Consider replacing the whole system with the heat exchangers and muffler
from the 72-74 or 79-83 Type 2.  These had exhaust manifolds integrated with
the heat exchangers.

If your heat exchangers are not in great shape, you end up with fresh heat
exchangers in the process.

Consider that the "U" tube that connexts your exchangers to the manifolds
are no longer available, and if the metal on the flange is wearing thin, its
going to go soon too.  A little money spend on the other type of heater
boxes could solve your current problem, and avoid future troubles too.  Of
course, you may need to replace some sheet metal in the cooling system under
there, and there may be differences with the sheet metal heat risers going
to the flap boxes, and I'm not sure how the 72-74 boxes hook up with the
alloy cooling shoud.  If anyone knows, I'd be pleased to find out.
