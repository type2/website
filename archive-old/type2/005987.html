When I had similar problems with the clutch on my 
'73 Kombi, I found  that my pilot bearing, which centers
the transmission input shaft, had failed.  The clutch was
actually working but the chewed up needles in the pilot 
bearing had seized the flywheel to the transmission input shaft.

The new pilot bearing cost about 4 dollars but I had to pull the
engine and remove the flywheel to replace the bearing.
