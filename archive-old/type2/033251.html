i have a significant other and we both like bench seat busses better than
walkthroughs... because...

bus anyway, and she ends up climbing over the seat to get stuff.  She asys

bag, its quite nice i'd say.

so in summary, i'd much rather have a nonwalkthrough bus than a
walkthrough because its easy enough to climb over the seat, and with a
bench you get a good spare tire well and IMHO its just much cooler :)
