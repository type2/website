I'm with Gary on this one. Leaving the heat exchangers on seems to
balance the cooling air as VW intended for the heads. I had purchased a
set of real cheap aftermarket heat exchangers years ago after running
"J" tubes for about 6 months and noticed a considerable drop in head
temp. I'd say stick with heat exchangers in the circuit, just not real
leaky ones!
