I've spent quite a bit of time in Mexico, and it seems that most
USAians who got in trouble were behaving in ways that they never
would at home.  Walking out of a bar dead drunk after flashing a roll of
money is a perfect invitation to a mugging in either Bogota or Boise.  If

Slain in Columbia."

Now, for some VW content:  If you have an early bay, Mexico has to be one
of the best places to travel.  Less worries about parts and repairs than
travelling in the US!  Every little town has a repair shop with extensive
experience with aircooled vehicles.

Best travel book for Mexico:  "The People's Guide to Mexico."  It's even
fun to read if you never intend to travel south of the border!
