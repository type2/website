 Brad,
 There are two possibilities here. The wipers switch in my 1971 kombi has
to be isolated from the metal dashboard. If it´s not it will shortcut. I
think there´s some internal problem with it. The same thing happened in my
type3. Had to put plastic washers on both sides of the dashboard metal. To
get you home, just try to pull out the switch and let it hang in the cables
under the dashboard so the switch housing doesn´t touch any metal. First
unscrew the knob and then the nut that holds the switch. Tap carefully on
the slots till it loosens up.
 The other possibility is as you mentioned water in the switch. This will
draw some major current and drain your battery very fast. 

Hope this helped you.
