You were probably measuring the play in the bearing/case interface, not
the crank/bearing interface.
This is not a good thing, though I have driven tens of thousands of miles
on an engine similarly afflicted.

Be sure that you don't get the endplay too tight.  It's hard to measure
with the bearing moving on the case, so err on the loose side.  Do split
the case at the earliest to remedy the loose bearing
