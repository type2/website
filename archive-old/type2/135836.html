Hi Greg,

I'd just like to point out to the westy and westfakia owners out there 
the importance of using the SAME foam for all the parts of the bed in a 
westy. If the foam is softer in one area than it is elsewhere then it 
will be more compressible and as a result you will not be sleeping on 
an even surface. The result of that will be a poor night's sleep, a 
sore back and a spouse who won't want to come camping with you.

Don't go cheap on your mattress. Do it right, be kind to your spine!
