The California emissions busses used Cat's from '76 on, the federal
(49-state) version from '79 on.  Most Cal-emissions models used a cat that
was tucked along side the drivers side heat exchanger, resulting in a heat
exchanger and exhaust system that were California-only. The federal '79
model had the cat going left-to-right after the crossover pipe, directly
into the muffler.   However, I have seen some Cal models from '76 on that
had the latter layout, so go figure.  If your '79 bus has the latter layout,
everything you need is still available.  But if your cat is tucked along
side the heater box, the parts are not available.  In both cases, a '79
would have had a cat, as they were federally mandated from '79 on.
