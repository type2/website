The joint has a slot that allows the tapered pin to move through the angle
needed to allow the suspension to move.  If the slot is misplaced, then
the slot will not go in the correct direction.  This can cause bad things
to happen!

To be safe, get another ball joint and have it pressed in correctly.  Once
a ball joint is removed it cannot be pressed in again safely, as I recall.
