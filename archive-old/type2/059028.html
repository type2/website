You'll have to look at the engine, but is there a possibility of
fabricating a custom moustache bar that clears the filter pump?  This
would seem an ideal solution.

I don't agree with the "change oil often" technique.  Changing the oil
at short intervals doesn't stop dirt from circulating with the oil. 
Dirt will still be trapped in the lifters, which are particularly
sensitive to grunge in the oil.

If you can't fab a special moustache bar, then you'll have to go to
some sort of remote filter setup.

I'm really interested in how this goes, as I will probably be
following the same path shortly.
