At 11:19 PM 10/8/96, Steve Haney wrote:
>Does anybody know what the MAXIMUM oil pressure should be for a 1.7l
>type IV engine should be?  My VDO reads around 60 psi while the oil temp
>is below 180 degrees.

Since I haven't seen this answered publically yet, I'll take a stab at it.
60 psi is not abnormal.  My own engine sits at 70 psi when cold, and will
go a little higher if I rev the motor up (but I try not to do that).
Anyway, the pressure relief valves limit oil pressure to 28 psi at the
bearings.  60 psi is great - don't worry about it.

>Is it possible for engine to leak oil as a result of the pressure cut
>off valve failing?

Don't know the answer to that one, but I've never heard of one fail on a
properly maintained engine.
