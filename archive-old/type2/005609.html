>1) What sandpaper type (number) should I use to get the rust down?
>2) Can I get the rust down to where I need to with a hand (non-electric)
>sanding block?

Try a 50-80-120 grit disc in a drill. the grit depends on how much rust there is.

>3) What would be a good primer/rust inhibitor to use on this area?
>4) Would a primer/rust inhibitor from a spray can be a wise choice?

A couple of coats of Rustoleum rusty metal primer is the cheapest way out,
but primer is porous and must be top-coated, so do a couple coats of color.
