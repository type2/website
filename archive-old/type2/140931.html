Subject: Re: [T2] How difficult is welding ?

Welding is not that difficult, the more you do it the better you will get.
No book or video will make you a good welder, only practice will. However, a
book or video will get you started in the right direction. I never welded
before I bought my mig welder, I read the manual that came with it and
started with some scrap metal. I'm 3 years into it and still can't weld a
nice looking bead, but that's what a grinder is for. As long as there is
good penetration. Buy a good welder (Lincoln Electric or Miller to name 2),
that will make thing easier ...nothing worse than a cheap welder! Replacing
heaterchannels is a pretty big task. I'm going be doing it next month to my
friends 63 Beetle as well as the pans and the bulkhead. Should be fun!
