people no matter how fast or slow you drive.

safe doing same.

and will pass you as though you are going to do just that ...... even if 
the side of the road ends.

because they have never had to understand the needs of other drivers on the 
road. They just presume that have all the right to take whatever they want 
on the concrete..... and they will take it if given the chance.
