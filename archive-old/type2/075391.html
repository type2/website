The inner wire is nylon coated.  I wouldn't had any lubricant.  Just
make certain that the heater box levers are lubricated and
functioning smoothly.  

The trick to doing this job is knowing what to do at the rear end of
the boudin tubes.  Important thing is removing the nylon caps at the
rear ends of the tubes.  You have to bend the tubes slightly to do
this.  Once the caps are off you need to remove the small plastic
pieces in the tubes that wrap around the cable.  Now you can remove
the old cables and insert the new ones.

I did this on my 71 last year.  If you need more info, pmail me.
