Adjust your emergency brake (tighten it) and I can almost guarantee you the
pedal will firm up.  To test beforehand, pull your emergency brake on
partway and drive around a little.  The pedal should be rock hard.  Your
emergency brake should stop the bus by pulling out 4 or 5 clicks - I'll bet
yours goes out farther than that.  First adjust the stars, then adjust the
nuts on the emergency brake cables up at the front.
