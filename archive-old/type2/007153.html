My two cents worth... :-)

I have a bugpak extractor and I "had" a bugpak muffler too (it lasted about
two months before it broke).  This is on a 74' type 4 motor.  I am now
running the same extractor and a dynamax muffler that a custom exhaust shop
help me setup.

My suggestion would either be stainless steel stock muffler (Bus Depot or OE
Vdub) or an S&S extractor and the muffler of your choice via a reputable
shop.

My brother has the latter on his 77' ASI and its quiet and sturdy.

Hope this helps,
