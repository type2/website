Dirty points = low voltage
Low voltage on the primary side = low voltage on the secondary side
Got a voltmeter?  See what your voltage is going into the coil.
Disconnect the green condensor lead and see what the voltage is on the output side of the coil.
Connect the green lead and see what the voltage is with the points open.
