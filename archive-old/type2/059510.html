There is a dirt road going from Chiracahua National Park in 
southeastern Arizona that goes across the mountain range
into New Mexico. My daughter drove while I and three of my 
sons and my other daughter stood on the seat with our heads
poked out the sun roof. A very memorable experience, though
we had to duck under some low branches. My wife and 
mother-in-law were with us, but did not stick out their necks.
They were worried about flys and other bugs, but at 20 to 
30 mph you can dodge the bugs. 

Anybody know if we broke the law by not wearing our seatbelts?
