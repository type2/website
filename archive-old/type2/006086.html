Martin,

What you propose is straight forward, as long as the bug case casting has
the areas cast that are needed for the bus mount. I think some earlier cases
will not work. It's been about 10 years since I did this, so my memory is
fuzzy. Compare to your existing case to see if it looks feasable. Use your
existing engine/mounts to determine the specifics - hole placement and depth
and tap size.

Also - Be VERY careful not to drill too deep - if i recall the pressure
relief valve area is just behind one of the mount holes.
