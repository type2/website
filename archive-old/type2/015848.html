Right now, those cabinets are plentiful in the boneyards - I wouldn't pay
$10 for one.  No reason to feel bad about chopping one up unless it were in
brand-new condition.  Many Westy interiors get crushed along with the rest
of the camper at auto wreckers every month - why isn't anyone upset by that?

As for whether to remain stock or modify/improve, I came to that crossroads
a few years ago and decided on the latter.  I do a lot of camping in my bus
and I decided I really wanted a more efficient interior layout than what
Safare gave me originally, although I am trying to preserve the more unique
aspects of the layout like the U-bed and seating arrangement.

In the extreme cases, I am still bothered though.  Like when someone rips
out a complete mint Westy interior and replaces it with a bare plywood
storage cabinet and a piece of 4" foam.  If you're going to do that, please
start with a passenger bus, and save the original rear seats up in the attic.
