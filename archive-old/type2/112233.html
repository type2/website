Mike ,
I know I mentioned this before--
But I just thought I would bring the correct process up again--

Use the Vari Prime to seal down all the bare metal, and unknown type of
paint first--THEN--apply a quality ( Ultra Fill, or Uro Prime ) high
fill primer over that---then sand and paint--
It's the correct way to do this job--don't short cut it--you'll be
crying the blues down the road if you do---
