You need to grind or file the edge of the alternator where the accellerator
pump lever strikes it.  Be very careful not to grind to much off.  Just set
the carburator on the intake manifold and pull the lever down, note where it
touches, then grind some more until it clears.  I use a die grinder
 Snap-On 26,000 RPM ).
