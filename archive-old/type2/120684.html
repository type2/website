By the time you buy a big enough inverter to run a
desktop (would need at least a 300-400 watt inverter,
to the tune of $100 - $200) and buy an LCD monitor
(cheapest I've seen lately is around $250), you can
buy a good used laptop that should do everything you
need it to -  Lotsa used 700-800Mhz laptops on eBay
and the web for ~$500, and you can run them using a
$20 100w cigarette lighter inverter from Radio
Shack.... not to mention you can take it with you when
you aren't in the Bus, into the coffe shop, etc.
