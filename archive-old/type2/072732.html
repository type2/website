Look for a small notch in the rear crank pulley (should be on the  engine
side of the pulley.) Line this up with 0 degrees on your advance gauge
which would be mounted to the rear of your motor.    You need to see if # 1
valves are both closed and  the intake valve has just closed (so you don't
get # 3 TDC instead)
