Michael,

Ok, basically what you want is something that works...Fi not an option.

*If* you keep your bus in Alabama, you can probably put the weber 
progressive on it.

The problem is this....some of these carbs are for the &quot;performance&quot; crowd 
who are willing to put up with a little pain in order to get the 
power....and to pay the big bucks forthe &quot;performance part&quot;.

The general probem with a single centermount on your bus is carb 
heat...you will never be able to live with it in a cold climate as it will 
start freezing up on you....and when you get down in the 30's in alabama, 
you will probably be walking.

So, what I hear that you want is something that just works well for 
everyday driving.

http://www.busdepot_dot_com/details.jsp?partnumber=4306700

much better in the winter, but then you have to buy linkage and *TWO* 
carbs instead of just one.

You can get those here:
http://www2.cip1_dot_com/ProductDetails.asp?ProductCode=C13%2D43%2D5207
+ carbs.
