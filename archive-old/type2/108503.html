Subject: Re: [T2] Pushrods hydraulic lifters with solid rods

ALL typeIV pushrods are hollow. That is how the rocker arms get oil to be lubricated.
The part that confuses people is the definition. Solid lifter pushrods are not solid. They are aluminum tubes
with steel ends. Hydraulic lifter pushrods are one piece steel tubes. When someone says they have 'solid
pushrods', they do NOT mean they have pushrods with no hole in them! They mean they have the thicker aluminum
pushrods.
