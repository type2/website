I don't think the SU's will work. The stock Solex and all replacements I know are downdraft carbs. Your SU's are side draft carb and will not work if you mount them vertically (piston and dashpot oil). In a sandrail you might have enough room to mount them on the side of the engine, but in any other air-cooled VW application?

If you have an intake for a 1296 or 1500 Spitfire engine, I'll trade them for a set of rebuild Solex (for European spec 2L) or a set of Baby Webers (off a 914).
