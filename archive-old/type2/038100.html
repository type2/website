Another easy test for the general working of the aux air valve:  With the
engine stone cold in the morning, pull the hose leading to the aux air valve
from its attachment at the air intake elbow and blow into the hose; air
should pass easily into the air distribution box.  Replace the hose and
start the engine to let it warm up.  With the engine completely warmed up,
kill the engine and repeat the test; this time no air should pass into the
air distribution box.
