Subject: '80 engine's out

Well, today I finally got time to drop the engine in my Westy.  First time I
have done that!  I had some minor problems but got it done OK.  I had to do
this alone and it would have been easier with some help the first time.  I
used a hydraulic floor jack and kinda lowered the engine a little too far,
too fast.  The engine got stuck on the lower engine-transmission studs.
It's hard to manuver that engine on your own.  All in all, a good afternoons
work!  Engines out and partially stripped.  But will it really go <back> in
there?! :-)

The bad part was finding the things that need work.  Now I know part of the
reason my heat was so poor last winter.  One side heater control box was
rusted out much worse than I had thought.  Fuel lines were also bad news,
but I had planned to replace them anyway.  Please replace those lines, volks.  

Now I think I'll go get a beer, relax, and watch the Olympics.  Another busy
day tomorrow.
