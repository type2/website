Don't know what's causing one brake not to work but you don't need a 36mm 
wrench.  Just remove the 2 smaller bolts on the outer side of the drum.  The 
drum will come right off the hub.  You may have to loosen the brakes with 
the adjusting stars as a "lip" sometimes builds up on the inside edge of the 
drum from the shoe wear.  You only need the 36mm nut removed to work on the 
bearings (I know because I made the same assumption once)
Just thought I'd save you the trouble.
