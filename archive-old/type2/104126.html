Subject: Frustration sets in due to timing woes on 72 bus

Hey here's the scoop: I bought a 72 bus a few weeks back for $200 and it
runs great but had major acceleration issues. So a great smooth idle,
instant starting etc...
But it would barely accelerate once I got changed from 1st to 2nd etc. So
I checked the vaccuum advance and it was ruptured. So I replaced with the
exact #925 that goes with the 73 distributor . I checked and double

So when I got the new vaccuum advance in place and got the distributor

deal...
And to make a long story as short as possible here's what the deal is:

My engine will not run when the distributor is set at 10 ATDC! It will
idle very very low and just sputter out. To low.
But, if I advance the timing to well before TDC then it will idle very
smoothly (but it still doesn't accelerate, I took it around the block).

Good compression and all that.......
