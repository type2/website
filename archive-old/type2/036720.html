In some 83-8X Toyotas, namely 4-runners and the 4wd tercel wagon, there is a
'clinometer' mounted on the top of the dash just to the right of the
instrument cluster. It is a device the indicates on two planes (though
roughly) the current angle at which the vehicle sits with repect to 'level'.

The housing of this device, i think would make a perfect housing for 2-3 small
gauges - either on top of the dash (my choice) or under.  

If you ever get chance, check one out in a junkyard - the 4wd tercel is the
funky, high-top looking little car.
