Actually, 150 watts can be pretty hot.  Alot of the heat will be dissipated
through the metal of the engine, but if you give it some time, 150 watts
will be enough to warm the oil and engine so that it will start easier on
those cold mornings.  I'm assuming you'll be plugging the heater into a
timer so that it operates at least 20-30 minutes before you start the bus.
Make sure the AC wire leading into the unit is not frayed or damaged in any
way, because the heater won't get as hot, and in a worst case scenario,
could cause 110VAC to be present on the metal portion of the heater, which
would be bad.  That's a problem with only having two prongs on the plug.
