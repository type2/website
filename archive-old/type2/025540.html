Shelby,

I installed an early bay child's cot on our 1972 bus. It was a bit of work
to get it right but It can be done. If you have the right cot the poles will
be of equal length and round. at the end of each pole the is an ear the
hooks into mounts that are screwed into the A and B pilars. One pole goes
between the two sides of the windsheild and the other goes behild the front
seats in the B pilars. If you dont have the mounts go back and get them.
There is only one "sweet spot"  where the poles can mount. If you need
serious installation details let me know.
