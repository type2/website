A better way to find #1 TDC:

Remove the 3/4 valve cover and watch # valves.
Turn engine in the direction of rotation. Watch the
exhaust valve [end valve]. It will open and then
close. JUST as it closes, the intake will start to
move. STOP, when the exhaust and the intake rock back
and forth as you move the crank back and forth, this
is the point of rocking. Look at your timing mark on
the pully, it should be on zero. Look at your dist
rotor, it will be pointing to #1 to FIRE. If not, move
your plug wires to match.
