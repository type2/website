Kombi is short for KOmbination it is one of VW's more 
utilitarian busses essentially a Panel Van w/ windows. It 
has/had no interior panels/headliner in the rear.

It could be ordered with or without seats or with or without 
a sunroof.

The seats were easily removeable making it capable of 
carrying cargo very easily.
