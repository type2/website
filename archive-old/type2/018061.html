Hi everybody,

Last weekend I made a tiny step forward in my quest to decode the
options plate. (For those of you who don't know what I am talking about:
the options plate is that little plate behind your drivers seat or under
your dash with all the numbers on it.) On the VW meeting in Exloo (The
Netherlands), I met a guy with a late 70ties Westy Brochure. It showed
all the color combinations with along with the VW color codes used on
the options plate. Now I know for sure which code stands for which
color. Here's what I found:

  Paint color:

    Chrome Yellow   9156
                    9157*

    Brilliant Orange        9226
                            9227*

    Sage Green      9451
                    9452*

    Dakota Beige    9826
                    9830*

    Pastell White   9926

    *) with Pastell White roof

The brochure also revealed that option code 'P21' and 'P22' both stand
for a Westfalia interrior.

I updated the T2 color codes list. The URL is:

   http://www.tem.nhl.nl/~molen502/bus/t2colors.html

For more info on the option codes plate:

   http://www.tem.nhl.nl/~molen502/bus/opcodes.html



Vincent.
