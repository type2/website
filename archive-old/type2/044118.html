walt-
what you need is one of 2 things. the first is called a push-button. it is a
button in your cab that activates your solenoid. i'm not sure how to do this,
but i do know how to do the second. hook a new wire with a
female spade to the ignition terminal on your solenoid., and wire it to your
battery.
turn the key in the on position and touch the wire to the + terminal. should
start it
everytime. it is most likely that the wiring is fried, and this will be your
best bet to solve that.
