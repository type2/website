[edit note, I asked Karl to explain this short reply further, and he
replied a second time with this next section:]

The 6 ply and 4 ply is in the tread area. The side wall is 2 ply but the

difference is the thickness of the sidewall. Michelin MX 185/14 
passenger tire tread is 4 ply, 2 steel and 2 rayon. The side wall is 2 
ply rayon. The load limit is 1450 lbs.
Michelin MX 185/14 reinforced has the same ply material in the tread and

sidewall but the load limit is 1540 lbs. The difference is in the 
thickness of the side walls. 
Thicker side walls reduces the flexibility of the tire.
