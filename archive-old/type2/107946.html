A typical VOM can handle enough current to safely measure the memory
sustaining power to a clock or car stereo, or to detect a SMALL short with a
high resistance.  However, anything larger than that could fry a VOM that's
designed to measure 300 mA max. If you suspect a larger drain, be careful
with your meter, or buy one that's designed to measure different amounts of
current.
