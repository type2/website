Subject: Re: [T2] Leaky pushrod tubes

Also, an up-date in regard to taking on and off the valve covers.  Use
drum brake adjusting pliers for the removal and installation.  I
beginning to think that it's they're secret purpose because I never used
them when I redid my drum brakes .. hehehe

When you're pulling the spring back up over the cover, you just kinda
grab the spring in the plyers and close the plyers and it just pulls it
up.  It works sooooo beautifully making the process very painless.  I
can take the spring off and then put it back on in under 20 seconds..
can't do that very easily with a screwdriver!

Mike
