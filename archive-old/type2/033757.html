I made my own tire cover using vinyl fabric from So-Fro fabrics and a 3"
or so wide vw logo off the hood of a bug (get them for $1 at swap
meets).  I'm sure someone on the list has taken a pic of it at some
meet.  Trace the tire shape onto the fabric backside, adding about a
1/4" all around.  Cut 0.75-1" outside of this line (the more accurate
the better).  Measure the circumference of the tire and add about 3" to
this, and cut a piece this long, and wide enough to cover the tire with
a 1" kerf added plus an extra 2" that will be folded over to form a loop
for a cord tie.  Sew this all around the circle, allowing the final 3"
to overlap itself.  Sew the loop for the cord, which will be fed thru
the loop plus an extra 16" total to tie a bow.  The overlap with the
string bow tie will be at the bottom of the cover.  Find the center and
place the hood emblem thru holes you melt in with a heated nail.  I used
"push nuts" from a hardware store to hold it on.  Still looks great
after 4 years.
