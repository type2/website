Down where i live there was a vw shop that was in business about 40 years
until the owner passed away a year ago.  He made a living off of pulling the
thermostat and flaps off of every motor that ever came into that
place..............self-trained, and had a big clientele.

type 4 motor. with flaps and thermostat takes forever to warm up on a really
cold day.  I can only imagine the wear and tear vans take that have this
stuff removed in cold climates.
