I call it an engine compartment seal, part number 411 813 225, $19.95 on my
website for a German one. (You can search by part number, now that you know
it, and order that way.)  It just pushes in; you can do it without removing
anything although you have to be somewhat of a contortionist to squeeze it
around the back.  It is crucial to the cool operation of the engine; if it
is missing or damaged, hot exhaust air can be sucked in by the cooling fan,
increasing the engine operating temperature to deterimental effect.
