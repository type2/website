When I re-assembled my '78 Westy, I made sure all of the factory heater
parts were intact and sealed. I also made sure my front fresh air vent was
sealed and that my door seals and window seals were good. I also made sure
my thermostat and all my tin were intact and working. I am glad to report
that my heating system works excellent without modifications. It isn't HOT,
but that is what a jacket is for in the winter. If you can't see your
breath, it's working at factory specs! :)

(this goes for my '68 Double Cab as well, all intact factory parts)
