Hi all,

All '76 and later model fuel gauges there are
adjusters on the back of the
gauge. Remove the fuel gauge and on the back are
two small holes that with
what looks to be gear cogs just inside. These can
be moved with a steady firm
pry using a pen tip. You should adjust these hot.
Meaning get some long wires
and hook it up with the key on. At first I though
they were full and empty setting
adjusters but I think otherwise now.
Anyone care to work a dynamics problem? Anyone
messed with these?
