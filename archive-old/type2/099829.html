I think your friend is "special". Putting oil in the gas would do
NOTHING for the engine, the gas is isolated in the area between the
cylinders and the heads, the only place it would come in contact with
anything to lube it would be the intake port guides. The oil would also
effectively lower the octane of the gas, and also lead to nice carbon
buildup in the heads and piston tops. This is a no win situation. I
can't think of anything postive to come of it except minimal lubing of
the intake guide. Courtney
