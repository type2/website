True but modifications sound pretty extreem...  Basically it is a bolt 
in job for the transaxle.   you need to readjust the shift rod (let it 
slip together and redrill the hole in the rod) where it joins behind the 
drivers area (under the bus of course) by the amount the newer 
transaxle rod is longer.  make sure you keep all the angles correct.

info.
