first, is it really too far gone?  The thing to look at is the body, as
anything mechanical can be fixed, and usually a bus that has been sitting has
the bits, unless it's been parted out. except often the motor is missing.    

Look at the main frame underneath- they should be solid.  The ones than run
the length of the bus.   Don't worry if the outriggers to the jack point are
rusted- they can be replaced. 
Is it just some dents, dirt, and been a storage shed with a little rust on the
floor, 
or is it mushed, parted out, and has trees growing through it?

If you can, look at the M code tag on the metal partition  behind the
passenger seat and post the info.
