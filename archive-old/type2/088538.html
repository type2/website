Take the turn signal assemby off and clean the contacts with Q-tips soaked
in rubbing alcohol.. You would be surprised how dirty these can get and it
can cause strange, intermittent problems. Manually operate the switch and
you will see where these contacts are. Then see how it does. I would do this
before I started tearing into any wiring. Also, check your taillight grounds
and your chassis/transmission ground. Jan B
