Subject: Re: [T2] hydraulic valve lifters

On Mon, Jul 26, 2004 at 02:12:09AM -0700, Colby Simpson wrote:
> i just adjusted my valves (~500 miles since rebuild). The Bentley
> said that for hydraulic valve lifters...all you need do is adjust
> them for zero clearance and then 2 more turns clockwise and then
> lock them down with the nut. Is this right? Tighten them? sounds odd
> to me. I have not driven it since adjustment...just moved the van to
> another spot. Sounded noisy and then quieted down some. I don;t
> think it is as quiet as it was after the rebuild. Anyone have any
> experience with this?

yes, the bentley is correct. There are some here and there (i'm one of
them) that get better results by anywhere from 1-3 turns instead of 2.
It is correct because a hydraulic lifter will auto adjust to what the
valve needs. If the engine sits a bit the lifters can (and do) bleed
down which causes them to make quite an awful lot of racket. It should
quiet down after a bit though.
