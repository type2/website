Subject: Re: [T2] 80 Vanagon odometer not working

Well I figured out what was jamming the odometer. the reset button presses a
lever that moves and allows the trip odometer to reset. there is a steel shaft
that has a coil spring attached to it that stops the lever from going too far
into the odometer wheels. this shaft had moved to one side slightly, allowing
the lever to jam the gears. moved the shaft back into position. Now every
thing operates smoothly. All I have to do now is find the lost spur gear and
I'll be counting miles. Does anyone have a spare gear they want to sell?
