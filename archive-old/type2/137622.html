>  3. is there anything i cant do to correct the inherent problems w/

Yes.  You can replace it with either the correct stock distributor, or 
a suitable replacement.  I installed an SVDA from aircooled_dot_net in my 
'76 and I love it (my original distributor needs a rebuild).

In any case, you should not run a 009 in a Bus.  Actually, I'm of the 
opinion that the 009 should not be used in *any* VW.  It was not a 
stock distributor on any VW engine.

step in trying to clear up this condition.  Eliminate those variables 
before hunting any further.
