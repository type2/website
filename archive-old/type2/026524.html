I'll agree with that 100 percent!!  Anytime I'm driving on a hot day (90
degrees F. or more) and sustaining speeds of 70 mph or more (4000 rpm+), my
oil temp gets "really" hot.  I don't have a number to put to that since my
temp gauge doesn't show numbers, but it'll climb up to the 2/3 mark.  When I
drop to 60 on the highway, the gauge will drop to just below 1/2 and when I
slow to 45 mph going through a town, the temps drop a little more.  On a
cool day (<75 degrees F.) I can run 4000 - 4400 RPM for hours without going
over the 1/2 way mark.
