Subject: Re: [T2] transaxle oil

On Thu, 23 Dec 2004, Rudy Cooper wrote:

> I'm going to change the oil in my transaxle.  Reading
> from the original Muir book it doesn't seem to be that
> big of a deal, but I wanted to get some first hand
> knowledge from any of you out there.  Any surprises,
> what type of oil should I use ?

GL4.

People at shops will tell you that GL5 replaces GL4, but you should not 
use GL5 because it will corrode parts in your tranny.  I believe NAPA 
still stocks GL4.

I believe you want SAE 90 weight.

Otherwise, no big surprises. After filling, take it for a test drive, then 
recheck level and top off as appropriate.
