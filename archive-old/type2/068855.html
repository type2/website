>Bob also notes that the spark plugs can be gapped much wider, 
>does anyone know how much wider?

The instructions accompanying the CDI unit suggest .040 to .045
inch gap, although an equation they present (.375 X CR= gap in
inches) would yield .053 for my engine.  This seems kind of wide
to me though--I think they had higher compression water-pumpers
in mind.
