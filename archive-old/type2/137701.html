I've done too many different years of buses and vanagons. If yours is
like the one I remember, there is a valve under the floor that opens
the vent at the lip at the front of the cargo area, just behind the
front seats. Look under the bus for a large round duct feeding this
vent with a cable activated valve. It is easy for this to get corroded
from exposure to the elements. You may be able to free it up with some
kind of spray product.
