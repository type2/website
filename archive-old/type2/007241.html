I always wave, even if the waveage is not returned. Boaters always wave. 
Fishermen don't seem to. At least down here in Miami/South Florida/Upper Keys. 
(Scuba divers don't wave). 

When I used to wear my Florida Keys floppy straw hat, cigar in mouth, (hat used 
to be required attire for anyone driving my '71 Campmobile, cigar optional), 
most VW bug/bus owners waved, silly grins on their faces. I looked dopey and 
didn't care. 

Now that my '71 is 90% restored, shiny paint, flawless body, I've noticed less 
wave action. I guess people think I'm a more serious character 'cause my bus is 
clean. Maybe I should buy a new floppy hat.

Moral: Wave all the time, whether they drive a splittie, a late model, Vanagon 
or a row boat.
