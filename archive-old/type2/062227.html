> How hard is it to do a restoration on the poptop? Replacing the seals
> and canvas tent? This one needs both.
 
The poptop comes pretty clean if you power wash it, when I just got my
72 we towed it to a car wash on the way home and gave a good power wash
to the whole thing, looked much better. Bleach also seemed to do the
trick for the luggage rack, have not tried the poptop yet.  Be careful
taking the poptop off, its heavy and will scratch paint.  The seal you
need is about 60-70 $ and can be put on with a hammer and short 2x4. I
will be cleaning my top up this week end and let you know how it comes
out.
