Hi,
I just did the same thing you are contemplating-same dizzy too, a 205T.

My changeover wennt really well, and I noticed an immediate difference.

I also rebuilt a 30 PICT2 carb, but it leaked, so I put back my *&^#%*
Brosol until I can find another carb to rebuild.  Even with just changing
over the dizzy, the difference was very pronounced.  I have 5 busses/bugs,
all with 1600 engines, all with 009's.  You get used to the slight
hesitation, and in time, you get used to it.  I never realized what a
difference the 205T would make, or I would have done it long ago.

John Connelly at Oldvolks_dot_com REALLY know his stuff about Distributors, and
has been a great resource for solutions to problems.  You should log on to
his site, and he is great about answering questions.

Good luck, and let us all know how it turns out.
