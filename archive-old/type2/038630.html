Are you sure you are getting a trigger signal?
You should have a 3 wire harness coming down from the heater blower. The
black wire goes to + on coil. The white wire goes to - on coil. The 3rd wire
goes to the oil pressure sender.
You should have a single white wire coming across the engine from the
passenger side. This also goes to - on the coil.
If either white wire is not on the negative side of the coil, you do not get
a trigger signal to fire off the injectors.
Also make sure both ground wires are connected to the ground lug mounted
under a case bolt under the left front of the plenum [the football shaped
air box on top of the center of the engine]. This is the ground for the ECU.
