I bought my first 1971 in 1978 and everything was original.  The was no way you
could open the hatch from the inside and there was no tug knob.  I saw several
71's in junkyards twenty years ago and never have I seen one with a tug knob that
unlocked the hatch.  The only Westfalia I saw with an original equipment lever for
opening the hatch from the inside was my in-laws 1970.  The wood panel was
specially cut to expose the lever on the lock.

What I did was make a small cut in the wood cover and attach a rod to a lever on
the latch that could be used to unlock the hatch.
