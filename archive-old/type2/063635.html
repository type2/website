Subject: Hand cleaner

One trick I learned from the good old boys down in Lousiana was to scrub your
hands with clean motor oil before attempting to wash with soap.  Grease and
dirty oil disolve real well in clean oil.  It works.  Nowadays, however, I tend
to use gojo.  John Jensen in Annapolis.
