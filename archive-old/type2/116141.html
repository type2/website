Subject: Re: [T2] Running problems with a 78 fuel injection Bus

To me it sounds like the cylinder head temp sensor.  Once it dies after
starting to warm up simply connect a wire from the sensor connector
to ground, this will simulate a warm head signal, and try starting it up
again.  If it works, go get a new cylinder head temp sensor.  It's about
the only thing that changes as the engine warms up - the signal from the
temp sensor in the air flow meter should not change as the engine
warms up.
