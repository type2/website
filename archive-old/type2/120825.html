I am looking for another set of these now that my OE Agilis are approaching
50K miles. There is really nothing else that does the job for these vans.
Dont even consider a non-reinforced tire for the Eurovan. I had a set of
non "C" rated BF Goodrich tires on my previous 1993 Eurovan and found them
to be unstable in hard cornering. Last summer, with over 40K on my Agilis,
I had to swerve hard to avoid a car that ran a stop sign. Even with this
tall, heavy van, I had good control at about 50 mph and was able to avoid
an accident. Snow traction was excellent for the first 35K, but is now to
the point where performance has noticeably decreased. Overall, treadwear
has exceeded my expectations for the Eurovan.
