I'd suspect the condenser first, even if it is new.  The condenser is
there to protect the points by preventing excessive arcing.  If the
condenser is bad, the points will go fast.

It might also be the coil, but then you would probably also have
running problems.

Best advice is to replace points, condenser and coil.  If the problem
goes away you can narrow it to the specific component, if it's worth
your while.
