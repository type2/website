Subject: RE: [T2] Isopropyl Alcohol to Water to Gasoline Ratio?

You probably want to drain the entire tank.  Then add maybe a quart of
industrial alcohol. Slosh it around, then dain it again, then fill up with
fuel and maybe some Heet or more alcohol (same thing). Don't use drug store
rubbing alcohol; it's already got plenty of water and won't absorb any more.
You want the industrial stuff used for cleaning electronics...it's almost
100% alcohol w/ almost no water (flammable!).

If the vehicle mostly runs OK, you could just try adding a couple bottles of
Heet with every fill up. Eventually this will get all the water. How long
depends on how much...
