Bonjour, Luc,

Back in 1980 my mom drove a 71 Westy, and my dad put a BN-2 in the front on
the floor in front of the passenger seat. Being handy with tin-snips, he
then vented the heater output into the defroster vent.

It worked great, the windsheild defrosted in 10% of the time that a
water-cooled car would take. And the passengers didn't reall mind the new
footrest.
