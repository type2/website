On Fri, 23 Feb 2001, Doyt W. Echelberger wrote:

> I can't tighten down the exhaust headers at the heads on my 1976 2 liter 
> Westy because the threads are stripped where the studs fit into the 
> heads.  The only solution my mechanic knows about is to take the heads to a 
> machine shop and have helicoils put in. Are other solutions available? Can 
> I get this problem solved without taking the heads off and moving them to a 
> machine shop?
> 
> Another idea the mechanic had was to drop the whole engine, drain the oil, 
> and take the whole thing into the machine shop, where it could be flipped 
> upside down and the helicoils installed without removing the heads.

There's no real trick to installing Helicoils.  You drill the stripped
hole out with the correct size drill, tap it with the supplied tap, and
screw in the helicoil.  A machinist could probably do it best, but I've
done a few with good results.
