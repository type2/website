Nobody has mentioned liberal use of a lubricant that the paint and rubber 
will be happy with. I have used silicone spray with excellent results but 
some will tell you this will cause fisheye problems on future paint jobs 
and I have used liquid dishwashing detergent (lots of water) with good 
results.

Use lots of this stuff and apply it right before you set the seal into the 
window opening.
