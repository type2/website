DO NOT USE RADIATOR HOSE.  Gas will eat it.  It will probably last for a while 
but in a year or so you may be spewing gas again.  Until then you might get a lot 
of gunk in you gas.  If you get the silicone type of rad hose it will last longer 
since this is more resistant to gas but not completely.

Get a piece of 2.25" inside diameter straight fuel hose reinforced with string 
(not wire).  This will be pliable enough to bend to the required shape.

Hopefully I'm talking about the right hose.  I didn't read the original post but 
I figure you're talking about the 4" hose from filler neck to tank that I was 
inquiring about on the list a few weeks ago.

part?  Personally I think you could make much more money if you just had someone 
fabricate the for you.  As long as you sell them for less than $90 you'd be able
