I've used my puller setup once in 15 years, but I've never seen a puller bend
the center of a drum. I'd be god-awful impressed if it did happen, especially on
a bus that had been recently driven, since the forces of the drum's rotation
would tend to keep it from rusting on too solidly. Not that it couldn't happen,
but I've never seen it.
