Where do you have that fuel pump mounted?

Under the tank in the frame rail?

Or up in the engine compartment?

Electric pumps are pushers, not pullers. By that I mean they need to be gravity fed. They do not like to have
to suck fuel from the tank. Mechanical fuel pumps are pullers.

When an electric fuel pump is mounted up in the engine compartment, it pulls when new but as it gets older it
has a tough time pulling.
