I wouldn't either.
As far as i have been able to deduce, wear in valve guides mill mainly affect
the cooling of the valve itself; the more distance between the guide and the
valve, the less heat transfer between the guide and the valve, hotter valve=
shorter valve life.

As the guide wears, you would probably start burning a little oil and
eventually the valve loses its head.

I really don't se a direct line of causation between p-rod seal leaking and
valve guide wear, nor do i see a relation between comoression and valve
guides.
