Find a "saddle" plumbing Drain/waste T connector at Home Depot and cut a
hole in the heat pipe, attaching the saddle with some calk and large
hose clamps. Run insulated ducting through the floor under the bedseat
and to the vent register.

the spare tire closet.  I did this because I turned the closet into a
tools/stuff  cabinet with its door flush to the sink cabinet so the
waste stool could easily sit there (against seat belt holders) for
climbing up to top bed.  The blockage was cause by the carpet-covered
board I placed the to let the stool be flush to the higher floor between
front seats.
