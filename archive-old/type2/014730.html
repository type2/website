Skip the puller, hit the thing with a hammer. I have spent 10 years doing
brake jobs and never used a puller. Get a very large hammer and smack it
squarely. That will break the rust loose. The problem with pullers is, the
center of the drum is stamped steel, it is rusted to the hub, you can pull
hard enough with a puller to bent the center of the drum. It will be a paper
weight after that. When useing the hammer, you must hit it at the hub, knock
the studs out first if needed so you do not destroy them.
