Are you sure that isn't a sleeve toward the head end of the bolt?  The shock
usually has a metal sleeve inside the rubber bushing, that the bolt passes
through.  Sometimes it rusts to the bolt, and looks like one piece.  If this
is the case, you should be able to remove the sleeve from the bolt by vice
grips or driving/pressing the bolt out.
