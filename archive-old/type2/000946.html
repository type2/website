If you don't already have them, I would hunt down a doghouse fan and oil
cooler to match.  A full flow oil filter (since the block is already
assembled, you may want to use the CB Performance oil pump with both the in
and out on the cover, but Berg says you can drill the block by using
compressed air to blow out the chips as you drill and tap it, which allows
you to use other covers).  I use a deep sump, but it reduces ground
clearance and I don't think it makes the engine run much cooler. External
oil coolers are controversial.  I don't use one, and don't feel a need for
one. And gauges.  Cylinder head temp for sure, and oil temp and pressure
couldn't hurt.  Other than that, have fun!
