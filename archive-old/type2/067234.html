If you can find it- the stuff you need is called Baltic Birch and is found in
10 and 3 mm thicknes. It is in sheets approx. 5 feet by 5 feet and is very
strong for its weight. It does not have any voids and the surface is usually
quite bland as far as wood grain goes.
