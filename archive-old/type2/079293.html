If it was caused by silicon (ie grease) it would cause fish eyes. This
sounds more like incompatible or poor quality paint.

I have never painted a car with spray cans, but if you paint enamel over
acrylic you can get a problem just like this. If this is the case, you
should not paint with spray cans and start again with real paint.

I would say it is caused by the spray can paint being poor quality (no
offense intended), or by the original paint underneath being incompatible
with it (in which case you could put on another 2 heavy coats of primer (you
need more than 2-3 light coats)).

I hope this helps....
