Is this the carb you have? http://www.larrysoffroad_dot_com/int22680.gif

My 73 bus also had a single carb conversion when I bought it and I just
replaced the carb with a new one. The above picture is of a weber DFEV
progressive which is most likly what you have. Your electric choke may look
a little different as well as the fuel intake connection might have a fuel
filter screwed right into the carb. If that is the carb you have, there are
two idle adjustments, a fast and slow. The fast is for when its cold and the
slow is for normal idle. The fast idle screw is behind the electric choke
assembly. The slow idle screw right near the shaft that the throttle linkage
connects to. You should be able to easly see it and adjust it through the
engine door (outside one). I'll have pictures on my website soon I hope.

I don't know anything about the distributor though...
