Paul Williamson wrote:
> 
> Beth,
> 
> A few questions -
> 
> What kind of tires do you run?  If they are passenger tires, that may be the
> problem.  Look at the Tire FAQ on the type2_dot_com website for more info about
> tires.
> 
> How old are the shocks (front and rear)?  Old shocks will allow your bus to
> track from side to side whilst being tossed around in the wind.
> 
> Do you have a front stabilizer bar?  It's this big bar that run underneath
> the front beam and down the trailing arms with two clamps on each end (on
> the arms, not the beam).  If you are missing that or it is an old stock
> unit, you will get tossed around as well.
> 
> Check out the front and rear of the bus and let us know what you find!
