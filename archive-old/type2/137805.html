Subject: Re: [T2] [t2]Rebuilt engine still stalls

I hope your fuel system is in good shape. Symptoms of flooding are
like symptoms of running lean. But flooding won't seize your engine,
running lean will. Always fix the cause of a failed engine before
running the new engine, or the new engine will just suffer the same
fate (unless everything was fine and your old engine died of old age).
If you suspect flooding, check for black smoke from the exhaust.

Your symptoms also are similar to those of a clogged catalytic converter.

Check Phil's ideas too.
