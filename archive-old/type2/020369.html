[...]You may have the problem described a while ago on the vintagevw 
list.  As I understand it, the 009 has too limited a range of total 
advance.  So, to get the proper advance at speed, you are forced to set 
the advance at idle too high.  This cranks up the idle speed, so to 
compensate, you are forced to crank down on the carb idle adjustment too 
much, so now there is not enough air flow through the idle circuit.  It 
sounds like a vicious circle.  You need to get the proper dizzy in there.
