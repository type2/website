Hi Jim,

VW/Wetfalia changed hammock styles around 1974 or so, you need the "newer" cot 
with the adjustable "U" channel poles (easily identifiable and the fact that 
they are finished in a glavanized finish) as opposed to the "older" black 
painted round tubed poles that you got. If it is any consolation it is a common 
mistake.
