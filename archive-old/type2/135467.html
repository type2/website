I have been unable to order these lately either from Bus Depot or German Supply. From Bus Depot the small seals were brown but he large ones green (or vice versa).

Whatever viton seals you find, be sure to have them on hand to replace all of the seals. If you replace just the ones that are leaking but remove the tubes to inspect the others, you may find that the original black seal has swelled with oil and will not go back into the bore.
