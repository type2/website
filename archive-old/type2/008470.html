Well, they're the right size and load rating so they should do fine. 
The only bad press that Michelins have gotten here are that they tend
to separate their steel belts which can really mess up the fender
wells.  If you get a new vibration, be sure to check your tires for
partial separations (with all tires, not just Michelin!)

BTW, were these tires rated M+S?
