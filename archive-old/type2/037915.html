I've seen some catalogs where you determine which part is appropriate
for certain years by using the VIN as the cutoff (or 1/2 year). The last
six numbers of your VIN represent which number your bus was in the
production line for that year. You might want to go to the type2 library
and look up the M-codes to determine if your bus is really an "early 72"
or just has had some weird experiences down the line. October sounds
pretty early to me (my bus's birthday is 10/2/72, #29210 off the
assembly line), but model-year 72 was one of those transitional years
where serial number order could make a big difference.

Hope this helps,
