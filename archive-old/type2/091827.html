Mike,

Good question---and I have have often wondered about the  same
thing---and I have been doing body work for 29 years.

I have often thought that to properly prep the metal you would have to
prime the bare metal first, then apply the body filler.

The problem is if you do this, the chances are pretty good the Bondo
won't stick.
So what do you do?
Acid wash the bare metal with Metal Prep, then apply the Bondo---then
seal, then prime.
The acid wash will leave behind a protective barrier that the filler
will stick to preventing any further corrosion in that area.
Then zinc chromate seal the area in question, then prime.

This system does work well.
