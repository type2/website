Rent a car dolly, they work great.
Remove the gearbox side of your drive axles and put heavy freezer bags
over the ends to keep your CVs clean and tie the bags on securely with
wire or use Duct Tape. Tie the axles up close to the frame with steel
wire (not electrical copper) you can travel faster and most car dollys
have brakes and thats a big help.
