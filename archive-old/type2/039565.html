I hate to disagree (or it could be the dyslexia) with the Co-administrator,
Mr. Dolan, but I must.  The 68-72 shaft is a different length than the
73-79.  The 68-72 is about a 1/2 inch shorter than the 73-79.  Last year I
stripped the stub axle on my 72 and ordered one through Ronnie of
Type2parts_dot_com and the one he sent was the 73-79 and I did not notice the
length difference till after I tried to tighten the nut down and would not
tighten anymore and still had a gap so I pulled it off and laid it next to
the stripped axle and it was longer so I called Ronnie and he sent another
(the right one) no charge
