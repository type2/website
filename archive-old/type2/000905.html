Hi Michael,
it might have been a Eberspaecher B1L or a Webasto HL18B. Both B's mean
Benzin = petrol, non Diesel. Both L's means Luft = air.
Both are as half as big (small) as a shoe-box; ca. 1.8 KW heaters.
The only additions are external fuel-pumps and exhaust-pipes.
I've had both in different type 1s (self-installed),  great "modern-style"
heaters. More efficient and easier to install than the old BL4/6's.
Very reliable.
But not so stylish. Roughly 900 $ each new here in Fatherland.
