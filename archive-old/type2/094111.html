Decel valve mounts to firewall. Back left hose goes brake booster (left of
air distributor), Front left connected right, Yellow hose needs to go behind
s-boot to throttle (hard reach behind near firewall).  This is usually a
small hose.

Right hose in yellow circle goes to auxiliary air regulator.  Look for a
small plug at the bottom front right of the air distribution box under the
fuel lines/wiring harness/plug wires.

It depends on how your coil is oriented.  2 wires connect to Terminal 1.  1
goes to the condenser (or black box in my case) on the distributor. The
other goes to a wire running out of the wiring harness near the ECU
connector.

On terminal 15, one black wire goes to a pair of wires come from the back of
the fan hanging down.  The other goes to a fuse that go through the left
side of the firewall (grommet)
