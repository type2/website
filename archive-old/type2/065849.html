> The overflow pipe from the fuel pipe was soaked aswell, but I am not sure if

I would worry about having a tear in the diaphragm of your pump. This 
could be bad news. The new gas seems to be eating up some of the older 
seals that are on cars - this might be the case, but is pure conjecture. 
Also I just bought a burned out 71 T2 that looked like it sprang a leak 
at the fuel pump. It is not too hard to remove the fuel pump and check 
it for leaks.

My 2 cents
