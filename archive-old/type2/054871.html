On Thu, 13 Jul 2000, raverporn safari wrote:

> So ive heard everything from needing a 20inch one to a 24 inch one. I havent 
> seen even one over 21" so far so IM guessing 19-20 should be fine?

NO :-( 19" is not high enough.  mine goes to 19 1/2" and if I put it under
the shock mount in the rear, jack it up all the way, put a jackstand under
the rear torsion bar housing, and lower it, the unloading of the rear
suspension will just *barely* keep the tire off the ground (maybe 1/2").  
At the rear tire, between the ground and the bottom of the frame on my '73
there is nearly a foot of air (11 3/4", i just measured it). You have to
go up a foot from there to get the tire off the ground, which means that
24" is fine, anything less will be a PITA.  You *can* use a jack with a
shorter lift. I do.  All the time. And curse it.  All the time.

> Is 19 inches the majic number I should be watching out for? or do I need 
> higher?

In my opinion, 24" is the majic number, honest!

> ive been doing some shopping around and ive heard of people carrying this

Barry Burneskis found this set, nice jack stands and a 24" jack, on sale
at ?K-Mart? some time last year and posted it to the list.  I did not take
advantage because I had a jack and stands. Since then I have seen what
Barry bought and I am kicking myself every time I use mine.  I strongly
recommend looking 'til you find the right jack.  

On a side note...jack stands.... do not buy the tube-in-tube style with
the sheet metal saddle...  they are a joke.  Look for heavy guage steel on
the base and a cast saddle. Buy like your life depends on them, cuz it
does!
