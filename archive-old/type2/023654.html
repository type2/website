30 degrees  ATDC for 009 is wrong.  I think you meant 30 degrees BTDC.

If you're static timing (engine off), use 7.5 BTDC.  To time at 7.5
BTDC, you must first know what the mark on the pulley is.  I know
that for a 71, the mark is at 5 degrees ATDC and is used to time with
a strobe with the stock distributor and all hoses connected.

You need to find out what your mark is at and then use it as a
reference to create a mark at 7.5 degrees BTDC for static timing.
