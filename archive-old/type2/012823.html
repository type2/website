Hi Bob,

I agree with your idea of keeping it easy to grab.  In
fact the compact list I was thinking about could be
like a cell phone version of the present list.  Within
a state it would be sorted by city, and have the
essential information like name, email, phone, and
crossroads pop up fast.  I've got it for North
Carolina, but it took a couple hours to cut and paste
the AIRS info into a spreadsheet.  If someone could
figure out how to splice these "cell phone lists" from
each state onto the web site, maybe volunteers could
do their states and send them in.

By the way, I like spreadsheets because they seem much
simpler to me than data bases.  Being simpler, I think
they would be easier for most of us to handle and
update.  On the other hand, data base experts I'm sure
would want to do this with a data base.
