think.  If you have a dual port with a header, go to a single port with a
stock bus exhaust and a carb with a smaller venturi, maybe some smaller
valves (modified 40 hp heads perhaps..) torque o-plenty after that, but
your high RPM performance will suffer mightily, hehe.
