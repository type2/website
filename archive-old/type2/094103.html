On 8/6/02 9:24 pm, "Pickyourwinda_at_aol_dot_com" <Pickyourwinda_at_aol_dot_com> wrote:

> Also I am replacing my Engine and have decided on a VEGE unit.  I was
> intending on replacing it with the original 1600 AD engine but I am
> considering going for the 2L instead could anyone advise on what else I may
> need to do to upgrade i.e new carbs etc. and any alternatives to VEGE?

Vege (Dutch) have a good reputation.  If you are going for an off the shelf
rebuilt they would be a good choice.
Changing to 2L - this depends a lot on geography.  Where are you?  I know
that outside North America these engines are quite scarce, so you might have
trouble finding a complete set of tinware, flaps, fan housing etc.  These
are no longer available new.  You might need to buy a complete used engine
just for the bits.  There are aftermarket carbs and linkages available so
this might not be such a problem.  The other thing is the transmission - I'm
not sure but you may need a different one.
