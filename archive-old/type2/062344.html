Cat litter.  Unused of course.
Seriously, go to a feed store and get some absorbent material usually 
used for cat litter.  It's a naturally occurring mineral that readily 
absorbs oils and other liquids.  About $5 for 50 lbs, cheap.  You may 
have to use one of those SImple Green/Citrosolv/etc stuff afterwards 
to get the oils buried deep in the concrete, depending on the surface 
finish of the concrete.
