Markus:
I'm not sure a 1641 is a good idea. The increase in displacement is achieved 
by using cylinders with thinner walls, and such cylinders don't last as 
long. My last rebuild, which resided in my 70 Westy, was a 1641, I'd say I 
got about 8,000 miles out of it before something horrible happened. I don't 
exactly know what, as it's still sitting on my bench waiting to be 
dissected. If you want more displacement, you should bore the case to accept 
larger jugs.
