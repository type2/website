 Bear in mind that the speedometer on a Bus (or a Porsche for
that matter) isn't supposed to be "correct".

 Regulations in the UK, and other European countries, I believe
(though I don't know about the USA) state that a vehicle speedo
must read within -0 and +10% of the road speed. It's a given,
therefore, that a speedo that's within manufacturers spec will
read high, probably by about 5% or so depending on how much
they've allowed for pressure variation, tread wear etc.

 The 195s are nominally about 2-3% bigger than the 185s, so 
closer to the "correct" reading.
