> "Why do some VW hoses have a braided covering?"..
> Here it is. When you, or something else,

> of those old "Chinese finger trap" puzzles. How about that? The hoses push on

Interesting theory, but I'm not sure if the braid has that much effect with
thick walled hose.  Hoses tend to stick after a while anyway, so that you

I think the main reason not to use clamps is because it's just not necessary
if the hose is not under pressure (or is under vacuum).
