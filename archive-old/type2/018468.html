Charlie:
Sorry to be so late in joining this discussion. I basically agree with
your ideas. I think the first such support network that I'd heard of was 
the BMWMOC (Motorcycle Owners Club). They have a book called Anonymous
or something and the listees are only listed by city and first name and
what they can offer. There's some merit to the anonymity. It takes away
the possibilities of having the list used for purposes other than the
original intent. (Ever hear of SPAM?)
I'm personally not very shy or reclusive but everyone has their own
limits and I fear that with a web-based public list we'd have a lot less
volunteers.
Still, I'd support most anything you want to do. It sure woulda helped
me a few times!
