>I have a crack at the bottom of my water tank, in the threaded area 
>where the pump motor screws in. Is there a type of glue that can be 
>used to seal this crack effectively?. 

I've had good luck fixing various plastic tanks with a soldering gun.  Just 
heat up the gun and carefully draw a "bead" where the crack is.  If you need 
more plastic to work with, melt and "pull" it from the edges around the crack.
