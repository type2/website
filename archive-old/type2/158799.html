Hi Corbett,

There are other options to swapping tanks. I have been running an  
FI'd 2L engine with a 1973 gas tank for two years now. I put a T in  
its single outlet and have had no issues with it. Fuel rail pressure  
is not any different than when the return line runs directly to a  
bucket on the ground.

A couple years ago Jamie Rivers fabricated a dual-outlet plug that  
fits into the original screw-on 68-74 outlet, that approach has  
worked well for him when he went FI with his 74 westy.
