Subject: Engine urban legends.........

I have a customer/listee that has for years listen to the advice of an old
vw mechanic, and his bevy of assistants........

now the old guy was in business for 35+ years, so can't knock him for
that......and he is now deceased.  One of the mechanics that believes in
pulling thermostats, air flaps, etc............ANYWAY,

is a constant source of income for me.  I am all the time fixing the stuff
these guys half- assed.  It never ceases to amaze me, the pearls of wisdom
that this bunch of fellows that associated with the old-timer come up with.

1.  Run the engine rich..........that way the heads run cooler. Pulled
customers heads, caked with carbon.......seemed proud of the fact he was
running rich.

goofballs have done that it is at least off by 10 degrees......._dot_usually
because there is a big vacuum leak they are masking

motor................not the whole engine and trans at the same time mind

was helping a listee the other day put in his motor and he was insistent
that one of these fellows said this was the only way........he is a real
nice guy, so I bit my tongue and did not ask why the hell he was not there
helping since it was so easy this way?
