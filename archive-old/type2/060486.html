> Is it that difficult to work with new rubber bushings?  Does the inner
> bushing HAVE to be on the spring plate to get the proper angle?

No, and No.

The angle is determined by the splines while the bushings locate the
torsion bar in the vertical plane.  If the whole bar moves up and down
a bit it won't have any effect on the spring plate angle.  I've had no
problem working with new bushings.
