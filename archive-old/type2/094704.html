Dont know about car parts but many years ago i used to pack 50 lb bags 
of sugar back to a baker friend on the us side of the border from 
mexico.  This was back in the sugar embargo days and us sugar 
skyrocketing out of range.  You have to call customs and ask what the 
tariff points are.  some items bring back 6, the next item might be 0.

customer at the flaps i work at the comes in for fancy rims to put on 
his truck.  he makes one delivery/pickup run per week for his brothers 
business.  He sells the rims in mexico, and comes back on basic rims.
