It's interesting to note that if you cannot produce a fully tapered rear
section on a moving body (like an airplane tailcone) it is best to have
the rear end abruptly squared off.  That way, the air itself detaches
cleanly and flows smoother than if the rear had rounded corners like those
of a bus.  You might want to modify your bus model with abrupt corners on
the rear and try it again.
