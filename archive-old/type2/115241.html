 Hi Matthew,

 Having ruled out a vacuum leak, it sounds like your problem must be
 ignition based.  You state here that you did not adjust your timing, but
you
 say that you replaced your points in an earlier message.  If the dwell
 changes by even a few degrees it will have an effect on the timing.  You
can
 change timing without an effect dwell, but you can't change dwell without
an
 effect on timing.  Also, did you replace the condenser when you replaced
the
 points?  The symptoms you describe could possibly be caused by a bad
 condenser.
