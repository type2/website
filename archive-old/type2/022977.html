I don't recommend using old seat belts. Part of the safety of the seat belt 
comes from it's ability to stretch in an accident. As a seat belt ages that 
stretch goes away. This leads to cracked ribs and other nasties in an 
accident. I suggest you consider shelling out the cash for new ones. You 
can get them from JC Whitney:

http://jcwhitney_dot_com/product.jhtml?CATID=107692 or
http://jcwhitney_dot_com/item.jhtml?ITEMID=20489

I just bought 2 new ones for the front of my car. The where easy to 
install, and are nicer than the stock because they retract. I am very happy 
with them.
