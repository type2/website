I have found this strange behavior, and come to the conclustion that this is
the problem.

When you have points on the engine, they produce a slight "drag" on the
distributor pinion shaft. This DRAG also helps return the vacuum advance
plate to the "no advance" position. If your advance mechanism is a little
stiff, when you remove the points (to install the pertronix/compufire), you
also remove this little bit of "return". The result is a vacuum advance
plate that never fully returns to zero. Disassembly, inspection, cleaning,
and lubrication will result in "normal" operation.
