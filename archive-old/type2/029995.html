The gap spec that you use is the one specified for the vehicle, not the
one on the plug box.  0.7mm is the spec in my book.

> OK, let's see what kind are in the motor now.  They are much longer in
> the threaded area, and gapped much bigger than what the book said.  These
> are Bosch as well, W8DC, and gapped to ~0.065.

Do you have a Mexican engine or replacement Mexican heads?  If so, they
are designed to take the long-reach plugs.  Original heads were designed
to take the short reach plugs.  The length of the plug threads should
match the length of the threads in the hole.  

A short plug in a long hole
would shroud the flame front and possibly cause misfires.  A long plug in
a short hole might cause pre-ignition because of the exposed threads
getting hot.  It might even get in the way of the piston!
