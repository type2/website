Excellent move bolting it back together the way it is supposed to be.  The
bus motor mounts (the big rubber ones that attach the engine to the mounting
bar at the rear of the bus) are very soft and were never intended as primary
supports.  With the transmission bolted to the rear (front is front) mount
and the engine secured to the transmission there should be very little
movement allowed.
