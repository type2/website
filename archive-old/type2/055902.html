I vote for the last one, 2 turns in.  But, is this a new engine that you
are putting in and trying to initially set up, or are you having problems
like noisy valves?  If not, then I'd go with the first suggestion and do
nothing.  That's the main advantage of hydraulic lifters:  not having to
adjust them.
