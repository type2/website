This happened with my 72.  Yes, there is a nut welded to the inside of the
frame.  I got the biggest drill bit I had, stuck it in my 1/2 inch drill and
drilled a horizontal hole in the frame right where the nut would be.  I think
I drilled two holes side by side.  With the hole I was able to hose the bolt
and nut with penetrating oil, cram a screwdriver in to hold the nut, and
remove the bumper bolt.
