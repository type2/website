Are we trying to get the lug bolts or nuts off? The lug BOLTS are pressed
into the rear wheel hub through a splined hole from the inside. The NUTS
(even if rounded off) can be loosened with an E-Z OFF socket hammered
on and turned with an impact wrench or breaker bar and pipe extension.
E-Z OFF sockets and sets (GREAT for salvage shopping) are available
from MSC Ind. Supply Co. <www.mscdirect_dot_com>
If you've got a PC, they'll send you a CD-ROM. If you've got a Mac, ask
for the paper catalog. In the catalog, the sockets are on page 1397
(big book).
