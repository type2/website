When the flapper valves are turned off, ALL the heat that goes thru the 
heater boxes vent out the flapper valves....right at the 
transaxle/starter/axle area. TOO much heat!  In late 77, for the 78 and up 
models, VW made different valves. The new style has the exhaust heat exiting 
a hose and then going out pipes to the rear on each side. Go here and look 
at the valves. The ones with a mushroom head dump right there.
