You can install valve seats yourself with a refrigerator and an oven.  Cool
the seats, heat the heads.  Actually I think the procedure is done with
liquid nitrogen on the seats (contraction) during production.  Once the
temperatures equalize the press fit is quite permanent on properly fitted
parts.  If your cylinder heads run under 300 degrees after warm up, your
motor's not running ;-)
