Matthew,

Go back to the carb rebuild part and look for the accelerator pump 
diaphram and the "injection nozzle" that a quick squirt of gas should come 
out of.


Kind of a u shaped brass tube at the top inside ofthe carb.
3 to 1 odds says it is stopped up.  Look down into the carb(with safety of 
course) with engine not running...you should see a strong stream of gas 
come out of the nozzle on the end of this tube.

Beware!!!!  If you take it out of the carb body to test it, you need to 
consider carefully how to get it back in(it's a press fit).  I have 
revived many bugs where this part falls down into the engine and gets 
stuck in a valve.  You *don't* want this to happen.


The other possiblilty is the vacuum diaphram that advances the timing in 
the distributor.  You can test this one by sucking on the hose going to 
this diaphram and watching the plate inside the distributor move.

I am assuming all standard bug parts on this one....no 009 distributors.
