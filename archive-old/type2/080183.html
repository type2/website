At 12:41 AM 11/14/01 -0800, Mike Soultanian wrote:
>I was reading the instructions that came with my new rings, and it says
>to remove the ridge in the cylinder at the top of the ring travel or
>risk messing up the new rings.  How do I do this?
>
>I just ordered a craftsman honing tool to break the glaze on the
>cylinders.  Will this be sufficient to drop that ridge down?
>
You use a ridge reamer. DONT try the honing tool. Check piston fit below
the ridge (slide in the piston and slip a feeler gauge in beside it.)
You'll probably find that there's too much clearance, and that you need new
jugs.
