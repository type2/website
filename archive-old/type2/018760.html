I take a spare adjusting star and screw with me under the bus.  Then I
place it next to the backing plate in the same direction as the star and
adjusting screw I am getting ready to adjust.  I rotate the star and
determine wether I should rotate up or down based on wether the screw
goes in or out.
Makes it a lot easier for me
good luck
