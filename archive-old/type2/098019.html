Think about it.  The reason that it feels cooler to the touch is that it
isn't transmitting as much heat through the metal to you.  A thermos
bottle is cool to the touch as well, but it keeps its contents hot!

heat well, they flex a bit to conform to the gasket surface, and they were
cheap to make.
