Left if you're laying on your back with your feet sticking out the rear
of the bus.  It's on the right (passenger in the USA) side (FIF) below
the 1-2 cylinders. Remove the push rod tube protective cover that goes
between the heater box and the case and that's where it's *supposed* to
be. 

The connector on the flapper rod moves around 45 deg from full closed to
full open.

BTW, latest news is the type IV thermostat is NLA new. If you can find a
new one, BUY it!
