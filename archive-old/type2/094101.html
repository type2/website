I did my tank and replaced my fuel lines a couple of months ago.

IIRC, there are two size, 7.5mm and 10mm.  A small 10mm section goes between
the outlet of the fuel filter to the inlet of the fuel pump.

If you search the archives you'll find a lot of talk about getting 7.5mm
line from the Mercedes dealer for $15/ft.  It's up to you.

5/16" is a little too big.  7mm is a little too small so it should squeeze
on ok. Bus Depot and Bus Boys both sell 7mm Continental FI fuel line.

There are two metal lines underneath: long one from tank to fuel filter;
short one from pressure regulator to tank return I think.  They aren't
available to my knowledge.

I replaced mine with fuel hose as a *temporary* measure until I can find
some new pipe and get it bent to the same shape.  The original pipe I kept
seems to be copper plated steel.
