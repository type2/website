If one cannot obtain replacement screw studs with M9 x 1.25mm and M8 x 
1.25mm threads at either end, fit a HeliCoil threaded insert or TimeSert 
threaded bush, into the cylinder head and use normal M8 x 1.25mm pitch studs 
instead.
