Niall, just pop the cover and loosen the spring a couple of stops. (Loosen
toward the way  the flapper swings). It'll get better for a while, too rich and
it will run bad too and the tail pipe will be sooty. Just take it easy and you
will get it better.
The position fo the richness screw ONLY effects the richness at idle and not
when there is any significant flow. (IOW, idle, not driving richness.)
