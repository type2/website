Flat-faced lug bolts???   No way, ALL MB wheels and wheel bolts are tapered.
All have steel inserts in the lug bolt holes. All except the M class, the
140 body S class, and the new 220 body S class are 12mm bolts. VW and the 3
I mentioned above are 14mm. If you knock out the steel inserts, then the
wheel lug holes will fit the studs....but you loose the tapered seats for
the wheel lug nuts. Do NOT knock out the inserts, drill them larger.
