> > How long did you live out of your bus?

Once four of us for two months travelling in Mexico.  Another time I lived
out of it for two months in Cambridge MA, the Blue Ridge Parkway and
Chapel Hill NC in february.

> > What camping equipment did you use?

My 66 camper had the fold out bed, fold-up table, seat behind drivers seat
facing backwards, and icebox with water spigot, no sink, behind passenger.
I used a single burner propane backpacking stove when alone, coleman white
gas other times.  Because I parked on city streets most of the time, I had
to practice stealth camping.  No lights after dark.  I have always written
and would put my typewriter (1974) on my lap and type away until time to
go to sleep.  I was glad there was no poptop because it was cold anyway.
My ceiling had insulation behind the woodgrain paneling.  

> > What about showers and calls of nature?

I peed in a bottle I'd put in a bag and empty in the gas station down on
the corner, where I'd wash and poop.  Urban camping is quite an
experience, especially when there is 18" of snow on the ground.
