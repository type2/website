I just received Kymco's "end play measuring tool" which consists of a
magnetic base, a dial indicator, and instructions. Made in China, $45
+ shipping.

Quality looks fine so far, but then again, I have no reference and
haven't actually used it. Precision is 0.001", essentially the same as
that of generic feeler blade gauges.

Valve lift can be measured by unscrewing the magnetic bloc and
replacing it with a 1" long 8mm nut. The other end of the nut is then
screwed onto a rocker arm stud. This solves the problem of finding a
good place to put the magnetic bloc on aluminum heads.
