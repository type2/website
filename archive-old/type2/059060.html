Besides the suggestions that have been made regarding checking the float in
the carb, you might want to also take a look at your mechanical fuel pump.
I did a long block swap a few years ago and was shocked to see my oil
level waaaaay over the limit after its first spin around the block.
Turned out that the pump was leaking right down into the case (perhaps the
stress of swaping it was enough to breach the internal seals?).  Changed it
and drained the oil real well, changed the oil again after a couple of
days, and all was peachy.

As to how much damage you did to the motor, look at the bright side: you
got rid of all that messy carbon buildup!
