Matt:
1. You don't need a counterweighted crank to get it to run as good as it
did!
2. I have serious doubts from several clues- rod bearing shavings, bore
the case, cracked valve guide? these are not usual type4 problems. The
alleged well-worn cam , in my opinion, looks worse than it is, and would
probably run quite a ways yet.
3. Someone should run a compression test on this thing. With new rings,
the p&c's couldn't be any worse than before. I think they've got
something screwed up. The compression test should at least piont to
whether it's inside or outside.
4. Any good shop that takes your money should at least be able to figure
out whether to shim down the compression ratio.
I'd almost be tempted to take it to someone else, just to get a more
objective opinion. I think these guys needed to make a boat payment
(thanks Tom and Ray)! Maybe a couple hundrred more and they'll put the
plug wires on in the right order?
Let us know what you find.
