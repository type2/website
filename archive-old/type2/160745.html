Hi George,

I think you have a severely worn or broken camshaft on your hands.  
Have you measured the respective depths of the lifters in the bore?  
If the troublesome lifter is riding lower than the others or does not  
rise as high then the problem is with it or the camshaft.

Here's a source for a magnet that should be able to get a grip on the  
lifter:
http://tinyurl_dot_com/dnrnj

Once you get that lifter out you will know whether you are going to  
have to split the case.
