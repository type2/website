Proper torque is... tight.

You'll have a heck of a time getting a torque wrench on one nut on each of
the intake manifolds.

Question:  Has anyone looked at the use of the metal intake manifold
gaskets versus the paper gaskets?  I've been a bit leery of the metal
gaskets, as they don't seem to be a tolerant of mismatch as the paper
gaskets.  I used paper on the last engines I did, along with a film of
hylomar.
