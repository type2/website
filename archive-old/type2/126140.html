an instant of
going dead as if you turned the switch off for a second and then back on.

by you
at 75 mph 18 inches away you haven't known real fear. The tow truck driver 
wasn't
real happy there either. I never saw a tow truck driver work so fast.
   After that second failure I gave up on Pertronix and went back to points. 
I have
never been stranded because of point failure. If you use Pertronix be sure 
and carry a
back-up set with you.....
