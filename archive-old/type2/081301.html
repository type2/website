Subject: Re: [T2] Brakes!!  Pad, Shoes, Wheel Cylinders

Actually the nut can stay on !!!
Little 10mm set bolts hold the drum on(unlike earlier buses). Dont break
'em.
This job can be done in a couple hours.
Getting the shoes backed off enough to remove the drum is usually the
hardest part(seized adjusters).
Get some liquid wrench on them the night before.
Once the drum is off its fairly self explanatory.
Whenever I do my rear brakes I remove the adjusters and clean them with a
wire wheel and put neverseize on them prior to reinstall. This way they stay
free till next adjusment.
Good luck.
Brian
