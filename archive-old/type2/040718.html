[Hi Dave,] The rusty areas that you are talking about, are quite common rust
spots on type 2s, I had the same problem on mine.  when I got a quote for
mine, they wanted a few hundred pounds!! to sort it out, well I thought sod
that! So I went out and purchased a welder for about £100, and did some
practiceing on some scrap metal first, then I repaired the rust myself by
first cutting out the rust with an angle grinder, then welding in some
patches, to replaice the rust, I then ground down the welds, put some seam
sealer on them then primed, and top coated the job with a couple cans of the
appropriate white, sounds all to easy does'nt it, well the truth is it is!
after a couple of hours practice, you will be producing perfectly
acceptable, and vertually invisible welds, trust me! and it will save you a
fortune.
                                good luck Steve.
