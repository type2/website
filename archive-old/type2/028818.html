I recently became aware of two orders that were entered via our website
around the timeframe of July 24-29 but did not reach us.  It seems our
server "burped" a couple of times during that period (a situation which,
according to our provider, was resolved by the 30th).  While I don't think
there were any widespread problems, I also can't rule out the possibility
that there could be one or two more orders out there that did not actually
reach us.

Here's how to make sure we got your order.

When you first place the order, the website will generate a confirmation
number.  This occurs only after you click on the "submit order" button at
the bottom of the last screen.  This means that your order made it to our
web host's server.  At the same time, as I recall, it may also send you a
"canned" email containing that same confirmation number.

You will then receive a personalized email from Jerry De Baun, confirming
your order.  This will occur either on the same, or at latest the next,
working day from when you place your order.  This means that the order made
it from the server to us.

If you do not receive BOTH the confirmation number AND the email from Jerry
De Baun, then the order did not reach us.

Even under the best of circumstances, every once in a while an order gets
lost in cyberspace.  This is an unavoidable fact, and is precisely why we
incorperate not one but two confirmations so you can be confident that we
got your order.

The two orders I know of may have been the only missing ones.  But if anyone
else placed an order and did not get both a confirmation and an email from
Jerry, please email Jerry at sales_at_busdepot_dot_com just to make sure we got it.
