open, high beams to indicate a problem.

front of me but he's too close to be safe, I flash my bright lights.  
Trucker stays in current lane until I briefly turn my headlights off, 
thus indicating that it is safe to go in front of me.  Trucker makes 
safe lane change to spot in ffront of me.  Trucker signals thanks by 
turning off his tail lights.
