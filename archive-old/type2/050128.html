Conduction is ensured through good metal-to-metal contact.  The purpose of
whatever goop you put on the connection is to exclude air and water, which
will corrode the metals.

Truly conductive greases and sealants are few and far between.  Most are
graphite-based and quite messy.  You really don't want to use them except
in specific applications!
