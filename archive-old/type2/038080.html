Steve is, of course, exactly right.  The VW type 3 and all Porsche 914's had
Bosch d-Jet fuel injection on their type IV motors.  There is a removable
module (held in by 2 screw) where the connector is.  The module is called
the trigger points and it sends a signal to the FI brain telling it when to
open each pair of fuel injectors.  I haven't tried it, but I'm told you can
use the distributor even after removing the FI.
