15 days is plenty.  We made it from NC to CA in three days but we were
pegged at 75mph for the whole trip and it wasn't that comfortable.  We have

near Tahoe can be rough as well.  Especially during this time of year.  Of
course, all of this is assuming that you are willing to hop on an
interstate.
