All sounds well to me, but my experience is with a 1600DP engine.  Concerning 
the run-on: is the idle shutoff solenoid connected?  (If your engine is FI, 
just disregard that question.)

Regarding the idiot light: I, too, have a VDO oil pressure gauge with the 
double sending unit.  At idle, I get the occasional idiot light flicker 
(sometimes even a steady light), but pressure never drops below, say, 7 psi.  
All in all, my engine is pretty healthy AFAIK.  I have considered going back 
to the old, original idiot light sender and just T-ing it with a VDO single 
(pressure only) sender.

CHT temp for me is about 325 after warmitup and with no load on the engine, 
but get me going up a hill, it will go up to 375.  Any higher, and I start to 
worry.  If it is a looooong grade, I will pullitover and rest, if I'm almost 
there I will tuffitout or downshift.
