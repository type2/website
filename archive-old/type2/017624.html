Bob;

I think your problem could be a plugged up PCV valve.  That's the little
device that regulates how much of the crankcase vapor is sucked back
through the intake manifold and then mixed with the incoming air fuel
mixture to be burned, hence reduced crankcase pollution.  They are
usually cheap and wont be a waste of money since it probably needed to
be changed anyhow.
