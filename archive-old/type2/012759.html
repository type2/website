Stan Wilder wrote:

I had exactly the same problem. I experienced a sensation like the engine
was hiccuping.

The solution was very simple, one of the ground wires that is almost
impossible to see under the air intake box was almost completely worn
through. It was just hanging on by a few threads. 

The problen cleared up when I corrected the bad ground condition.

I'm not saying that this is the only condition that will cause your
problem. It's just one of the hardest to detect.

I'd also check the fuel pump preasure.

Hope this helps
