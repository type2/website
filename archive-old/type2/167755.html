Just went to a campout a couple of weekends ago in the bus.._dot_carrying
4 people and gear....two of the people were.....large.....

We got to taling about it as it was *up* a lot of hills and I had
to downshift a lot.  The last 5 miles were done on dirt roads at about 
20mph.

We figured we were at close to 1200-1500 lbs of people/stuff....and that 
was before I remembered that the A/C system alone probably added another
200 lbs.

I drove it to work twice after that and then filled up.

Gas mileage was 18.5mpg...I was thrilled cause I had really pushed it up 
and down the hills going and coming....and yes, I had the petal to the 
metal. Guys, I have *never* babied a VW engine...I have rebuilt waaaay too 
many of them.  I just took a T1 engine apart that I rebuilt in 1996.  I 
had about 56,000 *very* abused hard miles on it and it really did look 
good....except for the cam gear that got loose...1,000 miles after I 
turboed it.

When I checked my leak down, it wasn't bad at all...less than 1% or so, 
but my compression seemed really bad, therefore, my conclusion that the
CR on the heads was really low.
