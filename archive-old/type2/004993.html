You are having a problem with the pipe between the heater box and flapper
valve, correct?

Your info on the year differences is wrong.

72 to 74 and 79 Fed version have ROUND holes in the heater box end and
corresponding ROUND tubes.

75 to 78 have OVAL holes in the heater box end and OVAL pipes.

In order to use the 72 to 74 boxes on your 78, you need the 72-74 ROUND
pipes.
