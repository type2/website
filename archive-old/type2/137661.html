Subject: Re: [T2] Generator pedestal breather tube question

It is a one way valve. It's purpose is to vent blowby out of the
crankcase, but for the most part, the crankcase vent hose will be
sucking to create negative pressure inside the crankcase. The one-way
valve serves to seal that tube and enable vacuum to be created inside
the crankcase instead of sucking outside air into your carburetor
through the vent tube.
