The CA Bureau of Automotive Repair will provide a list
of dealers that sell antique smog parts.  If you cannot
find the part at these places, you could get an exemption
to your smog test.  

*Call the parts locator to get your client number and the
phone numbers of part dealers.

*If you can't find the part at the parts dealers, call the
parts locator back and explain that the part is not found.
They will ask for your client number and send you a letter
stating these facts.

*Take the letter to the smog referee to get an exemption
to the smog test.
