yes, you need to run a new wire.  The way I did it was run a wire for the 
brake lights and left the turn signals alone.   In front near the fuse box I 
unplugged the wire that ran from the MC brake switch from where it attached 
to the turn sig stuff, and ran it into a wire that went to the back.    If 
you get a 14.4 cable for your gauges (if you have some) the wires are all 
prewrapped in a casing
