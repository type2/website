"drwilson":

If your Bus was lowered with one of our adjustable beams, you simply need
to reset the adjusters located in the center of it and then re-align the
suspension. Make sure it is off the ground when you do this.  If it was
lowered by removing some of the torsion leaves (the wrong way), then you
will have to replace the missing parts.
