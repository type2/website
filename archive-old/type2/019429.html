Does your distributor have a centrifugal advance? If so, it sounds like the
weights in the distributor are not returning and are resulting in a high advance.
When you shut off the engine and the distributor stops spinnng altogether, the
springs can finally return the weights and the advance is normal..... until you
rev it again.

It you have a vacuum advance, it's something else!
