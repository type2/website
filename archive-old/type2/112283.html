A drill press for sure--and a good table vise to hold it--and a
appropriate Milwaukee hole saw--they have more teeth per inch than the
average hole saw--

It could be done in a standard bench vise and a good hole saw--but make
sure the drill is a 1/2"-and it as a accessory handle on it--just behind
the chuck--
When it grabs--hold on to your shorts--it'll try and tear your arm off--

Clean the welding area's real good with a grindin wheel or a wire wheel
on a 4" hand grinder--and be sure to apply anti spatter spray at the
welds --it'll help the area clean and welds spatter free--

Have Fun--
