There is a procedure to test the FI temp sensor that is built into
the Air Flow Meter (AFM), by using an ohm meter on pins 27 & 6.  It is

are bad :-( and I have to get a rebuilt one since the AFM temp sensor is not
"user serviceable".

But, I put one in the freezer and tested 26000 ohms, then heated it by putting it
on top of the toaster oven ;-) and got 900 ohms.. which makes me think that the
sensor isn't stuck.

Question:  Is it possible for the temp sensor to be bad and still give me the
range of 900 ohms to 26000 ohms.  Has anyone else tested their AFM Temp sensor
