The 74 dizzy is a dual vacuum unit distributor. It times at 10 degrees after
because it has manifold vacuum
connected to the retard side. This gives you 16-18 degrees of retard timing.
As soon as you open the throttle, manifold vacuum drops close to zero. The
spring in the vacuum unit overcomes
the low vacuum and you get an instant 16-18 degrees of advance. This brings
your timing back to about 7.5
degrees before TDC.
