you will have to remove that final allen bolt
to get the rotor off. either get a new wrench or grind 1/4"
off  yours ( to sharpen the end ). but if the hole is really

with penetrating fluid to break the grip of the threads...
you won't be using that bolt again, so you do what you have to .
