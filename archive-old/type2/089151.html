Try switching to straight 40W or 50W Valvoline Racing Oil. It's too =
thick for cold weather starts, but should help with oil pressure during =
the warmer months.

Also check the electrical connection on the oil pressure sender, which =
is located at about the 10 o'clock position from the distributor as you =
look at it from behind and above the engine. The oil pressure sender =
sometimes cracks and leaks oil and may eventually blow, sending a gusher =
of oil all over the engine. It's cheap to replace, about $9 or so.
