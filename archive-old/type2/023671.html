If you buy new assemblies, try and find the real Hella parts.  I bought some 
new units that I think were made in China and they are real crap.  I used a 
greenlee punch and enlarged the holes the sockets fit into.  I bought some 
USA made generic sockets at Napa and pop riveted them to the plate.  I am no 
longer able to put the back cover on, but my tail lights are now brighter 
than ever, and the bulbs fit in great.
