    Look end on at the connector slot, you should see a small square opening
on one side of the slot. If you push a small jeweler screw driver in that
slot, and pull on the wire it will come out. The connector has a small "tab"
on it, make sure it is bent up enough the lock in place. Push back in to
reinstall, pull to check to see if its locked in.
