Subject: Re:tranny/case part tach?

That little magnetic proximity sensor on the case and the inductive pick-up on the #1 plug wire are some of the pick up points for VW's old computer diagnostic system. It was an early attempt at on-board-diagnostics (OBD). I've always thought that this flywheel sensor would make a dandy pick-up for an electronic ignition system, since even with a compu-fire (or something) the timing wanders too much for my liking. I think because crankshaft end-play affects timing at the dist.
