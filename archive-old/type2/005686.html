I tried to track down a source of turnkey [completely VW factory built] FI
engines last December, without success.

All the turnkey engines I found, available in the USA, were new VW Mexico
longblocks that were imported to the States and built up on this side of the
line by the distributor.  I was never able to get a straight answer as to
the source of the added parts, but I suspect many [if not all] were
aftermarket bits and pieces.  And they were all carbureted.

They build and/or install 1600 FI engines in new [as opposed to "New"]
Beetles at the VW Mexico plant.  These engines incorporate the very latest
case design and alloy, the latest FI design, and all the other type 1 engine
upgrades and refinements.  And they supposedly produce something like 67HP
:-)

A couple of years back a List member in the UK bought a new Brazilian Type 2
with this engine, and his reports of the bus's power and performance really
got my attention.

Somewhere/somehow there must be a way to [legally] obtain one of these
engines.  If anyone on the List knows of a source I hope they post the
necessary information.....
