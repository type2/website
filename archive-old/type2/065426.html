My experience took months to unfold the first time. (I had two go bad at different times) At 
first it occured once or twice a week for a short period of time. After a few months it was 
doing this approximately 25% of the time. When I pulled it apart I figured it probably could 
have continued for some time before a failure might have occured. (maybee another 6 months or 
more) I didn't experience an actual failure so I can't comment on this. My suspicion is that if 
the joint is making the noise it needs to be replaced. I believe the ball bearings and the 
races they travel in have worn troughs and the rapping occurs when the ball bearings rotate 
into a specific orientation with respect to the races. I suspect the noise results when the 
bearings bind and then release. Sort of like when the planets are in a bad alignment. Its not a 
good thing!

This is my rationale as to why the problem comes and goes. Everything has to be just so for it 
to happen. anyway... this is my theory.
