Well, I have to disagree with you about the race issue.  In this race, speed would be the last factor to consider.   Busses, regardless of HP, are so similar in terms of maintainable speeds that it would be negligible compared to navigation, planning, maintenance, etc.  Everyone would not choose the same route.  And even the map sources disagree on which is the shortest.  And the shortest may very well take the most time.  There are many factors one would have to consider.  Speed wouldn't be much of one.  And the carabus idea would take the competitive fun out of it.  JMHO...KR
