     PROD. DATES	TRANS			CHASSIS NUMBER        ENGINE   ENGINE CODE

The first two numbers in the vin are the model
21-delivery
22-Micro
23-Kombi
23-Campmobile
26-Pickup

The third number is the last year in the date
1974-4
1968-1969 have only three then three digits in the remainder so
219 000 000 is 1969
219 0000 000 is 1979

FYI allot of things changed in mid 1974 at chassis # 214 2164 059 so be
careful when buying parts make sure the you have your chassis # handy. I
hope this helps.
