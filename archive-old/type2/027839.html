Russell I haven't seen an expert reply yet.  i had the same problem.  I 
suspect your steering wheel spokes no longer point left-right.  I don't know 
the technical term but there is a section of the lower ring of the wheel 
that extends into the switch and it contacts the cancel cam.  If the wheel 
is mis aligned it prevents the switch from staying on.  You can remove and 
realign your steering wheel (what I did) or rotate your switch parallel with 
the spokes.  It might also suggest that your alignment up front is off.
