Subject: Fw: [T2] Timing marks on 69

The deepest notch should be to the left, it is TDC. You line it up with the
case halves. Set your timing at idle to one of the ones to the right, it
should be about 7 before.  Now measure 1 3/4" around the pulley from the
deep TDC mark, and scratch a mark on the pulley. Mark it with whiteout. Set
the timing at full advance(about 3000rpm)  it should hit that mark, 28-30
BTDC. Watch it advance, it should be smooth, not too jerky.
