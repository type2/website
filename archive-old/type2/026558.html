Anyone with a 20 year old bus should take a gander at their ball joints from
time to time.  The rubber boots get split and the grease goes away.  Then
you've got a ball-and-socket joint (put your fist in the palm of your other
hand and move it around - that's what a ball joint looks and acts like)
rubbing around with no lubrication.  Symptoms of badly worn joints include
loud clunks when turning into parking lots and not-enough-then-too-much
turning when navigating curvy roads.

>extract them.
