Bill,

If you look under engine then cooling air control you'll see the
illustration you are looking for. The thermostat is under the left lower
heat channel. Quick check, if the thermostat is puffy when cold then its
bad. These are sealed and when they heat up they expand (some gas inside,
air probably good question). When the engine heats up the thermostat gets
hot and opens then the cable lets the flaps open to allow air to blow via
the fan.
