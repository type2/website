Here are two methods I use to help save the endangered spotted (or
herbaceous) bandwidth.
When tempted to respond to a post, I first consider if the reply is of
interest to the general list, if not, I will p-mail the poster. Second, I
sort my messages by subject, and read the responses already posted. If
somebody else has covered the material, there is no need to "me too". These
are simple steps, and perhaps obvious, but they can cut the volume
substantially.
