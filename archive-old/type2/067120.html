During my misspent youth I worked summers painting street lines, putting up
signs, digging dirt, etc. for the local town. The head line painter guy and
I drove around in an old police dept. Greenbriar van. It was painted black
and white on top, said "Longport Police Dept." on the side, and even had
working siren and police lights. The engine was in a hatch in the floor in
the middle of the engine compartment. It must have been from the early
60's, and was garage kept. I think it would be quite a collector's piece
nowadays, but, alas, its current whereabouts are unknown :( (they traded it
in for a Ford pickup). Did VW make a similar police truck to go with the
fire truck/ambulance/etc.?

Also, a friend of mine at work is from mainland China. I gave him a ride in
my '66 the other day and he said that, in China, a very similar van was
made, but he didn't have any other details. He said it wasn't as ornate as
a VW on the inside (he was very impressed that my bus has an ashtray). :)
