Sharon:

Some of the most common causes of overheating are:

* Missing or damaged cooling tin
* Missing or damaged heater hoses
* Missing seals (spark plug, or engine surround seals)
* Incorrect timing
* Lean jetting
* Something stuck in the fan
* Compression too high
* Slipping fan belt
* Thermostat shutters not fully opening
* Leaking intake manifold boots
