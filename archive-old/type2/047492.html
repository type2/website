I had a couple of nylon netting gear hammocks left over from our old boat.
Got them at West Marine for a few dollars each.

I needed some more storage up front for stuff while we slept in the back of
our camper.

I made some hooks out of steel rod so the gear hammocks can be hung from the
Westy child's cot hooks, and I can hang 1 or 2 hammocks as needed.  This
arrangement works very well, and stores in a compact package when not in
use.
