I don't know about the tranny fluid in the engine.. I'm sure we'll hear
from the other list members about that one..

in regards to the relay, it's the AFM (airflow meter) that you put a
stick or something in to force the flap open.  Take off the air filter,
and then stick a screwdriver or something long into the airflow meter
and push the flap open while the iginition is on.  the fuel pump should
start up...  The reason for this setup is to ensure that when the engine
is off (no airflow), the pump stops.. and vice versa..
