Unfortunately, if you have a type 1 "bug" case, there isn't any metal
to drill and tap.  The bosses weren't cast onto the case.  That's why
you have the adapter.  The only way to get back to stock configuration
is to replace the case with a universal type 1 case.

I've known of people who have done OK with the adapter, but I'm
awfully leery of hanging the entire weight of the engine from the oil
pump studs.  That mounting location supports half of the weight of the
engine/transmission, and also takes the reaction torque loads of the
rear axle. A pretty tall task for those little studs.
