ramps when I refilled the system and it went more
smoothly than ever before. I just opened the bleeder
at the throttle body and the bleeder at the heater and
it pretty much just filled. I had to add about a half
gallon later, so I had at least a half-gallon of air
pockets and expect there are still some. -BaH
