this is probably a 71 bus/late bug/autostick distributor.  rebuilt with a
single vac can instead of dual vac for ease of use, and because dual vac
cans are $50 and single vac $15.  This is the redheaded stepchild of
swapmeets and VW shops, no-one wants the dual vac dizzies.  I have a few,
all purchased for $5 or less.  2 good ones.  Learn how to identify a good
dizzy, and save some money. (done by feel, i cant explain have a good VW
buddy who knows show you... or wobble the shaft on a new distributor up
and down and side to side, see how that feels, look for a used one as
close to that as possible...)

> effect on either dist.  It WON'T cure the hesitation problem and fuel economy
