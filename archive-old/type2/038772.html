It would be pretty easy to make one from a old cooler adapter.  Plug one
side and epoxy a valve stem into the other side (or drill and tap if you
want to be elegant about it).  Bolt it onto the prospective oil cooler
with the correct seals, then pressurize it with air.  If the air stays in,
the cooler is good.
