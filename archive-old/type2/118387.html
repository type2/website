Subject: Re: [T2] 82 Vanagon Heat Exchanger/Exhaust hangers?

There are supports for the rear exhaust at both the left and right sides.
These supports bolt on to the cooling fan shroud and then attach to the
exhaust.  I believe you can see the right-side piece in the diagrams for the
cooling system.  The left-side piece varies depending on whether the exhaust
is Federal or California; didn't see this piece in my quick look at the
usual sources.
