Subject: Re: CB antenna on a westy roof

I had a similar problem years ago.

I'm a Ham Radio Operator, and wanted to mount an antenna on a westy.
I cut a hole about four inches in diameter, in  the fiberglass in the middle
of the top just above the drivers seats. The a mag mount goes through the
hole and mounts on the metal roof, and it will work fine, and the cable can
be routed into the cab from there.
