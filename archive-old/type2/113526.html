Absolutely, by inspection, not time or mileage. Replace only when wear or 
defects are observed, not before, not after. Typical replacement occurs 
upon erosion of the contacts beyond 0.2mm from new. Same with the rotor and 
plugs.

Everywhere it gives time or mileage, insert "by inspection".
