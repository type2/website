I'll get the old heat shield out of the trash and take some pics of it
with my trusty Polaroid that I just remembered I have. Will post ASAP --
when I scan the pics at work tomorrow.

To describe the piece, it's a flat piece ~2 to 2-1/2 long with two
brackets sticking up off it about 3", each with a small tab/bend at the
ends. The bend has bolt holes where it attaches onto the tin. The flat
piece is straight on the rear edge and has a curved bit taken out of it
to clear the fan shroud, and a smaller notch for the oil filler tube.

BTW, I took a hammer to the muffler to clear for the oil filler tube and
it works great and is very easy. I did a lot more hammering than I
really needed to do, I guess, because now I've got about 1/4"-1/2" inch
of clearance all the way around. The R&R of the muffler was easier the
first time, though! The VW gods got their share of my knuckle this time.
