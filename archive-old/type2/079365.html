lights flash no matter what direction signal is selected,,

flasher relay , but there is 2 dash lamps,, both wired to flash at the same time

then maybe you have a bad connection to that lamp... or a burnt out lamp,, or a
bad ground...

prove that you have 2 working lamps,, then go looking for the bad connection
