It is possible to fit a VW Beetle twin-port engine into a 1968-79 VW Type 2, 
but you will need a special rear engine mount adapter.

One of these was featured on page 20, of my J.C. Whitney, Summer 1996 VW 
Catalogue (Catalogue No. 50J), listed as part No. 37AR8020X, priced at 
$11.99. J.C. Whitney, may be contacted, as follows :-
