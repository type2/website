Vacuum depends on your altitude.  17-18 MAY be possible at sea level.  But 14-15 is closer to normal.

Vacuum also depends on timing.  A 79 Fed should be timed at 7.5 before. The more retarded it is, the lower the
vacuum. More advanced = more vacuum.
