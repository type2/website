At 04:01 PM 8/3/96 -0400, you wrote:
>How heck do I remove speedometer cable from the dashboard? I looked at 
>the left front wheel, where the speedometer cable attached to the axel. I 
>tried to removed, I cant. Is there another solution for it? 

Undo the clip holding the cable to the dust cap.  The pull the cable out
from the _inside_ of the spindle assembly.  Then disconnect it from the back
of the speedometer, and pull up thru the dash tray.

Installation is the reverse of these steps :)
