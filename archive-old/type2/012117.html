Stuff to try:

1. Vise grips and a hammer - a lousy method, but
occasionally necessary

2. Cut a slot in the top of the bolt, and try using a
big screwdriver.

3. Take your chances with a torch and paraffin wax,
and then use vise grips and a hammer. 

4. Use a die grinder to grind the heads off the bolts,
and worry about how you're going to get the rest of
the bolt out later.

5. Panic. 

-John
