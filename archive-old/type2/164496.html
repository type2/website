Oddly enough, the '68-'69 three sensor-system does work.

The light going on when the pedal is depressed means that one of the
circuits in master cylinder is bad. 

Soft brakes usually means the rear brakes aren't adjusted or one of the
wheel cylinders is leaking. BUT, coupled with a working warning system, it
points to the master cylinder.

If you want to double check the master cylinder is not bad, you can also try
new switches if one of them is not functioning correctly. Also pulling the
switches and cleaning them out is a good idea since they can gunk up with
rust and resin.
