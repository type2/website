The best advice I got for this, which won't help you now but will the next
time your changing plugs, is to tape your socket to your wrench. This is the
only reason that I didn't lose my socket down there. Of course, this didn't
stop me from losing half the plastic covering on the old plug down there,
but that's another story.....
