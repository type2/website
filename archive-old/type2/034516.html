i will not disagree that a full flow is better.  just remember ALL vw
bugs coming out of mexico (i believe as of 1998) have the full flow
pump/filter combo on them!! (they also all come with electronic ign.,
hyd. lifters, and i think most are fuel injected). all these new
improvements are on there for a reason.  i would take a pump/filter
combo than NO filter at all, especially since i can put one of these
on in an hour, rather than pull an engine and COMPLETELY tear-down to
put the fittings in.  the cost is about $60, compared to about $150+
to get the case tapped, good fittings, the proper hoses, etc., plus
tear-down time.

yes you can get a crappy aftermarket suppliers.  we all know who most
of them are.  i love swivel valve adjusters, but NEVER made by
bugpack.  look for a oem vw supplier of the filter/pump combo and you
will be fine.
