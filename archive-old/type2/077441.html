NOW one small problem I had with mine and it wasn't the Bus Depots
fault. but the flange for the tailpipe was misaligned and while it can be
run without the tailpipe it is much better to have it.  Had I bolted the
tailpiece onto the muffler in the orientation that came it would have
pointed up at a 45degree angle towards the rear. but it wouldn't clear the
apron or the bumper that way.... I just cut it about an inch in from the
flange on the tailpipe, realigned it and rewelded it back up...works fine
but you might want to check on this rather than wait til your almost
finished and suddenly discover the tailpiece will not go on...like I did.

not sure if this is a common problem or I just happen to "luck out" and
get the only one that didn't fit.
