Subject: 009 DISTRIBUTOR ADVANCE

The following information was gleaned from the June '92 issue of DB & HVW's.

Approximate total advance German 009 at approximately 2250 RPM is 24 degrees

Approximate total advance Brazilian 009 at approximately 2600 RPM is 21 degrees

German 009's are marked "GERMANY" on the bottom of the distributor. Brazilians
are much more common than German.
