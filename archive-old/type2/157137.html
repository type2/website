Subject: [T2] How to identify my engine size?

The engine size is encoded in the Engine Serial
Number.  The first letter or letters indicate which
engine series.  Use this hyperlink below:
   
http://www.customspeedparts_dot_de/content/en/technic/motorenuebersicht.php
   
My 78 T2 for instance is a `GE` series motor.  2.0
Liter, Fuel Injected, Federal, Hydraulic Valves, and
so on.
