last 14 years. I guess that puts me on the outside of this group looking in.

was that the '77 had a solid factory exhaust. So if there are any of you looking
for a good '76 2.0l engine we should talk.

I will be taking a close look at the engine over the weekend. I know that the
starter, alt, injectors, intake manifold, and other parts will go with it.

I will post a more formal list of what is there soon. Any thoughts on what an
