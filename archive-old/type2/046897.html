Jack, the 34Pict3 will work.  however, you must jet it!  If you try and run
it stock  the 1776 will run too lean.  as in destroy your new engine.

I ran a 34PICT3 [jetted] on a 1776 in a 69 bus that was used to drive up and
down the Baja California Peninsula [delivering supplies for my sons, surf
shop] to San Jose Del Cabo Mexico, from San Diego Cal, [1,100 miles each
way] under  EXTREME driving conditions. it's hard to believe, but a 55
gallon drum of resin, just fits standing upright through the side slider.
had 0 problems with engine performance!  When the bus was sold about 5 years
ago had over 150,000 miles on the engine and it was still running strong....
