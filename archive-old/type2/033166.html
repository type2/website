Hi Amelia,
The two problems could well be related, as the things you describe as not 
working are all ignition controlled.

After disconnecting the battery (VERY IMPORTANT) undo the screws that hold 
the steering column onto the flat part that goes back under the dashboard, 
swing the column to the side and examine the wires going to the ignition 
switch.

VW placed a metal tag on the end of the wire which is inserted into a metal 
ring on the end onf the ignition switch. The ring is then squashed to make 
the connection, and its possible that one of these connections has come 
loose. If so just resquash the metal ring - which should be possible in situ 
- otherwise you will need to remove the electrical end of the ignition switch 
and solder the connection.

On my old 73 I once had a complete electrical failure of all ignition 
controlled electric's due to this happening.
