Yup Chip,

Sounds like the heat exchanger.  Two possibilities:

1) Take off the offender...clean the heck out of it, then repair
   any leaks with muffler repair stuff (I'd recommend the type
   that uses a fiberglass 'wrap'). All flaps have it.

2) Check out some graveyards, and yank a used one. 

In any case, you probably should clean them BOTH up and check the
flaps in each one. Bearing grease, etc.  Then check the cables so
they open properly.  Then 'ya got good heat ! A precaution would be
to get one of those cards (mentioned in previous posts) that measure
HC and CO in the interior.  Available from airplane supply places.
(They are cheap! 3-7 dollars)

Hope this helps,
