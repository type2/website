in NC. One day while covering the ignition system in auto tech they

that this student had never paid attention before, but was for some
reason, very interested in how the ignition system worked. 

well the next day he wasn't in class. At the end of the day my advisor
was called in to see the boss (principle). Earlier that day he

students was pulled over 2 or 3 counties away driving a stolen VW
Beetle.

attention that day so he could hot wire the thing. It apparently took
quite a while for my advisor to reason with the principle about the

how an ignition system works is just enough to hot wire a car.
