The best advice I can give in this area is to go look at a lot of camper
Buses, Westys, Sundials, Safares,Dorms, and any others you can find. Compare
the interiors of all of these. Look at different years, talk to the owners
and see what they like, and do not like about there camper. If you find one
that is what you want, take a roll of picture, and some measurments,
drawings and whatever to take back with you. Then, draw your own plans. Many
of the common things, sinks, door handles,ice boxes, stoves, can be had at a
local RV supplier. You will want to incorporate them into the design as
needed. One mans plans will be what he likes, but may not work for you and
your passengers. I know that if there are listees in your area, they would
love to show off their campers, look them up and ask around. You may also
want to look at a few non-VW based camper to get ideas. 
  After all this, you may decide to just go buy a used parts camper and swap
the interiors, but at least then you will know what you want.
