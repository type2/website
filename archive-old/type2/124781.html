Your 71 may or may not have a air duct on them. Most passenger buses did. Westies do not.

68 and 69 did not have the ventilation grille in the lower rear corner. I believe this started in 70, maybe
71.

76 and up Westies have three round screw holes in the door panel for the arm rest. Buses have two odd shaped
holes for the pull strap.

Overall, the outside shape is all the same.
