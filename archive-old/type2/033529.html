>From your description, it seems likely that your motor has a thrown rod.
You might be lucky and just have the pistons rings rusted to the cylinders,
but I'd put my money on a thrown rod.  

First thing I'd do is remove all 4 spark plugs, put the bus in neutral, and
try to turn the motor by hand (wrench on the alternator nut should do it).
If it won't turn over, you might as well get ready to remove the engine for
a rebuild.  Sorry to be the harbinger of doom, but that's the way I see it.
