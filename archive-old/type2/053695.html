 this is the front engine cover plate that fits over the front of the engine
( i call it the fire wall tin) it should have two or three little clips,
that clip, over the engine case just above the flywheel.  the vent tin that
attaches to the back of the oil cooler goes through that rectangular hole.
it should also have raised  edges on the shoulders of it for a tight fit
against the engine seal.  If yours dosen't have the raised edges, you have
the one that is for a bug engine.
> Is this indeed the rear cover plate? (rear means back

> plate on a bus?
no, and yes, -- the rear cover plate is the one that has the round holes for
the fresh air hoses,  and the cut outs for the heat risers with two thredded
holes on each side of the heat riser heat shields to screw into. You do have
the two little heat riser heat shields?don't you?
