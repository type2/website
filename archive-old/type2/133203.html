Guys,

78 Champagne edition with intermittent wipers.

Just went thru this last Saturday.  Turns out that my wipers just got 
slower and slower and then just stopped.  When i restored the bus, I spent 
a lot of time on the wipers, relubing, checking the motor and contacts and 
testing...

When the wipers got really slow, the intermittent relay started buzzing as 
well....

Turns out, all I had to do was pull the connector off of the motor and 
then put it back on...worked like a champ then.  Next time I am up under 
the dash, I will clean the contacts all again.

Important point...when you have the switch unplugged from the harness, and 
turn the key on, my wipers start to run...


The switch(which I had out as well) does look like a perfect candidate for 
a small micro switch that would run an electric washer pump.  it is on my 
list for the near future as I alread have a universal pump from the local 
FLAPS that will work on it.

Richard, my original switch look like yours and does run the wipers for 
about 4 wipes...maybe part of the intermittent wiper stuff that came std 
on the Champagne edition?

BTW!  Does any one have, or can the give me a reference to a wiring 
diagram that shows the intermittent wiper relay?  I *have* bentley, but 
could not find the relay info, even in the 79 wiring section...did I miss 
it?
