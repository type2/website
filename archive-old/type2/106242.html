Back in 92 I had been plagued with wiring problems on my 71 westy, my favorite
camper at the time, and it really burned me not finding it. After a month a
friend of mine got up under the dash while the wipers were going and found the
wiper arm hitting against the dash wire bundle. At first look there wasn't any
real problem, but after hitting it with light at different angles we found a
wire with insulation scraped off and the wiper arm hitting that area grounding
out that circuit. I repaired the wire and a number of others and tied the
bundle up out of the way. I learned long ago that somewhere in small print
there is a license from VW to modify the wiring harness in any fashion the
owner wants. I've only owned 9 dubs of differing types since 69, but everyone
of them had thrashed wire harnesses. Most I could live with but some had to
have extensive repair.
