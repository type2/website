You don't say where you are, but here in California the smog shops are 
supposed to check for proper functioning of the EGR valve.  No proper 
function = no pass.  The EGR is supposed to introduce inert gas (exhaust) 
into the engine, cooling it and reducing NOx emissions, but they don't 
test for NOx right now, only proper EGR function.  Yes, I would think it 
would be illegal to alter the emission stuff.

California.  Can't say about your locality.
