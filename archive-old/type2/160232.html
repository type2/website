Mine will light up on occasion too, I just reach over and tap on the  
brake handle and it will go out.  There is a little switch under the  
dash that the brake will press against when it is disengaged.  If you  
have adjusted your brakes recently, then check to see that the lever  
is going all the way back and pressing against the switch. Adjusting  
the cable will pull it back into the right position.
