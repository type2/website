First of all, I'd like to say thanks to everyone on the list who responded
with a ton of helpfull suggestions.

Second, I'm happy to report that the broken off stud has been removed, the
exhaust is back on, the bus is back up and running just as well as before!
Success!!!

And finally, to tell you what finally worked.   I was finally able to remove
the stud (thanks to a hepfull hint from an older gentleman that use to be a
race-car mechanic) by putting on a nut and tightening it up the stud.  Then,
take a cheap socket just slightly smaller than the stud, heat the stud up
with a torch, then hammer the socket onto the stud.  Re-heat and pound it
again.  Then, slowly turn the stud out.  One of the studs came out with just
the socket.  The socket broke off on the second one, but had burred up the
threads so badly that when I attempted to take the nut off it could no
longer pass over the burred threads and took the stud out.    Breathe a
heavy sigh of relief.

Seriously, thanks to everyone.  I love this list.
