> What is the Mercedes-Benz part no on that wheel nut?

Before 72 will not fit. Older as in 107, 108, 109, 110, 111, 113, 114, 115
body wheel bearing nuts will not fit. Even though 107's were made to 89,
they will not fit.
These nuts are right hand thread only, they will not work on the left side
of a split or bay window....only both sides of a Vanagon.
The MB part # is 116 334 03 72. They are not cheap from the dealer, they
have a suggested list price of $19 each and do not include the 5mm headed
allen bolt. AFAIK, they are only available from the Mercedes dealer.
