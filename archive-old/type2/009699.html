For those of you in the Wisconsin/Illinois area:

Captial City VW Club based in Madison WI is presenting 
their 2nd annual VW Picnic and Car Show on Sunday, June 
6th beginning at 8 AM and concluding at 4 PM.  This 
year's location is at Sparby's Christmas Barn, 2 miles 
east of Waunakee on Hwy 19 to the north of Madison, or 
take I-90 west from Madison to exit 131 (Hwy 19), then 
go west for 3 miles.  Trophies will be awarded for the 
first 6 Participant's Choice, Longest Distance, Daily 
Driver, Best Paint, Best Interior, Best Engine, and Must 
De-Rust.  There is an award for the club with the most 
entries (other than Capital City).  There will even be a 
piston throwing contest!

Registration closes at 11 AM and the awards will be 
handed out at 3 PM.  $6 entry fee, spectators are 
admitted FREE..  Food is available on the grounds or 
bring a picnic basket.

For more info see: 
http://home.att_dot_net/~capitalcity/page5.html or contact 

Kathi Staubli at 608-246-0407

Hope to see you there!
