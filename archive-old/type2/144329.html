On a 79, you need to specify if you have a Calif
version or a Fed version. 2 totally different exhaust
systems. Lower tin is totally different between the 2.

79 Cal has electronic ignition, no resister pack on
the left side of the gas tank panel, an O2 sensor, and
a cat that lays front to rear between the left heater
box and head.
http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/exhaustcomp1979-cal.html

79 Fed looks like a 78 on top, but has 72-74 heater
boxes with square ports.
http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/exhaustcomp1979-fed.htm

Go here and look at R and W. Click on each and you get
the year differences.
http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/aircoolsystem.html#tin

R is:
R. 021-119-355BU Warm Air Duct, Lower Left, Used 1972
to 1974, 1979 to 1983 Federal Only 

R. 039-119-355AU Warm Air Duct, Lower Left, Used 1979
California Only  

W is a problem for 79, either version:
W. 021-119-356CU Warm Air Duct, Lower Right, Used 1972
to 1974, 1980 to 1983 All 

W. 021-119-356EU Warm Air Duct, Lower Right, Used 1975
to 1978 All
