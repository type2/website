If your parts are clean and no sealer is applied you
will not have too much of a problem here. I always use
something to snug down the cylinder but never as snug
as assy torque. The point is that a few 0.001" error
(bigger) in deck ht will not make any difference IF
you are using off the shelf shims of the next higher
size. If calculations say you need 0.051" shim and you
use a stock one that is say 0.062" (not really sure
that this is stock?) you will have a very slightly
lower then planned CR but thats OK! Now if you are
going to custom grind shims to get that perfect 7.3 CR
thats another story.
