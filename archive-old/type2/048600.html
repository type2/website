Don,  I had never done it before, but I recovered the front buckets on my
'70.  You will need the seat covers, complete, seat pads (I used foam, but
the purists like the original horsehair), and a set of hog rings and pliers.
Do one seat at a time so you don't forget how it's supposed to fit.  It
takes patience and time, but it's really not that hard.
