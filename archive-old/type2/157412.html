On Thu, 26 Oct 2006, Douglas Sornberger wrote:

> Is it feasible to install a bigger ring & pinion in the stock 71 tranny?
> Can it be done w/ out removing the tranny or is that a stupid idea?

You can't do anything to the tranny without pulling it, except check the
fluid!

The easiest route is to just replace the transmission with one from a
later model bus.  However, keep in mind that the '71 transmission was made
to work with the small engine and a taller gearing won't give you a faster
bus!
