Hi Volks,

The high-riding clutch pedal in the westfakia degraded this week, with 
the engine suddenly developing a habit of outrunning the transmission 
in second and third gear. Not good, esp. with a 1000-mile vacation 
looming in 8 days.

So this afternoon I liberated a 215mm clutch disk from the spare parts

back to my brothers garage and raided the stash once again for a 215mm 
flywheel. No big deal.

210mm flywheel are not going to fit into the 215mm flywheel.
