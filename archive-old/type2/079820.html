I just rebuilt a TypeIV 2000cc and I had the same oil pressure problem of
*Excessive*. Found a bur on my plunger, checked it with a micrometer,
filed it down and reinstalled it. Oil pressure now at 70 PSI at 3300

at operating oil temps around 210-220.
