Scott,

I think your wiper arms have loosened up from the shafts.  Pop off the black
caps on the arms and tighten the nuts down.  If neither the arms OR shafts
move then the linkage has most likely fallen apart. I'm betting on the loose
nuts.  Happens all the time.  The owners manual should be obtainable from your
local VW dealer for less than $20.00.
