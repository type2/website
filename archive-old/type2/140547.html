The switch up front is the ground circuit for the relay. No power there.

If the fuse is melting, then the blower motor is drawing too many amps.
You could put an ammeter in instead of the fuse to check the draw. But that would be a waste of time. You need
another blower fan.

Can't tell you much about the bilge fans. The stock system works fine for me.

There are 2 versions of the heater fan, early and late. Early early ones are metal housings. Early ones have
the fan wires coming out as a harness on the side. Late ones, the 2 wire harness plugs into the end of the
motor.

I have a few good fans here. Do you need early or late?
