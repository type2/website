As points age, the amount of gap they need to get tp 50 degrees dwell
becomes less and less. A new set requires .016" gap; I always change them
when the gap becomes less than .010".

Of course, you could always send The Bus Depot or aircooled_dot_net a few
dollars and come away with a points eliminator, which will solve this
problem for good.
