Had as similar problem with my 79. I found that the eratic idle was a worn 
distributer...replaced the dizzy...and that ceased. Then within the same 
month the van would start ok, but as soon as I got up to a certain speed it 
would shut down and then it would restart, until I got to 30mph, then the 
same thing would happen.  Sooooo, I took off the carb and cleaned it 
out...didnt do it!!!, then I replaced the fuel lines and filter...nope!!!  
Finally, after running out of options, I found that the connections to the 
coil were loose on the + side...duh!! they were still attached, but the 
vibrations at 30mph would disconnect them.  Hope your problem is that simple.
