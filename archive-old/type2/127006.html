> Replacing the engine, had a thought of moving the odometer to read 00000 , is

You can do it.  I did it to make a new odometer match the old odometer.
Basically, you have to remove the shaft that holds the number rotors,
reset the number rotors to the number desired, and reassemble.  Not too
hard, but not too easy either.
