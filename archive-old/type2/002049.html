I ran dual Kadrons on my stock 1600DP prior to restoring my vehicle. They ran
great right out of the box. They hesitated a little when shifting but I hadn't

mileage. I now have dual ICT's but my good friend Rob Dahlgren is running them
now on his 69 bus with extractor exhaust and is tickled to death with them. Look
in the archives for some of his recent posts on setup and performance.
