Hi Tom and all
My 76 fuel injected Kombi has been fitted at some stage with a 1700 motor
but the fuel injection has been retained.Goes well now that an alternator
problem(which affected the fuel Injection)has been fixed.
So I guess if your 1700 is in good shape you could find the FI system and
Fuel Pump and all associated relays and harnesses and use a FI 2000 setup.
If the engine is on the way out you may get a better deal buying a used 2000
engine complete with FI and rebuilding it ready to install.
