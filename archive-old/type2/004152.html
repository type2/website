Greeting listees,  Three years ago I tried using a Ford starter solenoid for
a battery isolation circuit switch,  it didn't last!  I called a friend who
worked at an RV center and he had a battery isolation relay/switch for $12,
WOW what a bargain.  I hooked a cable to both large posts on each side of it
and connected these to my two battery positive posts.  Then i mounted the
thing to metal (it must be grounded to work).  The small terminal I hooked
up to my ignition switch.  When you are camping (ignition switch is off) the
contacts are open, and you are running your camper off of the second battery
only, and when you are starting or running the engine, then they are
connected!  Works great!    Jeff Adams,  71 Riviera Penthouse
