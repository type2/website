Krupps,
        The '68 to '71's have a brace that runs accross the back of the
engine that supports both the engine and tranny whereas the early buses and
all beetles have a brace on the tranny (T1's).  In other words, a T2 engine
will fit on a T1 b/c all that is needed is the four main bolts to hold the
engine to the tranny.  A T1 engine wont fit in a T2 b/c there are no bolt
holes for the engine brace on both sides of the oil pump cover.  There is
an aftermarket adapter that bolts to the oil pump cover to convert a T1 to
fit in a T2.  I have heard opinions go both ways, though.  Maybe someone
else on the list could share their experience.
