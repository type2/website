You have to weld in tabs at the back end of the assembly to make the roof lift
up when fully closed.  You'll probably need to look at a sunroof bus to get
the right shape.  You also need to install drain tubes.  If you don't the rain
will leak into you bus, more like rain in your bus, causing rusting from the
inside out.
