On Wed, Dec 11, 2002 at 01:31:16PM -0500, Jay Ollom wrote:
> Unfortunately, I have a leaky pushrod tube that I would love to 
> clear up.  Do they make adjustable pushrod tubes for the bus, like they do 

As far as I know, no. But you don't need them :). The t4 style engine
pushrod tubes can be removed with the heads in place. I would just
order a set of the viton rod seals & replace them all. It's very easy
& mostly painless. Basically the steps are this:

1. Remove the valve covers.
2. unbolt the rockers & remove the pushrods.
3. the tubes just slide back out of the head.
4. replace the tubes w/ new seals on.
5. replace pushrods.
6. replace rockers & adjust valves.
7. replace valve covers.
8. enjoy adding oil less often :)
