Hi Billy,

The spring sits around the shaft of the pin between the head of the pin and
the recess in the ball through which the pin fits.

From memory the pin protrudes about 12-15mm through the ball i.e. much
closer to a half inch than an inch.

At one time I lost both pin and spring and made a new pin by taking a bolt
of the appropriate diameter and length and ground the hexagonal head down to
be circular and of a size to fit into the recess in the ball. It was like
this for around two years before that van hit the scrap yard with no
problems changing gear. So I would say don't worry too much if you don't
have the spring; however without the pin gear changing will be near
impossible!

Good luck
