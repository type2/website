I wouold cehck out the Thermo-time switch & the cold start valve.  The
thermo switch tells the cold start valve when to inject gas based on temp &
time & only when the starter is cranking.  It may be injecting too long or
the cold start valve may be leaking even when not injecting fuel.  Follow
the Bentley for testing the 2 components.  Hope this helps.
