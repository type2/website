On Tuesday, July 22, 2003, at 12:30 AM, David Schwarze wrote:

> At 10:27 PM 7/21/2003, Greg Potts wrote:
>> What's the jet size I need to refit a pair of solexes from a 1700 to 
>> work on a 2L??
>
> They will work as is, but if you really want to jet them optimally for 
> fuel mileage, I would go with the following:
> 132.5 main
> 170 Air Correction
>
> For a very safe-running engine that gets lousy mileage, try:
> 137.5 main
> 155 Air Correction
>
> 132.5 mains are not a common size but are available.  130 and 137.5 
> are more widely available because they were "stock" sizes.
> 170 Air Corrections are on the large side, to offset the increased 
> displacement.
