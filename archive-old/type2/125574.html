David,

My experience with Mass inspection is they are overly strict when it comes
to inspecting cars. It's a nice backhanded way of them forcing you to drive
a new car and pay the state more money in taxes. I've heard of them failing
cars for pinholes of rust or even slighly opaque lenses in newer cars. I
live in CT, but my wife's family is from Mass and I hear lots of horror
stories.

My advice... if you have holes, worn tires or Godforbid, ehaust problems.
Don't go into inspection until it is all fixed. I don't know what others
will say but I wouldn't rush it and instead make sure she's close to 100%
before going through. That or move south of the border... we don't have
inspections for cars under 25 years old in CT and over that, they're still
pretty easy.

Chris in Windsor, CT
