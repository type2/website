On my '73 I placed the antenna in the middle of the luggage rack.  This antenna is spring steel, about 42" and base loaded with a magnetic mount.  The metal in that area acts as a ground plane and the flexibility of the whip and magnet allow me to drive through low overheads without any breakage as the antenna just gets knocked over.  In this position with a stock CB and in flat country send and receive  have been up to 25 mi. (40 km.)
