Hi Volks,

I  was able to get 20mm M7 bolts from a local fastener supply house... 
but they wound up referring me to the national warehouse (Luckily it 
was only 4 miles away) where they had 27 bolts in stock.

I bought a dozen, since they have a minimum order of $10 and I think I 
might have another 215mm flywheel at home somewhere.

Moral of the story: M7's are not commonly available. So when you pull 
apart a clutch and flywheel, bag the bolts and tie them to the flywheel 
for future reference. Same goes for the slide tube that supports the 
throw-out bearing... At BusFusion I found out the hard way that it uses 
M7 bolts as well.
