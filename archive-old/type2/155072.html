I once experienced a sudden loss of forward motion
on the freeway. I coasted to to the side and
discovered that the recently replaced CV joint had
come loose. Curse the idiot mechanic! Oh wait, that
would have been me ;-).  Fortunately, all or most of
the bolts were still there. Among the meager stash
of tools I had on bard was a pair of the smallest
vice grips. After finger tightening the CV bolts, I
clamped the vice grips on the bolt heads and was
able to tighten them well enough to get me home.
 
Love them vice grips!
