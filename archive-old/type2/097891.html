I have found two relays to be the best--one for the low beam
circuit--one for the high beam.

They do function better with two seperate feed wires to the hot lead to
the fuse box, and two seperate grounds, one to each relay.
10 to 12 ga wire is plenty good---and with 40 amp relays your good to go
up to 80 / 100 H-4's with no adverse effects
