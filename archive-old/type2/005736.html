In addition to everything Sean said, one of the first things to do on
the FI end is to check the fuel pressure, and check to see if your
system is staying pressurized after the engine is shut off.  If you
look at a FI trouble-shooting chart of "symptoms" and "possible
causes", one item listed in almost every case is "fuel pressure
problems."

If your "hard start" situation is only when the engine is cold (and
vacuum hoses and ignition check out OK) then I'd suspect the cold
start system.
