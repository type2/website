Lars:
A few suggestions:
1. Take a few photos. A good old time non auto focus camera with a
flash, stop the lens way down and set the focus at minimum. EVERYTHING 
will be in focus! I once did this with a Porsche 911 and you could read
the part numbers on the wire harness! Make a few different exposures and
angles to make sure every thing shows and put a piece of white paper
behind things to hilite them. Photos are cheap compared to time.
2. Use White vinyl electricians coding tape, write in ball-point pen,
tape around the wire and stick it to itself like a flag. Stretch the
tape a little so the tape is tight on the wire.
3. Don't worry too much. Many people on this list can talk you through
this, that's the easy part.
4. Draw a few sketches to remember which bolts go where, etc.
