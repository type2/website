90% or more of all fuel injection system faults coming into our  shop 
or that i have seen in my own maintenance work  come under the heading 
of operator maintence (the lack of)  the simple task  of aircleaners,

recommends that you use in your engine) every few months will keep that 
from happening.  I have  found injection systems to be easier to 
maintain  than carbs on many vehicles.
