George is completely right here. The dashes are universal. However you do
need the padding above the dashboard. This includes the handle for the
passenger and a straight piece of padding above the instrument panel. The
following picture clarifies what i mean. Look at www.vct2.nl/dashboard.jpg
for a naked dashboard. This picture was taken in a Type2 fire truck from
1968.

It should also be possible to get rid of the padding but there will be alot
of glue to be cleaned off the metal surface.
