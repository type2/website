ok, use a test light not a meter, operate starter, light should flicker
WEAKLY.

IF you get the light check
. check presure in fuel ring
. check fuel pump operates

IF no flickering light
. check double relay, power out from center slot and vehicle ground, test
light should light, if no light check ground

if light here check wire between series resistance and double relay
terminal 88b, if it is ok, replace double relay

....
other things that could cause this problem but less likely
. break in wire between term 1 on distributor and term 1 on the ECU
. bad ign points
. condenser shorting to ground
. also check the connector off of teh intake air sensor, term 6&9 200-400
ohm,  term 7&8  120-200 ohms
