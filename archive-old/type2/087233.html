Drew,

Gotta be one or more of several things.

The return fuel line is getting stopped up or the pressure 
   regulator closes and won't open...equals 125psi + on injectors
   and fuel ring.

The injectors are hanging full open or pressure is forcing them open...

The afm is sending a signal that says I am at WOT(wide open throttle)..and
the ecu believes it and starts opening the injectors a lot.

You really need to bite the bullet and go buy a "real" fuel pressure
gauge that you can hook up and look when it goes full rich.
Hi pressure when it dies means regulator getting stopped up or failing.

I suppose when this happens, you could pull the gas line between the
regulator and the gas tank, put this could be a serious high pressure
gas spill...and dangerous....

I had the opposite problem for a while from garbage in the tank, but one 
of the listees had my type problem from a gas line getting kinked when 
warm.....there's a thought.....
