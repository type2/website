At 12:34 AM 7/18/2001, you wrote:

>For this reason, I'm thinking of moving the battery to underneath the
>rear bench seat.  I guess the major concern about this is venting.
>Would this be a major health concern.. it puts off hydrogen when
>charging, right???

I believe it only puts out significant amounts if it is overcharged.  I 
have had two batteries inside the bus for years (inside battery boxes) with 
no problems, health or otherwise, but the only reason I did it was because 
they wouldn't fit in the 2nd battery tray.  I still don't consider a 
battery inside the bus to be an acceptable long-term solution unless the 
battery box is sealed and vented to the outside, or unless the battery 
itself is sealed.

Sealed batteries are great, but can be expensive.  Probably your best bet 
would be to pick up an Optima at Costco for $99.  Great price for a quality 
battery and you can safely keep it inside your bus.  You will still have to 
cut a hole in the body for the positive cable though (don't forget to use a 
grommet).
