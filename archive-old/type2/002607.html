or the like, the amount of power you'd need to pull out of the
generator/battery would probably be too much to get anything useful. I had
an electric windscreen demister for my Beetle, but it was so pathetic I
demanded my money back.

I suspect the in line heater you are thinking of is a fan you fit between
the heat exchangers and the cab. That is reported to be very effective, but
you still need those heater boxes in good order.
