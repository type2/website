The big gear in the motor gearbox has a tendency to slip on its shaft.  It's
not 'keyed' to the shaft, just a push fit.  I solved this by judicious use
of epoxy and it's held up fine.  If your gears turn out to be stripped, you
may need to get another motor from a junker.
