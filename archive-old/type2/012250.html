Only way to plug these are to tap em for 1/4 or 3/8 normal pipe and
use a socket head pipe plug.  All T4 galley locations can be tapped
and this done, but those and others are tough to do.  Depending which

some of those around the oil cooler and relief valve, and requires you to
grind your reamer and tap short enough to tap the shallow holes.

Anything push in is going to fail again.
