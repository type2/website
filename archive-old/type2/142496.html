SAE 40 is not recommended for sub-zero (Celcius) starting so the switch to a multi-grade would be beneficial given the cold temps you mention.

20W50 can be too thick for winter but it depends where you live. I personally would use Catrol GTX 15w40 over a 10w40. The smaller the difference between the two grades the better the oil's ability to keep from breaking down (so they say, depends on the engineering, etc).
