Jerry,

Your parking brake is sticking, it happens to me about once a year.  You
need to get some high temp antisieze paste (there may be a special kind for
brake work but I just use the regular copper-based stuff), take off the
rear drums and clean and lube the moving parts inside the there (but
sparingly).  Don't get any lube on the shoes or the drum. You may need to
lube the P-Brake cables as well.  Release them from the parking brake
handle at the front and then you can pull them out of the backing plate a
bit to lube them.
