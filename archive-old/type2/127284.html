maybe you got the plug wires positioned wrong on the dizzy cap.

with the engine off. tdc at the firing position for cyl #1. (both valves
on #1 will be closed)
Make sure that the rotor is pointing to the wire for #1.
Then make sure the wires  clockwise around the cap are 1432.

If this does turn out to be the case dont feel dumb. Ive done it more than
a few times.
