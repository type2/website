1. you remove the air flow meter
2. you insert a tube from the MAP sensor (mounted in the MS box)  to a 
place on your intake plenum
3. You need to fit a throttle position sensor (TPS) to your throttle body 
(TB).  This might be easier to do by getting a TB with a TPS in the 
junkyard that would fit to your plenum.
4. Don't forget the crank sensor (I forget what MS is using for this)
