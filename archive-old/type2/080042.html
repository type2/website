>The lower hose is the easy one - I'll suggest that you do it with the
>engine out but it can be done with some cussing with the firewall out.
>Disconnect and remove the battery (batteries). No sparks desired with open
>gas tanks. Aircooled drivers and aircooled busses are in short supply
>already...
>
>The hose material can be obtained from a marine supplier (who sells boats
>there locally?), a diesel repair shop, or NAPA (or the equivalent
>professional parts store).
>
>There are plenty of cooling hoses that will fit but I suggest you buy hose
>rated for fuel.
>
>Seems like mine only required about a 20 degree bend and I was able to fit
>a straight hose with a slight bend in it. I added hose clamps (I don't
>think mine had clamps originally).
>
>While you are at it replace ALL of the vent hoses and fuel hoses, they are
>easy to get to right now. It probably needs it anyway...
