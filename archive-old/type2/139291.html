ones that are hollow? I tend to cast them aside (not out -- what bus

is to keep tools clean. The first time I tried to clean one, many years 
ago, water got into the hollow and then never, ever, ever came back 
out.

Well, I just figured out how to deal with this.

[smacks forhead again]

Blue silicone caulk. Just seal the holes off with the blue stuff, and 
you're good to go.
