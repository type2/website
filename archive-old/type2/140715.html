* whoa, no!!!! You need to spot weld the coupling hub to the cage.
OUT of the bus.  BEFORE you put it in.
# 24 is the coupling http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/Microfiche/t205700.gif

See the rectangular stamped steel cage?
See the round hub that is pressed on to it?
Where the hub is pressed over the rectangular flat steel is where it slips.
You tack weld it to stop THOSE two pieces from moving...... and they will!
