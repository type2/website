I put a Mexican thermostat setup on my 1776 single port head/doghouse cooler 
engine. The rod that came with the CIP kit worked perfectly, it dropped 
right down through the hole in the fins and the thermostat spun on. There 
were no fitment issues, it was pretty easy.  I didn't have an older rod to 
compare it with, so I can't answer that one.. My bus runs nice and cool, and 
has great heat. At any rate, it hasn't overheated at all. I can always grab 
the dipstick after  a long highway run.
