You must ignore the second digit of the VIN number, as that just denotes
body type (camper, passenger van, cargo van etc.).  Which means that going
by VIN number, you _should_ have the earlier style. Also, my reference lists
a manufacturing month change of 12/72, so you might want to check it that
way as well. As you noted, neither guarantees anything completely as
sometimes VW switched things around, but your odds would be good.
