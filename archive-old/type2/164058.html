effective, and yet, rarely is it instituted. The benefits are numerous

don't even get me started on bottle placement. I think I may want to rig up
my sink so I can have an overflow valve on the drain for auto filling. Often
too I wonder if we might need an injector to help create a mist. I think
this topic is fascinating. It keeps me up at night.
