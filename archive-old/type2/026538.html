Beware!  Shops like this typically "make" a muffler for special applications
like VWs.  They take a cheap-o muffler that would go on a Japanese 4-banger,
cut the pipes off of it and WELD it to your heat exchangers, possibly using
pieces cut/welded from your old muffler as well.  I've seen it done and it
ain't pretty!  Beside hacking up things under your VW, they're also using a
muffler that is designed for an entirely different engine.  Wrong back
pressures, heat ranges, etc.
