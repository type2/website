At the risk of starting another FART, I love my Yokahama Model 370 197/75
R14.  They are M+S rated, 8 ply, Load rated D.   The Yokahama 356 are

370 on the rear and 356 on the front.

with a side wind and when monster tractor-trailers pass me on the
interstate.
