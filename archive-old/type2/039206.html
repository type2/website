Dave,

My '77 Westy had the same problem that popped up at Everybus. The city power
would not run the fridge, but I noticed that if I turned the battery charger
knob, the fridge power light then came on. I traced the electricals and found
that the fuse in the back of my AC/DC converter under the bench seat was blown.
I pulled the wooden cover, and pulled up the converter. There is a door on the
back of the converter and a fuse inside. Hope this helps.
