The 76-79 Westy came as a standard or deluxe model.  The standard model came
with an icebox rather than a fridge and a sink, but no stove.  I think the
standard also lacked the trash can and fire extinguisher.  Both came with
all the curtains, child's hammock, front center table between seats, back
table attached to swing arm, swivel passenger seat, pop top, cabinets, and
wonderful plaid interiors.  The only extras the deluxe had was the stove
with a flip up cover, AC/DC fridge (not propane), trash can with matching
plaid cover, and fire extinguisher.
