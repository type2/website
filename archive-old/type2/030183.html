I use a standard floor jack with a wood platform mounted to it approx
12x12". I think a much smaller surface would be a problem because the jack
will have a different tilt in different positions. Especially when all the
way lowered my engine would probablky be hard to control and tip off with
such a small surface. And when you roll it around it may get tippy as
well.
My best advice would be to find a high lift SUV type floor jack with about
24" of lift so that you can use it for all sorts of things and mount a
plywood platform to that when you do engine or tranny R&R.
