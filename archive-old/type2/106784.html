Subject: Re: [T2] Sway bars

Richard, I ran the 7/8" front and rear sway bars for a couple of months. 
Wound up getting rid of the rear, keeping the front. The rear bar did not 
seem to make a diff. on my '78 Westy, and for my uses, the reduced ground 
clearance was not worth the seemingly insignificant handling improvements 
provided by the rear bar. (For what its worth, I traded the rear bar to Greg 
Potts for a Hurst shifter a couple of EveryBusses ago). But the 7/8" front 
bar is the cats ass. Very big improvement over the stock bar. I'm also 
running BFG 27X8.5X14 A/T ko's, and KYB gas up front. The rear are air 
shocks, and my front suspension is in showroom new condition. Get the front 
bar. You won't regret it.
