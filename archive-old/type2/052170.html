[...]  Here's a tip to help prevent leaking push rod tube seals.  Before you
install the push rod tubes, take a moment to pull apart the accordian ends
on each one.  This effectively makes the tubes a bit longer.  Then when you
tighten your heads down they will crush back to exactly the right length.
I've never had a leaking tube since I started doing this.
