NEW MAGAZINE

from Jazz Publishing (publishers of Total VW)


The Volkswagen Type 2, Transporter, Bus, Kombi, Camper, Van, Microbus... 
whatever you want to call it... is one of the best known motor vehicles of
all
time. More than just a van, it has become to many people - to use a well
worn
phrase - a way of life. Over the past 50 years the VW has been seen in every

corner of the world, being driven by every type of person of every age and
background. It's a classic vehicle that has seen many changes but always
remained true to its original premise - a box on wheels. But what a box...

The broad appeal of the VW remains, and it's popularity remains undiminished
- in fact many would say it's growing every day. Older 'split-screens' are
now
true collectors items that can be worth small fortunes. 1970s 'bay-windows'
are
gaining respect as a real classic while remaining a practical everyday
vehicle, 
and 1980s 'Wedge' Type 25s are now finally being accepted by the purists, 
being the last link to the companies' original product - the Beetle. They
all have
their place within 'Volkswagen Camper and Commercial', a new magazine
designed to appeal to anyone with an interest in these most unique of
vehicles.

The publication looks to cover all aspects of buying, owning and running a
VW
Type 2 in the 21st Century, from purchase guides to lead prospective owners
through the minefield of buying a 20+ year old vehicle, to tips on keeping
them 
running, articles on original and restored examples of all ages and types, 
different camping conversions, practical upgrades, accessories, even ideas 
on places to visit...

The VW Camper is often seen as a vehicle for the young, and many magazines
only concentrate on appealing to them with modified vehicles - 'Volkswagen
Camper and Commercial' looks to a wider audience, including the purist to
whom only 100% originality will do, to the average family who simply see the

VW as a cost effective (and more practical) alternative to a £40,000 mobile
home.
Even the older individual who fancies recapturing some of their youth when
they
owned a VW in the '60s or '70s... full circle! A van for all people, with
now a
magazine to match.

Volkswagen Camper and Commercial will start life as a quarterly magazine
with the first issue going on sale the 15th December 2000. It will only be
available
to purchase by subscription, certain retail outlets or via the internet:
http://www.volkswagencamper.co_dot_uk

Subscription to 4 issues of Volkswagen Camper & Commercial will be £12.80
with single issues costing £3.20.
