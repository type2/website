	[]  It sounds like it is running for a few seconds on the cold start
valve, or fifth injector.
	This is completely independent of the rest of the FI system, , just
controlled by a little time/temperature controlled relay called the Thermal
Time Switch. So it is possible that for some reason your FI 
	system isn't functional. Probably a bad ground, or bad connections
somewhere.
	Put a test lamp across your one of your injector plugs, and see if
the light flickers on when you crank the engine. 
	If not, your main injectors are not firing.
