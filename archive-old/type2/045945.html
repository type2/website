Mickey-

That EGR light is turned on every 15k(?) miles by a little black box mounted
inline with the speedo cable and located "behind" the driver's kickpanel
(i.e. between the kickpanel and the sheetmetal) near the headlight.  The
collision probably tripped the contact in the box.  The box might also be
causing the weird noise you're hearing - there's a bunch of gears in there
that are probably no longer running smoothly.

If this is the noise problem, you might a) replace the box with a spare from
a junker, b) try what another recent poster did and remove most of the works
from the box, or c) try replacing the entire length of the speedo cable with
a box-free version. Or just ignore it.

Hope that helps!
