Driving from SLC to L.A. in the winter, I stopped for hot coffee in
Heber City, UT.  I spilled a little coffee on the dashboard of my bus.
Before I could get a napkin to wipe up the spill, it froze to the
dash!  To get the engine warmed up (no stock thermostat, shame on
me) I used duct tape to close off all but the upper and lower slot
in the stock air scoops.  Engine ran fine for temps in the 20F range.
Once I got to Beaver, it had warmed up a bit and the snow on I-15
melted away, so I removed the duct tape.  

Yes, in my limited experience, it did work.
