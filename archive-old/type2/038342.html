Tim:
    I just did that yesterday on my 69. It is pretty easy. I ordered the
entire scraper/crome trim piece from Ron _at_ Bus Depot. All you need to do is
roll down your window,  remove the panel from inside the front door remove
the two bolts that hold the glass from the apparatus that raises and lowers
the window and let the glass rest on the bottom of the interior of the door.
There are five clips that hold the scraper/crome to the door which need to
be pried off before you pull the whole piece out. Installation is easy
(simply pop it in where the old one came out) just be careful as the crome
trim is very light and will bend under it's own weight.

Hope this helps,
