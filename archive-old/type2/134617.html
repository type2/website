You are not leaking brake fluid into the engine. It is slowly filling up the booster 
on the master cylinder side. Soon, the fluid will fill the area and you will not be able 
to stop. The booster will be destroyed.
Right now, you NEED a new master cylinder. 
Depending on the level of brake fluid in the booster, you may need a rebuilt booster.
 
If you cannot do the labor yourself, the parts prices are fair for retail list price.
But the labor price is too high.

If you can do the labor yourself, the parts are cheaper.
