Subject: Some Midwest Fall bus campouts

August 31st  thru  Sept. 3rd - Busses No Where Near the Arch, St. Louis,
MO, information:  Bill Bowman <BussesBytheArch_at_compuserve_dot_com>
Very large gathering of busses.  Next to a lake.  Large caravans and plenty
of bus conversation.  Only VW's can join campout.

September 21-23 - Josh Rogers Memorial Campout, Turner Falls OK,
information:  Art Long <along_at_mmcable_dot_com>
Anybody welcome, no matter what you drive.  Fun for the entire family.
Beautiful campground in some ancient mountains beside a clear rolling stream
with some nice waterfalls.

October 5&6 - Busses and Balloons, Albuquerque,
information: <JPHenrikse_at_aol_dot_com>
Camping in a church parking lot, wake up to hundreds of hot air ballons.

October 12&13 - Missouri VW Fest, information:
http://members.sockets_dot_net/~rickd/vwfest.html
or Rick Dennis at: <rick_at_microcollector_dot_org>
Beautiful tall oak tree campground with a caravan to the historic Herman MO.
Lots of great shopping in small speciality shops along with great german
food.  Anyone welcome.

October 26&27 - Bustoberfest, Indian Cave State Park, Neb,
information:  "No Quarter" <silent_at_beatricene_dot_com>
Wonderful historic campground with fall activities.  Beautiful fall colors
on the trees.  Evenings can get cool this time of year.  Plenty of fun for
the entire family.  Anyone welcome.
