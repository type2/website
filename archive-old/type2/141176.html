I AM very puzzled as to why they machined just the clutch surface.
You have to machine the same amount off the face where the cover bolts on, on stepped flywheels.
Otherwise the geometry is off. Then when the disk wears, you are out of
leverage to hold pressure. That is why your wing nut is at the bottom end of the threads.... you are out of 
the range of travel.
Either you need to have them redo the flywheel correctly or get another one.
