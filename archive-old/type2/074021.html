Came across two vans fitted with those aftermarket
sunroofs over the years...both were duct tape shut.
Might have been the installations, but the severe
side-to-side curve of the roof seemed to lead to e-
ventual leakage. The aluminum frame can be bent to
fit (and with enough screws, held in place), but the
plastic insert "wants" to stay flat and with only a 
few clips locking it to the frame (four IIRC), spreads
back up and away from the seal. As I said, these may
have been "one size fits all" units and obviously not
type 2 specific. The same fitting problem comes up
with roof racks for the van; lots of off the shelf racks
fit the width of the bus but won't clear that arch.
