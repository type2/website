Seals I've used for VW have all been overlength and required trimming after
instalation.  Do you see a channel the remains of the old seal is in.  It may be
pinched in a few areas.  Slide in the new seal and pinch the channel at a few
points to hold its position.  If it is tough to push in, I use a soapy water
solution for lubrication.
