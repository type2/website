>>>rob: the steering box has an adjustment bolt- check the bentley.
>>>if you have a lot of slop at the steering wheel , this can help-
>>>
>>>jack both front wheels off the ground (use jack stands ),
>>>grab the left front wheel and turn it sharply, you should be
>>>able to see the slop in the drag link ( slight movement off-axis ).
>>>have a helper do it while you look, after you get good you can do it
>>>alone. grab the drag link and wiggle it hard to see, also. or put a vise-
>>>grip on it and jerk it.  eventually you will find the play... it doesn't have to
>>>be much to be bad, but you should be able to see it, failing that, have
>>>your mechanic save it to show you, and try the feel on the new one
>>>before he installs it...
>>>
>>>wiggling the wheel up and down is a test for wheel bearings, and side to
>>>side is a test for tie rods and other steering components ( drag link, etc.... )
