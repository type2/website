John,

You're right about the blower, but someone else already told you that.
If you haven't done it already, make sure the holes for the heater
pipes... the ones the fan would hook to... are plugged until you get the
heater stuff back in there. Otherwise, you're going to be sucking hot
air into the engine compartment from under the cylinders... not a good
thing for the engine cooling situation.
