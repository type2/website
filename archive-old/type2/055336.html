Just a short caveat on bus insulation.............
When so doing, make sure that you have not created pockets that will 
entrap condensed moisture created by your preventing the free transfer 
of moisture and vapor.
Insulating is good for the passenger.
Turning the bus shell into isolated pockets for "water making" is not 
good.
