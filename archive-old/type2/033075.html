	Mick,
	I've checked my VW parts book and for 72 (the last update in this
book) the nuts and washers for the rocker arm attachment assembly
are listed as:

    Key   Part No     Description       Qty   Model
	(6) N 12 234 1  Spring Washer B 7    8    66 hp
    (7) N 11 007 1  Nut Hex M 7          8    66 hp

	Which is how 10 foot-lb of torque or 1.4 Newton/Meters can 
be enough torque.

	Obviously this is the Type 4 engine since the quantity is 8, not
4. The Type 1/2 head has only two nuts and washers on a side, with
all four rockers on a single shaft. The Type 4 has two shafts per head,
one per cylinder, so total 8 of each type of fasteners. 

	Any hardware store that stocks spring washers should be able
to supply a standard 7 mm size.
