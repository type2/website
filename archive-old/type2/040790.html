> automatic starters are different,  the Auto starter can be used in most
> manuals but a manual starter cannot be used in an automatic bus

The Auto starter can NOT be used on 2.0 L manuals. It can be used on a 2.0 L
auto trans.
Does one of your starters have a 1/2" blank space between the Sprague clutch
and the pinion teeth?
If yes: this is a 2.0 L manual starter.
The 1.7 and 1.8 starters have no blank section. The pinion teeth meet at the
overrunning clutch. [ btw, that Sprague clutch is also in those turnstiles
like at grocery stores, so you can enter but can't exit ]
