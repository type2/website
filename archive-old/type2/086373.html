Matty,

You can remove the thermostat and clean it with solvent and even a wire
brush just be careful. I would not worry about the oil that is leaking on
them to much. I would worry about the leaks though. Removing the heads
without pulling the engine is actually more work I think. I would invest in
floor jack and some simple stands. actually you can do it all with one jack
and no stands. But again it is a little trickier. You can get an engine
stand for about $100 again depending on the work you want to do, it could be
well worth it. Complete gasket kits are about $70.00 and have almost
everything you need. Extras are rear front and main seals and trans main
seal. About another $20.00. We just finished another 20.00 gasket overhaul
so let me know if you have any more questions about this.
