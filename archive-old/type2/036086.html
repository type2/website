Many times I have removed an engine (just 3 day's ago a '76) and then let
the bus down on all 4's and moved it back beside the barn.

It is easy to raise the bus, put it on jack stands (4 of them), remove the
engine (MUCH easier lifted in the air), lower the bus, push it where you
want it.  You should have the entire procedure done in less than an hour (2
if you have a friend helping you).
