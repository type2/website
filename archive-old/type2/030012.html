Some oddities of the tire trade are in action here.

The larger the section number, the higher the load rating for a given
size.  an 85 section (which is the default section number) is stronger in
a give size and load range than a 75 section.  In order of strength from
strongest to weakest, here are the tires used on buses:

185R14 Load Range D
185R14 Load Range C
195/75R14 Load Range D
185R14 Passenger
195/75R14 Load Range C

Strange, isn't it!  I guess that a squatter section relies more on the
tire structure to support the load and less on the air.
