Brian:
I think your clutch isn't releasing for one of 2 reasons:
1. Broken clutch cable
2. Broken welds on clutch release shaft.(Pedal moves, cable moves, has
slight resistance because of the spring on the release lever, but
because the throwout bearing is not pushing the clutch fingers in you
don't feel much resistance and the clutch does not disengage.) This is a
lot more work to fix than just the cable because it involves taking out
the engine to get to the shaft, but it's not as bad as fixing the
transmission.
