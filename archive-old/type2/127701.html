Sorry, but the above is absolutely incorrect. The
double vacuum distributors all time retarded at idle,
assuming of course that everything is hooked up and

degrees total advance and let the idle advance fall

same with a single vacuum distributor but it's harder
to do with a double vacuum distributor unless you are
using a chassis dyno to tune with.
