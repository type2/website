	Before you look into more expensive options, have you confirmed that the
	offensive part is the clutch? Several other parts can cause a
	transmission not go into gear, including the shifter missadjustments,
	loose shifter rod parts or bushings. Worth a look at the very least.
	
	> What can I expect to pay to have some one fix my clutch?
	
	I don't have a price, but you will be paying for a Removal and
	replacement of the engine which should be a good chunk of change. If
	possible it's a great oppertunity for a bus owner to get the feel for
	R&R by doing it yourself. Should be a reasonable job over a weekend,
	with the most basic tools.
