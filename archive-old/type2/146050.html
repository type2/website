Good--your not trying to supplement what isn't there in the first place.

The only thing bad I can tell you is that the AC Delco air shocks have
an exposed air bag and will and can catch a wild stone or rock and will
punture.

However, the Gabriel Air Shicks have a totally independant shock unit,
which means wherever or how much air you put in them the shock unit is
totally separate from the air bags--you still have a shock absorber
regardless of how high you jack the vehicle up. 

This is a bonus for a good ride.
