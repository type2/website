Pro car thieves commonly use tow trucks, even flatbeds, to do their dirty
work.  People don't pay any attention to tow trucks loading up, even with
alarms blaring.  It gets around all of the possible anti-theft strategies.
Only anti-tow thing I've learned is to park between two other cars and
turn the wheels as far as they will go.  If the tow truck cannot pull the
car from the space without hitting other cars then the thief might move
on.
