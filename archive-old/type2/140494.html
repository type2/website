Don,

You might do a Google search on valve lapping. I've lapped more than a few
valves but seems like for the past 20 years or so the message is that
lapping is not necessary or desired. It produces an inferior finished
compared to the proper grinding stone. Recently, a machinist explained that
lapping creates grooves in the valve seat and face with even the finest
compound. Once the valve wears in and sinks a bit these grooves no longer
align.
