I've seen one case of CO poisoning, and it is just about in the "Darwin
Award" category.  I was desert camping (not with a VW group) and one idiot

door uninvited.  They were both unconscious and barely came around when
they were carried out of their van into the cold desert air.  They
escaped with headaches from hell.  If their friend had just decided that
they had gone to bed early they would have been dead.
