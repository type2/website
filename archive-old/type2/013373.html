The pertronix drop in points replacement system.
1) eliminates the condensor.
2) Mech advance unit comes with a full circle
mounting base.
3) Vac advance unit comes with a half moon (roughly)
shaped mounting plate.
4) the mech advance unit will not fit in a vac advance
dizzy due to the mechanism that takes up half of the
dizzy.
5) The Unit's base mounts to the exsisting plate in your
dizzy useing the same attach points as the points assembly.
It comes with a fairly thick, plastic gap gauge. DO NOT
HOOK IT UP BACKWARDS as it will fry. Also take care to use
the thick gap so that the sensor doesnt contact the
doughnut that goes under the rotor. This will overheat the
sensor and it will die.
