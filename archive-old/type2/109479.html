William-

I have that exact configuration in my 74.......1800cc with a Weber
progressive conversion from dual carbs. I also run the SVDA distributor from
John at aircooled_dot_net. I have no complaints at all abut this set-up. I have
good power, reasonable gas mileage, and excellent reliability. Mileage is 15
in town and 20 on the highway. I know that going back to a good set of dual
carbs may help me make some small improvements, but I question the
cost/benefit aspect. (My bus came with the Weber installed so I'd have to
shell out for a complete dual carb set-up from the manifold up.)
Anyway, I've had good results with the Weber. By the way, this bus is my
daily driver as well as my long haul road tripper.
