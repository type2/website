On this, John probably means basically the Bosch Super.  Particularly
in my experience the Indian made Supers.  German made old stock,
or German tri-electrode are great plugs.  The Bosch (and any other)
platinum plug with a small center electrode isn't a great idea in
an air-cooled vehicle as by their nature (particularly carbed cars)
they are going to run rich sometimes and those plugs foul
instantly.

NGK does make a great plug.
