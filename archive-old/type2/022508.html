Well, the replies I got indicate that the fewer interfaces, the better for 
sealing, plus one answer stated that even a head gasket isn't really 
necessary.

So, if I use one .090" (largest I could find in any catalog; .100" would be 
better) base spacer and leave out the head gasket and the stock base shims, 
I'll get 7.5:1 CR. Further, that puts the "stack" back very near factory 
specs, so the valve train geometry will remain correct.

From what I've read that this isn't too high for regular gas if I am careful 
with the timing. That is what I'll do if there's no downside to it.
