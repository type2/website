Is it possible that you have somehow gotten early (71-72) spindles on
your late bus?  A long shot, but possible.

Pull the disks off and measure the distance from the mounting surface
(where the end of the hub bears on the disk hub) to the inner surface
of the disk.  Compare this measurement to the original disk.  If it's
more than a mm or two different then you've got the wrong disk for
your spindles.
