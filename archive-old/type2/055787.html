RON is a measure of octane.  It is not the measure of octane used in teh
US.  VW says 91 RON = 87 US CLC pump octane.

You are throwing away money if you are in the US and are using anything
over 87 octane on a stock engine.  You don't need it and VW didn't say
you need it.
