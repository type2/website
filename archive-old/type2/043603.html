I did this with my engine and all the tin.  It does wonders for removing all the
caked on oily gunk.  It also will remove the paint and you are left with bare
metal, which is exactly what I was looking for.  I went to clean my license plates
and didn't think about the paint removal aspect and it was the strangest thing to
pull two bare metal plates out of the soup.

As for painting inside the fan shroud, a wide dispersal painting device (rattle
can) can mist in paint in most of the cracks and crevices.  I don't think that the
air will mind looking at the few runs that might develop using this method.

About the 50 pound bag..  I don't know if Ian dumped the whole bag in there at
once, but I have found the stuff to go quite far on small amounts.  Typically, I
use about a cup of TSP to 3 gallons of water.
