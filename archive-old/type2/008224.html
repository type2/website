Since the throttle shafts are steel, they would gall if they were run
on steel bushings.  Not what you want to happen!

to a normal VW driver.  Even the steel-on-aluminum stock arrangement
is good for over 100K miles in most cases.  Brass would outwear

is replace them, say every 200K miles.
