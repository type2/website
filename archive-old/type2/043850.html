Sounds like something's acting like a check valve in the system.  If you had 
the same problem with both old and new flexi lines and wheel cylinders, the

the front lines join (maybe at the master?).  I didn't look at your master 
cylinder so I can't say...

Maybe loosen the line at the master cylinder and give it a gentle pump to 
see if a chunk o'dookie comes out.
