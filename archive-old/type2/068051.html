  Anyhow my main question is....are there any special procedures to selling a

California requires a smog certificate to register a car in California, not
to sell it out of state.   I bought a bus in California, and sold it to a
friend here in Washington.  Washington state law requires an inspection when
registering a previously out of state car in this state.   Make sure the
title numbers match the bus' numbers.    Thurston county does not require a
smog certificate to transfer title, so we didn't have to worry about that.
