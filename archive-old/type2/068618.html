They're on there pretty tight.  I took a chisel to mine to get it
off...just whacked it until it split and came off.  Didn't hurt
anything, as long as I stayed with the race and not the axle itself.

on without a problem. (Actually "bangs" on - even strain as ya go)

I was told by an expert that was the way to go on it.  Worked for
