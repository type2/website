I'm not sure where the "proper" place is, but on my '72 bus it goes right
onto the motor, which makes sense to me because you want the starter
grounded as best as possible.  Remember to clean the area of the motor you
bolt to with a wire brush or sandpaper for a good connection.

photo at:  http://www.pauldottrip_dot_com/vw_bus/neg_batt_cable.jpg

When I installed a second (non-factory) battery I put the 2nd battery ground
onto the other side of the motor the same way (black cable):
