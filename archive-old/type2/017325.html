Leo,

I'll bet you come closer to 6k lbs than you think.  A Westy is heavier than my
ASI Riviera, and I drive across ODOT's scales every now and then.  Empty water
tank, no food, a minimum of gear, one human, no dog, half a tank of fuel, full
propane tank crosses at 4900 lbs.  Add a couple of humans, food, dog, full
tanks and the usual assortment of sundry items and I'll bet it's over the 6k
limit.  Go with the "D" rated tyres if you can afford them.  I run
LT27/8.50-14 "D" rated tyres at 50# all the way around.  Just air down when I
get on washboard roads.
