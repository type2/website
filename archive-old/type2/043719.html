It has been some time since my last posting (building a house, not much
time for busses). I've heard rumours about a Diesel converted bus here in

Why diesel ?

Gasoline prices are roughly about 4 1/2 Dollar per gallon here. Diesel fuel
is about a dollar  cheaper and i have the possibility to buy a good used
1600 Turbodiesel engine from a friend. Most of the Vanagons are diesel
powered here, so parts will be easy to find at the junkyards. The
combination of a diesel engine and heating based on water circulation would
make it possible to use a bus for daily driving around the year. 
I could simply buy a diesel Vanagon but i don't like the Vanagon -no i
won't go into word battles about why!
