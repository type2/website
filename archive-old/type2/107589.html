Bentley shows these grounds associated with the FI:

-Pins 5,16,17
-Dizzy to Coil (-)
-Solenoid (Ground strap to starter)
-Starter (I think this is provided by being bolted to the bell housing)
-Fuel Pump
-Battery
-Double Relay (This should go under one of the screws holding the relays to
tin)
-Throttle Valve Switch (If you have one, but I think these were pre-1977)

The backup light connectors shouldn't create a FI issue.

When you crank, do you get any hint of starting, or just nothing at all? Are
you sure the distributor is firing in the right order and at the right time?
