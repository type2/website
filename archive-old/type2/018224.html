Mark Van Dam wrote:
> 
> The PO of my 72 Adventure Camper was a serious Deadhead, and the bus is
> covered in all kinds of stickers.  Unfortunately, several of them are
> stuck directly on the paint of the rear hatch and engine compartment
> lid.  Anyone got any recommendations on how to remove these stickers
> without damaging the paint?  I was hoping there is some kind of magic
> solvent that will take the stickers off, but leave the paint unharmed.
> The PO also "played for the other team" so to speak, so I really would
> like to get these rainbow stickers off my camper.
> 

Try a hair dryer set on high, heat the sticker and as it starts to curl
up and/or wrinkle and soften, scrape off gently with a single edge razor
blade (obtainable at a paint store).

On glass you can try soaking a piece of paper towel in acetone and
soaking and scraping the sticker off as mentioned above.

Good luck!
