Bill:

Sounds like you might need to replace your brake hoses.  With the passing
of time, the hoses swell up internally and restrict the flow of fluid to
and from the wheel cylinders and calipers.  The simptoms include uneven
braking, sticking brakes and the ones that you mentioned.  Any hoses more
than 10 years old should always be replaced.  Hope this helps.
