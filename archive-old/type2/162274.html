didnt have it, so I thought I would go ahead and get some replacement

Me:  "It's unlisted."

Me:  "Why do you need my address for a cash transaction?"
Her: "Our computer system is down and we just need that information for
      receipt purposes in case you need to make a return."
Me:  "Why do you need my address for a cash transaction?"
Her: "Sir, we just need that information."
Me:  "Ok. 123 ANY STREET"
Her: "Sir, we need your REAL address."
Me:  "That IS my real address."
Her: "No, it's not."
Me:  "You know, I can get these batteries and Wal-Mart for cheaper and
      THEY don't need to know my address!"
Her: "Well, I suggest you go to Wal-Mart then."
Me:  "Fine!"
