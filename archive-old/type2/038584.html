should be OK.

As far as Michelin goes, check the archives.  Some have had failures with
the belts stripping off of the carcass and whipping the heck out of the
wheel well and battery trays.  I had this happen to me, luckily on a
C*evy, not an important vehicle.

Personally, I went for the Bridgestones and am very pleased with them.
