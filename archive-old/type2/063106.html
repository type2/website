Yep, I agree with that.  And it can depend on what you wear too!  Back in
school, when I used to drive back and forth between Carlsbad NM and Socorro,
NM, (in a Ford Torino, sorry) I would occasionally wear a Stetson "cowboy"
hat while I was driving.  When I did, an entirely new group of people would
wave at me, usually other people in cowboy hats, often driving pick-em-up
trucks.  (some of them had baseball caps on, but these invariably said either
"John Deere" or "CAT: Diesel power" on 'em.)

Now if I was to wear that hat (now long gone) in San Francisco, there might
well be fewer cowboy-hat-wavers there.

Hmm, I'll hafta' put some peace signs 'n stuff on my '66 and drive around
rural Texas wearing a cowboy hat to see what sort of reaction I get. :-)
