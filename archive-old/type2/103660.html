Kool.  And you may notice that I have some black electric tape around the 
holder.  Look closer...

This is for the passenger side only.

On the driver side, it is just basic.

Why??

Well, These damn holders are so strong, I can use the 44 oz cups from 7-11.  
It is just that they are so heavy that they 'push thru' the open fingers of 
the cup holder; the weight causes the fingers to spread open and the 
huge/heavy 44 oz cup to flip out.

But with the black electrical tape (this is the kind of tape that my dad 
used for everything some 50 years ago, so I tend to use it myself now.) 
holding the fingers closed, the little bottom of the 44 oz cup fits nicely 
in the holder and the cup does not flip out.

I did not do this on my driver side because I can still put some fatter cups 
in the driver side holder, making the driver side holder more versatile.  I 
usually put the 20 oz coffee cup in the driver side, but if I am going for 
the largest cup I can get at 7-11 because of the summer heat, I use the 
driver side holder for the BIGGIEs, and just pour off some Coke in a smaller 
cup for drinking as I drive.

The passenger side holder with the  tape can still hold a 12 oz can of 
beverage.
