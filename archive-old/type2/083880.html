next two hours removing an absolutely clean tank....grrrr. I wanted to check
the tank since the engine was out. I guess I shouldn't be complaining. The
tank required no cleaning, recoating, or replacement.

would suggest that they drain the tank and rent a boroscope to view its
condition through the filler neck. Unless you know that there's a problem,
it may not be worth your time to remove it.
