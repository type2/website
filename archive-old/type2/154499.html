Mike,

There is no difference in the fuse box according to my very early print
Haynes Manual, but '69 is a funky one-year-only fuse box. I have seen a few
on BustedBus' shelves.

However the Euro spec rear lens is a two color unit with an amber turn
signal light on the top and the red brake light on the bottom. The bucket is
also different with two sections and are larger. They fit right into US
models. The lenses can still be purchased new from suppliers with Hella
products. I think Ron has them listed.
