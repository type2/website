Stan..
I have the same tach. with 4 wires coming out the back
red=switched power, black=ground, green=coil, blue=lights
 
As for which is connected to which terminal on the back, I'd have to take it out to find out for sure but it should be pretty easy to find.
I would assume "G" is the black/ground.  use a tester hooked up to a hot wire and probe the other 3.  The one that lights up the tester should be the Red/switched power wire.  To find out which of the remaining 2 pluhs is lights or tach, just test them both individually with a hot wire-the one that lights up the tach.would be the lights.  The last would be the coil!  Hope this helps?
