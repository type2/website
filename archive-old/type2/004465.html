I used a GPS all last summer. It was great, apart from giving
stats like avg speed, total time, stopped time, max speed, distance covered
etc, I have marked all the camp sites as waypoints and have downloaded the
routes onto my computer, so now I have a map of exactly where I covered last
year, an electronic diary.

Plus, in theory you should never get lost again! It's not cheap and doesn't
replace the feel and pleasure of good maps, but a GPS is a lot of fun and
highly recommended.
