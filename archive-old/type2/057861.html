A few years back someone instrumented an engine in order to test
whether the "cool tins" would reduce cylinder temperatures. 
Apparently, the results showed that the cool tins did not lower
cylinder temperatures when compared to the stock tin pieces. 
Considering that you have to bang up your existing tin to install the
cool tin, I don't think that it is such a good idea on a type 1 engine.
