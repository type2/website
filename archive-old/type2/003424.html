I did this last year.  Yes,  it'll help to have access by taking the little
circle thing off but I could not have done mine with out taking out the fire
wall.  I can do this with out dropping the engine because my two bottom
screws are no longer in place- and if I bend the upper engine tin out
enough, I can pull the fire wall out.

a more detailed account.
