Robert, Bob:
Actually, ATF would clean out a trans pretty good, but it's a little weak on
hypoid lube ability. One could probably just put a couple of quarts into the
transaxle and leave the car up on stands while running the engine and trans thru
the gears for a few minutes, then drain it back out. ATF is really high in
detergents.
Having said that, I'm not sure why this would ever be necessary. As one who has
taken apart (and put back together) over a hundred manual tranmissions, they just
don't get that dirty inside. Chunks of metal, but most trannies have magnetic
drain plugs for that.
