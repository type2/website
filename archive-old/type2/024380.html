 Best way to go is to use a heater like the Propex where the heat 
output is seperate from the combustion exhaust. This way you get
safe heat with no open flames, no combustion byproducts and no
hot spots for sleeping bags to fall on. These units are more 
expensive than a conventional radiant heater but usually include
thermostatic control, blower fans etc.

 Do some searches for Carver or Propex.
