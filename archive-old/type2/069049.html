At 10:01 PM 11/22/96 -0500, you wrote:
>Re adding better bed to 72 Westy:  Get some 1.5-2" foam at a foam place,
>about a 4x6ft piece.  With a little measuring and ingenuity, you can
>replace the cot with a board (1/2" ply, the good stuff is pretty stiff, and
>you can beef up the edges) that "hinges" in the back and can lift up with
>the top when wanted.  You can make elaborate catches in the front, or be as
>simple as screwing in two eyescrews into the boards that run along the
>sides of poptop and slinging a rope across that catches under board to help
>lift with top. The Euopean version of the 71 I had came with a factory
>Board with foam that had the rear hinges and two rubber grabbers on each
>side in front.  Make a cotton cover for the foam with snaps for washability
>and you will be comfy- and the mat can be used in an outside tent too.
