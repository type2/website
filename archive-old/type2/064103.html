I don't own one of these, just know what I've read about them and been told
by the guys at the RV place. The model 3100 (1600-2800 BTU) only uses an
1/8th lb of propane per hour. Size 11 3/4" X 10 1/4" X 3 1/2". Only 6 lbs.

Here's what is says about them in the brochure.

"Olympian A Model Catalytic Safety Heaters produce heat by combining L/P
gas and oxygen in a platinum impregnated pad. This establishes a chemical
reaction (combustion) resulting in the release of heat...operating at all
times below the temperature necessary to support flame combustion. It is
this combustion without flame that produces a low intensity infrared
catalytic heat that warms like the sun."

"Catalytic heating is a no-waste form of heating since no heat is lost
through a flue or chimney. Flame type heaters waste up to 45% of all heat
produced. Catalytic heating is nearly 100% effecient, resulting in a more
effecient and low cost heat"

That sounds good to me. I like the cost and they warranty for 3 years.
