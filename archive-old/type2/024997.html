I feel that I must speak out in defense of poptops. I put a '75 top on my '67
bus and it's one of the best things I've ever done to the bus. I didn't have
any troubles with the back hatch as someone mentioned. the only thing to do is
make sure to weld in a good subframe to replace the roof frame you remove. I
took a weekend and did it, although I am lucky enough to have access to welding
equipment, and a good friend who knows how to use it.
Basically, cut out the roof, and the middle cross brace. Weld in the new frame,
attach the bed, and top, and you're all set!
I highly recommend it, and have thoroughly enjoyed mine for the last 2 years.
