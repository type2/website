testing.  I went with a portable fridge (RC3000) made by dometic.  it

as well as 110v 12v and lp hookups.  it comes with a regulator and hose 
to hook up a small propane torch type bottle but i had a new hose made 
at the local rv gas place to hook it up to my van gas system as well as 
  another hose with regulator to connect to  standard BBQ type bottles. 
I have a  small power inverter in the van so when moving i use the 110 
volt  connection and when camped switch to gas.  the gas has 3 
settings.  on a recent campout down at RMMW i used the high for in the 
daytime and  stayed under 50 degrees.  at night i  set it to the lowest 
setting and it was still at the freezing point in the morning.  regular 
thermostat knob for 12v and 110v.  very happy with this unit  as i  can 
put away if i dont want it in the van and i can move it around at will.
