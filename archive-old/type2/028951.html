number for a headlight switch part. The correct switch for a 71 vw bus is
211 941 531 E. After market switches may or may not have the escutcheon. I
would recommend using your old one if it fits. This item is not as nice as
the original but, what can you do? BUStedVW has new switches and used ones
for these 71-79. The new ones sell for $35.00 and the used ones sell for
$21.00. There is usually no returns on electrical items so we recommend that
you do not try and rig something to work. Test all used switches with a
gauge before installing and beware of hot wires while installing headlight
switches.
