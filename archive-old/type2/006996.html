I have found as David and Clara have that the WCM grey door
rubber firs well but that it is way stiffer than the OEM
rubber and hence IMHO comprimises the ability of the door to
seal properly. FWIW alot of the WCM rubber (particularly the
top of the cab door "scrapers" to be WAY too stiff and make
opening and shutting the door a real pain in the butt. I
have heard good things about WW but have no direct
experience.

On a realted note I have heard that the WCM rubber for the
cargo area pop out windows to be REALLY stiff so much so
that peeople have actually broken windows attempting to shut
them (push on the outside of the window ina n attempt to
shut it and crack :(   )
