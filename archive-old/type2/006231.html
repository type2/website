Of course, check the bulbs and sockets, clean the ground connections, make
sure wires are attached, etc. I did all this a month ago and found nothing
wrong at the light itself. Turned out to be the pressure switches on the
master cylinder. I replaced both of them (I have a 74--don't know if the 71
also has two. List?) with Wells brand from my FLAPS and the lights work like
a champ. If you do replace them, don't forget tp bleed the cylinder after
installing.
