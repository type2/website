I just built a new 1776 type 1 engine for a bus using all NEW components,
case, crank, rods, cam and all bearings. All bearings were plastigauge and
were all exceeding factory specs, oil pump checked for clearances. This
engine had low pressure when hot, WTF. Changed out the VDO sender and gained
5 lbs pressure (both senders were new out of the box). Still oil pressure
seemed low at idle when warm, is 40 pounds at all rpms regardless of temps.
Just for the heck of it I tried 3 different sets of springs and relief
plungers to see if I would get any different readings, conclusion, no
changes, even changed oil pumps, still no changes. This engine now has over
2000 miles on it in less then a week and all readings have remained the
same, at idle hot 5-10 lbs pressure, 40 lbs when driving at all temps.
