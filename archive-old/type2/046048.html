Gel-cells are also available for aircraft.  Aircraft charging systems are
pretty much off-the-shelf automotive parts with aircraft price tags.  The
manufacturers of these gel-cels don't indicate that anything special is
needed in the charging systems.

I think that the difference is in the usage.  Starting batteries can
handle more charging current than deep-cycle batteries.  Most gel-cells
are deep cycle designs, whereas the aircraft gel-cells are starting
batteries.

I charge small gel-cells by simply paralleling them with the starting
battery.  Never had a problem doing this
