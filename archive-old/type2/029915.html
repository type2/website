OK, first you need to get good lighting and a comfortable place to read.

Second, the wiring diagram is only "sorta" laid out how your vehicle is
laid out.  It takes some study and comparison to figure out where the
wires _really_ go.

Third, it pays to know what the terminal numbers mean.  There are
references on the net that match the DIN terminal numbers on the switches
and diagrams to the circuits they control.  For example, 56 is the
headlight circuit on any German car be it VW, Mercedes, BMW, etc.

Be patient, study hard and it will come to you!
