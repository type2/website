Hey Chip

I have a suggestion, but it's based on my experience with my
'60 bug.  When I got the bolt out of the rubber bushing in the
head of the shock, I had a similar problem.

The new shock had a metal collar already in the rubber bushing.
The old metal collar had fused to the bolt.  I cold-chiseled the
old collar off the bolt, polished up the bolt, smeared a bunch of
the aluminum Permatex anti-seize onto the part of the bolt where
it goes through the metal collar, and reattached the works.

Hope this is useful on your Type II!
