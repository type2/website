I was going to respond to Ron about 'putting rear housings on the front' but 
I never did. Unless you want to drill a 3/4" and 2 1/4" holes in your door!, 
you can't use rear housings with lights. All you have in the door are 2 
screw holes for screws that go thru the reflecter. The rear housings are 
held on by snapin clips in holes, one above where the screw hole would be 
and the other hole below where the screw would be. The short screws on the 
rear only go into the housing to hold the lens on.
