OR--if you did suck it down--
If the low side gauge you have with the Wally World fill-er up set up
reads high right away--the expansion valve is plugged--and this is why
you weren't getting any AC in the first place.
Pretty common problem in an older car where little particles of garbage
float around.
The capillary tube in the expansion valve is tiny--real small and if it
gets plugged up---No fredonia will be flowin' thu the system.

If you had a manifold with both gauges you'd see the high side going
nowhere.
