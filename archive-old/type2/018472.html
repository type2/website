Joe,
You may be on to something there.  Steve Dolan's '73 w/ '78 FI sometimes
does this exact same thing and when it does it also doesn't start or
start very well.  On his, when it does this, the only way to shut off
the fuel pump is to disconnect the battery.  Weird.  

Unfortunately I don't know what the problem is but possibly the double
relay or some sort of wiring problem creating a feedback loop of some
sort.  

You may wish to troubleshoot the double relay per the instructions in
Bentley, maybe it will shed some light on your problem.  Also inspect
your wiring and make sure it jibes with your wiring diagram.
