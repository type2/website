> In trying to  get the notch on the edge of the distributer to
> point in roughly the same place as the old one I find that the vacuum can
> willhit the engine tin.

which notch are you trying to align? the little "line" on the edge of
the top of the metal, or the notch the cap must fit in? They are most
likely different (at least on mine they were). Try this. Rotate engine
till you get to TDC of #1 cyl of the compression stroke. Then see if
you can rotate the distributor body around so that the rotor points
towards the small "line" case into the distributor body. If this fixes
it you will need to reindex your plug wires. No big deal, just start
at #1 and work around.
