> The left turn signal on my 71 Westy has been flashing

> or flasher relay, so I relaced these with new parts. I

> Both the front and rear bulbs DO flash when the signal

I had a problem like this when the + strap from the battery was not 
connected to the solenoid properly (very loose connection, and since 
that's the starting point for the entire electrical system...).

I would check that and the battery ground, make sure they're nice and 
tight.
