Subject: Re: [T2] Volksfest in Ohio, anyone camping?

The 11th annual Volksfest is located east of Columbus Ohio.  Hours are 10
am - 4 pm, rain or shine.  Car show is $8 per bus and driver, $3 visitor, 12
and under free.  Admission without displaying your bus will be only $3.  No
open containers.  Not that any of us would think of that.
Directions are:  From the west:  70 east through Columbus approximately 20
miles to exit #118.  Go north approximately 5 miles to State Route 40.  Turn
right and Watkins Rd is on left.  Location is Watkins Memorial High School.
From the West:  70 West to exit 122 and go north, approximately 5 miles to
State Route 40, turn left and Watkins Rd is on your right.
There are 27 different show classes, and over 350 vehicles are expected.
You gotta come!!!!
