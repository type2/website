I've had mixed results. I noticed the cast iron pulley on a couple of old
generators but never knew they were integral to the 69-71 setup.Also the
900mm belt is a bit more difficult to find on the east coast at the various
FLAPS. Unwittingly I've used the cheap pulley on the 69-71 generator and
run into problems - but not as serious as using the wrong backing plate.
Using the wrong backing plate has been a definite no-no for me.
I had to change the backing plate on the side of the highway because of it.
The moral of the
story is: spread out a rag and examine all your parts. Ask yourself - does
this go with this? Where did this come from? What size belt does this take?
Study & research and make
lists before mis-assembling  - it's better than being heckled at a rest
stop in the middle of the night with the motor out.
