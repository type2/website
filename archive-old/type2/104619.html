>How do you tighten it up without removing the engine tin? The nut is at the bottom.

One way is to remove the fan housing.  You can then get a thin open-end wrench on the nut by inserting it from the side, under the tin.  A lot of work to remove the fan housing, but less than dropping the engine.  You could also just hand-tighten it.
