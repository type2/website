No, it will not fit in anything but a 79 Cal/80-83.5 Cal Vanagon
distributor. No way, no how. In order to use the Cal distributor you will
also need the electronic ignition coil, the module w/heatsink, and the
harness.  The coil and module is on Rabbits and Audis. The dist. and harness
is bus only.
With all the pieces it is a drop-in swap that works very well. I have 60,000
trouble-free miles on my 77 using it.
