Yes thats normal Landon
Have someone turn the engine over by hand while you watch
the valves.
Intake opens
Compression - then both valves close
Then exhaust valve opens
Each cylinder has this cycle it goes through as you probaly know.
Thats why you have to turn the engine to TDC to set the valves for
that cylinder. Both valves well be closed then on that CYLINDER
and only that cylinder. So only set the valves on one cylinder at a time 
till you move to TDC for the next cyl.
Or else you could get a tight valve and well we all know
what would soon happen after that, right class? :(
