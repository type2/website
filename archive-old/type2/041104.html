 I believe that the Agilis range replaces the older Michelin van tyres like
the MXLs and MXTs, as well as the XC-4S and XCA. There are different models
suitable for busses as follows:

 14" Rims:

185 R14C AGILIS 61 TL 99R		(6-ply rating?)
185 R14C AGILIS 81 TL 102R		(8-ply rating?)

 15" Rims:

195/70 R15  AGILIS 41 RENF TL 97S	(Reinforced: note lack of "C"
suffix)
195/70 R15C AGILIS 61 TL 100R		(6-ply rating?)
195/70 R15C AGILIS 81 TL 104R		(8-ply rating?

205/65 R15C AGILIS 51 TL 102T		(Higher speed- VR6 T4?)

 They also do some 195/75R14s (106R), if that's your thing. Check out:

	http://www.michelin_dot_ca/ca/eng/news/agilis/1.htm

 ... and links, for the full story. Interestingly, they don't seem to do
