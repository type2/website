[...]Well said, and this expresses my feelings about the subject, to a "T".

As vignet, my 14 year old son wouldn't be caught dead riding in the 71 Westy bus
a few years ago, although he did like riding in our 67 beetle.  Now, the bus is
his "transporter" of choice.  He says it's got "soul".  A few days ago, he had
some friends over, and they all went out to see the bus at one point.  My son
showed off all of the camper features.  Made me smile.

We go on several camping trips every year, one of which is 11 or 12 hours driving

time from our house.  People are amazed that we have the nerve to go that far in
such an old vehicle.  What amazes me is why anyone would want to travel in
anything less reliable and easy to fix than an ACVW, no matter how old it is.  As

long as you maintain it properly, it'll treat you right.

I get all kinds of "I used ta have one" comments from people, and all I can think

is how lucky I am that I still have my ACVWs and didn't sell them like those
people did.
