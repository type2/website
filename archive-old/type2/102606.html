On Wed, 5 Feb 2003, Peter Nielsen wrote:

> On my new 1976 van, I found they stuck the seals to the cover with silicone
> adhesive.
>
> Seems to make sense, and it wasn't THAT hard to get off.

Silicone doesn't work well in contact with oil.  If you use it and don't
get it _all_ off you will get a leak next time.

I have better luck with non-hardening sealants like Hylomar.  They're
tacky, but never need to be scraped off.  Scraping causes scratches, and
scratches cause leaks.

If you _must_ use silicone, use the blue Permatex stuff.  It peels off
easier than the black/clear/red stuff.

And, of course, never put anything on the head/gasket interface.
