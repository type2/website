Rob,

The rear most seat for a '79 is the folding rear back. I think it had
brackets to detach and fold the back down and maybe even a longer rear
carpet that covered the back of the seat. I think that they also had head
rests. I know the middle seats did. This should be '74-'79 era. The earlier
ones would probable work with some modifications of back rest brackets. The
floor mounting is the same in all years. Hope this helps.
