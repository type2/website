Subject: Re: [T2] fuel shutoff valve

Get needlenose vise-grips, and on a grinder or sanding belt, round the
edges of the tapers.  Slide rubber tubing or poly tubing over the
tapers.  This will safely compress the gas line from the tank.  Practise
on a piece of gas line to determine the minimum pressure required.
