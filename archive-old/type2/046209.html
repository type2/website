You just use paint, Ben; hit http://www.sikkens_dot_net and download the
Polysurfacer (TDS 3.5.1) Colorbuild Undercoat System (TDS 3.2.18) and
Autosurfacer 940HS (TDS 3.2.12) technical data sheets. Those are the Sikkens
primers for fiberglass.

Then, if you can't get or can't afford Sikkens paint (which is expensive),
take these to the local paint store and ask if they have anything similar.
They should.
