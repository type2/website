I'm in the midst of having a 78X88 built which works out to 1900cc.  My
engine builder, who does a lot of Berg engines, recommends thick walled 88s
(machine in) for maximum cooling.  Anything larger (P/Cs) would require an
external oil cooler.  I told him I want to go up hills in 4th gear so he
recommended single port heads with dual Kadrons which he says will draw
better and create more torque (for going up hills) than dual intake.
