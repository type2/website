BE SURE to take measurements completely around the tent so as to know how 
high the pop top erects around the entire perimeter.
e.g. ........... How long/high are the original pop top walls as measured 
completely around the erection after it is snapped into place......... Make 
the new ones the same dimension.

You have to look/plan ahead to make sure you are not too long (saggy) or 
too short (rip) on side material stapling positions.
