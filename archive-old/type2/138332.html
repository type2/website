Good aftermarket solution? Go to the grocery store and find a copy of the Street Rodder, Street Scene, etc. magazine. You'll have alot of fitting and cutting to do but I've used an aftermarket fuse panel in a street rod with excellent results.

Alternative: RV catalogs or Marine catalogs.
