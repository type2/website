I have a bubble window bed, table and rear-facing seat installed in 
my '65 deluxe.  The table and rear-facing seat are fairly trivial to 
install. (I have a walk-through which was modified from the bench 
seat arrangement by the PPO.)

The fold down bed fits in as well but does not contour perfectly with 
the lower front facing plate abutting to the walls.  The bed's a tich 
smaller.  I also didn't secure the mounting points through the wheel 
well arch.  I just hated to drill holes in the pristine metal there.  
I've been living out of mine for about a month now waiting for my new 
home to be finished.  Very comfortablly too....
