Well, you might check with the shipping dept of Solar Turbines.
They surely have big trucks going to Houston on frequent occasions.

independent hauling companies.

shipper in the midwest that made regular runs to Houston. He would load up 
my stuff and charge me just a few extra dollars to get my stuff down to me. 
He made the run each week, and it was extra money for him, at no additional 
cost to himself.
