Normal.  The input shaft is on splines inside the transmission and has a

I tend to think that "when in doubt, replace the disk."  I probably
replace them too often, but I've never worn one out and had to pull the
engine to replace it.  When installing the pressure plate, a
mini-mag-light flashlight makes a perfect pilot tool.

> The flywheel and pressure plate look and feel smooth...is it good practice

Works for me.  If the clutch was smooth before the pilot bearing problem
then the surfaces are probably fine.
