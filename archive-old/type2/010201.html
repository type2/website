The trick is called a "brake line wrench"  It looks like a box end
wrench with a bit nipped out so that you can slip it over the brake
line.  It holds the nut like a box wrench so it cannot slip.  Please
try one of these before you resort to vise-grips.  I may be the next
one to own your bus :)

I usually squirt the fitting with Liquid Wrench for two or three days
(a couple of applications per day) before I try to break them loose. 
When reinstalling, I put a dab of anti-sieze on the threads (keep it
out of the system, though!)
