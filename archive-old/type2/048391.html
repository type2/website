Subject: Re: [T2] Gas in oil, please help (long)

I would check to see if you're injectors are leaking.  Hook up a fuel
pressure tester, start the pump running (start the engine and turn it
off), and then watch the fuel pressure.  If it's dropping or doesn't
hold for at least a half hour or more, that means your leaking gas
somewhere.  Now, whether that would leak into the case through the
cylinders, I can't say, but it's a good thing to check anyways..

Mike
