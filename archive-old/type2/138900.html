there in the first place is probably a better use of the grease. Even better
dip the end, minus the threads of course, in some plastic dip that used for
coating the handles of tools.

As the clutch wears the fingers on the pressure plate move toward the
driver. This means less pedal effort is required over time but I doubt you'd
notice it especially as the cable stretches.
