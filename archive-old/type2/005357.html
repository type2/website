Yes, its true, there is a definite mathematical (not precise, though)
relationship between oil temperature, oil pressure and revs. In fact, did
you know helicopter pilots use the other two of these readings, should one
of the instruments fail? I've often pondered on the relationship, but then
it also varies with oil level, sideways acceleration (ie. going round
corners) and the condition of the engine (as well as oil grade)!

If it makes you feel better, by oil pressure was lower than yours,
consistently, and its temperature used to soar a little as well.........an
old haggard engine no doubt....but that's another story.....
