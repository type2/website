Hi Lee,

NAPA sells 2 1/4 inch fuel filler hose which is the right size for the 
71. The NAPA part # is 1055-NBH. Searching for 'fuel filler hose' in the 
archives will yield some hits suggesting various marine hoses, etc. but 
if you have a NAPA near you that is probably the easiest place to get it.
