If the spark plugs are out, it should have fairly consistent resistance.
Sounds like the maybe pressure plate or clutch disk...I think the flywheels
are pretty bullet-proof in a T4.

Since it turns now, you might try installing the starter, cranking with the
plugs out, and working the clutch (full travel) a few times while cranking.
Maybe that would help to seat things evenly? Charge the battery first.

I assume the fan and alternator are not rubbing or binding?
