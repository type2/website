Rae,
2 real possibilities.

Either the engine compression is not sufficient to hold the Bus on the 
hill (do compression test to verify) or the clutch is not fully engaged 
or is slipping.

If you were having a clutch problem I'd expect you would notice some 
slipping in normal driving as well.  So definitely do a compression 
test to see if that could be the problem.
