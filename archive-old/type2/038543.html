Well, those tire rating schemes seem to change on a yearly basis, but the
one number that really says it all is the "MAX LOAD" number.  If it's
1540lb or larger you're in business, according to my owner's manual.

The other numbers (92, load range, etc.) seem to vary with tire size.  For
example, a 195/75R14 in load range C has less load carrying capacity than
a 185R14 _standard_ load tire!
