Dave, and everyone else too,

   " It all started a couple of weeks ago when I was taking my daughter to a 
concert.  Pushed the clutch in, there was a faint 'ping' and the pedal felt 
looser than before.  The following day I had a job interview and just as I

greasing, and replacement if needed.  That way I get to decide where and 
when I have to deal with them.  And of course I have spares on board, just 
in case.
