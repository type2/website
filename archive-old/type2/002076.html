Unless you have California emmissions (which requires a cat), you can just
replace your stock setup with the similar-to-stock-looking Pacesetter
exhaust system.  This system costs $79 complete, has the port for the egr
filter, and does away with the crossover pipe completely.  Not quite as
substantial as the German system, but not too bad for the price, and roughly
equally quiet.
