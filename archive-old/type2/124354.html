> Quick question: Will the generator, generator stand,

> dog house fan shroud? I am getting a rebuilt 1600cc DP
> engine to put in my 68 bus. I wanted to use the
> generator, stand, and fan from the old SP engine with
> a set of dog house tin and shroud, but I wanted to

I converted my 70 bus to doghouse recently. Used original 70 generator and
stand and tin at end of generator. Fan housing and wide fan and doghouse
tin were from a later type I. If the DP engine is a Type I you will need a
larger/bus tin piece at the front (flywheel side) to fit the bus body.
