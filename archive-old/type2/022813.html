Hi- Only things I can think of are that a wheel cylinder is sticking (rear
brakes)  or that the rear brake return spring(s)  have lost their pulling
power !! - these should be replaced when you do the shoes - I'm not sure that
everyone does but I guess one day they will give up. Maybe it is difficult,
but can you jack up the rear wheels when the pedal is "wooden" and see if the
brakes are binding- that at least will narrow down the problem to the front or
rear ?.
