Subject: Re: [T2] spare tire well water/rust

Here are some possibilities which may cause water in the spare wheel well.
Perished seal to rear side window.
Perished seal to rear hatch window.
Perished rear hatch seal
Incorrectly adjusted rear hatch lock so that hatch does not shut firmly.
Leaking pop top.
Penetrating rust in the gutter.
Penetrating rust in side window frame
Penetrating rust in rear hatch window frame.
and dont forget the obvious leaking bottle of water stored in luggage area!
