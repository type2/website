Yes, the wire "bails"  ( or bales?)  wear out over time and lose their
tension.   Replacing them helped immensely and stop my valve cover gasket
leaks.  Also, check to see if the valve covers are warped by putting them on
a flat surface;  I used a old metal office desk that in my barn.
