Pardon me for dispensing unwanted advice.  But I'm gonna do it anyway.  I'm 
sure that you can find some low prices for that seal.  But remember that low

monoxide to enter the passenger cabin.  Need I go further?

talking to Ron Salmon.  There's too much to risk here just for the sake of 
the lowest price.
