Mates!

Identified two causes to the light symptoms:
(1) - Rear: corroded ground terminals.  Fixed!
(2) - Front: used a too-long screw a couple of days before when I replaced a
headlight beauty ring.  It drove right through the harness, literally split
the high-beam wire.  Big short!  Cut the wires, screw-on connectors &
electrical tape, re-installed everything (w/ the correct screw), replaced
the *melted* #5 fuse, and now everything's cheeky!

Lessons learned:
Use the right screws for the right job.  And clean terminals every so often!
