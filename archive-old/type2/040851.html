Craig,

	Have you observed that the oil light gets the negative polarity in the
wire coming from the oil pressure sender? Try connecting the buzzer this
way: Connect the negative wire (black) to the oil light sender wire and the
positive (red) to a fuse wich is after the ignition lock. 
Don't connect it between the sender and the light, put it paralell with the
light! This way you have them both working simultaneously, the best of both
worlds!
 I use a door buzzer in paralell with the oil light on my roofless bug, the
sunshine outpowers the oil light :-)
