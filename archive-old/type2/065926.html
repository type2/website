Sounds like a master cylinder to me  (seals starting to leak usually). 
Also, if you recently rebuilt your brakes, there will likely be lots of 
air, so just keep bleeding the brakes - and do it in order of farthest 
line away from master cylinder to the closet line to the master cylinder, 
and then bleed the master cylinder itself.
