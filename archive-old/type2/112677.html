What I see here is:
1)  There is a spectrum for owners ranging from the just keeping it 
running don't care how it looks to the only drive it on sunny days, in 
the morning.
2)  Owners who would rather work extra to pay for the repair/paint/body

bus every Sunday evening - but what happens the next day happens.
