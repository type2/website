Steve and list:
Not quite right.
68 and 69 are the same (and will bolt into a 64-67 if you want ball
joints.)
70 through 79 are the same pattern EXCEPT 70's in the U.S. usually don't
have a vacuum booster, so you'd have to add or remove the bracket
depending on which way you are going. 71 and 72 have different spindles,
calipers and rotors (smaller).
