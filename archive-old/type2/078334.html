Hard to say because we all have our own ideas about money and what things are
worth. I paid $3200 for a 78 westy deluxe in good condition with 130k miles. I
spent $3000 more putting in a new engine, starter, bellows, one flapper box,

from reputable builder) which means I have at least 100 hours invested.  Note
that I have not gone thru the brakes, suspension, etc, which means that the
work and expense are not yet done. I like what I now have and consider the
total expense to be reasonable.

If the bus in question is as good mechanically as it seems to be cosmetically
then $6000 is reasonable. At least to me.
