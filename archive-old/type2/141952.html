>several controls on the carbs,

http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/DualCarbs.html

>and a vacuum
>switch on the roof of the engine compartment.

The unit on the roof is the vacuum advance cutoff:

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/VacuumHosesCarb/VacAdvCutoff.jpg

It's described in Bentley as disabling the vacuum advance while in the lower 3 gears. It was an emission device and hurts the engine in my opinion.

>Also, there is a vacuum "biscuit" on each of
>the intake manifolds.  What are these for?

I think those are the vacuum based temperature controls for the EGR pipes. See Bentley. If you don't have one, you should get one asap as it's much better than any other VW bus repair manual.
