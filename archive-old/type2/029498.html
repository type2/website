My gut feeling is that the engine bay is at slight negative pressure.

The "scoops" on bay window buses are actually pointed more to the side
than the front, so there's little ram-air effect.

Also, the scoops don't extend very far out from the body, so the intake is
in the stagnation region, again with little ram effect
