The Type 4 engine, definitely. It's a bigger engine, hence doesn't need to
work as hard to push the Bus to the same speed as the Type 1 engine will.
And I think the Type 4 engine is prettier than the Type 1 engine...since
appearance is important for this car, pretty is good. Get the fan shroud
polished and the tin painted the same color as the body, and it will be very
impressive looking. Add to that the stronger bottom end...

Now bear in mind that Type 4 engines are more expensive than Type 1 engines,
but it's worth it.
