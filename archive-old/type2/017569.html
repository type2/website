Actually I have seen a Bus down in southern Mexico that was set-up as a Movie
Bus...  this old feller rode around to different villages stopping for a day or
two showing 2 movies each night (this was early '70's).  High roof Bus with a
Pull down silver screen on the driver's side and a little table for the 16mm
projector.  Inside he also had a little gas generator to run the projector in
really remote/isolated areas...  I used to spend summers with my Grandfather down
in Southern Mexico and Central America.  Apparently there were several of these
traveling movie Busses at the time...

If it is one of these, it would be a shame to gut it....
