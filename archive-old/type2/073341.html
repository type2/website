>    Hello Everyone, when I pulled the valve covers off my heads during
>disassembly I found no retaining springs holding the pushrod tubes.  Is their
>absence highly unusual?  The tubes stayed in place fine without them (they
>would not push through the heads with considerable force).  Will it be
>necessary to replace the springs during reassembly?  I have a 1700 engine.
>Any ideas appreciated.

Since VW designed that engine and found it necessary to include the
retaining wires ("parts left out cost nothing and cause no service
problems") I would not run without them except in an emergency.  If this is
such a situation, I would at least use some silicone on the pushrod tube
seals to lessen the chance that a pushrod tube will come loose.

Whether or not you remove the silicone when you obtain the wires is up to
you.  Although I am in favor of NOT putting the silicone on in ordinary
circumstances, it may not be worth your trouble to remove it, especially if
you have no leaks.
