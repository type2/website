Subject: RE: [T2] Storage/Prep Oil Question

Part manufacturers have some sort of spray grease they put on their
parts to prevent them from corroding while in storage.  Find the
thickest oil or spray grease you can and give it a good dousing.  White
lithium grease is the easiest product to find that will adhere to parts
and not drip away.  Also put the engine in a big garbage bag and tie the
opening off.  This should help to prevent any condensation, dust or
rodents from getting into it.
