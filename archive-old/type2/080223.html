Jim,
Just read this month's Hot VW's issue, and they tested a single quiet
pack, a dual quiet pack, and a fat boy (page 60).  Engine was a mild
1641, 009, dual Brosol/Solex 40 carbs, timing _at_ 30 deg advance, 130 mains,
45 idles.

Single:  81.9 hp _at_ 4500 rpm
Dual: 82.8 hp _at_ 4500 rpm
Fat Boy:  84.6 hp _at_ 4500 rpm.

I run a single quiet pack and a stinger on occasion.  The single is nice
and quiet, stinger is obnoxious.  Seat of the pants, the single QP gives
me enough backpressure for the hills, lower rpm stuff.  For being loud
and on flat land, the stinger is more fun.
