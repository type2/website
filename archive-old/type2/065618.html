> My accelerator pedal stick on a little from time to time.  It never stick
> full throttle, but usually just above idle.  I can manually reach down and
> pull it up to drop the idle down.  Is there a spring that returns it to 
> the idle position on the foot pedal, fuel injection, or both?  Maybe it
> just needs lubrication since it goes a little hard at first.
> Any ideas,
> Brett
> 

ALmost for sure you have GUNK sticking your throttle. The most likely 
places are at the exit of the ACCELERATOR cable and the throttle 
linkage and return spring. First, disconnect the throttle cable and 
check to make sure the carb itself is returning the throttle plate 
to idle setting. If that is just fine, it is probably excessive dirt 
and grease holding and sticking the cable.
