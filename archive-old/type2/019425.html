On of my local lumberyards carries 1/8" birch veneer plywood in 4 X 8 ft
sheets.  Look for a lumberyard that caters to cabinet makers - they should have
it.  I have not checked, and my bus is at home, but if the ceiling panel needs
to be more than 4 ft wide, a seam with a 1/8" ply cleat behind it to reinforce
it could be done.  Use thin A (cyanoacrylate) adhesive, available at any hobby
shop that sells RC airplane stuff, for an instant and permanent bond.
