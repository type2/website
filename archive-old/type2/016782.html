Reground cams are a cost effective choice when rebuilding your
engine.

The purpose of the cam is to open the valves a certain amount at
at certain time.  The amount of lift and the timing can be ground
with a smaller circumference. All that you do is adjust the rockers
to take up for the reduced clearance on the non-lift portion of
the cam.
