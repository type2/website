Whoa, stop....
What is the distributor part # in your bus?
Is it a single vacuum advance unit or a dual unit?
A dual unit will time at 5 after IF you have manifold vacuum at the inner
nipple at idle. This will be dist. # 021 905 205S or 021 905 205AB or 022
905 205AB or 021 905 205AC or 022 905 205AC.
A single advance dist. will time at 7.5 before. This will be # 021 905 205P
or 022 905 205S.
Do NOT try to run a single advance distributor at 5 after.
You cannot go by engine number, you have to go by the distributor you have.
If you have a dual unit and it has manifold vacuum on the retard side at
idle and you have the timing set at 5 after.... disconnect the vacuum and
you will find your timing is now 7.5 before. [If you have # 021 905 205N,
you would set initial at 10 after because retard vacuum is 16-18 degrees,
still would be 7.5 before without vacuum]
