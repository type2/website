Subject: Re: [T2] Strange Ignition Issue

I'm guessing that your distributor might be worn out - does the distributor
shaft have side-to-side (radial) play? Up-and-down (axial) play can usually be
adjusted with shims added to the shaft. Does the vacuum advance operate freely
when you suck hard on the vacuum port? Are the centrifugal advance weights
lubricated and swinging freely in the bottom of the distributor?
