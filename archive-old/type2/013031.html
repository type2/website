>From Olympia and the South:


Take I-5 North to Highway 512, Exit 127. 
Follow Highway 512 east for 10 miles to the Meridian Street
South Exit. Turn left under the freeway. The Fairgrounds
are on the left. (Note: During the Fair, you may be
required to turn right onto Meridian Street at the end of
the freeway ramp. If so, go to the first signal (15th
Avenue), and turn right again at 5th Street. The back side
of the Fairgrounds is on the right side of the street.
Official parking will be visible from there. 
OR
Take I-5 North to Highway 512, Exit 127. 
Follow Highway 512 east for 8 miles to the exit marked 9th
St. SW / 94th Ave. E. 
Turn left over the freeway and go down the hill for 1.4
miles. 
Turn right on 15th Avenue SW. 
Turn left on 5th Street. 

>From Seattle and the North:


Take I-5 South to the Puyallup Exit #142B in Federal Way.
As exiting, stay in the left lane. 
Turn right at the traffic light at the end of the exit
ramp, then turn left at the first traffic light onto
Highway 161. 
Travel approximately eight miles into the Puyallup Valley.
(Highway 161 becomes Meridian Street in Puyallup.) Continue
south through town. The fairgrounds will be on your right
at 9th Avenue S.W. 
OR
Take I-5 South to the I-405 North interchange, exit 154A,
to Renton. 
Take Exit 2 to Highway 167 toward Kent/Auburn. Travel South
on Highway 167 for approximately 20.2 miles to Highway 161
South/Highway512 West. 
Travel 2.3 miles to the second exit, Meridian Street South.
At the light at the end of the exit turn right. The
fairgrounds are on the left. 

>From Bellevue and the Eastside:

Kent/Auburn.

on Highway 167 for approximately 20.2 miles to Highway 161
South/Highway512 West.
