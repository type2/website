At 09:13 AM 10/10/2000 -0700, you wrote:
> can I fix a double relay or do I need to get a new one? 

If you take off the case, you may well be able to see the heat stressed
points. If you can file the points perfectly, they then have much less mass
to absorb heat and will fry in a few days or weeks, that is, if you can
somehow get the tension of the copper strip reset right. 95% of the time
this is a total waste of time/money. 

Buy a new one, or get a salvage part and hope for reasonable life. Same
double relay will be found in F.I. Beattles.

failure.
