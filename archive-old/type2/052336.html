I just replaced the fuel pump on my 71 because the diaphram was
leaking.  When
I drained the oil, five quarts came out instead of the usual two and a
half.
When I took off the fuel pump, I still had both fuel lines connected and
could
see fuel dripping from the base of the pump which was an obvious sign to
me
that the diaphram was leaking.  I installed a new Bocar pump that cost
$15.
