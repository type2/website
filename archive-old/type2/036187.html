Subject: Re: Why not jar cylinders loose?

Sometimes the cylinders will stick more to a head than to the case and come
loose anyway when the head is pulled. To avoid this, you can hold a cylinder
in place by clamping a vice-grip on a stud, between two cylinder cooling
fins.
