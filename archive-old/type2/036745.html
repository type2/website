VDO has a temp sender for the sump plate. It involves drilling a hole
in your sump plate (not to be confused with the oil strainer plate).
I bought a pre-drilled one from bus boys. I didn't want to screw my
original one up by drilling the hole in the wrong place. (try
searching on sender AND sump for more info on this).
