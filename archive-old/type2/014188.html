Well, it all depends upon how readily parts are available by parcel in OZ, and how much time you are willing to wait to get back on the 
road if you break.  A partial list of stuff to carry would include:

Front and rear wheel bearings
CV joint and boot
Carburator, complete
Distributor, complete
Coil
Generator brushes
voltage regulator
Fuel pump
2 spare tires.
small floor jack
Socket for 46mm nuts.
Brake pads and shoes
throttle cable
clutch cable
asperin
a good book.

I'm sure that there is more and others will fill it in.  Nothing is better than the feeling of breaking something and having a replacement 
available from your parts box.
