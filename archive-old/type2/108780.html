> Has anyone ever tried those LED cluster bulb replacements for tail light
bulbs?  They are supposed to be brighter and turn on faster.
> Autozone has them for $16.00 a pair.  Are they worth it? The rear lights
on my 71 could use more brightness.  Do you have to make any changes, other
than the bulbs, to use them?   I bought them off ebay for $14 a pair.  I

My originals are brighter.  The way the LED's work is they aim all the light
out the lens as opposed to the originals that use the reflective back of the
housing to increase brightness.

The LED's themselves are brighter and faster than the original bulbs. I
installed one LED and one original in my 71 Single cab and hit the brakes at
night.  The original bulb was much brighter than the LED.

As for changes made, none it is a straight swap.
