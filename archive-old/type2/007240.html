In my own experience after filling the tank to the top and driving it I would
get leakage due to the fact that I parked sideways on a hill with the gas tank
facing down. Turns out the seal on the gas cap isn't as good as it could be and
gas was leaking past the seal. If this is the case with your bus (the leakage is
only after filling up, your seal isn't the best, only leaks while parked on
hills) either get a new gas cap or don't fill the tank so much or don't park on
hills. If this isn't the problem then I don't know what to say.
