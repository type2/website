Stainless harware is not always better. SS tends to be a lot softer, less strong, 
than original or 'standard'  hardware, of course you can get SS in several strenght 
classes, but still it's a thing to keep in mind. If you have a VW or Porsche Dealer 
in your area just order original, if you just buy a couple of nuts and bolt at a time 
it'll be just as expensive, and in a lot of cases even cheaper, I sell original M6
Porsche washers for less than $0.03. Keep everything greasy, even if it's only the 
part of the bolt or nut that you don't see.
