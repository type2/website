OK, just to clarify, please note that _none_ of the axial motion of
the CVs is due to the splines.  Even with the washers removed it's a
pretty tight fit between the shaft and the CV splines.

Rather, the play is designed into the CV joints themselves.  They
allow the axle to bend up and down (doh!) and also to move in and out
to compensate for the change in distance from the trans to hub as the
wheel moves up and down.
