On Thu, 6 May 1999 ZR72_at_aol_dot_com wrote:

> how can i find out if the case has been align bored??  i have  a 
> micrometer to mic the crank, etc.  But dont I also need to check the case??  
> If this is not necessary let me know and I can get started on this project.

When you pull the case apart, look at the back side of the bearings.  If 
they are other than standard bearings they should be so marked, 
indicating undersize for crank turnings and oversize for align boring.  
However, that only tells you what was there and doesn't tell you if 
everything is still within the new spec.  Either take the case to a shop 
to have it measured or invest in an inside micrometer to take the 
measurements yourself.
