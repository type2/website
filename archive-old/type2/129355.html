Subject: Re: [T2] Can't get lug nuts off!

I reccomend using a socket with a breaker bar. Once you have the socket 
on the lug and a good amount of force on the breaker bar, hit the 
breaker bar where it connects to the socket with a sledge hamner. The 
direction of the hit should be towards the brake drum perfectly 
parralell to the axis of the wheel stud. A couple of good whacks with a 
heavy sledge while applying a good amount of torque with the breaker bar 
should break them loose. Hope this helps - Jeff
