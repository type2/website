Check to see if you have a fabric outlet in your area. Reupholstery 
shops sell it at a premium, but not too bad a price if you can get by 
with "scraps". They use it for tops. Some RV centers carry a supply, 
used for awnings, and the like. I've even seen it for sale at some 
fabric stores.
