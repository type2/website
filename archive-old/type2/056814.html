Check your fuel pressure.  If your pressure regulator has gone south, 
the symptoms you describe can be the result.

If you find that your fuel pressure rapidly drops to 0 after turning off 
the engine, then get uder the car with your vice-grips, sized to gently 
squeeze closed the fuel return line, and have someone start up the car 
again.  The instant they turn it off, clamp down on the vice-grips.  If 
your fuel pressure stays high (or falls to 0 much more slowly than 
before) you've found your problem.
