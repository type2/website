Jeff,

Sami Dahklia came up with the *best* idea for backing out stuck
adjusters...

disassemble the brake shoes;
use a big screwdriver or vise grip to remove the center "bolt" portion of
the adjuster;
then put an M10x50 bolt in the adjuster star and crank away!

when the bolt bottoms out, it will either twist the star loose or it will
force it up and out. either is fine.
