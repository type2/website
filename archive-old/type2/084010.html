I put '79 Front disc brakes on the '69 front beam.
You will have to make/weld a bracket to hold the power booster
Whole thing works great and stops my '60 DC like right NOW.
Stay away from '71 as I believe those are one year only parts

and still have the old wide five bolt pattern on the rear.  You have
to decide which spare to carry.
