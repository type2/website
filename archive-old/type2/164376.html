VW was correct.

Whoever you talked to at Bus Depot is WRONG!

All FI aircooled engines use the phenolic block with paper on each side for
intake gaskets.

Part number 022 129 707 F  You need them.

Go to the Bus Depot link here:
http://www.busdepot_dot_com/details.jsp?partnumber=022129707F
and read at the bottom what it fits!
