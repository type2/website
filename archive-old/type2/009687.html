David:

Sounds like good advise.  The best thing to do is use a stock Doghouse
system with the thermostat system intact.  Use an oil temp and cylinder
temp Gauge to make sure everything is staying within a safe temp range.  If
you find that the oil temp is climbing above the safe range occasionally
under harsh circumstances (extremely hot weather, hill climbs, etc.) then
adding an additional oil cooler inline after your full flow filter with an
oil thermostat and a pressure regulating pump cover might be in order to
stablize things.
