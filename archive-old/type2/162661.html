real competition (Corvans', and Econolines) but in the 70's a few things
changed,.   one the chicken wars caused the "Truck" versions to get a very
very high import tax, reason they stopped importing the pickup truck (even
built one in the USA on the golf chasis in PA)

first new bus in 71 the dollar was worth 4 German marks, just before
Germany went to the Euro the dollar was only worth about 1.6 German Marks.
