John:
You're right about the under cylinder tins and the ducts to the heater
flappers, but there are 4 other parts you'll need also: L & R lower
front cylinder tins and the support brackets  at the muffler- the left
is just a link w/2 holes that goes from the fan housing (?) to the
muffler joint, the right is actually the alternator adjustment bracket
as well and its lower end supports the muffler. I'd say without these
two additional supports the exchangers will probably droop and cause
leaks at the cylinder head- heat exchanger joint (which is always a leak
prone area anyhow!)
