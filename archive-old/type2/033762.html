There are steering stabilizers like used on RVs that control sway from road
wind from passing big rigs but I  don't know if there is anything specific
to VW buses. Gusting wind is bad. Your bus is like a big balloon blowing in
the air.  Camping World is a huge national RV (KMart like) store and they do
phone catalogue sales. Their number is 1-800-626-5944 or
www_dot_campingworld_dot_com. See if they have a VW application for a steering
stabilizer and consider it.
