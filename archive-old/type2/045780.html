Anyone needs any of this stuff, please pmail me. I got dibs on one set
of beetle popouts.

As for the bus... Its a '73. Bodywise, it's ok. It has both bumpers, all
seats. Needs: windshield (cracked), tires, the part on the dash that
holds the steering column in place (and turn signal assy. and ignition

access door, the bolt that holds the fan hub on... It is missing a few
minor things, but you'd have to have this much just to drive it. I
haven't had a chance to check the condition of the engine or carbs.

This is a project. I would rather sell it as a parts bus to someone who
can use it, rather than try to get it back on the road, but if you
really want a '73 and are pretty ambitious, it *could* be brought back
to life. You can do it, not me. I have enough stuff to do around here.
