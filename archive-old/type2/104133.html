> Yea, this one again. Could somebody out there give me a definitive answer to the age old question: Does the
engine seal go in glossy side up (facing the top of the engine) or down? And whichever way it's supposed to
be, could I also get a reasonable explanation as to why?

water when the tires throw it up. The gloss side shuns water.
