Another "better living through plumbing" tip for freeing *heavily* rusted
fasteners,
pipe fittings and etc. is soldering flux.  Apply.   Heat.  Repeat as
necessary.  The preferred brand is Laco - it's some of the more agressive
flux out there.  Flux is nasty and pretty caustic, especially after
heating. Clean all remnants up.  I can't recall the last time I used this
trick on car parts.........should work though.
