Subject: Re: [T2] rust removal & paint job???

The reason the primer is rusting is the main ingrediants in primer are
clay, and talic----which hold moisture.
It rains out, the primer holds the moisture, causing the underlying
metal to rust.

I would approach the problem like ths----get a 6" air obital sander ( a
DA ) and run it over the whole body with 180 paper, and seal off the
sanded area with two part epoxy sealer / primer.

This will hold off the metal corrosion, until it's time to paint.

Happy sanding----
