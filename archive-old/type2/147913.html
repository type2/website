There is a VW bulletin floating around where they specify not using a head 
gasket on rebuilt engines. However, to maintain the compression ratio you 
need to put a shim under the cylinders. Some have also said to lap the 
cylinders into the head to prevent leaks. In your case, I would use a head 
gasket if there is already one and save the cylinder lapping for your next 
rebuild! ;)
