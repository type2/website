Do NOT confuse these ports with EGR. THESE ARE NOT EGR PORTS! They are AIR
INJECTION PORTS.
EGR is Exhaust Gas Recirculation. Exhaust flows from the exhaust manifold
thru an EGR valve and then INTO the intake system to dilute the fuel mixture
in order to lower the combustion temps to lower NoX emissions.
Air injection is fresh air that is injected INTO the exhaust near the ports
to complete the burn in order to lower HC.
