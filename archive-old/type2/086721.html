There are a few routes you can take depending on where you are trying to
get. I personally am a fan of the stock exhaust stsyems. All of the
aftermarket extractor/quiet pac/monza style systems I have bought were
cheap. Both in price and quality. If I were in your shoes I would consider
getting the Stainless Steel setup from Bust Depot. I think  you will have
to go to earlier (72-77?) heat exchangers but I have heard lots of good
talk about this system. You will find it hard to get some of the stock '78
stuff but the earlier parts are pretty easy to come by. It all depends on
how shot your heat exchangers are. If they are in good shape you can
probably save some dough by just replacing the elbows and muffler. Try Bus
Boys Inc. for prices too.
     One last peice of advice for they novice exhaust enthusiast. If you
are going to remove the entire system including the manifolds from the
heads be very careful with the 8 nuts (4 on each head) which hold the
manifolds to the head. If your exhaust is that rusty these fasteners are
likely to be toast. If you break a stud here it can be very tricky to fix
without dismantling the motor. Use lots of your favorite Liquid Wrench/
Holy Water and soak them overnight.
