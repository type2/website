Subject: Re: [T2] How many 34PICT-3 designs are there?

Hi Richard,

According to Dennis Jolliffe, my local Solex Expert, there are 
differences in each year of German carbs, and they are different from 
the Brazilian models as well. Not only did each carb change from 1972, 
73 and 74 model years, but there was also a different design for 
automatic as compared to the manual trannies. Dennis has quite a few of 
these, and has found a local shop that will press new bushings into the 
throttle bodies.

One afternoon last summer Dennis showed me 4 different variations on 
the right hand (1-2 side) carb, and I have no reason to doubt that 
there are more out there. The left carb seems to be a bit more 
standardized.

The gaskets in the rebuild kits are often missing holes for passages, 
and have holes for passages that aren't there, until you look at one of 
the other carbs.

Fuel injection is a picnic in comparison. Once you get it running, that 
is.
