> If you have one of these carbs, pean that plug better, ASAP.

The common repair for this is to clean the plug (which is usually
found somewhere in the tin) and the carb very well with spray carb
cleaner and paper towels and then to use fuel proof epoxy to hold the
plug in place. I usually do this the first time the carb is off the
engine so that I can place the plug horizontal so that the epoxy stays
put better.
