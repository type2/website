The one I got from JCW a few years ago came with instructions to call the
manufacturer for a special diode, and wire the isolater differently, if used
in an old VW.  I did so, and it worked for less than a year and failed.  The
manufacturer exchanged it, the second one failed several month later, then
they exchanged it again and the third one failed.  At this point I bought a
much heavier duty one from an RV place (rated at over 2x the battery
amperage), and it, too, failed after several months.  I don't know why the
failure rate was so high, but at around that time I sold the bus anyway,
sans isolator, so it became a moot point for me.
