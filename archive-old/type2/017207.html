Matthew, Rachel:
I've found a big help in adjusting valves is to get a screwdriver with a
cross-handle on it. This exact tool happens to be the chain adjusting
tool that came with my Stihl 020 chainsaw. I'm quite certain that anyone
can purchase such a tool at your friendly Stihl chainsaw dealer for
about $6.00 US. It's just a medium blade screwdriver with a combo
sparkplug socket-13mm. socket welded across the end to form the handle.
Using the cross-handle allows a little more leverage and much more
control of small movements of the screwdriver blade. (Those screws tend
to be a bit sticky.) It's also a handy 13mm. socket wrench. It's made in
Germany, so it should feel some extra kinship while lying under your
seat with the lug wrench.(For those of you who feel that wrench karma is
important.)
If this helps an old salt such as myself, I'm certain it should make the
job easier for a novice.
