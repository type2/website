The correct fluid for our transmissions is 80W90 with a GL-4 specification.
GL-5 is the newer version but has EP(extreme pressure) properties that make 
it "too slick" and may also be harmful to some of the metals inside the 
transmission.
It is incorrect for our transmissions.

I was just at Pep Boys, and they carry a GL-4 80W90 gear oil.  Forget the 
brand off the top of my head.  $2.99 a quart or they sell it by the gallon.

You will need approx 2.5 qts.   Don't forget; pull the add plug first, before 
draining.
