Usually CHEAP vise grips slip off. I found out years ago that with vise
grips, you get what you pay for. I no longer buy any other than Vise Grip
brand. Even Craftsman are junk, but a lot better than the China/Taiwan crap.
Put the allen socket/wrench in the bolt head as deep and tight as you can.
Then put the vise grips on the head of the bolt and squeeze them as tight as
you can.
