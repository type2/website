Subject: Re jacking up the bus +cable bypass to heat flapper box

Best way I've found is to drive the rear onto ramps and then jack up the 
front to level, using jackstands in front and jack left in place.

On daughter's bus one guide tube for heater cable is impossible; I ran a 
wire from the flapperbox to an eyebolt in the stiffener for the rear 
jackpoint- the wire ends in a 1" keyring - she pulls it to open the heat 
and wraps it around the eyebot shaft.  Thus she has 50% heat adjustable 
to 100% - now who will claim this is an imbalance to engine cooling?
