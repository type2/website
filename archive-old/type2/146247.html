the hole in the back of the divider on the seat platform where the retractor
gizmo is mounted turns out to be something that the PO (or someone else since

bought it in 1978.  He must have also put in seats with the head rests from a

head rests were the norm for this year and that my other '71 without them was

manual reveals only manual belt instructions and the photos of the seats in
the manual show no head rests at all.

Learn something new every day, even about a vehicle I've been driving (and
personally repairing) since the Carter Administration.  Thanks!
