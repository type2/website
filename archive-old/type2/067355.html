I think it's a heat shield.  It's a very hard to find piece, because they
tended to rust out and fall off.  I ran without one for a while and it
eventually caused my foam engine seal to get crispy on the lower parts in
the back.  I'm sure it affected the temperature of the air entering the
engine, too.  Good thing to have, if you can get it.
