I also put anti-sieze all over everything with one additional step.  After putting a good bit into the hole, I unscrewed the "bolt" nearly all the way from the star and filled the threaded hole in the star with anti-sieze also.  Then I put the assembly into the hole and turned the "bolt" into the star, forcing the anti-sieze out the bottom.  The idea was that this would fill the cavity underneath the star beyond capacity, forcing it up the sides and ensuring that the star/bolt assembly was *ahem*... as lubricated as possible.
