Uh, not really.....
1) the center-to-center nipple measurement of the 68-70 masters are 100 mm.  Vs 85 mm on the 71-79. 69-70
lower reservoirs are crap material.

2) 71 and up masters for bolting up to boosters have an extension AND a square groove for the o-ring. 68-79
have neither. You would have a massive vacuum leak.

3) 71 and up masters are designed for disc in the front/drum rear. The chamber is larger for the disc
calipers. They have more volume, therefore the master volume is larger.
