Hi Folks,

I thought you all would get a kick out of this.  Yesterday, I was out for a 
drive in my 1979 Westy, and I got pulled over for speeding!  The officer was

also a good check for my speedometer -- it was reading 75 when I passed the 
officer.

Fortunately, I got off with a written warning, but I now have radar 
confirmation and written proof that my Bus will as fast as a "normal" car!
