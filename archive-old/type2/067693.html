Subject: Prelim report on GEX engine.

After finding a crack in the case of my 1600 D/P, I decided to get
a rebuilt from GEX, in Baldwin Park, CA (East of L.A. 15-20 miles
or so).

Price:  $619.00  (+150 core charge, which I MAY get a little back)

Guarantee:  1 year or 12K miles, parts AND labor _at_ $36.00 an hour.

Engine probs:  Only prob was the oil cooler stand area (part of the
               case.) 3 holes for the offset part, the "stud" hole
               was stripped, had to be tapped.  Also, the crank
               bearings were .60, which means they are maxed out.
               All else seems fine.

First Driving: All's quiet and oil staying fresh-looking.  Temps
               are cool.

Nice Plus:  Mention the Internet when you order and they toss in
            a Bentley manual or a Muir for your year.

They have an Webpage at (from memory)  www.gexintl_dot_com/  or search
for gex engines...

So Far So Good.  Folks were very friendly.
