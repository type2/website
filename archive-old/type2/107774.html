An update to my Newbie question regarding type 4 case welding.  Thanks for
the feedback regarding the weldabilty of the crank case.  I got the tab
welded back on (so-so job :( and took the case, crank and rods to the local
VeeDub machine shop for fitting of bearings.  I hope all goes well with the
case knock on wood, I do have an old AW case to fall back on if required.

serviceable or not.  My camshaft and solid lifters were pronounced DOA so I

out for.  I've replaced all my shift rod bushings and rear fork (had to use
