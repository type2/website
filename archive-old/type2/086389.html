Kyle,

Check the easy stuff first. Check that all your vacuum lines are plugged in.
Point gap is a huge culprit. Get yourself a set of feeler gauges and a flat
blade screw driver. Set the gap to .4-.5mm.  A timing light is a necc. bus
owner tool. Check your timing 5 degrees after TDC if Cali bus, if not Cali.
then 7.5 degrees before TDC. Another guy is the cylinder head temp sensor
front left on cylinder head. This doesn't sound like it but make sure its
not loose.
