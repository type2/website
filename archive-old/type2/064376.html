Phil,

If this is happening primarily going into first gear I'd be you have an oily
clutch (probable cause is the main oil seal on the front of the engine or
the transmission input shaft seal--you can tell what's leaking by smelling
the residue--mmmmm greasy residue..).  If this is the case, when you do
replace your seal(s) and clutch, make sure you check the crankshaft endplay
as excessive endplay is one cause of the premature demise of the main oil seal.
