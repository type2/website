Another trick that we use, is to either put some vaseline, or thick grease
on the seal while installing into the head, or to superglue the seal to the
headpipe, in just a couple of places, very sparingly.

Always, Always, Always file the headpipes true with a large flat file before
install, you may save an exhaust leak,.
