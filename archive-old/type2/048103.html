Yes, that is their intended use.

The intended use is as follows: 
measure the wide part of the slip in repair tube, and cut your heaterbox
off so that when this is slipped in it will have the same overall tube
length.

Slip in the repair section.

Weld it in place, and you are done...

I bought them at my VWFLAPS here in town, any VW shop should be able to
get them. Like $1 or $2 a piece if i remeber right.
