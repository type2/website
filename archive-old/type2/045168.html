the trick I use for sticky tubes is simplicity itself.

use a pair of gas pliers (these are the standard type in everyones
toolbox) open em to the larger setting and grab the tubes with the round
part of the pliers all the way at the base near the case, in fact have
them touching the case and grip firmly but gently, wrap a rag around the
tube to protect it too if you want (I use the rubber jar openers)

tilt the pliers towards the center of the engine and the tube should pop
out


              __________|
   engine    |          |head
    case     |----------|<----push rod tubes
             |#      
      ______ |#
              # <--- pliers
           <--# 
      push this way
