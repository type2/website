Subject: Re: [T2] Type 4 Push Rod Tube Install hints ??

At 08:06 PM 10/11/2002, you wrote:

>Hi, just wondering if there is a trick or something in sliding the push rod
>tubes on a 2 L Type 4 engine.   There is that sharp edge there, not 
>tapered on the
>head. .... that edge was catching it and difficult to push in.

I use a triangular scraper to shave off those sharp edges, then while 
installing, use a very small screwdriver to work around the seal, tucking 
it in. Use no sealant on those o-rings, just some light oil. Silicone 
grease would also be ok. They have to move without binding due to heat 
expansion.
