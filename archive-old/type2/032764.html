A flat spot in acceleration can also be caused by a flat spot in the 
cam.  I'm not sure if this is what you are experiencing... But it is 
possible.  It can also be worn lifters.  If you are over 100 Grand in miles 
with out a rebuild and you have a habit of not tuning up often, changing 
oil or treating you motor with respect... it could be an internal problem.
I do suggest looking at the comments another person posted mentioning the 
jets of the cabrs first... and if that doesn't help... It might be time to 
look inside the case for the problem.
