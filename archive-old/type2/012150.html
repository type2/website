James,

$200 - not likely, and if so, not a good welder. Now
for $300-$350 you can get a Lincoln SP-100, which is a
nice wire-feed welder, which is convertible to MIG.
The MIG kit will cost you another $100, and the gas
and bottle another $100 beyond that, but is well worth
it. 

A lot of local community colleges have welding
classes, and if you've never done it, it's well worth
the time and money to take one. 

-John Cloonan
