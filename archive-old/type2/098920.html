Go to http://www.mcmaster_dot_com/ and search on " Adhesive-Back Damping Sheets
". Scroll down and you'll see the cheapest prices on these dyna-mat type
sheets anywhere.

I've also heard people talk about using spray on truck liner as a sound
deadener. Most of the stuff I found was $70 - $100 a gallon. I searched some
more and found this product called SANI-TRED LRB that looks just as good if
not better and at a cheaper price. http://www.sanitred_dot_com/ The website is
close to chaos, but the product is intriguing. They have some decent
pictures of the product and all its applications. 

LRB is $54.75 a gallon and a quart of the thickening agent is $13.50. Adding
the thickening agent will allow one to put on a single thick layer and also
use it on the ceiling of the bus. 

Following is a description of the product from the website:
