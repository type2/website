My two cents:

Don't know about stock or in-line fuses, but my old cigarette lighter in
my bus had that same little "spark plug wire" attachment for the power,
which was the first time I'd ever seen anything like it. I just replaced
the whole thing with a standard universal cigarette lighter from my
FLAPS, splicing flat push-on connectors to the existing wires.
Everything works fine. Sounds like you might want to replace the whole
wire, though.

BUT -- I just bought a boat version cigarette lighter for the 12V plug
in the cabin, which has a little removable map light in place of a
lighter element, and what should I see but that little spark plug
arrangement for the power wire! So, if you don't want to futz with
splicing your wires, you could look for that marine-style setup. I got
mine at KMart in the boating supplies section, and it came with the
female connector to splice to the existing power wire.

I guess that hook-up is just a variation.
