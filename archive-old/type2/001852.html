I had a similar problem with my '79 FI.  Check the EGR valve.  Bentley had a
reference to stalling and idle problems do to a misadjusted EGR valve.
After quite a bit of trouble shooting it turned out my EGR valve was
misadjusted.  I adjusted the valve (so it would not recirculate gas when
cold) and the vehicle idled fine.
