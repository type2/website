doesn't look like the one in the catalogue being a sort of scissor jack that

still had 2-3 inches to spare with the tyre clear of the ground by 1/2'.
Claims to be genuine VW but is Brazilian with no VW logos. I suspect it is
the one supplied with new type 2's. I'm pleased with it anyway (no FART's on
why a bottle or trolley jack is better please !) as it will hide in the van
nicely and is easy to use, very little effort required to lift the van.
