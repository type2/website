I've got this set up as well.  If I ever get around to putting the axels and
rear tin back on so I can drive my bus, here are my plans...

A year or so ago I met a fella at the coast with a similar set up and he
showed me what he did.  He basically turned the "closet" section into a low
cabinet.  Took a sawz-all and removed the top portion of the cabinet, right
at the level where it would (and does) make a great armrest for anyone
sitting in the seat.  Said he did not even have to remove the cabinet from
the bus.  I sat in the drivers seat, and it opened that blind spot right
up.
