#59 is easy!  It is part number  211 611 833C  called a Grommet. Available 
from the VW dealer IF he is willing to order it for you. Cost is around $1.70

74-79 is the shortest, only goes to the floor at the front corner of the drivers seat.
68 is a metal tube.
