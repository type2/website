Your clutch cable may need adjustment, or replacement.  I have similar
symptoms and, so far, it has always been a maladjusted clutch cable.  I have
had a cable fail, the clevis pin had worn all the way through the end of the
cable.  Naturally, this caused a continuing adjustment problem as the cable
became essentially 1/4"+ longer as it wore out.
