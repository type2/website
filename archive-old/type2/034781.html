The socket for a Type 1 engine flywheel nut is 36mm and I think up until the
advent of the Type 4 engine for the Bus the rear axle nut on a Bus is 36mm
too (maybe?  It is on Beetles, Ghias, etc.)  Somewhere around that early bay
time they switched to the 46mm axle nut but at the same time (maybe again?)
switched to the composite drums, so that you can do a rear brake job without
removing the axle nut.

If you are just buying this stuff I would go for 3/4" drive for all of it,
36mm or 46mm.  You can sometimes get away with 1/2" drive for the 36mm but
the way that breaker bar bends, I would recommend something a little
stouter.

As always, YMMV.
