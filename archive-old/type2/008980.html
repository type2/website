 Steve, 
are you sure 16 is a ground??? I believe it should be 15.
Follow the harness across the back by the distributor. You will find a group of small harness's coming out of the main harness. One with a blue connector, one with a brown connecter, and one with a black connecter. These go to cold start, thermotime, and aux. air. You will also find 3 white wires coming out. 15 and 17 are tied together with a female spade connecter and 5 is separate with it's own female spade connecter. Both of these go under the left side of the plenum and connect on to a ground connection under the forward-most case bolt [under your EGR valve, if you still have one].
