Subject: Oil pressure sender relocation kit

The VDO kit is NLA. I searched forever and found only one (germansupply
still had one on the shlf). However, there is a better alternative:

T-4 Oil Pressure Sending Unit Relocation Kit
Price: $ 24.95
Part #: LGU0018
From aircooled_dot_net.

I have this on my camper and it works fine. Chances are  that it will
outlast the VDO kit since it uses s/s hose instead of rubber. The VDO kit
included a bracket, the aircooled_dot_net one does not. But a hose clamp works
just fine. First, I had mounted the sender on top of my coil. Now that I am
running Vanagon electronic ignition and have relocated the coil to the side
of the spare wheel well, the sender is mounted in place of the coil. Hose is
just long enough for this mounting position.
