as far as lining it all up goes...
   
  make sure the point on the rotor button points straight at the notch in the rim of the dizzy..at the same time the notch in the fan pulley lines up with TDC 0 mark on your timing scale....
   
  also you can once getting it to this point... check #1 plug hole with a pencil to make sure your piston is up...(gently ) dont break nothing off in there.. erasers, lead, pencil, etc/./.
  if all these things are right as i wrote  you are at TDC.... or surely should be..
  if it comes down to that poiint and you still arent sure you might want to look at your valves to see whicvh ones are open at what you think is TDC that will tell you all you should need to know .. 
   
  DO NOT START THE BUS UNLESS YOU ARE SURE YOU DID THE VALVE ADJUSTMENT AND ALL RIGHT....
