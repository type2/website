Burps, hiccups?  On our 10K trip in the US, with 150K on the engine, we
were In Arizona/New Mexico near Saguaro National Monument, when it was
*HOT* in Aug.  I experienced stalling at stops, and had to keep punching
the throttle to keep going- and uphills had no power.  When we got to

retarded and the dwell like 65 deg!  Rubbing block had split in two. 
Had to let it cool an hour, as the distributor was too hot to touch to
rotate by hand.
