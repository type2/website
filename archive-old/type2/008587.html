All,

Shortly after buying my Westy (back when I actually had some money) I
ordered the following from BusBoys:

~front and rear Sway-Away sway bars
~VDO tach and bezel
~VDO oil temp, head temp and oil press gauges and the nifty little
mounting plate the sell
~VDO speedo (doesn't fit, but I liked it and still have it)
~Under dash parcel tray (was out of stock and still is, as far as I
know:-(
~replacement faucet for my sink

All of the parts arrived within 4 shipping days. The invoice was
correct and I got a catalog with my order. True, it was a poorly
zeroxed and stapled copy, but it serves it's purpose.

I had no problems with them whatsoever. In fact, I talked to the owner
about various repairs and he was very helpful.

I do get the impression this is a very small and laid-back operation
though. Possibly not too computer oriented either (that may explain
the unfinished web page).


Jaime
