> I'm not sure where to get a new/rebuilt tank, but I saw an article in one
of
> the VW mags that described a rust-removal procedure which may help you.
>
> Get some sharp-edged gravel (a couple of pounds should do it), pour it in
> the tank and shake the heck out of it for a long time--a couple hours of
> shake for five minutes/rest for ten should do it. The tank restored in the
> article was out of a Bug, so the Bus tank may need two people to shake it.
> This shaking with gravel knocks the big rust chunks off the metal.
>
> Then get some tank cleaner (the Bentley manual lists a couple of tank
> cleaners, and POR-15 sells one), block off both outlets, and clean the
tank
> with this stuff.
>
> Go to a gun shop and buy a pistol cleaning kit. In it is a little wire
brush
> on the end of a metal rod. Use this to clean the outlets.
>
> Then paint the tank with POR-15's gas tank paint inside and just regular
> rattle can paint on the outside, then reinstall the tank.
>
