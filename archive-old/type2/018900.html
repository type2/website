Dave:
VERY few 411's and 412's had manual transmissions, so it's likely that
the 72 parts bus has a bus clutch anyhow. However, new discs are so
cheap (and that IS the main part that wears out), that I'd seriously
just consider a new disc. Unless the clutch is worn to the point where
it's REALLY slipping a lot, the pressure plate is probably not worn
much.
