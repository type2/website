The Tourist delivery Programme was an option in the
late 60ties en early 70ties (don't know the exact
period when this was available).

Anyhow, In the USA and Canada it was optional to buy a
bus at the dealer and have it delivered in Germany.
Then travell to Germany and drive it around Europe for
a few months. Finally the bus would be shipped to the
USA.

website address was http://www.70camper_dot_com/ , but it
