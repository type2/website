Hello folks,

The early campers had these belly pans. The early campers where simply the
regular chassis with the roof cut for the pop top. This seriously
compromised the structural integrity. Therefore they decided to weld the
pans for some added torsional rigidity. I think '71 they added some
structural members to the roof and eliminated the belly pan.

We do not have any at this time. I have one customer on the waiting list.
