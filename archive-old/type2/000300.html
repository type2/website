This is similar to the results that AVP have reported.  Leonard, the owner,
insists that in actual use the difference in the shape of the ports on
'72-79 heads is so small that the copper gasket will fill in the difference,
allowing you, in practice, to use any heat exchanger on any year head
without an exhaust leak.  A few customers who have tried this have reported
similar results.  If you can get a tight seal, there certainly is some
benefit to mixing and matching.  For example, the '72-74 heat exchanger and
exhaust setup is both less expensive and better quality than what's
currently available for '75-78.
