I've heard of this problem, though I haven't experienced it myself.  
"Crud" (scientific name) in the tank (most likely rust flakes) clog up 
filter, shutting off flow of gas.  After a while the flakes settle off 
the filter, at which time you can start up again.

Probably the best cure is removal of the tank and a thourough cleaning.
