There are LED replacements for 1156 and 1157 bulbs. They are so bright that
you'll have a whitish light coming from behind the lens with a small
circular pattern that pierces the lens instead of a giving off a nice red
glow. Want that classic beetle taillight look?

I don't think they are as effective as having true LED taillights with clear
lenses and faceted reflectors. I've seen a lot of motorcycles with modified
under-tails that were ruined by modifications. No wonder DOT doesn't want to
approve them: most make the lighting less effective.

I think it's best to color match the LED to the lens if you do experiment.
I've seen Halogen 1156/1157s for sale but haven't tried them, again, because
of the cost.
