If you don't have power going to the coil it is probably a loose wire or
fuse in the ignition switch.  ECU and AFM have nothing to do with sending
the primary current to the coil.  Find a wiring diagram in the Bentley or
Haynes and start tracing current.  Sometimes fuses are faulty even if they
appear OK because the element becomes brittle and unreliable.  There are a
lot of wires under the dash of a 77 that can short out, blow fuses, and
cause problems.  Good luck.
