Michael,

It's (kinda) mmysssssss, where mm is the model (22) and y is the
year (1971) and sssssss is the sequential serial number.  This
system started in 1965.  Not sure when it ended.  So, my '67
Deluxe is 247142402, as in model 24, year 1967, and the 142,402nd
made that model year.
