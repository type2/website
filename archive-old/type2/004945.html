I think that if you pull the two wires off of the the brake pressure switch
(the two that are involved in switching the brake lights - not the one that
is involved in the warning light on the dash - see your wiring diagram) and
short them together, the brake lights should go on, provided everything else
besides the pressure switch is in proper working order.  You can also test
the pressure switch by connecting an ohmeter across the two terminals
associated with the aforementioned wires, and observe whether or not the
pressure switch contacts make when you press the brake pedal. I'd do these
tests before removing the pressure switch - could save alot of unecessary
work....
