On Mon, 25 Jun 2007, MikeQ14 wrote:

> I have a '71 or '72 beam in my '74 westy.  Just try to install the brake
> caliper and it doesn't fit properly.  Has anyone used '74 front calipers on a
> '72 beam setup??  if not can I use a spacer to move the caliber back in order
> to work??/  other option I guess it to change the spindle, will this fit the
> beam and just bolt in??

You might be able to space the caliper to the correct position, but it
would be best to use the late model spindle, disk and backing plate to go
with your late model caliper.  The braking ability won't be greater, but
the pads are thicker and will last longer.
