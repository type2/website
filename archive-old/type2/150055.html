The screw you need is m8. I don't have my parts book at the moment, but the
length is about 30mm. An m8 x 30 allen socket cap screw will work perfectly.

Something like this:

http://www.boltdepot_dot_com/product.aspx?cc=25&cs=132&cm=77&cd=1607

Hardness of metric fasteners is different than SAE. Most fasteners on a VW
(including this one) are grade 8.8.
