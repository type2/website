> pick up to a 'peak' and then die as you over-advance).  Once you've found this
> 'peak'  turn the distributor back at bit (about 1/4 " retarding timing about 3

I've used this procedure, but I back off the distributor until the engine 
starts to cough a little then move it forward enough to stop the 
coughing.  This will keep the timing from being set too far forward and 
overheating the engine
