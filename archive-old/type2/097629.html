Subject: Door removal:

Hi Mike,

    The tool you need here is an impact driver. Any good parts store, tool
truck, Sears, etc. It looks like a large "Screw driver handle", with a 3/8
drive on it. Use a 6 point - 6mm socket on it. With your 6mm "socket" on the
drive, hold the handle with a twisting tension on the handle to the left or
off direction as you hit the end of the handle with a (BDH), the bolts should
brake loose and come right out! BTW, (BDH) = Big Damn Hammer!
    Good luck.
