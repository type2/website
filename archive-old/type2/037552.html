Yellow Metal?

Way back when I was in the Navy I remember our snipes talking about 
problems with cadmium coated metal, which had a yellow tint, and 
petroleum products. For a time cadmium coated fasteners were being 
used as a replacement for stainless steel and other corrosion 
resistant metals, price being the driving factor. However, there was 
a toxic gas or something being produced when the cadmium fasteners 
came in contact with hot oil so they were banned from the propulsion 
spaces, we still used them up on deck. I wonder if there is something 
similar going on in our trannies.
