On this tank cleaning issue, I have taken the tanks out of the Bus and
tossed in a large hand full of gravel from my driveway, added a gallon
or 2 of water and then I shake well. The gravel brakes loose all the
crap that is in the tank and cleans out the gunk that wants to stick to
the bottom. I then dump out the gravel and flush once or twice. Its
cheap, easy and safe. Oh ya, air dry in the sun for a few hours.
