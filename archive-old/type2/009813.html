Sounds like it's complete rebuild time.  Time to either take the
engine to a competent rebuilder, equip yourself with Muir, Bentley,
and Wilson and rebuild it yourself, or get a new engine from
Beetlemex, et al.  Which path you follow depends upon the amount of
time and money you possess.

_Don't_ buy a "crate engine" from GEX and similar rebuilders.  The
parts for a good rebuild cost more than they charge for an exchange
engine, which should give you an idea of their quality.  If a part is
95% worn out, it is still "in spec" and will be reused in the engine
they sell to you.  Learn from my mistake!

At $1050, the Beetlemex brand new engine is pretty darned attractive. 
It is a few hundred more than the rebuild parts would cost, but you
get a brand new case.  The existing cases are getting pretty long in
the tooth and the chances of yours being good is questionable.
