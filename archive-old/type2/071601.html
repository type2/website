The heater blower for the early Vanagons is _belt driven_, on the same pulley 
that drives the alternator (directly behind it -- rear is rear).  Yes, it's 
slower than a late baywindow.  
Properly rated tires are even more important than in Buses, as the Vanagon 
body is heavier.  See the Vanagon_dot_com site for details.  
Do not reset the tripmeter while in motion -- Vanagon odometers are a bit 
fragile, and doing this can break one.  The Vanagon mailing list archive has 
repair instructions for this problem.
The large engine bay hatch is nice.
That's all I can come up with at the moment.  Hope it helps.
