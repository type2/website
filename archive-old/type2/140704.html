Subject: Re: [T2] Oil Leak - Larger than normal

First, check your crankshaft end play. When you grasp the lower pulley(with 
the bus not running of course), can you pull it in and out enough to feel it 
move or hear a clunk? If so, that is enough reason to create a leak on the 
front main seal. If it isn't moving much, you still will need to drop your 
engine, remove the flywheel, and replace the seal with a quality Elring or 
similar seal. Also a good time to check your pilot bearing and consider 
replacing your clutch, throwout bearing and the o-ring in your flywheel.
