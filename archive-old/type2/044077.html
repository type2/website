>Can anyone give me a run-down on how to remove the engine lid for a '72 bus?
(AND the >re-installation) I checked the library and the archives, but to no
avail.

If you work on the street be prepared to be out there until its done. I used a
3/8 drive
electric drill and a few bits. I drilled out the center rod in each hinge - took
awhile
then when it was time to reinstall, I broke the head off some appropriate size
carpenter
nails, greased and drove them in with a hammer. This method won't win any "best
of show"
awards, but if your bus is a workhorse it will be just fine.
