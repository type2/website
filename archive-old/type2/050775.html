I gave the drums more than a few whacks with

observed. I'm thinking issue is bigger than
a parking brake, but definitely brake related.

I have clutch, neutral and can find all gears
including reverse. Motor starts right up.

Brakes were serviced professionally less than
3500 miles ago and were dragging slightly.
