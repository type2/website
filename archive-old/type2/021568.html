Make sure all the connections are very clean.  I had once where the
wiring harness to the battery got really nasty and things would still
kinda work.. but sometimes it'd just click and nothing would run.  I
took off the old harness, wire brushed the fat cable, put a new harness
on and it worked great.

I think the 500 watt amp helped speed up the corrosion on the terminals
:)
