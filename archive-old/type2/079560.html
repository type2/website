Hi Volks,

There's a carabus being planned as a fall colours tour of the "Backbone of
Ontario" that I thought might be of interest to some type2 members. There
are six buses attending, and more are welcome.  We're starting at
Rattlesnake Point Conservation area at 8:30 AM, weather permitting. With the
leaves in full autumn glory up here, it should be a good trip, and the
scenery and small towns on the route will make for some great pictures.

The "Backbone of Ontario" is actually the Niagara Escarpment, it runs from
Niagara Falls all the way up the Bruce Peninsula to Tobermorey. Bring a CB
radio or Walky-talky if you have one, the Carabus will be communicating on
channel 14. 

Let Jamie or I know via P-Mail if you're interested or want more details.
Check http://www.hrca.on_dot_ca/rattle.html for more details on where we're
meeting, and a look at the scenery and colours to look forward to.
