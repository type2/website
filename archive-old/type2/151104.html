VPC= Volskwagen Products Corporation...it was at one time, a &quot;wholly owned 
subsidiary of vw&quot;.  Made all air cooled A/C and cruise controls and 
accesories.

Semi-difficult to switch over dependon on which &quot;original&quot; A/C system you 
have...VPC or someone else's.  

Change the dryer, change the compressor and oil, change/modify the 
compressor mount(depending), change compressor hose ends, check for leaks, 
charge with Freeze 12 and drive off into the sunset
