Shannon:
There may be some incompatibility in the mixed up parts. I don't know
whether or not it would cause the problems you're experiencing.
72's used a rubber o-ring at the top of the carbs, 73&74 used a square
sectioned round gasket. I don't know if you can effect a seal by mixing
parts. Maybe you'll need to use the 73-74 air cleaner with the later
carbs. The dry type air cleaner may give slightly better performance at
the expense of less filtration. It's no worse filtration than any other
modern car, however.
Are you sure you've got 12v. to each idle solenoid? Then try exchanging
the solenoids with your old ones. That's where I'd start.
