Topher is Topher Waring, a middle school shop teacher in VT who I contacted
perhaps a year ago after reading about him in a National Geographic World
(NG for kids).  He has a 71 that he's converted to all electric power and
his schoolkids have to reinstall the whole works every year as part of their
work:  motor, batteries, disconnects, etc.  It's a great story, and he does
it on a school budget shoestring.  The kids cut their own adaptor plate out
of plate aluminum.  It functions as a bus (imagine, a bus as a real bus) and
errand runner for the school.
