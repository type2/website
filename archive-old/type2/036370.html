Bob:

AFAIK, the hall ignition came only on '79 and up california emissions
busses and vanagons.  I'm not positive, but I'd bet you would need the hall
distributor if you wanted to convert to the VW hall ingnition.  You would
also need the special bosch coil along with the control module that mounts
on the firewall, oh and there is the wiring harness that goes between the
distributor and the control module.  Other than that, its a pretty sweet
setup, but these parts can be difficult to find.  If you want to eliminate
your points (and the frequent need for adjustment and retiming) the easiest
and most cost effective way is probably the Pertronix ignitor.
