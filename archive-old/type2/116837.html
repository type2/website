My engine case is tapped for M10-1.0.  I was thinking about opening up the 
hole in the cooling tin so that I could attach the P/N 360006 sender 
(M10-1.0 threaded) straight into the case (ala Steve Dolan), but I read 
about the problems with tightening up the sender without removing alot of 
the engine tin.  So, I decided to go the extension hose way.  The way I have 
it hooked up is this: M10-1.0 adapter threaded into the case, 1/8 NPT grease 
gun hose attached to the top of the adapter, 1/8 NPT union (coupler) 
attached to the other end of the hose, pressure sender attached to the 
union.  The only place the threads don't match is between the 1/8 NPT union 
and the P/N 360006 sender.  With a P/N 360009 sender (1/8 NPT threaded), 
there is no mismatch.
