Subject: Re: [T2] Head Gasket swap on 1.7

On Sat, 15 Nov 2003, Sage Green wrote:

> I want to eliminate my head gaskets on my 1.7 engine, and make up for the
> space lost in the head cavity by placing shims at the base of the cylinder
> barrels.
>
> How much 'shim' must I use?

Take a micrometer.  Measure the thickness of the gasket.  Use that much
spacer.

Better practice would be to "CC" the cylinders and heads to achieve
volumetric balance and the compression ratio you desire. See the following
article in the library for details:
http://www.type2_dot_com/library/engineg/shims.htm

> Better yet, can I just put head gaskets at the base of the barrels?

No.  They are different diameters.
