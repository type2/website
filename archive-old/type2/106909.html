Shims are available to reset correct compression. They go between the 
bottom of the cylinders and the case. Always standard procedure to check 
deck height and make this adjustment.

>(compression was 122psi at #1, 0psi all others)

Actual measures and inspection can confirm good rings/pistons/cylinders. 
Put each ring by itself in it's cylinder and hold it up to the light. If 
you can see light between the ring and cylinder, you aren't spec no matter 
how you measure it. Requires only ring removal tool and source of light to 
verify.

Pouring gas in the inverted heads will reveal any leaks in the valves. Gas 
in any port verifies no seal. A few tablespoons of gas is all that is 
needed for this verification.
