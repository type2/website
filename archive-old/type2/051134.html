Eddie, You said bolt, I assume this is a double ended stud with one left
hand thread. In that case if the threads are intact, then you might be able
to get it back in this way.... Get 2 head nuds and put them on the top of
the stud together. then with 2 spanners hold the lower one and tighten the
topone. They bind together real tight. put the stud in and do it up using
the top nut to tension and use 2 spanners to separate the nuts, if there is
not enough exposed thread to use the 2 nuts you can cut one(or both) down
until its (they are) quite thin and it will all still work.
