Well, the choice seems to be between doing body work and doing engine
work.  The type 4 engine won't be all that cheap to fix (I'd allocate
$2k to that).  The bodywork won't be cheap to fix either.  Which do
you feel more competent to tackle?

Before you buy the dealer bus, find someone who knows how to detect
Bondo and take 'em along.  I am _very_ suspicious of new paint jobs,
since they often cover up bad prep work, gaping rust holes, etc.  It'd
be terrible to buy that bus and find out that it had more rust than
the running bus whose owner is at least honest enough not to disguise
it.

In either case, talk 'em down a little.  Offer $500 and give yourself
some bargaining leeway.
