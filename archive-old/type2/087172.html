Rob,

Heater box "overflow" dumps....when you have the heater flaps closed, the 
hot air still flows thru the heater box and flapper controls, but gets 
dumped out ofthe rear of these pipes instead of going into the passenger 
cabin.

My 78 has them...connected with the regular looking black paper and foil 
flexible hoses.

My guess is that the vw engineers decided to dump more hot air out of the 
engine thru there to try and keep the heater box internals a little 
cooler....
