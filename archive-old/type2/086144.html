exhaust system that I hadn't even announced yet.  He inquired about

him pre-order a couple of kits.  They arrived at the Depot today.

This is a stock Bus muffler, except with a Stainless Steel body rather than
mild steel.  I are getting it from the same South African supplier who
supplies our '72-74 and '86-91 stainless mufflers.  Many on this list have
bought the '72-74 ones and have commented favorably about them here.

Also included is a single piece damper/tailpipe.  This is the stock pipe
used on European spec busses, and is essentially the same as the separate
tail and damper pipes used on U.S. spec exhausts except that it's all one
piece.  Finally, an installation kit (gaskets and hardware) is also
included.  As packaged, this fits '68-71.  If you want one for a splitty,
email me.

I should note that only the muffler body is stainless, not the
damper/tailpipe or the flanges.  However, the price is no more than a mild
steel system, and the muffler should FAR outlast any mild steel muffler.
Since it is a duplicate of the stock muffler other than being stainless, it
will look, perform, and sound the same as stock.  I think this is an
absolute bargain at $99.95.
