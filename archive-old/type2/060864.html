You've about a 75% chance of fixing it with a regulator(& brush)
assembly. Take the battery cable off, take off 2 wires, the screws
attaching the regulator, put on the new regulator, put it back together.
Darn near the easiest repair on a Vanagon! I'm guessing around $20-$28
at list mentioned parts places.
