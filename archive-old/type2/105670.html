From what I see in the scematic the relay set up is hooked up similar to
the Vanagon relays that I have been selling to the folks with Vanagions.

I use a red wire off # 30 on the relay to the power source.
A Black wire off # 55 on the relay to
ground.

It looks like on the scematic, that you would split the yellow wire off
the dimmer for the L/H headlamp to the switch, and run the "in" wire to
# 56 on the relay, and #57 to the other side of the split wire to the
headlamps--or out.
Duplicate this proceedure for the white wire for the R/H side.
