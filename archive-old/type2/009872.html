WHOA   Stop!! You can't set a dual vacuum unit distributor staticly at the 
timing specs that's called for at idle.

You do not have vacuum on the retard side with the engine off. If you are 
going to set it staticly, set it at 7.5 BTDC. When you start it you should 
have vacuum on the retard side and then it will go to 10 ATDC. If it doesn't 
you have 2 things wrong: either you have no vacuum to the retard [inboard 
nipple] side at idle or the vacuum unit is bad.
