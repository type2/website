OK---Here's the solution to sewing a straight line---even on an ole
treadle machine---

Go to a store, or singer sewing center, and they have a magnetic plate
that you can lay along side the foot of the machine, and when you run
the material through the machine--it keeps it straight going into the
needle---works good--I used one of these to make my Wal-Mart orange
curtains---
