I installed a shelf under the dash (similar to the splitty shelf).  Two
small Radio Shack Minimus box speakers sit on the ends of the shelf.  Good
sound, but if you're a Golden Ear (tm) you may want something better.

You can get similar results with surface-mount auto speakers, but I had
the others sitting around.

I supported the stereo at the dash opening (mine came pre-hacked).  the
rear of the stereo is supported by a strap attached to one of the holes in
the dash for the original speaker.
