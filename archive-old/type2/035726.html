You have bad sensors. If you had an insulation problem, you would have 2
different readings depending on where you measured. If you measure from the
wire to the base of the sensor and then measure from the wire to the cyl.
head, the figure should be the same. If you have an insulation problem, i.e.
a poor connection thru the threads, the latter reading would be higher or
infinity. Not lower.
