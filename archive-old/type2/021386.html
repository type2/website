I removed the AC from my '73 many years ago.  I had to replace the
left rear tin piece.  To close the hole that was hacked by the VW
dealer's AC installer for the compressor, I had a local metal
fabricator/welder replace the missing body metal.  Furthermore, there
were holes cut into the left rear wheel well and in the cockpit floor
to pass the refrigerant hoses through.  I had the wheel well hole
welded shut too.  This has kept dirt from entering the engine room.
