I-80/I-71/I-70, 
. probably the shortest route.

. fewer AIRS members


I-95/I-78/I-70
. maybe the most AIRS members
. lots of service and help along the way

. NJ turnpike
. PA turnpike
. OHIO turnpike
. some pretty stiff grades

I-78/I-81/I-70/I-68/I-70

. pretty level run 
. still civilized country but away from most traffic
. this is the truck route
. avoids the turnpikes
. prettiest route, fall colors will be beautiful

. this is the truck route
. one stiff grade, in MD (Sideling Hill)

I-78/I-81/I-40/I-24

. most level route
. truck route to South West
. pretty route

. longest route
. some parts pretty isolated
. some long steep hills
. weather can get pretty bad in the Mountains


personally I would probably take the I-78/I-81/I-70/I-68/I-70 route

a few very stiff grades)
