Rick,

You might get an implied value, but it will never be close enough.

Narrow band O2 sensors swing across a certain voltage(about 1 volt) 
depending on whether the mixture is rich or lean.  No extrapolation of 
how much rich or lean though.

A wide band O2 sensor gives you a better indication, but the kits
run close to $400+ and still do not give you a proper CO number.
