Before you put the tire on the front, decide if the additional
interior space is really worth it.  I've seen very few buses with
front-mounted spares that didn't have the sheet metal stoved in behind
the spare.  Other drivers seem to think that the tire is a nice soft
cushy bumper!

On the occasions where I carry an extra spare (like when I head to the
boonies or the snow country), the spare goes on my rack.
