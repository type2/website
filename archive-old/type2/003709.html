The '79 Westy has a water pump mounted on the bottom of the water tank,
externally.  If you don't have one, Bus Depot carries them.  Mine was broken
off at the neck when we first got our Westy.  They are easy to replace.

The fridge, at least in the '79, runs on ammonia.  Yours is probably a
Dometic.  Do an internet search on "dometic" and you will eventually get to
their site.  They will gladly provide you with a manual for your fridge.
Ours is an oddball, top loading one (RC 152) that apparently was only used
in the '79.  They still had the manual.

In the archives is, or was, a "Westfalia Refrigerator Troubleshooting" guide
www.type2_dot_com/bartnik/fridgediag.htm which can help.
