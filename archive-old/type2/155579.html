Subject: Re: [T2] Head torquing method

> I was wondering if there is a way of torquing type 4 heads without
> dropping the
> engine.
>


Yes, but it's about as much work. The effort you save by not dropping the
engine, you spend by working within the engine compartment.

Remove exhaust
Remove intake system (carbs or fuel injection)
Remove fan and shroud
Remove cooling tin
Remove valve train
Torque!
Reassemble
