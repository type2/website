Mark, check the backup light circuitry first. Find which fuse (might be by
itself in the engine compartment in an inline fuse holder) and take it out
of the holder. See if the engine still dies when you put it in reverse. If
it doesn't then you know you have a short in that circuit, so start tracing
back from the transmission connection till you find it.
Hope that helps,
