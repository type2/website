You cannot put a flap in the bus filler neck. The unleaded neck is molded
with a smaller hole.
The flap was NOT there to keep fumes in the car. It was there to keep idiots
from putting leaded gas in an unleaded-only vehicle.
Starting in '74 all cars sold in California as of model year 75 were
required to use unleaded fuel, which used a pump nozzle that was smaller in
diameter. Leaded fuel was still available but the nozzle was standard size.
The restricter made the hole smaller so you could only put unleaded in the
tank.
