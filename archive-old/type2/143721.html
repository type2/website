Jason,
Quick question:
When you are idling AND the timing is at 10 AFTER...
Disconnect the inboard [on the flat side] vacuum
retard hose.

Does the timing jump to 7.5 before?

Does the idle speed increase?

If neither happens, DO NOT SET THE TIMING TO 10
AFTER!!

Set it to 7.5 BEFORE, you are severely retarding your
engine.

That timing spec is ONLY VALID IF YOUR VACUUM RETARD
IS WORKING and the engine is idling.

I keep bringing this point up EVERY time a dual vacuum
timing question comes up and I have to repeat it over
and over.
