> Whoa!!!   Do not use the hairspring to try to make any type of
'calibration
> adjustment'. You only set the needle on at the correct position once to
set the
> preload of the spring. Look at the face of any VDO speedo. At about -20
mph you
> will see a short white line [at the bottom of the face, to the right of
the 10
> mph pin]. It will be visable just to the left of the V in VDO. That is the
> calibration point of the needle. When you put the needle back on the
shaft, it
> must point to that line. Then you push the needle on the shaft. Move the
needle
> to the pin and gently lift it over the pin. That's all you do to set the
preload
> on the spring.
>
