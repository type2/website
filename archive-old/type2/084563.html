>I would be interested in hearing from anyone about the difference between
>the 107i and the 91.

Well from the specs, lift is the same and duration is increased 9 degrees 
at .050.  That means more overlap, less vacuum.  You will lose low-end 
torque due to the overlap.  You will have to spin it faster to get into the 
powerband.  Your mpg will likely go down too.  On the bright side, you will 
lose the stoplight wars by a slightly smaller margin.  :-)
