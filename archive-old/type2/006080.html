Subject: RE: Oil temp gauge mounting

I recently installed 3 gauges in my 71 (oil temp & press + CHT).  I used the
molded plastic pod sold as an accessory by VDO.  I ran the wires thru some
of the holes for the radio speaker grill located top center on the dash.
After everything was hooked up, I used self stick Velcro tape to hold the
plastic pod to the top of the dash.  This arrangement allows for easy access
to the gauges and wiring, and is completely reversible back to stock if
desired. Looks good and no exposed wires when you use the speaker grill
holes located under where the pod will be located. The plastic pods come in
1, 2 and 3 hole versions.
