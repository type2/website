also.... My headlight problem (embarassed) was due to a sticking ignition.
When the weather turned cold my ignition didnt want to spring back all the
way after starting. It took me a lot of tedious testing and trouble shooting
of all my connections (if you recall it was the wipers and headlights that
wouldnt come on in cold weather, only after about 15 minutes of warming up)
to finally crawl up back into my pilots chair and say "SCREW IT!" and turn
off the engine, only to see the headlights flick on for a brief second. hand
on the key i turned it slightly forward and they came on again. then I
started Blue up and noticed I could pull the key back a fraction or so, then
the beams popped on. I feel great at not being stranded anywhere at night
now. I also feel foolish for leaving Blue at work for two days because I
didnt want to drive at night with no lights. Little prob little fix big
headache.
