I had the same problem on my 74 bus for about a year. I had to keep  
tightening the rear right 46 mm nut every couple of weeks, when I made sharp  turns I 
could hear a grinding noise. After a while it started to shear the  cotter 
pin, I believe what was happening the nut was jumping a  thread or two shearing 
the cotter pin. I replaced the wheel shaft (spindle)  and that corrected the 
problem. Picked up the wheel shaft at a junk  yard for $15 bucks. That 46 mm nut 
has to be tightened about 200ft pounds or  better.
