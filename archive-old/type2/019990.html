> Does anybody know what the website is to decipher your vin number?

At:
	http://www.type2_dot_com/m-codes/

You can decipher your VIN and even more...

The VIN can be read as follows. The first two numbers stand for the
model

	21 - panelvan
	22 - microbus
	23 - kombi
	24 - deluxe microbus
	26 - pickup and crewcab
	27 - ambulance

The third number is the modelyear:
	8 - 1968 (9 digit VIN)
	9 - 1969 (9 digit VIN)
	0 - 1970 (10 digit VIN)
	1 - 1971 (10 digit VIN)
	2 - 1972 ....
