theres a technique to those *********......LOL...
i have found that if you pull down on the cover as you put the wire on and hold in place dont let it slide up  you will prevent leaks ....
they have to be on exactly right...
 
i use cork gaskets and always put a little sealent behind the gasket... thats between the cover and gasket not between gasket and head..... no no....
 
then set cover up there and hold in the down position as best as possible and then put wire bailer on easily without moving cover,,.,.,.,
 
also check to make sure that no one has bent the cover anywhere around the rim...
 
good luck.. once you get the technique you wont forget....
