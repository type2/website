I have not heard anyone say to just replace the lifters by them- selves. You
always need to replace the pushrods with the lifters. The pieces you also
need whether you change the cam or not are the spacers and wave and flat
washers between the rocker arms. Other than a wrecking yard engine, I have
not found these offered new by anyone. They are pictured in the 68-79
Bentley. Section 5, page 62, figure 17 - 8.
