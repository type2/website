What I have used ( many, many,times) is a regular 1&1/2"  U shaped muffler
clamp.  I get them from Napa for about a buck apiece.  They straddle the
header pipe where it goes over the J tube that comes through the heat
exchanger.  You simply tighten them up just enough so that you don't crush
the ( pipe) j tube, and wahla, your exhaust leak problem, and backfiring are
eliminated!
