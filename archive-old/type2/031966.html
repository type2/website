It's more important that your timing "is correct" in the sense that the 
right sparkplug is sparking when its particular cylinder is near TDC on 
the compression stroke than whether it's firing at 5 deg before or after 
TDC.  You should be able to get you timing "close enough" using the notch 
on your diststributor and your timing scale.  Do you have your plug wires 
attached correctly?
