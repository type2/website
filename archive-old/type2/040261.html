This is NOT, I repeat NOT, EGR. This is air injection from the smog pump.
EGR comes from the exhaust and flows into the intake.
The smog pump pumps fresh air into the exhaust ports to complete the burn of
the over-rich exhaust.
Any time you have holes going into the exhaust chambers, it NOT EGR.
