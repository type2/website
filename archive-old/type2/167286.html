On 7/23/07 8:54 AM, "Greg Potts" <greg_at_pottsfamily_dot_ca> wrote:

>> can you drill the case for a top pull out dip stick with out
>> getting fillings in the engine (I don't want to take it apart)
> 
> I would be careful, but if a spark-plug is do-able, a dipstick tube
> should be. At least in this case you can access the back of the hole
> through the case breather.

You can't get anywhere inside the case from the breather tower, it is
blocked off with only a circuitous route to the sump below.

The access to the shavings created would be through the inspection plate on
the bottom of the case. The location of the top-mounted disptick hole is
directly above the so-called "taco plate".

You could also use a type 1 dipstick adapter on the case filler hole if you
have room for it.
