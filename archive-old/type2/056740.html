Locking the rear hatch will not prevent in from being opened from the
inside.  All locking does, is rotate a lever on the push-button mechanism
that presses the latch, the same latch that the inside opener operates.  I
don't know of anyway, other that a slide bolt or clasp or something along
those lines, that will keep the hatch from opening with the inside opener
installed.

Lets keep the kids (and drunks) safe.
