When I had my 2110cc motor built the shop recommended that I add the 
cooler.  I have had zero problems and my bus doesn't act starved for oil 
nor does it over heat with it... Sorry you had such a bad experience TP, 
but sometime products just don't like working for certain people.
If Your engine is still stock... There is no reason for the additional oil 
cooler, the stock system works quite well.  And according to Gene Berg, 
will actually out perform any after market product, in this situation.  I 
actually do NOT recommend mounting the cooler under the bus.  They mounted 
mine where the Air Intake is on the side of the van.

Good Luck!
