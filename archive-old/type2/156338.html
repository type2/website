Subject: backfiring

Well, I finally got around to fixing my backfiring problem.   Single Weber carb.  Thanks to Mark Dearing et. al. who offered  good advice.  I started with something simple.  Turned the  mix screw 1/4 turn CCW (richer) and then the idle screw 1/4 turn CCW to  bring the idle back down.  Drove 10 miles.  Instead of Kaboom  I got a good Pop!   Repeated the process and drove 10 miles.   A little poof.  Did it all one more time, 10 more miles, and  nothing!  Good idle, engine runs good, and no backfires.  I  guess I was running too lean and had set the idle up too high to keep  it running.
