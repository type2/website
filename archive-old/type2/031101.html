>      I have a pinging problem on my 79 Std.  Stock 2.0l with all stock FI.
> When I got it it was timed at the required mark of 7.5BTDC & I was getting
> pinging in 4th gear up some mild inclines.  It does not seem to be

> timed it about 5BTDC & it still pings.  This is my 13th VW & I have never
> had one ping when all was in spec.  Any ideas?  Thanks.

try a higher octane gas and see if it goes away, that is one way to check
to see if it is really a "predetonation" problem.  if it does maybe you
will have to continue using it, as I have been told by some WAG is that as
carbon deposits build up on the top of the piston over years it can raise
the compression ratio enough to need a higher grade of gas.

of course this could be all wrong too, but a tank of gas is quick easy
check anyway
