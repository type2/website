a little "trick" that might help without any disassembly.....take off the
valve covers and push down on the pushrod side of the rocker arm.  a
hydraulic should have some give, the solid will be "solid".

also look at the top of the push rod, a hyd push rod is solid, a solid
pushrod is "usually" two piece, but this is a less reliable method.

they are ADJUSTED differently too so you need to be sure before adjusting
them.  Hyd are far far better IMO
