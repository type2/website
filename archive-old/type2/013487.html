It looks like the PO installed aftermarket coil-over shocks.  This may be an area of concern, namely, why did the PO use coil-overs?  Usually, coil-overs are used because the stock springs have gone soft (in any vehicle, not just VW's), or for higher-performance suspensions.  I'm hoping those coil-overs aren't hiding a torsion-bar problem that you'll discover when you take them off.
