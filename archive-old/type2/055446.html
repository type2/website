Knocking is not good, and you can't always hear the pre-detonation, just the worst
of it.

Two suggetions Will. First, check to see if it is advancing too far. Connect your
timing light and tach/dwell, and see what the advance is at 3300 RPMs when the
engine is warm. It should be 28-30 degrees advance max. I use this as my timing
and adjust the idle for wherever it lands (in my case at 2 degrees BTDC with a
dual vac with the retard hose plugged as per advice recieved here).

Check the EGR circut. The EGR shuts off at 3/4 throttle and above (I believe) and
the richer fuel mixture contributes to the knocking, but if it is knocking at
below 3/4 throttle your EGR may be plugged or inoperative.
