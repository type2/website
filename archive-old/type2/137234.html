And you did a great job too Hal, I balanced my rods end to end using Mike
West's method which consisted of an engineers ruler (one of the prism shaped
ones), and a small scale from school. He also explained how to find the
longest rod using a piston pin and the same straight edge with feeler gauges
 He was a genius. He has left us now, and gone on to that next big adventure
in the afterlife. I also used my wife's scale at work which measures 1/10
grams to see what my flywheel weighed after I'd turned it down on a school
lathe. Lots of people use simple methods which work well. I congratulate you
on your abilities to think of simple ways to get the job done!
