The the wire from the ignition switch, condenser wire from coil, or red and
black wires from coil (electronic ignition), were probably crossed.  I had a
friend who helped me work on my bus and even with years of experience he
accidently switched the wires and fried my Pertronix.  Puff, puff and $60
down the tubes.  It was an expensive lesson, but I pay much closer attention
now.  Not sure what you mean by "replace the distributor" but its unlikely
any serious damage was done.  Coil should be fine, but you obviously need to
replace the points or condenser depending on which wire was fried.  Good
luck.
