Subject: [T2] very good horn question

My horn had the wires totally removed. Unless you're looking for stock
resto, you can do what I did:

1) Run a wire from one of the fuses (there is one designated for the horn)
on the fuse box through a hole in gutter under the instrument panel to the
positive terminal on the horn. I don't remember if the horn terminals are
polarity sensitive; if they are they should be labeled.

2) Run another wire from the horn neg terminal to the ground wire that goes
to the horn button (hopefully this is intact inside the column)

3) I also removed the horn button assembly and cleaned the circular
contacts.
