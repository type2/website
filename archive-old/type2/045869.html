There's yet another alternative...

ATWOOD makes a marine battery tray/hold down setup for Grp. 24 & 27
batteries.  They're inexpensive  -
~$5.00 as I recall.  Consists of a plastic tray that has side extensions to
accept threaded hold down uprights, and they attach at the top to a plastic
fixture that spans the battery's top.  As the whole assembly is plastic
[black] it's totally non-corrosive.  The design requires not tools to
clamp/unclamp a battery.

I don't know if there's room for a plastic battery box to fit inside
[ATTWOOD makes them as well].

I've seen these locally at West Marine, Boater's World, and Schuck's Auto
Parts.

I also carry a plastic battery box on the left side of the engine room, in
the "auxiliary battery" space.  It contains 5 qts. of CASTROL 30W oil, a
small can of brake fluid, and a plastic funnel for adding the oil without
dumping it all over the engine.
