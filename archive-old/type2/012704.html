Sean:
A fully charged lead-acid battery should be between 13.2V and 13.8V with no load on it.  That's why the charging voltage on most cars is over 14 volts.  If it measures less than 13V with what should be a full charge in it, then it's on its last legs.  Also, make sure your battery charger is good.
