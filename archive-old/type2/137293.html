>The type 3 clock is held to the dash with a couple of springy fingers. 
>Reach up behind the dash, squeeze them towards the clock body, shove
>forward through the dash hole.

Those springy finger things are located at 3:00 and 9:00.
Squeeze them and push the clock towards the steering
wheel and out of the dash.
