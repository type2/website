I drovew around for quite awhile on three cyliders when I dropped a valve
seat and lived in denial of it for a month.  "It's just not timed right"

I wouldn't suggest it, but if it's the only way home, go a head but be
careful and watch out for the gas that will come spewing out of the plug
hole.  Now that I think about it, don't do it.  Too much chance for a
fire.  If you can keep the plug in the hole, by undoing the plug wire to
prevent firing, then maybe it will work.
