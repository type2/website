and I had at least one on each head pull out. They admitted it was their
mistake.  I had to call and call and call to get my refund on the shipping

have the extra $ that you don't mind spending on trying to get your heads
back to them and waiting for your refund.

Go ahead with AVP.

Just my experience and a warning.  And yes I did call Bus Depot when I had
all these problems and was told to call AVP direct, which is what I did.
