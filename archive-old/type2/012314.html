4.57 is stock final in the 091 late box.  About anything is available sort of
depending what you want to do.  5.43 and 5.86 sets from diesels are
VW factory parts.  Weddle can cut off a waterboxer shaft to give you
a 4.86.  The gear ratios are all over the place depending on the year,
look in Bentley.  There used to be a great German site showing each
box by code leters with its contents but it is kaput.  Changes are basically
minor within the stuff sent to the US anyway for those years if at all so,
whatever Bentley says should be close enough.
