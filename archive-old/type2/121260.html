Carb cleaner works wonders on cleaning this aluminum up, Berryman's dip can
is the best, but be cautious, it's wicked stuff. I have a small homemade
bead blaster if you have air, that is the absolute best to use inside and

up nicely and let the aluminum shine. If you must paint it, use plain paint,
it should never get hot enough to cause paint to burn in this area.
