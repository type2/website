I think I understand that TypeI pumps can be used in TypeIV engines (don't
hold me to this) and it sounds like perhaps that's what Aircooled_dot_net is
offering.  But the most common cause of low oil pressure is plain wear and
tear on the engine's bearings.  The oil pump lives in a pretty benign
environment and, absent abuse, generally lasts longer than the rest of the
engine.  It's not clear from your post whether you're thinking a new oil
pump alone will solve your problems or if this search is associated with a
planned rebuild.

I've rebuilt 2 TypeIV engines and in both cases the oil pumps were fine and
performed satisfactorily in the rebuilt motors.
