VW DEVELOPS LIGHT FUEL-EFFICIENT VEHICLE .... Volkswagen is developing a
light-weight carbon-fiber fuel-efficient car, "The Detroit News" reports.
The new car will weigh less than 1,320 pounds and use less than three
liters of fuel per 100 kilometers. The car is expected to be in
production within a few years.

POSSIBLE MERCEDES MINIVAN .... According to "The Detroit News"
DaimlerChrysler will decide by the end of this year whether to build a
Mercedes-Benz luxury minivan. The decision will mark the first step in
formulating product strategy for the new corporation.
