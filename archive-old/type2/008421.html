While "louvered window" does mean "Jalousiefenster", "Jalousie" does
not really mean "louvered".  It does men "louver" apparently, but more
in the sense of something you hang on a window of a building.  Louvers
in a car would be "Lüftungsschlitze", literally "ventilation slots",
which we'd never call a Jalousie.  Seems a camper window is more to do
with a house than a car.
