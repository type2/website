> Please help me unfix it by telling me which way to reconnect the vacuum
hoses:
> The advance goes to the intake air distributor vacuum?

NO!  The advance [outer nipple] goes to the nipple on the front of the
throttle housing. This is ported vacuum. You should have a tee and the other
line goes to the EEC valve on the back of the air cleaner.


> The retard goes to the rear of the throttle valve housing?

NO!  It goes [inner nipple] to the nipple on the right side of the plenum
between #1 + #2 intake runners. You need a tee and the other line goes to
the fuel pressure regulator.

Set your timing at idle with the vacuum lines connected to 5 degrees after.

> They also disconnected the small vacuum line leading to the EEC cut off
valve
> on the air cleaner body, where does that one connect?

To the advance side of the dist.
