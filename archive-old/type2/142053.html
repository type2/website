Are you saying Bus Depot sells repair panels for the rust/rot that develops at 
the bottom of the windshield?  I need some for my 69 Bay!  To answer your

If the panel is too short, one "should" be able to weld-on an extension.  The 
trick is to make an offset in the extension, so that the upper surfaces remain 
flush.  In other words, you have a lap seam with the overlap toward the inside 
of the Bus.  Tools for making such an offset are generally available... just 
can't recall at the moment, but I would start with autobody tool sites, maybe 
rust panel sites, even try Horrible Freight....  Glenn
