At 05:44 AM 04/14/2003, you wrote:

>Hey guys,
>
>         Another quick question... one of the hinges on my "new" Bus is
>busted. The little rod that goes through the hinge seems to have snapped.
>What's the easiest way to repair this? Do I hammer the old one out, and
>slide a metal dowell in there?

Yes. Grind or file off the ends of the rod and knock them out with a pin 
punch. Penetrating oil is advised. Cotter pin of the right size is a quick 
replacement, though stainless would be good to prevent rust.
