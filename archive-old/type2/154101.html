 How much work did you do on the oil pump.
 New?, check actually gear clearance as per Bentley?
 Did you make sure that the cover was absolutely flat and without
 gear marks in it?  Did you use the correct cover gasket?...the one that is
 not much thicker than a piece of note book paper?
 After those are correct, then it is all about bearing clearance and wear.
 Personally, I would consider 11psi at 700 rpm and 190f oil to be well
 within the good to excellent range.  At 1000 rpm I would expect the
 pressure to come up a little more...
