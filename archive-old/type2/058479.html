I put a bug engine in my '69 bus about 10 years ago. I bought a set of 
cheap metric taps and drilled and tapped it myself. I used a hand drill,
so I was very careful. I used a couple of increasing sizes of bits until 
I got to the right size for the tap. 

NOTE: Be very carefull not to drill too deep! The main oil journal for
the
pressure relief valve is in line with one of the holes!
