Jason,

Welcome to owning a bus in Colorado! You should have seen the old BustedBus
yard. It was a 24 hour wasp farm. I have found nests (both occupied and
abandoned) in every bus I have ever been under.

often and wash the outside and underside of your bus whenever you can. For
some reason by washing my buses, wasps leave them alone. I guess they figure
they are of value if they are clean so setting up house inside is too risky.
