Well, in all honesty, I was really into Japanese performance before I got
into VWs.  The knowledge I obtained working on 1.5L motors makes me wonder
what it would be like to set up a PRACTICAL turbo system on a bus.  I also
thought about a 30-40 hp shot of nitrous oxide.  Here is the deal:  I am
probably going to build another motor with spare parts in my garage.  Next
summer,  I will swap out my reliable stock motor with this one and
"experiment" with different power adders.  I will let you all know my
findings.  Nitrous oxide might be the extra boost needed for the hills.  Of
course, I really don't think the stock rods can take that much abuse.  The
benefit on N2O is that it is not so much of an "integrated" system like the
turbo, and can be switched on and off according to your needs.  I suppose
you would just switch it on and watch the oil temp : )
