Subject: Re: [T2] Leaking Oil

On Thu, Dec 11, 2003 at 08:45:28PM -0500, Michael Edwards wrote:
> My 76 is leaking a lot of oil.  The valve cover gasket is an obvious
> fix.  I pulled the tin that attaches to the heater boxes and the
> push rod tubes are leaking too.  I was just wondering, are there any
> other likely candidates that can be dealt with with the engine in?
> Looks like more is leaking up front.

Mike,	With your style engine replacing the push rod tube seals is
cake. I would start with that and trying to get your valve cover
gasket to stop. Clean the area up real good and then see where else
the oil comes from.

On the drivers side right above this area are a few other good
candidates for leaking, although not as easy to fix as the pushrods.
One is the oil filter and it's mounting, another is the oil cooler,
and yet another is the oil pressure sender. I'd just try the pushrods
first and see what becomes of that.
