On Mon, 17 Apr 2000, Cassandra wrote:
> Was wondering if anyone has heard of a product called Motor Up? It's an oil
> additive that's supposed to increase performance and decrease wear and
> tear... It also apparently (I did say, apparently....) removes all the built
> up dirt, grease and gremlins.....

Don't do it!  Nothing you put into your crankcase will improve upon the
oil that is already there.  The oil has been carefully designed to do its
job and adding some unknown (to the lubricant chemist) substance to the
crankcase will not do anything good.

Any product that claims to remove dirt from the engine must put that dirt
in suspension in the oil.  do you really want years of built-up grunge
suddenly released all at once in your VW engine?  If the dirt
is stuck to the inside of the engine it won't hurt a thing, but in your
bearings it will do very bad things.
