The shims are supposed to be under the pulley nut (behind the big washer) 
or between the pulley halves. To adjust for a smaller length fan belt you 
put more of the shims between the pulley halves.

To adjust for a longer belt you put the shims under the nut and washer.

It just changes how high the belt rides in the pulley by changing it's width.

Your adjustment does not change the position of the alternator/generator 
pulley in relation to the crank pulley b/c the rear half of the pulley is 
always in the same position.
