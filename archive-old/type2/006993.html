the way I remember it, there is a "calibrated orfice" fed with a specific
pressure.  The orfice size required varied with the engine (size?).  There
were two guages.  One verified the supply pressure to the orfice and the
other read the cyl pressure on the "downwind side" of the orfice.  If the
orfice is too big, the pressure will equal the supply pressure.
