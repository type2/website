So, It looks like the change from latches to screws may have been a mid year
change. The 69 I got my cabinet out of had the latches. It also had the 68
style Mirror (long chrome,2 screws). But the vin was right for a 69. (2X9) I
am sure that Westfalia did not follow Model year changes strictly unless
required to by a change in body design. So they may have made changes mid
year if they saw a need, the number were small enough to be able to do that.
I have also heard of people with a 72 Bus (old bumpers, low front indicator,
etc.) with a 73 Model year Title. Westfalia lagged a little behind.
