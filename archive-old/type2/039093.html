BTW, the idea of "ply rating" isn't a blatent attempt to mislead.  All
tires used to be made with cotton fiber plys and back then an 8-ply tire
really had 8 plys..  Later on, stronger materials (Rayon, nylon,
fiberglass) were used and the ply rating system was invented so that
the strengths of the tires could be compared to the older materials.
Obviously, the ply rating system has outlived the use of cotton
plys!
