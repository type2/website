Jason,

Try the easiest thing first....   Wrong rotor?????
Non oem distributor???...evil PO put different dist in?

I believe that the 1847v is the exact same Pertronix that I installed
in my stock 78 distributor.

I was probably using a 009 rotor in it though...

Going back and looking at the pics, I still don't think the sleeve
is down far enough.

Measure the sleeve and then measure from the bottom of where the sleeve 
will install upwards....mark it with a magic marker or something.

Push rotor on...does it cover up the mark after it is on?..then
something is definitely not right...triple check dist number 
and check for correct rotor.
