At 12:40 PM -0700 10/6/99, canebrake_at_yahoo_dot_com wrote:
>The PO of my 74 Westy (rebuilt 1800 with stock Solexes) decided he didn't like
>all the smog gear, so he removed it .. when I got the bus, I strobe-timed it
>to about 7.5 ATDC but it still ran like crap.  Took her to my trusty
>neighborhood mechanic who performed some sleight-of-hand with the carbs and
>told me of clogged idle cutoffs .. he then timed it to about 8 BTDC due, he
>said, to the absence of smog gear.
>
>Does this (current timing setting) seem reasonable, or am I in the process of
>burning her up?

Depends on your distributor.  If you have the stock dual-diaphragm
distributor with both vacuum hoses hooked up, 8BTDC is way too much advance
and could possibly harm your engine.  If you have an 009 or
single-diaphragm distributor it's probably okay.
