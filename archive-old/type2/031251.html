> A couple of questions though: The trusty Bentley has the following as

> 
> On cars with fuel injection, disconnect the injection system wiring
> harness from the coil, the injectors, the intake air distributor, the
> crankcase, the tempature sensors, and the intake air distributor and its

there is a wire on the coil that needs to be removed, but the coil also
has to be removed to take the engine out (quirk)

the injector wires themselfs do not have to be removed, BUT there are two
plugs on the firewall that do have to be removed, one is the injector
resistors.

there is a plug on the back side of the AFM (air flow meter) also need to
remove the entire intake system above the air distributor but not it 

take the wire apart on the temp sensor, there is an inline plug.

also there is a wire on the voltage regulator that goes through the fire
wall.

don't forget there are TWO fuel lines, one on either side of the engine
