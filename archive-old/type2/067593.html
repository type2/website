15N
426 030 139
UA 2112 383883 7058158

15N    = November 15, 1966 Production Date (1967 model year)
426    = I believe is a USA export number (no proof)
030    = Panel or Kombi delivered in primer (doesn't jibe w/color code?)
139    = sealed beam headlamps, red tail light lenses - export model
UA     = USA, ? delivery port
2112   = Panel Van, Cargo Doors Right, LHD
383883 = Velvet green over velvet green w/Mesh Grey interior
7058158= VIN# (add 21 from model # to front of #)
