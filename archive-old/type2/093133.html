I broke TWO in the same 5 miles by breaking the cage inside. This was on an 
'84 Rabbit 'vert that I owned in Italy. Same CV joint design as the bus. It

wasn't him "milking me". He wasn't the type of person to do that anyhow. He 
was doing me a favor. We used parts from the local salvage yard so it was 
CHEAP to fix.

Rough road, 15 mph, 1st gear.

Bad luck or engineered failure (tolerances that said 185.5 miles is the max 
CV life)... <grin!>
