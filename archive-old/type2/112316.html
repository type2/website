Jason,
Either the glass is short-or the metal behind it is going away--
I think the glass could be short--

The Black silicone is your best bet--
Get the 30 year stuff.
Pull back the rubber, and with a caulking gun, run around the whole
perimeter of the windshield--
Have a quart of mineral spirits ready to go-and a bunch of rags--
when you let go of the rubber the silicone will squish out from behind
it--
With your index finger and the rag soaked in the mineral spirits--make a
nice fillet around the outside of the rubber--an even one--all the way
around it--
You'll hardly be able to see it, and the leak will be a thing of the
past--

I've been doing this fix to Mack Trucks for 30 years--no more rust or
leaking around the rubber rear or windshield moulding's-

Have Fun--
