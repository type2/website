Hi Alex,

Actually, it's *really* easy if you're replacing the seals and don't want to
save the originals. Just slit them from the outside with a carpet knife, and
then press outwards from inside the vehicle. An assistant would be a good
idea -- they can catch the glass before it hits the ground.

Getting them out without damaging the seals is a bit harder, but that's not
what you said you wanted to do.
