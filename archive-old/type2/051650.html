One source of individual tent poles of various sizes and types is CAMPMOR.
Their online catalog is at:    www_dot_campmor_dot_com

Especially if you are rigging or modifying a non-Westfalia tent, or putting
together an awning or tarp setup, most of your needs are available at
CAMPMOR.  I'm not aware of any other outlet that sells individual bits and
pieces like they do, although companies like Coleman and Eureka can supply
individual parts for specific tent models they've sold.
