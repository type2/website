    I bought the Da'lan Hitch while setting up the Auto Parts store where I
worked as a dealer...  Fits my '73 Westy automatic just fine...

This bolts to bottom of bumper (I did a little improvement here by
reinforcing the bumper bottom with 1/16" steel plate), then steps down a bit
and forward to attach to rear engine crossmember... I have towed beetles up
to 40-50 mile with no problems...

    The folks at Da'lan are very helpful and are usually open to any
modifications you might want (I've been asking for a receiver type hitch
rather than a fixed tongue).

    As I bought the Adjust-a-ball set-up (1 7/8" & 2" balls) the wiring
converter and the hitch all at once they gave me a really great price (plus a
small discount  for starting up a new account)...  The whole set-up cost me
right at $125...

    Overall I'm very pleased with this company...  No longer associated with
the above un-named Parts store or Da'lan, so I have no interests other than
as a Very satisfied customer...
