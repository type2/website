Just switched tanks on our 71 SC (way easy to get to :), and according to 
Bentley..and Haynes, you might also have to change the foam seal around the 
bottom feed through area, and the 4 (?) small area seals that precent the 
tank from "squeaking". ( I think that was their term). I carefully removed 
the tank..and reused the old ones..as on SC's, to redo is no big deal. 
Haven't had a long road test on it yet..so may be looking for the seals just 
described. Good luck in finding the sender seal..and hope you don't need the 
others.
