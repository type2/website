the pedal end of the steel tube was still in good condition. But the
cable was starting to snap strands at the linkage end. So it goes..

through. It goes from the tube to the firewall. But there's no fitting
or anything at the firewall. It's held in place by the cable which I
can't imagine is a good thing since it's going to rub on the firewall
