I don't know much about Boston Bob, but I know a
Raby-built engine is one of the best things you can do
for yourself. I've spent hours at his shop, seen the
environment in which he builds the engines, and have
seen the quality of the parts he uses and the work he
puts into these motors. I've also driven a bunch of
the different configurations he uses, and he's
recommended some things to do to my own rebuilds to
make them better. 

A GEX engine is a waste of money. Jake's motors will
cost you more in the short-run, but if you consider
that they last a lot longer and perform a lot better,
that's money well-spent, and a savings in the long
haul.
