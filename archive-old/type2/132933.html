> is there a difference between points for mechanical vs. centrifugal? got

The differences are mostly in where the plug for the condenser is located.

For our engines, generally the plug is either on the bottom of the distr
(and uses a round insert) or on the side.

The points for the bottom-plug generally have a slightly longer wire, and
a straight connector.

The points for the side-plug often have a slightly shorter wire, and a 90
degree connector.

It's possible to make do with the wrong type, but..
