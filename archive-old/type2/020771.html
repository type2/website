    Now that you've used all those fuses, make up a test light from one
of them.  Solder wires to the end caps, then connect the wires to the
socket of a BRAKE light bulb (use one of the brass shell type)...BOTH of

things because it is a 12 volt bulb.  Current is high enough it will run
many small accessories, and yet protects the wiring against shorts.
