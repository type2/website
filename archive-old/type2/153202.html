Even with a broken spring leaf in my old front axle, the bus rode more
or less normal - just a little softer and lower. If a bump in the
driveway results in changes in ride height, I would first check for
rust. Good chance that the beam is rusted and flexes where the arms
attach. As strong as the spring is, I would doubt that lack of chassis
lube could introduce enough friction for the suspension to stay down.
