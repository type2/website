I'd leave the heat shield there.  It could save you or someone else from
a really nasty burn.  Once the engine is shut off the converter stays
hot but the heat shield cools fairly quickly.  I used to work at an
exhaust shop so I know this from experience.

To solve the problem go to an exhaust shop and ask them to install an 02
sensor bung for you upstream of the cat.  It'll probably cost less than
10 bucks.  Sometimes they'll do it for free.
