The braided cloth covered hoses are standard "by the yard" items in metric
>from Germany. The rubber 90 connectors will have a number on them. Measure
the inside and outside diameter of the hoses. The FI smooth hoses are also
Standard metric German manufacture. The breather hose is very special material
and size. I can get you good pricing on everything but the breather hose, dealer only, if you will provide the measures and numbers.

The hoses are not cheap, but they do last 15 to 20 years, if you use the good
clamps and don't overtighted them.

No need to get hosed on hoses.
