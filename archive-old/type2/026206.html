As far as I'm concerned the only good reasons for taking the spark plugs out
are: its time to replace them; you are playing with your carburation and
need to find how clean you're burning; or if you question your compression.
I'm a believer in, "If it ain't broke, don't fix it."  Just do regular
maintenance to keep it that way.  I've had a spark plug strip out 100 miles
from home (P/O cross threaded it) and don't like to tempt fate by taking
plugs out just because.
