Sunbrella fabric that the more expensive PLF canvas uses.  My supplier for

depending on year).  All of these are three-window canvases.  I stock them
for immediate delivery, so you can have it within days of your order.  I've
sold literally hundreds of them throughout the years and gotten very
positive reports from users.

vanagons).  There is also an orange one but personally I'm not nuts about
the color (the same neon-bright orange color of a highway road cone or a

most of these from PLF, so pricing and lead-time are about the same as if
