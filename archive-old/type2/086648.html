It sounds like the 1800 heads will fit according to David Schwarze. Make
sure you switch them both. Your engine should have oval port exhausts. I
think it was 79 when they went square. If the heads fit and are in good
shape you should be okay then. I have never done this so don't quote me. I
guess your exhaust should fit as long as both heads have the same exhaust
ports. What about the head temp sensor if you are running FI? You may need
to drill the new head out and tap it for this sensor, unless you are
running carbs.
