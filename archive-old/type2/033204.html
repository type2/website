Folks,

This is so true. Just finished rebuilding a B series 1600 DP for my 71
Campmobile. (For Sale) Ran the engine and was surprised to hear a rapping
sound. I initially thought it was from the heads, which were fairly new. I
installed another set of larger valve heads, reassembled and reinstalled the
engine. Started her up. Same sound. I then called a VW mechanic friend and
had him come over. We took a piece of breather hose and did the stethescope
treatement. No rapping from the engine or heads. Put the hose on the center
of the exhaust. Loud rapping sound. I couldn't believe it! How could an
exhaust make a noise like that. I installed a merged with single muffler. No
more rapping, lots of smooth power.
I think the baffle in the stock style empi had somehow come loose from a bad
weld or something. You can not make the noise by shaking the exhaust. Only
by running the engine. I lost big hours and a nice set of large valve heads.
Moral of the story.
Don't buy EMPI!
