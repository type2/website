The split arms with the sideways bolts are the NHTSA
recall replacement arms for split buses. 

Split bus arms are straight ended.

68 only arms fasten like the earlier split bus arms:
You have to lift the blade/arm away from the glass and
tighten/loosen the screw.

69 up arms are held on with a cap nut.

All bay window arms have a 45 degree bend at the end
and the end is looped into a U.
