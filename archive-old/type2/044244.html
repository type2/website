In regards to the ECU knowing your at full throttle, that is a yes. 
There is a "full enrichment" switch mounted on the throttle housing.

I was talking with my mechanic a while back and he had mentioned that if
the little contact slider plate goes out (inside the AFM), he would
order a new little PCB and replace it, instead of replacing the whole
thing...

Did you check the resistances of the AFM as described in the bentley
manual?
