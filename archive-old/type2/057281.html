Regarding various comments on side to side play on CV joints, this was my
post above some time back.

I just noticed in Haynes that it mentions that an instruction went out
sometime from VW to the dealers telling them to leave off the concave washer
when renewing the joint. So if your CV is original its quite likely there
will be little side to side play, but if it's like mine you'll be able to
move them side to side about 1/4 inch. It runs fine.
