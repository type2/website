A/C info here.  I had passed the earlier a/c question on to a very good
friend of mine who is an refrigeration expert.  We worked at Volkswagen
Products Corporation designing a/c for a lot of v/w products in the early
70's.

Here is his reply...


The best way to update any R-12 A/C  system is
to replace the R-12  with a refrigerant named FREEZE 12.  No need to
change the oil or anything, and can be added right in with the existing
R-12..  The conversion to 134A  in an R-12 system never works right
becuase of the specific heats of the refrigerants is to diverse.  A 134A
system requires 30% more condensor than an R-12 system.  The condensor
conversion is too difficult.  The freeze12 is a blend of several
refrigerants and the blend is designed to deliver almost identical
performance as r12.
