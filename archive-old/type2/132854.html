The plan is to bolt the replacement engine and tranny together and install

Someone on the Buskatiers list reminded me of the method I use when working
on my bug. I spaced it out completely when asking the original question.

Get both sides up a "safe" height and on jackstands ("safe" may not be
quite high enough to roll the engine under -- I'm taking off the air
cleaners but not the carbs).  Then, take one rear wheel off and roll the
engine in through the opening.

I probably will go next door (lumber yard) and buy 6' of 4"x4" so I can
span the bus and use the jack just forward of the rear wheels to get up on
stands.
