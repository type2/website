Steve: 
In brushes, size matters. Length at least. Also important that the
springs are okay.
The generator should be a 211 number, but the 38 amps is good, so I'd
not be too concerned about it not being the right part. I'd take out the
regulator and take off the cover. Sometimes you don't have to know too
much about it to tell that it's shot. That was my case anyway. The
Bentley does have all the specs and how to set the contacts in the
regulator, but mine were just fried!
