Those are called Deltic Diesel engines.   They were used extensively in
locomotives and ships for quite awhile.  Two crankshafts rotate in the
same direction, while the third rotates opposite.  Two stroke diesels,
with the porting in the middle of the cylinders handling exhaust/intake.
Extremely powerful engines.  Torque monsters.  Mechanically, they're a
nightmare of gears, and probably an absolute bear to get timed.
Generally, you would have banks of 6 pistons (one "set", so it was 6,
12, 18, or 24 pistons.
