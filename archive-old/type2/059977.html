An old welder showed me this one: Empty the tank and blow it dry with
air - then stick the filler hose tube on the exhaust pipe of a *running
vehicle* leave it on for five minutes or so,remove it and  do your
welding. The carbon monoxide being heavier than air will completly
displace any OX in the tank -No OX- NO FIRE - This is foolproof .I have
used it many times on auto and bike tanks.
