Amazing that this can happen where there is stringent vehicle
inspections, isn't it!  This example shows the folly of thinking that
mandatory inspections can yield increased levels of vehicle safety. 
There's always a crook out there who will pass any rolling pile of junk.

The final arbiter of safety in a bus is _the driver_.  While the
dealer involved in this sale is obviously derelict in their duties,
the real blame lies with the customer who drove away from that dealer
knowing full well the hazardous nature of the vehicle (it's kind of
hard to hide a bad steering box and brakes!)

Sounds like you're doing the right thing to fix this bus, but once
this is done why sell it?  Sounds like it will be a good ride once the
mechanicals are put right.
