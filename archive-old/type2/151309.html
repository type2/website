Hi Mike,

If there's power to the solenoid, then the double-relay sends it to  
the fuel pump. When the starter is released the power to the fuel  
pump comes from the switch inside the AFM.

The remote starter is still powering the solenoid, so the system will  
work the same no matter which switch you trigger the starter with.
