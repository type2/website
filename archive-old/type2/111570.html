I feel this is something real simple that I am overlooking and when it is discovered I'm gonna slap myself."Sounds like timing and or firing order.When you are TDC, # 1 cyl should have both intake and exhaust valves closed, with the piston out as far as it goes.The TDC mark on the pulley should line up with the center of the case. If that is all good make sure the rotor lines up to the notch on the dizzy, then check to make sure all plug wires are in the right order 1-4-3-2 (cw).Check all your plugs to make sure they are not fouled.P.
