I have had that happen to me before.  When you take the pump off the motor, 
try rotating the shaft and see if it falls down into the block just a 
little more.  What was happening to mine was the shaft had gotten wedged up 
a little higher and over extended into he pump.  It wore out the pump in 
short time, twice.
