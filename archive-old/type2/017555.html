Tom:
If you have long AND short rods I don't think you have 2 two-liter
engines. Two liter engines have shorter rods so that they put all the
extra 5mm. stroke at the bottom of the cylinder and not at the top and
bottom both. (71mm.-66mm.=5mm.)
I'm sure you could use a calipers to measure the distance top of pin
hole to top of piston. This should help you compare pistons. While
you're at it measure the diameter. 2-liter's are 94mm, 1.8's 93mm. bore.
Measure across the skirt perpendicular to the pin.
