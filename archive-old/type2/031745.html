the flat plate front carrier is for an auto tranny it is significantly
different than the Manual tranny.

the plate shown in Rocky Mountain catalog is indeed the auto tranny mount
NOT the one for a manual, it should indicate this but doesn't.

compare drawing in Bentley
manual chapt 6, illus  4.1
auto  chapt 7, hmmmmmm  no decent illustration, but in 1.1 notice the
housing on the far left. it has a slot into which the  flat plate
slides. the slot is lined with rubber to serve as snubber.

also it appears the 73-75 auto tranny may have used the same as a manual
tranny but having pulled mine enough times I can assure you that is the
part for a 76-79 auto tranny...!

also is another reason why the manual is easier to pull out as engine
tranny combined and an auto tranny is easier to pull out as seperate units
