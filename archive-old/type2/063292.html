It should be no major problem. Naturally, try to avoid scratching the 
original paint any more than necessary. Take a can of spray primer and a 
few Q-tips. Spray the primer into the cap (watch your eyes), until you 
get a puddle. Dip the Q-tip and apply to the bare metal. Wait until 
dry. Repeat w/ a paint similar to the orginal color. To get "fancy" you 
can apply heavy coat of wax after several hours (don't rub, just apply, 
let dry, remove the excess). The heater duct flanges should cover the 
"ugly", and the paint will protect from rust.
