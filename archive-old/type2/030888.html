only the type 1 engines have a rebuild kit (AFAIK) the type 4 engines are
fuel injected and have a high presure electric pump.  these buggers are
EXPENSIVE so if you can find a good used one so much the better (Rocky
Mountain #0 580 463 016 $ 214.50 (1974 is $312.75!)

> any light on the subject.  Also, what is the best way to clamp fuel lines -

use a round (not six sided) pencil,  I have small dowels, also I have put
a stopcock valve in my fuel lines so I just turn the valve. (also great
when parking to keep the vehicle from being taken) I used the brass gas
line fittings, with ball valve so there is nothing the gas can damage.
