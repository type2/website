Well, I've been over this engine (recent rebuild) and I can't get the
freakin thing to run smooth or hold an idle.  This is the first time I
have had trouble like this in an engine I've rebuilt.  But of course you
learn something new with each one. 

I installed a set of 41 heads from Mofoco.
After driving I found a persistant oil leak.  Thinking it was the tubes,
I pulled the engine to reseal them.  The leaking continued. Upon closer
inspection of the head I discovered a manufacturing defect that allowed
oil to drain right out from the bottom of the rocker arm area. I
purchased a new head (I couldn't return the defective head yet because
it was still on the engine) and this solved the problem. 

I feel I'm going to have to pull it again based on the compression
readings I took this past weekend.  I never checked the compression
ratio with these new heads thinking they were probably the same volume
as stock.  Well the readings may confirm what I fear most.

#2 - 128

#4 - 115

Warm compression test
