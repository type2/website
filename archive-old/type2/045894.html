First I would take a look at your bentley where it describes the shift
lever mount.  Under there is a piece of metal that keeps you from 
putting the tranny in reverse without pushind down on the stick.  If
this piece breaks, it could cause your shifter to get kinda sloppy.  It
was broken on mine (76 campmobile :)
