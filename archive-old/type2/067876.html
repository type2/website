Just because it is marked *H* does not mean it is a 1500. It could have
been rebuilt to 1600 just by changing in jugs. The 1500 did not have the
cross member holes in them according to the books I have. How does your
engine mount at the rearend?

 
> Question #2 is, of course - how should I time this thing? I'm going to
> do it static; just can't figure out what the best timing for the thing
> would be.


Static timing depends on the dist you have. Run no more than 28 - 30
TOTAL advance; set the static at what ever point you need to get that
total.
