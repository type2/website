HC is unburned fuel. You have that correct. Too high = too rich if CO is
high or misfiring if CO is low.

CO is burned fuel. You are NOT too lean, you are TOO rich. You need to lean
it down or lower your fuel pressure or replace your wornout injectors.... As
injectors wear, the pintles pound into the seat, making the point taper
grooved. Like a carb idle screw if you tighten it too tight. The ECU tells
the injector how long to stay open but it can't tell it how much to inject.
New it injects X amount of fuel per millisecond, worn it will inject maybe
twice as much for the same millisecond duration.
