If yes, he has a 69 bus. 68 to 70 buses were single port engines with a 30 pic whatever carb. This carbs
vacuum advance signal is mapped at a max of a little over 3" of vacuum. The large diameter of the vacuum unit
on the vacuum-only advance distributor is designed to give Vacuum (Advance Only): 17-19deg _at_ 1.3 In. Hg,
32-35deg _at_ 3.2 In. Hg.

The SVDA dist is designed for a 34 pict whatever carb. The amount of vacuum needed for the vacuum unit is
tremendous.

Distributor................VW Mexico..... Bruck
      Vacuum... advance... Inches of Vacuum Inches of Vacuum
      1 Degree..... 3.5 inches..... 4 inches
      2 Degree..... 4 inches..... 5 inches
      3 Degree..... 5 inches..... 6 inches
      4 Degree..... 6 inches..... 7 inches
      5 Degree...... 8 inches..... 8 inches
      6 Degree............. ................ 9 inches


Double the degrees above for crankshaft figure.

On the large vacuum unit, 3.2" gives 32-35 degrees.
On the small vacuum unit, 3.5" gives 2!! degrees.
