Back in '95 I bought a bunch of stuff from them and was very satisfied. I 
bought the shorty dual carb manifolds for a Type IV, the carb linkage, the 
911 cooling kit (don't like the fiberglass but the aluminum parts I like),

replies were broken English. Very patient and helpful.

VA (I was in the Navy) and then they forwarded it to me on the ship. Got it 
at sea near Haiti but of course had no car to put them on... <grin>

Box was tattered, cargo was not.
