Subject: Re: [T2] Rear Brake Backing Plate Removal

> Does anyone know any secrets or sure-fire methods to removing a stuck
 backing plate?
 
 ----------------------------------------------------------------------

Easy. Just below the 2 lower bolts you removed from the backside on the bottom is a dowel pin.
Get an old 6" long 10mm bolt or a large pin punch and drive the dowel pin out from the back side.
The backing plate will pop right off.
