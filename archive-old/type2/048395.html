There's an oil deflector ring at that end of the crankshaft on a Type 1
engine; assuming it's there, that part is no fun to replace since you need
to split the case to do it, and I'm not really sure if it's necessary.
Excessive end play could keep this from seating properly.

I'd like to know if this is above or below the tin--above, maybe the oil
slinger and possibly (longshot) the oil pressure sensor--if it's cracked at
the base, maybe but not likely. Below the tin? Oil pump, oil slinger, galley
plug, crack in the case...
