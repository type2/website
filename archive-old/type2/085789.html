Subject: Re: [T2] Re: Wheel rivets

I've put new clips in on several occasions. I ended up making a tool to hold 
the clip and rivet on the wheel. I used a 3/8s by 1 inch piece of bar stock 
and notched it to clear the clip. Then I ground a dimple in it with a 
carbide burr. Then I just used a pair of vise grip panel clamps to hold it 
all down. I heated the rivet with a torch to make it smash a little easier. 
I can do one in a matter of minutes now.

The first time I did one I used a couple of c clamps and that method doesn't 
work very well, but it is doable.
