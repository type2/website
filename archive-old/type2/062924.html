At 06:44 PM 9/8/96 -0700, you wrote:
>Ok, I'm stumped. How do I take the door handle off to get the door panel
>off in my '61 Kombi?
>
>It's killing me, and I'm going to laugh when I hear it.

There is a pin you have to push out.  You need an ice-pick like device to do
it.  The hole the pin goes in is larger on one side of the handle than the
other.  Push the round trim piece in towards the door to expose the pin.
