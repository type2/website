after you remove the valve cover and check to make sure that your valves are
adjusted correctly to .006,  and the valves on that side are not all
completely closed.  Check the intake manifold where it connects to the head
on the left side.  Sounds like you have a vacuum leak.

to verify it's leaking*sucking air* -- with the engine at idle spray some
WD-40 around the manifold & carburetor connections, on each connection one
at a time.  If the engine changes speeds or speeds up at a particular
connection, you found your problem......
