i dont know what distributor you have. typical static timing will usually
end up around 7.5 degrees btdc (ish) better to time it with a strobe.
if you have one with advance set it so max advance is maybe 32-35 degrees.
i set mine like 32.
if your strobe doesnt have advance you can do a little math and mark the
flywheel at the 35 degree mark. i used to do this but now i have a degree
pulley on there.
