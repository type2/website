 I recently did a post on painting . . and while I did go get a 
gun of hvlp type for the final paint, it was more of a cost thing.

 You don't need special guns to put these new paints on with . .

 In fact we put all the primers on with a detail gun . . old style

 The spec sheets for all these PPG paints come with recommendations
for the high pressure guns also .. 

 The big hassle with the epoxy, or any of the 2 part paints is that
they have limited pot life . . 2 hours or what. . . .

 Doesn't lend itself to "scrape, brush, primer" like one does with
the spray can or 1 part paint . .

 The trick here is to have a freezer about where you can store 
your mixed 2 part till you can use it . . 

 So far we've kept a 2 part paint in a regular fridge's freezer
for over two months with no kicking off or other deleterious affects.
