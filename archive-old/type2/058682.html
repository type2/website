I have a fairly recent Wolfsburg West catalog that shows the boots as 
p/n 211 711 183B "Boot, shift rod, 1957-1979, two per car" _at_ $6.00 -- 
this is a 5/97 price sheet, BTW.

I've ordered mine through Ron at the Bus Depot -- he had to do some 
digging but I *think* he may be able to find the whole shebang -- 
boots and all *three* bushings.  Don't forget the plastic bushing 
inside the nose of the front shift rod.

Other part numbers per WW and RMMW (who carries the nose bushing and 
front rod bushing):

nose bushing 211 711 197
front rod bushing 211 711 179
rear rod bushing 211 711 185B

Are those confusing or what?  BTW the WW catalog shows #179 as NLA, 
but it's in the new RMMW catalog.  I advise comparing the new & old 
bushings CLOSELY when received.  And if Bus Depot gets stumped, I'd 
appreciate knowing other sources for this stuff.
