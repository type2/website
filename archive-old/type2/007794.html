Hi Dan (and anyone else contemplating this mystery!),

An original '71 bus manifold (211 part # prefix stamped on the center
section) has a 1/2" port welded to the manifold.  I use a bug manifold on my
'71 and like you, made an adapter to accomodate the small port off the end
casting.  Most original '71 bus manifolds (one year only part--bugs had a
slightly different center section--only "difference" was no port was welded
on!) don't exist anymore because when the heat risers get plugged and a bus
manifold replacement isn't available, people just swap on a bug manifold and
either braze on a 1/2" fitting or use an adapter off the end casting to
accomodate the '71's power booster.
