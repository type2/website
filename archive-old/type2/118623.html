> I was replacing the fuel pump on my 1976 FI bus. The line from the tank

 But I need to replace the braided line from the pump to the engine. What

YES, it is high pressure fuel. Go to your local Mercedes-Benz dealership and buy 2 meters of 7.5mm fuel hose,
part number 916030-000527. It will have the MB star on it and say 7.5 x 3.
Do not use cheapo worm drive hose clamps with open slots in them.... they will cut your hose. Use the factory
VW hose clamps or MB hose clamp, part number 916002-012100. They will have the MB star on them and the #12.
Do NOT use Gates 5/16" fuel hose! 1) it is low pressure hose and 2) it will slide off....
