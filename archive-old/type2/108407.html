The cable that broke snapped about 12" from the trannie end of the run.  Had
the whole thing installed and ready to go in about 30 minutes.  Made a run
and tightened the wing nut up a tad when we stopped.  Shifts smooth and with
less effort than before.  Which isn't surprising considering the condition
of the old cable.

last installer had used a cotter pin and a split hollow pin together to hold
the clevis to the clutch lever.  Thanks to whoever it was that suggested it,
I'd purchased the clip along with the cable.  I'm picking up another cable
and clip in a few weeks as spares, too.

$800.00 and not having my car for 10 weeks (had to pay it off before they'd
release it) to fix the clutch on the Sentra.  I'm sooooo happy. :O)
