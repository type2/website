Hi all,

I have a 78 with a 2.0L FI engine with Pertronix ignition. This has about
10k on it since a rebuild by Boston Bob. Normally, she runs beautifully. I
put her into storage last year, and it was running fine. When I went to take
her out, she wouldn't fire. I pulled the air cleaner and made sure the AFM
flap was free and was activating the fuel pump. I used starting fluid, and I

looked for any egregious vac leaks, but couldn't find anything. My plan is
to  go and change the plugs and possibly the coil. I may also try to static

place and working.

Thoughts on what simple thing this might be, or where to start? I know the
