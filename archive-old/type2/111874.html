Hey there Kyle,

You can use the 72-74 style muffler. I've got one on my 79. Check out the bus
depot's online catalog (or order one, it's a great reference):

http://catalogs.google_dot_com/catalogs?hl=en&lr=&ie=UTF-8&issue=16766&catpage=cover

The part # is 071 251 053E (page 34). You're gonna need gaskets and a tailpipe
too.

They didn't have the SS one in stock when I ordered, but even tho' my muffler
has been holding up fine, I regret not getting the SS one (071 251 053ES).

I did not like the extractor system that was on the bus when I bought it.
