Had one like that in my '73.  It was the blower for the heaters.  Draws 
air from the engine compartment and sends in past the heat exchangers to
heat it up, then up to the front of the bus to exit thru the inside vents 
and warm the passengers.  The blower came on when I moved the little red
tipped heater handles on the dash to turn the heat on.
