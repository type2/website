Blake,

This is right up our alley.

This part is called a radius arm. The Part number that you are looking for
is 211 501 066 J this is the right radius arm and fits the years 1968-1971.
The radius arm changed at the end of 1971. This is a bit involved but
doable. The biggest problem would be if the pivot bushing froze. This part
can be seen our web site under Rear Axle and then Double Jointed axle. Let
me know if you have any other questions. If you need this part let me know
we have them. Just a bolt up under the body and a few bolts to the back of
the wheel bearing housing.
