Nope, the low profile tires have a lower weight rating for a given
pressure and load range.  Terry's tires are load range C (I think),
and rated at 1435lbs at 50PSI.  This is too low a load capacity for a
bus by about ten percent.  If you run 195/75R14 tires, you have to get
load range D.

I learned the same lesson on my bus, and now insist on 185R14 tires. 
The 195/75s are used as chain tires in the snow.
