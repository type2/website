Although I am not in digest mode, I also run 10.3. I assume you use 
"Mail" to read mail. Rather than "reply to all," I simply hit reply, 
then add "type2_at_type2_dot_com" on the "to" line (I actually type "ty" and

message that you want to quote before hitting "reply." Only the 
selected text will appear as a quotation.
