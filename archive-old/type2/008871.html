Re the suggestion to glue magnetic stripping to the front doors;
I simply used stick-on velcro fuzz all around the window frame, and sewed
the hook side to netting (easiest way is to stick the fuzz on, then attach
the hookside pieces, then slap the screen over this and "tack" the location
of the pieces with a hot melt glue gun.  Remove carefully and sew on the
velcro- I used some thin 1" webbing OVER the netting to creat a nice
looking sandwich.  These peel easily to pay toll takers or open the vent windows.
