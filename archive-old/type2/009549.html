Perhaps my words were a bit strong here.  Some folks have had good
luck with the oil-pump adapters, particularly the adapters that also
attach to the case bolts lower down.  This additional attach point is
important because the rear mount takes all the torsional loads from
the engine, as the front mount twists without much resistance.

However, if the case already has the bosses cast in, I doubt that the
adapter will fit over them.  In this case, the obvious solution is to
drill and tap the bosses for use as originally intended.
