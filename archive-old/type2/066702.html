If you have any intention of driving this vehicle for any length of time, 
buy a new case. It's worth the money. Used cases are one of the reasons we 
so many posts like "...I was cruising down the freeway when my motor blew 
up. Can't believe it, it only had 5,000 miles on it..." To paraphrase Bob 
Hoover, unless you know the history of the case, and know that it has NEVER 
been overheated and stretched beyond its limits, you're better off buying 
new.
