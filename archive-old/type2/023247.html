Hi Melissa, 

The spring steel "starnut retainers" are somehow crimped 
into place. Once they are gone VW suggests to replace 
the backing plates. But they too are NLA from VW and the 
last known price was about $50. I have seen the lil 
springs somewhere at the shows over here in Europe, but 
that too has been a while. What you could do is go to a
Porsche dealer, or call a classic Porsche parts supplier, 
and ask for these clips for a, say, 1960 356. These look 
like half the thingie that was/is on you bus and have a 
little hole in them. On Porsches they are held on with 
a little screw. If you have some of these, you could 
repair your backing plates, either by welding them into 
place, or by drilling a hole, and using a sheet metal 
screw to hold them in place, or even tap the hole and 
use a little machine screw. 

Hope it helps,
