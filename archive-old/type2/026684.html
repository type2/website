Michael, I'm glad the relay worked well for you.  I like the clever way you
rigged it to the alternator.  But, please see my earlier post about the need
to modify the alternator so that the batteries will receive the full
charging voltage.  Simply connecting a standard isolator to a Bosche
alternator will result in low voltage output and the poor battery charging
conditions you were experiencing.  With the proper isolator and the simple
alternator modification, an isolator should work just as well, and (no
flames or FARTs please) perhaps better under certain conditions such as when
there is a large voltage difference between the two batteries.
