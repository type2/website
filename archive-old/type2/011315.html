I also have just a regular bus.  It's official name, such as on the 
insurance info, is stated as a Kombi.  I've also heard these referred to as 
Station Wagons.  I've always called it a bus or "The Bus" but definitely not 
a Micro Bus.  Purists will have a fit if you call a late model bay window a 
Micro Bus.

I also installed a custom homemade bed in my regular bus.  I just removed 
the back 4 person seat and made a platform that extended the hump over the 
engine compartment up to the middle 3 person seat.  This made a long enough 
(when laying slightly diagonal) area to sleep quite comfortably.  You can 
then store all your gear underneath the platform and still carry passengers 
in the one back seat.

Also, what's the deal with campers and Westies.  It seems alot of the time 
when I tell people about my bus, or try to sell it, they don't seem to care 
after they find out it's not a camper.  I know the campers are cool and all, 
but regular busses are just as good.
