Since modelyear 1973 or 1974 the heater outlet is controlled by a lever
on the dash. The one that has "DEF." beside it. If it is in the lowest
position all the heat goes to the windshield. In the middle the heat
goes the your feet in the cabin. Turn it to the top and you'll have heat
in the back as well. Often this system doesn't work anymore like it was
new. Perhaps one cable is broken or not connected to the lever anymore.
