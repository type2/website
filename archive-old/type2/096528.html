Craig,

Sounds pretty good, but for a bus, you need to lose the lightweight 
flywheel.  You need the regular one, otherwise you are going to get 
a lot of "bucking" everytime you let up or get on the accelerator.

Sell the leftovers for big bucks and get something else.

I saved this cam site long ago...time to check it out...

http://www.amotion_dot_com/tech/camtalk.html  

Some folks claim the sp has more umph on the low end...which you need in a 
bus.  A *very* mild cam might help a little on the top end...but are you
really going to go there in a bus?

Just my opinion, dave
