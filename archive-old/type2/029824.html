The Rockford boots seem to be a bit shorter than the stock boots.  You
need to move the "Axle" end of the boot toward the CV (it won't be between
the "bumps" any more).  Use narrow worm drive hose clamps to secure the
ends of the boot to the flange and axle.  Don't tighten them too much!

George <3+ years on Rockfords.  Still no cracks!
