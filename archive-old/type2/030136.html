I won't hurt to replace the condensor.  What you do is pop the cap loose
and then pull the rest of the distributor and work on it on the bench.
Much easier to get at things and
if you drop the little screw you just tip it out.  When you're done and
the gap is set you pop the whole thing back in.

Don't be scared of the thing!  It's very simple and easy to work on.
