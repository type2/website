Hi Nikki and thanks for stopping by at Jumbo Rocks.  We are Dennis and Cindy
Bergey from Santa Barbara, CA.  Cindy and I want to thank you for inviting
all of us from the list.  We would have never gone to Joshua Tree if it
wasn't for that effort!  What a great place.  We will go back again and
again.  We had no idea what a nice park that is.  Giant rocks everywhere.
Some awesome hiking trails.  Good vibes!

We arrived at Jumbo Rocks on Friday and got the last campsite at about 3pm.
We looked around for other Volkswagens from the list but didn't find any
till Jack came after dark in his '73 Westy.  We met some boy scouts that had
an '87 Westy in their group and chatted with the owner, Bob, for a while.

Saturday afternoon we realized we had not brought enough water so we went
into 29 palms for the night and got showers and rejuvenated at a local RV
Resort.

Sunday we explored the southern end of Joshua Tree including Cottonwood
Campground where we saw Nikki's, David's and Jack's VW campers parked.  We
were headed back to Santa Barbara at that point and only got a "wave"
goodbye from everyone riding in the jeep, however, it was a very nice
weekend and we met some nice people.

Hope to see you all at future campouts!

Dennis and Cindy Bergey
