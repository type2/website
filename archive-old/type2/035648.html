> SO. WHAT in the tranny is boogered that is spinning when it ain't s'posed

This is most likly your gear oil getting real thick when cold. When it gets
thick, it causes friction on the moving parts, making them want to move
together. Once the oil thins up (from heat), the problem goes away. My bus
has done this ever since I've had it. At first I thought it was maybe old
bad oil that for some reason was getting thick to easily at night. I gave it
an oil change and it still did it. A couple mechanics have told me that it
can be fairly normal with cars such as old VWs.
