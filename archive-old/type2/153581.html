Subject: Re: Oil pressure sender relocation kit

I wouldn't be too concerned about vibration. However, proximity to the
distributor may be an issue. If you look at Steve Dolan's installation with
enlarged hole in the tin, the dizzy and the oil pressure sender are really
close. Raising the oil pressure sender, may make this area even more
crowded. Also, on a dual carb engine, this is where the throttle cable runs.
Not a big deal if you have the tall early intake manifolds. But you may run
into clearance issues with the short manifolds - like those used on dual
carb Vanagons.
