start my car for months and I couldn't figure out what the problem was.

> You're right!  This is a classic symptom of excessive resistance in the
> electrical system.
> The warning light on generator systems compares gen voltage to batt voltage.
> If the gen voltage is low or not there, the light burns bright.
> However, if the batt voltage is lower than gen voltage, the light will glow.
> In your case, I'll bet your gen is charging okay and you have a bad
> connection somewhere
> which is lowering the batt voltage.  Maybe wiring connections, ign switch,
> or the like.
