Subject: Cost of creating a VW list CD

I know that the VW list CD may not go anywhere, but I thought it would be 
interesting to throw out some numbers on CD production for the benefit of 
the list members. This is one of the low cost quotes that we received 
lately. Pressing cost includes two color art work provided by client, 
price goes down obviously as the qty. goes up:

Qty. 500
- ---------
Initial up front cost        $650.00
CD Pressing (_at_ $0.65 each)   $325.00
jewel cases (_at_ $0.30 each)   $150.00
                             -------
                   Total    $1125.00
               Cost each CD    $2.25



Not a bad price, but someone would obviously have to incur these costs up 
front.
