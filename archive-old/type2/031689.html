I have a 78 Automatic and routinely remove my engine without touching the
tranny.   be warned though this is one heavy engine..  also there are a
few things on the automatic engine that are not on the manual that have to
be done.

. the tranny fluid fill tube must be removed
. the accelerator linkage is different and is attached to the tranny. it
must be disconected at the throttle body and taken apart it can easily be
bent in removeing the engine.
. if you have an early (73-75) auto tranny there is some vacuum tubing to
the tranny
. you have to remove the coil, trust me you have to remove the coil
