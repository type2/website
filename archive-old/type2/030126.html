> The flasher may be bad. Where is the flasher located? Thaks!

It plugs into the bottom of the fuse block.  You have to disconnect the
battery (to prevent nasty sparks!), unclip the fuse block (the clip is the
metal tab in front with the rectangular hole) and lift the fuse block.
The flasher relay is one of the little boxes on the bottom of the block.
