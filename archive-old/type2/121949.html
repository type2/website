Subject: RE: [T2] 72 heater controls

The flapper on the pipe, up front and under the dash, going to the
defrosters work opposite of what it looks
The two levers (fare right) that should of had red dash knobs, have to be
all the way down to open the flappers, this pulls the cables up and out of
the guide tube to open the heater boxes to let the air go to the front. 
The other to levers that should have blue knobs are the fresh air vents,
these should be all the way up to push the flappers to the front air vents
down to close them.
Just remember heat is cable operated, fresh air is all hard linked to the
front vents. 

Hope that helps!
