7.5 BTDC is right.

If the timing is too retarded the engine will never start.

Too advanced and the engine will 'ping' and you'll watch the fan kick  
backward just before it stops. Same ping can happen if the firing  
order is not right.

Further things to confirm:

1) Power to the coil.

Turn on the key and check there is 12V at terminal 15. Terminal 1  
connects to ground (and there is also a white wire required for fuel  
injection).

2) Spark plug wires are in the right order.

1-4-3-2 is the order starting from the notch going clockwise.

3) Distributor is the right way around:

http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/FAQ/images/31-DrivePinion.jpg

If this is backwards then you need to swap the spark plug wires  
around to compensate.

4) Fan notch is in the right position:

http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/FindTimingMark.html
