the norm.  Everything seemed normal, so on we went. Then the engine died and
the oil light comes on.  I rolled  to a stop on the shoulder, and went
around to the engine, opened up the hatch and was welcomed with a *hot
dipstick and smoke coming out of the oil fill tube.  Thanks to the kind
people of N.W Oklahoma who stopped to help push the bus onto a side road.
