VW memories:

So when I was growing up in CA my mom had a VW Karmann Ghia whose
previous owner had installed a oil filter kit consisting of a roll of
one-ply toilet paper.  I remember her changing the roll occasionally.
This was the days before personalized license plates and the number
portion happened to be "007" - more than one note was left on the car.
Funny, huh?

My first car was a super Beetle convertible - red/black.

Then when I was in college in San Diego a roommate drove a suped-up
(sp?) Ghia whose license plate said "IEATV8S".  He worked on VW's and
later lost part of a finger to one.  Oooops!
