>> Also, What is a good item to use when plugging the gas line to the 
>> carb?
>
> You can squeeze the hose shut with medical forceps (I believe that is 
> what they are called) but I have heard that this will split or weaken 
> the rubber.
>
> I use either a Phillips head screwdriver or a pencil. If need be I use 
> a hose clamp to squeeze the hose down on the pencil.
>
> Be sure that your rubber lines have at least a grommet any place where 
> a rubber hose passes through the sheetmetal.
