Rob, I recently took the belts out of the back of my 76' Westy and plan on 
giving them the same treatment that worked on my Jeep a couple of years 
back.  Wash them in soapy water, (w/ a brush if needed) rinse, and let them 
dry out completely.  Then take some 303 protectant and wipe a light coat on 
your belts.  This will let them slide easier through the adjuster and give 
them a nice shine.  Make sure to use 303 and not Armor All, as Armor All is 
petroleum based, while 303 is water based...less chance of degrading the 
belts.  Hope this helps
                                                     -Mike
