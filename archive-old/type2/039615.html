In the same situation, I replaced the generator.  Be sure that the one you
get replaces the one you have.  The stock generators were specific to the
bus, but many of them have been replaced with bug alternators.  the plate
that attaches the generator to the fan shroud is slightly different for
the two different generators.

The alternator route will work as well.  Be sure to get good quality
parts.  Some poor-quality alternator stands are floating around.  Don't
forget to switch to an alternator-style fuel pump as well.
