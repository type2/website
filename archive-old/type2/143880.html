On Mon, Jun 06, 2005 at 08:22:30AM -0400, Messa, Corbett (GE Infrastructure) wrote:
> I am helping a friend try to install a set of CB performance carbs on his Type
> 2 bus. I have the same set, but never installed them, currently running stock
> carbs. We got them on and the bus started. After it ran for about 2 minutes or
> so, I turned it off. It ran ok, but was somewhat erratic at idle. I noticed
> that the right carb manifold was hot and the left carb manifold was cool to
> the tough.

I am running the CB set on mine with no problems. I would condider
that perhaps the cool side is not firing correctly. Pull the plugs
one by one while running, and confirm all four plugs are firing. It
could also be a vacuum leak on the hot side. An erratic idle would
lead me to believe that perhaps either they are out of sync, or one
side just isn't doing it's job. good luck.
