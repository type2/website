Justin:
Yes, you'll probably need a new disc, maybe a new pressure plate, of
course a new throwout bearing, but one thing more: The release forks
tend to wear flat on the area where the bearing rides and that tends to
make the clutch shudder during release. Seems like I read that in the
Vanagon list and it's often hard to detect. A new fork would fix it,
maybe some grinding work might do it too.
