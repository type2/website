Quick question ... I want to get expert opinions.

I have a recently rebuilt engine that suddenly has backfire problems.  It
has about 15K since the rebuild (on the rough roads of the Alcan
Highway), and hasn't given problems until recently.

Any thoughts would be appreciated.  I have to take her to Maine soon (I'm
in Illinois), and while I am fairly certain she would make it with no
