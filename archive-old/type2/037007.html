In the Bronx the procedure is as follows:
1. Go to the hardware store and buy a padlock and two piece locking clasp.
2. get out screw gun and screw clasp into the bus next to engine door.
3. screw other part of clasp on the door across from first part.
4. shut door and put padlock on.

That's it.
