Subject: Re: [T2] accelerator cable

Not knowing what cable you started out with to work with the Weber can
make this a toughie for replacement.  But here's a guide to actual cable
lengths for the Type 4 Engine on a Bus:

1972 Only Dual Carb - 3678mm (144.82") (211 721 555 J)
73-74 Dual Carb - 3655mm (143.91") (211 721 555 T)
75-79 EFI - 3458mm (136.16") (211 721 555 AA)

Hope this helps :-)
