silicone is excellent lubricant for a glass installation.
however, the residue will wreak havoc with future painting,
it will cause "fish-eyes" and adhesion problems.
you might consider a spray bottle with soapy water .
it will be a little less slippery than the silicone, but will
still do the job. 
you might also try a thinner cord, like 1/8 " for an easier pull.
and glass should not be installed in cold weather ( below 40 F ).
( especially the windshield ).
one more trick is to heat the seal for two minutes in the microwave.
