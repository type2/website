        Don't panic yet... (maybe later :-).

 Depending on the nature of your rattle this could just be a timing problem.
 Your lack of low-end pull and rattling at WOT sound to me like a symptom of overadvanced timing. Check your points (dwell & timing) before
getting too depressed.
 I wouldn't run 10W30, though. You don't need a thin oil in an aircooled VW and a thicker base
is less likely to break down. I use a straight 30 weight oil but a good quality 20W50 is also
recommended by some.
 The main message seems to be to avoid "cheap" oil and to change it often: most folk who've voiced an opinion seem to go for
1500-3000 miles between changes, some claim less!
