Well a got a few replies like this so I'm replying to the list and answering
them all.  Basically the apron was cut out, a flat piece of steel was wleded
to eithier side of the engine bay where the arpon was mounted and this was
drilles and tapped with a thread, four on eithier side.  The apron was then
cut a little shorter and plates were welded to this and then drilled, my
apron does not sit flush with the body though, I had to move it outwards
about 2 inches to fit the engine.  From above it looks like this.
