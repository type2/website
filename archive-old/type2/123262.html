On Mon, 12 Jan 2004, Rick Taylor wrote:

> I've been building VW engines since I put myself through college in the
> early 70's and the best thing you can do is buy yourself a nice set of
> scales.  Today's digital scales are really nice and not too expensive.  I
> balance the rods first, mark the weight on each, then weigh the pistons with
> the pins and mark those too.  Matching the heaviest piston with the lightest
> rod and so on I can get the sets matched very closely.  There may be a
> little grinding on a rod or two, but you can really come close this way.  I
> also put the two heaviest sets on either number 1 and 3 or 2 and 4 that way
> they offset each other.
