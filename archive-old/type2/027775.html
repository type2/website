Dear Joe.

The modification to the ventilation booster fans is as follows:-

(1)  Obtain one or two (dependent upon how many dashboard, blue ventilation 
control levers there are) white nylon spacers, with riveted brass 6.3mm 
spade terminal. These are normally found (one per vehicle) on the pivot stud 
of the dashboard, red heater control levers, of 1972-79 VW 17/18/2000 Type 
2s.

(2)  Position the white nylon spacer(s) in place of the standard black nylon 
spacer(s), on the pivot stud, of the blue ventilation control lever(s), so 
that the metal lever makes contact with the brass rivet, when the blue 
ventilation levers are pulled dawnward.

(3)  Instead of connecting the brown earth (i.e. ground) wires from the fan 
motors to the vehicle bodywork, connect them instead, to the riveted brass 
6.3mm spade terminal(s), of the white nylon spacer(s).

The metal housing and plastic blades, of your particular ventilation fans, 
do not affect the mofification procedure!
