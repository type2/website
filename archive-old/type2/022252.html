Check the ground to the wiper motor and the switch.  Been there, done 
that.  Got so irritated after my 3rd switch (kept telling the FLAPS guy 
that the switches were the problem) I had to take a breather.  After a 
while I went and had a look again just cleaning and checking everything 
again.  Ended up being a couple of wires were switched around and the 
ground on the switch plug was not the same as the chassis ground.  When I 
removed my switch from the dashboard (thereby the chassis ground) 
everything worked correctly.

I have a diagram somewhere, but I don't think I could find it.  I would be 
happy to try and help by remembering the schematic if you need though.
