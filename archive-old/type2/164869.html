Subject: RE: [T2] Grounding

Nathaniel,

The '68 doesn't have a wire-grounding system. It is a chassis ground system.
That means that the grounding wires actually ground to the small slip-on
post on the inside of the dash below the cluster cut-out.

The dash being metal, grounds through the 6 bolts on the sides and the 8 on
top of the windowsill. It is confusing. If you don't have the slip-on post,
just ground the wires directly anywhere on the dash or the firewall.

You will see that all of your turn signals, reverse lights and headlight
buckets also are missing ground wires. I think it is a '68-'69 thing only.

Jon Stiles
