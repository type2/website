I have read in countless articles that early Baywindow Busses were 
chronically underpowered.  Perhaps you've seen the same sort of drivel.

Goodwill truck off the road.  Passed him like he was standing still.  And

appears to be nothing more than a 009 with a vacuum advance.  Geez, good 
things do come in small packages!
