Hi,

VW made bay window busses with a factory fitted high-top, BUT they were only
fitted to panel vans. So if your looking for a Combi or Camper this will
have been a conversion! O.T.O.H there were LOADS of after market high-tops
available, but these will have been deleted soon after the bay was replaced
by the Vangoon. If you do find a high-top that fits (or can be adapted - try
mazda & toyota roof lines) it will have to be bonded onto the body to keep
the strength or the van will fold around the first bend you take!!!
