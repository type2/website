> filling with oil is to also remove any possible moisture condensate
> (air) also and prevent corrosion. MB 78 Westy

Galvanic reaction requires an electrolyte which oil isn't.

If you're going to go this route, I suggest loosening the rockers to
relieve the valve springs, fill the crankcase with oil to the top, remove
spark plugs, fill cylinders, and put the whole works on a shallow pan
because it's going to leak somewhere.  Use only new oil, as old oil will
have acids that are bad for the engine.
