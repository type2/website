Sue, you don't want to put a temp sensor at the pressuer sensor pick up. 
There is no oil flow to that area, and your temp gauge will be worthless. 
Best and most common location for the temp sensor is in the sump plate, for a 
type4 eng, and the dipstick for an upright.
