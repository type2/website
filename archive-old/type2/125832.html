Jamey --- It helps to keep a bit of tension on the cable so that the pedal end 
doesn't fall off, while you are bringing up the adjustment at the transmission 
end.  Attach a couple of rubber bands, maybe an easy-to-stretch spring to keep 
tension on the trans end, then place the eyelet on the pedal end; then go back 
to the trans end and bring the adjusting net to its correct position.

Of course, this is after you have fixed the broken fork problem with a new part 
and/or the help of a welding buddy.   Glenn
