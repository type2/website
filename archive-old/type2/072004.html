I put an interesting semi-option to AC in my '79.  It's called a "Fantastic
Fan" and is a 16" roof-top exhaust fan, with an acrylic louvred top (like a
regular vent) -- what I do is, try to park in the shade, then only open a
window on the side that is away from the sun.  I put the fan on "exhaust"
(it's 2-way), and it will actually keep the inside pretty nice, and almost
AC-cool if the whole van is in shade.

While I realize this is not feasible for driving around, esp. in Arizona, I
thought any other westy owners, particularly pet-owners, might be
interested.  With the unit wired to my camper battery, I was able to leave
it on for long periods of time, and it stayed cool enough to keep my dog
happy while we were in "No Dogs Allowed" places.

Just my cool $.02 worth.....
