The seat may be "sat out" or worn oddly.  It may be whoever owned the
Bus in the past had a real habit of sitting on the edge of the seat (scary
driver?!?) and wore out the springs in the seat.  Check the underside
of the seat to check for broken springs (another possibility).  Depending
on what you find, it may be easier to replace the seat.  Wolfsburg West
sells really nice quality horsehair pads, just like the original seat pads
in your VW, I have them in my Beetle and my Kombi and they make all
the difference in the world for comfort!  If you have broken springs, they
may be able to be wire tied back together, but it might be just as well
to search for a seat in a parts bus or at the swap meets this summer
and rebuild a seat with good springs.
