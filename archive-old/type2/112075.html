The Hagus mirrors are good, but after a few years the translucent bead
around the mirror tends to get brittle and let the lens go.  I put the
mirrors in the backings with industrial double stick foam tape and then
filled in the gap around the lens with hot-melt glue, leaving a drain gap
at the bottom of the lens.
