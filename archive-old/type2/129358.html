Phil, I am with your wife (hmm, maybe that doesn't sound right...) on 
this one.

I am the happy owner of an '83 944, which cost me $6K CDN 2 years ago. 
It is the most fun, reliable, robust, good looking, and fastest car I 
have ever owned.  The parts are more expensive than a VW certainly, but

don't worry about that (www.pelicanparts_dot_com is a good example).  For a 
glance at the most well-known online community go to www.rennlist_dot_com.
