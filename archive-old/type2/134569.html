Let's be honest here, cv boots are a pain in the ars. A lot of people can't 
or won't do it. It is really really messy and tricky. Special tools are 
required, the twelve point socket head, is really hard to find.

You can run into all kinds of problems with this job that could leave you 
hanging.
Strip out a bolt and you have to cut it off. (need new bolt and hack saw)

You can have a hard time pulling off the snap-ring.

You can twist the bearing too far and all the balls fall out, then what do 
you do?

How are you going to clean all the old grease out?
