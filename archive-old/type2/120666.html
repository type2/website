The injectors and their associated gaskets and hoses are not considered to
be part of the engine" but rather part of the "fuel" system and thus the
gaskets are not in the engine set.  Those gaskets are available through
most on-line vendors however.  For example see:

both the "Distributor Pipe Sleeve" and the "Fuel Injector Seal Kit" are
