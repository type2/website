The plastic inserts are designed to snap on and off over the body of the 
bolt.  If you end up breaking them, they can be purchased at the dealer's 
and/or list vendors and/or well-equipped FLAPS.  As to getting the 
various bearing out, they are tight fits so you need to work at it a bit 
to get them out, but they'll come.  Don't use any prying tools to remove 
them since you run the risk of damaging things.
