Subject: BusFusion 2004 T-shirt design contest

    BusFusion announces the BusFusion 2004 T-shirt design contest.  We are
inviting the VW community at large to submit entries for the BusFusion 2004
T-shirt.  Previous BusFusion T-shirt designs can be seen on the BusFusion
website.  The following are the criteria to this contest.   Voir la version
frangaise deus SVP.

BusFusion 2004 T-shirt Design Contest
1.    Contest open to all who may be interested.
2.    T-shirt design must depict the ideals of BusFusion.
3.    T-shirt design should include the location of the event, Almonte,
Ontario, and the dates, June 10 ~ 13.  If they are not, these items will be
added to the T-shirt if your design is chosen.
4.    T-shirt design will be on the front of white T-shirts.
5.    T-shirt design must have four colours maximum, black is considered one
colour.  Shades of a colour are permissible to increase colour variation.
6.    You may enter as many submissions as you want.
7.    The winning entry becomes the property of BusFusion for their exclusive
use.
8.    Judging will be by the BusFusion organising committee.
9.    Submissions deadline is March 31, 2004
10    Winning artist will receive a prize to be announced later.
