 I'd start with the basics- given that it was running, and not making any 
horrible noises, it's probably not catastrophic. First place to look would
be the electrical system (rule one of car breakdowns: "It's always
electrical").

 So: do you have good spark? Timing right? Pull the plugs and check the 
electrodes for carbonisation- that might tie the problem to a particular
cylinder. Check compression while you're there.
 Try changing the coil.

 I'm guessing that this is a 1600cc upright, since a pancake wouldn't
have needed a rebuild for unleaded (or a fanbelt, as such).
 Is your choke OK? Fuel filter blocked?

 Where are you, BTW?
