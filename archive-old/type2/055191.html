76 to 79 Westies came in four exterior colors.

Mango orange = orange plaid [not in deluxe style]
Sage green = green plaid [non-deluxe + deluxe]
Dakota or Mexico biege = brown plaid
White = blue plaid

I listed them in order of ease of finding plaid material.... good
luck
finding the blue:)
