you have an early vacuum shift auto tranny and almost everything internal
is NLA, however it is exchangable with the later 010 auto tranny which is
still rebuildable

very accurately adjusted since the detents are in the linkage in the front
of the bus (this was one of the major flaws of that design, later trannies
010 have the detents in the tranny.
