On 12/24/03 11:02 AM, "Terry K" <CTONLINE_at_webtv_dot_net> wrote:
 
> the profile between the solid lifter set up and the hydraulic are the same--

No.

Hydraulic cams are not the same profile as solid lift cams, the ramp - the
portion of the cam the goes between the lowest part and the highest - is
steeper on a solid lift cam. This is quite easy to see if you put a stock
solid cam beside a stock hydraulic cam.
