I mated a normal bench mount VW stand to a generic roll around stand.  I had
pretty much ran out of bench space in my shop and I like the idea of having a
mobile stand.  I see no reason why you could fab a mount for your VW engine.
But hear are some other ideas I incorporated in to my stand.  Instead of
rotating the engine with a short handle (a draw back on most el cheapo stands)
I replaced this short coming with an old chrome 3-spoke steering wheel.  Not
only does it make the engine a breeze to rotate, but it makes moving the stand
a lot more fun.  The other addition I added was a pipe with a gland nut welded
to it for building cranks.  It's so nice!  One more thing to think about.  Be
sure you have a way to get your clutch pilot tool in.  I have a hole in the
face of my stand and just slide the tool forward when its time for the clutch.
Another advantage is I always know where my clutch poilt tool is!!
