At 05:26 PM 9/3/2002, you wrote:
>Theoretically if the engine was running lean and creating excess heat,
>would the cylinder head temp affect the oil temperature?

Theoretically, yes.  In practice though they seem fairly independent.  CHT 
(and exhaust temp) is a very good indicator of mixture/combustion problems 
while oil temp seems to be more load/rmp dependent.
