 I think the issue is with the induction system rather than the transmission, though that probably confuses things even further.
 The US models after '73 were fitted with Fuel Injection. Despite what you read on the list this appears to have been done only for the US (or North American? No, maybe just US & Canada) market(s). Everywhere else got carburettors (corrections welcomed, if required).

 I also have a CJ series engine- with the stock Solex PDSIT carburetion and distributor it should be timed at 7.5BTDC. Vacuum hoses off. This assumes >=91RON petrol at sea level.

 Mine has a lable to that effect on the fanhousing and a dot at the appropriate point on the timing scale.

 That's the stock setting.
