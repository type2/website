This is just a vacuum gauge with pressure side also. I
don't know what yer bus is, but if FI, pressure gauge
needs to be waaaayyyy up there and built to handle
gasoline. FI rail pressure up to about 40psi but can
go over 100 if you do something stupid!

Vacuum gauge is nice to have for general diagnoses. If
the needle flicks at approximately one half of engine
RPM you may have a bad valve at one cylinder or if it
more than flicks, you may have a broken ring. I use it
to tell me if I should do a compression check.
