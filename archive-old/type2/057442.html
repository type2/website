I did a test with this.  My old '78 Bus had an oil temperature gauge 
installed.  When I got the Bus it had a very poorly installed a/c 
compressor.  Whoever did it hacked the left rear tin pretty severely, 
and there was a big open spot there.  And that Bus ran hot.  After I 
rigged up something to block the gap, preventing hot air entering from 
below, I noticed a serious drop in oil temp.  That's all the proof I 
need.  Similar thing with the '75 Beetle I once had.  Idiot PO didn't 
have an engine seal in place.  This was before I knew enough about VWs 
to know that a sealed engine compartment was important.  That thing ran 
REALLY hot.  I know I killed that poor engine driving it around without 
that seal.  You say yours never got hot.  I disagree.  You just didn't 
notice how hot it got.
