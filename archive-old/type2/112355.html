I had a similar problem on my Beetle.  Turned out that one piston in 
one front wheel cylinder was frozen (corroded) in place.  You might 
want to take off the your left front drum, and then have a helper 
SLOWLY depress the brake pedal.  See if both wheel cylinders are active.
