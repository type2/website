 NO!!!!

 The number on the tyre is the MAXIMUM pressure the tyre can take, and
the load is tha MAXIMUM that it can handle (at this pressure, since
a high pressure is needed for a heavy load).

 You should use the VW specifications in your owners manual (or
something pretty close). From memory that's 30psi in front and
42 psi in rear for a full load (3/4 tonne). If unladen they
recommend dropping the REAR pressure by about 4.5psi, which
gets you ~37. hence the text you quote is spot on.

 The sidewalls tell you what the tyre is capable of- the owners
manual tells you how it should be used.
