Well I hate to admit it, but I also painted mine with rustoleum. I
sandblasted em and then primered em and then I just sprayed them with
rustoleum. They looked good for a while but I have black brake powder from
my front brakes and it adheres nicely to my rustoleum. I have scrubbed them
and can't get all of it off. I suggest a lot of coats and maybe a clear coat
too. They did look really good though.
