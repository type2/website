I agree with Ron here, but would like to make one additional provision
[how can you tell I have been answering RFQs all week ;)].  When you
change the asbestos gasket/seal you should change the filter in the
"black plastic accordion tube" as it is felt and will be contaminated
with asbestus fibres.  The catch [Catch-22...there's only one] is
that, last I checked the filter could not be obtained separately from
the enveloping tube...unless you managed to aquire filters from a late
model 'loaf...or decide to run without them.
