Nope, the GVWR listed on the door sticker is not the weight of the
vehicle -- it is the maximum ALLOWABLE weight including passengers, gear,
etc.  So if the sticker says GVWR 4900 lbs then it means "4900 lbs is the
maximum load for this vehicle, including the weight of the vehicle itself."

You take the GVWR minus the true weight to determine how much more gear you
can load up.  Of course, who knows the true weight of their Bus? :-)

The actual weight may be 3500-4000 lbs or so depending on the model and how
much crap you regularly carry around :-)
