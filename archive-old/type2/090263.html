>   The pod is made as follows: the base is made from 
1.5x .75" "  wood
>stock that has plywood paneling stapled to the bottom. It

>rectangle that fits into the luggage rack bottom.  You can

the box base

>fastened to the other side of the base.  Lay this agains so 
paneling and
>trace the INSIDE shape of the bend with pencil. (If you are 
too big,
>send a kid in to do this).  Cut out 2 doors this way.  
Fasten them with
>piano hinges to the bottom frame.
>At the top, for "stopping" the doors, I traced part of the 
curve onto
>some 2x4 stock and cut it out.  Glue it in place; add 
screws from the
>outside to keep it there.   Get cabinet locks and use these 
to lock the
>end doors.  Paint the masonit throuroughly; remember it is 
made from
>"paper" but as a curved arch, you can sit or stand on the

driver side trip
