 CONCLUSIONS
 -----------
 Because most Michelins where 185R14 reinforced and most Yokohamas where
195/75R14 load range
 D, tire manufacturer might be influencing the results more than differences in
tire size or
 load range.
 
 Michelin (185R14 reinforced) and Yokohama (195/75R14 load range D) seem to
work very
 well on VW buses.  Yokohama might have a slight edge over Michelin, possibly
because of the
 larger tire.  Both recommended.
 
 Do not recommend private brands.
