Bob Hoover was the most visible of a number of people on this list who 
dispense advise, wisdom, and humor. It would be nice for us if he were

Folks like David Schwartz, George Lyle, and Mike Benthin were steeped

Tim, Scott Lyon, and others with a wealth  of technical knowledge. And 
everything that Bob Hoover could say in words, Richard Atwell has four 
pictures on his website to illustrate.

And if you want someone with lots of character, well, there's Don.

It would nice to have Bob Hoover back, but frankly I think he would be 
redundant.
