I am a relatively new bus owner, 1 year, but I have to chime in on this one.
The 71 has a powerbrake assist, called a servo (ask me how I know)  If you
felt unsafe, and the braking was hard, the servo is blown.  Period.  You can
get these remanufactured,(new over $400 if I remember correctly) and the
best source I found was out of Texas that actually rebuilds your core.
