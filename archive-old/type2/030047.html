Adam,

To get the upper torsion arm off, you *must* remove the bump stop!  Then
slather grease on the horn, then pound like an SOB.  they are a bear to
remove, I am sorry to say.

To remove the bump stop, look inside the horn it sits on, ther eis a clip
like the clips that hold the brake lines in place.  Spray it with Kroil,
then bang it a bit with a screwdriver and hammer, then grip it with a
needlenose vice grip and pull it out.  then lift[1] the arm off it and it
will pry out.
