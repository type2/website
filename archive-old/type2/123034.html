Subject: How do you check a hall sender unit? - was Cant get a spark?

Best and easiest way to check a hall sender, pertronix, kettering points or
whatever is to use a simple test light on the negative side of the coil
while someone cranks the starter. If you get blinks you got working points,
hall sender etc... Don't have a test light? Use a volt meter or make a test
light with the tail light bulb wiring from your mom's car....
