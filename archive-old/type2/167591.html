unless you have a known donor engine I would be leary of doing all that
work and getting nowhere, also you have an auto tranny,  the odds of
finding an auto tranny engine is slim and none. so you have the problems
of getting the engine prepared for use on an auto bus. setting endplay in
the field is not something I would relish, and if you swap out the
flywheel for the flexplate you will have to set endplay.

on the trailer, or even just removing the half shafts and towing it on its
wheels.  go VERY VERY slow and stay  of the interstates and you might get
home with no problems.  borrowing a hitch for the bus might be possible.

just some random thoughts, getting stuck on the side of the road is bad
