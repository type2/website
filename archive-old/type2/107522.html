The oil pressure wire should come from the harness that goes through the
bottom right side of the engine comp. firewall.

There is another wire from the same harness that goes to the coil +

There should be a single wire near the left taillight harness for the fuel
pump, which also goes on the coil +

There is a single wire from the FI harness (from the ECU) which also goes to
the coil -

The distributor wire goes to coil -
