Not to lead you astray, but my buddy Dalton has a regular house a/c
installed in his Riviera. It just fit in the middle window. He just plugs it
in when he gets to the camp site. He showed it to us a couple of weekends
ago and when he opened the door whoo hoo!!  it was cold in there. Plus you
only have to pay the 10 bucks a night for camping and you can run your a/c
all weekend!! I noticed Wal dffdsaffsad had them for 125 spanking new.
My dad gave me one that fits inside my front door window. It is small enough
to be portable. I put in the window and strapped  it down. It cools the
whole bus down to about 60 degrees in an hour. When I am done using it I can
just put it in the storage building until next trip. Of course if you want
a/c all the time this won't work without your generator, which looks
portable too!!
