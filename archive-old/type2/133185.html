Tight exhaust valve...leading to hotter burning cylinder.

Timing/dwell out of spec very slightly...otherwise all plugs would have 
showed a little hotter burning.

Blocked cooling (check fan for leaves or rag)....just enought to make the 
one "problem" cylinder run a little hotter than usual.
Slight intake leak for that cylinder intake.

Do you adjust valves before the compression test or after?

Probably not a really big deal unless it continues...if just one cylinder, 
then probably just a tight valve adjustment.
