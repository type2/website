Courtney,

That someone would have been me. They are not necessarily oversized, but
some are. Rule of thumb: if it is an OEM piece it is most likely not
oversized. If it is a cheap Dutch or Danish repro it most likely is. It does
not matter for rocker panels since you have quite a bit of room to maneuver
anyway. It does matter, however, for panels that go in between others - e.g.
doglegs.
