I forgot to mention I added water and baking soda before I started scrubbing.

Nothing cleans rusted metal at this price like muratic acid. I've used it on battery trays, gas tanks, seat frames. I've even used it to clean electrical contacts. Once the surface is truly clean, you can use a phosphoric acid solution to etch the metal before priming/painting.

It's no fun to use and dispose but I works so well I'll continue to rely on it until something better comes along.
