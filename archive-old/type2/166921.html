YES!!!! I just did this on my 71 and could not figure
it out either at first!! But, thanks to Bentley, I
discovered that you actually have to remove the vent
wing window frame in order to have the room to slide
out the window regulator.  The vent window frame
attaches via a few screws located under the rubber
seal onthe top of the window and a couple of screws
inside the door. Once you disconnect this thing and
slide it out of the way, you will have JUST enough
room to slide out the regulator.
