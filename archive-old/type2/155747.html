Swapped points. Old points were odd, as I mentioned before. No pip on the
pads, but the pads weren't aligned when I pulled the distributor. But no
difference.

New condensor and coil at the same time (limited daylight). Idle
smooooooothed out. So it was either the coil or condensor. When I get home
and have time I'll swap them one at a time and see if I can reproduce the
issue.

The coil was the old Bosch original black one. I got a new blue from
vwparts_dot_net (Vee Dub in Huntington Beach) along with the condensor.

coils either work or don't, not fade as my Autozone one did. This time all I
asked was "What coils do you carry?" and he said "Bosch". Even though there
were MSDs up on the wall, he didn't even mention them. Good. So $40 for the
coil and condensor and I'm good. The coil is a Brazilian blue coil made in
2000 according to the label on the box.
