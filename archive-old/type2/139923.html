I applaud your sensibilities in making it look stock. The Type 3 Karmann
Ghias came with fog lights and use a relay with a 211 part number that
ties into the headlight wiring under the hood. When I was researching this
hard to find relay (211 953 207 for the 12v version) I discovered that
Busses in Europe could get fog lights as an M code. In the T34 the fog
lights are wired so that they will work with no headlights or low beams
but not with high beams. I think it was probably done that way so as not
to overwork the circuit. The fog light switch for a T34 is a simple rocker
switch mounted under the dash near the column where it isn't seen but is
easily accessable. One time I wanted my fog lights to work but didn't have
a working relay so I plugged in a regular headlight switch in its place.
It worked but of course I had to go under the hood to turn them on and
off.
