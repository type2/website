If I get a new nose, I'll go with Bus Depot for $139.00 (plus shipping,
remember). I haven't yet decided if I'm going to get a new nose or find
a donor bus. The advantage to the donor bus isn't just the monetary
savings (which aren't that significant), but you get replacement
ductwork and stuff that might also have been damaged by the impact.
However, depending on where you are located, finding a rust-free used
nose may be difficult.

Either way, the cost estimate I keep hearing is $1000-$1500 for the job.
