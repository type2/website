Subject: what are belly pans

For the record

The removable pan that covers the pedal cluster, master cylinder, 
shifter etc is the usually referred to as the pedal pan or pedal 
cover.

Belly pans are flat metal sheets with an X stamping in them. They are 
welded to the main frame rails, outriggers out the the rockers. The 
provide frame stiffening for buses with sun roof or slider doors on 
both sides of the bus.

For example, beyond the what used to be an outer rocker panel you can 
see one of the pans.  The roughly cover the area below the cargo 
floor.
