With our older aircoolers, it can sometimes be hard to get the right stuff
from many local parts places. Usually you have to be the expert in order to
ensure you get the right stuff. I had my bus for about 8 months before I
found the T2 list...what a nightmare! Many of the folks here have come to
rely on a few vendors with web-based service who really specialize in air
cooled and/or buses in paerticular.

These guys do rebuilt distributors, and they seemed to be able to identify
the one I needed without any trouble:
http://philbingroup_dot_com/

These are some of my favorite general purpose bus vendors:
http://www.busdepot_dot_com/
http://www.busboys.cc/
http://www.bustedvw_dot_com/

You can always try the dealer, sometimes you'll be pleasently amazed, but
it's always expensive, and sometimes they just don't want to do the research
for air cooled stuff.
