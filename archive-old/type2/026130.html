Isaiah,

Sounds like the pin in the ball at the base of the stick may have come out /
sheared off.  To check, undo the 2 bolts at the floor and pull the whole
thing out.  Poking out of the front of the ball should be a pin about 1/8"
diameter (you may have to scrape some grease off to see).  If this pin isn't
there, the whole stick will be able to swivel round.  If it's not fixable,
you should be able to get a stick from a junkyard.
