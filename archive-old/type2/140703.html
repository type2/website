I am assuming you have a 76-79 Westy???????

The pump screws into the tank. After you screw it in with teflon tape and tighten the hose clamp on the neck 
of the tank where it screws in, does it still leak?  Or are you missing the clamp?

Yes, the tank comes out, but if it leaks with teflon tape and a tight clamp;
you need another tank.

To remove: Pull out the filler tube [carefully!].
Remove the large rubber baby bumpers.
You already have the false panel removed since you are playing with the pump.
Loosen the inner clamp and unscrew the drain hose.
Walk the tank out.
