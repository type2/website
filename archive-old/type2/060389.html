Not exactly.  "Water" means water.  "Boxer" means a horizontally opposed,
or flat (180 degree V) engine.  I guess because the pistons appear to be
boxing with each other, in a backwards sort of way.  The only common
waterboxer engines (that I know of anyway) are the 83.5-91 Vanagon and the
Subaru engines.  All of the other water cooled engines are just plain
"Water".
