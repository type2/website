I've used grease around the valve cover lip to hold the gasket in place
while angling it into place on the head.  Rock the cover side to side a
bit and u/down to feel that it is flat to the head.  I have a wooded
shim about 1/2" thick that I insert above the cover to keep it from
riding up as the bail is lifted via a brake adjusting tool or a pry bar
fulcrumed against the heater box.
