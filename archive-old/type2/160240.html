Subject: Re: [T2] 77 bus shift rod bushings

hey paul honestly the 2 bushings inside the tubing there might not need to be replaced....
  those are the two you have to take the tranny and engine out for.
  the front bushing is under the gear shifter but you will need to crawl under the bus and take front pan off and look up at your bottom of the shifter and see where the rod is and there should be a donut looking bushing there at some point in the rod.
  if there isnt this is ok to just replace it either way ....
   
  also...
  check your shift plate up top that has two 13mm nuts ... just pull your boot up and give it a look dont loosen it if anything just make sure it is still tight and hasnt came loose...
   
  and finally... check your shift rod coupler that is located at the nose cone on the tranny..
  if the shift rod coupler is at the least bent or so then you could be having a easy fix...
   
  i would replace and check all that before thinking about a engine or tranny removal...
   
  good luck to you..
