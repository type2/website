Since the above tires are based on a European standard, the following tires
will be much easier to find in North America:

Even though the above are standards, you will find tire companies producing
tires outside these standards.

   SL = standard load
   XL = extra load
   RF = reinforced construction
ETRTO = European Tire & Rim Technical Organization (European equivalent to
T&RA)
 T&RA = Tire & Rim Association (North American equivalent to ETRTO)
