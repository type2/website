The sound you describe may be caused by a loose tin screw being tossed =
around by the squirrel cage fan.

You might be able to patch the hole in the muffler with a little gas =
welding.

'78 is a very desirable year. Type IV engines are considerably more =
expensive to rebuild, but a properly-maintained 2.0 liter should provide =
a lot more power and last a lot longer than a Type I (Beetle) engine. =
Plus, from '78-on, hydraulic lifters were standard - no valve =
adjustments. Add an electronic points/condenser replacement unit, and a =
tuneup consists of merely changing sparkplugs, oil and filters.
