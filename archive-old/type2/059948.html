This brought back the old memory of how I did this.  

I had girlfriend sit in car with clutch fully depressed, foot on
brake, transmission in fourth gear.

I put big wrench on the pulley nut and wiggled the engine back and
forth.  The pressure plate spring pushed the engine off of the output
shaft.  No pulling on the engine was required, as the pressure plate
spring (and my girlfriend's foot) did all the work.
