Subject: Seen FS - '67 Westy

I called about a locally advertised bus this weekend. Turns out it's about
a mile from where I live (Cheyenne, WY).

Upon inspection, I found a quite delapidated (sp?) but very solid, mostly
complete '67 (non poptop) camper van. I assume it is a Westy although I saw
no ID plates. Body has _very_ little rust and is really straight except for
a shot it took in the left rear corner while parked on the street. Paint is
bad - currently dark green, originally white I believe. Has a 1600 dual
port installed. Supposedly rebuilt along with gearbox and brakes "a couple
of years ago". Interior is kind of rough, but appears complete and
restorable.

If any of you wannabussers out there need more info, let me know and I'll
see what I can find out. Was it not for approx. 162 current projects
waiting for attention, I'd prolly jump on this one.

Oh, almost forgot, when asked about price, the seller said $1000.
Justifiying that, at least to himself, by saying he had bought it for $300
and had put $1300 into repairs. My quess would be more like $500-$600 would
be a fair price. I could be wrong on this (in either direction), but I'm
sure you'll let me know if I am. ;-)
