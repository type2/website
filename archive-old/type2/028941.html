I just did this exact same thing in my '72.  The window was stuck in the
almost up position (about 3 inches from the top.)  The handle just spun
when turned, window wouldn't go up or down.
The Bentley is right on about how to remove the rather large mechanism
(regulator), but it can still be a pain in the A, especially when the
window won't go down.
So the gears of the mechanism are stripped and must be replaced.  It is
all one piece and must be replace as such.  I removed all the bolts
(seven in all) and slid the whole unit down until I could remove the
bolts holding the window to the lifter (the left "arm" of the mechanism
had to be pulled out of the door to do this.)  And then removed the
mechanism per Bentley.
note - after doing this, I realized that the window was only resting on
the 2 bolts that hold it to the lifter.  You may be able to simply lift
the window off those bolts to lower it.
Hope this helps.
