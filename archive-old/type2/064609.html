At 6:49 AM 9/27/96, Adam Sidky wrote:
>Just a quick question but is there anything else holding my exhaust
>manifolds or my mufflers or that whole exhause system to the engine other
>than 22 years worth of rust and decay?  I've taken off all four nuts from
>each side but no amount of prying and banging (a la Muir) seems to move
>anything.

Maybe.  At the back of the heat exchangers there are two little covers held
in by screws where the heat exchanger meets the fan housing.  Remove them
from underneath.  I'd recommend removing the muffler first, and then the
heat exchangers one at a time.  Laying underneath the bus, grab one with
both hands and try to rock it gently from side to side while pulling down.
Shut your eyes while you do this or you will get dirt in them.
