I'm not familiar with the 300, but it sounds like it looks at a single
channel at a time.  Since most people care more about their position than
they do about their altitude, they probably update the altitude less often
than the position.  Before SA was turned off, they actually slowed the
display update down so that the "wandering" wouldn't show up so much.

My Garmin GPSIII+ updates altitude every time it updates the position
(about once per second).  Works pretty well, but I wouldn't depend upon it
in the clouds in an airplane!
