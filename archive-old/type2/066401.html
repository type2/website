The Vast Majority of Bus owners learn quickly.  The owners shortly find
that the VW engine is very "forgiving" - up to a point.  Changing oil,
since it's only 2.7 quarts, and adjusting valves, are the biggies.

Almost all "hippies" (including myself) know more about their busses than
ANYONE else, including mechanics.  The symbiotic relationship with one's
VW that seems to naturally arise, is a direct correlation to how someone
cares about his life, friends, etc.  In short, good people that take care
of their VW's, are usually people that take good care of their portion 
of reality and friends.

The above rule does NOT apply to those that try to have major power trips
about racing - power cores - BEATING someone (why bother, we've already gone
to the moon at 25,000 mph, why worry about 176 mph?)  - and the REAL deal,
more and more, is to live our lives in some harmony with our environment.
In fact, it could be looked at like:  If you can't handle a REAL car, like
a VW bus, then you will be stuck dealing with big hogs that you'll always
worry (like penis size) won't quite "measure up" to your friends.

This "sickness" of over-consumption, seems to be still happening, even
though everyone that gets a bigger/badder car with a bigger "thump"
stereo system, eventually figures it out - "Hey, why I'm I still un-
statisfied and unhappy ?"  At least most figure it out, eventually.
