>-Since a change may have already been made , how can I tell them (alt.&
>gen.) apart by loooking at them?

I've never seen a generator in a type IV motor and I can't think of a good
reason why anyone would want to put one in anyway.  The alternator will
have Bosch written on it somewhere... hmmm... and an alternator will have a
wire for the field whereas the generator won't.

>-Where exactly does the Ground (-) from the battery connect to the frame or
>engine?

It connects to the body of the bus right in front (FIF) of the battery
tray.  I believe there is just a bolt that screws into the body.

>-What solvent can I spray onto all these greasy push-on electrical
>connectors so I will get a good connection?

I would try carburator cleaner or brake cleaner.  Be careful, the stuff's
flammable.
