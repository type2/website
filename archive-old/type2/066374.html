Subject: Pertronix in a standard disributor

Before changing to a 009 distributor I used to use my PERTRONIX in a standard
distributor....

If my memory is correct after removing the points from the standard distributor 
I had to remove a further "bananna" shaped plate held in by the position 
adjustment screw. Any way the "bananna" shaped plate to which the PERTRONIX
is bolted went in dead easy replacing this lower plate. (Keep all the bits in a
bag tucked in your tool box just in case....).

It may well be a good idea to hold off fitting it any way to ensure you don't 
invalidate any warranties.......
