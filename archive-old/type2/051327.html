There was a time in California when drivers of certain vehicles were
required to retrofit smog devices.  Most of these retrofits took the form
of some sort of crude EGR system.  Our beetle came with the remnents of
one such system.  A hole was drilled in the heat riser and a special hose
nipple clamped on.  A hose went to some sort of mystery diaphragm valve
and thence to another hole cut in the top of the air cleaner.  The only
control of gas flow seemed to come from the valve and there was no
connection to manifold vacuum as in a conventional egr system.

Fortuntately, the smog czars quickly found out that the stock engine was
usually cleaner than the retrofit junk and the requirement to retrofit
went away.
