flavours myself.

Sieversen near Hamburg converts Bays to the Vanagon/Rabbit Diesel 
engine.  He already did a dozen or so, semi-professionally.  The

optical change to the bus is the exhaust.  If any of you are 
interested, p-mail me for information (he's not online yet).
