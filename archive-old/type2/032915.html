Dear Shockless in CO,

Normally, there is no coil around the shock, so your old setup is not stock.
Some listmembers have had good luck with coil-over shocks, but others have
not as it ended up breaking the attachment points.

Shocks just act as dampeners, whereas coils lift the vehicle. Coil-overs are
often used to cure a sagging rear end, although the proper way would be to
adjust the torsion bars (or replace them if broken).
