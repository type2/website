> -The two hoses from the distributor advance canister.

I think in general the hose from the outer nipple on the vacuum can is the
advance side and goes into the throttle body and is tee'd at the EEC valve
on the air cleaner.

The inner one, the retard side, tees into right side of the air distribution
box between 3/4 cylinders where the hose goes to the pressure regulator.

> - The hose from the EGR valve.

http://www.bug-bits.freeserve.co_dot_uk/ramva/fiche/t201810.gif

This diagram is for the SVDA dizzy but you can see that there is a 3-way tee
(dist/air-cleaner/throttle). I understand on your model with the
non-mechanical EGR, the tee is 4 way and the other line goes to the EGR
valve.
