Any time you burn a hydrocarbon you get water, propane burns real clean only
gives off water and carbon dioxide, as long as it is pure and burns completely
here's the equation for the combustion of propane

c3h8 (propane) +5 (O2) (oxygen in air) = 4(H20) (water, aka moisture) + 3(CO2)
(carbon dioxide)  + HEAT(ahh toasty warm now, thank you chemistry!)
