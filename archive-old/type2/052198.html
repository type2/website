Press release:

America's Most Wanted to feature the story of Kristin Laurite on
February 24th, including interviews with Bob Weir of the Grateful Dead,

Mark your calendars...Saturday, February 24th, Fox TV's America's
Most Wanted will feature the story of Kristin Laurite, a 25 year old woman who
was murdered on August 25, 2000 at a rest stop along I-40 in Arkansas.
She was traveling across country with her two dogs in her 1971 VW Bus to 
start a
new life as a schoolteacher in California.  No witnesses, no suspects.
