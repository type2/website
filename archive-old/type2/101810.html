At 01:34 PM 1/20/03, Dave C. Bolen wrote:

>Do you have to add wire to the kit to get it to go all the way to
>the front of the bus?  If you do have to add wire, then wouldn't the
>junction where you added wire give you the same problem as a switch?


Yes and yes unless you use the correct thermocouple wire that the gauge is 
calibrated for.  If you delve around on the omega_dot_com site, you will find 
connectors and thermocouple extension wire.  I used to use the stuff all 
the time for furnaces that I maintained.  Worked in two different 
facilities, heat treat place and a pottery/tile factory.  The pottery place 
had huge kilns that were on tracks and insides were  big enough to put a 
bus in and then have room to spare.  In the heat treat facility, I had to 
replace thermocouples on a somewhat regular basis.  2000F+ is hard on a lot 
of stuff.
