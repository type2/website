I stopped by Lowes when I was in Columbus, Ohio last week.  I found this
stuff on the shelf.  It was US$60/roll.  I talked to the guy there and he
gave me the skinny:

OC (Owens Corning) WeatherLok
Item number 11615
$59.60
Any Lowes should be able to order it even if they don't stock it.
Was in stock at Lowes Hilliard #037.

The stuff is peel and stick, and somewhat flexible.  The non-sticking side
is impregnated with some kind of sand-like substance.  Probably great for
rooves, worthless for VW's BUT I still think this stuff will insulate well.
I plan on coating the floor with it, then one layer of 3/8" carpet jute.
