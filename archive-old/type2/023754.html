 The front studs are 10mm longer than the rears, so if you only need
a little more length then you can re-use them.

 The fronts are 45mm, the rears 35mm. Vanagons (as Karl points out)
had bolts at the front so they don't have long front studs.

 I ended up buying them from an aftermarket wheel supplier here in
the UK. The only source I know of in the US is the Porsche specialist
Pelican Parts (good website), they have a range of different lengths
and I think (ie I don't know for sure) that they are the same profile
as VW ones but the price adds up if you need to buy 10-20. Worth 
getting a couple to check first, perhaps?

 Good luck.
