The 75 sets at 5 after because it is supposed to have a dual vacuum unit dist with retard vacuum at idle. IF
you are running the same single vacuum unit on the 75 as on the 78, then you need to time it at 7.5 before and
verify that the vacuum hose going to it is ported vacuum [vacuum appears when you open the throttle]. Here is
the specs for the correct 75 dizzy:

Bus & Pickup 1975-Early 1976 * 1800 (75), 2000 (76) Manual Trans

Distributor: VW 021-905-205AB, 0231 176 048, 022-905-205AB, 0231 181 012
Vacuum Can: 07 125
Timing Set At:: 5deg ATDC _at_ 800-950 rpm w/strobe, vacuum hose(s) connected
Advance/Retard Range: Vacuum: 8-11deg Adv _at_ 7.9 In. Hg, 11-13deg Ret _at_7.9 In. Hg; Centrifigual: 11-17deg _at_
2000 rpm, 21-25deg _at_ 3600 rpm
