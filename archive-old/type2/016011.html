	When i bought my electric cooler box it was supplied with a cut out
device. The device was supposed to be plugged in the cigarrette lighter
plug as an "extension cord" before the cooler box. It cuts the power to the
cooler box when the voltage goes under 11.5 volts. 
Later i connected the device to a relay (12 Volt 40A) and now it cuts out
the power to all my (electric) camping accessories when voltage is too low.
This is a cheap way to protect the second battery from draining. The extra
cost for the device was 100 Finnish Markka, about 17 Dollars.
