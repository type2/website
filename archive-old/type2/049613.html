> Reinforced?  Yes, its a 6 ply.  Passenger car tires are 3 or sometime ply.

This is the ply rating.  Most passenger car tires are rated 4-ply, but
only have one ply in the sidewalls and three under the tread.  Truck tires
are rated 6 or 8 ply, but have 3 or 4 in the sidewall, and 5 or 6 under
the tread.  The reason for the difference between the ratings and the
actual plys is that the ratings are based upon the strength of cotton ply
material.  Modern materials (nylon, rayon, kevlar, fiberglass, steel) are
a lot stronger so fewer plys are needed for the same strength.
