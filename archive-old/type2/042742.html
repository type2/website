Not too sure about lift height, but my first jack was a sears floor jack (2.25
tons)
which worked great and cost me about $90 10 years ago. Don't leave it in your
bus
no matter how late the job finishes, as mine was promptly stolen. I went to a
flaps
after that and bought a 2.5 ton jack called "big red" for like $79. I've had
that for
the last 7 years and it's seen mondo use with no problems. Okay well it's
getting a
bit tired now.

Works fine for most bus applications. On B*g engine pulls I use ramps in
conjunction
with a thick piece of wood between the jack cup and oil sump, and that makes up
for
the limitations on jack height. go for it.
