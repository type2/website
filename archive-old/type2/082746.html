My last bus, a 1970 Westy was not owned by a hippie.

local mechanic)  I took care of Valve adjustments, oil

doing so, but it is part of life.  The engine resides

boyfriend.  I gave it to him)  With a good engine this

of this bus and I am glad I bought it.  The buses are
polar oposites... currently there is only one sticker

covered.
