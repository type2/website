On 10/19/05 10:34 AM, "Edward Woenker" <ewoenker_at_kconline_dot_com> wrote:

> I don't know if anyone has run across this before, but I cannot seem to locate
> the correct bolts to connect my tailpipe to my muffler on my '74 bus.  I went
> to a machine shop and they cleaned out the threads on the muffler and the
> correct tap to fit the threads turned out to be 11mm X 1.25 pitch.

There is something wrong here. The threaded holes in the muffler-tailpipe
flange should be m8 x 1.25. Somewhere along the way the holes have been
enlarged. The repair should not be too difficult - either weld some studs to
the muffler or retap the holes - by welding in some metal to drill and
retap, or use an threaded insert.
