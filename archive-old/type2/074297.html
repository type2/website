Subject: Re: [T2] SOLEX carbs  in a '72 camper

1972 bus: 1700 cc type IV engine, 34 PDSIT -3 and -4 carburetors.  They are
essentially 3 carbs, 1 for each side and one for idle.

I also have a 1972 stock 7 seater (blue/white delux) with this engine and
carb set.  I have rebuilt these with the kit that is available from several
vendors.

Fuel shut off electric valves for each side and for the idle circuit.

There are electric chokes on each side.

If anyone has an extra choke, I could use one, the connector broke off of
one of mine.
