How To Check 300 degree Oil Temp Sender

Disconnect wire from sender.

Check the resistance between the terminal and ground


322 Ohms =  120  Degrees
105 Ohms =  180  Degrees
64 Ohms =   210   Degrees
35 Ohms =  250    Degrees
18 Ohms =  300    Degrees
