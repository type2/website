Subject: Re: [T2] Won't stay started

Stock carbs? Make sure you have your brake booster lines sealed very tight
or blocked off at the intakes, make sure the balance tube is sealed tight at
all 3 points, use the dashpot to open the throttle some so you can get to
the back of the bus. ;)

Also, try using a remote starter. That way you can be at the engine while
cranking it over.
