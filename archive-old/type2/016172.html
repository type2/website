It's probably the cylinder head temperature sensor.  It is located on the
drivers side head (#3 cylinder) and has a white wire that runs to it from
the main fuel injection wiring harness.  Wire may be loose, but if the bus
runs fine until it warms up the sensor is probably bad and needs replaced.
Fairly common preventive maintenance part.  It screws in and out much like a
small spark plug with a wire attached to the top of it. About $15 at the
local import parts store or online retailer.  If this doesn't work the air
flow meter also has a temp sensor built into it.  Try the cylinder head temp
sensor and let the list know if that fixes it.  Good luck.
