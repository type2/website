how much presure, the ring needs 35psi to operate the injectors properly

> The plugs were next to pull and they were dry as a bone. We used a spray 

This says there is no fuel being injected,  this will happen if

. insufficient fuel presure  (35psi in the ring)
. clogged injector (but unlikely all 4 at the same time)
. no signal to injector
. clogged fuel filter

if you have low presure check to see if your fuel pump is running.  should
be able to hear it, it is behind the drivers rear wheel on the frame.


remove an injector (take the trigger wire off the coil so there is no
spark) put a container in front of it, run the starter, fuel should be
injected into the container, it should come out as a fine cone shaped
spray

if no spray check
. wires reversed on fuel pump
. presure regulator defective
. wire or hoses to fuel pump reversed
. is fuel pump defective
. check that double relay is operating
  to check double relay
   .  connect test light to terminal 88y and 85 you should get current
     no light means break in wire between 88y and battery
   .  if light then check between 86a & 85, operate starter, you should
     NOT get current.  if current, replace double relay
   . if no current check for breaks in the wire between terminal 50 on
starter and 86a on relay
