	Howdy volks!,

	Have a 2.0L motor, w/ 1.8L heads, solids lifters, and 100 cam built  
by AVP.  Ever since installed 2 yrs, 5000 miles ago, the motor has

little low for fresh motor w/ 5000 miles.  I am lost, and ready to  
throw in a SVDA dizzy.  But I don't think its the problem.  Have  
found no vacuum leaks either.  The bus runs well when the air is  
cold, but crappy w/ ambient temps above 60-65.  Today it was 80, and  
it ran like crap.  It passed the dipstick test after running on  
highway for 15 minutes at 60mph (had to keep pedal almost floored).
