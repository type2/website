Hello List,

This is not a happy day. I went outside to tinker with

running, and running strong. I started to notice that
the engine noise became louder. I am not that
experienced with engine noises but it sounded like a
lifter slapping back and forth from a valve that had
come out of adjustment. I did not cut the engine off
immediately and continued to fine tune it. Within a
matter of 5 seconds, the noise became louder and
turned into a loud clanking noise!!! I turned the
engine off immediately and did a visual inspection of
the motor to make sure that a screw driver or
something had not become caught in the fan belt. I
turned the engine over a few more times and the noise
was there, louder than before. I turned off everything
and came back inside.  I am hoping that the problem is
a rocker arm assembly has come loose or one of the
push rods has freed itself. I fear that the rebuilder
did not torque everything to speck and things have
gone ballistic inside the engine case. Tomorrow I plan
to take the valve covers off and see if something
simple has come loose. I will then do a valve
adjustment. I don't know. When I reinstalled the
rocker assemblies, I torqued everything according to
Bentley!
