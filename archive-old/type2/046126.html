It's all a matter of distance.  If you increase the distance through which
you move from the point of impact to the point where you stop, you will
reduce the forces imposed on your body, and therefore the injuries will
be lessened

Actually engineering this is the trick.  You have to make the "nose" stiff
enough to absorb the energy of the crash, but not so stiff as to prevent
the nose from collapsing.

In any case, this will add at least 2m to the length of the bus to provide
a good crush zone.  It would certainly look odd!
