Keep in mind that the torque value on those bolts is only 80lbs! I removed mine from the '72 motor without a braker bar. I did not use a flywheel lock either. I think I put an extra socket on one of the opposing bolts and put a pipe wrench between that the one on the ratchet ( I think I used my torque wrench) to remove for leverage.) be careful not to over torque up reinstall. 

While you are in there replace the pilot bearing ($2 part.)
