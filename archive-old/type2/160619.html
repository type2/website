Hints from Heloise #12 - Loose female connector.  If you use pliers on the
female electrical connector to tighten up the grip just a tad, many times
you will push too hard and the female connector will close right up, and you
will have a difficult time getting the female connector on the male.  Or, if
it is old metal like on our buses, the sides on the female connector will
break.

Take a vice grip pliers and finger tighten the pliers on the female
connector.  Then, release the vice grip for a moment, turn the knob on the
vice grip just a half turn, and now close the vice grip on the female
connector.  Not tight enough?? Try again with another half turn.

This procedure prevents using too much force, and limits the length of
travel on the vice grips.
