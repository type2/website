Hi Phil,
In my oppinion and experience the H is straight forward and back the slant 
your are having to apply is probably due to wear in the mechanism. The most 
likely wear is in the guide plate at the base of the stick. Or it could 
simply be missalligned.

The picture on the ash tray is on a slant mearly to allow for the push down 
needed to engage reverse gear to be shown.
