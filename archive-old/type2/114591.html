I've got Mercedes-Benz alloys on my Bus (79), but you've got to hog the
bolt holes out by 1/16 to fit the VW studs and use a ball grinder to dish
the steel inserts to accept the VW acorn lug nuts. At the back a slight
relief is needed to clear the 10 MM bolt heads that hold the drums in
place. The offset is close to the stock wheel. I would guess that the
wheels that fit the M-B pattern should fit the VW, but an offset might
scrape at the rear... ??
