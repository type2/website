      You know, I just had a second thought on this -- are you running
FI or carbs? I've never run FI with a 009. I had a 050 (like the 009,
but better for buses) with my engine and a Weber, but when I put in FI,
I also switched to the stock dizzy, since that works much better for the
T4 engine, and especially for FI, which assumes it's there. I really
would recommend getting a stock dizzy anyway, for the T4 (although I
always used the 050 on my '69 upright engine, and preferred it
definitely) as the T4 really needs that extra 10 degrees or so of
advance that the 009/050 doesn't have. FI could maybe have a real
problem with that, and, in fact, it could be unburned fuel going into
the muffler and burning there which causes excess heat in that area.
