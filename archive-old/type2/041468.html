Chris,

You could press the flywheel toward the case with two C clamps, and
rig something that you would check with a feeler gauge when you remove
the C clamps and pull the flywheel the other way.  We did this during
a panic overnight rebuild of a Formula Vee the night before an SCCA
race.  Got through the race OK, but when we rechecked, we found there
was significant error.

FYI..... dial indicators are not expensive.  Also very useful when you
are determining if you need to install new valve guides.  Check the
Harbor Freight or Amazon (toolcrib) websites and you will be
pleasantly surprised/relieved.  Also many fleamarkets (if you like
that venue) seem to turn up machinists' tools of various kinds.
