On the original VW fishe there is indeed such a device. The number is ok.
There should be a memo from VW with number A27/68. This memo tells more
about this device. I Don't have this info. Perhaps someone else??
There also is an retractable step with the same number without the "A" This
was special for the split screen bus with sliding door. Sometimes you see
thsi device mounted on a ambulcance truck. You can recognise them at the
strange big divice mounted at the bottom of the sliding door. I think I can
find a picture from such a device. I put it on the mail ASAP

4 screws M5 x12
