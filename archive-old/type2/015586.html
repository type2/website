The key to dealer parts is finding a parts man/woman/other that knows what
it's like to not be able to get a needed part, and understands with
millions of old VW's still registered there is a viable market, but if the
only time you call them is when the part is not available at a discount
from others, they get the idea that every $20 worth of parts you order
requires 20 minutes or more of time and effort to find and order, well the
economics gets out of wack. When you find someone who will pursue and
deliver these parts, consider ordering more from them. It may cost a little
more but such service is worth it's weight in gold and can nip these nasty
parts problems in the bud.
