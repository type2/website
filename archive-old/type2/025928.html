Gail Doles sends:

> Going to replace push rod tubes on a 2.0L FI.  Does the spring have to be
> compressed to do this?  And any information would be appreciated.

What spring?

All you need do to replace the pushrod tubes on this engine is to remove the
valve covers, rocker arms and pushrods; pull the old tubes through the head;
put the seals on the new tubes; slip in the new tubes; reinstall the
pushrods and rocker arms; adjust your valves; and put the valve covers back
on.
