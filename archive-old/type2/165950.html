Hi all,
A have had 2 friends with '76 busses and both busses had metal walls  
behind the front seats.

You can see an example of them at the following link:
http://www.thesamba_dot_com/vw/forum/album_page.php?pic_id=337260
http://tinyurl_dot_com/2meozc

My 79 does not have these.

I think the walls are great.

extinguishers, maps, junkmail, and random bits of fluff and lint.
When sitting on the floor facing backwards, those walls provide a  
mighty nice back rest.

My question is this:  Were those metal walls removable?  If they are  
removable, is it possible to fit them to a post '77 bus?
