A good FLAPS should carry inch sizes of Bundyflex, tin coated, rolled steel
tubing. 3/16" is common brake line size, 1/4" does fuel lines and some brakes,
5/16" and 3/8" fuel lines and cooler lines. VW use the 3/16" for brake lines. I
don't know if they use inch sizes for fuel lines or if it will work. If not, here
is a place (drum roll please!) where your friendly VW dealer can help. It may be
in the back of the book, or the bottom of the fiche, but somewhere  they have
bolts and other hardware  that have numbers that start with N, (like N90305201, a
2.1 waterboxer connector hose for the intake runners), I think that's where fuel
line is. Good friendly local independent VW parts stores can also get true German
hardware from distributors like Wurth. For brake lines, I get the 50 foot roll,
lets you replace the long front to back one in a type 2 all in one piece. Just
unroll up by the front beam and thread it through, flare in place (double flare).
Not as easy as I've made it sound, but definitely doable.
