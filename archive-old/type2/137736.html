Hey John, 

Go by where the rotor is pointing when at #1 TDC, not by which way the
dizzy is pointing.

Your drive shaft is just out of sync, but that doesn't matter. The
important thing is that you know that when you are at #1 TDC your
rotor points at #1 spark plug wire.

Then plug in the rest of the wires per firing order, clockwise direction.

If you have this straight, you still may have to tweak the timing a
bit to get it to idle. A jumper to the starter solenoid is handy for
this so you can make your starting attempts from the engine
compartment instead of having to go forward to the key.

If in doubt about your cards, work the throttle to see that your
accelerator pumps are putting out gas. Spray some carb cleaner down
the throat for a prime to see if it fires off that.

I've never had luck rebuilding stock carbs. I end up adjusting the
idle speed screws way high to get them running, and then they have no
power, so I end up canning them for a better carb.

John
