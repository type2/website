They are EGR valves. At the same level on the back of each manifold is a
bolted on pipe. Follow it from the left carb and it has a tee where it goes
forward thru the front upper tin and then down to the EGR filter. This is
exhaust. The balance tube also goes to the intake manifolds, but it is just
mounted to the front upper tin and has rubber elbows on each end.
