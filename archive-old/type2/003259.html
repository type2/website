Honing the cylinders is very easy, just use a constant in and out motion
with a low speed drill, and don't overdo it.  Afterwards, wash the cylinders
out with hot water and dish detergent, then immediately dry them.  Use new
rings, of course.

Any black hi-temp or bbq spray paint should work on the outside of the
cylinders.
