Hi Justin,

Check your pressure relief valve. You may need to install a new/different
spring for more tension.  You should have more than 4 psi at idle.  I would
think at least 10 psi.  with a stock 1600..


Stalling:  Sounds like: 1. Vacuum leak at the electro-magnetic idle switch
2. Brake booster hose/connections leaking. 3. I know you have the valves
adjusted properly, but every vw engine that I have ever worked on, will
start to stall *when you come to a stop* as the valves get tighter. This
seems to be especially true if you do a lot of freeway driving.....
