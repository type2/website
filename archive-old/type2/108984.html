Subject: RE: [T2] growling while driving over 35mph

Could be lot's of things...wheel bearings & tranny's come to mind. Does it
occur in more than one gear? If you shift to neutral at highway speed does
it stay or go away? Does the tone change with vehicle speed or engine speed?
You can usually narrow the source down by considering what parts are moving
at what speeds under what conditions. Also, crawl under the bus and look at
and wiggle all the drive line and suspension related parts.

Have you hit any bumps lately? A seriously misaligned tire might make funny
noises. Tires inflated correctly?

I've found the Muir book to be very helpful diagnosing funny noises.
