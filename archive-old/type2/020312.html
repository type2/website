One of the best tips I ever got about driving a bus in the snow is... "Act like
there are eggs underneath the pedals and you don't want to break them"  Just go
real easy on the pedals...don't slam on the brakes or accelerator...  downshift
while braking if its really slick out.  Last year I drove about 100 miles in a
blizzard to go look at a Binz.... it was kind of scary...but worth it :-)
But keep in mind that I live in WI... and nothing closes until we get quite a
bit of snow...  from what I hear from other listees out in the western states...
if a couple inches of snow is had...people freak out and the town basically
shuts down.  I guess its just where you live....
