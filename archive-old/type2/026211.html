Good thing you caught it when you did.  My steering actually stopped
functioning (luckily in a parking lot) when I found the same problem.  It
turned out that the bolt that's supposed to pinch the coupler flange to the
steering box had stretched.  Make sure you replace that bolt with a grade 10
bolt.

You shouldn't need to undo any of the wiring with the exception of the horn
wire but you will need to uncouple the column from the dash and unbolt the
cover plate on the floor for maneuverability.  The plastic collar between
the cover plate and the column will probably need to come out as well.
