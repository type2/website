I ddon't have any specific information on specific heat other than the 
synthetics perform much better as coolants.  This is due to two effects, 
both a better heat transfer capability and less heat produced in the  
first place, due to lower internal friction.

I've seen the oil temp drop a bit more than 30 degrees F in a Bug  by 
switching form GTX to Valvoline Synthetic.

Any of the full syntetics should help lower the oil temp.  Mobil 1 15w-50 
is a good value, and easy to find.
