I've no experience with Kirtland, but I have had a set of Uniroyals in
195/75R14.  They showed considerable "stepping" on the front tires after
about 10,000 miles.  My front end is in good shape, so I doubt that the
stepping is due to any problem there.  I replaced them with Bridgestones,
which didn't
seeem to step the same way.  The Laredos were also considerably smaller in
diameter than the 185R14 Bridgestones.  If you do buy the 195/75R14 size,
be sure to get it in load range "D", as "C" isn't strong enough in this
size.
