VW eventually claimed that it had been replacing the heater boxes as part
of a "study" (not a response to an implicit lawsuit threat or a "silent
warranty", such as the vasserboxer engines had) and stopped replacing them
when VW concluded there was no danger to health present.

One prescient listee has pointed out since that the Le Coz team was never
observed to take samples from the heater ducting in the cab, or from the
heat outlets of a running bus, which seems peculiar for a study designed to
determine exposure levels.

AFAIK no data were ever released by VW (or, to be fair, requested of them).
