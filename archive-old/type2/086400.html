Hal,

The lock core can be removed. There is a small screw on the inside of the
shaft. Remove the inside handle push out the handle. You will see the screw
down by the flange. Remove this and the lock cylinder will slide out. Now
what? Find a good used handle from a '68-'73 ($20.00 from us with out a
key). The handle itself has the key code, record this number then pull out
the lock cylinder  carefully not to loose any of the sliders/ tumblers/ or
springs. A lock smith can cut you a key for about $20.00 or buy a complete
used handle from us with two keys for $40.00.
