Subject: 1978  2.0L FI Riviera Engine/Tranny teardown

In mid-Oct I am going to park the bus and drop the engine/tranny on a 1978
2.0L Fuel Injected VW (Rivera model).  
I bought it in Jan 05 from the 2nd owner who in Oct of O4 had 2 heads
replaced and basic work done on it.
Main goal is: Make this bus as solid as possible and only do light/normal
maintence for the next 5 years.

My goals are:
Replace what needs to be replaced, and be proactive about replacing stuff
that is on the fringe. ( I know when you replace one hose you should do the
same to all.  So I am looking to do that as well.)

Get a firm understanding of my engine/tranny and guts. 

Wire in VDO Gages/Inverter/2nd Battery.  (I am going to go for the Dakota
Digital gauge one that is 3 in 1)  Also going to put in solar panel on top
so I can recharge as needed.  This will also allow me to jump my fellow bus
pilots as needed!  

 
One buddy who is going to help suggested replacing/updating these:

Belt                    Fuel Pump? Fuel Filter
Oil                      Exhaust gaskets, Intake manifold gaskets, Orange
RTV Silicon
Oil Filter (external or within sump?)
New Plugs
New Wires?
Rotor/Distributor Cap/ Valve Cover Gaskets
Points if you don't have electronic ignition, or just buy electronic
ignition...it is much better

etc.

What else would you recommend?
