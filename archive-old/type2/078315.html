I was able to fit the wood headliner into my Westfakia, so it looks
westy-like enough for me although purists would notice there are still
traces of vinyl on the door pillars.

The big giveaway is on the 2L late bays. On transporters VW got rid of the
bulkheads behind both seats in 1976(???), but the Westfakiawerkes type2's
carry a bulkhead behind the driver through to 1979.

This is evidence gathered only by personal observation; I'd be happy to have
this further clarified, expanded or corrected with additional data.
