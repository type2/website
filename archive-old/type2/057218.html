Your not the only one, though the vast majority of posts seem to be positive.

I ordered a 10mm head stud kit from them.  It was dropped shipped from
RMMW.  When after a week it didn't arrive I called BD and they said they
meant to call and let me know it had been delayed.  When it did arrive, the
washers weren't there.  I called again and they said they meant to call me
to let me know they were on back order.  Then those arrived.  6 months
later when I finally got around to installing them, I find out RMMW
mislabeled eight 10x188's as 10x240, making my set only half complete.  BD
said to send them back and they would refund me, which is cool.  I got the
impression that the late date was not a problem, but they weren't exactly
willing to replace, only to refund.  They told me that if I wanted to order
the correct studs that I would have to go direct, not through them.

I get the impression that they are a bit overworked at the Bus Depot.  I
will however continue to order from them.
