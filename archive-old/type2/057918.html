There's a Weber carb book...I don't have it either, *but*...listee Brian
Lenahan gave me a two page excerpt for tweaking the idle on the progresive
Weber you have. It boils down to this:
1. Set idle speed screw to yield 1000RPM
2. Turn mixture screw (that low one in the middle) in, until you get a 50
RPM dropoff, then open it up 1/4 turn.
3. Reset proper idle (850-950 on my 78).
