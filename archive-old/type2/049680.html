All you do is build a modified front housing for a VW transmission that 
handles an output shaft bearing next to the shift rod. You extend the 
pinion shaft out of the front of the tranny right through this new bearing.

ratio - maybe existing pinion shaft and differential out of a matching 
transmission?

Might want to add a viscous coupler to minimize binding in turns.
