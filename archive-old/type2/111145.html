On Mon, 30 Jun 2003, John Baker wrote:

> Anyone know any good tricks for getting the starter
> bushing out of the trans bell housing while trans and
> engine are still in bus\bug??

Tap the inside of the bushing.  Use a bottoming tap to extend the full
threads to the bottom of the hole.  Thread a bolt into the new threads and
keep turning until the bushing comes out.
