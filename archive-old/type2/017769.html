Bobb,
  To remove the round plate on the right hand side of the engine compartment,
use a screw driver to pry it off evenly.  There is nothing else holding it in.
If you had FI, you would also have to remove the computer so you can see the
fuel filler connections.  NOTE:  Make sure your gas tank is less than 1/4 full.
To remove the wall b/t the tank and the engine compartment, there are two screws
that are reached from the bottom of the bus (get on your back).  They are near
the bottom edges of the panel and are accessible with a longish screw driver.
To remove the the panel without removing the engine is possible.  I posted a how
to on replacing the fuel neck filler, which I suggest you do if you go to the
trouble of removing the said panel.  Search the archives for Fuel Neck Filler.
It also explains in detail how I removed the rear gas tank panel.  Or more
importantly, how I reinstalled it.  Having done it once, I could do it again in
two hours easily.  Probably spent four hours the first time, as I was also
helping a friend install an A/C blower in an Audi 5kqt (an even larger PITA).
If anyone needs the how to article, pmail me and I'll send it out too.
