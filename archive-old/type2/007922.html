If I where to put together a bus tire page, I was thinking about including the
following:

 - Charts showing ETRTO (European tire standard) dimensional
   specifications for 185R14 tires, including SL, C and D load range tires
 - Charts showing inflation pressure vs load for the above tires
 - Charts showing T&RA (North American tire standard) dimensional
   specifications for 195/75R14 and 205/70R14 tires for various load ranges
 - Charts showing inflation pressure vs load for the above tires
 - Explanation of speed ratings
 - Trade-offs between highway, all-season, snow and high performance
   tire designs
 - Links to major tire manufacture web sites and contact info
 - Links to other tire related sites
