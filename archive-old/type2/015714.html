>Well the ones Bus Depot sent ended up being
>from AVP and arrived cracked where the exhaust port goes thru the head. 

If you're talking about cracks in the exhaust port where the manifold
attaches, this is not supposed to be a big deal.  Mark Stephens quotes some
article from VW which claims that that area of the head may
"stress-relieve" within the first few minutes of operation.  I have a
friend with a rebuilt set of heads that arrived from Mark Stephens with
lots of cracks in the exhaust ports.  If the cracks are near the valve
seats, that's a whole different problem and it's serious.

Again, I'm skeptical since I've never had such cracks in the exhaust ports
of any heads I've run.  You'll have to decide if it's a problem for you.
If you're having a mechanic do the work, I guess it's a problem.

>Not
>to mention after paying 2nd day air it arrives a week after I ordered it.

the shipping.
