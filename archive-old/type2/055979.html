  === The Bob Hoover Series =====

The Exact Sender feature has been popular!   To use this, you have to do
a normal search, then find a specific message.  Once you are on a single
message, there is an option to show all messages with that exact sender
on the FROM: line.  However, the sender match is very specific... even a
single character cosmetic change to the e-mail address will constitute a
different sender.  For a given person, it takes a little effort:

1)  The three different list servers represented the e-mail addresses in
different ways.
2)  Sometimes your ISP, such as AOL, change their software and that
makes it different.
3)  Then people like Bob just re-install their software once in a while
and opt to change their name.  For example, Bob Hoover has changed the
"visible" portion of his e-mail address (at least) three times in the
last 18 months.  In the e-mail profession, this is sometimes called the
"display name", and isn't the same as the e-mail address.  Although Bob
has kept the same "address" over time, slight variations in his display
name cause differences for an exact match.
