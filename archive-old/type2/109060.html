I'm thinking of removing the pop-top of my 78 westfalia and instead
installing a sliding ragtop over the whole length of the pop-up hole (behind

a very strong connection bus<->ragtop. What happens if you drive with both
front windows open (lots of air pressure in the bus), will the ragtop just
pop off?
Right now, this is just an interesting idea for me, but please feel free to
comment. And if the owner of the bus with the mentioned conversion is on the
