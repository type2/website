As I said when I wrapped up the Koni group buy on Monday, Koni had promised
me an undetermined additional quantity as my "last shot" of them, to be sent

happened, I'd make them available to the list on a first come basis.

shocked (no pun intended) to be informed that they are already on their way

previous conversations, I hadn't expected to see them for at least another
month or more!

group buy. For Type 2 and Samba contributors only, there will be a special

availability will be opened up to the general public.

started turning down other orders for custom runs. So, barring another Koni
regime change, the ride is over.
