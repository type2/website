 At any rate, Bill, you seem to misunderstand how the thermostat and flaps
work. Once the engine warms up -- which as I said before, is pretty damn quick
-- the thermostat and flaps just stay wide open until you shut the engine off
and it cools down. There is no "auto-regulation" of any sort from that point
on. They don't help cooling, they just help to get it hot quicker.
