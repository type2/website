that
> this could possibly be a problem with the brake booster? maybe a bad check
> valve or similar problem in the booster?

Probably check valve.  Test it in the driveway and see if the motor
smokes...then it would mean the booster, possibly.

The rear seal would involve dropping the motor.  I would anticipate
inspecting the clutch,amend more than likely needing to replace it.  If oil
has been leaking there, the clutch is on its last legs.  DO NOT just replace
the disk, replace the pressure plate and throw out bearing, too.

Count on putting a front seal in the transmission at this point, too.

If you are paying someone to pull the motor, get as much for your money as
you can.
