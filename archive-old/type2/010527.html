I doubt that it would stop the engine from starting, but it's easy
enough to check.  Get a piece of wire about a foot long, bend the last
1/8 inch about 90 degrees.  Take out the plug and use this wire as a
feeler to see how deep the threads are.  The difference between the two
depths is significant.  I really like the idea of going to 3/4 inch
reach plugs, as this has always been a weak area in type 1 heads.
