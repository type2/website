John...Why not think of buying your own MIG welder? They are not that
expensive. I don't know how anybody running and old vehicle could be
without one. OK....They do take a bit of getting used to but it is not
difficult.  I am currently moving anti-clockwise round my van repairing any
rust I find by cutting out the offending holes and thin metal surrounding
them with a grinder (the neighbours love me) and welding in new metal
mostly salvaged from a set of Dexion shelving. A bit of further grinding
and some body filler and its good for years. You can even give the finished
repair a good thump and it rings like the original metal (useful when
selling the van in the future).
 I am not a pro welder but  I have a lot of experience.If you need any tips
then just ask.
