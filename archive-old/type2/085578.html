Subject: Re: [T2] Gas Cap

Not quite the right year for advice but I bought a very nice German gas cap
for my 78 from busdepot.

It was also hard to get off at first: the seal seemed too thickat first. I
found that this variety of gas cap can go ontwo ways: start vertical the
twist 90 degrees clockwise, or start horizontal and turn 90 degrees
clockwise.  You get far more leverage the first way.
