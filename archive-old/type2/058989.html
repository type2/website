I talked to Mark Stephens about this.  I was happy when they said that they
recommended installing the temp sender at the FI temp sender location
(where I have it) rather than at the spark plug.  They said normal temps at
that location should be between 250-350 degrees.  I've heard other people
whos senders are at the plug say that they run ~100 degrees hotter than
that, so I think your temps are okay.
