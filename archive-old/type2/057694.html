engines.  They have a front end just like a late-model baywindow.

To see a Brazilian Kombi, go to:  http://www.volkswagen_dot_com.br/

and the engine installation was designed by Volkswagen de Mexico.

combination seems to work much better than the waterboxer.  There
might be some sort of arrangement to have these made in Brazil for the
Mexican market, but this seems unlikely considering the protectionist
