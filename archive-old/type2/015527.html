Read my post again. DO NOT use these detergent addatives more than once
every several thousand miles. They are only for occasional maintenance and
too much can cause problems with fouled spark plugs. The cheap stuff may
advise you use it every tankfull, but that's just how cheap stuff is
marketed. I wouldn't use that stuff even once. Techron may suggest every 3
tanks. Bull-roar!! If you are recontaminated in that short of time, the
solution lies elsewhere, not in another quicky shot of detergent
concentrate. If you don't note results with the good stuff from one
treatment, your problem is not likely contamination and another can is
wasted money.
