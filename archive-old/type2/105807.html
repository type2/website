CHROMIUM  CONTAINING
The second branch also contain 12 to 18% chromium BUT
has a LOWER carbon level (less than 0.2%). Since the
carbon is low, these grades have a different
metallurgical structure and are called "Ferritic"
stainless steels. They have the following
characteristics:

 Are magnetic
 CANNOT be hardened by "heat treatment" (always
 used in the annealed or softened condition)
 Weldability is still poor
