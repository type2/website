from day 1 and GEX swore that it was just the break in period.  at 3,376

cyl"  on teardown it was discovered that there were 2 VW pistons, 1 Cofax

this engine in about 10 years it was interesting how they could have
possibly put a "New set" of pistons and cyl in the engine.
