ok, most days i am the only bus on the road in the NE phoenix area...

scottsdale a late bay is often parked near a fabric store (this

about the closest to an authentic delta green repaint i've ever seen, 
too.

escalades, H2s (FUH2!) and monster pickups, it was like crusoe seeing
