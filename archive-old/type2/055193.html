Ok y'all.  I just gave my friend the sunroof from my 79 for his 68.  Yes it
fits and works, but it is not just a bolt on affair.

First off you need the tabs off the donor to put on the donee bus.  These tabs
push the back of the sunroof up when closing.  These need to be welded on.
Measure good, because where these go is very important.

Also, what we need help with is.  How do you put the drain tubes in?  Westy's,
at least the 68 have a big triangular hole in the front corners of the hole
(over driver and passenger).  When it rains the water is coming inside.  How
did y'all work thru this?
