The 6th Annual High Country Bus Festival
July 26, 27 and 28 - 2002
near Boone, NC

There will be some added activities this year,  check the webpage for full 
details and schedules;  but here are some of the highlights:

- The VW Family Feud:  join host Blistered Dawson for family fun as VW Bus 
nuts square off against one another.  Look for the question Survey to be 
posted to the list soon

- Tech Class:  Watch and learn from a Master Bus Guru!

- Trading Buses:  2 bus owners trade buses for 6 hours and each one re-does 
something to the other's bus.  Will they get new curtains? A new jump seat?  
A working spedo?  who know's!  If you would like to submit yourself and your 
bus as possible contestants, please email Cliff Styles:  
cliff.styles_at_cmcsg_dot_com

- As always there will be an award ceremony on Saturday night.  Afterwards 
we will be entertained by THE LOST RIDGE BOYS,  a local bluegrass band.  We 
will also have a Karaoke machine out on Friday night,  so bring your 
earplugs :-)
