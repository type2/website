Yes, it can be done, and in fact it was done from the factory for
European delivery, as some countries had preferential tax structures
for small displacement engines.  The engine tin is somewhat different,
so the trick is to get the proper European tin to match your '72.  I
seem to recall that Bus Depot brought some over some time back so you
might see if they have any left.  Ronnie at http://www.type2parts_dot_com
might be able to help as well, as he has good connections with the
European tin benders.
