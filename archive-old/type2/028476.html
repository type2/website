Spray a small amount of penetrant around the circle formed by the gap
between the hub and composite drum. Lay the bus on it's side for proper
penetration ;-)  These can be a mutha' to remove sometimes.  Hit it where it
mates with the hub. You need a plastic or rawhide mallet or better yet some
careful taps on the drum at the hub with an air hammer...it'll come off.
There's a more brutal way involving physics with the two bolts removed, but
try this first.
