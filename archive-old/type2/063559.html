If you have a leak in the exhaust system, then you'll get this.  If it's
just like a little popcorn sound, it'll be a little leak.  That's not bad,
except chasing it down might be a problem.  Could be anything from the
exhaust manifolds on out to the muffler.

My '77 recently had a nice job done on it's exhaust by the local VW
guru who took the time and care to change a busted stud at the exhaust
manifold, and welded up one of the heat exchangers.  The exhaust no longer
pops when the bus is driving the engine (deceleration in gear).  Yay!
