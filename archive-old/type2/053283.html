Thanks!  Midas quoted me a ~$500 repair job on the brakes.  They wanted to
replace both my rear drums at a meager $99 apiece as well as both wheel
cylnders for $60 each.  Of course the shoes were bad too, etc. etc.

Needless to say, I took the quote with the parts list down to my local
junkyard and got 2 freshly turned drums, 2 wheel cylnders, 4 new shoes,
cotter pins, castle nut removal tool, and a few odds and ends for $100.

I'm going to redo my brakes this weekend. I've never fussed with wheel
cynders before so it ought to be fun and worrisome!

Thanks for the thoughts!!
