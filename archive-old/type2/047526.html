There is a 021 905 205S dual advance distributor, and a 022 905 205S single
advance distributor.

Full mechanical advance in the 022 905 205S is 20.5 to 24.5 degrees at 3400 rpm

while the 021 905 205S  has 21 to 25 degrees at 3400.
