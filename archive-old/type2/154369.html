Zach:

When I pulled my engine, I ended up replacing 4 pushrod tubes as well.  I
had to take the head off because #3 needed to be re-drilled.  If you're
going to replace the seals, I would replace the tubes as well.

There is a gasket/seal set up on California import's website that gives you
a seal/gasket for everything.  My bus is a 78 FI 2.0L engine.  It costed me
$65 plus shipping.  I replaced all the gaskets/seals I could.

The tubes I got from my local flaps.  I think that they were $8-9 each.

I am 1.8 years new to being a bus owner, but what I know is: Do it now
instead of doing it later saves a lot of swearing and glaring from the wife.
