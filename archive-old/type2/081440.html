No, you are not nuts or insane.....you do have a 014 on your dist. The 311
number leads me to believe it is a type3 dist. But the T does not show up. If
it is a type3 it would have to be pre-FI because FI distributors have trigger
points in the base. You do not have a cover on the other side, do you?
A 1500S with twin carbs before Aug 65 has a 311 905 205D.
A 1500S with twin carbs from Aug 65 has a 311 905 205F.

Here is the correct dist. for your bus:
