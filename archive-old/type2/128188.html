Yup, they do look a little(lot) rich, and you are
right it could be the mixture, jets, and choke, one
you can mark off is the choke, take the breathers off
and watch them to make sure they open when warmed up.

right, I believe it's in till it drops then back out a

changing the main jet sizes till the plugs clear up.

the jets be sure to put the same size on both carbs.
