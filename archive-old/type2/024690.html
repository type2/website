I have to say, that early this year, I switched from dino to synthetic
without any other changes and noticed a 10-15 degree drop in my oil temps
under the same driving conditions (weather stays the same here in CA for a

Santa Cruz mountains at 50-55 without getting too hot.  Hurray for
synthetics.
