Subject: Fall cookout in Charlotte NC


Hello all,
 I would like to invite anyone near the Charlotte NC are to join us for Our
Fall Chill and Grill cookout.

The Fall Chill and Grill will be held on October 6th at Reedy Creek Park in
Charlotte NC

Folks will start arriving around 11 a.m. If you want to come early you can.
The park has hiking trails, a fishing pond, nature trail, soccer fields, and
Frisbee golf course.

Bring enough food for yourself and extra to share if you want. There are
grills on site to cook on.

**Donations**
We ask that everyone who attends donate 2 cans of Non-perishable food .
we will be collecting coats and winter clothing , so if anyone has warm
clothes they would like to donate please bring it with you. These items will
be donated to ,as of now, an undecided charity.

Directions:
The cook out is located just off of WT Harris bvd . WT Harris is exit 45 on
I-85.

If you are south of Charlotte, take I-77 north to I-85, go east on I-85 to
exit 45.

If you are west of Charlotte, Take I-85 east to exit 45.

East of Charlotte? Take I-85 west to exit 45

 From the north? Take I-77 south to I-85 , I-85 east to exit 45

At exit 45 take WT Harris Bvd south to Rocky River road (about 2 miles).Turn
left on Rocky River, Go about 1/3 rd mile park is on the right..
