If there is enough stud available then vice grips and torch and some
penetrant will work just fine. Like with Mark, I heat up the stud and
surrounding metal and let it cool some and spray it down with penetrant.
As it completely cools it draws the penetrant around the threads. I then
use a small tip and heat up the metal around the stud and then vice grip
the stud and work it and heat it until it comes loose. Run a tap into
the threads to clean them out and then replace the stud with some
locktite.
