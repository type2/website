	I won't help  -  my approach is simple not elaborate

>engine (FI) how do I determine what is too little or
>too much?

	If you get a water/fuel ratio in the range 5 - 10% you'll get 
most of the benefit without needing a huge water-bottle or refills 
between fuel fillups.


>
>Do you think a bus FI cold start injector might
>deliver too much?  You may not be familiar with that as
>I'm sure they must not be as common there as here.

	I know nothing about this device.

>
>I was thinking about building a DC pulse unit to
>adjust the WI spray.

	Snap out of that digital worldview, bwah.  This is an 
analogue process -  the suction in the inlet pipe (before it splits 
into a manifold) varies according to need, so the only variable is 
the needle size and you're done.  Most likely a tiny needle e.g 
0.51 mm ID will do.  Make sure needle point is in middle of pipe so 
as to prevent induction bias.
