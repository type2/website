> My flywheel has a smallish groove worn in it from the seal

Is this groove (1) on a Type 1 flywheel and (2) on the non-clutch side of
the flywheel, inside the hub area? If it's there, that's not wear--the PO
forgot to put the O-ring into the machined groove in the flywheel.

If it's on the outside of the hub area, I'd just deburr it and go with it
the way it is.
