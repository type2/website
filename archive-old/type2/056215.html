Don't know about installing your own pop top (let me know how it works out
though), but as per the sink:  I have a Vanagon Westfalia and personally I
like having the sink very much, but don't find it absolutely necessary.  I
have a 5 gallon solar shower (cost $20) that I keep on the roof in the
forward storage area of the pop top, and I put a longer hose on it so I can
run it through the side window.  Voila, hot water running in my Westy.  It
drains right into the sink, or, if I didn't have a sink, a bucket.

Just a tip.
