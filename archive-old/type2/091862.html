New rubber step pads are in order.  The old ones (probably after 32 years 
now) have weathered to a hardness something on the order of diamonds.  When I 
took mine off (I did my bumpers last year) they were as tough and hard as a 
rock.  Got the new bumper pads from West Coast Metric and they fit like a 
glove.  Go new (yeah, they're a bit spendy) and you won't regret it.
