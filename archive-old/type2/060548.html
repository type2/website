Hey Stephen and listees,
  I just did the same thing on my 70 Bus and it is a great thing. Make 
sure you get the internal regulated type, it's much simpler. It only has 
two wires to hook up. One (the blue) wire goes on the spade connector 
and the other is the big red one that goes to the battery. No fuss and 
no mess of wires, and 52amps to boot. I know that it was not stock on my 
70 bus, but I think its worth it, it looks like it belongs and only the 
type 2 nuts will know the differents. I van now run fog lights or 
driving lights with out fear. If you can afford it and are not a purist, 
I would do this convertion. And it can allways be returned to stock if 
needed.
