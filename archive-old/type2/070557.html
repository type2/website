Joe,

You need the remote locator kit which I only found at Bus Boys.  It comes 
with a bracket and I mounted the sender on my fire wall.

If you use a offset box end wrench, you can put the wrench over the hose and 
using a slight angle you can tighten the lower end where it threads into the 
engine case.

I used sealant on all the threads.

Or you can cut your tin larger to allow the sender to fit.
