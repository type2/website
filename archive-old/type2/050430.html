You need to have the sleeve around the pilot shaft to use the late clutch.
You can use an early clutch on a late housing by swapping the fork, but
it's might hard to add the holes for the sleeve to an early housing!

To do this you have to grind a bit of relief in the washer next to the
bushing to accommodate the safety wire.  The reason that I do this is that
I had one come out once and it trashed the bearing, bent the fork, and
