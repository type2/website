Sam,

get the Muir book.  he discusses this exact question in detail.

some things to get you started:
-metric socket set
-metric open/box end wrenches
-multimeter
-various screwdrivers
-spare clutch & accelerator cables
-spare belt
-spare points/condenser/distributor cap
-pliers &/or vice grips (needle nose & regular)
-feeler gauges
-spare set of valve cover gaskets
-spare fuses
-spark plug gapper & spare plugs
-Bentley manual & Muir book
-small pry bar
-jack & tire iron
-fire extinguisher!
-spare section of fuel line
-spare fuel filter
-extra engine oil
-extra oil screen or filter
-spare coil
-small flashlight

hope that helps,
