Keep in mind that Haynes manuals come from England.  There were no
carbureted Vanagons sold in the USA.  USA buses came with
carburetors until 1975, when Bosch L-Jetronic fuel injection was
introduced.  As for non-USA VWs, who knows.  Those Europeans took
a while to adopt fuel injection, because they didn't have stringent air
quality laws like we do.
