Too late now, but next time consider replacing the Bowden tube as well.
They're not expensive.  A new tube and well lubricated cable will greatly
improve clutch action and control.

It's also important to have just the right amount of bend in the Bowden
tube.  I don't have the VW manual at hand, but there's a specific curve that
must be established, using shims if necessary.
