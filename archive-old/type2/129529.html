Been there-on our 71 I made a shelf out of plexiglass that hinged flush
to the side of the door and had one of those "knee" hinges that locked
in place when you lifted the shelf.  To make it lay flush, I had to cut

Used it to hold the gas stove.

 lower bracket, makes it possible to hang a shelf from the outside of>
