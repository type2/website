Subject: Re: Right Rear Emergency Brake Arm

Bentley, and I believe Haynes, have an illustration of the components of the rear brakes on a 1971 and later bus.  This illustration shows that the parking brake lever is attached to the rear shoe just as it is on my 1972 Westy.  All you need to do is take off the two bolts that hold the drum on, 11mm wrench, remove the drum, may be difficult with the rust, remove the rear brake shoe and disconnect the parking brake cable and take it home.  The lever is held on by a larger diameter pin and circle clip.  Take the lever off and put in on the new brake shoe.

Hope this helps.  If ytou need more info, let me know.
