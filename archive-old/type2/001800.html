Sean Bartnik wrote:
>Today's fun fact:  The Bosch L-Jetronic system used in our Buses uses
>these inputs to decide on mixture:
>Intake air volume
>Intake air temperature
>Cylinder head temperature
>Engine RPM

While the above ARE the only inputs to the 'brain', don't forget that the
pressure regulator adjusts fuel pressure based on vacuum (a.k.a. load). This
provides greater fuel pressure (and thus a richer mixture) with an open
throttle.

Please correct me if I am wrong.
