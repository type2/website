Re greasing and covering a joint with bad boot:
I did a similar thing to mine 50,000 mi ago- injected grease through a
hole drilled in the top, then wrapped polyethylene all around the joint
head and parts of the arm.  Then used stretchy tape all around the
above, but not beneath, where the nut is.  This has held up; trick is to
do it before crud grinds in and wears the surfaces.
