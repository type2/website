Bus outings range from very structured to very informal.  If you want to
do the organized event thing, you will need to arrange a venue,
facilities, finances, relations with the locals, etc.  

If you keep it
informal you could just publicise "let's meet at [location] on [date]" and
see who shows up!  Nobody's really in charge, which may be better from a
liability standpoint :)

Both types of event are fun.
