Is the butterfly actually changing in the choke?  Is your stairstep coming
in full open?  Does it start if you hold the butterfly closed?

Do you have an oil temp gauge -- if you have a major air leak, then at cold
you aren't getting rich enough.  Another symptom would be overheating when
warmed up.

Check your intake manifolds, the boots near the end and all vac connections.
What type of distributor are you running (stock dual or did you switch to
009)?

Gaskets under carb, did you renew these when you did your last carb removal?
Gaskets under the manifolds where they meet the head?  (Metal ones can not
be re-used at all, they crush).

Still can't find it?  Do a compression check... what is the history of this
engine?  Are all 4 cylinders giving equal power (pull plug wires 1 at time).
