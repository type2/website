Electric pump? Stock or aftermarket? Filters all clean, including the carb
mesh screen?Check that you are getting fuel out of the pump in a continuous
flow, use a bucket to check this, WELL away from any spark etc., unless you
have the marshmallows and wienees handy for the bonfire. Check the pumps
specs, how much gas should be in the bucket for a certain time. Web
progressives are difficult to set up, but once running are very good carbs.
Let me know how you do, I have a complete article on tuning them from the
"Performance Engines Book 2" by HotVW's.
