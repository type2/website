Been poring over the archives, and the advice seems to be: go with the

Thanks for your help!

Before signing off, here's a tip which might be useful:  

Had some problems with the fresh air fan in the bus - switching it on
always caused a fuse to blow.  The shaft of the motor seemed really stiff,
but i was wary of taking the thing apart in order to clean it out.  My
solution?  Spray some ***contact cleaner*** inside through the small hole!
A bunch of dirt washed out, the shaft spun freely, and the motor actually
worked when switched on.
