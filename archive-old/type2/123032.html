Kyle,

Without trying to sound like a broken record check all vacuum lines for
leaks and cracks, really look close. Although on the surface it sounds to
me like you may have a fuel problem. Carbs or FI? You could have crap in
your tank blocking the intake for the fuel pump in that when the blockage
is not obstructing the flow it runs great but when it blocks the hole your
engine is starved for fuel? Try checking the fuel filter, draining the tank
and replacing/cleaning the screen on the output line in the tank if
possible. I cant recall if you can unscrew that fitting on later models, I
seem to recall being able to do that on my 71'.
