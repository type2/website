Subject: Re: [T2] weird miss at part throttle.

Bob,

Identify the fact that it is one cylinder or not first.

Disconnect one spark plug and drive it....move on to the next one untill 
you identify that it is one cylinder or an overall miss.

Check the resistance in all spark plug wires from inside the distributor 
cap to the plug end.

Check the inside of the distributor cap for arcing and/or a crack in the 
cap.  Check the outside for any evidence of arcing.
