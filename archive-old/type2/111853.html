from ggm

a 914 head has three studs for the intake manifold, a Type IV head has 4
studs. you can see the studs (nutted) by crawling head first into the engine
compartment. or if you are lucky, you can open your engine compartment
access port and just look down.

To get the head number, you have to pop off the valve cover and work really
hard to see the numbers under the rocker arms. You should have a number
something like this 021.101.371S. not that number exactly, but that format

this is a wonderful site that has lots of info on VW engine heads

http://www.tunacan_dot_net/t4/tour/heads.shtml
