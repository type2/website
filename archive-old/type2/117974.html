Greetings List,

decide what, if anything, to seal things up with.

And finally the libarary talks about dish soap. Though I figure this is just 
applied to the interior sides of the seals to aid the whole pull through 
string installation process.

So what is the best?  What would seal the best, keep the rust away, not hurt 
my not-so-cheap paint job, last the longest....

Seems to me like the Window Weld Sealer 08606.
