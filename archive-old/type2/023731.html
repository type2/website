torque for a
bus because it is big & heavy (the alternative is a close ratio gearbox and

pancake
(67 SAE hp_at_4200, 101 lb/ft_at_ 3000) and make sure it is better before
proceeding,
otherwise you may find you have a bus that's hard work to keep rolling and
no
fun to drive.
