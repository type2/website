Jupiter is a house brand of Hercules, and the same tires are also sold
under the Merit brand.

I had a set of standard load (passenger) Hercules on my bus before I
knew better) and they were pretty good tires.  The only negative I
found was fairly rapid wear.  The tires finally failed near the end of
their tread life by internal belt separations that caused odd
vibrations.

The LT rated version of the tire should be great on your bus.  I
nearly bought a set, but noted that they were not M+S rated, even
though the standard-load version of the same size was.  Strange. 
Perhaps they've corrected that oversight in the last year or so.
