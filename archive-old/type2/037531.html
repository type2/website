It can cause an oil leak at the base of the filler, and or an exhaust leak
at the pass. side flange..  See my post earlier about this particular
indention..  I ended up just rearranging the metal to not contact the
filler tube.

Also, continued contact between the muffler and oil tube will probably
cause your oil temps to rise a bit.
