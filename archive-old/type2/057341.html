One of the more frequent and annoying problems is the sticky starter
problem. Unless you have a fairly new starter/solenoid you may want to
either replace the solenoid or take it apart and clean/grease it. Removing
the starter is easy with the engine out and a rather difficult and messy job
when it is in.
