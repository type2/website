73 bus is up and running.  Had a few problems after changing fuel filters 
and adj. valves yesterday.  Ended up being the timing. Lock nut on Dist. was 
not tight and it sent the timing to a different dimension.

Note: I'm using a 009 dist. and even though I "static" time the engine it 
still would not start.  I had to continue advancing it til it started.  
Then, put the Tach Guage on it and set to 30 degrees.

Runs fine and Electrical wiring is almost done!
