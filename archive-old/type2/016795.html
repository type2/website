Not sure about the differences between the States and the Netherlands,
but the early bus with the DP engine-- it's a pain, but can be done,
just lay on your back and look up, you can find the gas line in front of
the bulkhead.  You can remove it from the tank, etc. Lots easier,
though, if you have the engine out.  CAREFUL- gas will come out when you
take it off!  Drain it first.

The 78, assuming it's the same, has two hoses to the tank (F.I.) and can
be gotten to relatively easily underneath.  If it's not the same as the
US version, I suspect it's pretty close and you can still get to it. 
Same caution applies!
