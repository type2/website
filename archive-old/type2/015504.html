Just a few things, quickly. 

Exhaust flange and port leaks show themselves as black carbon in the
vicinity. If they are substantial, they report themselves audibly. 

Make sure the tailpipe is in good order. If you have an 'extractor' with
the tailpipe/s centered, the fumes will rise across the back of the van at
speed. The fumes can enter the rear door hatch if the seal is not
functioning. Check that rear upper hatch seal.
