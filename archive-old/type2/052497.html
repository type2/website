If the gauge is reading too high you can get it to read lower by adding the 
appropriate resistance in the circuit.  This will work on all the oil/water 
gauges with thermister (resistance varies as a function of temp) type sender. 
And will work on the CHT gauge which has a thermocouple sender (output 
voltage varies as a function of temp).
