I have read a few replies to this question but none addressed the cooling system problem you will be facing
when you remove the heater box shielding or the lost bottom support for your alternator bracket.

Got a thermostat?  It needs the front lower tin and the pushrod tin to function properly. Otherwise, with all
the cold air going by it, the engine will never run with the flaps in the warm position. The flaps will always
believe the engine is cold.

Do you have a never ending supply of alternator support brackets? Without the bottom end firmly supported, the
top end constantly breaks.
