>All:  Just installed dual 40mm Webers on my '72.  Rus very well, with

>the bus demonstrates a lag just off idle.  Any ideas how to correct

You'll never be able to get great performance and driveability and still
pass emissions with the dual webers.  If you passed emissions, make
notes on EXACTLY how your carbs, timing and points are set so you can
repeat it in the future.  Then set up the carbs to run good for you.

The simplest fix for the stumbling problem is to replace the F11
emulsion tubes with F7's.  But I gotta warn you, your already poor gas
mileage (around 19 mpg, right?) will drop to about 17.5 mpg.  Your
performance will be better, your head temps should be lower, and your
acceleration should be better because the F7's provide a richer
transition.

BTW, I'd like to know what size engine you're running along with your
carb's idle, main and air jets, and venturi size.

Yikes!  That's WAY high!!  The local Porsche guru (he's an a-hole, but I
