I don't know much about this light. It stopped working on my '78 Kombi
at some point in history, never was able to fix it. I figured that I
would notice when thing would get bad. If I were you I would go through
the whole system and check if there is anything leaking (hoses,
cylinders, brake lines, etc.) If nothing is leaking than hopefully it is
the light that's wrong.

A friend of me owns a 1982 Mexico bug. He had the same light going on
for years everytime he pushed the brake. I thought "that's interesting:
a light that indicates if your brake lights are working", until he told
me what the "B"-light really stood for. Anyway, the brakes worked fine
all those years.
