Jim Hood sends:

> OK, for those of us who are ignorant, can someone explain what an alternator
> cooling boot is, maybe what it looks like and why it's good to have one?

It's a little rubber duct on a T4 engine that lets air flow from the fan
shroud to the alternator. It looks like a squished rubber hose--it's
rectangular in cross section. It's good to have one because your alternator
will cook if you don't have it, and so will your engine.
