Subject: Re: [T2] accelerator cable

> Anybody got a best guess as to which one I need for the single carb sitting right on top in the middle of the 1.7L engine?


As long as the linkage lets you pull the cable through, just get the
longest and trim to fit. If you have a soldering gun or iron, solder
the cut end so it doesnt' fray. Or wrap some tape around it.

The stock dual carb one (and others I assume) has a nice cast end on
it to fit the linkage... Something for the set screw to tighten down
on. If you find you need something similar, a bit of solder at that
point works wonders as well.
