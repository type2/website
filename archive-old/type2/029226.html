On my 71 one notch was too much and burned up my Yoko's in 17k miles with
too much spring.  I would HIGHLY recommend getting a little plastic degree
thingy and getting the angle to what Bentley says (would think Haynes would
say the same thing).  To get mine right I had to move both the inner and
the outer splines.  I would also use talc unless you like sqweeks...

My ride height looks good but I haven't noticed any real difference in
handling.  Then again since it already handled like it was on rails it's
hard to tell, although over 130mph it does feel slightly more stable ;)

My opinion is to go for it, FWIW.
