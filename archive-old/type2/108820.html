My muffler is getting louder and louder, and a close examination shows no
holes or leaks.  It's not either of the exhaust setups in the microfiche
files.

I need to replace it before my landlord sets his foot down about the noise.

Frankly, it looks a lot like one of those "cherry bomb" mufflers that kids
(back in my youth) use to put on their cars to make them louder.  There's a
"4 into 1" or "2-2-1" set of collectors, then the "cherry bomb"-looking
muffler which runs back across to the right where there's a 90 degree pipe
that turns to the rear.  The muffler itself is tapered at both ends but
expands to 2.5" or 3" along its length.

only thing even close is item #3714 on this page:

right, with the 90 degree pipe coming out of the muffler about 6" in from
