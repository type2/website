The volume control screws will only help your idle.  If you want to
seamlessly adjust for altitude, convert to fuel injection.  Seriously.
Carburators are not capable of adjusting themselves for altitude - the best
you can do is jet the carb to run best at the altitude you drive in most,
which in your case would be at or slightly above sea level.  I should
mention that not all fuel injections can adjust for altitude, I believe
only those with a mass-air sensor can do it.  I'm not sure which year
busses have mass-air sensors on them, probably only the later ones - I'll
have to let someone more knowledgeable about bus fuel injection address
that.
