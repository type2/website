Here's my storage method for nuts/bolts:
 Cut the tops off 1/2 gallon milk cartons so you have 5" high "boxes".  If
you use polyethelene, you can even use cleaner in them.  Staple the flat
sides together with an office stapler- you can create "flats" of boxes this
way with some order to them.  The boxes stacked this way give more usable
space than round countainers.
