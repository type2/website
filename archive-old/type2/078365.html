I think the early engine I have is either an 80 or 90 HP engine. Don't know 
what the torque rating is but probably proportionally more than a VW.

I know you can move to larger jugs and and get more displacement.

The late model Corvairs had a bit more bore and stroke. They can 
retrofitted I hear.

The earlies are 2.4 liters (145 c.i.) and the lates are 2.7 liters (164 
c.i.). You can build a 3.1 liter (190 c.i.?) by machining in 2.0 Type IV VW 
cylinders and jugs.

http://www.corvairkid_dot_com/190cid_01.htm

The engine is almost complete - carbs, cooling, generator (can convert to 
alternator) and assembled. I was told the engine ran when pulled. Take it 
with a grain of salt of course. You'll need exhaust (don't all old engines?).
