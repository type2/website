Brendan,
Have you tried to adjust your clutch cable?  I have found that sometimes this cable will stretch or otherwise loosen up, causing hard shifting.  Follow the cable underneath from the pedal to the back of the bus where it connects to the little arm on the side of the transmission.  There should be a wing nut at the very end of the cable behind the little arm I just mentioned. This nut\arm release the clutch when the pedal is pushed and the cable is pulled. Tighten this nut a few turns (in essence to "shorten" the cable a bit) and try the clutch again.  If this doesn't help look at the linkage and then the clutch itself.
Peace, John Baker
