The easiest way to find your TDC mark on your pulley:
Remove the 3/4 valve cover.
Rotate your engine IN THE DIRECTION OF ROTATION [not backwards!]
Watch the movement of the #3 cylinder valves.
You will see the exhaust open then close.
Immediately, you will see the intake open and close.
[If the exhaust opens instantly after the intake closes, you are going the
wrong way]
Keep turning and when the exhaust JUST closes and the intake JUST starts to
move, STOP.
Look at your pulley. If you have a mark pointing to the seam, that is Top
Dead Center, Zero.
If you don't, get white touch up paint and paint a mark on the pulley.

Now go here:   http://www.oldvolkshome_dot_com/ovhignbo.htm  and find YOUR dist.
number on the list.
Click on the application. Read what your timing specs are supposed to be.
Time your engine to the distributor you have.
