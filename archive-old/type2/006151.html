I don't know about type III, but EC is the engine code for 1.8 Porsche 914
engines from the mid 70s.  The engine is almost identical to the VW engine,
but with a different drain plug and oil filler/dipstick set up.

Of course, over the years, the engine could have been rebuilt with hydraulic
lifters.  You should be able to see them if you take the rockers off.
