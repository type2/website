Subject: RE: [T2] Synthetic gear oil okay?

Make sure that the oil is GL4. I'm suspicious about oils that are both
GL4 and GL5 rated. GL5 is corrosive to the brass synchronizers in the
transmission. Of course, I don't know how long it will take GL5 to eat
the sychronizers.

Redline makes a synthetic that's GL4
http://www.redlineoil_dot_com/products_gearlubricants.asp

By the way Mobil 1 synthetic gear oil is GL5 and is not recommended for
VW trannies. Mobil told me that when I called their 800 number.

Steve
