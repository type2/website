Subject: Re: [T2] weird oil cooler

Not as wierd as the one I made for my 71 bus back around 1974-took the 
strainerplate and braised on 2 1/2" pipes- one covered over the takeup 
tube so oil could be sucked through a finned cooler and a Per 1 filter 
mounted in series between the body beams near the tranny.  The other 
just went into the sump.  I believe I used an oil pressure gauge and it 
worked with multivscisity oil.  In winter I bypassed the 
cooler....Always worried that a hose could snag on a branch or hump in 
the road, and ended up screwing a screen of some kind over it all.  Took 
a bit to prime after an oil change.
