This is not normal.  Previous post of mine disclosed various problems 
with non-built-in-resistor coil.  One of the symptoms is that points last 
about 200 miles before being fried (need the resistor to lower power 
to points).  Another possible cause is the condenser is shot.
If any of the wiring from the coil to the distributor is under size, 
through age or mis-wiring could cause improper voltage.  Be sure that 
you are using the correct points for that particular distributor.
