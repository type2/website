John,

    You do not want a 1776 in a 73.  The 1776 is based on a Type 1 bug
motor.  Your 73 has a type 4 pancake motor in it. (or should have)  I had a
73 with a 2L motor.  It will work great if you can work out the carb setup.
I started out with a Weber 2 barrel & went with a set of Dellorto 40mm.
Those were hard to tune but ran great once I got it.
