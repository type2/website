Hi Steve,

Sounds like a Coleman.  I have one in my Riviera.  If you remove the grill is
there a 12/120v switch on the l/h side?  Is the exterior vent about 6x9?  If
so, that's a Coleman.  If you'd like, I think that I've got a pic showing the
vent that I can send to you.

The bad news is that Coleman is no longer in the RV business so there is no
parts or service available.

Mine bit the dust when I neglected to switch over to 12v and fired it off. 
Melted most of the insulation off the wiring.  I now have a 10,000 btu
hydro-flame unit to replace it with.

To test it, all you need is a propane tank with regulator (like for a
barbeque) and a 12v supply.Should be a piece of cake.
