Check that voltage regulator. I've got a local friend here in Cookeville 
that also has a '78 (Riviera) and it eats a regulator about once a year. 
These are from Advance Auto Parts here in Cookeville and the bus seems okay 
in every other way. I figure that Advance just sells a cheesy part and my 
friend likes to shop there. She buys them, I install them, and she's happy.

Advance will (used to have) the alternator but it's a special order item 
through their foreign auto parts catalog (Berkely, Binkley, something like 
that) - i.e. not necessarily in the computer - in the paper catalog. If you 
have a store operated by 17 year olds you might not have any luck as some 
I've met dunno how to look it up if it's not in the computer. See if 
they'll let you look it up in the paper catalog.

Usually overnight though.
