While we certainly don't aim to replace the Full Moon Bus Club site

Moon group.

possible, working with all of you as part of that, and create a  
virtual space online for this family of Bus (and other VWs) owners to  
congregate. We have an incredible amount of server space and  
bandwidth available to our site, so it can grow without many  
technical issues. Any content contributions you wish to make will be  
greatly appreciated. Suggestions or comments are also appreciated.
