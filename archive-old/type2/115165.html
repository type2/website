The bird beak version is NLA. It's only required if your state has a vehicle
inspection system that knows it's supposed to be there. Despite the fact
that unleaded gasoline is no longer available stupid states like CA insist
you have the vehicle in original condition and restrict the entry of
unleaded nozzles in some way.

The hypocrisy is that if you have the bird beak but it's ripped at all you
fail as well.

To replace it you need to pull out the pipe. This means, removing the paint
can lid, the ECU and the firewall to upcouple the pipe from the gas tank and
pull it through the body from the outside.  Learn the orientation of the
filler neck to the pipe and replace it will the new one and feed the pipe
back in.

It's a pain to do it. I'm not sure there's another way because the opening
is so small.
