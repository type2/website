That's not quite the right answer. I'm not sure where I could even find JUNK
grade parts for a type 4 engine, unless they were used. Well, maybe Brazilian
bearings and rings, even then they are not that bad. The real reason is the
other 7/8 of the job. It should be time, as in labor in cleaning and
reconditiong the parts, replacing the cam/ lifters, finding better cores, if
an exchange, etc. (I'm saying that there might be $125 difference in parts

worst, and I'm not sure I'd trust this guy.
I think you need to find someone with good credentials (which, of course, is
what you asked) or second best, get an AVP or Boston Bob motor. Of course,
that changes the dynamics of the transaction, and you might have to ask the
vendor for names of a local shop that would just install an engine.
