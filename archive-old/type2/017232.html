      As long as the distributor *gear* was installed correctly, there
is no way the dizzy itself can be installed wrong. It is possible,
however, that the gear might be off a tooth or so.
      Another thing to check is whether the fan hub (this is a T4
engine, right?) key is not broken. If the fan hub moves on the end of
the crankshaft, you can time it all day long with a timing light or
static and the timing will show "correct" but won't be -- I burnt my
heads last year because of this.
