There's a great "database" web app called "Webteacher's Webdata",
licensing is cheap, he may even GIVE you a copy for free as it's
nonprofit and and public service...  Fella's name is Rob Young.

Webdata allows users to own their own records and manage them with
password protection.  Admin has full access.  The resultant database is
fully searchable.  It allows for links and pics, too...

It'd be the perfect thing for AIRS.  The static pages are really a
pain...

variations like CA, ca, cal, etc...
