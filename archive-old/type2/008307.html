You don't need a new manifold.  Just contour the end of a 1/2 inch
tube to match the OD of the manifold below the carburator and then
braze the tube into place.  Once the tube is brazed on all the way
around, drill inside the tube to open a hole into the manifold.

You could run vacuum hose all the way up to the booster, but it is
better and cheaper to mimic the tubing in the late buses.  There is a
SAE vacuum hose that is nearly identical in ID to the metric hose but
that is a lot cheaper.  You don't get the nifty fabric cover, though!

Swapping the late rear suspension is also a good idea.  You will love
the composite drums!
