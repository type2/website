>	So my question is what should be connected to the open end and what
>should control the opening and closing of this valve (i.e. there is a
>control cable connected to a lever, but nothing seems to move it from
>the environ controls.

The '79 (and '78) if it still has the original valves had a 2" corrugated
paper
hose down to some exhaust pipes that ran along the engine attached to
the motor mount frame.  '72-'77 just vented to the atmosphere so don't
sweat it if your hose is missing, or if the pipes are gone as well.

The heat (red) levers should pull on those cables, they may be stuck or more
likely stretched to the point they don't do anything.  Cables are fairly
inexpensive
still I think.
