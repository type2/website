At 08:16 12/04/99 -0600, Christian vlot wrote:
>I have a 72' with dual 34pdsit carbs.  The left carb seems not to be
>working.  I also have a GT sports style muffler so to two heads have
>their own exhaust.  The left exhaust is cold will the right has warmth. 
>The compression on both is the same just the temperature is different. 
>The power loss is minimal but it is there.  What is going on?  

Obviously your left bank is not firing.  Are you checking the exhaust temp
at idle?  If so, run the bus at 3500 RPM for a minute or so and then see
what the exhaust temp is.  The idle circuit might be bad on the left carb
which would cause the temperature differential at idle but both would be
hot at higher RPM since the main fuel circuit would kick in then.  This
would also explain the minimal loss in power.  My guess is a plugged idle
jet on the left carb.
