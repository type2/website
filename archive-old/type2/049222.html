The Bus is a 1979   7, 8, or 9- seater.   The engine number is missing 
a digit. If the number is actually  CJ0019599X then the case is from 
between 12-'75 and 8-'76. The X tells us that the engine was rebuilt 
at some point, it could have been a factory exchange unit. It might have 
been built up as a 1979 engine, it is also possible that only the short 
block was replaced, and that the original heads were re-used.
