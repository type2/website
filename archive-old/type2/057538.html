Finally, after a year of meticulus work on my '73 bus(Capt. Geech) all is
working and I took him for a cruise last night.  I kept checking the
engine to look for signs of trouble, guess what?  The thermostat which is
a refurbished one from Bus Depot wasn't allowing the flaps to open.  The
engine compatment is really hot and I am worried so I release the tension
on the wire to let the flaps open.  It cooled off at idle noticably in 5
minutes.  
	I live in northern Idaho and I thought the flaps would be a good
idea in the winter but until I get a temp guage for it the flaps stay
open.
