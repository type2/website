>2.What is causing this unwillingness to run? 

Volkswagens are small precise engines. An old chevy 8 cylinder pickup can run 
forever on 5 cylinders. A VW, especially
a bus, needs all four to run properly. If it isn't running on all four you 
overheat the motor, the oil thins, causing many problems, 
hard starting being a minor one, destruction of your main bearings, pistons 
and cylinders being more expensive problems.

>3. How can I fix it tonight? 

Pull the motor, take off the left head. remove the cylinders and check for 
scoring on the walls, pieces of piston lying around, 
broken rings, that sort of thing. If everything looks reasonable, replace the 
rings and the head with the bad valves and put the
motor back in.
