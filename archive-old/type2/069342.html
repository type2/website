If your '81 has it in the same location as my '84, there's a
computer-printed paper sticker on the body, under the fuse box on
the left side of the (LHD) steering wheel, just inside the
driver door.

My '84 has a code of LH8U; the touch-up paint officially calls it
Bronze Beige Metallic, but it's brown by any other reckoning, so I
wouldn't be surprised that yellow is officially ivory.
