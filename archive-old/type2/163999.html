Description

A truly international vehicle, this is the story of VW's timeless classic,
the Type 2 Transporter, Bus and Camper.  From its origins to the present
day, the history of this familiar and much-loved vehicle is chronicled in an
evocative and detailed style.
The Author

Malcolm Bobbitt is the author of more than twenty marque histories and
general automotive books, a number having appeared as foreign language
editions.  As contributor to several periodicals and newspapers his work and
photographs have appeared world-wide.  Member of the Guild of Motoring
Writers, Society of Automotive Historians, and the Society of Authors,
Malcolm is acclaimed for his books on Volkswagen, including an earlier VW
Bus publication released in 1997 by Veloce.  Married to Jean, Malcolm's
activities include public speaking, classical music, collecting motoring
ephemera and getting behind the wheel of his early British-built Citrokn DS.
