out down at the place where all us sailboard freaks hang out on the
lake. We noticed her living there in her car, but paid no attention,
it's her trip, right? Then she bought a gallon of white house paint and
a brush and painted the car -- I mean the whole thing, tires and all,
inside and out. One surfer whose into drug rehab went and asked her how

pretty well dumpster diving and begging. But yeah, he could probably use
a new engine to get him a little further south.
