Eons ago I worked as a test driver at the General Motors proving grounds.
Any instrument that needed to be observed was placed in a pod on top of
the dash (the exception was fuel pressure, which went on the cowl outside
the windshield for fire safety)

If you go this route, be sure to use an enclosed pod that covers the back
of the instruments and the wiring.  Makes things neater and prevents sun
damage.
