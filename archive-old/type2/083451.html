Yeah, they do exist.  My last set of Cooper
Mastercraft R/D (same as Cooper SRM) were 195/14C
but load range D with a max load of 2000+ lbs.

handling was a VERY VERY DRASTIC improvement over
the passenger all seasons that were on the
vanagon.  

The only thing I disliked about these tires was
the tread was A/S but not all terrain.  They
filled with mud easily.  I really like to drive
in mud or wet grass with a little assurance.  So
now I'm riding the cheap all terrains Liberators
from Wally world in 225/70/14 and a max load of
1600.
