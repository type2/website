What i would do...

Get pop-nuts.  When i disassembled a 68 westy i found that they held on
the rear brackets with a thing that looked like a pop rivet but was
threaded.

You just put it in like a pop-rivet, and then you have a blind nut. I
believe they are available in stainless or aluminum.  easy as pie.

try www.mdmetric_dot_com for a source.
