the cam in it that says "not recomended for FI"...so I *can* be a power

bus!...maybe not well yet, but he is running.

My other push is to get rid of lots of parts off of my stock fi system.

No more resistor packs
No more afm
No more double relay
No more cold start injector

Uses stock head temp sensor
Uses stock injectors and intake manifolds
Uses stock throttle body

You can tune it with a laptop.
The code to do this is available on the net and free!

A lot of those guys on that list are doing really weird stuff that equates 
to drag racing and that is why they are building the fancy fuel rails and 
throttle body setups.  The ones that are in to this sort of thing say that
when they switched from afm type stuff to MS, the difference was just 
incredible!

that!!
