I know this one! It's the breather box hose and mine wasn't even on my
bus when I got her. Two months and a couple of mechanics later, David
Schwarze was the one who notice it and mentioned I might want to get one
of those. It's not vital, but it's a good idea. I think he said it
routes the blow-by gases to the air cleaner so it doesn't gunk up your
engine (correct me if I'm wrong, David or anyone else). If I recall,
it's standard 1/2" air hose, available at any FLAPS.

Of course, you have a 71 which would have the upright engine, and I have
a 73 which has the pancacke engine so I may be wrong.
