Interestingly enough, it seems like there are quite a few complaints
about Pertronix.  Now, that could be because the Pertronix have a higher
failure rate, or maybe there is just a small install base of compu-fire
on this list.  Either way, I'm going to switch over to the compu-fire

points have worked fine.  However, I'd like an upgrade, just like I did
with megasquirt.  There are issues either way you go, but I think that
the benefits are worth not having to deal with adjusting points and
checking dwell.  If worst comes to worst, I can always put the points
back in.  I'm willing to take that chance...
