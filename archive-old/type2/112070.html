I would still shift the shocks side to side.  Shock mount bushings going
bad can feel tight as a drum to hand pressure and still sound like this.
You just cannot exert the 3000 pounds of force that the bus can.
Shifting them costs nothing except some sweat and if the problem moves
with the shocks, then you know to replace.

>   I have not checked the torsion bar, but there is no grease leaking out of

There is no grease in the torsion bar, just some talcum powder to ease
