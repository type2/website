On 7/13/06 3:19 PM, "Stephen Diercouff" <sgd_at_tfm_dot_com> wrote:

> 
> I'm looking for advice as to what I should check on it before trying
> to put it in service

The engine is probably fine.

Take out the plugs, squirt oil into the plug holes and if it turns over
easily, I would say just run it. As long as it was assembled properly with
assembly lube, it should be pretty clean inside.

If you are storing an engine for a long period it is best to remove the
plugs, squirt some oil in and turn it over with a wrench, do this every few
months and the engine will be good as new inside.
