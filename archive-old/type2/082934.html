 The problem, as you already know, is the fact that Baywindow models have the support beam that has the extra engine mount.
There is an aftermarket mount you can get than is part of the oil pump cover, but it basically means that you're putting unnatural weight on the oil pump which has a job to do all by itself.
Yes, I'd give it a miss......Type 2 1600's aren't that hard to come by or if you're willing to fiddle with tin ware you can always convert a Type 3 flat motor to an upright one because it has needed mount like the Type 2 1600 engines.
