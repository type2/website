the wiper lever for the delay function.

Since the lever is just a switch, you've got to have 3 positions and off.

something to function as the delay position that you don't have on the lever.

delay relay or simply a switch to move the low speed wiper function from 
normal low speed to delayed low speed. Either way. I'd just buy a fancy 
looking toggle switch for the delay. Maybe use the VW early baywindow rear 
dome light switch so it looked stock. Pulls out - is on or off and mounts 
in the dash near the headlight switch.
