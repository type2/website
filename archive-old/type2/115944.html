There are several versions of the pedals afaik. 68-72 has one style of
linkage, 73-79 another. Within the last one, the pedal changed slightly in
76 and the pivot pin changed during a year I can't pin down.

Given all of this most of the vendors list all pedal components as 68-79
which is confusing.

I haven't seen a 70 pedal assembly at the junkyard in a while but I believe
it doesn't use that spring. That spring is used on the later assembly: one
end presses against the floor and the other end the the pedal. It wraps
around the middle of the pivot pin.
