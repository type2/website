Greetings Campers,

During a particularly slow day at work this week, I ammused myself by
designing a useless but cute decal for the rear window of the '78
campmobile. Using the familiar "Fahrvergnugen" design, I mutated it into
"Fahrgencampun" (have I seen this elsewhere?) Anyway... is there anyone out
there whose command of the German language is better than mine, who knows
if "Fahrgencampun" bears any similarity to a real word or phrase? I know
"Fahrvergnugen" translates roughly into "the joy of driving", or something
close to that. My fear is I will be driving around with a sticker
proclaiming "the joy of sniffing bicycle seats" or worse! If anyone would
like one, let me know and I'll make extras. Thanks for your indulgence.
