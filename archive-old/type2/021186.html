I doubt if you ever will get the hub caps back to 100%---But the best method i have found
is placing the hub cap in sand---like at a kids playground--Put the chrome part facing
down into the sand--Then Lightly start tapping out the dent --from the Outside of the dent
-towards the middle--Do Not start in the middle----A hard rubber mallet works best--take
your time--After a while you will get a feel as to just how hard to hit the hub
cap--Always better to Light then too heavy--, TWWM, uj
