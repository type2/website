Sorry, but if they are bad you toss them.  Get a new one and two new
boots.  You only have to remove the bad joint from the axle, then you can
put both boots on from that side.  I have had excellent luck with Rockford
cv boots (http://www.rockfordcv_dot_com) and not so good luck with Lobro and
Qualitat boots.
