A better match for the 1600SP is actually a more common 30PICT-2, stock
setup on 69-70 in the US.  You can also use the universal 30-31 PICT
replacement.  My experience with my 34PICT3 was the new one was soooo much
better than the one I carefully cleaned and rebuilt.  Also with a header
exhaust you may want to increase the jet size a bit.  I wrote about that
earlier, Solex and jet should find it in the archives, or I'll fill you in
on P-mail.
