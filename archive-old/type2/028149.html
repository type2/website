The time has come to pass on of the busses on to another that will hopefully

The History :
I bought it from the second owner who was using it to haul stuff once in a 
great while. He had way too many cars and the soon to be wife made him sell 
one. I got to pick from the lot, I picked the bus of course. The original 
owner was a pastor apparently. I think he mentioned them using the bus to 
transfer kids to functions and whatnot. The owner I bought it from tinted 
the windows (did a pretty good job) and added a nice new shifter knob (its 
that naked lady with her back arched you see on semi's - I really liked that 
touch) and mounted the spare on the front.

I have owned it almost a year. In this time I have done the following :
replaced :
all cv joints
hydraulic brake
EGR canister
positive batt cable
head temp sensor (1 and 2)
vacuum advance unit
rebuilt the stock distributor (75 has a few parts that are 75 only)

In addition I found new rubber for all of the floors, just like new.
I have the front rubber floor mats also.
Installed a Z-bed (see www.zproducts_dot_com)
relay for second battery
12V hookups connected to second battery
aux lights for when bus is running or not
I have the black plastic tire cover and strap that holds
it to keep it from banging around.
And there's a nice little black console that high enough to sit
between the front seats.
The kick panels are perfect, as is most all of the paneling.
I have the center seat and all the seatbelts for it as well as an
extra rear bench seat also in perfect shape.


What's wrong? :

The nose has that little bit of rust in a few spots under the windshield.
The nose is also banged up a little from the tire being mounted on there.
I don't have it mounted on there now but I have the piece if you want to put 
it back on.
The drivers side rear quarter panel has faded paint marks, and the rear 
cargo hatch has marks from some a-hole that keyed it on new years this year.
It uses oil, but a typical amount.
It idles high sometimes, someone said the linkage underneath needs to be 
adjusted. I was going to do this as the last thing before it was perfect,

What else.... its a really nice Transporter and would make a great daily 
driver. I have been driving it every day on the highway to and from work. I 
change the oil every 1500 and the oil filter every other change.

The PO said the motor was rebuilt 40,000 miles ago. he did not have any

I can't think of anything else right now other than to say I love this bus, 
and I hope the next owner will enjoy it as much as I have.
