Robert,

Since I just replaced the rear shocks on my loaf, this scenario is still fresh
in my mind:  (assuming at least 74-79 is configured the same) The upper bolts
screw into integral threaded brackets that you can't see from the outside.  My
guess is that either the bolt or bracket is partially stripped - try pulling
the bolt out as far as it will go, then shim it tight between the bolt head and
body, back it out a little more, re-shim, back out, etc. until the threads bite
or the bolt is all the way out.  

Best o' luck to ya ..
