How can it?!?
35 minutes with no thermostat and your engine will barely reach 150 degrees if you are lucky. The thermostat
controls the flaps that control
air flow over the heads and cylinders. No thermostat or no flaps [on a type 4 engine is a total mistake] means
the fan constantly cools off the heads and cylinders and it will never reach operating temp. Unless the
fins are totally plugged with crap......
