I took this to mean that Cha had seen a dorm-style fridge at Wal-mart. If
the question was about a Powered-Cooler style 12v unit, much of the same
applies.
You will still require increased charging capability and battery bank size.
In round numbers: A cooler drawing 4.5amps at 12v DC will kill your 100A/H
starting battery in about 9 hours (100 A/H divided by 2 to get usable
capacity divided by 4.5A minus a bit for efficiency loss).

These coolers (in my experience) chill enough to keep beer cold, but are not
really capable of keeping meat or dairy at a safe temperature.

That being said, if you want cold drinks while you are driving they are
great!
