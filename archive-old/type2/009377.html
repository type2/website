Rebuilds are expensive because the part the fails is the worm and peg,
and fabricating new worms and pegs requires a hefty investment in
machining and heat-treating.  Once the parts are in hand, the labor of
assembling the thing isn't much.  For this reason, it really isn't
practical to rebuild them yourself unless your worm-and-peg are good.
