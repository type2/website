Sneaky trick so you don't have to bang on em.  works best if you have a
slotted wheel.  
1. I don't take the wheel off the hub
2. put wrench on nut (cotter key removed)
3. large screw driver (or other similar BAT [big assed tool]
4. put screw driver in wheel slot and over(under) the wrench, apply
presure to screwdriver, which acts as a lever on the wrench, and no
forces are applied to the half shaft.

hasn't failed yet
