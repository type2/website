Subject: Re: [T2] How hot is HOT?

When you overheat too far then the pressed in valve seats begin to come 
loose and you 'drop a valve seat'.

You are running way hot in my opinion. I have never run more that 375 
degrees under ANY condition --- and that includes towing a Hyundai Excel 
from Norfolk to Chattanooga with a Beetle (Type IV powered).

I would pull the VDO sender out and dip it into a pot of boiling water 
(don't touch the sides of the container) and see what temp it reports --- 
212? Then it is right one and your engine is in fact running hot.
