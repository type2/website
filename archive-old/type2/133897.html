Good evening,

I released the parking cable; and compared my new shoes to the
originals for fit before beginning the job.

After taking a break and reading the wisdom found here, I was able to
rectify the shoes with the drums. Fully assembled, the wheel now turns
freely so next i'll put together the other one.
My T2 was manufacturered 6/71. 
Turns out the solution to the problem was just to grab each of the
shoes in one palm, exercise my torso, and give the assembly a nice
shake, shove, and jolt together. Something finally seated propertly. I
released the adjusting screws as far in as they could go, and put the
drum on with tight bolts...seems to be just what was needed.
