I will also throw out that an even easier way to add more headroom to
the bus is by finding a Sportsmobile top. These tops tend to have more
room under them (mostly due to a stright pop up like the rivera) & can
provide a penthouse upstairs for the kids to sleep. Installation is a
snap (well compared to putting on a real westfalia top). Instead of
all that welding all you need is a really big hole in the roof of your
bus & some screws. The frame that the fiberglass hooks to is
substantial & seems to make up for any loss of structural strength.
Eventually I will write a article on how it did it, but till then look
here for what the product looks like.

http://fasterlaster_dot_com/albums/Sportsmobile.shtml

The top is, in my opinion one of the goofyest looking pop tops out
there, but it matches the driver in my case :)
