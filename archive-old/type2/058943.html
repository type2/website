Well, what's to stop you from tearing it off yourself and 
tossing it?  

This isn't nerve gas we're talking about.  The whole 
"EEEEEEEEEK!  Asbestos!!! BOOGABOOGA!" scare got 
started when people who worked around the stuff all
their lives developed respiratory problems.  The threat
posed by a five-foot by five-inch sheet of the stuff is,
as far as I'm concerned, about the same as that posed
by an errant meteorite.  Wear a dust mask.  Be careful
cutting the straps which hold it on.  Put it in a 
garbage bag, tie it up securely, take a shower, do your
laundry. 

If you want to be *really* safe, douse it with water to 
help prevent dust from flying before removing it. 

End of problem.
