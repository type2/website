At 01:28 PM 09/27/2003, you wrote:
>I had one exhaust gasket that would not come out of the head. (The round 
>copper gaskets between the heat exchanger and the head.)  I have tried to 
>pry it out with a pocket knife and a small screwdriver blade bent at a 
>right angle, but it will not budge. Any ideas about how I could get this out?

A shot of PB Blaster, then take a medium screwdriver and use the business 
end to tap-tap-tap all around the perimeter a hundred times, 'nother shot 
of penetrant, more tap-tap-tapping. It just may come loose. If not, get a 
90 degree hook scribe and try to force it under the copper at various 
points around. Once you get it to move a hair, work it back and forth and 
it will become looser and looser and move more and more until you can get a 
good grip and force it cattywampus and out.
