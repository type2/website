Dear Dave & Dasha,

Definitely get the bus as solid mechanically as you can before you go.
Consider collecting likely spare parts (maybe a spare carb, from what
you've said?) for likely use en route. Locate a good machinist before you
go, in case you need to order anything heavy while on the road.

Regarding where to go: On our VW honeymoon, we bought an annual pass to
the US National Parks (about $75 at the time, for both of us) and a copy
of a book with a title something like, "Camping the U.S. for $5/ night and
less." We hit half a dozen National Parks in 7 western states, camped in
dozens of locations without paying a dime for camping. We also had some
serious engine trouble despite all my precautions, but we were prepared
(for the most part) to deal with it.

Good luck and congratulations,
