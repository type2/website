>-I've tapped a spark plug hole and it was pretty easy but considering =
>how big the flange on the exhaust header is I can see that the stud MUST =
>come out perfectly square - so has anyone done this while lying under =
>the bus and can you give me any pointers/advice?

I did this laying under my bus for lack of money. Could have gotten ALOT 
more expensive had I made the slightest mistake. If you drill too far 
you'll go right into the combustion chamber I believe. I used - catch this 
- Chevy small block exhaust studs. The threads were coarse enough to bite 
the stripped hole (once drilled larger and tapped) and still not so large 
that the headers wouldn't fit (with some drilling on the header pipes). Oh 
- and that was all I could get locally in my small town. I've got maybe 30K 
miles on this Frankenstein repair and no leaks. YMMV.
