Billy,

This *should be* an easy one.

You don't have to drop anything to get this one in.

Put the trans in 1'st or third before you start.

Once you get the center out bolt(between the rubber bushings)
move your shifter to 2nd/4th and you should 
have plenty of room to get the old one off of the trans and the new one 
in.

You may need someone else gently move the shifter around after you get 
ready to get the new one hooked back up.

Did on my dc last weekend.
