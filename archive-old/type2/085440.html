Tried DuPont, no dice. They probably have a waterborne system they sell to

Sikkens only has the waterborne primer, no waterborne topcoats. This is
strange; with the environmental consciousness of Europeans, you'd think
they'd be first in line with one.

Same deal with PPG. I did find the PPG SX1060 primer-surfacer, which is
specifically designed to be applied with a roller.

waterborne topcoat. (Fun direction in their waterborne primer TDS: "Store in
sealed, lined, airtight cans to prevent mold growth." Mold on car paint?)
