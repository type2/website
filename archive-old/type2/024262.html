I have a 78 westy and had a similar problem. Mine was 
caused by a chaffed wire on the harness where the motor
plug is, it was fine when I was troubleshooting it with 
the emergency brake on in my driveway. As soon as it was
raining(on the highway, turn on the switch, pop goes 
the fuse.) So I found the wire the brake linkage was 
chaffing and it has been working fine since then. Hope 
this was a help.
