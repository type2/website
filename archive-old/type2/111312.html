The only thing you need to be concerned about when it
comes to oil is the weight, if it meets standards like
API SJ (basically every major oil manufacturer),
whether or not it contains detergents, if it is
synthetic or regular oil.  And that is it.

get that out there...  :-)  Have a good one.  Good
