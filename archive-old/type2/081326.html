> I am assuming you mean the starter spins but does not engage the flywheel?
> If yes, are you still using the 72 starter?
> If yes, it will not work. You 1st need to try a 76 and up starter. The
drive
> is longer. Up to the 2.0L, the teeth on the drive are at the sprague
> [over-running clutch]. On the 2.0L there is a 1/2" blank space between the
> teeth and the sprague. The teeth stick out further. This is also why you
> cannot use the SR17X self supporting starter on a 2.0L manual trans, but
you
> can on the auto trans.
> I would try the starter and if it did not fix it, then the other fix would
be
> a 210 or 215 flywheel.
