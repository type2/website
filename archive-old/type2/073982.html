Subject: Re: [T2] Red Oil Light

It could mean a number of things. Here's what I'd look
for:

1. A short in the circuit for the light
2. A bad oil-pressure sender
3. Low oil pressure

Obviously, you should hope for numbers 1 or 2, rather
than 3, as three could indicate the need for an engine
rebuild, or at the very least an oil pump.
