Subject: [T2] RE: Muffler on 71

The two pipe/peashooter muffler is indeed a bug muffler.

It has been said on this list that one should not use the bug muffler on a
bus due to bus aerodynamics resulting in exhaust being sucked into the
engine compartment by the tailwash of the bus.  This can cause fumes to
enter the heating system.  The bus muffler/pipe assembly routes the exhaust
to the side and thus out of the tailwash.

That said, I have been using bug mufflers on my 71 Westy for many years, and
have not noticed any fumes in the heat other than when I have had problems
with the muffler to heat exchanger connections.

One advantage of the bug 2 pipe muffler is supposedly more horespower due to
less backpressure, but I doubt the difference is significant.
