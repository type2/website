Ray, one of my customers makes Sandblasted Wooden Signs. He uses plain ol'
filter sand he buys by the truckload. His crew wear activated-carbon masks--

cheap chemical goggles. (On the goggles: chemical is the key point--those
don't have vent holes in the sides. Cheap is the word because they last
about a day, and they use motorcycle-racing tearoffs on them.)
