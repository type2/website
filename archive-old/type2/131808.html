This is the setup for the internal regulator type of altenator, you would 
eliminate the existing regulator. If you have the external regulator type of 
altenator then you have to do it this way:

>But if I do this, how do I incorporate the voltage regulator???
>The alternator has the D+, D- and DF designations.
>As for the regulator, it doesn't have these designations.  But the wires

These connections should be made between the regulator and the alt.:
Brown          = D-
Bluish/Green  = DF
Red(small)     = D+


This should be the same for both types of altenators:

Red(big)       = B+  This goes straight to the starter/battery. The two big 
wires you connected together.



>The wires I had hooked up for my generator's regulator were:

D+ at the regulator side. You might have to splice this in.
