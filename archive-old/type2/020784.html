Seth:
You are correct, you need a new fuel pump. You might be able to get justa
diphragm kit, which would be about half price. The hose is hooked to a vent on
the inside of the fuel pump so that when it fails, the gas runs out rather than
into the crankcase, contaminating the oil. It seems like that fuel pump takes a
bit of finagling to get it out either 6mm allen or 12 point bolts. The whole
carb setup is quite difficult to set up to get running correctly, but when it is
right it is really quite powerful and thrifty. Very little different than the FI
which superceded it. These dual carbs were used on all 2 liter air cooleds clear
up into the Vanagon era in Europe and Australia. The FI Might be a little more
trouble free, might have a little more power, but not much. FI is definitely
cleaner, smog-wise.
