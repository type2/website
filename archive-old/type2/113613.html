These are pretty decent strippers. Not very heavy for crimping though.

http://www.lowes_dot_com/lkn?action=productDetail&productId=77615-000065663-104CGV

The ones I stay away from are the ones made from 1/8" stamped steel, often 
black or chrome plated. Most often found here in connector kits where you 
get 500 connectors and these crimpers for $10. Beware... <grin>

I'm not a "tool snob" <grin> and I won't say you've got to buy the $50 
pliers but there is a significant difference in the cheapie pliers and a 
decent set. If we were professional mechanics or electricians we'd probably 
see the value of the $50 pliers but the cheaper ones will last a shadetree 
mechanic a lifetime. The cheapest pliers might make it through the weekend. 
Not that they'd break but that I'd throw them into the river out of 
frustration.

Hope this helps.
