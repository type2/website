What he said, plus the following:

When I redid my hold downs, I bought 3 of the black rubber replacements from
Bus Depot.  Turns out, the rubber parts are the same for all three hold
downs, it's the metal parts that are different.  Even on the side hold
downs, the metal parts of the new hold downs didn't quite fit.  So, I
drilled out the rivets that hold the metal parts to the rubber parts on the
new and old hold downs, and swapped stuff around so I had the old metal
parts on the new rubber parts, which I re-assembled using the appropriate
size screws and nuts.  Looks good, works good.
