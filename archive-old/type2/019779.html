I bought a replacement tray from one of the mail order parts places (WW, I
think).  It fit pretty well, but I had to trim it in places.  I tack welded it
in as many places as I could, then caulked it with silicone.  Then I coated it
top and bottom with Eastwood Battery tray coating, and it has held up really
well.
