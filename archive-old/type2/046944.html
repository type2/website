>How good is the reproduction rubber they sell _at_ the Bus Boys or any other source??  

Varies.  I also have gotten different quality rubber on different days from the same vendor.  The only really bad stuff I got though was from J.C. Whipme.  Avoid them for seals.

>Do I just push from the inside out?? Any "secret" techniques??

Sure.  No sharp movements (no hitting, tapping, shoving, etc.).  Use firm, steady pressure with outstretched palms to spread the force over a wide area.  Get someone to help you.
