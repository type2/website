Yes it is.  I understand these things.  I still maintain that my rear
brakes will not lock up before the fronts.  And despite what you say, it
has quite a lot to do with the amount of weight over each wheel.  You can't
figure any of the "physics" out without knowing the weight first.  The bus
has an IRS rear suspension (read lots of suspension travel) with a lot of
weight over it, even *behind* it.  The drums do not have nearly as much
stopping power as the discs.  That biases the braking towards the front
already.
