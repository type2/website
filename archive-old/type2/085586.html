Hi Steven,

Even after the hidden screws are pulled, the panel is often very hard to
budge. The edge of the panel is folded bback on itself and is wedged  into a
groove in the engine bay so that it resist coming out. Put on a thick pair
of leather gloves, grab one end and reef on it repeatedly until it lets go.
Lots of sharp, strong tugs and it will eventually come loose.
