On Sun, 14 Jan 2001, Santori Matthew wrote:

> Hello fellow listee's!
> I have a brief question. can 185/14 tires be put on a
> 1978 camper van? I currently have 195/14's on my bus,
> but a friend gave me 185/14's, and would like to put
> them on. Suggestions?

That's the stock size, if you haven't abbreviated any "/75"'s from the
designation.

This size has to be load range "C" or better.  Standard load tires don't
cut it!  Be sure that the load rating on the side of the tire is 1540
pounds or greater.
