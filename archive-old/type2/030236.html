Sounds like you fried your coil by using the "on" ignition position to
listen to the radio.  Teh VW has no "accessory" position so if your radio
is wired to the ignition switch you must run the engine to listen to the
radio.  Some wire the radio to an "always hot" source on the fuse block,
but that means that the radio will not go off when the ignition is turned
off.

In any case, try a new coil.  Even if the one you have is good, you should
keep a spare around.
