Marcus-
The secret trick to removing a FI connector is to
use a small screwdriver or dental probe to relieve
the "bail" wire. After many uses, these connectors
do just pull off, because they lose their "bite".
Many curious FI problems are caused by poor
wire connections, so be sure to pull on the connector
and not the wires.
