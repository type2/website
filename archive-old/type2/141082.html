I think this change occured around '72. Mounting holes are identical, but
one set of calipers is for thicker rotors and pads. Don't ask me which is
which, but you will easily see whether you have the correct one when you
have them side by side. Going by Martin's post, the early ones should be
Girling and late ones should be ATE. If that is the case, worn ATE pads will
fit Girling calipers, new ATE ones will not (too thick). Girling pads will
fit either caliper.
