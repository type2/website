Paul,

You are indeed very lucky -- if I read this, you make no mention of a fire
extinguisher.  US$10 investment that every home mechanic should have
on-board.  Get two, you never know when you can pull over and help someone
along side the road.

Fire trucks are great, but there are sure a lot of places -- especially on
the road -- where preparation makes all the difference.

P.S.  On your wire harness.  How far did the fire go?  Is the starter and
battery gone?  There really aren't that many wires that go to the rear
engine compartment on a type1 engine -- might be easiest just to do some
proper splices and extend the old ones.  Re-doing all the front wires is a
PITA, not worth it if it isn't required.
