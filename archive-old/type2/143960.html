Correct VW belts don't have teeth.  Best advice is to get the correct belt
and install it.  For a type 1 engine, the correct belt is a ContiTech
9.5x905

Your toothed belt might just have whipped itself
inverted, or it might have internal damage to its cords, which will cause
it to derail or fail.
