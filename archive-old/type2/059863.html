I, too have found that my steering wheel doesn't line up properly
after steering box replacement.  Nothing I did could make it line up
either.  If I moved the arm one spline either way, the box ran out of
worm gear before the wheels were fully turned to the lock.  As you
noted, the coupler only goes on one way.

My feeling is that the boxes vary in their steering wheel position and
that the normal thing to do when changing boxes is to reposition the
steering wheel to match.  Unfortunately, this is another "special
puller" job if you have an early bay.
