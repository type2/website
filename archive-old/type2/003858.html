Yep, the brakes are not self-adjusting.  You have to adjust them every so
often.  How often depends on how much you drive your Bus.  The more quickly
you wear down the brake shoes, the more often you will have to adjust them.

When you adjust the rear brakes the parking brake adjustment will be back to
normal.
