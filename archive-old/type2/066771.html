In September issue of VOLKSWORLD they did a 'how to' article on making and 
fitting a large bore exhaust to a Bettle. To prevent it rusting they painted it

I don't know where you would get this...

a brand new box, AFTER removing all the primer (other wise the primer will burn 
taking the outer paint with it).

the name and address that I got mine from. They do them for 1.6 and 1.7 to 2.0 
engines. Cost for my 1.6 was 100 UK pounds ($150 US) plus sales tax and 
carriage.
