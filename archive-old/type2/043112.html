When I was removing the bushings from my sway bar, I found that a
Vise-Grip Model 12LC worked perfectly for compressing the bushing so I
could remove the clamp.  A picture of this model can be seen at:
http://www.vise-griptool_dot_com/pi/toolSrch.asp?itemId=12&autoBrand=17&autoProduct=5
This pup will open to 3-1/8" (79mm), so it spans the bushing housing
nicely.
