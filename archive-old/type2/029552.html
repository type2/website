You have a type2 body and a type4 engine, true.  The reason for the
reference to type4 is that '68-'71 buses came with the type1 engine and
buses in Europe came with the type1 engine until '79.  It is good practice
to mention that you have a type4 (usually abreviated to T4) engine even
if you post with the year because there are a lot of people from across
the pond on the list.  Also, at this late date, there are a fair number of
T1-engined '72-'79 buses here in the States (bastard basket cases usually
