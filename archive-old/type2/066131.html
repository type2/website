Scott,

Same thing happened to one of our vans a couple of years ago when our
daughter fought with the gearshift and got it stuck in fourth. The
thing NOT to do is try to force the tranny into gear, because you risk
breaking the bracket. 

Check the linkage first, but it really sounds like you broke the $15
bracket (available from the dealer only) that is located just inside
the nose of the tranny. The nose is on the front (FRONT is FRONT). It
is really fairly simple to replace, and if you are careful and use a
modest amount of Permatex 2, you won't have to replace the gasket,
though the gasket doesn't cost much.

Good luck.
