Chris, are you sure the outer fly wheel was worn from the seal?  That is odd
(maybe not).  Anyway, the rubber o-ring problem you mentioned:  I had the
same issue.  I looked inside the fly wheel and saw no space to put the
o-ring so I assumed I had a "special" fly wheel that did not need it.
Needless to say, the motor leaked at the rear mail seal.  In fact, that is
the ONLY place it leaks (rare for a vw).  Well, I pulled the motor again,
tore off the fly wheel and upon closer examination, THERE IS A RUBBER O-RING
IN THERE!  It was so old and used that it turned the same color as the
flywheel and was flush with the groove.  If you feel in the recessed area
with a very small screwdriver or your fingernail, you will be able to tell
that it is softer that the metal.  I jabbed it with a screwdriver and it
came right out.  I pulled an all nighter last night to replace my fuel tank
sender and thought I would take another look at the fly wheel while it was
out.  I am glad I did.  Who knows, I might have one of the only leak free
VWs for a few months.  Hope this helps.  Check it again!
