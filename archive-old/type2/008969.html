1. Mask off the splines.  You don't want paint on the splines!

2. Prime the shafts with suitable primer

3. Paint the shafts with the desired color.  Stock was black, but let
your inhibitions run wild!  Allow the paint to dry.

4. Determine how much your wife/SO likes VWs.

4A. If your wife/SO loves VWs, walk into the kitchen and put the axle
shafts into the oven.  Bake at 250F for an hour or so.

4B. If your wife/SO isn't fond of VWs, consider replacement of
wife/SO.  In the meantime, sneak the axles into the kitchen and place
in oven at 250F for an hour or so while wife/SO is out.  Make sure to
turn on the vent fan!

5. Allow axle shaft to cool and remove from oven.

Done!
