The first time I removed an oil seal from a 2.0L engine I used a small 
claw hammer that happened to be around.  Put the claw behind the oil 
seal, braced the head on the crank (maybe padded with a thin piece of 
wood) and levered the seal right out.

They have oil seal pullers that are thin metal tools, with a head with 
2 claws of different sizes.  Around $7 or $8 at most FLAPS.
