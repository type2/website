> If you have much of a ridge, expect the piston to cylinder clearance to be
> excessive. If it is out of spec, new rings won't last long. Replace pistons
> and cylinders. A spezial tool is used to remove the ridge on motors that
> are designed for this type of rebuilding (Not VW AC). It is called a ridge

> the cylinder you will have honed a big taper into it. Just can't be
> expected to work. Measure the clearance between the piston and cylinder at
> the top just under the ridge. Compare to spec. Before you waste time/money
> with overhauling used cylinders.
