I, and others on the list, have had this happen - several while driving. If it
has cracked then its probably very rusty inside and close to failure. This
pending failure is life threatening!

I don't wish to be a bearer of gloom and doom - but the best advice is to keep
your bus off the road until you have fitted a replacement front beam. 
On replacement I found several holes on the inner faces of the shock absorber
towers too - next to the chassis where they are covered up.
