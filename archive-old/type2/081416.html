I normally chill on the digest, but I just fixed this exact same
problem. On mine it was the linkage from the steering box back to the
beam. Everything seemed pretty tight at first, but with a bit of a pull,
the link tie rod end would pop (or "click") and then had an enormous
amount of play. My bus was almost undrivable becase it wandered so much,
It had almost 30 degree's of play on the wheel before the steering
acivated. Now, with that one link and ends replaced the difference is
collosal. I'd say this is worth a second, more violent check. Remember
how heavy your bus is on those joints. It may not appear with a mere
mortal trying to wiggle them, but the force of your bus could do it. I
thin a new lnk with ends runs about $20 canadian. Eh.
