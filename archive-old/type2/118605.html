My rebuilt transmission was acting (hard to shift into first gear when
stopped) up so I switched my tranny oil over to "Redline" brand synthetic
MT-90.  It made a significant difference for my transmission but the oil

show.  It still isn't 100% but it is certainly more drivable now.  I wish
that I had used it from the start.
