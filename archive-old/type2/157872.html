Do this prior to getting nervous.
drop the trans pan and look how much metalic shavings are in the bottom
of the pan.

If there is a pile of metalic particles--you've smoked the clutches.

If it's not too bad, take out the filter and clean out the filter
screen, clean out the bottom of the pan --put it all back together and
filthe trans up with Ford Type F.
More grabber's for the clutches, and she might be good to go.
