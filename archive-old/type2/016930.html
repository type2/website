Gotta jump in on this one guys...been there, done that.....I drove the
rockies in 85 when I moved to Vancouver for a short time from Ontario....71
bus had a rebuilt 1600 engine, mild cam, deep sump & single quiet....ran
like a top thanks very much...did the speed limit & then some all the
way...& the bus had everything I owned in it including a spare engine in a
crate & my dog.....was a great trip til I went to park in Burnaby, and the
steering knuckle sheered off the front axle....

OK, I had to go to third a few times, but I passed a bunch of trucks in the
process & waved a lot...sometimes employed the famous Trudeau salute on
occasion <GRIN>.

Lay back & enjoy the trip...just be sure the brakes are in good shape...and
the tires too.
