These campers are not campers as much as like the current EV MV or 
Multivan.  Not quite those either.  Interior is a table with facing bench 
seats.  There are two slightly different versions of the silver 
"camper" - one has "more" of a raised roof(34C) than the later (34D).  I've 
had all three over time.  Earlier there was the panel van (34A).
        http://www.rio_dot_com/~arne/34.jpg  - for a picture of all four
        http://www.rio_dot_com/~arne/models.html

There was 1600 fast back Type 3 and a 1200 Saloon Beetle back in these 
days before "SuperFast" lowfriction wheels - like HotWheels.  Had both of 
these.

Don't know what VWs they've done as Superfast...

If I'm lucky I'll manage to find all of them in the box I left them in at 
my Dad's house years ago when I went to college...
