A quick check of the archives reveals that the
European method of measuring octane is different than
in the U.S., so the 91-RON rating on the inside of the
fuel cap is meaningless unless converted to U.S.
standards.

between the 3. I'll run regular and pocket the change
for my next motor, thank you very much;-)
