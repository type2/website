It worked it's way loose. Those 6mm studs strip easy and so one shouldn't 
overtighten them. Engines live and 
die this way all the time. See the Idiot book for
"Stick's personal torque meter" Basically, Stick tightens those nuts until he 
see's a tendon move slightly in his wrist.
Try it, it works.
