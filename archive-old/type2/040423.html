The problem here is that those two adjustments are effective, but only for
what they do.  They cannot compensate for a bum head temp sensor, cold
start valve, or aux air valve.  Any of these could be causing your problem
and all the idle air and speed adjustment in the world will not cure it.
To adjust the AFM air screw, you really need an EGA.  The post today from
Chuck Meanley was an unbelievable low price for one of these pups...
usually they are a grand or more, even used and broke!  I own a DIY
version and it works "OK" but it is surely not the real thing :-(

Chuck, BTW, if no one has snarfed that pup, I am very interested!!! please
get with me and we will work out details!!!
