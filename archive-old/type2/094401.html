Subject: Re: [T2] how hot?

Holes burned in pistons are usually from timing making the spark fire too
early where the engine starts to "ping".  Extremely hot heads may cause the
valve seats to come loose.

Why are your heads running hot?  Are you not driving your air cooled engine
correctly?  Driving technique and incorrect timing seems to be the major
cause of overheated heads.

I pull a 1300 lb Scamp with my 2L'76 westy up mountain passes with an engine
that is getting close to 180,000 miles and no overheating on the heads.  Oil
will run 260 with 40 pounds pressure when we were close to 10,000 feet.
