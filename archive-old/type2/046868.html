The animation at the link below shows a cutaway of a piston/rod/crank/valvetrain in an overhead cam engine. In a VW engine of course there would be pushrods and lifters between the cam and the rockers, but the 4 cycles of the 4-cycle internal combustion engine are demonstrated quite nicely.
