I "built" a pressure bleeder using:

Bus windshield washer bottle
  (the owners manual says the bottle can take up to 40PSI) Brake reservoir
cap (bus depot $0.42) Tubing (home depot $2.00) Brass nipple (home depot
$2.00)

And a bike pump with pressure guage
(connects to the bottles inflation valve)

with pressure gauge.

so I could change out the cap end, so that it
