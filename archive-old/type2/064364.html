You're right.  They don't make the Michelin XZX any more.

My tire guy says the replacement is Michelin LTX 195/75-R14.  It's a little
wider and has an all-season tread.  I got a set of these last month and I
really love them.  They're wonderfully quiet, really improve the handling
of the van, and costed a small bundle.  But heck, tires are critical.

If, on the other hand, you just need one tire bedcause of the blowout I
don't think it would be wise to mix these LTX tires with your old XZX ones.
