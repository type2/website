Subject: Re: [T2] single vs. dual port

I just rebuilt my '70 engine, which had single port heads on it.  I too, 
wondered if I should convert to dual port, and my machinist & mechanic, whom 
I trust very much (over 30 yrs of VW exp.), said what you have heard -- 
single ports tend to be more reliable, and sometimes the dual port heads 
have a tendency to crack bet/ the plug holes.  As for horses -- I wondered 
the same (I too carry a lot of gear) my machinist said that a new carb & 
pistons and cylinders would give me what I needed (mine were ancient), and 
that buying new heads and manifold would not be worth the cost for the few 
extra horses.  I stayed with the single port.  My engine goes in today, so 
we'll see.
