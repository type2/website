Your present generator or alternator will be sufficient, as you are
not going to be pulling the 800W continuously.

You can add a second battery for those extended high-decibel sessions,
 All you need is an isolation relay that is available at most any RV
store.  Don't let them talk you into a solid state isolator as these
can be more trouble than they are worth.  Run the stereo from the
isolated battery.
