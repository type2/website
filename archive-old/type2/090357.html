Chris-
I'm reading the list mail out of order so you may have solved the problem 
by now, but I'll tell you what I think I remember anyway.
I have a 72 westy that had the same problem with the brake light going on 
only when my foot was on the brake. As I recall the problem was that
water had gotten into the rubber boot on one or both of the brake
preasure switches on the master cylinder. I think the light goes on when
there is a different reading from each switch. Anyway, I dried and
cleaned the switch connectors and sprayed some wd40 or maybe PB Blaster
on the connections. That fixed it. I also hear that the switches tend to
fail after a while. I bought some but never put them in.
