Hi Bart,

Here is a solution that sounds hokey but it has worked for me. Don't 
worry about running the cable inside the tube - run it along side the 
tube and attach it to the tube with nylon cable ties that you can get at 
a flaps. Use a cable tie about every foot along the length of the tube. 
This setup has worked for me for at least 10 years. The ties might get 
brittle every 5 years or so but they are easy to replace.
