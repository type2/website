Just went thru the same thing with my instrument panel removal.  First I removed
the radio to give me access to the littl white plastic pins.  Then used a
7/32 socket to push on the pins so I could compress the 4 split pieces at
the same time and the pins would pop out far enought for me to pry them out
with a screwdriver.  The socket has to be large enough to compress the 4
split ends, and small enough to still push the pin out after it has
compressed the ends.  
Took about an hour of pushing, sweating, and saying nasty words, but I got them
out without breaking any of them.
