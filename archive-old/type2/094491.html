Richard says:
 
>I measured my new clamps against the originals I took off but I haven't put them on yet.  Where did you have difficulty, at the injectors?

Yes, at the injectors, with the 7.5 high pressure benz hoze and MB clamp, the fit is tight. I sheared of the injector mounting stud trying to push the hose on the injector. The rest I will do by removing the injector and pushing hose on in my hand.

The fit over the rest of the fuel rail has been snug, but not difficult. I have also sanded the accumulated funk off the rails to make them shiny.
