I agree. Where I live we have a saying: Förstör inte vänskap med pengar!
(For those of you who don't read swedish well: Don't destroy friendship
with money!) 

The list works best if we don't mix money into it, wherever there is some
kind of "free money" involved there will be people who will go for it. We
have no controll of who all listees are. Someone might just pull their
broken bus out  from his backyard to somewhere on the road, then send an
"emergency" mail to collect money or parts to fix his bus, unsubscribe from
the list and live happily ever after.
 
It would also be interesting to see if I could get funds to fix my engine
when I'm busted and broke somewhere between Kaaresuvanto and Kilpisjärvi.
Could I take the list to court if I did not recive any help? And in wich
country?

It's OK to collect money to support the admins and other people who are
keeping this resource in this great shape. With a "Road service fund" we
will get the discussions of "who paid this and that much" or "he never paid
anything, he should not have had any help". That would be more boring than
any FART seen this far.

Let's enjoy our busses and the list untouched by echonomic interference.
