Doyt mentions frontal area as a concern. I don't believe that is really 
the biggist issue. Your bus has *most* of that FA covered (engi9ne has 
to push the FA of the bus anyhow) and airflow over the bus will divert 
over the trailer, meaning that you might add 20% (just a guess) to the 
rig's FA.

However, braking is a real problem; doubling the weight of the rig, even 
with electric brakes on the trailer, means you are driving a juggernaut. 
At most, you ought to be looking at teardrop trailers.
