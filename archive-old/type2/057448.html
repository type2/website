Tom,

The A/F mixture that you care about is not set by turning that little
screw.  The screw sets the idle mixture.  The "main" mixture is determined
by the size of the venturi, main jet, and air correction jet.  Check your
main jet and make sure it is the correct size -  I don't know what that is
for your bus, I only know the dual Solexes in my '73.
