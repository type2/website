Subject: Swing to IRS Swap!

Hi all,
        I am new to this service, and I think it's great!!11

My question to pose to all of you is this-

I want to switch my '67 bus swing axle to IRS.  BUT, I DO NOT want it 
lowered AND I want to use newer bus parts, not a mismash of bug parts.  I 
think this is possible, but I haven't tried a dry run on it, because I am 
trying to get opinions first.

Here is my plan:

Use of a standard type I IRS transaxle, with larger Flanges (say from a 
Thing) for the CV's.  Probably have to get CV axles made to correct length. 
Use of CV housing and assembly from a '68 and later type two.  I know I 
will have to modify or fabricate the trailing arms.

What do you think?

Anyone try this?

I am open to any suggestions.
