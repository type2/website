Man, you guys are brutal. It's pretty ironic that you as a Bus owner would
critisize a Fiero for catching on Fire. It was ONLY the 1st year Fiero that

block after 30 some odd thousand miles causing oil to spill on the hot
catalytic converter. This was solved the following year and was never an
issue for later Fieros.

during the 80s. People always tend to exaggerate things that they know
nothing about. I can't tell you how many people I've had tell me that the
Fiero is dangerous because it flips over when you go fast since the engine
is in the back. People who have never owned one, or even know anyone that
knows anything about them tell me this??? Do they think they're doing me a
service by warning me? Or maybe they're trying to show me their lack of
knowledge. There has never been a single incident in history of a Fiero
flipping over when it goes too fast. Maybe they can tell me that my car's
about to flip over when it's doing 130+ miles an hour down a deserted
highway. I get all kinds of stories from people who have never owned one, or
