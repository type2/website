Follow steps thus:

Verify rest voltage of battery. If not at least 12.5volts, charge it. If it 
won't hold, replace it.
Verify battery case and cables are clean and tight, both ends. Same for the 
ground strap at the front of the trans.
Verify 12 volt feeds to the double relay. See the fuel injection section in 
the Bentley.
Verify 12v at the fuel pump.

Do that much then let me know what's up. Your issue could be any of many 
things and we must rule them out one by one to find the culprit.
