Subject: RE: [T2] ECU faulty?

Sounds like you verified 12V power to the fuel injectors. Did you check them
all? Did you use an ohmmeter to see if the ground connections to to
injectors are actually being grounded when the engine cranks?

I recall you checked the ECU ground to the case...how about the case/tranny
ground to the chassis?

Also check continuity from the big ECU plug on the FI harness to the
injector connectors (all of them), and to the three case grounds. Bentley
should tell you which pins go where.

Still sounds like a ground problem somewhere. I'd check everything else
again carefully before buying an ECU ($$$)
