I don't get the "wicked haircut" reference, but if you're talking about 
things making it easier to poop while on VW bus trips, the answer is to 
get yourself a quality porta-potti.

Christa and I have a Thetford porta-potti which we keep in a box behind 
the passenger seat.  I built the box specifically for the purpose, and it 
not only holds the porta-potti but has compartments for toilet paper, 
porta-potti chemical, a toilet paper dispenser just like home, a place 
for books and magazines, and storage for about a grillion maps.  A lid 
pops down over the whole thing so one can sit on the box while making 
dinner at the westy stove--it's just the right height.  Total footprint 
for the box?  Maybe 30" wide (it doesn't project into the passageway to 
the front at all) by perhaps 16" deep.  And no, kids, no smell at all.

Absolutely essential for the kind of back-country exploring we like to do.
