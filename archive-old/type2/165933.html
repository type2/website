It might be nice to pull the plugs and squirt a little
oil in the cylinders - turn the engine by hand with
the plugs out - the first turn, then spin it with the
starter for a moment... put plugs back in and fire it
up. Warm it up, don't worry about the lifters making
noise, drive around the block a few times, then drain
and change oil, filter. -BaH
