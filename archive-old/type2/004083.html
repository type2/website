I don't think so. First, there is no evidence, at all, that the front has
been changed out. And the fact that the column and bracket match other '69s
leads me to believe these are right. The holes through the bracket that are
supposed to match up with the dash  - don't.

So, given this process of elimination (and the fact that it was kluged
together with a block of wood and some bolts when I got it) lead me to
believe that we have a non-69 dash. If the front were changed out, it might
change the forward/back location of the holes, but the holes in the bracket
are too close together side to side to match the holes in the dash. About
3/4" off on either side.

Granted, I have no clue as to WHY someone would have changed out the dash,
but there it is...
