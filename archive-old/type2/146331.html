Harbor Freight has a 12 volt solar charger setup that is $249.  It is
constantly in their magazines that is mailed to me, but, I just tried to
find it online and was not successful, hopefully someone else will give
the part number.

controller and what appears to be some compact flourescent bulbs fixtures.
