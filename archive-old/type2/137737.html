Hi Don,

Gerald sounds right on. If nothing he suggested checks out, I'd say
you have a dangerous wiring short situation that could cause a fire if
not found. With your battery discharging so fast, you may already have
melted wire insulation that needs to be found.

I don't remember what year your bus is, but on earlier models, you
could remove the hot wire from the coil and run a direct jump from
battery to coil positive and drive temporarily on that. Don't think
you can get away with it on fuel injected models.
