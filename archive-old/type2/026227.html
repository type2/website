It sounds like you have an early (62-3) Type 3 engine which would make it a
1500.  If you use it in the bus I would suggest changing it over to dual
carbs (64-6) or even dual port w/carbs(67).  I could supply you with engine
tin & exhaust parts if you decide to go that route.  What you will need, in
any case, is a way to direct the cooling air into the fan housing at the
back of the engine (rear of the vehicle).  In a Type 3 there is a cavity in
the body coming from the vents and across the whole rear end of the car and
then a large rubber boot directing it into the fan.
