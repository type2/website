> Leif,
> 
> Swap a 1968-70 bell housing onto the 1974 tranny, cut 3/8" off the
input 
> shaft, and swap the studs & front mount from the 1970 tranny to the
nose 
> cone of the 1974 tranny.

I did this recently and I kept the 76 bell housing but swapped the
input shafts. The newer trans has the "ears" on it but they do not hit
or interfer with anything. One thing to keep in mind is that the final
drive ratio may differ. I found that I cruise at a lower RPM but have
a little more trouble climbing hills. Good Luck
