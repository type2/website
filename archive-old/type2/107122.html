Hi Volks,

Here's an update on BusFusion 2003;  June 12th - 15th in Almonte
Ontario Canada

BusFusion 2003 is fast approaching so we wanted to inform you of recent
updates to the BusFusion website at
http://members.aol_dot_com/BusFusion/bfhome.htm. 
      
The new T-shirt design has been chosen and is now shown on the "T-Shirt
& Dash Plaque Logo" page.  Hope you like it as much as we do. 
Congratulations go out to the designer, Mandy Alves.  The "Sponsors
2003 & Door Prize" page has been updated with news of this years
sponsors to date.  If you or someone you know wishes to be a sponsor or
donor of a door prize please contact us !

Among some of the activities that you can participate in this year will
be the BusFusion VW Bus Painting, the BusFusion Art Show & Sale and the
BusFusion Flea Market.  So get prepared to participate !

New for this year, we will be collecting the camping fees and are
giving discounts for those who pre-register.  See the "Registration
Form" for details.  The deadline for pre-registration is May 26, so get
your registration in before then and save.

Stay tuned for more news as plans develop and let's make BusFusion 2003
another huge success.
