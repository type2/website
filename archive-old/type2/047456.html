List,

Could the alternator be the problem? My fan shroud
routes a channel of air to
cool the internals of the alternator. My guess is
that if this gets hot... then you might
not be providing the bus with the necessary
"running" voltage.


I had similar problems:
Although the ground wire from the Ignition Module
was the problem.

I would check to see where yours is grounded.
Its a larger brown wire that should be grounded to
the metal frame.
Not the gas tank cover.

I'd get a new stock Coil, Distributor Cap and
Rotor from a VW dealer.
It's a fairly inexpensive repair. [See type2_dot_com
for the part numbers.]

If that's not it. I'd question the internals of
the distributor itself.
There are specially lubricated contacts that we're
required rebuild on my car.

Tools.. yea. get as many as you can afford.
