FWIW my Weber and Sunbeam portable grills had leaky valves (and the bottle 
would empty itself slowly over a period of days) but my Mr. Buddy heater

the tank valve. Much more economical as locally they get $10 to fill a 
large tank (20 lbs?) and less running around for new tanks.
