Before Fiddling with the accelerator pumps, check the numbers written on the 
side of your distributor. Write them down and compare them to the numbers on 
the tune up 
page of the Rocky Mountain Motorworks catalog. Make sure you know exactly 
which distributor you have, then you'll know for sure where the timing should 
be (before or after Tdc, and how far, also make sure from the same page that 
you bought the right tune up parts for the distributor you have) Then put the 
timing in the right spot, then test drive again. If the problem persists, 
then look to fuel delivery and accel pump.
