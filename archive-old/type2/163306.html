I have exclusively used Crane systems for the past 30+ years (back into the
days when they were known as the Allison) on any VW originally equipped with
points (and one Toyota PU too).  Installation is easy and straight forward.

happy with their lack of reliability, so I've stuck with the Crane,
something that works and works very well.
