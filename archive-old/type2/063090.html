Your gas mask idea is good, but you need to attach a vacuum cleaner running
backwards to the fresh air end of your hose, otherwise you will just be
rebreathing the first couple of feet of air in your hose.  Or, if there are
valves in the system you will be working too hard just to breathe; let the
vacuum cleaner supply the air.  A good 3/4inch wqter hose will supply plenty
of air from a vacuum cleaner.  Used firefighting breathing apparatus will do
well, too, -- for the masks and such.  There may be places near you that
stock the used stuff, cuz the fire depts have to replace it on a schedule
regardless of whether it is working OK or not.
