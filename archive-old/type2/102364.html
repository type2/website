I have the 1st Edition Volkswagen Type 2 Repair Manual. NOT by Bentley, this is the real VW factory manual and
it covers 74, 75, and 76 USA buses.
I was looking for a vacuum diagram for the dual vacuum unit distributor to see where retard gets its signal
but I didn't find any. 79 Cal gets it from the rear of the throttle housing by the idle speed screw.
But what I did find was rather interesting.
In the section of 'ignition system', pages 28.2 and 28.3, are specs on the distributors for the ED 1800 and
the GD 2.0.  Towards the bottom of 2.0 page is "Speed limiter in control unit cut-in speed" and it says 5400
rpm!!!
For the 1800, it says "Speed limiter* cut-in speed" and the asterick says "For California: speed limiter in
control unit". ED speed limit is also 5400 rpm. [1974 Cal 1800 w/auto trans came with EFI, all other 74's came
with dual carbs]
By 'control unit', I am assuming the EFI ECU?
I searched the 68-79 Bentley and found zero reference to this.
