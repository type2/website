Brian:
Bleach kills mold and mildew.  You can mix it with quite a bit of water maybe a
cup to a gallon of water. You may be able to take the seats out and put them
upside down so that gravity will draw the bleach into the cushions. be careful
you don't put so much on that it comes through and discolors the vinyl.
Just sitting outside in the sunshine and wind will kill the mildew too, but keep
the rain off them. I'd not leave them in the sun for more that a couple of weeks
cause the UV is pretty hard on the sructure of the vinyl.
