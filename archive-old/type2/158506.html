Yes, a 195 is bigger than a 185 IF you do not consider

the middle aspect ratio number.

The 195 is the width of the tread in millimeters.

The aspect ratio number is the width of the sidewall
in a percent figure. 

The correct 185R14 tire has no middle aspect number.
Therefore it is a 82. 80% of 185mm is 5.8 inches with
a diameter of 25.7 inches.

The 195/75R14 is still 5.8 inches of sidewall but the
diameter is smaller: 25.5 inches. 

Go here: http://www.miata_dot_net/garage/tirecalc.html
and type in 185 80 14 on the left and 195 75 14 on the
right and you will see the difference.
