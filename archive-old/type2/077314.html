A 1970 used a different tranny mounting and a 1600 engine.......the mounting
setup is completely different.  Rear apron of bus is removed, and you do not
need to jack up the bus to pull the motor!  Moustache bar with motor mounts
on the end that goes to the frame.

In your case the mounting between a 2liter and your 1700 is exactly the
same........Longer moustache bars with mounts attached near center.
