Keep in mind that bus engines typically run rich vs. stoichiometric  
air fuel ratios. Those inexpensive O2 sensors are very binary by  
design. They can tell you is if you've suddenly leaned out (e.g.  
17:1) but you'll find that the engine will run poorly and the gauge  
will simply confirm it.
