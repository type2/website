My experience (71 westy, stock 1600 DP, well broken in) has been:

Around town:  Oil temp - 180, CHT - 275

Highway, level: Oil temp - 200, CHT - 300

Highway, uphill, wind, etc.: Oil temp - 210, CHT - 325

Many other listees have reported higher than normal temperatures during
breakin.  My engine was well broekn in before I installed the gauges, so I
have no experience with that.  Also, many listees have reported type 4
engine temperatures significantly higher than yours (like, normal oil temp =
260), so based on that, yours is lookin' pretty good.
