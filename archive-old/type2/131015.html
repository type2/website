Well Id have to suggest what I did after finding it on the type2_dot_com page.
20$ digital cooking thermometer (with audible alarm at any set
temp)...couple of lengthened wires, bit of extra copper tubing from any
auto/hardware store, a little JB Weld.  Put'em all together, replace
dipstick and you have the single cheapest digital oil temp gauge ever.

section I think.

instead of just replacing the drain plug, but Im not the one to be specific
on that.
