If you do things properly in a DYI rebuild, you're *not* going to save
any money vs. buying a longblock from a reputable vendor.  So, rebuild
your own engine if you want the "experience", rebuild your own engine
if you want a *better* rebuild than a most purchased longblocks (i.e.,
you're going to balance rods and pistons, equalize deck heights, etc.)
but don't rebuild it because you think you're going to do it cheaper.
