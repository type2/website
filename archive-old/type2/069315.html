With regard to preventing battery shorts and to enable quick disconnects
we have on sale in the UK quick release connectors. Instead of a bolt on 
connector they work by a lever clamping action. 

The lever is integral with a plastic housing (one red, one blue) that 
covers and insulates the terminal completely. It also has a half way 
setting to allow anything to be connected by crocodile clips (jump leads, 
inspection lamp etc).

In the event of a fire these are very easy to disconnect. They are also 
sufficiently easy to give you no excuse for not disconnecting the battery
while you do that electrical job.

The cost over here is about 10 UK pounds ($15 US) for a pair - you will 
be more likely to find them in a caravan center than an auto-shop.
