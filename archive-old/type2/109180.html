>>There is more to this than simple stock-original-naziness.  There is a
fundamental difference in the way the bus alternator works vs. almost every
other alternator out there.  The bus alternator is fed cool air through a
duct directly into the middle of the alternator.  Other alternators rely on
recirculating the (presumably cool) air that surrounds the alternator in a
water-cooled car who's engine is open to the atmosphere.  If the AC Delco
alternator was mounted in the stock bus location it would suck super-hot air
in from around the heads and exhaust and fry itself very quickly because it
is NOT DESIGNED to work in such a hot, confined space.
