The dryer is the cylindr(most likely in the driver's side front wheel 
well.  Should be availabel from almost any FLAPS.  You probably have a 
hole in the body righ over where it is mounted as there is a glass "view 
port" in the top of it so that the installer could watch for when all of

Evaporator is what is inside the bus and gets cold(hopefully).

Compressor pumps the freon around the loop.
