Hi Eric,
It sounds to me like you have a '73 head, ('72 didn't have them and the
head changed in '74). Those two holes, I believe, are part of the air
injection system used in conjunction with a smog pump set-up. What
should be in each hole is a threaded tube. On mine I've just welded the
ends closed as I no longer have a smog pump. Perhaps if you go to the
wrecker you can find a pair of these tubes to use on your head.
