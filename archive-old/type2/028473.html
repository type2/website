Importance: Normal

Clear a path to the rear, brake lever off, clutch depressed, Bus in reverse,
rev up the motor to 2500 RPM, dump the clutch.  Brutal but usually
effective.  Sometimes takes more than one try. If it's reeeeeeely stuck and
this doesn't work, try spraying the shoes with BrakeKleen or the like.
Getting the shoes wet can sometimes loosen the grip of improper storage.
Repeat brutal force method.  I know, I'm an animal...
