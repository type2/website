You can be pretty sure that the pump reading is accurate, as they are
usually calibrated by a weights and measures agency.  As with all
measurements, your accuracy goes up with increased quantity, so go most of
the way through the tank.
 
> 2) Is my odometer reading true? I don't think so. I am currently running 14/185/70 which is undersized in comparison to the original tires to which I assume the odometer/speedometer is calculated. I do not know how to correct for tire size. Is there a simple factor I can plug in? 

You can be pretty sure that your odometer reading is _inaccurate_!

To calibrate your odometer, you need a 10-mile stretch of road that has
mile markers.  Note your odometer reading, drive the 10 mile stretch.
Note the odometer reading again and subtract the two readings.  Take the
difference and divide 10 by it.

This is your multiplier.  Simply multiply the distance driven on your
odometer by the multiplier to get true distance.
