Indian Head gasket goop. It is a thin dark colored "glue" that an old 
mechanic I knew swore by. You can still get it - different brands and names.

It acts like a sealant and a light glue and I have taken to applying it to 
everything including the seating surface of crank and shaft seals.

Can't hurt and I think (THINK) it keeps my motors a little bit drier. You 
can also apply it to studs and such...
