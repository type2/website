I had the bumpers from my CE restored last year.  By restored, I mean they 
look better than new now.

They removed the big bend/crunches in the rear one, plug welded a couple of 
thin spots, painted the inside, and rechromed the outsides.  I was amazed 
at the quality and straightness of them as I was sure they would reject the 
rear one as "Unrepairable". The chrome looks "deep", much more sheen than 
new, I believe.

I paid $550 for the pair. They quoted this sight unseen based on the year 
and model, so I believe it is a flat rate for these and not per hour.

I dropped them off locally (Madison, wi) and they were shipped from there 
to Minneapolis.  Took 5 weeks.
