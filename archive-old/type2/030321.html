To my knowledge, the function of the flaps is to:

1. ensure quick warmup in all temperatures (even Havasu in summer!)
2. regulate engine temp in outside air temps below 60F or so.

The first is a no-brainer.  The second conclusion I came to after gluing
little pointers to the flap shafts so that I could observe their position.
They seem to go wide open in temperatures above 60F, but they modulate the
flow in temps below that.

Even warm-up?  No data.
