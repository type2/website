I don't know about American electrical regs, but in the UK the guidelines
are that there is a maximum length of cable from the external hook-up point
to the ELCB/distribution point; I think its around 18" or 2'. This will
dictate where the socket roughly goes, then its a case of finding somewhere
conventiently flat and unobstructed at the front and rear. Obviously putting
it on the same side as the sliding door would be problematical as the door,
when opened, would interfere. But apart from that you've a free choice.
