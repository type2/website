They aren't hard to make.  I made mine out of 1/4 masonite with a 1X2
strip along the edge to stiffen the shelf.  The rear edge is curved to
match the sheet metal (made a pattern from cardboard by trial-and-error).
Covered the whole thing with 1/8 foam and black naugahyde.  

It is mounted
with four "hangers" made from 1/8 steel wire, the front (FIF) two hung
from
screws on the air duct, the inboard rear hung from the E-brake bracket,
and the outboard rear simply hooked to the lower lip of the dashboard.

Works great, and you can customize it to your needs.
