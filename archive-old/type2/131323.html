Ok, I paid way too much for this basket case. $700 and $200 in registration. 
See pics at the link in my sig.

engine compartment far enough to try to read the numbers.

I timed it when it was running. I read the Bently on adjusting the carbs.

It seems the more I do to it, the worse it gets. I can't get it to idle at 
all now. And when I run it up in RPMs it backfires constantly.

It's still got serious wiring problems. Hi beams = left headlight. Low beams 
= right headlight. Wires that are corroded, high resistance, or just don't 
seem to be connected to anything.

Sounds like a sledgehammer hitting the engine repeatedly. Great. Busted 
connecting rod? Who knows.

Spark plugs were sooty (probably due to me dinking with the carbs), but 
otherwise ok.

gave up. Tomorrow's street sweeping and the bus will get a ticket. I don't
