Gesell <trgesell_at_compuserve_dot_com> wrote: 
Sorry to say, but if the brake drum splines actually stripped, I'd have
trouble believing that the stub axle splines could be in decent shape. You
can inspect them of course, look for any missing chunks or bent, worn,
asymetrical splines. Other than splines, i'd think the stub axle is pretty
bullet proof. Obviously be prepared to replace the bearings. I had to beat
my stub axle pretty firmly with a plastic mallet to get it out and back in
the bearing housing; tight fit on the inner bearing race. I think that's
normal.
