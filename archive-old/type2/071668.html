I installed these on my '74 Westy, but it originally had dual carbs, so fuel 
injection replacement is likely different.

The threaded holes in the base of your manifold are for hooking up your 
vacuum brake hoses.  You can also connect the retard side from your dual 
vacuum advance dizzy (if you have one).

I don't have the charcoal canister hooked up.  The engine ventilation hose 
which would have connected to the air filter on the original setup, is now 
stuck under the engine tin at the seal to vent under the engine.  

You may have trouble getting the linkage set up.  If you bought these at your 
FLAPS, go back to them for help.  If you bought them from Performance Parts 
Warehouse, they have one guy who works there that knows VWs, but he is hard 
to get a hold of.
