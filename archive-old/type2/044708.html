At 3:00 AM -0500 8/25/00, Nikki Elko wrote:

>On the old carb (which I have right here) there is in that place what looks
>like a jet with a BB jammed in it ("0(8?)55 Solex" -- the slot's kinda
>mangled, so it's hard to read that first number). I just looked at the
>"new" carb, and it's got something similar in that spot, too. What gives?

The idle cutoff solenoid is also an idle jet.  55 is the size of the jet.
On your old carb, someone has replaced the solenoid/jet combo with just a
jet.  I did the same on one of my carbs and will do the other one when I
find another 55 jet.  Less wire in the engine compartment and it works the
same.  Older single-carb engines had the simple jets from the factory, but
I'm not sure what years.  I think the solenoids were installed to prevent
run-on but it doesn't seem to make a difference with my bus.
