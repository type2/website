know,  I recently put brand new heads (AMC unfortunately), rings

miles on this rebuild so far and here are my results.
I've listed results by cylinder order 1, 2, 3, 4.
Vacuum gauge is connected to the sensing port which is just below the
throttle valve.
Compression: 100, 95, 96, 95.
Vacuum at idle the needle flaps around wildly between: (9-14), (5-16),
(8-14), (8-14).
Vacuum at about 2000 rpm is much more steady: (9-11), (12-14), (10-12),
(10,12).
