John,

After you have the felt out. you must remove the vent wing. Next remove the
four upper and rear felt clips. These are the clips that held in the felt
that you have already removed. Sometimes they come out with the felt. They
just slide out and pull down. This chrome trim is the outside scrapper. At
the bottom where the scraper is are some push clips, similar to the door
panel clips. these just need to be pried out. I would start with the upper
most forward part and work back and down around. Do not worry about bending
it a little. Just do not kink it. I hope this helps.
