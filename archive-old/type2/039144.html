ALWAYS question wizbang ideas. They are experiments until throughly tested,
with hard data obtained from controlled conditions. Olive oil withstands
much higher heat than petro lubes. It doesn't follow that it is a solution
to overheating for overloaded or non-spec motors. If your not getting good
results with spec lube, something else is not spec and a non-spec lube is
not likely to improve things enough to save your wallet.
