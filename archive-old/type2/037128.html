Hi Daniel,

My name is Willem Bron, I am dutch and live in Sweden. I own a 74 Panel
truck converted to camper...
I drive on LPG (liquified petroleum gas)! Have a 60 liter tank and with the
normal 55 liter gas...
The milage is around 15.5 l on 100km. I guess that's around  15.5 mpg. On
fuel I drive around the 19-20 mpg. I have carburators.
In Holland 35 % of all cars has a lpg installation.
If u wanna know more about this look at www.autogas.nl (it's also in
english)
My liquid gas is evaporated in my exhaust. But this is now not anymore
admitted, just because of new legislation. It works perfect.
But driving on lpg gives u less power, u can definetly feel it when
accelerating hard...
So from fuel to lpg: u lose performance and milage but because in Europe lpg
is so cheap and fuel so expensive, u win always. I guess that's not the
situation in the States! Because fuels is soooo cheap!!!
And it's environmenatlly more friendly!!!
I fu wanna know more feel free to ask me
