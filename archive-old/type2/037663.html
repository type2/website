You're right, it is the lockout for reverse (so you have to push down on the 
shift lever to get reverse).  The tab goes on the passenger side.  I know that 
sound backwards, but if you pick up a pen and stick it between 2 of your 
fingers and pretend that's your shift lever, watch the bottom of it while you 
move it to where reverse should be (top part to the left and towards you).
