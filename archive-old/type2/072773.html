Check the individual contacts on the AFM plug.  Sometimes they rattle out
of the plug socket and don't contact or contact intermittantly (I have
recent experience).  Unplug it from the AFM and inspect if all contacts are
projecting from plug.  If not, carefully peel back cover and push contacts
back up in plug.


Also have you checked your fuel filter lately?  I keep a see-through one in
front of the bosch one to tell if the tank is gunking up the fuel.
