The key to the dizzy part number you are looking for is the vacuum unit that
is supposed to be on it. Short Bosch number 07 092. This unit has 2-5deg Adv,
11-13deg Retard. The 2-5 advance is what sets it aside as being for a bus. You
can raise your sites and get another dizzy that will meet the centrifugal
specs of 12-16 _at_ 2200, 22-25 _at_ 3800 by looking for these additional numbers:
043 905 205A, 113 905 205AA, 113 905 205P, or a real close 021 905 205E [yes,
this is a 72 1700 dizzy...]
Using one of these, all you need to do is find the correct 07 092 vacuum unit.
This unit will have 853 on the arm. The long Bosch number is 1 237 121 853.
Good luck!
