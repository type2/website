Me? What do I use?

Well, after bad experiences with Champion and Autolite I don't use them. 
Engines would not tune with them and I don't know why. The Autolites were 
used on a Mazda Rotary engine and that engine requires two different plugs. 
Autolite's catalog specified 1 plug style. Engine went from running good to 
bad with new Autolites and then back to good with a set of Bosch or NGK 
plugs (I don't know which). Champion has been bad for me in several engines.

Now I just use cheap NGK and cheap Bosch plugs with good results. Probably 
change them more often than necessary. (10K).
