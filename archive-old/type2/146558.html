Subject: Mr Heater Buddy

For those of you who went to Everybus this year, you remember that the
days were beautiful, but the nights were in the upper 30's and lower 40's.
 We would have been super uncomfortable had it not been for our buddy
heater.

We just cracked a window 1/2" and put the buddy on the floor between the
seats, heat rises, so my wife and I and baby Sofia stayed nice and warm. 
We did have problems with the water accumulation on the windows, but that
was really a minor nuisance really.

The Oxygen Depletion System (ODS) is what sold us on the buddy heater. 
Basically, you can't kill yourself if you follow the instructions and
provide a vent for outside air.  And if you don't, the ODS shuts it down.
