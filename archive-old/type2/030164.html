I use a group 24.  Larger, more reserve, fits in the same footprint, but
is a bit taller than the bug battery.  VW used group 42 batteries because
they already had them in the factory.  Group 24s are very common because
they were used in C*evys and a number of Japanese cars.

I also recommend that you put the battery in a plastic battery box to save
the battery tray.  Worth the hassle.
