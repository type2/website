I forget which is which, but the one on the vans has a vacuum controlled
fuel pressure regulator that allows manifold vaccuum level to affect the 
amount of fuel injected. I believe the other system has a pressure sensor
that measures manifold vacuum, and tells the control unit which adjusts the 
amount of fuel injected. This is from memory form a Porsche 411 web
page somewhere that descibes the second system. Other than this diffenence,
and the control unit difference, most of the other parts are on both 
systems.
