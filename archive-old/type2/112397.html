using the FI bug block for building motors . It seems to be stronger, but it has

owner wants. I also always carry a spare elec pump when traveling . Can be 
used on just about buses except FI  ones. Yes they can be  a danger in accidents 
but only if the key is left on. Later and have fun   Mark Dearing  Salem
