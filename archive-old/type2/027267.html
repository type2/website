Dear Jack.

RE: Installing oil pressure sending unit, on a 1972~79 VW 17/18/2000
    Type 2 engine

For the spacer mast, I used two female-female, in-line metric brake pipe 
connectors and two old 1976~79 VW 2000 Type 2 engine, valve-rocker 
adjustment screws (drilled through on a lathe), all of which have M10 x 
1.0mm non-tapered threads, which fit perfectly into the matching, 
non-tapered hole in the crankcase.

It is difficult to describe a banjo fitting in words, but it is commonly 
used in hydraulic and fuel injection systems, to create an easily pivoted, 
right-angled joint, using a ring connection resembing a banjo and a hollow, 
cross-drilled bolt.

published in VW Motoring in December 1996.
