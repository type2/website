I feel that this article from the library needs to be augmented.

The bastard transmission is simply a European 1600 transmission used
from 74 on (VIN 214 2000 001).
The 75 transmission in that article really is a 74 introduced with the
1800 engine.

I think were simply dealing with 4 transmissions in our T2bs - one for
each engine (1600, 1700, 1800, and 2000)

Here is what I have found in Stefan Doliwa's VW Transporter Book (Die
technische Dokumentation 1950 - 1990):
August 1, 1973 (1974 model) VIN 214 2000 001:
1600 engine - ring and pinion 7:38, 4th gear 62:51
1700 engine - discontinued
1800 engine - ring and pinion 7:34, 4th gear 27:24
October 1973 - Achsuebersetzung (what is the English term here?) from
11:49 to 11:48
August 1, 1975 (1976 model) VIN 216 2000 001:
ring and pinion from 7:34 to 7:38
gear ratios adapted to new engine
