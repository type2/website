You should still check - or have someone else check - why you failed 
emissions with a non-functioning sensor.  The system *should* be capable of 
passing emissions without the O2 sensor in place.  The O2 sensor is there to 
give feedback to the ECU and keep emissions in spec longer as parts wear and 
other specs - timing, etc. - change.  A working O2 sensor can "push" the 
system into compliance, but it can't fix something that's failing.
