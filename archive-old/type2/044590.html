take the four bolts off the sump pan (drain ATF first)  on the under side
there is a round plate with a "tail" off to one side, it is held on by two
screws, remove this plate, it is the filter, and clean well, replace and
clean sump pan.  replace the ATF and see if this helps

sounds like you ATF pump is starving, also if the ATF pump fails this is
also a symptom, fixing it is next to impossible.
