On a type 4 engine, do NOT rely on the engine number stamped in the fan
housing. Go by the real number stamped in the case between the fan housing
and the square casting that the oil seperater sits on. A little tough to see
but better than going by a fan housing that may or may not belong to that
engine. The housing can be swapped real easy.
