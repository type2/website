My '71 has hard points for 3-point belts in all seats.  Common Audi/VW
inertia reel belts are easily adapted to the front seats.  I have sirocco
self-adjusting lap belts in the rear seat, but no shoulder harnesses yet.
That area would take a bit more work then the fronts to install inertia
reel belts.
