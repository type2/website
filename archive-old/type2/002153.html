Meredith:

I'm a little confused... you say you had "ignition" after your first
stall, you assume you "have spark", but you're thinking it's a bad
coil?  If you really have spark (test by pulling a spark plug wire,
hold the end near the engine, and have someone crank it over) then I'd
discount the bad coil issue.  You can do some limited testing of the
coil with your meter, when you get it, but bad coils frequently
announce themselves by leaking.

If you really "have spark" then I'd be more inclined to think along
the lines of what would cause your fuel system to shut down.  Maybe a
bad double relay?
