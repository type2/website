On Wednesday, March 26, 2003, at 01:28PM, rich racioppa <rjracioppa_at_netscape_dot_net> wrote:

>I lost part of the right side of the gas pedal at the bottom, it falls 
>to the floor, do i need a spring or hinge or something.  I think i can 
>get these through besdepot, but need to know what it is called.

There is a hinge welded to the floor and a spring and pin go through the hinge. A c-clip holds the pedal to the rod that connects to the accelerator cable.

There are two styles of pins: 68-75 are shorter.

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/MoreParts/04-BottomPinIsFor68-75.JPG

Also the hinges are slightly different. The 68-75 hinge has no holes and has more bends.

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/MoreParts/06-WhereAreTheHoles.JPG
http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/MoreParts/05-StateOfHinge.JPG

I have a 78 bus and didn't find the right parts at BusDepot. CIP1 seems to have the correct hinge C27-211-721-613, as well as both 68-75 and 76-79 pedals to choose front.

I'm not sure of a source for the correct pin but I'm sure you can use what you have now. The return spring comes in two flavours: 73-75 and 76-79 to match the middle part of the pin.
