Brad,

remove the rubber stop (best done before you even jack up the bus). Unscrew the hex screw and nut holding the torsion arm in place. Jack up the other side (remove the rubber stop first). Your upper torsion arms should now rest on the beam extension that holds the stop. Get the biggest pry bar and hammer you can find and work it slowly off.

Jacking up the other side of the car takes off some spring action but not all. For reinstall mount all 4 torsion arms on the unloaded springs and then start working to upper ones back into place.
