   You walk into any plumbing shop or dept. and you can probably 
 find what you're looking for.

 We are talking "sweat fitting reducers". You will still have to 
solder it on there tho unless you want to take a chance on epoxy
which I would not.

 An alternative would be to stick a piece of "proper" size hose on 
the tank, come down to about six inches from the pump and put a "hose
to hose" reducer in to bring it down to the proper size for the pump.

 This would save you having to "sweat" the fitting onto the tank.
