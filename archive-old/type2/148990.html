On Jan 12, 2006, at 5:03 PM, Chris Slaymaker wrote:

> I've heard tell that these new "meximotors" have domed pistons for  
> a reduced CR and cooler running temps.  Can someone corroborate?

The crate motor with hydraulic lifters has domed pistons and a 7.75:1  
CR. This is deemed by many to be too high for a long life bus engine.

The solid lifter version of the same engine has dished pistons and a  
lower CR.
