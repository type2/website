Hi,
I found these speakers that are coaxial inside a kidney shaped hard plastic
enclosure. On the bottom there is a screw hole. I placed them upside down,
facing forward on the ceiling just above and slightly forward of the z-bed
seat. They are mounted on the ridge of the opening where the small cot
attaches. (I have a 71). They are lower profile, but are only meant to be
for used for treble. I was thinking of hooking up a set of those computer
speakers that come with the subwoofer, from Cambridge Sound works. They are
only around $100.00 and sound great. I would put the subwoofer under the
z-bed. We shall see.
