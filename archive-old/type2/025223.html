Hi Mark,

Where to start is the archives.

  http://vw.i405_dot_com/

For reference, you have a type1 engine.  This is the same engine used in a
bug.  Do you have an Alternator or Generator?  Try
http://www.i405_dot_com/vw/stand/ for photos.

On the archives page, try some searches like this:

   "generator near charging"
   "generator near regulator"

Hope this gets you started!

I also suggest you go to a junk yard and locate a volt guage.  This HAS TO
BE one of the easiest guages to hook up -- all you need is a + and -.  A VDO
one out of any VW / Audi would be nice... but any should do.

P.S.  REPLACE YOUR FUEL LINES, you should take your time to learn about the
fuel lines and various places that fuel flows.
