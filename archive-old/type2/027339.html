Thank you to all that gave advise on getting my 76 going again.

One of the hoses coming from the intake manifold came off, and found a few 
other hoses that must have blown off when it was backfiring.
I reconnected the first hose I found, wouldn't start, then finally found the 
last two, the larger of which coming from the intake manifold and
