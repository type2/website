enough to warm up the exhaust header pipes.  AMAZING!

Many thanks to those who responded with test-running and break-in
advice.  After realizing that I'd hooked up the fuel  lines backward at

there.

The carb seems to have bad bushings on the throttle plate - gas was
dripping off either end of the throttle plate shaft.  I have a spare
carb I'll throw on and try again.

for this setup?  It's stock except the header.
