> 1) what wires are the bare nessesities to make a coil shoot a hot spark from

power from the battery (via ignition switch) to the + & the green wire
from the distributor are all that it needs to run, if the carb has a
fuel cutoff solenoid you will need that powered too. I have always
just used the visual spark test like you have done to test spark.

> spark with both a brand new coil and the coil that was on it when I removed
> the engine.

What about the points? are they adjusted correctly?
