Upon the realization that there were no Full Moon Bus Club campouts during

We will be camping alongside the Nolichucky River, at the Nolichucky Gorge

TN. This campground was built by many rafters, kayakers, and hikers hours of
fruitful volunteer
efforts. It is positioned within one of the deepest gorges in the entire
Southeast, and has great
water accessibility, not to mention literally bordering the National Forest
and the Appalachian
Trail. All beverages and all dogs are WELCOME within the campground, so it
will be perfect for us!
At only an hour's drive from Asheville and just 2 hours from Knoxville, it
makes for the PERFECT
TENNESSEE camping experience!

If you're into mountain biking, watersports, hiking, eating, fishing, or
just plain doing nothing,
then this is the campout for you. The middle of August will be hot, but the
water will be cool and
the pines that we will be camping under should provide with ample shade.
Experience August as it
ought to be experienced... with your VW friends, under a full moon, at the
Buses On The River
campout!
