Subject: Re: [T2] Exhaust Nuts - Copper Nuts are not copper!

On 11/23/03 1:46 PM, "Jeff Myer" <jmyer_at_Myermountain_dot_com> wrote:

> One additional thing to consider with regard to the nut material for
> exhaust:If you use the copper nuts, and they get rounded or just plain
> stuck, they are by far the easiest to get off using a chisel.

The exhaust nuts vendors refer to as "copper exhaust nuts" are not made of
copper - they are copper plated steel. They are quite hard, will not round
off at all. The are self-locking which is important as the torque on these
is quite low.
