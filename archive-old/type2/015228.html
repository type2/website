jet included in the kit to adjust for the "lag".  I never noticed any lag
in my 75 bus, or the 79, for that matter (same setup).  I think the extra
jet was a 70, which is swapped with the 55 in the secondary idle holder,
then the 60 pimary idle jet & holder is put in the secondary, and the 70 in
the primary.  Any way, I find all this a lot easier than blowing out idle
jets every day or so on the beautiful fancy really spiffy looking dual
dellortos that were on the vehicle previously.
