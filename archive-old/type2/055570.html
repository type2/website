Don't look for a consensus where *opinions* are involved.  IMHO, the 
best camper was the 1959-61 Westfalia SO-23.  Nice functional camper 
kit with exceptional craftsmanship - nice *wood* cabinets with brass

Generally the quality of craftsmanship of the Westfalia was higher 
than that of Sundial.
