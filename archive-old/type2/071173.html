Hello Scott,

I'd try a synthetic such as Redline MTL but any gear lube or
additive will not magically repair worn parts.   A worn out part
is worn out and will need to be replaced.   Is the noise a 'howl'
type noise that seems to be louder when you are under load?
If so, then I would agree that it is probably the ring and pinion 
set that is causing the problem.
