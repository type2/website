HI,  I know it's not Friday, but I was unable to get to my email till this
morning...anyway...I couldn't let this go by...

Last weekend, As I was driving in a city in MD (don't want anyone to start
pointing fingers) I saw a lovely White and orange Westy a couple of cars
ahead of me.  Then something tragic almost happened, a little extra speed, a
failure to yield at a traffic circle, and non-antilock brakes came close to
extinguishing the light of one of these wonderful vehicles.  If the oncoming
car had not of been as quick with the brake as they where several people
would have been hurt, and there would be one more "parts car" and one less
bus sighting.

PLEEEEEEASE be careful out there!!!!!  Keep in mind that these treasures do
not come with "anti-lock brakes" and precision steering.  They are not your
5-star safety rated mini-van.  They are not sports cars.  Most aren't off
road vehicles. They will not corner like they are on rails, and they
certainly wont stop on a dime.  They truly are not your typical automobile.
These vehicles are becoming harder and harder to find.  I'm sure if you
don't want to take care of the beauty there are others out here that will.
