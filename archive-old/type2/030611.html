Dan is right that the VW kit is better than many of the aftermarket kits,
but I have found an additional trouble spot in this setup.  On mine, the
clevis pins and cabl end used to go bad with amazing speed and a close
examination showed that the hole in the plate on the arm was very oval.  I
replaced that dog-bone shaped plate and have had no trouble since.  Take a
look at the plate that the cable goes thru if the cable is breaking at the
clevis pin.
