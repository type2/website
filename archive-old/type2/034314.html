Subject: My "free"  tranny removal tool

My first tool for tranny removal cost nothing- picked up a 19mm (socket
size) bolt taken from an engine mount at the junk yard (about 3" long),
stuck it in a vise,  and hit it with a torch, bending it 90 degr. near
the hex end.  Stick it inside a 3/8" iron plumbing pipe and have at that
drain plug!  Other forms are the GIANT allen wrench and my favorite, a
3/8" socket with the hex shape at one end.
