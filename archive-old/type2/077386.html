This may seem obvious, but have you looked closely at the bottom of the 
pedal? There is a little "l" shaped piece of round metal that goes into the

pedal, thinking my cable broke. I was on the hellish interstate in Dallas in 
July (argh - hot!) I got underneath with my new cable when I realized that 
the old cable was fine - the l shaped piece just came out of the hole. Maybe 
your problem, maybe not. Good luck...
