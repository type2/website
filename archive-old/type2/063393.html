On Mon, 16 Sep 1996, Steve Haney wrote:
> Does anyone know of the VDO dipstick sender featured in the West Coast 
> Metric catalog fits the type-4 engine in my '72?  Also, can the sender 
> be used to check the oil level or do I continue to use the stock dip 
> stick?

Yes, it will fit.  The effective length is adjustable, so it works to 
check oil level as well.  You add your own "full" and "add" marks to the 
sender dipstick by slightly scratching the dipstick with a file, using 
the old one to guide where you put the marks.  Installing the sender is 
about the simplest thing I've ever done on the bus.

I've had mine 2 years.  Love it.  Works great.
