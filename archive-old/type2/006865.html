Boy I sure wouldn't want 50 psi in my front tires -- talk about
squirrley -- and unsafe. I never put more than 29 psi (VW recommended
even on full load) The front is so much lighter than the rear. The rear
vary, again by recommendation, 39 psi, 42 psi fully loaded. This is on a
'71 Hardtop Westy. I'm running out the door right now but I have
documentation of a '71 Westy's (w/pop top) weight at less than 3700 lbs
-- wet. Max load about 5000.
