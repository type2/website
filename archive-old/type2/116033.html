Subject: Re: [T2] Jalousie Window won't close

Take the plastic knob off and inspect (one phillips screw).  It may be
stripped.  It probably got stripped because of too much resistance on the
mechanism/windows.

(new plastic knobs are available from all the usual bus part places).

Once the knob is off, try turning just the metal knob post
(cover with a rag and gently use vice grips so you don't trash the metal).

--
To improve performance,

Remove the entire window assembly (15 or so phillips screws).  Remove bug
screen then disconnect the main louver arms from the knob gearbox (snap
rings).   Wash/clean the window joints, squirt WD-40 into all the joints
then manually move the windows up and down (as a group of 3) a bunch of
times.

Put everything back together, you'll be amazed at how much easier it is to
open/close the jalousie windows with the knob.
