> Also, what is "v_at_l" or FRIDAY.TGIF ?

V_at_L...  this list started off in 1996(?) as a list for all VW buses run by
a guy named Gerry Skerbitz (sp?) at U of MN.  The address was
vanagon_at_lenti.umn_dot_edu.  When Gerry quit, we split the list in two, between
air and water cooled buses, water cooled vanagons moved to
vanagon_at_vanagon_dot_com and Ken Hooper started type2_at_type2_dot_com to cover air
cooled buses.  Two years ago, the aircooled list split again between early
buses (1950-1967) on vintagebus and late buses (1968-1983).  When we got
our own machine the question of what the machine name ought to be... I
thought the answer obvious :-), which is why the machine name is
lenti.type2_dot_com (type2_dot_com works fine, so many of you don't see it :-)

on FRIDAY.TGIF, I have no clue :-(  Anyone?

> ALSO... where's the "registry" I read about?

is a lot of cross-posting between the vintagebus list and the type2 list,
mentions of it turn up by times, but AFAIK (as far as I know), there are 
not registries for bay window buses or for vanagons (volunteers? :-)
