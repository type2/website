If your bus suddenly develops a mysterious vibration, be sure to check
your tires for tread separation, which shows up as heavily worn spots
on the tire or obviously lumpy tread.  You don't want to peel a tread
and wipe out your sheet metal!  While otherwise excellent, Michelins
do have a reputation for tread separations.
