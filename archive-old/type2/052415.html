Subject: Re: [T2] lightened flywheels for hill country

You want that extra momentum from the heavy flywheel to help carry you up
the hills.  With a light flywheel, your engine will slow down quicker.  A
light flywheel is for quick engine RPM's such as for street racing or
dunebuggies where you "snap" the gas pedal and jump around corners.
