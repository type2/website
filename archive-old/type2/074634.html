thought this might be relevant.  Some people have had good experiences =

 Anything that would delay the shipment, went on back order so I would =
(will) get everything else a.s.a.p.  And one item is to be drop shipped =
direct from the supplier.  In all, I ordered 20 items totaling about =
$426, paid with Visa.

shall see.
