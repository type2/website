4 years of non use can lead to several problems, depending much on ambient
humidity during period of sitting.

1. Drain all the old gas from the tank. Fill with fresh. Change the fuel
filter.

2. Fully charge then load test the battery. Replace if required.

3. Test charging specs. If the regulator or alturnator shows problems,
replace as required.

Expect possible problems with fuel hoses, injector spray pattern, and flush
the brake fluid and check closely for leaks. Does it go without saying???
Change the oil and filter before further running.
