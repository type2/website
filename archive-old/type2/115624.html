Assuming that the engine has stock parts, if you look inside the valve
cover, under the rocker arm you will see a part number for the head. This
will give you an idea of the displacement. Compare the numbers to this
chart:

http://www.tunacan_dot_net/t4/reference/cylhead.htm

Might as well check both heads while you are at it. I know of at least one
person with a 2L engine who had one 1.7L head and it was a huge surprise to
him. The 8mm valve adjustment screws was his first tip-off.
