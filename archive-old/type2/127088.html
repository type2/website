I agree with Mr. Lyle that US 50 is the way to go. My addition to his
highlights would be to come down out of Moab on I-70 to US 50 and take 50
over to Delta and collect some trilobites.  Do it in town if you are in a
hurry or head out to the quarry if you have the time. After filling up (no
gas next 10,000 miles!) take US 50/Utah and Nevada 6 over to Ely.  Take 50
toward but not to Austin, NV.  As you pass the various ranges to your
south in the Toiyabe National Forest (motto: Wrap it up, boys... we're
DONE!) at least one of the dirt roads on the left down the valleys leads
to a spot where you can pull over for the night and get up at 3AM, go out
for a break and NOT SEE AN ELECTRIC LIGHT!  It may sound trivial, but this
is the only place in the continental United States where I have found that
this to be possible.

If you choose to travel I-80....
