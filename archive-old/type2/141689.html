>Are there options out there for going all stainless(sans heat
>exchangers

Find a pair of Type 4 heat exchangers (as in 411) and cut the outer tin off. 
This leaves you with essentially a pair of j-tubes...

A stock 72-72 muffler should bolt right up...

OR

You can go crazy like I did. I had it in my head to put a Porsche exhaust 
(with header) on my '77 lowered Bus. Once the j-tubes were installed I 
grabbed the exhaust and attempted to bolt it up.

No way..... the header hit the motor.

But, there was a solution. IIRC, Scott Lyons of Germansupply, cut the 
flanges off the header and welded them on upside down for me and then it 
bolted right up...

Freakin' awesome...

Bus sounded amazing (loud)
