It depends on the distributor.  If you still have the stock double diaphragm
dist then the timing at idle should be 5 deg after top dead center.  If you
have a single diaphragm or 009 dist then it's about 7 1/2 deg before tdc.
In any case check the total advance with the engine revved up to about 3000
rpm.  Total advance should never be more than about 30 deg!
