ok i'm working from memory here so bear with me. plus, when i did this 
it
was on a '72,
so there might be some differences.

look underneath the ignition switch at the underside of the steering 
column.
there should be 4 screws that hold the steering column in its upright
position.

remove those 4 screws & pull the entire thing towards the driver's seat.
you'll see a push-button type mechanism & that is your steering lock. 
push
the button in to disengage the steering lock.

for safety, make sure the bus isn't going to roll or move during this
procedure!
