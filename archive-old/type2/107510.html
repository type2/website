I looked in Bentley, and it agrees that a true 77 has no microswitch, but a
late 76 might.

The old "throttle valve switch" was removed after vin# 226 2077 583, but the
"microswitch" was used for a short while after that, then it went away
leaving no electrical switch on the throttle assembly.

ECU P/N :022-906-021AA
