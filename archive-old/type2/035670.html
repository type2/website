Hippie Days 2000
A VW Road Rally
March 4 & 5, 2000
AVWA's Fifth Anniversary For This Event:
Great Family Fun Featuring VW Shop Tours, VW Boneyard Tours, One Hour Super
Sales, Great Food At Every Stop,  TV & Newspaper Interviews With Awards For
Coolest Hippie Dude, Coolest Hippie Chick, Coolest Hippie Couple, Oldest
Hippie, Youngest Hippie and Coolest Seta Wheels.
We'll travel the corners of Tennessee, North Carolina, South Carolina and
Georgia at posted speed limits or less dressed as 60s era Hippies (optional)
in VWs decorated (also optional) with Peace symbols & daisies.We'll do the
usual gas stops and visit with German Automotive in Fletcher NC, Vintage Auto
in Greenville SC, VW Parts in Central SC and Denton's Imports in Homer GA.
While there you'll be able to go boneyard shopping or purchase anything from a
fanbelt to a new Mexican Beetle (test drives available).Each of our hosts will
provide food. An awards lunch will be provided by the AVWA & Mountain VWs of
Sevierville, TN and will be hosted by Denton's Imports.
Re-live the 60s. Join us for all or part of this fun VW event.
