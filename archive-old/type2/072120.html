Do a quick compression check, black smoke is a sign of fuel probems, not
engine problems.  If the compression is within 30% of each other and none
are below 100 psi, you still have some life in the engine, but need to
figure out the fuel problem.  Excessive fuel can wash the oil off the
cylinder walls which will make the rings wear faster.

If it's in nice, or just OK shape, and the compression looks good, offer
them $1200 and see what they say.
