Another way we do oil temp on engines with external oil systems, is to tap
the filter mount, directly in the flow of oil, our full flow type 4 engines
have the sensor there, and it works absolutely great, as heat from the case
is nil. I had 3 oil temp senders on the 2613 in the beetle, one in the sump,
one in the filter housing and one after the last of 3 oil coolers, before
entering the case. The variations were about 100 degrees on some occasions,
especially after crusing at near 90 for over an hour or so..
