Bay window buses have the inside mirror positioned so that you cannot see
the horizon through the rear window.  I'm not sure about the vanagons,
though.

The caution here is not to fiddle with the rear suspension unless you're
_sure_ that it is out of spec.  I adjusted mine till I could see out of
the rear window and was rewarded with strange handling, tire wear, and
torn CV boots.

At rest, buses normally sit high at the front, since the driver's weight
is missing.
