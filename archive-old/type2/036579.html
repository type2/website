along this note George....

I siezed an engine once (MY OWN).

Turns out the clutch adjustment was too tight. When this was coupled with a
HD pressure plate, the thrust bearing had no lubrication, and the crank and
flywheel grabbed the thrust main, and wasted the crank and the NEW $600
case.

A piece of advice, make sure your clutch isn't adjusted too tight. You may
get away with it, but you may not.
