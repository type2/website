An MSD 6AL (contains rev limiter) ignition box will solve this problem by
keeping the engine below redline. It's cheaper than a new engine, anyway,
and some happy MSD users claim better gas mileage.

Of course, you could always just build an engine big enough to let her drive
60MPH in third.

girlfriend or one who won't drive a manual transmission.
