Subject: Re: [T2] starts but dies?  78 westy

Does the bus run once it's warmed up? or does it just die now all the time
if you let up on the accelerator? If it dies only while warming up, you may
have accidentally bumped the electric connection to the cold start valve
(which is really just another fuel injector buried under the plumbing
roughly in the centre of the engine top area. Look around for a loose wire;
my next guess is that you have accidemntally knocked a vacuum connection off
 there are many of them, start checking along them for a disconnected one. I
think you will find you have missed one.
