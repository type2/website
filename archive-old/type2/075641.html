Re: the 78 Westy patterns - I would not count on them being the same as
those of earlier years.  I think, for this project to remain valid, we all
need to keep track of what year Westy we are taking our measurements from,
and strive to keep all patterns from like years together and separate from
those of other years. As we go, we will probably discover that certain years
are similar or identical.

For example, I would expect to find that 68 - 72 will all be identical, due
to other known similarities between those years.  But right now, I have no
way to prove that.

I imagine that later year Westy's will also fall into groups of some sort,
but I don't know enough about them to have any clue how that will turn out.

I propose we move ahead with this one step at a time.  The whole thing
started with a question about certain panels in a 71 Westy, IIRC, so let's
focus on the 71 Westy first and try to address the original poster's
question, then move on the the larger picture.

I agree, having this info available - all panels for all years - for
download, would be a great thing.
