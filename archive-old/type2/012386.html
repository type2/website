They are the Hindu variety.  Commonly available through AutoZone for
about $0.95 the plug.  Now the German ones are as well, it seems they
spottily sold either German or Indian depending on availability.

I've had one misfire new out of the box and have seen em with ugly
threads.

For the money though I don't think you can beat the Bosch tri-electrode
copper or tri-electrode silver.  Those plugs last forever, can see a mileage
gain on a non electronic ignition car with a weakening coil or leaky wires
and are a great plug.  I'm not sure off the top of my head what the proper
plug for an air cooled would be, and there is probably no T1 engined tri-
electrode plug.  I tend to use German Supers mostly as they are so
damn cheap.
