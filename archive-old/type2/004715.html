It's not that difficult on a type 4 engine. I doubt if its possible on a
type 2. My engine has carbs and if you get these and the electric fan off
the tank slides out. If you've got FI I don't know what to expect. The
hardest bit I found was getting at the nuts holding the long straps around
the tank. You need a long reach 13mm socket and even then I ended up
destroying the left hand flapper valve on the heating system. Actually it
was so rusted it just fell off.

Took about 5 hours in and out without touching the engine mounts.
