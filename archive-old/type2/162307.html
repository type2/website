Chris,

It looks to me like it could be "flashing" created on the install of the 
valve guides.  I wouldn't consider it a big thing if you could get it off
without too much trouble.

As long as there are no cracks in the aluminum hiding underneath the 
flecks, I wouldn't worry too much about it.

If the aluminum head surface was "pittted" where the two pieces came off, 
just smooth it gently with a dremel and go on.  You have a lot more 
surface area holding that valve guide in than those little pieces.
