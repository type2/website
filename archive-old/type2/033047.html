> When I had the same problem I discovered that one of the air hoses
> connecting from the outlet hose of the AFM to the air distributor via
> the auxiliary air valve had sufficient free play at its connection to
> allow air to leak into the system (basically a vacuum leak that allowed
> air to temporarily get to the cylinders without being measured by the
> AFM, which results in too lean a mixture). After the engine warmed up,
> the auxiliary valve shuts down so the problem goes away, only to return
> when the engine needs to be started again when cold. I put a clamp on
> the offending connection. So check the hose connections in that part of
> the air distribution system.
