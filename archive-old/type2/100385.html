Has anyone ever attempted to rebuild the seats in their bus?  I don't mean
simply new padding and covers, but actually replacing broken springs, etc. to
bring the seats back to their original level of firmness.  I put new cushions

fine.  I also just thought about replacing all the spring material in the seat
