  The battery cells contains wertical plates placed next to each other .
When the battery gets older a plate in a battery cell can come loose and
sort of "shortcut" that cell. The result of this is that the battery
delivers a tension of 10 volts. The capacity of carrying current trough
that cell is now reduced. That´s why you still had power enough for the
stereo an some small lamps. when you tried to start Brunhilda there wasn´t
enough current (that´s the amperes) leading capacity. It´s like putting
three good and one bad battery into  your battery operated flashlight.
