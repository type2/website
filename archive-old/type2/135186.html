The brakes are only connected to the engine by the vacuum line on the 
booster.  If you have used so much fluid that it had leaked to the booster 
and overflowed that and travelled the length of the bus to the engine 
itself, you do have a neat problem.  If that is truly what the mechanic 
said, I might look for a second opinion.  You might check into loss at the 
wheels outside the brake assemblies or inside where it might be burning off. 
Have you niticed any strange smells?  Let us know...
