Look at the right end where the muffler would attach. See that 2" round pipe
sticking up? [not the best drawing....] That is where the heater fan tube
attaches. Not thru the upper cylinder tin like 72 to 78 and 79 Fed engines. On
those other engines, the heater box is sitting where the cat is on a 79 Cal
engine.
Go to this page and scroll down to Engine Tin 72 through 83:
http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/aircoolsystem.html
G is 79 Cal only. Part number 039-119-527
Sorry, no pic.  But if you are looking at the engine and the corner tin, it
will be in the shape of a large L. Where the part of the L is _, it meets the
center piece. The 2" hole is in the _ section where it curves down to meet the
center piece.
