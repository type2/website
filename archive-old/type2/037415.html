I'll put in my 2-cents.

My VDO tach runs dead steady on the freeway, so I would not consider any
wobbling "normal". Also, with your '74 tranny, which, I believe is a 4.86
R&P and .88 4th, you should be running around 3500-ish RPM _at_ 60 MPH, with
185/75's. So says Scott & Maggie's RPM-->MPH calculator at:

http://www.scottandmaggie_dot_com/gearcalc.asp

You can put your actual numbers in, and see what comes up.
