Bay burning up on the freeway. The camera crew got on scene just as

burned out, tires burned off of the rims. It was a sobering picture.

power, heard a pop and tried to get over, but traffic wouldn't let 
him (sound familiar). It caught on fire in the middle lanes. He tried 
to put it out with his fire extinguisher, but obviously that did not 
work.
