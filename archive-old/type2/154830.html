Downs: None Yet

Eurosport Automotive
Phone: 303-423-3715
Address: 5615 Lamar St., Arvada, CO, 80002
Specialty: General Automotive Repair. VW, Audi, Porsche. Mostly performance
tuning.
Ups: Friendly staff and good communication with vehicle progress
Downs: Seem to have some personnel issues
Rating: 8
Reason: They were a 10 until they cracked my perfect 1970 DC steering wheel
when doing a shoddy alignment. They did not offer to replace it, redo the
alignment or offer or admit to it. Probably was a bad technician.

BustedBus
Phone: 303-545-5084
Address: Private, web only. Parts_at_bustedbus_dot_com
Specialty: Soft to full restoration work. Typically specializes as a parts
reseller, but he does do quite a few soft restos.
Ups: Specializes in Late Model Bays and has the knowledge to match
Downs: Currently only has part-time helper
Rating: 10
Reason: Always honest and informative. Contact through email only.

Wright's German Motors
Phone: 303-420-2123
Address: 5609 Lamar St., Arvada, CO 80002
Specialty: Full VW and Audi service
Ups: Completely up on Type 2 trivia and nuances. Cheapest found yet.
Downs: Some strange personality issues
Rating: 10
Reason: Did a perfect alignment and some diagnostic work. Fair price
although the front desk guy is a little crusty to deal with.

Injection Labs
Phone: 303-404-0515
Address: 10900 West 120th Ave. #C, Broomfield, CO
Specialty: Aircooled Porsche and VW motors. Fuel injection conversions,
repair and tuning. High performance and Race engine building.
Ups: Incredible knowledge and engineering for engine tuning and building
Downs: Small shop, one employee = a longer than average wait
Rating: 10
Reason: As an engine builder, EFI specialist and turbo specialist, no
Porsche enthusiast "in the know" will take their rig to anyone else.
Expensive, but he might be the only person in Colorado that can build you a
100K+ mile motor. He is also the only builder in Colorado that can properly
rebuild a VW Type 4 cylinder head.

Verner's Automotive
Phone: 303-444-8307
Address: 3280 Valmont Rd., Boulder, CO
Specialty: All VW's, all repairs and rebuilds
Ups: Has a good following and plenty of years of good reviews
Downs: Some recent honesty issues. Hope it isn't a trend.
Rating: 5
Reason: Recent reports have been Verner's is getting very sloppy with Bay
Window and Vanagon owners in particular.

Pine Beetles
Phone: 303-838-7218
