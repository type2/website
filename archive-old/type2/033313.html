I measured Uniroyal 195/75R14s and found them to be 1/2 inch smaller in
diameter than the 185R14 Bridgestones.  The tread was about a half-inch
wider, but the sidewall width was actually a bit narrower than the
185s.  These tires have been relegated to winter snow chain duty, since
they have such nice wheelwell clearance.

The difference in running is probably small, but I'd just as soon see
people get the correct size, so that the correct size will continue to
be produced.  Folks in Europe might have better supply, since it is
used on several makes of minivan there, but the last vehicle using this
size and imported to the US was a '95 Nissan.
