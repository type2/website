I'm not sure mine is working.  I rather doubt it.  But I've found that the engine runs much better when the oil temp reads about 120F.  I call that the magic number.  So I always warm it up until it gets there or close to it before I drive.  Haven't noticed the sludge since I started doing that.  Newer cars with computers compensate for it but I still don't think it's good to start driving on a cold engine.  One of these days I'll get around to checking/changing it.
