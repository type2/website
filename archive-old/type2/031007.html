stopper until I can do the job right.  As everyone else says it doesn't
stop the "Cancer" but it will keep the rain out.  I usually wire brush the
loose rust off, hit it with a couple coats of "Extend" then cover it with
the tape. is usually good for a year or two holding action til I can get
to it to do it right
