On the '73, a slightly fuller story:

The FI is aready there, and working, on the '78 engine.  Because I was

since I had the dead '79, I chiselled the air filter mount off that body
and put it in the '73.  Looks absolutely stock :-)  Gas tank was not an
issue either, just used the '79 tank.
