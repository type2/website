Hi Stewart,

a little help... First, get the Muir book, "How to Keep Your Volks-
Wagen Alive".  You can check your engine number by looking at the
stand the alternator/generator sits on.  It faces rear and should
have something like   AE nnn nnn nn  or something on it.  (the 'n's
stand for numbers.  You can write it down and post it to this list,
if nothing else.  Many here have detailed info on when that engine
was built, etc.  

As for rebuilding it, itza BIG job, and I would try to run it
first to get an idea how it is. But if you do it, (Muir and Bentley
books), you'll have a lot of satisfaction and a lot of happy miles !!
