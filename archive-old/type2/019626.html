I know this sounds crazy and simple, but check your battery terminals. 
My terminals were badly corroded on my 76 2.0Fi and I couldn't use any
accessories or start.  I changed the starter (then changed it back),
checked the wiring, ignition.. got a new battery, everything.  I
couldn't figure it out for about a year.  Sometimes, especially when it
rained, the electricity would go out on me, then come back.  Then, when
I replaced the terminals harnesses, it was all back to normal.
