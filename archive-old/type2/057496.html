I'm not sure about Indiana law, but there must be some provision for a
mecanic's lien on the car.  This would allow the mechanic to take
title of a car to recoup unpaid bills.  This would _not_ be the same
as a junk title, as the mechanic usually sells the vehicle at auction.
 In the case of the bus, I'm sure that there are more charges in
storage fees than in the actual value of the bus, so it would belong
to the mechanic and he is free to make whatever deal with you that he
wants.

George Lyle

I'd be leery of other methods of acquiring title, as the original
owner might pop out of the woodwork and want the bus back.  If you've
used some "expeditous" method of getting title you might find yourself
up on fraud charges!
