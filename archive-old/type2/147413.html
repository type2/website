Yes, when cold and thermostat is indeed collapsed (shortened), you pull 
the flap closed (downwards where the clamp is) and then tighten the 
clamp in that position.  If the thermostat has failed (relaxed, ie, 
longer) and you do this, it will NEVER loosed the flap pull and you will 
overheat when warm.
