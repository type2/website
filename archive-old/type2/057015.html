I've been reading the on going thread on VDO gauges I put both oil gauges
below radio (drilled holes with metal hole saw) and was able to fit the
spin-on mounting thing-a-ma-gig, the ring that spins on can go two
different ways and only fits one way, it's tight and you have to have the
gauge just through the hole as you start the thread but then it tightens up
nice and snug and looks great (didn't use a face plate looks better without
imo.) It also worked out that both gauges read just what I wanted them to,
oil pressure around 40 most of the time, oil temp around 210, YEA!
