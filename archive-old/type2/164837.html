Blake,

By chance are you using generator straps on an alternator equipped engine?
The Generator straps are smaller and will stretch and break. I had a problem
with this on one particular bus. I finally switched to the captive bolt
style strap which held tight.

The scraping sound is either your fan on the fanshroud or your crank pulley
on the tin. Since have to pull the doghouse and shim the alternator per the
Bentley/Haynes instructions, so you will see if there are wear marks on the
doghouse.
