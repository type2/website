Sounds like you have the brake booster line hooked up correctly:

http://www.nls_dot_net/mp/volks/schem/vac.gif
http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/78%20Camper5/132-LeftIntakeRunner.
jpg

Page 18 of the Automatic Transmission (AT) chapter in Bentley shows a
similar Y on the other side of the firewall on an older T4 engine.

I believe this Y is a piece only installed on busses with AT. Someone else
on the list had a problem recently with this tee on his 76 and he had a
screw shoved in the hose. His engine died when he removed it because he had
a MT and there was no where for the hose to go: major vacuum leak.

If you have a MT like he has, you'll need to block off the hose because it's
a transplant from another bus, otherwise the 3.5mm hose hooks up to the
vacuum unit on the AT.
