> My question is regarding general maintainance. What the best type of oil to
> us in the 2000cc engine? What other things should be looked at on a regular
> basis? Any information would be helpful. Thanks!

drop the tranny pan, put in a GOOD fluid and clean the filter, this is
frequently overlooked, also check the final drive which uses a 90 weight
gear oil.

I always use a synthetic oil in my 2L engine with excellent results (but
there is a lot of controversy over synthetics)

check all your brake lines and fuel lines, the fuel lines especially as
they are run at a pretty good presure (28psi) which can leak and start a
fire

also the cold start valve should be replaced with the newer ones, the old
ones would fail in the "on" position and could cause a fire, the newer
ones fail in the "off" position.  (not a common occurance, but once can
ruin your whole day!)
