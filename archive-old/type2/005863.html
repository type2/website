                                                 Happy Holliday!!
    With all the discussion about hard line tubing for brakes, I thought I
would remind everyone to be sure and use "Seemless Steel Tubing", and
"Double Flare" ends. Never use "Compression Fittings" or "Copper" tubing for
brakes. Always mount any length (run) of tubing over about a foot long to a
solid surface. Never run tubing near the exhaust, or any moving object.
    Play safe!
