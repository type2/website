Arvid:
I'd crawl underneath and check that all the wires hooked to the solenoid
are okay. I had similar problems with a 73 once and it turned out to be
a corroded-off lug on the wire that takes the main battery power from
the solenoid to the main harness. I could just be a loose nut there too.
When working on this terminal either disconnect the ground cable from
the battery (chassis end of the cable is easier), or be real careful as
your wrench is hot in relation to the surrounding metal and will readily
start welding anything it touches.
Another good thing to check is the condition of the connections of the
transmission to chassis ground strap near the front mount. 
With the rain the Pacific Northwest is having these seem like even more
likely trouble spots. (Yes, it made national news!)
