Hi NIck (et al),

Yes the rear sliding door hinge is bolted to the door easy to replace. Lots 
in the archives on how to do it.

Yes the hinge is the same on 68 up, panels, kombis and minibuses (except that 
its right/left handed depending on what side the door is on)

yes wire the door up - do it NOW - If the U part of the hinge is detached 
then your door will fall off if you open it!
When you come to do the replacement you will need to support the back of the 
door and its quite heavy
