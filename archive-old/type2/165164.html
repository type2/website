Chai, remember, these bolts were installed and torqued DRY at the
factory. Any amount of oil residue or whatever will significantly reduce
the amount of torque required to bring it to spec. I usually spay the
bolt holes, nuts or studs with brake cleaner and allow them to
completely dry before installation. Even then, I would be a bit
conservative, say 20 ft/lbs for a 24 ft/lbs fastener. Hope this helps,
Timo
