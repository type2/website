Your seat should swivel.  Open the door, sit in the seat, pull up on the
small rod between your legs, (no, no the other one :), and swivel toward the
drivers seat.  It's easier to swivel if you are sitting in the seat, but
with a little WD-40 or silicone you can get the seat to swivel without
sitting in it. Good luck.
