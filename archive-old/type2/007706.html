My normal use of the bus is for short trips, so the miles don't go on
very fast.  2000 mile oil changes are a bit more often than the
recommended 3000 mile intervals.

I do a valve adjustment with each oil change since my hands are
already oily and I like to keep tabs on the health of the top end.  If
one of the valves starts to lose its clearance repeatedly then I know
that the head needs attention, hopefully before something breaks loose
and trashes the pistons.

Yes, you can get by with less frequent valve clearance checks, but
more frequent won't hurt anything.
