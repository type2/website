I have friends who have had good luck with the Good Sam organization.
They are a lot friendlier to folks who pull trailers than the AAA.

OTOH, I used to live in a semi-rural area and apparently the local
Good Sam affiliate was telling folks that it was OK to dump their tanks in
a drainage ditch about 1/8 mile from our house.  When it rained, guess
where their effluent went?  Yep, right down the ditch in front of the
house!  Whenever we caught someone dumping they invariably had a
Good Sam sticker on the back of their vehicle.  Always had a bad feeling
toward the Good Sams organization since.
