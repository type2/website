Get rid of the bug muffler. Your bus rolls along at the astonishing rate of
55mph(ask me how I know), with a vacuum formed behind that loaf like body.
The vacuum sucks in the exhaust from the bug muffler. Any leaks in any seal,
guess what? You are breathing carbon monoxide. Not worth it. I learned this
on the list from others smarter than I used to be!  There is a good reason
why the bus muffler pipe exhausts to the side, believe me.

I just ordered a stainless steel stock muffler setup from Bus Depot for $99.
Last muffler you'll ever have to buy. Tom
