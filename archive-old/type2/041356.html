Hello All!

Well the weather  This weekend looks like it is going to be GREAT!!!!!!

If you want to find the Lake Powhatan campground, drive like this ->

>From exit two (practically in Asheville) on I-26 take 191 South; the
Interstate exit sign says in small numbers 191 and in big letters Blue Ridge
Parkway. There is a brown state recreational sign pointing the way; 6 Miles.
(actually 4.5 by the fambly car, not official VW mileage)

Pass the Biltmore Square Mall, it will be visible on your left. Ingles will
be on your right.

At either the fourth or fifth light, depending on which way you were coming
from on I-26, turn Right at Bent Creek Branch Road. There is another brown
state recreational sign at the intersection to guide you.

Travel on about 200 or 300 yards, there will be a sort of Y in the road.
Bear to the left, as the brown state recreational sign indicates. I believe
that at this Y the name of the road changes to Wesley Branch Road. I am not
sure, but it doesn't really matter anyway.

Pass the North Carolina Arboretum.

Pass the Hard Times Trailhead.

The road goes straight into the campground.
