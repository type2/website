However, the altimeter isn't all that accurate.  Figure on a couple of
hundred feet each way.

If the arrangement of satellites is particularly poor, I've seen errors of
2000 feet.  Sitting in my driveway, I saw a reading of -1500 feet!  After
a couple more satellites locked on, the error dropped to about 100 feet.
