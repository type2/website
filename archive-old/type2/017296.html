Regarding the size of the combustion chambers.
Presuming you have a 2.0L engine.  If the size of the
chamber changes by 1cc the difference in the compression
ratio will change by less than .1.
Probably not enough to worry about.

However, I am a strong believer that you should do what you
think is important to YOUR engine.  It gives you that feeling
of oneness when the baby fires up for the first time.
