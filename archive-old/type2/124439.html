I don't know of any adjustment. When mine did this, the wire connector had
come off the cold start valve. You've either got a problem with the cold
start valve, thermal time switch, or temp sensor. Until you can trouble
shoot it, I was able to just squirt a shot of carb cleaner into the air
distribution plenum to compensate before starting.
