Some nice carpeting I use is rice matting purchased per square foot at Pier 1
Imports. A way to keep the ends from fraying is candlewax. Wax the edges and 
does not fray. If use a white candle you can't see it. Smells very natural too.
Easy clean just a quick sweep.
Just an idea.
