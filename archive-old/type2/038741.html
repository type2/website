Having worked in the auto industry, and searched for good tires for my
Kombi, I came across literature for the Michelin MX.  The MX was listed in
the literature as being specifically for the Vanagon, but because it is not
a commonly stocked tire, you really have to know what you're asking for
or you may end up with MX4 passenger tires...

I understand and can relate to the general distrust of tire dealers, and am
skeptical when I take my old VW's to anyone for service I'm not able to do
for the car myself.  I've even been told by the local VW dealer they won't
do
a state inspection (which on my car is essentially a horn and light check)
because they don't have anyone to work on aircooled VW's...(I guess the
newer cars must have watercooled electrical systems or something)

I have 2 more MX's on order for the Kombi and will have them put on
while the Kombi gets its body worked over, and look forward to the
improvement they will have on the handling.
