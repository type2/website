>Crazy?  How to fix replace a valve spring without pulling the engine, or can you?

Disconnect the battery ( ground strap ).
Remove the valve cover and rotate the engine by hand until both valves are closed.
remove the spark plug and feed in 3 feet of 1/4 inch rope, then remove the valve spring
with the screw-type valve spring tool ( Sears _at_ $10 ). You just gotta love this trick !
