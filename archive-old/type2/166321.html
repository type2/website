Hi Richard- think I told you and the list about my gas tank blockage 
woes. Not being able to locate proper hose (not even the MB dealer could 
supply it) I used FI rated 5/16" hose (0.9mm larger) between tank and 
fuel filter. It clamped down fine on tank outlet stubs? I hate braiding 
as it hides if rubber is cracking. Also I wonder why the stock pump has 
that large inlet and is not transparent- the replacement pump (Ford F250 
truck Bosch I had to use) works fine but requires standard size inlet so 
I got a clear filter. Easy to tell if it's rust silted or likely has 
some large piece blocking the inlet pipe at the top.
