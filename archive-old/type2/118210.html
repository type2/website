Trivia mode on!

There were very few colors offered in the US for the early bays (1968 to 1970)
Red exterior (my favorite) had either all black basket weave of a two 
tone gray patterned like the one in the ad.
Blue exterior had black interior but I have seen a few blue interiors .
Elm Green ( I had a 1969 as nice as the one in the ad ) had black or 
two tone gray.

In the rarity dept. you would find the 1968 one year only Arizona 
Yellow seen at
http://www.72camper_dot_com/Page%2017-back%20cover.jpg
Lotus white model 22s are also uncommon but I have seen one in a 
junkyard with a red basketweave interior and it was WAY COOL.

The above applies to model 22 and 28. Trucks were usually tan 
basketweave. Konbis came in a few flavors of tan and I seen one US 
delivery with gray hardboard panels and I thing it was a 1968.

My current 68 was Arizona Yellow but was resprayed powber blue :(. 
The interior is black basket weave.
