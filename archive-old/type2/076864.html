I would look under dash for bad ground on switch.
Also try a brand new blinker relay, check fuses,(sometimes the simplest
things................:-0
If you hav a schematic and a test light you ought to be able to trace the
voltage to where you lose it.
Keep in mind that the power on the dash lights flow backwards, e.g. the
metel backing plate gets the juice and the bolb provides a route to ground
thus lighting up.
