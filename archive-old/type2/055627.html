Uh-oh, not again.  Short and sweet, the only reason you need high-octane
gas is if you have a high-compression engine.  VWs don't (not stock,
anyway).  Haynes says 91 RON octane (which VW also said).  Trouble is,
we don't use the RON method in the US.  We use the CLC method, which is
(RON+MON)/2 (check the sticker on the gas pump and you will be forced to
agree).

91 RON = 87 CLC.  So VW says "use the cheap gas."  

For more proof, see 
http://www.type2_dot_com/bartnik/octane.htm and
http://www.type2_dot_com/bartnik/manual/pg26.htm
