Subject: Re: [T2] Van sitting for two years, what can I expect?

If it were me I would bring a HD pickup and trailer or tow dolly. 
Has the van been run at all for the past two years, or did they just park it and leave it where it sat? It sounds like it won't start or run now. Has the soon to be PO tried to get it to run and failed, or just put it up for sale without knowing if it actually runs? 
Unless proper storage precautions were taken, the gas tank could be rusted and filled with nasty stuff. Even worse, the entire fuel system could be gunked up as well
How about the tires. If the van has been sitting in the same place, they could be flat spotted. The sidewalls are likely cracked from sun exposure.

If you can get it running when you go to pick it up. you'd be wise to have a shop look at suspension, steering, brakes and tires at the very least. 

Also, even though AAA will get you home, being broken down on the side of the road is a PITA, and breakdowns sometimes happen at the worst possible time and place.
