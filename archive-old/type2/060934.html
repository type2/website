Radial tires list number of plys, ply rating, load range, and load
rating.  The only number that really means anything is the load rating
in pounds.  Load range ("Standard", "C", or "D") is very deceptive as
tha actual load-carrying capacity of a load range varies widely with
tire size.  A load-range C tire in 195/75R14 has much less
load-carrying capacity than a standard load tire in 185R14.

The Korean tires I've run have been adequate in quality.  No
catastrophic failures, unlike a (supposedly high quality) European
brand.
