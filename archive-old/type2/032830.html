I don't understand what you mean by "auxillary wires" but to have an
ammeter work, you have to have it "in line" with the main (and only) + wire
to the battery.  This doesn't generally work very well because of the
location of the alternator and battery.  Voltmeters are a better choice to
monitor the condition of your batteries.
