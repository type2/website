Be aware that headerwrap traps moisture and can cause rusting quite quickly. On my hiperformance 2276Cc engine, the header was wrapped in it, and rusted quickly due to condensation/moisture between the wrap and the header. I would suspect any salt water due to winter driving etc, would REALLY enhance the corrosion.
