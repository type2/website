Basically, it is just a big washer that goes on your flywheel
large bolt.

The outside diameter is a little smaller than the seal...but not much.

Start the seal, put the washer on your flyweel bolt and tighten it till it

though!

You may be able to find a *large* fender washer  locally(Lowe's or 
other)...and you may need a 
couple of them or a spacer...haven't looked in a long time and am at work 
right now.
