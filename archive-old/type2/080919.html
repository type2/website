> Quick simple-ish question here that I really need to know quickly:
>
> What is the size of the pressure plate bolts on the 72-79 flywheel?  The
> Bentley claims M8x20, but that size is not in a format I recognize.  Usually
> it is something like M8 diameter x 1.25 thread x 20mm long.
>
> Any help is greatly appreciated... Trying to get a '74 Westi engine in and
> to Edisto Freeze Yer Toe by midnight tonight :)  I have a side bet with Dave
> Easterwood for a 6-pack of Guinness that this bus will get there, and I hate
> to loose bets!

Depends on your flywheel:
72/73 1700 uses a 210mm clutch with 8mm bolts.
74/75 1800 uses a 215mm clutch with 7mm bolts.
76 up 2.0L uses a 228mm clutch with 8mm bolts.
The 8mm bolts are 1.25 pitch. 1.25 is the 'standard' pitch for a 8mm bolt.
That is why the pitch is not given. The 8mm bolt normally uses a 13mm wrench
[Japanese cars use a 12mm wrench].
The 7mm bolts use a 8mm triple square socket.
