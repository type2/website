Jack:
Regrinders grind more off the base circle than off the tips of the
lobes. Old time hot rodders were able to grind the base circle
substantially smaller and leave the tips about where they were thereby
gaining quite a bit of lift. I think regrinding now is done mostly to
stock specs or by welding material onto the tips for  higher lifts and
durations. If anyone really wants to know I'll call Web-cam and ask em.
