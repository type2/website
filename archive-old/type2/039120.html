Here are some possible causes (some you have already mentioned).  All of
these need to be checked. If each are just a bit off, it will add up to one
hot engine:
1.  Timing too fast
2.  Carb too lean
3.  Missing or incorrect tin
4.  Engine seal damaged or missing
5.  Obstruction in the cooling tin
6.  Oil level
7.  Driving Habit
8.  Vaccum leak
9.  Valve problems
10. Oil cooler restriction
