pcs. ( this is what runs on the track slider )  You have to replace it...this

it fixed keep the mechanism and slider bar well lubed and clean...it's easy

then yougottofixitagain...oh, and don't forget the bottom slider and 
mechanism, that usually gets full of all kinds of crap to...
