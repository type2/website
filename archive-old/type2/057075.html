In pulling the heads off of the type IV motor that is being used for
the  conversion into a Thing.  We found somthing on the valve seats that

bolted it up again until they decided to ship the entire bus to the
wrecking yard.

Remember, there is a big grove in the seat and corresponding
indentations (although smaller) in the valve, just like would happen if
a bolt was wedged in there.  (This was cyl #1)
