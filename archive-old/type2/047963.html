	Hi Fred,

	I have been using a similar heater on my 1971 kombi. The brand is "Calix",
a Swedish manufacturer of all kinds of car heating devices. Mine is for
220Volts and rated to 125 Watts. It replaces the sump plate and has a oil
drain plug in the middle. 
125 Watts doesn't seem much but if it's plugged for an hour or two the
engine starts like in warm summers day even if the outside temp is at -30
Celsius. Even if it's called a "block heater" it's supposed to heat up the
oil to improve viscosity rather than the entire engine block.
