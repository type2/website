You're the winner of a cylinder head for a Wasserboxer Vanagon! <grin>

As far as sending the wrong part back to the supplier to exchange for the
correct one, it's pretty much the standard practice with most suppliers in
the auto parts world and you should be refunded that shipping cost.  Some
places will even send a replacement/correct part, but require charging you
for it again and then refunding you (to include the return shipping
charge[s]) when the wrong part is received back to them.  Just to send a
replacement item out at no charge (especially something as expensive as a
cylinder head) and trust you to send the wrong item back immediately just
isn't done in this day and age.

If they've got internet, take a digital pic and email it to them.  Maybe
that will sway the argument in your favor.
