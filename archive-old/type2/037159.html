automatics should shift smoothly, even to the point of not even feeling
the shift on a 76-79 automatic in good condition.

did you smell the tranny fluid. it should be purplish/red and should not
smell (burned)

my first guess would be a burned/bad brake band or planetary lock... (hand
to describe the parts without my manual handy) but read "expensive"
expect to put several hundred into that tranny.  

unlike the earlier VW tranny the later one has two different planetary 
brake systems, one a band brake the other a disk brake.  one or the other
seems to be malfunctioning...  could be very dirty filter, or sticky
weight in govenor, but I would put my money on a bad planetary brake.

if your really interested, pull the bottom pan and check for metal flakes
and in the filter.  (the Bentley manual explains how to pull the pan and
check the filter)

there is usually a little bit of "sludge" in the bottom of the pan but
much more than a thimble full means the tranny fluid hasn't been changed
lately, much more than a flake or two of metal look real close.
