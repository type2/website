why not take off the head and cylinders and lap the cylinders into the 
head so you *know* you have a good seal.  While it's a mild pain to get 
the cylinders off, it might make things easier in the long run.

Also, a quick way to see if you have some kind of seal would be the 
prussian blue, or just draw with a sharpie on the cylinder and rotate it 
a small bit and see if it wipes off on the head.

Also, when you have your head off, pour some liquid into the intake and 
also into the exhaust (one at a time, of course) and see if it holds it 
without leaking through the valve.
