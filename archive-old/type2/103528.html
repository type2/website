I like to keep some little medical clamps around.  The kind you see on TV 
with the long handles and the finger loops at the ends.  They have different 
sizes, and they click closed at varying degrees of tension.  They are useful

place during installation, hold wires while soldering, all kinds of stuff.

drug stores.  Highly recommended addition to the tool box!
