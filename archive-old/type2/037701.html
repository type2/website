I have solved this by using a modfied air filter system that sucks WARM air rite out of those warm air ducts on either side of the engine, (on the inside)...I dont have heat and that fan in the engine compartment. the basic thing is in cold weather..."find warm air from the engine".

I hope this helps. I am no turbo brain with this but it has worked for me in VERY cold Ohio winters!
