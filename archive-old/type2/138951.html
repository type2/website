> The rationale behind the WR 1 is straightforward. It is not "another"
> solenoid, it's a relay. Its purpose is to reduce the resistance in
> wiring between the battery and the starter. This is ohm's law, which

The wiring between the starter and solenoid and the battery is as short as
it was before the WR-1 install.

the solenoid by about 10 ft. as well as divert the current that powers the
solenoid bypassing the high resistance of the ignition switch and it's
wiring.
