We use the icebox in our '76 standard Westy, pile all the loose clothing
and blankets we have on board on top and on the side of it, works GREAT!
ie, come back from 3 day trips with ice still solid. We also save any
"Omaha Steaks" styrofoam boxes we come across, they're made of dense foam
and work very well. We have a Coleman which is a handy table but not much
good for keeping stuff cold...

For $125 you can buy a *lot* of ice...
