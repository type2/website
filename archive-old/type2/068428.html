One note on WHY you should wire it to the buzzer or to a continuosly hot
wire and NOT to any wire which is hot when the key is turned to the "on"
position:

If you run your radio with the motor off but the key "on" you run the risk
of a burnt coil, as the coil will be charged when the key is in it's "on"
position.
