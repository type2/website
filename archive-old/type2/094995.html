friction as well as chemical probability starts to creep in real quick.

blow.   I have seen engineering articles that stated the odds of any 
automotive engine hitting 100k was very small.  this dated back to the 
mid 80s.  there was a note in the article that stated that changing 
heads at something like 50k would reduce risk of engine failure.  the 
vast percentage of engine failures i have seen AC or WC have been heads
