They must also want to sell a lot of carbs for their advertisers.

If the butterfly valve contacts and rests on the carb bore, the 
sharp edged steel butterfly valve will quickly wear a groove
into the soft aluminum carb body, destroying any pretense
of a smooth idle or idle transition.

Turn in the fast idle screw until it just touches, then 1/2 to 1 more
turn. That will make the gap small enough not to leak, but the carb
spring will put force on the fast idle screw, not the carb bore.
