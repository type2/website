When we last discussed the thermostat cable issue I believe that you 
mentioned that you had a 69.  If so, you are in luck manual-wise.  Volkswagen 
itself published a service manual for the early (68-70) baywindow busses.  
This manual addresses all areas of Type 2 maintenance for those years.  Some 
very detailed stuff.  Internal wiring diagrams for switches, for instance.  
However, some procedures are of the "barebones" approach to writing.  I find 
that, for my needs, my other manuals supplement this one quite well.
