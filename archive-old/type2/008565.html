You buy, borrow,  or beg a copy of the Idiot's guy, and immediately
turn the the chapter labelled "red light on!" and start from
there.

You also bum a ride to the closest VW flaps, and buy plugs, points,
condensor, cap, rotor, oil, feeler guage, new fan belt, and
oil sump gasket kit.

Saturday morning, get up bright and early, and do a full tune up
including adjusting the valves and changing the oil.  By this time you
have the book, and you'll know how to do it.

Between the tune up procedure, and the red-light procedure, you should
have a sweet running bus by saturday afternoon.
