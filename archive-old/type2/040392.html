I have a setup somewhat like that. I got a 8 X 6 tarp and put 2 screws in 
the drain gutter on the side of the bus. Then I got some rope and tied the 
tarp from each end nice and tight around the screws, then I take 2 tent 
poles and put them on the other side of the tarp at each corner and tie a 
piece of rope to the poles at the top and secure it to the ground with tent 
pegs.
