Hi Blake,

My method has always been to coat the entire gasket with vaseline.
It soaks into the cork and 'softens' it.  The key thing at that point
is to make sure that the surfaces are clean and she seals right. They
always come off easily (unbroken), and they get replaced when they
NEED to get replaced.  Squiggle the covers around gently until they
hit a spot where they won't move.  O' course, make sure the covers
are OK...flat surfaces and clean.  Perhaps there's a burr on your
heads somewhere ?  

Hope it helps, Good Luck,
