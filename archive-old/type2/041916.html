David Schwarze wrote:

>
> As Karl said, you are using the wrong distributor for your application.
> However, all is not lost - you can use the distributor you have if you
> disconnect and plug the vacuum retard line and set your initial timing to
> about 0TDC.  This will give you the total advance you need, and will work,
> as in the engine will run and not self-destruct.  However the advance curve
> may not perfectly match what the fuel injection expects, and you will be
> running a few less degrees advance at idle.  I expect that those two things
> would not make much difference but I thought I'd better mention them.
>
