Gary,

This does not sound like the original floor to me.  On a '75 I stripped a
while back, with what seemed to be the original floor, the construction
was: 4 pieces of ~3/8" fiberboard cut to fit the recessed area of the
floor (the corrugated section), but not the door edge.  This was covered
by a piece of 1/8" masonite.  Over the masonite was a sheet of plastic and
vinyl flooring.  The masonite and vinyl had some ~3/4" oval head sheet
metal screws at the door, but was otherwise held in place by the
cabinetry.  I *think* this was original, but (a) maybe not and (b)
different years may have had different construction techniques.
