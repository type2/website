On Thu, 2 Aug 2007, Jason Meyer wrote:

> Do I need to purchase a tank and then fill it? Where might I get one? I saw
> one at Harbor Freight, but the tank was $79! And that was still empty!

you need to rent or purchase an empty tank, and then take it to any
compressed gas place (Robert's is a big one)and they swap it for a
full tank. I use an 80cuft tank (mid size, maybe 3ft high) and it was
about $40 for an Argon/CO2 "welding mix", which seems to work best.

$79 depending on the size may or may not be a good buy. I don't think you
want much less than an 80cuft tank (I have 2) unless you don't hardly do
any welding at all.

you also still need a regulator for the gas tank. need one specifically
for Mig welding gas or an adjustable one.

> Since it seems that the gas portion of welding with my MIG is no more a
