Hi Ben,

First check the coupler at the rear of the shift rod where it connects to the 
shaft into the gearbox. This looks like half of a universal joint and has two 
rubber blocks in it which cause all sorts of shift problems when they fail.

If that isn't your problem then check for wear on the stop plate at the base 
of the gear stick - access obvious just undo the bolts at the base of the 
gear stick. If its worn it will be obvious with deep gouges in the faces. 
Either replace with a new one or apply a bead of weld and file down to shape.

Good luck
