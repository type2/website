Tim:

I would have thought the gaskets you received with heads would be the 
fairly thick gasket that goes between the cylinder and heads, not the thin 
cylinder spacers that go between cylinder and block.  The latter shims 
come in a variety of thicknesses that you can only determine with the 
engine partially assembled, so a head provider could only guess at the 
right dimension.
