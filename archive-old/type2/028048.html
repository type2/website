Incidentally, Knoebels Amusement Park, for those who are interested, is well
worth visiting, very reasonably priced, clean, and with lots of rides for
young kids as well as adults and older kids.  No admission fee - you can pay
by the ride or buy a day-long pass, and the per-ride prices are quite low.
Even the parking's free. One of the oldest remaining family-owned
free-admission amusement parts, with a long history.  www.knoebels_dot_com .
