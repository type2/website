An easy way to tell which seat your Deluxe (or Kombi) had is by the holes
in the mat if it still has it, or by the seat mounting holes welded in the
belly floor.  I'm not sure if the full or 2/3 middle seat was necessarily
dictated by the walk thru/non-walkthru models.  Hope this helps a little.
