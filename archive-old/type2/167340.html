Sure can!
You might be able to find a socket in your collection that has the correct 
dimensions <close is ok> 

To remove the race,, I might add...  you can use a cold chizel or punch... 
or big screw driver, look for the notch in the hub that lets you get a good 
grip on the race... clean out the grease to find it easier. 

To install the new race,, I have used a vice to press it in, using a 
matching socket with a slightly smaller dia.  or the old race passed over 
the grinder enough to make it slightly smaller....
you can tap them in with a hammer,, or use a press,, or squeeze them in with 
a vice..  just go easy when starting so they don't start off crooked. 

hope that helps
