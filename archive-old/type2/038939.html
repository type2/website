On a stock 72 engine, the connection goes through the front (Front Is
Front) engine tin on the lower right (passenger) side.  My guess is that
you'll find an open, 1/2" diameter hole in this area.  With an aftermarket
carb, there's no telling.  The PO might have simply disconnected it because
there was no place for it on the manifold.  If that's true, you might have
to drill and tap a port for it yourself.  The area it will need to attach
to is the plenum beneath the carb, before it goes off into the four pipes.
Check this area for plugged ports - maybe you'll get lucky.
