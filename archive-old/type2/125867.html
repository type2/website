Hey Blake,
           There are a number of reasons a battery can go bad.  If your vehicle 
has an alternator with a bad diode, that will toast it.  If your generator 
regulator is 'way out of whack, that will toast it.  If you just purchased the 
bus, it is not inconceivable that the PO gave you a marginal battery, AMHIK.  
Obviously, your charger results indicate the battery does not want to hold a 
charge.  At this point, it would not be wise just to install a new battery 
without doing some testing.  It can be a PITA to pull the existing gen/alt and 
take it to your FLAPS for testing.  If your vehicle is at all driveable, you 
could get a "comprehensive" charging system test at some FLAPS.  Maybe others 
on this List would have further thoughts...... Glenn
