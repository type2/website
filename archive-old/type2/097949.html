long bend a 90 degree at one end about 2 inches long. cut out a U shape on 
the end thats bent. drill a oversize hole in the bend. use air in the cly. to

the valves. seen a whole bunch of these wore out. Means pulling the head at 
that point. The rope in the clys. works but is a PITA compared to the air 
power. The valves tend to give making re-atttacting the keepers a job.
