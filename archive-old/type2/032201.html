--> Well, among other things, if you were misfiring on one or more
cylinders (bad spark plug wire, perhaps?  Bad points?  Some other ignition
problem?) you'd have a lot of unburned fuel going out the exhaust.  This
would probably burn in the converter and result in exactly what you had
happen. 

I suspect the reason your van bucked and stalled is that the overheated
innards of the converter collapsed and plugged off the exhaust.  If this
is the case, reinstalling it would be, obviously, inadvisable.
