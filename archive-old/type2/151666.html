Coming late to the party, but!

For 30 + years I have used the yellow 3m weatherstrip adhesive on the 
valve cover side.  

Make sure that the head to valve surface is absolutely clean and smooth.

My valve covers *never* leak and I usually only change them about once 
every two years.

When I do change them, a sharpened putty knife between the cover and the 
gasket get the seals(and glue) out pretty easily.
