Just to set the record straight, I was in error on that post in regards to
year applicability of the Enrichment/Lean-Out Resistor Unit.  It only fits
on 1972 32PDSIT applications.  73-74 did not use this unit.  I believe I
made a follow-up post on that when questioned by a few list members and
they were right.  My apologies.  BTW, I have a couple of NOS Carb Tops with
these units still for sale for that hapless 72 owner who needs one. - See
the Oddball Page on my website.  I also have a NOS Right Side 32PDSIT
Carburetor for the Man Trans 74 - again see the Oddball Page.
