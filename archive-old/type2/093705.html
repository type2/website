Steve Dolan once told me that 15" is on the very low end of acceptible vacuum. I bet he would say not to run the engine for any length of time, especially drive it with a reading of 10".

At sea level I think a FI bus in great running condition (compression 135psi, new hoses, etc) should be able to read 18".  I have got it to read 17-17.5" and I'm 600 ft. above sea level (vacuum drops 1" for every 1000 ft. you are above sea level).
