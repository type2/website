Any suggestions for securing the wheel against turning?

My 72 Riviera has a pair of rather firmly attached axle nuts. I put my 3/4"
drive ratchet on a nut, put a little chock block under the wheel, stood on
the end of the ratchet. The ratchet's two feet long, so my 150 lbs means 300
lb-ft. No movement in anything. I jumped up and down on the ratchet, and the
wheel turned.
