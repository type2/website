> With reference to the vacuum can replacement, is it possible to replace just
 the vacuum can on the original dual-advance distributors (74 with dual

Yes, 2 screws and a C clip hold it on. I am assuming you have this distributor?

Bus & Pickup 1974 * 1800 Manual Trans Dual Carbs
Note: Bosch lists this application as Federal Models Only
Distributor: VW 021-905-205N, Bosch 0231 181 005
Can Use: VW 021-905-205J, Bosch 0231 173 009 or 010
Vacuum Can: 07 115
Timing Set At:: 10deg ATDC _at_ 800-950rpm w/strobe, vacuum hose(s) connected
Advance/Retard Range: Vacuum: 7-12deg Adv, 16-18deg Ret; Centrifugal: 9-14deg _at_ 2000rpm, 21-25deg _at_ 3400 rpm

The 07 115 vacuum unit means you should have 953 on the arm. The 'can use' dist number 205J, has a 07 113
vacuum unit:

Bus & Pickup 1973 * 1700 Manual Trans
Distributor: VW 021-905-205J, Bosch 0231 173 009 or 010 > 021-905-205N, 0231 181 005
Can Use: VW 021-905-205N, Bosch 0231 181 005
Vacuum Can: 07 113
Timing Set At:: 10deg ATDC _at_ 800-950rpm w/strobe, vacuum hose(s) connected
Advance/Retard Range: Vacuum: 7-12deg Adv, 16-18deg Ret; Centrifugal: 9-14deg _at_ 2000rpm, 21-25deg _at_ 3400 rpm

The 07 113 should have 925 on the arm.

07 115 = 953 = Bosch part number 1 237 121 953
07 113 = 925 = Bosch part number 1 237 121 925

Both have the exact same curve.
