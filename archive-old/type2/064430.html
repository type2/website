I painted my newly-rebuilt Type IV engine, block, cylinders, heads & all 
with Rustoleum semi-gloss black, diluted about half-and-half with naptha 
or paint thinner, I don't remember which.

After almost a year's driving, the paint's still in fine shape (I used 2 
or 3 light coats).  Seems to work fine for me.
