Subject: VW Fest 98 campout details

Heyyy,

It's time for the Micro's to Camp!  come to our event, VWFest 98 to be held at the Lazy Day Campground in Mineola, MO.  It will be held the second weekend in October -9th thru 11th.  This is an all VW event.  I nice mellow time to reflect on a busy camping season with kind folks.

To get there:  Mineola is between St. Louis and Columbia on hwy 70.  Take the Danville Mineola exit at mile marker 170, and go south on hwy J approximately 2 miles.  The campground is on the left.  You can't miss it!  The campground is quite nice, with heated bathrooms and electric on most sites.  

Costs:  $10 per night per camping unit (this could mean $10 for as many vehicles as you can fit on a space of ground that has been designated as a "camping unit", but don't count on it).  If you want to hang out but not stay at the campground, the campground is charging a $3 entry fee per person for the entire weekend.  This will qualify you for door prizes and contests.  Anything to donate?  Whatever will be gratefully accepted.

There will be a chili cook off on Saturday and a fishing contest all weekend.  Saturday we will take a 17 mile scenic caravan to the historic German town of Hermann, to check out their famous "Octoberfest" celebration.  There are wineries to tour, beer and brats to be consumed, and the fall foliage is fantastic.

More questions?  Call or e-mail Rick Dennis at 1-314-488-5256  allerleivw_at_mocty_dot_com
or write him at VWFest c/o Rick Dennis, 253 E. Booneslick Rd., Jonesburg, MO 63351

See you there!
