Some folks have indeed transplanted Porsche 356, 911 and 912 engines into 
buses, but they are rare and were NEVER stock!  Not something you need to worry 
much about; it will be fairly evident if the bus has either the 356 or 911

the bus stock L-jet fuel injection or transferred the 914's D-jet.  Other 
than that, the 914's engine is very similar to a bus engine and the conversion 
shoulsn't cause any problems.
