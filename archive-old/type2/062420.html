I did something similar to what Michael describes, but I used a $3.00 red
funnel that has a handle and a half-dozen screw-on adapters to change the
size of the neck. I slipped a clean piece of garden hose onto this and into
the filler hole. Neat thing was that the handle allowed me to "hook" the
funnel into the channel at the top of the bus, freeing my hands and allowing
me to watch the filler hole more closely.
