I would check the donut shaped bushings that are between the spring plate and the
torsion tube.  My Bus had a similar problem and it drove me crazy trying to hunt it
down.  Sometime later, when I adjusted the rear suspension, I found the bushings were
badly worn.  I got it adjusted, put new bushings in, and the clanking noise went away.
Expensive?  All it took to fix was about 10 bucks and half a day.
