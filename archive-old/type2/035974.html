Volks,
On the FI engine the safety screen would have to be removed to rotate the 
crankshaft with a wrench. So my alternative has been to put the van in 4th 
gear, on a level surface, and roll it backwards, adjusting in 4 steps.
Anything unsafe about that, since we can reach the valves, albeit with some 
puffing, without any jacking?
Also, about the two-step adjust formula, that's the first time I've heard 
that idea. Are you sure that the adjustment will be as accurate as the 
4-step? I got the impression that the engineers thought the cam location for 
the "extra" valves to be adjusted via that procedure  might not be in the 
same position as if the cylinder were at true TDC.
