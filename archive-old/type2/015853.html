	Hi again.

	For those who are really bothered with the dead spot caused by rare
cabinettary, curtains and such this may be of help.
Go to your FLAPS and ask for a replacement truck mirror glass. Here in
Europe there seems to be some kind of standardisation on truck mirrors, if
you crack the mirror on your truck you just have to buy the glass not the
whole armature. These are slightly konvex so you get a larger view. Sadly
enough there are nothing that will fit the bus outside mirror so you have
to buy a slightly larger one. Then have the guy at the local glass/window
shop cut the mirror to fit the busses original. It's hard to cut konvex
glass with the help of a normal glass cutter. Don't ask me how I know....
Got forteen years of bad luck left from my last attempt:-)

This way we don't need to see busses with oversized truck mirrors on the
road, something that will make the stockisthewaytogo-guys glad.

Larger inside mirrors are IMHO dangerous in their own way. They take up
space in the windshield wich is more dangerous than the blind spot on the
right side of the bus. 

Be different!
