A few weeks ago, whilst driving Splitty, I waved at an early
Porsche 356 (my dream car). To my extreme delight, he waved
back!! Splitty's and my proudest moment.

random people (old men on bicycles, little kids in push-
chairs). It amuses me if not them. On the way to the ferry
last week Damon and I waved at a guy in a Carrera -- he
looked at us like we were lower alien life-form (with a mixture
of disbelief, horror and disgust). It was very funny :-)
