Please note that the GVWR does not tell you anything about how much the
vehicle weighs.  What it does tell you is the MAXIUMUM permissible weight
including cargo and passengers.  That is, you can load the thing up until
it weighs 5072 lbs. and still be within safe limits for the chassis and
brakes.

Someone with a Vanagon owner's manual can probably find the exact weights
in the specifications section (that's where I found them in the Bus
owner's manual).
