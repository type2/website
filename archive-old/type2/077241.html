> Years ago, shortly after Bosch started making these, I bought a set for a
> 1600 DP T1 engine.  I had a very bad experience, fouling, running hot,

> bad thing" in the T1 engine, so I ditched them and have never looked back.

> fouled too easily and that the heat range was wrong for the T1 engine.
>
> The caveats to this story are:
> (a) it was *not* a new engine;
> (b) it was a long time ago, so the plug design may have changed.
