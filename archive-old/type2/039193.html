I prefer to start in the back.  with the center in front of the fan,
there after working it around towards the front of the bus. on the right
side (looking toward the front of the bus)about halfway to the front I
stuff it all through to the underside and put that part up from under the
van.  the wiring and fuel lines and air cleaner leave very little room
from the top.

if there is a gap it is in the center of the firewall.  reasons, shorter
pieces to work around the harder parts in the front, if there is a gap in
the foam the center front has no heat source so there is less heat to seep
in if there is a gap....also if per chance it begins to unravel or come
out it won't get sucked into the fan
