VW put a van heater under the center of the floor (BA-6???) so it can be
done.

I have a BA-4 mounted under the front of my van, in the  same location as
the AC condenser radiator.  I put a seperate fan essentially in the glove
box, with ducts to the heater, output is fed back into the regular heat
duct just behind the front axle

there are some problems, not much space, vulnerable location, and it gets
dirty as all get out.   
advantages, there is a very short heat path, recirculates air mostly in
the cab area, easy to work on

I would be very reluctant to put a heater inside the passenger compartment
because of the CO threat.  also when running they do make some noise. and
personally I don't want either gas or fire inside my cabin no matter how
well it is controlled
