The 1mm step is a 'head gasket' (it should be removable) and is stock in most type4 engines. 
Allegedly, VW released a 'white paper' around 1990 that specifies that these be removed and the decrease in deck height be compensated for with a shim of the same height between the cylinder and case.
I replaced them in my engine and added a .070" shim under the cylinders to lower the compression even further. Long story.

You have two right side (1/2 side) heads from the 1800cc dual-carbed 1974 bus engine.  These heads have the biggest stock valves VW ever released in a bus engine.  Shouldn't make a difference that they're both 'right side' as long as the air injection ports are sealed.
