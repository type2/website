I would check EVERYTHING for leaks, especially in your intake.  Check
all the hoses that run  to all the various places from the  intake 'S'
tube.  Check the line that runs to the aux air regulator and make sure
that the little elbow isn't broken.  If you're vacuum lines are old, put
new ones.  It's cheap and easy to do.  Check that your distributor is
working correctly.  If not, rebuild it, or oil it, or one of the two. 
Make sure you're timing is correct and you get your proper advance.

also check the manifold vacuum hoses (one that goes to the fuel pressure
regulator and decel valve), and the power assist hose for you brake.  If
you want to be hard core, check your injector seals because those could
be leaking.  Might as well replace all your fuel lines while you're at
it.. hehehhe.. granted it's a little time-consuiming :)

check the first few things and see if that helps, if not, move to the
more advanced.  But don't go messing with your AFM until your sure
everything else works..
