Hi Jen,

Its likely that you won't find a 46mm socket in 1/2 drive so you will end up 
with a 3/4 inch drive socket and breaker bar. these will be easier to find 
somewere that specialses in tools for lorries or Farm tractors (or VWs)

The torque wrench problem is easier to over come - put a long scaffold pole 
on the breaker bar, put a mark on it at a measured distance from the centre 
of the nut, divide the required number of foot pounds by the distance of your 
mark from the centre (in feet), hold the bar at the mark, stand on the 
bathroom scales and pull up until your weight 'increases' by the amount 
you've just worked out.
