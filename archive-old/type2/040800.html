On Fri, 2 Jun 2000, Alex Wisniewski wrote:

> I have some heater boxes which look original and
> and since just the case around the boxes is in bad shape
> was wondering how you went about patching them.
> My exhaust system is of now and I am figure this is the
> best time if ever to work on it.
> Did you buy one of those exhaust patch kits at a FLAPS?
> and if so does that stuff work on big holes?

No, I made patches from sheet metal and brazed them over the holes.  I
also made sheet-metal sleeves to close up the egged-out holes where the
exhaust pipe enters the can.  This is a permanant fix and has lasted for
years on our two VWs.

The "muffler bandage" kits might work for a temporary fix, but I'd be wary
of the fumes they
might introduce to the heating system.  Better to bite the bullet and get
out the torch.
