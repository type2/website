Hey fellow buggers,
I am a tool and die maker, for 15 years...

Just a thought about ez outs... DONT BUY CHEAP ONES !!!
When you have a broken stud, you can still do a wide variety 
of "surgeries" to remove it, such as heat, the welded nut, drill
& tap left handed, or ez out.  But, if you break off an ez out,
(which happens much more often with cheap oriental ones)
then to quote the old proverb "Houston, we have a problem"
The best way to get a broken stud out, which has a broken ez out in
it is with an edm machine...which most people, and most machine
shops don't have.  Please, do yourself a favor, get good 
ez outs.
