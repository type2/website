I can agree completely with Bob,

I snowboard 2 to 3 days a week up here in the Canadian rockies during 
the winter, in temps that sometimes drop as low as -30. With my bilge

hitch. I have a "block heater" that is bolted on the bottom of the oil 
screen plate and I wasn't prevented from starting at all, well other 
than when my bus was a brick of ice and I couldn't open the doors. The 
block heater is just a heating element really, the only annoying this 
about it is the power cable melted where it contacted my exhaust at 
some point ... luckily I found that out the other day while doing  an 
oil change so I can fix it pre-winter, its only two bolts and its off.
