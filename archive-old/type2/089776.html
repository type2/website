On my 71 1600, I capped off the air ducts from the fan housing
and mounted a three speed blower motor in the spare tire well.

great for heat (lousy for noise, tho')...
If you live in a very warm climate or are using the engine in a
dune buggy, say, the air ducts might not be used and the "extra"
air better used to cool the engine...
