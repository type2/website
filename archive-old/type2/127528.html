Subject: Re: [T2] Whats the secret for removing push rod tubes??

On Thu, Jun 17, 2004 at 08:52:15AM -0700, John Baker wrote:
> I have been working on a 2.0 liter 78 westy that has
> BIG oil leaks from the push rod tubes. I am replacing
> the seals, a few of the push rod tubes practically
> fell out when I removed the rocker arms, but another
> few are being very difficult!!! So anyone have any
> tips on removing stubborn push rod tubes from a 2 liter??

I have used this system before and it works well. Take a vice grip cut
some fuel hose to put over the sharp edges. then close it around the
push rod tube. not tight enough to actually close over it, but just
enough so when you move it toward one of the flaired ends it will
grab. then tap the grips w/ a mallet, while twisting. It seems to keep
from scarring up the tubes and bending them.
