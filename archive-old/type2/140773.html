The seal you need to replace is the complete master cylinder. The brake fluid is draining into the booster. 
Your booster is filling up with brake fluid.

Now you also need to replace the booster also. Too much brake fluid in it destroys the valves in it.

With or without vacuum, your bus is unsafe to drive NOW.
