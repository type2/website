Joel its very funny, but I too installed the rear glass on my back hatch
this weekend. I got mine from Bus Depot too.

I found it easier to start at the bottom and work the sides and then up. I
used palmolive this time, but gojo hand cleaner works best. You know the
kind that smell funky, but not with gritty stuff in it. Coat the inside of
the seal and the rope (cloth). Go slow with someone helping. We found it
easier to push and pull the window til the lip of the seal cooperates. It
was tough to pull the string out in a couple of places, but it agreed with
some patience. Never go any farther if the seal isn't on right. Fix it with
a hook tool or screwdriver and then go on. It was kind of tough for us too.

Here is a link to mine:
http://www.72camper_dot_com/69exteri.htm
