REF: Dipstick gasket:  This is the rubber bellows tube that connects the
dipstick tube passing through the fan shroud to the dipstick tube on the
block. It is almost certainly the original and almost as certainly
perished. If you are agile you can replace it with the shroud on the bus.
Remove the dipstick, remove the old bellows, insert the new bellows, and
replace the dipstick.
