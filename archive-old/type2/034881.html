I used shock cord, (the stuff bungee cords are made of).
You can find it in bulk at hardware stores.
I just stretched it above and below the windows and threaded it
through a wide hem in some homemade curtains.
I also stretched a piece above and behind the front seats to block the front
and just let the bottom hang free.
