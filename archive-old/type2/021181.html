On the T4 engine I use a pry bar, some call it a crows foot, some call it a
heeled pry bar, It is about 11" long and has a point at one end, and a bent foot
with a heel at the other end.. the bent end lets me lift the bale over the
detent bumps in the cover , then the point end to walk it the rest of the way
off 
I use the bent end to replace it, till it is lifted up part way,, then i change
to pry using the other end against the rocker cover, and the bale..to pop it up
the rest of the way
I usually use a hammer to tap the cover forward and back a little to ensure it
is seated
I forgot this tapping check twice now, and it has haunted me 2 miles down the
road
