For what it's worth -- the center bolt bushing has been pulled out
of my rear carrier for as long as I've had the bus -- 7 years/90 000 mi.
I have had the big bolts break on me, which is a pita....the crank
pulley falls into the top of the rear carrier, burning up the fan belt
and immobilizing the bus.

I think you're safe running it with the big bolts, but the next time
you have then engine out, I'd at least think about getting a sleeve put
in.
