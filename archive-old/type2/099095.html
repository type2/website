John,

I've got a '73 panel that a PO put a 1500 SP in.  The transmission is a
'72 but I believe it is the same as a '73.

Performance is a little better than snail like and I can cruise most
roads at 60.  On hills I drop down to 45 or so in 3rd.  

Short answer: It will work, but I'd rather have a Type 4, say around
2500 cc.

One important thing...  He will need the filler that goes between the
type 1 engine tin and the type 4 engine compartment.  I got mine (PO
didn't have one) from Creative Car Craft in Florida.
