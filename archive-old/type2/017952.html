It's unusual that there would be no notch. It's more common for the notch to 
be nowhere near where you think it should be. You
don't need the notch to find top dead center for #1 (see Muir), once your 
there, clean the rim of the distributor with solvent until
you find the notch, then swing it around until it lines up with the rotor 
notch.
