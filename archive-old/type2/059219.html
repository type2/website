At 01:11 PM 10/6/98 -0700, GRAHAM.MINGST_at_chase_dot_com wrote:
>Does anyone know how to replace the rear engine mounts on a 78 bus?  I have
>read through the Bentley book, but haven't been able to find a section that
>covers this.  Both mounts look like they are shot.  I found a piece of one
>of them laying on the bar they bolt to.  How critical are they? How much of
>a PITA is it to replace them?  Any help would be greatly appreciated..

They are easy to replace but I hear they are expensive to buy.  Replacing
those mounts is the only time you should ever put a jack under the engine,
short of engine removal.  You will have to take off the rear tin and
possibly the exhaust and fan housing to get at the upper mount bolts.  This
is time consuming, but not difficult.  Just support the engine with the
jack (just support it, do not raise it!), remove the crossbar, then remove
the mounts.
