Subject: RE: [T2] Re: re: por-15 on pop tops

The directions I got say that it has to be top coated if it is exposed to
sunlight. The never state exactly what will happen, but they say that the UV
will harm it. Another problem might be that the rust is still active because
it is getting moisture from behind? POR 15 needs to be applied to both sides
of the metal, if it is a small hole, or it will not be very effective.
Surface rust should not matter, one side will work. Try their chassis coat
black over the POR 15 for UV protection or their primer and your own
topcoat. I have things around the house that have been painted with just POR
15 for almost 1 year and it has not changed color yet or rusted through.
