That is the 'brake pressure regulator'. It is used on 71 and up buses [and
just about every vehicle with disc front/drum rear brakes]. It limits the
pressure to the rear brakes under hard braking to stop rear wheel lock-up.
Value?  I don't know.
Need to have? Very very rare to go bad.....
