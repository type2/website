Having owned and sometimes lived in VW busses over the years, one of my
'hobbies' is MacGyver-ing the already excellent and well thought out interior
into something truely livable. I am planning to set up a web site with this,
and Free Parking (who likes to pay $12 a nite to sleep in a parking lot) as
their themes.

I like keeping the soft bags of 'Blue Ice' in the freezer section packed
pretty solid. They freeze up nicely on a four to five hour drive, and keep
the fridge cool for days afterward.

can be drawn to keep systems going, as I recall the fridge draws about 8
amps.
