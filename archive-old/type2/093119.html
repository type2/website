Subject: Oberg oil filter, was: Re: [T2] Temp Guages?

At 10:44 PM 8/15/2002 -0400, you wrote:
>I have an O'Berg oil filter..there is some sender on the top that is
>disconnected, could this be a temp gauge sender?

No, that is the sender for the "clean the filter" warning light.
You MUST get this hooked up.  As the filter collects crap,
the pressure builds on the "in" side--which bends up, and will
eventually ruin the filter screen--while the pressure drops on
the "out" side--less oil gets to where it's needed.  Eventually,
none gets there.  Obergs DO NOT have a high pressure bypass.......

To calibrate, loosen retaining nut, tighten screw until light just goes on,
then back off a half turn.  Tighten retaining nut.
