>  Any helpful hints on  a starter?  Gone!  Just clicks, need to touch it with
>a hammer to start, and I need an extra person, it wont just let me hit it
>and start.


Take your starter apart. Clean and grease everything. It is your solenoid 
hanging up. Common problems for a VW. There are "hard start" kits which 
consist of a relay and a bit of wire for $20. You can duplicate these kits 
with a typical relay ($6 at Radio Shack) or a Ford starter relay (some say 
it won't work but mine has puttered along for a long, long time). The Ford 
relay is closer to $10-$15 but I had a used one on hand.

What is happening is that due to the key switch (ignition switch) being in 
the front, the battery in the back, and the starter in the back - the 
battery current has to flow all the way to the front of the vehicle, 
through the switch, and back to the starter. With age corrosion can set it 
and electrical resistance goes up and the starter reliablity goes down 
(coupled with a starter that due to age needs some maintenace).

Muir has a long explaination of this problem in his book and the hard 
starter relay schematic.
