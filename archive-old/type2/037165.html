You need to make sure, first of all, that there is ample ventilation around
the amp. Most of the newer amps have thermo-switches in them that will cut
off the amp if it gets too hot.  Think of it as if it were your engine (heat
= bad).  If that doesn't help (or is not the case) then all I can say is
check your connections.  Your bus should have enough juice to run the thing.
