This definately sounds like the axle nut to me too. Same thing happened
except all bearings were in good shape and the CV joints were new. Basically
the shop I had do the brakes for me tightened the axle nut like a US car
would be done. This nut should be put on with about 250 lbs torque so if you
can spin it (the one with the problem) without a cheeter bar and standin on
it then you probably found your problem.

I use a 3/4" drive breaker bar with a six foot pipe over it on a 1 13/16
socket to get the job done.

Mine wouldn't quit this blasted noise no matter how tight I got it and it
burnished the mating surfaces. So I ground the nut and the hub mating
surface flat so I wouldn't have to buy parts. Did this about a year ago and
not a problem since. It, like you said was VERY LOUD too.

If you search the archives for this, a little over a year ago I wrote a
detailed how to on the repair.
