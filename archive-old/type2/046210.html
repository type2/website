When I painted mine I used some paint recommended from a hardware
for the poptop - but it had to be applied with a brush (though now I'd just
pour it in my compressor sprayer).  Anyhow, it was hard not to leave brush
marks.  So when I painted the luggage rack I just used normal white
(Krypton)
enamel spray paint.  It looks better (took a few coats) than the poptop and
hasn't pealed off or anything yet, though it might some day.  I don't know.
If I had to paint another poptop I'd just use the normal spray paint found
anywhere.  It works fine for me.
