My powers of intuition tell me that it's an after-market relay formerly
used to power an A/C blower or something similar.  My former '79 Bust
had one of these and it drove me crazy because I assumed (U<--ass-->me)
that it was part of the starting circuit.  The "150-10" part is sort of
confusing, though.  If something on your bus decides it needs 150A you
may want to jump out before it explodes!  I would try disconnecting it
and see what quits working.  If nothing quits working, ignore the relay.
