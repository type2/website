Hi Ben,

The BN-4 was fitted to the bus from 68-74. It is usually installed on  
the left side of the engine compartment. It will fit a westy or a  
passenger van. The BN-2 is from the beetle, the BA-4 model that was  
fitted to the 411 and 412 models. It is no longer sold new, and AFAIK  
is not available as a kit.

The technology is safe and efficient if it is properly maintained.  
(The same rules apply to your bus) Links to the manuals for operation  
and maintenance are available from the type2 library.

There's a North American distributor of Eberspacher products:
http://www.espar_dot_com/htm/specials/bn2bn4.htm

They currently sell a gas version of their diesel heater which is  
very popular with the 18-wheeler market.
