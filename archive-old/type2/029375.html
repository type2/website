If you have a rear supported engine, and your tranny ground
strap is disconnected or rusted through, the only
other good path to ground is your clutch cable.
Pump enough current through there and it will
get smoking hot.

Check your ground strap. I recently added an extra
ground strap on a friends Type 3 this weekend,
>from the engine block to the body of the car, and
it smoothed out the idle as well. We had replaced the 
braided one from the tranny, but it had corroded up
in less than a year. The new one is up out of the road grime.
