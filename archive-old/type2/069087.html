The reading should be constant for Volkswagens, at least that's my
understanding from reading Ben Watson's "How to ... Bosch FI" book, p.81.
According to him, you should be getting:

40-300 Ohm between terminals 6 & 7
130-260 " " 6 & 8
200-400 " " 6 & 9
100-500 " " 7 & 8
70-140 " " 8 & 9

You may also want to check the wiring harness by measuring resistance at
the harness connector from the ECU.
