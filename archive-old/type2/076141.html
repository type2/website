On tires, I saw a great tip from a Rialta owner for a snow tire option that
isn't a offroad tire. Nokian makes a commercial load range D snow tire in
185R14   and 195R14. I found the info on Nokian's site but have not priced
them or checked availability, other then noting that Tirerack doesn't carry
Nokian tires. Here are links to the Nokian site, these are to the framed
pages, if you go to the regular nokian site you need to look under
products.LightTruck/SUV.Commercial in their nav menu
185R14 is the 10C, aggressive pattern
http://www.nokiantires_dot_com/html/hakka10C.html
195R14 is the NR09, looks more all season.
