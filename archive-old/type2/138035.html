The problem is you need to convert the propane fuel from a liquid (it is a liquid in the tank) to a gas (LPG carbs are gas/gas).  In a watercooled system, this is accomnplished by heat exchanging with the coolant.  Not possible in an aircooled system.  However, one could just as easily use the air-cooled coolants (the air or better, the oil).  One just cannot buy a ready made heat exchanger like this, thus this needs to be engineered.  Not that big a deal, really.
