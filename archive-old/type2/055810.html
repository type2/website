All I know is I keep the timing dead on in both my vehicles and run 87
octane, and I never get any pinging (and yes, I've driven my Vanagon
with the engine cover off.  Damn loud inside, but no pinging :-)

All I'm saying is that according to VW, the minimum octane gasoline for
the engine is 91 RON 

They also say immediately below that in the manual, that in the US, the
CLC method is used, not the RON method.  They explain that the CLC
method is the average of the RON and MON method, i.e. (RON+MON)/2.  They
also also say that the CLC number is usually 4 points less than the RON
number, and they explicitly state that 91 RON = 87 CLC.

So all I'm saying is that VW specifically said that in the US, use 87
octane gas.
