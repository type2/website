If it has a clear coat on it, then rubbing compound or wax should remove 
the black. If it is straight alloy, then try some aluminum polish (sold 
at motorcycle shops, and such). I remove most of the dust from my car at 
the 2-bit car wash. The "tire cleaner" seems to take it all off.
