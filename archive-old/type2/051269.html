This isn't quite true VW did fix this problem but only if you purchased an 
optional extra. 
Rear view mirrors were produced in with three different length stems, as 
standard the shortest length was fitted. 
I cann't comment on the medium length of stem but if you can get hold of a 
long stem mirror they are brilliant as you look back along the road instead 
of down onto it.
Over about 5 years of searching I've found three - one is in my van and the 
other two are in friends vans who also value the considerable improvement.
So keep on looking in those boxes of mirrors that you see at swop meets and 
auto jumbles.
