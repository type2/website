I would recommend pulling the engine and removing the head in question for
a detailed inspection. You could possibly have the crack welded and the
head fly cut. Depends on where the crack is, how bad it is, if in fact it
is a crack and not a burn valve or loose seat. I would not consider running
this motor at all again until I had a look inside. You may be able to fix
it cheaply now, if you continue to run the motor on 3 cylinders you are
most certainly asking for bigger problems later (translates to bigger
$$$$$ later). All this is of course IMHO and as always YMMV...
