Hey there Mark,

Look into private escrow services (yep, just like a mortgage escrow).

You deposit money into the escrow account, and the money isn't released to the
seller until certain conditions are met (delivery of vehicle, title, etc).

Do a search for "escrow" here:

http://forums.pelicanparts_dot_com/forumdisplay.php?forumid=8

It's a Porsche site, so these guys (and gals) are very familiar w/ send big $$$
to private sellers from a long distance. Lots of good "research" material
there.

Another well heard mantra there is to get a PPI (pre-purchase inspection) from
an independant shop that's familiar w/ the type of vehicle you're buying. 

Hope this helps,
