> The number 3 plug is the preffered location, but I have always had trouble
> getting the plug in without mangling the ring (dual port, upright motor.) My
> next engine uses 12mm x 3/4" reach plugs and I was wondering where a suitable

You absolutely must have the sender under the plug or the data will be more
or less useless.

We wrote an article on CHT gauge installation:

http://www.germansupply_dot_com/xcart/customer/home.php?gspage=customer/main/tec
h/cht/cht_install.tpl

If you are building a new engine or have the heads removed, cut a slot in
the spark plug counter bore to locate the sender, it makes a world of
difference to how the sender fits, and stays put on the head. No more
mangled senders.
