> This dizzy/carb combo is legendary for this. Best is to lose the 009 and get
> a real dizzy. One with a vacuum advance. Alternately you can adjust the carb
> to the point that you will get lousy fuel economy and it will run nearly

I disagree. Stick with the 009 its a superior distributor. You don't want to 
have to rely on a vacum advance distributor. 
They're ancient. When using an 009 with a 34.3 there will be a dead stop upon 
shift up through the gears. 
Learn how to drive it best to eliminate that - overlap the clutch with the 
gas pedal a bit more and it will go smoother. 
By the way, the 34.3 is a mediocre carb on its best day fresh out of the box, 
so you might consider switching to a 30pict1 
and an adaptor plate, this will also help your dead spot.
