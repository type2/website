I used these metal pieces from Bus Depot in my repair:

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/Bodywork/23-WindshieldRepair.jpg

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/Ron/06-NewMetal.jpg

The pieces are the right length and height and general shape but you have to
tweak them slightly to duplicate the compound breaks of the original sheet
metal. If you do you will have a perfect fit with your new seal.

What's nice about the kit is you can use one piece or all 3 and considering
the total cost of repair I think it's worthwhile to use new sheet metal than
tear out and old piece of metal from a junkyard bus which is what's commonly
done.
