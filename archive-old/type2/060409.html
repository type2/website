while David is of course correct, I'd like to add that Porsche also 
makes waterboxers lately.  FWIW, the usual terms we colloquially use 
in Germany translate to "waterboilers" or "waterpumpers", and 
"airhowlers" respectively.  All in good fun of course, I own both 
flavours myself.

Back on the subject of engine conversions, a pal of mine from 
Sieversen near Hamburg converts Bays to the Vanagon/Rabbit Diesel 
engine.  He already did a dozen or so, semi-professionally.  The 
radiators are hidden in the engine compartment, so that the only 
optical change to the bus is the exhaust.  If any of you are 
interested, p-mail me for information (he's not online yet).
