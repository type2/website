I'm sorry.  I was not clear, but the reason that manifolds and heads don't
mate is far more likely to be a warped manifold than a mis-tapped stud.  That,
combined with VW's insane "DM" insistence on perfect tolerances.

having a selection of good used manifolds.  I tried three different manifolds
and not only did none fit the studs, none "didn't fit" in the same way.  I
used to just drill out the holes to a larger size as a matter of course, but
John Anderson showed me how to adjust the studs and I've been doing that ever
since...  the ends on those manifolds are *hard*!

I am perfectly willing to blame a rebuilder for problems of their making, but,
IMHO, this isn't one of them.
