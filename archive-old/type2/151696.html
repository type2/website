However!  Go to the beach, grab a bunch of wet wood into a pile and pour 
about 1/2 cup of gasoline on it.  Assuming the wind is coming in from the 
ocean, stand up wind about 15 feet, toss a match in that direction and 
!look out!....*instant* bonfire.

Same problem as ignition in a car engine...too lean...bad ignition....too 
rich...bad ignition.  This is how tankers carrying volatiles survive 
moving instantly flammable stuff across the ocean....the air above the 
surface of the volatiles is deliberately kept too rich to support 
combustion.
