When the alternator on my VW needed cleaning, I disassembled it, and washed it
down with mineral spirits. Home Depot had a can labeled mineral spirits and a can
of paint thinner, which was 100% mineral spirits. The can labeled mineral spirits
was three times the price of the paint thinner. Also while the alternator is
apart have the bearings replaced by an alternator shop - it's cheap and a good
gaurantee.
