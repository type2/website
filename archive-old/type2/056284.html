I have a 90.5 pistons with a 69mm c/weighted crank (gives 1776CCs), a 
110 cam, 040 heads with heavy (double) valve springs. I run twin 
Kadrons with standard jetting and an S & S extractor/muffler set up. 
I also have an external oil cooler but it's not full flow. 

The power/torque is great. Only have to use 3rd for big hills.
In my '72 bus I get (US)19-20mpg in rolling hill country at around 60 
-65 mph
