I just replaced the seals on my vent windows - tedious, that flimsy
metal will bend too damn easily.

I used 4mm aluminum "medium" grip rivits from the Home Depot and put a
nylon washer between the 2 pieces of hinge.  It's not "stock, but it
works.  I had purchased 2 stainless rivits from a boat store, but they
were too small in diam.

Another listee stated that Wolfsburg West (??) has the original rivits.
