Since my grandparents live(d) in Ardmore, I've had a chance to see some
of Mann's work.  There's a beautiful bug running around town that was
painted by him.  The paint work is flawless; it looks like dark glass.

this place.  I went by there on a Sunday, on the way back to East Texas. 
They were closed, but I  looked thru the gate.  Lotsa VW's - looks like a
great place to explore!  8-)
