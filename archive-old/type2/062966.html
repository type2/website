Overfilling may damage seals in some cases.  Also you wouldn't want your
crankshaft and rods to have to wade through too much oil.  ;)  

I check my van's oil first thing in the morning before I start the engine
and on level ground.  On trips I check the oil last (after pumping gas,
doing windows, etc.) at gas stations. If it is low, I top up to the minimum
line by estimate, as if I fill to the max line, it is always too full. 

Hope this helps,

Tom
