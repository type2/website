The backup-light switch is a series switch.  It allows current to flow(on my 
'70 at least, from the coil + terminal) from a power source, throught the 
switch to the lights when reverse is engaged.  Did you check continuity on 
the switch, both IN and OUT of reverse?  I see you say you cleaned the 
switch, but you may need to replace it.  Have you applied power directly to 
the reverse lights?  Might be a bad ground.  Have you checked the inline 
fuse between the power source and switch?  There should be one.
