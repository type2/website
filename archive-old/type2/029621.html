This could be serious so check it right away. Something has slipped in the
steering linkage and that shouldnt happen. Make sure to check the flexible
steering coupler, steeringbox area and drag link.
Go for the steering coupler first if it goes you lose steering (very bad)
You an have a friend move the steering wheel back and forth while you
examine all the steering parts below for any play that doesnt transmit to
the wheels.
If your not sure get help.
