Subject: Re: [T2] making valve cover gaskets stick to valve covers

On 6/8/03 8:19 PM, "elijah" <elijah_at_sisna_dot_com> wrote:

> Is this really necassary?  everything i've ever seen, read, and been
> told says to only glue down the cover side... then again it's never been
> explained why.  so it begs the question - glue down the head side too?
> Why or why not?

Anything you put on the head side is going to squish out as soon as the bail
is applied over the valve cover. The cork gasket will compress and seal on
it's only unless the head surface is not flat.

The biggest problem is cleanup. Next time you have to change gasket because
you removed the cover anything that hardened on the head side will require
cleaning.
