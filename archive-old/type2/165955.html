What Mark was referring to is the oil pressure idiot
light sending unit which is directly forward of the
distributor. If you look down from above, you can see
it. It has a wire attached to it and a black neoprene
"skirt" around it to prevent cooling air from excaping
through the hole in the tin. Pull that skirt out of
there (carefully) and then watch this sucker while the
engine runs. It's a very likely candidate. -BaH
