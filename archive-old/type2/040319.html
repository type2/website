The problem is not the nut becoming loose. It cant as it is pinned in. The
problem is that the inner surface of the drum/rotor that thrusts against
the bearing stack wears away from the tiny movement. As it wears the
clearance becomes more and it just increases the wear. Im not sure if this
is caused by worn splines or if it is just a fact of life. Seen it many
times. If I hear any squeaks at low speeds or if I see and sign of the hub
moving against the nut I retorque.
