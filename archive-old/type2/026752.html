I do not know about a bus specifically, but my father had a 60's Vespa
Scooter titled the same way. Basically sold the scooter to them and then
bought it back. When they bought it, they titled it in their state and then
gave him a bill of sale and a title when he bought it back. He then got it
registered in VA. Worked good for him since he made one scooter out of
several and all where found in the woods. He just grabbed one of the VIN
numbers.

Seems like it would work fine if you are sure this bus is not being looked
for. Hate to see you get it titled and fixed up, then the real owner decide
they want it. I am not sure what kind of legal rights they have to it, but
you may want to check into it.
