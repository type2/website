Jason,

I'm pretty sure only FI engines had hydraulic lifters, so even if your CJ
engine is a '78 or a '79, it will have solid ones.  If you have not
attempted a valve adjustment before and you don't know when it was last
done, now is the time to do it.  You should have 0.006" valve clearance,
which indicates that you have solid lifters.  Hydraulics should be tight.
Another way to tell is to remove one of the pushrods.  In
hydraulic-equipped engines they are thin steel.  In solid-equipped engines
they are fat, hollow Al alloy.
