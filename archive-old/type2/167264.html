Hi Jeremiah,

For a good test of your magnet, try pulling the #3 lifters and see  
how you do with them. In my experience they should slide out of the  
case pretty easily, there is no lip on them or the case. If the  
lifter doesn't come out of the case, it has been mushroomed by the  
cam, and the odds on either case or cam damage are pretty high.

Since you're in there already, try turning over the engine and watch/ 
measure the movement of the lifters; if there's a difference in the  
range of movement of a lifter then you'll *know* there is cam damage.  
If not, then you just have a lifter/case issue. Either way you're  
probably gonna have to split the case.
