My most recent experience with the front brakes being locked is because the
rubber hose near the wheel itself had collapsed. I took them loose the other
day and no fluid would come out of them at all. Once I put the new hose on,
fluid comes through easily. I cannot answer your questions about the master
cylinder, but you might need to change the hoses near you wheels also.
