It was this inconsistency that had me alarmed at first, and, combined
with my limited knowledge, sent me down a paranoid path by questioning
the entire documentation content. My problem has been resolved, as are,

had with aircooled, the tech said that static timing between 7 and 12
BTDC was correct for the SVDA. So.. what was wrong with my
installation? hehehe... I had the #2 and #4 wires backwards. <pause for

carb and it runs pretty well now. It still has an "unclean" idle that I
can only describe as an incomplete detonation (to novice ears). Perhaps
the mixture isn't quite right. Once the bus starts moving, though, it
runs great. The throttle response is fantastic. Good-bye flatspot. I
plan to spend more time dinking with the weber, but, for now its
running better than the with 009, so it can wait 'til Saturday. I may
just wait until the vaccuum diaphram wears in.
