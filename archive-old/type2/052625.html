According to Muir AH is 71 to 73 Type1. I can't find my Wilson book 
but I recall it stating that AH was also used in 1971 bus. I have an 
AH in the '67 bus and it does not have to bosses for the hanger. I 
~think~ 1973 was the first year of the "universal case" so in theory 
if the motor came from a 1971 bus or a 1973 Be*tke then it would have 
bosses. With your luck it will not :)
