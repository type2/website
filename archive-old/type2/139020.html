Just so our non-metric neighbors will be informed, the inch hole saw needed
is 2 1/16". This can be a difficult size to find, but Milwaukee and others
make it. Don't try to use a 2 1/8" size, it is too big. If you can't find 2
1/16", better to use 2" and open the hole with a file or dremmel.
