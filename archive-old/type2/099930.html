I'd like to hear some more opinions because I've been considering this also.  I had terrible wind buffeting until I replaced all of the shocks with KYB gas-a-justs and now it's no why near as bad until a big truck passes you. The biggest improvement I made to the steering was to replace the clapped out steering damper.  Prior to that the wheel used to yank back and forth over simple speed bumps.
