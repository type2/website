The first thing to realize is that your bus is never going to be as
spritely in traffic as other vehicles.  There are a only few vehicles
that are slower.  Most other motorists view you as a relatively
stationary object and that's fine.

Best thing is to take on a regal attitude.  You're seated on a high
throne with an excellent view of the outside world.  The rabble swarm
about as you smile benignly down upon them.  Occasionally one will try
to get your attention by waving their finger at you or yelling, but is
it really worth diverting your royal attention for such trivia?

A humorous thought, that, but not too far from my driving attitude.
