All you really need to do is find the last owner that the
DMV knows about. Or any one after the one that the DMV
knows about. Just give the DMV a reasonable account of the
ownership trail. Leave out things they do not need to know.
It's going to cost you a transfer fee for each owner.

BTW, if you have AAA, the DMV work can be done at a AAA office.
Beware, though, that the AAA will err on the high side when
computing fees. At the real DMV, you might get someone who
gives you a break.
