Subject: Photovoltaic Power on a Bus

The latest issue of Home Power Magazine (#54 - August/September '96) has 
an article on a gentleman who has put five 75 watt PV panels on the roof 
of his '69 Kombi. It's an interesting article. 

Initial thoughts: Very robust well thought out electrical system, which 
is totally separate from the Bus' own electricals.

Expensive as all get out. >$4K for 2,000 watt hours per day, but he can 
run for 3 days on his batteries and he doesn't have to run his engine to 
recharge his batteries.

Had to retrofit with a '71? front end discs to compensate for loss in 
braking power when he added 400 lbs of traveling weight to his bus.

For more info on HomePower Magazine, check out their web site at 
http://www.homepower_dot_com
