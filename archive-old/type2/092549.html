At 09:51 PM 7/9/2002, Richard wrote:

>I too had a fuel pump failure on a long trip but had no spare, and no parts
>available (long week end and a long way from any shops). I managed to
>continue my travels by connecting a small 12V electric compressor I had to
>the fuel tank breather (in the engine bay), and connecting the inlet and out
>let hoses to the fuel pump together. Then it was a matter of putting a few
>holes in the breather hose so the pressure didn't build up too much.
>This worked a treat and kept us going for a few days (and ~200km) until we
>could get an electric fuel pump!
>Handy to remember if your stranded without a spare fuel pump and have a tyre
>pump.
