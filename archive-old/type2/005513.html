The library at type2_dot_com lists " tent" and has a lot of
information , including mention of the date code location
for the Drive-Away tent.
On my new one, purchased from the original owner,

of the front door on the orange canvas, while the inspector's stamp
is on the gray floor material just below that.

not finding an actual tag sewn into the floor/ wall seam
( This tag not to be removed -under penalty of law :) but
the owner assured me that never happened.
Other tents do have a tag, sewn inside at the left of the door
or under the left side window ( facing out ).
