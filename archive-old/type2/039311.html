I replaced the right side heater cable in my 1970 Kombi before knowing the
list existed and had no problems feeding the new cable into the metal tube
without the help of a guide string.  I just paid attention to where the
cable had been, and made sure I used the same holes.  Unless Volkswagen
changed the type of guide tube in the later bay window busses, I wouldn't
anticipate a problem.  The key is to pay attention to what you take apart so
you can put it back together.
