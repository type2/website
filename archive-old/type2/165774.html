search on the Hankook website.  Didn't have any trouble at all.  Went to the dealer, asked for a
set of RA08 tires in 185R14, waited a few days for them to come in, had them installed.  And this
is in the middle of cornfield Indiana.  I'd hope it would be just as easy in WA.
