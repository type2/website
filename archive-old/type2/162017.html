Just a reminder. September 8-10.

Exactly four weeks from today we'll be savoring the good times we had  
at the TENTH anniversary of WetWesties' inaugural gathering, The  
WetWesties/Bus Pilots ReUnion at Catherine Creek State Park (CCSP) in  
Union, OR. (Actually the first one was a "Union." Since then it's  
been a ReUnion...)

Please consider joining us in our celebration of good buses, good  
food and good friends. This event has been held yearly at Catherine  
Creek State Park outside of Union Oregon on the first weekend  
following Labor Day since Jack, Cris and friends joined a select  
group of Boise Bus Pilots here in 1996.

Lisa and I will coordinate the camping fees. When you arrive, just  
tell the Camp Host that you're with WetWesties and they'll point you  
the right direction. Cost will be $6.50/vehicle/night. Multiple  
vehicles per site is fine.  Parking on the grass is NOT fine. Too  
early to tell if we'll be able to have open fires.  The fire danger  
is currently high, but I saw a herd of young bucks in town this  
morning. Fall may come early and it did rain a couple days ago.

State Park Info:
<http://www.oregonstateparks_dot_org/park_17.php>

Note: CCSP is in a canyon. Analog cell phone coverage is spotty and  
digital is non-existent. Mom won't be able call you.
