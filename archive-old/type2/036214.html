There's an Australian company that make a rear disk brake kit for Kombi's,
they cost about $750AUD from what I remember and run the standard bolt
pattern etc and are a straight swap and include everything you need to do
the job.  The company is called Volks Engineering and they also do adapter
kits for engines.

I've spoken to them several times and it's basically a one man show, but
he's very knowledgable and easy to talk with, we spent 30mins on the phone
discussing engine conversions even though he knew I'd already done mine and
was just after a price on a tough pressure plate.  Anyway... if anyones
interested I'll track the number down again.  I here that with rear disks
the nose of the Bus tends not to dive as much and it makes a huge
difference, I'm planning on puting them on my V8 Bus when the cash becomes
available.
