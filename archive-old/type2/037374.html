Shelby,

The earlier loafs had the hinge in the front. I don't remember what year
they changed, but apparently, Westfalia wasn't making enough money on
replacement tops from stupid owners (like me) who forgot to latch the tops
down before driving away, so they put the hinge in the back. This resulted
in instant income, and therefore became standard ;).

Another theory would be to put the upper luggage rack atop the driver,
thereby evening out the front/rear load.
