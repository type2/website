-- The 3rd tailight (my experience) will come with a
wire to run to each tail light and a grnd. I spliced
in
to the brake light ckt right at the brake light(s)
connector and ran the wire(s) up the air intake vent.

and fished it under the headliner as it was pushed
from the outside.(rubber grommet insert used)
I ran the wires into the openings where the hinge
assembly goes on the back hatch door. the light is
mounted at the bottom of the window. (mine is hinged
so i can flop it out of the way of the curtain).
