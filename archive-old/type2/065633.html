>think all it requires is running some wires, but i could be totally wrong.
It's easy.  There's a wire to +12V when the ignition is on, and ground, and
one to the dash light circuit for the light in the tach, and one that goes
back to the ignition (negative side of the coil).  When running this wire,
you might want to run 2-3 extra wires alongside at the same time.  This way,
if you want to add other gauges in the future you're all set.  Just bundle
the extra wires up at each end and leave them unconnected.  And use different
colored wires.
