Well, I gotta say, on the one hand, the price is a shocker!  and David is

If you were to say to yourself, "I want a showroom fresh, new VW van,
price is no object", then I think that you could do much worse than to buy
a low milage original 75-79 van (probable cost, <$4000) and take it to a

probably be much less, but I'm using east-coast off the top of my head
figures here.  Our vans are a bargain!!!!
