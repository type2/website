I had a leak in this area on a new engine I installed on my bus and it 
drove me crazy.  

I think the easiest and safest method for finding leaks is to pressurize 
the system with an air compressor and then use soapy water on all suspect 
areas, looking for bubbles.  I attach the compressor to the hose that 
runs into the aux air regulator (engine cold so the regulator is open), 
plugging the hole in the air intake elbow where I detached the hose.
