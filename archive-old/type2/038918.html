The wire coming from the ignition switch is a ground (or negative) wire.  The 
OTHER wire coming into the buzzer is the positive wire.  You cannot get 
"juice" from the wire coming from the ignition switch.

The wire coming from the ignition switch first goes through the door switch 
(which is a DPDT switch -- two switches in one -- 1 for the dome light & 1 
for the warning buzzer).  You would need to take the wire (brown) from the 
ignition switch and wire it opposite a hot wire on the activating circuit of 
the relay.  Then on the other circuit of the relay (the one the relay 
activates), just tap into a wire that is always hot.  This would be the power 
wire to the stereo.  If you need a diagram I can scan one in and send it to 
you p-mail.  I'm not an ASCII artist.
