How many times do you need to be corrected for this false info??
A vacuum advance uses PORTED vacuum. PORTED vacuum comes from ABOVE the
throttle plate. The MORE you open the throttle, the MORE vacuum you have.
PORTED vacuum comes off the venturi.
If you are full throttle and you have zero vacuum, you better check your
tach. You seized your engine and you have no air flow into the engine.
