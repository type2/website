The factory preload on the spring is the white mark at the bottom of the face. All VDO speedo's have this
white line. This is where the needle is set to and then you gently lift it over the pin. Being as it is
accurate now, you might want to lift it back over the pin and see where it points to.
The line is about 1" to the right of the pin and about 1/4" to the left of the bottom VDO.
