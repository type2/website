to the left of the vin number. Right where the curve in the corner is. A
while back I found a bus at a yard that had A/C and then was removed. On it
was a piece of molded plastic that was made to fill the corner of the body
where the seal went. It was held down with 2 screws. This piece was not
home-made. It was designed and made for this purpose. I have not seen
another one in the past 5 years. Before I mailed it to someone, I looked at
it and thought seriously about checking around locally to have it
duplicated. I didn't and have been kicking myself in the butt ever since.
