Re those red tubes to spray GumOut or Wd40 or Liquid Wrench:
I save those tubes; there are spares in the tool box.  If you keep the
cap of the can, bend the tube into the cap (need the "double" caps that
are the size of the can).   If you lose the tube, clip the ends off a
q-tip, most are hollow plastic tubes.  Drill out the hole in the spray
tip if they are too large.  I extend the plastic tube by slipping the
q-tip over the red tube. You could tape a q-tip tube to the can and slip
the red tube into it for a holder.
     Now here's a trick to direct the spray around corners- wrap solder
around the tube, or that soft hobby wire in art stores.  This you can
bend to hold the tube at any curve.  MB
