I've got a couple of different opinions on this.  Leonard at AVP says there
is only one size of spacer for distributor drive shaft height on the type
IV.  He also said that they never cut the 'shoulder' on the case where the
spacer rides so it should be no different than when I sent it in.  The Tom
Wilson book infers that there are different sizes available from VW, but it
(Wilson) is talking generically about type I and type IV engines.  BTW, can
distributor drive shaft height be measured without dismantling the engine?
I feel pretty sure I could get enough advance (I need about 4-5 degrees to
get to spec at 5 degrees ATDC) if I could get a thicker spacer, but I don't
know how good that would be for the two gears involved.  The more I think
about this, the more I think the spacer might be the problem here.

way.
