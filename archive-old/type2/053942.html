Hey Alex,

I'm not sure about 84 into a 77, but I've always heard that vanagon seats
will easily slide into a bus.

However, when I actually went to a junkyard to give this a try it did not
work.  1) The passenger side of my bus does not have the sliding rails (it
only has a couple of hooks where the seat is inserted).  2) You can't pull
the rails out because they are welded in 3) The rails on the driver's seat
are of slightly different design.  On the bus, there is a thing that looks
like a comb that swivels down in order to lock the seat into place.
However, on the vanagon it is a bar that drops down to lock it into place.

I played and worked and pushed and tried to get them to fit (desparately
want nicer seats in the bus), but it was to no avail.  Now, I'm just looking
to buy some new replacement seats for my bus.

Until Later,

Darrin.

PS> If you try to, remember to watch your fingers.  Its a tight fit in there
between the rails and the steering wheel and while trying to match up the
rails and get everything to slide in place you could easily mash/tear a
finger.  As me how I know :)
