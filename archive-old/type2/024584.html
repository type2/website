I have not done it, but you could use a wiper switch off a Bug or early
Bus(like my 71), they are mounted in the dash and there is a hole behind the
pad on your dash for such a switch. I think all pre-71 VWs had a dash
mounted switch for the wipers, at least the Bugs and Buses. Get a wiring
diagram from say a 70 or 71 and wire it the same way to the switch of the
same year. They even have the hose attachment so you can still use your
windsheild washers.
