"ball-point pen" spring.  It sits in a recess on the top of the
distributor drive gear in the case.  It provides a bit of upward
pre-load on the distributor shaft.  Necessary? Who knows.  But I
subscribe to Dr. Kettering's philosophy that, "Parts left out cost

*need* to be there, it wouldn't be.
