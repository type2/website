Hi Peter,

The transmission swap you're looking at is a VERY big job.

Since you do not know what condition the auto tranny is in I would not 
recommend going any further... There are a myriad of small details to 
resolve in order to create a proper shift linkage and the hangers 
required to mount an auto transmission in a 72 bus.

And without the upper engine hatch it would be impossible to check the 
transmission fluid level.

It's been done before, but it is NOT an easy thing to accomplish.
