Yes, you should be able to take the motor from one and pop it in the other.

a few things, though- if the bay motor is 12V, is the tranny in the 65 
clearanced for 12? correct starter/bushing? If you are running 12V in the 65 
then you don't need to swap the electrics over.

Clutch.  some of the later VW have a different throw out bearing and clutch. 
I am not sure when they changed- about 70 I think.   if you use the later 
clutch on the earlier throwout bearing it makes a horrible noise.  So make 
sure the clutch has the circle in the middle- not just the arms.

the later pre heat is a different size, and you want to use the splitty air 
cleaner.

Oh- if it has 71 bay window heater boxes on it- the cables wont line up with 
the places on the heater box, but you can tie them open when you are cold.

There always seems to be a few things that can use altering when you swap a 
motor from one VW to another.
