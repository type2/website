I'm curious:  Why are you replacing your servo?  No vacuum assist?  And
are you sure you are getting vacuum at the servo?

Also, if you haven't replaced your MC in a long time, its possible that
it has been leaking brake fluid into the servo so watch out when you
start to remove it.  You don't want to get a face full of fluid!!  And
if this turns out to be the case, you'll want to replace your MC anyway.
