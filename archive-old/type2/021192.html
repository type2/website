Westies in the Woods IX
Friday, May 25 through Monday, May 28
Stokes State Forest, Group Campsite A, Branchville, NJ
sponsored by The Bus Guild (New Jersey chapter of LiMBO)
Highlights
Friday:     Welcome night and campfire sing along (Bring 'em if
                        you got 'em -- instrument that is!)  
            Crash the Cruise nite at Sonny's Drive-In
Saturday:   Caravan to local flea market and Stewart's Root Beer/
            Dairy Queen
            Pot Luck Turkey Roast and Bonfire — We supply the turkeys,
            you supply the rest.
Sunday:     Caravan to Buttermilk Falls for scenic hike.
Monday:     Whatever!

Nearby Attractions: Appalachian Trail, Lafayette Outlet Village,
antique shops, Delaware Water Gap, mining museun

Water and public rest rooms on site (Sorry, no hook-ups/showers)
Site available from noon Friday, through noon Monday

legal mumbo-jumbo
Park regulations DO NOT PERMIT DOGS in overnight camping areas.
Alcoholic beverages are not permitted under park rules.
- also there was a ban on ground fires except in
portable grills)

To Get There ...
From Interstate 80 (East or West) 
Take Exit 34B and follow the signs to Route 15 North then  Route 206
North. 
The entrance to Stokes State Forest will be on the right-hand side.
Make a right-hand turn onto Coursen Road. 
(at Stokes Forest entrance sign). 
Follow Coursen Road past the office to Kittle Road (at “T”). 
Make a left-hand turn onto Kittle Road and go until you see group
campsites (also on the left). 
Site A is on the right-hand side of the left-hand spur.
