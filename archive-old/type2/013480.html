so, it wouldn't go INTO gear, or, once IN gear, it wouldn't go anywhere?

If it wouldn't go into gear, I'd say it sounds like your clutch cable, 
which is not too
difficult to replace ( i carry a spare).  To check this, just get under 
your bus and look
towards to top of the tranny from LEFT (LIL) side.  You should see a 
little lever arm
with a cable attached to it.

If there's no cable, or, if the cable is slack, you've found your 
problem.

The key to adjusting this cable once you get it back in, btw, is to hold 
the cable with a
pair of vice-grips while you turn the wing-nut that adjusts the clutch.  
If you fail
to hold the cable while you're doing this, it will turn WITH the nut and 
eventually start
to fray, then snap.
