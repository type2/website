I pmailed Mike that the 3rd wire goes to the back-up light switch on the transmission. It comes out of the
fused end.

Power starts at the 15 term of the coil, goes to the two wire end of the in-line fuse, un-fused power goes to
the double relay, fused power goes to the reverse light switch.

In-line fuse holder:  The two wire end is the inlet side, the one wire end is the outlet side.
