At 10:27 AM 8/30/96, Mark Sudul [4590] wrote:
>What should I use to clean up the garage floor (cement). I have dripped
>a lot of oil on it and we cleaned out the transmission bell housing
>onto the floor too. :p I forgot to put down some newspapers...
>Preferably there is something environmentally friendly that I can use.

Simple Green works fairly well, and is (supposedly) environmentally friendly.
Wipe up all of the oil you can first, then pour a little simple green down
and scrub it with a stiff brush.  I use it straight, the instructions say
to dilute it.
