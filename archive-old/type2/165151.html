Hi Bob,

Crappy running at power (but good at idle) is a classic symptom of a  
failed coil.

Other possibilities include fuel pressure, but you've already ruled  
that out. Maybe the regulator has gone bad, and/or the decel valve  
isn't dropping pressure when it should be. I would look around for a  
known-good coil, and think about replacing the decel valve. A  
compression check and a new set of wires might also be a good idea.
