Pete,

Joe here in Boulder Colorado. Don't take the shroud apart! You will find no
bolt to unscrew. This bolt you see from the outside is a stud VW farmed out
this one so ( N 014 443 12 STUD AM 8A X 30) is what they call it. You need
to extract this by threading two nuts onto the stud, tightening them
together and try to back it out. If not then you can try and us an easy out
to get it out. If you have trouble with finding this metric stud, then give
me a call I'll get you one. Also I have the brackets as well. Give us a call
if you need any help and or are in the area.
