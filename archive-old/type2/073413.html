Subject: Re: [T2] Vac advance distributor hoses. Where do they go?

At 8:30 PM -0500 8/3/01, David Kelly wrote:
>
>Where do the vacuum lines hook up from the vacuum can
>on the distributor? I saw a bus the other day with the
>bottom vacuum line connected to an EGR valve and the
>top one to a vacuum line on the left carburator. I do
>Not have egr (72 year model carbs). Where do they hook
>up to? Does one vacuum line go to one carb and the
>other vacuum line go to the other carb?

This has been discussed many times and the answer can be found in the
archives or in Bentley, which you should have a copy of if you own a bus.

The port on the outside of the vacuum canister (vacuum advance) goes to the
port on the left or right carb that is near the center (vertically) of the
carb, facing the outside.  The port on the inside of the vacuum canister
(vacuum retard) goes to the slightly larger port near the base of the left
carb.  Set your timing to 7.5 ATDC with both lines connected at 900rpm and
adjust from there to suit your tastes.
