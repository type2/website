This is something that's always intrigued me, frankly. Has anyone ever 
tried using an aviation engine oil in a VW? They're available in 40 and 
50 weights, and even some synthetic formulations. I would just think 
that VW engines are a LOT closer to small GA aircraft engines then they
are to a typical wasserbox car (in terms of temperatures, clearances, 
etc...)

Downside, of course, is price. Aviation oils are ( I think, I don't do 
the buying, I just do the oil changes ) about twice what car oils cost. 
Like most things aviation related -- fewer units, more price per unit.

I can tell you this much, though. A good friend of mine runs Aeroshell 
100 (50w) in his motorcycle and swears by it. He claims that it reduced 
wear by about 25% on his last overhaul ( he puts a LOT of miles on his 
bike.)
