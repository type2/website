If you're willing to work hard enough, you probably could rebuild an
engine without every removing it from the car! :-)

 However, in the long run it's just easier to drop the engine and tear
it down to the point where you can really see where this oil is coming
from.  It's *higher* numbers that indicate thicker oil (e.g., 50
weight is "thicker" than 30 weight) but you don't want to go this
route.

If it's not a really bad leak you may just want to live with it until
you can fix things properly.  Keep an eye on your oil during your trip
and be prepared to wash the back window regularly!
