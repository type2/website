Just remember the Bob and Doug McKenzie all-purpose metric conversion
formular:  double it and add 30.  5 degrees Celsius = 5+5+30 = 40 degrees
Farenheit.  50 kilometers = 50+50+30 = 130 miles.  6-pack of beer = 6+6+30 =
42 metric beers.
