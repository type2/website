> Has anyone use a stitch/spot timer kit on their welder? What does it do?
> Does it make the welding easier? Looks better? I have owned a Lincoln
> 100 for 2 years, and yet to master the welding like in the books. I saw
> this as an accessories on the Lincoln and was wondering if it is worth the
> money.

It's used to make (ta da!) stitch and spot welds, which are very short
welds. Very specialized stuff--if you don't have the MIG conversion kit, get
that; if you do have it, next time you get inert gas ask for "trimix"
instead of "MIG mix." MIG mix has argon and carbon dioxide in it; trimix has
argon, carbon dioxide and helium. Trimix is more expensive yet is easier to
use.
