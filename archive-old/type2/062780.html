If you decide to leave the windows in while painting, a good trick is to
take a string and lay it in under the outer edge of the rubber. Then mask
the rubber and window. If you can follow me, this allows you to paint just
under the rubber trim. Subsequently when you're all done and remove the
string, the rubber will now lay over the painter part. This gives a nice
clean finish, without those tell tale stains of paint on the rubber.
