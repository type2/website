Interestingly enough, I had the exact same crack on my 1500 case in my 
Ghia. I found it when I disassembled the top end for inspectin when I got 
the car. I figured someone had over torqued the head stud and pulled on the 
case saver too much.  My crack ended before it got to the boss, but did 
come over the top of the case.  

My fix (until I ever rebuild the engine and have it welded) was to drill 
the end of the crack to stop it from continuing. I used a 1/16" drill bit, 
if I recall. I then opened up the crack slightly with a dremel and JB 
Welded it.

I ran the engine all this year without difficulty.  No leaks from there 
that I can see.

If you have it tore down, you might consider welding it.
