You almost answered this question yourself: "push rod retaining wire".... put
the word 'tube' between 'rod' and 'retaining'.
They hold the pushrod TUBES in place

If they are touching any pushrods....they are installed wrong.

>Should this wire be in contact with
> the underside of the push-rods? Mine are.

NO! They hook on each end into the rocker arm support. Then each bend goes into
the head at the top of each tube.
Got a 68-79 Bentley? Go to section 5, page 32, fig. 10 - 4. Kinda sucks but that
is about all there is...... If you back up a page, you will see it in the
exploded pic as # 32. The bottom right pic shows the correct position of the
rocker arm supports. I have taken quite a few engines apart and they were upside
down. They really mangled the wire trying to get it to stay in.....
