I have the Bus Depot Stormshield cover - worth every penny (CDN or US) IMHO.
It fits my 71 poptop Westy perfectly.  My "new" paint job looks great even
after 4 or 5 Pennsylvania winters.

I previously had the Wolfsburg West Evolution 4 cover - also a nice cover
but it did not fit my Westy (left the lower 6 inches of the body and the
bumpers uncovered) due to the added height of the poptop. Would have been
fine for a non-poptop bus and in fact I sold it to a friend to use on his
non-poptop Westy.

Use of a cover makes a major difference in keeping the bus clean and greatly
increases the longevity of the paint job.  I use mine all the time when I am
not driving the bus - winter and summer.

I'm not sure what you mean by "grease it up" in lieu of using a cover, but I
have to think that any method of keeping the elments away from your bus will
help to preserve it.
