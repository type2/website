The other issue with that sender is that the case thru bolt near the sender
(top rear one) must be facing the right way for the sender to fit. I found
this out the hard way. The bolt heads must be on the 3-4 side of the case.
If the bolts are installed pointing the other direction, the tip of the bolt
will want to interfere with the sender.
