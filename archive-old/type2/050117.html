Most of the anti-corrosion type products, as used on battery terminal are
just 'Vasaline' or Petroleum jelly with a dye put in for colo(u)r! Also the
base for 'Napalm' etc... This a problem if your connection does 'arc' as
these compounds are flammable. If you look on many PCB's you will see that
they have been coated in a varnish, so if you can clean terminals & then
spray an acrylic varnish on them this should work as well as the jelly based
products
