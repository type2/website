Yep... and if you really can't afford a compressor, most tool stores 
sell a canister. It's basically the same air tank that's mounted on 
inexpensive compressors, with a guage and a filler nozzle that looks 
like a tire valve.  It has about 6' of hose, with a tire filler 
nozzle and quick release. Simply fill it up... it'll hold up to 100 psi.

They cost about $30, or less, for the smaller size. It's really 
convenient to lug out to the north 40 to fill a tire on the tractor, 
too. One charge will fill a low VW tire. I use it at work to dust out 
PCs. It's cheaper than buying the compressed air. Once I modified a 
nozzle, I can dust out a couple PCs on a single charge.
