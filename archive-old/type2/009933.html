To find TDC : remove either valve cover. If you remove the left one watch #3 
valves. If you remove the right one watch #1 valves. Turn the engine in 
direction of rotation. After no valve movement, the first valve to move will 
be the exhaust. Watch it open and close. STOP just when the exhaust valve 
closes and the intake just starts to move. If you go too far the intake will 
open too much. Just go around again and stop when the exhaust is just closed 
and the intake is just starting to move. At this point your piston is at 
TDC. Look on the inside edge of the pulley  (towards the front of the bus at 
about 11 0'clock ) for a tiny V mark. It should be at zero.
