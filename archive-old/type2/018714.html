I'd like to share with my fellow Bus drivers on the Wood issue.

Many of you may have noted my sign off referring to "Janis", '71
Westy/Campmobile/dave edition.  The dave edition refers to what I feel are
respectable personalized wooden accents.   I have replaced my instrument
panel with a burl walnut, and all panel surfaces will be a red mahogany stain
held in with brass phillips head screws.  It is my plan to re vainer all
interior Campmobile fixtures and surfaces with a tasteful wood.  The stick
shift rod is now a 25" muskidine vine twisted sapling.  Put some 1940 wooden
radio knobs on the stereo.

Janis interior makeover is a long long way from completion but I love the
progress so far!

Wood when combined with the practical and special applications will always
enhance that "Busma" inspired karma that these wonderful machines have.

Through "Busma" her karma will blossom
