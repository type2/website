Hi Jane,
vibration through the steering wheel would suggest that the wheels are out
of
balance or that the tracking/camber/toe-in needs to be reset; but the
grinding noise would suggest that you need wheel bearings replacing.

These are not expensive, from memory under £20 per side from German And
Swedish and during replacement the tracking/camber/toe-in should be reset as
a matter of course.

But before you spend any money visually inspect the front tyres as the
problem could be caused by a bulge in the tyre wall.

With the noises you are getting I would recommend not using the van for any
distance (or at all) until it's fixed.
