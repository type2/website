Hi Jack,

That's about opposite. It should be at least 85% open when cold, if
not 100% (been a while since I've worked on one). While the engine is
running, 12v current is applied to the unit which causes the valve to
close. The purpose is to allow an alternate air pathway other than the
primary idle air circuit for engine suction to reach the flapper and
allow more fuel to be delivered. It is equivalent to having your foot
on the pedal slightly. When the valve is closed, idle is normal.

John
