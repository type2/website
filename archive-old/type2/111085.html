On Sun, Jun 22, 2003 at 12:55:30PM -0600, George Finnell wrote:
> When I step on the throttle, it catches (sticks, even though I've

> runs fine at higher RPMs. I've tested for vaccum leaks with no

Here's a quick check for you. It's possible that the vacuum can in the
distributor is kaputt. Causing both a vacuum leak & essentially a
mechanical only advance. Just disconnect the hose going to the
distributor & plug it. Then hook another hose to the pot, connect the
timing light up & start the engine. If it advances when you suck on
the hose you are probably ok, but if not Well that's something to look
at.
