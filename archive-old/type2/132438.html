Overall diameter varies from tire to tire, even within the same manufacturer
and model. That said, the overall diameter for the Bridgestone Deuller
195/75R14 is 25.7", the _same_ as Yokohama Y356's in 185R14's. The BS spin
811 revs/mile, and the Yoko's 808. What's that? Around a 0.37 % difference?
I suspect if you bought four matching tires, and you had the capability of
making the measurements, you'd find this much variance among them.
