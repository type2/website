The slot in the dist drive for the T1 engine and the slot for the dist
drive for the T4 engine get lined up at separate angles!  This is shown
(tho puzzlingly) in the Bentley manual on page 24 of the engine section.

Type 1 case:

With #1 at TDC, the slot in the dist drive should be at
right angles to the case seam (parallel to the rear of the case).  the
slot is a hair off center and the slot should be toward the rear of the
bus/case.

Type 4 case:

At TDC for #1, picture a line thru the center of the drive hole in the
case that is parallel to the seam in the case.  Now rotate this line anti-
(counter-) clockwise 12 degrees  the off center slot should be at this
angle, with the slot away from the centerline of the case.

Now go look at the pictures in the book, and see how hard these
descriptions were to write :-)

A lot of dist misalignments are caused by old timers (like me) who have
worked on a lot of T1 engines using the same spec on T4's, then wondering
why the vac can runs into something.
