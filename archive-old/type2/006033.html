I had the same (or similar) problem in my '77 Porsche 911 when I installed the Pertronix breakerless ignition.  There is a requirement, in big bold letters, in the instructions that the coil have a minimum primary resistance (I think it was 0.6 ohms).  Too low, and the system won't work.  I had to install a ballast resistor in series to get it working.  It burned out the first Pertronix unit, which was fortunately under warranty.  The Flamethrower coil does have adequate resistance.

Hope this helps,
