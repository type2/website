On Mon, 22 Oct 2007, Shana Nelson wrote:

> I was crawling around under the bus and spotted a REALLY dirty fuel filter
> which I plan to replace tonight.  Are there supposed to be two fuel
> filters?  I was only aware of the one in the engine compartment.

Carbed buses originally only had two fuel filters, the one in the fuel
pump and the one in the fuel tank.  Over
the years, people have added filters in many places.  Between the pump and
the carb(s) is the usual place, but some people put one between the fuel
tank and the pump.
