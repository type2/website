  did I  miss???

Furthest first, then next closer, etc.
Never drain.
Fresh fluid in the reservoir - keep checking to not
allow to run empty - bleed right rear first until
clear fluid comes out, then left rear, etc.

Good sign is that the switches are working cuz the
light goes on. That means there is no air bubbles at
the switches.

You still have air. Start at the right rear and go
until you get a series of bubbles, then on to the
others. Sometimes you have to go buy another bottle of
fluid!

I have never been able to do the gravity flow method -
or else maybe I have never tried it! -BaH
