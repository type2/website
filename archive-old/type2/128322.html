Subject: re: [T2] how to clean up used fridge/sink

It is totally appropriate to disinfect with bleach, but not clorox.  Be
sure to buy a bleach that has no perfumes or anything else in it.  Check
the label, be sure it says sodium hypochlorite, nothing else save water.

After filling the sink, fridge, put a few drops of bleach in it.  Or, make
your cleaning solution in a bucket with water and a few drops of bleach.

The chlorine in the bleach will all evaporate away after just one night. 
It leaves just a little bit of salt, so little you won't taste it after a
thorough rinsing.

This is disinfecting and it is usually necessary a few times a year.
