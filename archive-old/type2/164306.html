Subject: Re: [T2] Petronix Electronic Ignition

You can slide a deep-well 14mm socket down over the top of the distributor 
shaft, on top of the magnet sleeve. Tap very gently a few times until the 
magnet sleeve is fully seated. Don't tap too hard or you'll break off the 
top of the magnet sleeve. (Don't ask me how I know this.)
