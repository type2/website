Personally, I weld a 1" tab of flat stock to one of the sides of the head.
A piece of 1/8" steel, 1" long and 1/2" wide works dandy.  Then wrap
a layer  or two of duct tape to the shank of the bolt to keep it from
sliding back thru when you are trying to get the nut started.
