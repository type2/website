There are connecting rods that attaches the motor arm to the wiper arms
these silver metal rods just snap into the pivot. When the motor is of via
the ignition switch for know look up under the dash and see if one looks to
be hanging. You can pull the glove box off pretty quick to see the wiper
rack a little better. If this is not the problem then the nut on the motor
arm came off.

Ok switch. Either you have a faulty switch, na I think that if you reach up
under the dash and grab the back of the switch you will feel that it is
loose. You will need to unscrew the knob and get a pair of snap ring pliers
to tighten the flat washer looking nut with two grooves in it.

I hope this helps. You can find a good illustration of all these parts on
our website www.bustedvw_dot_com under electrical equipment then windshield
wipers. Good luck
