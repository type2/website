Although not reported as a problem on a bus, this 
sounds very much like a problem I had on my previous 
bus.
In my case it was not only loosing top speed but was 
stugling (more than usual) on hills.
As a temporary measure I took the fuel in line off of 
the pump and blew down it using my electric tyre pump.
I was then able to drive normaly.
As a permanent fix I changed the fuel lines, drained the 
tank and cleaned the in tank strainer.
As I had no further problems I put this down to a partial 
blockage in the fuel lines either due to dirt, rust or 
rubber.
