Ned (& the List,)

In my experience, rough running FI with "rich" symptoms is very often
caused by a non-functional or loose "Temp Sensor II."  This is the
sensor over cyl #3 that tells the ECU what the engine temperature is.
Without this signal, the ECU sees the engine as cold and richens the
nmixture accordingly.  Grab your 14mm end wrench, and check its
tightness.  Will probably be loose.

Good luck.
