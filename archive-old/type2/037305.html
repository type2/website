Hi Matt,

you will need to remove inside handles and the trim panel to access the lock 
mechanism.

When exactly this happened to me on my previous bus a few years back it was 
simply the screw on the inner end of the lock barrel had come loose so that 
the mechanism didn't move with the key. If you are unlucky the screw and its 
serrated washer may have fallen into the bottom of the door.
