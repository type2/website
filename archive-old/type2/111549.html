On Sun, 17 Aug 2003, Ruth Bajorek wrote:

> I have a 76 Westy. I was looking at the relay/box thing behind the blower fan
> and noticed it had a black wire running from it not connected to anything. I
> have the Bentley, but I don't know what the relay is called, so I don't know
> where to look on the wiring diagram. Can someone help me figure out where the
> wire goes, or can you tell me where to look on the wiring diagram?

The relay is called "the heater blower relay" and is J14 located on
current tracks 4-6 on the Bentley diagram or at:
http://www.vintagebus_dot_com/wiring/bus-76.jpg
for a 1976 bus.

Looking at the diagram there is no black wire listed but please note
that Bentley is not perfect.  You shouuld also know that the location and
function of that relay changed significantlty during the late 1970s and
that very few buses get to be 25+ years old without any modifications to
their electrical systems.  Under the usual regime a black wire should be
unswitched 12v power.
