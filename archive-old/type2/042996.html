Some states only require a bill of sale or a tag receipt for vehicles over a 
certain age because they  dont even have a title. GA and AL are two that are 
like that. Florida SUCKS when it comes to these things.

Just make sure that the VIN number that is on the paper work you have 
matches what actually is on the vehicle and NOT just whats on the little 
removable tags. Look at the numbers that are actually stamped into the metal 
on the bus.

refused to help me out.
