Most BLM land is available for camping, but there are few developed
campgrounds.  You simply pick a spot and camp!  Unfortunately, the rules
vary from area to area considering how near or far from roads to camp,
campfires, length of stay, etc.  If you're paranoid, check with the local
BLM office for instructions.  If not, just be inobtrusive and you probably
won't be bothered.
