Meredith-

The double-relay in my '79 is unpainted metal, but one of our busses may
have had it replaced at one point.  It does go on the firewall, towards the
left-hand side IIRC.  You will probably need to go to a VW parts place to
get one, either locally or online.

Another possibility for your symptoms is a bad rotor (in the disributor).  I
had one go bad, and as soon as it got too hot it would stop passing current.
You've *probably* got something else going on, but a rotor is cheap and easy
to replace, so it'd be worth a shot.
