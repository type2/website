middle of nowhere border crossing east of Glacier national park was a real 
PITA.

some guy that cross examined us. Once we got to the part asking us the kind 
of car we had, he straight up admitted "oh, well that's why your up here

with 'ya'

rolls of film (the canisters anyway) and took a few items inside to make 
sure they were allowed.

dog food stuck under the rear bench seat from the PO.
