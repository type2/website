Michael,

I have done 2 "crate" engines and had excellent results from both.

My first was a 1914 from MOFOCO which sits in my Double Cab. It has
performed flawlessly even when run in fairly extreme conditions. I have
heard that MOFOCO "stock" 1600 engines leave something to be desired, but
their custom engines have excellent reputations.

The second was a new Mexican 1600 dual port longblock I purchased from my
local VW shop, NuVintage. It sits in my now sold '70 Deluxe and has run
perfectly with no leaks.

If I were you, I'd do the Mexican motor. However, I don't think* any of the
Mexican motors (someone step in here if I am wrong) come with a tapped
thermostat bracket bolt hole. You will have to drill it out and tap it,
which isn't a big deal at all. I had to do it on the 1600 crate motor I
bought. I think* it is omitted because the blocks were originally for late
style Mexican EFI motors which have different cooling tin.

Hope this helps.

Jon Stiles
