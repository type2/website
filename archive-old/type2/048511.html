Your oil weight should be based on what your engine needs, not on what it takes
to heat the bus in cold weather.  You don't want to deprive your engine of the
lubrication it needs just to heat the passenger compartment.  I've always used
30 weight.  I guess if I lived in an extremely cold climate I would use
something lighter as the owner manual recommends.
