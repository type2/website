There is Tire Size and Tire Size....
What you really need to compare are  the Revs per Mile of a given tire.
This is supplied by the manufacturer and is either measured directly or
calculated from previous data and takes into account weighted radius and
slippage.

If we just take 2 examples you can see the point.

Bridgestone 603v
185/14  25.7" dia  811 rev/mile
195/75 14 25.6" dia 825 rev/mile

But
Yoko 356
185/14  25.7" dia   808 rev/mile (C)  809 (D)
195/75 14 25.8" dia 808 rev/mile
