Yes, it is threaded. On the inside. Where the plug
that you remove to slip the hose over the outside
goes. Not threaded on the outside unless something
rare and unheard of by most of us! Just a simple hose
& clamp will do it. Be sure to test with the regulator
under high vacuum and low vacuum. Once the gauge is
secure you can start & run the engine. At fast idle,
reading should be 2bar; with vacuum removed from
pressure regulator (pull the vacuum hose loose)
pressure should be 2.5bar. Bar is approx. 14.7 at
sealevel on a nice day. -BaH
