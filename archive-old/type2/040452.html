Generally there is a tradeoff between comfort and handling.  You can have
one or the other.  If you want a comfortable ride, don't bother lowering
your bus, spend your money on good shocks and improve your handling at the
same time.  OK so this contradicts my first sentence, but money talks.
If you put shorter springs on your bus it won't get any lower but it might
get narrower :)
