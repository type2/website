David,

I put a '76 "091" tranny in my '69 bus, and love the gear ratios in there. I
have a 1915 pushing things, so I can still keep with traffic going up hills,
and yet cruise _at_ 3200rpm at 60mph. The difference in 1st, 2nd, and 3rd gears
with the 4.57 R&P makes city driving much more enjoyable, and the jump to
4th (.88 or is it .89? who knows the number of gears?) is less of a burden
on the engine that the .82 in the old tranny.

Vote 1 for a 091 tranny.
