Subject: Counterfeit VW parts

I can understand VW's position in regards to counterfeit parts. 
Someone installs counterfeit brake parts and they fail causing a 
fatal accident. VW may have to defend themselves in a lawsuit and 
would would probably a hard time convincing a jury and the public 
that it was not their part that failed if it has VW stamped on it.

The PR from a spate of accidents that is not their fault would be 
worse for them than the PR from going after counterfeiters.
