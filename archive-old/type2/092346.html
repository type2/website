Hi All:

I have an 82 Federal Vanagon, aircooled 1970 cc with FI. The 82's came
with Bosch's L-Jetronic FI, which for federal vehicles run open loop
without an oxygen sensor. Looking through the Bosch L-Jetronic manual it
indicates that running open loop will ruin the catalytic converter
sooner as the air/fuel mixture may not remain ideal. As I have just got
all the parts to replace my old exhaust system (including new cat) I
want to make them last as long as possible.

From what I can tell the ECU can accommodate an oxygen sensor on pins 24
and 23 (shield). Has anyone ever added a sensor to a federal vehicle?
Does only the ECU for CA support the oxygen sensor as I think the ECU's
for CA have different part numbers than the federal ones? One difference
is that the CA ECU's do not need the series resistors for the injectors.
