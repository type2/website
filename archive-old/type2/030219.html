Well, years ago, I was watching this NASCAR race... there they go at 200
MPH... suddenly one of the cars shoots a plume of smoke and drifts into
the infield... later they asked what went wrong and the only answer was
"the engine blew"  Sometimes the damage is so severe that determining what
went first is impossible.

On my rear axle:
the back of the castellated nut was boogered;
both ends of the hub were boogered;
the inner race of the outer bearing was both boogered and split across.

That's as far as I pulled it apart, but I am sure that as I get deeper in,
I will find more wrong :-)  I flopped in a nice looking unit from a
junkyard here and will be on the road in no time :-) I am taking the unit
and a second spare home with me and will rebuild and install them over the
summer.  I will take some pics of the damage and post them when I do.
