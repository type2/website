It should be noted that just because your distributor number ends with 009
it is not necesarilly one of the infamous "009" aftermarket distributors.
Here's a list of a few of the original Bosch distributors found on 914's
and, as you can see, 2 of them end in 009:

Bosch Distributor, 1970-72 914-4 1.7L  0-231-174-001  
Bosch Distributor, 1972 914-4 1.7L  0-231-174-007    
Bosch Distributor, 1973 914-4 2.0L  0-231-174-009    
Bosch Distributor, 1974 914-4 1.8L  0-231-181-009   
Bosch Distributor, 1974 914-4 2.0L  0-231-174-011    
Bosch Distributor, 1975 914-4 1.8L  0-231-176-048   
Bosch Distributor, 1975-76 914-4 2.0L  0-231-172-021 

If it is an original from a 1.7 or 2.0 914 it will have an electical
connection on the lower part of the body with a 4 pin (5 maybe?) connector
for the fuel injection trigger points.  The 1.8 motors used L-jet FI so they
don't have this feature.
