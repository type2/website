> ***********************************************************The cheapest method I found for improving heat in a van was to build a 
curtain behind the front seats. I used quilting material, with the 
built-in layer of "silver" stuff to help radiate heat forward. Total 
cost was under $20. I used it in a 73, so I did have the advantage of an 
improved heater (via the blower & larger engine)... but up 'til then, 
I'd always had a draft on my kneck, until I traveled at least 20 miles, 
on a mild day. To see out the window, and allow some heat to circulate, 
I cut a small opening in the blanket at mirror level.

When things were warmer, I could bunch the blanket up, using some strips 
of the cover material as ties. 

When I find an old split, I think I'll look into *SOMEHOW* adding a 
blower into the system. Having driven both, I think this is the major 
improvement they put in the "later" vans.
