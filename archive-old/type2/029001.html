After putting up with rattling and squeeking cabinets in my Westy, I finally
pulled in for a pitstop at the friendly local big box hardware store and
picked up some 1/2 diameter sticky back felt pads.  I think they are called
"furniture protectors".

A sticky felt here, a sticky felt there.  Generally, I put them two to a
swing out door, on the sides and top of the box the drawer slides into, and
on all four sides of the "portal" behind the sink.  Where ever doors, lids
or drawers can rattle, squeek and bang.

I drove off, and over some railway tracked.  Thunk, thunk.  A nice solid
sound.  All, and I mean ALL noticable cabinet chatter was now gone.  Of
course, its still sounds like a VW, just not like one about to come apart.

Best $2.50 I ever spent on Gretchin.  Just a reminder to those Westy owners
who may be putting off such a simple cheap improvement.  Don't get them too
think, or things won't close when you are done.  The 1/2 round pads worked
perfectly well.
