Way back when, no-one knew that these old vans would be precious.  They were
cheap useful transportation for people who had a little imagination and more
than a little willingness to put in a bit of time with a tool box.

(expensive)brackets was left off (as it usually was.   Besides,  If you painted
flames around the hole in the bumper it looked kind of cool.  Hey!  Dual
exhaust!
