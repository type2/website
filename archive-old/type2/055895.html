if your steering damper is bad, you can get a sort of vibrating wobble when
you hit a bump in the road, that you can steer out of.

So replace your steering damper.


All the bits in the steering add up.  A little wear here a little there, and
you start blowing around in the wind.   Why not get a new drag linkif the end
is worn.  It'll steer better.
