a respirator when spraying anything is a must IMO.

Personally i like the POR15 brushed... if you have pockmarked metal the
brush tends to cause the por15 to pool in the pits evening things out, i
dont think that would happen as well when spraying.  But i have yet to
spray the regular stuff... but if the metal is all surface rust and no
pits i think spraying would be better definitely...  spraying almost
always tends to give a better coat... (though if you have good brushes and
the right paint you can get it glassy, i think that where cars are
concerned spraying is optimal.)

With the regular por15 i have yet to get a less than perfect finish with
my brush...(unless i let it run, heh)

I have never used it anywhere but under a fender though... i dont think
i'll use it in a visible place, i will go with tried and true there...
replace metal sandblast etc.
