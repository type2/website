>i have recently installed a rotary/electric fuel pump .. 3 1/2 lbs of fuel
>pressure .. do these pumps suck gas from the fuel tank or do they use gravity
>to get the gas and then pump it out to the carbs?  reason i ask is i mounted

Pumps do not suck .... Period.
Their design may be such that they can still have acceptable inlet fuel 
flow at some pressure less than 14.7 psi (1 atmosphere), but they all still 
require adequate NPSH in order to maintain prime under proper flow operation.

I suspect your pump does require a positive head to prime itself.
On the other hand, it may require positive head to prime, and yet still be 
designed such that it will continue with proper performance with inlet 
pressures less than 1 atmosphere.

Without knowing exactly what you have, my answer will be a guess.
Can you call the manufacturer?
