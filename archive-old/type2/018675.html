TBone:
Been there, tried that! The internal linkage in the nose cones functions
differently between the 2 and the slots in the rails are facing
differently. I think the cases might interchange if you'd care to remove
all the insides from the vanagon trans, replace the shift rails w/ the
76-79 rails and reassemble. To do this you'd need a few special tools
and about 6 hours (for me). If you're gonna take one that far apart
might as well SELL the functioning Vanagon trans and fix the loaf trans.
The Vanagon trans probably has more market value.
