The one real disadvantage I've found to distributed resources is that
they tend to drop out from time to time.   For example, I "took
advantage of the power of the web" by just linking to some further tent
info at other sites, but some of them have vanished.  Link URLs change.

fascinating may be yanked by the site owner for reasons unknown.  etc.
etc.

I agree in spirit with the web-ring concept, but it would at least need
some master index/search capability.  Something like ... the type2
library page.
