 The heat does tend to have a distinct odour- either of hot oil, if you
have oil in the heat exchangers or of hot metal, if you don't. It
shouldn't be severe, though, just a friendly scent rather than an 
overpowering blast of noxious fumes!
 If your engine is running hot it would tend to be smellier.
