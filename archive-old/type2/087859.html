Your numbers are off with the years you list:
75 is 0 280 200 012. This was a 6 pin AFM. It fits 74 Cal w/auto trans, 75
1800, and 76 2.0L up to the end of 12/75.

1/76 and 77 was 0 280 200 018. It is a 7 pin AFM.
78 and 79 Fed was 0 280 200 020.
The 020 superceded the 018. This means you can use the 020 on 1/76 and up, all
2.0L bay buses except 79 Cal.

The 0 280 200 022 is 79 Cal ONLY. It is not designed to work on anything else.
[80 and up Vanagon is 025]
