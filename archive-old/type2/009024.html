On the positive side: a black wire from the harness coming down from the heater fan [this harness should have 3 wires in it: blue to the oil sender, black to + on coil, and white to – on coil], a second black going to the in-line fuse on the left channel.
On the negative side: green fom the the distributor, white from the previous harness, and white from the grey covered injection harness [this is a single wire coming from the passenger side].
