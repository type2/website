Here is my opinion on your questions....
Oil changes really depend on the climate your in, and the type of driving you
do, In your part of the world, If your going to drive from Late October to Late
April, and you only go short distances Less than 30min drive, then you will have
to change your oil more frequently.

If you drive LONG distances,, you should change your oil more frequently,,
