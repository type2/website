> I was driving my bus home last night when the engine quit (1600 DP

> I've never had a coil fail before.  Is this common or could something

Well as someone else said, a bad condensor *can* cause a coil to fail.
Letting the bus sit with the key on (to play the radio) can also strain
the coil.  But after 20-30 years, just age can cause failure as well.  It
is not common, but it does happen.
