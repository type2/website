> When I turned the engine over the first time, it squealed like a stuck pig
> until you pushed the clutch down. I tried doing some adjusting, but it didnt
...
> I pulled the engine today and found the end of the guide sleeve(covers the
> input shaft) was chewed up and busted a little. THe Brasilian TO bearing
> feels a little odd when turning it by hand(maybe just me) and the ring that
> was on the little fingers on the pressure plate was dangling on the input
> shaft when I pulled the engine out.

There is your problem,  You have a late model throwout bearing and an
early pressure plate.  Late pressure plates allow the throwout bearing to
ride directly on the fingers without the ring that you found dangling.

You need to replace the pressure plate with a late model item.  Since the
throwout bearing is questionable, then you need to replace it with the
proper late model part as well.

The clutch disc is the same for early and late.
