They will, but be sure to check the shipping rates first.  I shipped a
transmission (I packed it) through Mailboxes, Etc, and they shipped it
(and charged me for) "2-day" when I wanted Ground.  Cost me about $40
because I didn't check UPS' website first to calc the rate.

You might also want to check with a trucking outfit.  You could strap the
beam to a pallet and ship it as (I think) "used automobile parts".  The
shipping industry rate structure is byzantine, so it might be good to talk
to someone knowlegeable in the business.
