You said it was stalling when you came to a stop, right? I would put my
money on the valves needing that first adjustment. On a new engine ( or head
job) I always run the engine for a while get it warmed up set timing,
carburetor settings etc.  Let it cool down, until it's cold, and adjust the
valves.  Then I run it for about 50 miles and adjust the valves again.

What kind of dizzy do you have?
