I have *finally* cataloged the Official VW Parts Microfiche that I 
purchased back in January.  I have already sold all the Audi fiche 
(thanks Tom F.) and am now ready to sell the extra set of VW fiche.  

I have priced these to sell separately.  They are the large format 
(roughly 7" x 9") and list the parts available at the date of 
publication.  So the '93 EuroVan will have lots of parts depicted for the 
sheet published in '93, but the '68 - '79 Bus will have have quite a few 
parts "no longer available".  Along with the parts listed are nice line 
drawings of the parts and their relationship to each other.

The publication dates range from 1978 to 1994 (most are 1992/3) and cover 
most models.  Prices range from $3 - $20 per sheet depending on model, 
age, number of pages printed, and how I was feeling the day I priced them.

I have a list available for anyone interested.  Just ask ...

(Sorry, the '67 and earlier Type II fiche is not available)

When I first talked about this back in January I received several 
requests for specific fiche, but I have since lost that file.  I regret 
that my poor memory forces me to begin anew ...
