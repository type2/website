Hi Brian,

	The best product that I have found for removing stickers is called
Goo-Gone.  It is available at most hardware stores or office supply stores.
WD-40 will also remove the stickers, but will leave a oily residue on the
paint.  If the stickers have been there for more than a few months out in
the elements there will more than likely be a bit of discoloration in your
paint finish from UV fading.  Be sure to try these products in an
inconspicuous place first to be sure that you like the results.
