there seems to be two ways to get the heads on past the upper tin


1.  the official and D method, take everything off the top of the engine
and remove the upper tin.  this is how VW and the manuals all say to do
it, it adds at least an hour (usually more) to a head removal.

2.  cheat,  in this case you bend the corner of the tin that blocks the
head (pulley end) flat remove the scews that hold the upper tin and just
lift it enough to get the head on and off.

have done it both ways, have never had any trouble gettin the tin bent
back to shape (work carefully) or getting the head bolts torqued.

it is real tight on the gen/alt side (cyl 1/2)
