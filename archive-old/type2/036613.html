According to Tom Wilson's book - "How to Rebuild your Volkswagen Air-Cooled
Engine" - engines with letter code "GE"  are listed as " '79  2000(cc)
Calif. only Vanagon heads."

According to the "Volkswage Official Service Manual"

  Ignition timing (1975 cars            5 deg ATDC with vacuum hoses
     and 1979 and later                      connected
     Calif. cars)

  Ignition timing (1976 and              7.5 deg BTDC with vacuum
     later - except 1979 and                hoses connected
     later Calif cars)

That extra "0" might be an "*" (or some approximation thereof) indicating
the engine had been rebuilt.

So, if it's got its fuel injection, proper distributor, etc. I'd say time it
at 5 deg ATDC.  If it's got carbs, some other distributor than original,
etc. then you'll have to experiment.
