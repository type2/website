Heat the lugs with a torch. Sometimes that helps to break them free.
I've heard of people heating lugs with torches, then pressing a candle
against them. The idea is that the molten wax will wick into the
threads.

Also spray them repeatedly with PB Blaster
http://www.blasterproducts_dot_com/

Be careful not to strip them. You might try taking them to a different
shop with some higher power lug wrenches.
