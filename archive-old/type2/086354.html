Brennan,

I am not sure what you are getting from the bus depot. The '72-'74 stock
system would consist of the heat exchangers (one piece), connecting pipes (4
two forward and two aft), muffler, tailpipe and gasket installation set.
There are 4 copper oval gaskets to mate the exchanger to the head. There are
2 trapezoidal gaskets to mate the exchangers to the muffler and a triangular
gasket to mate the tail pipe to the muffler. Also some new copper self
locking nuts are needed to bolt it all up. The four pipes are different than
you stock system The flapper boxes (valves) will do fine.
