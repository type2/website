On Sun, Jul 20, 2003 at 11:35:39PM -0400, Collie wrote:
> Is there a way to adjust the master cylinder\brake light switch so that the
> brakelight is activated sooner?

I had the same problem in my 79. What I did was to remove the switches
& clean them. Let them dry. Then pump once or twice to flush out the
small passages in the master cyl that feed the switches. After doing
this & bleeding the system again, I did notice that the lights came on
earlier, but still not as quick as I had hoped.
