-->  I had the glass over my fuel gauge/idiot light cluster come loose in
my '75.  The rattling drove me nuts until I took it apart.  Mine looked
like it had originally been held in with little plastic hooks (broken) and
glue.  I just stuck it back in with RTV, and trimmed off any that showed
after it dried.  It's not the prettiest way to do it, but it doesn't
rattle now.

The culprit here, I think, was me constantly tapping the glass to free up
a sticky fuel gauge needle. ;)
