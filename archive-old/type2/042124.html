My guess is that while he had things hooked up backwards he melted the
insulation on the points rendering them always grounded.  You get one
guess as to how I know this can happen.

Solution is to replace the points.  You will find that the wire leading
to the points inside the distributor is a crispy critter, having produced
that impressive smoke that was shooting out of the vent holes on the
bottom of the distributor.
