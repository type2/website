I have converted a few cars that had sealed systems to 134.  No problems...I
believe the problems occur with a system that has problems...i.e.  most vw
systems......someone needs to replace o-rings, etc, vacuum test system
before converting.  I think that the internal pressures are different for
r12 and r134, and thus the hose ratings may be different.  If your hoses are
shaky, by all means replace (have a shop crimp old ends on new line) before
converting.
