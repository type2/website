The flux cored wire welders do not work nearly as well as MIG, only
to be used outside really where your gas gets blown away.  Strong
supporter of harbor freight I am I caution against their MIGs, I
strongly considered it but called and found that the stock warranty is
like 30 DAYS or so and a 2-3 year one is like an extra 75% of price.  I
was not impressed.  After looking locally at the cheaper under $600
Lincolns and the Century stuff available from Sams Club, I went with
Sams larger Century, warranty was right, price right for a 220V unit
with its output, and quality looked much BETTER to me than the Lincoln
units.  YMMV.
