Erich:

If this is a '73 auto trans, there is one simple reason that it might not
shift properly.
I think you should take another look at this proposed purchase;  see if the
vacuum hose from the trans to the manifold is intact.  If it is missing or
leaking, the auto trans will not shift properly.

What you do with that information is, of course, up to you...
