It sounds like the brushes in your alternator might need to be replaced. I haven't had any 
problems with my VW vans alternaturs but I have on other cars. On my '84 Vanagon the 
brush/regulator asembly screw into the back of the alternator and are easy to check and or 
replace. I dun't know if your alternator would be setup the same. 

What you describe as a flicker of the battery light at higher RPMs is something I have 
witnessed when the alternator brushes were worn. Something to consider anyway..... If your 
alternator is not functioning and your battery is nearly dead I wouldn't push start it to go 
anywhere because the ignition and whatever other electrical drains will take whats left in the 
battery and leave you dead!
