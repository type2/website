Assuming your speedometer is accurate (and that's a big IF based on the
'72's I've had experience with), here's what I do:

Fast hill-climb speed in 3rd: 45mph (~4100rpm).
Leisurely hill-climb speed in 3rd: 40mph.  Easiest on the engine IMO.
Minimum hill-climb speed in 3rd: 35mph.  Lower than that and I slow down
and grab 2nd so as not to lug the engine.

When I'm pulling hills, I upshift from 2-3 at 30mph and 3-4 at 50mph
(~4400rpm).  If I have to pull a hill in 2nd, I keep it at 25-28mph.
