There are two screws (maybe four on some years?) on either side of 
the transmission that come up from the bottom.  Don't know why VW did 
that but it's a pain if you don't realize they are there.  Also if 
you are doing this with the engine in, you can get a little extra 
room by taking off the distributor cap and rotor, and by unbolting 
the rear hanger bar and letting the engine drop a little (like maybe 
an inch).
