I was in Mexico, Belize, and Guatemala recently with my 72 Westy. In
Mexico I bought the still available CHROMED sliding windows.
It is a set of three, as on the left rear there is also a sliding
window.
There is no sliding window for behind the sliding door available for
safety reasons (stick your head out and someone opens the sliding door).
It is a mystery why no-one has started to import these windows. They
have all the DOT stamps and are available with different tints and in
green. I'm sure they would sell well.
