Your 022 905 205 E dizzy came from a 411 with 1.7 engine.
I do not know the model numbers that these engines fit, which used the E dizzy:

W007334 to W057034  022
W057035 to W129581  M553/022:2
EA000001 to EA039464 M553/022

M553 should be USA specs.

The engine numbers appear to be 1970 yr model for the 022.
Aug 1 1970 to July 31 71 for the M553/022:2.
And Aug 1 71 thru early 72 for the M553/022
