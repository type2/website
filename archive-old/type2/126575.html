wouldn't be allowed to own a car more than 10 years old unless it's a
100% restored classic (in their eyes, of course)? I have. I've run
into both apartment management and housing associations with that
rule.

eyesores. They don't wan't to insult their fine sensibilities by
having to see us. So they convince themselves that they need to
restrict the use of these vehicles. Since they're not using the latest
technology, they MUST pollute more. Nevermind that a new vehicle would

their decisions.
