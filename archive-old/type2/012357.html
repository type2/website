This one has really intrigued me as it is a seamless little idea, but
I see a couple of issues, though I don't know for sure if they are dealt
with or not.  Does it have a method to bypass that little filter, as in, if
it were to get full of too much crud (I know it shouldn't ever happen,
but...) will it still get oil?  Second, is filtering the suction side such
a great idea anyway from a restriction POV in general.  Most filter
installations of course pass the high pressure oil though so this isn't
an issue.  All in all, I don't know, neat idea, but maybe not smart.

>The second item was a replacement oil pump with and inlet and outlet already
