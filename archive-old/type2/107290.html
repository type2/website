Yes, all but two. The NLA molded hose from the s-boot to the decel valve and
the NLA molded hose from the s-boot to the oil breather. The latter failed

>If so where were you able to find all the different sizes? I noticed
> that some of the hoses had part numbers listed. Are those still available from

I'm sure some size are still available at the dealer as they must still be
in common with modern VWs.

I measured all the nipples and connectors because hose was hard to find and
I wanted a list I could fit hose to in the future. There are only a few
sizes available. I'm sure if you hunted down every European make you'd find
all the sizes.

I got some small pieces from Bus Boys and some locally. Between the two I
covered all the hoses, except the two NLA molded hoses.

VWFLAPS:

3.5mm, 4.5mm (maybe 5mm I can't recall), 7mm, 12mm

Bus Boys:

B. Hose, Valve to Plastic Tube
Hose/Boot - Center Air Plenum to Air Intake Runners
Hose - From Air Intake Elbow Boot to Aux Air Control Valve
Hose Elbow - Aux Air Control Valve to Hose above
Hose - Aux Air Valve to Center Air Plenum
