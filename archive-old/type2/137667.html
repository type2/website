Subject: Re: [T2] Bussing on "winter" gasoline

Hi Brian,

Older cars don't like oxy fuel because the seals are older and tend to
break down and leak under exposure to this fuel. The formulation
difference is not significant enough to cause such a radical change in
gas mileage.

I'd look more to fuel delivery issues. Are you running lots of short
trips where the choke is engaged and doesn't really warm up enough to
fully disengage before your trip is ended?

As you operate the accellerator through the various RPM's, observe
down the carb throat and ensure there is no leakage or dripping inside
caused by ruptured accellerator pump or stuck needle valve.

You may also be using more throttle to compensate for fuel
condensation on cold intake manifolds.

John
