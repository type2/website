Did you run a wire from one of the 2 spade terminals on the starter solenoid
to the double relay? When the starter solenoid gets the crank signal, the
relay also gets 12v to run the pump. The wire goes to terminal 86a of the
relay.
If you have this wire and it is connected to one of the top 2 spades on the
solenoid [NOT the lower one!] then disconnect it and touch it to the battery
post on the solenoid. Listen and you should hear the pump running strong and
steady. If it is not running, get a test light or voltmeter and see if you
have 12v at the pump. If you do, you have either a bad ground at the pump or
a bad pump. If you don't, you have either a bad double relay or the wiring
is incorrect.
Do not give up on the FI so quickly.....correct the problem 1st.
