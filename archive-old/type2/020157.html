Before resorting to drilling holes, is your poptop seal in good shape. 
I got one from Ron _at_ Busdepot (~$20 IIRC) and have had NO problems
since.  It's a nice tight seal between the poptop and window frame. 
Also check the condition of the wooden mounting blocks on the front
hinges.  One of mine was rotten (from rain leaking around the bolt),
allowing the bolt/poptop to lift up ~1/2 inch.
