Stan Wilder wrote:

Sounds like you've got a bad ground wire, something is drawing down your
current, a loose or poor ground will do just what you're describing.. I'd
just double ground the engine with a jumper cable to test it.

Feel of the sides of your battery, if they are swolen out it will mean
you have an internal short. Wash your battery top down with baking soda,
a dirty battery can also drain down current.
