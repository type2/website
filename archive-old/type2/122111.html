This is most likely a very common and easy to fix problem. Look on the type2 
home page under Library, look under "S" for "Starter", and read the Starter 
Relay Installation article.

You could probably use a relay other than the one specified (I think a 
standard Ford starter contactor works). In the meantime, you might be able 
to get it to start when hot by crawling underneath the bus and beating on 
the solenoid with a screwdriver handle or small hammer.
