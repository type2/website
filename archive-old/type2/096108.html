Any change from dino to syn should be done at a full oil change (and
filter if have one)

I have run synthetics for many years,  when we tore  down my 2 liter for
rebuild around 183k, unknown lubes for 114k synthetic for last 69k we
found the engine to be in remarkably clean condition and very little
unusual wear.  rebuilt to spec and ran good quality single weight dino for
break in and synthetic ever since, still not a drop of oil used between
changes.  added about another 60k to it since rebuild. still appears to be
quite clean, change oil at about 4k change filter on every oil change.
