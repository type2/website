Jack-a-Roe,

The arms are not specific to which cylinder they are over, but they are
specific to which valve they are over - exhaust or intake. 

I believe the one with more 'meat' between the arm and the bearing part goes
over the intake, but i could be wrong - no visual reference available now.  
If you install them wrong, the adjuster screw will not line up correctly over
the valve stem.  It is fairly obvious when you have the wrong arm over the
wrong valve.  Compare the rocker amrs and notice the differences.

Does this make sense to you?  Look at your arms - check your bentley.

I'd say you have them wrong. - i almost did it wrong till my local VW guru
said "No! They ARE different!"  You probably haven't damaged anything but
maybe an adjuster screw or two...
