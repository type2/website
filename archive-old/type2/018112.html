Scott:

Well, you can either get a different exhaust system or different heads
(or whole engine). If Ron would do the switch thru AVP and the freight
isn't a problem, getting a 78 style engine might be the easiest.
The 49-state 79 heater boxes are probably superior to the 75-78 style,
however, because they're larger (like the 72-74's) and also the square
ports have much improved gasket design. If you can get past emissions
people, the best plan in my judgment would be to use 79 heater boxes and
a 72-74 muffler (which eliminates the crossover and y-pipe). This is the
muffler that I prefer to use on 49-state air cooled vanagons also. The
front lower sheet metal is slightly different as is the piece that
covers the bottom. I have a set of barely useable 79 heat exchangers
that could be patched if you are a good welder and have more time than
money. The freight would be the biggest part of the price, probably want
$10 each. I've also probably got some or all of the 79 style tin.
Ron may sell 79 heat exchangers, probably around $300/set.
