I had the very same problem with my 1970 Campmobile - definitely no disks to
warp or servo to get sick.  My VW "specialist" insisted that it was a warped
front drum despite the fact that all 4 drums checked out fine for
eccentricity.  This was ~1500 miles after this guy did a complete rear brake
rebuild.

I finally found a left rear brake shoe's lining had come adrift and a large
chunk of it was intermittently being trapped between the steel shoe and the
drum.  The brand new drum was gouged far beyond salvage.  And it never made
a sound that was discernible inside the bus.
