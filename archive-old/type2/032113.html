Never much cared for Newt Gingrich, but he made an astute obvervation
["astute" = "I agree" + "I'm not actually prepared to admit that he
thought of it before I did"].  While zipping along the Autobahn at
comfortably high speeds, he realized a difference between Americans and
Germans: 

If the German government lowered the speed on the Autobahn to 55mpn [or
its kph equivalent], everyone would drive 55mph until the next election,
at which time they'd vote those representatives out, vote in some that
would raise the limit again, and then go back to driving like bats out of
hell. 

By comparison, in the USA, everyone would complain bitterly, continue to
drive 80mph anyway, and re-elect 90% of the same representatives back into
office.
