memory, I recall the frequency of best utility to capture attention was 5 
to 7 flashes per second. I installed such a devise on my BMW R90S. I have

safety item. You get a whole higher order of magnitude better response from 
followers. Indeed, better recognition stats were obtain for daylight tests 
as opposed for night time.
