Yes there will be, and it can cause damage if you mix clutch parts.  Since
you have the new style transmission and throwout bearing, you should get
the new style pressure plate (no ring to match throwout bearing) to match.
If you want to go the other way,
you have to remove the sleeve and replace the cross-shaft and throwout
bearing with old-style parts.
