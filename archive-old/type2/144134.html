I have a set of these "Hagus" mirrors, and I can testify that the metal
doesn't rust and
the glass quality is excellent.  Unfortunately, the translucent plastic
around the glass does deteriorate in the sun over the years.  I finally

ask them to switch to a UV-resistant plastic.  These mirrors are sooooo
close to being perfect, but they really dropped the ball with the plastic!
