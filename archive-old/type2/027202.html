  Sounds like the old "voltage drop" problem. I installed
a 12 volt relay back by the starter and operate it with the key. The relay
provides a short path from the battery to the starter. seems that long wire run
>from the back to the front and all the way to the back again can cause problems.
I put this relay in 7 years ago and havent had the problem since. I got my
relay at my local Vdubb place.... they called it a "hard start relay"... I have heard of
people useing a ford starter relay too.
