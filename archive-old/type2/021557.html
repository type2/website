-Check all of your spark plug wires (try swapping or buy new ones if
they're old)
-Get new plugs (couldn't hurt)
-change out the injector seals
-test the injectors (good thing to do while changing seals)
-UNLESS YOU HAVE NEW FUEL LINES, CHANGE YOUR FUEL LINES NOW!  It's cheap
insurance and it's much easier when you have the injectors out!
-Heed all safety warnings when playing with fuel :)
-Check your valve clearances
-Try swapping injector connectors - don't worry about mixing them up,
they all fire at the same time
-Make sure the spark plug wires coming off the distributor cap are going
to the correct cylinders
-Check the distributor cap to see if there's major buildup
-Do a compression test on all the cylinders
-Check your fuel pressure
