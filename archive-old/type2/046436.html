Shawn,

     What I did was drill & tap a couple of 1/4" x 2" x 8" flat bars.  I
drilled them 1/4"-20.  Then I had a friend slide them up on the inside
between the airbox & the front sheet metal.  I guided him until I could get
a 1/4" hex head bolt through the bracket, front sheet metal & into the flat
bar.  What this does is slightly distribute the load of the tire & bracket
across a wider area.  It also allows you to bolt into something stronger
then thin sheet metal.  Any question drop me a line.
