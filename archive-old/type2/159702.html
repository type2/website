Subject: distributor caused poor starting, fast idle and random loss of
  power.....

Methinks the 75 bus hard starting, "random loss of power" and random 
fast idle has been solved-daughter's bus has Pertronics, so such users 
forget that a timing light is useful for other things- while playing 
with the idle I noticed that while checking the RANGE of the advance 
that it would go to max but not always come down, causing a faster idle 
-and TAPPING on the distr body helped- ah-ha, centrifugal weights are 
sticking?  Tried using a penetrant/lube under plate- NaDa, so I yanked 
the distr and put in the spare points one from Ted Finemann's $5 parts 
box (thanks Ted, been carrying that around for years) and lo and behold, 
goes easily to 7 deg BTDC, idles fine on warm up and the milage jumped 
back up to 22 mpg on her trip back to school.  It also helped that I 
replaced the calipers- see add. post.
