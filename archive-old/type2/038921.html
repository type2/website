Nikki,

I suffer from a similar affliction...  I don't look at
the glass as being half-full, nor do I see it as
half-empty....I just worry about my ability to refill
it before it's empty!!! ;-)

Many people who know me, think I'm a pessimist, but I
disagree. I'd simply rather prepare for an emergency,
than be caught short.

My thinking is this...if I prepare for the worst, and
it happens, well...I'm prepared. I'm not happy that
the event occurred, but at least I was ready for it.

If I prepare for the worst, and it *doesn't* happen,
well...I'm happier still!

I figure that I'm pragmatic, and that's not a bad
thing.
