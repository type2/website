> I'm not sure what year bus you have, but I have a '78 and it's supposed 
> to do that. There is a spring-loaded catch on both hinges. So, let's 
> say you have it set up as a bed, if you lift the seat portion, the 
> catches will click and now all you have to do is pull the seat towards 
> the front of the bus and the backrest will rise up and fold into the 
> seat position. 
> 
> Now, usually to unclick it (when it's in the bed position), you just 
> keep pushing the seat porting towards the back of the bus and it will 
> click again and then you can pull the seat back down (towards the front 
> of the bus) to the normal bed position. You might want to check out 
> your catches because they can get bent and/or have broken springs.
