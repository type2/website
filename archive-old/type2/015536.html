Engine maintenance inspection required.

1. compression balance
2. ingition energy measure or strong snap at the gap and timing verified
3. verify no vacuum leaks
4. check and adjust CO with a proper guage, unless this one has an OXY
which you can monitor with       a VOM. Just turning screws by guess and by
golly results 90% of the time in no solution or    worse problems.

Follow Bentley procedures in all cases. If you don't have the tools, buy
them or hire someone who has them and experience with Bosch KE Jetronic
systems.
