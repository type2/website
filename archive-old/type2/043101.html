I think the math here is not quite right.  A lever half as long requires
twice the force to achieve the same result.  So a 9" wheel would require
a 100% increase in force to do the same as an 18" wheel, right?  I think
the equation is (Original lever length)/(New lever length) = Fraction of
original force required.  So a 12" wheel would be 18/12 = 1.5, or 50%
more force.  It looks like you extrapolated linearly from the 17" value,
but the change gets proportionately greater with each additional inch
lost from the lever.
