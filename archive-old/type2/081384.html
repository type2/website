Here is the section on GL-4 [nothing about MT-1!]:

LUBRICHEM GL-4 EP Gear Oils are mild EP gear lubricants designed for a wide
range of automotive and industrial applications. They are specifically
recommended for manual transmissions and differentials in automotive, truck,
and off-highway vehicles not requiring a GL-5 product. API GL-4 gear oils may
be used wherever GL-3, GL-2, or GL-1 gear oils are recommended. Available as
SAE: 90 and 140.
