Strong suggestion: Stop driving the bus until you identify the cause and 
fix it.  It may be the steering box breaking loose from the front axle. 
Mine did this. Boxes tend to rust out internally and then break away from 
the axle. Some shops weld them, but many owners will replace the front axle 
at this point.

Some people are no longer alive, and their buses are in scrap yards after 
the wreck, because of these failures.
