Did you put a ohm meter on it and work it
through it's range? You should see a pretty
smooth meter movement when you do this. If the
meter doesnt respond smoothly as you move the
float then the sender has dead spots. Old senders
can work differently depending on their spatial
orientation.

sweeper arm degrades over time and the mechanics
get sloppy....
