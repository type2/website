>>  Can someone identify a aluminum riser block on my '67 that is bolted
>>between the carburator and manifold? It is about 1 1/2 inch tall and has
>>some sort of mechanism sticking out the side that has a vertical plate about
>>2" x3" bolted to it. the plate says 28 DR-15. As you look at the engine from
>>the rear of the bus you look right at it, as it directly in front of the
>>carb. It kind of seems to be some sort of a governor but I have no idea. Any
>>thoughts would be appreciated. thanks

Yes, thats a governor.  To adjust, take off the topmost front screw holding
on the tab, and tighten the top bolt to kick it in later, loosen it to kick
it in sooner.
