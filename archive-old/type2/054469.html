Possible Answer 1:  Adjust the choke flap on your carb to be closed when the
engine is cold..

Possible Answer 2:  My old upright engine used to do such when the timing
was too retarded - what kind of distributor do you have?  A 009 would need
to be revved up to get enough advance that it doesn't die when put under a
load (especially if the advance is already a little too retarded)...

Timing, mixture, all that stuff needs to be right on..
