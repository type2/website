The PO of my '67 had taken apart the ignition switch to fiddle with the
tumblers. He then taped it together, and with time, the tape wore loose, and
produced the symptoms you described; basically from loose and weak contacts. I
put the switch back together tightly, an it works fine now. Those switches are
sealed units, but sometimes the crimps wear loose, and contact is not perfect
within the switch...If that's the case, you can tighten it and re-crimp it.
