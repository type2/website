Secondly, I was warned by a few folks that this tow bar is made from 2"
tube and has only one cross-member.  Measuring at about 6 feet long, it
is subjected to too much torque while going around turns and twists up
like a pretzle.  Not good.  I'm thinking I'll have one made from angle
iron, with more cross members and attach it to the torsion tube in much
the same way as Joe's does.
