Subject: 71 Westfalia Engine (Camper top installation)

The pop top and luggage rack mount with sheet metal screws.  You do not have
to take out the headliner to install them.  There should be holes in the
roof of your bus that match the mounting holes in the pop top brackets and
luggage rack.  The luggage rack should have rubber standoffs (that the
screws pass through) that support it above the roof.
