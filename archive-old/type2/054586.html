the biggest problem with the progressive kits is inadequate manifold heat.

very difficult to tune because of it. Get a properly heated intake, and your
job is a lot easier!

The progressives are a bit of work to jet, but once you are there they are a
fantastic combination of power, drivability, and mileage.
