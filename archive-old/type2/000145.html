David,

I have the rear upper cabinet in my '74, which came off a '78 Westy.  It 
wasn't hard to install, you just line it up and drill the holes.

You didn't mention if you have a hard top or a poptop.  There is a 
difference.  The hard top rear cabinet is round at the top and the poptop 
rear cabinet is flat on the top.  Also, the upper cabinet is designed to meet 
up against the rear closet.  You may be able to center the upper  cabinet so 
it looks okay.  I will have to look at mine to see if this is possible.
