I assembled and disassembled my stock '77 earlier this year, and there is no
throttle microswitch on the 77. I know other years did have one. The wires
might be related to the diagnostic plug which is in that area (maybe for the
diagnostic rpm sensor?). Also, I think there are wires for a high-altitude
mixture adjustment which would not be used unless you're above 4000 or 5000
feet.
