Tom gently admonished:

A couple of thoughts as to your problem:  1)You've managed to pull a bearing
half out of its saddle in the case, a bearing half kept from rotating by a
dowel.  Now the dowel won't line up with the hole in the bearing, preventing
things from going back together properly.  2) This distributor drive gear is
hung up on the camshaft gear.  (I don't understand how you were able to
"twist the camshaft out" of an incompletely parted case.)

It sounds like you did minimal disassembly before trying to part the case so
remove the distributor drive shaft if you haven't already.
