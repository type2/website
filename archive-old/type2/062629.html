Subject: New smog laws in CA?

On the way into work this morning, I listened to a talk show that had as 
it's subject the new "Smog II" regulations that will be put in place 
shortly (if not already). I only got about 10 minutes into the show 
before getting to work, but the basic ideas I heard were:

1) Cars previous to 1975 (pre catalytic converters) would have a more 
difficult time passing the bi-annual smog check. 

2) Smog check prices would go up, and the information taken during a 
standard smog check would be sent directly to Sacramento. 

3) There would be road side "spot" emission testing.

One of the guests on the show (guy from Air Resources Board) intimated 
that the areas they would be focusing on strongest were the 
Davis/Sacto/Stockton areas.

Has anyone heard any more on this? Does anyone know of a centralized 
source for more information?
