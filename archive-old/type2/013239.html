Minor tidbit of Riviera trivia,

Named after the Volks dealer in Hillsboro, OR that
commisioned ASI of Vancouver to do the work.  ASI is
still in business doing other things now.  They are
built on a standard bus chassis which I have been told
is not the case with Westy.  

Appearently the Westy is done of of a sturdier
platform (anyone out there who knows more about this,
please shed some light).

Follow this link, if your bus looks like this odds on
its an ASI Riviera.

http://www.geocities_dot_com/vwcampin/asirivieralinks.html
