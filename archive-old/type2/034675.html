I believe the three letters stand for UHF (ultra high frequency), Long wave
and Medium wave.

The long wave and medium wave bands are both AM bands (amplitude modulated),
we still use them in Europe, though not so much these days, and the UHF will
I presume be a short wave band.

UHF is in the range 300-3000 megahertz and is used mainly for television and
I think some kinds of communication, like emergency services.
