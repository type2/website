Hey folks,

I just want to say that Tom Wilson's book "How to Rebuild Your Air Cooled
Volkswagen Engine" was indespensible in my rebuild. Clear pictures of just
about everything you'd need a picture of.

Bentley had all of the info - but was dispersed throughout the manual.
Wilson's book is an absolute step-by-step on identification, diagnosis (should
you rebuild?), removal, tear down, reconditioning, reassembling and
reinstallation for ALL stock air-cooled VW engines.
Muir was there for me, but Wilson was clearer.

my .02,
