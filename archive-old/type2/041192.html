David, I posted a couple of weeks ago, about the exact same problem with my 
"Lecoz" exchangers.  I replaced the left side with a new Dansk from 
JCWhitney, and it was higher than the lecoz unit.  My S&S header system which 
fit wonderfully before the replacement, took a bit of fiddling with to get it 
to fit, and the muffler rubs on the bumper bracket.

Now, as for the exhaust studs.  My FLAPS says that 8x9 is original, and my 
virgin original '74 1800 has 8x9 on all exhaust studs.  This engine has never 
been apart before I removed a few studs off of it so I could convert my 8x10s 
to helicoils.  I swapped out the original motor when it developed valve guide 
problems at 125,000 miles, and kept it in my garage for parts or future 
rebuild.

I could be wrong, or it's even possible that late 70's bus heads used the 
8x8s.
