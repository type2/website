The tolerance between the plunger and body of the lifter is the tightest in the entire engine. When the lifter is subjected to dirty oil that scores the inside, the eventual result is destruction which is usually preceeded by excessive lifter noise. When the bus is driven under those conditions the lifters fail as you saw.
