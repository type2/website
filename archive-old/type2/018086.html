Matt:
If replacing the entire axle beam assembly, bear in mind that only 68-69
will fit. 70 and later have much larger flanges and will not fit.
If you're just talking about the moving parts being worn out, this is a
normal repair item, comes in a kit and includes the pin and bushings and
seals. The whole bracket will eventually rust out and it may be
necessary to take it to a welding shop andhave it rebuilt. Good axle
beams are available from junkyards in AZ,CA,NM,TX, and so on. I paid
about $150 delivered in about 1991 for one from Phoenix (can't remember
the name _____ Brothers Salvage. Gave me excellent service, though.)
They'd be a little hard to find in the north.
