A zip-tie will prevent this embarrasment, I promise you.  Various years ov
Volkswagens have varying numbers of ring terminals attached to that post,
but the one characteristic they all share is that they are all full rings.
A zip-tie or the twit-tie from a bread bag will keep them all in one place
an prevent "lost" or "forgotten"  wires.
