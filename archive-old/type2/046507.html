Inside my driver's door jamb is a Vehicle Identification
Number (VIN) sticker.  It should bear the month/year of
your vehicle's manufacture, serial number, etc.  Look at
the bottom margin of this VIN sticker and you'll find
something that looks like this:

GVWR  LB.  4961
GAWR  LB.  FRONT 2227 / REAR 2800

I think these may be the numbers you are looking for.
They came off my Deluxe Westfalia made in May/1973
and sold in California.


You could use the tyre pressures listed in the owner's
manual and on the sticker inside the fuel filler door.

I think you are on the right track asking Michelin for
their recommendation.  I have Bridgestone RD603 tyres,
and Bridgestone was happy to provide this information.
