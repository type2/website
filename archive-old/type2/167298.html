Correct. I have swapped 6-pin and 7-pin setups and not noticed any  
significant performance difference.

In theory, there could be a problem at very high RPM on a 2L running  
1.8L ECU, because there is more airflow through the system than the  
ECU was programmed for. But with redline at 5400 RPMs, it isn't an  
issue for real life operation. No-one revs that high (intentionally)  
anyway.
