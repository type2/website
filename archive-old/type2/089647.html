Hi Steve,

The horn is negative switched i.e. the switch is on the earth side of the horn so running a direct feed from the battery to the horn will not spark as there is nowhere for the eath to go.

Use a jumper from the other side of the horn to earth to test the horn. 

If it still does not work (and fuse is good) then you have a fault in the wiring from the horn to button to earth, I'm sure there must be zillions of postings in the archives that will help you fix problems with the horn button.
