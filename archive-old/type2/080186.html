One trick I was told to use while trying to get my front and rear shift
rods apart was to heat it up hot and then get it cold real quick.  I did
this a few times in a row and the two seperated beautifully.

I used a propane torch and a rag soaked in a bucket of ice water.  I had
previously tried just heat and it didn't work...
