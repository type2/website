While you go through the engine exchange process, you can trouble shoot the FI
function.  I have found many FI wiring looms damaged by mechanics who like to
shove sharp meter leads through the insulation, breaking the wires, rather than
going to the connector plugs.  Most other FI function can be duplicated on the
bench without the engine. This is also a very good way to gain a good working
knowledge of the FI system and its function.
