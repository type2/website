yes, best to get any suspension problems fixed before spending all that

this is definitely an interesting problem! yes, i would go ahead with 
the minor adjustment that you read about in Bentley.

however, first you might want to spend a little bit of money & see if 
you actually have a definite problem. take the bus to an alignment shop

in Bentley. it might be money well-spent.
