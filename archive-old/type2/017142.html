They are probably Mexi-buses- basically 73-79 style with an 1800 Golf
water-cooled motor vertially mounted in back, and a Darth Vader-style

American southwest. VW de Mexico shows them on its website. $18-19,000,

if I could get one into Iowa legally. (I have often referred to my 69
