To join several hard vacuum lines in my MB cars, I find two styles of 
moulded
rubber connectors.  Three-way connectors (Y-shaped) and four-way connectors
(X-shaped).  Here are some more MB part numbers...
X-shaped: A 117 078 00 45
Y-shaped: A 117 078 01 45

For you junkyard scroungers, on any '80s vintage MB you'll find a bundle
