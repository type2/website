Jeff,
I have a '70 fuse box you can have, but it is not in the best of shape, 
although it is useable.
What I found out is.............
There were two types of fuse boxes used in the '70's.
One was made out of a white plastic ............. Good
The other uses a more yellow plastic ........ Bad; becomes brittle with age.

What I have in my two '70's now is the white, and I still have a "yellow" 
one I removed from Casper.
I also have the support frame that the fuse block is mounted in.
Epoxy and some ingenuity might get you by with what I have here in stores.
