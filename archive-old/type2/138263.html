products with the Porsche logo on them and the cost has
little to do with the product, but more to do with the cachet

ing to tread a fine line...It's logo implies value for money

paid tons of Euros for the Bentley name is another attempt
to upgrade it's image...And then there's the Phaeton...And
the is-it-on-the-road-yet Bugatti...

for any manufacturer to not have one on the showroom floor

GM, who put a SAAB badge on the Subaru for just that reason...:-(
