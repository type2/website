Stew,

Here is the skinny. The thread is a 10X1.0. You want to get a bolt and cut
off the head and cut a slot in the top so you can use a flat head screw
driver to thread it in. Use some red lock tight and screw it in, then use
some JB weld on the top to seal it off. You should try to count the thread
in the head and cut your stud that length. This is about 10 to 15 threads,
usually. The major concerns are first not to disrupt the air flow and second
the valve. The valve is about 1 1/2 to 2 inches down.
