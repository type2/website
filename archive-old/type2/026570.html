Take a look at your rear shift coupling.  It is a squarish metal frame with
2 rubber inserts on the sides - about 1.5" square.  It attaches to the
transmission shift shaft via a set screw that should have a piece of safety
wire through it and on the other end, there is a long (2"?) set screw that
runs through the 2 rubber pieces and sandwiches the shifter shaft between
them.  The rubber pieces wear out (and fall out).  These things are cheap
and fairly eash to replace.  Worth looking at anyway.
