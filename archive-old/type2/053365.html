I suppose you could, but then you want to worry about bottoming out.  If you
read what it says on the pages I listed, it says that changing the ride height
too far from stock can cause extra wear on cv joints.  If your bus is already
sitting too high, lowering to stock might be ok.  But if you're looking to make
it a lowrider, go with the aftermarket kits, and do more research.
