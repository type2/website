Subject: Re: [T2] What kind of ignition coil do I need?

Marc said:
I have an 81 Vanagon that was originally CA emissions.  Following the advice of the Bus Depot, I've replaced
the exhaust system with the 49 state exhaust and run a new oxygen sensor into the crossover pipe.

 I think the ignition coil is bad.  Do I have to stick with a CA one, or can I switch to a 49 state one (which
the store right down the street has and I can get today...)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you still have the factory electronic ignition, you HAVE to use the correct electronic ignition coil.
NOT the 49 state coil!

The coil you need is the same as a early rabbit with electronic ignition: 211 905 115B

The 68-79 Bentley has a typo. They say 211 905 115C but this is wrong. It is correct in the 80-85 Bentley
Vanagon book.

The Bosch short number for this coil is 00041.
