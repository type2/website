This is great advice if you own a 68-73 Bus, but the 74
up
Bus has a different type of handle. You no longer have
the
ability to lift the handle to suck the back of the door
in.
You are forced to 'slam' it to make it shut. 
  The best thing I can suggest to do it to clean and lube
the door real good. This involves pulling the door off
the
track, cleaning all the rollers and tracks, greasing them
and putting it back together. Then use the Bently and
adjust the door. I have had several of these, with the
proper lube and adjustment they work great. I had a 77
that
was smooth as silk. While you have it off the track be
sure
to look at all the springs, rollers, guides, and track
for
wear, and defects. I have had to replace some of these
parts.

what I can do.
