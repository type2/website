OK say you don't have a "Big Ass........."
This is where math beats brute strength.  Foot-pounds means feet times
pounds.  Stand on a bathroom scale where you can apply force with your
breaker bar.  Say it's a 250 ft.-lb nut.  Mark the bar 5 ft from the center
of the nut.  While watching the scale, when 50 lbs. is taken OFF your
apparent weight, therefore applied to the nut, times 5 ft., you have 250
ft.-lb.
