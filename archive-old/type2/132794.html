I have a Blaupunkt Las Vegas model CD player in my 77.

We put the wire to the fusebox to the taillight fuse (#11 I think), and mine
has an inline fuse. We just used the inline fuse holder from the previous
stereo.
The bolt and nut on the back are for a support bar, if you use one. I had
one existing with the previous stereo. The deck should have come with some
sort of sleeve that will attempt to keep it from sliding back and forth.
