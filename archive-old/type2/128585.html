Mike,

If your going to use a Dupont fnish go for the Chroma Base.--better
paint than the econo Nason--

You can urethane clear over One Shot--the lettering paint, and it will
flow out pretty good---

If they lay enough of the clear over the hand painted area's--it can be
wet sanded with 1500--2000 and buffed to a smooth, unlumpy finish--

Another suggestion.
Find a Dupont dealer--and for the final primer coat use Duponts Uro
Prime--
It's a 3 part urethane primer--add a shot of flex agent to it--

It's super easy to sand-won't attract moisture--and makes for a super
base for any finish coat--


Also--heave the painter add a shot of flex agent to the clear coat--it
repels chips , dents, and solvents real good--it makes the finish coat
almpost bullet proof--

Hope ths answer's your question---
