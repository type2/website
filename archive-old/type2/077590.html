My only and best advice would be to consult www.busboysinc_dot_com and look for
the rebuild kits for your seats. They sell new covers and padding. You just
need a pair of Hogring pliers to install. The only other option I am aware
of is to get yourself a nice set of Vanagon seats and slide em in. They go
right on a 78' you just have to remove the drivers side left arm rest. In
your case you may need to unbolt the runners from your old seats and bolt
them to the Vanagon seats.
