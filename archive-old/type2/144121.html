#11 is the plastic fan. It WON'T blow a fuse!
#2 is the motor and fan assembly. It can blow a fuse.

Before you use the Mossberg repair style [replacing
parts before testing by shotgunning.....] unplug the 2
wire connector on the back of the motor and put a new
fuse in. Turn on the fan, see if it blows. If yes, it
is not the motor. If no, plug in the motor. Does it
blow the fuse? If yes, replace the motor.
