OK, in this instance, forget Bentley.... it shows the
74/75 sensor location.

In your case, it is on the left cylinder head, just
forward of the #3 intake runner. On top. 

This is if the left head is still on the left side:)

Find the 3 and 4 injecter connecters. Follow the grey
sheath to the main loom. It will go left and right.
Left goes to the double relay and the injecter
resister pack. In between the injecters and the relays
is a single wire. Follow it to the sensor.
