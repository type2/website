Guess I need to through my thoughts in here.

1.  Because of work schedules and school, my family does a fair amount of
camping in the summer. Here in west Texas the night time temps stay
around 90 degrees. If I want my wife and kids to go camping, there will
be AC. There will also be running water, hot showers and electricity
available, so no generator worries here.

2. It is rare to see anyone without AC around here unless they are in a
tent.  Even the pop-up campers have AC.  I have yet to here one so loud
it bothered me in my campsite.
Ask the folks down on the Gulf Coast about camping in the summer.

3. What you think is silly, others feel is absolutely necessary.  God
made us all different, thats what makes life so interesting.

My Westy presently has no AC on it.  My family has not camped in it
during the hot weather either
