>1) Tried to time the engine yesterday, and the timing marks are gone.
>Is this a decal, and if so, where can I get one?

Look for a tiny notch on the pulley(fan).  As to any decal, no.  There is a
plastic scale shaped like a fourth of a circle more or less that has "o" and
several BTDC and ATDC increments marked.  This is attached just left of the
fan pulley.  Contact a list vender, a good Cali junkyard, etc for the
plastic piece.
