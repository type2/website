Hi Volks,

It's been  mild winter in the Northeast.... The tundra around Toronto  
still hasn't frozen over!  Regardless, the TCWBC will still be going  
on as usual on the weekend of February 18th.

TCWBC is a one-night campout near Hamilton, Ontario Canada; keeping  
you comfortable with serviced sites and flush toilets & showers
