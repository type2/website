On a Safety-Kleen parts washer, there is a wire that holds the lid up. If
the solvent in the tank catches fire, the wire melts and lets the lid drop,
which is supposed to cut off the air to the fire and cause it to go out.

Next time the Safety-Kleen guy comes to our shop, I'll ask him if he knows
where to get some.

A spring, some of this wire, a few eyebolts...maybe a grommet for the cable
to pass through so you could put the bottle inside the Bus. I would want a
second cable up in the cab just for backup.
