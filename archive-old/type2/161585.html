Don, There are quite a few enzyme-based products that will get rid of the 
smell. Before our GD Cat was fixed, we used "Nature's Miracle" for his

bottle should be 6 bucks. I guess it's worth a shot before junking or 
re-doing the upholstery. At least check it out on the web.
