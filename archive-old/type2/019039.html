Not a 'bastard' engine.  First year of the type4 'pancake' engine.  No access
hole over the engine.  Makes maintenance a bit more of chore.  1700 motor is a
reasonably reliable piece.

Sounds like a score!

Having owned a '62, a '77 and an '84, I find that I prefer the loaf.  Has the
most 'character'.  I'd rather drive the '77 than the Vanagon even though the
Vanagon is more 'civilized'. However, having Vanagon seats in the '77 has
probably colored my opinions a bit.  Not an easy install, but worth every
minute of the time it took to do it!
