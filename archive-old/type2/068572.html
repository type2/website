Twice I have towed '74-79 buses with a U-Haul rented towbar. Costs about 
$20 to rent for a day, same price for one-way or local rental (at least in 
my area).  Have seen other (non-UHaul) towbars that did not look like they 
would work, though.
By the way, towing is probably a good idea.  If compression is very low in 
one cylinder, driving the bus any distance on 3 cylinders is a surefire way 
to overheat the motor and do even more damage.
