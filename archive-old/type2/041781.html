I guess I wasn't reading closely enough.  I'm not sure what the analyzers
can tell you - what you need is a wideband O2 sensor, which some
performance tuning shops will have.  The only easy way to tell what your
A/F ratio is under part or full throttle is on a chassis or engine dyno.
If you don't want to remove your engine from the bus, find a chassis dyno
with a wideband - that's what I did.
