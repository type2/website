By 'AFC' I assume you mean Air Flow Meter [AFM]? If yes, then the 75 AFM
should be part # 0 280 200 012 which fits 74 Cal 1800 auto trans, 75, and 76
up to 12/75. This is a 6 pin AFM. It does not have a temp sensor in it. AFM
# 0 280 200 018 fits 76-77. # 0 280 200 020 fits 78 and Fed version 79. The
018 and 020 are 7 pin AFM's and has a temp sensor in them. The 018 subs to
the 020 and are interchangeable.
You need to count the pin terminals in your AFM plug. If your 78 harness has
7 pins, then you cannot use the 75 FI stuff.
[ you said your 76 is using the 75 parts, this is possible if the 76 was
built before 12/75. If it was built after 1/76, then someone has put on ALL
the 75 parts, harness and all.
Did they also put on the 75 EGR valve? 76 and up is all mechanical.....]
