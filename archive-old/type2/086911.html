FWIW dept, I started using synthetics in my 71 van back in 1975 when Mobil
1 first came out, haven't looked back yet, in all of my motor vehicles
since then including a Mercedes and an Audi I have run only synthetics and
have not had a single oil related failure, the Mercedes had 584k on an
unrebuilt gas engine when sold, I have over the years been going to other
synthetic "stuff" as it becomes avaliable.  my current 78 has 100%
synthetic "fluids" in it since I switched to synthetic brake fluid about 2
years ago.   YMMV of course but I have yet to detect anything made worse
by the fluids in her.....
