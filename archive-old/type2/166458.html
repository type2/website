I believe what that refers to is: When you have the
vehicles weight off the suspension, the arm (the flat
steel piec that goe from the torsion bar to the wheel)
is perched upon a small ledge that is the limiter to
keep it within range. Loosen the bolts on the end of
the torsion bar tube and pry the arm out over that
ledge (Be sure there is nothing in the way like an
ankle!) I use a big screwdriver. -BaH
