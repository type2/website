This has happened to me before, what happens is the sleeve from the old shock
gets rusted to the stud that sticks out for for the shock, when you remove your
old shock the sleeve stays put. I usually soak it for a while with WD-40 or the
likes then use the vise grips to turn it, screw it, hammer it off. By hammering
it off I mean hitting the vice grip handle, not the stud. I would remove the one
that's on the stud, not on the shock as I would think it might be glued or
attached some other way and would ruin your rubber when you try to remove it.
Good Luck
Meredith
