For all you die-hards out there. I'm just finishing a book called "Small
Wonder" by Walter Henry Nelson. It documents VW's history from DR. Porsche
and Pre-WWII days until the 60's. LOTS of information regarding the design
and engineering aspects of the VW. The DM method seems to be getting a lot
of attention on the list, but when you speak of VW, you should probably
refer to the DM method as the "DR. Porsche" method. Msr. Nordhoff also had
GREAT impact on the manufacture of the VW, while influencing design in the
post war years.

A valuable reference book, for the true VW fanatic.
