I'm fortunate in that my local flaps carries a very large inventory of VW
parts in stock.  They sell these items over the counter at prices generally
lower than even the "discount" mail order places.  What they don't carry is
bus rubber or sheet metal.  I've bought these items (body  panels/rubber)
from BusDepot and the shipping took a long time (several days longer than
promised when I placed the order on my dime).  The rubber parts had no part
#s or any other identification on them.  I have no idea where they were
made, but they were not the same quality of the original parts.  They did
"fit" and they do "work".  The sheet metal parts were of good quality and
the fit was fine.

Would I do business with Ron again?  Yes and here's why.  I believe Ron is a
good guy and is honestly trying to provide what he says.  He appears to be
trying to rectify some of the difficulties customers are experiencing.  He
is correct in saying that the extra attention, fast shipping, large
inventories etc. cost money which we the consumer must pay for.  I've owned
and operated a business for over 20yrs and I get the same responses from
time to time from a customer who thought they should have gotten the
Cadillac treatment while buying their Kia. (only a metaphor)
