This is what the mark on the body of the distributor is for. When the notch on the pulley points to zero on the timing scale the rotor will point to the mark on the distributor:

http://www.benplace_dot_com/tdc/100_0283b.jpg

The pulley points to zero twice TDC #1 and #3 so keep rotating the engine until you line up both correctly.
