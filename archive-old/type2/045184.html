2a. Why can't you field-modify the Gene Berg dipstick? Get a little piece of
wire--long enough to crimp two connectors onto it. Put a ring-type crimp-on
on one end and a push-on male side on the other. On the wire going to the
front of the car, put a push-on female side. Solder all the connections to
ensure lasting continuity. Use shrink tubing to insulate everything. Attach
the ring-type crimp-on to the dipstick, and use a wire tie or something like
that to attach the wire going to the front to something solid.
