NO sealing compound is used anywhere brake fluid is.

I am assuming you have NEW seals between the reservoir and the master?
#58 here: http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/Microfiche/t205100.gif

If yes, then you have one of two problems: either the reservoir is cracked, # 1
or # 2 [you never gave the year of your bus] ( # 1 is 69-70.... # 2 is 71-79)
OR the sealing grommet between the tube and the reservoir is leaking, # 59.

The CORRECT seals between the reservoir and master WILL fit correctly.

The seals between the reservoir and master are part # 211 611 817 A
The seals between the tube and both resrvoirs are part # 211 611 833 C
