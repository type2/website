I used a cast iron skillet. Heated the camshaft drive gear with the
distributor gear laying on top, and heated it until a drop of water sizzled
on it. (Clean 'em first, otherwise, lotsa stinky smoke.) Then used a thick
leather glove and dropped the cam gear and the dizzy gear on the crank.
"Clank... clink..." Worked like a champ. Oh, and make sure the leather glove
is THICK.
