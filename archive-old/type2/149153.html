5400 rpm is the limit.

>Second, now that I've got a tach, I notice that I'm idling a bit high ~1200
>rpm.  I'm guessing that I'm going to have to read up on tuning.  Any pearls
>of wisdom before I begin?

If your timing/dwell are in spec then you can lower the idle speed by turning the large screw on the throttle body clockwise.

>Oh yes, one more thing. I am new to busses and, boy is it far from the motor
>to the dash (wire wise that is).  As I am planning to get a CHT gauge and I
>know that wire length could very well be an issue. what is the preferred
>path one takes to run wires from engine gauges (CHT, Oil Pressure, Oil Temp)
>to dash?

17 ft. is the length from spark plug #3 to the dash including a little slack. Run the wires in a loom and keep them away from larger wires carrying current such as the 6.0mm2 red/white wire that connectors the battery (via starter) and the fusebox.
