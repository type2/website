On Tue, 6 Aug 2002, Chris Mills wrote:

> Why are the rear aprons on the late bays non-removable?

My theory is that there was a change in repair philosophy with the type IV
engine.  The type I engine was repairable by just about anyone and they
made it easy to remove.  They type IV engine was a different animal, much
more reliable, and they expected it to be removed only by those with
professional equipment.
