New joints sometimes come with really sharp edges that don't get along
with the axle.  The dry moly lube they put on the joints often interferes
as well.  I've taken a jeweler's file to the splines on new joints and to
the end of the splines on the axle near the snap ring groove to good
effect. I don't get carried
away, just deburr the ends and clean out the Moly lube.  The joint, once
started, should go on with minor encouragement from a brass hammer.
