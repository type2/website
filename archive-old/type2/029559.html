I use a group 24 battery, which works well.  I think that our buses came
originally equipped with a smaller b*g battery, but the group 24 fits fine
and is a lot easier to find.  It also has more plate area.

I usually buy batteries at my local pep boys for about $45 each.  These
come with a 72 month warranty, and usually last for 5-6 years in our mild
climate.  A bus doesn't need much battery to start, so you don't need to
be picky about specs.  The bigger then numbers, the better!

You should select a supplier that you are pretty sure will be available if
the battery dies before it runs out of warranty.
