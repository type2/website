I believe that you have two problems.
1) The seals go on the other side of the floor, so that they are pushed up
against the bottom of the floor when the pedal is at rest.  (I don't believe I
have ever seen one on the inside of a bus.)
2) As someone else mentioned, you may be missing the pan that covers the
mechanisms, thus allowing much more air movement than the system was designed
for.  This pan should always be used, as it protects vital parts from weather
and road hazards.

Good luck,
