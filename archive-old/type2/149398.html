setup, and there is something on the throttle of the '76 that is not on the 
'78   This may feed back into the ECU Brain, so I don't know if the harness 
is the same.  I am going to use a '78 harness and a '78 throttle and a '78 
ECU.

  The charcoal canister setup is different.  I don't know if I can use the 
existing '76 setup with a '78 engine.

  Is the decel valve the same??

  The air distribution box is different.  I am going to use the '78 box, but 
block off the EGR recirculation.  The EGR valve sits on the '78 air 
distribution box.  I don't know what's happening with the '76, but I am 
going to remove ALL the EGR configuration from the '78.

  I am going to bring in a turn-key '78 FI engine with exhaust and heater 
(heat exchangers and heater control box) setup.   '78 cooling tin, although
