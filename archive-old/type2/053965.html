I have used it and it has been an essential tool with my new engine which is
larger and has a higher compression than stock but I am using the stock CA
FI unit and exhaust.

I think it's worth the cash but I spent a lot on my motor and needed to make
sure I was getting it to run richer than stock.  The fuel injection was
continually trying to put the ratio at about 14 to 14.7 to 1 and I needed to
be at 13 to 13.5.  The gauge allows me to get a pretty good idea of what the
mixture is under load (driving on the freeway) 
Getting the fuel injection to run richer was another story.
