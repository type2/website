My '76 2L had dual Solex that I replaced with a single two barrel
progressive Weber. I can tell that there is a big improvement in starting
and acceleration. Unfortunately, just before I did the swap the engine
dropped a valve seat and I haven't experienced the full difference between
the different carb setups. Engine is getting fixed this week.

Most everybody I talked to on the different web sites felt fuel injection
was a better application and I know from my 87 VW  fuel injected Golf that
it is very fast.

If I had a choice on my bus I'd like to see how it runs fuel injected.
