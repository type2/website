If the stickers are paper, try hand lotion.  Apply it every couple of
hours for a day or two and remove the sticker.

Lighter fluid also works, but I don't know about the paint.

Vinyl stickers will usually yield if they are heated with a hair dryer
or heat gun.

Soon your bus will be ready for those "Quayle for Prezdent" stickers! :)
