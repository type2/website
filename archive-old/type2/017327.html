Sounds possible that the idle is set too low, the light will come on
when the rpm's drop too low.  This is FI, I assume?  Opening the idle
screw out a bit might help.  Another thing to look at carefully is the
points.  It sounds funny, but all the books say to begin troubleshooting
FI by looking very hard at the condition of the points and the timing. 
If in doubt, put in new points & condenser and set the timing to the
book value.  Then adjust the idle speed.  Check back if that doesn't fix
it!
