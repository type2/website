Interesting, I just learned something.
Looking up your part number I noticed that the nut size changed during late
modelyear 73.
As we all know the steering box changed beginning modelyear 73, but with VIN
xxx x195236 the nut size was increased (from M20x1.5 to M24x1.5).
The VIN looks like the year end number, but its not indicated as such. With
the nut the shaft changed too.
The new part number is N 011 170 1.
I bet the rebuilders don't indicate this in their lists.
