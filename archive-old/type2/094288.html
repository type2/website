> 
> I had this problem a couple of years ago.
> 
> Replace the master cylinder.
> 
> Remove the old cylinder (this is easy).  Get about 3 feet of plastic
> tubing.
> Push as much of it that you can into the bottom of the servo and then cover
> the end with your finger and then pull it out to ground level but leave the
> servo end of the tube at the bottom of the servo.  When you remove your
> finger the old brake fluid will siphon out into a container.  When all the
> brake fluid is siphoned out you can install the new master brake cylinder.
