Another way you might try would be to pound in a 1/4" Standard hex key
into the cap screw.  It's a tad larger that the 6mm, and may give you
enough bite to get it out.  Even more so with a pair of vice grips on
the screw head.  This worked for me last weekend on a stripped cap screw
holding the fuel pump to the case, where there isn't room for a pair of
vice grips....
