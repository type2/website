Hello Norm,
The semi-auto-stickshift is a kind of auto tranny for the beetle and the
karmann Type 14 only.
You have 3 gears (plus reverse), one for hills, one for city-use (0-50 mph),
one for freeways.
No clutch pedal, when you touch the stickshift, the work is done
electropneumatic. A torque-converter
allows to start and stop in every gear. Strange solution, especially
designed for low hp engines as the beetles.
The Auto-Busses were always equipped with fully autos (self shifting), the
advert must be wrong.

I'm just coming home from a long sunday trip with my 73 super-beetle auto
semi stickshift. You love it or you hate it. I love it.
So does my wife. No acelleration, but easy to use. Previous owner was born
in 1909.....

If godfather wanted us driving shifters, he had given us three legs.
