OR, a triple square socket, depending upon what the bolts take,  Allen and
triple square do not interchange!

> On this subject...make SURE you use backing plates
> (three per CV) and brand-new lock-washers (available
> from VW dealers) when you re-install the CV bolts.

I use OEM serrated washers and backing plates and reuse them with no
problems.  I think that the original serrated washers and plates are
superior to the "new" stuff coming along now.

> Also don't use anti-sieze and keep the bolts and their
> holes free of CV grease.

I use anti-sieze and simply retorque the bolts to the factory torque
values after driving a few miles.
I've heard enough horror stories about stuck CV bolts that I don't want to
risk those things siezing up.  One more crawl-under is well worth it to
me and I've never had a joint loosen up on me after the retorque.
