Hi Craig,

There's a big difference between having a shop do your work and doing
your own. If I had your engine, I first would check compression before
tearing down. If compression was adequate, I would not worry about
what the p&c's look like. To me they look like they are just starting
to break in, but it is difficult to judge from a photo. Since you have
it in a shop, I presume you pay to have your VW worked on, so at this
point there is no point in not spending the extra $85 to replace the
p&c's so as to be safe rather than sorry. If it was mine, I'd chance
it, but I'd be the one doing the work, not paying someone else if it
turned out they were bad.
