Any battery is capable of causing serious harm if overcharged.  Don't let that scare you away from using this battery, but do keep an eye on your charging voltage.  A while back my regulator failed and my alternator was pumping 50 amps into my battery continuously.  Fortunately I have a VDO voltmeter mounted right in front of my face and I saw it pegged at over 15V.  I was able to pull over and repair the problem before it caused serious damage to my batteries and maybe even worse as a result.  I like gauges.
