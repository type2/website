Mark:

I've got 2 AFM's that I've tested as you describe and both of them were 
somewhat out of range (high).  Yet, both seem to work just fine in my 
bus, so I've not thrown them in the trash :-).

I'm not sure of the electrical characteristics of these temp sensors and 
how sensitive they are.  If you were a degree or two high or low of "room 
temperature" I don't know how that would affect the resistance reading.  
But the fact that you're seeing changes in resistance to the gross 
temperature variances suggests to me that the temp sensor is working.
