> Anyway, their bus is an automatic with FI.  The gang says it starts

> tune-up parts (plugs, rotor, condenser, points) to give it a 
> look-see.  I don't want to touch the FI because it scares me :).  Any

Start with the ignition stuff because it needs to be in good shape anyway.
But the fact that it starts OK but cuts out after a few minutes (when it
gets warm?) suggests FI problems, like maybe a bad temperature sensor.

If you have an ohm meter, check out the readings on temperature sensor
#2 (the one in the head, front left) when it's cold and when it's hot.
At least check to see that it's tight in the head, the wire's not loose,
etc.
