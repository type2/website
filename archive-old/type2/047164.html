The fuel lines make a loop that is pressurized and returns the fuel to the tank
that isn't used for the engine so unless both the return and the intake are in
your can the pump will just drain it. Remember to replace those gas lines.  The
weak link in the fi is usually the airflow meter so check it with an ohm meter.
I forgot exactly which terminals to check and what the resistance should be but
Bentley gives this.  This test takes only a minute or two and all you have to do
is know how to read an ohm meter.
Good luck
