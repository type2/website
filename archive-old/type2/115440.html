The correct cleaning solvent for crud and rust and flakes and goo
from an old tank is acid. At the proper dilution, it cuts into
everything, including varnish, goo and steel. When properly done,

with a proper sealant. If the metal is not too thin afterwards, you
get another 20+ years of functional use.
