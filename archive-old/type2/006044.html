I believe the EGR valve on a '78 is mechanical and part of (attached to) the
air distribution box.  If it's still hooked up there'd be an arm that
attaches from it to the throttle lever.  Prior to '76 (or so) I think it was
an electronic widget above cylinder 4.

The decel valve is a three vacuum hose metal diaphragm looking thing screwed
to the firewall.

Also, you can't adjust the mixture on a fuel injected bus without a CO
meter.
