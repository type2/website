FYI... final consensus seems to be that the Costco Kirkland $48 tires
are fine if your needs are low-end "C" load.  If you had a type IV
powered Westy (heavier engine + camper stuff), I would suggest a "D"
load tire... but for those (like my purchase) who can get by with
1435lbs _at_ 50PSI capacity, these are fine.  And they are "All Season"
with M+S rating, allowing me to cross the Cascade mountains <grin>.
