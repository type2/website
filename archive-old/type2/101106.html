On Tue, 12 Nov 2002, Paul Klein wrote:
>On Tue, 12 Nov 2002, Matt Roberds wrote:
>> You disconnect the wire between the turn signal switch and the brake
>> light switch.  This makes the turn/stop wires into turn wires only.  On
>> your Bus, this is probably done most easily by unplugging and taping up
>> the 54f wire at the hazard flasher switch.
>
>OK, that's cool, easy enough, but will my 'Zerds (hazards) still work?

Thinking about it some more, no - only the front turn signals would flash
in hazard mode.  If you unplug the 54 wire at the hazard flasher switch and
leave the 54f wire connected, you'll isolate the brake and turn functions
(the rear yellow lamps will not light up when you step on the brakes) but
the yellow lamps in the rear will still flash in hazard mode.

>So do you think I should just unplug the wire out of the master cylinder
>and run a new one to the brake lights, with a jumper wire?

That would get you closer to the original VW design.  Also, unplugging
the black/red stripe wire at the master cylinder is equivalent to
unplugging the 54 wire at the hazard flasher switch.
