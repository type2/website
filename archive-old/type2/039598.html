All,

I've run my '69 with 7/8" Sway-Aways for a little over
2 years and I've never had any off-road problems,
either.

On my last camping trip, we (wife, son, me and a bus
*full* of crap;-) went up a fairly steep, rutty, muddy
road to get to our final destination with *zero*
problems. I had to think my way through a few spots
where the mud was too deep, or where large rocks lay,
but that was it. We even passed a campsite with a
Toyota 4x4 and the owners smirked at us as we
passed...I guess they thought we'd get stuck, but we
showed 'em!

The biggest drawback to off road that I have, are the
street tires (Pirelli's) and the lack of power my
tired 1600 develops.

Also, I find that even the most avid camper spends
considerably more time driving on paved roads than off
road, so the benefits from the sway bars outweigh the
detractors, IMHO.
