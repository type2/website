Further to this, I have used carbide Dremel bits ground (diamond grinding
wheel) into a spade drill shape (flat with a wide angle between the
cutting edges-look at a concrete drill)  Used in a RIGID drill press (mill
is better) with HEAVY CONSTANT pressure these will go through taps/easy
outs and most fubars. Usually surprisingly easily.  Buy a few as they are
inexpensive and somewhat brittle.  Shown to me by a very knowledgeable
machinist/teacher after I had broken a tap at the end of a long and
complex machining operation.  Much better than slitting your wrists and
lots cheaper than EDM.
