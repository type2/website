As usual, one part at a time.  Eliminate the ignition switch as a problem by
using a manual one and see if it helps....bridge it, junp it, buy a manual
starter button.

See my electrical rant from several years ago about cleaning *all* your
electrical connections....including the transmission ground strap, both
battery terminals, the wires that actual go to the starter, the starter
contact lugs...etc.

I have found that my 78 FI starts incredibly well if the battery is really
charged up properly.
