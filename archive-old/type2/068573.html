Good point, Bob. You can't look at one failed part and condemn them all. 
For the complete Lucas experience, one must own a car sporting Lucas 
electricals. <grin>

Lucas DOES have a bad rep in the US. XKE owners weren't considered 
"experienced" until they had survived their first electrical fire, 
and failed electricals were the most common complaint they had with 
their cars. The old Sunbeams and Hillmans I owned would carry me 
anywhere, but I always carried a complete electrical rebuild kit in the 
trunk. It was fun driving a car that you could fix along side the road.

Lucas has come a long ways since the 50s, 60s and 70s, though. I know 
several owners who've put over 60K on their cars and haven't had any 
electrical problems.
