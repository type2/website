You won't find a green/yellow plaid westy stool because one didn't exist.
Only the deluxe Westies came with a stool from '74-79, which means orange or
blue/green plaid in '74-75, green plaid (with some yellow) in '76-77, or
brown plaid in '78-79. Chrome yellow Westies were not deluxes, hence no
stool offered in that exact plaid color.

By the way, I do have a green plaid stool for sale for $65.  It does contain
the same green and yellow colors as the chrome yellow Westy plaid, but with
more green than yellow (since it came from a sage green Westy).  Of course,
if you already have a good stool but in the wrong color, reupholstering the
lid with correct fabric is the obvious solution.  In a pinch you can cut
some plaid out from the underside of the upper bunk mattress since that area
is never visable.
