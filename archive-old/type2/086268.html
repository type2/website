Hey Paul,
I had this problem with my 71 super beetle. The solution was remove the
fancy chrome air cleaner and replace it with the original oil bath air
filter. This was because the carb was simply running too lean with the
aftermarket air cleaner. I don't know if you would have room or not for the
beetle air cleaner so you may have to try something ingenious.
If indeed you do have a round chrome air filter try to wrap your hands
around it to block most of the air intake. This is how i pinpointed my
problem. I also adjusted the timing by ear rather than with the timing
light. When i checked it with the light i am a few degrees advanced. I left
it because it ran nicely.
