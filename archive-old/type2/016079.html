Hi Jack,

What you describe has a lot of implications, but not the ones you mentioned.
Perhaps you were already aware of them.

Changing the exhaust will change the airflow through your Kadrons, so the
carb will likely need to be re-jetted or you may at minimum need to adjust
the accelerator pump rod.  Gene Berg's writings cover this specific
situation as an example on the Kadrons.

As for the heat risers and how they impact the air flow... I would consider
that a minor change to the "system" compared to other changes you have made:
1) use of Kadrons instead of stock carb.  2) Associated air cleaners on
Kadrons are much less restrictive than VW cleaners.  3) Your 1776
displacement change...

All of this leads to much more air going out than the stock muffler was
tested with by VW for (although it works from 1200 to 1600 with various
carbs, it may do fine at your config).

Does one of your Kadron's have provision for breathing off the oil filler?
I'm just curious.

I'm going through the same learning on Dual Kadrons + 1600DP with "dual
quiet pack exhaust".
