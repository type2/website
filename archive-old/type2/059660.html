I actually thought the Aquatred gig was a gimmick myself to, until I bought a
bus with them already installed. These are pretty nifty tires!  I am currently
living in Oregon where it pretty much rains at least 8 months out of the year.
I have noticed that my 69' camper (Albert) corners and stops much better with
the Aquatreds (no more sliding into intersections-but watch for flying coffee
cups).   An I'm talking a fully over loaded camper here-kids, wife and all
there unnecessary gear.  I like these tires and highly recommended them if you
have to much money laying around..but then again you have to remember that I
got my tires for pretty darn near free! (bought a bus for $800, sold the bus
for $1,500-kept the tires)
