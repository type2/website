I don't think that the axle tube has come away from the frame [but I
must admit that I am not the worlds most efficient lawn mower...though
I must have yanked at least a bail of hay around the bus;)].  The rear
suspension just looks like there is no torsion bar [or the torsion bar
inside the tube has given up] as the swing plates are as far up as

than the engine and trannie ;)

plate under the front is sitting in the cab and is in perfect
shape...of course, the stuff that it was suppose to protect might not
be so well off.

When I go back I will take a bottle of mineral spirits to clean the
plate behind the front seat...I couldn't read anything on it this
