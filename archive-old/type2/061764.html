 Yup, ditto- not so much a car you can camp in as a truck you can live in...They're still on the small side by US standards but definitely big for European roads/carparks/garages etc.

 Incidentally the clock thing behind the speedo was a tachograph- a recording device to ensure that drivers adhere to the legislation on working hours, meal and sleep breaks etc. It records the speed of the vehicle against time and is a legal requirement on vehicles over a certain capacity (about 3.5 Tonnes, I think).
