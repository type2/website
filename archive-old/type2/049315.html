Pump the tires on the move? Oh boy.

If you have an army base near you, go to the town close to it and look for
an army truck. There are two kinds I know of that have on-the-move inflation
systems, the new-generation 5-ton and the Family of Medium Tactical Vehicles
2.5-ton. You would not believe how complex the air plumbing out to the tires
is--I don't think you could do it on a Bus without replacing a *lot* of
parts on the wheel hubs.

I'd just get one of those little tire-inflator compressors they sell at the
FLAPS and use that.
