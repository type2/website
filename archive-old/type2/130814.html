Hi Jason,

I sent a looooong message to Andy off-list about this, but I think some 
clarification on-list couldn't hurt.

There is no reason a late pop-top can't be used on a sunroof. True, you 
won't get the double bed without cutting a LOT of metal, but that 
double bed isn't a requirement for a lot of people. The fibreglassroof 
WILL fit fine over the sunroof hole, and if an upstairs bunk is 
required I would expect that a 68-72 upper cot could be rotated 180 
degrees and installed up top.

Some people never sleep upstairs, they just like to have the additional 
storage space and headroom available when camped.
