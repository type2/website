if there were no turn signal bits on the front of the bus, odds are that it is
a semaphore bus- barndoor or up to 60 euro version.  

The little bulb in the headlight housing is a driving light- comes on when the
knob is pulled out half way (if it is working correctly- mine doesn't, but
part of my wiring is messed up) .  If they are rigged as turn signals that is
a custom dealie.

another dating hint- if the top cargo door hinge is just above the belt line,
it is mid 61 or later.  If it is halfway between the beltline and the roof, it
is early 61 or earlier.
