The nose that is available is for the 72 and up years. The turn signals are 
up high beside the air inlet grill. The piece does not go down far enough, so 
have to also put on the available lower nose skin (68-71) to make it 
complete. To make it work for a 71, and have it look like a 71 is a huge job 
of cutting and welding. Also the peice has an ugly bump on the front that 
sticks out for the VW emblem as these are really replacement parts for 
brazillian busses.

All that said, I had it done for my 71 by the Metal Wizard (Metwiz2_at_aol_dot_com) 
and it looks nice. He does great work, but anyplace else would prolly not 
even take it on. Prepare to spend the shop rate for a lot of work.

My friend just had a similar problem with a tree and he was able to find a 
complete nose for a 71 at a swap meet for 65.00. He said a list member 
offered him one for 400.00.

Write Kelly Brown (Metwiz2_at_aol_dot_com) and ask him about the job.
