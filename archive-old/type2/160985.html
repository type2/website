>I'm a new member to this list. I just recently bought a '78 transporter, 
>and the guy I got it from pointed out an oil leak, suggesting that I 
>replace the push-rod bearing seals. Does anyone know whether we'd be 
>able to do that without pulling the engine?

He's talking about the push rod tube seals.

You can do the job without pulling the engine. You will only need to readjust the valve clearances afterwards because the rocker arms in the head have to come out.

You'll need viton seals. They come in two sizes and you'll need 8 of each (16 total).
