nose behind the front bumper. This rust has been cut out so it is a hole.

roof had a small hole near the passengers side rear roof gutter. It is a 
strange rust hole, almost like someone had drilled there for an antenna. It

stock.

PERFECT except for two problems. The previous owner cut out two sections of 
the bed to repair and never welded them back in. So, there is a whole strip

an access panel was cut over the engine and that sheet metal panel is 
missing.  The side gates are in really good condition. I can see some bondo
