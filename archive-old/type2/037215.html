walt,
my first guess would be pushrod tubes, notorious for
leaking as anyone on the list can attest to.   i would
go underneath the bus and take the tin off each side. 
its the tin directly under the head, should be just a
couple screws.   take the bus for a cruise around the
block so its warmed up, climb back under and watch
where the oil is comin from, odds are pushrod tube
seals.   as for spray washing the underside, should be
ok, because the tin should protect the engine
compartment, just dont go crazy with it.   might want
to try some simple green sprayed on a nice warm motor
before cleaning.   just my $.02
