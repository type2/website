At 10:28 PM -0500 8/22/00, Smittie wrote:

>The 1973 Bus did not come with a 2.0 FI engine. The 1973 came with a 1700
>cc, normally aspirated engine (there were some expections where it came with
>a std 1600).

In the US, all 1973 model year busses came with 1679cc ("1.7L") engines.
They were all normally aspirated - in fact the only Type 2's that were ever
*not* normally aspirated were those with TDI engines (I don't think there
have been any in the US).  Fuel injection or carburation has nothing to do
with being normally aspirated.  Normally aspirated refers to the lack of
forced induction, such as a turbocharger or supercharger.
