Sounds like a milder (and more frustrating) version of the problem with my
wife's bug.  Electrical short causes sparks which interfere with the radio
and then cause the engine to cut out.  You might want to examine the wires
and harnesses near the engine where things vibrate.  It could also be an
intermittant
open.  If you can get it to happen at predictable times, you can hot wire
the distributor with a jumper from the battery and see if the problem
disappears.
