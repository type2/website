Support the transmission with a jack.  Then using a GOOD hydraulic jack
placed directly under the drain plate of the engine, pull back and remove
the engine by itself.  No need to remove the transmission, much easier this
way. you can always remove the trany later it only weighs 70 lbs. and on the
71 the engine and transmission  are much easier to do separately!!!
It's a piece of cake! You can do it!
