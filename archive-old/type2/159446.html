Chris,
	I have rebuilt pistons on my old MB and hopefully this information will 
carry over to the bus calipers. I didn't get my pistons aligned very 
well and had to readjust them after assembly. There was a section of the 
piston surface that contacted the pad that was raised. I used a file 
pressed against this edge to rotate the piston to the correct angle. I 
had just rebuilt this so there was still assembly lube on the seals 
which might make it easier to rotate, but worth a try. Good luck!
