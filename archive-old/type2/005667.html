You can use either a 3 or 4 bolt cam as long as the oil pump matches.  Gene
Berg says that their blueprinted oil pumps can be used with both 3 and 4
bolt cams.  There are definate oil passage matching issues, especially with
some of the south of the border cases. A comparison of the depth of the key
from the mating surface of the pump should tell you which pump is which (the
4 bolt pump is deeper).
