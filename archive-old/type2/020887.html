These are pretty thick and rarely go.  If compromised they will let air
into the intake and run you lean.  Before removing, I'd seal them up
over the outside with E6000 sealer (urethane), check boat or plastics
supply places- this stuff is great on canvas, hoses, etc, flexible,
strong, and sticks far better than silicone.
