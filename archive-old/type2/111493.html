All the bay cot canvas is the same size.  But they do shrink.....
The bars are different lengths:
68-73 are 56 1/4" long.
74-75 are 57 1/4" long.
76-79 are two piece collapsible, but the 74-75 will fit.... you just won't
 have a place to store them.
The half moons are mounted higher up on the A and B pillars on 68-73 
buses.
