So basically yeah you got to go and use all the tin and acessories from
the '73.  The vanagon FI would go in easily enough though if you put
in a FI bus fuel tank.  Would have to cut some holes in the '73 tin or

and just use a generic cone style air filter from CB performance on
some sort of adapter flange on the air filter or get a FI bus air filer
and snorkel hose.  I actually sort of wondered how tough it would
be to mod the vanagon tin for use in a bus on that alternator side
to get rid of the nightmare bus alternator installation but have never
even bothered to go root through my tin box and find out.  I think the
vanagon alternator is only 60A or so, so not much of an upgrade just
a bit more convenient when you need to replace it.
