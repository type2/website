the old thread:
> the '72 and I think '73 front brake calipers and rotors are becoming
harder to find and the '74-'79 are everywhere and half price.  Can I
switch to the larger calipers and rotors from the later buses without
changing out the hub and backing plate that is currently on '72?

The hard-to-find ones are '71 and '72.  After this, VW responded to the
complaints of too-frequent pad replacment by designing thicker pads.
these required a thicker caliper.  This required more wheel clearance,
so the spindle was redesigned to move the caliper inward.  This changed
the centerline of the disk, so the disk is now different.

In short, you need the caliper, spindle, disk, and the backing plate to
do the conversion.  You will not feel any difference in braking
ability, but the pads will last longer.

Early pads will fit late calipers, but not the other way around.
