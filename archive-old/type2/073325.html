>I know if I need to replace the current ball joints?
>I drove my bus last in November and there were not any noises or clicking or

How does your steering feel?  Ball joints play a part in that equation.  If
in doubt, you could take your bus into a shop and have them checked.  All
of my busses ball joint boots were split when I got it but I got by for
over ten years by periodically stuffing grease into the boot before I
finally replaced them.  I had it done by Meyers in Las Vegas - a first
class shop if you are anywhere nearby.  That was one job that I decided not
to tackle myself.
