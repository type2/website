Jeremy,

There is a special tool to press in the bushings. However a large threaded
rod, the appropriate fender washers and nuts will do the job just fine. Get
a threaded rod with a diameter just bellow that of the spindle and a fender
washer whose outside diameter is just larger than outside diameter of the
bushing. The kit should have instructions.
