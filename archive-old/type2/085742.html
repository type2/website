In my Muir Book Chapter XX page 435, co-authors John Hilgert and Peter 
Achwanden discuss a problem with a 75 transmission in a 78 bus. Oil seal 
failures along with pilot shaft bearing  failure. Cause was “the pilot shaft 
on a ‘75 is too short for a ‘78 only a ‘76 to ‘78 will work.”

same.
