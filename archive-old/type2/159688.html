Subject: Re: [T2] how do I test my fuel pump?

Lee Pedrazas wrote:
 > Is there an easy and safe way to test my fuel pump?  I've got a fuel
 > injected 77 westy.  I had posted earlier about my starting troubles and
 > decided to check the fuel filter.  The gas that came out of the line


Yeah, it's pretty easy.  Just pull off the air filter, turn on the 
ignition, and stick a screwdriver in there to deflect the flapper door. 
  It should kick on the fuel pump and you'll hear the fuel flowing 
through the fuel lines and out the pressure regulator.  You can also 
hook up a fuel pressure guage on the driver's side fuel rail and then 
run the pump to see if it's putting out a descent pressure.

You might also want to dispose of the bad fuel and fill it up with good 
fuel.
