Gles,

What ever you do prior to even thinking of purchasing this Bus--do buy,
rent, steal,or borrow a compression tester.
This will on test tell you more than maybe possibly anything else on the
engines overall condition.
Low or no compression---forget it, unless of course your into engine /
head rebuilds.
Good compression---it may be a buyer---depending on the overall
condition of the Bus, and the mood you happen to be in that moment---you
have to lay out the cash for the purchase price.

Later,
