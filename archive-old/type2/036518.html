Dave and Darrin,

I have a Blaupunkt deck in my dash that fit with a little additional 
grinding, but many shaft-style stereos will fit right in.  

To overcome the noise of a bus, a power amp is a good idea.  I have a 100W 
amp mounted on the nose under the dash.  

For speakers, I used Radio Shack die-cast mini speakers.  The two in the 
front have 4" woofers and fit right under the dash and above the parcel 
shelf.  These run off the power amp.  The two in the rear have 3" woofers and 
mount on the shelf that goes across the back over the bed -- a perfect fit.  
The optional mounting brackets make it work.
