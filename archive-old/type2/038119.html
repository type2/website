E-bus is just a few days away and I wanted to inform everyone of a few last
minute developements.

1) The '67 is sitting in the driveway with the motor out and the left RGB
torn down so I will be camping in the '72 Westfakia (yellow and white with
roof rack) Please check in with me to register and pay your camping fees. I
dont want to hear any crap about how the bus has not had a bath since the
last EveryBus :)

2) The carabus to Farmington will leave at 9:30 AM on Sunday. When we arrive
we will park as a group and take a bus count. I will go to the registartion
tent and alert the organizers to our presence at which time I will receive a
$2.00 rebate for each bus in our group. I will return to the buses and slip
$2.00 into each bus through a window that is slightly down. This sounds
crazy but discount coupons were not available so.......

3) Lot's of folks have asked me to reserve them a space at the campground.
What we have done is reserve a loop and it will not really be possible to
hold individual spaces. Two or three buses will fit in a site. If you need
hookups grab a slot that has them. If you do not need to plug in you can
camp in the infield areas aroud the shelter or fire pit. There is room for
well over 150 buses. At present it looks like we will have a sizable
gathering so please be kind and share whatever space you feel comfortable
with sharing.

4) Please bring huggers and cups if you are going to comsume spirits. We had
a good crowd last year and the rangers were cool because we were cool :)

5) Have a safe and fun trip to Greensboro and I look forward to meeting you
all !!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
