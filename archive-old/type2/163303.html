Kragens/Shucks/Checker/AutoZone/AdvanceAuto, etc., for most likely less
under a Wells Brand part number, of which escapes me at the moment (I failed
to enter the # in my database when I last saw it).  Richard Atwell has
mentioned it before, but I can't find it on his site at the moment.
