Last summer I indoctrinated my 13 year old niece in the fine tradition of
backyard mechanics.  She helped me with some repairs on the bus and quickly
learned that I rely on the 10mm and 13mm wrenches a lot.  The next time she

removed these two wrenches from the bunch I had scattered around.  Since I
was unsuspecting and convinced I had misplaced a wrench, it took me a long
time to catch on (I guess a frantic, greasy guy rifling through a pile of
tools is funny to some people!!!! Grrrrr).

I'd say she learned what makes the bus AND me tick(ed)!!
