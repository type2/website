The metal that holds the pin may be fine, but the "curls" on both that 
part and you accelerator pedal are deformed from 30+ years of useage.

You *can* bend them back.  Take the pin out and the pedal off and look
both of them over carefully.  Does the pin flop around int the curls of 
both pieces?.._dot_use pliers and *carefully* reshape and bend them so that 
they are straight and tight like they used to be.

Re-install and then move/bend the whole pedal to one side or 
another(gently!) so that the rod goes thru the hole in the floor smoothly 
without touching the sides.

Be creative.._dot_can you make a better one than the vw engineers did?
In my experience, this is one of those things that we can do a little 
better on in our bus than the original...
