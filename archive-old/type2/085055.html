Hey Rob,

The fuel spray you are seeing is from the accelerator pump.  This spring
loaded diaphragm pushes a stream of fuel out of the top (emmulsion?)tube
when the accelerator is pushed in.  This spray should be even and strong
(not a dribble).

The vacuum from the running engine pulls the rest of the fuel charge when
running. So it is difficult to tell what other thing might or might not be
clogged.

Eric Bradway just rebuilt one of these about a month ago... Eric are you out
there?  He may be in Vermont on a journey now because I have not heard from
him lately...  Anyway Eric is good technically and would be helpful here.

...  From my experiences if you have a good accelerator pump stream it will
run... To some degree at least, to make it to FMIJ :)  Granted idle sucks.
