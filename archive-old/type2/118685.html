Sounds like the common "someone started it with their foot on the gas pedal and it blew a hose off"!

But I am concerned about your remark: ' The air bypass idle speed control on the airbox has no effect.'
Are you referring to the screw on TOP of the air flow meter? If yes, that screw is not idle speed. It is idle
mixture. The idle speed screw is in the base of the throttle housing, below the throttle cable.
