I like the numbers that Bob was quoting there from the melling pump. Are
they not both 30mm pumps? The Schadek and the Melling that is? This motor
has about 12k on it. I did not replace the pump when I rebuilt it. Should

iron and theoretically could expand at a different rate than the case...
