Mine sounds like a diesel as well!!!!! sometimes. I once heard a VW with an
Audi 1.6 turbodiesel, it sounded exactly the same.......but seriously, that
type of noise sounds like a problem with the exhaust system - either the
silencer or the heat exchangers. Does it backfire as well on deceleration?
If so, then you've got a leaky system. If not, its probably baffles or
something internal, assuming all is stock and present.

The reason your noise is apparent at these conditions is probably because
that's when its under load. And it also occurs at very low speeds, like 600
rpm or so. Have you ever heard a VW Camper with no exhaust (or, for that
matter, any car with no exhaust, ie. drag racers) rev up? The noisiest bit
is when you slam the throttle down, not necessarily at max rpm.

Even though its just a text message, that noise doesn't sound like a mechanical
fault.
