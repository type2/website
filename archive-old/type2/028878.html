Stephen,

I have had a few occasions to get to know these gauges. One thing you might
look is the vibrator or stabilizer. This is a metal box about 1 inch by 1/2
that stabilizes the voltage to the fuel gauge. This keeps the needle from
jumping around as the fuel splashes while driving turning ext. Always use
great caution when working with anything related to the fuel tank especially
anything that involves voltage, current, and resistance.
