carbon dioxide and water, but as they deplete the oxygen the combustion
efficienty drops and you start to get carbon monoxide instead of carbon
dioxide.  This makes you dead.

I've a friend who was a cop in Nevada.  He got the bright idea to use a
catalytic heater in his car while on a stake-out.  Luckily, cops have to
check in by radio every hour or he would have died.
