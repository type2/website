The reason for the bargain price is that they are boxed under the a brand
name that Boge is no longer going to use, which is Technum.  They had been

same shocks that are being shipped under the Boge name. I got them directly

These are an oil filled, not a gas-charged shock, just like the stock ones
that Boge made for VW.  They would be just the ticket for someone who wants
to maintain his bus's stock ride, rather than opting for the somewhat
stiffer ride (and higher price) of a performance gas shock like the KYB
Gas-Adjust.
