etc.....Please remember no matter what VW unit you choose, you will not have a
road burner...your average Aerostar will suck the headlight out of your 72..I
drove a vanagon with a 3.5 liter Rover V-8 ( I have sick,*sick*, friends...)

2.0 will fit but the heads require that you use the vanagon heater boxes which
reqire the vanagon shrouding wich may have to be modified to fit..etc......
