I recommend and use NW Connecting Rod. They are in Seattle in the
industrial district south of Alaska Way. Every Sunday, if not every day,
they have a classified ad in the automotive section of the PI/Times. You
will get quality parts and workmanship and a motor that will last under
proper installation and maintenance. Lots of VW knowledge in their shop.
They do both air cooled and Wasserboxers as well as the 4cly inlines both
gas and diesel. A seriously good machine shop and builder.

I've had no problems whatsoever with 5 or 7 engines from them in as many
years. Sorry, I don't have their phone no. here at home, but any Yellow
pages will find them.
