On 11/27/06, Anymouse <honeybus_at_gmail_dot_com> wrote:
> Plase refresh me on why disconnecting the battery  while the engine is
> running on a fuel injections system is a no - no.  Thanks
>

The battery acts like a power conditioner. It buffers spikes and
brownouts to provide relatively clean power to the computer, stereo,
etc. The act of disconnecting and connecting the battery will also
cause power spikes. The ECU is too expensive to risk.
