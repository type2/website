Subject: Re: [T2] What's the practical low end for the endplay spec?

Speaking from a gauging and measurement standpoint, I have the following
observations:
1.) The difference of .00024" between the rounded off number and the
direct metric specification is really of little consequence when working
in the environment that we are all typically working in.
2.) There are many factors that can contribute to gaging error including
the measurement system (indicator, feeler gage etc.), User
interactions,environment etc.
3.) IMHO, I beleive that due to the potential for measurement error and
the fact that you do not know which way the error is likely to be
working, I would shoot for the middle of the specified range.

Hope this is of some use - Jeff
