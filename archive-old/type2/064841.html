>While carbon dioxide will dissolve
>in beer--providing fizziness, nitrogen will not--it will only push the beer up
>the hose and into your glass.  Stout, like wine, should not be fizzy (wine
>becomes champagne when dispensed with co2), hence the use of nitrogen.
