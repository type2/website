Andrew, and everyone else to,
   To convert inches to millimeters multiply the inches by 25.4 to get the 
metric equivalent.  So .010 inches = .254 millimeters.
   Conversely, to change millimeters to inches, multiply the millimeters by 
0.03937 to get the inch equivalent.
