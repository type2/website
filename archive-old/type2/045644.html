Hi Chris,

14 foot pounds on the exhaust stud nuts.  I use copper 8mm 1.00 pitch lock nuts
with a 12 mm head.  Easier to fit a box wrench on than 13 mm nuts.

The 6 mm bolts can handle approximately 7 - 8 foot pounds of torque.  Red lock
tite helps here also.

Just my experience,
