What I'm suggesting may be wrong, so feel free to disagree...

If you have 2 six volt fans and they are in parrallel (ie both connected to +ve 
and both connected to earth) then each will have a six volt drop :-

                    /-\
    6 volt +ve -----| |----- earth
                    \_/

                    /-\
    6 volt +ve -----| |----- earth
                    \_/

If the two fans are put in series then there will still be a six volt drop
on each fan :-

                    /-\          /-\
   12 volt +ve -----| |----------| |----- earth
                    \_/          \_/
