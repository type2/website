Or, as someone else mentioned, those folks who back up or pull out without
looking.  Or those idiots on car phones!!!!!!!!!

Saw a bumper sticker that I really want:  Said "Hang Up and Drive!"

and/or pass inspection.

So, I don't use it much, but when I use it it becomes vitally important.
