Subject: RE: [T2] Carb balancing

Do not touch the linkage for idle adjustment. The linkage comes into play
for sync at higher RPMs.

On my carbs (36-40 PDSIT w/o central idle) idle speed adjustment is done
with the throttle plate stop screws. Probably different with stock 32-40
PDSIT as my books say not to touch throttle plate gap on these.

But the basic idea is the same: get idle perfect without linkage and then
use the linkage to even out acceleration. I.e. you need the linkage
adjustment to stop the engine shaking at certain RPMs.
