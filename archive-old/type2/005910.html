It means you have half a case. That is the casting number, no use to you.
Look for the engine code on top of the engine next to the case seam,
either at the forward end on the left half, or at the rear  down below the
breather portion. There are two letters in front of a series of numbers
which identify the engine size. W,EA,EB are 1700; EC,AN are 1800:
GA , GB are 2000.
