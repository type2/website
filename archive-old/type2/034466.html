parked next to a POS '80's Ford sedan with a sour-looking old guy sitting in 
the passenger seat.  After checking my mail, I came out to find him examining 
a ding (one of many) near the top of his front fender.  He started scowling 
and harrumphing in my general direction, while pointing at his car.  I

just like you!"
