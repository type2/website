now of course this is for a manual tranny, the auto tranny has a very
different removal prochedure,  I highly discourge taking both off together
for a couple reasons
.  the auto tranny is VERY heavy and combined with the engine you are
pushing a few hundred Lbs.
.  the auto tranny does not have the tranny shaft that the flywheel has to
clear so it doesn't need to be pulled back as far, and it doesn't have
quite as much alignment problems as the manual
.  the auto shift linkage is a real PITA to adjust

> > the tranny and engne together was a pain in the butt for one thing only for
