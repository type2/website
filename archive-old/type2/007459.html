The problem here is that the pitman arm travel is just barely enough
to take the steering between its physical limits (locks).  If you go
to either side of the center position you will find the steering
limited by the worm in the box, not by the stops on the swing lever. 
I have a feeling (not substantiated, but I don't feel like risking
$300 to find out) that allowing the box to go to the end of the worm
is damaging if it hits the end with any force.

Also, the steering wheel play is a minimum at the center position.  If
you use any other position, you will have more play in the steering
wheel while driving straight.  If the box is later adjusted at the new
"center" position, it will be too tight at the real center position.
