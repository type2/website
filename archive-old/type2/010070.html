OK, its in and it works. Its just like the cruise control on any "real"
car. But..... Even if I had followed every step of the directions it
still would not have worked without some slight changes. The system I
put in is made by Audiovox and is available at Pepboys and JC Whitney
for about $100. I put it in a fairly stock 75 with stock injection and
manual trans. I did not have to use magnets on the drive shaft but auto
trans cars will need to. I hope to put up a web page with the details
in a week or so.
