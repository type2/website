Hi Max,

In my 74 Westy, I noticed a similar fuel odor.  It was stronger with a full
tank.  I found the fuel filler hose from the filler pipe to the tank had
deteriorated and was leaking.  Repair was fun as I had to remove the engine to
remove the tank to get at the hose.  If you have a large plate on the right side
of your engine bay, it's removal will facillitate the repair.   You could also
check for a puncture to the tank.  Maybe, you just have a poorly sealing fuel
cap.  Depends on how your luck is running.
