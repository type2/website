Marcus:
Sounds to me like the cold start valve is not working. This could be for
2 reasons:
1. Valve is plugged or othewise inoperable.
2. No electrical signal to cold start valve (bad thermo time switch)
Look at Bently for test details and remember:BE REALLY CAREFUL AROUND
RAW GAS! Bently says this too, also a good time to replace those old
hoses which will fail eventually with 30 psi gas in them and if you're
not lucky can burn your car completely. I once bought a 75 bus parts car 
that was so burned that the only good parts were the front axle beam ,
the trans, and the internal engine parts. There is no margin for error
here!
