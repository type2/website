Walt this is a classic case of what has happened to me on two earlier
Westies. The starter is overheated by the engine after a good long run and
just won't operate. I used to get lucky and sometimes it would work after
cooling down to normal and a good whack with a hammer. Unfortunately the fix
I went through was to buy a new(rebuilt) starter and fashion a heatshield to
protect it from the engine heat. This seemed to cure the problem.
