June 24:Clermont,FL Lakeridge Winery Tour:, Orlando Quattro Club's
special
invitation to VW Club members. Here are the details from the Audi
Quattro
club: Join us for our semi-annual to Lakeridge Winery in Clermont.
This time
it's for the winery's annual Harvest Festival event. There will be
live
music, art & crafts booths, food, vintage cars and, of course, wine!
Free
wine tasting and tours will also be available. Admission is just $2
for club
members and $2 for non-club members. We plan on meeting in the
parking lot
at Kelsey's (Alafaya & University shopping center) just before Noon
to
caravan over to Clermont. We should be able to park together when
we're
there. If you want to go directly there, look for Club cars by about
1 p.m.
Lakeridge Winery is 6 miles north of Rt. 50 on U.S. Rt. 27 North in
Clermont, FL. The phone number is 1-800-768-WINE.Tip: Bring lawn
chairs or
blankets to sit on. The winery only provides a limited number of
chairs.
Christian J. Long President - Quattro Club of  Florida Orlando,
Florida
