Hey Jamie -- two responses:

same vintage, same color even!  Where are you located (geographically)?  I am

(2) Why are you allowing a little sheet metal to dictate your design?  If it 
bothers you to cut away parts of the rear apron, get another from a boneyard,

the welds to the crossbar.  A little muffler heat on the receiver isn't going 
to do it any harm, but you might arrive at a stronger design over-all by 
putting the receiver in the same plane as the crossbar.... YMMV
