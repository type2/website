Bar oil is specifically a chain saw bar lubricant.
I buy it at the farm store, it is also available wherever chain saws
are sold or repaired. Sears, Home depot, the lumber yard, etc.
I pay around $8 for a gallon, and use it for it's original purpose.
It has about the viscosity of manual transmission gear oil.
To us this oil for an undercoat means annual treatment,
including power washing road dirt.
