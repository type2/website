Subject: Re: [T2] replacing a poptop seal?

The old seal will just pull off. It may be glued, but a little tugging and
you are done.

Clean it up with most anything or even lightly sand to get any old glue off.

The easiest way is with the top raised a bit, maybe put something under the
rear of it like a box to lift it off the roof. Start at one side and slip it
on. I didn't use glue, but a lot of people swear by 3M adhesive. Just go
along shoving the new seal on. Make sure its seated real good. If you do it
right the ends will be even when you are done.

Hope this helps!!
