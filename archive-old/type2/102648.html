To make this understandable, put it in math terms:

Work = load/distance = lb/ft = Torque(for our discussion)

"work" does not involve units of time
"power" does,

Power = Work/Time = Horsepower (for our discussion) 

It's like speed vs. acceleration.

Thats why the quarter mile time isn't releated in Torque figures. There's no time involved in the torque equation.

Now a formula that relates a quarter mile time accounting for both would be interesting.
