Okay, Hercules, if you can clamp a set of vise-grips on the remaining part
of the stud, try that first. If you can clamp on a vise-grip, the stud
should just screw right out. Since this stud lives in oil, it's not likely
to be rusted in.

Also run outside quick-like and unhook the ground from your battery; this
will keep anyone else from trying to start your Bus.

A couple of days with no oil in it, so long as you don't try driving it
anywhere, shouldn't hurt your engine. (I would say won't, but absolutes are
bad here.)
