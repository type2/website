	The main variables in ignition timing are deg advance at 
ldle, and some idea of the advance curve with revs.  We gather a 
digital timing light is a v good instrument for those.  Dwell, which 
you'll no doubt be able to read also on the digital timing light, is 
of less importance than those first two variables, because if both of 
these primary variables are right the dwell can't be far off specs 
(given that the fibre heel (syn. rubbing block) is following the cam 
freely).  I'se bin into more detail  - see archives  -  but those are 
the basics.

	not a kosher name; best to stick to common terms.
	instruments analysing exhaust gases usually measure 
absorption of IR at wavelengths characteristic of the main gases 
which have dominated theories of air pollution (esp photochemical 
smog).  Gas chromatographs (separating the respective gases) aren't 
generally needed.  Mass spectrometers, shattering the mixture into 
all possible molecular fragments, can be used but are needlessly 
expensive.
	At leat one IR exhaust gas analyser  -  U of Colo I think  - 
works as you drive past at ordinary town speed, reading by an IR beam 
across the street.
