On Fri, 18 Jun 2004, Alan Beck wrote:

> Would someone be able to advise on which channels I should monitor on the CB

Truckers frequent Ch 19, though they often use other channels on specific
highways.  Bus folk monitor Ch 2 on both CB and FRS

> I am going to match my AM Antenna whip to CB using a tuner that allows the
> continued use of the AM/FM radio as well. 35 smackers for the cheap little
> box.

Don't expect great performance from that setup!

> Also going to put in a "thru the glass" 2 meter ham radio antenna.

Those work well if installed carefully.  Be sure to comply with the
"distance from metal" specification in the instructions and clean the
glass very well before installing.

My antennas (CB, HF and VHF) are mounted on my roof rack.  You can
get a wire to the roof rack with no visible holes by going into the engine
bay through a hole behind the spare tire and then up through the air vent
to the roof rack.
