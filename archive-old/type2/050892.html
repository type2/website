Sean Bartnik mentioned a '70 camper in the Sunday Washington Post paper, at 
301 898 5601.  It is really a '72 camper.

The story:  sitting outside 7 years, pretty solid with moderate surface

Has keys, and possibly a title.

All four tire seem to have solid air pressure (after 7 years), and it looks 
like it could be towed.

Lots of junk inside, including the rear bumper.  Has a child cot.  Does NOT 
have a drive away tent.  Don't know the condition of the pop top canvas.

Worth the $300, but I would offer $200 if title was available.

it stands, just off route 15 at 10798 Utica Mills Circle, Thurmont MD.
