 I think VW originally installed fiberglass batts for insulation,
about an inch thick and unfaced (no foil or paper facing on either
side).    Mine were neither  wet nor soggy when I re-did the
interior of "The Hobbit", so I do not see why fiberglass insulation
could not be used in those areas.   There was no insulation at
all in the front doors, probably because water is definately going
to get into those areas and needs to be able to drain out the
bottom.  Mr. Bulley has covered this area thoroughly in his excellent
insulation and heat article.
