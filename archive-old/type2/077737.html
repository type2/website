> Evan,
> I had the same problem so.....
> I turned my carb around so the "front" is actually facing the "front" of
the
> bus, then had a spacer made at the local machine shop (about 1/2" thick)
> that goes between the carb and manifold tubing, with the corresponding
hole
> machined quite nicely. This allowed the throttle linkage to move freely.
> Then the accelerator cable fits on the correct side of the car, which is
the
> original place, less wear.
> This has worked quite satisfactorily for me. Oh, I also use a spring
> attached to the throttle linkage, going to the rear of the bus so that the
> mechanism returns to the idle position properly.
> I use the spring from my old stock dual carb system. Works just fine.
