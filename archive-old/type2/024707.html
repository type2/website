Once again this is something that VW themselfs fixed 30 years ago.

There is (were) in fact THREE sizes of rear view mirror availlable - the 
mirror is the same size but the length of the stalk is different. Normaly the 
shortest one was what was fitted and even with the rear of the bus at normal 
ride height these tend to look down on the road.

Over the years I've found 4 or five of the longest arm ones at VW shows just 
chucked in boxes of T2 mirrors I've paid between £1 and £5 for one ($1.60 to 
$8.00 USA) and sold them on to friends for what I paid.

I've never seen one at the medium length.

These long stem mirrors are realy great for improving the rear visibility as 
they allow one to long along the road rather than down onto it.

Its a part well worth looking for in scrap yards and at shows. Keep your old 
mirror and swap back before selling the bus so that your long stem mirror can 
be transfered to a new bus.

The parts book also shows side mirrors with much longer arms but I've never 
seen any.
