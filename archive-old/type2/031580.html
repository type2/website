take off your rocker assembly.  sight or use a straight edge along the
tops of the valves,  they should be all the same height, if some,
especially exhaust are higher it is a sign that you have either valve or
valve seat wear.  probably head pull time..

if all are even change to the next higher octane gas for a while and see
if that helps. it is just now about the time that gas goes to a different
formula for the summer here in the East, and probably many other areas so
this could be having an effect, and it is cheap and easy to check. 

I find I need 89 octane in mine in the summer so I just run it year round
and my C/R is quite low

basically I think you may have 3 seperate problems

. bad gas
. bad timing/valve lash
. possible valve seat problem, 

I would check em in that order
