> the idle to the specs for a 4spd bus, which is lower.

> 78 to idle higher, by adjusting the idle screw. Drove me nuts, as i
> thought is might be the aux. air value, etc, etc. Ended up being the
> connection to the brake booster line. Fixed that and it idled like
> a
> champ, and didn't stall at stoplights anymore.
