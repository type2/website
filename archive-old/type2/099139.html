In my 71 Westy, I installed a panel between the seats that rested on the
edges of the seal pedestals.  In the front of it, I added holes with
mesh underneath for cups.  Behind that was a flipup panel covered with
carpet- lift it up, and the console faced straight up beneath it. -
actually it was my CB, but you get the idea.  You could step on this or
over it.  The speakers were mounted in that space near the bottom of the
kick panels- sound better than in the door panels.  I cut my own panels
out of plywood paneling.
