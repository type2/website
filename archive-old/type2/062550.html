My wife was less than thrilled to see another VW in the yard, as I just sold my Beetle the other day (she thought we were done with VWs for good).  She did comment that the Bus has more character than the Beetle did.  I take this as a good sign.8^)

Anyone that's interested, the campsites are $4, and potluck items are welcomed.  If anyone is _REALLY_ interested, I'll give out more info.  Should be a good weekend, seeing as there's lots of nice scenery up there in NC.
