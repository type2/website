Even a dedicated spark plug socket sometimes won't hold the plug as 
securely as you'd like, especially as the rubber insert ages.  I usually 
put a couple of short strips of masking tape over the end of the socket 
and down an inch or so into the "well" of the socket.  This takes up just 
enough room in the socket to provide a snug fit with the plug.  You might 
try that with your deep socket instead of buying another tool.
