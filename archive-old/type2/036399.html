Subject: Re: 009 dist in a 1700

the 009's have a fairly inconsistent advance curve.  it has been my
experience that setting the 009 on a 1700/1800 is best done by setting total
advance.  buy an advance timing light from sears ($60) and set your timing
to 28 degrees total advance.  this will give you a great running engine. 
and without fears of pinging from the timing being advanced to far.  

this advice applies to all dizzys.  they all wear out.  check total advance.
this is the ONLY way to be sure of 1-not to much advance and 2- getting the
most advance without ping.
