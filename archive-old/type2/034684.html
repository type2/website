>I broke one of the fan blades off trying to get the bolts loose on the 
>fan, how much will the missing blade throw off the balance of the fan,

While it won't kill you engine, I'd definitely recommend finding a
replacement.  Used fans are not terribly expensive (like $5-$20?) and are
pretty easy to come by.  I expect the missing blade will cause a slight
imbalance and could eventually shorten your engine's bearing's life a
little.
