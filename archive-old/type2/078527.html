>Evening,
>I  have another question I hope someone can answer. I have a 78 Westy
>with a stock 2.0 and the threads for the temp
>sensor on the head are buggered up. I can't get the sensor to sit flat.
>What size is it so I can get a
>tap and clean up the threads.

M10 x 1.0, extra fine 10mm, sort of hard to find but a good Ace hardware
and I mean good or machine supply place should have it.  In a pinch you
can "make" a tap to chase the threads or even cut new out of an old worn
out T4 valve adjusting screw.  Just grind/file 2 or 3 cutting edges into the
screw and grind the end to a bit of a taper.  I used one of these for some
time till I finally broke down and bought one.
