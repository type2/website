Subject: Re: [T2] 411 Engine

Checking the oil in a 72 with a type4 motor will be tricky.
The oil filler tube on a 72+ bus is obviously different than the nonexistant
one on the type 4.
You may find that the inlet for the filler tube on the type 4 is there but
has a blocking pate over it, in which case you could take the filler tube
and fan housing off a wrecked bus motor and install it on the t4 giving you
an accessible oil filler and dipstick.
If the inlet is not milled in the case I wouldn't let it stop you ,I've seen
the t4 used in buses ,it's just an extra pain not having top access to the
engine on the 72s.
