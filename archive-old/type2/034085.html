Diagnosing engine sounds over a text-based medium is one of the hardest 
things in the world.  If your "banging sound" is maybe mor like a loud 
"ticking" sound, that is in more in the higer frequencies and IF IT GOES 
AWAY shortly after start up, then it might be nothing more than a lifter 
that's not pumping up.
