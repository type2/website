I've had a couple of '71 Westy's with the front tire mount (installed by
previous owners). Never had a problem with any of them. I like having the
tire out of the bed area to give my 6'5" 240# body a little more room to
stretch out. The tire well is covered with masonite and foam to extend the
bed. Also, the well is a handy place to store my tool roll right by the
engine compartment. For your rig, which I suppose has the full width bench
seat, you can come up with all sorts of storage ideas. I don't think that
extra supports to the bumper are necessary nor desirable. Others have
reported troubles with the front tire mounts, but I like the fact that I
don't have to worry about miscreants stealing my VW emblem.
