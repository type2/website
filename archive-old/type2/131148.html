Subject: Frequent Frustration with a Reluctant Relay

On my '69 bus, there is a constant problem making the dash turn signal
indicators flash.  About five years ago, I replaced the original relay (it
caught fire) with a replacement.  That one flashed the interior lights for a
few months, then quit mysteriously.  Ever since, it's gotten stuck on
occasion, will not flash the outside(actual) turn signals, and you have to

that the stickiness and the lack of interior indicators were somehow
related.

This has continued even after installing a new relay.  Argh.

best when you pull the switch for the hazard lights.  That's what I don't
understand.
