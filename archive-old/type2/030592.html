 I agree with this but i site an odd example that might be your culprit.
on my 71 Honda CB500 motorcycle (i know bear with me) i started to
experience the same symptoms you described. it happened almost overnight
but the symptoms started while i was riding in the rain...which tends to
wreak havoc on my exposed plugs and such...so i thought...it's just the
rain n. but the rain stopped and the symptoms didn't so. i had a new
breaker plate (points and such) so i thought well i better pop this in and
set my timing. so i did. but it didn't help. so to make a long story short
it was my first cylinder (1 out of 4) that had a bad sparkplug gap. i have
no idea how it got that way, but that's what it was.

so check your plugs first..you never know  when the plug gremlins have
changed your spark plug gap.
