Yes, been there and done that.  I already sent a reply directly to Bill
about this.  The type 2 and type 4 engines are much heavier than the type 1
or type 3.  Using only two arms, we have seen the case crack due to the
excessive weight of the engine.  Because of this, we use a two arm fitting
to assemble the short block.  Once assembled, we switch to a four arm
fitting to assemble the remainder of the engine.
