Bill:
It might be a loose shock, particularly if the noise is "new". 
The tie rod SHOULD twist somewhat freely as both tie rod ends are
spherical. It should not have perceptable lateral movement. This is
where a good front end alignment shop comes in handy. I just take it to
them, they tell me what to fix, I bring it back and they align it!

arrangement. They aren't too keen on chasing parts for furrin cars
