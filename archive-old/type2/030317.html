The gasket that sits between the intake air runners and the head on FI
engines (part # 022-129-707F) is a sandwich of a shiny graphite
impregnated ?paper? surrounding a hard black ?plastic? core.  You can
(and should) get replacements from any of the vendors on the list, or
Olympic, here in Northern Virginia, for <$10.
