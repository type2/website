I used a wire wheel to clean up the rims,, then primered them with a yellow zinc
chromate primer,from a rattle can, then painted them some color of white
automotive spray paint,,

weights on the back of the rim,, and still look great!,,,

drum,,, when the paint on that area gets hot, it melts,burns off,, and the wheel
nuts get looooose,, and the wheel can fall off
