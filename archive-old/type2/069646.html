I think it was Thom F. who was uncertain of when the small window/big window
change occured. I just flipped the month on my very cool officialized
NEATO/Bulli Catel/SSVC (UK) calendar. The "History:" text (with apologizies
to the clubs) reads:

August 1, 1962: Divided front seat replaced bench type seat (VIN 971 550)
August 1 1963: large rear window replaces small window, larger tailgate
fitted (VIN 1 144 282). High Roof models continue with small rear windows
until 1967.

More historical blurbs are mentioned but you'd know that if you were a
member of any of these clubs and were enjoying this excellent calendar!
