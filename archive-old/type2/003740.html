I just use the wire brush on the grinder followed by sandblasting for the
tight spots.  Prime with PPG DP 90, and either leave it like that (semigloss
black) or CLV 9000 gloss black top coat.  Obviously, you can use different
DP numbers to prime for other colors.  I have never had any trouble getting
DP to stick to clean metal.  At least here in hot dry SoCal, the need to
etch is largely superstition.
