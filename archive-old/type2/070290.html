Check your serial #. Per Bently, Kombi's and Campmobiles in 1972 range from 232
2000 001 to 232 2300 000.

It's entirely possible that the front skin was replaced at one time to repair
the results of a suicidal deer. I've *never* seen a wrap around bumper with turn
sigs up high. Check the bottom edge of the front skin (just below and behind the
bottom edge of the bumper). Compare its condition with the rest of the body.
Does it look a lot newer?
