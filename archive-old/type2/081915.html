Ray,
Reach up into the hole under the spare tire well.  The well be a metal line
follow it up and down eventually you will find a 2 inch piece of fuel line.

anyone out there has not changed it themselves recently please do ASAP... We
could have had a major fire on the way home if hadn't of know and caught it.

This line is on pancake engines.  I am unsure if it is on uprights.
