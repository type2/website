In your bus configuration, I think the best location would be inside the
jumpseat behind the driver.  You could put a cover over it so you could
still store stuff, but you could take air from where your feet would be if
you were sitting on that seat and duct it both front and back out the side
of the seat into the walk through area.  I wouldn't put it under the bus, a
heater is too nice of a tool not to keep it inside.

Then buy a relay and put a thermostat on it!!  Nice and toasty all night at
what ever temp you set it at.
