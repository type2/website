Mike g. wrote:


Now, rotate your engine counterclockwise.  This is BTDC.  As in,
anything happening during this time is occurring before your engine
reaches TDC.  

Now rotate your engine clockwise from '0 TDC' and that's ATDC.  As in,
anything happening at this time is After you engine has reached TDC -
aka ATDC.

Hopefully THIS will make some sense to anyone who needs the info. 


I agree with Mike

Put a timing light on your engine and watch the mark move.  Mine 
_always_travels counter clockwise (to the left) as the engine is revved.
 
This works for people who agree that flyweights and vacuum cans
cause the spark to advance.  That advance means firing the plug
sooner.  That firing the plug sooner means BEFORE TDC.
