I used the the VDO three hole plastic pod for the guages (CHT, OP, OT) I
installed in my '71 Westy.  I assume you bought two of the two holers.  I
just used sticky backed Velcro to hold the pod down to the dash, in the
center over the stock speaker location.  The wires come up through the
existing speaker grill holes in the dash, so this installation is completely
reversible should I or anyone else ever want to remove it.  I would think
that this method would work for your intended setup, too.  It does hog space
formerly used to put "stuff", but, otherwise it works well.  I especially
like that the guages are highly visible to the driver in this location.
