Go to your nearest tennis pro shop and take a look at their grip selection.
Last time I went, they had leather grip for a tennis raquet in 10 colors at
$9.95 a roll.  Each roll was 3 foot long.  I used two and now have the
fattest, most comfortable steering wheel.  They also had a variety of cloth,
terry cloth, and other materials from black to neon starting at $3 a roll.
Wal_mart or other sprts stores might even have this stuff in their sporting
goods department.  The part I like is that it is very easy to install, self
adhesive, and the adhesive that is left behind when you take it off comes
off with rubbing alcohol....  easy, neat, cheaper than a flaps leather cover
which won't fit your wheel....
