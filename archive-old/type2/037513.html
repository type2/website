I had the key snap off in the ignition while on vacation on a holiday
weekend at night on a lonely country road. This is how I got it started. 

I crawled under the bus and cut 5ft of ignition wire (my ignition key was
useless so what the hell) I attached one end to the hot side of the battery
and the other end to the hot side of the coil(thats the side furtherest
away from the center of the engine or the side that is NOT connected to the
points/condenser/dist....got it?) Then I used a scewdriver to jump the
starter or you can just bump start it in gear. I drove that baby from
Monterey to Los Angeles just like that.
