Yes and no, depends on the year of the bus. Early bays used machine screws [bolts] and flat washers, part
numbers N 010 210 7 and N 011 666 10.  They were 6x1.0x10
Later bays used hex head tapping screws, part number N 013 890 5. They were 6.3x13
Hex head tapping screws look like sheet metal screws, but do not have a sharp point.
I do not know the year break, but I do know 76 and up used them. They have a 10mm head.
