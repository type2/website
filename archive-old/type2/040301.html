> This will wound like a silly question to most of you, but what is EGR?
> Is it to do with US smog control or something?

Exhaust Gas Recirculation is a system that, well, recirculates exhaust gas
back to the intake.  The idea is to lower combustion temperatures by
introducing inert gas to the intake.  Lower combustion temperatures result
in fewer oxides of nitrogen (NOx) being produced.
