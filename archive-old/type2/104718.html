On 3/12/03 7:12 PM, "HightAndy_at_cs_dot_com" <HightAndy_at_cs_dot_com> wrote:

> What's the aprox weight of a 78 Westy?

> I'm looking to replace my current car with something that'll have a tow rate
> enough to tow the bus incase it's ever stranded.

It depends what you have in it, but unladen figure 3300 lbs for any Westy.
Add people, gear, a full water tank, spare tires and the number starts to
approach 4000 lbs.

See the manual for more data and this message in the archives:

http://type2_dot_com/bartnik/manual/pg79.jpg
http://www.type2_dot_com/archive/type2/094078.html

An "American" car with a class 2 hitch could perform the emergency towing
you are thinking about if the terrain was flat but you'd be pushing the
limit. Any big truck or truck-like SUV with a heavy duty class 3 or better
hitch will have no problem.

I had to push my bus uphill once with the help of three other people. Busses
are heavier than they appear.

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/78%20Camper/07-JoeUnloading.jpg

This is a picture of my bus getting towed by a 2002 Ford Expedition on a
heavy full size trailer. We had no problem and we were rated for ~7000 lbs.
