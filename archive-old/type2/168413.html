Subject: Re: [T2] White smoke

Look for brake fluid in the line from the booster to your intake manifold.
Sometimes the master cylinder will leak brake fluid into the booster.  When the level of fluid reaches the vacuum port, it will get sucked into your engine and cause thick, white smoke.
