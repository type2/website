Take the rocker arm off the engine (two nuts) and watch the valve.

If the valve keeps coming toward you when you remove the rocker arm, there's
a possibility you've got a valve seat that's dislodged itself. Only cure for
that is to replace the head.

If this is the case, a head replacement only takes a few hours, depending on
how good you are at dropping engines. Remove engine, remove the stuff from
that side (heat exchanger, manifold end, tin), remove the eight head nuts,
remove the head and look at the valves. If one looks like it's sunk into the
head, put on a new head and put the engine back together and back in.
