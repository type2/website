*** May 16,  St. Louis area ***
"DUETSCHMARK 3"  Sponsored by the Metro Volkswagen Club.  All Type
volkswagens are invited for the cruise.  Starts in St Louis at 10 AM at the
landing near the Admiral Casino.  Cross Eads bridge and up Hwy3 across
Brussels ferry and lunch on the island.  Cross eagles ferry to 94 across
Lewis and Clark bridge back to Illinois side.  For info, contact Joe Stumph
at (618) 277-9097

*** May 16, Denver Colorado ***
"Volkswagens on the Green", Memorial park, Arvada 9am.  For info, contact
the Volkswagen Enthusiasts of Colorado club at <VWOTG_at_t/~bugnyou>

*** May 22, Effingham, IL. ***
CILVA "Bugfest", sponsored by Central Illinois Volkswagen Association  (not
just for bugs) 99', on Mid-America grounds.  All VW's welcome.  Free:
registration, 500 t-shirts, 500 dash plaques, tech. demonstrations, SCAT
engine giveaway, and a paint the bus contest (71' panel).  Camping can be
arranged nearby.  For info, contact Pete at <Icculus415_at_aol_dot_com>

*** June 4 & 5, Kansas City MO ***
"Busses by the Boat" camping event sponsored by the "KC Kombies" a NEATO
chapter.  Beautiful primitive (restrooms provided) camping by the Mighty
Muddy Mo River.  A short walk (about 2 blocks) across the parking lot to
the Bug Blitz show and Argosy Casino.  This will be an informal and fun
pre-show event.  All VW's are welcome to camp.  For info contact Pink
Newell at <PINKNEWELL_at_aol_dot_com> (816)587-2838.

*** June 5, Kansas City MO ***
"10th Bug Blitz" at the Argosy Casino sponsored by the MoKan VW club.
Primitive camping next to the show close to the Mighty Mo river.  For info
contact Pink Newell at <PINKNEWELL_at_aol_dot_com> (816)587-2838.

*** June 11-13, Springfield MO ***

(417)889-8813 or Bob Hufford at <bhufford_at_mail.orion_dot_org> (417)833-2047

*** September 3-6, St Louis MO ***
"Busses By the Arch" is always held over the long Labor Day weekend, this
year it's Friday thru Monday, Sept 3, 4, 5, 6th, 1999.  The cool new

Hannibal, MO.  For info, contact Bill Bowman
<BussesBytheArch_at_compuserve_dot_com>  This is a NEATO & LiMBO event.
