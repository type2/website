True, but if you want the stock distributor to be properly oriented so the
vac can has room for proper setting, then you must install the drive gear
properly oriented. You can reorient the gear non-stock to accomadate a
non-stock distributor. Be certain the rotor points to the mark on the
housing with no. 1 TDC. A clearance problem with the vac can or condenser
means either the gear is not right or it's a non-stock distributor.
