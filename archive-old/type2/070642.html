Most 1600cc cases (I think when they started making them in Brasil) 
are universal cases that have the bosses cast in for the hanger bar. 
The alternative is to get the oil plate adaptor. The OPA bolts 
between onto the stud for the oil pump cover plate. This will allow 
you to use a non bus/universal case in a 1968 to 1971 bus. I heard 
bus boys still sells them. You may have to insert longer studs for 
the OPA install.
