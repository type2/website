On Mon, 18 Jun 2007, Greg Potts wrote:

> Hi Nathan,

> Dropping the transmission with the engine is no major sweat, but I
> would NOT recommend trying to install them together, particularly not
> if you are working alone. Way too heavy to manage, unless you have a
> particularly good jack available.

If it's an early bay like mine, removing and installing the engine and
transmission together is the only way to go!  I can't imagine trying to
mate up an engine to a transmission that is barely dangling on chains or
somesuch.

If you have a floor jack, bolt a foot-square piece of 3/4 or 1" plywood to
the "cup" to provide a flat surface.  The balance point is about where the
engine joins the transmission.
