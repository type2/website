Have you checked the spark plug wires, rotor and cap
for cracks or tracking, and changing out #2 plug?

Compression sounds low, but even all around, 
indicating to me that your gauge is wrong, or valves
aren't set proper.  
Hydrolic valves, 1.5 turns past touching.  Go by sound
when determining when set screw touches push-rod.  I
wiggle the rocker back and forth while screwing down
until I cant hear a click anymore.  Then 1.5 turns
further. I set valves on hydrolic lifters when warm
(not hot, ouch!) to ensure pumped up lifters. 
Good luck,
Rick
