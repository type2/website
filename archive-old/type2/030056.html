Missy,

Pretty much a no-brainer :-) Look at your coil.  There ought to be a black
wire to it that carries 12v from the main wiring harness on the
passenger's side of the engien compartment. There also will be another
black wire coming from it that goes to an inline fuse-holder and then into
the tin under the double relay.  check?  Is there a fuse in the
fuse-holder?  OK, now, crawl under the bus in front of the driver's side
wheel and look at the nose cone of the trans.  there is a switch there
that has two wires to it.  one goes to the fuse holder, the other goes
back into the backup lights.

The places to check for 12V with a VOM are:

At the coil... got 12V?  the iginition is on, yes?
At the fuse holder... both going in and coming out.
At the switch on the trans... one will have 12V, one won't. That's normal.
Shift into reverse and power should flow thru the switch.
with the bus in reverse and the key on, check for 12V at the reverse light
sockets.

If you have any problems, let me know.
