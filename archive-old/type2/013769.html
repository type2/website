Make sure that when you install the seal that the seam ends up at the rear of
the engine - towards the front of the bus - up on the firewall.

Porbably obvious for most, but for whatever reason i installed mine so that
the seal was donw under the cooling fan. You can never get the ends to meet,
so there's always a little gap - best that the gap not be directly under the
fan, where it will suck in hot exhaust/heated air.
