On 2/15/04 8:08 AM, "Michael Knight" <vwdormer_at_hotmail_dot_com> wrote:

> Although I am the Heir of Superman my Xray vision doesn't seem to penetrate
> the these super German Engine Cases. So how would one establish that an
> engine has Hydraulic Lifters without splitting the case. I am sure this is
> an easy answer for some of you out there. Can it be done? If so how to?

- remove valve cover
- undo push rod tube retaining wire
- remove rocker arm and shaft
- remove push rod
- pull out push rod tube of choice
- use magnet to remove lifter from bore
- inspect
- clean area and replace all
- do valve adjustment
