Hello List!!

I can't contain myself, I have to brag about my latest find.  

I bought a '68 7-seater for $300.  It has 2 dual port engines; the one in it 
runs but oil gushes from the oil cooler; no matter, I got it as a parts bus.  
It came with a second dual port engine, the PO claims it has a leaking rear 
(front) main seal.  Inside, it's been partially stripped in preparation for a 
restoration, and most of the parts are there.  There was a footlocker full of 
parts inside.  I found some interesting stuff, like all 4 round reflectors in 
good shape, a spare '68 door handle (something I REALLY wanted), and the rear 
bumper splash guards in good shape (also something I was looking for).  I'll 
have to wait for more time and warmer weather to really pull everything out 
and "inventory," but I think I made out like a bandit on this one!  I plan to 
use the nose to replace mine and some other body panels to take care of 
cancer on mine.  Can't gut mine for the "new" one as it has special 
sentimental value (father-in-law bought it new, wife grew up in it...).  When 
I'm done with it (won't be a while...) I'll offer any parts I don't want to 
the list first.
