Hi David!

I have a 1973 1800cc Pancake engine in my 1973 camper bus, and I set all
my valves to .006, and the work just great.

On your year as well, intake and exhaust valves should be set the same,
at .006.   There is good instruction about how to do this in the Idiot's
manual by John Muir.

I asked a lot of folks as well before I set my valves, and although I
got a few different answers overwhelmingly, most folks agreed on .006

Good luck!
