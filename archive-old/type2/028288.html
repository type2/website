> 1.  how long can I leave the fridge on?  Will the battery go dead?

You should have a second battery that runs the fridge.  That battery will
generally go dead after 4-8 hours.  My experience has been that the amount
of cooling provided by the battery alone is so little that it's not even
worth leaving the fridge on while parked.  I do run it while driving,
although it does little more then.  What I mainly rely on for colling is ice
in sturdy ziploc bags.  I save the fridge battery mainly to jumpstart the
car if the main battery runs down!
