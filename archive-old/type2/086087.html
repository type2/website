  One of the easiest ways to do it is find an airline that fly's non stop
from wherever in Germany to the closet airport to you and have your seller
take it to the airport and put it on an airline as freight. I brought over 2
trailer hitches and a huge box for Rustbus man, cost about 80 dollars. All
international airlines carry freight.

Ronnie

ps. after you pick it up you will have to walk it through customs and pay
whatever duties may be owed, but you will be avoiding the brokers fees.
UPS, DHL and Fed Ex all have ridiculous brokerage fees, but you pay for the
convenience of there service
