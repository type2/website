What?  you did not like hot air from the engine coming into the cab?

That is fresh air pushed across those ceramic pieces and into the cab.the
heat is transferred from the exhaust pipe, thru the ceramic, to the air that
is going to the cab......_dot_millions of cars used this process, it is not
dangerous.

It is only dangerous if you have a greasy old motor that has no engine seal,
leaky exhaust, and or no continuity of the fresh heated air tube from the
back of the car to the front.  Then, and only then, are you forcing smoky,
CO filled air into the cabin.

Gas heaters are great, but are really not made to run constantly to heat
your car.  Their is a club called the KC Kombis that has a seminar on tape
you can buy.
