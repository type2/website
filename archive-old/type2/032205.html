running well -- and it'll never run as well as it did on FI, on the

the straw that broke the camel's back.

I may come to regret my decision eventually, but I wanted to make it clear
it wasn't something I entered into lightly, or through fear of the
unknown.  I'm an EET student...electrons don't frighten me. ;)  The idea
of spending $275 on a rebuilt ECU that wasn't even returnable did,
however, bother me.  You can bet I checked the wiring harness and all the
other components every way I knew how, before I gave up, though.
