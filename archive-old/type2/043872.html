8th Annual Eureka Springs Volkswagen Show     August 25th-26th-27th 2000
Friday-Saturday-Sunday
Location: Eureka Springs, Arkansas at the Inn of the Ozarks
This years show theme "Year of the Bus".
Type 2's will be parked in larger spaces this year!
FREE general spectator admission.
Registration fee is $20.00 PLUS 2 cans of food.
The NWAVWA and the Arkansas Volkswagen Volksmeisters present the "8th Annual
Eureka Springs Volkswagen Show, Swap Meet, Parade and Tourcade" at the "Inn
of the Ozarks Resort and Convention Center" in beautiful Eureka Springs,
Arkansas.
Cookout on Friday evening. Swap Meet starts Friday afternoon. Pre-Registered
Show Vehicles will be staged beginning Friday afternoon. Show, Swap Meet and
Parade on Saturday and the Tourcade Sunday morning.
We are expecting 300++ VW's this year with 40 show classes plus specialty
classes. This years trophies are ceramic beer steins
that are made in Germany.
We will have tons of  indoor (air conditioned) and outdoor vendors
A non-perishable food drive for the benefit of the Ozark Area Food Bank will
be held in conjunction with the show.
NO sun tents or large umbrellas are allowed in the registered show area!
