Well, it is fersure not any VW fuel pump I have ever seen, on a carb or on

pump.  It may well be a junkyard pump from some other manufacturer and it

and see.  You would need an FI fuel pressure tester to be sure you had the
requisite 30+psi pressure rather than the 2-4psi pressure needed for a
carb.
