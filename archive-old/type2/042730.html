Hi All,

I bought my 71 Westy from my nephew at his house
warming party. While he was giving me the tour of
the 5 acres, I spotted this bus that apparently
doubled as the jukebox for the nights festivities
outside. 

There was a bluegrass tape in the deck - it actually
came with the bus when he got it. I don't remember
the name of the band, but they were playing a humorous
tune called "Ode to Billy Boner."

Since this was obviously divine intervention, my Westy 
has been known as Billy Boner or just Billy ever since.

This is the only "thing" that I own that I've attached
a name to.
