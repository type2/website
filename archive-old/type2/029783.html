I was thinking about this and pulled out the calculator.  A 1/4 inch hose
as an ID of 0.250 in.  6mm converts to an ID of 0.236 inch.  The
difference is .014 inch.

I've seen a greater variation than this in the fuel inlets/outlets on
various lines, pumps, and carburators on VWs.  The metric hose fits a tiny
bit tighter, but one should still be careful of the fit, regardless of the
hose used.  If the hose is a good friction fit it will make a good seal.
If it slides on with little effort, the seal won't be all that good, even
with clamps.
