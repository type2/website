Basically he is asking you to pay for their stupidity.... you dont have to
pull the hub to get to the brakes.

All 71 and newer busses have composite drums, that means the drum is held
onto the hub with 2 11mm bolts.  remove those and the drum separates from
the hub easy as pie (in theory.)

Often the drum is rusted to the hub, but this can be fixed with a hand
sledge heat and penetrating oil in short order 9 times out of 10.
