Electronic ignition?  The new module can fail the heat test.  Did it have
similar symptoms before you installed the module?

I've had a new condensor fail after warm up.

The pick up coil in the distributor would be another place to look.

Best to check these items when they are in the fail mode if it is heat related.

Your ignition coil has a set of resistance specs to check.  Those, too, may only
fail when hot.  That VOM meter is sounding like a pretty good investment.
