The backup light switch is located on the front of the transmission on the
drivers side. Its not hard to find because it is the only thing on the
trans that has wires coming out of it. Run along the two wires and make
sure that they are all good. The switch fits just like a brake light
switch on the master cylinder (just screws on with a wrench).I am not sure
about price because I have never had to replace this before.
