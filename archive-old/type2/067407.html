Ben Pon (St. Pon?), the Dutch importer, is generally given credit for the 
idea of the VW Transporter.  It supposedly was inspired by the "shuttle 
craft" used in the factory to run parts around.  Basically a Beetle pan 
with the driver sitting over the wheels (but I can't remember if it was 
the front or back wheels).
