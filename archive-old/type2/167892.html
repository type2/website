so, is too hot.On days of 40* C, on the highway, my engine only gets up 
225*F ( and the roads have a lot of long hills in B.C.)
  I like these gauges because they look good in the dash, are easy to 
read,simple to install and when I'm on a road trip I like to keep a 
constant lookout on how the engine is performing.
  Any wiring  that had to be done I ran the wires underneath the van
