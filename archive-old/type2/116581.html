400 is nothing to worry about if you have the sender mounted on a spark
plug.  I have driven mine at over 550 and it didn't blow up or fail
catastrophically.  It did spit out a spark plug insert eventually so now I
have an excuse to replace the heads (I knew all along I was trashing
them--hopefully my pistons aren't too melted) and reset the compression to

here that 450 is "do not exceed" temp for airplanes.  That would include a
safety margin but running constantly at that temp would seem to reduce
longevity.
