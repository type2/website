I just wanted to share a few thoughts about this show hosted by the Gorge Vw
Club. My daughter Kate and I drove up for this show last year and it was one
of the most enjoyable times I've ever had at a VW event. For those who know

great back roads to travel make this a "must do" on my list. Just wanted to
share.
