--- Dadeeo <dadeeo_at_net-link_dot_net> wrote:
> Anyone have a good tip on how to remove the
> frozen screws that hold the cylinder shroud to
> the head without breaking them? Specifically the
> ones either side of the intake manifold. I
> twisted the heads off when trying to remove
> them.

Lots of liquid wrench!  Apply it repeatedly for a few days before you
pull the thing apart.

When you're ready to take it apart, get a drift and put it on the top
of the screw.  Give it a few sharp raps with a hammer, which helps to
break things loose.

Once you get the things apart, be sure to use anti-sieze to prevent
future problems.  You will probably end up taking it apart again!
