(like that was news to anyone who knows me :-)

The specific way I have gone nuts this time is that I have bought a rusty,
bondofied '79 passenger (partial westfakia).  The PO story is that it just
stopped running and she could not get it restarted.  It is about the
saddest bus i have ever seen rolling.  The oil is 1.5 quarts low, there is
*NO* exhaust (headers, no muffler), it is wearing way more bondo than
should be legal, etc. etc.

I am going to part it.  That said, I would invite offers for bits, cuz I
really don't need anything off it, I just hated to see it go to the
crusher intact.  If you need anything from a '79 other than sheet metal
(there is, as far as I can see *no* salvageable sheet metal on this bus) I
invite you to do one of four things:

1) write me for directions to a stripping party this coming Saturday;
2) let me know what you want and come and pick it up over the next couple
   weeks;
3) let me know what you want and *promise* you will pick it up at
   Everybus;
4) Arrange with a friend/fellow list member in the DC/Baltimore area to
   pick it up and ship it to you.

Note that "Have Steve Dolan ship it to me" is *NOT*  on the list of
options!  This is a personal failing.  I do not mail things.  Only under
extreme duress and with constant pestering.  It sucks, but it is a
personal phobia of some sort.  I am sorry.

On pricing, I would kind of like to  recover what I paid for it,
but I am a dedicated socialist and will basically let you set your own
price, as long as *you* need it for your bus. If I see it turn up on an
