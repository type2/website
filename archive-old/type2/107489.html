Subject: RE: [T2] clunk clunk clunk from the rear, when turning right

The clunking while turning is classic CV joint failure noise. I had it a
year or two ago. I was able to drive a few weeks carefully.

Some play in the axial (left-right) direction is normal; if you move the bus
a little, you'll probably have play on the other side too. I believe this
play exists to allow the the wheel to move up and down (changing the
distance from wheel to tranny) without pulling the shaft out. Radial play
(up-down / forward-back) is not normal.
