Subject: My first bus camping trip (EveryBus)

     Marcus said he would be sending out a full trip report with picture 
links of our fun ride home (it should have been about 5 hours but ended up 
being about 12!).  I just wanted to say that I had the best time at this 
first VW bus camping trip and met some really great people.  The whole scene 
was just pretty amazing.  There seems to be less and less people of any 
really quality out there in this world, but there were about 150 busloads of 
them in NC this weekend!
     The ride out was fairly un-eventful.  The camping trip was awesome!  
The ride home was a bit trying, but we made it.  As for bus technical 
content:
     If your pressure sender ever goes bad on the way home, you can use a 
spare brake line with Teflon tape on the threads to seal it up.  Cut one 
fitting off, plug it with something, and put a couple of hose clamps on.  
What a cool idea!  Also, you probably all know this, but make sure you check 
the brass fuel fittings that go into your carbs.  Mine popped off about 30 
miles from home and thank god it didn't catch on fire.
