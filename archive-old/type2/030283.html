On Fri, 13 Jul 2001, Michael Benthin (MUTS) wrote:

> I thought "staking" is called "peening"??

I think of "peening" as a process applied to an area of a surface, such as
"shot-peening".  "Staking" is applied to specific locations to displace
metal in order to hold another object in place.  For example, the "dime"
on solex carburators is staked in place.
