Halsey Import Parts
7721 NE Halsey
Portland, OR
800-792-0081

The Parts Barn
11700 SE Stark St.
Portland, OR
800-500-8001
503-255-8001

John's Import Auto Parts
4701 SE 24th Ave.
Portland, OR
503-231-1601
