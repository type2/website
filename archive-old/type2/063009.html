        I am one of the intrepid Inuvikkers with a laptop. Both myself and
Ron used Apple PowerBook Duos, and ran the regular 110 power adapter off a
cheapy inverter ("PowerToGo" I think?). Of course, I don't have any clue

and it's only designed to be away from the docking station for a short
time. Ron had problems with his, but I don't think they were power
realated, more like software problems. And of course, there was Hoover,
sticking out from the pack as usual, with a full size PC, 2 heavy duty
inverters, and I believe 2 isolated batteries...   ;-)
