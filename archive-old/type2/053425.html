Ronnie,
According to the 1970 Transporter Manual, the weight limits for a single cab 
are as follows.  I think these should be identical for a 68 pickup.

Unladen weight ..................2634 lbs.
Payload .........................2293 lbs.
Gross vehicle weight ............4927 lbs.
Permissible front axle load .....2193 lbs.
Permissible rear axle load ......2734 lbs.
Trailer without brakes ..........1100 lbs.

Hope this helps.
