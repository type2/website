1) they work on pressure.

2) there are 2 of them on the master cylinder

3) if you do not flush your brake system every 2 years [like everyone should but never does!!!] then crap in
the fluid settles into the bottom of the master. This plugs the holes in the switches.

Buy 2 new switches. Remove the 2 old ones and admire the crap in the holes!
Flush the master and the reservoirs with clean brake fluid. Install the 2 new switches. Bleed all 4 wheels
until clean fluid appears.
