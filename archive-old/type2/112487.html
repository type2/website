what sort of body work doe sit need?  rust or dent repair?

1) buy a copy of the 63- 67 bentley and study the body diagram with the lines 
where the pieces were welded togetehr to form a bus.

2)  find people with buses and walk around their buses and google at them.  

3) go to VW shows and ask people and look at buses.  

One things is an eye for how to fix it, like how it should be,  and the other 
is the skill (and tools) to fix it.  Rumor has it that east coast buses have 
bad salt rust and need lots of bits cut off and welded on.  Most of the work 
in welding is prep work, cutting out the bad old piece and fitting the new 
piece.   Get good ear and eye protection.  Wear sturdy clothes.   Learn the fine 
art of using a grinder.  :)

Body work is a pain.  I'd not want to do it for a living.
