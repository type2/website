when I rebuilt my 2 liter the head was flycut because it was not square
(GEX head)  there was then insufficient deck height, so my mechanic used
three shims under the cyl. to get (I think) .045 or so.   the heads
torqued down fine and there have been no problems so far (6,000miles)

and of necesity he also used a single .025 shim between the head and cyl.
when finished deck height was slightly below what is spec'ed.  but I
wanted the CR down a little on mine too.
