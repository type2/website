Is your tank leaking,, or any of the hoses?  No welding allowed !!

the weld unless you blow thru with too much heat.  lol,,, so don't do that!

sparks land on the tank.. or heat transfer. Soak the batt of insulation in 
water.. for an even better insulation ,, spark arrestor.

I have a heavy wool blanket that I drench in water and lay over the area 
when I have something I don't want damaged by sparks.

myself,, I don't have any worries about welding right on the tank,, so ,, 4" 
away is like a mile.

torches to cut out stuff that is right beside fuel lines, tanks. etc.
