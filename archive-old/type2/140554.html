 Fast forward to EveryBus 2004.  Mark Dearing came to help replace
AJ's clutch and brought along a VERY cool wrench that fit over the
gland nut at one end. At the other end it had a gear that meshed with

30 ft/lbs you wound up with 270 at the gland nut.
