My experience is that you need to replace both exchangers at the same time.  
The new exchangers are different than the old ones.  The exhaust ports are 
about 1/2" higher, and if you only replace one side, your muffler won't match 
up.  Other than the exhaust ports, I have no other problems with the 
exchangers.  Of course, I may have a set from a different batch.
