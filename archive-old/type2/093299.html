Subject: Re: [T2] I smell like exhaust fumes

Are the engine seal and the engine door seals in good condition.

I can't think of a single clamp that would let exhaust into the vehicle - 
only exhaust leak out where the fan might ingest it and send it through the 
heaters.

Also sounds like your flappers aren't working if you are getting air flow 
from the engine to the interior.

Switch mechanics or find a friend. Somebody needs to give your VW a gander 
before you hurt yourself.

I'd buy a Bentley manual and start inventorying the engine and engine bay 
to ensure everything that is supposed to be there is. This way you don't 
rely on the dubious abilities of this mechanic and you learn alot at the 
same time.

Lastly I would question the condition of the heater boxes. Internally they 
may be failing and leaking exhaust versus this break in the pipe.

Where exactly is the break?

Many of the clamps can be replaced with hardware store varieties just fine.

Good luck!
