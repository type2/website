Hi Chris,

I have 2 suggestions:

1. Make sure you're getting full throttle when the pedal is at the 
floor. Have someone hold the pedal UP when tightening the linkage to 
ensure it's tight, then check the action when the pedal is on the 
floor. If you aren't asking for full throttle the bus will be quite 
anemic.

2. Compression test. This will also give you a look at the plugs, to 
check the mixture.
