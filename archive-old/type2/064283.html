I used my mounted CB in the Van and my buddy used a hand held on a 
road trip caravan last month. The hand held did great when he was within 
sight of my van, any further and his signal broke up. However he 
could receive me when he lagged far behind.  Since my SO and I take 
separate vehicles occasionally, I ordered a Uniden handheld 40 
channel with car power plug for $40 from Damark. A mag. mount antenna 
from radio shack will probably increase the range.
