AFAIK, the type2 website resides on its own machine, so we are not
really space-limited by anything but our hard-drive size.

One problem with individual sites is that they tend to come and go. 
Often I have gone in search of some tidbit of info to find that the
site I bookmarked no longer exists.  The information is valuable, but
for one reason or another it has not been maintained.  Better, IMO, to
put the information in one or two sites where it will be backed up and
be more or less under the control of the listmembers.

For those who choose to place their information on their own sites,
The links through the Type 2 Library allow them to be included in the
database while maintaining close control over their works.
