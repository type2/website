Subject: RE: [T2] extra battery wireing....

Here is what I did for hooking up my Aux battery. 
With the two relay method you can still start the bus when the Aux battery
is completely dead and still have full voltage to the starter from the main
battery with no drain from the Aux battery when you turn the key over.
Use the appropriate Amp ratting relay _at_ 30 Amp or better and I used 10g wire
from main battery to the relay and to the Aux battery.
This is always a heated subject (if not a FART) and there are many different
ways and theories on how to do this.
This has worked well for me, enjoy.
