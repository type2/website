now, and really my first introduction to Vdubs, i can

almost every triple A guy in the area and i am
currently in need of an engine rebuild as we

head out my window and catch a glimpse of it, and
suddenly its all worthwhile.   theres nothing like the

tieing up traffic, getting into conversations with
strangers everywhere you go, tons of stuff like that. 
 plus at the end of a day of driving i can pull over
and go to bed, snug as bug in a rug.   these buses may
need a little more TLC than your average Toyota camry,
but they are simple in design, and the more you learn,
the easier they are to work on.   my bus has taken me
on 2 cross country trips, and to numerous shows all
for a lot less than a $300 car payment.   she might
keep my wallet light, but at least its sporadic.  
This of course is based on the premise that i do 90%
of the work myself, might be different if i couldnt
work on it myself$  oh well, just my $.02, since youre
asking, im sure deep down you know it too, they are
worth it:)......that is of course until im elbow deep
