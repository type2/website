>       2: I need one new headlight assembly (i.e. glass lens, bucket
> thing, etc.) can I use a beetle one or does anyone have a spare one for
> sale?

Bug and bus ones are different.  Bugs have the adjusting screws 180
degrees apart; busses have them less than 90 degrees apart, on either
side of the mounting screw.  THey will fit, but the light will be
pointing the wrong way.
