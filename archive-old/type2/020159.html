Even in the CIS systems, the relay relies on the coil as a trigger for
the fuel pump for safety reasons.  

Fuel pump running + ignition on + wreck=TROUBLE 

Scott Frazer's crew cab FI page documents "fooling" the relay similar to
the above. The point of the test is to see if, after the fuel pump runs
for a few seconds, does the cold start problem still exist.  If running
the pump prior to ignition solves the problem, your fuel pressure
regulator may be bad.
