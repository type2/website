After tightening up the nut things are better but I still have the humming that seems to be indicative of an iffy bearing so I guess I'll have to strip it down and investigate futher. As a couple of people pointed out, this would be a good idea, anyway.

 Hopefully when I get in there all I'll need is new bearings. The van is pretty low mileage (80K) and I suspect that the PO's have skimped  on chassis maintenance.

 I'll start collecting the parts as I hope to be able to do this in one pass- I want to try to avoid having the van off the road while I wait for parts to arrive if I can.
