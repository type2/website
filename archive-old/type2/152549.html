 You can't disturb the piston skirt, that is what keeps the
 piston running straight in the cylinder. Pistons are not cylindrical, they
 are conical. The skirt of the piston is larger in diameter than the top and
 this slight cone shape helps to keep the piston from cocking inside the
 cylinder. If you cut material away from the skirt you will disturb this and
 the piston is ruined.
