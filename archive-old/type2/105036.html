On Tue, 18 Mar 2003, Richard Atwell wrote:

>  I finally got my hands on a bugpack catalog (#24) and noticed that
shown on page 39 are Type 4 finned aluminum valve covers (4540) that bolt
onto the rocker arms (no bails). I thought these were only available for
Type 1 engines and now I'm excited. Has anyone tried them?

Before you get too excited, consider that the cast aluminum valve covers
have to be precisely flat, and the head surface also has to be completely
flat to get a good seal.  The stamped valve cover has a bit of flex built
in so that it will even conform with a not-quite-flat head surface.  You
may be more leak prone with the cast covers!
