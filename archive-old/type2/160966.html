Hi Doug - You could use anything that you can shape to
fit and clamp in place. The stock tube makes it a lot
easier. You may or may not be aware that those
openings need to be sealed if the hoses aren't in
place and the   flaps on the electric blower need to
be functional as well. With my quad headtemp gauge I
was able to experiment with a lot of different
scenarios and one was running without those openings
closed off. I do not remember the numbers, but it was
considerably hotter with them open. I used about 4
layers of aluminum foil wrapped over the stub and
secured with a hose clamp when I didn't have the hoses
on.

The opening provides an easier path for cooling air
than the path it is supposed to take around the
cylinders. Without the resistance of that path being
closed, you aren't getting proper cooling elsewhere.
I would think that you would be able to find that
component used, but as I said, anything that you can
make work will work. -BaH
