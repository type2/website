Christopher:
Primer will do NOTHING to prevent rust, but rather enhance rust. Primer is made
to enhance adhesion of panit to bare metal and it also has a lot of
attractivness for water. (It draws the water into the surface just like it does
the paint.)
A good thing to coat it with would be POR-15. This is a tough (REALLY TOUGH)
waterproof coating that stops rust. This stuff is incredible. It needs no
topcoating to work. Look in the archives to find where to buy it.
