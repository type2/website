Ric:
The points HAVE to be right to have the right timing. A person could
eyeball the point gap to within .005 inch, but that could be a
difference of 15 degress in the timing. The points also have to function
for the FI to work. Well, it's obviously functioning. 
Take a dwell meter (or feeler guage) and a timing lite, that'll fix 90%
of the problems.
