Great ideas but if you don't have the means to do the below, try using carb
cleaner.  Get a can of it with the plastic straw tube attached and stick
the straw in the open bleeder valve with the brake line plugged (the
bleeder valve opening should be a close fit to the diameter of the straw).
Spray, and the propellant should force the pistons out.

-David
