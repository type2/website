If yer gonna pull things apart, I'd like to pass along this advice:

A) Have a look at the following:
http://volksweb.relitech_dot_com/cvjoints.htm

B) The bolts; clean the heads out good before taking a tool to them. 
Whack each with a good blow to help 'unstick' them from the
hub/transmission.  Check if it is a 6 (hex) or 12-point bolt head and
use the correct tool.

C) Reassembling the CV joint; they only go together correctly one way. 
Remember the words 'narrow-to-wide' (this is covered on the above
page.).

D) If you buy new joints, don't disassemble them.  Put 'em on and grease
'em up.


The way to reverse the CV joints is to pull both axles and swap the
right wheel-side cv joint for the left wheel-side one.  This will spin
each wheel-side cv joint in the opposite direction.
