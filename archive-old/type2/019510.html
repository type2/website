Voltage regulator.  Follow steps in book to determine if the alternator or
regulator is bad.  I am just guessing, but I think the brown wire is a
ground.  Replace the wiring harness between the alternator and regulator.
You can usually find a good harness on a used alternator.  Be sure you have
your alternator checked by a reputible automotive electric shop while it is
out.  Simply replacing the burned wire and/ or regulator may not solve the
problem.
