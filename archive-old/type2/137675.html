Subject: Re: [T2] 68 disk brake conversion/ powertrain upgrade Q's

I don't think a frame support is necessary. My understanding is that
VW only put in that wrap around for DOT safety compliance.

Also, literature I've read states T4 engine is only 40 pounds heavier
than T1. My experience is that I can lift a T1 engine with one helper,
but the last fully equipped T4 engine we bought took two helpers. The
last T4 I bought took only one helper to lift was it had no muffler or
clutch on it. If it was even a hundred pounds heavier, I would not
have been able to lift it.

A T1 engine is nice because your bus was made for that. After a few
months on this list, I have decided T4 is a longer lasting superior
engine if you watch your head temps to avoid dropping a valve and care
for your exhaust studs.
