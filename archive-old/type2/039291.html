keep in mind that the lower number I quoted did not include a certain
amount of unusable fuel (1/2 gallon or so)  This is fuel below the level
of the tank outlet.  The purpose of this unusable fuel is to provide a
settling area for grunge and water where it won't be sucked up all at once
by the outlet.  It's a good idea to pull the in-tank filter when you
replace the fuel lines and allow the water/grunge out.  If you leave it
in, it can rust your tank!
