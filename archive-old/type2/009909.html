CB is the engine code for 1972 and early 1973 dual carb manual transmission
engines. Used starters can be cheap but you might also want to go get one
>from an auto parts store that is warrenteed. I recall some posts suggesting
that buy one for a manual transmision as it will fit and provide a little
more umph to your start. Auto trans startes have about a 1/8 or so more
horsepower in the starters motor.
