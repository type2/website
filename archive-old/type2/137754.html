Subject: Re: [T2] Alternator Questions

At this point I feel very confident that your loose wire is the one
that was supposed to be connected to the starter. At this point you
could temporarily hook it to the battery so you can test the voltage.
Once you verify everything is in order, you can move it to the starter
where it belongs. You may want to run a remote starter wire from the
starter solenoid to the battery so you can start the bus from the
engine compartment and thus minimize the running time while you check
for abnormal voltage.
