to start it again, sound of cranking was r-r-r-R-r-r-r-R-  (one -R- being

lotsa pumping of the gas it started but just barely ran at idle, still
pumping lotsa gas.  Eventually I was able to rev it up a bit, and it was

busted something-or-another inside the cylinder.

after setting to the book's recommendation (7.5 BTDC).  But it started and
ran ok Fri, Sat, Sun AM though I didn't drive it more than about 10 miles.
