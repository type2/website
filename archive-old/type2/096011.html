If when you say pops you mean backfires through the exhaust that is due to
a blown exhaust seal somewhere in your system. If its a type IV the source
is likely to be your exhaust manifolds where they connect to the head.
Unfortunately the fix for this can often end up leading you down a slippery
slope of broken exhaust studs and having to remove the head. Check for
exhaust leaks...
