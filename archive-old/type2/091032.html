the best piece of advice i can give you is to TAKE YOUR TIME
in finding the right bus. if you find one you really like,

be another (& probably better) bus for you to buy if you
really want one.

side of things, b/c those types of things can always be fixed.
rust is going to end up costing you the most money in the
long run.
