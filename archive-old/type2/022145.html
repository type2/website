Are you sure you have a stock size degree pulley, or is it a "power pulley"?
If it is the latter, it is smaller in diameter than stock, and needs a
special size 
belt.  It will also slow down your fan and may cause cooling problems.  I
STRONGLY recommend a stock size pulley, degree or stock!
