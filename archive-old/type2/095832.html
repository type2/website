I think you may need to take the nose cone off completely (which means
engine and transmission out).
When my previous bus became stuck in third gear the before inspection
diagnosis was that the bracket which holds the selector in place was broken;
but in fact all that had happened was that the inner end of the shift rod
which is J shaped had come out of the rubber bushing in the eye at the top
of the selector, a new bushing was all that was required.
