Comercial vehicles were not imported into the US after 1971 (as a result
of the "chicken war- tarrif wars") and that included single cabs (261),
double cabs (265), panels (211) or ambulances (274). Any such vehicles
in the US later than 71's were probably grey-market cars.
I'm guessing you're in the US, but I may be wrong! Such a vehicle would
not be all that uncommon in the UK or European continent.
