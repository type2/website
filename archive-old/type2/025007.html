Well howdy all. Those of you who have been around for a while might remember
the list t-shirts I printed up waaaaayyyy back when there was only one bus
list. Enough people have been asking me to do more, so I have finally agreed.
So, here's your chance to get a new and very much improved List t-shirt! Now
you can have something stylish to wear to bus events or to the office, to show
others your true coolness, and also easily recognize other listmembers at a
glance.
These have been officially sanctioned by list and domain masters Ken Hooper,
Thom Fitzpatrick, and Steve Dolan.

I've updated the design a bit to reflect the changing nature of the list. I
don't have pictures yet, but I shall describe it as best as possible. You can
go to my website at:
http://www4.ncsu_dot_edu/eos/users/r/rapalmer/www/
to see a couple pictures of the old design. This one will be somewhat similar,
but even better, and feature even more busses. There will be a total of 9
busses on the shirt, including 3 front views (Split, Loaf, and Air Cooled
Vanagon), and 6 side views, including a splitty camper, crew cab, sunroof
deluxe and panel van, and a late model single cab and pop-top.
I haven't discussed colors with the printer yet, but there will be at least 3
different colors, plus black and white.
Across the top of the shirt will be the word
NETVERGNU:GEN,
which is bastardized German for interNET pleasure. It's a takeoff from the
Fahrvergnu:gen VW campaign a few years ago.
The officially sanctioned www.type2_dot_com and www.vintagebus_dot_com addresses will
also be on the front of the shirt.
As a new feature, I'm going to offer the opportunity for all the early orderers
to have their email address or website address listed on the back of the shirt,
for a couple extra bucks. This will ONLY be possible for the early orderers.
obviously, once the shirts are printed, I can't add your name or address.

I am also doing a new batch of the Air Cooled Search & Rescue Team shirts.
Those went really fast, and are a great design, featuring a fireman's type
logo, with a VW logo in the center, and a picture of a splitty and early
Beetle, with the words "Protect" and "Save" on it. Lots of people have seen
this design, and can attest to it's desirability.

Finally, I'm doing a shirt specifically for the double and single cab owners
out there. Since VW trucks are super cool, and if you have one, you know you
get twice as many comments as bus drivers, I wanted to have a shirt just for
trucks.
It will have side views of a splitty double cab, and late model Single cab, and
the word "Truckin'" above that.
