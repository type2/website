sounds like a 2/3 brake band slip or servo problem, make sure you have
sufficient ATF in the tranny, remember it is checked with engine warm and
with tranny in N.  put on parking brake then slowly shift thru all the
gears P-R-N-D-2-1-2-D-N-R-P-R-N then check......should be between the
marks.
