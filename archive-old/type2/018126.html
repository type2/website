Kevin: 
You need new rubber hoses. They deteriorate from the inside and collapse
plugging the return flow of fluid. They will pass fluid one way but the
pressure to apply is MUCH greater than that to retract.
Might as well do the rears, too. Take the line nuts apart verrry
carefully because the steel lines tend to rust into the nuts.
Hope that helps.
