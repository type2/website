Chad,

Sounds like the clutch might be out of need adjustment.  Usually a person
can feel the clutch engage with their foot, and if it isn't then the clutch
might be the issue, either worn out, poor adjustment (cable could have
streched) or there is oil in that area and no traction.  do you see oil
under the car (more the usual?)

Or, if the exhausted isn't bad (black smoke) and the engine still starts
and runs it could not have any power from the distributor settings (timing),
or value settings.  However these usually don't go out graduatly along a
highway.
