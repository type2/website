OK. So I started her up this morning and started spraying carb cleaner
until I isolated acrack in the manifold! The rubber "boots" are fine. Doesn't matter how
much I spray them, theRPM remains the same. But that crack in the manifold...

I pulled the carb and manifold.

the manifold as good asI can. Used a file to roughen up the surface and applied a liberal coat of
JB Weld over the areaof the crack.

and polished it some. I looked for identifying marks but could find none.
