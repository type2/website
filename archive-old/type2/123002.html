I dont know how became the self appointed gas tank ventilation guru but
before we go any further what engine are we talking about? When ever you
post a question to the list it is imperative that you give as much detail
as possible or necessary about your bus, year, engine, modifications from
stock as well as where you are located so volks close by can help.

There should be 3 hoses we are discussing here. Two are about 1/2" in
diameter and the third is vacuum hose size. The first big hose runs from a
port on the fan housing which air blows out of while the motor is running,
to the canister. The next big hose runs from the canister to your air
filter. If you are going to run this system teed into you crankcase vent I
would install one way valves in the hoses try a marine supply store
plumbing section if FLAPS can't help. The third smaller hose runs from the
t in the tank vents to the canister.
