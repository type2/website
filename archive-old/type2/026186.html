Having recently parted out a Bus (salvaging the gas tank) I would have to
say that the easiest way to replace the hoses are with the engine out and
the access plate removed.  Your problem may, in fact, be a leaking vent hose
rather than the filler hose.  The filler hose is best tackled by removing
the tank.  Then you can slip the new one on as you put the tank back in
place.  By the way, once you think you have all the screws off of the access
plate (and still can't force it off), look underneath as there are more
screws facing the ground.
