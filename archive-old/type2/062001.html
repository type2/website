> jonathan '74 RetroWestie (Ru/\/\ba)I don't know why you would bother with a govenor, unless you plan on 
racing your rig. If you are concerned with overrevving, I'd spend the 
money on a tach.

In '65, VW equipped their vans w/ a cheap governor, to prevent 
overrevving while the engine was in break-in. It consisted of a spring 
in the rotor. When RPMs got above a certain point, it would short 
(open?) the rotor, and you couldn't get the engine above the "safe" 
operating speed.

After 30 years, mom still complains about the ticket she got in our 
Deluxe. She says she was in 3rd gear, and there's no way she could have 
been going faster than the posted 35 mph.
