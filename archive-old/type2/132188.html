I've noticed you can get butt connectors that are heatshrinkable with a
inner middle section of solder. Doesn't seem like a huge time saver or
improvement but I thought I'd mention it anyway.

If the goal is to have the original look you can't beat non-insulated
terminals and a roll over crimper. I redid all the electrics in my engine
compartment that way while the engine was out to chase away any future
electrical gremlins.
