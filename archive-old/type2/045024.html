> Just to be clear, the 210 and 215mm flywheels and clutches will fit inside
all 72-79 bellhousings.  The 76-79 flywheels and clutches will only fit the
76-79 bellhousings.  I have heard that you can do some grinding on the 72-75
bellhousings and make the later flywheel and clutch fit, but I have never
tried it.  You can also bolt a 72-75 bellhousing to a 76-79 tranny with no
problems.  I am fairly sure that the starters are specific to the
bellhousings.


The starters are specific to the flywheel. The teeth on the 210-215 are in a
different spot than a 228. You have to use the longer starter drive pinion
on a 228 flywheel. The starter for the automatic flex disc/ring gear is
self-supporting and will work on the 210/215 flywheels but will not work on
the 228.

I believe he means to use the 72-74 muffler ON the 79 heater boxes. This
will work ONLY if it is a 79 Federal version exhaust. The 72-74 heater boxes
[except 74 w/EGR] and the 79 Fed boxes are the same except for the exhaust
