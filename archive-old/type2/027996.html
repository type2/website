Harry, the "certs" are intended. They will be in your rebuild kit. You didn't need to remove them to split the case.

I've just recently split my first GD ('77 2.0) case and I found it had many more case parting bolts and nuts than the earlier style cases. Keep looking carefully under the gook and gunk. Try next to the # 3 cylinder in the back; there's one very well-hidden nut in there. Once you get all the fasteners, the case will split just about as easily as the Type 1 engine.
