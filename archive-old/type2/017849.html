H Steven Dolan wrote:
 
 I don't know of a full width bed that is stock for the 68-71 series
(tho with VW's anything is possible and Chris may be on to something). 
OTOH (on the other hand) I *know* that the standard settup for 74 and 75
westies included a full width Z-bed, so if you want one, those are the
years to look for.  The one thing I advise *strongly* against is going
to the 68-73 westy settup.  The stock settup for those years includes 
full-height cabinet behind the passenger seat allong the reare quarter.
this cabinet creates a *huge* blind spot that IMHO is dead flat unsafe. 
I have a '73 and call it the "suicide cabinet".
