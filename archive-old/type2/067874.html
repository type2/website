Anton,

The easy way to tell if you have a 1500 or a 1600 built on a 1500 case is
to look at the intake manifold.  If it is a single port (one continuous
piece of pipe that comes up off the 1-3 head, goes over to the 2-4 head
with a "T" in the middle for the carb), then it is probably a 1500.  If
you have a dual port (aluminum casting on the 1-3 head, goes up to a
rubber gasket (aka boot) that connects it to the center tube (carb T's in
here) and over to another gasket and into another aluminum casting down to
the 2-4 head) then it is a 1600 no matter what the code on the block.
Many 1500 cases were rebuilt as 1600's because it was cheeper to get 1600
jugsa and heads than 1500's.  Economies of scale.
