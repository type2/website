I am just using a multi-meter for now as I do not have the O2 sensor permanently

cost around $150 for a universal type sensor (used on the 95 Honda Civic and
others). One site for a nice build it yourself article is
http://www.techedge_dot_com.au/vehicle/wbo2/default.htm .

it up) still seems like it will work (can hold 20 inHg of vacuum).
