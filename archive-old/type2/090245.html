Ive got the impression you have a 1600 with a stock sender.
Stock sender should be off with just a few psi.
If it was me I would have checked the pressure with a gauge and replaced
the sender if it was out of spec. If its correct and the engine is tired I
would have used a heavier oil and possibly upped the idle a tad.
Stock senders should all be about the same. the VDO dual senders come on
at a higher pressure like 7 psi I think. My dual sender used to
blink with my old very tired engine but never now.
