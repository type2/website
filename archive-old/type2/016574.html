OK, here's my take.  I suspect the piston is also burnt and will need
replaced.  a set of jugs is fairly cheap, and putting the in is quicker
than rings.  Also, at least lap in the valves on both heads.  If you're
in big financial difficulty, get a used, similar jug for that #4 and
swap it in, but lap the valves, or you you wil probably be sorry because
one will begin to leak and it goes downhill from there.  Valve spring
compressor is cheap to rent/buy and you can do the valves in about 1/2
hour.  You also have a possibility that one or more valves are damaged,
so a couple of spares might be in order.
