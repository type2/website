Steve your hands must be small :-)

Since mine are so big the only way I could get the washer nozzles off was
from the front, the only bad thing is you will BREAK them upon removal
-( However if someone from the rear can push in on the tabs you might get
lucky?

The pattern of the metal where the nozzles snap into are like a +, you just
grab them from the front and twist 90 degrees as stated, however when you do
this from the front, you "snap" the retaining portion of the plastic.

The ones on my bus where badly oxidized and cracked so I didn't worry about
breaking them, however I think the originals where gray, the new ones I
bought are Black.

Hope this helps if you can't get to the tabs from the back!
