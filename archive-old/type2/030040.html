As I recall, there were some severe safety concerns about the "collapsable
spare" tires, such as some of the exploded upon inflation killing their
owners!  If this is the type of tire you are referring to, I doubt that
the intervening years has made the rubber any stronger.

OTOH, if you're talking about the high-pressure temporary spares, they
work fine, but only on smooth hard roads.  If you get off the beaten path,
a full-size spare is a must.  I often carry two.
