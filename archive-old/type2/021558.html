Oh yeah, and download the bosch FI test manual and go by those
instructions AFTER you've tested your ignition system per bentley.  You
might also have bad resistors, bad connections to/from the
computer/wiring harness, etc, or stuff like that.. but first check the
ignition and make sure you got a spark and that all the seals are nice
and neat and fuel's flowing well.  Then hit up the FI tests.  If you go
through all the ignition and FI tests, you'll learn alot and have a nice
running engine...
