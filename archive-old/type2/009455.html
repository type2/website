Greg:

We have found that 009 distributors and 34 PICT carbs don't seem to work
well together.  Most VWs suffer from an off-idle stumble and other
performance problems with this combination.  Using either a stock dual
advance distributer or switching to a 30 PICT carb seems to greatly improve
drivability. Make sure that the stock distributer you put in is a Dual
advance unit.

You didn't mention what "mild performance" cam was put in there.  If it too
much of a performance cam, that can cause a crazy idle.
