If you're going to start sorting out part numbers and pistons you need
Tom Wilson's book, How To Rebuild Your VW Air Cooled Engine.
It has all of the interchange info.
Briefly:
Most 2 L VW CV pistons are dish type.
Porsche are slight dome dish type depending on year, uses a different
cam, smaller lighter flywheel, different heads w sodium filled exhaust
valves, different distributor.
There is a distinct difference in the two Type IV engines if you got two
on the same table.
