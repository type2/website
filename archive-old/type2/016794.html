You may be "lucky" and have a problem I have encountered.  Sounds
exactly like it.  In my case, the clutch center became detached from the
rest of the disk, the part with the springs on it "parted out" and there
was no change in the feel of the clutch, just couldn't get any movement
out of the bus, even with "speed shift" techniques.  It went nicely into
all gears once stopped, but just wouldn't "go".  A simple replacement of
the clutch disk did it.  Since you say you  have a new clutch, it
springs right to mind (always check the last thing you fixed is a mantra
of us professional troubleshooters.)
