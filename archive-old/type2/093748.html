Hi Glen,

I assume you mean CV boots?
If so then the answer is that there is no such thing as 'quick boots' for a VW T2.
On a T2 the CV boot rotates so a quick boot with its unbalenced weight of the line of bolts up the side would soon cause damage to rotating parts.
