If the fabric looks sound (not rotting or especially weak--if you can
poke your fingers though or easily tear the fabric, ya don'twanna try
this) get a large spray bottle and as you (or someone helping you) is
raising the top, dampen the fabric evenly all over untill you can
CAREFULLY get the top all the way up.  I would moisten further when
locked inplace and then let dry.  The top, now, with luck, should go up
and down easily.  you could also moisten the fabric with a dishtowel and
water if you like, just try to get it evenly wet.

I hope this helps,
