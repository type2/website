Subject: Re: [T2] '76 VW Stalls _at_ start-uo

The thermal time switch is only for turning on the cold start valve
when cranking.

Your aux air regulator prevents stalling when cold by increasing
throttle. It this is not working, it could be causing your problem.

If you have anything disconnected or removed, that could affect function.

If you caused a fine crack at one of the vacuum hose junctions, it may
leak cold and grab or seal when it warms up.
