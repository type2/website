the 73 bus has a vacuum shift tranny.  the vacuum shifter can be adjusted
maybe that is what he means, but I would suspect a vacuum leak rather than
a problem with the vacuum valve.

this is an odd ball tranny but can be replaced with a late model tranny
(75-79) without too much hassle.

if it starts off from a stop without phutzing with the shifter, try it on
a hill....then I would wonder about it.._dot_can you feel it shift, 1-2  and
2-3...is it the downshift that it is not doing???

the more common problem is upshifting not downshifting...sit at stop on a
hill, put it in drive, basically give it solid gas...you should get the
1-2 upshift around 15-20mph  and the 2-3 upshift around 35-45 don't listen
to the tranny but rather listen to the engine RPMs' the VW tranny can be
very deceptive in it's shifting.  because it is a planetary drive it
shifts quite smoothly
