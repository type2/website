Subject: Re: [T2] torque wrench

Do it yourself.  Clamp the square drive in a vise, hang a known weight at
the handle end.  Multiply the weight by the distance from the drive to the
handle and compare the value to the reading on the wrench.  +/- 5% is
normal for new wrenches.
