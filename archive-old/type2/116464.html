  There is a great VW shop in Frankfurt, I don't remember the name but he
has a lot of cool parts. I am sure if you ask around someone will tell you
where the vintage VW parts shop is at. If you find it see if he has any NOS
steering column locks or cab divider glass and seals left.
   Another hint is to take a list of part numbers and go to the dealers
parts department ( there is a dealer on about every street corner) you will
be amazed at how many NOS parts the dealers still have. clue is don't go in
as an arrogant American, befriend the parts guy and they will be very
helpful.
