Subject: Re: [T2] engine stands

I don't think the bench mounted ones would even come close for a type 4
engine, it seems barely adequate for a short block type 1. I finally did
what a few others have done which is to use the bell housing off a tranny
and mount it to a 1 ton V8 engine stand. That gives me 3 stands as I also
have the bench mount stand as well as a VW dealership stand.

For working alone or when friends vanish, the best money I ever spent was
for a hydraulic lift table. For me it goes like this. Pull engine on trolly
jack, slide engine over onto lift table, roll table over to floor mounted
engine stand and lift engine into place. My back is so much happier.
