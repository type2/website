NO  a 211 Q is for a 71 1600 upright engine bus. Wrong!
Your distributor should be a 021 905 205E and should have a dual vacuum unit 
on it.


>Also, my single Webber progressive setup has a vacuum port on the manifold.
>  Would this be the correct place to tap the vacuum if I bought and used
>this distributor.

NO  it would be correct for the inner nipple, retard side, of the 021 E dual 
unit. The outer nipple, advance side, has to go a vacuum port above the 
throttle plate = no vacuum at idle.
