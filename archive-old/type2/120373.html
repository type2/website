Just a reminder . . .

Just a quick note to remind everyone know that the
Northwest VW Club's Spring Meet is coming up fast! 
Actually a lot faster than before since Spring Meet
XVIIII 2004 will be one month earlier this year!!
Please let all your friends know about the date
change!!  We don't want anyone left behind.

Spring Meet XIX 2004
Sunday, March 28, 2004
Western Washington Fairgrounds
Puyallup, WA

Spring Meet Pre-Registration is now Open!!  You may
Pre-Register for the event on our website
http://www.northwestvwclub_dot_org/ Please be sure to read
the Pre-Registration information carefully. There are
major changes this year.  We have added an extra
incentive to Pre-Register this year. Pre-Registrations
received before March 19 will be allowed to Setup on
Saturday Evening March 27th. Those not Pre-Registered
before March 19th will be required to setup Sunday
Morning, March 28. No Exceptions. (Mailed
Pre-Registrations complete with payment must be
postmarked by March 19, 2004)

Pre-Registration Deadlines

Pre-Registration closes March 19th
All Registrations received after March 19th will be
considered Day of Show Registrations.
Additional $5.00 per item for Day of Show
Registrations.

To reserve your same Swap or Dealer Spaces as last
year:
Registration and payment postmarked and received by
February 28, 2004.  (Heads Up!  Deadline in Two
Weeks!)
To request changes to your Swap or Dealer Spaces:
Registration and payment postmarked and received by
March 14, 2004.
All other Pre-Registrations:
Registration and payment postmarked and received by
March 19, 2004

How to Pre-Register
You may Pre-Register by either using the Online
Pre-Registration Form or by downloading, printing and
mailing the Adobe PDF Registration Form from our
website. Payment may either be mailed or sent through
PayPal.
Complete Pre-Registration information including the
Downloadable and Online Registration forms are on our
website, http://www.northwestvwclub_dot_org/

Thank you for your past patronage and we look forward
to seeing you again at Spring Meet,
