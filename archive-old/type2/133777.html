Anyone try Seafoam?
It's a solvent type spray and/or liquid that you allow to get sucked
up into the motor (until it stalls!) and let sit in the hot
cylinders.

After you let it sit awhile, you fire it back up and there is
supposed to be a tremendous amount of sooty smoke that comes pouring
out of the tail pipe-  and after awhile things are supposed to be
right as rain.
