As long as the gear oil you bought is GL 4 you should be OK. Do NOT use it
if it says GL 4/5, GL 5, or GL 3-4-5. All Valvoline gear oil I have seen has
NOT been straight GL 4. The most common brand of GL 4 is Sta Lube. Sta Lube
says GL 4 on the front label.
