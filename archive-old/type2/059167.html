I replaced the floor in my camper (born on date is May '73) about
12 years ago.  The icebox drain hose found a way to pour water
betw the steel and wooden flooring.

The original floor consisted of a few hardwood blocks,about 2x3x1/2"
in the low spots of the steel floor.  Then a sheet of 1/4" masonite
followed by 1/2" plywood.  Grey linoleum with a pebble finish on
top of all that.  The wooden 'furniture' holds the floor down.
