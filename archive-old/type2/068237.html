thingermajigger.  It would slip before it was "tight enough".  I'd
look for the 12-point one.  Unless your CV is already toasted, its
probably going to last the week it would take to get a local tool shop
to order you one.  (I say probably with all the knowledge and
confidence of someone who still looks over the manual when doing a
valve adjustment, so have some salt with this advice:)
