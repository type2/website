Run in torque double hmmmm!  Torque preload is calculated in to the factory
spec given termed tightening torque.  Don't believe it?  Try torquing a T1
engine strainer cap nut (6 ft lbs) or a T4 strainer nut (10 ft lb) with some
"other" method.  You'll be wondering why something broke.

http://www.torqueleader_dot_com/specify.htm gives you the math if you really
want to see how engineers figure out the spec. It also includes the
variables to the equation for oiled fasteners.
