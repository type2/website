>My real question is when I
>installed the sensor I hooked up the (heat sensor wire) the to wires going
>to the gague, but I ran out of wire so I added about 2-3 feet of wire to

>orignal wire that came with the VDO gague. Will adding this wire give me

If you add it at the gauge end, it shouldn't change the readings
appreciably as long as there is no resistance in the wire you added.

>I don't know why it runs hot...the motor is rebulit, new

I think others have posted similar temps, which is why I said it doesn't
sound excessive.  What are your oil temps?  That would tell you something
about whether your engine is really running hot or not.

Since you have a FI motor with catalyst, you might also check that your cat
is not plugged, and that you are not running lean (check the spark plugs).
