You'll need to get the packed dimensions and weight, obviously.  My most 
recent international parcel went via BAX.  They had the best rates of anyone 
going from the US to Australia. $150 for a 49lb dimensional weight package.

going down under, so you might get lucky with them.  UPS is usually my last 
choice for international shipments.
