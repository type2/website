Better than a flywheel lock, just get a 4 or 5 foot length of 1-1/2 angle
or similar, drill two holes to match the cluth bolts (not opposite, just
skip one bolt), bolt it on, and have at it.  You won't have to stabilize
the engine and you won't risk breaking the case flange.
