It sounds like you have a mid 70's bug Motorola alternator.

Does it have a odd shaped M in a circle on the edge near the pulley?
Does it have a white ceramic section on top held down with four screws with 3 terminals in the middle that
resembles the back of a headlight?

If you answered YES to both of those questions...... you HAVE to find the bug that alternator came of and get
the voltage regulator under the left side of the rear seat and the harness between it and the alternator.
