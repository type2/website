FYI: In case you don't know your engine code or displacement, here it is.

8/67 through 7/70: B
8/70 through 7/71: AE
Manual trans from 8/71 through 7/73: CB
Auto trans from 8/72 through 7/73: CD
Manual trans from 8/73 through 7/74: AW
Auto trans from 8/73 through 7/74: AW
Fuel injection from 8/74: ED
Fuel Injection from 8/75 (except 79 and later Calif.):GD
California Fuel Injection from 8/78: GE

Piston displacement: B, AE: 1584cc
                     CB, CD: 1679cc
                     AW, ED: 1795cc
                     GD, GE: 1970cc
