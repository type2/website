On Sun, 20 Aug 2000 MRJRM12_at_aol_dot_com wrote:

> I installed the Hella dual battery kit exactly as per instructions, and it 
> worked great for a week, then I noticed aux batttery wasn't charging.  The 
> 15A fuse attached to the relay was blown.

Is this fuse in the hot wire to the aux battery?  If the aux battery is
discharged and the alternator putting out like it should, then the wire
could see more than 15A.  I'd suggest a 30A fuse if the wire is 10ga or
better.  Even better, there are circuit breakers that come in small
packages that will reset themselves if tripped.
