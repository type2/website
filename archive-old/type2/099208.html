Hi Duke, it's my time to help now!
The 6 side Allens screw are OEM VW, the 12 point Torque screws are part
of a replacement boot kit, so that means that your axles were rebuilt
before!

A standard 6 point Allens hex wrench wiil fit and could get them loose
but you could also stripped them, 6mm 12-point HEX wrench are available
for cheap, even if you remove the bolts with a vise-grip (easy trick on
the Bay but not on the Vanagon) you won't be able to screw them back
properly without the right 12 point socket.

I bought a 6-size torx socket kit for $30 (5,6,7,8,9 and 10mm)
Check my site for more info on rebuilding the rear Axle.

week. (my 7th time!!!)
