Here is a lesson in welding 101---

For a optimo welding surface all the POR, paint, primer, rust,
corrosion--whatever-- must be ground clean as a whistle---
Makes the welding much easier, as well as not contaminating the weld
with garbage--

You can burn into garbage---but it is a better idea to clean as much as
possible where you can get to----then  run the weld into the ca-ca if
necessary---at least the majority of the weld will be clean and strong--
