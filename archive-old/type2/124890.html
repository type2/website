It is possible to remove the head without pulling the engine but it's a 
bear. I would consider pulling the engine if you CONFIRM that there is 
internal damage. I would keep testing first before pulling the engine for no 
reason.

Unfortunately the 79 head on your bus has square port heads, and the 78 will 
have the oval heads. This means that you would have to swap both heads and 
the heat exchangers/exhaust too.

You can pull the heads with wrenches, but to fix anything like a valve, etc. 
you would really need to take it to a machine shop. Other things you can do 
yourself, but let's cross that bridge if we get there.

I really think you should check compression and recheck all the other items 
before declaring a bad head or internal damage.
