William,

According to everyone that I have talked with there is no way to get the ball 
joints out without removing the torsion arms -- even on a bug. It takes a lot 
of pressure with an hydraulic press.

Check out my experience at http://coyote.accessnv_dot_com/wayback/b_joint.html

My choice regarding the torsion arms was to order the set of arms/ball joints 
from Wolfgang. I had difficulty finding a place with the proper equipment to 
R&R the ball joints. The fittings that drive the ball joint in & out are 
different from a bug. All the VW shops only did bugs.
