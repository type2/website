The switches get air bubbles in them and the bubbles
cannot escape. Sometimes you can loosen the switch and
bleed it in that fashion, but often not. Some pre-fill
the switch. From a highly reliable source when I
bought my new MC a few years back - if the old
switches work, use them. The new ones are very poor
and not reliable. As with anything, there are
different manufacturers I'm sure... Mine got so that I
had to push so hard on the pedal to get the
brakelights to come on, I'd darn near have tp lock the
wheels up! I added a mechanical switch activated by
the brake pedal arm. They're about $8 at FLAPS. Stlii
has not addressed imbalance or possible imbalance in
your system, but if it stops straight, what more do
you need (except for the damned light to go off!) -BaH
