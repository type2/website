Subject: RE: [T2] Rear End Noise ?

Doesn't sound like CV joints. Does sound like what my rear brakes did after
I adjusted them incorrectly due a frozen star nut. Jack up each rear wheel,
rotate forward & back and listen. Set parking brake and try to rotate each
wheel both ways. Adjust rear brakes. You might have something loose rattling
around in a brake drum back there. Most braking force is provided by the
front brakes, so you might have no action on one rear drum and not notice
driving. But needs fixin' obviously...

Also look for things hanging loose underneath, and check clutch cable
condition and adjustment.
