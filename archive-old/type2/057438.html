    Well, I just had to go look at a fan housing again -- and for the
life of me I can't see where you get that idea. First off, the flap on
the left is mounted on the bottom, and closes upward, whereas the right
flap is mounted at the top, and closes downwards. When both are fully
open, they don't do a cotton-pickin' thing to direct airflow anywhere.
And if they did, it would be very different for each side, which would
promote "uneven cooling = head warpage", etc.
    What it does look like, however, is that the left flap blocks a lot
of the air going to the oil cooler when it's fully open (down), but I'll
have to wait until tomorrow to stick the other parts together to fully
assess what happens there.
