The 1st number 9.5 or 10 is the width of the belt at its widest point in mm.

The 2nd number is the length of the belt in mm.

No, 10 x 890 does not mean it is a 900mm belt less 10mm.

If and only if, you use a wider belt and you have the shims to do it, then you will need a longer belt. Not a
shorter one.
