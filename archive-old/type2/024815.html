I think the pancake motors, or at least the 2L motors in the late bays used a 
more heavy duty starter, but I am not sure.   There are a number of 
variations on the upright 1600 type 12V starter, some start with 311 or 113 
on the part number.  The one I have in the 61 is real short, I was told that 
if it was longer the reserve tap stuff would hit the starter (?) .  But yes 
there is wide interchangability .  often when I get a starter (or some other 
bits) I say it is for a 69 beetle, as that makes the parts people have an 
easier time looking things up.

have one in my bug and one in my camper.
