> Can someone tell me how I'd go about finding TDC assuming the pulley has
NO markings on it whatsoever.

Eastwood company makes a whistle that screws into your spark plug hole.  As
the piston hits tdc, the whistle stops.  No need to pull the valve covers,
etc.  The whistle only blows when the piston is on the compression stroke,
so you won't be 180 degrees off.

Eastwood part no.  46048 Top Dead Center Indicator          21.99

 Eastwood Company has a website.  Just search Yahoo.
