This reminds me of time I poured a can of STP fuel injector cleaner into the tank of my 1972 Squareback in 1983:  The cleaner worked great - in fact it dissolved so much crude, etc in the gas tank and lines that it clogged TWO injectors!  I remember being stuck during troubleshooting because I had a "loping" idle and I would pull off one spark plug wire from the distributor and nothing would happen!  I pulled off another (it just coincidentally happened to be both the plug wires that were connected to the two injectors that were clogged) and nothing changed!  I didn't proceed any further, just dropped the car off at the VW dealers.  Two hundred dollars (injectors = $65 ea plus labor) later I was back on the road.
