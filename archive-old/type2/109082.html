Your 73 should have a dual vacuum unit distributor, correct?

The key to setting the timing at 5 after is 1) it has to be at idle and 2) you HAVE to have vacuum at idle on
the retard side of the vacuum unit, connected AND working.

Do you have vacuum at idle on the retard [inner] nipple?
If yes, when you disconnnect the line, does the idle speed up?

If you answered NO to either of these questions, you CANNOT set it at 5 after and expect it to run correctly.
