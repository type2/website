Subject: Re: [T2] electric fuel pumps

You are making sure that the electric fuel pumps are for carbs, right? There 
are 2-4 or so psi pumps for carbs, and then there are 35 psi pumps for fuel 
injected engines.   Be sure you don't have one of those. The float needle on 
your carb can handle the lower pressure when in good shape, it may also be 
getting tired.
I don't like electric pumps, I don't trust them. They work for a while and 
then die, as you've seen. For sure I'd carry a spare as you did, good idea.

If your bus is a 1700, it may still have a mechanical pump down there. 
They're hard to change and expensive, but probably more reliable than the 
aftermarket electric cheapos. I do run stock mechanical on my bus.
