Jeremy,

On the right (RIR) side of the tranny there should be a small round unit
with a opening that a hose is attached to (or should be attached to.)  That
is the vacuum line that goes to ....well, originally it went to a vacuum
port on the original dual carbs.   Since you now have a single carb, the
manifold should have a port on it (usually under the carb) to attach the
tranny vacuum line and the brake booster line to.

Take a look in the archives for info on this very common conversion. Also
think about a copy of the Bentley book.

Good luck.  Feel free to p-mail me regarding this (i.e. the auto tranny);
As for your other questions, I'm sorry but I don't know what the bendix is.
Weber is the most common single carb mentioned on the list.   As for the
brakes, well, I think you'll be rebuilding them after you get a core from a
junker.
