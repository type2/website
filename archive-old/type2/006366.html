This is a fairly common phenomenon.  Radial tires' belts will sometimes
acquire a "set", for no apparent reason, and no amount of alignment fiddling
will resolve the "pull".

Most of the time swapping front tires from side to side will take care of
the "pull", but in some situations there's nothing to do but scrap the
offending tire and start fresh.
