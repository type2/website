heres what i use so that i dont lose my parking brake:

i took a 4" by 4" block of wood and put a fence staple in one end, and
then tied a rope to it.  tied the other end to the bulkhead under the
drivers seat.  

to set parking brake:  put block behind wheel with rope running back into
the van and shut the door.

when leaving:  creep ahead (or back) to release pressure, pull rope and
block back into van.  

not the best solution, but it works.  at least until the day when i
replace my ebrake cables.
