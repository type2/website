Vacuum hoses. Start with the one on the back driver's side of the air
exchange/plenum/whatever-you-call-that box in the middle. There's a T
connector there that plugs into the side of the box and then into 2 big
hoses, including the one that goes to the brake booster. I've had to
replace that T with a copper plumber's fitting. The geometry of the
connections caused this aging rubber connector to break apart.

I'm pretty confident you'll find your problem in the vacuum connections or
hoses.
