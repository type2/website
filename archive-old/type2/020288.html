[...] I've had this problem several times in the past, especially with radials.
They tend to develop a "set", as a result of bad alignment or other suspension
problems, and will not roll straight after that.  New tires (after correcting
the alignment problem) are the best way to correct the situation, although
moving the problem to the rear makes it less noticable, as you found out.
