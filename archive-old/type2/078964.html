The pictures of the gas tank removal job I did yesterday on my 78

To all of those that replied with hints about this job thanks!
To all of those that warned me when I got this bus and it had

This is *after* it sat for 3 weeks last summer with a gallon of lacquer
thinner in it....and it still looked like this.
