>The following racing series run alcohol: the Indy Racing League (fuel
>injection, no turbos), CART (FI, turbo in 2001 changing to non-turbo in the
>next few years), World of Outlaws (carbs), Alcohol Funny Cars and Dragsters
>(not sure what they use for induction) and Monster Trucks (carbs with
>superchargers, IIRC). Monster trucks run alcohol because most monster truck
>races are held indoors, and alcohol exhaust is less likely to kill the
>spectators than gasoline exhaust. I'd say you could run either carb or FI.
>
>One thing I know you have to worry about is the construction of your
>induction system. Alcohol will dissolve parts of some carbs; get a copy of
>Stock Car Racing magazine and you'll find carbs specially set up for alcohol
>racing series. These won't dissolve. (These are also Holley 650cfm
>Dominators.
