> [..]As per my previous post, I've been using a bug (2 outlet w/ peashooters)
> muffler on my 71 westy for the entire 16 years I've owned it.  I never could
> understand the reason for the bus (1 outlet) muffler, but the exhaust in the

I've done the same, although I now have the "proper" bus muffler.  I've
never noted any exhaust fumes at all, nor have I noted any change in
power.  The exhaust tip of the bus muffler is really teeny, so it would be
logical to have a bit more back pressure.  I always thought that this was
why the bus engine was rated slightly lower in HP than the bug engines.

The bug muffler also helps ground clearance behid the left rear wheel.  I
scrape a lot more with the bus exhaust.
