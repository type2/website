current engine when rebuilt started it up and immediately shut it down,
sounded like it was going to explode.  turns out somehow one of those
sheet metal washers got into an intake runner, pulled the head, removed
the remains of the washer, thank goodness it was soft alum and not steel.
checked the piston and head very closely for damage and other than some
marks on the piston top all was well, put engine back together and it is
still running in my van about 30K later.
