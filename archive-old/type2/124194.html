>Will this stand work for a type 4 motor????
http://www.harborfreight_dot_com/cpi/ctaf/Displayitem.taf?itemnumber=32915

I think you need a bit heavier stand. The stand in the picture a nice wide
backside with a wide beam with two straight rolling wheels; but the "front"
end only has <one> point and is therefore way too easy to tip over sideways.
I have a similar el cheapo HF stand, but my stand has two wheels on the front
with a wide beam that is much harder to tip. I have rebuilt Type IV engines
on my stand, but I sure wouldn't try to start an engine on this stand.  I
couldn't just directly bolt on the engine. I had to make some "adapters" out
of 3/8" bar stock to mount against the engine and then get a bunch of short
pipes and long bolts to adapt the stand mount to fit the engine. I leave off
the flywheel because I can't get it through the mounts.
