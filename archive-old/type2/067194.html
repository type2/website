> Hi Jonathan,
> 
> I've got mine in the front wall (5.25's).  In mine there's an
> under last rear seat area with a cardboard wall along the bottom.
> That's where I'll put mine.
> 
> BTW, the speakers will sound better if they are in "phase".
> 
> 1) Hook up one speaker, leave the wires to the 2nd unhooked.
> 
> 2) Put on an album with a lotta bass.  Try the 2nd speaker wires
>    both ways.  When the phase is right, the speakers will boom
>    the bass *together* in a pulse effect.  You'll also hear it,
>    and, if loud enough - you'll feel it !
> 
> As for the rears in yours, after phasing, (with lots of wire), move
> 'em around while playing some music.  Sit in front and listen.  There
> will be 2 spots where you'll like them most.  This is the "Zen" method.
> 
> Hope This Helps,
> 
> John A.
> '71 Blunderbus
