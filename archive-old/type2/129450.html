Hi Pete,

I would suggest you stick with the FI troubleshooting procedures in the 
Bentley Manual. If you don't have one of these it is the BEST 
investment a poor man can make in their bus. There is also an FI manual 
available at Richard Atwell's site at http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell.

According to the Bosch FI manual, this is what you should do:

1. Check electrical components: Distributor, coil, plugs, wires, dwell 
and timing.

2. Check valves, compression and oil pressure

3. Check all hoses for proper connection and good condition.

4. Check fuel pressure.

5. Check cold start valve & TTS

6. Check Aux Air inlet valve

7. Check Air Flow Meter


The trick is to NOT SKIP stuff just because you "know" it's OK. Verify 
everything in proper order.
