Subject: New starter in - Thanks all!

Earlier, I described my wierd new starter and asked about putting in the new
bushing it came with. No one had any info on the starter's extra spade
connector, but as for the bushing, some said definately put it in and some
said don't bother, it's too hard and not necessary.  

Well, I decided to replace it just to be sure.  I thought it would be REAL
hard, or that maybe I'd even have to take the engine out to drive out the
bushing.  But several people came up with the idea of using a bolt, or
better yet a tap to screw into the bushing and hopefully pull it out.  At
first I didn't think it was gonna pull out, but then half of it broke and
came out.  Then the other half was easy. Great idea!  
Just when I start to freak out thinking I've gotten into something over my
head, clear minds on this list come up with good ideas for a managable way
to do it.  This time, thanks go to Thom, Paul, Jeff, Josh, and Sean.
