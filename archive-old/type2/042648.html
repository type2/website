 I have a theory that one source of the confusion is that VW changed the way
they set up the gearing between upright and pancake engined busses as the 
torque from the pancakes increased. Initially (~72-73) the pancake engines
had a higher top gear and the same final drive as the uprights but then as
the 091 transmission was introduced and the low end torque of the 2.0l 
pancake increased they simply used a different final drive to "regear" the
091
for the different engines (or for "mountain ratios").
