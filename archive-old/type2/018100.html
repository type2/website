I love scambling around in scrapyards and being a fan of comfort over
authenticity (saving that for when I win the lottery) I've just bought a set of

scrapper and you're away!

Volvos also have knockout gear sticks (stainless steel), window winders and
billet door catches. I also got a mint condition Motorola radio from a Talbot
Alpine for £5, oh, and I also salvaged a load of thick french-polished walnut
door and dash panels from a 2.5lt Rover, they are going to trim up the 'Vert
instead of Karmann's crappy plastic wood dash.

Vive la difference!
