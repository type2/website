For the umpteenth time:

You have two sources of vacuum. Ported and manifold.
Ported vacuum appears when you open the throttle. Ported vacuum is used on 
the vacuum advance and EEC purge valve on theback of the air cleaner. Ported 
vacuum comes from the nipple on the front of the throttle housing.
Manifold vacuum appears when you are idling. It is used on the retard side 
of the dist. vacuum unit, the decel valve, and the fuel pressure regulator.

You need 2 tees hook up your 79 correctly.
>From throttle housing nipple --> tee --> vacuum advance and purge valve.
>From nipple on plenum between #1 + #2 intake runners --> tee --> decel valve 
and pressure regulator.
