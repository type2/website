David,
	I use Redline S1 Fuel Injector Additive exclusively on ALL of my
VWs.  I have tried all others(having worked in the parts biz) and it's hands
down, noticeably the best.  You may have to go to a speed shop to get it
though, most FLAPS don't carry it. As for the oil add., I have had luck with
good 'ol Marvel Mystery Oil.  It is a non-synthetic, high viscosity oil that
actually has better qualities under high temps and pressures than your motor
oil.  It buffers the oil that is in there quite well.  I will say that if
you're looking for miracles, don't add anything to a gummed-up crankcase.
Start with a fresh engine and maintain it, don't try to resurrect a dying
one!
