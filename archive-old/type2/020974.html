Beth:
No, not that hard at all. Basically, you just have to remove the oil filter,
loosen the compressor, and remove the fan. (loosen the alternator, remove the
alternator belt, remove the 3 bolts that secure the fan to the hub, wiggle
the fan out the back.) Figuring out the right belt size Might be a problem,
(just looked at the info sheet for the VPC that was in my 78, it says ZVW 219
222)
BTW, I have ALL the literature and templates that came with my VPC air
conditioner. I installed it myself back in about 1984.
I'd  be alittle concerned about this mechanics abilities if he thinks this is
a big job.
There may be many other things wrong with this system, but the belt is the
easy part.
