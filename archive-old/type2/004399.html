My suggestions, in no particular order:

1.	Check/replace the rubber boots at the manifold ends.
2.	Check that the breaker plate in the dizzy (assuming stock vac/mech dizzy)
rotates freely.
3.	Check that the vacuum advance can (assuming stock dizzy) is good.
4.	Check brake vacuum line and vacuum servo for leaks.
5.	Check carb for proper idle and choke adjustments.
