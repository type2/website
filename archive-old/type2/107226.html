Harbor Freight VOM  Item 30756-1CRB CEN-TECH $9.99 is on sale for $4.99 (in

I have a P30756. It is quite accurate because it uses the 7106 IC chip. I 
have dropped it on hard surfaces many times with out problems.

Here are some readings on a 3/4 amp (not a VW fuse) sample taken with some 
of my shop meters set on the 200 ohm range. All the meters do a good job of 
measuring this low value sample should that be of interest.
The Flukes have an extra digit over the other meters but if you are on a 
budget the CEN-TECHs at less than $20 are hard to beat.

Tektronix 2237   0.7  ohm
Sunpro CP7676    0.7  ohm


Readings were corrected for the zero off-set reading obtained with the the 
test leads connected together.
