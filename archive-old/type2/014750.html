One thing you need to be aware of in mounting the spare tire this way is
that the offset of the wheel is such that it will only mount on those tire
hangers one way. If you want to mount it with the hub cap out, you will have
to extend the bolts, or make a new mount. I am working on the new mount
because I am wanting to mount my tire hub cap out also. I happened to get a
mount from another listee that mounts to the bumper like a brush gaurd, but
is for the tire. I am going to extend the mount out to hold the tire the way
I want. This way the weight is held up by the bumper, not the nose sheet
metal. I will post some pics when its done.
