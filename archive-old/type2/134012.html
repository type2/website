shifter plates are cheap. If yours shows wear replace it. For initial
adjustment, engage 2nd gear, adjust plate so shifter lower shaft is
perpendicular to the floor. Now test shifting. If you still hit the e-brake
handle, shift the plate rearward bit by bit. Just a small movement at that
plate is 2" or more of knob movement.
