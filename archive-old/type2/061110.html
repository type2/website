You can buy the Bosch RPM Limiter Rotors for about $25.  You can choose the
cut out point at 5400, 5800, 6500, 6800, etc.  They are just a replacement
rotor that works on centrifugal force to open to contacts once a certain RPM
has been reached.  You can get 'em from the mail order houses.  Some of the
parts guys on the list might even have them.
