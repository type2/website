people that use it have no idea of the way to use it. I have some pics in
file from the last 4 years of restoring VW's alone that will show you what
i'm talking about , I have a 57 and 55 both here now that were major bondo
wagons and each had fiberglass in them to, in addition to newspaper, spray

rear corners were sculpted from bondo, as well as the drip rail and all the
skylights on the drivers side. These owners will tell you bad bondo work
will cost you in the long run. I'll be posting some pics first of July on my
pages of Kents 56, in these it will show the use of metal to metal filler
and another type of filler used to do the smoothing of the 56. i'll post a
