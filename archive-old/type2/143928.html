On Fri, 10 Jun 2005, Don Tuleja wrote:

> Change of thread direction...
>
> Reusing the metal intake manifold gaskets? Or replace with paper ones?

Don't reuse metal gaskets.  The first use flattens out the embossed
sealing ridges.

I prefer thick "paper" gaskets.  Make sure that they fit the head opening
well.  On some DP engines pre-made gaskets tend to interfere with the
cylinder head tin and need to be trimmed a bit to fit.
