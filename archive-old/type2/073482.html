At 11:39 AM -0500 9/3/00, Victor Cosby wrote:
>can anyone confirm or refute this:

>i have heard that the oil plate on the bottom of a porsche 914 engine will
>fit the later model air cooled bus engines (you know, that plate on the
>bottom with the little bump and 2 bolts), that the 914 engines came with oil
>temp senders factory installed, and that the sender hole is the same size as
>the vdo sender for their oil temp gauge (9/16" dia).

off of your dipstick in order to use the Porsche plate with the VDO sender.
I have had one on my bus for a few years now, and despite what some may
tell you I get useful data from it.
