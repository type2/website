A little info on the 78' Transporter that was in the Washington Post.  The add
said

"'78 Bus, brown, dual weber custom carbs, monster engine, body kind of rough,
$1000 obo.  202-489-7590, leave message."

The bus is in pretty good shape and the mechanic who worked on it really babied
it.  It says "body kind of rough" which is not really true.  This bus is pretty
solid looking, the only real problem is the rust under the windshield.  I am not
sure how far this has gone, no holes yet.

This bus is a good deal for those who like their bus to motor.  I'd buy it if I
had another set of hands and feet to drive with.
