Tom, your Fed vacuum unit is a single, advance only unit. Inorder for the diaphragm to move, it has to have
backside open to the atmosphere. It does this by being open to the inside of the distributor housing.

By using the Calif dual vacuum unit, you need to leave the retard side open to the atmosphere. Unless the
diaphragm ruptures, you will not be sucking crap into it.

I have been known to put a vacuum hose on it and just letting it go towards the front of the engine. Open to
the atmosphere and in a cleaner air enviroment......
