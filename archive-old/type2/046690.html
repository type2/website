Hi Joe,

Disclaimer: This is not my recommendation for a permanent fix.  This is the
bailing wire fix Joe asks for.

First:  Install a set of high performance oil system pistons and springs with
30 wt oil.

Second:  Switch to 40 wt oil and let it warm up a bit.  (Use a block heater?)

Third:  Try the high volume oil pump.

Drive very carefully and easily, staying close to home while you build a
replacement engine.
