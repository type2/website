140k on a Bus motor? You, sir, have impeccable car care skills.

The oil pressure control valves (you need a flattip screwdriver with a tip
an inch wide to get these out) are in the bottom of your engine case. I
think they're just crudded-up. Go under your Bus and look on the bottom of
your engine for the two screws that this screwdriver will fit and remove one
of them. Under it will be a very strong spring and a metal slug. Drain the
oil first. Remove one valve, clean it and the hole it went into, put it back
in (use a little screwdriver to push the slug up and down to ensure it has
free travel), and remove/clean the other one. You'll need a rich assortment
of obscenities to get these in because that spring makes it hard to do. This
should fix the problem.
