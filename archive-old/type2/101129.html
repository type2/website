The TMI set that I got for my 78 appears to be ok(haven't installed 
yet)...but!  the two pieces that go under the front seats were NOT
finished correctly and i had to send them back.

in...don't know about the fit yet.

TMI that somehow they weren't doing a very good job of quality control.
