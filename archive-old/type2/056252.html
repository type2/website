David Greenland <greenl.d_at_apple_dot_com> wrote to the List:
> My hitch is rated at 110lbs tongue weight and 1100 max towing, but that
> may be a limitation on the way it's mounted (using the rear bumper mounts).
>
> If anyone has a hitch rated higher than that, I'd like to know how it's
> mounted. (on a '75)

On my '72, it's rated for 1,200 kg max towing weight.  It consists of 
a tubular structure with brackets welded to it.  The brackets bolt to 
where the bumper mounts normally bolt, and the bumper bolts to the 
tubular stucture.  The actual "hook" bolts to the tube too, through 
the bumper.  The original bumper mounts are obsoleted.

For '73 and later, I've seen similar hitches, but with the "hook" 
welded to the tube.  These come in either 1,200 kg or 1,800 kg 
versions.
