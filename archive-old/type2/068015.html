I replaced the tank on my '81 with one from an '84.  The tube coming
off the tank to the fuel pump is identical to that on the '81.  
The fill tube on the '84 is plastic and smaller than the monstrous 
metal thing onthe '81.  Perhaps the gasket on the tank is the 
only difference there.  I liked the '84 one better and installed
it on my van.
