I do not remember what year it happened, but the latter levers are all
plastic. The early ones are metal with a rubber/plastic knob. The all
plastic ones can be replaced by the early style, but it is a little work.
You need a lever assembly out of an earlier Bus, and may have to remove one
of the extra levers (early had 4, late had 3).
