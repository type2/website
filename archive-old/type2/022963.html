At 02:50 PM 4/11/2001, Adam Stapleton wrote:
>hey all,
>
>i have a 1975 westy with a 2000cc engine, why is it that when i look for 
>replacement parts in all the catalogs parts are listed as 1800cc 1974 to 
>1975 or 2000cc 1976 to 1979.  Why does everyone act like there was not a 
>2000cc in 1975????

Because there wasn't.  Not in model year 75, which went from about 9/74 to 
8/75.  If your bus has a tag in the drivers door that says it was built in 
9/75 or later, then it is a 76 model year bus.  Confirm this by looking at 
your VIN number on the dashboard or in the engine compartment and seeing if 
it starts with "236" (the "6" means 1976 model year).
