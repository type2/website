The reliable, sustainable cruising speed for a stock '70 1600 is more like
65 MPH.  They will go faster (especially downhill!) but not for very long.
If you really need speed, you'll need to get a freeway flyer trans and a
more powerful motor, or just get a later model Bus, like maybe a Vanagon.
The '70 Westy was not really made for speed.  Enjoy!
