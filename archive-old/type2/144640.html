gasoline that had 5% diesel fuel added.  I was an mechancical engineering

and ran it through our octane test engine.  I forget the exact numbers,
but I can tell you for sure that definitely reduced octane.  Remember that
Diesel engines work via detonation, which is Bad News for gasoline
engines.
