My tire search started with what the Bentley manual recommends.

It states this for a "Delivery Van and Station Wagon from April 1972":
"185 R x 14 C"
"Correct replacement tire must bear the letter "C" immediately after the 
number 14 in the size designation-and not elswhere.  The maximum load 
Inflation pressure listed on the tire should be 55 psi."

For "Campmobile from August 1971"
"185 SR 14 Reinforced"
"Correct replacement tire must bear the word "REINFORCED".  The maximum 
Inflation pressure listed on the tire should be 40 psi."

It also states for "Campmobile from August 1971" rear tire pressures "Up to 
3/4 payload" should be 37 psi  and "Fully loaded" 40 psi.

"Delivery Van and station Wagon from April 1972" rear tire pressures "Up to 
3/4 payload" should be 40 psi  and "Fully loaded" 44 psi.
