BTW, to all ... new arch*ve feature, go to:

http://www.i405_dot_com/vwarchive/default.asp?search=IISindex

You can do a lot more complex searches and you also get ranks for best
hits.  With this screen, you can now do searches like:

  1) loaf AND NOT split AND brakes
  2) Brake** NEAR Convert**
  3) Yoko*
  4) (Yoko* AND 197*) OR (Yoko* AND 7*)

#3 has been the most requested feature, you can now search words with
wildcards!  Now you can catch all the various spellings of Yoko tires!

#2 is also very powerful.  A word with "**" at the end will match all
variations.  "Brake" will also do "Brakes".  "Convert" will also match
"Converted", "Converting" and other variations of "Convert".

#4 Shows how you can combine these features.  In this case, it looks for
messages that have both words starting with "Yoko" and "197x" or "7x".
So someone who said "Yoko" and "1973" would get a hit.
