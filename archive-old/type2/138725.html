Subject: Cylinder head temp guage report

Just opened and tested my new CHT guage. Ordered it from Aircraft Spruce &
Specialty. It's a Microflight CH001 with included sensor. For $49.95 +
shipping I think I got a useable product. Being about half the price of a VDO
guage I didn't know what to expect but I guess I am satisfied. I tested it
against 2 Thermometers on a heated block of steel. DEAD ON at 200, 300 and 400
deg. F. I am impressed. The only disapointment I have is it has no light of
its' own, but my VDO guages spill so much light behind them I don't think it
will be a problem. Also the guage feels light. But I guess with that little
meter movement it doesn't need to weigh much. For those of you who don't like
to support the Chinese Economy DON'T buy this guage. otherwise seems worth the
money invested
