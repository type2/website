Hindsight is 20/20 but next time she should just sell her car "AS IS".

She could have written a bill of sale saying something like "This car 
is being sold for parts use only.  If the new owner intends to use it

new owner."

While you are *supposed* to smog cars before selling them, the 
California DMV will let you transfer ownership without a smog 
certificate.

The new owner will have to deal with the smog stuff if they decide to 
put the car on the road.
