There was a magazine a couple of years ago called VW Custom and 
Classic.  They reviewed all of the available ignitions.  There are all 
basically the same.  It comes down to how much you want to spend.  I went 
with their top recommended pick which was electromotive-inc_dot_com.  Most of 
the faithfuls on this list disagree with my choice due to its high costs; 
however I can honestly say it is the most awesome system I have seen.  Most 
of the listees prefer the $20-$100 drop ins that require a 
distributor.  and a normal coil.
"Speed and performance cost money, How fast do you want to go?"
