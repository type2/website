Not sure what you are referring to. The 75 to 78 muffler is the easiest to remove.
All you have is a band on the right side and a compression clamp on the left end.
Here is a pic of it:   http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/page34.html

A is the muffler, D is the band, the muffler attaches to the end of E or a cat with a compression clamp.
