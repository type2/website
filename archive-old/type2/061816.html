I'm finally going to get my new bus. It's a '71 Westy, white in color,
complete with all the trimmings. Mark Dearing of Salem Imports rebuilt the
engine for me as I bought the bus in Salem and it had a blown engine. I must
say the Mark has done a fine job of putting up with the constant changes I
made as I read Bob Hoover's sermons. " Make sure the Hoover bit is  there,
balance everything, make sure of this and make sure of that, etc. Mark even
sent the fan pulley to be balanced. Anyway, I pick it up on the 14th of Jan.

I think this is one of the best Christmas presents I could get. I have been
busless since I sold Beary, my '75, and I went through a bad 'cold turkey'
withdrawal. I can't wait to get the new one.

I want to send a special Merry Christmas to all the wives of the listees. It
seems that we all wish each other merry tidings but leave out the "other
half or SO" They put up with so much. For example, if my bus develops a
malady I am a person possessed until I find the answer and I get"...is that
ALL you think about" So to each of our wives or SO's a very happy holiday
and thanks for putting up with us.

To the rest of the list, may your travels be safe and uninterrupted and may
God grant us a year of peace and good will. God bless us everyone. TBear
