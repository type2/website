Hi, Wendy.

Eighteen-hundred for a rebuild of an 1800cc does seem high to me, if only
because after you're finished (as if) spending all that money, you've
still got an 1800cc. Remember, we're not talking about BMW's here. You
could probably get a shop-rebuilt 2000cc with fuel injection for less than
that. I bought a used engine from the famous Bob Donalds (of LiMBO) last
year for $100, drove it across the U.S. and continue to drive it around
Seattle, if that gives you any idea.  You could try Beaver Creek Bob's on
Hwy 101, about 10-15 miles south of Tilamook, or call around to the local
engine rebuild places until they point you to some better options.
