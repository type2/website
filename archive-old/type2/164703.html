Subject: RE: [T2] replacement body panels

Ned,

 

My "opinion" is that since all panels on a bus are structural, being that it
is a uni-body design, welding is the best technique for repair. However, if
adhesive can be used in situations where welding will damage the surrounding
metal or welding is just too difficult, I would consider it. 

 

Off the top of my head, I don't see how a bus would necessarily benefit from
adhesive because of the way the joints interconnect with spot-welds to form
a cohesive structural element. The spot-welds have to be drilled out to
replace many of the panels, so in theory they should be re-welded to obtain
the factory structural rigidity. Perhaps a combination of both would be
best, but I would shy away from just the adhesive.

 

My 2 cents.
