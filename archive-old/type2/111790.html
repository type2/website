well i am no mechanic but i have had the shakes before and i will just got over what i found was wrong with mine and what took the shake out and then you can compare that to whatever anyone else tells you and make you a check list and finally quit doing that shimmy shimmy shake... LOL..

1.check bowden tube make sure has right bend on it..

2.check wear and tear on clutch cable.

3.check wing nut on cable for how tight or loose it is..?

4.check tightness of bolts for transmission connected to motor (all of them)<this includes the top transmission mounts>

5.flywheel could be warped?

6.clutch disc may need replaceing?

7.rear main seal?replace?

8.make sure little ring inside flywheel isnt leaking cant remember the name of it but is right inside the flywheel.. dont want any oil getting on the clutch disk or pressure plate.

9. check pressure plate may need repalceing.

10.you could just be off adjustment with the flywheel ,clutch disk and the pressure palte.

if so there is a tool little plastic do hicky that looks like the splines on the tranny that you can stick in the flywheel after dropping engine and line all three of those back up right with..

may just be shakeing cause either the bowden tube doens have the right bend or might be from the loose tranny bolts or transmission mounting bolts up top..

i found mine shakeing really bad once before and all i had was loose bolts then another time it was from the cutch disc..

hope this helps...
