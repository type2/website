Advise on rust removal.

Sand or media blasting is best.
If you can't do that a wire brush on a drill, die grinder, or on a 4"
electric grinder is next best---

Now---for the corrosion that you can't possibly get to---mix the proper
amount of Dupont's metal prep in a spay bottle and mist a coat of this
stuff on the bare metal--it'll get the rest of it--and leave a
protective coating on the bare metal.
This is the way a body shop would prepare the bare metal--and then spray
a coat of Duponts Vari Prime zinc chromate acid etch primer over that.
Sand,fill prime,sand, and paint.
No more rust, and you have a properly sealed surface that will last for
eons.

I have yet to find any body shop, be it automotive, truck, heavy
equipment, or RR train that uses this miracle in a bottle POR-15.
