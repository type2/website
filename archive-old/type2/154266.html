 believe that I should see some sort of resistor symbol on the wire run from the injector to the FI Control

diagrams" section of the manual.  Of the symbols included there, the symbol closest to the injector symbol is
one defined as "electromagnetic relay", which kind of makes sense; the injector triggers fuel flow, not
electron flow.
