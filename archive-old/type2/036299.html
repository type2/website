Subject: Re: smelly left turn - gas filler tube fix?

I had the same problem in my 78 Westy-lower large rubber connector
between tank and fill pipe had little cracks in it.  Fixed it in situ by
removing the "brain" and working through the hole, covered the old pipe
connector with silicone calk, followed by a wrapped layer of flashing
(cut from aluminum stock) that had more silicone gooped on it.  Put hose
clamps around this and cranked it tight- voila, no more smell.  I also
have a cracked inlet elbow, but fill my own tank (so they don't jab it
with the gas nozzle) and stop at the first click.
