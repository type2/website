I can't answer all your questions, but I wouldn't
depend on an electrically powered heater for much, for
reasons given in other answers to your questions.

When we used to drive a bus year around as our only
vehicle with 3 small children in Wisconsin, where some
weeks 20 degrees below zero was an average
temperature, we installed a gas heater (maye a BN4??)
from a beetle in our 1974 bus.  I put it under the
front floor, inside the removable cover that protects
the underside of the pedals, etc.  It blew heat at the
passenger's feet.  A mechanic friend sold it to me in
running condition with new stainless steel exhaust
fittings, and it did a great job.

I kept the heater and have photos of the installation
somewhere, but can't find them at present.
