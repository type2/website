Well, as some of you know I am currently stationed with my family in

the hebmuellers and split window bugs not to mention the off the wall

racing a stang and everything else in between.  AWESOME!!  But to all good 
things an end must come and thus it did with a wild show to remember.  And if 
your in the area, stop by I'll tell you more about it.
