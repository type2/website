On your second trip to the FLAPS (Friendly Local Auto Parts Supplier) 
you may want to check out some tires. If your bus isn't already running 
with Load C or D rated 185/14's with dual-ply sidewalls you will 
sincerely appreciate this upgrade, it makes a huge improvement in 
handling. Ray Nemo wrote a great tire FAQ following a long thread here 
on tires, it's available at:
