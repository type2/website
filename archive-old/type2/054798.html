Don't know about trannies, except that there is a difference
between 1600cc (Type 1 engine) and 1700..2000cc (Type 4 engine)
trannies.  I *think* they're interchangeable though.

>From '72, Type 4 engines fit into Buses.  There's a lot of
small items to be changed, or so I'm told (never having attempted
to do, well, anything to a Type 4), but the general body is
the same.

>     Nuts.  I just remembered.  The 79 is automatic.  New question.  Can
> I take the 2000 auto and put it in the standard 73?  Alternatively, can
> I take the 2000 and the auto trans and put the whole deal in the 73, and
> please remind me again why I don't want to do that?

Don't know if you can mate an auto engine to a manual tranny,
I'd guess no.  You can install an auto engine and tranny
into a manual bus, a pal of mine has such a beast.  I'd
discourage it.  You get worse gas mileage, more maintenance
hassle and parts problems, and not that much better
performance to show for it.

But then, I've always been a Type 1 engine guy, so maybe
I'm biased.
