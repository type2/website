Subject: RE: [T2] How do I tell if it is the battery or alternator?

Measure the bus voltage (pun intended) at the cigarette lighter. Then start
the engine (jump it, whatever) and measure the voltage at the same location.
The voltage should be higher (like around 14) with the engine running (if
not, try revving to about 2000 rpm). If there is no increase, or a voltage
drop with the engine on, then the alternator or voltage regulator are
suspect.

I assume your bus is new enough to have an alternator instead of a
generator.
