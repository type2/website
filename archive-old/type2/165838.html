I have read, in this list, several references to
"intake gaskets" in the past couple weeks. I decided
if I kept quiet that I would eventually get enough
clues to figure out what they're referring to. Okay,
this is it! WHAT do you mean by "intake gaskets?" The
only possible thing I can think of is the gaskets at
the phenolic spacers at the bottom of the intake
runners and injector blocks where it attaches to the
heads. I have never, ever had a leak there and can't
imagine anybody would. Only other things could be the
hoses that attach the plenum and runners or the
throttle body-to-plenum (I've had problems with this.)
The hoses connecting the plenum to the runners mostly
self-seal except when they've gotten hard and started
to flare out at the ends.

So what are intake gaskets? -BaH
