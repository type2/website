Keri,

Yes,

The rod with the two wholes is for the 69 or
upright oil cooler set up. That is the rod is
strait and
slides directly onto the flapper boxes lever arms.
Your 71 has an offset or dog house oil cooler
which
requires the rod to jog around the oil cooler.
This rod as you stated has only on whole on each
end and
requires the us of an adapter. These adaptors can
be seen on our site under <Engine> then <Cooling
Air Controls>
They are item number 28. We have several of these
and can add them to our on-line catalog.
