I had a slightly different version of that same exhaust system on my '79 bus.
That cheap, Chinese POS lasted maybe 9,000 miles before it fell apart. It
literally came apart and fell off the bus twenty miles from home on the tail
end of a 5,000 mile journey to Vancouver, BC and back.. I'd even repaired the
cracks in it with a gas welder before starting off on the trip.

I replaced it with this one:

http://www.busdepot_dot_com/details.jsp?partnumber=071251053EK

which has performed flawlessly for about six years now - quietly and without
leaks. It's German and looks like stock, but has no provision for EGR or cat.
It's also much lighter than the Chinese extractor exhaust system.

I'd like to see an aftermarket complete exhaust system like this German one,
but one which has provision for a 3-way catalytic converter.
