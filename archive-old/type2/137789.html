Hi Kyle,

Again, I'm not as technically literate as Karl, but I can say I have
worked a lot with donor starters and mixed and matched a lot of
armatures and cases to get running starters.

Basically, if you've got the stuff, all you can do is check for
interchange fit. I found you need to watch for differences in length
of the case. Also some solenoids mount with a 3 screw pattern and
others with a two screw pattern, so those are not interchangeable.
Also I found when robbing watercooled VW's, some nose assemblies have
a different offset and will not bolt up to the transmission housing.

John
