>I'm no expert, but what I've always done is:
>
>1.  pump the brake pedal a couple of times, then hold it down with 
>light-moderate pressure
>2. open the bleeder on the wheel cylinder and allow the fluid to 
>flow.
>3. Close the bleeder valve
>4. Release the brake pedal
>5. Go back to step 1.
>
>Obviously you need two people to do this.  If your wife is the 
>brake-pumper be very nice to her!
>
