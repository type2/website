Having recently put together two Vanagon 2.0l engines I can attest that 
they have quite a bit of apparent endplay when the crank is loose in the 
left case half, and yes, it WILL affect your walk-out test.  The cam 
needs to be shoved back toward the flywheel end of the engine to perform 
this test or you'll get funky results.  My stand was at such a level I 
could push the crank back with my knee and hold it there while using both 
hands to rotate the crank.
