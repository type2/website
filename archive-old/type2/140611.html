Since your previous post mentioned that you are removing your 'stock dual carbs' I am ASSUMING you have a 
73-74 bus with a 1700/1800 engine?

If yes, then anything by #20 in the 1st link is not it. #20 is the rear [rear is rear, front is front towards 
the steering wheel] upper corner tin. Nowhere near #3 cylinder. [at least you got a type 4 part picked:)]

Now in the 2nd link, #18 is the left lower pushrod cover tin, but it is a type 1 engine piece. [In the 2nd 
link ONLY 10 and 43 and the few little pieces by them are type 4 parts]

Go back to the 1st link,   http://www.ratwell_dot_com/technical/Microfiche/t201000.gif
all the pieces pictured in the top section down to the screw at #8 are type 4 pieces.

#20 is the left lower pushrod cover. It can be installed in the bus.

#32 is the front lower tin at #3 cylinder. It is held on by 2 screws going down from above thru the front tin, 
left upper cylinder tin #1, and the bracket on the heater pipe between the heater box and flapper valve. NOT 
really replaceable with the engine in, but is doable with a struggle.

#36 is the rear lower tin at #4 cylinder. It is also the duct for the oil cooler.

I have all of these pieces......
