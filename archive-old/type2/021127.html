I found my late '73 Westfalia luggage rack attached with sheet
metal screws.  Three screws in the back go through tall rubber
stand-offs.  The rubbers in the front are more like washers and
fit betw the steel roof and the fiberglass rack.  Try and line
'er up with the pop-up closed and resting on the rack.
