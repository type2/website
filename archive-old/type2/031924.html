> soft brass) to get out the bolts.  when I got the bolts out there were some
> sort of plastic certs on the bolts which is obviously the reason I had to
> use the punch.  Do I need to get new ones on these for the rebuild?  What
> are they called?  Also,  the front of the case started to split with a
> couple of hits from the rubber hammer and the back seem to be a little more
> difficult.  Do I need to remove the flywheel for any reason before I split
> the case.

Yes, you need to remove the flywheel to split the case.  There are 2 
fasteners surrounding the camshaft endcap.  The lower one (a stud/nut) is 
behind the "bellhousing" portion of the case and can be got at without 
removing the flywheel.  But the upper fastener (a bolt/nut) can only be 
removed after removal fo the flywheel.

The plastic inserts (funny how the manuals don't really warn you about 
these) are a standard part of a gasket "kit", which it sounds like you'll 
be getting in any case.
