  http://www.cs.rochester_dot_edu/u/jag/vw

There is also a mailing list for the old Split-window buses.
Send a message with the word "subscribe" in the body
 to    vintagebus-request_at_type2_dot_com
You will recieve a confirmation message, which you must reply
to and then you are on the list.

There is another bus list for for water-cooled Vanagons,
Syncros, and Eurovans. to subscribe, send email to
Listserv_at_gerry.sdsc_dot_edu        with text of
subscribe vanagon John Doe
(where John Doe is your name). to post to the list itself
email to Vanagon_at_gerry.sdsc_dot_edu
The current status of the vanagon list is always available
at http://www.vanagon_dot_com/links/lists.html
