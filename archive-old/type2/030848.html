iI orient all three oil ring gaps 120 degrees apart - paint dot one goes
up. Other two 120 from that. I _never_ put _any_ sealant of _any_kind on
anything but the case halves, with very few and very rare ecxception.
Surely NOT on cylinder bases. The paper works fine. Out of dozens and
dozens I've only ever had one that leaked at a cylinder base and it didn't
leak enough to be noticed except when I tore it down years later.
