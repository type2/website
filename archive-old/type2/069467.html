The "180 degree engine turn = 90 degree rotor turn" is very much correct. I
start with the engine at top-dead-center and move the engine back
counter-clockwise, stopping every 180 degrees and adjusting the valves in
1-2-3-4 order. The way of Saint Muir is the answer!
