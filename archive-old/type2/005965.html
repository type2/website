All of the 4 speed 412 wagons and 1974 or newer sedans had a 3.90:1 Final Drive Ratio
The earlier sedans with 4 speeds had a 3.73:1 Final Drive Ratio.
All of the Automatics had a 3.67:1 Final Drive Ratio 

I wouldn't try any of these with a stock motor but it would be a solution for transplants (ie Corvair or Subaru motors)
