If you replace the rotors you will need the bearing races which are pressed
into the rotors. If you are careful and use a drift you an remove the
races, clean and inspect  them along with your bearings, re pack with
grease and reinstall. Provided the bearings are in good order and the races
are not damaged. You need to use caution installing the races. If you do
not have the tool to do this maybe try to borrow one. It is easy to score
the race when you install them into the rotor.
