No sir, 68 are one-year-only IF you still have the 68
only wiper mechanism.

How can you tell?  Look at the shafts sticking out the
body. If they are smooth, they are 68. If they are
splined with a threaded section, they are 69 and
later.

68 arms attach like 67 and back BUT the arms are not
straight on the end. They are longer and have a 45 deg
bend. 68 arms look like these:[the ad says 67-68 but
it is from the UK, 68 buses came out in 67 so they
probably think they are 67 bays when we in the US know
better:)]
http://www.thesamba_dot_com/vw/classifieds/detail.php?id=219320

69 up arms have a hole in the center for the nut to
hold them on. They look like these:
http://www.thesamba_dot_com/vw/classifieds/detail.php?id=221377
