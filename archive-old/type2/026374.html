	Christopher,
I would pull the dust cap off of the drum(or rotor's) center, and remove the
wheel bearing.  Chances are, this bearing is too tight, or it's welded
itself to the spindle somehow.  If that doesn't work, it is probably a brake
shoe(drums), and you(or she) will need to back the adjusters loose, and pull
off the wheel/drum together.

	Eron Crites
