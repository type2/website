If you do not put power to the electromagnetic cut off, you will not go!
This electromagnitic valve shuts OFF the fuel to your idel jet when you
turn your key off. 


> What do I connect it to?


Connect it to the hot wire GOING TO your coil, not to the lead from your
coil to your dist.


> I have the automatic choke hooked up but don't really know how to adjust it.


With the engine COLD, set it so that the choke plate is JUST closed.
There are marks, but this will get you *close enough*. ;>)


> Is the wire connected to the auto choke supposed to always carry +12V when the key is on?


YES.


> Or is this wire supposed to go the cutoff. --


BOTH -- you need power to three places, coil, electromag cut-off, &
choke.
