Jeffrey,
If your horn works, you need not worry about the brake switches.  When
it comes to electrical troubleshooting, you check the easiest first.
 Look at the contacts in your tail light assemblies, and then under the
dash, for the fuse block connection. 

With the tail lights, use a continuity tester on the wire ends while
someone pushes your brake pedal.  This should let you know if there is
power coming to the rear.  Are your turn siglas out, too?
