Subject: Re: [T2] Pressure Relief Valve 74 Westy

That spinning cover is an interesting dilemma. You are going to have
to get it out in order to repair it. Either the cap or the case is
stripped and will have to be fixed. You are going to have to figure a
way to apply downward pressure to the cap as you unscrew it, perhaps
prying the edges with a screwdriver. Or expoxy a bolt or something to
it and pull down as you unscrew. Or drill it and tap it to get a bolt
into it.

I wouldn't worry about the valve. It's above the spring that will fall
out when you get the cap off.

John
