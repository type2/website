These are probably load range C tires, which don't have enough weight
capacity to meet the VW specification in this size.  The VW spec is
1520 pounds.

Load range D tires usually have 2-ply sidewalls.  C rated tires can
have either 1- or 2-ply sidewalls.

I'd hold out for 2-ply sidewalls.  The extra stiffness really improves
directional stability in a bus.

The price quoted isn't all that good.  I recall that I paid about the
same for 185R14 D-rated Bridgestones out the door last summer.  If the
$55 price isn't mounted and balanced you're not going to save anything
on this "sale".  You pay a lot for the "Goodyear" name!

You likely know my opinions re: 185R14 vs. 195/75R14, so I won't bore
you with it again!  :)
