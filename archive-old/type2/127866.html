Depending upon your sound level needs, you may find the die-cast
aluminum-cased speakers to be better than big speaker boxes.  No, you
don't get the thump-thump bass, but you save a lot of space and even have

neighbors!  My own setup simply had two of these speakers sitting at the
ends of my home-made parcel shelf.
