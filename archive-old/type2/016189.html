>Also, my next step is to change the oil
>and filters etc... Is there an oil treatemnet or addative that is
>recomended for air cooled engines? 

I havn't used any oil additives in my bus, but I have used Duralube in my 89
Jeep with 260,000 miles and it doesn't use a drop of oil.  I added a new
additive called Energy Release to the power steering pump and it actually
fixed it.  Before I added the ER, I could barely budge the steering wheel if
the outside temp was below 30 degrees.  Since I assumed I need to replace
the steering box I assumed I had nothing to lose.  I added about 4-6 ounces
of Energy Release and now it works like nothing was ever wrong.  I'm
actually amazed because I have never noticed any difference (good or bad)
with other additives in the oil, transmissions, differentials, power
steering pumps, etc.  As far as fuel additives I use STP fuel injector
cleaner about every 5,000.  It works well and makes a noticable difference
at idle and starting.  Others use Techron and I tried it, but it seemed to
work about the same as the STP.
