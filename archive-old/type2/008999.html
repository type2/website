This sounds like one goes to the cold start injector, one goes to the
thermo time switch (under the manfold, on the block between 3 and 4)
and one goes the aux. air valve (under and to the right of the cold
start injector).  What you'll need to do is get the wiring diagram 
and check the continuity on each plug terminal and find where they're
supposed to end up.  It's tedious but not difficult.
