I would have to call my '74 poptop Campmobile (Tigger), a B/A/C mix (hey,
don't expect a Libran to make a straight choice, eh?).

The bodywork is all original & unrusted, except for a patch in the nose
about 6" wide that's bogged up (clean behind so I'd say a minor parking
ding), and a scratch in the lower driver's side panels where someone backed
a trailer into it. The Paintwork, while original, is faded & could do with a
respray.

Mechanicals have nearly all been rebuilt/replaced within the last 6-7 years.

The camper interior is clean & tidy, but it's a "lived in" look ( the knocks
& scrapes that you get from going away every weekend & holiday in the old
girl).
