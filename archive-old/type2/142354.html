The schematics are not so bad. As I recall the top horizontal line represents the pos side of the battery, and the line at the bottom the chassis (neg). As a wire connects to anyting it is numbered. That number is found on the switch or electrical item most of the time. That item is also numbered and can be ID'd in the legend. 

Each wire has a color and that also is ID'd in the legend. The contacts inside the switch are presented in the drawing of a component many times and all of the ground terminals are ID'd.
