work last week(it was origionally much better worded and structured, but its

around today? granted there were fewer produced in the 50s, but the mid 70s 
werent exactly land-mark years as for as production was concerned 
either...im sure therell still be a few holding onto theirs, and there will 
always be museum pieces, but how many daily drivers will we still be seeing 
come 2020? i invite any of you west coasters to come over here to the other

how many vws are still on the road? theres the constant attrition of wrecks, 
rust and just plain abandonment going on on a daily basis. with production

parts and then where will we be?
so, again, how much longer do we have?
