this is an excellent reminder......but here is a side note,  by using the
standard small hose clamps you run into the problem of overtightening them and
actually cutting the rubber fuel lines with the sharp edges of the clamp.
remedy.....go to your local flaps or specialty race shop and they carry any
size hose clamp that has a smooth bore on the inside.  none of the screw type
clamp edges to cause any cuts of anykind.  try them you like em.!!
