Subject: Re: [T2] crankshaft interchangability?

This modification will turn your 2.0 engine into a 1.8 engine. VW made
the 2.0 motor by offset machining the 1.8 crank to increase stroke to
give the added displacement. Consequently the rod journals on the 2.0
are smaller. If you use the 1.8 crank with the 1.8 rods you should be
okay. If there are other issues with deck height due to piston pin
position, others will have to add in here. A starting point for
reading is here:

http://www.tunacan_dot_net/t4/tech/crank.htm

John
