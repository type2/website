> Hey Volks-
> I'm having a problem locating tires for my '77 Westy.  They have '185 SR 14' 
> on the existing ones, and no one seems to have these in stock.  
> Unfortunately I had a blow out this morning, and have to get these ASAP.  
> Any suggestions on where to go?

Go to your local Firestone/Bridgestone outlet and asked for RD603V in
185R14 Load range D.  They sometimes have them in stock and if they don't
they can get them in a day or two.

Above all, don't let the immediate need for a tire leave you open to a
tire dealers' claim that a 195/75R14 is "the same" as the correct 185R14.
Get the proper size and you (and your bus) will be much happier.
