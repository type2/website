Hi Archie,

The VW specialist garage which I've used for 10 years is Continental Spares 
at 435 Wells Road Bristol 0117-9776544. They are a family firm that go back 
with air cooled VWs for the best part of 50 years.

However it does sound rather like you need a complete replacement engine. You 
could get a cheep re conditioned engine from Volkspares (I have heard of 
people who were not satisfied with these engines, but also others who were 
happy), a new VEGE engine from Just Kampers (and others) or go the whole hog 
and get a new 64bph 1600 FI engine from Beetles UK.
