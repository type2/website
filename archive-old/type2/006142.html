hey dude....
we have a 1968  microbus that we got for $150 out of a junk yard.....it
didn't have a motor or any interior.  We got a '69 bug motor put in with a
new clutch......The motor (1500) had to be tap and dyed for the
configuration of the Bus motor mounts....the only other thing we have found
so far that needs special attention is the heat exchangers.  The heater
cable mounts for the flaps on the 1500 are on the *inside* of the exchangers
rather than the *outside*.  Stock bus mounts for the heater cable is on the
*outside* of the exchanger......guessing the 1500 bug and 1500 bus had
different heat exchangers....but I am no guru.  hope this helps.
