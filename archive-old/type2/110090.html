I have an electrostaic set up for paintingheavey equipment--no over
spray--it all goes to the machine, and the total amount of material lost
into the air is minimal.

enamel--and that's it.

No laquer thinner, no hardner, just zylene, Tolene, or mineral sprits
for reducer--

You would get a fast coverage paint job but no longevity with the
synthetic enamel.
It chaulks out and fades in about 6 months in the sun--
