Subject: Valve adjustment tips & tricks

A small door panel puller makes a handy tool for popping the valve cover
bails off and on. It's a small bent pry bar with a plastic handle.

Nail polish remover gets the sticky bits off the edge of the valve
covers where the gaskets go on. (I recommend the brand which comes in
gel form in a pump bottle.) Wash, rinse, and dry before installing gaskets.
