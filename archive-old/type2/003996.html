Well, the conversion issue is a definite possibility. Particularly with the
Westy plate. The VIN is not on the dash, but is behind the *passenger's*
seat, not the driver's seat. The VIN plate looks original, i.e., does not
look like it has been moved at all. The Westy plate...not sure. As I
mentioned, the veneer color is too dark, so I can't be sure what is going
on. The only cabinetry left is the base of the bed (where the plate is
located), and a center console thing (cup holders and all) in the same
veneer as the bed base. The console fits directly between the two front
seats. Since I don't recall ever seeing a center console called for in a
'69, I don't think it's original, and since it matches the veneer on the bed
base exactly, I wonder about the lineage. As for the Westy plate, here's the
info:

SO 57
Fabrik no.: 019xx (can't read last two digits)
1969

So, at the minimum, the plate year for the bed base and the model year of
the bus match.
