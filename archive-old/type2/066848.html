As for convenience its nice to be able to replace the muffler with 
removing the whole extractor unit( i.e if they both dont wear out at

be noiser, but usually after the instalation of the header it will 
produce a little more low end torque. I installed mine about a year 
ago and am quite happy with it... (except for the noise!!)
