have to say, it is a sweet motor.  Not terribly powerful, but smooth,
quiet, and reliable.  I believe it has a short stroke, because that puppy

it would blow up, but it never did. :-)
That motor pulled me and two friends on an 18,000km trip around Europe and
never let us down.  I've even got pictures of when I swapped the engine
into the Taunus.  It looks a lot like the 2.8, of course...
