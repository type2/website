I recall the reason for specific oil viscosities was that the oil also acts
as the cooling system and the oil pressure relief valve was two stage. If
the oil is too thick when it's warm then not enough flows through the oil
cooler. The engine gets too hot and the oil breaks down. I used straight 30
winter and summer back in my beetle days. Use 10w30 now in the Westie and
HD20 for the winter.
