Pour money on it Idea:
If it were mine, I'd consider replacing the fuel pump *and* the fuel lines.
Also check the condition of the wire that leads to the fuel pump.

Diagnostic suggestion:
Run a small wire lead from the fuel pump circuit back up to the dashboard
and power a small light-bulb with it. If the problem is with the electricity
not getting to the pump, you'll see that light flickering at the same time
as you lose power.


And BUY A FIRE EXTINGUSHER!!!
