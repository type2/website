I use 1 inch masking tape.  Don't try to stick it to the parts, just
wrap it around and stick it to itself.  I use a "sharpie" marker as it
is quite indelible.  Small parts go into small plastic bags which are
closed with the masking tape.

  The main thing that terrifies me about doing this is
> the prospect of not being able to piece it together again.

Label things and make diagrams as you go.  There aren't that many
connections, but it only takes one bad one to waste an hour (or a
month!) trying to fix.
