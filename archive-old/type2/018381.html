That's not right. This VIN is from a 1971 Microbus. You can read it from
the 1st three numbers in the VIN:
2 --> Type 2
2 --> Microbus
1 --> model year 1971


> It says the last B series ended in 1970 in type 2s with # B 5 230 000.
> My bus year is supposed to have and AE starting number. Muir doesn't
> give all the vin numbers though. He runs through the 21x xxxx xxx 
> series but not the 221s. Maybe they had different numbers? 

It is right that VW only uses 21 numbers when they indicate VIN's. This
is a bit confusing. Actually you shouldn't mind the 2nd number in the
VIN. It is only the 1st (indicating the model, type 1,2,3 or 4) and 3rd
(indicating the model year) number that are important, and of course the
serial number which is indicated by the last six digits. The 4th digit
is not important.

Your bus should have an AE engine. So it probably has been replaced by
an older engine.
