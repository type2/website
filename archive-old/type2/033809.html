Here we go again!

Earlier this year over 55 VW buses gathered together for a weekend of bus
fun for the entire family. They came from as far away as Boston, St Louis
and Hamilton Ontario in their buses loaded with kids, bicylcles,dog and all
the other stuff we carry around in our buses like floor jacks, spare parts
and potato cannons:) It was so much fun we are gonna do it again!

The event will take place on April 14,15 and 16 2000  in the same location
as last year, Hagan Stone Park which is located just south of Greensboro,
NC.

Please visit the website for details including maps and trip reports from
last years EveryBus. If you have pics that you want to email from last year
or link to your own exciting EveryBus page just let me know.

Hagan-Stone Park is a 400 acre park, campground and wildlife refuge located
just south
of Greensboro, NC. HSP has lotsa walking and hiking trails, playgrounds for
the kids, and
peaceful areas to just get away from it all! EveryBus will be taking over
the RV field for
the weekend as well as a picnic shelter. We have reserved 30 campsites that
include
hookups and picnic tables. Tent camping will be allowed in the RV field for
our group. Any
overflow of buses can be accommodated in the tent loop or other RV loop.
Fees for
camping are $12.00 per night per campsite. I will be collecting so just look
for the ratty
72 "Westfakia Camper" so you can sign up.

HSP is a public campground so...... Pets are allowed as long as they are
leashed and being
attended to. Quiet hours are 11pm to 7am. etc etc etc. Please be cool. You
know what I
mean :)>
