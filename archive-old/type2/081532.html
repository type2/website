with both armrests into Roadcow. I feel it was the best thing I've ever done

since I drove with it slightly reclined and adjusted as far back as it would
go. It worked great and survived a few panic stops without mooving. Yes it
is a bit taller but I had some people who were about 6' tall driving Roadcow
without any problems.
