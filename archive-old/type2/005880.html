While none of my VW's have done this, I have had it happen on non - VW's.
The symptom is the rear brake shoes glazing and chattering, due to excess
heat.  You may notice that this problem is at it's worst during or after a
long and/or hard stop.  Sometimes, it is due to undersized brakes, but
often, it a due to a malfunctioning proportioning valve, which is causing
the rears to assume too much of the braking load.
