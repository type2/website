Subject: Don's Road Trip - Heading Home (1)

After fixing the fuel filter and getting everybody loaded into the Guac, it
was around 4pm. My wife still really wanted to visit her old friend in Big
Bear, so up we went. We took the "side" way through Redlands (hwy 30) since
her friend lives in Sugarloaf. Got there around 6:30, visited for a while,
then headed out for some dinner. We ended up at the Carl's Jr that I worked

Travelodge on Main St. AVOID. Non-smoking room smells like smoke, cig ashes
UNDER the bedspread, a dirty footprint under the bedspread on the other bed,
roaches, thin towels, and $80 a night. Ugh.
