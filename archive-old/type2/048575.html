Oh yeah, speaking of oil...I know everyone here uses Castrol, Valvoline or
Mobil 1 in their engines, so this isn't for us, but warn your grandma about
checking the SAE service on the oil she may find in the supermarket. I was
in a supermarket the other day, waiting on my wife, and happened upon the
automotive section. They had a strange brand of oil sitting there at an
insane cheap price, so I picked up a bottle and turned it over. "Exceeds API
Service SA." I think Oil of Olay exceeds API Service SA.
