I had a 1958 Isetta 600 for several months.  When I decided that I wanted to
sell it to fund my Subaru Sambar Van Passion, I needed the title.

It was easy.  I downloaded the affadavit off of the va dmv website,
(dmvnow_dot_com) signed it in front of a notary, sent it with sales tax ($35)
title fee ($10) and administrative fee ($30).

Waited 40 days, because they had to do a vin search etc, and I got a legal
title.

My isetta then became so much more valuable for just $75!!

If your state does not allow a similar process, then there are several title
companies in Alabama that will do it for $200-$240.

By all means, don't break the law!!
