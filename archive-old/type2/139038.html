On 12/23/04 1:26 PM, "Rudy Cooper" <rudycoop_at_pacbell_dot_net> wrote:

> I'm going to change the oil in my transaxle.  Reading
> from the original Muir book it doesn't seem to be that
> big of a deal, but I wanted to get some first hand
> knowledge from any of you out there.  Any surprises,
> what type of oil should I use ?

GL4 if it's a manual. You'll need to buy a gallon.

To drain, remove the fill plug then remove the drain plug.

To fill, you can buy a pump that fits on top of the container and pump it in
until it flows out of the fill opening. Just level the bus first.
