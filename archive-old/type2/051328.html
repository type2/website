Glad to see someone else is trying to run a Type 2 hereabouts!
Suggest you check
1. Bowden tube - does it have enough of a bend in it (an inch at least)add
washers if needed to increase the bend, and is the stiffener overlay tube
section (about 3 inches long) in the middle of the Bowden length.
2. The cable tube under the body  - is it firmly attached to the cross frame
where the Bowden tube fits on to it. If it can move, then the Bowden tube
will too.
