in a dynamo the field coils rotate inside stationary magnets. In an 
alternator the magnets rotate inside stationary field coils.

The dynamo needs higher revs to begin producing its charge and it produces 
less of it. For the vw 1600 flat 4 a dynamo produces 30Amp and an aternator 
55Amps

Dynamos are no longer used because moderns cars have powerfull lights, 
radios, heated seats, heated windows, electronic brains etc etc and therefore 
need a generator which is more powerful (within the size constraints) than a 
dynamo could be.

So to fit a dynamo instead of an alternator would be needless downgrading of 
your vehicle.
