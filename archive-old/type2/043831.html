early syncro, but I gather they are pretty hard to

 Here's a list of alternative wheels from a UK bulletin

don't put too much store in it... however it should provide
a good place to start looking. Note that because it lists,

 Sean comments that some of these may not be available in

to the US, where Seat Alhambras, for example, are probably
not very widely available....
 These are also mostly cars, not "trucks", so it is probably
more useful for alloys than steel rims.
