Let me second George's observation below and amplify on it a bit.

My first reaction to '70 seats, even after I repadded and reupholstered
them, was not good. I was used to bucket seats.  But time went by and now I
love the old seats and I wouldn't exchange them for anything.

At first the older bus seats seem primitive. But after driving a bus for a
while (or riding in one as a passenger) you begin to appreciate how
perfectly designed they are, the spring quality of the seat  matched to bus
suspension.

Besides they just look right.

Anyone else feel this way?
