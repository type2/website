I do it all the time with no problems. Just make sure you really button up
any holes, like intake ports etc. Don't aim the water dirrectly at these
spots. I've found that inexpensive oven cleaner by EasyOff left to soak for
awhile really cuts the grease. It says don't use it on aluminum, but I've
been doing it for years and no worries. Toothbrush, bottle brush etc to
clean. After that blow dry with yur compressor and wait till it's totally
dry. Then a THIN coat of flat black lacquer (its the thinnest paint) spray
paint to dissipate heat better and you're good to go.
