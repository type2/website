Wilson considers compression of 95 and above acceptable if the engine
is okay in all other respects as you describe, providing all readings
are within 75% of the highest reading (p. 15).

Manufacturors calculate normal compression test readings from
compression ratio, so yes, if you have lowered ratio, then even a new
engine will read lower than factory specs.
