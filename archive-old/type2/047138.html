There are lefts and rights, mirror opposites.
The reason 68 to 73 is 56 1/4" and  74-75 is 57 1/4" is not that the 74 is
wider.....it is because of the location of the half-moons. VW dropped them
down lower in 74, so the bars are longer.
68 bars are black and round. They bend very easily. I think VW realized this
and remade them starting in 69 out of stamped, 3 sided galvanised
steel....much stronger.
I have about a dozen sets of half-moons. If you want  a set, what's your
address?
