There are some lovely little stoves designed for use in smallish cruising 
sailboats and the like.  Bulkhead mounted, insulated stacks (so as not to 
ignite a wooden boat), "air tight", heating and cooking capabilities, and so 
on.  Very efficient, very compact, very pretty (lots of bronze and brass) and 
incredibly expensive.

One of these would serve nicely in a Type 2, but the price is prohibitive....
