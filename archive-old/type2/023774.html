 I don't try to remember the up/down thing. I visualise the thread on
the adjusting screw that I'm working on (they all have the same, 
conventional, right-hand thread) and identify which way the screw 
would rotate to move the brake shoe in the right direction: that is,
it would "unscrew" to move the shoe outwards, or "screw-in" to
slacken it off.

 The star then rotates in the opposite direction to have the same effect
on the shoe, so I work the prongs appropriately.

Hope this helps- depends how you like to remember things!!
