Richard had a few questions/suggestions:

> Moving to a thicker oil is a good idea. Why didn't you decided to dump 
> all of the 10w30?

We were expecting--and got--some cold weather in northern Maine.  With 
temps in the low 40s, I worried that 15-50 might be too thick.  My VDO 
OP gauge showed 60+psi on cool mornings with 20-50 (non-synthetic).  I 
was getting nervous about blowing seals.  Maybe it's just the 
experimenter in me, why not 12.5w40 :-)  I've been very pleased with 
the blend's pressures.
