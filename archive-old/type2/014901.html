At 05:17 PM 2/24/99 -0800, Tom Young wrote:
>I've been trying to get the brass distributor gear on my 2 crankshafts,
>(rebuiling a couple of 2.0l air-cooled engines) and haven't succeded yet.
>
>Has anybody done this before, and can give me some hints as to how it's 
>done?

I use the Betty Crocker method.  Clean the crank gear that drives the cam 
and the distributer gear.  Bake in toaster oven at 450d for 5 minutes.  
Remove from toaster oven with channel locks & they slide right onto the 
crankshaft.  Garnish and serve.  Feeds one.
