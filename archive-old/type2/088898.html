No, a Vanagon harness will not work on a 79 Cal only bus.
79 Cal is one-year-only. There is no injector resister pack. The resistance is
built it to the harness and ECU. The double relay is 9 pin instead of 11.
There is a O2 sensor but no 60-40 relay like on a Vanagon. The AFM is 79 Cal
only, ends with 022. Vanagon is 025. [there are other differences but no need
to list them, since it won't work....]
