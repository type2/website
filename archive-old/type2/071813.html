Chris, the screws are #6  3/8" Pan Head Phillips Zinc Plated.  You should not 
have any problems with galvanic action.  There are six on the long sides and 
two on the short.  Check the window to see where the original holes were, but 
you don't have to put them in the same place.  Might even be stripped there.  
Could put as many screws in as you like.  Holes are drilled very close to the 
edge of the window frame lip.  Don't overtighten.
