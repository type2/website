BusFusion 2003, a Volkswagen Camper camping event now in its 
third year, will take place from June 12th through the 15th 
in Almonte, Ontario, Canada. In conjunction with Artsfusion, 
a local arts festival, there will be many events you can 
attend or participate. Some BusFusion activities are VW 
parts Flea Market, Bus Painting, VW Art Show & Sale, camp 
fire, and draw for many door prizes. Camping is again on the 
Almonte Fairgrounds right in town on the Mississippi River 
with a short walk to all festival events and stores. 
Registration before May 26 is $10 (includes BusFusion Dash 
Plaque) or $20 (includes BusFusion T-shirt & Dash Plaque).  
Registration at the gate is $15 (includes BusFusion Dash 
Plaque).  Camping $15/night, paid on site.  Visit the 
BusFusion website for complete details, pictures of previous 
BusFusions & preregistration forms; www.busfusion_dot_com. 
BusFusion is sponsored by CCVWC, LiMBO, IWCCC, & WOTC.
