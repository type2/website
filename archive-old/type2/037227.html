If the cable is good, then the problem is probably at one end or the
other.  I'm betting on a broken clutch fork, but it could also be a worn
out lever on the clutch pedal pivot.  If youare getting full motion on the
clutch arm on the transmission, then you need to pull the engine and check
the throwout fork and shaft.

Seek and ye shall find the problem.
