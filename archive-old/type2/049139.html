   Actually I was trying to get around the RV plate thing more than anything- 
although it's cool that this spun off into an insurance thing. 
    My insurance agent  (an independent) doesn't care whether it's a westy or 
not- the way my insurance works with him is-  I bring down any VWs I 
purchase- he takes 5 pictures of it- 1 on each side, then 1 interior shot - 
then asks me how much I want to insure it for. Then I pay a premium based on 
the "stated insured"  amount, as far as collision and comp goes.  Liability 
he bases on "collector car" rates- which require me to store them "primarily 
in the garage" (whatever primarily means). 
    My liabilty ends up being about half the amount that it costs me for our 
daily driver cars.
   FWIW- I used to be with State Farm. Had them for 8 years. Never had a 
particular problem with them- but a few years back I got on the phone and did 
LOTS of comparison shopping. Found in general here in Wis- that independents 
were cheaper. And my agent seems more personalized than those big companies 
can ever get, of course.
