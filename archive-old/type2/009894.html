Centrifical _at_ 1600                8   - 13
            _at_ 3400               20.5 - 24.5
Vacuum      _at_ 7.9 in. Hg          8.5 - 11
Initial timing _at_ idle                7.5

Total                            36.5º - 43º

You will be lucky to get 25 out of a 009, that's why you need to set initial 
to at least 15 BTDC but you run the risk of pinging and you lose the power 
gain of vacuum advance.
