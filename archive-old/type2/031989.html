Wade:

I know these nuts get re-used all the time.  I would guess the thinking 
here is: you don't want anything going wrong inside the case, especially 
something as simple and cheap as nuts.  Also, I believe that some earlier 
versions of the Type IV engine had you peen the nuts to the connecting 
rods to make sure they didn't come loose.  If you re-use these nuts the 
peened portion of the nut's going to cut into its seating surface of the 
connecting rod, possibly gouging off small metal flakes.

I got my new nuts from Bus Depot.
