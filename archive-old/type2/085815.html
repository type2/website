Subject: Re: [T2] Teflon Tape on Brake Bleed Valve Threads?

I was working on the brakes for akron U's mine baja car and had to do some 
research into this myself. All of the manufacturers said to not run teflon 
tape. The bleeder seats on that taper at the end. If you put teflon tape on 
then that taper can't seat properly which causes it to leak. Brake fluid 
will soften the teflon tape. We had a lot of problems with leaking brakes 
until I told them to take the tape out.
