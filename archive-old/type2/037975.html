Howard,

The hole punch you are looking for is called a "Chassis Punch" and is used
to punch holes in electrical boxes, so electricians use them mostly.

They are a pretty neat tool, but unless you are going do a bunch of holes
(I have some that we use to punch holes in race car and dune buggy dashes
for the gauges) they are not very cost effective.  You would be money ahead
to buy a good multi hole saw.

You can buy them at any good electrical supply (usually a wholesale shop)
where the local contractors get supplies.  Each size of punch will cost you
$80 - $150 depending on size.
