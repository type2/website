Subject: Re: [T2] rocker arm spring

Be aware that this "spring" is the pushrod tube
retainer. When properly installed, it holds a slight
amount of pressure downward on the lip at the end of
the tube. I found that it was easiest to install it
not worrying about whether it was against the lip,
then after the upper part is seated in the grooves of
the rocker shaft carriers, reach in there with
needle-nosed pliers and pull upward until it pops into
place against the lip on the end of the tube. Repeat
for each. Easy. -BaH
