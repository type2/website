Hi Luc,

You have to remember that the air which is comes out of the heater vents is 
pushed there by the fan in the fan housing; so ANY oil / fuel contamination 
in the engine compartment will result in smells in the engine compartment.

IF I were a betting man then I would put my money on the smell of oil coming 
from a leaking (or badly fitting) crank case breather pipe - while using the 
throttle fumes will be sucked through the carb (via the air cleaner) but with 
the throttle closed a leaking breather tube will let fumes into the engine 
compartment and therefore into the heater system.
