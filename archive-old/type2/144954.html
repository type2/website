T4 parts are stupid expensive and Vanagon parts
are you gotta be kidding me expensive.  I find
myself wondering why I could buy a 350 chevy

The part situation for the vanagon isn't going to
change. Vanagons were produced in Germany and
South Africa only.  Since there were no vanagon
plants in Central or South America, there never
was a supply of affordable parts and never will
be.  As the German parts become NLA.  It's over. 


To a lesser extent, the same can be said for T4
