Jim Mowreader says of painting: 
* Primer alone will not protect the metal. 
* Using rattle can enamel over your real-car-paint primer means you get
to strip off the rattle can paint plus the real-car-paint primer when
you want to get the Bus painted. 
You need a "washprimer" under the high-build primer/surfacer. This will
keep the bus from rusting until you get the topcoat on it.
