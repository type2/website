California is not small. Where in California are you? ( :) )

Seriously, LA County's Department of Public Works organizes frequent
household hazardous waste (HHW) collections at various places throughout the
county. I imagine most of the other counties (especially most of the SoCal
counties) have similar arrangements.

Here the link to LA's schedule:

http://dpw.co.la_dot_ca_dot_us/epd/hhw/schedule.htm

You would be suprised how much toxic "chazerai" you have laying around your
house. This is an easy chance to get rid of it.
