Odds are that the full coating of JB Weld would crack and peel off due 
the different thremal expansion ratios of the metal exhaust pipe 
versus the JB Weld.  Try it in a small area and see.  Be sure that it 
goes completely around the pipe and for at least 4 inches lengthwise 
to eliminate *edge effects* and relate back on test results!

It will also change the conductivity of the pipe. The additional mass of 
the JB Weld will increase the ability of the pipe to retain and transfer 
heat through conduction to other parts of the system.
