please, please, there is no set '12V' or '6V' size pulleys.   there are
different size pulleys, but unrelated to voltage.

the 36hp style crank pulleys are largest.  i prefer the largest possible
crank pulley for my motor, but the 36hp style are flimsier, though not as bad
as the25hp ones.   ive found about 5 diff sizes of crank pulleys

i know of at least 4 styles of generator pulleys.   the earliers ones use a
different spindle.   another kind had two prongs that fit into notches, and
another has a oval with flat edges shape hole in the middle to keep it in
place.   sizes also may vary, though not as much as with the crank pulleys.
