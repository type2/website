Well... I have a couple of Vanagons (WBX) with the hall ignition.  The system
requires: the hall generator, an ignition amplifier, an idle stablizer and the
special coil. You might be able to pick up the whole system at your local
boneyard for cheap.

Note, I've tried (unsuccessfully) to install the system on my '77.  It will
run, but for some reason, it tells the ECU to go to full rich.  Haven't quite
figured out what the problem is, but I will one of these days.
