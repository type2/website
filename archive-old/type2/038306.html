A Kombi is the base model window van Volkswagen offered.  Its model
number is 23, 231 being a LHD model, 232 RHD and 235 being LHD
with Steel Sliding Roof (sunroof).  Structurally, the Kombi and the Camper
are almost identical.  If you look at the opening in the roof for the
camper,
it is the same opening as for the sunroof.  The Kombi came as a 5 pass.
vehicle, with painted hardboard for side panels in the rear passenger area
and with only the area around the front passengers finished like a normal
bus.  The Kombi has "cal-look" rubber--no aluminum trim around the
windshield or the rear window.  The Kombi has a matte/semigloss painted
interior from the B-pillar rearwards.  The Kombi came stock with stationary
vent windows in the front doors.  If your bus has a fully upolstered
interior,
chances are, you own a Microbus.
