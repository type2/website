as well as having the ability to stand up and do whatever you need to 
do at any time, I'm 6'2 and if I'm standing up straight my head touches 
the ceiling but when do I ever stand up straight, really ? <slouch>

creative. I really like how bright it is thought, lots of indirect 
light comes in.

I couldn't be happier with mine.
