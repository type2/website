Not being familiar w/the '72, please take w/a complete saltlick.  Are you 
certain these fumes are entering via the heating sysem?  To ensure your 
safety, it would be wise to go over all of your fuel line, especially 
out-of-sight sections (like where line passes through engine tin, etc.) and 
clamped connections.  Do you have a gas heater?  If so, is fuel supply 
route/lines good to go?  Make certain that the smell you're encountering is 
(hopefully) coming from exchanger refuse, and not fuel squirting out 
somewhere (around a clamp on a dried-out hose, maybe?) and running into your 
exchanger(s) or somehow sending vapors into the cab.
