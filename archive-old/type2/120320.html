There are a bunch of people in Germany who run diesel engines in their
buses. Apparently, the preferred engines are the 1.7 Turbo Diesels out of
late Vanagons or 1.9 TDIs both on 5-speed Vanagon trannies. They get good
milage, great torque and provide for cruising speeds around 90 mph with some
potential left for passing.

There is a guy who makes conversion kits which (as far as I know) contain
little more than the engine supports.
