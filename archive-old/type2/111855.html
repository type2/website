On Mon, 29 Sep 2003, Todd M Jaspers wrote:

>         The foam that surrounds the tin-work in my Bus is completely
> shot. I'd like to replace it, but I want it to be something that provides
> a little bit more of a seal. Are there any after market replacements for
> these?

You want something that seals well, wears reasonably, and is very flexible
to handle the vibration.  Foam rubber will work perfectly and last for
years!  Your old one is probably 30 years old!

> What kind of tape do you guys use to seal up the small holes and such?
> Should I use the metal muffler tape?

The tape should be able to flex.  Cloth duct tape seems to work well.  Get
the stuff the pros use, not the hobbyest "Duck" tape.  If it's not hard to
tear, it isn't the good stuff!
