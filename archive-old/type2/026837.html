Dear Eddie.

I encountered similar problems a while ago, when sorting out the 
installation of a 1971 VW 411LE engine (with Bosch D-Jetronic fuel 
injection), into my 1973 VW 1600 Type 2; which I believe is a very similar 
configuration to the 1975~79 VW 18/2000 Type 2 engine (with Bosch L-Jetronic 
fuel injection).

My solution was to slightly enlarge (by about 10mm) the right-hand 
oval-shaped hole, in the bodywork, immediately to the right of the central 
hole and similarly enlarge the hole in the transaxle support bracket. This 
seems to give satisfactory access to the throttle-body linkage, without any 
strain on the cable shroud.

Having access to e-mail and the Internet, only through the local public 
library, I have no means of posting any pictures on a web site.
However, in exchange for a few light-weight second-hand, USA specification 
1972~79 VW Type 2 spares, I will gladly send you copies of my colour 
photographic prints, by airmail.

Do you have manual or automatic transmission? I have surplus to 
requirements, a spare throttle cable for the USA specification, 1975~79 VW 
18/2000 Type 2, with automatic transmission.
