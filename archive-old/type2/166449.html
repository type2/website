Hi Eddie,

I can tell you from personal experience that you CAN go that fast  
with that engine/transmission combo, for hours at a time. But do  
yourself a favor and be sure to get a CHT gauge if you intend to go  
out there and "push the envelope".

Problems with heat in the type4 engine come from load. High speed =  
high load. As someone mentioned already in this thread, it takes  
twice the HP to go 80MPH than it does to go 60MPH.

Level ground at 80MPH is an acceptable load with no wind. 50MPH with  
a 50MPH headwind is not acceptable; the heads will surely overheat. A  
long hill at 75 MPH will overheat the heads, and so will a short hill  
at 80MPH.

The 091 transmission will give you 5% lower RPMs in 4th gear, which  
will help a bit with higher speeds on level ground.
