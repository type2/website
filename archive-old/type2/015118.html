	John,

Before you go into the PITA job of taking the wiper motor out, try to
unscrew the wiper switch from the dashboard. Mine works perfectly when it
hangs free.
It seems like when the switch fails it connects the plus side to the shell
of the switch. Isolating it from the dashboard with plastic washers or
letting it hang free under the dashboard is the cheap and fast fix.
