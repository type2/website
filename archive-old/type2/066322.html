Well, Jean, here in the US we use these stickers called "Deadhead" stickers as a
scale indicator to how much a VW bus is trashed. The more of these "Deadhead"
stickers on a bus, the worse the condition of the bus, and the nearer the bus is
to it's death, hence the name "Deadhead" sticker.  On buses that have their
windows totally covered with "Deadhead" stickers, those are usually are not far
from being junked.
