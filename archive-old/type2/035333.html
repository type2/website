Unlike the other two seals, the flat seal must be glued, since (as with the
original VW design) it lacks the internal metal clip.  Also, there are
literally several inches of "stretch" on the side seals. If the poptop seal
came up 1/2" short, just pull a little bit (perhaps one side) of it off
again and pull it slightly when reapplying.  It will stretch a tiny bit and
will fit perfectly.
