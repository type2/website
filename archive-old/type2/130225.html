Hi Wayne,

With a good quality marine enamel the results for a do-it-yourself job 
are outstanding.

Interlux premium Yacht Enamel is just such a product, it goes on easily 
with a brush and roller and does an EXCELLENT job. At $30cdn/Quart, it 
had better be good!!!

1. Clean the top thoroughly.
2. Sand lightly with 220 grit paper.
3. Wipe the roof down with a thinner-soaked rag to get rid of the dust.
4. Paint., sand lightly & coat it again.

There should be enough after 2 coats on the roof to paint the bumpers 
at least 1 coat to match.

I painted my top three years ago and it still looks great.
