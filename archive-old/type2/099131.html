Yes, extreme richness could be bad temp sender, poor ground to sender,
or broken wire connector.  I've had that wire break off.
If it's the temp sender and you have no spare, try grounding its wire to
the ECU when warmed up.  You can get some resistors from Radio Shack-
1500 ohms should get you started; 400 ohms if it's warmed up.   With a
volt/ohmeter and a rotary pot, you can even dial in the resistance you
want.
