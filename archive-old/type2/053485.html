On Wed, 14 Jun 2000, Chris Collier wrote:

> I ordered a long block from AVP and starting to change over the rest of
> the parts from old engine. This is a automatic and I need to know the
> size of the 12 point bolts that hold on the plate to crankshaft. Is this
> tool easy to find? Nobody in this area knows.

if I recall from the last time I had mine off it is the same size as the
one for the CV boot bolts.  I found a set of Metric 12 point socket
wrenches at my FLAPS (Pep Boys I think) for about $20 had the right size.
these are about 2 inch long shafts with the correct end on one side and a
hex head to fit a socket wrench on the other end.  they have worked well
for a number of years.

don't forget you have to set the end play the same as on a flywheel on the
flex plate too
