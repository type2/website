On Sat, 6 Oct 2007, Mike Jackson wrote:

> I picked up one of those adapters from Harbor Freight that allows you to
> refill the disposable bottles.  Fill up a 20 lb. bottle at the propane
> supplier and then refill the little 1 lb. bottles from that.
>
> Not entirely safe for transport in some locals.  I believe that you can't
> take a refilled disposable bottle through traffic tunnels here in the states
> for instance.

Worse than that.  Just the act of refilling them is illegal.

That being said, the reason that it is illegal is that the disposable
bottles don't have a provision against overfilling.  If filled completely
full with liquid propane the expansion of the liquid in a hot bus could
rupture the bottle with disasterous results if an ignition source is
nearby.

So, if you refill, make sure that there is some "slosh" in the bottle when
you shake it.  If there isn't, hook it up and burn off a bit of propane
before you store the little bottle away in the bus.
