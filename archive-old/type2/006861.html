Jay,

When I put my last engine in I had it drilled and tapped for full flow
oil cooling/filtering and ran into the same problem. I did have to notch
the rear mount. I used a Dremmel tool with a cutter wheel -- quite a few
cutter wheels. The metal that the cross member is made of is pretty
tough. I had some one hold the cross member up while I crawled under and
scribed about where I needed to cut. I took several additional trimmings
but I only wanted to remove what was necessary. I also had to put a
small window in the sheet metal that goes behind the pulley. When all
together for the last time I filled in the gaps with RTV sealant. 

The reason it fit in the Baja is that there was no rear mount.
