> I was wondering where I can obtain a fuel pressure gauge.  Also I am
> wondering how to proceed.

Truck on down to your local Ace Hardware store and pick up a simple dial
water gauge.  Get one that looks like this:

http://tinyurl_dot_com/x552

but get one that goes from 0 - 45  or 0 - 60.

Then get a 1/4" hose barb that'll screw on to the brass fitting of the
pressure gauge.  Add a couple of hose clamps and a foot or so of regular FI
hose and you're in business.  For $10 - $15 you'll have a perfectly
servicable pressure gauge for a Vanagon.
