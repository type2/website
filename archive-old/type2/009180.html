Tom

Sounds like a clutch problem. Look closely at it and whatever is in the bellhousing
(ie, any oil? gearbox or engine oil (gearbox oil stinks!)) Sorry, no experience
with Type 1 flywheels, only my Type 4 flywheel which is much simpler to get off.

When you pull a motor, look very closely at replacing the clutch, heat exchanger
linkages and cables, missing tinware, thermostat, accessible oil seals (depending
on how much time, and how much of a sptripdown you're doing) and engine mounts.
Also it's the ideal time to do a full service.
