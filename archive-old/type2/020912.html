White knuckled is an understatement.  My arms are still sore from 
yesterday's trip!  I had to go up to Green Bay (about 40-50 miles each 
way) for work yesterday.  I managed to get in a pack of semis for a 
little bit, and that was nice.  I was able to keep up speed and not get 
blown around.  When the wind was behind me, I'm sure I could have done 
90mph if I wanted to, but when it was a headwind, I was down to 45 at

wind much better for some reason.  Headwinds still suck, although not as 
bad, but it is much better in crosswinds.
