If the dust boots are intact and the car is not sitting in a pond, leaving
it unattended should be no problem, at least insofar as lubrication is
concerned. This doesn't mean that you don't have a squeaking ball joint or
some other front end component. Keep in mind that the stuff that moves up
and down is at the outside of the suspension - this makes isolating the
source of the noise a bit easier. If you can duplicate the squeak by
bouncing the van up and down in place you can usually put a "listening
stick" up against various parts of the suspension and find the source that
way.
