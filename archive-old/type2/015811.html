Bill,

Check this simple one first. 
The gearshift is bolted to the floor with two bolts, when loosened they
allow you to adjust the gearshift. It has happened that these bolts work
themselves loose after years of hard use, resulting in a sloppy feel
getting worser fast.
Another one is the bushing in the front end of the shift rod coming from
the tranny. You need to remove the belly pan to check this one.
