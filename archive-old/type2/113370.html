FI plugs should look like this: greyish with only black on the edge. They run leaner than a normal carb setup so don't freak out if you are reading any "standard" plug reading guides with pictures.

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/Maintenance2/177-Closeup.jpg

Here's another picture of my plugs when the thermostat cable broke and the engine wasn't heating up properly. Even though  it ran really rough the plugs still didn't foul that badly:

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/Maintenance2/218-Plugs.JPG

When there is oil on the threads it means it was probably only loose in the head. 22 ft lbs. IIRC.
