I refinish WHOLE trucks, not just the frames.
I do not spot repair anything, nor touch up rust spots.
I repair the affected area, and refinish, to better than factory
specifications.

scratch in the paint in the engine compartment.

under the paint.

Ah,Ha !!

VW had actually used a zinc laden primer under their paint !

Amazing !!!

I am shocked as well as appalled, that VW had the tenacity to use this
type of primer, and to think that it has managed to last 27 years
without turning to dust.

In life, as well as in business one learns from his mistakes, and
through experimentation.

Miracles in a can, I have tried, and tried, and tried.
Nothing can substitute doing doing body work, or refinishing, the
factory recommended way, ( or better) if you expect to experience long
term results.

And seeing as repairing rust, and/or performing body work,and
refinishing, is such a labor intensive job, I for one only want to do
the job once, and do it correct.
