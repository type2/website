It sounds like you are missing the lower tin betreen the engine case and
the heater boxes.  These are important for proper thermosat functioning. 
We sell those if you need them.  You should also have two small pieces of
cooling tin just above the push rod tubes.  

The pre-bent metal fuel line is also available new.  Make sure to put a
rubber grommet where it passes through the tin.
