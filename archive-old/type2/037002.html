 I know this as Taiga Green, which I presume is the English translation
of the German. My '77 is two tone, with "Dove White" over Taiga Green.

 I'm pretty sure that my Haynes "restoration handbook" lists this colour
as Taiga green, too. It's a good colour for a camper because it's bright
enough to be visible on a dark road and yet blends in reasonably well
at a campsite. Not too easy on the eye, though :-).
