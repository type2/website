What I did was setup a test fuel ring in the engine compartment.  I had
a line coming from the fuel pump to the left-side of the fuel ring (hose
extended to reach to the bumper) and then a hose going back to the
pressure regulator.  Then I  hooked up some wires to one injector at a
time (two on the ring) and turned on the ignition and the afm flap and
hit the power and watched the spray pattern into a glass cup.  I only 
had one that  was bad so I just stuck the injector in the hose backwards
so it would push all the dirt back out.  after that, it was fine.  I
tested the second one on the ring, then hooked up the other side of the
fuel ring and tested the other two in a similar manner.  Also, I used an
extra battery so that there was no arcing near the injector spray.
