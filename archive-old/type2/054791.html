Subject: Re: Heater Box Exhaust pipes?

They run along the frame rail to the rear. They attach to the vent tube on
top of the flapper valve. They work with a syphon effect when you are moving
to draw off the heat from the heater boxes with the heat turned off. If you
just have the ealier round vent caps, they just dump heat off at the flapper
valves.
