Subject: VW Show-Chattanooga-12 July

Air Cooled VW Show this coming Sunday, 12 July-9:00 a.m./6:00 p.m. at VW of
Chattanooga, the area's VW dealer. Parts vendors and swap spaces will be
there. Judging starts at 2:00 with the usual set of classes, 3 awards in
each class. Admission is 2 cans of nonperishable food; $5.00 per class for
enter a vehicle for judging. This show is the first event sponsored by the
Chattanooga VW Club and the local VW dealer. We hope it will become an
annual event.

VW of Chattanooga is located just off of Highway 153. Take the Shallowford
Road exit and go east. Turn left at the light next to the Waffle House and
follow the signs.
