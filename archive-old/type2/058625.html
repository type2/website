On Mon, 21 Sep 1998, Dave Groshans wrote:
> ...Now I'm ready to replace the pushrod tube seals and valve cover 
seal.  The Bentley book states that the heads do not have to be removed 
to replace the seals.  Has anyone done this before?  How is the tubes 
removed?  Are they pulled through the head? ...

Yes, it is a fairly simple job.

1.  Remove valve cover, undo rocker bolts and remove the rocker gear with 
the bendy piece of wire.  Keep everything in order on a clean rag.

2.  Pull out the push rods (be careful to keep them in order).

3.  Pull the tubes out, twisting gently.  Grab them with a cloth rag 
exerting minimal pressure.

4.  Remove old rubber o-rings.

5.  Clean everything.  Very important.  Use solvent and thoroughly clean 
the areas where the o-rings sit in.  I recommend putting new push-rod 
tubes in but it is not essential.

6.  Put new o-rings on the pushrod tubes and gently re-insert until the 
satisfying "click" of them seating in place.

7.  Replace push-rods, little bendy wire to support them and then the 
rocker gear.  This part is a little tricky.  If you forget how the wire, 
etc. goes in, open the valve cover on the other side of the engine and 
have a peek.

8.  Tighten bolts to 15 lb/ft and readjust valve clearances (cold engine).

You might find these instructions on various VW archives, but the 
procedure is basically the same.  Good luck.
