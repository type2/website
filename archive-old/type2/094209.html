Subject: Re: [T2] Hydraulic lifters (I'm all ears)

I use a piece of heater hose and put it on the outside of the valve cover
above each rocker arm.  By going down the cover, you should be able to tell
which one is the noisy one.

But what you will find out is, as you replace the noisy one, the next
nosiest sounds loud, so you'll want to replace it and so on.  Better to just
replace them all at once, after all, if one went bad, the others are soon to
follow.
