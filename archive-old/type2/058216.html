    Or you can do like I have now -- the 228mm flywheel and 002
bellhousing with just a bit of magnesium ground off the high spots
inside the bellhousing. Doesn't really take very long either, with just

automatic starter works with this -- neither the '73 nor the '81 starter
works at all.
