Subject: Re: [T2] Red Oil Light

You need to check the oil pressure with a manual gauge. I would not run the
engine until you do so. If you have good pressure, it is most likely a bad
sending unit, or a shorted to ground sending unit wire. I would use a
factory sending unit if you have to replace it, as the pressure ranges on
the aftermarket can be off quite a bit.
