Shane:

I recently wrestled with a squeal when I replaced the brake discs and pads 
on my '76 Campmobile.  After dismantling the front brakes three times I 
finally  installed some aftermarket brake shims (and a second set of new 
pads).

The shims I used were Beck Arnley part No. 084-1122 and should cost you 
around $3-$5 at your local parts store.  Please note that these are not VW 
shims (which retail for around $7-$11 each (saving you up to about $35) but 
will will work quite well.  They are simply a heavy fiber material with a 
sticky back.  They fit exactly on the back of the disc pads on my bus.

Good luck.  Let me know if you have any other questions on these items.
