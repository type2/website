Actually, of course, Karl and Bob are correct, the purpose of the "shiny
side" is to keep oil from the engine from impregnating the seal and it
*ought* to go toward the more likely oil source (that is, down)

for appearances sake, and they never gave me a problem :-)

sense in many years.  Most are extruded "H" sections and it makes no
difference which side goes up or down.
