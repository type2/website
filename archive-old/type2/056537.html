Okay, I'm having a warm start problem, and I was just talking it over
with a friend.  He suggested, "Find out where the voltage drop is".
With a voltmeter, make sure you're getting close to 12 volts across
the Bosch relay, and at the solenoid, and at the starter.

If you're dropping a lot of voltage before you get to the relay, it's
in the ignition switch or the wiring to/from the switch.

That's one thing to check.
