There is a white grease stick that comes in tube that looks like a giant
tube of chap stick.  Great stuff, I use it on everything from zippers to
the moving parts on my hydraulic tubing bender.  Can be used to reduce wear
on door latches and if you rub a little on the rubber seal, the door closes
easier and it won't freeze shut!!

I got it at the local True Value Hardware store in the isle where they keep
the special lubes such as 3 in 1 and WD 40.
