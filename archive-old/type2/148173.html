Subject: Re: [T2] Curious about engine temps

THey are not really related. Oil temp goes up with RPM, Head temp goes
up with load. So if heads are hot, and you rev up the RPMs to get
lower head temps, you get higher oil temps. If oil temps are high and
you drop RPMs to get lower oil temps, you get higher head temps.

Considering your investment, you really can't afford not to own an
accurate Dakota Digital gauge. About $100 plus about $20 for the
sensor.
