Pat is on the road again!!

This morning, I called Pat and told her the best bet may 
be swapping bell housings. When I got there, she had 
already swapped out bellhousings.
The diagnosis was easy on her tranny! FUBAR!  Gear CHUNKS 
along with the drive shaft fell out of the bell housing. I 
am not familiar at all with tranny insides, so I cannot 
really say what the domino effect was to get to that 
point.

waggled, nothing. Dropped the engine and brought back from 
out of the Bus. I couldn't find my clutch alignment tool, 
so I thought the broken drive shaft would make a fine one. 
 I went to insert it into the clutch disc and it was on an 
angle!!  So, we removed the pressure plate and realigned 
the clutch. Back under the Bus. Within 5 minutes... BAM! 
Swweeet!!

But of course we had one more lil deal to work with, 
adjusting the plate at the base of the shifter.(DANG IT! I 
could not find anything on adjusting that plate in the
