Chris:

The pilot bearing is a press fit into the flywheel.  There's no need to 
remove the flywheel to refit the pilot bearing.

The pilot bearing is most easily removed with an appropriate sized 
puller, though there are other methods that also work.  The new pilot 
bearing (a $2 or $3 part) is then "pressed" into place.  The word 
"pressed" is in quotes because everybody uses a socket of the appropriate 
size and then gently tap, tap, taps the bearing into place.
