 the front one is cake to get out. you have to pull the engine and
transmission out to remove the rear shift rod.

2 or 3 bushings depends on the year of the bus. busboys website has the
details on the cutoff, i think, but i am almost certain 78 has 3 total.

the replacement rear bushings are pathetic quality, impossible to install
without doing quite a bit of work to them. they are very poorly molded and
don't fit on the rod. the front bushing i would buy from the dealer, the
rubber quality is completely different between the dealer ones and the
aftermarket.
