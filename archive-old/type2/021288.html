Make sure your terminal posts are really clean.  I'm serious about
that!  Mine were nasty once, and for a year I couldn't figure out why I
wasn't getting any battery power.  It would be on and off.. it was
REALLY annoying.  Changed the starter, checked wiring and then finally
changed the terminals.  worked like a charm from then on.. then I got
rid of the bus... and bought another, of course :)

Also, if you bus got hot, your starter may have temporarily seized..
