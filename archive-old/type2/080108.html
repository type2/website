Subject: Re: [T2] rebuild wont start! any ideas?

I had a similar problem when i rebuilt my 75 FI bus and went to start it. 
Took me the longest time to figure out the problem. This may sound stupid 
but the distributer shaft wasn't in the right position. It was 180 degrees 
off. Scince the timing mark on the fan lines up with TDC twice for every 
engine cycle It's easy, atleast for me, to set the distributer so it ends up 
firing on the exhaust stroke rather than the compression stroke. To fix, I 
just set the engine to TDC, pulled out the distributer and shaft and then 
turned the engine 360 degrees (back to TDC) and put everything back 
together. Don't know if this is the same thing you're experiencing but, 
thought i would share. Good luck!
