It's total hindsight, but I wanted to throw this out for general use.
When putting a new engine in it's always a very good idea to remove
the oil pressure switch and make sure that the engine will pump oil
out the opening. Not only can you verify a pump is primed, but even
more important (like this case) that there is actual oil flow.
