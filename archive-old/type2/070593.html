I may have found the source of the oil leak in the '79 type4 motor 
but I need some help because I do have a Bentley Manual available.

around for the bolts. First I found the bottom one. There was not

not have a Bentley handy to see how the setup is supposed to be. Can 
anyone confirm that there should be two bolts holding the metal

diameter and thread size?
