I dont think the US market ever got the kind of highroof you have in
finland.  You are probably thinking of a factory made steel highroof.  We
have none of those in the states unless they were imported from another
country.  There may be some pre 67 ones, but i doubt there were ever any
post 67 ones here. (unless someone dragged one back from europe...)

the kinds of highroof busses that i think he is referring to are where a
regular bus had the roof cut out and a fiberglass camper style high roof
was added.  They are somewhat uncommon, but i have seen quite a few in my
bussing days... There used to be a 74 or 75 that lived just down the
street from me.

hopefully this clarifies the "highroof" confusion.  Maybe we should call
the fiberglass conversion ones something different to avoid confusion with
the factory models for our international friends.  Anyone have any ideas?
