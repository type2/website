I know this is only somewhat related, but I just had to throw it in. I was
at my local Lowe's Hardware store and was looking around for a knob for my
SO-69 Stove, and saw that they have fuel line in bulk. I looked closer at it
and it is real good stuff, it is rated at 200PSI, and is FUEL rated. It is
less than a buck a foot and had a nice shiny look to it(black). The only
down side is that it is only available in 1/4in, 5/16, and 3/8in, no MM
sizes. I do not know if 1/4in is close enough to 5mm to work correctly or
not, but the price was right. This does seem like it would work great for FI
and Non FI systems.
