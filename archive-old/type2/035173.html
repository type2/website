Jen,

I think the following is correct:

AM Band= "M"
FM Band= "U"
Shortwave= "L"

It sounds like you might have a vintage Blaupunkt or (even better!).
a Becker radio.  Two places can help you with this but the second one
has a much better web site for viewing old radios (an very high prices!):

Continental Imports (Florida, I think):

http://www.continentalimports_dot_com

Walter Odemer, Inc.:

http://www.primenet_dot_com/~odemer

If you visit the Odemer site, click on "antique radios" and see if you
can find your unit.  If not, e-mail Walter for a verification and
repair quote--he always gets back to you in a couple of days.
