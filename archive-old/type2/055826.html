builders...G. Berg and the likes....Leaded gasoline was of a higher Octane
content then today's Un-leaded gas. True or not?  From what I understand even

Leaded gasoline.

The cure for this problem seems to be the lowering of compression in ones
engine.   And lowering the Compression ratio on a stock air-cooled 1600 to
around 6:5-1 to 6:9-1 seems to be the choice of most folks.  I have been also
lowering my compression ratios to this figure on my recent rebuilds (having
seen the effects of detonation on one of my own well cared for, somewhat
short lived motors).  I have yet to run these engines long enough to get a
long term result.
