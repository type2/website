The wire that should be hooked to the electromagnetic cut-off valve runs
from the coil and also the automatic choke and I think the pilot jets. On my
69 its a little simpler, but if the wire is disconnected it won't idle worth
a cr_at_p. Check the wiring diagram in Bentley to be sure I'm not full of it,
then hook the thing up correctly to see if it works, if not then go buy a
new one. If you don't have Bentley I can scan a copy of the diagram from my
book (also I think they are in the type 2 library for some years, and they
are in color).
Hope this helps,
