My advice is stick to stock 94mm pistons and liners.  You are asking for
trouble by going to the larger ones.  To utilize them you'd need ported
heads and probably other intake and cooling mods.  I've never had a problem
with my stock 94's leaking either, so why the extra bolts?  They're for
people running huge bore and stroke, high compression engines.  The average
bus owner doesn't need such things.
