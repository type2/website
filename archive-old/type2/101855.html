Yow!  The pressures on the sidewall are the maximum

> Any tire pressure that is given in the

No, but if the tire size is the same, then the pressures in the manual or
on the data plate are far better than the maximums.  In most buses the

less pressure.  Load range D tires are rated for 60PSI, but there's no way
that you want to run that much on a bus!
