There was a post earlier today in which a person described lowering the
engine some and removing the tank that way. I guess if you have FI you
could do it but honestly pulling the motor is not that big of a deal. On my
78' I leave the trans bolted to the motor and just pop the front trans
mount, mustache bar, and rear trans hangers, a few wires, clutch and accel
cable, and a fuel line and carefully lower the whole shebang on a floor
jack. Install is much easier this way as you dont have to wiggle and push
to get the input shaft into the tranny...
     I bet there are othe rthings you would love to take care of while that
motor is out anyway. Easily done in a weekend.
