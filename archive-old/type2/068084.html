Someone asked:

>How are busses as off road vehicles?

Well, IMHO, buses are pretty awful off-road vehicles (they are NOT off-road
vehicles.

However, having said that, they do have certain features which make them
very good at dealing with poor roads: High ground clearance; rear engine
improves traction of driven wheels; independent suspension (which makes for
low unsprung mass).

The VW bus's biggest failing for off-road (as opposed to poor road) use is
the front overhang -- it simply does not allow the approach angles
necessary for off-road use.

I have taken several of my buses on roads which barely warrant the title
(including some marked "4WD only") and they did very well, tho a limited
slip diff would have come in handy.

VW buses do need some kind of "road" to drive on. However, they are good on
very uneven surfaces and in deep sand, but without a limited slip diff and
aggressive off-road-type tyres, keep well away from deep mud.

My 2c
