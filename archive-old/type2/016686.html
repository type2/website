Chris:
Two things, reverse order:
1. Your last statement that you're about to put the bolts back in and
pull the trans with the engine has me a little concerned.
It's been a few years, but it seems like I would always get the rear
trans mount bolts out,lower the engine and trans as a  unit and after
it's down a few inches you have a bit more room to remove both upper
nuts, the left one ahead of the engine tin, the right one ahead and
lurking lower than the throttle body.
2. Yes the upper starter bolt and the right upper trans-engine bolt are
one and the same! Use a ratcheting 17mm box wrench to remove the NUT
from said bolt.(see above for location)
