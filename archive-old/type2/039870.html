Hello listers, et. al:

I'm replacing the 1 1/2 gal. propane tank in my '76 Campmobile and have 
ordered a new one from a local RV dealer/parts supplier.  It's pricey ($56) 
compared to the Home Depot, gas grill type large tanks.  I'll be replacing 
the regulator and the gas line to the stove top too.  Might as well!

My local propane dealer refused to fill my tank last weekend.  Here in 
Washington State (perhaps it's a Federal reg too) the small tanks must be 
recertified after each 5 years.  Larger tanks are recertified after each 7 
years.  My tank was last certified in 1992 and could not be recertified.  
Large propane companies are, apparently, the recertifiing stations.  Our 
state Ferry System has regs on transporting RV's with propane tanks as well.

Best regards,

Walt Fogle
'76 Campmobile
