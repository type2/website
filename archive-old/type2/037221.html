This works for early bays, to access the fuel sender without pulling the
motor and tank cover.  I understand VW actually did this and sold a seal to
cover the hole.  From the rear, on the metal deck, measure from the left
trim panel just behind the back seat toward the middle 31".  Measure from
where the deck just starts down frontward toward the rear 1.5".  This is the
center point of a 3-3.5" hole you cut with a hole saw.  Attach the wire and
plug the hole with the item of your choice. (I used a kitchen sink stopper,
ha).  Now you are set when your sender craps out. (count on it, it will).
