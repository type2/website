Hi Ian,

I used silicon sealer on the cover side of the gasket for a year or so. It worked great at stopping the leak but was a real PITA to clean off when changing gaskets. I am currently using Permatex Hylomar HPF on the cover side of the gasket as suggested by others on the list. It stops the leak and is easy to clean up when changing gaskets.
