As usual, one part at a time.  Eliminate the ignition switch as a problem

See my electrical rant from several years ago about cleaning *all* your 
electrical connections....including the transmission ground strap, both 
battery terminals, the wires that actual go to the starter, the 
starter contact lugs...etc.

I have found that my 78 FI starts incredibly well if the battery is really 
charged up properly.  The difference after being on a charger and then 
starting it is amazing.  I've noticed this on modern american FI cars as 
well.
