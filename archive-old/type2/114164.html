Hi Karl,

The 74-79 canvas and Fibreglass I have seen are identical. But I have 
seen differences in the latching mechanisms and props in the early 
late-bays.

There is a 74 pop-top on my westfakia and the hold-downs are different 
than the later years.

76-79 components are the same, but I wouldn't bet on the 74 and 75 
being identical; I think there was some evolution of components during 
the first 2 years.
