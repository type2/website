I too use these bottles for my stove & lantern while camping.  I don't
store the bottles in the bus while at home, but when camping, I just
keep them in the storage compartment next to the icebox/fridge when
driving, and outdoors somewhere while at the site.  That storage is
next to the typically packed icebox and never gets that hot.

As a bit of warning, I wouldn't advise holding onto partial bottles.
Maybe it was because I bought an off brand, but twice now I have found
a bottle to leak after having been used (though many times before I
have had no problems).  Best bet is to get a quality refillable one.
Particularly since you may be disposing of less than empty bottles,
make sure you dispose of them properly.  Most campgrounds will take
care of them for you.
