I have attached the product data sheet for the Delo 80W90.  This product
does meet the performance requirements of API Service
Designations GL-4 & GL-5.  The borate based extreme pressure additive
package utilized exclusively in the Delo gear oils will not
chemically attack yellow metals, such as brass and bronze, therefore
making it suitable for applications containing yellow metals.
However, conventional extreme pressure additives that utilize sulfur
phosphorous compounds will attack yellow metals. Thats why it
