 Yes, but there are several different styles of Merc wheel from this 
period (two decades) and (as someone pointed out) two different bus
bolt patterns for a 5 lug 14" wheel. So this advice doesn't actually
help very much.
 Addressing the bolt pattern is just the first issue. Others are:

 (1) Strength
 (2) Offset
 (3) Bolt/stud clearance size
 (4) Nut/Bolt seat profile
 (5) Thickness of mounting surface
 (6) Hub centre clearance
 (7) Wheel nut clearance
 (8) Suspension/Brake clearance 
	(note the two nuts attaching composite rear drums on late bays)
 (9) Compatibility with spare wheel
(10) Availability of suitable tyres

</patient>

 There are probably others I havn't even considered yet.
