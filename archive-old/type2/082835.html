Automotive paint is a big universe and while I would be surprised if it
were possible to make an automotive-grade paint in a latex base, I would
not be shocked.  I think of latex as a very "soft" base for paint, but in
the olden days, flats were latex and gloss was enamel.  Today, latex
enamels are all over the place in house paint.  Chemists have done amazing
things before, and may yet make an acceptable latex-based paint that is
hard and glossy enough for automotive use.
