Well, I can't speak to your problem, since I sidestepped it when
I bought proper VW parts before installing the aftermarket stuff.  The VW
parts are easily available from wreckers (D&D in Sparks, NV had several in
the bin when I bought mine),  I do note that the alternator is a very
close fit on the stand and there is no room for error on the part of the
stand or tin.  The Chinese stand was different in details from the VW
part, so it's not a direct copy.  My belt lines up perfectly.

I believe that the total cost of the used stand and tin was $28.  Well
worth it, IMHO.
