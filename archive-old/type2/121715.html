Right, the relay doesn't have to be hefty.  As I understand it, the 
relay only provides the "signal" input to the starter solenoid.  The 
problem being solved is insufficient "signal" current in the stock 
set-up due to old, tired wiring combined with the current having to 
make a round-trip to the front of the Bus.
