Know that if you do this, there will be several negatives:

1) Your transaxle will be 1/2" shorter than before (your gearshift knob
will wind up closer to the dash than before).
2) Your motor will be spinning much faster on the freeway - top speed will
likely be reduced.
3) You are getting a bushing instead of a bearing in the front of the trans
(where the shift rod comes out).  It will wear and leak faster (ask me how
I know).
4) You are getting a trans that is weaker than your old one in general.

On the other hand, you will be able to drive up a 35% grade.  Maybe you
like that kind of thing?  If not, I recommend that you pony up the extra $$
and get the 091 tranny.
