Try WWW.tirerack_dot_com
They deliver fast and were cheaper than anywhere in town (including Discount 
Tire and Costco). Of course then you have to pay someone to mount them about 
15/each. Overall it was still cheaper for me.

I put the Michelin Agilis 61s on my 80 Vanagon Westy and love the way they 
look. They give the van a nice "factory" high ride/look. My van is up on

lighter, 14 inchers. A couple of folks on the vanagon listserve swear by 
them. FYI- the Vanagon Westy is about 5200 lbs.
