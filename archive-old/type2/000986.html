I recently replaced my filler hose on my '73. I think it has the same hose
as the '72. Anyway, I used the Gates 57mm hose with a 2 1/4 muffler pipe
elbow bent to about 80 degrees (got it from a local Meineke shop - $5). I
found 90 degrees a bit too sharp. Make sure you leave enough steel past each
end of the bend to allow the clamps room to grip and the hose to seal. It
has worked great and I have had no leakage (liquid or fumes).
