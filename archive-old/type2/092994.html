The header pipes are not sealing to the exhaust pipes or not sealing to the 
head.

Could be as simple as a rusted out muffler.

Start it up cold and feel around underneath near the seams in the exhaust 
system - feel for puffs of air. Also look for signs of soot.

repair. I would just take it apart and replace all of the gaskets and 
bolts/nuts. Clean everything as you go.
