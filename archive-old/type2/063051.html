It seems to me that you would need something like two hoses, one for IN, one 
for OUT, with flapper valves or some other flow control device to keep the air 
moving in from the fresh side and out to the spent side.  Otherwise, you will 
be just moving stale air into and out of the hose (unless it is very short), 
except for a very small air exchange by convection along the pipe or tube.  
Maybe a muffin fan on the output to keep the air circulating would make an 
adequate fresh-air mask. I would be careful about putting too much pressure on 
your lungs (either by forcing them to suck or expel air through an excessively 
restrictive opening, or especially by pumping air into them by blowing air 
into a sealed mask over your face).

Maybe a SCUBA guru would be of help in this.
