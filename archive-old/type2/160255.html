Subject: Re: [T2] oil temp sensor

I thought I had a picture that Karl or somebody sent me but I can't find it.  You really don't need one, though.  It has a raised part on it.  You can drill either to the right or left of that raised part mid-way between it and the edge.  That's what I did and it worked fine. If the raised part is pointing up, I drilled to the left of it.   Don't use thread sealer or you'll mess up the ground.  Just use a good seal and it shouldn't leak.  I didn't read the directions good and used the thread sealer and mine was erratic until I grounded directly to it.  If you don't have a good ground, your readings will pretty much be useless.  Good luck...KR
