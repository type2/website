According to VW's engine survey, this engine started out in life as a 1967
40HP (SAE) 1200 (European Beetle).  With the "X" designation, looks like
VW's rebuilt it at least once in it's lifetime.  It is of course, not
original for the 56 Truck, which used 36HP 1200's through May of 1959.
