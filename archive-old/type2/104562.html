From your previous message, you say you have a carb? What happened to your fuel injection?
If you had FI, you would have a rubber hose going from the tank to the drivers side frame rail. There are 2
plastic clips mounted to the floor for the hose to sit in.
The 7.5mm hose goes to the filter, then a short piece of 10mm hose connects between the filter and the FI
pump. Then a short piece of 7.5 hose goes between the pump and the metal fuel rail connected to the 3/4
injectors.
If you have a modified fuel system, no one can tell you what you have unless they see it....
