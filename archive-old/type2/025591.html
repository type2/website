Here's what I'd do. First, click on the link in my sig and print that out.
It contains a parts list for a Type 1 engine. Then start hitting
aircooled_dot_net (John, how is my master cylinder order coming?) and Bus Depot.

Order a case first, and before you do anything else send it to Rimco or
Painter's Grinding to be drilled and tapped for full-flow oiling. Once
that's done you can use a spin-on oil filter on your Bus.

Can you do this with a $1175 Chirco zero-mile engine? Yeah, but you need to
tear it down, which kinda defeats the purpose of motor-freighting an engine
in.

On a Bug, I wouldn't/didn't use the external oil cooler, but if you can find
a good place to mount it, it might not be a bad idea in a Bus.
