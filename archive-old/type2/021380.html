Coke will clean aluminum (heads, cases) nicely due to the phosphoric
acid, yes, but I must take issue with the statement by Jim Mowreader:
"I don't think the little dab of phosphoric acid will hurt your stomach;
search for "pH of bile" to see why."
    -It won't hurt your stomach but will hurt your bones- both calcium
and phosphorus are needed to make bones, but there is a feedback
mechanism that will cause calcium to leave bone when an excess of
phosphorus is present.  It's been shown that "cokeheads" (or consumers
of lots of soda containing phosphoric acid) are more prone to
osteoporosis.  A study of juveniles driking lots of soda a day showed
poorer development of their bones, especially girls.......
