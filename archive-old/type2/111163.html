> Changing oil on one of my toys that required 300cc replacement...  I'm
> conversion handicapped, can someone translate into oz?

If you look at the side of most plastic oil bottles you will see two
scales, one in ml (cc) and one in ounces.

For rough measurements, a quart is about 1000cc.  300cc is therefore about
3/10 of a quart.  3/10 times 32oz/qt is about 10 ounces.

For more accuracy, a quart is really 946cc.
