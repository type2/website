Hi Jen,
There is a special little tool that you can get for just this purpose! Rocky
Mtn. Motorworks has them for $8.95-$9.50 (depending on the year of your bus)
and Ron at the Bus Depot probably has them for less. The part is called a
rear axle nut removal tool (pretty clever) and the part # for the 46mm nut
is 87496. All you need is this a hammer and a socket wrench to steady it.

Have fun,
