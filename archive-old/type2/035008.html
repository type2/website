The 1974 Bus used two different turn signal switches during that year.

Early 1974  211-953-509H  $79.25
Late  1974  211-953-513K  $55.00

Both are in stock.  The best way to get the correct one for your Bus is to
get the part number of of your old switch or to write down your chassis
number.
