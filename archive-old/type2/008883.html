THE WEEKLY GUIDE TO TYPE2 / VINTAGEBUS RESOURCES

Ok, folks, here's some sites that will be invaluable to the Type 2
newbie and oldtimer.  It was created to help reduce traffic on the
lists by providing a quicker way to get that crucial piece of
information that you need to fix your bus.  Always try to check these
sites before posting to the list.

For the folks who have "frameless" browsers, see below for the new
frameless home page.

Don’t fret if some of these sites don’t work yet.  Lotsa dust is
flying at the website!

LIST ETIQUETTE GUIDE:

Here is the short version:  

1. Be Nice.  We're just talking about VW buses here.  Keep it light!
2. Write about technical bus stuff.
3. Other Bus stuff on Fridays only.
4. Never post server commands (like "unsubscribe") to the list.  The
server doesn't pay any attention to the list and it annoys the heck
out of the admins!

It’s not such a good idea to pmail the administrators with messages
such as "the list is down", since there may be 1000+ people in the
same boat.  How would you like to get a couple of hundred pmails in
your box all telling you something you already knew?

Also, for reasons that become obvious if you think about it, you don't
want to post messages to the lists with questions such as "Is the list
down?".

The best way to go is to see if you can read the list via the
Type2_dot_com website.  If no posts get delivered there, it’s a good bet
that nobody on the lists are getting them either!  On the other hand,
if the list seems to be going merrily on without you for a day or two,
then you might have been unsubscribed for too many bounced messages or
some other reason.  Try resubscribing and it'll probably fix your
problem.  If it doesn't, an nice pmail to the administrator might be
appropriate.  

It’s always appropriate to send pmail to the admins telling them about
the great job they are doing.  Remember, they don’t get paid for their
considerable labors, so your kind words are a major part of their
remuneration.
