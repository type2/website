I think Harry may have recommended this method in "Secrets". I use a piece 
of green Scotchbrite pad although I don't see a problem with steel wool if 
you are going to wash the case before assembly. Once the case parting 
surfaces are clean use Curil T for reassembly. Curil T seals well and is 
easily removed. Aviation Permatex also seals well but is very difficult to 
remove even with a scraper.
