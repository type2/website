Subject: Fuel tank in '72 Westy: PROGRESS!

All:
Just thought I'd finish my string on the fuel tank removal, cleaning and installation in my '72 Westy.

Yes I have the engine out - way out.  I bought a fresh AVP 2.0L w/manual fuel pump case.

The tank was a BAS^%&D to get out, carefully at least, but I finally got it out and to the radiator shop for a good boiling and coating ("RedCoat", whatever that is - it sure is RED though...).

I glued all the felt strips and plastic pads back on.  I had removed these very carefully with a razor blade prior to cleaning.

Bentley says the following about installation:
"Installation is the reverse of removal."   ARGHHH!!!

Hey, it went back in a LOT easier than it came out!  Only took about 15 minutes.

If you have your engine out I recommend you remove the panel, get the tank cleaned and coated and put it back in.

This weekend the "new" motor goes in and hopefully I'll be driving my Westy by Monday.
