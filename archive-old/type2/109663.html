Theodore,
Not really answering your question, but giving you something to think about.
I'm planning an extended trip in my bus at the end of the year (6-12 months)
and what I will be setting up is slightly different. In the stock location
for the primary battery I plan to fit two batteries (yes they will fit). One
for the bus, one for aux. power. They will be a little small but adequate.
This does two things. Firstly reduces the load on the alternator and
secondly it frees the other side up to mount a small generator. Honda make
some very small very quiet 4 stroke generators (8 amp and 10 amp). This
combined with a solar panel should provide all my power needs, even it I
don't move for a few days/weeks in one spot.
