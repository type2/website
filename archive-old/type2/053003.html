June 2 - 4 (Friday - Sunday): Fifth Annual Busses on 66 All-VW Bus
camping event. Located at the KOA Kampground (1-800-562-1228) off Historic
Route 66 near Springfield, MO. Sponsored by SAMBA, GROOVE, NEATO and
LiMBO, these three days of VW Bus fun and fellowship are a must for anyone
attending the June Bug Jamboree. For more information call Lance Moeller
at (417) 889-8813 or Bob Hufford at (417) 833-2047 or e-mail
bhufford_at_mail.orion_dot_org

Chesterfield Village is located just NW of the intersection of James River
Freeway (south edge of Springfield) and Kansas Expressway (take Hwy. 65,
south from I-44 -- or north from Branson, MO -- to James River Freeway
(cloverleaf interchange), then west on James River to Kansas Expwy.,
north to Chesterfield Village which will pop up quickly on the left (looks
like old brick buildings).

Or, if coming in Hwy. 13 from areas north, just stay on it as it turns
into Kansas Expressway (as it crosses I-44) and follow it south all the
way across town to just before the James River Freeway and you'll see
Chesterfield Village on the right.

The KOA is easy to find if you plan to camp.  Take I-44 west past
Springfield to Exit 72 and follow the signs (south of the Interstate as
you bisect the golf course).
