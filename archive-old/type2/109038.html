Subject: 72 Westy Fuel Pump Rod problems - Solved!

It was the rod not being able to return back to the cam lobe.
I took the rod, put it in the drill and took a piece of 600 grit wet/dry
a minute later it's a nice smooth fit.
Looking inside the case it looks like they put a bronze bushing in there
Hard to tell now that the motor is in.

Then, in putting it back in I put the gaskets backwards causing a fabulous oil
leak.
Flipped the gaskets around, and now all is well.
