Duke---
This is how I repaired my broken shock mount.

I ran a bolt into the hole--and welded it up--- ( to keep the mig welder
from splattering in the threads ) took the bolt out---and drilled a
1/8th" plate to accept the bolt through it---and welded the plate to the
frame as a gusset 
so it wouldn't blow out again---

I added a plate to the other side too---
No more broken rear shock mounts--

Ground the welds down a bit and undercoated the frame ---

About an hour job--

Good Luck--
