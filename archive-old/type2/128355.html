I've used NAPAs,  They lasted a few years and finally started leaking when
I loaded the bus heavier than normal.  Handling was OK, but I'm not all
that picky.

> by the way, I put a 29 dollar Mex muffle on a 71 Beetle eng.  ...had to
> pressure it a bit to get all the bolts lined up but finally got it...it does
> ok but seems a little loud. I think I need to go back through it and

Yes, that's the problem.  You have to flex and bang the thing to get it
into place and then there is residual stress on the engine and muffler.

Doing it properly by heating and bending the muffler flanges with a torch
is very time consuming, as it is a trial-and-fit procedure with four
different flanges.

Better to get a better muffler to begin with.
