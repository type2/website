On Mon, 22 May 2006, dan radom wrote:

> What size is the nut on the end of the rear wheel on my 78?  It's the
> funky one with the pin through it.  WIll the drum come off with the aid
> of a bead blow hammer, or do i need a puller?  Duroshark do you have a
> drum puller by chance?

The nut is 46mm, but you don't have to remove it to take your drum off.
It is a composite drum, so the hub stays put.

It might be a bit tough to get the drum off, but apply penetrating oil and
wiggle it back and forth and it will eventually come off.

Put a thin film of anti-sieze on the mating surfaces when you put it back
on to make the job easier next time.
