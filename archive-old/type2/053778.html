Since they don't allow you to shoot people just because they need it ("he
needed killin" is no longer a usable defense even in North Carolina), and
this guy does, you may be pleased to know that JCWhitney sells kits
consisting of rolls of about 20 different colors of wire. Get one of those
and a pound of push-ons (where you can get push-ons by the pound I don't
know, and believe me when I tell you I'm looking!) and rewire it one wire at
a time.
