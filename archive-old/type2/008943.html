> Now I'm out of options other than doing it myself. Problem is, I

> later model tanks have more lines coming out of them to accommodate

You can get ready-made plugs for the unused connections or you can
simply use short sections of fuel line with a metal plug pushed in the
free end.  Some folks use a ball bearing slightly larger than the ID
of the tubing, some use a bolt with an unthreaded shank.
