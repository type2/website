The scenario:

Getting ready to drive 120 miles to Tulsa on Saturday morning in the 78 
Champagne editon to go to the VW show.  Drove the bus to work yesterday

The problem:

78 all standard 2liter FI manual.

plenty of power...but! at cruise, I have either a "surge" or a miss...I 
can't decide which....or why!  Great spark to all plugs.  Remember, this 
engine has about 2500 miles since built.

Soooo, I pull it all back out, put the pertronix back in(a whole nother 
story), and go for a test drive...same problem.  The vacuum plate moved 
smoothly by hand, but when I tested the vacuum can by sucking on it and 
watching the plate, it was not smooth...felt ...ummm jerky and "rough".
