On Wed, 20 Nov 2002, Chris Mills wrote:

> >I have a 20+ year old door mirror which needs restoration.  The silver
> >backing is flaking off in many places.  Does anyone know if new glass
> >can be mounted in the original mirror frame?  Or does one completely
> >replace the entire fixture to restore the mirror?  Thank you in advance.

I have a set of the German replacments from Bus Depot.  Several years old
and no trace of corrosion.  However, the plastic lens surrounds
deteriorate in the sun and crack.  I removed the surround, mounted the
glass in the housing with double-stick foam tape, and filled the gap
between the lens and housing with hot-melt glue.  Looks closer to the
original than the plastic lens surround.
