Check the "Got Pole Problems?" heading at http://www.supernet_dot_net/~jclark/vw/loaves.html for some ideas.  I'd guess conduit's strong enough.  Follow the 
canopy masters link at that URL and you'll find lots of solutions.  I've seen an occasional set of canvas-less poles for sale on eb*y.  Another one to check with is JP 
Henriksen (sp?) on the vintagebus list - he made a nice set of homebuilt poles for his Privy tent and may have some suggestions.

>Also: a few of the rubber loop cords which hold the top of the tent to the 
>framework are missing. Are there replacements for these? I've seen mini 
>bungee cords which I think might do the trick, but repop or OEM styles might 
>be best.

I don't think anyone's repopping *anything* for the original _Westy_ tents -- I think Dawn/PLF was repopping sundial/riviera tents but that's all I know of.  For the 
rubber loops, I think a piece of rubber tubing tied/glued/"welded" together at the ends would look really close.

My prob has been more with the plastic hooks on said rubber loops - they break off.  I have a bag of metal S hooks I carry with the tent and just use those as needed 
until I can find something more apropos.  The hooks would be a nice thing to find oem/repro.
