and ghias had a one year only 6volt 200mm flywheel    what years is the motor 
that you are planning to use  If you dont know the year post the motor numbers

also have to change the gen, stand, coil, choke, idle jets if electric and the

,200mm one and wont just fit right on. I would take another 12 volt ,200mm flywheel
