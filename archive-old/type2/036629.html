Subject: Leaking roof

If you investigate the 'Koolseal' website in the US, you will find they
produce a white mastic roof sealer.  It is not available in the UK but this
may be helpful in solving your leak.  As you have a pop top it may well be
that the rivets on the roof panel have allowed water ingress.  It is a
common fault, with all sorts of repair techniques over the years.  Bitumen
impregnated hessian was one I recall.  Things have moved on I am sure.  The
Koolseal product range seemd to offer some solutions.
Contact 'Just Kampers' for a catalogue.  They have a range of replacement
vents available which should be of assistance.
There are other companies advertising in various UK magazines that can
provide apres or replacement pop tops as desired.
