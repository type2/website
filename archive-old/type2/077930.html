You might experiment by switching the thumping tire to the other side (left
for right, right for left) and see if the noise moves;  that would be a good
indication that the wheel / tire is the problem and not the CV joint.
Re-capped tires are less and less available here in the U.S. - maybe you'll
hear from folks in other parts of the world where they're available and are
more familar with them, but I recall that treads starting to separate will
make thumping or unusual noises.

Good luck.
