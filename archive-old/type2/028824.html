They're not expensive, and I sell them.  But in an emergency you can simply
"bypass" the sensor by grounding the wire that goes to it.  This will make
the ECU think that the engine is completely cold (or fully warmed up; I
forget which but if memory serves me correctly it's the former; full ground
equals cold engine). This may not result in the optimal fuel mixture but
will generally work well enough to get you home.
