Do not assume that you will be the last owner of your bus. If you 
maintain it well, you won't be. Someday you will be the PO. So this is a 
prod to future POs not to be pissants.

If you do a non-spec alteration like Hoover's bulkhead fitting, document 
it and put the documention in with rest of the bus's paper trail. In this 
case, just print out the instructions, draw a rough picture, and stick it 
in the package.

If you wonder why you should bother, when the PO before you never 
bothered to do this, just see Plato, "The Republic."
