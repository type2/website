Jim,

I personally believe you would be better served by upgrading your shocks
if necessary to Bilstein or Koni and adding better sway bars as previously
suggested by another listee.
Busses has good clearance and being able to get under there without
a jack to change an oil filter or adjust the clutch cable,or give that starter
solonoid a good whack occasionally is a luxury (relative term in a Type2)
that few other vehicles have.
However if you still want to do the lowering thing,it might be worth
checking out:        http://www.kruizinwagon_dot_com.au/

I know they are a bit far away for you to to drive over and talk to them
but they have a lot of good info and products available to lower
busses safely.Have a look at the website and see what the lowered busses
and double cabs look like at least...
