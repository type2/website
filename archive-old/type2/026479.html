>1. Can I clean and reuse my oil cooler? How? Remanufactured 
>ones are available($120) but why not remanufacture my own?

Just pour some gasolene in each of the holes of the cover, cover them with
your fingers or favorite rag and shake 'er up.  Dump out the gas and repeat
the process 2 or 3 times until the gas comes out clean.  I blow the thing
dry with my mondo-shop vac (DON'T VACUUM it dry!!!), but any compressed air
or even a nice, sunny day will dry it out.  Use appropriate precautions when
working with gasoline (duh!).
