I purchase a new Brazil 30/31 6 months ago for the backup 1971 bus.  This
has a "questionable" 1600DP.  Due to a overheating I had, I decided to
re-jet the main jet.

These Brazil Solex come with a 120 Main Jet -- which is more suited to a
15000 than a 1600, right?

My bus had more power when I went to 127.5 and even went up to 135 -- which
had even more power, but starting to run rich.  I'm running a BugPack Single
Quiet Pack exhaust, which is a factor.

I purchased the "base plate" with mine, which adapts the smaller throat to
the dual port intake, plus is nicely offsets the carb to the left, away from
generator / alternator.

I'm still having the same overheating, but a lot more power :)

If you like the 009 dizzy, this is the carb to use;>)
