On Sun, 28 Apr 2002 milam_at_bellsouth_dot_net wrote:

> I am going to add a CB radio to my bus.  Went to Radio Shack, they have
> one on sale, cool.  The problem is the selection of antenna.  I do not
> what to use a magnetic mount, new paint.  I am deciding between the
> large buggy whip bumper mount and a cell phone antenna look-a-like that
> sticks to each side of the glass.  Anyone using either of these?  Any
> suggestions for alternates?

Neither of these will be ideal, but ideal isn't really possible.

The bumper mount is too close to the body and won't radiate properly.  The
through-the-glass antenna is probably better for you situation, but it is
pretty small for that frequency range.

Better for a bus is a 4' firestik whip (found at truck suppliers) attached
to the drip rail or to the luggage rackk.

The through-the-glass antenna will probably meet your needs for
caravanning.  I prefer FRS, but sometimes your buddies only have CB.
