Nevada doesn't get much good press!  :)  Luckily, these towns have a sense
of humor and don't worry too much.  Winnemucca and Battle Mountain have a
friendly rivalry for tourist visits and feature billboards advertising
such things as:

Winnemucca:  Paved Streets!

Battle Mountain:  Running Water!

Winnemucca:  Cruise the Humboldt! (with a picture of a cruise ship)

(the Humboldt river is a foot deep on a good day!)


If you decide on the I-80 route, you should stop off for a Basque dinner
either in Elko at the Star Hotel or at Winnemucca at the Winnemucca Hotel.
Reno has Louis' Basque Corner, but I prefer the more rustic places.  Take
your appetite!

If you really want to "rough it"  You can go to Tuscarora from Elko and
then cross a lot of the state on the gravel road over to Midas.  Be
prepared to fix and camp!
