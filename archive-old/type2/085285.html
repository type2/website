The correct curtain rods are made out of alumininum, and are about 1/16"
thick,x 1/8th wide, and are attatched by two tiny sheet metal screws on
each end.

The sliding door rod follows the contour of the inner track from behnd

They are flimsey, so don't let your passenger's swing on them when
entering or riding in the rear of your Westy---
