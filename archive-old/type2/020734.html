Hello from the 221,000 mi Westy with never rebuilt engine-- and Yes, I
have all the original FI stuff on it, even the fuel pump.!   Much more
reliable than the fun I had with a 34 PICT3 carb and a Holly-Weber carb
on a 71 bus.  Only problems I ever had were due to some hose sucking air
after the AFM (when it's intermittant, can be frustrating- and that one
turned out to be that little black y connector buried near that black
breather box.  It doesn't have a lot of bite and any end crack can make
it leak.  Which makes me wonder- could one use the smoke stick radon
mitigators use to look for air flow in cracks in your cellar slab?  You
could see where smoke was being sucked in.
