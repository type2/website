when I was unable to get the runner boots I took the metal tape (about 4-6
inches wide) that is used by homebuilders to seal foil based insulation.
This stuff puts duct tape out to pasture.  I wrapped a piece of this over
the boots all the way around (PITA) and secured the ends, on either side
of the boots with a wire tie,  cleaned it up and it looks almost factory
spec to the uninitiated.  and better yet, made a perfect seal!
  I found the stuff at Home Depot, not real cheap but I use it for
everything, hardly touch my roll of duct tape anymore.  (will even seal
water leaks, and  makes temporary rust patches)
