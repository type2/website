Hey Randall,
	I don't know the details about the Pert. ignition, but with the
Crane that I have you'd better not use a Bosch Blue Boy!  It has an internal
ballast resistor which is a definite no-no.  I'm running a crane coil as
well.  If I'm way off on that point, perhaps the brand of cap/rotor is the
issue.  I was in the AP biz for years, and I have discovered that not all
tune up parts are created equal.  I'd stick with the allmighty Bosch, the
Nascar/Borg-Warner, NAPA's Echlin line, Accel, etc.  Stick with something of
quality.  Many are just plasticky junk, but the Big Boys use heat resistant
materials, and the B-W stuff even has brass contacts.  I've also replaced my
cap with a watercooled equivalent that has posts instead of holes for the HV
wires.

	Eron Crites
