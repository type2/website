Sounds like the engine is getting hot, and tightening the valves from
constant expansion and compression, a normal and deadly behavior from a type
1 engine, in a bus, with rebuilt heads....

The valves will begin to stretch, and drop, or they will drop a seat..Find
out why the engine is getting hot, and the problem will be solved with a
valve adjustment....

I see this all the time!
