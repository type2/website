Darrin:
    The rapid clicking sound you heard with the key on "on" was more than
likely an electric fuel pump. If you crawl under the bus and search for the
noise you will probably find a little metal box with a couple of fuel lines
attached (or perhaps a confused woodpecker). I have replaced the little
kickboards in front with mahogany door skins (I've seen others on the list
who have done similar things with various types of wood), they are cheap,
look great and can be cut easily with a sharp utility knife. My bus' PO used
fabric adhered to his old kickboards with contact cement that looked ok,
too. That is the extent of my knowledge. If you have a Bentley manual it
should tell you what switch is which (if you don't you should get one they
are an invaluable resource).
Hope this helps,
