>Hi guys,
>Has anyone installed a fuel pressure guage in a FI bus.
>I was looking at capillary tube type(non electrical) oil pressure guages the
>other day wondering if one of them could be used.Being able to view fuel
>pressure from the drivers seat seems like a good idea.

If you use a mechanical fuel pressure gauge, be sure to use one with an
isolator so that fuel does not enter the passenger compartment.  It is a
major safety hazard and might even be illegal.  In the racing world, it is
not done.  There are gauges that have an isolator outside the passenger
compartment which "switches" the pressure from gas to a more inert fluid
which could be used, but they are a PITA to get working properly.  My
advice would be to go electric.
