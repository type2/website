I had a similar experience with my 82 vanagon, had to take off the shift
housing and use a prybar to get it out of 3rd. The trans was totally out
of oil. Put shift housing back on, put oil in, worked the shift lever
back and forth, it still works 2 years later. However, these are also
the symptoms of the dreaded 3rd-4th synchro hub self destruct syndrome, 
but I think that happens with 150,000 plus miles and more often 2.1
liter Vanagons (maybe more stress), though the parts involved are the
same as on the early Vanagons. For more info look at
http://gerry.vanagon_dot_com/archives/vanagon.html
