If you are really careful, you can split vacuum hose (sold by the foot)
into a 'C' section and use that. It will stay on reasonably well. It seems
to like to fall apart after about 2 years, though. Mabye it was the
rubber formulation I used. Windshield washer tubing would 
prob. also work. The hardest part is splitting it in a straight line
for the 15 inches you need. I prob. wasted 5 feet when making 30 inches
of the stuff.
