Turning the little screw in leans out the mixture.  By turning it in until
the engine stumbles you are finding the point where the mixture is just too
lean, winding the screw out one turn from that position richens the mixture
to where you want it to be (it works out that way).  To get even more fuel
economy unwind the screw even less than a full turn, just make sure the
engine isn't overheating to the lean mixture.
