Gary:

I'm standing here looking (not literally) looking at the engine from
my '81 van, on which I've just installed new motor mounts.  I don't
know what you mean by clearance between the oil filler tube and the
support being "minimal"; there's only about a 1/4" between the two
on my motor, and I'll bet that shrinks once the weight of the engine
is supported by the mounts.

I would think you could work on the mounts without removing the engine.
I would loosen the trans carrier bolt, support the engine with a jack,
remove the bolts between the carrier and the chassis (first supporting
the engine with a jack) and then lower the engine/trany enough to get
to the mount bolts.
