I finally have my EGR system installed on my 75, thanks to Karl's'
assistance in locating parts. This list and the people on it are great!
Now that all the plumbing is in I have a problem with it. When I let off

gas. I suspect the vacuum in not connected to the correct source. Looked

valve lapping compound to ensure the plunger could seal well. All ideas
are most appreciated.
