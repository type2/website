There are two things I know of that normaly cause what you discribe.
The first is the Condencor, these go out and cause the engine to shake and
buck. It will seem to run fine at an idle and then only get worse as you
drive. Stopping to cool things off helps for a little while, but it comes
back. 
The second is only if you have FI. There is a Temp Sencor #2, it tells the
computer that the engine is warm or cold. If it fails the engine do what you
discribed, but only when warm. It will run fine when cold. Look in the
Bentley manual for more discription and testing of this. 
Both are cheap and easy to fix. A condencor is less than $10 and the Temp
Sencor is about $25-30 and easy to get at.
I do know that there are other possibilities, but these seem to be the most
common failure on a Bus. I have had both fail on my Buses. The best
investment you can make is to get a Bentley Manual, it will help you test
and fix your Bus.
