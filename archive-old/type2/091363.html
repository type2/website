On Thu, 5 Sep 2002 Gunnys_at_aol_dot_com wrote:

> on a stock pully for a 71 bus can anyone give me the measurement from the TDC
> notch to 7-10 degrees BTDC ... thanks for the help!  scott

If it's a real '71 pulley, you have a notch for 5ATDC and a stamped mark
on the opposite edge for TDC.  Measure this distance, then measure 1.5
times that distance the other way for 7.5BTDC and twice the distance for
10BTDC.
