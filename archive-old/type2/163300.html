True as well.  Brazil and Mexico were the same way for VW, Bosch & Hella.

(VWG/Airhead Owner) goes to China several times a year in the efforts of
maintaining quality control of the manufacturers we deal with.  We're quite
confident that in the case of the CV Joints VWG/Airhead offers that they're
not dealing with a "GEX" type of company, at least as far as I can tell.
Nothing's perfect of course, but quality control is an on-going thing and
it's a job that's never done.
