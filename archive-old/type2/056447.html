I saw the plans for AutoStadt 2000 while in Wolfsburg this past January and
a few weeks ago as well. Not only is there going to be this amusement
park/hotel, but the VW Museum is going to be in a new building at the site
which is about three times the size of the current museum.

The opening of AutoStadt (auto city) is to coincide with the Hanover Worlds
Fair in 2000. Right now Wolfsburg can only be reached by local train
service from Hannover or Brunschweig. The Deutsche Bahn is putting in an
ICE (InterCity Express - bullet train) line between Hannover and Berlin and
one of the stops along the way will be Wolfsburg.
