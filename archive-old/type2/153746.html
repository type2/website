You said you have a '78 so the timing mark on the *pulley* is on the FRONT 
side of the pulley; it's a little tiny thing, sort of like somebody took one 
stroke with a hacksaw to the pulley edge.  Find the mark and enhance it with 
a little white paint so it'll stand out when the timing light "stops" it 
when the plug fires.  You then compare where the mark is when it's "stopped" 
to the timing scale.
