Hi Gary,

very good chance its the nut that hold the wiper arm onto the rotating spindle has worked loose. Just pop the plastic dome cover off and tighten with a 10mm spanner.

If it's not that then possibly the splines are striped from the end of the spindle or in the arm in which case the 'offending' part needs to be replaced.
