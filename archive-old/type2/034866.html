A word of caution:  many bits will drill an oversize hole.  Hand drilling
makes it worse.  Small bits in a cheap set are occasionally (often?)
mislabeled as to size.  Solution:  drill hole in scrap metal, find bit with
shank that will *just* fit, measure bit with micrometer.
