never to order parts from afar when time is tight.  To do so will get
you disappointed sooner or later no matter what supplier you use.

wayside.  I doubt that the heat exchangers you get from RMMW are

in order.  JC Whitney used to guarantee delivery of your order within
6 weeks and would usually have it to you in a blazing month!
