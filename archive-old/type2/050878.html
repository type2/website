>Can anyone fill me in on my airconditioning? This has a large compressor? on

>complex vent system attached under the main dash up front. There are 6 (I
>believe) adjustable vents on this system with a min/off/max knob on the left
>side, and a temp? knob to the right. It does not appear to be working, but

The compressor is mounted "backwards".  The belt is behind the fan housing.  Unfortunately, it's a narrow, non-standard belt and I would be willing to bet that yours is missing.  Last I checked the belts were still available at the dealer for about $20, but be warned that replacing the belt might just be the beginning of the costs associated with getting the A/C working.
