I used to take motorcycle trips every Spring.
I once wrote a magazine article titled "Cure for the Blue Funk"
which talked about what to take on a trip.
It applies to anyone camping, no matter what their vehicle
of choice is. Here's what I said:

First, take all the stuff you think you need, and spread it out on the garage floor.
Then separate it into "essentials", and "nice to have's"
Then throw away 95% of the stuff, and take twice the money.

It works for me  :-]
