I recently had this problem in my '65 in that all of a sudden I couldn't get
the bus into 1 and 2, but R, 3, and 4 work fine.  I haven't changed my
linkage yet, but did find a way to get around it.  I push the shifter all
the way to the right while it's in neutral ( the middle of the H partern )
and then left and into 2, and then finally straight up into 1st.  May help
you get around until you have time to fix the linkage.

Get yourself a Bentley manual and a Haynes manual. The information is in
there. I had a similar problem with my '71, went through and replaced all
the bushings and the shift coupler. Not hard to do (and I was able to
replace the shift coupler without touching the transmission, contrary to the
books). Better, but I still had a problem. Tried loosening the two bolts
that hold the shift lever to the floor and shifted the shift lever base from
side to side, problem solved. You'll have to experiment for the best
position.
