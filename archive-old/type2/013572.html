Sounds like an idea worth looking into.
After many visits to auto wrecking yards I've never seen them sell an
engine access hatch so they should be cheap.
I'll bet you could forego the welding and just cut the perimeter frame
from the donor and pop rivet it in place with some good double sided
insulating tape for a seal. Then drop your hatch cover down.

I've been working through the big hatch on an 83 Westfalia for seven
years and have installed heads and cylinders with the engine in the car
(Oh! my aching back.).

If you make this retrofit you'll love it.
