I asked the same question about our 72 Westy. I got mine for $400. and I
thought WOW, it wont take much to fix it up, no body panels to replace.

Any way less than a month latter we have spent over $2,500. just trying to
get it on the road, I'm waiting on a AVP engine and other things and that's
why the bill has increased so much. We did have a budget set and we have
come very close in this short amount of time.

The wife, the dogs, and I have gotten in the bus and had a meal, we've
played cards at the table and we have even taken a nap on the bed, we have
already enjoyed our bus without it being on the road. We plan on moving it
to the back yard during Christmas and having an official Camp Out. Any one
want to join us, it's called the Murray Back Yard Annual Christmas Camp Out.

So.... if you have $2,500 to $3,000 for a bus project I say go for it. IT
WILL BE WORTH IT......
