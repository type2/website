Dawn recently went through some personal problems, which put PLF somewhat on
the back burner for a while.  This resulted in lengthy delays on some

circumstances. When people order PLF canvas from me I usually tell them to
allow a good month (whereas I can get canvas from other vendors within
days). On the other hand, she makes an exceptionally good product, so it's a
trade-off of time for quality.
