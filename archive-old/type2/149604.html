I've used it to soak rusty engine tin in. Works great but don't soak 
stainless steel. The acid will at least pit it or disintegrate it very 
quickly. I don't know what kind of metal the bails that hold valve covers on

to soak something in diluted muratic acid make sure it's not made of 
unobtainium.
