Correct, the ecu controls the time the injecter is open, not the pressure.
BUT the ecu has NO idea of the AMOUNT of fuel going thru the injecter. More
pressure = more volume. The ecu only tells the injecter how long to stay
open. If you have low pressure, you will have low volume going thru the
injecter for a given open period. If you have high pressure, you will have a
higher volume going thru the injecter for the same given open period.
