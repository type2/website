The flow through this tube is quite low, driven only by the pressure
differential between the exhaust port and the muffler outlet.  I don't
think that it will make any difference if you simply cap off the
flanges.  Be sure to take care of the holes in the tin as well, as
they are a big cooling air leak past the hottest parts on the engine!
