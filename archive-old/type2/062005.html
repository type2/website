It's not all that weird, but I wouldn't go and buy extra heads just yet. 
On my '73, they messed up the head when putting in the seat (and peaned 
it over to make it "stick". "Oh, that's normal. Don't worry, we do LOTS 
of these heads, and they never fail.").  It failed in 8 thousand miles, 
during the move to my new house (of course). 

You can try having a local shop weld up the heads, then turn then down 
to the proper size (if they do LOTS of VWs... such as some of the hi-pro 
shops in CA). Personally, I'd find a good supplier and get a rebuilt 
head. Inspect it for obvious defects (like on mine), and return the old 
one for a core.
