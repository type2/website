Well the first warm day of summer & the list went alive with the good old
"Is my oil O.K, what is the general opinion of the list?" punctuated by the
faithful hole filler,"What tires should I use?".

But I here you say "You sentimental fool! What about now?". Well last year
the same as this year echoing across 5 decades, several frozen digits & a
misty wind-shield the immortal question:-

"WHAT ABOUT HEAT!!!!!!!!!!!"

Tales of frozen significant others muttering unspeakable suggestions
involving F*RD's & water cooled engines, I'm sure will follow. Along these
lines "What oil should I use in winter?" will rise up & defy the great &
holy FAQ. Even the  diverse "What tires are good in snow?" will cause our
resident swimsuit model to set his latest Terry Prachet asunder, to vent his
anger on the defier of both the great & holy FAQ, but the inspired word of
charter!

 Yay! though you walk in the valley of Scrap yard, you will not find a
working BA6. Nor will you find the secrets of the fuel metering system, for
it is written "The last git will destroy traces of a mint BA6 while getting
that 50 pence rubber bung from the scrap yard". And may God have mercy on
your soles! (coz there gonna FREEEEEZ!).
