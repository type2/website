I've had good luck with Rockford boots.  Two years and no visible
cracking.  Much better than the Lobro and Qualitat boots I tried
previously.

http://www.rockfordcv_dot_com

One caution:  the rockford boots are a modern design, without the
accordian convolutions of the originals.  The axle end of the boot should
be placed outboard (toward the CV) of the locating "lumps" on the axle,
instead of between the lumps.
Otherwise you will stretch the rubber too much at full extension of the
suspension.

And, of course, YMMV!
