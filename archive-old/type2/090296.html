Hello List,

The subject of gas cut foo valves made me think of the
cut off valve used on my motorcycle's gas tank. Here
is an article about converting a generic petcock to
use on a sx650. Any way, I thought the picture could
be helpful. I know that these are made to screw
directly into a gas tank but you could just take a
large diameter piece of fuel line and slip it over the
threaded end with a hose clamp. then, you would just
need to buy one of those plastic fuel line step down
fittings and connect that to your fuel line from the
tank. If I am not mistaken, the filter on it comes out
if it makes it easier. This little gadget only costs
about $15.00 from what I understand. 

Here is a link to the article.

http://www.650motorcycles_dot_com/Petcock.html

I have not tried this conversion but it might be
plausable. That is my $0.02 worth. Have a great day!
