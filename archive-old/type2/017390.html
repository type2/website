Joe:
There should be absolutely none; no loosness is acceptable, it will just
get worse. The forces between these 2 parts are not very even ,
harmonic, perhaps (unlike that of the oil pump drive, which is constant
and in one direction only). So if there is loosness it will get worse.
I'd really just consider tightening the rivets with a hammer and an
anvil or rivet set. Of course, maybe you can just get him to replace it.
There are about 6 or 7 different gear sizes to selective fit the lash;
how did you decide which one you needed?
I'm also of the opinion that maybe you should tap the cam holes for
bolts, face off the area on the gear and attach the two with bolts.
By doing this you could use your old gear and keep the lash correct.
I'm a little mystified how the aftermarket handles this problem - VW
shows 7 different cams, the only difference being the gear size.
