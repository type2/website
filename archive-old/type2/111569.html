There are a couple points where I would differ.  On an absolutely unknown
engine, I would adjust the valves to .008 rather than .006.  They might
clatter, but they would run and there would be no chance of burning them.
I would also insist on 15" of vacuum. 10" will get the engine running,
but it would not idle there and would overheat in short order.
