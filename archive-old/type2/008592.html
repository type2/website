It is doable, and not really a bear if you do not know anything else.  
 
You can add one though, I saw one a few months ago.  Cut the access hatch
and surrounding metal out of a 73 or later, and cut a hole (slightly smaller
than the piece you cut out of the donor bus) in your 72.  Lay the donor
piece over and weld/jbweld/glue it on.  The guy I saw used JB Weld and
silicone to seal it up.  

I bought a 75 bus (with bad motor, no FI, shot interior, no passenger
window, and nasty dog smell inside) for $125 for the tires ($75 for bus, $50
for the tow home).  I just got a reciprocating saw (mmmm power tools:), and
am going to do the engine access hatch swap.   

Yes the 75 is repairable.  I am going to seal up the hole I make in it, and
either stick an old (but running) engine in it and clean it up to sell it,
or sell it as is.  Of course I need to find the tittle first.  At least
