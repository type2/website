Can I get it re-keyed to my current locks?
Yes, I am certain that you can.  It is likely to be pricy, unless you do
it yourself.  The process is fairly simple (Nikki even taught *me* to do
it :-), but the problem is that you have to get the cylinder out first.  
The cylinder is usually retained by a fairly fine roll pin on VW locks.
Sometimes the drilling for the pin goes all the way thru, sometimes it is
a "blind" drilling.  The drilling on the lock Karl sent me is of the
"blind" variety.  That is the pin is easy to press in, and I have no idea
how you would get it out!  Perhaps it could be drilled out with a fine
bit, but I would want a competent machinist to do this.  I sure ain't up
to it.  Alternatively, a hole could be drilled from the other side to
provide access for a punch to drive the pin out.  I dunno... I just put
mine in and added the key to may chain.
