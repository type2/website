I'd recommend the perusal by a fitting shop witht he correct optical
instruments as well (I think they are called "Optiflex").

But another thing it could be is that one of your tyres is distorted. I had
this once, not on a Bay window bus but another VW Van, and the vehicle
pulled stronly to the right. When we swapped over the tyre, it pulled the
other way. The mechanic with his keen eye spotted the distortion, but I
couldn't see it! However when the tyre was replaced, all was well (I had had
the tracking and wheels balanced at the time).
