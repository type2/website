Hi Mark,

I know of no-one on the list who has installed a Jeep 2.5L engine in  
a 68 type2. More common engine transplants come from porsche,  
corvair, toyota or inline-4 watercooled VW's (gas or diesel). Whether  
it will fit largely depends on exhaust, electrical and intake  
plumbing, which are impossible to guess at since they will be unique  
to your install.

Most people on this list are going to tell you that the best engine  
to use in a 68-79 type2 is an air-cooled horizontally opposed 4  
cylinder. The original engine is adequate for any load the vehicle  
can safely carry, although it probably won't deliver the fuel economy  
you desire at the speeds you wish to travel. Spend money on  
performance improvements, instead of a conversion.
