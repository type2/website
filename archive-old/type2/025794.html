Yeah, but it's a pain in the ass.

Start by removing the brake drum, then the shoes, then the star adjusters.
Clamp them in a vise and, using a big-ass pair of vise grips, crank on them
until you get the screws broke free. When reassembling, just dip the end of
the screw into a little axle grease (get about a thread into the grease,
don't coat the whole screw) and screw them in and out a few times. Put a
touch of grease on the outside of the adjuster for easier movement.

Or remove the old ones, throw 'em as far as you can, and put in new ones,
greased of course.
