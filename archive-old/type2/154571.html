Rent or borrow a gas generator. The smallest should work with an AC sawzall or a skilsaw with a metal cutting blade. I used both to remove the entire front of a '67 bus. A quality, non-Harbor Fright cordless sawzall should be able to do the job. Take extra batteries and blades.
