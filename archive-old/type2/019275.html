Last year when I rebuilt my 2L, I encountered a similar problem the day I
went to re-install the engine. The stud didn't break off; it broke "out".
The head material around the stud gave way thanks to being weakened by the
use of an oversize Helicoil repair (not me!). The machine shop that my
mechanic uses came and picked up the engine, hauled it back to their shop,
welded up the broken area, re-tapped it, inserted a new stud and returned
the engine, all in a couple days time.

Total cost: $75.00. Aggravation level: none, after the intial swearing.

I'd take it to an experienced shop, but see if they can replace the stud
rather than weld the exhaust to the head. (Did I read that correctly? That
DOES sound barbaric!) Price it and compare to the cost of a new head.
