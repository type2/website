Hi Blake,

You should never use a hose clamp on your CV boots as they are unbalanced. The clamps used had a compression section in them which although it results in an imbalence is much less than a hose clamp.

In the abscence of a prober CV boot clamp then a wide electrical tie wrap will do the job - cut the end off as close as possible to the ratchet grip to minimise the imbalance - which as a tei wrap is very light will be minimal.
