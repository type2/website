I thought I might weigh in on this thread.  I have yet to install the 
sump sender I bought.  So I looked at it again and to me the sensor part
doesn't touch the case at all.  The VDO directions say to drill a 9/16"
hole in the sump,  the sensor portion is 1/4" wide and at least 3/8" to
1/2" above the sump plate.  The sensor is secured by a nut threaded from the inside(top) and not from tapping and threading directly to the sump.
I'll see if my gauge is accurate by throwing the sensor in some boiling
