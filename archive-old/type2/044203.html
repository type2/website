take a closer look, this fastener is not a bolt, it is a stud.
and a tricky one at that. it is a step stud, meaning it
has two different size threads at opposite ends, with the
additional tricky section which centers the rocker shaft.
it comes in only one grade of hardness, and is not found
at fastener supply or hardware stores.it is a dealer item.
