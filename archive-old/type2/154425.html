You are confusing two different terms, even though they refer to tensile strength.  

Grade anything, 5 or 8 etc., applies to SAE bolts.

8.8, 9.9, 10.9, 12.9, 14.5 applies to Metric fasteners.

A Grade 5 SAE bolt is equal to a 8.8 Metric bolt. 

Here is a chart: http://www.boltdepot_dot_com/fastener-information/Bolt-Grade-Chart.aspx

[I like how in metric, they don't make anything as cheap and soft as a plain head SAE bolt!]
