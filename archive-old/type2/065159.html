At 10:44 PM 10/4/96, Randolph G. Shaner wrote:
>Hello
>I have a 74 bus with a 1800cc carburated engine. I would like to
>set the valves but the pulley does not have any marks on it.
>I removed the inpection plug but could not get my head in there
>to see anything. Where are the marks supposed to be on a type4?

There is a single notch for TDC on the pulley, but it is really hard to
see.  Best bet is to try and catch your fingernail in it rather than
looking for it.  I think it's  on the back of the outer part of the pulley.

>I know where they are on a type1, but can't seem to find them
>here. Also, the manual says to adjust the intakes to .006 and
>the exaust to .008. I had never heard this, and would like some
>opinions on it.

That's the right way to do it.  Gives the exhaust a larger margin of safety
since they run hotter.

>Also, which valve is intake and which is exaust.

Intakes are the two in the middle (think of how your intake manifolds are
positioned).
