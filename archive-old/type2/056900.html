VW WON'T BUILD PERFORMANCE CARS .... Volkswagen canceled plans to build a
12-cylinder high-performance sports car, following the acquisition of
Lamborghini, according to "The Detroit News". VW was concerned that
customers would not pay the $169,000 price tag for a car with a VW badge.
