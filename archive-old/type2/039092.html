Stop trying to use the lock!  You will break something!  I found out the
hard way!

What has happened is that a small spring has broken in the lock mechinism.
This spring makes it so that the door automatically unlocks if it is
closed without the trigger depressed.

The fix is to replace the spring.  See the following article in the Type 2
Library:

http://type2_dot_com/rvanness/lock.htm

While you're in there, be sure to clean and lube the lock cylinder and
lock mechinism.
