Mike,

This specific problem has actually been discussed in a previous 
tune-up/dist/timing thread on one of these lists.  If you have are having a 
problem with your dist. not returning all the way to the correct_idle_ spark 
advance, it needs to be disassembled and cleaned.

Dirt, crud, etc. is causing the springs, weights and other advance parts to 
not quite return to their "at rest" low rpm positions.  Disassemble it to the 
degree that you are comfortable, clean it up good with solvent, work 
everything around well and relubricate (per Bentley) with a bit of motor oil.
