It is probably from the fiberglass top as I had something very similar with
my '79.  Check the condition of the rubber seal that goes around the base of
the fiberglass luggage rack over the cab.  If there is an air gap between
the bottom of the seal and the body, then that's the problem.

Pull the seal down until it contacts the body all around and this should
take care of the problem.

Sean Bartnik
Springfield, MA
