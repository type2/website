of a nasty, grimy, mildewy, moss-covered poptop before.  I just spent

scrubbing with ENTNT (degreaser/cleaner) and bathroom foam spray.

I don't know why not. I cut the bleach about 1/2 with water and slop it up
there with a mop. Let it sit a few minutes or until it is obvious that it's
done its job and then hose it off and finish washing the car. In our Atlanta
humidity count on doing it every other month or so til cool weather takes
care of the mildew.

Since yours has been neglected you probably ought to pressure wash it to get
the barnacles and heavy grundge off first.
