Hi there, One thing worth checking as far as the overheating goe's, is the
air intake at the back of the engine. I once made the mistake of keeping a
rag wedged beside my battery, for wiping my dipstick, the trouble is it
somehow made its way to the air intake at the rear of the engine! causing a
parcial blockage, thus causing overheating problems, the moral of the story
is *never* keep any type of rag in the engine compartment of your bus!
