If you have a stock engine here's the short "upright exhaust" guide...please
get a stock exhaust!!! Use copious amounts of penetrant on the head studs
and care in removal.  Likewise use a bunch of anti-seize on every nut and
bolt in reassembly.

The correct parts for a 68-71 are:
211 298 009A muffler kit
211 298 055 damper to tail pipe kit
211 251 301A bracket
211 251 051 E muffler
147 251 157 damper pipe
211 251 237B tail pipe
