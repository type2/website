heat conducted out to the finned sump and loweing the reading- how do
they insulate from that?  The sump has different surface temperatures
when in traffic or the open road; same if it rains.  The dipstick sender
I made from a meat thermometer hangs in the oil without touching the
bottom.  Yanking the sender and getting the dipstick is not such a big
deal, and I do it at every gas fillup.

temp.   MB  227,000 mi on original engine
