Taking a few ramps a little faster than intended, in the rain and/or sloppy
snow, provides a brief but intensive tutorial in Type 2 handling

In my experience with a '68 hardtop Westy and a '70 poptop Westy the
ultimate "stance" is definitely understeer.  It can be manipulated by
judicious application and release of the brakes.

Oversteer can be induced, especially at higher speeds, but ultimately [at
least with stock ride height, suspension, and tire inflation] understeer
rules.
