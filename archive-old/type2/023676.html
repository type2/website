Depends on which instant polaroids you mean - they have a new product
out - Pop Shots (disposable polaroid captiva cameras) which cost around
$20 pre rebate - you get a rebate when you send in the used camera (the
postage is already paid - polaroid supplies you with the mailer) you get
10 shots - a regular polaroid camera can average $60 and a pack of film
is $20 - one pack of polaroid film is 10 exposures - sometimes you can
get a twin pack for $20 - it would be cheaper and the images would be
better quality of you got a couple of the low end APS cameras - I've
seen them for around 10 dollars - the film is around 6 and processing is
another 5 to 6 dollars (maybe a little more) - image quality would be
better for the web - plus kodak could burn the images to CD for a
minimal price - just can't help myself when people start talking cameras
and photography (BFA in Photograpy, with emphasis on digital media).
