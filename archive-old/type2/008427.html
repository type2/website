John,

Regarding the deck height on the 2.0 Vanagon, I recently put new pistons
and cylinders on what appears to be an previously unmachined case and
stock crank and had a deck height in the 0.035" range. I had planned to
leave the gaskets out at the cylinder bases, but with a deck height of
only 0.025" with this configuration, I thought better to include them and
bump it up to 0.034 to 0.035".   Wilson mentions 0.040" being the
minimum, but then again there is several comments that do to apply to
Type IV's especially late Type IV's (oil baffles, bolting in oil pick-up
tube, etc).

I searched the Bently and Haynes manuals, but no mention of deck height. 
I called the VW shop that put my crank together (30 years with an
unlisted tel no. - they must be pretty good!) and when I told him I had
0.035", he said "Your there now you can have a beer!" So based on the
opinion of one, 0.035" seems fine, but

 **Does Anyone Have a Factory Deck Height Specification For a Vanagon
2.0?***
