GL 3/4/5 meets or exceeds pressure resistance specs for all 3, but the
additives are corrosive to Brass syncros, so it will eat them up... figure
cut your tranny life in half. So technically they can put 3/4/5 on one
bottle but its misleading...

Get stuff where the highest rating is gl4, if it says Gl5 anywhere on the
bottle run away!  Straight GL4 is available at NAPA, Pep-boys, and jiffy
lube (bring in your own cotainer and they'll fill it, but you are better
off finding a NAPA or a Pepboys)
