when i was looking through the library on the type 2
website i was reading about the timing and the type 4
engine does not have a specific mark it will line up
with like the bug motors.   whenever ive adjusted my
valves i just work off where the rotor goes and ive
had good luck with that.   as for your tin, i would
try and secure that back in place, those old german
guys put it on there for a reason so id keep it.   i
actually was just searching in the archives about
engine tin and was reading a post from bob hoover (vw
guru) and he was telling of the importance of those
very tins you speak of...so fix em up and put em back
on.   i was missing some tin on my bus engine when i
first bought it and was learning about it, and that
engine does not exist anymore...so i try to keep my
tin in good working order.
