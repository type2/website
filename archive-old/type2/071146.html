Hi Ray,

Now I cann't claim to be a brake expert - but the following may be helpful.

With a 74 there will be a pressure regulator between pressure cylinder and the
rear brakes. SOME of these have a bleed valve on the top of them so if you
have one bleed there before the back wheels.

Also the way the regulator works is by a ball bearing sliding forward when the
rear end of the bus lifts so if you are parked facing down a hill the
regulator could be already cutting off the flow to the rear wheels. so bleed
the rear brakes on the flat or facing up hill.

Good luck
