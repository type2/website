Just picked up the plastic coathanger pieces that mount into the '67 Westy
just behind the driver and passanger's heads. Part number 255-857-629-581 is
still available through the VWOA dealer system. Each side has a two piece
config to make "the coathanger." Specifically this is the white plastic
trumpet shaped item not the aluminum coat "hook" that is typically in the
Westy closets etc.
The cost was $1.88 each. They were speccial order and didnt take long to
arrive at the dealer. I ordered four~ two for the RFH and two for the stash.
