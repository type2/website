Joe,


I disassembled, cleaned and reassembled both of the calipers from my
78partsbus for use on the 74.

Not a biggie.  Inspect everything for damage. Careful with the rubber parts. 

You can use very fine (600-1000 grit) sandpaper to clean the bores and
pistons.  

Clean everything with soap and water and then dry it all thoroughlly after
using the fine grit and maybe some steel wool to get all the buildup off of
the steel parts.  Be certain everything is absolutely clean and dry before you
decide to reassemble.

Lube it all up with DOT3 and slap it back together.

If none of the parts are seriously damaged (o-rings ripped into, pistons or
bores gouged)  it will work fine.  Follow your Bentley.

You can do the same to you master cylinder.

have fun,
