Subject: Re: turn signal trouble

It seems to me that you might have a combination of problems in order to
get that symptom. First of all, I had a friend point out to me that my
brake lights were different brightness and that I probably had the brake
and turn signal wires reversed on the unit. Sure enough, and when I
changed them around my blinker light in the dash started acting correctly
on the side I swapped. The second thing I suspect you have wrong is that
your brake and signal wire is also swapped at the blinker unit causing the
light on the other side to blink at the same time. A color wiring diagram
will be your best friend.
