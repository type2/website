You have not lost part of your distributor, but rather part of your 
distributor cap!  Cheap to fix :-) -- Pop the cap off, and check out the 
center post from the inside.  That spring and bullet are supposed to fit 
into the hole that is now there.  The bullet contacts the rotor and 
sends the electrical power that is to be your spark thru the rotor, 
where the rotor sends it to the appropriate spark plug.  So either pop 
the bullet and spring back in the hole or be a big shot and spring for a 
new distributor cap.  Get a new rotor while you're at it :-) -- Bosch 
please :-)
