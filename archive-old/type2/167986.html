Subject: Re: [T2] Heater control box to accordion tube cuffs

Keep in mind that the Bus or Vanagon "cuffs" were not originally supplied as
a replacement separately with the accordian hoses.  It's only later on when
VW finally learned of the asbestos problem that they started supplying the
silicone "cuffs" for the Type 1 and Bus to 79 Accordian Hoses (the Vanagon
red ones were originally silicone, beefy looking pieces as shown on
ratwell's site and never have been available separately new).

Your can get the proper silicone (grey) for the Type 1 and Bus to 79 here
(plenty in stock):

(111 819 945 OE)
http://www.airheadparts_dot_com/viewDetail.asp?strMasterCat=4251&idproduct=5659

Or you can keep searching for that junker 80-83.5 aircooled vanagon to rob
the beefier red ones from ;-)
