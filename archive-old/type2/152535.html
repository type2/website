Karl,
I am glad you are onboard this thread. After my research I think that
not all 1971's came with dished camshaft gear.

I believe "early 1971" came with dual port and flat cam and 21mm oil
pump. While "late 1971" (starting in May 1971 - Bentley chapter 5,
section 17) came with dished 4-rivet cam and 26mm oil pump. This
matches with the Bentley but Wilson got it wrong. My 1971 is an
early-1971, manufactured in 09/1970.

My wild guess is that VW discovered in May 1971 that the dual port
engine with its larger carb and doghouse oil cooler could also use a
larger capacity oil pump. So the "late-1971" got the 26mm oil pump and
that is the last upgrade that would go in the early baywindow.
