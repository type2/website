Know all ye Type I motor heads that most have been assembled improperly.
The carb pre-heat tube that runs from no.4 exhaust pipe to under the carb
and across to the no. 2 exhaust pipe will not flow warm exhaust gases with
the same gaskets at the pipe flanges. One of the gaskets must have but a
5mm or so hole only, so a differntial in pressure will create a cross flow.
If your not set up this way, you are throwing away 5% or more in gas milage
and will not get the immediate benefit of fast warmup from cold start.
