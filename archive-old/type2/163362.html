Subject: Re: [T2] where to find pressure plate bolts?

Steve -

My experienced advice is to obtain the correct bolts from VW themselves,
they've been the only source I can trust on this style of clutch cover
bolt.  I don't know of any aftermarket vendor that carries the correct bolt
(except maybe Bus Depot).  Order 022 141 197 C - (Bus Depot shows them for
$1.81 each).  Note the photo - these have a special head for the 215mm
flywheel and you should not trust your clutch to inferior grade strength
bolts or improper hex heads.

http://www.busdepot_dot_com/details.jsp?partnumber=022141197C
