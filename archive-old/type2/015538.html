If I was u, I would take it back and explain, "Jeeze, this isn't running
right. Could you 'untune' it so it runs at least as good as it did before
you fiddled with it???"

Better yet, find a mechanic who won't do "tune ups" but will follow
Bentley, RE: MAINTENANCE INSPECTIONS. The mechanic should always drive the
vehicle BEFORE and AFTER working on it to confirm results before returning
the vehicle as "fixed".

If it was running too rich, he would have seen it as carbon fouling on the
plugs. Also, if he was following procedure, the mixture would have been set
per spec, keeping in mind compression balance and also with fresh oil. If
you don't have these things in mind, you get "tuned out" not "tuned up".
