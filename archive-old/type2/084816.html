If it is really cold right now where you live you might also be dealing 
with a carb that is freezing up.

If not, be sure you have made a trip to the junkyard to accumulate the 
parts for a proper air cleaner and the hose they use to grab heated air off 
of the exhaust manifold. I fought with one of these a few years ago for a 
friend and it was hell.

Just look for anything with a carburetor. They all had factory "heated" air 
cleaner arrangements. You'll have to find one that fits and they usually 
use a small vacuum line to move the flapper inside.
