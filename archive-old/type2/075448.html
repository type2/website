When this bus was last running did the charging system work?
If so I would double check the hookups.
The wires at the regulator are easy to mix up in that dark corner.
One goes to the dash light and the other goes into the alternator harness.
One is blue and one is black.
Easy mistake to make.
Nothing sucks like putting a new $200 part in and having the same problem
you started with.
