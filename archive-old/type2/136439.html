  Look for rust! (no pun intended.) battery tray area is one trouble
spot to look at.. depends where you are at as to how severe rust

work..

then you may have headaches down the road... invest in a manual and
look at the pics in it vrs. what you are seeing on the bus.. such as in
the engine compartment... the closer to stock the better.
