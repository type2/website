At a quick glance:
Catalytic converter on a Fed model will be in-line with the muffler. Cal
model, it is in-line with the left heater box.
Fed model has an 11 pin double relay, Cal has a 9 pin.
Fed has resister pack on left side of firewall.
Fed has points in distributor.
Cal has electronic ignition.
Fed has an AFM that ends in part # 025
Cal AFM ends in 030.
