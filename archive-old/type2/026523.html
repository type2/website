Gotta disagree with that one.  An 009 will hit max advance way below 3500
(ie about 2700 - 2800).  I don't know what the advance curve is with a stock

running mine on my dual-Weber-ed 1.7/1.8 hybrid (don't ask) for 8
years/70,000 miles and I know it is right.

issue?  It is important enough that the person who asked the original
question needs a correct answer since the wrong one could destroy his
