Yes, it should run continously.  Just yesterday I was pressing the flap
open to keep the pump going to hold pressure.  Then I would test the
injectors.  It's a much easier way than cranking the engine to get the
pump to turn on.

I would check and clean all the contacts involved.  I would highly
recommend using CAIG's DeOxit and ProGold to clean and restore all the
contacts.
