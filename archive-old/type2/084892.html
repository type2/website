The engine and tranny have to be separated but the engine does not have to 
come apart if it is the rear main seal (which it sounds like).

It will simply require engine removal, flywheel and clutch removal.

New seal - grease it where the seal touches the crankshaft.

Back together.

You can get this seal from any FLAPS.
