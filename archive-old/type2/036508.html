I didn't see anyone reply to this, so I'll make a suggestion. I assume you 
are trying to bolt the exhaust to your heater boxes.  You will need to loosen 
up the heater box studs on the heads, install the exhaust and slowly tighten 
everything up.  Don't force anything here, take your time and if it won't 
tighten up, then its possible that your exhaust has a flaw.  You may need to 
drill your flange holes out a little larger.

You can also run into problems trying to loosen the exhaust studs.  They 
often break off and your simple exhaust replacement may turn into a nightmare.

Good luck.
