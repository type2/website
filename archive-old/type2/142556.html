The thermo time switch (TTS) controls how long:

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/FI/42-TTSSide3.jpg

The element will warm and reduce the cold start valve operation interval based on ambient temperature.

It's mounted on the engine like so:

http://homepage.mac_dot_com/ratwell/.Pictures/Engine/49-ThermoTimeSwitch.jpg

The initial air temperature will govern how long it will initially operate. As the engine warms, the interval decreases because of the heat radiated from the case.

TTS is connected to terminal 86 of the double relay which is powered by Terminal 50 on the starter and/or ECU pin #4. See N17 and F26 at the very right of the diagram:

http://66.34.72.138/wiring/bus-77-78.jpg
