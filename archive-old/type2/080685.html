Chris,
	Just be sure to mark everything.  About a year ago I made the
mistake of pulling my dash board out because I had a short and the
wiring was messy.  I did not mark any of the wires before disconnecting
them from the fuse box.  This was where I screwed up.  Even with the
Bentley and other resources I could not put it back together myself and
ended up paying a full $1000 to get it put back together.  Yes, I have
learned my lesson.  :)  What saved me was a few pictures from a very
helpful member here in the list.  I received these only after the shop
spent many man hours on the vehicle that I still had to pay for.  Mark
everything, take lots of notes, and read a lot about what you are going
to do, and you should be ok.  Good luck.
