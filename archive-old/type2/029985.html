My '71 has the same holes, but no stop.  I have noted that the cable has
quite a bit more travel than the carb needs to get to full throttle.  I
adjust mine so that the pedal hits the floor just before the bellcrank on
the carb hits its mechanical stop.

If the mechanical "stop" in the system is at the carb, you'll put a lot
more stress on the linkage, cable, etc.  Then you'll find yourself with a
broken cable in a parking lot in Elko, Nevada.  Don't ask me how I know
this :)
