Errr...did you see this bizarre internal battery problem with your own

Like others I am very suspicious of this. You had symptoms of a
high-resistance starter problem (drive hard, let the bus sit a few minutes
and heat-sink, then try to start it back); chances are you had a corroded
ground connection like the rest of us get twice a year it seems.

The problem is that fixing a ground problem is nearly free, it requires
only a wrench and a wire brush, no consumables. The "remedy" is a new
battery, doubtless with a hefty markup.

You should hang out here and learn to diagnose these things. I wouldn't go
near that shop again, to reward them for teaching me an expensive lesson.
