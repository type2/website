Subject: RE: Sticking a tap in a heater box

You can't just tap sheet metal like that, it doesn't work.  The heater boxes
have clinch/weld nut inserts on them (like your engine tin).  You might be
able to use a self tapping screw, #12 maybe, or weld a nut on, but it needs
to go on the inside so things get tricky.  Do you know any mice with welding
skills?
