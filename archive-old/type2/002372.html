Here is a quick test to check valve timing:
Remove left, 3/4, valve cover. Turn engine in direction of rotation and
watch #3 valves. You will see nothing move for a while. Then you will see
the exhaust valve open and close. Just as the exhaust closes the intake will
start to open. Stop. At the point of the exhaust closing and the intake
opening #1 should be at TDC and your timing mark should be on zero. If
neither happens, your cam timing is off.
