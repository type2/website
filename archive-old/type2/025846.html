If you really have a Mexican engine your compression ratio is so low that
you can safely go higher. But you're bored out, you don't know what your CR
is, you don't know what you have. Bob Hoover used to call the process
"work-up", you advance the timing then drive, do this until it pings, then
back off. Summer is different than winter.

A nifty EMPI degree pulley is worth the money.

Sometimes my wife can hear ping while sitting in the back seat even though I
think everything is fine. Get somebody to listen in the back.
