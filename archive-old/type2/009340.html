Leslie:

Most of the publications we have studied on oil temperatures for air cooled
engines indicate that 180F to 220F is the best temperature range to be in.
230F is not too bad for the freeway.  270F is too high.  You might want to
check your timing and compression.  Adding a second oil cooler is also an
option.
