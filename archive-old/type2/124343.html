Subject: '77 Riviera's first trip

After almost 7 months, my '77 Riviera was ready for it's first big camping
trip.  I've got some photos linked below of the bus, campsite and
surrounding areas near Rockport in Northern California.  My wife and I
loaded up our son and left Pleasant Hill Friday night, stopped over in Ukiah
at my folks house, then left Saturday morning at 6:00 a.m. for the long haul
up the Redwood Highway to Rockport.

http://latentanalogy_dot_com/rockport/rockport.html

Thank you so much to the Type2 list members, your emails and the archive
answered any question not covered in the manuals when I was resurrecting
this bus. My bus is far from perfect, but it's camp worthy and most
importantly, safe to drive for over 6 hours straight!  Many times I almost
gave up on the project, but it has all ended up being worth the effort.
There is nothing better than waking up on the beach in a bus you've built up
yourself.  Well, except maybe getting your camp packed in under an hour
while your relatives are still trying to get their 5th-wheels and motorhomes
ready to roll.  :)

Thanks again everyone!

Cody
