I had exactly the same problem on my '70 with a VDO tach. There is a very
cheap fix for this that involves splicing a little tiny resistor, available
at Radio Shack for .70, in the line that runs from the coil. I am sorry I
don't have the exact part number for this, but you can find it in the
library or archives. Works like a charm.
