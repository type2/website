At 11:43 19/04/99 -0400, Timothy J. Hannink wrote:
>I am looking for a spare 4 speed transaxle for my 75 bus. Would prefer
1975-1979 vintage.

Be careful, 75 transaxles are not the same as 76-79 transaxles.  75 is an
oddball 002, and 76-79 are 091's.  091's are 1/2" longer in the bellhousing
and the transaxle itself (1" total) and require different starters,
bellhousings, and flywheels.  The gear ratios are also different.  It can
be made to work, but you can't just swap one for the other directly.
