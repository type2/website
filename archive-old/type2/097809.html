match lifters and cams . even if you are doing a patch job you always put the

lifters wear all the way till nothing was left. As for solid verus hydlics 
solids give more power , more hassles also. hyldrlics work fine less power 
less hassles also. best thing you can do to a Porsche 914 as adjusting the 
valves on these badies are a PITA.  Later and have fun  Mark Dearing Salem
