    You don't have to break those exhaust studs because of frozen exhaust
nuts, unless you want to!

    Take a very sharp chisel, place it on the flat of the exhaust stud nut
aligned with the stud, strike (that's Yankee for hit it with a hammer) it a
couple of times in the same place, the chisel will leave an indention, along
with this indention, it will spread the nut slightly, just enough to loosen
the threads making the nut very easy to remove, saving the stud. BTW discard
the nut when reassembling.
    If you can get them the factory nuts (with the heil coil insert) are
easer to remove next time. They come in a factory muffler installation kit,
along with the right screws for the sheet metal, etc..
