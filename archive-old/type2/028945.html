A dead give away is the chassis number the third digit is the last digit in
the year. i.e. 218 bla bla bla is a 68 or 78. FYI 68 and 69 have only 9
digits and 70-79 have 10. Be careful with the engine code here as both 78
and 79 are GE. 78 is GE 0 007 083 thru GE 0 039 331 and 79 is GE 0 039 332
thru GE 0 058 786. The exhaust on the 75-78 has a triangular flange on the
rear of the heat exchanger that the exhaust pipes attach to. Good luck.
