Wanted to share a few of the finer points of curtain making:

-Sewing machines are pretty cool.  Especially 60 yr-old Singers.

-9 yards of fabric, 48" bolt is plenty of material to do double-thick
curtains for all side windows and back, including the inside of the
antilanechange closet,  but lacks enough to do the front surround.

-it does have enough to make some cool coordinating pillows and napkins,
along with stylish tie backs for the curtains, though.

-Your wife will call your mother and tell her that her son has taken up
sewing.  Raucous laughter will ensue at your expense.

-Your wife will discover that the curtains look pretty nice, and will
start to get really fired up about accessorizing and the "look" that you
will have at the next campout! (ulterior motive)


This is a do-able weekend project, and involved very little going-outside.
As I had an illness and it was quite cold, this worked out beautifully.  I
highly recommend this project to anyone with a sewing machine and some
time.  

Now it's off to go look for some more cheap fabric to recover the seats!
:)

HINT:  Shop the remnant tables and stores!  My fabric cost $.80 per yard!
