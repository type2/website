congratulations, you don't say what engine they put in but this is pretty
generic. when I rebuilt my type IV last summer this is how I was taught

(remember no valve adjust as it is hyd, and I have an oil filter)

using a good quality 10W-40 multi weight oil
for the first 1,000 miles do not drive at a constant speed and try not to
pull any really tough hills or overly high RPMs.

. turn on engine, run for 2-3 minutes,
drain oil' change and filter
. turn on engine, run at different RPMs for 15 minutes (no drive)
change oil and filter
. 100 miles 
change oil and filter
. 300 miles 
change oil and filter
. 1000 miles
change oil and filter, check head bolts (set valves)
. 2000 more miles 
change oil and filter put in 5W-50 synthetic motor oil, full tune up

go to my regular maintenence routine


my routine and it is pretty much standard

enjoy your wheels
