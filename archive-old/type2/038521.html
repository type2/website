but the lights I want to replace are clear... Also,
the 3,000mcd is only available in red. The other
colors drop substatially in intensity/brightness from
there.

I suppose I could go with green LED's behind the
turn-signal indicators and generator light, blue
behind the high-beams and parking light indicators,
and red behind the oil-pressure light....but what

my selection to red, or blue....neither of which
sounds too appealing!

Also, if I went to a colored-LED, I'd do my new VDO
guages as well, so...red is out, as the red-needles on
my new VDO's will register as black (right???). Blue
would only be slightly better (I imagine they'd be a
bright purplish color)....still not a good option.

My whole point in attempting this, is to not only be
able to see my guages better at night (especially in
traffic), but to get my stock guages on par with the
'brightness' of the new VDOs. As it is, the new VDOs
are so bright, they overpower the old ones, making it
hard to make a quick glance at the speedo and be able
to read it without waiting for my eyes to adjust. Does

So...I may just end up putting the new VDOs on a
