 There are distinct differences between the;
211 829 193C (1964 to 1971) and the 211 829 193E (1972 to 1979) rear hatch
seals.

 The 211 829 193C has sharper upper corners, a different profile (close but
a little different) and different notches on the bottom. The 211 829 193E
has  more rounded upper corners, different profile and different notches on
the bottom.

  I just went out to my storage lot and looked at both a 1971 and a 1972 bus
with original rear hatch seals and verified the fact. It does appear that
either seal would fit in any year of bay window and it really would be a
matter of how anal retentive you want to be concerning the seal.

  I do stock both numbers and they both have the VW logo and VW part number
molded in them. I do they get directley from a source from Germany and have
been told that these are made in Germany for VW.
  The 1950 to 1963 part number is 211 829 193 up to chassis no# 1 144 281
