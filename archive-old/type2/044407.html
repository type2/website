On all 3 of my 77 Westies, when you move the right heater lever all the way
down with the key on, the fan runs.
The lever has a gound connection that closes the relay mounted to the blower
bracket.
There is no connection whatsoever with the regulator.
I just looked at the wiring diagrams in Bentley for 73 thru 78. No
connection with the regulator there.
