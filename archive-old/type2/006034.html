Here is a link to an essay on complete rebuilding of the EGR system/exhaust 
injection system on the 1974(?)-1983.5 Type 2 motor. Hope it helps.  The 
only changes:
In step #9 the "intake distribution pipes" I speak of are the tiny (3-4 mm) 
pipes inside the intake manifold that inject exhaust into the intake 
stream; NOT the 30mm intake runners.
In step #20, change "?back off the adjustment one-half turn?" to "back the 
adjustment off one-and-one-half turns". The reason: If the EGR injects 
exhaust too early in the acceleration curve, it will cause a slight, 
predictable hiccup when you try to smoothly leave a stopped position.
