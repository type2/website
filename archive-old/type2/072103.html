Hi Todd,
This is usually caused by the water running down your door windows past the
scrapers and into the door, then it runs out by the seat base and onto the
floor.  If the plastic sheeting is present and correct behind your inner
door panels, the water should pass harmlessly out through the holes in the
door bottoms by the step, so if you have never had the panels off, now is
the time to check.
