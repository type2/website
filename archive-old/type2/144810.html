for the long.

still struggled putting it back together correctly.  it wasn't until i
recalled someone mentioned the cvj stuff in the type2 library.  after

together correctly.

the rest came pretty easy from there.  three turns of the wheel and i was
washing my arms and hands and changing clothes for the test drive.
