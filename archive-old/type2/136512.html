Subject: Re: [T2] CV dished washer

On Sun, 23 Jan 2005, Will Anderson wrote:

> what's the deal with Bentley recommending to leave out the dished washers on
> the inside of CV joints??  This is the first time I've ever had them
> completely apart.  I'm replacing torn boots, the joints on this side seem to
> be very tight still.

Apparently, the snap rings tended to pop out if the spring washer was
used, so they deleted the washer.  If you're pulling the CV then leave the
washer out, but I wouldn't pull the CV just to take the washer out.  After
all, it's been secure in there for a very long time!

Remember that you only have to remove one CV to replace both
boots.  If one boot is more torn than the other remove that CV so that you
can clean it more completely.  If the boot isn't torn, then all you have
to do is wipe up the grease with clean paper towels and repack the CV.
