>  What is, in your opinion, the most useful, indespensible, 'changed your

Various books suggest that a spanner with a bent shaft is usful for removing
the carb on a 1600 single carb upright engine - personaly I've never found a
problem with a straight spanner. But I had some spare cash one day at a show
and bought a set of curved spanners (curved like a crescent moon). I think
they only cost about £8 UK ($12 USA), but their usefulness is far beyond that
price - giving access to lots of ackward corners. 

So for unexpected usefulness far beyond the price payed my vote goes for
crescent shaped spanners.
