Matt,

You may want to start here:

These are the timming instructions for 009 BOSCH DISTRIBUTOR......

You need to set the timming at 30 degrees before top dead center *BTDC* with
the 009 distributor.  to accomplish this you must 1.  Connect accurate
tachometer or tach/dwell meter to the engine.  2.  Connect stroboscopic
timming light to cylinder # 1.  3.  Run engine at 3200 rpm.  4.  Loosen the
distributor housing and turn it until the 30 degrees Before Top Dead Center
*BTDC*  mark, illuminated by the timming light, aligns with Top Dead Center
*TDC*.  Tighten the distributor in this position.  5.  The static or
iginition timming recommended by VW may or may not be correct after
performing these steps.  DONT CHANGE IT, full advance has been acheived by
the aforementioned procedure and would be corrupted.

Also, since VW cases are not inscribed with timming marks, I recommend
having a  FLARS determine where those marks should be with a degree wheel,
prior to installation.

Hope this helps.......
