Hi er-
I have recently found similar- after looking at the fuel hoses they appeared
OK but when I drained the tank and started dismantling, the one that attaches
to the steel fuel line above #3, broke easily. I had also previously put a
grommet on the metal line where it goes through the firewall but noticed that
there were two grooves in the metal where it had previously rubbed against the
tin. I am replacing the metal fuel line and probably doing the "Hoover" mod to
avoid future problems. My advise to anyone would be to replace the whole fuel
line and check the steel tube when you get a new bus and you are not sure of
its history!!! .. better safe than sorry..
