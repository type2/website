Subject: July 1998 CostCo tire report, $48 for 6-ply "C" load house brand

I searched the archives and the most recent reports about Costco
indicated that the Uniroyal Larado C rated 6-ply tires were the
choice... but the last post with Costco anywhere in it was 9 months or
older...

So I went today, half expecting that they didn't carry the Uniroyal
anymore... and they don't, at least not here in Seattle.  What they do
have is their house brand tires, "Kirkland".  They have ONLY ONE
SUITABLE TIRE in the entire store, they don't have a 14 inch LT tire of
any other brand or size.

I went for them, as the $48 price was decent.  These are 6-ply (2 on
sidewall) "C" load tires, more than suitable for my '71 bus.  195/75R14
... which seems to be the main size that most on the list are going with
(based on my archive searches).  $7 to have them installed, no charge
(at least in WA) to have old tires disposed of.  Westy owners,
especially later models (1972+) may opt to go elsewhere to get "D" load
tires (higher load, typically 8 ply).

I'll report if I experience any problems.  Drive great so far.

  Stephen Gutknecht
  Renton, Washington

P.S.  They actually use a torque wrench to install the lugs.  At least I
