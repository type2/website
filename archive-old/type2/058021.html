Subject: rusty front beam swing arm

When in grad. school I had the dreaded "20 deg. of free play" in the
steering.  Swing lever housing was completely separated except at the top
front edge!  Wrapped wire and rope around it, limped it home, and laid
under there with a welding torch to weld 1/8" steel bar completely around
the torsion tube & the bushing housing in a diagonal double pattern.
Worked until I could find a junkyard beam for $60!   I've seen fixes with
giant hose clamps just to get one home....This is a prime spot I check
(scrape off all grease and look for signs of rusting out).
