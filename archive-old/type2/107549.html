If the AVP engine came with the flywheel mounted then I'm sure they put one
in.  If you used your own flywheel then you *still* might have one since
they're easy to overlook; it may have been there in the flywheel all along,
you just didn't notice.

I believe the felt washer or seal is there to help keep grease from
migrating from the pilot bearing out to the clutch.  I'm not sure how "bad"
it is not to have one, but I sure wouldn't drop the engine just because of
that.
