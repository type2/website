Hi everyone,

Well, things are progressing nicely for the "Busses Nowhere Near The Arch #19"

sights.  I'm already starting to load up the BNNTA event truck (an original
paint, low mileage Sealing Wax Red '59 Double-Door Panel) with all the event
supplies, as well as a nice supply of various parts and tools just in case
anyone limps into the event.  The e-mails and phone calls are picking up as
usual, and we're getting excited to see all of our new and old VW friends and
their Busses for the long Labor Day weekend!  We hope to see some of you for
the festivities very soon!
