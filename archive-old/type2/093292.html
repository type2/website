My '49 Chevy simply used bolts through the bed.

We used stainless allen head screws when we restored it. I suppose you 
could get a bunch of little rubber washers from FLHS (Friendly Local 
Hardware Store) and put them between the slate and the bed. I would also 
consider a spot of tub and sink sealer under the head of each bolt. Maybe 
follow that up with a rubber washer on the underside of the bed above a 
flat washer above the nut.
