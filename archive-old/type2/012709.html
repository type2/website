The Bus came with out a padded dash in Europe. I do not know if it was all
buses or just some model. The padded dash is basicly the same as the non
padded, but they added a few extra hole to allow the foam pad to have something
to attach to. If you grind off all the pad, you will need to fill all of these
holes. A lot of work, but possible. Take a look at the Euro Bus web pages and
you will see what I mean. Also look up under your dash and you will see all the
holes you will need to fill.
