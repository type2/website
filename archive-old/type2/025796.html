That bumpy coating under your bus is probably an asphalt-based spray-coating
which is used to help protect the bus from stone-chips and deaden the sound
at the same time. At least that's the case with my '79.

"One-Step" prefers to be applied to clean, grease-free surfaces. Applying it
to road grime or asphault under-spray won't do a thing, you have to clean it
and give it time to dry first.
