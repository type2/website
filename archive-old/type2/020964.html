> The single relay essentially goes in line between your
> alternator and your aux battery, triggered by your ignition switch.  When
> the ignition is off, the relay it opens, isolating the starting battery from
> the camping battery, so no matter how dead your aux battery gets it cannot
> kill your starting battery.  When you turn the engine on,  it connects the
> aux battery back to the alternator to be recharged.  You hook up anything
> you wish to run while parked to the aux battery (via a fuse) and this way
> when parked they run directly off of the aux camping battery.
