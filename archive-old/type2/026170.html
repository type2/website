The hitches for '68-'71 Busses are a direct bolt on part.  There are
threaded holes on the outside of the frame extensions for the outer 2 pieces
of the unit.  Those 2 pieces hug the frame above the bumper brackets.  Then
the center bar bolts between those 2 pieces.  The tongue has a bolt hole for
a matching hole in the bottom of the bumper.
