Subject: Re: [T2] Painting muffler

I don't want to be the bearer of bad news, but prep as you may, and
paint with what you want, the paint just isn't going to stick, on a
exhaust system.

Why?

Don't know.

The heat just turns the paint to dust after awhile and it will flake
off. ( even VHT )

The only thing I know of or am aware of that will stick to an exhaust
system is ceramic coating them, and this process isn't cheap.

Painting them with anything will give you short term results.
