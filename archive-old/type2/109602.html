Subject: vacuum level

Well I tried my junk yard decel valve find today. No go. It drove the idle up
to about 2k rpm. Good thing I only paid 5 bucks for it.So I did some more
exstensive testing. As per the Bently I removed the hose from the valve to the

then released the throttle I got vacuum through the unplugged hole. So the

missing something? I only have problems with the high RPM's when the engine is
warmed up. I hate vacuum leaks! What is the correct vacuum level I should
have?

For good news at least I found out why its running rich and getting poor
mileage. Fuel pressure was at 40lbs with no vacuum. New regulator goes in this
weekend.
