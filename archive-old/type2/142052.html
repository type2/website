Subject: Just a thought about holidays

John, you say, "...the swaps tend to include vehicles..." and I am not sure 
that is necessarily the case.  I guess it's a matter of where you draw the 
line.  While it would of course be more economical not to rent a car, there is 
the matter of liability in case of accident.  I think that exchanging cars as 
well as living accommodations would worry most people who own a house and car --
 certainly it would worry hell out of me.  Probably a better idea is to work 
out reciprocal guest arrangements: A visits B for a week, then B visits A at 
some time later.
