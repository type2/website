Perry,

If you're going to Phoenix to check out VW's, whether it's a bus or a camper, 
be sure to check with LeSueur's VW in Tempe.  It's at 1109 E. Curry Rd. just 
east of Phoenix.  Tel. 480-968-6611  They bill themselves as the largest 
unofficial VW dealer in the country.  When I was there recently they had 7 or 
8 buses and campers, as well as about 30 other VW's.  They are not an 
official VW dealership.  All the vehicles are reconditioned and checked out 
used vehicles.
