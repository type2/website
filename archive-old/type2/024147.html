I cannot comment on the quality of Metrinch tools but I quickly realised when 
I bought my first van 9 years ago that imperial sockets and spanners were a 
waste of space in my toolkit. I removed them, and stashed them in a cupboard

expensive set of dual purpose tools would end up being a waste of money when 
a cheeper metric only set will suffice.
