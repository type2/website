> 3. ANY HELPFUL HINTS ?

Remember that this car was designed to be put together by teenagers :-)
Look at all the VW mechanics who advertise that they "worked at the
factory" or were "trained by VW" and remember that all these buses are
20-50 years old!  Subtract 30 years from the face of that mechanic and you
are looking at a teenager!

Sure, VW had lots of very experienced workers who did a heck of a job, but
to be sure that any worker could be assigned to any task, they had to
design to the lowest common denominator... i.e. your typical 18-year old
boy :-)  And the first time they walked thru the factory gates, the didn't
know a damn thing about fixing or building cars either!
