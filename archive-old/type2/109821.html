Subject: Re: [VB] Which clutch & TO to use??

If the to bearing is the early style you need the clutch with the ring on it 
to match.  otherwise it makes a horrible noise when you step on the clutch, 
and I guess will eat up the TO bearing.    I like the 9 spring clutches.  Feels 
better to me.  Think they cost more, but worth it.  I am assuming the tranny 
IS original and clearanced for 12V FW.   BTW, good time to check/replace teh 
starter bushing, and greas eup that clutch cable threads/make sure teh wingnut 
spins so you can adjust your clutch easily when the motor is in.
