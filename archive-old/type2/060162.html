      Certainly must have blow-by if the dipstick is blowing out. You might
check to see if the breather is clogged -- hose kinked. Disconnect the
breather hose from the throttle housing end and blow *into* the crankcase to
make sure it's clear. If you just replaced the fan end crank seal, then what
is happening is the pressure in the crankcase is blowing it back out. One
other thing to check is the fan hub that goes into the seal -- it might have a
groove worn in it. But sounds like blowby.
