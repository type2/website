On 11/3/05 10:49 AM, "Ahlgrim, Kevin L" <kahlgrim_at_iusb_dot_edu> wrote:


> Does anyone have any other ways to check the compression on an
> automatic?

Do a leak-down test instead. You don't have to spin the engine, only rotate
it by hand. You need the tester and a compressor. Do a google search for
"leak down tester". You can make one for about 30 bucks give or take. It
will cost more if you want fancy gauges, but a rudimentary one can be made
for really cheap.
