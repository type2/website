I wanted to thank the quick respondents on this issue and summarize
suggestions:

1) borrow/obtain an oil pressure gauge to get actual readings (I have
one on my '71 that I'll borrow)

2) check the oil pressure relief spring for length & tension (and
perhaps replace with an HD version?)

3) use thicker oil (I'm thinking straight 40 weight - this is Florida)

4) jack up idle to keep the light off (certainly workable if this isn't
a daily driver)

Thanks, folks!
