	Yes, the only point to the EGR light is to remind you to check
your emissions equipment. Not just EGR, but also charcoal canister,
air injectors, etc, etc, plus the correct functioning of carb or
injectors, distributor, and so forth. What it really means is time 
for a major service, as defined in the maintenance manual. A major
service should include a complete inspection of all the stuff on
the engine.
