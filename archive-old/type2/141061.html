The round end dash vents are fresh air only. No heat.
The vents at the feet are heat.

The blue levers are cold air. UP is off.

The red levers are heat. UP is off.
The right red lever controls volume. All the way down opens the heater flapper valves and turns on the blower
fan.
The left red lever controls where you get the heat. Halfway down is floor, all the way down is defrost.
