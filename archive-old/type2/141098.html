I bought my 70 bus new in Nov 69. I did drive it wide open for a couple of 
years. Much of this mileage was from San Jose to Squaw Valley and back. I 
recall downhill speeds of 85 mph. I went through a couple of set of heads 
and finally the case cracked behind the flywheel. I installed a CB Mexican 
case machined for full flow, a Rapid Cool external cooler (stock cooler 
removed) balanced the engine, VDO oil temp and press gages. Ran it gently 
this way for about 25 years/70,000 miles. Couple of years ago another new 
case but with factory doghouse, new SP heads, new cam, original rods and 
crank. Doghouse seems to keep the temp down 10 or 15 degrees more than the 
Rapid Cool.  However, I will never drive wide open for hours and I slow down 
whenever the oil temp climbs above 210.
Phil
