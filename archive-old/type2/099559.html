Subject: Re: [T2] Help with starter electrics

At 03:12 PM 11/11/2002 -0800, you wrote:
>Just finally got my engine back in my bus and the
>starter will not work.  It is not even clicking like a
>dead starter would, it just does absolutely nothing
>when you turn the key.

Make sure the rear end of the bus is up on jackstands
before doing the following test, I don't want you running
yourself over:

With all wires connected, jump (with a remote starter switch--
or a screwdriver, if you don't have one) the post with the battery
cable to the push on connector terminals.  Does the starter run?
If so, there is a problem in the wiring/ignition switch/etc.
If not, then either the starter is not grounded, or is faulty.
Pull it, and examine per the Bentley Manual.  It may be
that your solenoid has gone south.  I highly recommend
installing the Bosch starter relay kit--details can be found
in the Type2 Library, the relay can be obtained from the (now)
famous Bus Depot.

>Is there a special order to putting on the 3 wires on the terminal bolt.

No, just clean them up with sandpaper, or a whizzy-brush.
