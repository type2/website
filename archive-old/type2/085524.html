My 7 seater is  72.  Working on the engine is, what is the term? oh yes,
fun, like the games Twister is fun.  I have often found myself almost
completely inside the engine compartment for certain procedures.  But, it
really isn't that bad.  I agree with David that it is easier than most V8's
or FWD V6's to extract the spark plugs.  Everything is logical and
accessible and simple.
