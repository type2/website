The best tip I got with the wire was to turn the power and the feed ALL the
way down. Also if you can get a piece of brass (like a door hinge) behind
the well and will reduce the splatter. Dont push the tip to the work let out
the wire about an inch. My welding still looks like crap, but it is much
stronger.
