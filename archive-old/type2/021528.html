Eddie,
I don't know how good your engine skills are, but it's never to late to
refresh them, *especially* when you own a bus :)

But, if it were me, I'd do one job at a time.  Get the brakes fixed and
then take a look at the bus yourself.  Check all the hoses and make sure
their going where they're supposed to.  If you don't know where they're
supposed to go, figure it out or ask, we'll tell you.  Then, run through
a full diagnostic, spark, compression, etc, etc.. and you'll at least be
better prepared to spend more money if you weren't able to fix the
problem yourself.  The Bosch manual has a good diagnostic for the whole
FI system.  Bentley also has stuff to check...  

I think it's always a bit more comforting to know what's wrong with your
car before you bring it to the mechanic...

Mike
