At 12:35 AM 4/9/2003, Gary Watts wrote:
>Does anyone have any information on Electric or Propane conversions for type
>2s?  What I'd love to do is convert the bus to electric and wire in a small
>Honda generator making it a hybrid.  Has anyone done this?

I'm sure someone has tried it.  Know that a "small Honda generator" isn't going to be nearly enough to keep the batteries charged while you drive.  An electric bus will draw 30-40,000 watts or more during acceleration and maybe 10,000 watts while cruising.  I guess a small generator could improve the range somewhat but it isn't going to make the vehicle self-sustaining.
