Yes, you can run hydraulic lifters on a solid cam. You will need the steel
pushrods and the spacers, wave, and flat washers between the rocker arms.
See the pic in the 68-79 Bentley, section 5, page 62, figure 17-8.

I have 72,000 miles on my 77 Westy engine with no problems.
My daughter has well over 50,000 miles on her 77 Westy also.
