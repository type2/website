Get the VDO 3 1/8 gauge from RMMW with the adapter ring. It fits into the
dash to the right of the speedo and looks factory. Wire the lights to the
speedo lights so they dim like the rest and run a single line back to the
distributor. I use lots of tie wraps to keep wire hidden.
