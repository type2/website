 Firemen's Park in Niagara Falls, Ontario. The Jitter Bug has continued to be

  Show in Ontario that keeps the enthusiasts coming back year after year. It's

 Fame" has always been it's "Free Admission" to spectators and it's "Fun"

                       checking out every bodies Kool set of wheels.
