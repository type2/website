There is a whole sequence of events that is supposed
>to happen during warm up on FI that are strange and mysterious. 

Two things occurs during warmup. Mixture (pulse width) is lengthened in
proportion to temp sender by ECU and tapers till fully warm. Air bypass
meters extra idle air for a temp indexed period. A sticky bypass valve
would explain similar symptoms, but not EXACTLY 45 seconds to fault EVERY
TIME, and then for only an indexed period till warm, then all is functional.
