I recently acquired the Service Bulletin Updates to the VW factory manuals
for the years 68-70 for Type I, II, and III. This book is 8 1/2" by 7" by 6"
thick.. Cover's VW booboo's..

Any way, the metal bent  pipe, that everyone throws away, from the
distributor to the vacuum pot on the dizzy, is put there to prevent gasoline
>from running from the carburetor and damaging the diaphragm in the vacuum
unit. It is bent to arch up from the carb and then down to the distributor.

I don't know if this makes a difference in the grand scheme of things, just
found it interesting. TBear (try pulling your hose from the carb end and
seeing if it is wet with gas, mine was)
