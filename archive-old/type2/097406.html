You are misreading something here.  The pulsed signal test is B+ from terminal
1 on the coil. The test is to:
1) pull MPC off ECU
2) test light between term 1 and ground
3) crank engine, light should flicker or blink

The next test is the ground test:
1) ECU disconnected and MPC plug disconnected
2) test w/test light from terminals 10 and 5, 10 and 16, 10 and 17.
Test light lights up = bad ECU
Test light off = bad ground or open wire between term 10 and double relay term
88b

Ground is either on or off, NOT pulsed...
