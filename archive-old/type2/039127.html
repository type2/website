you should definitely order a new foam engine seal
IMMEDIATELY, that might not solve your problem, but it
will certainly cool that engine a bit.   the engine
compartment should be almost airtight as far as the
tin is concerned, those gaps are letting the air pass
through when it needs to be passing through the
cooling fan and directed through the oil cooler and
across the engine.   you could kill this motor driving
around with missing engine seals or tin (ask me how i
know)   aside from that nice lookin motor:)   good
luck and you should post a couple bus pics on your
site so we can check out the new ride:)
