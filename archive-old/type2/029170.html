What Andrew says below is absolutely true. You don't have to go through
all that to simply work on the brakes. Technically you can just
loosen and remove the wheel and drum as one unit and work on the brakes
that
way. You can also have a shop remove the wheel from the drum with an air
gun. My perspective is geared more toward giving you the ability to change
any future flat tires
yourself rather than rely on AAA or a shop.
