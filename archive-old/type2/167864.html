Subject: Re: [T2] E-Brake vs. Foot brake

Here's a tip for setting your parking brake:

Press down hard on the foot pedal while you pull the parking brake handle.
This way you're using the power of the hydraulic system to put the pads in
hard contact with the drums, and the cable system just holds them there.
This works on all cars.
