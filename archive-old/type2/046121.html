FCAW (flux core) always spatters more than mig. If the Polarity is set backwards, it
spatters hugely (and painfully.....). Check that first thing. Also make sure that
you don not have too much ventilation (a wind blowing the sheild away...) as that
will cause excessive spatter.

I got the MIG adapter for my Lincoln Weldpack 100 before I got all the way throught
my first spool of self shielding wire. I also use c25 and love it!!! MUCH better
than fcaw.
