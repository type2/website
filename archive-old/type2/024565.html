I don't know if you have any kind of RV supply stores like we have here in
the states, but I would look into the tanks used on other camper/RV's. You
may be able to find a tank that has the right dimensions. It will all be
custom fit, but it will help if its close to the right size to start. The
fresh tank will have to have a small 12v pump to get the water out. The
waste tank will have to have a large valve to dump with. It sounds like a
workable thing to do. Keep in mind those tank will add a lot of weight when
full if they are real big. So plan accordingly.
