Why do you say the Phillips head style screws are the worst?  I've worked
with both and generally prefer the Phillips head since you're not as likely
to "cam out" with your screwdriver if you come at the screw at something
other than a perfectly "square" angle.  There's a spot or two on these
beasts where there's no way you can get perfectly "square" with the driver
because of inference from other parts; regular slotted screws are a real
PITA in these places.
