Well, I went to pick up the '59 DD Panel last night.  I am not 
calling this one a Search & Rescue because #1: I found out about 
this bus over 6 months ago & #2: If I had not gotten it, someone 
else surely would have.

Back when I first heard about it he wanted $800 for it & I passed 
after talking with him.  He lives with his wife & mother-in-law 
and they said something had to go, & this "project" was in the worst 
shape.

Everyone saw the description in the ad.  Here's the real poop:  
No front nose skin with strange remaining metal up front.  I asked 
him what he used to cut the nose skin off with and he replied "tin 
snips".  

Thinking I had heard him wrong I said, "Tin snips?", to which he 
replied, "Yeah.  Took me about 20 minutes.  I used to install car 
stereos."

I honestly had no idea what nose skins and car stereos had to do 
with each other, but I decided not to push the issue.

The bus needs a nose skin transplant, new cab floor corners, dog 
legs, rocker panels, battery trays, rear corners, transmission, 
engine, wiring harness, driver's door, 15" bus wheels, lenses, 
instruments...

Uh, this list goes on & on...  I have a shell.

Good points:  It is a 1959 Double Door Walk Thru Panel (semi rare).  

At least I have the skills & patience to put it back together.
I like it!
