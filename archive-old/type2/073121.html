I didn't really understand that either. I can imagine a hypothetical case
where a person might sell the engine (let's say it's a very smoky engine)
out of a car, then volunteer to have the car scrapped. Somebody takes the
engine and brings a dormant junker back to life with it. In this case, you
have scrapped one car but in reality you have removed zero gross polluters

But Mr. Pederson specifically is talking about parts "even if they don't
