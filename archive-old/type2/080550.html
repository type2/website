     I had a similar problem with my 78' rebuild. The flanges for the
heater boxes,manifolds, and elbows were all rusted and warped and thin. I
took a grinder to the flanges, got them nice and shiny, then carefully
welded (mig) beads of metal to all 3 sides of the flanges. After it cooled
I took the grinder again and ground down the welds to make them flat, now I
had more metal to seal with. I also used a straight edge and a file and
carefully got every flange straight. I suppose you could drop some $$ and
replace all those pieces wiht new or good used ones but when I looked that
stuff (in good shape) was as rare as hens teeth. A friend of mine who is an
excellent welder and builder of bike frames has been considering getting
stainless steel tubing and manufacturing these components for himself and
possibly for resale to other bus people. The problem is that 'most' bus
people would not be willing to drop $800.00 on an exhuast system...
