The readings seem really good for a heavy bus, in my opinion, the only one I
question is the oil pressure..How many RPM was the engine turning at
20-25PSI??

The Berg dipstick is not always accurate, the gauges seldom vary, with
sender relocations, as our test car has 4 senders, (sump, dipstick, and a
tapped main oil galley and one directly in the housing for the oil cooler
adaptor inlet)) The gauges are all VDO, and they stay within 10 degrees of
each other, except  the one going into the cooler, which is almost always 20
degrees hotter.

Contrary to what the heavens may say, I still believe in gauges..
