 Suggestion

 Disconnect the double relay, and fuel pump wires. run a "temp" wire from
the battery to the fuel pump, and hook a ground up to the other side,
respecting the polarity + and -, check fuel pressure at the rail, should be

good, so back to the system that feeds the pump power.  Reverse what you did
so far.

note to the note on the top right hand side of the page if you recently
replaced the starter.
