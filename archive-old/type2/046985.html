said _he_ wouldn't consider a trip in an under 3-4K bus he specifically

 The other thing is that the fact that you paid $400 for a bus doesn't
mean it's a $400 bus. The volk on this list are going to be the ones
that pick up bargains and have the spares and expertise to build a 
reliable vehicle out of someone elses "unreliable POS".
 If you've had a bus for six months, replaced worn out parts with
ones that would have cost hundreds of dollars to source professionally
and invested a couple of hundred hours in preventative maintenance
and basic renovation, you can't really describe it as a $400 bus: it's
a $3000 bus that you bought for $400!!!
