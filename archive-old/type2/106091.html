I sculpted the "ErgoKnob" (TM?) by mounting and then squeezing a large
dollop of (VERY) quick set RTV silicone into place on the shaft with my
right hand.  A few minutes later, I had a rough model of a knob custom
fitted to the human hand (well, my hand, anyway).

prototypes cast in resin (plastic).  One to mount in Giacomo to test it's
design in actual use, and the other to clean up into a master for production
casting.
