I know that we're all a bunch of bus-nuts who eat, drink, and sleep
these things; but there seems to be a group of people out there who have
never seen a camper bus, nevermind ride in one. 
I just took five people to lunch in my bus, three of which are well old
enough to remember when air-cooled VWs reigned supreme. Based on the
sheer awe, and silly (to me) questions - you would think they were
riding in a space ship. It was as if I had created this thing in the

them all was that I had a closet in my van.
