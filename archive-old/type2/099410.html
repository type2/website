become elastic and relax the bolts. The tapered seat area should be masked 
off, or the powder coat scraped off immediately coming out of the oven 
before it gets hard. I swear, you will need carbide to take it off after it 
cures. Amazingly hard paint. Same goes for the mounting plate to the 
drum/hub. That should be metal to metal, no paints on the back centers of 
the wheels either.
