This is the easiest service an engine can get!  Engines don't get fatigued
and need to rest like we people do.  As long as you keep them supplied
with fuel and air they will continue in a nice steady-state operation at
the temperatures that they were designed to operate at.  It's the warming
and cooling transitions that are hard on engines.
