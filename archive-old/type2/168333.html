My understanding is that regardless of the year, with FI, you should never
even have to touch the peddle to start it. I had the same problem with Zoey
and came to find out that vacuum leaks were causing the hard starts.

Since I got my major leaks taken care of (S-Boot, AAR Boot, Brake Booster
Boot and various small vacuum line) zoey will start in 20 degrees as well as
my Civic. The only issues I have now are that she runs like crap since she
needs a tune job.

If you have to pump the gas in a FI, things are not right. With carbs, you
will have to pump - sometimes, but not with FI.
