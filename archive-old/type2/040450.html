Yes, the fan housings can be swapped between engines. Never trust the engine
number on it. Find the real number on the case. It is stamped in the flat
section at the base of the square vent box. Looking at the engine from the
rear, look over the top right of the fan housing at the parting line where
it meets the crankcase vent box.
