It seems to me that you can take your receipt and warrantee card back
and at least get a refund.  They should have put the tire serial numbers

manager.  If you aren't satisfied then, take it up with the regional
manager (if it's a chain store) AND the Better Business Bureau.  Make it
clear that it's a safety issue besides being ripped off.
