Hey there,

Original options, color, etc. can be found on the M-code plate that
is located in the bus. 

To find out where the M-code plate is located, and how to decode the
plate, point your browser to:

http://www.type2_dot_com/m-codes/  

Vincent Molenaar's excellent webpages should answer you questions.
