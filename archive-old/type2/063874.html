A number of states will allow you to use plates from a particular year.  The
sitpulation is that you locate the plates yourself(check swap meets). You need
to have them with you at the time you go to register the plates, as they will
need to be inspected, to ensure they are up to standards (ie not rusted beyond
recognition), and that they are not currently registered to someone else
(stolen).  I am sure that there will be a small registration fee, but not
too much.

Hope this helps.
