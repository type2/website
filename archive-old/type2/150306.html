Hi Frank,

I think you have the ignition wires confoosed. The ignition should be  
a 2-stage switch. Stage 1 is power to coil and accessories. Stage 2  
is a momentary contact that sends power to the starter solenoid.

The symptoms you describe lead me to the conclusion that you have the  
starter solenoid wired to the coil wire power.
