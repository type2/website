I would not expect a filter to keep the oil clean enough to allow it to
run 60K miles. Seems to me the breakdown of the oil is just one issue that
calls for frequent changes. Synth oil might still have its viscosity up to
spec after 20k or 30K, but it would certainly be badly contaminated with
dirt, gasoline, minute metal parts, and other lovely pollutants that would
affect its ability to lubricate properly. Seems risky, but then nearly
every cab, van, and car is used differently, and its hard to find real
hard, empirical evidence of exactly what is the best for each engine. 

At least with the way my engine goes through oil, I don't worry about it
breaking down, as much as I do running out!
