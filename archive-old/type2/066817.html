A word of heat exchange caution here is warranted.
Although the same OVERALL heat would be transferred, there is a point 
at which TOO much volume lowers the temperature to a useless value.
Think of the extremes.  A hot metal plate, low volume air = hot temp
extremely high volume of air = slightly warm air.  There is some optimum 
volume point, so experiment.  I would suggest a variable resistor for 
that reason.
High volume to push out old cold air from cab, low volume for 
defrosting windshield.
