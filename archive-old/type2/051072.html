229 does not mean they are the same. There are 4 different ECU's for 79. 2
for Fed version, 039 906 021D for manual trans and 039 906 021E for
autotrans. 2 for Calif version, 039 906 021 B for manual trans and 039 906
021 C for auto trans.
Save your money, they are darn near bullet-proof. In the past 5 years I have
only seen one ECU fail....water got in it.
