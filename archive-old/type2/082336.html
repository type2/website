Another tip: After soaking in P B Blaster (my preference, YMMV),
try putting a socket and breaker bar on the nut and giving it a
goodly rap clock-wise before attempting to loosen it.

You're chucking the shock anyway; cut the ring surrounding the
rubber on the shock to remove the shock from the stud. This 
gives you clearance around the nut for a nut-buster if it comes
to that.
