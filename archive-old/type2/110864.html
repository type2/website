Hello Chris,

can't help you on a local source for the steering damper, but you 
will probably need the bushing as well when you install the new
one.

as far as the tie rod ends go, if the hammer trick doesn't work
(which i think it will with some penetrating oil), then 
rent one of the 'pickle fork' tools from FLAPS. yes, it will
destroy the old tie rod ends, but since you are replacing,
that doesn't matter.

also, get an alignment _immediately_ after replacement. on the
first bus i had, i replaced the tie rods & the drive home(& then
back to the shop) was _very_ scary.
