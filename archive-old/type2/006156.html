Ted,

The Y356 is the highway tread and the Y370 is the Mud & Snow rated tire with
a more agressive tread pattern and probably a bit noisier.  When I was
buying tires a month ago, the Y370 was available only in 195/75 R14.  The
Y356 was available in 185 R14 (stock size).  You could put  the wider Y370s
on back and the skinnier Y356s on front.
