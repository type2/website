Jack,
The system only opens the injecters for a set period of time. The volume of fuel that flows through the
injecter is controlled by pressure.

The reason the regulator is contolled by manifold vacuum and Bentley gives a spec for it:  Think of a
carburetor with an accelerator pump. When you step on the throttle, you get a shot of fuel. Same thing happens
with EFI. When you step on the throttle: manifold vacuum drops, fuel pressure rises, more fuel flows through
the injecters. You get a rich condition.
