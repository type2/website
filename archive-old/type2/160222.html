  Hi,
     When cranking the engine, there is often a pop in the AFM just before it starts.  This does not appear to be ambient temperature related, and it can occur with either a warm or cold engine.      The same pop always occurs if the accelerator is slightly depressed when cranking the engine.

     I checked the cold start valve for leakage, and it is OK.  I checked all of the vacuum hoses and connections, using Richards excellent diagrams, and they are OK.
