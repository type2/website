Since both your problems began right after changing carbs, it's possible
that for whatever reason an over-rich mixture is flooding the combustion
chambers and some of the fuel is washing past the rings and into the
crankcase.

Another, more remote, possibility is that the fuel pump was somehow
disturbed during the carb change to the point that it [the pump] is now
leaking fuel into the crankcase.
