We left Reno Nevada on Sunday on our way to Niagara
Falls for the 'Busses by the Falls' get together way
up north in Canada. Overall, a pretty uneventful ride

show.

on such a great show! We might even drive the 
2500 miles (one way)again next year.

to see the Statue of Liberty tomorrow and on to
Washington DC after that.

a great time!! One week down and five more to go.
