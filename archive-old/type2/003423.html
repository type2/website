>I am i the process of getting all the parts together to replace the
> exhuast and heater boxes on my 78 Westy.
> I am going to put on heater boxes from a 72-74. I have the boxes and the
> exhaust new from the Bus Depot.
> I went with VW boxes and the stainless muffler. I am wondering what else
> I will need to complete the conversion.

You will need the left and right heater tubes that go between the heater
boxes and the flapper valves for a 72-74. They are round on the box end.
Your 78's are oval.

You will need the long alternator support/adjusting bracket for a 72-74. It
bolts on the outside of 72-74 boxes. On your 78, it bolted to the welded-on
bracket on top of the exhaust pipe between the box and the transverse pipe.
You cannot make the 78 fit.
