Whatever color you have behind the doorpanels, or under the floormats, or
any other hidden spot is most likely the original color.

The westy's had more like five colors: orange, 2 colors of yellow, green
and white.  The two most common colors around here are the green and the
orange.  Yellow and green both had green door panels, but the yellow had
seat covers that had some yellow stripes.
