It doesn't really matter too much which goes over which.  You will need some
glue to hold them in.  Try 3M weatherstrip adhesive, which is what I ended
up using.  Don't ever plan on taking it out again! 

If ya wanna be real fancy, you can cut each piece at a 45 degree angle, and
then glue them together and to the body.  The original piece was one solid
piece, which is why you don't recall the over/under issue.
