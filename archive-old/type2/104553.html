>I am replacing the fuel line on 71.  About 2 ft from the tank, I had a fuel

That's the preferred location for some.  It's outside the engine compartment,
so a leak would have less disastrous effects.  It also allows you to use an
uninterrupted metal line from the firewall to the fuel pump.

and the fuel pump.  DO NOT PUT IT BETWEEN THE FUEL PUMP AND
THE CARB(S).  The mechanical VW fuel pump is designed to pull, not push.
