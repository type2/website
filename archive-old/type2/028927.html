"Westie"/"Westy" is a slang term for any make of vehicle converted by
Westfalia into a camper.

Type 4 refers to the type of engine - this will have a large aluminium
casting at the rear which acts an air duct and a fan visible through a grid
at the centre rear.

I notice as I read some of the replies to your question that you were being
told that Westfalia ONLY put wedge shaped opening roofs hinged at front and
rear on T2 Transporters - well this just ain't correct as they also fitted a
straight up pop top take a look at www.vanfest_dot_org/1997p25.htm for a 1978
Westfalia T2 Transporter with a straight up pop top.
