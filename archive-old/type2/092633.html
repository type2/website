Since standard procedure is to adjust idle with the engine at operating 
temps, sounds like that is all you need to do. Are you saying you have the 
throttle plate adjusted to spec and the idle screw all the way out and it

Bentley. If it still won't idle, verify no vacuum leaks. Could be just the 
manifold nuts need to be tightened.
