On Mon, 8 Apr 2002, jeremy norris wrote:

> Volks,
> could somebody offer some definitions for me:
> Burned Valves:

Valves eroded by hot combustion gases so that they leak.  Commonly caused
by too tight valve lash.

> Valve Lash:

The play in the valve actuating system, typically measured at the end of
the valve stem.

> Also, what are the risks to valves that are tightened to .002 (too tight)

Burned valves

 or
> loosened to .010 (too loose)?

Loss of power, possible "hammering" of valve train components

> Finally, when a shop does a "valve job" what does that include? (besides
> presumably, adjusting the valve gap).

Removal of head, inspection for cracks, replacement of valves, valve
guides and seats. (if it is a good job)
