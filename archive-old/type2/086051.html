	I too wanted to avoid the dreaded door mangle, so I mounted my 
tweets(Boston Pro) on the top of the dashboard.  I mounted the surface mount 
brackets(included with the tweeter hardware) to the outermost(from center) 
defroster vent screw locale on both sides.  The tweeter is a bit 'off-axis', 
but the response doesn't suffer much at all.  You can barely see them, since 
they blend right into the black dash-top.  I ran the wires past the felt 
edge of the dash, and down below to their respective crossovers.  The 
midbass speakers are in the factory door location.  I have since, purchased 
shallow 8" woofers that will be mounted in the factory speaker location, and 
the mids will be moved higher in the doors.
