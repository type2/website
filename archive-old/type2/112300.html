Elijah,
I have found that Permatex "High Tack" on the valve cover and
gasket--let dry and stick that sucker on there--
It's a done deal--no leaking.

9 times out of 10, the cover surface is Ca-Ca--and this is where the oil
dribbles from--not between the gasket and the head.

Now if your really desperate--and have some sort of other problems--GM
gasket sealer is the ultimate best of all the best--but you have to let
it set up overnight, prior to starting the engine--nothing leaks through
this stuff., and is very easily removable, if you want to work on the
engine later on.
