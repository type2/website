If you don't mind destroying the screw, you could use a gizmo called a screw
extractor. Imagine a thread tap with absurdly coarse threads--like half a
thread per inch. You drill a hole of the correct size into the stuck screw,
put the extractor in a tap wrench, and turn it into the hole. I've yet to
find a screw this gadget can't un-stick. They come in sets to cover a range
of screw sizes. Hardware stores, FLAPS, Harborfreight, etc., should have
them.

Good luck...
