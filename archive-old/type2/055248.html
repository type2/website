To answer the silent question "how much harder could turning the wheel
possibly be?", the standard wheel is about 18" OD. Following is a table of
wheel-size and additional force required (rounded to the nearest %) to turn
the wheel:

17" = 6%
16" = 11%
15" = 17%
14" = 22%
13" = 28%
12" = 33%

No need to go much further than that. With a 12" wheel, the only "racing"
you'll be doing is the 1/4 mile.
