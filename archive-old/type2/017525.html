Here's some information from list posts and manuals.  

"CA" transaxle was used through the 1972 model year. This one seems popular 
with off-roaders for dune buggy tranny conversions.  Its tough and geared 
low.  There was a "mountain" geared version of this, the CB, and also limited 
slip versions of each of these, the CC and CD.

"CN" and (maybe "CM") type 002 transaxle was used in 1974 and 75 with the 
1800.  They had different gearing.  

"CP" type 091 transaxle was used from 1976 - 79 and had gearing designed to 
take advantage of the additional hp of the fuel injected 2 liter engine.  It 
is a desireable trans. for any bus upgraded to the FI 2 liter and is 
definitely a "freeway flier".
