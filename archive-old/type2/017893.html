I absolutely concur.  The compression test will tell all.  I wouldn't worry 
about re-using the old pistons and cylinders unless they
were really worn (and I've never seen a really worn set).  I would probably 
have re-used the rings too.  I know they seem like a
wear item, but if you were not burning oil and your compression was strong 
and even then they were probably okay. If you
replaced the rings without honing the cylinders, that could easily be your 
problem.  The rings and cylinders sort of wear into
each other to make a good seal.  New rings with "old" cylinders will mess 
that relationship up.
