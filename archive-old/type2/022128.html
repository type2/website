Alan, 
I have the 67 and earlier shop manual for the bus that shows this.  That
bar was used for opening the rear hatch from the inside.  VW actually put a
small cable with a key ring on it (I actually can't remember if a cable was
used or the key ring went directly through the hole) so when you pulled the
key ring in released the hatch.  This was done stock to kombis that did not
come with rear interior panelling, as those were usually used for work trucks.
