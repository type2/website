The idea of the return line is to prevent the fuel in the line from
getting too hot.  The fuel tank is used as a heat-sink.

Putting a "T" in the line would negate this function, as the fuel
would simply circulate.

If you really want to avoid welding on your tank (an excellent idea!),
then you might consider fabricating a "special" tee fitting with an
extension that runs the fuel return up through the fuel outlet.  There
probably is enough cross-sectional area in the outlet to allow both
functions to take place through one hole.
