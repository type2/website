IIRC, one spade connector is for the wire from the ignition, and the other
goes to the FI system to tell it that starting is being attempted. Both
wires connect to the same point, so go to a FLAPS or Radio Shack and get an
adapter with a single female and two male spades. Should run you about .19
cents.
