   OK, here's the latest on Nikki's journey. Just got off the phone with
her and she's in kinda limp mode. She called from Eldorado, Tx, about

troubling problems with the bus in the aftermath of ingesting a deer.

be rolling freely.

long distance diagnosis is as puzzled as mine. Can't really put a finger
on it.

Schwartz who, though not a VW guy, is mechanically inclined. All
attempts to reach Rex Rogers have been in vain, most of the numbers I've
received haven't worked for one reason or the other, but Mike Gensler

on the AIRS site is off by one digit. (Should be 915-659-0154 instead of
the number on the AIRS site.)

277. She promised to keep in touch with me with progress reports and if

please p-mail me and I'll pass them on to her when she next makes
