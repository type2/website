On 7/20/05 7:47 AM, "Gary Watts" <garyb_at_fatmedia_dot_com> wrote:

> I have to crawl under the bus, and need to know where to put the jack
> stands.  I usually just drive my 77 riviera up my ramps and crawl under
> it, but I need to have the wheels free to move so that I can adjust the
> brakes.

Use the rear axle tube at the front (watch the brake cable), and the cross
member just behind the jacking points up front. Never use the jacking point
itself unless using the factory jack because you will crush it with jack
stands or a floor jack.
