PROPEX is a UK firm which makes a propane furnace which
has been sucessfully installed in VW buses/campers. It fits
under the rear seat, and is small, 14x6x4.5". Fully vented,
no cutting of body metal required, though you do have to put
to smallish holes in the floor for intake and exhaust. 
Cost of one unit, with duties, shipping and general
grief, approaches $600.
