Rick,

The wiper shaft and therefore wiper arms are '68 only then '69-'72 and
finally '73-'79. The '68 are almost impossible to find. The '69-'72 are
available used and the '73-'79 are available new and used, all from us. Also
keep in mind that all the shafts are interchangeable. You can put the later
model shafts and then wiper arm on any wiper rack. It's a big job to pull
them but then when it rains it pores.
