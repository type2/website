hotel room in Paris, home of the metric system, the drain pipe will be an
inch-sized pipe!  For whatever reason, plumbing is one of those fields
where inate conservatism has overcome reason.  The drains on Westies are
inch-sized fittings and you should be able to head down to any plumbing
supply house and pick up replacement parts, in both the US and in Europe.
I don't know if this is true in OZ, but I would be willing to bet a beer
