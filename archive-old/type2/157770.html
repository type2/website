I always try to remember to go back after a couple
days of driving and re-torque critical bolts like
these. Also remember that when you re-use critical
bolts that you don't know what condition they are in
as for whether they are stretched to the max or have
internal cracks. Over-torqueing new stuff is one
thing, but even 10% over on old parts might be a risk
- you never know until you know! - BaH

 On this subject...make SURE you use backing plates

 from VW dealers) when you re-install the CV bolts.

 their

 bolts falling off...ask me how I know. After having
 several CV joint's fall off, I also add 10-20% to
 the
 published torque value.
