Westy, runs fine but does blow a qt of oil per fillup (no sign of
burning); oil pressure this summer depended on temp- at a high of 250

rpms and is sort of a sound I've heard on bugs when the differential is
going (a shhhr-shhhr noise).  I CANNOT hear it when my head is in the
engine, only when I sit up front.  In the engine compartment I hear a
slight knocking on the left side- distr/cam? I had a rod bearing go on a
bug, and that was a decided knock on acceleration.  If it were a rod,
would this sound be diminished if the ignition to that cylinder were
removed temporarily as a test?
