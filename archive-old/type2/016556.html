> 1.  Those gasket kits we get from the standard vendors some pieces seem
> real thin (ie. the paper cylinder gaskets between the cylinder and the

> home made gasket?
> 2.  Do you recommend using adhesive when sealing the 2 halves of the
> case together or are the machined surfaces enough?

I've heard (Muir, I think) that a bit of GaskaCinch and a single sewing
thread (like a Coats & Clark, white) will seal it better than anything. 
The thread squishes out and becomes about 1/4 inch wide, looks like a
bit of plastigauge when you take it apart again, but I never had a leak
doing that way.  Sounds weird, but it works.

And you don't need any more than the tiny paper gaskets, unless you put
in the shims to reduce compression (see "deck height" in the archives).
