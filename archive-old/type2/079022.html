Bigger filter should not affect system in any way that I am aware of. But
definately put it prior to the pump. Never put anything between the pump
and the rest of the system. You will surely affect fuel pressure and FI is
very sensitive to that. Just because your filter is bigger doesn't mean you
dont have to change it as often though.
