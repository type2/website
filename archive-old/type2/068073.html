I've always heard about possible injuries from hand cranking engines.  My 
grandpa told me when I was but a wee lad, of how he knew lots of people 
who broke fingers, wrists, and arms from a backfiring Model T when he was 
younger.  He showed me how you would crank start by using the "flat part 
of your palm".  It was those who actually "grabbed" the hand crank that 
ended up getting hurt.  Ah for the luxury of electric starters - when 
they work that is.
