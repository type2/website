Subject: Re: [T2] dipstick for 1600

Drain the oil. Pour in 2 quarts. Start engine to charge the system. Shut it 
off. Let it sit for 5 minutes. Level should read just a bit over the add 
line. If not, you have the wrong dip stick. Consider also the dip stick 
tube could be either the wrong one or not installed properly.
