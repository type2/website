  if the rest of the alignment is ok but the steering wheel is just not
centered then, yes they just need to adjust the drag link.

the drag link, and please dont be offended if you already know, looks like
a 3rd tie rod but it runs forward to back rather than side to side.
you adjust it just like a tie rod. pop one end off, center the steering
wheel, turn the rod (drag link) end so the length matches, pop it back in
and tighten it up.
