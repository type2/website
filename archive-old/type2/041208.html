My bus has that exact same problem.  There should be a how-to on it in the
library.  What you have to do is pull the plates off the torsion arms and pull
them out to release the pressure, turn them up a notch (each notch is said to
give about three inches lift on good torsion bars that don't sag) and shove them
back in.  It's not as painful as it sounds.
