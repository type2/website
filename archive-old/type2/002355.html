limiting rotors they are in fact available in a smear of ranges from

damaging in any way to use one of the rotors with a regular non limiting
electronic or CD ignition of some sort, damaging to the ignition that is,
say an MSD 6A or the stock CA Vanagon or whatever, the sort of ignition that
carries warnings about not pulling plugs without grounding them.
