You're right, I was mistaking the oil pickup tube for the windage tray.  
I had even looked at this picture before but had forgotten all about it.  
(These rebuilds are taking that long!!)  But, it seems clear that the 
Vanagon engines I'm rebuilding never had windage trays; the picture and 
accompanying text in Wilson's book seen to indicate the pickup tube bolts 
to the windage tray through some sort of hole in the "bell" on the 
pickup tube, and neither of the bells on the pickup tubes I have have 
this hole.
