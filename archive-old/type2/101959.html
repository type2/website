I had similar problems with my 74. Turned out to be a bad brake booster.
The brake booster would not release the m/c completely. I found out
after I swapped everything else ... Check whether there is fluid in your
brake booster. The easiest way to do so, is pulling the vacuum hose out
of the booster and stick a small hose (like windshield washer tubing) in
there and try to syphon any fluids out. Watch out - if it is water it'll
be rusty and it may also be/contain brake fluid. If there is brake fluid
present, you need to replace/rebuild your m/c also. On my van, the
booster is connected to vacuum, but what caused it to go bad was the
water inside. So this may or may not apply to your situation. I have no
idea how a not connected booster would behave. My $0.02 worth ...
