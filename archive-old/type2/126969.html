I've had luck with BIG vise-grips.  Just clamp then onto the sleeve
tightly enough to leave grooves in the sleeve.  Unclamp.  Repeat at a
different location.  The goal is to end up with a bunch of grooves all
around the sleeve.  Moving the metal expands the sleeve a bit and it
should loosen up.
