 i've never understood why vw would make a hinge without a pin. but they did.
you will have to remove the hinges and drill them  to .200 to receive a  3/16 "roll pin"
so be sure to have the pin in hand first and drill carefully in several increasing steps
(start with 1/8 " ).  you will have to align the holes so the pin will work.  i also mig weld
one end of the hinge/ pin although the pin does fit tight, and you can grease the hollow
pin with a needle grease gun attachment.
