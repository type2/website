Kendall "standard base" oil probably has the best reputation in the 
industry for being "THE" oil.  The really good mechanics use this stuff. 
I commented before about the oil, assumming it was a synthetic, and 
attributed the lower oil temp to the oil not soaking up the heat (as the 
VW engineers originally intended - this way the case does not soak up the 
heat). I am pretty sure the oil is fine as long as it is not a synth base 
oil. My only other recommendation is using a straight 30w or 40w instead 
of multi grade. I hear the multi grades break down quicker.
