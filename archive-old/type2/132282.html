Subject: Re: [T2] Need Test for Oil Pressure Gauge

On 7/31/04 11:00 AM, "Andrew Stockton" <apstockton_at_riconnect_dot_com> wrote:

> My VDO oil pressure gauge suddenly stopped working.  Is there a quick test one
> can do to check as to whether the problem is with the sensor or the gauge?
> The wiring seems to be fine.

Most VDO pressure sender output 10-180 ohms so if you get a variable
resistor in that range or a bunch of resistors you can test the gauge by
replacing the sensor with the resistor (attach one end to gauge and ground
the other end to the case).
