Re the Berg temp dipstick going off too readily- I've had the same thing
happen, and it wasn't because it was too hot. You have to line it up right
or the little temp spring rolls around and grounds out on the road draft
tube. You have to look back there if the light comes on, I panicked one day
thinking I had no oil pressure. All I had to do is turn the dipstick a bit.
The engine was at the correct temp.
