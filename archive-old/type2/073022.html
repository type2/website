Dear Chrissie,

Please get this checked out and solved before you drive the bus again. We
had similar symptoms with a bus. What happened was that the metal box that
anchors the steering mechanism to the axle beam had begun to rot out,
gradually pulling away from the axle beam. Look under the middle of the
front of the bus, where the steering mechanism attaches to the middle of
the axle beam. Check out how that box is attached to the axle beam (it
should take about a minute). If it releases completely, you can't steer the
bus, which is a bad thing.

The box can be rewelded to the axle beam under certain circumstances.

Please get it checked out, though.

Good luck!
