A quick note on Dixie Bus Gathering, to be held in Central Ohio on the
second weekend of August.  This is the 5th year for this event, and has a

Club show.  They tout this show as the largest single day event East of the
Mississippi.  Usually over 400 registered vehicles.

Campground, a vintage car show for the enthusiast, and huge cauldron of
chili for those who like to eat!!
