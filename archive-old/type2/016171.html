I recently replaced all the vent tube connectors on my 76 Westy and there
were several.  The one that produced the leak was behind the spare tire
well, but the ones behind the firewall on top of the tank were also leaking.
If I remember right their were at least 3 or 4 in there, one behind the
spare tire well, one near the fuel filler neck, and one directly above the
engine.  It is well worth your trouble to change all of these with a good
quality fuel hose.  Don't use vacuum hose because it isn't designed to be
exposed to fuel and it will harden and break.  Good luck.
