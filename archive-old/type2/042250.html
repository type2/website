Hey Ben,
Did you recheck your timing.  I pulled out my distributor, flipped the
drive dog, and then put the distributor back in the *exact* same
position, and the timing was advanced 24 degrees at idle(I finally heard
what pinging was).  I'm glad I checked this because I could have killed
my engine!

So, the plastic shouldn't have caused *too* many problems unless it got
into the working parts of your distributor.  Get the new parts, make
sure that the advances are working correctly(vacuum and mechanical) and
make sure your timing's correct.
