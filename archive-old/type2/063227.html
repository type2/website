On both my '70 and '71 Westy, the metal Westfalia tag on the rear seat said
"Campmobile 70".  I chalked this up to Westfalia making the label in the
calendar year of their conversion (or maybe they just overproduced the tag
that says '70 and slapped it on both the '70 and '71s!!).  Or perhaps the
'68-'69 Westy interior is slightly different from the '70-'73 and the plate
designates the first year for that specific interior style.  Anyhow, FWIW,
I've encountered what you are seeing.  If anyone knows the real reason
behind this label mystery, please post it so I can add it to my useless VW
trivia databank!
