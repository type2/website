Brad-

If you check out http://www.scottandmaggie_dot_com/gearcalc.asp, there's an
Excel spreadsheet (if I remember correctly) that gives the conversions
between RPM and MPH for each gear.  You can use that to make a rough
estimate of what your tach should be reading based on your speed (assuming
the speedo is about right).  At 3200 RPM, I think your bus should be doing
about 40 MPH in third gear or 62 MPH in fourth.
