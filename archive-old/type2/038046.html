My bus, a 2-liter with dual carbs, delivers peak torque between 1800 and
3100rpm.  Given that you will get best mileage at peak torque, and that you
will get better mileage the slower you drive (because of less wind
resistance) I can make an educated guess that my bus would get it's best
mileage at 1800rpm, or 30mph (in 4th gear).  But I can't drive 30mph
everywhere.  :-)
