You got lucky.  When the the generator light goes on, the procedure for an
air-cooled engine is to get off the road to a point of safety as fast as you can
and turn the engine off.  Don't try to make it to "the next exit."  You don't
have that much time once the belt breaks.  Unfortunately, I have second hand
experience in this department.
