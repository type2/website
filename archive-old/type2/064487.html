I have successfully used Lysol Toilet Bowl Cleaner to remove the
brake dust buildup on my plastic '84 Vanagon wheel covers and the
painted wheels themselves.  I have no idea how this will react
with alloys, but it *can* discolor metals.  It makes brake dust
removal very easy, but be sure to *rinse thoroughly* and do not
leave it sitting on surfaces very long.
