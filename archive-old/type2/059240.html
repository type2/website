Wow, a poptop Safare.  I've never seen one in the flesh.  This is probably
one of the rarest busses in the U.S..  I have seen maybe a dozen Safare
campers in all the years I've been paying attention to VWs, and of course I
own one of those dozen (the first one I ever came into contact with), but
all of them have been highroofs.

I have a Safare owner's manual on the web, check out

http://www.tiu_dot_net/~des/vw/bluto/manual/

I think it mentions the pop top version in the manual, but all of the
pictures are of the highroof, which I think made up almost all of Safare's
production.  How do you know they were in Hayward?  My manual says they
were in Paramount, near Los Angeles, but I couldn't find any trace of them
there a couple of years ago when I was passing through.

If you decide not to buy this bus, please pass the information on to the
list, or to me personally.  Thanks,
