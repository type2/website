installed my VDO oil gauge. I'm not saying the VDO gauge is the cause - 
just that it confirm suspicions I had about my engine's oil pressure.

even more troubles - right?

My piece of info to offer - if you have leaks around your engine on thread 
holes, oil pan plugs, and pressure senders - Loctite brand has a hydraulic 
connection sealant. Looks like red Loctite but seals very, very well on 
oily, hot places. Don't think it would stand up well to fuel. This will 
seal the oil pressure sender without electrically isolating the sender 
(which the sender needs to ground itself and complete the circuit).
