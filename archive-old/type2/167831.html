roughly above the clock position, in a small panel such as is 
commonly sold with such guages.  A slab of dark Corflute or similar,

prevents distracting reflections in the screen.

bicycle inner-tube knotted below heater slots in the top of the dash.

windscreen by moving fore-&-aft and more importantly by slewing.
