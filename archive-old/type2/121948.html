Hi Richard

The only plugs besides the recommended Bosch W8CC I would try in a type4 is
the Bosch W8DTC or the W7DTC (cooler for the summer) plug that is a Copper
triple electrode, I have noticed a difference in performance with them, I
would stay away from the Platinum plugs in an air cooled , I have heard of
several reports of the porcelain coming loose and blowing out! 
Do a Google search on "sparkplugs_passenger.pdf" and use the "INTERNET
PLUGS" link this is a vary good reference glide from Bosch for there plugs.
