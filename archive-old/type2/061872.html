Andrew

Colour's always going to be a personal thing, but I remember going through
this same thought process a while back. So I wne to Vanfest, looked at 100s
of different colours, cme away thinking I want something else!!!

Firstly I'd keep with the 2 tone (at the bulge) - unless you're doing a 'cal
look' style custom.

Eliminate the following: Green (I hate green!)
                         Black (hearse/shows up ALL defects in panels)
                         Red (fire engine/ambulance)
                         White (plain Jane)
                         Grey (looks like primer)

Pastel shades work really well, for instance the baby blue, pale yellow, and
several variations of blue/green, olive (yes, I know I said green before, but
I've seen nice green ones).

My fav. solid colour would be orange, great for the "its a VW Bus and it looks
lovely in orange, but you're Ford would look silly in that colour" look. Pink
and white looks good if done well, looks terrible if done badly.

BTW I plumped for dark silver/white, and, to this day, I've only seen about 2
other buses that colour. And it looks lovely with smoked lenses and clear up
front.
