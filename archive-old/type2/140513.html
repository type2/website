Jamie- Mine and Adam's(and many other people's I suspect) bay window buses 
have had this very common problem-they just don't start worth a cr**.  I 
solved this issue by running a brand new 12 gauge wire from the ignition 
switch back to the solenoid.

How to test- volt meter, not just a circuit light. Put a voltage meter on 
the pulled off wire from the ignition switch at the solenoid(important- the 
voltage at the front is meaningless) and then get your daughter to turn the 
key. I was getting about 6 volts. Adam was the same.  Old, corroded wires, 
bad connections. No hot start relay needed IMHO. With the new wire, there is 
12 volts being delivered to the solenoid. My bus has never started so well.
