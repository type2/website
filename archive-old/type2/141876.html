Seriously sounds like the front shocks are shot. Also check everything else - tie rods, ball joints, etc. Also switch the tires around and see if the bad behavior goes with it. 

As for the engine adjustments, seems way off in several categories telling me that 1 adjustment was off requiring a whole other bunch of adjustments to compensate. Check those injector seals and EGR valve too. Mine were bad throughing everything else off too. Start with mechanical adjustments (linkages, valves, distributor drive verification) and then move to vacuum system integrity and then finally adjustments to timing, dwell, and fuel.
