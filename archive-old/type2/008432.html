It might, but the heat transfer area on the filter doesn't amount to
much.  Also, it's likely that the oil is somewhere near the air
temperature anyway.  Figure that the air is around 80C (assuming that
the thermostat is doing its thing).  At this temperature there isn't
enough difference between the air temp and the oil temp for much heat
to transfer either way.

VW de Mexico has enough confidence to run this setup and I know from
personal experience that it can get might hot in those parts!
