I think the key of the issue is a tire with a solid side wall rating to
handle the high center of gravity of your bus for stability.  I have always
bought the higher end of the "best tire" that I could afford.  My last
purchase on my 71 Westy is a set of lt195/75radial lt's all seasons tires
from Wes Schabb, which when driving on several terrains in Oregon have never
let me down.

Buy the best you can afford, and know your limitations..
