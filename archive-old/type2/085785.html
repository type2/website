This might be a long post but I want everybody that might be able to help 
know all the details.

1971 Westy.
Stock 1600 dual port.
Rebuilt motor bought by previous owner and put in less than 10,000 miles 
before I got it.

Ran fine with stock dist and carb.
But wanted a little more power for the long trips so here is what I did.

Installed brand new Kadrons.
New 009 Dist. with pertronix ignitor and coil.
Extractor exhaust with quiet pack muffler.

Problem is:
Idles perfect.

Idle mixture screw is adjusted as per A.J.'s site.
Compression checked on all cylanders between 100-120.

Timed to 10 degrees BTDC at idle.
But dist only goes to max 28 degress BTDC at around 3000-3500 RPM.
