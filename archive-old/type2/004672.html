White smoke may indicate ingestion of brake fluid through a leaky servo
diaphragm.  Worth checking servo assistance if you has one fitted.  You
should retain enough air for three or more power assists when the engine is
off and you push the pedal immediately.  If there are less it could be the
diaphragm.
