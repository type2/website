Yes, buzzers drive me bonkers, too.

I took the cube buzzer box found below bottom of fuse box base . . .

and I bought a 'ding - dong' chime from Radio Shack (a couple of bucks). I 
wired the 'ding -dong' chime to the guts of the buzzer box. I needed the 
base of the box for the prongs to go back into the fuse box base.

This was the smallest chime I could get, but still much too loud.  So I 
glued some foam rubber on the chime body to reduce some noise.

Not overly loud, but just annoying enough.

Key in ignition (on or off), and driver door opened, and 'ding - ding'...

switch (no soldering needed).  Works great!!  Excerpt if I push the head
