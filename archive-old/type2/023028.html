You have to get rid of *all* of the existing rust (sandblast till it's 
gunmetal-grey) and then cover the surface with *many* coats of something 
that will keep the water away from the metal.  I use Corroless from 
Eastwood Restoration (www.eastwoodco_dot_com I think).  I noticed water pooling 
underneath the seal as well, not sure what can be done about it but I don't 
really care anymore since it isn't hurting anything.
