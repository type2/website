> Swap the tanks.  You'll be a lot happier knowing you set this up right.  This 
> question has come up a couple times before and I always make this same 
> observation:  With the return going into the tank (where it's supposed to go) 
> you are using your tank to cool the gas that is coming back from your VERY hot 
> engine compartment.  With the T-fitting, you are recirculating the already-hot 
> gas and making it hotter.  Live in Alaska?  Maybe not a problem.  Drive in 90+ 
> degree weather?  Plan on vapor lock.
