I think that the main component of this stuff is methanol.  It has the
effect of leaning the mixture out a bit.

It's a bit of a scam, really.  Most cars will pass smog on the first try
without any special preparation.  A lot of folks are nervous about this
and get the stuff as "insurance".  If they pass, they are happy.  Even if
they don't pass, most people won't go through the hassle of applying for
the refund.
