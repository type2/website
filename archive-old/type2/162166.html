With the pump running unrestricted (no pressure regulator), 1.5-2L/ 
min is the minimum output. When I tested my pump, it did 2-2.25L/min.

With the pressure regulator attached, there should be at least 1L/min  
coming out of the PR when the injectors are not firing.
