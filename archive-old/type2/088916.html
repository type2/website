It's normal for oil pressure to drop dramatically with increase in oil =
temp. When I was putting dino oil in my '79 Type IV engine, 40w =

provide good oil pressure.

I think that 50w might be too thick, unless you're driving through the =
Sahara Desert in summer. It could cause the front seal to blow when =
starting the engine on a very cool morning.
