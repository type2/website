in an unmarked white box, with a date stamp of APR 15 1981.  I expect these

rings/spacer.  [Nothing on the sheet indicates manufacturer.]

down on the compression rings. [they are NOT marked 'top' as you would 
expect]

The #2 rings have a NOTCH edging on the OUTSIDE.  One side [top or bottom, 
that is the question] is notched.
