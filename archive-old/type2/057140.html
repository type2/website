They're wrong.  '76 is 2-liter (and a different clutch diameter from the
'75-back 1.8 liter).  Regarding the fuel line, the cloth line was actually
superceded by a smooth jacketed version.  But be absolutely sure you use the
special high-pressure version, or else your bus may burn.
