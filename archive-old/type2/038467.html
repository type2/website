(74 transporter, ongoing rebuild began summer 99)

List,

After new points, condenser and plug wires - she belched forth smoke
from the assembly oil in her cylinders and came to life!

I held the rpms  until the thermostat opened, then a little longer and
killed it, then i did it again.  I have a little oil leak from the oil
pump and a small one from the cooler - but she runs like a top.

We got the passenger side front arms, knuckle, caliper assembled last
night. Starting to look like a bus again.

Gonna try to get the driver's side done saturday or sunday, bleed the
brakes and she hits the ground.  Been on jack stands for almost a year.

Got a few cooling tin leaks to attend to and an exhaust leak to fix before
it sees any serious road time, but these are the last days.

Feels good, i tell ya. I was giddy last night and was chipper and aware
this morning.  Finishing (well- almost) a project helps the head, i tell
ya.

Thanks again to the list - wouldn't have done it with out you!
