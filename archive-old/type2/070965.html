I volks- If it is from cold, check the ground strap on the gearbox/tranny to
chassis- I had a problem with this a while back- the strap was broken. This
was intermittent and always the first start- afterwards it was OK. I replaced
the strap and the problem disappeared. Another common thing is the "hot start"
problem but this is well documented in the archives- one solution is to
install a relay so that the power to the starter solenoid goes directly from
the battery and not up to the ignition switch  and back. I did this fix so if
you want info' pmail me and I'll be glad to help out.
