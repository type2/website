Eddie,

Have someone pump the brakes while you're underneath the bus, looking at the
master cylinder. You might be able to see where it's leaking from. It might be
leaking from where the brake light switches screw into the master cylinder. If
so, unscrew the brake light switches, wrap Teflon tape around the switches'
threads, and screw them back in.

Good luck.
