Concerning air filters. Probably the least expensive and easiest thing to do
would be to install some K & N filters that clamp around the throat of the
carb. A example of what I'm talking about can be see here
http://larryscorvair_dot_com/Merchant2/merchant.mv?Screen=PROD&Store_Code=LCP&Pr
oduct_Code=LCF-005&Category_Code=LCF Note: the filter shown probably isn't
the correct size diameter, but it gives you an idea of what I'm talking
about. If you go to the K&N site you can see that they make filters in
almost every configuration possible.
http://www.knfilters_dot_com/universal/universal.htm

For all the people who are about to flame me, let me digress and say that I
believe oil bath filters are the least restrictive and most efficient
filters that you can put on your vehicle. I'm just giving a recommendation
for quick, easy, and effective.
