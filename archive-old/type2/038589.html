Don't use an EZ [to break] out.
Is there any hex left in the bottom of the 6mm allen screw?
I use an impact driver with a 6mm allen socket and a BFH. Wail away until it
pops loose. Even rounded ones come loose.
The impact driver at Harbor Freight is good enough. It is one of the few
tools they have that you can beat on and it works. #37530. It is 1/2" drive.
You will need a 1/2 to 3/8 adaptor since most 6mm allen sockets are 3/8
drive.
