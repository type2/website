> 1. The gas question...is it really okay to run 87 octane?

Yes, if your engine isn't pinging you don't need higher octane.  The "91"
octane requirement is a european system whose ratings are a bit higher
than the american system.

> 2. Oil-I live in sacramento, where it gets about 100 degrees on average in 
> the summer-I'm using strait 30 weight now-should I change?

I'd recommend 10W40 or 20W50 in the summer.

> 3. Speed-How fast should I drive on the freeway-it screams past 60mph
