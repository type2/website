The little box is an aftermarket (generic) relay installed to eliminate the
classic turn-the-key-nothing-happens syndrome. One wire connects to the
battery, one to the starter solenoid and one to the wire from the ignition
switch (apparently this one gets ground from the mounting screws). Using a
jumper from the battery, touch each of the connections, the one that makes
it click is the connection for the starter switch.  One of the others
should be marked with a B (hopefully) for battery if you clean off the
grease. It might be worth getting a new relay at your generic auto parts
house just to get the wiring schematic and a new/reliable relay. They only
cost about $5.00
