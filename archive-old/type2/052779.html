Before messing with the fuel filter, fill the tank with gas and check
  the flowrate at the fuel pump. Just disconnect the fuel line at the
  pump inlet and have a small container to catch the gas. If you get a
  good flow then the filter is ok. Good flow is anything greater than
  a quart in five minutes. Saves you the trouble of disturbing the
  connection to the tank if there is no problem.
