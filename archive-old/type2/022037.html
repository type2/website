Hi,

You are right the transmission shouldn't whine, and it 
could indicate wear. I would change the tranmission oil 
and see if that makes any difference (remember to 
remove the filler plug before the drain plug - if its 
empty and the filler plug won't come out...)

Routine maintenance may fix the surging.
As the bus is new to you it would be a good idea (if you 
havn't done so already) to replace points, condenser, 
spark plugs, leads, rotor arm, air filter, engine oil, 
petrol filter, then you know where you stand on all these
common expendables.
Of course you would combine the above with tappet 
adjustment and a tune up.
That will be a major slice of a weekend to do all that, 
but it will give you a known datum from which to work 
and will eliminate the causes of many common faults.

Good luck
