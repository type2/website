Dave,

In my 83 air-cooled automatic, there is one vacuum outlet on the IAD
connected to the fuel pressure regulator. The other outlet is on the
throttle body, and it is connected to the distributor. If you reverse them
the engine runs poorly and very rich. After some pondering, I would guess
that the throttle valve is connected with the tee to the fuel pressure
regulator and the IAD. I hope that this is some help.
