You can put in a spark plug insert, you have to tap out the hole.  Use some
grease on the tap so most of your shavings stay with the tap.  If some of
the shavings get into the cylinder, don't sweat it too much, they are
aluminum and will be passed out.  The only danger is some might be trapped
under the exhaust valve which may hold it open for a short time, but then
should clean itself out.  If you don't feel comfortable doing it yourself,
take it to a shop.  You don't need to pull the engine and the whole process
takes less than half an hour.
