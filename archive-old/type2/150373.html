Subject: Re: [T2] Re: Short range FM transmitters

The AM-band kit will need a longish wire antenna, but won't be so sensitive
to obstructions (like metal Bus bodies) as an FM-band transmitter.  The
fidelity of the sound will likely be about the same with either mode given
the level of circuit sophistication found in a simple kit.  Individual radio
receivers may have been optimized for FM fidelity however.

The main differences for a given power will be (1) range (AM longer, FM
lesser) and (2) noise (AM noisier, FM quieter).  For practical purposes, I
doubt if it makes much difference within the area of a typical Bus
gathering.  Either way, it sounds like a fun project and a good idea!
