Lordy, why is it that having a lawyer absolves people of having *any*

If the seat mounting method looks unsafe, then don't do it.

What it seems that no one remembers is that it is *not* the seat that 
holds you in, but the seat belt.  The seat mounting only needs to be 
strong enough to hold the seat in place in the event of a crash.

And, if you are not wearing a seat belt, then having the seat come behind 
you as you fly thru the windshield is probably not going to make one iota 
of difference about the injuries you may or may not sustain.
