A couple of thoughts here:

1/4 inch brake line is 6.35mm in diameter, which is larger than the stock
fuel line tubing.  You should use a smaller OD tube to avoid stretching
the rubber line.

Rubber line is appropriate wherever there is going to be relative movement
between the connections.  If you use steel line in a place where it flexes
it will fail.
