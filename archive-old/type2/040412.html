To be fair, these, too, are built by Bosch in Mexico, not Germany (as is
much of Bosch's product these days; their remans generally aren't
remaufactured in Germany either).  But these are brand new, not the
remanufactured ones that are coming out of the same factory.  These were
another "gray market" find, apparantly originally intended for distribution
outside of the U.S. (And outside of South America, I suppose, given that the
Mexi-busses don't use this starter.)  I'm not sure why Bosch has been
shipping only remanufactured ones to us here in the U.S., when this proves
that their factory is/was equipped to build new ones.  Perhaps these were
meant for countries where Bosch doesn't have a strong aftermarket presence
and isn't equipped to process core deposits/returns.  Who knows.  In any
event, for $99 brand new, versus about 50% more for the reman Bosch (plus
core), it's definately a no-brainer.
