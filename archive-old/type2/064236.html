After I blathered that I thought ten thousandths were overkill for engine
building, somebody sent me a note and said that when he had rebuilt a
Corvair engine recently he had measured the valve stems for spec. Said the
spec required a tolerance of a few ten thousandths.

That gave me pause--here somebody is writing specs for an internal
combustion engine in ten thousandths of an inch, and (this is damned
amazing) that somebody is General Motors Corporation. Curioser and
curioser!
