> I have a third relay on my fuse box. It is smaller than the one for the
> dimmer and the one for the turnsignals/flashers. It is located on the
> passenger side of the fuse box. It appears to be one space over from the
> end. Out of this relay comes a gray wire and a gray wire with a black
> stripe. One of the wires goes up into a big sheathed bundle. The other is
> attached to the relay, but not at the other end.
> Does any one know what this relay is for and where the other end of the wire
> should go.

It's the door buzzer. It goes off when the key is inserted and the door is
opened.

The gray/black wire goes to a terminal on the ignition switch hence the
bundle. The grey wire goes to the black contact switch in the door-jam. When
the switch is closed it grounds the circuit and the buzzer goes waaaah.
