To save Karl some heartburn, I'll fill in:

Here's the exact path your fuel travels if you're set up stock:

I'll note connections to vacuum/elec as (###) as well:

1 tank

2 filter

3 Pump (elec.)

4 #3 injector (elec.)

5 #4 injector (elec.)

6 cold start valve (elec.)

7 #2 injector (elec.)

8 #1 injector (elec.)

9 fuel pressure regulator (vacuum)

10 back to tank

That's all there is and there ain't no more. Your pressure regulator

gets vacuum from the engine to do it's job. If your mechanic tested it per the
Bentley (official) manual, then your problem is somewher else. What are the
symptoms again?
