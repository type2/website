"Conventional Wisdom" strikes again.  Change your driving habits, change your
carb, change your gear ratios, change your vehicle.  The 009 is an acceptable
distributor in your dragster 2180 bug.  It is not an acceptable distributor in
a 1600 bus.  Straight mechanical advance causes flat spots, lousy mileage.  If
you can change all of the above, it'll work just fine.  If you want to drive a
1600 bus, change your distributor.  A 205 works well.
