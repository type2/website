I got a Carfax once. It doesn't show everything, just the legally
required reportable things, like transfers, insurance salvaging, smog
inspections, etc. Salvaging is a mixed bucket. You can have a
perfectly good vehicle with a salvage title that might have just been
turned into a wrecking yard for a blown motor or couldn't pass smog.
On the other hand, you can have totalled cars that were uninsured and
were rebuilt without being reported to DMV.
