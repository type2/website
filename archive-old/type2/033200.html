    Well my mystery is getting closer to being solved (I hope). Today I
put my bus on jack stands and listened to the sounds of the transmission
in first, neutral and reverse with a metal rod as a stethoscope. The
noises were loudest in reverse and were coming from the upper front of
the transmission (where the gears live). When I changed the oil in the
gearbox, there were no chunks or even slivers of metal to be found. So,
here is my conclusion: it's not good. If anyone has an opinion on
whether I should buy I good used or rebuilt transmission I'd love to
hear why and where I could find one. If anyone has a guess as to what
appears to be wrong with my transmission (loud clacking in reverse) and
whether it can be fixed by a marginally talented mechanic (me) feel free
to let me know.
