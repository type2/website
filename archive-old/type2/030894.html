OK, I will use the Rocky Mountain Motor Works numbers etc. (Bus Depot uses
same numbers

1.   remove old fuel pump
2.   block off old pump mount, use RMMW part VC14 001 ($9.95)
3.   use electric fuel pump conversion kit RMMW part 938-439 ($52.50)
  and follow the instructions

finished, should take maybe 2 hours

(fuel pump will not work with Fuel Injection, but yours doesn't have Fuel
Injection because they already have a special electric pump)

the block off plate will not work on 72-79 buses you will have to make one
for them
