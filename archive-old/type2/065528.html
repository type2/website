The Bus Boys "Blueprint" poster specs the width of a '62 Deluxe
at 1800 cm, measuring out-to-out on the rear bumper.  That
translates to 70.8" I went home at lunch, measured my '67 Deluxe
at 71" (5'-11") out-to-out at the same location.  It's 73"
(6'-1") measured to the outside of the (round) mirrors.
