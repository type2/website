Subject: factory stereo in a baywindow?

Last fall I noticed a baywindow bus at a local metal salvage place.  When I
stopped to inquire about being able to get some parts off of it, they gave

but in my haste I was only able to pull it and cut wires.

Anyway, I figure the front dash speaker was used for one channel of the
stereo, but where was the other channel?  I don't think this bus (nor my
'73) have any provision for 2 speakers in the front dash.  Did they just
mount a speaker in the back somewhere?  Anyone have one of these installed
