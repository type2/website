> Where do you connect your CHT gauge?
On my '71 Westy I cut the loop off the connector and wedged what is left 
between two fins on the underside on the # 4 cylinder head.  If you do it 
this way try to approximate the "spark plug" location and be sure to keep the 
sender away from the cylinder head studs.  They are big heat sinks and you 
will be essentially be reading the case temp.
I just built an engine for my son's '72 Beetle.  I JB Welded the sender to 
the underside of the # 3 cylinder.  It's too early to say how well this will 
work, but it should work out really well.  The JB folks say their product is 
good to 550 F.
I hope this helps.
