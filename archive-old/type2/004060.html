As someone who once worked in the International Clearance area for a major
overnight shipping company based in Memphis, TN, I know for a fact that
packages are only spot checked.  And the amount of them even spot checked
depends greately upon the frame of mind that the customs inspectors are in
at the time.  THe chances of your package or shipment being assesed duties
are about 1 in 15 or 20.  But that is only with one shipper at one point of
entry.  Probably holds try most everywhere else also.

Of course, you ARE duty bound, as an American citizen, to report any duties
that are due if you are not assesed any!  HA!
