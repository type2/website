Hi Chris,

This is a big job. It took me about 100 man/hrs of work to install a 
late-bay (74-79) top onto a 79 transporter body. It could be done by a 
professional welder/bodyshop in about half that time. Have a look at:

www.pottsfamily_dot_ca/westfakia and check out the "Westfakia Creation 
Gallery".

The main issue is with the double bed, it makes the hole in the roof a 
LOT larger. And the roof gets heavier in the process as well.

But since my bus now sleeps 5, I'm certainly not going to talk you out 
of it.
