The just-in-time manufacturing process is (we were taught) based on current tax laws which tax inventory. Also that wharehouse costs money to maintain, build, heat/cool, and naturally there is a certain amount of inventory loss to theft, accidents, and of course frequent design changes could render a wharehouse of inventory obsolete.
