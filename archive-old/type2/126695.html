There is a cheaper, better way to solve both of your problems and never have
to set points again.  Buy a Pertronix or other Hall effect triggered kit.
As long as your shaft is not wobbling more than 0.050" ( told to me by
Pertronix engineer) the magnetic collar will not hit the sensor.  If you add
a high energy coil as well you can open up your plug gaps, maybe  to .0050or
.0060, and get better starting and mileage as well ( I have not yet
installed by new coil so can not confirm from personal experience.  The
points are a bear to set, and if you have any shaft runout, the dwell will
be all over the place.  Mine is now rock steady and should never change
