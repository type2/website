Then just confirm the circuit with your multimeter. Check drop across batt
+ and big bolt solinoid, then solinoid to switch, then through switch, then
back to small terminal at the solinoid, then to ground through the
solinoid. Anyplace your dropping more than 0.2v is weakness adding to the
problem. Just do the voltage drops with a trustworthy meter.
