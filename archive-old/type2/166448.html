Subject: Re: [T2] First Bus / Rebuild or Buy Longblock ?

Hi Roger,

First off, what year is your bus??   If it has a 1600 in it, I would  
suspect it's  a 68-71 model; though the 1600's  were offered in 72-79  
buses in europe and Australia.

If your bus is a 68-71, you don't have the right hardware to install  
a 2L. Your cooling system, exhaust, alternator and engine mounts are  
completely different from what is needed for a 2L engine.  You would  
be much better off rebuilding your existing engine into a 1776cc   
configuration instead.

The one advantage to building your own is that you know precisely  
what's inside. The downside is that there's no-one else to blame if  
something goes wrong.
