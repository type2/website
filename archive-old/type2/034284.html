Well in some turbine powered aircraft there is a unit called a fuel/oil heat 
exchanger.  As the name implies heat from the oil is transfered to the fuel. 
  Cools the oil and heats the fuel.  Now those of you with external coolers 
could probably run you fuel and oil lines side by side for the winter and 
them seperate them in summer to prevent vapor lock.
