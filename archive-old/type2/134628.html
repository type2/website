I see from your pics that it is a Calif Vanagon, so yes you should have an O2 sensor.

Follow your EFI engine harness from the center over to the left. You should have a single
grey wire that goes to the #3 temp sensor. Then a small group that go up to black relay. 
Then a large group that goes to the double relay. Then a pair of 2 wires that goes to each 
injector. All these wires are white in the grey sheath. Now look for a green wire coming out 
of the grey sheath. It is the only non-white wire in the whole EFI harness. That one is the wire 
that goes to the O2 sensor.

Follow the green wire and it should connect to a terminal sticking thru the engine tin.
The other end of that terminal goes to the sensor. The sensor is screwed into the end of the 
catalytic converter that is parallel with the left exhaust manifold and left heater box. 
Between I and F in this pic: http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/oemstockvanagon.html
