On a serious note.  If it is just a standard modular strobe light it
has everything to do with the manufacturer as _some_ strobes are gas
injected and actually require boxed disposal because even an inert
bulb has high pressure gas in it.  Some units can have a bulb
swopped out, but they are high end and usually for light shows,
etc..  The small ones for your house that are found at Radio Shack
are disposable.  If you have one of those, it's a fixed unit.
