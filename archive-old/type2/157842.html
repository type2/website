Hi Chai,

I didn't see mention of a fuel filter being changed. A blocked fuel  
filter might let the float bowl recharge at idle, but not when the  
fuel flow is increased.

Suggestion: Pull a spark plug immediately after it stalls. If it is  
black and wet, you have flooded it and the problem is with the carb.  
(or too much fuel pressure)  If the spark plug is brown and dry then  
it's a problem with not enough fuel supply.
