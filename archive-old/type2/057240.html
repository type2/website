It's not a cooling flap, it's where a heater pipe is supposed to come
through the tin.  If the heater pipe is missing (it should go to your heat
exchanger) then it would leave a big hole which would let hot air in.
Since you can't just walk into Chief Auto and buy that pipe, I'd credit
your mechanic for using his noggin and sealing off the leak the best way he
knew how.
