 You may want to try J.C Taylor if it is not a daily driver.  They specialize in Antique auto insurance and I have them on the 55 Pritschenwagen, 66 Beetle & my Packard.  They denied insurance for the 66 Deluxe and said that they do not insure buses, but I think that the real reason is that the only pictures that I had of it still had the wild hippy paint scheme and now it is n primer.  You have to send in pictures and they will approve or deny you on a per vehicle basis.   They are pretty much in the business of insuring only antique restored vehicles.  If it is a daily driver you may be SOL and I am damn surprised that they took my P-wagen.
Btw, $300 per year is pretty reasonable in Chicago.  Insurance rates are based on what zip code you live in.  The next cheapest reputable company that I found was Farmer's.
