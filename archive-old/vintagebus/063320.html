I have sometimes had problems like this with my timing light when
the timing light pickup is too close to another plug wire.  Say
I clamp it on the #1 wire but the clamp is also lying on #2 wire --
sometimes I'll get some erratic flashes.  Moving the clamp to to
somewhere on the #1 wire where that wire is all by itself usually
helps.  My timing light has a "shielded" metal pickup clamp and
this still happens.  You might try clamping on right next to the
plug, where the plug wires are fairly far apart.

might be helpful in the future.
