Well, you get what you pay for.  Everyone seems to be obsessed with
*cheep* when it comes to buses.  I had a kid from Florida *beg* me
to sell him my panel *as* *is* - with no rust repair.

bought - was $800 and needed an alternator.  My '56 was $400, and

the car for $3000 so far!   And I still don't know what I'm going to
do with it...
