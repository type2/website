know think I know why, which some empirical testing should prove. My SP
manifold MUST be bent. The old motor's passanger head was so flycut, the
engine was narrower, so I believe it got bent in some when it was torqued
sdown on the poor old engine, and now doesn't seal 100% on a properly built
mill. Will test with WD-40, and confirm this, but I do believe the spare
manifold has to come out and get installed.

calculated AF, with hight 02, and high HC, should point to a small intake
manifold leak, where running under speed to produce vac, and enough
enrichment and idle speed produces a posisble, but dirty idle. This seems to
be the only permutation of events to lead to these numbers.
