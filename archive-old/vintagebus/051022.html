Subject: RE: [VB] What exaust should I use?

Just remember the exaust studies. The air flow over the top of the bus will
drag your exaust up and to the side and be allowed to be sucked back into
the fresh air intakes. You must vent exaust at least to the side of the bus
and slightly down to prevent it from being cycled back into the vehicle.
Bugs and type 3 don't have this problem because air flow is different over
them.
