Again, take this as an opinion, but if the choke is adjusted too far shut,
the choke itself will do nothing unless the power is enabled.  When I say
"Do nothing"  I mean, it will no close and WILL WORK regardless of the
adjustment.  When the power is enabled, the "bi-metallic strip" takes
control over the choke flap, forcing it to close an incorrect amount.
