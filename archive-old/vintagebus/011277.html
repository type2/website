What it sounds like you have is a bench seat kombi right?  What you want then 
is a full length seat with a folding backrest (near the doors)  Just look at 
the floor (belly pans are under the floors on certain buses) if you have 
welded in brackets for a seat just inside the cargo doors (just past the 
sill), then that is what you need.

The 2/3 middle seat was most commony used on walkthru busses (busses with 
bucket front seats.)  I think ill put together a page with al the different 
middle seats in a day or two so you can see the diferent seats.
