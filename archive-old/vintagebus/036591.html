 the only difference  between 63/64-on the left 1500 aircleaners and 64-67-on 
the right aircleaners is the breathing air tube location- whether the air 
cleaner is on the right or left the air breather tube connection should be 
towards the center of the bus- towards the engine.   So if it is on the left 
you need a long breather tube, like a 40hp. thats the deal  with the 40hp 
style-  the ones with the triangle bracket you attach at the coil screws, I 
have had to make and adaptor piece so THAT kind with the metal tube from carb 
to cleaner lines up right when used on a 1600, but the 1500 kind should fit 
the same- you are using a shortish metal piece on top of the carb, then a 
rubber connector piece, then a metal piece that bolts to the ceiling hanger 
bracket right?  I hope that helps.
