At idle speeds the gen isn't charging and you are eventually running off
your battery. With accessories on, i.e. headlights, brake lights, radio,
etc, then there is a further drain on the battery and you will see the
gen/oil light slightly glow as the voltage level in the battery is demanding
a charge and the primary regulator relay is engaged but the generator is
running at too low of an RPM to deliver it. It's common and nothing to worry
about. A bright red light is something to worry about.
