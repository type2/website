It sounds like a good deal to me. I don't think I could throw one together
for that price. My regular "one size fits all" tow bar cost me at least that
thirty years ago.

I don't know about the rental houses in your area but here in the North East
they don't like to (or won't) rent a dolly without one of their vehicles
that has a hitch. Usually that is a truck or the high dollar big SUV.

If you buy it you will probably get your $80 back the first time you use it,
then when you need to help someone out or need it again, it will cost you
nothing. I stand corrected it will cost you storing it until then, but I'd
bet you can find a place.
