Hey Rex,

This is how I was told to time w/ a light and it works for me.  Look for the 
notch in your crank pulley this is top dead center.  Make a dot w/ paint so 
its more visiable.  Measure 28-30 degrees after top (to the right of the 
notch) dead center, this will be for your advance.  Take your dizzy cap off 
and look for the notch in the rim of the canister.  Turn your engine by hand 
untill the rotor button points at the notch (#1) and the crank pulley is 
closest to the vertical crack in the case.  Hook up the pos/neg from the 
timing light to the battery and put the clip on the #1 plug wire and start 
him up.  Make sure you have already loosened the 10mm nut on the dizzy 
clamp.  Rev him up and make sure the advance mark goes to the case split.  
It may not idle exactly at TDC but make sure it does advance completely at 
2500-300 RPM.  There may be some errors in this but my bus likes it!  Hop it 
helps!
