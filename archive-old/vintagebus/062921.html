  In AZ they have a book (MVD) that tells you where all the VIN numbers
(primary and secondary) are on cars goinf back to at least 1960. They (AZ
MVD) will not accept the plate in tha cab only, they use that as the
secondary and use the stamped numbers in the engine compartment for the
primary.
  What really screws them up is if you show them the engine compartment
numbers on a real early bus and then they want a secondary and there aren't
any. Then they see the chassis number on the bulkhead,then the shit starts,
into secondary to prove that one number is a vin and the other a chassis.

think some makes had it before then but I don't think any VWs did.

VINs like 215 xxx xxx starting in 1965.
