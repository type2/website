working on cars. Anytime he spends time not billing for time he is
losing money. Knowing how many people are in general toward mechanics

have spent way too many nights worrying about how he could pay the

of working on a billed job. Moral of the story, cut the guy some
slack, just because he won't work on your bus doesn't mean he's lazy.
