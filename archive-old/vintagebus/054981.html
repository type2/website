Hi John:

Charge up the battery and then get the multimeter out on DC setting. Just
turn lights on, put multimeter leads on the correct battery poles. If the
voltage starts dropping and a 0 volts flat line situation can be extapolated
in about 2-3 minutes it's the battery. Alternator problems - once the
battery is charged and the vehicle can be started, attach the multimeter
leads which should read at a constant voltage above 12 somewhere which is
the alternator doing it's things. Don't know the VW specs but my Volvo is
14.6.... ask me how I know!! Mine was a dead battery.
