you can use tempered or laminated.      call you r friendly local glass shop,
and ask about prices and if tinted glass is available.   Having a template is
good, but be aware that they don't cut it perfectly, and you have to be
careful with laminated or it'll break.   Tempered, that stuff is tough.  I
dropped an intake manifold ten feet onto a piece of tempered glass and it just
bounced.

VW buses used tempered through 60 on all windows, laminated 61-63, and the
back to tempered.   US spec buses have laminated windsheilds, euro spec
tempered.    Having your bus with the other than standard type for its
destination was an option as well.
