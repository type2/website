Do you mean OC25 or OC51?  The OC51 is for the Diesel Rabbit and the 
beefiest VW filter I can think of apparently seeing regular 150PSI 
pressures.

The Purolator 39001 (HP1 version of the 30001) gets my nod compared to a 
OC51.  The only issue I cannot tell is the actual pressure on each that the 
by-pass would be activiated, and I never will know that information so kind

passages to flow more oil.

... Plus Purolator is "Made in the USA" which goes a long way in my book 
these days.  Mahle is made in Austria.

In reality both are great filters and cannot go wrong either way, I just 
prefer the 39001.
