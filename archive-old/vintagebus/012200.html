Last spring I had my motor torn down (got bits caught in
two cylinders) and decided to replace the exhaust.  I bought
new heater boxes, and welded up all the flanges to make the
whole assembly tighter.

The good: way more air volume than before, no funny smells.

The bad: the new style heater boxes do not heat the air, so
I have more air, just not as warm.
