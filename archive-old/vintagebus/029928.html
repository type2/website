I think one thing that needs to be pointed out about the proceedure
outlined in this post for cleaning out a heat riser tube is that it is
extremely dangerous. If you look at a can of caustic soda, also known as
lye, you will see the skull and cross-bones symbol. It is there because
that stuff is really that dangeous. It turns your flesh into soap using
the moisture in your skin. It does that to your eyes too. The damage can
be done before you feel it. It's especially foolish to use it with
boiling water. Don't do it.

I hope that got the point across. It was a little OT, but I saw another
post just in the last few days that also mentioned using this stuff. Use
your lye for cleaning your drains only. Follow the directions and heed
the warnings on the label. Lets all be safe, please.
