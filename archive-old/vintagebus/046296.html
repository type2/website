Real clamps are nice, but you can also go to the hardware store and get 4 
turnbuckles, and grind the one end so it fits under the rain gutter flatly, 
the other end hooks ove rthe edge of the rook.   Use a hose clamp to hold it 
to upright leg of rack for stabilisation.   
No, it's not as pretty, might not want to use this on fresh paint/shiny bus.
........but it will help a rack be functional.  I have used this method for 
many miles with stuff piled on rack w/o problems, but not since my bus got 
paint.  Then I sorted out a rack w/ real clamps and material to keep it from 
scratching the paint.
