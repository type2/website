Chris,

The hinges can be cut from "piano hinge" stock available at most good harware
stores. I found some stainless steel stock for mine and it works great on all
6!

I saw some guy mentioned in Hot VW's "Transporter Talk" a month or two ago
who makes or remanufacturers "jail bars".  I found some old ones at a swap
meet. The tubing was pretty bad, so I tossed them and just kept the fittings
and the vertical center piece. The tubing is just aluminum tubing which is
easy to find and cut to length.
