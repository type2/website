If you really want to see a really clean IRS bus conversion come to BBB V
and see Matts 60 DD conversion. This conversion used the 68 type 2 trailing
arms and components. I was told by someone that the 68 only type 2 spring
plates will fit right on a split, the only thing you need to do is weld in
the pivot points for the arms. This was done by cutting into the forks and
boxing them in at the torsion housing, then weld in the pivots in the boxed
sections. His bus rides at stock height and now has the options of numerous
transmission and gear choices, also the strength of the late bay suspension.
If I get his bus back up on the hoist in the shop I will photograph this
conversion and post it somewhere on the net.
