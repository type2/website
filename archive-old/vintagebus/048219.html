usually the motor is good for a core.   You take all the stuff off and strip 
it down to a long block (as per Muir idiot manual)  to give to the 
rebuilders.   Then you put your stuff back on.  A bad motor does not mean you 
have to convert to 12V, you have all the working 6V stuff right?   6V is fine 
and dandy.

BTW, while the motor is out, drain your trans and RGBoxes, put new axle boots 
on, and refill wth GL-L tranny oil.   Install cleaned center plugs, then fill 
boxes, last fill  center section.   Warming the trans oil in a bucket of hot 
water before filling is nice for pumping it in easier.   Might as well put in 
a new engine to body seal, and clean all your light grounds and hots too.
