Maybe shorty trucks and busses were an attempt to put the driver closer to the heater?  Search the archives for heat tips, there was a lot of ideas a year or two ago. Fill all the leaks above the engine seal, make sure the flaps & thermostat work, seal up the heat ducts, insulate the long tube and put the pedal pan on the front.
