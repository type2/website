When we do these vintage reprints of VW workshop manuals we usually try to
cover the same chunks (model years) that the factory covered with the
various editions. Trying to combine various chunks can be difficult and
usually requires some significant editorial time to figure out what should
be included. We also feel that remaining faithful to what the factory
originally did with these manuals is the best way to reproduce the
information accurately. I suspect that May of '59 would be when the bus got
upgraded to the 36hp motor and therefore a whole new manual was put out by
the factory, correct me if I'm wrong on this.

The reason I put a May 1959 start date on the book is because that is where
the '59-'63 transporter workshop manual that we have starts. I determined
this from the technical data section at the back of the manual.

If you wanted to let me take a look at the '53/'54 and '55 manuals that
