Yep.  Gotta agree.  If you are talking about taking a good running bus and 
stripping everything off of it (parts, not accessories) and selling the shell 
then the seats, engine, trans, etc. well then yeah, that's kind of a car 
whore thing to do.  If you are talking about pulling accessories off that is 
completely understandable.  I have a few things on my Binz that I have moved 
from bus to bus to bus, kind of like taking your appliances with you when you 
move.  

The other aspect is the value of the accessories can be a significant part of 
the bus.  Safaris $500, Ambulance fans - $400,  trip speedo - $300, side step 
- $100, Roof rack - $300, shifter extension - $25 8-).  There is over $1600 
and there isn't even a bus in there!  There are a lot of busses out there 
that go for less than that.  Very hard to sell a $1000 bus with $1600 in 
accessories and get $2600.  Much easier to sell separate.

FYI - I have seen the Ellis Barnambocamper.  Sweet ride and a bargain at the 
price with or without all the extra goodies.
