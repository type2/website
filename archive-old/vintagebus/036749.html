well, just keep a bunch of belts with you, as since the option of driving 
more conservatively probably won't appeal.     to be more specific, speedily 
revving to 5,500 is yes likely to affect the belt.   quickly revving up and 
down would affect it more than smoothly revving to a top speed and cruising 
in 4th.

other question- are you pulleys lined up properly?  The crank pulley in the 
same line as the gen pulley?  if they are not, then belts will be affected.  
The reason for this offset could be a 38A gen, using diff gen front cover 
plates, or diff gen pulley.
