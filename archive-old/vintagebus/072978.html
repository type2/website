Subject: Center Pivot Bushing

Helped a friend replace his center pivot bushing and pin today.
Unless a normal bus, the problem with his was that it was way too
*tight*!  Steering the bus was difficult, and near the end of
the travel in each direction, the poor thing would let out this
"scroink!" sound.

The biggest issue was getting the old one out.  I ended up rigging
a puller and a huge piece of angle iron to lever against it to get
it loose.  The new one went in like normal.  It probably took
an hour to get the old one out, and 30 minutes to get the new one in,
reamed, and everything assembled.

I took detailed pix, and eventually I'll get around to filling in the 
text.  This is an easy job when you have the correct tools!
