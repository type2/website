>a grade 8 6mm bolt

No such animal.  Metric bolts have property classes like 8.8 (just a
tiny bit stronger than SAE Grade 5) or 10.9 (just a tiny bit stronger
than SAE Grade 8).  Sometimes the "8.8" on the head of a metric bolt
gets confused with a "Grade 8" SAE bolt.

http://www.atlanticfasteners_dot_com/tech_help/tips-004.html

The most common metric bolts at the hardware store are 8.8.  In new cars,
8.8 is pretty common, while 10.9 and 12.9 bolts get used in some high-load
applications.
