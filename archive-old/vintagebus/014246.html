"Wilder" <wgrippo_at_soe.ucsd_dot_edu> wrote:
Clamping the heater hoses on both ends in the engine compartment is a
good idea - those things always tend to come loose.  This may not be
the only source of your smell, however.  Underneath your engine (lying
on the ground) there should be two (probably rusty) heater boxes that
surround your exhaust on either side.  These are the "beginning" of
your heat's trip to the front of the bus. Following the large (~3-4
inch diameter) hoses from here to the front of the bus, you may
discover cracks, holes, etc. which could also explain where most of
your heat is going.
