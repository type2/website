All,
	Miguel at Beetlemex wrote me that the compression ratio on the
Beetlemex 1600s is 6.6-1.  Gene Berg told me the same years ago when he
talked me into doing my 1600 SP engine with a CR of 6.6-1.  Gene
recommended timing the engine at 10* or higher BTC.  I've been running
mine at 12-13degrees BTC for the last 80,000 plus miles.  Tony may get
better performance out of his Beetlemex if he trys timing the ignition
to 10*BTC rather than, say, 7.5*BTC.  10*BTC is what VW used when the
engines did run at 6.6-1 CR.  The low CR engines do put out less power. 
You will notice this!
