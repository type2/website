covers your not getting a "breath of fresh air"! I think what he was saying 
was to run the breather to the air cleaner, and another set of hoses from the

the higher chance of back pressure that may be caused with running dual

engine. Especially when one of those little H*ndas pull up next to you!

don't get any lube down to the cylinders where the Germans meant for it to be!
