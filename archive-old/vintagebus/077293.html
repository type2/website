Ellis took his first honeymoon (1980) in a Porsche Guards Red 1970 Ghia vert
and the first wife. Extremely little money, (no credit card<s>), VERY little

The Ghia and 1# Mrs. are long gone. I pined over the loss of the Ghia for years
and then finally saw it at a "St. Mary's Food Bank" show in central Phoenix.

ex. I walked away from both memories on that day.

VanBondo says, Fancy vehicles are like fancy wimmen. Both are exhillerating at
first, are proned to high maintenance, high cost, eat up too much of your time
cant really park them just anywhere and are subject to high rates of theft. Age
and wisdom is that a good old daily driver will get you where you need to be
without as many head/heart aches and worry.
