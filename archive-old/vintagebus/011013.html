I think your bus is permanently FUBAR due to running around like that.

but seriously, I think it's okay.   Drove the 54 i used to have w/no front 
shocks (AWOL from DPPO) and one broken rear shock for about 600 plus miles 
before finding some replacements.    The snubbers on the rear axle tubes were 
added mid 64 I believe.   One peg was broken off on the tranny I used to have 
in my 65,   Don't know how much difference it makes.  Then again I could 
probably stand to get new shocks.   Just another little bit that works better 
or doesn't.   Guess I would feel better about hauling heavy loads with 
everything in place, a westy kit does add weight.  The rubber snubbers are 
evidently great as dog chew toys, I have seen replicas in various colours 
made for that purpose.  Available probably at your local doggy stuff store.  
Don't know if those ones are good for buses though.  DRove w/o rear shocks 
for a bit on a bus I had, cause one fell apart, so I took the other off to 
make it even.
