Paper and cardboard gaskets need to be sealed before using. Hoover's
'sermons' had a good one about this. I use Indian Head Gasket Shellac, paint
it on the gasket and let it dry. I'm sure others can write in and explain
why you would never want to do this, or that you should use spit, or shoe
polish or whatever. For every chair made there is an ass to sit in it. As to
the oil, that's a good oil for Alaska right now, but it all depends on the
temperature conditions where you are driving. I am partial to single weight
oils, others like multi-grade, but if it is cooler weather where you are, as
a rule, you go a step lighter in the winter, heavier in the summer. Tell us
the temps you will encounter until the next oil change interval, someone can
help you out.
