The 30/31 (from Ronnie) that I put on mine worked perfectly from the first
start.  It comes with an adapter for the dual-port manifold, or you might
be able to scrouge up a 30mm manifold center section.

Do put a _good_ filter between the tank outlet and the pump.  I've found
that the 30/31s aren't tolerant of debris in the fuel.  I had to pull mine
apart and give it a good cleaning to get rid of some fiberous stuff that
was caught in one of the jets after about 4000 miles.
