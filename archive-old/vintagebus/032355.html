 From my experience converting my '64 SC to 12v,

1.  The 6v gas gauge will NOT give a correct reading if run on 12v.  Other 
on the list can give a huge dissertaion on the proper way to drop the 
voltage to it.  I lucked out and scored a '67 12v guage.

2.  Any standard 2-wire flasher relay will work. There are 3 wires going to 
the current relay, one of which is for the indicator.  Attach the other two 
to the 2 terminals on the new relay and the indicator to whichever side 
makes it flash.  Worked for me...
