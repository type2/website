One item I learned right off the bat; CHASE ALL THREADS!!!!!!!!!!!

Chroming adds a reasonable amount of stuff to the part... mostly in the
first copper layer.  The second layer, nickel, is the layer that gives the
hardness and it too is reasonably think.  The chrome is really only a very
thin layer.  The chemistry of the whole plating process is quite
interesting...
