I've done quite a bit of research on compressors while I was looking for one
for my home shop.  To surmise all the research, this is what I've come to
conclude.

Buy as BIG a compressor as you can afford.

That being said, I would think that a 5 hp TWO stage 60 gallon CAST IRON
compressor would be where I would start.  I found one used for about $300, but
that is the low end.  I'd expect to spend about twice that.  New they run more
like $1200 and up.  Expensive, yes...but well worth the money.
