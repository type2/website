  You can get a 6 volt case trans from Rancho if you request. They also
sometimes have 6 volt cases machined for 12 volt flywheels (which I prefer.

will need to use your nosecone as Rancho does not furnish bus nose cones.

started to beef up the bus trannys do to a few problems they were having
with people running largewr then stock engines (at extra cost, of course)
