Subject: Weekend flea market stop...

NQ's post reminded me of my quick flea market experience this past 
weekend. Last week the local Eur-Asian shop owner had mentioned he 
saw the front of a splitty bus at the little local flea market. So on 
Sat. afternoon when my wife decided to head into town, i tagged 
along. We made it out there at 5:00 PM just as the places were 
closing up shop. As well pulled around to park my wife said, "there 
it is....it's yours!"  Not too much to my surprise, there sat the 
front end of a bus I took to the local metal yard about 3-4 months 
ago. Now, before anyone gets excited, this front end was made up of a 
LOT of bondo. And the window frames were nice and rusty as well. But 
what they got it for was an art piece I believe. I told one of the 
'ol boys running the place the story and they loved it. They were 
already passing it along as I walked off. Their favorate part was 
that I got $9 for the whole shell and this fella was selling the 
front end for $100! I paid $75 for the bus and made about $500 off of 
parting it out as well as getting a lot of stuff for my own busses. 
So that's cool. It's just funny to me 'cause I ALMOST cut the nose 
off to make.......something out of it. I was really just trying to be 
good and get rid of some junk. You know what they say...one man's 
junk....another man's treasure!
