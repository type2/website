Subject: Re: [VB] Pitting

Grind down the bumper to bare metal. Fill the pits with fiberglass body
filler. After you sand the fiberglass filler smooth, I would put a coating
of Epoxy primer (2 part, not the kind you buy at the FLAPS) and a coat of
sandable primer. Sand the primer smooth and then apply the top coat.
