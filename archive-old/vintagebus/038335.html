The PO had a few of pieces of 1/2 inch x 3/4 inch seal 
cut from the seal that goes around the cargo doors installed
to keep it from crashing.  I was going to use closed cell foam
of the same dimensions found at most hardware stores,
until I ran across a used seal in good servicable shape
at a wrecking yard for $3.  It only broke in one spot
when I removed it.  Saved a hundred dollars for more
important and safety related things, like brakes.

After installing the correct shape seal, I think that the
1/2 x 3/4 seal would work in most areas, worth the
few bucks to try, assuming one isn't a perfectionist.
Do test with a water hose prior to using nature's rain
as a test, however.
