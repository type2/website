There are a few things that it might be.  The pumps are notoriously sluggish if they have been sitting for long periods.  They will often take more than the stated voltage to get them going after a long sleep.  They usually need a rebuild too.

Also, the heaters have specific cycles in their operation.  Especially the pre BN4's.  Sometimes the blower will operate a certain amount of time(for combustion chamber cooling) before voltage is supplied to the pump again.  Check the library for specific details.
