tang and a different piece that the stick holder in the cab bolts to.   The

stick.  I seem to have had the same problem you ran into (or a similar one) when I put a later shift bushing and thing the bushing goes in in my 65 truck, so I took the plastic thing out and found the holder thing with the metal tang.
 
the straight shift stick does not have the pin that holds the stick from rotating.  that was added when they went to the bent shifter.
