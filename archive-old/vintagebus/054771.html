If you do the 68 rear set up, you need an IRS conversion, which is basically
even more difficult and complicated than the rig you have now. Bus Boys
sells these kits, and are adjustable for ride height from stockish to 4.5
inches drop.
I would go with the redux boxes, which is what I did to go back to stock
form the same rig you have now. Get uncut spring plates, a trans (Ronnie can
hook you up really nicely with a Rancho outright) and bolt her right in.
