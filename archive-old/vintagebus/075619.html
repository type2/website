>My 30 amp relay has 5 terminals  labeled 30, 87, 87, 85 and 86.

Read the second link above.  The 10 gauge wire from one battery will
go to 30.  The 10 gauge wire from the other battery will go to one
of the "87" terminals.  Look at the relay carefully; if both "87"
terminals are marked "87" then you can use either one; if one is
marked "87b" then make sure to use the one marked "87".
