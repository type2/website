The 021N dizzy will work fine if you do not connect manifold vacuum to the retard side. The retard side is the
inboard port. Leave it open.
Set timing at idle at 7.5 before.

You only would set it at 10 after IF you have vacuum connected to the retard side. Looking at the specs:
Vacuum: 7-12deg Adv, 16-18deg Ret
you see 16-18 degrees retard. If you disconnect vacuum at idle, you would see the timing go from 10 after to
6-8 before.

If you look at the specs of a late beetle,
Beetle 1974 * 1600 Federal Auto-Stick Trans

Distributor: VW 043-905-205A, Bosch 0231 170 036
Vacuum Can: 07 059
Timing Set At:: 7.5deg BTDC _at_ 800-950rpm w/ strobe and w/single vacuum hose disconnected and plugged
Advance/Retard Range: Vacuum: 8-12deg Adv; Centrifugal: 13-15deg _at_ 2300rpm, 22-25deg _at_ 3800rpm

and compare them to your 021N minus the retard section:

Advance/Retard Range: Vacuum: 7-12deg ; Centrifugal: 9-14deg _at_ 2000rpm, 21-25deg _at_ 3400 rpm

It is almost the same!
