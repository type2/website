        An interesting tid bit in regard to the plate registering. Floridians 
have had the option of registering the YOM for a long time now, I am unsure 
of the years though (I believe up to 1975). Anyway, I purchased a 1967 plate 
for my bus, which first has to be sent to the state capitol for 
authentication. Then, after about 3 weeks or so, you get it back and then may 
go legally register it. Well, upon checking out a little bit of information I 
found that I could get an antique plate. Which is what I did. Apparently the 
laws have been changed. It used to be 1947 and older. Now it is 1947-1970. I 
sure get some extra looks when people see the plate on the bus!
