you listen to Muir you do not need a choke :)  Also you may need to change
the carb electromagnetic cut-off switch, but I am not sure if it matters
what voltage....  Or you can clip the pin in there and not worry about it :)

the studs changed like Eric and Barb said there you go.

to grind them and polish them to get them to fit and look nice.  The
castings are horrid.  You may have this nightmare ahead of you unless you
have a 34PICT-3 sitting around, then to get a 34 running right is another
headache granted some have down to a science... or are really lucky :)
