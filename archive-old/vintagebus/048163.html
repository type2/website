Hi Lars,
Downward looking at the rear of the carb or as you face the right side of the 
carb turn it counter clockwise.  You can check how this works with a small 
mirror with the air cleaner offf the carb.  With your left hand holding the 
mirror just above the carb turn the loosened choke clockwise will open it and 
counter clockwise will close it.  After 15 minutes of running the engine the 
choke must open all the way!   Eric&Barb
