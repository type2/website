It seems that the original vs unoriginal is more important for 
passenger models.  You don't take as large a hit on commericals.
It also depends on how far you stray; that metal-flake perl paint job
will be harder to unload than a basic solid color.  Try and
stay away from metalics - they don't look right with that
much surface area, and they get dowdy-looking *fast*.

If you do have the color changed, have it done *right* - do the jambs,
dash, engine compartment, etc.  It looks tacky if you don't.

Otherwise just paint it the original color.
