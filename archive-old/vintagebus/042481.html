The appropraite sized drill bit and about 60 seconds does wonders for
removing screws that you dont care if the thing it screws into gets
buggered up.

If you want to use whatever it goes into again, make sure the bit is a tad
smaller than the threads, drill the hole PERFECTLY centered, and chase
with a tap.
