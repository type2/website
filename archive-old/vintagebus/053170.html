Subject: RE: [VB] Front brake drum seal problem, etc.

yes, i've seen this before. i believe you have a 68-70 front drum, which
will work on a split. you need to get the "adapter" seal that has the same
inside diameter as the early, but the outside diameter as the late drum. i
don't have a part number, but i know foreign aide in albuquerque
(1-800-658-5712) stocks them (if you describe the early/late drum scenario
they should be able to find it on the shelf no problem--it should be with
the 64-67 seals), and maybe ronnie does too, but i don't know for sure.
when you get the "adapter" seal, it should bolt on no problem.
