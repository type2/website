Neale,

My '62 like all other DCs (with the exception of a Binz) uses the right hand
cargo door from a bus (forward on LHD and rearward on RHD).  The door styles
(high hinge, indent or not etc..) would match the year of the bus/truck.

When/if you find a RHD DC the only difference will be that you will have a cool
"suicide" opening rear door on the left (wrong ;-) side of the truck.  Both LHD
and RHD DCs (enough acronyms for ya?) use the right door(the one with the
exterior handle).

Check out the Laurence meredith book "Original VW Bus" from Bay View books.  On
the title page is a great picture of your dream truck.  It is a '61 with the
cool (HTF) amber turn indicators in the rear.  More pics on pages 20-21

GOOD LUCK!
