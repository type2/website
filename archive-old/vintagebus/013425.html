Someone is pulling someone's leg here or we've all fallen victim
to another urban legend.  The August 1 date is a myth perpetrated
by VW.  I have Production Plate (M-Code tag) information on 1965
and 1966 model year buses showing the following:

1965 Model - Chassis 174,001 - June 29 - Tuesday
1965 Model - Chassis 174,370 - July 1 - Thursday

1966 Model - Chassis 000,363 - July 6 - Tuesday
1966 Model - Chassis 000,454 - July 7 - Wednesday

It may very well have been that new models were "available" on
August 1, but they were making them in July.

Lester, if 216.000.001 is within your reach, please pass along
the Production Plate information to us for posterity.  And, Jack,
yours was made between June 22 and June 29 probably the 23rd or
24th.  Having your Production Plate information would help too.
