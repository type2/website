Here is a cool tid bit for ya Robert...the rear cargo
areas on the early westys were painted by BRUSH not
sprayed!  Pull of your panels and you will see paint
brush strokes ending at the border of the tanish
paint.  And if you look VERY closely you may see
occasional brush strokes in the tan iteslf on areas
exposed.
  
My good friend Mike redid a `57 westy I sold him...had
the color matched off a piece of bus which never saw
daylight....he had it mixed in Centari Acrylic enamel
and actually had a flatner mixed in to give it that
satin look like the original had.  He applied it via
brush as did westfalia...and being Acrylic enamel the
stuff flowed out very smooth as it sat.  Looks
identical to the original.

If you wanna contact Mike about the paint codes ect
email him at <tcs_at_glasscity_dot_net>
