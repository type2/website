I have had a couple questions about this, so let me clarify me setup.

I installed one silicone gasket between the case and Berg 1.5qt deep sump.
No problem.  I then installed one gasket between the Berg sump and the sump
plate.  No problem.

For this engine I am not running the oil screen since it has full flow
oiling.  Also installed is a "cow magnet" attached to the sump oil pickup
tube.

with two gaskets and stock bolts.  In this scenario the sump plate bolts
would need to be extended.
