what style carb do you have?  Is it single port or dual port?   If the carb 
is square, you usually have 2 screws that adjust, both on the left side.   
You screw the big one in till it starts to cough and die, then screw it out 
three halfs turns.  Repeat withthe tiny screw near it, then back and forth 
till the adjustment doesn't change.  Also, check for vacuum leaks.   Any 
plugs fall off the carb?  if it is a square carb,  is the circle plug towards 
the rear still firmly attached?  What is you climate?  carb icing can make 
the motor stall during the warm up phase, you can watch the ice form on the 
carb and throat of the intake manifold. but that wouldn't cause black smoke, 
which implies runnning very rich. wonder if that top jet fell off the carb.  
or a spark plug wire is not on properly.
