Subject: Re: [VB] welding aluminum with a MIG?

The only way to weld aluminum with a MIG is with a Spool Gun. Aluminum wire
is too soft to push thru the cable to the nozzle. A spool gun contains the
spool of wire so that the wire only has to travel a short distance to the
nozzle. This is a production aluminum welding method. For small jobs and
relatively delicate work, the only method is TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas, also
known as Heli-Arc).
