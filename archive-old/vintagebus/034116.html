The only between cylinder head and barrel, shims (spacers, head gasket) are
C B's or Autocrafts, these are copper ie: head gaskets. I use these when i
build most of my none stock engines, 1776 or larger. I run 0 deck height and
use either a 40 thousand or 60 thousand head gasket, depending on the
compression ratio i'm trying to achive. On my 1776 single port and a 1840
(don't ask) engine that i built i haven't had to retorque the heads since
built, over 20,000 on each engine and no loose heads. This year will be the
true test as the show season has begone and i will be driving one of my
buses an average of 600 a road trip, every other weekend.
