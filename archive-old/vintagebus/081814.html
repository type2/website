You actually aren't far off...

When I did mine, I had to compress some sections and stretch others to
line everything up correctly. You'll notice there are several joints
where the pieces are glued together. Be careful stretching there. You
may even want to yank on the seal to separate those on purpose and
re-glue them with better adhesive because they may come apart as the
seal shrinks and expands over time.

The seal is molded with humps where they go over the waist of the bus.
I started there on both sides and massaged the seal to line everything
else up.
