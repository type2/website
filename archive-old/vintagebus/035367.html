Theres a thing called a spitznagel.
Like a slide hammer but works without making holes.
You can pull the dents up past level.
You can then use whats called a shrinking disc to put the metal back into its 
original shape.
Once you get good at it you can make the metal PERFECT, ready for paint with 
only a coat of primer and ZERO bondo.
Look in hemmings, theres a few places selling these shrinking discs.
