469446 in the RGBs is 1.4, whereas it is 1.39 in the rest of the 1200 range
and 1.26 in the big-nut RGBs.  The book claims to go back to December 1953
and is a dealer service manual, so I assume that includes the barndoors -- I
hope somebody will correct me if I am wrong.  I don't know much about the
early splits.  It provides info on the gear teeth if that would be helpful,
