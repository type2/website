We just ansewered almost all these questions to Virge and just posted them to 
the list.  If you filled the center section several times till the oil would 
not go below the fill opening on flat ground then everything should be fine 
and full.  If you are not sure just check the fullness of the center section 
again after it has sat for at least a couple of hours again on flat ground.
