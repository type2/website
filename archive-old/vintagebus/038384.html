The front end as a unit can be had for about $100-150 from a junkyard.

If I found a bus with a bad steering/front end assy, I'd plan on spending
$200, but I wouldn't fear that bus.

If it's steeing gear box:

The hardest part, IMHO, is removing the steering wheel itself and finding a
good place to remove the steering gear. On early buses (up to '67), the
steering gear and the arm going up to the steering wheel are one assembly.
They pull down, out of the bus, but you almost need one of those "pits" to
pull one out into...or you need to jack the bus up about 4 feet off the
ground (safely).

I don't know about rebuilding those gear boxes or adjusting them, so listen
for someone else to answer about that.

I think you can get a good steering box for $75-150 used...maybe $200
"rebuilt."
