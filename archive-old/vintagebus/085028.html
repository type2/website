kablammo' statements: the oil MUST be heated above 212 degrees on a

stuff is gonna get whipped to merengue from routine condensation.

deg, that is an AVERAGE... at various points, the oil is gonna be 
much hotter.

to change it to keep it as a lubricant and not an abrasive. also it's 
gonna stop being so cushiony between the whirly metal bits!

viscosity (to get higher numbers - this is called lying. or, in the
