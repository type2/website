May 04, 2002 - Spring Show 'N Shine 
Paso Robles, California 
Spring Show 'n Shine, Saturday, May 4, 2002 10am to 3pm

Country Cruise, Sunday, May 5, 2002, 10am to 2pm

The Central Coast Chapter - California, of the Vintage Volkswagen Club
of America, will be hosting its annual "Spring Show and Shine" in Paso
Robles Pioneer Park. The highlight of this years show will be the raffle
of a 1960 Ragtop Beetle. The proceeds of the raffle will benefit the
local food bank. The show will include a people's choice car show with
over 20 classes for any VW powered vehicle over 25 years old! There will
also be a swap meet with lots of parts for your VW. This is a family
event with activities for all ages. On Sunday there will be a cruise
featuring many of the cars from the show. Entry Fee is $10 for cars or
swappers, (swappers and vendors welcome), spectators are free. Camping
is available, call for info.

For all the info and maps check out the website. Tickets for the raffle
car are also availiable online.
