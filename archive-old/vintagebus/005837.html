Jon:

We see this all of the time with the torsion arm cores we get. If you can't
break it loose with a vise grip and a hammer, then you will have to use a
die grinder or angle grinder.

Just grind one side of it until you ALMOST reach the stud (be carefull not
to grind the stud). Then you can knock it loose with a hammer and chisel.
