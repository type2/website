We have had that happen once on ours.  Just a couple of push pulls of the 
knob and the valve stopped leaking and has done so again for several months.  
We set our knob so it is about 1/8" from all the way in when the valve is in 
the normal tank postion.  This allows for the slight spring effect of the 
cable housing and still fully throw the valve.  We have heard of rebuild kits 
but do not know who has them.   Eric&Barb
