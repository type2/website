Dear Folks,

The latest news on the Bus Tour is that Phil Gowdy had handed off to
Collison Corner and Collison will be handing off to Dave Ireland, from whom
the Hubcap will go to Denmeister, then Gregg Potts, and finally to Jeff
McKenna in Kingston, Ontario.

We have two trip reports from Phil Gowdy and some fun comments on the photos
from the one-use camera that Phil had processed on the web page at
http://www.type2_dot_com/~chuff . Click on "The Latest Update" link to take you
to the links for the photos and comments and Phil's trip reports.

The Tour will be re-entering the U.S. through upstate New York after
traveling from Cambridge, Ontario across to Kingston, Ontario. Shelby Bell
will pick up the Hubcap from Jeff McKenna and will take it to Kevin Ahlgrim
(hey, Kevin-please write me if you see this) in LaPorte, Indiana and so
resume the Midwestern Leg of the Tour.
