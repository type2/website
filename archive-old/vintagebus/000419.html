Hi Robert,
We hear your pain, and since we are in the same boat, and know what you are 
going through.  This style of window side glass was used from about 
1957-62ish  Our 1962 westie has this same Sigla laminated glass for the 
slider, and wing windows.  Due to this being laminate glass these pieces 
break very easily compared to tempered glass.  We have heard rumors that USA 
lawmakers had an idea that if laminate glass was good for the windshields it 
would be great for side windows.  The other drawback of laminate glass is 
that if water is allowed to get to the edges it attacks the plastic laminate 
between the two panes of glass causing it to bubble around the edges.
