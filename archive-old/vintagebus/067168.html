valuable item we've ever purchased was a TV/VCR that plugs into the
cigarette lighter. We've actually gone through 3 of them over the

and splitter for the TV headphone jack. Dad doesn't have to use
earplugs anymore.

some peace and quiet. Just regulate how much you use it.
