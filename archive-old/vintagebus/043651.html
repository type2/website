Here's a note on bearings...about a month ago I put all four new 
bearings on a single-axle 4x8 flatbed trailer.  The trailer and
the axle were both made in the USA.  The four original bearings
I pulled off were etched "SLOVAKIA".  I am not making this up.

In my quest to find the correct replacement bearings, I found
some SKF ones made in Mexico and Timken ones make in the USA.
I finally found the right bearings and bought four Timken
bearings - same part number.  When I opened the boxes, two
were made in the USA and two were made in Brazil.

With good quality control, and a reputable supplier, the country
of origin "shouldn't" matter.  Still, I put the USA bearings on
the inboard side, figuring they would see more load.  :)
