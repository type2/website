Hey all!

Just a quick update on the brake situation.  I took the Wolfgang
spacer to the FLACPS yesterday and held it up to a Varga Brazilian 
master cylinder (part number as marked on box is 211 611 021 AAB).
It fit perfectly...went down flush to the M/C flange, and there
was a bit of room between the spacer and the M/C "nose" where
the boot would go.

So, either I got the wrong M/C in the right box, or the spacer was
machined assuming that everyone using it would cheap out and go
for the cheap master cylinder.  Sigh.  I think I'll end up machining
the spacer, because I'd really rather have the ATE German M/C on
there than the Brazilian one.

living anywhere in the world other than the Los Angeles area,
