Just about anything can be done with enough cutting and welding, like 
installing a V8 engine.  VOA sold AC as a dealer installation in the 1960s.  We have

be found occassionally.  You can buy a new AC kit made for the hot rodding 
market for about $1100.00 today.  Do expect a drop in HP and acceleration when 
using the AC!
