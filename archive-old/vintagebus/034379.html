mmmmm,

Sounds like one is a leetle too tight and one is
a little too loose?

In my experience when fitting *new* pins and bushes
the bush is a tight interference fit but installed using a drift...
i.e.,  moves in quire smoothly but is tight.

The bearings and pins (greased) slide in (no tapping required)
but are tight and have no perceptible up and down play.
I'm wondering whether the bearings are actually going
to roll in your 'tight' side.

Aas a qualifier, my experience is limited to the earlier (21mm) link
pins and OEM parts.
