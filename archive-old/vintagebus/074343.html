  There are 3 genuine VW duel port heads out there in the market place with
only the Mexican VW duel port with long reach plugs. OOPs also the 041
(39X32) so that really makes 4 genuine heads. Neither of the Brazilian OE
heads have long reach plugs. AutoLinia (aftermarket heads) also come with
3/4 reach plugs. There are no single port genuine heads I have ever seen
with long reach plugs,, even though they are a little beefier casting then
early single port heads.

ronnie

noticed the tell tale cracks as the non long reach ones.

seats, not at the plugs. So single port crack just as duel port heads in my
observations
