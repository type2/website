66-67 buses had short inner door handles and a plastic handle that mounted
just below the sliding front windows that you pulled on to close the door. I
believe a 216xx is a '66 model.
'65 and earlier buses had large inner door handles that you pulled on to
close the door.
on a '65 you should have the one with the screw that goes through the handle
itself. The earlier ones use a pin that goes in from behind.
