Hi Paul,
They should be semi-gloss black, and the oil galley is to allow any seepage 
of tranny oil past the outer axle seal to go out the inner side of the brake 
backing plate.  Thus keeping the brake shoes clean of any tranny oil.  Very 
good idea to run a drill bit up into these drain holes in the RGB covers to 
make sure the passages are open and clean.    Eric&Barb
