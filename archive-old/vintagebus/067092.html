If you have a stepped crank, you must use a stepped crank flywheel.  You can 
use a early (stepped) FW on your later crank.   AT least that's the way I 
understand it, else suffer oil leaks.

If I were you, I would pull the motor and investigate the oil leak.  I think 
the later 40hp came with O rings, only the early 40hp (and the 36hp) cranks 
were stepped.  40hp and 1600 cranks are interchangable, so you could have 
either stepped or non stepped.   Check it out and replace your Main seal and 
oil cooler seals.  Make sure your clutch isn't soaked.
