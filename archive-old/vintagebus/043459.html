FWIW, my '64, 1 203 065, 15 November '63, has wheel wells like this.
It has the 46mm (big) axle nut too - I don't know if it's the original
tranny/axles but I suspect it is.

By looking in the archive at type2_dot_com, the "small" wheel wells seem
to have stopped sometime in '62 or '63.  

"VW Transporter: Die technichse Dokumentation 1950-1990" says "Wheel
arches on front and rear wheels enlarged, rear with crimp" starting
30 July '62, 971 550.
