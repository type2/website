Subject: Re: [VB] Tips on removing petrified window seals?

I use a razor blade (razor knife) to slice the old seal- run it along the 
channel.   You can cut eitehr side of the scraper bit, sometimes just one 
side works.  If they are all hard it it mosre difficult.  I use a small 
pointy thing /screwdriver to scrape any remnents left out of the frame.  Work 
for the seal on top of the door as well, though check there for bits that 
were mashed in to keep the seal from sliding out (as if!) Don't cut yrself.   
Hope this helps.

I use wd-40 liberally on the seal and the frame when installing.
