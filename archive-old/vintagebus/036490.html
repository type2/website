Hmm, that might be bad.  You want to use a tranny oil rated GL-4 as near to 
90 as possible unless you live in a artic climate.  GL-5 rated oil is 
according to tranny rebuilders bad for the brass syncros in the tranny.

in case you need to top the tranny or steering box in the near future.

water.

<< Drain the center section first and then the RGB's? >>
Correct.  Plus check the bottom of the drain pan after draining each section. 
 Look for steel shrapnel of bigger than a pin head and the more there is the 
more you should consider rebuilding the reductions, and/or tranny center 
section.  

<<Do the drain plugs ever become stuck or crack or anything?>>

replacing cracked housings.
