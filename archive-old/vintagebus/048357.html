without sanding, and I was wondering if folks have had success with it.  I 
noticed it was polyurethane isocyanate (which is good in my book), but it is

Here is the info on the Tie-Coat primer:
http://www.por15_dot_com/faqs/faqtieco.html

My Dupont supplier recommends Dupont Prime 'N Seal 2600/2700 to do the same

primer-sealer which does not require sanding before topcoating.
