Subject: Re: [VB] Shifting Issues

You can take the shift stick off the front floor, and clean up in the ball and socket joint and the shift plate, apply fresh grease there, and re-install.  You may have the adjust the plate on the floor after installing.  Then install a good boot over the shift stick to cover to place where the ball/socket is.   You can also grease where the forward shift rod goes through the forward shift bushing under the front floor.
 
Gear oil should be changed every 3 years/30K miles, whichever comes first.  While the gear oil is drained, replace your axle boots if they are cracked or torn.
