When doing Stephen's (your vw_dot_com's) Berg linkage, it took me at least 2-3
hours to get it set right to where the carbs opened evenly and stopped
evenly. Berg's linkage was not designed for the Kadrons and just modified
Weber linkage, after many frustrating hours I thought I finally got it
right. You need to use BOTH of the return springs furnished on the linkage
and make sure that the cable moves freely. On the guide tube through the
shroud I use a small piece of fuel hose clamped on the tube against the
shroud to keep it from sliding about. This is very difficult linkage to set
up and requires bending of the passenger side linkage arm to get the proper
stroke out of the carbs.
