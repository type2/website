> Then again in  March 63 (VIN 1 080 104) (now 10 innies)

I seem to recall reading somewhere that part of the reason for the
outie to innie change was for safety.  Not for people riding in the
Bus, but pedestrians, cyclists, etc that might get run over by the
Bus.  The idea was apparently to minimize sharp and pointy things
on the outside of the body that might injure people in an accident.
Hence, innie vents, door handles that don't flip out, etc.
