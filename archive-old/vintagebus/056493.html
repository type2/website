By the lever, there are two diff styles, one almun with rubbershish
grooved bushes, another with a brass collar, no other fun stuff. There
are about 4-5 different arms.
The bushing should be flush against the case. Hammering is a no no.
Perhaps you have some of that alumn thin stlye jammed in there?
I do not remember if the bushing is asymeterical. It might be that is
the issue.
I have also seen and arm with more than one place to put the inner c
clip.
Basically you should have small bushing in pass side, greased, greased
arm. Inner clip, bushing, cover/collar, spring, arm, clip, then mount
spring.
If you use the new style with the alumninum and the rubber bushes, one
goes inside, one goes outside.
Make certain the screw is in the hole for the bushing, not bashing into
it, and put that in tight last, I left mine in just a littl eto prevent
bushing from moving while installing, then ran it home.
Sounds like you need a new bushing. Bus Boys hooked me up with the
pre-63 style brass hefty one, and the HD throwout lever. The only other
thing is to make certain the arm is correct, and in the right attitude.
