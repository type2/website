First off, I'm hoping with your description of steel shims at the top of the
barrel between it and the head you are talking 40hp and not using the proper
copper seal. If you have a 15/1600 and are shimming at the top of the
barrel, that's wrong. Shim at the bottom of the barrel next to the block. I
hand lap my barrels into it's position at the head for a good fit there, but
never use anything between the barrel and head. That gives me the picture of
the Lost in Space robot waving its arms and yelling "Danger, Will Robinson,
Danger". That's the first place I would expect a compression leak.
