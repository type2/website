Best to run the 65 pounds pressure!  The tires you are using were not really 
designed for VW bus use in mind and thusly the old fashioned VW pressure

handling, especially in cross winds and/or when a semi passes you will not 
automaticly change lanes inadvertantly!   Eric&Barb
