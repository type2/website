$450 for a 71 Ghia Mystery Engine ?!!!
Did you save half the cost of a new bottom end,
or are you halfway towards one :)?!!
Check the endplay. If it is out of spec, pull the flywheel
and wiggle the main bearing.This is an indication of
high mileage and a primary consideration for rebuilding.
It's also a fine consideration for a refund:).
If you are keeping it, verify the HIOP.
Do a leak-down test, it is more definitive than compression
If these indicators are substandard, take it apart, linebore
and replace the bearings, rebuild the heads, install a
new oil cooler and p/c :)
Change the muffler to a proper bus exhaust.
Start a maintenance notepad.
