Subject: correct gear oil

I have been searching the archives and talking with local stores about
the correct hypoid gear oil to place in my redux boxes and the tranny.
I have come to the conclusion that a 80W90 hypoid gear oil is ok to
use.  The only factor I can not seem to find is an oil that has a rating
of GL4.  The types I have been finding have a rating of GL3/4/5 or ones
that claim to be a GL5 or better.  I have talked SEVERAL local parts
departments and they said that the GL rating was not important.  One
mechanic said that as you go higher in numbers, the better the
"protection".  Some one on the archives said that this was not the case
about higher is better.    Muchas Gracias.
