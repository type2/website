It is more to do with improvements in furnace design than improvements
in PVC. The new furnaces have such effecient heat exchangers that the
get the exhaust gas down to something around 100F, probably less than
the temperature of the hot water you run down the maybe PVC drain. It
is really wet air too, don't dump it in your chimney or let it run
down your exterior walls...
Bus content: PVC pipe is not appropriate material for your busses'
heater ducts. If you have to use HVAC parts on your bus use
sheetmetal. Foil duct tape might come in handy too.
