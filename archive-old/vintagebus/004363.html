You don't know the pleasures of starting a VW engine until
you do it with a 6V starter running at 12V. I have used this
set up for over 25 years with great results. I also use a
capacitive discharge ignition. It takes 1 second or less of
cranking to start the engine. If the engine won't start, you
can also drive at about 3 mph in second or third gear by
cranking the starter. Get you that last block to the service
station to fill up.
