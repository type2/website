Hi Rich,
Could be you over packed the grease.  You want just enough for the bearing
rollers/balls to be in a layer of grease, but not so much grease as to fill
the chamber up to the spindle!  Rechech the grease level now and recheck for
the black flaky stuff about 3,000 miles later.  As for the tie rod that
could
definitely be causing your steering problem.   Eric&Barb
