Curt, If you are using a new seal that has the white powder on it, make sure
you wipe it down with rubbing alcohol and then apply a thin coat in the seal
track. While the H2 cement is still moist it acts like a lubricant to help
slide the seal in easier, but doesn't deteriorate the rubber, neoprene or
whatever the seal is made of.
