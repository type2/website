As I recall, fuel line has to be certified and marked by the manufacturer,
same as brake line.  I doubt that the fuel line you get at your flaps will
be substandard.

the biggest problem with FLAPS fuel line is that it is sold in inch sizes
and no inch size corresponds closely with 5mm.

As far as quality of rubber, I'm not all that impressed with German
rubber.  Fuel lines that I replace every two years generally have cracks
in their ends, where the SAE lines I put in my C*evy 5 years ago look
fine.

I'll continue to use the German line for the better fit, but I will
definitely keep up with the replacement schedule!
