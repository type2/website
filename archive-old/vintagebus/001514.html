If you look closely at the face of a Beetle radio, you will see the
numbering for the display is painted on a slightly "angled" surface...  This
surface is sorta angled up so the driver/passenger is looking down on the
station numbers.

On a radio which came in a bus, the numbering on the face is painted on a
flat surface, pointing directly forward out the face.   (hope that makes
sense).  If you put a radio from a Beetle into a Bus dash, you won't be able
to see the station numbers on the face.   Thus implying that there were
separate radio types installed in Buses and Bugs..
