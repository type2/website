Steve, your gen helps the battery while running and your voltage regulator
kicks in to allow your gen to charge the battery when it falls below a
certain point, but you should be able to run whatever current demand your
battery can supply. I suspect that your points fried on your voltage
regulator. 30 amps is not quite good enough to keep your battery charged at
that constant of a current draw if everything is on, it will continually be
trying to play catch-up. If you can get a higher current gen/alt I would do
that. And replace your VR.
