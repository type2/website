The Bentley states that bearing damage will occur if the nut is torqued to anything over 250FtLbs.I do not remember if this applies to one specific reduction box type or both.

The torque wrench is your friend along with a paint pen to mark your progress so you don't miss anything.I know it sounds bad but this way you will not have parts falling off or self destructing because of being too tight or loose.I still get laughed at by my friends that work in service because I use a torque wrench on most everything,remember that if you have a vehicle serviced by anyone but yourself.
