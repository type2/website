Hi Chris, is this for a bus that you are getting going after it has been 
sitting a while?   if so, the most common cause of drums not turning is the 
brakes sticking, particularly if the ebrake was on for years.  SO, best idea 
is to bang the drum with a hammer to knock it loose.

What is wrong with the tranny you have in your bus?  Or do you just want the 
smaller gear ratio?  

the thing is, you could get a tranny someone says is good, but I would still 
take the boxes apart and check the bearings.   I got a small nut tranny ' 
works good' from someone who lowered his bus, and took the RGBox apart and 
found a couple bad bearings.  I was just collecting axle shafts at that time, 
and those can be worn bad.
