How about finding one used?

If you're going to just do a few welds here and there, the cheep one might
work, but one problem I saw with a cheepie that had sat a while is the
parts inside corroded; perhaps that could happen with any welder, but it
made this one example almost unuasble until I got in there and did some
spiffing up; the drive works were rusting and wouldn't feed the wire.

I like my Lincoln 100 (predecessor to the 115); I bought it several years
ago when I was working on the '56; my first welding project was welding
in a sunroof clip.  I've done a LOT of welding with it!  I've gone thru
about 4 25# spools of wire, and almost finished with a 5th, and countless
tanks of gas.
