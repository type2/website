Dissassemble, inspect all the bearings, and other parts.  Replace the outer 
axle seals.  Then seal the inner plugs against the spring plates with high 
temp silicon sealant.  

<<Secondly, how do I remove clutch arm off the piviot shaft?  Do I need a 
puller?  I removed the circlip but it won't come off.>>
Should come off with the C-clip removed.  If not then it is either rusted on 
due to not being greased well when installed, or someone beat it on and has 
mushroomed the shaft.  Either one probably means cuttin the throw out arm 
inside of the tranny bell housing.

<< While I have it off should I reinforce the arms by welding them up?>>
Most definitely, unless you enjoy removing the engine, and replacing the 
throw out arm often.
