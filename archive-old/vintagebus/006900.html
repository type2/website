<< why would an oil pressure gauge be better to start out with?>>
If one can not afford to get both oil pressure and temp gauges the pressure 
gauge shows more of what is happening in the engine.  When oil gets hotter 
the pressure it can be pumped up to drops off so the lower the pressure at 
the same RPM/MPH/Gear, the higher the temp.  On the other hand if the oil 
pressure drops this will not be shown by an oil temp gauge until after the 
engine is toast.
