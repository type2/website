On Thu, 22 Jun 2000, Kyle wrote:

> Anyhoo, crank it with the coil wire off until the oil light goes
out. Plug the coil in and fire it up.

It probably wouldn't hurt to pull the rocker arms off during this
procedure.  After you have cranked it for oil pressure, squirt plenty of
oil on the rocker assemblies,valves etc. re-install, check valve
clearances.  Double check oil level before running in the cam.  I even
overfill it.  As Kyle mentioned, don't use something like Castrol until
you get the rings seated or they won't ever seat. BTDT.  I used to use
Kendall 20 wt. non-detergent for the first few hundred, running it
gently then valvoline 30wt. non-detergent untill about 2000 miles, but
I've had trouble finding the Kendall oil lately.  At about 2000 miles 
Castrol goes in.
