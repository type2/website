The Bentley Book.
Printed by Bentley Publishers, I beleive this is considered the official
Workshop Manual. In other words any official VW mechanic had to have one of
these. The version I am referring to covers VW transporters from the year
1963-1967. I believe you could get this book from amazon_dot_com, maybe at
olkdvolks_dot_com or busdepot_dot_com. The book is a very technically oriented book.
When I first got my bus it came with the Bentley manual and John Muirs
Idiots Guide(I forget the exact title). Anyway Muirs book was way more
helpful when I first started trying to work on the bus-I still use it now.
The Bentley Manual has proved more useful-the more I understand my bus.
Basically when I first started looking at the bentley manual-i was like a
bit overwhelmed with the technical stuff. But thanks to the list and Muir I
understand it much better now.

I definitely recommend both books to any VW owner.
