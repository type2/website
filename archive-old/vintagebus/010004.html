Here's MY story on all this "controversy".

My family and I posted our info to the AIRS site in case we could be of some
assistance to an air-cooled VW person in difficulty  -  ANY air-cooled VW
person.  We don't give a rip if a BEM shows up or phones using his middle
tentacle to dial.  If he/she/it/whatever has a problem and we can assist in
any way, that's fine with us.  To do less would be sailing under false
colors.

I'm glad Shawn called us for help, and very sorry we couldn't provide more
concrete assistance.  I hope we hear from him again with a report on his
journey back to Spokane in the wilds of Eastern Washington, just so we know
he's OK.  But if we never hear from him again we certainly won't feel that
we've been "used" by some unworthy "outsider".

Perhaps it's a Pacific Northwest thing, a quaint artifact of our relatively
recent emergence from "last frontier" status.  But out here, when you tell
folks the latchstring is always out you really MEAN the latchstring is
always out.
