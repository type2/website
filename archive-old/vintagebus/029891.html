On my turkis 62 standard, the bottom color was painted first inside and out.
The white was then sprayed on second on the top (to the bottom of the belt line)
and inside on the dash, floor, etc.  The rear portion of the bus inside seems to
have just gotten over spray...and that was good enough.  In places where the
headliner has come off down by the windows, it just looks like overspray and
glue underneath ...and doesn't look too hot.
To the best of my knowledge, the only portion of my bus that has been repainted
is the roof...and the previous owner (that owned the bus for 23 years before me)
told me this.
