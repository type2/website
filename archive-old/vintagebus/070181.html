  I think bolting it to the torsion really depends on the weight you plan to
pull and the style of hitch you have. 
  I have had my hitch bolted off of the frame rails only for over 4 years
and 50,000 plus miles, always pulling a fully loaded 1 wheel trailer, no
signs of cracking, bending, stress etc. I wouldn't tow another bus with it
though, but good for 600 or so lbs.
  I reinforced the bumper brackets and also use plates on the inside of the
frame rail to help reinforce that.
  I also have one of those aftermarket angle iron bumper/hitchs it has you
drill new holes through the sides of the frame to mount it.
  Navy dave made some EXACT repo's of the original VWoA hitch and it just
bolts to the frame only.
  My new (yes NOS) westfalia hitch will be here very soon (customs has
decided they need to X-ray my container that has been in the port since 2
Fridays ago) and I will see how it mounts up.

  People have asked why I do not make hitchs for buses and I do not want the
liability associated with someone towing using a hitch I made.

ronnie
