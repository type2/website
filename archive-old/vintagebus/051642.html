Subject: CE Aluminum Safari's  first impression

    I just recieved a set of C E aluminum Safaris for a customer (he ordered
direct) and I must say WOW. The frames are absolutly awesome. They are
extremely rigid, no flex and are DOUBLE WALLED like OG ones. The only thing

would give them a 8/9.
