smell the puddle.  If it smells really icky sweet it is gear oil.   If it 
smells like engine oil its engine oil.  if it smell like gas it's gas.

if it's gear oil, put a hat on and look at your boots- scope out the 
situation- is it clearly leaking between engine and trans?  well, pull the 
motor.  ya ya.  okay- gear oil IN bellhousing?   then the seal is bad.  It 
rarely is.   If it is, pull TO bearing, and carfeully remove old seal with 
screwdriver (prying, do not scrapw where seal sits).  bent screwdriver is 
better.  There is a trick to it..  

but I'd suspect a axle boot first.
