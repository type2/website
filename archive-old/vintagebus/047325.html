Hi Ev,
Yes we do and we lightly paint the frame of the body to get that extra amount 
to seal it and allows it to lubricate the rubber making the installation go 
in slick.  Also we put a thin line of tar in the rubber seal in the slot 
where the glass goes so to seal the edge of the glass from water attack which 
causes that nasty white haze at the bottom of the windshield laminate glass.  
Make sure to set up the wire or weed eater line so as to start pulling the 
seal into the interior of the bus at center bottom of the windshield.  Then 
try to pull each end of the line evenly so as to pull the windshield/seal in 
equally side to side.   Eric&Barb
