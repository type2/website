You might check these sites out for NEW heaters.  If you sell your bus,
keep the heater.  A good reconditioned old heater will save you a bunch of
money, and are as safe.  Like I have said before, many people tell me gas
heaters are not safe, but no one has personally seen a heater that has
caught itself on fire.
