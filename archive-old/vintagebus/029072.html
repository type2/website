I had a homemade awning on my '71. I used 3 pieces of  PVC (painted to
match the bus), 2 90 deg. fittings, two flagpole holders mounted to the
bus, and a blue tarp (sorry!). The blue tarp decomposed in a very short
time... The whole thing rolled up and was fastened to the rain gutter.
Very cheap, but looked pretty kewl!
