no, deluxes used the same escutcheons as non deluxes.    plastic in front,

the earlier buses with the earlier inner handles (~60 & earlier) which had 
front inner handles chrome for all models, in the front used black rings for 
commercials, and white for passenger models (or maybe only white for deluxe).

earlier inner handles.

vans had non chromed inner handles.  They were metal, not plastic.
