people really hate easy cheap solutions, but here's one:

Take a magnet and a scrap of old T shirt and sew it into a bag.  Take a wire

clothes hangar and unbend it- bend one end into a loop through the bag o 
magnet.  stick down gas filler- betrer make sure the magnet fits!  make
crook 
in end of wire so it doesn't slip down the tank.  okay- gas cap only fits 
with one ear.  Take out each time you fill with gas, ..let dry while
filling, 
knock rust off bag, and reinsert in tank until it keeps coming out clean.  
Instantly solved hesitation issue, and II didn't have to change the fuel 
filter so often.
