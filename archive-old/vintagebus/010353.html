It's doubtful you still have the original heads on a '66.  If the rocker arm
hold-down studs extend about 3-4" down into the head then the correct
adjustment is .004".  If the hold-down studs screw directly into the head
casting right under the rocker stands then set them at .006".  With the
engine cold of course.  Most of the old .004" heads with the long rocker arm
studs were converted to the short studs long ago.  I doubt there are many of
them left.  They didn't work well.  The studs wore against the head material
and caused oil leaks.
