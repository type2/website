I've watched this thread and have held off responding until now.

In high torque applications, greasing of threads is not recommended. The standard
in the mechnical industry is to spec DRY thread torques. It removes all vareables
associated with lubricants.

A lubed thread will turn easier, and will allow you to over torque the bolt.
