So I was looking at Thom's registry, trying to figure out how many 23 windows 
wakthroughs are in there, and came up with a non-archive answered question. 

A model 24x M-081 is a 9 seater deluxe walkthrough, simple enough.  (FWIW, 
there are fifteen 24x M-081 23-window in the registry.)

I had always thought that models 25x (7 seaters deluxes) were all 
walkthroughs.  This is what the SSVC/NEATO/BK spec book indicates on page 7 
as well as Meredith's Original VW Bus on page 123.  However, the registry has 
some model 25x with and without M-081. Perhaps some folks just missed the 
M-081 (but they filled in other M codes).  And why would they need any M code 
if walkthrough was standard for 25x?  What does a 25x NON-M-081 bus look like?

So, are all 25x's walkthroughs?  Maybe this fits the old rule, there were no 
"standard" busses, or just another mix up at the factory. 

Lets get this critical piece of bus history cleared up!
