Hi,

You might try adjusting the lash. There is a screw with a lock nut on
the steering box. It forces a follower pin into the worm gear. If the
adjustment screw is pushing too hard, tight steering results.

The screw should be adjusted for minimum lash and smooth action. Just
like any adjustable bearing.
