This couple is traveling alone from San Diego through Central and South
America in a VW Westfalia.  They plan to ship their Westy from a South
American port to South Africa, then travel up the E. side of Africa with
England as their ultimate destination.

They have a website [  http://www.geocities_dot_com/losligato/  ] and a List for
frequent e-mail updates [the updates are also posted and archived on the
website] and e-postcards.  They also solicit e-mails from those following
their expedition and reply promptly.

Their reports are well written and fascinating, and their descriptions of
their "encounters" with local bureaucracies are enlightening.  Here are the
opening sentences of their latest dispatch:  "We have been pulled over by
the police 14 times in the past week.  No exaggeration, 14 times in seven
days."  It then goes on to describe the effect the Peruvian earthquakes have
had on their planned itinerary [they are in Peru now], and their experience
of the quakes.

This is definitely one of the better online adventures available, especially
so because of their mode of transportation.

Check it out.....
