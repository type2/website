The Brazilian mufflers have this problem, the tab on the muffler for the
dampner is in the wrong place,the Mexican O E M mufflers fit almost as well
as the German Leistrist, the German Ernst mufflers are O K when Liestrist
aren't available, the Mexican replacement (not O E M ) are crap. The dampner
pipes avaiable are all from the same place so that isn't your problem. So
what you will need to do is hammer, pound, bend to make it fit, but it
should work. On my buses the dampner pipe clears the bumper by less then a
1/4 inch, then the s bent tail pipe goes under the bumper.
