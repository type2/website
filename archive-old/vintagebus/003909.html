> I am trying to replace the vent wing seal on my bus. The seal is the one
> that actually holds the glass in the frame. I broke the glass and will now
> have to temporarily run plexiglass to fill the void. My question is when I
> get another piece of glass how do I replace the seal without breaking
> windows?

I use a large wooden Jorgensen clamp.
http://www.internationaltool_dot_com/jorgenso.htm for an example. The 12" and up
work dandy. They have independent jaws that you can align exactly to the
frame and glass and push it right together. Put something over the threads
on the clamp screws to protect the paint, soap the gasket and lay it between
the frame and glass, stuff it all in there and start cranking. Have fun
now...
