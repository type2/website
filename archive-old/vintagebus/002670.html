Looks like a Whale brand trombone pump.  I believe Whale still makes that
model, or a nearly identical newer version.

Whale pumps aren't inexpensive, but they are very high quality products.
Most can be repaired/rebuilt with factory parts/kits.

Try a West Marine or Boater's World store.  They may not stock the pump but
should have a catalog and be able to order what you need.  Pumps like this
are typically used on sailboats, so a marine store oriented to that trade
would be your best bet.
