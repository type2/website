(portable jumper battery pack), no difference. Pulled the motor, and
checked the end play (a suggestion from the archives), and removed one
of the shims as it was a little tight.

Forgive me, as I'm not looking at it now, but each wire in the circuit
was jumpered, and the one from the fuse box to the (? lights switch ?)
