I have a Bernie Bergman Stroker Crank in my speedy van... I have been very 
impressed with the feel and the smoothness of the idle.  I am also running 
MOFOCO 044 heads and the have been great as well.  No leaks, cracks and 
they stay relatively cool.  I didn't lighten the flywheel or anything like 
that, however I did have them 8-dowel it.  8 Doweling the flywheel isn't 
needed until you get over 1776cc.  So if you building anything smaller, 
don't waste your $$$$.
Lastly let me leave you with a few words of wisdom:
"If it is an important piece, or if it is under extreme stress (Like a 
crank ...) spend the extra money, or have to do it all over again."
"Speed costs money!  How fast would you like to go?"
