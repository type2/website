Hi everyone,

I just want to thank those who organized this weekend's mid-Missouri (Lake
of the Ozarks) camping festivities for such a nice time.  Members of all

middle of the State.  Nice location, beautiful weather, nice Busses and

me around too much while heading eastbound.  I hope the rest of you all had
a great Sat. evening around the campfires, and I'll bet that Rick Taylors
fajita's were great!  Have a safe trip home this afternoon!
