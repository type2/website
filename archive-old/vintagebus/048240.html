 - -- - - 
the first line is the date of manufacture - you buses birthday if you will.
in day- month (year) fashion.   so 
24-1 is the 24th of January

the second line is the actual Modification codes 425- N am specs, 081- walk
through (passenger model)114- 6 pop out windows

the two letters at the left on the bottom line are the port code- U for United
States, and A for port A whichever that isu a

model  2212-  standard mico LHD, cargo doors right

colour codes 434483- titian red below/beige grey above the waistline/mesh grey
upholstery

7086911- the whole vin is 227 086911, the 3rd digit means its a '67, so the bs
was produced  24 Jan 1967  .  The second digit is for model-  the 1st is for
type.   the last 6 are the serial Number of your bus
