be careful when you get a bus w/o title.

info like you want is rarely given to the general public.

In Wa state it is possible to get a title to an abandoned vehicle-   it must
be inspected by the state patrol, then you take their piece of paper to the
DOL, send a registered letter to the last titled owner, if they don't respond
within 2 weeks you can get a temporay title, which becomes  reglar title in
three years.   If the PO shows up within the three years, he/she can reclaim
the vehicle, but must pay you for any repairs done (receipts- time you spend
tinkering does not count).   Find at at your DMV or DOL what the rules are in
your state.   be persistant and polite- this is not usual, but the people in
the bureaucracy can eventually find you the info your need.
