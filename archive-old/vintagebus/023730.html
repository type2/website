I checked out the "outer ten inches of the cargo floor" sheetmetal I
bought from WW a few years ago(before I learned about Das Bulli Haus).
It is made in Brazil and does have "reinforced strike plate for the
cargo door vertical rods and seat mounting plates"  a little crude but
they are there.  The striker plate is very thin on the outside area
------=-=------ where the vertical rods go through.
