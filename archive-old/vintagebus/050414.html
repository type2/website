Hi Frankie,
If you have a USA bus then 56 goes to the dimmer switch B&W wire.

<<57>>
Goes to the front running lights Gray/B wire.  This one terminal we are 
missing due to using euro headlight switch that DPO installed.

<<58>>
Goes to the fuse box to light up rear tail lights, Gray wire.

<<58B>>
Goes to dash lights, Gray&R wire.

<<30>>
Main power wire from the fuse box, and also wire going to the ignition 
switch, big R& normal sized R.
