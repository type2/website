Sounds like you are using a post-71 clutch plate with a 68-70 pressure
plate. They look very similar to the earlier ones, but the post-71 hub is
beefier than the 68-70 hubs, which causes it to foul against the pressure
plate as you discovered. I am sure that if you change to the correct plate
you will marvel at how light and easy your clutch operation becomes.
