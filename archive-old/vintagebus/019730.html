Yes, also a marine epoxy or , believe it or not, or floor epoxy would also make an adequate
solution.

The floor epoxy, such as the Behr namebrand, can be found at the Home Depot, and can be tinted to
a color match.  I used to work the local Home Depot, and we would tint that stuff for all kinds of
reasons.  If I remember correctly, there is a "deep" white that should match near perfectly.  Any
serious home wears or paint store should carry this stuff.
