<< This all brings to mind several questions - who is actually going to
volunteer to slather tar on the inside panels of their VW?  Is tar the best
solution to this? >>
Well it is the best we have found so far.  It is the only material we have
found that actually repels rust.  VW itself used it for glue to stick down the
sound deadening panels on the type 1 floor pans in the 1960s-1970s and also
used it to glue tar board sound deadener into the front doors of the type 1 in
the 1970s.  Both areas in rusty VWs are rust free under and just around these
tarred areas 20-40 years later.  Due to this we paint the inside of floor pans
in out type 1s and at least the lower 1/3 of the inside of all doors of our
VWs to keep the rust away.
