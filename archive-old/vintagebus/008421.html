>should I clear coat it anyway while its still masked and in "painting mode"

go to your paint manufacturer rep for the specifics. generally clearcoats are applied very
soon after the basecoat,not 24 hours later. (causes adhesion problems). and there is no
good reason to leave the masking material on more than a very short time, as you are
about to find out. the solvents make removal interesting and the tape edge rough.
