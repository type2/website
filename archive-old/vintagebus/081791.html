63 1 ton front brakes have the lipped dust cap and take the same brakes  & 
front wheel bearings as 64-67. 
 thus lips in front = 64-67(1 ton) front brakes lipless = 55-63  (3/4) front

64-67 rear shoes, 55-67 use the same rear rear cylinders.
