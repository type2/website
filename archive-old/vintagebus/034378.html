Steve asks

	  >Can I reuse the old push rods?  They are straight and look fine.

	They also need to be round and within spec or else they
	need to be resized (big end) and rebushed (piston pin).
	Can check by assembling and using plastiguage on the big end
	and making sure the piston pin is a tight interference fit.  Resized
and
	rebushed rods that are balanced end-to-end are cheap changeovers 
	in the States? (do need a reputable rebuilder though)
