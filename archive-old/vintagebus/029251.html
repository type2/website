No problem from me, your's is good advice.  I found in the past, Ford sells a
"light" grease designated for use in their manual steering gears to avoid a
leakage problem when using 90 wt.  I have used Red Line CV-2 synthetic grease in
manual steering boxes in several vehicles and have experienced no problems.  You
are right to recommend against a heavy grease, as along with other problems, you
probably couldn't turn the wheel in really cold weather.  My thicker oil and
light grease information was only if David could not adjust his steering box, or
find a replacement.
