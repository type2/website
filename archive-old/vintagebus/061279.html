It's going to depend on what brand and product line you use.  Omni is cheap and easy
to use, but itsn't that great of a paint.  PPG CLV is a better paint, mid-range price-wise,
and is easy to use.  Glasurit or Sikkens are better paints, but harder to use and much
more expensive.  As far as color codes, your best bet is to actually take pieces down
to the paint shop and have them mix the color according to your sample.  There might be
a formula for MG/SG, but there's no guarantee that their rendition looks like what you
think MG/SG should be.  I have a friend who'd love to sell you a gallon of "coral red"
he had mixed "to formula" that looks like the results of an unsatisfactory dining experience
at Taco Bell.
