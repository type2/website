Again, sounds like you should try a bleed and adjust first.  If the adjust 
doesn't help, I'd suggest checking the hoses next.  Could also be gunk in 
the metal line to that wheel.  You did not mention if the right side brake 
shoes showed signs of being soaked with brake fluid so without looking I 
can't rule it out or point to that as a contributing factor.  I suspect 
that's not the case though because you say the wheel cylinders are not 
leaking.  When you bleed the brakes, pay particular attention to the 
condition on the fluid coming out.  Is it dark and chunky like a good cup of 
coffee?  That's only good in coffee, a bad thing in your braking system.

Good luck, and let us know what you find out.
