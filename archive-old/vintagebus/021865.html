I am assuming this is like all the others I have seen with two oil inlet
holes in the top and then a 90 degree plate with three holes for mounting on
it.  First you will need to get two 90 degree brass fittings because the
straight up fittings do not work so well in tight spots.  Plus you will want
to mount the filter upright instead of on its side so you do not make a mess
every time you change the filter.  I would fabricate a hanger off the left
bumper mount this will make it high enough not to get knocked off yet is
easy to access for oil changes.
