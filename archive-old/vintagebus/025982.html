Through much research in the archives and over at the egroups site,
I have gotten the following about rim rejuvenation:

First item of business is getting all the hubcaps clips off by either
smacking them with a chisel from the front side or center puch and drill

reattach new clips with either the rivets provided, other rivets, or a
small screw/locknut combo.  Apply finish (powder coat or paint).  Get
