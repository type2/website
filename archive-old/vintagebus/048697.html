Subject: Painted some Kombi stuff

Wanted to mention I got my bumpers, wheels, window frames, and a 
bunch of misc. parts painted. I hung them from the ceiling using a 
hooks and wire (I think Thom suggested this...or something like it). 
I did everything at once. I had 1/2 gallon of my white left over from 
painting the shell to use.  (that's before mixing in reducer and 
hardener). I used every bit of it! I can't believe how much it took 
to paint all those things. I think I got everything covered good. 
I'll have to get a good look again tommarrow. It's real hard with all 
those little places and different little angles to get it all using 
my sears paint gun. But I did it and everything seems cool. I've been 
stuck at this stage for a LONG time and I'm glad to have it behind 
me. I never did really do any straightening of my bumpers. So they 
won't look as good as they should. I guess I'm just kinda running out 
of steam. But the end is a near.
