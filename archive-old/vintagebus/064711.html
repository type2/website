That is a pre heat hose.  on mid 66 s and 67 it ran forward through the front 
tin and fits onto an outlet on the right side heater box.  This is a nifty 
set up, as the pre heat air is BLOWN INTO the air cleaner.   I suspect it may 
work better than the kind where the tube runs through the rear tin and picks 
up near the head.
