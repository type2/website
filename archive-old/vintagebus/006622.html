First of all do you have 8 mm  or 10 mm studs?
Second; does the case have case savers all ready installed?
  If your answer is no to the second question you have 2 options. First you
could use an oversize self tapping stud to repair (if you have 10 mm studs,
as a case with 8 mm studs should have case savers allready or you can insert
a case saver).             
    If you have 8 mm studes and pulled the case saver, there are oversize
case savers available for repair, these are also available if you have 10 mm
studs and pulled a case saver.
