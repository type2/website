Subject: Re: [VB] Wrong Muffler?

 Sounds like you have a 40hp bus muffler.   There are lots of these around NOS, which is nice if you are running a 40hp in your bus.  But it sucks if you buy one thinking it is for your 1600 and it is too narrow.  I have picked those up for $10-$20 at swaps for when I run a 40hp in a bus.  A fresh air 40hp muffler looks amazingly like a 1600 muffler.  A 36hp one is quite different looking.   If the muffler is like a cylinder, please let me know, I'd love to buy it from you.
 
The 40hp is narrower than the 1600.   So the muffler is narrower.    Also the rear tin is narrower.   
Sometimes you find a muffler is maybe 1/8 inch too wde or narrow, as heads can be different amounts apart depending on how much the heads have been flycut and/or how much the cylinders ae shimmed out.   But an inch or two sounds like the 40hp muffler is the culprit.
