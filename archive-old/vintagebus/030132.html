Isaiah,
How long is the "fat wire"?

That wire should be long enough to go uninterrupted from the dash to the
engine.  The sending unit is simple.  It is a washer has the wires attached to
the washer.

If your "fat" wires are cut short, you're in a minor bit of trouble.  I may be
able to help out.  But before we go down that road, just report back the
following:
How long the "fat" wires are and what is at the end of them.  If they are cut,
take a look at the insulation and note the two colors that are used.
