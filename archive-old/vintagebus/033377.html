you can take the switch off the column by unding the two little screws-  use 
the right size scredriver so as not to mush them and dont lose them.    now 
take the part with the arm, and undo the two screws that hold the cover on.  
dont lose those either.  slide the cover off, think you have to unscrew the 
knob at the end of the arm first. now you see the turn sig stuff.   you can 
leave it wires hooked up, and see if WD-40 helps.  It could.  some times the 
detents are worn, and sometimes the springs.
