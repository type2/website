Hi Jay,

This is what I am planning on doing in my bus.  I currently work for a Heavy 
Truck Transmission manufacture and one of our transmissions run on 24Volts.  
Since the US market is 12 Volts, we have a voltage doubler to raise the 
voltage to 24 volts.  In looking at this doubler it works from 6V to 12V.   
So it should work for the bus to give me 12V power to the electronics that 
need it, but will still be able to leave the bus at 6V.   The company that 
makes this "heavy duty" doubler is Sure Power Industries.  I know they have 
a website and you may want to check that out for further info.

Hope this helps,
