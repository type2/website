Dear Friends,

with this! It's because of him and the generosity of the list gods that we

It looks like John Anderson has the hubcap...ahem...baton now and is
planning to hand off to John Hartranft in Michigan sometime soon. I have

So, the plan now is to pass the baton around the Midwest then to head south
to Alabama and Mississippi and then west to Texas and there north to
Oklahoma, Colorado, Montana, Idaho and Washington then south to Oregon,
California, and jog to Arizona and then back to California and then on to
Australia.

This is NOT set in concrete! Any and all suggestions will be greatly
appreciated! I have not heard from anyone in Canada (as you will see if you
visit the website) and if someone up there writes we can change the route
when it is headed north.

I hope to have a list of drivers up on the web page in the next day or so.
That should facilitate some of the planning. Please feel free to comment on
the web page and the Tour route!!!! Helpful criticism and suggestions are
always welcome!

Thank you for your continued interest and support!
