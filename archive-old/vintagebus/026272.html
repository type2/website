In my humble opinion,
            While you have the front end torn apart,I'd change all worn out
parts! Make sure to check the center or pivot pin.
    Also I run my 64 with the Sway Away brand front anti sway bar and the rear
camber compensator, and I love it!!!
It makes the bus much safer and more responsive in the turns and in the high
winds here in the Great Plains.. Downside, the camber compensator will wear
your front trans. mount out a bit sooner, but I feel it's a small price to pay
for staying on the road.Again, only my opinion! Your not molesting the bus by
putting them on , they unbolt in minutes.
