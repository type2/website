Hi Colin,
1.  Are you using a stock aircleaner and muffler?
2.  Are you getting any fuel seep or leaks from the carb at either/both the 
throttle shaft ends from when you shut off the engine to five or ten minutes 
later?
3.  What is the ID number of your distributor?  Have you been timing it with 
a static light?  If so did you check the distributor advance the first time 
with a strobe timing light and a tach to make sure that it reaches at least 
20BTDC _at_ 2,500RPM?
4.  Are you using a stock lower pulley?  If so have you blueprinted it to 
make sure where the timing marks really are and put a 180 degree mark on it 
to do 2&4 valves correctly?
5.  If yes to everything above except #2, where are the flat spots you are 
encountering?  Especially at about what RPMs?
