Yes, if the flywheel is bound by the tranny housing then the starter is going
to do no good.  You need to unbolt the engine and back it out till you can
turn the pulley with the spark plugs out and then procede to move the engine
back in while turning the engine by hand to get the flywheel to do the cutting
job.
