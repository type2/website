One thing to check is the flange on the case where the oil cooler adaptor
mounts. I had an engine that leaked from around the oil cooler and couldn't
figure out where it was coming from. Turned out that the flange had a crack
and was leaking right out from the case. If you really cranked down on the
mount bolts this could be your problem. I had a local shop weld it for me
and it worked fine.
