You don't have a "main tank" and "aux tank" - it's all the same tank.
The reserve valve has two tubes, a short one and a long one.  The
"normal" position draws via the long tube; when you sputter and
make the switch, you draw from the short tube.  The two tubes
are about 1 1/2" apart, so with some math, you could calculate
the actual volume.  Word on the street is a little over a gallon.
For S&G, I cut down the smaller tube on my reserve valve by about 1/2", 
to make it easier for it to pick up the gas when it comes to that.
