Subject: Re: [VB] Stud size vs head type

On Mon, 14 Oct 2002, Rocky wrote:

> I had 10mm studs in my H cased single port 1600 and they pulled out.
> The studs were even anchored with threaded inserts. I installed
> oversized threaded inserts and 8mm studs. The 8mm studs and oversized
> inserts are working great. Apparently the 8mm studs grow at the same
> rate as the cylinders when heated and the 10mm studs do not. As far as
> I know you can use the 8mm studs with a AH case

The 8mm studs do not grow at the same rate as the case, but they are thin
enough to stretch when overheated.  When combined with case inserts you
may break the studs before pulling the threads.  Studs are
cheap, cases are expensive.

Unfortunately, the 8mm studs are highly stressed, making their strength
quite important.  If the metallurgy isn't just right you get broken studs
at much lower stresses than you should, as may be a problem with recent
Mexican longblocks.
