Subject: RE: [VB] center pin shift rod hell

wow, that shift rod coupler is always in the lime light.  You can tell if
your bus uses the short front shift rod (early) if the shifter is close
(2inches or so) from the front seat pedestal.  The later ones have the
shifter about 6 inches from the front seat pedestal.  If you mangled yours,
make sure you save the pieces.  Repop couplers can be found at any of the
major places (WW, wolfgang, bfy, etc) but the rods are more difficult so
you're best bet might be to go to a machine shop and have them repaired.
