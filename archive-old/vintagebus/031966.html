Unser the paper label is a water proof tape wrapped around the solinoid coil. 
 Due to automobiles low voltage water, even salt water will not affect the 
voltage much.  The only exception to this is the sparkplug cables due to the 
voltage carried by these is 20,000+ volts which then bleeds off into water 
easily causing the engine to missfire.  Over the years we have seen news 
reports about automobiles driven into bodies of fresh or salt water and still 
the headlights continue to shine underwater for hours.  Eric&Barb
