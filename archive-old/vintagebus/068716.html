The ID plate on the front vent is only on 1964 and later buses.  Plus any 
plates could have been easily unscrewed and replaced.  You will have to 
replace the cut out sheet metal behind the seats to get them from dumping you 
backward in an accident and probably turning you into a paraplegic!
