Ivan Dahlberg <ivan_at_eastvan.bc_dot_ca> wrote:
:  I need to get inside the case. All the bolts are off but it doesn't appear
:  to be coming apart without what feels like excessive force. How do I take
:  apart the transmission. 

Have you thoroughly cleaned the case?  Sufficient grease buildup can
act very glue like.  Also, are you sure this is a split-case
transmission?

Assuming the above to all be "yes", then my approach would be to get
a couple of spreader clamps (often used in woodworking; the jaws go
out instead of in).  One needs to be fairly small, so you can fit the
jaws with some wood padding into the nosecone end.  The other needs to
be large, so it can span the bellhousing (again with some wood
padding).  Open them both at the same time, very slowly.
