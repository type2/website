yes, there is a spring.

to replace the spring, remove the handle - Phillips screws in door jamb.   be 
careful not to mush them as it is easy to do.  Turn the handle over and see 
the remants of teh spring.

then see the pin that holds the pick to the handle rectangle?   If the handle 
was in the door it stands verticle.  you have to drive that out with a hammer 
and punch.  

The early handles for flat (nov 60 and earlier) doors has a diff rectangle 
base than the later ones.  theu also have different springs for the early & 
late bases, so you need to get the right spring.   Dunno about sources.  

Reassemble with grease.
