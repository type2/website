No washer.  I used hylomar on the surfaces to prevent leaks and a touch of

> has anyone found a good tool to tighten down the
> fastener for the oil filler neck? the best thing i've
> found is a 1 1/2" wide paint scraper, but there has to
> be something better than that.

There's your problem!

There are two tools that you can use.  One is a disc with two projections
that engage the slots on the internal gland nut.  You use this with a
standard ratchet and extension.  If you use this make
sure that the slots are clean and square.  Get a new nut if they aren't.
Hold the tool firmly against the gland nut and don't let it shift while
tightening.

The other tool is an internal cam wrench that expands inside the gland
nut.  I have no experience with these in this application.  they work well
for removing short nipples from pipe fittings, but who knows about gland
nuts!
