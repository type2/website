good question.  I dont know.  In any case I have not seen a battery cover 
that fits the 6V battery in my 53, so I dont have one on it.   the 67 would 
have come with a battery strap at least.  If you dont have one, like the one 
in my 65 is rusted through, I use a short piece of 2"x4" in between the 
battery and the rear of the bus, just so the battery wont slide back and mash 
the taillight assembly, which may cause shorts.  the battery does wedge 
between the places the battery strap ends are, so that keeps it from going 
sideways.   A plastic battery tray is not a bad idea, to collect any stuff 
that might come off the battery, keep it from rusting out that metal under 
the battery.
