When I have high torque starters built we take a six volt starter and have
it machined to fit the 12 volt bendix and bushing, add a 12 volt solenoid
and off we go, I have had one of these in 3 different buses now and still
works after 4 years, so to answer your inquiry, yes you can have the shaft
machined for minimal cost to fit a 12 volt bushing, cheaper and easier then
changing flywheels.
