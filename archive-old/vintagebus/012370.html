California DMV offices, particularly in urban areas, can be a real
pain to deal with.  "Take a number and wait for hours" is a normal
operating procedure.  If you are a AAA member, you can do a lot of DMV
transactions at a AAA office, where they are a lot more likely to
speak a language you understand.  I believe that the CAA and AAA have
a reciprocal agreement so you might be able to use this service to get
a trip permit.  Check with them first, however!
