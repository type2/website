The straightest and most rust free 1960 bug I have seen in a while. It has 
been modified a little, mostly in the engine and tranny dept. Oh this ones a

to the original ceramic green, but thats alright by me.

I havnt had a bug since 1985 but I am happy to get a little comuter. I still 
love busses, and would like to get another, but this is just too good to 
pass up.
The new owner looked my bus over, and really knows what needs to be done and 
how to do it. I think we all can appreciate that this bus will no longer be 
headed to the grave with a slow silent rust death and will be reborn as the 
camper it could be in the right hands. Maybe thats the best thing after all.
