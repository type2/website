Subject: RE: [VB] Progressive carb

You need to get one of the 74-79 bug fuel pump that has a 10 degree tilt at
the top. That pump was designed for the wide bodied alternator installed on
late model bugs and I have heard that this pump will work. There has also
been some differences in the center section design for the progressive. I
have seen three different types so far, and one does have a large throat
design. I have always used the little 3 1/2 PSI facet electric pump with
block off plate. It allows you a lot of room above it. These pumps are
available trough many of the ads in the VW magazines.

Just as a note, I had the same problem with the choke lever on a 73 Honda
carburetor I adapted for a 1910cc a few years back. My fix wasn't as
eloquent, I cut out a hole behind the choke lever in the fan housing and
brazed in a 2" freeze plug !.
