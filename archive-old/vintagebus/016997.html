> The reason the sign is stated that way is for LARGE
> trucks.  This means 
> tractor trailers.  If you are on a downgrade in a
> semi and you only use your 
> brakes, the brakes will heat up REALLY quick (like
> less than 30secs) and you 
> will lose all braking power.  The diesel engine in a
> tractor trailer has 
> engine brakes.  The engine brakes use the
> compression of the engine to slow 
> the truck down.  Buy using the engine brakes in a
> low gear and "stab" 
> braking you can safely manuever down a steep
> downgrade.  (stab braking is 
> slowing your vehicle 5mph below a safe speed,
> letting the truck gain speed 
> to your safe operating speed, then applying the
> brakes again to lower the 
> speed 5 mph)  The stab braking technique with the
> engine brakes will give 
> the brake pads and rotors time to cool down between
> use.
