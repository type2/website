Make your own.  Take a piece of metal tubing.  I think I
used a section of a handle off a cheapo floor jack after 
it reached the end of it's lifespan.

Use about 3-4 inches.  Cut a pair of opposing slots in the
tubing so it fits over the wings.  If this is made longer,
you can grip it with your fist too.  Then take a 3/8"
square drive tool and fit it into the other end of the
tube.  Take a hammer and smash two opposing sides of the
tube to conform with the square drive tool.  This can
then be driven with any sort of ratchet, extension, or
whatever.  Choosing a tube diameter close to the size
of the square drive eases the fit.  As always, file off
sharp edges for safety's sake.
