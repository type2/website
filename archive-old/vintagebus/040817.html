Over on the Ts list, things are a little bit more  on the 'dark side'
when it comes to customization.  One person has a 5 speed in their
T3, and my summarization is that unless you are racing or going after 
the 'cool' factor, it just causes more shifting.  It doesn't enlarge 
the envelope of gearing, just adds more within the existing high 
and low range.

There are large engine folks over there also.  Check out the website
at  www.vwtype3_dot_org and perhaps join the list for awhile.  One of
the leaders of the Dark side is off list for a while, but he'll
be back.  Be prepared for a volume of only around 50+ messages daily,
as it's the winter season, it'll pick up as things warm up.
