The best material to use is the same panel cut out of a junker bus.  If you
look carefully there is a curve in just about every part of the bus body and
using flat sheet metal will just cause problems if you want to make it look
like there was no repairs done there.  By the way the steel that VW used from
the 1950 through the 1970s was made in the USA.   Eric & Barb
