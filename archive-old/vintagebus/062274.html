I would try and find 373B heads to rebuild, better rocker arm pedestals and
head thickness. Also, if you are going to stick with the 40hp block and 64
stroke crank/rods, I wouldn't really go above 83mm cylinders. You really
want that engine to last as long as possible. Cutting the block and heads
for 85.5 cylinders weakens the whole thing. You also want to case save the
block for the head studs.
