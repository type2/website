Hi Chris,
The early exterior handles mount on the surface of the door.  The 61-early64 
have a rectangle depression that the exterior handle mounts to.  Thus if you 
use pre-61 handles with a post-60 door the handle tip will snap against the 
body.  If you do the reverse the handle will stick out alot!   Eric&Barb
PS If you use post-60 doors with thier stock latches on a pre-61 bus the door 
will not shut properly.  If you do the reverse the door will not close at 
all.   EEE&B
