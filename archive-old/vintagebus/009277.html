POR-15 might be a solution here although to repair rust holes without
cutting and welding try their POR PUTTY or POWER MESH Reinforcing Fabric.
Both should work great for the time being. As far as preparing metal for POR
15 I don't think you should worry much about Marine Clean and Metal Ready.
These things combined could be pretty costly. Try plain simple Parts Cleaner
or Mineral Spirits. Always worked for me. Now for the glass part, I saw some
windshield glass in a catalog from BFY OBSOLETE PARTS at $35.00 a pop. Check
if they still have these. And....Good luck with your bus! Don't worry about
the rust since she's 34 years old and is allowed to have some here and
there. Think about some of the modern cars, some only 10-15 years old and
already "gone".
