The plot thickens!  It turns out that the European sized metric tires, like
a 185SR14 for example, do not have an 80% aspect ratio, but 82% or 83% (I
forget which, but I think it's 82%).  This changes things just a bit.  I've
added the Euro spec tires to the list of acceptable 14" and 15" sizes for
buses.
