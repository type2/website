it depends on which handle you mean.   remov ethe handle, look for the tiny 
screw.   use the tiny screw to loosen (not remove) the tiny screw. Insert key 
in lock, remove key, tumbler should come with.   When removing key, realise 
that the wafers are spring loaded, and may pop out when you remove the key.   
 Front door handle require slight disassembly before you can access the tiny 
screw.  Dont strip out the tiny screws head.
