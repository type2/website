The archives also have a lot of info, so go ahead and look there, or ask the 
list questions.

FWIW "barndoor" generally means buses buildt early 55 and back, that have a 
huge engine lid. (as big ass a barn door).  The side hinged doors are called 
side doors or double doors (easily confused with double doors both sides) or 
cargo doors.   

I recommend a copy of Muir's Idiot book, which is great for general VW fixits 
and knowhow for the non mechanic.  Also a copy of the Bentley reprint of 
Volkswagen Transporter Workshop Manual 1963,  which shows a ton of Splitty 
specific (written for 63-67 buses when they were new, or actually for 
mechanics to work on them) stuff with loads of black and white pictures of 
clean straight non rusty buses to drool over..
