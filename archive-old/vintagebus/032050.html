I had a 28 pict on my 59 bus and got 31 mpg, though I can't credit
specifically the carb really, could have been a number of factors.     If
your bus is 6V, you can switch the 6V choke over to the replacement carb.
You could also probably pick up a used carb at a swap meet for significantly
less than $140.   Check to see the arm  the acc. cable moves isn't woggly or
it could do bad things like leak gas.    Are all your parts there?   The
little U shaped thingy made of brass that the gas squirts out of when you
pull the accelerator arm fell off my carb and made it running kinda off.
Gets stuck into the top of the bottom part of the carb and aims the gas down
the carbs throut.  mine ate it.   yum?
