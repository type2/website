on the vw wipers through 64 the wiper pivots that go throughthe body can be 
easiely snapped out of the socket connecting to the wiper motor.   For these 
VW made a different wiper pivot that incorporate the disconnects.   

For the 65-67 buses that have the stupid wiper frame, they made disconnects 
that attach to the pivots in the frame.  These dealies you put on outside the 
bus.   I had these on my 65 westy for a summer.  worked fine.  You must use 
the outies if you have the wiper motor frame deal.    You can also use them 
on the 64 &earlier regular pivots instead of getting early disconnects.   use 
the same wiper whichever you do,

I would think that bentley would show both kinds
