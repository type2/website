There are more part numbers for RHD models; the M-codes for RHD
walk-throughs were M 183 and M 184.

I can't say for sure if '62 was a one-year-only from these numbers,
but this is a '67 parts book and any one-year-only parts may have been
superseded to later parts by this time.  It does look like the driver's
seat changed for the '63 models, which would explain why the driver's
seat in your '67 is different than the one in your '62.
