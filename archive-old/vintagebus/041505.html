Keep in mind that moth balls with paradichlorobenzine (PDB) are considered
to be carcinogens.  The state of California will no longer allow me to use
PDB in my science labs.  Too bad, too, since it has interesting melting
properties.

It shouldn't be a problem for an occasional treatment, but I wouldn't
leave PDB in your bus all the time.
