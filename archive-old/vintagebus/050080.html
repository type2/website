I would be very careful about taking repair advice from parts guys.  I have
overheard some REALLY bad advice which waiting to be helped in parts
stores, especially chain stores like Checker, Pep Boys, etc.  I think it
would be much better to do your research (the Muir boook is the primer,
followed by the Bentley book for the real solid tech info), and then post
your specific questions to this list (after first checking the archives and
library at type2_dot_com).  The tech advice I've seen on this list is 10x
better than anything I've gotten from parts guys.
