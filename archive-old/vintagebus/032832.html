Hello,

The recent postings on rebuilding redux boxes got
me to thinking about the current prices on the bearings.
I called a local bearing house and gave them the 
numbers for the bearings to see what they wanted for
them.  

I am still in shock.

Granted, its only been about 18 months since I did
a complete rebuild on a redux box but the prices, while
they were retail/ over-the-counter, were enough to cause
me to flinch.  The 6305 bearings were $23 each, the 6306
bearings were $30 each (and you need two per side), and
the N207E roller bearing was $90.  These were either FAG
or SKF brands and I was told that they may have difficulty
in suppling the N207E as FAG may have discontinued it.
Taking off my shoes to use my toes to count I come up
with around $340+ tax for just the bearings.  

Makes you want to run out right now and change the gear
box oil, doesn't it?

Todd Hill

per year.  They never go down, only up.
