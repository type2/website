>Either way it is a '58 model year so I think it should have

Are you sure?  I think this Bus predates the idea of "model year"
a little bit.  The info I have says that they started counting
"model years" with the '59 model year on 1 November 1958.  I
think this Bus might qualify as a '57.

On the tail lights, Doliwa says 5 April 1957 at chassis 246 964 for
the two brake lights instead of the center one.  Bus Trivia: At the
same date, the angle of incidence for the license plate lamp was
changed to 8 degrees.  Remind me to bring a protractor to the next
show.  :)
