Listen to Clara's wise advice on this!!!

When I got my '64, the brakes were OK, but not great.
I decided to replace *all* the brake hoses and steel
lines on general principles.  I did a little test with
the old front hoses after I got them off the Bus...
I tried to blow through them, both directions, and
couldn't.  I hooked up my hand vacuum pump to each
hose and I could pull and hold a 25" vacuum on both
hoses in either direction.  This means that the front
hoses were really plugged up - I had little or no
front brakes!  I believe the hoses were original or
an old replacement...they had "Ate" molded into the
rubber.

"The ability to stop is often more important than any
other - cars, humans, whatever.  Do a good slow careful
job on your brakes."  -  Muir
