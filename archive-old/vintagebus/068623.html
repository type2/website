$500 for a similarly rusted out (and completely gutted) bus in San 
Antonio. There is a gutted "Westfalia" South of San Antonio on The 
Samba for many, many thousands more - albeit in better shape.

Yes, you can find cheap busses - a guy in Del Rio told me that there 
are several yards South of him in Mexico, if you have the money/time to 
haul them back. But if you aren't anywhere close, or don't know where 
those places are (and can't drive around for months finding them), then 
you gotta pay what the going rate is in your area.
