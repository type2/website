the heater.  Also, the way it sits in the Bus, the fuel tank is at the
same level or a little higher than the heater, so whatever you rig up

don't set the heater on your workbench and expect it to pump fuel up
from a jug on the floor.  It does have a fuel pump but I'm not sure
how much head it is designed to deal with.
