You got it!  Except that the default is sometimes 78, 80, 82, or 85
depending upon who is defining it.  For old bias ply tires the default
ratio was 100.

Tire sizes also only have to fall within a "range" of measurements, so
different tires of the same size can vary quite a bit in actual
dimensions.  Don't calculate to the gnat's eyelash and expect the tire
to match your calcs!

My 185R14s measure 146mm in section height (inner circumference of
bead to outer circumference of tread) when mounted and inflated.  This
works out to a 79 section ratio.
