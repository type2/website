I have some Hella H4 headlights made in Germany in my '74 Thing and they are
a direct replacement for the 7" sealed beam headlights.  You should consider
adding relays and better wiring to get the full benefit from these lights.
Another good idea, but a non issue for a split, is to add a protective film
cover as genuine Hella H4s are pricey.  I plan on adding some to my '65
soon.
