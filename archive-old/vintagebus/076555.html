36 hp went until may of 59, when the bastard 40hp took over. if i remember
right all the 36 and the very earliest 40hp still had the crank start.

between the box and body. i put a 1600 with later heater boxes in my old
early 59 4-5 years ago but i can't remeber the specifics of the tubes.

pressed bumpers?  those changed in august of 58 with the model year, a 59
should be the later style.
the only distinct 59 feature that comes to mind is the very early 40hp with
a crank apron.
