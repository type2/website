Before you get a new steering box, you really need to identify what is wrong 
with your steering.  maybe  your box is fine, but you have a bad swing lever 
and worn king pins, or a bent tie rod.  Open your Idiot Book to the "shimmies 
and shakes " section and read it!  Follow the prodcedure.  If you don'r have 
a idiot book (John Muir, one of my gurus) go get one.

I can deal with about 3 inches play in the steering wheel, that's my limit.   
the 61 has that, though it needs a little more work to make me happy. 

personally I like the KYB GR-2 shocks  I put them all the way around, all 4.  
 Very cheap from Kustom 1 warehouse.
