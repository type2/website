Bosch suggests that an overall drop of 0.6 V to the headlights on a 12 V
system is acceptable.  I'd guess that 0.3 V would be acceptable on a 6 V
system, but I'd try to minimize the drop as much as possible.  If
everything else was in top shape, that 0.35 V drop at the dimmer switch
is good for a 17%+ hit in light output at the headlights.

>Next I tried your tests as followed...pulled out far right fuse-it was
>a 16 amp fuse, and red.

It should be white, but OK for now.

>Turn headlight on low. Measured battery voltage at battery terminals-
>6.15 volts. Next I measured at one of the fuse clips, top clip was zero,
>and the bottom clip 5.93 volts.

You're losing 0.22 V from the battery to the fuse box.  Not super,
but that might be as good as it gets.

>Next I checked the voltage between terminal 30(fat red wire) and the far
>right fuse. Here I got a reading of .26 volts.

You're losing 0.26 V from the fuse box to the light switch.  This
can probably be improved and may be the reason for the warm wires at
the back of the headlight switch.
 
>Next between the terminal 56 (black and white wire),Here I got a reading
>of .35 volts.

You're only losing about 0.09 V in the headlight switch itself, which
is pretty good.

>Next I moved the black tester over to the far left fuse....here I get
>1.01 volts. This is the largest culprit I beleive.

Right.  This means you're losing 0.66 V in the dimmer switch wiring.
I am assuming this is still with the spade connector hooked up instead
of the dimmer switch.  If this is with the dimmer switch hooked up,
you're losing about 0.35 V in the dimmer switch and about 0.31 V in
the wiring.  Either way, this is a big chunk.

To sum up these tests:

+6.15 V  battery voltage
-0.22 V  drop from battery to fuse box
-0.26 V  drop from fuse box to light switch
-0.09 V  drop inside light switch
-0.66 V  drop from light switch to dimmer switch to fuse box
-----
+4.92 V  which is almost exactly what you measured at the headlights.

>I imagine it is the wiring between the headlight switch and the dimmer

I agree.  If I had to guess I'd say it's the white/black stripe wire
from the headlight switch to the dimmer.  It could also be the other
two wires coming from the dimmer, though.
