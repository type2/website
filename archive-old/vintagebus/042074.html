The answer is NONE!  The minimum rating for a bus is 1540 pounds.  This is
load range C in 185R14 and load range _D_ in the 195/75R14 and similar
sizes.  1400 is _way_ too low.

Search a bit longer and get the proper 185R14 (no aspect ration) size.
You will be much happier in the end.  The Bridgestone RD-603 works well
and is available at any Firestone dealer, which are probably happy to sell
_any_ tires lately!
