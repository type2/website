Hi Roger,
Use it for the air cleaner only then!  Multiwieght oil breaks down in  hot VW 
engine into the lower wieght listed.  So we use 10/30 Wt during  cool/cold 
seasons here and when temps are going to be staying well above 50F we  change 
over to 30 Wt..  We did try 20/50 Castrol and found the oil pressure  in cool 
weather was so high at start up that the oil cooler was at risk of  swelling or 
even blowing up.
