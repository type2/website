To do this you have to remove one end of the drag link.   Probably the end 
that is straight.  undo the screw holding the two half from turning.  
Position the wheel straight forward.  Position the steeing wheel so it is in 
the middle of the turning and straight across.   Screw in or unscrew the drag 
link so it lines up with the pitman arm.  tighten the bits back down.

Did that last week (2 weeks ago?) on the 61.  And greased everything I could 
find, including through the floor to the forward end of the drag link :)  and 
adjusted link pins, and adjusted st G box, and changed oil in st gear box.  
yum.
