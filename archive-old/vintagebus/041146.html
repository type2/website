Good day people of bus list -

I spent a lot of this weekend repairing and replacing wiring on my
bus. Thanks be to those color glossy wiring diagrams on Thom's
website. I got to thinking about what little modifications I could
make to confound joy riders and second-rate thieves. Check out my idea
(not yet tested) and tell me if you think this would work. It seems a
little too easy...

Right now the horn  positive wire runs from fuse 8 which I believe is
always hot. I'd move it to fuse 7 which I believe is only hot when the
ignition is on or hotwired at the front of the bus. Then splice into
the horn grounding wire between the horn and horn button with a little
hidden switch, which when closed, grounds the horn. Ignition on,
switch closed: loud noise. Ignition on, switch open: horn functions
normally. Ignition off: no horn, but that's fine.

Well? Clever, too clever, or just plain dumb?
