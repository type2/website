ALWAYS refill the tranny center section anytime you open or drain the RGBs!!! 
 Otherwise about 1/3 - 1/2 of the tranny center section drains down the 
tranny axle tube.  This is set up this way to help the RGB from going dry longer if 
a leak happens.  Giving the VW bus owner longer to find the leak before 
something catastrophic happens.
