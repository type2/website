yes, if your boots are torn, the thing to do is drain the remants of the oil 
out, replace the boots, and fill the RGBoxes, then fill the center section.

since you drove around on low oil, and something is funky, yes you may well 
have damaged your tranny.

I had this happen with the first splitty I bought, didn't know to check axle 
boots/oil, drove for a while, it started making noise (didn't yours?) friends 
said check the oil, there wasn't any and the boots were torn, so I replace 
boots, filled it and got 10K more miles out of it before total destruction as 
the damage had already begun,

Oops.

If it will shift now that it has oil, you will probably get some more miles 
out of it, but its useful life has been shortened.
