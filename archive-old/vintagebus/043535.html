Stuff you need to know if you don't already:

Get a copy of the "Idiot" manual by John Muir.
Get a copy of the Bentley manual.  It's for '63-'67 but a lot of the
  things in it will apply to your Bus.
Go to http://www.vintagebus_dot_com/wiring/index.html to get the color
  wiring diagrams (Bentley has B&W).
Spend a few hours each on http://www.type2_dot_com/ and 
  http://www.vintagebus_dot_com/ .  :)

Hmm.  My dad has the original AH 1600 in his '74 Bug and I'm pretty sure

were originally installed in about '72-'74 Bugs.)
