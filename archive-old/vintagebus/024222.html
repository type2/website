Most vehicles have personalities, VW's just seem to have
them at the extremes.  My history of VW's: My '64 was a 
delight, slow, reliable, easy to fix, never let me down.  
My '67 always broke down, and always a major brakedown.
I had it for 3 years, probably drove it 1500 miles.  The '64
for 4 years, drove for at least 60000 miles. The '67 Sqbk was
a very reliable vehicle until a few years ago, then parked.

The '80 Westi, while I kept driving it, it seemed to be
constantly needing expensive work.  Some are good
vehicles, some aren't.  By keeping at it and doing the
proper maintenance, you can get the bad ones better,
but it's a frustrating journey.  Haven't driven my latest
'67 enough to form a driving opinion yet.  It sits on the
driveway well though!
