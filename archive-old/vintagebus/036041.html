Hi Craig,
205R15 is a slight under sized compared to the original 6.4"x15 bias ply 
tires (Dia. 27") from the factory for type 2 1955-63.  165R15 is right on 
diameter (25") for the type 1 from 1955-1967ish 5.6"x15 bias ply from the 
factory.
  Make sure to get as high a load rating as possible and good tread that at 
the same time does not produce lots of road noise.  Like the old song goes 
"Don't let your wheels drive you crazy.".
