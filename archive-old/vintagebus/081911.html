Subject: Re: [VB] Time switch for heater

I had a switch in my old '70 that had a green face. It worked as a timer and as a regular switch. Just a quarter turn to the right than the heater would come on and I could turn it back to shut it off. If I went beyond the "quarter turn" it would start to wind up, kind of like a small oven clock timer. I think it was about 1/2 an hour than as it reached the end it would turn off. 
I'm not all that familiar with late buses but I know t3's had an actual clock timer in the clock on the instument cluster. If late buses had them as well, I'm sure they are pretty rare.
