I just mounted my backyard-made hitch this morning to tow a pretty heavy
trailer around.

modified to bolt directly to the frame of my bus. It's not pretty, but
should be looking nicer once I get it cleaned up a bit and painted.

attaches to the hitch rather than the bus. The only drawback is there is
a slot cut in the center of the bumper where the ball tongue goes
