Ther are 3 exhust systems out there thet DO fit buses
1.  Tri Mil 
2.  Thunderbird
3.  S S
4. I know I said 3 but Jeff _at_ Kymco makes a beautiful exhust and has been
thinking about doing a bus one.

You have to be SURE that you are getting a BUS header, these are made a
little different and will clear your rear valance without mods, but are a
little tight around the breast plate.
 
On the way to Kelly Park VWHoward lost his exhust (duel muff style) and we
had to stop in Bakersfield to find a new one, all we could find was a bug
header (Empi, Taiwan) and after installation VWHoward had to beat the crap
out of his rear valance for it to fit, thank GoD I had the 8 lb. sledge with
me for him to use.

I have personally used and sell the Thunderbird and the Tri Mil, I have sold
the S S but way pricey for most people, but very good quality, a little
better then the Tri Mil and the Thunderbird.

Comeing in the near future a Bus muffler with larger pipes and better flow
to use on these Hot Rod engines and maintain the apperance of a stock
exhust, will have same style outlet as a stock muffler in the original
location, just bigger to make the exhust flow better, install one of these
mufflers with Bergs new larger  diameter tubeing heater boxes and watch out!
