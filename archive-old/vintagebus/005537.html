When I mentioned videos, I wasn't talking MPEG.  The fastest way to become
disappointed with a video or audio project is to commit it to digital.  It
all ends up there eventually, and even regular video is "digital" in a way,
but what I really had in mind was to have people send in well-executed or at
least (hopefully) interesting home videos on tape.  Where do they send them
in?  Well, now, if that ain't the crux of the nub....I was hopin' that
someone with too much time on their hands and a video editing setup would
