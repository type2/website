Subject: Re: [VB] rims

15" wheels came on buses through 63, except 63 1 tons, and in early 64,  
about till the end of the pull handle, but didn't change at quite the same time  
as the door handle.   Sometimes you see 15" bus rims at swaps, they  have the 
larger 'rectangular' slots in them.   bus 15"s are wider than  bug 15"s.   If 
you look carefully at he face, you'll see a VW  stamp somewhere, and a date 
stamp within a circle with I think the month over  the year.  
 When your bus was new all the wheels were taken from the same pile,  so all 
would match down to the date code, and be made not long before the bus...  It 
is unusual to find the original rims on a bus still.   I'd like to  find a set 
of 3/61 dated rims for my 61 (whose birthday was yesterday... Happy  Birthday 
Red Bus!!!!), maybe one day....
 
oh, larger wheels effectively increase the gearing.  And the 15"s look  
cooler.  As the 14"s were used 64-70, there are tons more around, and  15"s end up 
being harder to find, thus cost more.
