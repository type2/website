With my Izzs safari's I had the same problem the rubber "appearing" to be
too long.  When I first put them in, I had 4" of extra rubber I couldn't get

to give me all that excess.  Don't pull or stretch it at all. Actually, try
and push it >together< at the corners and it should fit.
