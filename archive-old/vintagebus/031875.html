I still use my Miller 300 tig (15 years old) and WORE out my Lincoln,
Stepped up and bought a Hobart commercial, duel wire drive and variable wire
and heat. As for a small welder Lincoln I think is the best, as does my
welding supplier (who sells both brands). Better then the small Miller and a
few more features. Stay AWAY from Century, Taiwan parts, low duty cycles and
hard to get parts. FWIW, Snap-On welders are Century and I burnt my Snap-On
welder out in 6 months (alas, that was when I bought my first Lincoln). If
you search the internet on welders they are some dealers who sell Hobart,
Lincoln and Miller at a few dollars above wholesale.
