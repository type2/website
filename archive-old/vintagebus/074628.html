Donald F. Garies

GARIES, DONALD F. was born February 20, 1935 in Houston, Texas and passed 
away October 15, 2003 in Dallas, Texas. Preceded in death by his parents, 
John and Annie Garies. Survived by his daughter, Bonnie Jean Garies 
Shackelford and husband Tim; two grandchildren, Nicole Rene and Taylor Paige

his masters from UCLA. He was a Petroleum Engineer and started Garies 
Engineering, Petroleum Software Incorporated and Aardolie Oil Company. He 
was an avid Cowboy fan and in recent years a collector of Volkswagens. He 
loved his grandchildren and came to see them almost everyday and brought 
them donuts. Services will be 3:30 p.m., Saturday, October 18, 2003 at 
Restland Wildwood Chapel. Interment to follow at Restland Memorial Park. 
Family will receive friends from 6:30 until 8:30 p.m., Friday, October 17, 
2003 at Restland Funeral Home. Restland 972-238-7111 restlandfuneralhome_dot_com
