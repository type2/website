- - - - - 

the master cylinder (under the front floor) works all the wheels, the 55 
through early 67 is single cylinder, the 67 one is dual cylinder, you can put 
a 55-63 one on a barndoor, it will work, but works better with the correct 
50-early 55 one due to cylinder diameter.

if you mean the wheel cylinders for the 55-63 buses, the answer is that if 
you have 55-63 front brakes (the dust cap does not have the nifty bit to get 
pryed off with a claw hammer)  you need to use the 55-63 cylinders which 
unfortunately cost more.    

Flush your brake system every year to extend life of hydraulic parts.
