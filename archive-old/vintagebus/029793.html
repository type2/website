It sounds like you have the correct setup for the 1967 muffler.  The
dampener pipe comes straight back through the 'divot', make a 90 degree
turn to the driver's side, then the tailpipe fits on the dampener and
the end of that makes another 90 degree turn to the rear to poke out a
few inches, underneath the end of the bumper.  You should have 2 circle
clamps and a dampener support bracket to go with this setup.
