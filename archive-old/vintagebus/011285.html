I think the only difference between a 59 fuel tap tank and a 62-66 gas guage 
tank is the hole for the sender unit.  So, you can cut a hole for the sender 
unit, and install one, or save yourself the hassle and use a tank alredy set 
up for that, and you can install the tap in the same place (figuring you 
still want one)

You could also look for a stylee pre 62 gas gauge.  are you 6V?

Some DPO had done a bunch to the 59 SC I used to have.  It had a 15 gallon 
bay window tank, a guage hole cut in the dash with a VDO non VW faced guage, 
(and extra holes cut here and there in the dash, also a later shift coupler 
installed so there was no reverse till I got rid of it, and a ball joint 
68/69 front beam, and funky taillight, the front turn signals shaved and the 
wires hooked up to the front running lights. poor truck, ended up being 
decent though)
