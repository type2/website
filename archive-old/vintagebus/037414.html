Hi Mark,
Are you saying that you took the front wheels and drums off as a unit and now
can not remove the lugbolts?  If so then best thing to do is to remount the
drum/wheel assemblies back on your bus.  Lower the bus back onto the ground,
block the rear wheels and engage the E-brake.  Then use a least a 1/2" drive
rachet wrench or breaker bar and matching 17mm socket.  You might also have to
use a breaker bar to improve your torque.  Eric & Barb

I was unclear on what he was trying to undo from what- but this interpretation
makes sense.  It is helpful in saying things like the front or back wheel.   I
always loosen lug nuts with the rim on the car, resting on the ground, then
jack it up to unscrew them.  Lug nuts (or bolts really) take a 19mm socket,
though, which is the same as a 3/4 ".   I use a 3/4" impact socket 1/2" drive
on a long flex handle.   The long pipe (tall as me) is always close to hand if
needed.  Never broke a lug bolt yet :)
