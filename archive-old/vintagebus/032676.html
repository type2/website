a few questions:
--is the shift plate in correctly now?
--did this problem start after, for some reason, you changed the shifter
itself? they are not all the same.
--is your front tranny mount broken? if it is, it'll be dificult to shift,
and feel like you're shifting "through cement".
--is your clutch adjusted properly?
--is your FRONT shift coupler good? sometimes the grub screw(s) back off
and wear the shift rods. the grub screw(s) may      tighten up, but there
will still be rotational play between the rods.

these are a few things i'd check in addition to the previous ideas.
