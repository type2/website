Subject: Re: [VB] pulley leak

Hi JT,

Leaks at the pulley can be from several causes.

First, the oil slinger must be installed facing the correct direction.

Second, oil level must not be overfull.

Third, excessive blowby can force oil out.

In my case, a high compression engine build was aided by installing
the bugpack oil breather system. This reduced 95% of my oil loss
through the pulley.

A worn pulley can allow more than usual oil loss because the reverse
screw becomes less effective.

Installation of a "sand seal" can help here. If you have an aluminum
degree wheel pulley, it must be sleeved with steel to prevent the seal
from wearing out the pulley.

John
