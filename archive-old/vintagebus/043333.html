Hello all!

A friend of mine has a '64 Standard bus (and a '56 Oval) that 
he imported from Germany himself.  Both were originally sold
in Germany, not for export, so they have Euro-spec headlights,
not sealed beams.  He's never converted it from 6 volt.  (He's
a ham, but he just keeps a 12v deep-cycle battery right behind
the passenger seat to run his radios, and puts it on a charger
at home.  :)  )

These headlights are a bulb-and-reflector design, just like the
aero headlights on most newer cars.  The August '63 edition
of the owners manual that came with my '64 shows exactly his
setup in the "how to change your headlight bulbs" section.

The bulb itself has a spherical glass envelope about 2" diameter.
There is a round metal disc for a base, and three terminals on
three sides of a square (like the arrangment on US-spec sealed
beams).

Does anyone have a source for these 6v headlight bulbs?  The
few catalogs I've looked at only seem to carry the US-spec
bulbs.
