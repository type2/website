commercial vehicles came with black steering wheels and knobs.   Passenger
type 2s came with white ones.     except for 67 knobs, which are black and
their own style.

Now, westys are made on type 23s- kombis, but they get white wheels and knobs
(except the 67  knobs)  Just like they get painted inside in the back, which
regular kombis don't.

Buses with black steering wheels get black horn rings

buses with white steeing wheels get white horn rings

deluxes get wolfsberg crest center section, all other get black centers.

okay- white is not right- it is either grey (63? through 66) or ivory (before
that)

this does not apply to barndoors.
