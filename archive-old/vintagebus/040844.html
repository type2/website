Check out rubber supply stores.  Any decent sized town will have one.
Industrial rubber like O rings for hydraulics, fire hoses, and such.

There is a superglue made for rubber I've used for creating a turntable
drive belt out of a chunk of round rubber stock.

Be careful of GOOP, I have been burned a couple of times buying a tube
that had been on the shelf too long and was worthless.

The two part stuff sounds good.  I'd be tempted to glue on a fabric
support also.

Ask the rubber people what to use to clean it with.  Gotta be clean
for anything to work well.
