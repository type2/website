You might check out the tranny # for year manufactured.  Though the only 
three way to make sure is to open the tranny up and see what is in there, 
tach the tranny while driving or turn the input shaft 100 RPM by hand while 
someone counts how many RPMs a raised rear wheel turns at the same time while 
the other wheel is locked or on the ground.
