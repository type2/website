It means that your case has been machined at some point in the past.
This *probably* refers to align-boring the crankshaft bearing saddles
in the case 0.25 mm oversize.  (It *could* also refer to the crankshaft
itself.)  If the case was in good shape but you wanted to replace the
crank and/or bearings for some reason, you'd need to know this to get
the right parts.
