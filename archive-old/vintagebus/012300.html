In the book "How to Rebuild Your Volkswagen Air-Cooled Engine", Tom 
Wilson says on page 9 that case savers were installed in '71 and up 
Type 1-3 engines.  Later on pages 36-37 he says the change was made 
in '73, and at the same time they went back to 8mm studs (from 10mm) 
and began deep-studding the #3 upper rear cylinder-head stud.  He 
says you'll find lots of case savers with 10mm studs but they weren't 
factory.
