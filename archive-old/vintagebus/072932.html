Kevin, Years ago I had a 65 bug with a 6V 40hp. I got tired of my dim lights
so I bought a switch and a 6 volt relay with 20 amp contacts. I then ran
power to the switch from wire 15 and then to the relay to ground. I ran a
#10 wire from the battery positive to the relay 20 amp contacts and then to
the headlights. My lights were MUCH brighter.
