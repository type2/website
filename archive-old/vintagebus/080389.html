Have you checked out the throttle plate modification for the 34 PICT-3
carb to run with the 009? You need to solder the hole in the throttle
plate and re-tune to regain your vacuum at idle with a mechanical
advance distributor instaed of the original vacuum advance distributor
the 34 PICT-3 was designed for. Do you have the two or three vacuum
ports on the carb blocked also?
