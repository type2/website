Hi,

There are many discussions regarding voltage. My take is that you just
can't beat starting an engine on 12V with a 6V starter. I also find it
OK to run the wipers on 12V. Finally enough speed to keep up with the
rain. Others complain of shortened motor life, well, I live in So.
Cal. with little rain so I don't worry. The headlamps are a little
brighter and a slightly degraded electrical connection is less
noticeable. The tail lamps are definitely brighter and provide a
slight modicum of safety in that the poor driver behind you is more
likely to see brake lights.

Enjoy the ride.
Chris
