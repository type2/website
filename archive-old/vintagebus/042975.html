> What can be done to slow the rust process in the closed
> space between belly pans and floor?

You could undo the welds holding the belly pans on, and check out and seal
up the area. If all is structurally fine, then a sprayed-on coat of POR-15
can protect it near forever. Then button up the pans again and coat them.

> 
> The current owner thinks the rubber floor mats encourage

I went down to metal under the floor mat area, coated heavily with POR-15,
feather-primed at the two hour point,as recommended, then top-coated with
my favorate brand of beige-grey paint to match the rest of the car. When
the mats wear out the paint, I'll re paint. The mats won't wear the POR
out.
