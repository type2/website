DPs are weaker, tending to crack.  I hear that the new DP heads have a better 
casting, and reinforcements, but  at least 90% of used dp heads are cracked, 
while not even 10% of sp.  DP tend to crack between the spark plug areas and 
valve areas.  maybe the heads run hotter.  So I would rather put a sp 
together than a dp in terms of long life.    But if it runs, run with it!!!!
