 It started life as a sheet of 13/64 plate... you have
to remember, this was built outa scrap metal laying
around... anyway, made two disks, 10in OD/8 1/4in ID
and the other 6in OD /18mm ID... placed an old drum on
the larger disk, dykemed the bolt pattern, and drilled
5 18mm holes fer the lug bolts... the smaller one got
a 18mm hole drilled right in the middle. Then cut 5
pieces of 1 in x .250 wall round tube at 30 degrees?
opposite from each other ( been along time) and
started welding...after building the "Devo looking
hat", welded a 5/8in nut on top of the smaller disc
for the 6in bolt... after 2 weeks of
heating/beating/cussing this drum on the SC, I walked
outa the shop, puller still warm from welding... and
had the drum off in 5 minutes...:) I was not going to
tackel it with a grinder & drill motor....
