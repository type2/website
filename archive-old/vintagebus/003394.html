  I just did this same swap on the 63 panel. The 71 tank fits ok, you need
to fab some extra supports for bottom of tank (I used some 1" thick neopreme
rubber pads) and extend the tank mounting straps about 6". For the vent I
used a 5/16 brake line and ran it along the top of the tank and bent it up
through the filler area and then bent it 90 degrees so it fits into the
original vent/ overfill drain pipe that is in most or all? split buses. It's
nice to have twice the gas
