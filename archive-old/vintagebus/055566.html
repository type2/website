The culprit isn't the metal, it's the lack of insulation.  The roof is
exposed to the low temperature of the sky, so it radiates heat and lowers
its temperature below the dew point, causing water to condense out of the
air inside the roof.

The solution is to:

1. insulate the roof.  an insulating coating on the outside would be best.
2. Paint the roof white.  White surfaces radiate least.
3. put a dehumidfier inside the space, if it is well-sealed.
