These side "Foyer" awning tents are probably made by ASI/Riveria Camper
conversons out of Portland, Oregon.

awnings. A true Westy "foyer" awning attaches to the bus on two heavy duty
aluminum roof mounts.
Westfalia & Westy are too often abused as a generic camper name.  Not all VW
campers are Westfalias.
