I disagree, unless you have a source of NOS VW tin.  The original tin is
vastly superior to the aftermarket stuff commonly available.  It fits
better, has all the little internal vanes that the cooling system needs
and will probably last longer then Taiwan tin.  I tried a pair of
Taiwan cylinder tins once and almost fried an engine.  The Taiwan tin
lacked a small dimple that allowed the flaps to open, which means that 
if I hadn't checked the full travel of the flap lever it would have
jammed closed.  Used OEM cylinder tin will
probably need some minor crack repair where it mounts to the head, but
once repaired it will last forever.
