They were only on Australian and Italian
buses from 60-62.  I have one set, far from

semaphores in 59....)

They are very very very hard to find
here and very very very pricey.  I could probably find
a set but you'd freak at what
you'd have to pay (I actually refuse to pay it myself
and will live with what I have...) 

They do fit a standard SBL17 single filament housing.
