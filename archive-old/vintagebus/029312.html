Why would you use roofing tar for this?? All you are trying to do is to
lock out moisture. Why not just use a good quality enamel paint? You can
brush it on since it will be covered by the headliner or panels.
Personally, I would use two coats. The first coat would be a good zinc
chromate. This can be had for about $20 a gallon. The next coat, I would
probably use Rustoleum. It goes on nice and thick and my experience with
it outside in stromg sunlight has been very good. So it aught to last a
long long time on the inside of your bus. And its a whole lot less messy
than tar. I think it would seal so well, you could glue your sponge
insulation up there with no problem (though I wouldnt recommend it).
