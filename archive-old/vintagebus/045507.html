<< is there anyway to get the latch to release without mangling the door?  >>
Hi Dan,
First remove or at least loosen the rear half of the inner door panel and 
then the outer door handle.  Take WD-40 or so simular penetrating oil and 
spray it into the latch mechanism.  Press the release lever on the latch with 
a stout screwdriver to open the door.  Once open apply 30 Wt or higher oil to 
the mechanism and work it into it and lightly grease the latch catch on the 
body.   Eric&Barb
