There are 5 different carbs available for the type 1 engines made by 2
different manufacturers

1.  30 PICT 1 manual choke  Bocar	
2.  30 PICT 1 electric choke Borosol
3.  30/31 pict 1 universal (comes with adapter to convert wide base to
narrow base) Borosol
4. 34 PICT 3 wide base Bocar
5. 34 PICT 3 K-Cal wide base with extra arm for Cali. smog cars

All of these are avaiable and cost from 99.99 to 159.99

I prefer the 30 PICT 1 manual or electric but stay away from the 30/31 as
they use different jets and use 2 pilot jets or the 34 pict 3, east to
rejet. I have 25 different size jats for these, 5 different pilot jets, so
making the 30 PICT 1 or the 34 PICT 3 work is easy.
