Morning list,

Turns out that my "1961" doublecab is actually a December 1959
doublecab! I was around at fellow listee Christian Figenschou's place on
the weekend trying to wrestle a nice steering wheel off a parts bus of
his when he started checking out my M code tag.

Both the M code date line and the VIN agree, this is a '59 for sure.
Since buying my van 6 years back I have happily swallowed and repeated
the story I got from the PO. He said he was the first owner and that he
had  imported the van to South Africa himself. Christian showed me the
port code "SF" which means that the van was an official VW import, so he
didn't import it.

I guess it's possible that he ordered it but that wouldn't explain the 2
year difference in the age of the vehicle.

I must be a nut as I'm thrilled off my head to find out that my daily
driver is actually two years older than I thought. Glad it wasn't a '98
Toyota!

Later 

Michael  ('59 doublecab)
