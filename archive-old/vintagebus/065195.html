Sounds like you need some seals! Somehow water, cold
air or something is getting in your bus. Make sure
your seals are good then try to re-insulate your bus.

inprovement store should have either pink fiberglass
or NASA bubble/foil insulation. You can use contact
cement to keep the foil stuff on the body and use the
pink stuff as well.

info on this proceedure, it will walk you through it.

panels you are looking for.
