John asked:

 Will rims for a vanagon fit ok on a 67 bus?

 ---------------------------------------------------------------------

No way no how.

Buses up to the end of the 1970 model year are large 5 bolt pattern:
5 x 205

Starting in the 1971 model, the bolt pattern is small 5 size, 5 x 112.

The top wheel is 5 x 205, the lower wheel is 5 x 112:
