Someone has the wrong info in your parts books....
Up to engine number 7 336 419, the pipes were 32mm.
From engine number 7 336 420, the pipes are 35mm.
Engine number 7 336 420 came in bus # 1 048 085 which was built 12/62 as a
1963 year model.
You have miss-matched early heater boxes with a late muffler.
You need box numbers 113 255 107 and 108 which are also on late [carbed]
bugs....
