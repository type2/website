Check the plate on the right forward engine compartment and check it against 
the chassi number stamped in the engine sheet metal on that diagonal piece in 
the right rear of the engine compartment just forward of the rear apron and 
left of the battery tray.  Check for any sign on the top or bottom of this 
stamped area being cut and welded into place.  If it was there is a good 
chance the bus was/is stolen!
