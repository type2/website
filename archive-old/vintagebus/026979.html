The only reason I posted this was because it has been my experience that
a stronger *will* make you go faster. The cost of that extra speed will
probably be seen as shorter engine life as well as higher fuel expenses.
I limit my speed to 70 mph, not because the bus or truck won't go
faster, but mainly so my engine will last longer. If I can get a tranny
that will let me go faster for the same rpm, I might drive a little
faster. The decision will then be based on safety considerations.

That's my story and I'm sticking with it. 8^)
