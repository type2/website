On Thu, 20 Mar 2003, Dave wrote:

> What size fuse should these H4 have 25amp?

divide the wattage by 12 to get the current of the lamp.  Add the
currents of the lamps in the ciruit.  Double that to
get the fuse value, and then use the next higher standard value.

> I'm running 3 additional 10 g wires from the battery in the back.  They will
> be fused back there.  One wire for low beams and one for High beams

Better to use one wire for left high and low beams and one wire for
the right high and low beams, with double pole relays.  That way, a blown
fuse won't leave you completely in the dark!
