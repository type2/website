If you do run a cleaner through your engine, do change the oil a couple 
hundred miles after wards, w/o heavy driving in that time.   sludge broke loose

to aid cooling of the engine and make it pleasanter to work on,

w/ liquid wrench after cleaning as it gets and stays too clean.  (Bet you never 
heard that one before)
