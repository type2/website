If the machine is just a reader, US$50 or so.  If it can print them out
as well, it's worth more, but then you have to figure out if you can
still get supplies for it.  As far as the fiche themselves...I don't

updated, up to the point when they quit issuing fiche, that's a good
thing.

manuals too then they are more valuable.  You might check Ebarf for a
price reference.  People there seem to be selling sets for individual
vehicles, for $20 a vehicle or so.
