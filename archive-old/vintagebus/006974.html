Thin line tape is made to stretch and turn, the worse thing to do is do it
in pieces, these little connected areas will show up when painted. I just
take the thin line (1/4 inch) and start at the door seam and run it down and
around in the groove, if you miss, it can easily be pulled back then
reapplied. After this procedure, I use 1" masking tape and paper to complete
my taping off.
