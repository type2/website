Not a nice person...

I have been working now for the last 2 1/2 years to
"recycle" an old clunker as puts it.   In doing so I
have kept up my end in making this as fuel efficient
and non-polluting as I can.  This incudes the
installation of fuel injection (Miller CIS) and making
sure that leaks are dealt with and kept to as close to
zero as possible.  

I just did my first official mileage check and
averaged 20.7 MPG over to Bend (North Santiam pass
included).  This means that I get better mileage than
the Ford Expedition, Excursion, Lincoln Navigator,
Chevy Tahoe & Suburban, Toyota Land Cruiser, Lexus and
even my friends 1999 Ford Explorer.  

Now I have put several thousand dollars in drive train
to make this happen.  This said, it is still far
cheaper overall than a new(er) and IMHO I believe that
in 30 years this persons SUV will be yet another toxic
carcas in a bone yard and I will be slowly enjoying
the sceneary in my sixty year old bus (aka social
barometer :-)

Cheers!
