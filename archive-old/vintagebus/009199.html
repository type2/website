> How easy was installing the adjusters ? on the beetle you have to cut the
> upper and lower tubes of the beam and weld the adjusters , you have to
> uninstall the torsion springs also, this was pain , is it the same way

> 69 bus beam ??

pull the torsion springs
push aside the spring holding clamp that is inside each tube
drill a 1/2" hole in each of the tubes
cut the material out between the two holes (create a slot)
using a punch and the holes, knock out the peens from the inside out so that
the center holding clamp can rotate
line up the adjuster plate and weld
put everything back
