the nine digit vin number started for the 65 model year.  bus Vin# 1 173 454
would be made in Sept 63, making it early in the 64 model year.  If so, it
would have a wider back hatch than the engine lid, and the engine lid stay
would be an elbow, not a spriing.  The front doors would be the pull handle
type.   There is a little metal tag on the metal partition behind the
passenger seat- the M Code Tag.  The top line of it has the date of
manufacture, the number on the bottom right is the VIN.
