A while back I asked this and after much discussion and research and
argument, the answer was 7.5 BTDC.  Why?  Because the 009 provides 21
degrees of total advance and 28.5 is optimal for a stock or near-stock
engine.  The 21 degrees was verified with a dial advance timing light in
order to squash a few "I've forgotten more about Buses than you'll ever
know" losers.
