> VW made Transporter workshops Manuals -first one published in 1953, then
> updated versions in 1955 (for your non barndoor) 1959, and 1963.  (I suppose
> they kept making new versions after that)   These were originally made for
> internal use within the VW organisation.   And contain warning about letting
> outsiders have them.   At least some were printed by Richard Borek KG,
> Braunschweig  Hahn-Druckererei GmbH, Hannover.
>
> What people refer to as the Bentley, is a reprint of teh 1963 edition of the
> transporter Workshop Manual.  It covers 63 through 67 buses.     Do get it,
> it covers your front suspension, brakes, rear suspension, and quite a lot in
> between.   If you want the 1959 edition, search swap meets and look for them,
> you can sometimes find an original copy.  But I dont think they were
> published by Bentley.
>
> I think it is wonderful that VW and Bentley have responded to the rise in
