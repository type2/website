rust on the splines it seems like, gluing drum to stub axle, so be sure to 
grease it when you put it together.  Get liquid wrench and spray at the end 
of the stub axle.  Get sturdy screwdriver suitable for pounding on, and the 
BFH, and hit the side of the brake shoe through the hole in the drum, 
alternate sides to hit it evenly.  do make sure the screwdriver end is 
resting on the shoe.  I have an all metal BFSD for this and similar purposes. 
  This is a socially acceptable way to vent your aggressions, yelling and 
cussing helps.  Be creative.  Include the person who put it together without 
grease.
