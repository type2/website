Check the rocker assembly studs.  I had (stupidly) used nylock nuts to secure them down.  They ended up grabbing the stud too tightly and started to strip the threads.  I thought the valves were clapping and kept adjusting the valves.  I finally figured out that it was the assembly slapping against the boss.  I lock tited the threads and all is good now.
