Get soemthing to hold the frame steady.  Using a SHARP utility knife, cut
into the rubber along the channel at an angle / then again at the other
angle \ now you should have a triangular section which will easily pull
out.  Do so.  Don't be afraid to hit the blade on the inside metal - you
need to make sure you really cut it.  Also, you want a really good angle
going in there - get the shallowest angle relative to the perspective of
the channel that you can.   Basically you want to cut as large a piece out
of the middle as you can.

Now with a fine screwdriver, persuade one of the remaining three sections
to the center, and lift it up.  If you're gentle, you can pull the entire
"string" out this way.  Repeat for the other side.
