Are they new brake shoes?  If so you will have to adjust them two or even 
three times in the first month or two to allow the smaller outer diameter 
shoes to wear until they fit perfectly into the bigger inner diameter of the 
brake drums.  There after it still is a good idea to adjust the shoes to 
insure the maximum amount of sq inches of brake shoes against the drums.
