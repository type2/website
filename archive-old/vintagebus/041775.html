Jupiter and Merit are both "house brands" of Hercules tires.  They are
good tires, available in the proper 185R14 size in load range C or D.
When I last checked, the C and D tires were not M+S rated, while the
passenger-load tire was.  Same tread pattern too.  It's been a couple of
years, so they might have gotten around to having them certified in the
higher load range.

Don't be tempted by the passenger-load rated version.  I had two of these
tires separate their treads before I wised up.
