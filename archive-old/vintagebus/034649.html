A loose flywheel will make a horrible noise.  Torque it down properly and 
check the end play, and reset if needed.  this means buying a new FW seal, 
and do the o ring too while your are at it.  grinding the tranns with the FW 
also makes bad noises.  so, since yours is chewed, find a good one (12V o 
ring FWs are plentiful and should be cheeap, maybe $10 or free)

good luck, it may be just fine
