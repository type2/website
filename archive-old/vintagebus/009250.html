I've used this stuff when I restored my '65 bug. I didn't thin it down any,
and it went on great.  I had sanded down the whole bug to bare metal, and
primed it with this.  Now the drawback. I brought it to the paint and body
guy after that to have the final coats of my color sprayed.  It took TWICE
as much paint as it should have because that primer is like a sponge. I took
like three coats of the basecoat to get it to even look right. I would say
avoid it unless you have extra base coat.
