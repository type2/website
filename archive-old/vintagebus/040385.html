Thanks for the help on the fuchs quest.
There are several Ways to get fuchs on the bus...some good and some bad.
For me it started with my brake upgrade... I made my own straight axel kit
at the back, and machined a type 3 hub to accept  a 911 carrera disk and had
some callipers adapters made so they bolt up where the brake shield used to
be. For hand brake I used willwood mechanical callipers. Up front I used the
Porsche hub and disk with a combination of bay/Porsche bearings and a
machined adapter for the carrera calliper. I got all my brake parts of a 84
911 Carrera. When I was done I bolted up my  6x15" fuchs and was ready to
cruise...  simple really :-)
