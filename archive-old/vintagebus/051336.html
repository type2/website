The easiest way to find out what you have is to find a vendor copy of WAWD
parts catalog or a World Parts, IMC, Beck Arnley, etc. These are what I use
as they cover ALL years and models of VW's and all the parts are listed in
order. Makes it pretty easy to find application, year and model. When I get
stumped on a part (not in catalog) I resort to massive piles of OG parts
books, much more time consuming.
