The gas mileage you get with a freeway flier is higher than the gas
mileage from a stock geared tranny. I've seen for myself that this is
true under all of the driving conditions I normally encounter.

The bus is also quieter inside due to lower engine rpm's.

I'm sure a lowered bus would get better gas mileage if it didn't have
the RGB's, but it wouldn't look as cool. It would also get hung up on
the high spots in my driveway, which would make it hard for you to get
to where I keep my homebrew if you ever wanted to come by here on one of
your cross country trips.
