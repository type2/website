It is a small fan that fits onto the 3" heater channel that goes from the
engine to the defrost/heater vents. You cut out a section and clamp this fan
in and run a switch to the dashboard. It improves the heat flow by about a
million percent. It blows heat even when you are at an idle.

There is a company that sells a kit advertised in Hot VWs that is not wildly
overpriced, but it is available at any good boating store. Just ask for a 3"
inline fan.
