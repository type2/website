A timing analyzer is a timing light that allows you to dial in the total advance,
and set your timing at total advance rather than at idle.

"Desired advance" is a function of your distributor. Gotta know what dizzy you
have to figure that out.

Your distributor will achieve full advance at a certain number of RPM's, according
to model. Again, gotta know what your running for a dizzy.
