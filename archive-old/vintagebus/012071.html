> The Fel-Pro Neoprene Gasket sounds like a great solution, where can
you get these, should FLAPS have them?

Any FLAPS that carries Fel-Pro should have them or can order them.

The Fel-Pro gaskets have a "wide" section that matches a "wide" gasket
surface on the valve cover.  Some valve covers have a protrusion that
interferes with the wide section of the gasket.  If you have the
protrusion all you need do is cut the wide section off with a razor
blade.  It's a simple straight cut.

I glue the gasket to the (squeaky clean) cover with Fel-Pro yellow
gasket cement (a substance that looks suspiciously like weatherstrip
adhesive).  I use a very thin coat of permatex blue form-a-gasket to
seal between the gasket and head.
