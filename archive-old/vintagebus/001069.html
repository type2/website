higher rpms (at least for a bit) from the more hp, it will definately make

get up to speed.  That is why there is a general correlation with higher 
gearing and bigger motors.  Cuase you will do the same rpms at same speed and 
vice versa no matter what motor you have, and if you really want to go faster 
with the big motor you may find it helpful to change the gearing (or get 
bigger tires)
