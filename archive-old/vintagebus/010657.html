Hi David,
First check that both of your valve cover gaskets are not drooping from the 
top of the cover.  If one droops enough to allow air inside this will 
increase air pressure in the crankcase.  Next it would be a good idea to 
check you compression.  If too low the escaping gasses around the rings could 
push oil up and out.  Of course this probably means time for an engine 
rebuild.  Are you running the thermostatic bellow flap system?  Have you 
checked your carburetor jets to make sure your rings have not been washed 
away due to too much fuel?
 
<<It has an aftermarket oil filler neck with the hose going up to the

We have never liked those paper air cleaners.  If you have the stock oil bath

that changes anything.
