the breather hole on the oil filler.  One suggested that I run rubber lines
to breather holes on the valve covers and yet another one suggested that I
run them into the aircleaners on the dual webers.  Either way it sounds like

modification that should have been done - install a little tube right in the
rin as a "passthru" that your hoses could connect to and then a short length
of tubing underneath to the valve cover.
