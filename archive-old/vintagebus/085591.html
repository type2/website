Subject: Re: No fire from coil -update

Thanks for all the input people.

I did a trouble shoot again today. Strange.

Started with the battery. Bought a new one and asked the guy at flaps how
often do you see a coil bad out of the box. He said "rarely but anything
possible, so here take another for free to check it out" (he wanted out of
there as it was closing time).

Put the battery in and wired the positive to the positive on the coil. Neg to
ground. Touched the coil wire while disconnecting the neg. NO spark. Replaced
with the coil and tried again. Got a little tiny spark- this is good. I am on
to something.

Took the steering column off to see if I could coax a bit of function out of
the switch to try things out and was fairly successful. I was able to get it
to turn over part of the time. No spark at coil wire.

Looked at the points again and they are opening and closing, Checked voltage
at pos terminal of coil. 12 volts with key on.

Checked condenser and is bad (again a really old NEW part), so put in the old
condenser and it tested good! No spark at coil. Bummer.

Frustrated, I grabbed a dizzy out of the parts box and stuck it in. Good spark
on hand crank!

Had to redo the firing order as is one of those 009 type dizzys with a single
advance, but fired right up and ran just fine. Go figure.

I told you I hated that dizzy.

Ric
