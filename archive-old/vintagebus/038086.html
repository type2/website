why does the bar come out of the slot?  what is wrong with your adjustment 
stars, those usually are fine unless a tooth gets broken off from trying to 
turn them when they are rusted stuck.

yours brakes are wearing down too soon?   why?   how are your brake hoses and 
wheel cylinders?   bad brake hoses can cause brakes to stay on and wear down 
too soon.  Also if your brake pedal return spring is broken, it will wear 
down pads sooner  as they will wear unless you pull the pedal up with your 
foot.  Just replaced a brake pedal return spring on a 65 bus, fun fun fun, 
had to weld the stop back on as it broke off.

Also, don't you have springs in there holding it together.   Even when I take 
the shoes off the bus I have to pull hard to get the ebrake bar out, pull 
against the spring and mush the bar out.  Maybe you have a broken spring.
