>My 60 crew cab has a weird thing going on in the brakes. It pulls to the
right when you first step on the brakes. It stops pulling very quickly, even
though you still have the brakes on. If I step on the brakes again right
away it does not pull. If I wait about 5 minutes the first time I step on
the brakes it pulls again.

A small axle seal leak will do this. It is usually on the opposite side that
it pulls to. Pull to the right, leak on the left rear. Could be a wheel
cylinder sucking wind too, might have something to do with the pedal. Do the
hoses while you have the drums off anyway. It's cheap insurance.
