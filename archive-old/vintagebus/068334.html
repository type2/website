Congrats on teh new bus

If the brake pedal is firm, rather that squishy, then it is bled properly.

did you adjust the shoes so they drag slightly?  If adjusteing the shoes  
(all of them, 2 shoes per wheel) so they drag slightly  doesn't help, it's 
time to

adjust the MC push rod so there is only a mm of play.  you can check this by 
lying under the front of teh bus (under pedal pan off) and pulling down the 
brake pedal by hand.  how far till the arm actually starts pushing down the 
plunger in the MC.  you'ld feel the resistance.  hear a click when the push 
rod hits the plunger.  

FWIW to check the MC an easy test is pull back the rubber dust cover.  is it 
leaking under it?  It shouldn't be.  

Never hurts to adjust the brakes.   especially if the PO installed new shoes, 
which wear down to fit the drum, which means the pedal goes down due to wear 
rather quick at first.
