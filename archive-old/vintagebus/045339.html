Hi Jeremiah,
1.  You need a 17mm allen wrench or socket tool for the tranny drain/fill 
plugs.
2.   A 3/4 drive "L" shaped breaker bar from Proto (Lots better quality than 
Sears one.).
3.   Assorted sockets to fit the 3/4" breaker bar (Especially for flywheel 
center bolt, and rear axle nuts.).
4.   Flywheel lock.
5.   Bentley reprint of the VW bus 1963-67 shop manual.  About $70-80.00 but 
worth every cent!     Eric&Barb
