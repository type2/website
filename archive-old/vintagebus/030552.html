Most likely this guy is talking about "hammer welding".  It is a process of
straightening sheet metal with out using filler. Someone who is 'really" good
can get away with almost no filler at all.  It involves heating the metal up
and hammering it in conjunction with a back up dolly.
Consult your advanced body working manual for details.
