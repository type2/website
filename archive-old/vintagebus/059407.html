yes, the headlight should work with the ignition off. 

 see that the ig switch turns on the 15 circuit, and that powers fuse second 
from right. 
 But the headlights get power from the farthest right fuse- which gets power 
directly from battery and does not rely on going through the ig switch. 

 in fact, the ignition switch gets power from the headlight switch!

 some years the turn sigs only work with key on, some years they work all the 
time.
