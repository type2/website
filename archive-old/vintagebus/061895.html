door keys changed over the years

there was one style through 53 
next 54 through 63
the T series 64 - 66
L series 67 through ?70?

each time the key was changed the size and shape of the lock cylkinder was 
also changed.  so no you cannot take a lock cylinder out of a 62 style handle 
and swap it into a 66 handle. Cylinders I think are also different depending 
on which handle they go into.

There was a cosmetic change in the cargo handle when they went to the indent 
in the cargo door in 59.   Also panel van handles are shorter- no inside 
handle was used.  The engine lid locks are often a differnt later sseries of 
key, though you may have the L series.
