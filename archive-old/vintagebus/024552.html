Wolfburg West lists most of these components. But more normal suppliers
(I consider WW and WCM and BFY "botique parts houses") are available for
all of the bearings and seals, and even some of the gears. Ask a local
VW or foreign car shop or parts house if they buy from FAST ( IMC) or
WorldPac ( Fromerly ImPac)  or WAWD (in SoCal) and they can probably get
you better prices on many of the components. I called around and got the
best deal on bearings for my redux boxes (through the shop where I work)
from FAST in Portland OR. Problem with any of these companies is they
sell only to businesses, but their prices are good enough that even with
a mark-up they will be cheaper than the "botique" suppliers. 

Also, demand either SKF or FAG bearings. Avoid Timkin unless you drive a
ford, and never use any moving parts made in China or India.
