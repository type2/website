Hi,

To put a little science behind running the Cromoly pushrods at zero
gap, the expansion rate of chrome moly steel is about 1/2 that of
magnesium and about the same as the cast iron cylinders. As the engine
heats up, the case expands more  than the push rods and provides a
little lash. The aluminum push rods expand more than the cylinders,
and a little less than magnesium, so a little extra space is needed at
running temp.
