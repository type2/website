Hey everyone!

I thought I'd chime in for a bit just to say that I think this List is
great!  It's populated by a wonderfully diverse pantheon of VW nuts, or Type
2 nuts, and the range from green newbie who has just summoned the courage to
learn how to handle a wrench, to the amazing prowess of those who have the
parts numbers memorized and can rebuild an entire barndoor blindfolded, the
mad modmen, the "I've GOT to have this here correct bolt to complete my
resto" people, and everybody in between.

squabbles and bickering are one of the things that maintain the List's
character as a whole.

I feel grateful for the support and for the inspiration.
