Mike,
    Do it!
    Splitties are desirable for a bunch of reasons. They are the last of a
particular design landmark, The kiddies love them, they sit in your yard
making money, and they love you back.

    Brakes are not hard if you are logical. Just a small and quite easy
memory test &  jigsaw puzzle. Open two at one end, (front or back) and do
one at a time using the other as reference. Go for new wheel cylinders all
round if you can afford it, and recon the existing ones for spares in 2010.
Average skill  is enough. If you tow it home, get a friend to come help,
drink beer after.

Ive had both, and 12v is easier. Its not hard to do, check the archives.
    Good Luck.
    Barry Wright
