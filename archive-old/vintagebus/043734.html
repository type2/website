Per the online '67 parts manual, the seat bench (thing you sit on) 
changed part numbers at 1 270 003 in 3/64, as did the reinforcement
(I think it's at the top rear of the pedestal; see the diagram).  The
pedestal itself just has one part number listed, 265 801 903 C, but
with the 'C' there may have been three previous versions, superseded
to the C version when that parts manual was printed.  Links are...
