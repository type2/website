Gabe,

I am on the side of "dont buy JC whitney". Your money is hard earned and
you will be wasting  it on JC whiteny stuff.

I would suggest West Coast Metric. They are of reasonable quality, not
the best, but decent, and reasonable price.

Figure out what you need immediately, what you need down the line, and
what you can do without alltogether until you have a bunch of extra
money.

Post your needs (specific stuff) to the list. I am a WCM distributor,
and sometimes come upon spare stuff I can let go to a good cause for
cheap. I know that there are others on the list that sometimes have
extra stuff they can sell cheap to someone trying to save a bus.

Do it a bit at a time. Triage is the name of the game. Figure out the
most immediate needs and how much cash you have, and work on those. 

A little at a time adds up over the long haul.
Good luck, and do respond with specific needs. I'll be suprised if the
list cant beat JCW in price, quality, and helpfulness....
