I always buy real Bosch rebuilt generators, and included with
them are instructions on how to polarize it before use.  I'll bet
it somehow voids the warranty if you don't.  I bought one
last winter for the '55, and it had the polarizing isntructions.

Stay the heck away from the Chief/AutoZone/Kragen/etc starters,
generators, etc - they're C*R*A*P.  Spend the extra $30-$50 and
get the real deal.  I don't care about their "lifetime warranty" -
it sucks to have to change one of these things every year (I went
thru 6 of these cheap starters in the '56) and it's worth $50 to
me not to have to deal with a part that's essentially defective
the moment you pick it up off the parts counter.
