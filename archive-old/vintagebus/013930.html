`51 until present...still make Eurovan
westys..available everywhere but the USA...I have seen

between `51 an `59 very few exist.  Only 2 actuall
barndoor westys are 'currently' known to exist.  One
is in France and one owned by Darrell Adams in USA.

not even own a 'real' barndoor westy.  The have a mock
up of a `51 but she is a fake.
