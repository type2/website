yes- your back brakes could be dragging for other reasons, like bad springs 
or old fles lines.   to check, park the bus , jack up the back end,   spin a 
wheel.  pull the ebrake and let it off- any difference before or after.  do 
the same with the foot brake.  put it back on the ground, undo the rear axle 
nut, and inspect it in there.

ps older e brakes have zerk fittings so you can lube them.  grease is your 
friend
