  Chris (burnt bus) is a bus person. The reason she owns (owned) a bus was
to transport her kids and all the gear needed. A bus is much more economical
then any other vehicle that can serve the same purpose. Cheap to fix, user
friendly and has a great support group behind it. Yes she could get
something different (which she will do till she finds another bus), but she
is part of the bus community, the camp-outs etc. You don't go to a BUS
campout in a Celica. 
  She had to make a choice and the bus community swayed her in the right
direction. She is not money flush and cannot afford to own 2 cars so she
made a decision, which watching the list have proved that there is support. 
  If you burned up your Toyota I doubt if there is any group that could
really care.
  There is more to owning a bus then the paranoid feeling of it burning up.
  It's like when my shop almost burned down a few years ago, I could have
thrown in the towel and did something else, but it is the bus community that
supported me and helped me make the decision to stay in it.
