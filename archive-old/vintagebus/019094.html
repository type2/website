First off, you need a way to get the water out of them.  My personal
method is to drill small holes in the corners of each compartment.
Some people don't like this, the reply being "But now you're got water on
your floor!"  Like you didn't already?  I think I'm going to do the tiny
hole trick in the floors, too, to give the water a complete path to the
ground.

On to POR-15; yes, you can paint over it, but you need to lay down a 
light coat of primer when the POR-15 is still tacky, to give the
top coat something to stick to.
