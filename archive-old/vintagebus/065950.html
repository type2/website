The SVDA is a modified curve distributor taking the best from center of the
curve to fit most of the engines. I picked it because the curve picture on the
instructions showed a gentle curve from about 1000 rpm to 2700 rpm. The
plates, weights and springs are different than stock or .009 and it feels
smooth all the way up. I had a dual point .050 which I liked a lot but for my
truck, if it's not a 205, the SVDA is the one for me!
