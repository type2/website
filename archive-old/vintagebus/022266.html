My guess would be that you are getting 2195.12 revolutions per minute. The
way I get this is that if you are going 60 mph then you are traveling 1
mile or 5250 feet in a minute. Times this by 12 inches in a feet and you
are going 63000 inches in a minute. Divde 63000 by the diameter of your
wheel and you get 2195.12. I would venture to guess that this is your rpm.
