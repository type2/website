The bus is still sitting on its wheels on the ground right?   I use a nice 
long 1/2 drive ratchet with a 19mm socket and they come undone.  Never broke 
one.      Do clean up the bolts/threaded holes in RGBoxes and use a tap/die 
before installation-  it is easy to get them cross threaded when installing.
do loosen all the big nuts and bolts *before* lifing the bus off the ground
