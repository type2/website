Run a temporary hot wire from the battery to the coil and see if the bus
cranks (do not leave it on permanently it will overheat the coil if left on
24/7).  If the bus cranks then yes, you do have a bad wire, or contact in
the fuse box.  If worse comes to worse, run a new wire from front to back
(it's not original, but it works).
