I personally use a Compu-Fire, 3 different VW's 2000- 45,000 miles on them
with out a hiccup. The secret is having the right coil and wires. Many
people use a cheapy Autozone coil  which isn't internally ballast resisted
and/or the wrong type of wires which will cause premature failure of the
Compu-fire (and Petronix?). I am not a fan of Petronix, due to the changes
that have to be made to install and the PITA it is to convert back to points
on the Highway if it fails.


ronnie
ps; Compufire now has in there packaging a warning about the use of
incorrect coil and/or wires.
