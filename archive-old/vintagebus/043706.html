The later carbs have a linkage adjustment for the accelerator pump but
I don't think yours does.  It could be a broken accelerator pump
diaphragm maybe.  However if I was going to take it apart enough to
check that I'd just put in a new one from a carb kit. 

Have you checked around for vacuum leaks (carb base, intake manifold)?

Does it give any indication of whether it's getting too rich (black
smoke, "bloopy" sound to the exhaust) or too lean (backfire) when
it dies?  This might give a clue for adjustment.
