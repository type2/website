Hi Mathieu,
We are forwarding your problem to the list just in case someone else may have
a better solution.  We advise checking out all of the shift linkage for loose
or worn out parts first.  Also have you resently loosened your shifter bolts
or have they come loose?  If so your shifter base may be too far to the right
causing you not to fully engage 3rd and 4th gear.  If this is not the case
then it is probably a tranny rebuild time.  Eric&Barb
