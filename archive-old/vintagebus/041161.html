If you have the right seals, they should be a press fit into the drum.  You
should have to press them, or tap them with a block of wood and a hammer, to
get them into the drum.  Then they shouldn't go anywhere.  You might have
the wrong seals.

shoes can grab something terrible, leading to bus-ballet (pirroettes and
all).
