My birth certificate says "special color" or "special option" as well.
It denotes from the factory the Deluxe colors. In my case it's Chestnut
Brown and Sealing Wax Red. Biege Gray is the top color on Deluxes for
59. My RH headlight bucket says SPOR or probably SPOP which sounds like
it means "Special Option" in my case. I can't figure out the last letter
that well but "P" makes sense. "Special Option" is what the factory
called Deluxes, although I'm not sure how long this lasted.
