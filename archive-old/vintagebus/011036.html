Isaiah wrote:
> flywheel off [...] How can I check for case cracks? 

Clean the case behind the flywheel with brake cleaner. 
It will remove all the oil and associated gunk, leaving
the case clean and dry. Wait about 30 min and inspect.
Any cracks will become visible as dark lines on an otherwise
gray case. Leave it overnight and the cracks may begin 
weeping oil.
