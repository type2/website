selling busses to individuals who are going to care for them;  it's the

busses, and want to continue working on our own projects.  I've got enough 
projects to last a little while, but I'd rather sell some of the things I've 
collected to the people who want to get them going for themselves, not to the 
profiteers or speculators.  I own property in downtown Albuquerque that's 
worth about 7 to 9 times what I paid for it a little more than a decade ago, 
but I bought the place to live in, not to make some absurd profit.  I'd be 
glad to sell one of my busses at a loss to somebody who'd love it and care

don't see you out here taking possible projects out of our area.  Your
