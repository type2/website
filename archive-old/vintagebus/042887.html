> suggestions such as what type engine to build, carbs, 
Keep it stock, man!  A single-port 1600 may not be as powerful as a 1776cc
w/ dual Webers, but it will out last it (100,000 miles+++) for sure, and
maintanance it very minimal.  And if you're going for maintanance free
trips, go with a stock Soles carb.

great!

> tire sizes front & rear,

> special whatever to keep my breakdowns to a minimum?
Stock running gear, and a good tune-up.

> BTW, what's the difference between a standard and a Kombi?
A standard has interior panels with vinyl and chrome trim and pleated
vinyl seats upolstry and a cloth or vinyl headliner.  A Kombi has masonite
interior panels in the front and no panels in the rear.  They also don't
have cloth headliners and don't have pleated seat covers.
