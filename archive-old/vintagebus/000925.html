According to the '66 SO-42 Westy manual, the official names are (in
English):

"What-Not Cabinet" on rear cargo door, "Clothes" and "Linens" closets on
right rear wall, "Corner Cabinet" in left rear corner, "Storage Cabinets"
under rear seats, and "Ceiling Cabinet" above rear hatch (I prefer
"headbanger" for this one, but maybe that's because I was one in
high-school).

This manual is very informative and available for download from Thom's page.
It includes some dimensions and other details for those of you interested in
refabricating/resto (including that they are indeed made from birch
plywood). I've copied all the individual bitmaps into a single PowerPoint
file for easier reading/printing. I can email it to anyone interested, but
it is large, even zipped (~2.8 Mb) - not a problem for me to send as I have
a LAN connection at work, but will be a bit slow to open over a modem at
home.
