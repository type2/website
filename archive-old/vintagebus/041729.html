On Thu, 15 Jun 2000 Claralw_at_aol_dot_com wrote:
> I don't have howl with my bus roof rack, but some people said they did.  What 
> kinds of racks are the howlers? so I know to stay away from them

Howls are caused by resonances inside the tubes, just as you can make a
nice tone by blowing over the top of a bottle.

You can also get a "rushing" sound from the air interacting with the
tubes.  I reduced this by attaching a 1/4 plywood "floor" to the rack.
This might even reduce the drag a bit over a bare rack because the
turbulance that causes the noise also causes drag.
