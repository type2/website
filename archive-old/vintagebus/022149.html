1st line is Jan 18, 1966 prod. date
530 is ambulance step
430 is export options bundle
025 is seatbelt anchors
056 is steering column lock
119 is gas heater
181 is chrome hubcaps
191 is sub-floors
231 is LHD Kombi
523907 is Pearl White Paint
last number is 23(VIN)
