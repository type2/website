the link pins are the same top/bottom/ left/right, but YOU MUST keep them in 
the same set of linkpin/needle bearing/metal bushing.  don't swap top link to 
bottom unless you swap all three bits at once.  If your needle bearing is 
bad, you must toss it, and the link and the metal bushing, and insert a new 
(or good used for the cheap) set of three.

yes some have only 6, i noticed that too.  make sure you have the prescribed 
# on the inside, and leave them out of teh outside, as that should not affect 
the final adjustment.  do not use visibly damaged shims, some I 've seen are 
obviously rusted so they are not 0.5mm. that's from lack of regular greasing
