I should have mentioned in my last post...

The throwout arms made nowadays are much sturdier than the one that
originally came in your bus. The older ones are spot welded in 4 places
on the shaft, while the newer ones are welded all the way around. I have
had one break on me (which rendered my clutch useless), so I would
recommend replacing that while you've got the engine out.
