Moly (molybdenum disulfide) is the sh*t when it comes to lubrication. It
will coat steel parts and stay there under EXTREME pressure. It is widely
used in the machining industry to lubricate taps and other cutting tools
because the coating will cling to the cutting tool even under the pressures
necessary to cut steel. Buy the moly and use it according to the
instructions.
