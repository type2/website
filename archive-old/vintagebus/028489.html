I went to give the 62 a final inspection before pickup tomorrow... and i
noticed one last thing that i really frowned upon.  they had the motor

cooler :(  instead... the person who rebuilt the motor decided to put an
oil cooler on the front (FIF) of the fan shroud over the opening of the

engine...am i correct??  i do want this motor to last, and i plan on
taking it out to remove this setup and install a doghouse fan shroud and

the triangular air filter bracket...which i will need to mount on the
fan shroud....don't know how yet...but i'll figure that out later.  one
of my questions is... why did people do this???? this setup was on a
friend's 62 bus also...  that motor had one helluva rod knock to it on
#3... seems to me it got really hot quite often.
since this motor wasn't driven a LOT...and it still runs like a top..i'm
not toooo worried about it right now...as long as i change it all.  one
