Subject: RE: [VB] Auxillary battery

John, don't know what gen you are using, most of them in use nowadays
are 30 amp. They keep up OK with everything turned on and just a little
more, but you can see obvious voltage drops when everything is on when
you slow down to an idle where the gen/VR cuts out. Having an extra
battery with accessories is nice but also adds to the load on the points
of your VR. Your listed accessories, minus the gas heater, won't put
much at all of any additional load to your existing system.

Just for myself, I like the 50 amp Motorola alternators for two reasons,
50 amps and charging down to idle speeds.
