Hi Rich,
Best to inspect and remove grease if you have too much in there.  If it is 
too full it can cause the grease to be stirred between the spindle and the 
brake drum hub.  This heats up the grease up to the point that the grease 
looses it's lubricating qualities and thusly the possiblity of bearing 
failure.  Which can cause the bearing/s to weld themselfs to the spindle and 
drums at the same time, causing the spindle to shear off!
