Well, for one thing, it looks like the breast plate is wrong. Shouldn't it go
all the way around the oil breather tube, all mine do. And the carb/alt thing.
I ran into that an couple of times and one of my solutions was to cut the neck
bracket off of a trashed center section and file it smooth. You then get
another carb gasket and bolt this between the carb and the center section
extending the height just enough to clear the alt. You may have to buy longer
carb studs. The other method was to buy all the used carbs I could find an get
lucky finding a 74-79 alt type.
