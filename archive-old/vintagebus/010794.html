The purpose of this metal "hatch stick" item is to extend/prop open the rear
hatch of all generations of buses (in parked mode) yet keep it secured. The
prop distance is approximately 8"-12". The prop stick is neat for keeping
the rear hatch cracked for ventilation. Each end of the stick latches right
into the OEM latch mechanisms. Sticks are produced for Vanagons and older.
Ask about Eurovans if interested.
