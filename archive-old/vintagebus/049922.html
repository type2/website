The early buses are rated as 3/4 ton vehicles, the later ones ('63 up) are
one ton.  The later ones have larger torsion bars as well as brakes, and
perhaps the wheel bearings are larger also?

One modification I did on a '62 I once owned was to mount the front
anti-roll bar off of a '67.  It all fits w/ the '67 mounting hardware.
