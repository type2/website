switch on the MC. I put a solid state type switch on 
there that is attached to the frame rail and actuated by 
movement in the pedal arm. Lights go on before the brake 
travels through its free play, it's great for telling 
tailgaters to back off without actually braking.

>25. Brake fluid.  The higher the number the higher the 
boiling point!

DOT-3 brake fluid is the most common and fine for VWs.  
DOT-4 is not too hard to find, has a higher boiling 
point, and is compatible with DOT-3.  DOT-5 is NOT 
COMPATIBLE with DOT-3 or DOT-4 fluids; you have
to flush out the whole system before switching over to 
DOT-5.  I think DOT-5 is silicone oil based.
