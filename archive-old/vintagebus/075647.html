Those stores seem to be mostly in the western half of the US:
http://www.cskauto_dot_com/stores/locator.asp

Advance Auto Parts is apparently part of the same company, but I don't
know if they honor Schuck's/Kragen/Checker warranties or vice versa.
They seem to be mostly in the eastern half of the US:
http://www.advanceautoparts_dot_com/locator/index.html

Around here (OK/MO/KS/TX/AR), O'Reilly and AutoZone are fairly popular.
O'Reilly is mostly in the midwest and south central states:
http://www.oreillyauto_dot_com/B2C/changestore.ecatalog

AutoZone has pretty decent nationwide coverage:
http://www4.autozone_dot_com/servlet/UiBroker?UseCase=ST001&UserAction=displayStoreLocator

NAPA seems to have locations nationwide, but their locator doesn't show
a general map.  Their stuff seems to be a bit spendy, but usually pretty
good.  (They are a good place for GL-4 gear oil.)
