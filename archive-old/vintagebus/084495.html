On 1/31/06, Matthew Russell <volksie_at_gmail_dot_com> wrote:

> I did some searching locally for SPDT relays and everything I found
> was $20 or more ea.

Yikes!!  Bosch 0-332-209-150 is a pretty common part number for
installing alarms and other accessories. $2-$3 seems to be the going
rate online for just the relay, a bit more if it includes the socket.

> I was also wondering if it would be worth the effort to put a toggle
> switch on the pump somehow so I could run it for a few seconds to fill
> the bowl, say after I disconnect the line for some reason or need to
> rebuild the carb etc. Any thoughts on that?

You could also just pull the wire from relay terminal 85 off of the
oil pressure sender and turn the ignition on for a few seconds to run
the pump- once the fuel system is primed, turn ignition off and
re-connect the wire to the sender before starting it.
