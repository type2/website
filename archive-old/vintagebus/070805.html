court to testify that he fixed the problem himself but the judge apparently
weighed the evidence and the balance tipped your way. Good for you.

I just want her to know that the judge will _most likely_ want some proof that
a problem still existed after the 1st mechanic worked on the car and that she
had to pay (or, in your case, someone else had to pay) to have it fixed.
