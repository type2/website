Okay, postion the lock plate under the shifter on the floor so as to position 
the ridge that sticks upward on the rightside and forward.  Then rebolt 
everything back together.  Probably your biggest mistake is thinking that 
since you do not have third and fourth gear you need to move the shifter base 
more to the right.  Wrong!  When you move the shift lever to the right and 
rear to get third gear you actually have the bottom tip of the shift lever 
and shift linkage rod move to the left and forward.  So to get third and 
fourth gear you actually need to move the shifter base to the left!
