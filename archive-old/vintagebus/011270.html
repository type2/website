Hey Volks,

I scanned a pic of the RGB set-up on the late Brazilian splt/bay
hybrids.

They use a splittie RGB and trans, but the axles, instead of the
straight swingaxle, are double-jointed, using a pair of universal joints
each side instead of CVs. The trailing arm mounts rather ingeniously,
using the same bolts as mount the RGB and spring plate.
