Turning the burning of fuel in to electricity and back into heat is really a 
lousy system and ineffective with losses of energy at each conversion.  Plus 
the inverter will cause even more of a draw.
Best then to just go over your whole stock heating system and revamp it!  Are 
all of the parts there?  Do you need to weld up holes in the heater boxes and 
body tubing?  Make sure to run the thermostatic flaps system and even install 
a set of insulation panels from a set of type 3 heater boxes on your type 1&2 
boxes.  If still you have not gotten enough heat for your comfort/climate it 
is time for the gas heater installation.  Eric&Barb
