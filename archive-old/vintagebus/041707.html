I have two of them I bought from Ronnie at Old Volks.  After 2000 miles on
each they are working fine.  One has a slight oil weep (bottom of
engine is a bit moist, but never drips), the other is
completely dry.

I don't know if I would want to hot-rod these engines without opening them
up and going through them.  You'll probably want to run more compression
and swap the crank for a counterbalanced one.

All in all, I'm quite happy with them in the stock configuration.
