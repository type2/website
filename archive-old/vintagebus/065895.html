The seamed double brown is your speaker wire. One of the browns may have a
black line or a broken black line. That is the low side of the speaker
output, but not of any concern. Of the three wires on the other  the other
side, the brown is your dash light wire to light up the radio when the head
light switch is on, the black is ground, and the other could be a orange,
red or yellow, or striped wire, that is power. On my Sapphire IV it is a
black wire with a yellow stripe.
