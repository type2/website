But how many times will you replace it? Labor is a lot more then the part. A
few dollars more you would have had NEW. I find that most Discount flaps
rebuilds are just cleaned and repainted with out any new parts except brushs
and that is only in some cases. Check it out carefully before you install it
and save a lot of headache in the future. A good thing to do is have them
put it on there tester and runit for a couple minutes to make sure it is
putting out and that they polerized it correctly. I have had customers with
reverse polerized generators from the discount flaps.
