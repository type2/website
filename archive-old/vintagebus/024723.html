Bill Bowman had asked for more specifics on the adjustble reamer I
mentioned using on the front end center pivot bushings. I thought after
the fact that others on the list might benefit. The following is pasted
out of my reply, so it may seem disjoint.

> The reamer I've got is a "QuickSet" made by The Cleveland Tool Co., Size
> H, 15/16" to 1-1/16" (23.75mm to 27mm). I bought mine at a swap meet
> when I was about to do the job and already had the parts... I lucked
> out.
> 
> The MSC 94/95 catalog (800-645-7270) lists a fixed 24mm reamer for
> $38.63 (order #02298404). They list an adjustable Size H (must be a
> standard... learn sumthin' every day!) in either import ($20.67,
> #02239085) or USA ($91.21, #02231082). I guess since you're working on
> an import vehicle, you'd want the import reamer, right? <ha, ha> Really,
> though, for occasional use, the import would probably be sufficient, I
> would think. I say that as a hack hobbyist mechanic, not a machinist
> (but I am a quality tool nut).
