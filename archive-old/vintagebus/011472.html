First, my 'help' to Jeff was to pass along clara's speculation
about 195 being the sliding seat.  clara and I have had several
offlist discussions on this.  Hence her "not so sure" at this
point.  I received some conflicting answers from owners of buses
with M-195.

The NEATO Old Bus Registry has seven (including Jeff's) buses
with M-195:

Chassis #   Prod. Date
  889.549   01/15/1962
  897.764   02/01/1962
  906.637   02/20/1962
  976.510   08/06/1962
1.027.740   11/12/1962
1.032.682   11/22/1962
1.037.414   11/30/1962

I reached several of the owners by telephone or email and most
confirmed "adjustable" seats, but there came some questions about
whether this was a sliding adjustment or a dual-position
adjustment.  My opinion is that Jeff's double cab leans the
arguement toward "sliding" driver seat, but that the verdict
is still out.

Anyone else out there with M-195 want to add to the data?
