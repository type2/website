While perhaps not available as a new part, the 30PICT-3 carb (OEM on 1970
1600SP) has a high speed circuit, which is supposed to prevent lean-out
during prolonged high RPM running.  I once burned a nice round hole in the
center of a piston in a 1969 convertible (was running 85-90 mph when I lost
power and saw smoke behind me), so I think this extra curcuit may have some
value.
