Subject: Re: [VB] Clutch cable

It is normal to have to adjust the clutch after replacing it.
 
But if the pedal does not spring all the way up, either the spring is  
gone/broken/not in place right... look at the clutch arm out the side of the  
transmission... there is a spring around the arm...  the arm the clutch  able goes 
through.
 
but usually what happens is the clutch pedal pivoting getting rusty/crudded  
up.   then you need to remove the pedal from the arm under neath (13mm  nut 
and bolt) and then remove the clutch pivot... think a large bolt is under  then, 
clean it up, apply fresh clean grease, and reinstall.   
 
_http://failsure_dot_net/oacdp/5867part/367.png_ 
(http://failsure_dot_net/oacdp/5867part/367.png)  has  a picture
 
you might want to do the brake pedal pivot too.
