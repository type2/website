The cooler kit I got had the cooler, electric fan, thermo switch,
bypass, etc as one kit from Bernie Bergman. He sells the pieces (I

years now and the fan seems to take a beating in the location that I
have it and the cooler installed (just forward of the r/h rear jack boss
in the cavity under the van. There are photos on my web site for the
curious.)
