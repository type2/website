>From: russ henry <ventwing_at_yahoo_dot_com>

>The motor was at TDC, and wouldn't go any further clockwise. I can rotate
>almost all the way around counter clockwise -- maybe 350 degrees, but then
>it "thunks" to a stop again. Then I can rotate it back around clockwise to
>TDC, and get another "Thunk", and it won't go any further. Any ideas as to
>what could be the problem?

Take the plugs out and try it again (gently).  If you have a bunch of fluid
(water, gas, oil) in one of the cylinders, it can keep the engine from
moving - sometimes known as "hydro lock".  Taking the plugs out will let
you get most of the fluid out, if that's the problem... then you have to
figure out where it was coming from.
