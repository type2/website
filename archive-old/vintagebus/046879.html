Hi Matt,
You could install the E brake cables from the 1967 with no problems if you 
carefully inspect them for any signs of fraying and grease the heck out of 
them.  Personally we would buy a pair of new cable/s and install one new and 
one used on your bus/es.  That way most likely when the old cable on bus/es 
wore out the newer cable would be still in fine shape.  Thusly you would end 
up with a well staggered need to replace only one cable at a time on each 
bus.  Eric&Barb
