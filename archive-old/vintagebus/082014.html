Hi Eric,
Try Wolfgang International for the kit.  You can use a 1968/69 front beam
and use 1971-79 discs.  Problem with the later beam is it raises the front
about 1-1/2" higher.  So you either have to raise the rear or get lowered
spindles or weld in drop adjust sections into the front beam.  We are not
sure discs are really needed.  The only improvement we have heard is they
are supposed to keep cooler than drums.  Have only overheated the brakes
once when we were still using 1955-63 suspension/brakes and only due to Barb
not downshifting and dragging the brakes too much.
  Plus with disc brakes you will absolutely need to install a "Rear Brake
Pressure Regulator" (see VW "Official Service Manual" for 1968-79 bus,
section 8, pages 11-13 to prevent the rear brakes from locking first.
