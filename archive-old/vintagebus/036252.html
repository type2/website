the front floors tend to rust due to water leaking through the front windows.
The original floor mats have the hatch patteren underneath- keeping a space
between the metal and rubber mat.       I have seen  floors in the back ,
where it rusted by the sides due to water leaking through the windows (drip
line noticable from windows), but the middle is nice.  This leads me to
believe that leaky windows invite rust, not rubber floor mats. 

Keeping the bus sealed from water, and the heat working is a great idea for
preventing rust as well as for comfort.
