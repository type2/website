If you're really sure that the far right fuse is good, look at the top
terminal for that fuse.  See that really fat red wire?  That's probably
your problem.  Either that or you have bad connections at the fuse
block, like from the back where the push-ons are to the front where the
fuse clips are.

Another tip: If you give the year, model, and US or Europe when asking
electrical questions, you will get more precise answers.  :)
