the problem is that the top two are nuts and bolts, which means you have to 
hold the one end whil eyou undo the other side.   The earier buses do not 
have the locking bolt on the starter, which means put a vise grip on the nut 
in tthe engine compartmnet, then go under the bus with a ratchet, some 
extension, and a 17mm socket, and assemble the tools till the ratchet clears 
the starter, and undo that top left bolt.   

on the left side you put the vise grip on the forward (tranny) side, and undo 
it from in the engine compartment.

there is also a nifty tool with two ears and a place to fit the 17mm hex 
head, which is held by a magnet.  if you attach this to that forward end on 
the upper left bolt it holds the bolts from turning.

I recommed putting the lower nuts back on, undoing the  top ones, putting the 
jack unde rthe motor, then undoing the bottom ones.
