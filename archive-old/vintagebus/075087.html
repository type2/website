Did you check the deck height to see if maybe the new p/l set raised your
compression.

It also sounds like you have the timing to far retarded, this will cause an
engine to overheat faster then an engine to far advanced and harder
starting.

Set your timing at 28 degrees TOTAL adavance (009 with compufire). In the
last couple dyno runs we made we found that the engines made more power at
28 degrees verses 30 degrees total advance, less then 28 toal also lost
power.
Engine temps there seem to be in a decent range (could hold dipstick). I am
NOT saying this is where you should set it, just where I have found the best
results.
