Hi Craig,
That is because that size is closest to the T1 bias tire which was 25" 
diameter.  The 1955-63 used a bias tire about 27" dia, or 215/75R15, but 
205/75R15 radials are the widest we have been able to fit on the front end, 
and the pre-1963 buses with thier narrower, and lower rear wheel wells will 
only allow the 205s.  The 1964-67 used a bias tire about 26.5" diameter.
