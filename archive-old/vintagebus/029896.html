I stand corrected! After looking closely again at quite a few photos I'm
sure that all two tone buses from 50 through 67 were painted
bottom color first entirely then top color whether White, BG, CNB or whatever.

Again a good example 62 deluxe http://hannoverwerks_dot_com/62deluxe1.html
shows this clearly on the roof.

It seems that this was taken at a different approach. Two tone buses in
the 50's had the bottom color as the interior color. starting in 59 bus
interiors I think were painted using the top color. Also 50's buses were
dark on top and light color on bottom, 59-67 buses were light on top and
darker on bottom.
