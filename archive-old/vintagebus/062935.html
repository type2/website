The number off of my ORIGINAL mat out of my 60 DC 610 055 April 1960 is
*265 863 731 VW followed by a VW enblem in a circle and there is a star
before the numbers, the number is on the edge in the middle against the
bulkhead and you would be looking at it upside down if in the rear seat. Of
course I can't really verify that this is the original mat to the bus, but I
did by it from the second owner who never drove it because he had started
the restoration as soon as he bought it. The best part of this deal was that
when he started to restoe it he bought all the parts needed to do the job,
including NOS glass (all of it) from the dealer, all of the rubber (most
NOS), tailights assemblies NOS and all the little stuff. The cool thing was
that most of the parts were bought from the dealer or WCM when they still
had all the OG rubber.


ronnie
