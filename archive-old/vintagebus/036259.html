Diana,

Yes, I think that your problem is probably the "barrel type thing" that the 
clutch pedal pivot pin rides in at the frame.  When it gets rusty, it will do 
exactly as you describe.  It's not a big thing to pull the pedal out and sand 
off the rust and lube it.  Took me about 1 1/2 hours max (even as slow as I 
work) on my (daughter's) '67 a couple weeks ago.  I wouldn't put it off 
forever, 'cause it may continue to tighten and stay down just enough to burn 
up your clutch disk eventually.
