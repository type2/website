Hi Jen that's easy?
Take how much you weigh and divide that be the desired torque setting.
the result is the length of the lever you need.  Get a breaker bar and
slide a pipe on the end.  Measure from the socket along the bar the
distance you got in the above equation.  Put all your weight at that
point on the bar and voila! instant torque.  This works good only if
you're honest about your weight!
