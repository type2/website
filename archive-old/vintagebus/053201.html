  This work of art was done over 20 years ago.  The engine has the stock
Chevy bellhousing and clutch and is mated to the transmission with a flat
plate (I could remove it and put a stock VW engine in, BUT WHY) with a
coupler and extented input shaft. Most of the custom treatment was done
using 70's Olds parts, headlight grill, seats and rear bumper, The dash is
uncut and the bus still retains all it's OG glass including the windhields.
From what I can tell the center section of the body was removerd andthe rear
section moved up to the cargo door. No chassis hacks were done except in the
rear where all sheet metal was removed to allow the placement of the Chevy
engine. 
  My plan is to have it running and driving again for the Classic weekend. I
heard a rumor
