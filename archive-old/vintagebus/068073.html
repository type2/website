instead of through it.  Still without removing the front shift linkage the 
only way to do the job is to remove the front suspension from the bus.  If

from the rear linkage easier.  Then if you thoughly grease where the 
front/rear linkage bolts together you will be able to take it apart later to 
replace the two rubber shift tube seals, rear linkage bushings and regrease 
these.  Make a huge differance in shifting!!!
