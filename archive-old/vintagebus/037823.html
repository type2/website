As far as I know, Panels never came with body bumpers, only Deluxes did, but they could have been installed or it could be mounting holes for anything - bull bars, a superfly screw-to-the-body ladder, etc..

If memory serves me, Panels were second to last to get a rear bumper, behind Single Cabs.  SCs got it in early '54 so you probably have a '53.  A '52 or earlier would be a dead give away with the funky no-ventilation wing windows.  If it really never had a rear bumper, there will be no mounting holes for the bumper brackets, that's how my ex-'54 Truck was.
