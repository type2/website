Now I'm thinking that I haven't explained the airboxes clearly so here
goes nothing. They are mounted to the inside and near the back of both
side gates(so that if you drop the gate the box goes with it leaving a
slot in the bed above the rear of the engine bay). The boxes are about
450mm wide x  150mm deep x a little taller than the gate. The top edge
of the the boxes sticks over the top of the gate by about 100mm and the
opening is covered with mesh.The large flat panel of the boxes has an X
shaped reinforcinc pressing stamped into it. 

These are not DIY add ons as all the Dcabs that I have seen in S.A. with
their gates in place have them.
