I would listen to Eric And Barb on this. Yes, you see a LOT of used engines
without their factory thermostat and flaps, but how many miles are on them?
From raw experience in the eighties, I can tell you that a motor with the
thermostat and flaps in good operating order on a good motor will outlast
one without the proper equipment. I have built hundreds of motors and dozens
for myself, my experience says go with the thermostat and flaps on street
engines. If you are racing or sand bogging, that is a different situation.
