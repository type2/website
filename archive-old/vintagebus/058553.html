Subject: Fun in the Midwest

We are going to have a great time at the Bug Blitz, Bus Blast sponsored by
the Kansas City MoKan VW club on May 31, June 1 and 2.  We sure hope you can
join us.

This is gearing up to be one of the largest VW events ever held in the area.
We are literally getting people from all over the country from California to
Florida.

Buy a chance to take home the 1600 dual port rebuild at the charity auction
along with many other nice prizes.

pepper bacon for all the campers.

Sunday will also be the "drive the track" day around the world famous
Heartland Park road course.
