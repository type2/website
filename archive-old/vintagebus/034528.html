Hi Mike,
Yes it will work if the cam gear is the same size (Look for the number
stamperd on the forward side edge of the gear,) as the original gear and you
use the original drive gear on the crank.  We once knew of a fellow who did
not believe there was a difference in the cam gear sizes.  He installed one
that was to big and 30 miles down range it pushed the cam gear off.  Eric &
