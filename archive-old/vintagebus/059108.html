Brosol H30/31 on 1600sp:
Mains 112.5
Idle/Pilot 65
Aux 45
It is working well for me, I get above 20mpg pretty much regardless of
where or how I drive. (except breaking trail through the snow with
chains on a rough dirt road in first and sometimes second gear, that
kills the gas mileage!)  I got my jets from a local bug shop for maybe
$3/each. I would bet Ronnie at Type2Parts and Ron at the Bus Depot
both have them. If you are popping open your carb you might as well
treat yourself to a rebuild kit, maybe $15 or less.
