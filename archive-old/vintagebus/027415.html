You might note that most FLAPS sell the new style fuel pumps that look exactly
the same, alternator style or not.  The only difference between the new ones is
the depth of the actuating rod.

113 127 025C/D  (so called generator style)
113 127 025G (so called alternator style)

I have seen a few shops selling only the alternator style and just putting the
alternator push rod in the fuel pump box.  This solves the problem of the
customer who knows nothing his or her own car.
