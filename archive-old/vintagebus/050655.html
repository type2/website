> The "classic" way to clean out a rusty fuel tank is to throw a couple

I tried this method before and it just made things worse for me, as instead
of big chunks of crud I started to get really fine rust powder in the gas,

can of fiberglass resin from Kanadian Tire.  So far, so good.
