yes, big difference.  on a bug the fluting runs (if looking at the lens so
it is more wide than tall) horizontally.  a bus will be vertical (loking at

assembly.  this also ffects the way the light pattern is dispersed.  if you
put bug ones in a bus, you'll get a great view of the tree tops and a very
narrow beam of light on the road.
