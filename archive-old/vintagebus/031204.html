According to Michael Steinke of the Bulli Kartei Bus Club in Germany,
M code 544 is "Something special that didn't come at the assembly line--perhaps the cut
singlecab gates (544 - KW 544, KW means "Kundendienstwerkstatt"--"service room", a special
place for conversions done by Volkswagen itself)."
    He also mentioned that he knows of a double cab that is 4 months older than this one
with the same gate impressions (long, short, long) made of singlecab gates and a double
cab 2 months newer than this one with the specially molded double cab gates with all 3
impressions equal.
