I guess I don't understand what you mean about "lower nose repair piece" -
there are two different pieces - the inner and outer.  Alan's inner is a
zillion pieces of metal; the WW one is a single assembly made of several
sections welded for you.  I liked the WW one better.  The only flaw with
the WW one was a gap on both sides of ~3/4", due to the piece "bowing" too
much and not meeting up with the lower *outer* piece perfectly.

As far as the "V" section, I used a piece from another bus.
