3 engines with broken camshafts. That's what happens, when, where or how
long it takes is a crap shoot. The washers are hardened and if they get
between the crank and cam gear, it's bye bye cam. I have taken a few engines
apart that had waded up balls of dizzy drive washer in them and still ran
fine, till the rod bearings went away, but that's another story.
