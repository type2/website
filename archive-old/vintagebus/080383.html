Yup, that's too much oil and probably occurred when the oil was filled
when the engine was not level and thus gave a false reading on the
stick. Normally you can get away with a little overage if your engine is
tight and the front seal is in excellent condition. I get some leakage
out of the pully area on one engine that doesn't have a sand seal
because I park on a steep driveway.
