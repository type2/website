Actually, back when Super Vee was the thing, getting every available pony
from a 1200cc meant hand balancing everything, including the fan. A well
known hydroplane racing team in Spokane used to balance VW and Corvair fans.
Since they are sheet metal essentially, they would run it a small flying
wheel balance and then run an arc weld bead where necessary. They then
ground the arc weld and kept spinning it up until it was balanced to the
gram.
