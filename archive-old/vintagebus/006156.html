Good Luck finding 3 shims to total .023-.024 in.--the thinnest shims are
about .009".  In order to use 3 shims you'll probably have to use a metal
gasket between crank and flywheel.   These are usually about .010" in
thickness.  Then stack up three shims totalling about .034 or .035 et voila'
.005" end play--right in the middle of the spec.  (1mm = about .04")  I've
never understood why using  only 2 shims, in a case like this is a
problem...
