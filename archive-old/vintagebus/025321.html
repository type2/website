for a 1500/1600 you can have dished or flat cam, and coresponding oil
pump, no? What am I missing? The galleys didn't match up?
You have 40hps and 1300 and 1500's with a 17mm gear oil pump, with holes
for 8mm galleys, 19mm, 8 mm galleys, 20 mm and 10mm galleys( like
70-only or 70-71?) then 24 and 26mm for later 1600's with 10mm galleys.
When the bigger pumps came out ( 24 and 26mm gears) they dished the cam
gear to make room, ans so these pumps need the right cam ( or better,
cam needs right pump)...Unless you put a small pump in a case with a
dished cam....a small pump in a case with 10mm galleys....yeah, that
would goof things up a bit... 
Or then again, did you possible use an auto-stick bug oil pump? This one
is a goofy two-stage jobber. But it should have worked ok too....
