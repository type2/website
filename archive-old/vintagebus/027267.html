Hi,

My 67 leaked like a screen door on a submarine from the back of the RGB's.
When I unbolted the boxes from the plates, I realized my problem.  The plugs
had either rusted through or had been pounded in too far and somehow damaged (I
suspect the former).  I made a puller out of a old screwdriver (check out NQ's
site I got the idea from him) and punched a hole in the plug and pried it out.
Some new plugs from WW (or BBoys) and some good sealant around the
edges...reinstalled and not a drop since.  Very nice not to leave markings
where ever you go too!

I think the sealant between the box and the plate is just a Band-Aid.  You'll
be pulling them apart soon I'd bet.  That being said, I've only done this once
as described above...your mileage may vary ;-)

Steve
