From my own experience, you really have to "baby" the new ones that are
being made now aftermarket.  I have bought 5 of them until I got smart
and realized that they break with the slightest amount of tension. 
Definately do not force the vent wing closed using only the latch.  I
use my hands on the glass itself now and pull it in tight and latch it
after.  It's a royal pain to take out the vent wing glass to re-do a
broken one.  Hope that helps....---JD
