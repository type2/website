Hi Nate,

I have 34 ICTs and they have the balance tube between them. I'm not sure
what they would run like without the tube, since I haven't tried that.
The problem you describe sounds like a "too lean" condition. I'm using
my 34 ICTs on a 1600 DP with a stock cam. My jet sizes are 140 main and
170 air correction. You probably need to try bigger main jets, if you
don't find any leaks like Dave suggested.
