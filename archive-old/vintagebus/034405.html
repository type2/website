Hey Gerald,

Depends on the amount of stretching but heating
with an oxy torch will shrink the metal.  Just a spot
at a time.  Very localised, heat to just before
red glow. Allow to cool.  Heat again. Gently with
patience........

If you go to

http://www.pipeline_dot_com.au/users/hoopty/page6.htm

you'll see where I have use this technique to 'shrink'
some metal (pic on right top)...

being pulled out you can bash it in and bog it (urk)
or replace the corner (mmmmmmm....)
