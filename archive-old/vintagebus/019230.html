Safety wire rocks.If you don't know what it is or how to do it get down to
your local airport and look at an engine and talk to a mechanic.I am
surprised that Eric has not gone on at length about it's values.There are
many places you can use it on a bus:Redux to springplate bolts,shifter
linkage,use it to hold c clips together and many other uses like stitching
the bodywork back togeather on the race bike.It's a PITA to drill all of the
hardware for this but with the right tools and jigs it's worth it.
