I think you have two problems- one is the RGBearings, the other the whine.  
Can you live with the whine?   or are you going to get a rebuild so the whine 
goes away?   My bug has had a slight whine in 4th at about 50mph for the last 
40K miles. Guess you better deal with the bearings though.  

I was looking at the RGcover i took off my 61 on the one side- the outer 
upper bearing had gone- fallen apart- pitted little ball bearings dropping 
when I took the cover off- and the upper gear had moved so much that it had 
started chewing into the cover.  That can't have been a good thing.  Do put 
magnetic drain plugs in the bottoms of your RGBoxes.
