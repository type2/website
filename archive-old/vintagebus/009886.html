I had the same thing happen - intake valve on #3 -  on the 1600SP in our old
'68 Campmobile.

Just kept it properly adjusted and got another 12-15,000 miles out of it
before it finally was obvious surgery was necessary.  The engine was
otherwise healthy but had lots of miles, so we went the cheap route and just

current '70 Campmobile, and that was mostly charging up and down I-5 between
Puget Sound and Central and Southern California.  Never had a bit of
trouble, despite "conventional wisdom".
