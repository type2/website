>A few month back I picked up a full length middle seat for my bus, but
since then have come to learn that a walk through should of had a 3/4 seat,
is this really true? my bus has the extra key ways in the floor for a full
length seat but they are screwed in brackets not welded on ones.
>
Yes, most walk-thrus had a 2/3 middle seat but you can just check the floor
mounts to see what is proper or check the holes in your original cargo floor
rubber mat, if you still have it.  Real floor mounts are welded-in, as you
indicated.

The 62-63 2/3 middle seat is fairly difficult to find, although you could
always fake it by using a 64-65 seat and switching the grab handles.

I also have a full-length 62-63 seat that I would trade for a short one...

'67 seats have the kicked-out leg near the cargo doors.  '66 had this too?
 I'm not sure on that.
