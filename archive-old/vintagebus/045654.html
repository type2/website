take a pair of vise grips and attache them to the bolt in the engine 
compartment.

now take a 17mm socket and some extensions and a ratchet and crawl unde rthe  
bus and use the socket on the 17mm head of the bolt.  use enough extension to 
clear the starter so you can use the ratchet.

some earlier buses came with the 17mm bolt through the upper starter 
attachment, not the half moon headed bolt.

BTW, had you cleaned up the connections on the old starter, and where the 
battery ground strap goes to the body?
