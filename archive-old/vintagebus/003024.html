Reach around in front of the fan shroud (engine off) and see if a rag, paper
towel, beer can, etc. got sucked into the fan. Makes a wierd racket when it
happens. In the absence of any driving related indications, that is the
first place I'd check. Otherwise, you could have hit something on the road
and bent a brake backing plate into a drum, thumped the lower pully tin into
the crank pully, or something of that sort.
