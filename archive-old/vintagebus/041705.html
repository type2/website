Well, early Fords had the tank in the cowl, just aft and above the engine.
Just about every motorcycle ever built has the tank above the engine, etc.

I don't think that the tank position in a bus is as hazardous as some
think.  In a rear impact, the hard/hot parts of the engine will be pushed
under the tank and it'd take a heck of a hit to get to the tank itself.

If you get hit that hard, you have more to worry about than a punctured
gas tank!
