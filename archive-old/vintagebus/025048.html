pat.

the ground wire for your gas gauge is your gauge itself!  try tightening
the little nuts that hold it to the metal harness that holds it to the
dash.  mine's been giving me trouble as well.  sounds like juice is
being re routed back through yours.  I'd try another gauge, check the
voltage, make sure everything's kosher and equal.  usually, the sender/
sensor in the tank is not the problem.
you may want to check the relay on the bright switch. I've the wrong
switch on my lights, so when I turn them on the gauge automatically
registers FULL.
6v gauges will work for a while, then fry out.  I just recently switched
to a 12v gas gauge.
