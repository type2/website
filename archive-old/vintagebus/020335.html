If I remember from an earlier post, you have an early
67 bus. You probably have the single circuit master
cylinder. I have the same setup on my 67 ( #559) and I
replaced the master cylinder shortly after I bought it
4 years ago. Dont take any chances with the early
setup...Also, you might consider converting to the
late style duel setup. I'm sure I will convert mine
one of these days.
Check all of your wheel cylinders for leaks, could be
your problem.
