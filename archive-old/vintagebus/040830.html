Another drying technique, if you have access to one is a vacuum chamber.

> The only problem is, this book got wet, and alot of the pages are stuck
> together, and have mold. Could I "re-wet" the pages to separate? and
> then hang to dry?
>
First, microwave it to kill any mold, it does not have to be for very long.

From there, it depends how bad it is.  I had a shop manual where about 1-2"
of every edge of every page was stuck together.  I just went real slow and
95% of the pages came apart with no damage.  I didn't do anything to it, no
re-wetting, no magical chemicals, in fact I made sure it was very dry.
