In the official "VW Instruction Manual, Sedan and Convertible", 1959 edition,(sorry I don't have the BUS manual,but hey..) it says:

Eng. Oil:

above +86 deg. F ......SAE 30
from +32 to +86 F .....SAE 20 or SAE 20W
below +32 F ...........SAE 10W
below -13 F ...........SAE 5W
"it is recomended to have the oil changed at more frequent intervals...every 1,500 miles.
Exceptionally low temps- (SAE 5W)...change every 750 mi."

Trans Oil:

SAE 90
"where temperature is expected to remain below freezing point for an extended period of time, SAE 80 grade should be used."
