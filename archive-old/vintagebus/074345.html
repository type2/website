"Thick as toothpaste" and "never hardens", that sounds scary! Especially
around the case where tolerance is critical. Permatex 3-H is as close to the
factory sealant that I've found and I have never had a problem with it in the
last 33 years. It is the case sealant of choice for high performance engines
like Lycoming and Continental aircraft motors which are also air cooled. I
even use it on the high temp Corvair engines, once the block halves are
resurfaced of course. I would be interested on the longevity of your engines.
