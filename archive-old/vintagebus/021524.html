 -What size screw is used for the drivers visor? Also, any tips on unsticking 
the visor (a bit of rust) moving mechanism on a pressboard visor?>>
N 11 466 3  Tapping screw, countersunk raised head B 3.9X13.  There are two 
pivots on all bus visors so we are not sure which one you are referring to.  
Either one you need to loosen with penetrating oil.  The pivot where the rod 
and pressboard visor meet is clamped on by a screw that tightens the pivot 
area like a water hose clamp.  Just loosen the screw until the visor moves 
and lubricate with heavier wieght oil than penetration oil.  Then tighten the 
adjusting screw until the visor stays up even on rough roads, but still loose 
enough to be lowered/raised by only two fingers not both hands!  This 
adjustment may have to be redone several times over the first couple of weeks 
with the pivot surfaces polishing themselfs out.
