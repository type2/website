Yes, there is a conversion kit available to put a type 1 in a type 4 bus.
There should be no changes to the transmission or engine compartment.

The bay-window transmission is held up by a hanger system that does not
"dump" the transmission on the ground when the engine is removed like the
early type 1 bay-window busses.  The transmission engine mounting bolt
pattern is the same and can be swapped very easily with no modification.
The sheatmetal is the limiting factor in the swapping of the type 1 to type
4 motors.

Granted the '79 bus will have taller gear ratios to make use of the 2.0L
power where the type 1 motor may struggle...  Might want to switch
transmissions to an early bay unit that was matched for a type 1 motor...
Then again someone may have already done that for you?  Check the
transmission number to tell where it may have come from.
