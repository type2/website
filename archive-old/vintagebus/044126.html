I think the interior code should be SO-44, the most common one, with the clothes cabinet next to the Z-bed with the yellow vinyl covers.  If I understand correctly the SO-44 ran from 65 to at least 72.
---------------------------------------------------------------
It is SO42 for the splits(1964? to 1967) and SO69 for the the bays. The laminate material is the same until 1972 model years when it changes to a darker color. The fabric changes from vinyl to some form of Herculon in 1973 but it similarly colored.1974 and 1975 Westys are a departure and 1976 to 1979 are totally different.
