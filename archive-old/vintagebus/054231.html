Matt,

I am 6' 5" also.  I now have a '56 Kombi.  It is not a problem for short 
trips around town.  It is a slight problem over a long trip (over a 1/2 
hour).  In the '56, the shifter is back.  My right leg has a tendency to lean 
on it when crusin' in 4th and I have to concentrate to keep it off.  I solved 
the problem by getting a small wedge shaped piece of foam rubber that I stash 
behind the seat.  When I notice myself doing this, I pull it out, lay it on 
the seat kinda under my thigh, and the leg won't drift over. 

If you get a 60 (or is it 61?) the shifter is moved forward in the cab.  I 
had a 65 bus and a 71 bus when I was younger (and weighed less).  I don't 
remember ever having a problem with either one of them.  Also, there were 
significant changes to the seats over the years and even between models.  The 
56 Kombi has a one position bench seat, like it or lump it.  My 65 was a walk 
through and had separate adjustable seats because the spare tire was not 
behind the seat.  Look around and find one that you are comfortable with.  
Good luck.

Jim Cahill
56 Kombi
