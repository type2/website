Glad to help!  Make sure to tach your RPM in each forward gear and write down 
where each has 1,500 and 3,000 RPM.  We should be able to figure out what 
ring and pinion you have from this info.  You really never want to go below

high of RPM in third or way too low RPM in fourth inside of this gap.
