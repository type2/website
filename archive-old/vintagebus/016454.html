Barndoors have similar VIN plates in the engine compartment, but don't
have the "coded" VIN like later buses.  If it's a barndoor, it
will have a 20-###### VIN, I can't recall the last barndoor VIN.  The
early wolfsburg buses had 20-###### numbers as well, until they moved
the whole operation to Hannover, and they changed to the ###### (without
the 20-)

The number will also be stamped in the body below the VIN plate.  THe
number behind the front passenger seat is meaningless.
