On Sat, 5 Feb 2000, lawrence w winther wrote:

> hey bussers,
> I'm wondering if the procedure is the same
> as the type 1 for raising the rear end of the bus. Anything 
> different about doing this on a bus? Any tips?
> I saw a description this in a recent issue of VW trends.

The procedure is the same, but the settings are not.  Avoid the temptation
to crank in extra twist to carry your load, as this will result in rather
strange handling and tire wear.  Stick to the stock settings, which take
into account carrying a full load.  Buses usually look a bit nose-high
when parked, because the driver's weight isn't present.

passes the "bouce test" it should be OK.
