how is the star not working?  the clack is probably the detente spring 
clacking against the star, and this is normal.  Are you sure you are turning 
the star the correct way?- it can be tricky to visualise.  If you turn the 
star one way it pushes the brake shoe against the drum and then you can't 
turn the wheel (or the star).  If You can't turn the star any more, and the 
wheel still spins, you are probably turning the star the wrong way, so try it 
the other way.  If the same thing happens that way, no more turning and the 
wheel still spins, I'd look under the drum.   OK, the wheel doesn't 
turn...Back the star adjustment off till the shoe just barely rubs (you can 
hear it as you spin the wheel by hand)
Now go in the bus and stomp a few times *hard* on the brake pedal, and 
recheck the adjustment.

Did the brake Hoses get replaced during the brake overhaul 5 years ago?  If 
not, do yourself a favor and replace them all 4.  They are not mentioned in 
the Idiot book, so I learned they were supposed to be replaced after one 
failed on my bus and I had no brake pedal.
