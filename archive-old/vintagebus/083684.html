That 113M dizzy is vacuum advance ONLY.

Ported vacuum, the vacuum used for vacuum advances,
goes AWAY on full throttle. So in reality, at 3500
rpms you have zero advance. You are at initial base
timing because the spring overcomes no vacuum. It will
be 7.5 BTDC if that is where you set it.
