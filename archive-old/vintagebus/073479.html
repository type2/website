Back in the 50's-- 60's--there was an accessory lamp available from GM
that worked when you opened the hood---or deck lid.
It clipped to the underside of whichever place you ordered it---and it
also popped off and had a wire on a reel inside the lamp--so you cold
use it at night as a trouble light- the bracket mounted to the underside
of whatever--and the light clipped to it--pretty neat--

My 63 ans 64 Bonnvevilles both had them--

They are still available--you just have to do some sniffing around--
not really expensive either--maybe 20 --30 bucks.
