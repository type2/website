NACA ducts aren't as good as stagnation-recovery scoops (the fancy name
given to conventional scoops), but they have far less drag, which is the
reason that you see them on race cars and airplanes.  To be as effective,
the NACA duct has to be larger in intake area than the SR duct.

Neither are needed for a bus.  Good seals will give much better results.
