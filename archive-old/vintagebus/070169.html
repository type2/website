the rear tin is off a later bug or bus (68 on) it has the big pre heat hole.  
 splitties and 67 earlier bugs had the smaller pre heat holes.    But the 
crucial issue here is that you want a matching rear tin and tin between the 
case and crank pulley. Then there will be no gap.  the two need to match.  So 
do you have and extra tin lying around?  go find someone who does and find a 
pair of rear tin/between crank pulley and case tin that match.   If you get 
another rear tin make sure the width between the screws holes at each end are 
the same, there are 40hp rear tins that look similar but are slightly too 
small.
