Subject: RE: [VB] 010 versus 019 Dizzy

Look in the VW interchange book, it gives you all of the rpm/degree
infor on the various mechanical advance distributors and if there is a
#3 retard or not. Personally, I loved the 050 with dual points for
driving around town and to work in my bug. Power to weight with the
1600/1641/1676 and a modified 34 PICT-3 was great and gas mileage was
around 24 average on the highway. With my truck I have a different power
to weight issue which includes the reduction gears. I find that a
correctly built German 009 or a modified Brazillian 009 works best with
a modified 34 PICT-3. The 010 comes in too soon for me at too low of an
RPM and thus prevents good top end performance for those times I find
myself on the highway.

If I had my pick, I would stock only SVDA distributors for all my motors
and be done with it, but then, I'm not a rich man!
