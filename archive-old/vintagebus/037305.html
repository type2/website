I know this may not tickle anyones fancy but I saw it and I just had to
mention it.

A stock 1600 engine was put on the stand at the Mason County Fairgrounds
meet today. Bets were taken. The only mod was a dual carb set up. Running
flat out, at 6000 rpm, this motor, pulled from a running car, ran for 21
minutes! If it hadn't sprung oil leaks I swear it would have ran longer!
I am not a big fan of this crazy idea of ruining perfectly good engines, but
it made me pause and think....previously today a rebuilt 1600 ran for 4 and
a half minutes. The builder thought that was good. But here was a stock,
german engine, without balancing as far as we knew that lasted so long at a
high rpm. Amazing.
I wish I had grabbed that engine out of a junk yard!
