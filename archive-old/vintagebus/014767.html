As to my new Mexican engine(plug alert..that I bought
through Ronnie at Old Volks Home of Lake Havasu

attaches to the long block that could wear out and
sometime fail along the side of the road (which is

and tap the mounting hole to attach the bracket
holding the heater flap bellows.

have had no trouble/concerns regarding the reliability
of my engine. I have adjusted the valves several times
and have found them to be right on. I have not needed
to top off the oil anytime except during the break-in
period. So far, no leaks at all, just as clean as can
be...no floor spotting.
