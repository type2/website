I just opened up my Apple rubber catalog to check on seal
compatability with gasoline. There are two choices it seems.
The first is Buna-N (Nitrile) rubber and the other is
teflon. There are other choices for gasoline, but these days
it is mixed with ethyl alcohol, methyl alcohol and other
substances for clean air reasons and Buna and teflon both
pass muster for these liquids.

So, my trusty McMaster has listed a bronze 1/4" female npt
ball valve with teflon seals for $5.87 each. Item number
4726K16.  See catalog #105 page 268. Also try
www.mcmaster_dot_com.
