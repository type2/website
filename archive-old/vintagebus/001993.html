The plug is only on 'big nut' redux boxes. If you have big-nutters, you'll
need them. Just remove the old one carefully and you'll have no problem.
Tack weld a bolt to it and prize it out of their, then cut and grind it back
off. They are fairly substantial, shouldnt have any trouble. I imagine their
would be a freeze plug somewhere that would fit the hole, measure one while
you have it out.
