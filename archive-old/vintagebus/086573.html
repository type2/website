Jules,

Actually, I have started doing this to almost all of my VW's with the 
exception of my 78 bus.

I got sick and tired of having gasoline running down my arms and into my 
eyes and being a potential fire hazard.  It didn't seem to matter whether 
I had a good clamp on either end or not as there is always some residual
gas in the lines.

Running steel tubing thru the firewall always seemed to be a better idea
to me rather than thru the cooling tin.

The tubing thru the firewall will have much less movement than thru the 
cooling tin.

I have started doing this on stock bugs as well.  The only argument 
against this that I can imagine is that when the factory built bugs, they 
didn't have to run tubing thru the firewall as the body was mated to the 
frame.

Just because the factory didn't do it that way doesn't mean it's not a 
better idea now.

Just my 2 cents worth, dave
