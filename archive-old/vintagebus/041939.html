On Tue, 14 Nov 2000, Matthew Henricks wrote:

> I have been looking for tires for my 65 deluxe.  I have been thru the
> archives and have not found what i was looking for so i ask:
> 
> I seams to me like 185/70 R14 was the closest thing to the original 7.00
> R14.  Given that, what tires have you all found (brand etc.) that have
> looked correct/good on a all stock bus?  I do not what to go with
> bias-ply and do not want white walls.

185 good, /70 bad.  The stock bias tires had almost a 100 section ratio,
which isn't available in modern radials.  The closest you will get is a
185R14, which will have a section ratio of 82-85.  I've had good luck with
Bridgestone RD603V tires in 185R14 load range D
