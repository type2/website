Tim,

Unfortunately, there's no way to get the senders out without either removing the engine/fuel tank OR cutting (shutter. . .) a hole in the cargo area directly above the sender.  I've done the later to my old '77 bus, but as any later model bay owner can tell you, pulling one of those motors/firewall/tanks is way more involved than pulling the same out on a splitty.  With good tools, it could probably be done easily in an afternoon.  Good luck.
