There are some heater box repair pipes which are meant to be driven into
the exaust outlet of the heater box after the old pipe rots away.  I don't
see why you couldn't use the repair pipes to extend the heater box pipes to
reach your muffler.  The pipes are about 1.5 to 2 inches long, but could
easily be cut down if you don't need that much length.  The downside is
that they do of course have a smaller inside diameter than the original
pipe, so flow is reduced.

The best route is to buy the correct German muffler, and all of the proper
pipes and brackets.  Those were hard to find 15 years ago, the last time I
shopped for an exhaust...
