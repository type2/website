Hi Markus,
Thing rims probably do not move the tires out much, but the farther out you
move the tires outward the more often the bearings will wear out.  Instead
have you tried tires with stiffer sidewalls and/or checked your shocks?  Also
you should get one of the stock front anti-sway bars for your bus if you do
not have one already.  Eric & Barb
