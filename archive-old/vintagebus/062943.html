From what I have seen there are 2 different ways the bars are held in place
it the doors. On high hindge doors there is a spring bar (like a brakes
adjuster screw holder) that holds the bar against a square bracket that
guides it. The spring may be bent or broken in the door not letting it go
through. The later doors I can't say how they are guided as I have never cut
one apart to see, but they are easier to put the rods in.
