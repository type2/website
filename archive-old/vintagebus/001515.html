These were difficult for me to locate, your mileage may differ, here's the
facts:

After an approximate 1 week search, I found Barndoor front shocks are still
being produced at a reasonable price.

Brand:             Monroe
Part #:            LE100002
Compressed Length: 8.5"
Extended length:   12.875"
Eye size:          5/8"
Color:             Black

This stock is in their "Limited Edition" line so it normally must be
ordered.  The local Kragen/Checkers warehouse parts store just happened to
have 3 sets in stock and they were the cheapest at $42.99 for a set of two,
so I got lucky.  It was worth calling around to the local Monroe dealers as
price and delivery times varied, including one store which informed me "They
would have to be special UPS'd for $xxx and it'll take a week."  Can you say
SCAM?

It is a normal eye-to-eye style and did not come with bushings to drop the
eye size of 5/8" down to 1/2" (M12).  I was unable to find these bushings at
the Hardware store but found them at my local Fly 'N' Hi jacked-up truck
parts house.  They were nice and gave them to me.  Felt like I was in
another world in there...


Good luck in your parts searches,
