The only difference in the 1300cc case and the early 1500cc/1600cc case
was....    Nothing. I had a running 1300cc engine that was running fine
until it violently chunked a sparkplug out of it.  Well, it was
un-repairable, and do you know how hard it is to find a 1300cc head?!? What
I did was dig in my stash, and replace the pistons and cylinders with some
NOS 1500cc (same size on the outside as the 1600cc) units, and found me a
good set of single port 1600cc heads and bolted it all up.  Ran like a
champ. Heck, that 1500cc engine was one of the best engines that I ever had.
