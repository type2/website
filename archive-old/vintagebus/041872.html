>  Yes, two ways.  One way is to check the thermostat to be shrunk down to

Be careful!  The bellows isn't supposed to expand further than its bracket
will allow!  If you use the boiling water test, be sure that the bellows
is mounted in its bracket to prevent overexpansion.
