>hello,
>I was wanting to take a vehicle and have the underside of it pressure
>washed. then I was wanting to POR15 on it. Will the chemicals from
>the pressure washing have any negative effect on the por15 even if its

ANSWER - Power washing is a common way to clean a surface before using
POR-15, just make sure the cleaner you use is a good water-soluble cleaner
and that the surface is rinsed good and then must be completely dry.
