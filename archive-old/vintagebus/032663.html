sounds like bad hoses to me.  i've experienced this once and have seen it
many times.  replace them all.  it's not worth the aggravation just to do
one and then have another lock up on you.  also make sure you have the
correct freeplay in the master cylinder pushrod where it goes into the
master cylinder. if it doesn't fully release, it will maintain pressure on
the entire system.  ususally the brake lights will stay on if this is the
problem, rather than the hoses being at fault.
