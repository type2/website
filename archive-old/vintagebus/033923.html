Hi David,
To get to the two indentations in/on the rear shift rod in 1955-at least 74 
buses you have to remove the engine and tranny and then remove the entire 
rear shift rod.  Clean out the old grese out of the tube and regrese it with 
new!  Replace both of the rear shift bushings and replace the rubber boots at 
either end of the shift tube that the rear rod rides/slides in.  Wolfsburg 
West had these tuber boots availible last year.
