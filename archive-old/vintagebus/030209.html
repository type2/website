You are going to be hard pressed to find any sort of agreement when it comes to air cooled vw folks. What I have done in the past is to get as much infor from as many people as I can and try and make my decision from there. I agree that it is very frustrating when half the people say "if you do that your motor will die
tomorow" and half the people say that "if you do that and your motor will go 300,000miles". With the info I found, I came to the conclusion that I wanted a higher hp motor (1776) and therefore wanted some higher gearing. I went with the freeway flyer with reduction boxes to maintain the stock ride height. I used to take my
Crew Cab up skiing all the time and ground clearance was more of an issue. My tranny cost about $650. By the time you lower the back end and then lower the front to match and do all of the stuff it takes to really complete this task I think you will find that the cost is about the same.
Good luck making your decision,
