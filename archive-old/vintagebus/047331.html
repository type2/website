if they don't, check to make sure you have power to the sensor.  Take your 
test light and check both wires- one of them is hot, but they are both the 
same colour (black/red)   If there is no power there, your problem is before 
that- have you checked your fuses?

holders.  if you have power there, but the brake lights don't work, it could 
be a bad ground, bad bulb, bad bulb holder, do your taillights/turnsignals 
work?
