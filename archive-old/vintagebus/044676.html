The pop out windows have three seals.  On is on the bus body, on is on 
outside edge of the frame, another between the frame and glass.

If you peel back a bit of the seal on the body by the hinge, you can see 5 
slotted screws that hold the pop out to the bus.   If you take the seal on 
the outside of the frame off near the seams in the frame- in that channel 
where the seal should go are two slotted screws at each seam, but if it is 
installed in a bus you are drivering you probably do not want to rip apart 
the seal on the frame there, so just believe they are there.  If you get a 
bentley book it shows all this.
