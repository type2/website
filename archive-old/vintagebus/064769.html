I recently removed the interior from a Riviera camper to redo it.   The back 
piece by the spare tire (it's a walk through) had been sanded down through a 
ply of the plywood on the not showing side of the wood.
Not sure what you mean by the other pieces, but although pre bending wouldn't 
hurt, you do not need to pre bend the wood for the ceiling panels, the curve 
isn't that tight.   It is crucial to have the shape of the over-the-driver 
roof panels correct for a good fit, and make sure the panel fits easily into 
the slot on the air vent.

With the interior bare, you can fit a lot of firewood in a kombi.    We got 
two loads yesterday.    I have a pic if anyone is interested. -still gotta 
learn that webpage stuff.
