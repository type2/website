This is one of those things where I never win, but I gotta try anyway.   It's a
cyclical thing.  Everything goes fine and dandy for a while, then we get some
new blood, who gets bent when I try to do my moderation job.  I get the snot

quiet again until the next hairtrigger issue comes up.  If you don't believe me,
cruise the archives when they come back up.  I could probably automate this 
message via cron and nobody would really notice.

If I let the political/legislative/flame stuff fester - nay even let it start, I'll 
get flamed for not stopping it, and if I try to kibosh it, I'll get flamed for that 
too.  There is no middle ground.  It won't be but a couple of emails when some
pundit-wannabe will blame it on the democrats or the republicans or the whigs
or the tories, and the next thing you know, you've got a nasty flame war going.

mosey on over to vintagvw.  Bring nomex, but leave your tools at home, 'cause there

there as well.  Right this minute there's a full-fledged barroom brawl in progress.

and I'll deal with it again.   Compared to the type2 list admin junta, I'm a bleeding
