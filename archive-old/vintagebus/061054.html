wouldn't have been able to get the old one out without extreme measures!

I don't think that studs should be torqued into the case.  They should be
screwed nearly to the bottom of their hole, but not bottomed out.  My
thought is that bottoming the stud will cock it sideways which will yield
odd streses when it is torqued from the head end.

That's just my theory, but it seems sound.
