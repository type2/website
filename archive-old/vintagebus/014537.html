with a mystery trans I change it ASAP.  If it was dirty, I try to scoop the 
slime out from the drain holes with my pinky.   I change it then in 300 
miles, then 1000 miles intervals till it comes out clean.  Some times it 
takes a few changes.

Then again, tranny oil doesn't get dirty very quick.  When I changed the 
tranny oil for the second time in my bug (it was a 30K interval, about 5 
years), it came out clean, magnets looked good too, just a couple bits 
sticking to them.   So if it is dirty, you are really cleaning thestuff out 
of there, and that will make it drive a lot smoother and quieter and even 
last longer.
