Hi Phil,
If these heater boxes have the J-tubes sticking out of them they should be 
for both 25&36 HP engines from about 1950.  The squarish ones seem to be from 
1950 and where used up to about 1957.  The rounder ones started out in about 
1956 and went through the rest of stock 36 HP production.  It seems that VW 
had two suppliers of  heater boxes and J-tubes for a two or three year span.
