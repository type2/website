Hello all!

I got several responses to my query about books with changes to the
Bus through the years.  In brief, my list of such books is now:

Muir/Idiot manual

Bentley/'63-'67 workshop manual

VW/Parts books

VW/Progressive Refinements v1 and v2

Stefan Doliwa/VW Transporter, Die technische Dokumentation 1950-1990

Joachim Kuch/Volkswagen Model Documentation

NEATO-BK-SSVC/Pamphlet or brochure (out of print; possible new edition)

I had a summer job for four years as a phone rep, selling major appliance

>I think it would be very difficult to write a comprehensive book of
>changes for the public.   Would it be organised by year, by part
>section, by model ??

Ideally it'd be electronic so I could sort it by any of those criteria.
:)  The Doliwa book has this problem.  It is mainly broken down by
year - calendar year, not model year, so some of the changes on my
early '64 (15 Nov '63) are listed under '63.  Then each year is broken
down into sections: engine/clutch/heating, front axle, rear axle,
brakes/wheels/tires, body, electrical, and "general modifications"
(things like production starting at Hannover or a new model being
introduced).  Within each one of those sections, the changes are
listed by chassis number and date.  You have to do a little flipping
around to find when a particular change happened, but it does have
a lot of info in one place.
