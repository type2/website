 Hi Michael,
 Take the air cleaner off and with a small hand mirror and flashlight to peer 
down the carb throat have someone turn the ignition key on to power, but not 
start.  If the fuel soon afterwards begins to pump out of that brass pipe 
about 1/4" diameter coming out of the rear of the float reserviour at an 
upward diagonal you need to:
  1.  Adjust to about 3.5 Lbs. per sq. inch. (0.25 Kg/cm sq.) maximum or 
replace your fuel pressure regulator.
  2.  Replace the reserviour float.
  3.  Replace the float shut off valve.
  4.  install the gasket between the float shut off valve and the top of the 
carb.
