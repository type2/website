To replace the tires...  understand that the lift on my hydraulic jack
turned out to be woefully short :-( I *gotta* buy a higher lift jack! I
wound up jacking on the steering arm in the front on the right side to get
the tire up out of the mud high enough to get the new tire on.  Then I
went to the rear and jacked under the cover of the torsion bar.  That did
not lift the rear high enough, but it lifted the front *way* up!  So I
went to the cross member under the trans, which did lift the rear.  On the
left side, I just went straight for the trans crossmember at the rear and
for the rear torsion bar cover for the front.  the frame on this bus must
be *stiff*!!!
