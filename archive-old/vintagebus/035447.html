The problem with using a stock pre-1969 swing axle bug tranny is it has a .89
fourth gear.  The bus and K. Ghia used a .82 fourth.  Using the bug tranny .89
in a reduction gear bus will result in a loss of 5 MPH and you thought that
stock trannyed buses were slow to begin with.    Eric & Barb
