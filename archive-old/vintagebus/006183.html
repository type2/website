Eric-
Remove the starter and look at the flywheel teeth
and also the bushing in the trans for the starter.
Check for a loose flywheel with a screwdriver .
If the noise occurs only while the starter is engaged,
the problem will be there. check the teeth on the starter.
A bent crankshaft is unlikely if the bad sound is gone
with the engine running.Try using a 12 volt starter.
