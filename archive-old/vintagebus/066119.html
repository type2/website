Subject: Front Shift Rod Coupling: Why cut/weld rather than tap?

Hi,

I have to get my front shift rod out of the way so I can take care of 
the center pin. Like everyone else, the coupler set pin that holds the 
coupling in place is broken off and rusted in place.

and I think that was E&B's solution (from the archives). Why isn't

welding would introduce possible fatigue-related failure later on.

I cut it with the cut intersecting the set pin/screw hole?
