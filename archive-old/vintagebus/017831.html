If the bug is 6v and the bus is 12v, and they are both
going to remain that way, then you need to change the
flywheel and check the end-play on the bus engine before 
you pop it into the bug.  If the bug has never had a 12v 
flywheel, then you'll also need to grind the bellhousing.
