For cleaning paint guns which are aluminum, soaking in carburator
cleaner is the best. It takes the paint and junk right off and is not
corrosive like acid is on the skin. Just don't expose yourself too long
or you might have problems. Case halves should be boiled from what I've
heard but carburator cleaner as a first step might be good to get the
gummy build up off. Then boil with water.
