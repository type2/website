This particular single vacuum vacuum can is a "universal" type vacuum can
that replaces two different Bosch single vac cans:

Bosch 07059, VW 043-905-271, Fits 74 Federal Bug (Dist 0231 170
034/043-905-205 or 0231 170 036/043-905-205A)

Bosch 07060, VW 021-905-271F, Fits Bus Late 76-78, 79 Federal Bus, 80-83
Federal Vanagon (Dist 0231 170 093/022-905-205S).  I use one of these on my
68 Bug - works great!

$17 price not bad, considering the Bosch units go for average of $35 for
this style.

It will not fit and work properly on the 205T distributors (69-70) (I've
tried)

the 205T vac can (Bosch 07 024/113-905-271B) goes for an average of $38
these days.
