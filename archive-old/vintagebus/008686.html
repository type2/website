Did anyone stop to realize that all crows feet rim have a manufacturer stamp
of "Lemmerz" on them? (its small but visible on a nice clean rim) - never
seen Lemmerz on a non crows rim. Johann Lemmerz being the first man "to
invent a removable multi-piece (welded)  rim, that attaches with 5 bolts -
in 1946."  He opened the factory in germany right after the war. "Hayes -
Lemmerz" is now the largest rim manufacturer in the world.

a better stronger rim, that would flex less due to the crows foot pattern.

his rims in 59, so maybe that didn't gel too well with VW. Do barndoor rims
have Lemmerz stamped on them?
