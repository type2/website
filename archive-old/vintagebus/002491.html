My first suspicion is that the crankcase is not breathing properly. Is the
vent from the top of the oil-filler clear and connected to the air-cleaner?
Is the road tube (the one going down alongside the oil-filler) clear? If
these are blocked, you will force oil out past the slinger on the crank
pulley. Check the easy stuff first.
