> Is there a way to increase the pressure of the wiper on the window, this

Hi Sam,
Other than replacing your arms with NOS units or at least figuring out how to 
replace the springs inside the base, you can bend/bow the arms so as to 
create more pressure.  The other thing to MAKE SURE OF is that your wipers are set 
in such a way as to evenly flop as the wiper arm switches direction.  This 
often requires a slight twisting of the arm to provide this.  Otherwise the blade 
will pogo in one direction as it swipes, making any attempt at wiping the 
water off impossible.
