As you've probably heard, VW Mexico will stop manufacturing "old" 
beetles in a few weeks.

VW Mexico stopped manufacturing type2 busses in 2001. The last few 
years of production were actually bay window water cooled busses (see 
the radiator on http://www.paulDotTrip_dot_com/vw_bus/combi_mexico.jpg) 
but they were pretty much the same "loaf of bread" we all love.

While in Mexico I came across the ad that VW was going to run when 
the "Combi" (as they call it) was retired in 2001.

http://www.PaulDotTrip_dot_com/vw_bus/adioscombi.mov

Enjoy.

It is a 2.5MB Quicktime movie.  If you don't have Quicktime, you can 
download it for free from http://www.apple_dot_com/quicktime/download/
