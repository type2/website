Hey y'all!
My new 62 bus is finally sitting in my driveway after 3-4 months of
saving up the dough for it.  I've already written about it a few times

I did find some cool things in it though.  It had the original owners
manual, and about an 8"x10" picture of a cutaway view of a bus.  very
cool.  It also had the service booklet with only 2 pages left.  another
little booklet I found was a "just a few tips" booklet.  Its a yellow
fold out booklet that folds out to be about 2 feet long...its bus
specific and its indexed.  hard to explain.  I've heard about them for
beetles...but was talking to a few other people last night and they say
they've never heard of a bus one.  I snapped a picture of it with my
horrible camera and posted it at
http://www.luckyboys_dot_com/isaiah/booklet.htm

do things with the bus.  super cool item with neat little drawings.  if
