Some of the new master cylinders come with a shallower
depression in the end of the piston.  You MAY have to
pull your push rod out and cut off a little of the
end, and then round it back off.  To see how much too
long the rod is, I usually remove the master cylinder
mounting bolts, and the cylinder will move out the
amount of needed adjustment.
