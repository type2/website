Howdy, Volks,

This is a good conversion, but being the anal retentive type, I hate to
see the original brake pedal push rod cut. Go to the hardware store and
buy a bolt... M8-1.25, if I remember, about 2" or 2.5" long... and make
the cut down version of the pushrod out of this. Round the end by
chucking the bolt securely in your drill press and gently working the
cut end with a flat file. What? You don't have a drill press? Improvise,
but be safe. Make the end round & smooth. Touch it up with emery cloth
when your done with the file.
