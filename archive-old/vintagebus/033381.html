To register a car classic in WA state, you need YOM style plates and  also 
YOM TAGs.   (unless you buy the cheesy 'classic vehicle' plates)

currently registered to buy classic reg, and it is not supposed to be a daily 
driver.  I haven't paid reg on this bus in years. I have heard of someone 
getting pulled over and being told their tags are 40 years out of date by a 
cop who did not know of this old plate law.
