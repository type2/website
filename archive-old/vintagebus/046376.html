Are the brake hoses replaced?   When they get old they collapse in side and it
brakes unevenly.   Try bleeding the brakes- if the fluid does not come out
well at one wheel, there is likely a clog in the brake hose.     Old brakes
hoses go bad.   I had one burst once (total loss of all hydraulic brakes).
Are the ebrakes working right?

Check the wheel cylinders- if any are leaking rebuild or replace them- and all
on that axle.
