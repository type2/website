If you want soft ride get an old Cadilac.  Of course drivng that water bed

GR-2s by KYB are probably the best all around shocks fo the bus today.  They

heavily loaded over rough pavement.   We use the gas adjust shocks KYB makes 
in our 1963 SC and they are stiffer than thier GR-2s, but because the truck 
gets loaded often it is a blessing!  Eric&Barb
