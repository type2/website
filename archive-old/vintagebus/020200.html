In California, ya gotta have windshields, although it doesn't say they have to be closed!
I do know someone who got pulled over for it though!

http://www.dmv_dot_ca_dot_gov/pubs/vctop/d12/vc26700.htm

26700.  (a) Except as provided in subdivision (b), a passenger vehicle, other than a
motorcycle, and every bus, motortruck or truck tractor, and every firetruck, fire engine or
other fire apparatus, whether publicly or privately owned, shall be equipped with an
adequate windshield. 

(b) Subdivision (a) does not apply to any vehicle issued identification plates pursuant to
Section 5004 which was not required to be equipped with a windshield at the time it was
first sold or registered under the laws of this state, another state, or foreign jurisdiction.
