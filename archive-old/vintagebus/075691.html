Actually, there are (or were, in your case) four or five clips arranged in
a circle, which grip the hole in the bottom of the rubber thing. You could
check out a photo online somewhere, or maybe in the Bentley shop book, and

run 200 miles on the bumper til I could get to where I needed to be to do
the repair. I always keep the bumpers maintained in case it happens again.
I haven't a clue what it would have been like if the rubber wasn't there.
