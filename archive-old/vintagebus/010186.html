We ancient ones with long histories of involvement with funny little furrin
cars agree with Mr. Gensler's "TRUTH".

and usually involuntary, exceptions.]

where speed is impaired in spite of WOT and enthusiastic gear changing.

Encounters with massive immovable objects may impede one's rate of travel
despite WOT and manic manipulation of the gear selection apparatus.
