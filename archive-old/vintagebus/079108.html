The rivet for the latch should be a 3mm round head, solid aluminum
rivet.  Length should be around 6-8mm long with a 6mm round head.  I
cannot find anyone to source these but I have found some others that may
be close to original but I have yet to break down and order them to see.
I think the ones from WW have a flat head and are more correct to hold
the latch catch plate to the frame.
