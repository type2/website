In my motorcycle and VW experience I've rubbed elbows with a number of
celebs.  Some of these experiences were great, others were not.  The
difference seemed to be whether the celeb was truly interested in the
subject or was simply there because it was trendy.

Steve McQueen, at the height of his fame was actively competing in
motorcycle races.  He didn't bring his entourage and generally entered
under an assumed name ("Harvey Mushman," for one).  When he was at races
he was one of the racers, not a celebrity, and expected to be treated as
such.  He was so low profile that many of the other racers didn't even
know that they guy in the next pit was a Big Star (tm)

I guess that is the standard by which I measure celebrity behavior at VW
and other events.  If they're there to do the same thing that I am doing
then I respect their privacy and move on to more important things.
