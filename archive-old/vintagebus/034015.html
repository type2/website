I recently did the brakes in my 58 singlecab.....while the wheel cylinders 
are a little bit more $$ than a 66, I believe the 66-67 master cylinder is 
big $$$$.

My 58 prices......
German master cylinder 88$
Front wheel cylinders 28$ each times 4
Rear wheel cylinders, german,  26$ each times 2.
Brake hoses, german, 12$ each times four.
Brake pads, USA, 40$ four wheels.
Turning brake drums, 7$ each times four.

So theres about 300$ in parts alone.
Took me a day start to finish.
