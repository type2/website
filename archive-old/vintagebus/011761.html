THE WEEKLY GUIDE TO TYPE2 / VINTAGEBUS RESOURCES

Ok, folks, here's some sites that will be invaluable to the Type 2
newbie and oldtimer.  It was created to help reduce traffic on the
lists by providing a quicker way to get that crucial piece of
information that you need to fix your bus.  Always try to check these
sites before posting to the list.

For the folks who are "frameless" these URLs will allow you to bypass
the framed type2 homepage.  I don’t guarantee results, but they might
help until the frameless homepage is in place.!

Don’t fret if some of these sites don’t work yet.  Lotsa dust is
flying at the website!

TYPE2 LIST SITES:

The Volkswagen Bus Mailing List.  
This is the web site of the Type2 and Vintagebus lists.  If you go to
this site, all the sites below can be easily selected.
http://www.type2_dot_com

The Type 2 list homepage
http://www.type2_dot_com/type2.html

The Vintagebus list homepage
http://www.type2_dot_com/vintagebus.html

The Type2 Archive.  
This lets you search everything that has been posted to Type2 and
Vintagebus since the Jurassic (almost) 
http://www.i405_dot_com/vwarchive/
Note that it takes some time (months) for posts to find their way to
the Archive. To find newer posts, go to the type2 or vintagebus
homepages and select "read."

The Type2 Library.  
This has links to hundreds of web pages dedicated to our favorite
vehicle.  It is nicely indexed, so you can go straight to the topic
you want to investigate.
http://ourworld_dot_compuserve_dot_com/homepages/JJRodgers1/library/library.htm

Type2_dot_com website.  If no posts get delivered there, it’s a good bet

the great job they are doing.  Remember, they don’t get paid for their
