Subject: RE: [VB] Thanks to the list for the "Hoover bit" lead

If that piece or it's "arm" is missing then there will be a gap ~ 3/8" x
2-1/2" just before the cooler at the bottom which will let some cooling air
escape back into the engine compartment.  I wonder if this piece shouldn't
have some foam seal on it also to stop air escaping around it.  I have seen
some that had the remnants of foam on them.  The "Hoover bit" used to be
hard to find but with the current crop of beetles hitting the junkyards you
should be able to locate one along with the shutters that were also hard to
find at one time.  Look for fuel injected beetles as I think that they were
relatively untouched until after people got wise to the need for the Hoover
bit and shutters.
