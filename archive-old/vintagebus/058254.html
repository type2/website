We never had much problem with removing the oil pump.  Though in the 
non-pre-1972 buses VWs you have to work around the rear apron!  You will need 
to remove the muffler and so require a muffler gasket kit for later 
reinstallation.  Most of all you will need a oil pump puller tool so you do 
not ruin the engine case prying on it and scarring any sealing surfaces.

three times each to work them loose of the corrosion sticking the threads of 
the plugs in place.  Then they should turn with a wide slot tip screwdriver 
easily.
