>All I have is an old phone cord, could I use that instead of speaker wire?

If it's straight, yeah.  If it's curly it will work OK for the tail light
but not for the headlight.  The long run of curly wire will put a spin on
the electrons, so when they get to the headlight, they'll spin around in
there and come out the edges instead of coming straight out the front.  It
makes the light pattern of the headlight be real dispersed to the sides
instead of mostly straight ahead.

>How do you feel about scotch tape?

of glass out.  Since you no longer have a bottle, you have to then drink
the rest of the Scotch before it oxidizes in the air.
