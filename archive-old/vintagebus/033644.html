Take the wheels and tires off and roll it in on it's drums. That's how I
work on the upper body sections of buses (when I ever get around to it) and
works slick. You can even push a bus sideways on the drum edges if you have
smooth concrete without any cracks in it. No it doesn't wear the edges of
the drum down, at least not for me.
