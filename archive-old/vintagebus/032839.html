check the rear shifter linkage.  It is just in front of the tranny.  Lie 
under it with a flashlight while you have a friend shift the gears.   The 
linkage piece should not flex or twist, it should hole the shift rod firmly 
to the hockey stick.  If it is bad, replace it.   you can also check to see 
if the 8mm square headed screws are snug.  and they should have wire through 
the holes, tosted around the rod to keep them from backing out- make sure the 
wire is arranged so the bolts don't back out.
