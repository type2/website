What's your budget?  If you have the cash, have it professionally blasted
and primed, then lay on a filling primer, do you bodywork, and you're ready
to go.  A local with a '55 deluxe had his professionally blasted and shot
with PPG DP40, along with misc parts, for $1200.  That's what I'm going to
do with my '54.

Or, you can just sand and sand and sand.  Chemicals work fast, but it will
take a lot of time and luck to get them all neutralized.  I have avery sad
story from a guy on the Porsche list who had an early speedster dipped - it
literally ruined the car.
