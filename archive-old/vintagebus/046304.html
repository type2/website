Hi Steve,
The pressure relief valve spring is supposed to be 13/16" and goes in the 
forward hole.  The pressure control spring for dual relief cases is supposed 
to be 1-3/'4 long instead of the earlier single relief valve of 1-11/16" 
long.  Do not use sealant on these plugs!  Eric&Barb
