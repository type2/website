vehicle in neutral while moving, particularly and specifically on
downgrades (I guess they figure no one'll be wanting to do that while
going uphill).  You can use the clutch, though.  I believe this is in
response to runaway trucks whose brakes have overheated (i.e., the
scenario I was in above, except my bus didn't *quite* run away).
