Rex,

The rating you need to look at on your compessor is not the HP, but the
CFPM, or cubic feet per minute.

Most pressurized blasters with a small blast tip will run around 12-14 CFPM
at 100 PSI.  If you are running a HVLP spray gun, you will need more CFPM.
Even though the HVLP runs at a low PSI, it takes a larger volume of air.

Most pumps that run high CFPM are 2 stage with a fairly high HP motor (5-7
HP).
