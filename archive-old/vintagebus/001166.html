With all the fuses pulled the dash lights still work and the starter trigger
wire will still have power if you turn the key.If you look at the wiring
charts on Thom's page you will see that the ignition switch and light switch
are NOT connected to a fuse,they connect to the hot side of the fuse
block,so there may well be some parasitic draw from these two switches as
well as the clock if you have a delux.Also the front park lights are unfused
off the switch so look close and long before you start hacking a good
harness.The VW wiring is a piece of cake to work on compared to lets say a
late model F*RD or a vehicle with LUCAS electrics.LOOK at Thom's wiring
charts they work and you can run down to Kinko's with it on a disk have it
printed,laminated in the size of your choice.
