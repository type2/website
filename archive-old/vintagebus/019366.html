Hi everyone,

Here is the latest update on the Michigan Vintage Volkswagen Club (Chapter
of the national VVWCA) "Michigan Vintage Volkswagen Festival" in Auburn
Hills in May.  This is always a great Midwest VW weekend event, very laid
back and social, and because of that, many of us travel great lengths in
our VW's to attend.  This event is held at Volkswagen of America's
corporate headquarters in Auburn Hills, a northwestern suburb of Detroit. 
There are several changes to make note of.  Mark your calendars for a fun
road trip!

Bill Bowman

>Michigan Vintage Volkswagen Festival May, 20th 2001
>
>This 14th annual Festival will feature a special display of the German
automobile body builder Karmann's 100th anniversary in the automobile
business.  This years show will feature any and all Volkswagens through
model year 1979 and New Beetles.  Also featured will be all makes and
models of automobiles with Karmann produced bodies (Volkswagen
Convertibles, Porsche 356 and 914, etc,etc). A special display of car
Karmann USA Inc. is being planned.  The ever popular historical
"pass-in-review" is in the plans.  The VWoA headquarters Volkswagen Museum
collection will be open for your viewing pleasure also.
