Bus used to be mine. I bought it out of Payson, AZ. Had OG paint, zero rust and
only a slight grazing in the typical rear corner. I had it resprayed with
single stage Glasurit (matched property) primer-paint system. It was about the
straightest, most original buses that I had ever owned.

I know that Beto has continued to improve on the great mechanical and cosmetic
condition it was at when I sold it to him.

and steering was like new... but it was original not hashed together with
aftermarket parts. Was and probably remains pleasantly as tight as factory~ or
thereabouts. I put all new rubber in it and paneled the walls with the good
Baltic Birch. Beto completed the interior in fine fashion/craftsmanship.
