Rex,

One sure way to tell if you have a original SO 42 Westy is to look at the
M-plate for a "B" code (A,B or C denoted the package) and also to look on
the over head vent plaque (does a 63 have one???).  I've never seen a "42"
in a small hatch bus (pre '64).  That and the fact that you said the brass
Westfalia factory ( on the Z-bed) tag says '66 says to me that this is
indeed a retro fit into an earlier bus.

Pull up the flooring behind the passenger seat (where the icebox would have
been) and look for a 3" hole punched (not cut) in the cargo floor.  All
SO-42 busses had this hole, as it was for the icebox drain petcock and hose.

How does the passenger side closet fit in the rear with the small hatch?
Good find though.
