I keep hearing this one and can't help but cringe each time!

PVC (or more likely ABS in the size involved) isn't made to take the
temperatures that a healthy VW heating system can produce.  It probably
won't melt, but it'll outgas something you don't want to breathe.

There are metal alternatives that will be a lot closer to stock and which
will work much better.  Check out your local home center.

Insulating the pipe is a very good idea.  Leave the insulation as fluffy
as possible, since it's the _air_ that does the insulating, not the glass.
