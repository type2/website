   Actually this conversion can be done and maintain a stock appearance. My
61 single cab has a 4.5L Rover aluminum V8/auto trans nestled between the
frame rails coupled to an 8" Ford rear end. None of the engine components
stick out of, or above the bed and the tires tuck nicely inside the uncut
fender wells. You can't, at a glance tell it from a stocker, untill you
turn the key.
