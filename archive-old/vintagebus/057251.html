Subject: Re: [VB] REV LIMITER?

It's not a rev limiter - it's a *governor* - it works by means of a restrictor
plate that adjusts according to the flow down the manifold.  It's adjusted
via a spring under the aluminum cover - tighter == higher revs; looser == lower revs.
There isn't a direct link between revs and the restrictor, though.
