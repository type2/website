Good question!  VW did issue the Thing without heater boxes, but it was never

RPMs you cruise at and thing about your engine like fan width, lower pulley 
ratio, and cooler type.

<< I have no heater boxes, and my fan outlets are blocked off, rear tin holes

Lower tin helps force the hot engine cooling air out and behind into the air 
passing over and around the VW.  Otherwise it will just heat up the under 
sections of the VW body which will not help alot to keep you and your VW 
engine cool.
