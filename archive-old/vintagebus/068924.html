> I'm in the process of rebuilding my engine and I'm wondering if I should go to the extra expense and

If your generator is working well, stick with it.  If it's causing
problems, switch to an alternator.  If your bus has a 38A generator, you
will need a bug fan hub when you make the conversion, and these are _not_
provided in the conversion kits.
