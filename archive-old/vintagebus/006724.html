my new 1776 single port

oil temp pickups, 1 at bypass, 1 at oil pressure switch point and if I feel
like it I will install the type 3 oil cooler with a pick-up there also.
Should I add a dipstick also?? to get readings from different areas of
engine.

oil pressue switchs,  1 at oil pressure switch point and another at the type
3 oil cooler, this should show me pressure in and out of cooler.

cylinder head temp gauge, at number 3 cylinder and if I feel rich I'll
install another at number 1, good for cross reference.

air mixture sender (CB Performance) installed in exhust before muffler.

Tach, so I'll know my RPM's to tell what temps and air fuel mixtures are at
what RPM's

Vac gauge, so I can see how engine vac affects temps under different loads.
