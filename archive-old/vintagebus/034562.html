Hi Jamie,
The 1955-63 rear cylinders can be use on either side on the rear only.  While
the front cylinders of the same years are left and right side only.  Then with
the 1963 only one ton M 215 suspension and the 1964-67 rear cylinders that are
either side also.  Once again the front cylinders for these years and types
are left and right only.  None of the 1955-63 brake cylinders can be used on a
1963 only one ton suspension or the 1964-67 buses and visa versa.    Eric &
