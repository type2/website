>What do you guys use on the bow ends and clamps to protect your roof...

I used something I've been getting A LOT of use out of lately....an 
old bicycle tube! I cut sections out and put them both on the leg of 
the rack and the bracket right at the bottom where they clamp. It 
works real good. Real easy. Cut it. Stretch it to put in place. Done.

I've used that old inner tube for fixing lots of loose parts, to make 
gaskets, all kinds of stuff. You can get tons of 'em from your local 
bike shop for free. They don't work for them when there holes in 'em!
