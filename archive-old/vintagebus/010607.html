Definitely sounds like way too much fuel!  Probably doing wonders washing out 
the piston rings.  Check/replace your carb reseviour shut off valve and do 
the same to the float in there.  The latter could have a hole in it causing 
it to sink or it could just be too heavy.  Also recheck the fuel pump push 
rod for proper hieght or if using an electric pump only run it with a fuel 
pressure regulator ste to about 2-3 Lbs. per sq. inch.  Also check you carb 
jet sizes!
