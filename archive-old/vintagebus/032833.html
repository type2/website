A good steering gear box should be adjusted with the wheels pointing forward,
jack it up off the ground.  Tighten the adjustment sscrew in till it drags a
bit in the center, then back it off just a hair so it just barely doesn't
drag.   From what I read in the WV workshop manual (which is now available
from Bently) it says the tightest part should be in the middle.   Who rebuildt
it?   Give them feedback.   Hope this helps.   That VW workshop manual is
great for specifics on the splitty.   Yes, engine and brake type stuff is
covered by many manuals, but body and steering stuff is done so much better in
this book.  Bently and VW did a great service by reprinting it for public
availability.  Take advantage of the information.
