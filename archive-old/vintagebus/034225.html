A bug sender(113 919 049a) will bolt into you tank and will work as long as
you still have the voltage stabilizer (113 957 099a) on the gauge(this keeps
the needle from bouncing around due to float movement), some of the new bus
senders are float style (same as bug) but I haven't had a chance to check
the ohms on them yet, the old style bus senders (271 919 051b) were a tube
with the float inside.

I have both senders in stock, bug ones I have are manufactured in Mexico and
Germany,the bus ones are still the original style manufactured by Siemans,
Mexico.
