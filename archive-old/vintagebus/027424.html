This is my method of setting distributor timing, regardless whether it
is a vac dizzy or 009...

I make a mark at 30 degrees to the right of TDC on the pulley. With the
timing light set up, I rev the engine till the advance won't go no more.
Then I adjust the timing so that it maxes out just shy of my mark.

I use 30 degrees because I set timing on bugs on the other side of the
mark, so it is convenient for both bugs and buses.
