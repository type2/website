two ways to find out-

look above your head- if it has a headlkiner it is a microbus, if it has
paneling above the drivers head it is a combis.  Neither left there\?   look
at the sides of the overhead air vent- little clip claws- these are for the
headliner its a microbus- a horizontal channel in threre- it's for the panel
to slide into- a kombi

also- look atthe side of the fresh air vent for the aluminium tag- it will say
type 2X  somehwere on it.

a type 21 is a panel
type 22 a micro
type 23 a kombi
types 24/25 are deluxe micros
type 26 is a truck
type 27 is an ambulance.
type 28 standard micro with sliding sunroof


go nuts!

then you can explore the M code tag for more info- such as options and
origianl colours
.........
also- what I forgot to mention before.  you are talking commercial vehicles vs
passenger vehicle here- commercial are types 21, 23, 26, 27- and also have all
the heat going to the front- while passenger type 2s have heat in front and
coming out under the rear seat-
