I wanted to plug the VDO holder for mounting gauges in the parcel tray.  On
my '63, the opening in the parcel tray is just the right size to fit one of
these in.  No holes to trill or anything, just the pressure from the rubber
between the parcel tray top/bottom holding it in.  Perfect fit.  Just the
right amount of pressure without hurting tray.
