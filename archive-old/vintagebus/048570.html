The original tin is the best, but you can make the aftermarket tin hold as
tightly by taking a rat tail file and rounding out the areas that are
supposed to "snap" around the studs.

Most of the aftermarket tin was not rounded out enough so after a short
time, the tin would come loose and rattle.

With the original tin getting harder to get, this is a good replacement.
And BTW, you are correct, the type 3 tin will cool MUCH better, it wraps
the cylinder much further.
