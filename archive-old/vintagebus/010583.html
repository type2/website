 I get tired of the anti-hippie bus sentiment I see here a lot. I have been to
many Dead, Phish, and other touring-type shows. When I got my first bus, I
didn't know a thing about it. Yes, I even broke down in New Mexico, and through
NEATO got hooked up with a great person who helped us tremendously with the
bus. But, we also went out of our way to be good guests, and did cook dinner
(not beans), and were very grateful. Now I have built engines, and even busses,
from the ground up.  Sure people do dumb things to their busses if they don't
know better, but that's not limited to hippies, or any other group of people. I
have helped a lot of hippies with bus problems at shows. I have also helped any
other person I've seen in automotive distress when I could help, be they young,
old, rich, poor, driving a VW or even (gasp) some other brand of car.
Let's not be so selective in the people we help. Or, speaking as a hippie,
"Share the Love, man"
