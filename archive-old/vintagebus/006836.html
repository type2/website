Same nose damage as on my old 61 single, I drilled a hole in the headlight
bucket, put a stout hook in it, hooked up the chain to my hoist and let the
momentum of the bus rolling backwards slowly pull it out, then i welded
(tacked) a piece of 1/8 by 1 inch strap across the crease and repeated the
operation. After that the nose was out enough to use the nail gun to pull it
out the rest of the way. Total time start to finished nose 4 1/2 hours
