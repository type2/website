Subject: RE: [VB] valve guide question

Besides grinding the step away underneath the exhaust port for better blend
and flow I install 58mm exhaust guides to further eliminate flow restriction.
I wouldn't do this for a bus or truck motor though, because the excess heat
buildup for the power requirements would shorten valve life. Dune buggies,
bugs, and of course my trike are likely candidates for short exhaust valve
guides.
