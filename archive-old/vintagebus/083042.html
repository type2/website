his yard - he says its closer to the door so he can see it ... right!

steering wheel looks like a 7 out of 10 - engine is out and sitting behind 
it - oil bath air cleaner is still attached - rims on the rear - I bought em

sure - when did vw go to 14" rims?

It has a rough 80 to 90% complete Devon interior for a bench seat bus - this

Camperette - or Devon Caravette - or something like that - I'll also try and 
get what I can re: switches and gas gage etc... I bought the interior - it

does have some pretty nice cabinets that could go in my 60 SO-44 pop-top 
westfakia. Anyone know of any pics on the web of early 60's Devon's?

he is going to put it in the iSell for $2,500.00 - I might offer him

amazing - all the interior needs is a cleaning and seat covers.  It has a 
gas heater and radio.  Anyone know of any VW Beetle Resto shops in Ontario? 
I'd love a 65.

He has a pretty cool Notch back - looks to be beyond hope.
