The best thing would be a barndoor rear end. The reduction gearboxes bolt on
differently.   They have 1.40 gears in them.  The later small nut (1.39 gears)
have the smaller brakes and are closer to the b/d set up than the big nut
(1.26 geared) reduction boxes.

Another question is boltonability.   I have seen a later rear end ina
barndoor, but am not sure how it was done.   And it had no rear shocks.   So,
start looking for a pair of barndoor reduction gear boxes is my advice.
