well, the two i have:

 sp=(rpm*60)*(dia in miles)
    --------------------------    I .think. this one is sourced from George Lyle.
     (gear ratio*final drive)

 mph = (rpm * cir in ft) / (gear * final * 88)   This one comes from Joel
    						via v_at_l, from Car and Driver.

variations on these:

 R=S/(60*D/F)  where R=rpm S=speed D=dia(miles) F=final*gear 
 rpm = ((88*g*f)(mph))/cir.in.ft
