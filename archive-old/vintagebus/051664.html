yes, this is because there is a lack of wiring diagrams for 65.   the 65 one 
year only wiper motor is har dto find the non existant (AFAIK) wiring diagram 
for.  because it is not like 64, nor like 66.  

If you don't get an answer, Ill go check in my 65 for you.

but think of it this way.  power goes to wiper motor.   .  one out to the 
motor is always on, this goes to the parking circuit - so when you turn off 
the motor, it keeps going till it parks the wipers not right in front of your 
face.  That probably is the one with the two prongs.  or like you say the 
power to the interior light- that sounds right. The hot wire is ususlly 
black, i think wiper hot may be black/purple.  black means hot with power on 
(circuit 15)  but once it goes into the wiper circuit I think it gets another 
number.   same colour as in the colour wiring diagram for other years- 
mostly.  
so another outlet goes on when you pull the knob- to turn the wipers on.   
wiper motor grounds directly to body of bus, not through wiper switch.

you can test the switch with some wires, clips, a battery, and a test light, 
see which terminal does which.  make a map.

clean the wires a bit with nasty solvent to get the paint off.  carb or brake 
cleaner often works.
