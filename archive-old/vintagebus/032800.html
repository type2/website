Steve, Split List, 

It all depends what type of abrasive you are going to use.  Since you are 
doing this in the grass, and not recycling your abrasive, I would recommend 
DuPont StarBlast.  It is cheap and not too abrasive.  I would stay away from 
black beauty. 

Unless the rust is only very very slight, a sanding disk will not work at 
removing it since rust leaves pits in the metal and a sanding disk can not 
get in the pits.  A wire cup brush/wheel will do better than a sanding disk.  
Sandblasting is the way.
