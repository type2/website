I have factory installed safris on my `60 westy...also
have factory installed radidio!...my antenna is very
close to the stock position on the left hand side of
the drivers windscreen...I bet they just drilled the
holes a tad to the left to mount the
antenna.....thought I must add my antenna does rub the
safari ever so slightly when I initially open the
safari...you can see on the original paint where this

I installed factory safaris in my (recently sold!) `61
westy...had a stock radio and antenna...had to buy one

outta the way just enough to let it by.....not stock
but works well.
