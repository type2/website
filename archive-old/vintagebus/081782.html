Subject: Re: [VB] tow hitch

I have seen a variety of tow hitches made for splitty buses, and have  indeed 
used one on my 61 to tow various things, including a bug 500  miles.  It 
clamped to the rear torsion tube, and had up/down support  at the rear bumper with 
a curved piece the same shape as the curve of the rear  bumper.  Makes it 
annoying to pull the motor. I don't know if anyone  is currently making them.   I 
saw a westfalia tow hitch sell for $700  recently, but I think that was one 
to fit with a stamped bumper.  You might  peruse swaps and the samba 
classifieds.
