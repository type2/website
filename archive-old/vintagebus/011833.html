I use a regular doghouse cooler. My 63 runs at 200 to 210 in 90 to 100 
degree weather at 65 MPH, fully loaded and has a pop top. It seems to me 
if your bus is running hot then there is another problem. Is your engine 
bigger then a 1600? Mine is a 1600 single port. 
I would check for seals around the cooler, and I would check the the 
cooler is in good shape. I have had bad coolers in the past that just 
didn't work. Many years of gummy oil and engine bits can foul it up no 
matter how well cleaned out.
