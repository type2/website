I seriously doubt you will find 8 ply side walls..... you would probably break a tire machine 
trying to install them:)

Side walls come a one ply or two ply or reinforced.  It is the reinforced flavor you want.

The correct [VW recommended] size tire for your bus is 185R14. You will notice there is no 
middle number. The middle number is the aspect ratio. The tread is shown as being x number 
of millimeters wide. The aspect ratio is shown expressed in a %. When no aspect number is
given, it is 82.

If your tire 'expert' says there is no such thing as a 185R14 and you meant to say 185/75R14,
run away.

The 195 tire is not actually taller. It has a /75 in it. So it would be 195/75R14.

If you go here:  http://www.miata_dot_net/garage/tirecalc.html
And put in 185 80 [no 82 choice] 14 on the left and 195 75 14 on the right, you will see that the
195/75 is .5% faster. The 195 tire is actually .2" smaller in diameter.
