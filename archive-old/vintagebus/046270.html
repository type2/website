Hi Fig,
The main red wire from the starter to the fuse box does not go through a fuse 
on it's way to the head light switch.  It sounds like the brass clips that 
are pressed into the fuse box have corroded and/or loosened in the fuse box.  
You either need to replace that fuse box or better yet solder all the clips 
to each other preventing a bad connection that cause the build up of heat.
