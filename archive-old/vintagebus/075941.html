I might have said this before, but do consider getting a box of latex gloves. 
  It is really nice to just peel off the gloves and not have oil/scum on my 
hands.   For the greasey jobs like a suspension lube I use the nitrile (blue) 
gloves, as the latex gets soft and falls apart when all covered with grease.  
Also nitile ones are good against solvents, like when cleaning a carb.   I 
found nitrile gloves for $5 a box of 100 at Harbor Freight, and they are fine for 
people with latex allergies.
