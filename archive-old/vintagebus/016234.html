I found KYBs are extremely stiff and had a terrible
ride compared to stock...especially for early 3-4ton
buses.  Go with nice stock oil filled Boge or
Sachs...brazil stuff is ok but german is better.

ability...so don't buy heavy duty or gas filled shocks
just haul stuff...it is not needed.
