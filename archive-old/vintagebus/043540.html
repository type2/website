Hello all!

After finding George Lyle's list of "MX codes" (M-codes that VW forgot)
in the archive, I was inspired to start a list of "VWX" numbers...special
tools that VW forgot to mention.  The list has grown to 18 tools that
you just won't find in the VW literature, not even the "Tools for Local
Manufacture" book.

The current list, with contributors, is below.  Help fill in the gaps
in this list!  Note: If it's not Friday when inspiration strikes you,
private mail would probably be better.  I'll keep the list compiled
and post it a few more times until it seems "done."  Keep those cards
and letters comin', now!

Matt Roberds
mattroberds_at_home_dot_com

MR VWX-001 - BFH (Big F(ine) Hammer)
MR VWX-002 - 5' cheater pipe (rear axle nuts, flywheel)
MR VWX-003 - Small wire brush for all electrical connections
MR VWX-004 - Magnet-on-a-stick (fishes spark plugs and tin screws out of
               shrouding)
MR VWX-005 - Mirror-on-a-stick
MR VWX-010 - German/English phrase book (for cussing at your Bus in its
               mother tounge)
MR VWX-012 - Stocking cap
MR VWX-014 - Socket set used as drifts to install bearings and seals
MR VWX-015 - J-bolt with threaded end filed square in lieu of church key
               (my Bus came with this)
MR VWX-018 - Rag for front window defrost assist
MR VWX-024 - 24" monkey wrench (alternative to VWX-002) (inspired by Walt)
GL VWX-050 - Blue Wrench (gas torch)
GL VWX-051 - Friend with good hearing (for setting timing without lights or
               instruments)
GL VWX-052 - Rope, used for securing stuff to roof rack, pulling bus out of
               sand, etc.
GL VWX-053 - Shovel
GL VWX-054 - Friend with small hands (for retrieving objects that fall into
               the cracks)
