I got a 1 layer cover from Motorworks for the 66 Westy and found that it didnt
keep the bus dry at all.  It actually soaked up water and held it against the

my bus.  I would rather have the cover not in contact with the bus.  I would
build my own, but I just cant find any PVC angles that would help me to peak
the roof properly.  It may be worth it, you decide.
