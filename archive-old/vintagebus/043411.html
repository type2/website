W 0.77 is the "distance-speedometer radio" according to Bentley
p. E-14-1.  0.77 is the right ratio for a 3/4 ton (15" tire) MPH
speedo (for a 1-ton 14" tire MPH speedo it's 0.8).  So I would
think that the 2,61 is indeed a Feb '61 date code.
