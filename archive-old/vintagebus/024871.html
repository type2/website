I put clips on the wheels on a Thing.  The secret is to use Steel pop rivets
and not aluminum ones.  You may need a larger pop rivet tool than are often
used on aluminum ones, but it works.  Get 3/8 inch rivets.  I used a Dremel
tool and cut off disk to make it flush after installation.

You have to bend the clips out to get them installed, but they are pretty good
steel and the ones I had bent back with no problem
