The short slats are tapered as you would expect.  Realize that the
picture I showed on the web site of cut off slats is with the ruthmann
not pushed all the way forward.  With it on correctly, there is very
little space between the ruthmann frame and the cab wall.

of wood.  The slats are riveted on.  Hey, when did we have that
