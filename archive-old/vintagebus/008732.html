Stephen:

In 1963, VW offered the M216 option. Buses with this option were equiped
with slightly bigger rear drums and longer stub axles.  The gear reduction
was a different ratio than the regular non-M216 models.

These M216 Buses normally came with a 1500cc engine.  If you find a pair of
those rear drums, grab them. They are very very rare.
