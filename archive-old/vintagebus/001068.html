thanks for including you VIN, as it was a mid year change.   The "modified
36hp" motor aka bastard 40hp was introduced at CH # 469 447 on May 19 1959.
There also changed at this point to the tunnel case tranny from the split
case tranny.  So, yours is one of the very first post 36hp buses.   In the
36hp buses it is a bit tricky installing a motor with the later heater boxes
as they tend to hang up.

I seriously doubt you have the original engine, as those bastard motor
tended
to self destruct, which is why they were only made a short time, very
embarassing for VW.   Check the engine number and look in the back of your
Idiot book and see if it is in the right area.   The original motor would
not
have a Letter before the number.
