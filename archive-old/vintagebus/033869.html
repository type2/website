ASAIK, the 1.40 reduction gears were the barndoor ones (completely differnt
set up)

from 3/55 through 63s they had 1.39s - the little nut stub axles

and 64-67 have 1.36s - the big nut rear axles
I don't think the alternate non standard gearing ie 'mountain gearing' is as
common as people think.

so my advice if you are looking for 1.26 reduction boxes, look for big nuts.
they did change some stuff inside the tranny for 66/67, but the axles
/outboard gears remained the same
