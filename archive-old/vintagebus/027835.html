Gene Cornelius wrote:

> I was going to try to get my steering wheel aligned properly, but
> couldn't figure out the best way to do it.

I just did this on my Single Cab. You need to adjust the length of the drag
link. Making it longer turns the wheel clockwise (I think). Basically knock the
drag link loose from the steering box, get the steering wheel to center, get the
wheels *straight* and adjust accordingly. Trial and error worked for me the
first time, but once you get under there you'll see how it all works.
