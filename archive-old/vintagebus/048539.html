You can't always get to the blocked passages by removing the jets alone.
If you look on the outside of the carb, you will notice "ridges" on the
carb that look like termite tunnels.  These are the passage ways for the
fuel to flow within the various carb circuits.  Many times I have had to
remove the soft plugs that the factory put in place when they drill these
passages.  Usually where two passages meet will be the most likely spot for
blockage.  I have even had to drill the passages where two come together
and the factory passages didn't meet causing a restriction.  After you
finish your work, find some 1/8" solid core solder and replace the factory
plugs.
