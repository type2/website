how long have you had the engine in?   Maybe I'm lazy, but sometimes they 
seem to 'clean themselves out'.. with frequent oil changes, good gas and easy 
driving. Could be the rings need reseating if it sat a long time.  But you say 
it's been driven every day.  You can still do a compression check.. and check 
the end play.  Then again, was it oily smoke, or water vapour?   

But as long as it's where it is,   might be best (after checking end play and 
compression) to at least do rings and rod bearings if the bottom end feels 
tight, ps and cs look okay, and heads pass drip test.     but maybe it'll need 
more... good luck!!!
