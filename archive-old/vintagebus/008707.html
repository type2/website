Be sure to try the jack out at home before hauling it along on a trip, and
be certain that the jack has sufficient capacity to raise your rig when it's
fully loaded.

It may not lift high enough to raise a Transporter, so you'll need to work
out an arrangement of spacer blocks, etc.,  to make up the difference.

A chunk of 1/2" plywood about 18" X 36" is pretty useful to have along as a
base for the jack on soft/uneven ground.  And jackstands are _mandatory_ if
you plan to work under the bus.
