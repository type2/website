Yeah.  The '66/67 has two wires coming off one terminal - that solid
blue wire and the black wire with white and green stripes.  The
black/white/green wire brings the pulsing voltage from the 9-prong
box to the turn signal switch and the blue wire sends it over to the
hazard switch to flash the light in it.  There's no reason why you
couldn't just run a replacement blue wire from 'K' on the hazard
switch to '49a' on the 9-prong box if you could figure out a way to
use the guts of the '71 switch.
