I recommend finding a colour wiring diagram and keping to the original colours
used AMAP.  The colours actually mean things- brown is always ground, red hot
all the times, black hot w/ ignition on, etc.    These colours are easy to
find, but some  are not and I use pieces of old VW wiring harness.  I've been
looking in to find souces, but it seems I'd have to buy in bulk.

Going back later when it's done with the right colours is like going back
later when the bolts have been greased- much pleasanter and easier to work on.
