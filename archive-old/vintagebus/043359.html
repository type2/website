From Bosch's "Automotive Handbook", 4th edition:

X - M12 thread
    1.25 thread pitch
    17.5mm across flats
R - resistor
4 - heat range
C - 19mm reach
S - silver electrode

17.5 mm is an odd hex size.  18 mm would probably work;
11/16 is a bit small at 17.46 mm and 23/32 is a bit large
at 18.26 mm.

I have found that when buying Bosch plugs at AutoZombie et
al, you must insist that they look them up by the Bosch
number (W7AC, XR4CS, etc.)  I was trying to match some
plugs for a BMW motorcycle and they kept insisting that
a number printed on the ceramic (682) was the Bosch plug
number, and whaddayaknow, they couldn't find it.  When
I talked them into looking up the W... number they found
it right off.
