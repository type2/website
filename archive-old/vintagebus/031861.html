TMI is the originator and manufacturer of all the original (replacement) bus
interior fabrics. The platium (shirt) is actually a Mustang fabric and TMI
found it to be the closet there was to original without having to go out and
make it. They have allready invested a fortune in doing the print and
texture fabrics and felt along with some of the bus GERU's that the platium
fabric would work fine. It is almost an exact match to the platium skirt
material when faded/aged. How do I know, both of my 66 deluxes had the
original material under some funky aftermarket covers to which we compared
the TMI fabric to. I have been told though that if you really want to be
anal about the skirt that Transporter Restorations, aka Upolstery Haus has
the correct skirt material and will also make you the correct covers if you
send your seats to them to have them done complete. They do not sell just
covers.
