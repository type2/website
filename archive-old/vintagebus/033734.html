 Here's a few things that broke on the Alaska bus on it's way to the East
coast.
1.  generator
2.  reduction box bearings
3.  wheel cylinders and hoses

 These were the 3 major break downs and they did have a spare generator and
regulater (changed that in BFE Canada)

 Due to the outragous cost of parts and/or shipping to Alaska I would carry
as many spares as possable, starter and bushing, RGB bearings, wheel
cylinders and/or kits, front rear brake hoses, front wheel bearing set,
clutch cable, t/o bearing (or clutch kit) t/o bearing arm (in trans) bowden
tube and all the other stuff you mentioned.
  It was less money to buy a new generator from me and ship it to Alaska
then what they wanted for a rebuilt there!
