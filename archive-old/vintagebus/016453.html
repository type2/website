YOu can remove them with minimal, if any, damage by reaching thru
the front of the drumto the edge of the seal with a piece of wood,
and gently encouraging the stick with a hammer.  I like the early
style seals too, but they're NLA.  To re-install, use a flat piece
of wood across the seal and tap it back in.
