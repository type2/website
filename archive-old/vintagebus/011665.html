Shimmy can also be caused by a combination of loose wheel bearings and an
out of balance wheel and tire.  Might even be as simple as a bent rim or
a tire not properly seated on the rim.  

Any of these in combination with some linkage looseness is virtually
guaranteed to give a bus the wiggles.
