According to the data I have, yours "should" have had 14" wheels from
the factory.  The last models to change over were apparently mid-model-year
1964 models, starting in late December 1963:
 
  5 Aug 1963     1 145 427   14" tubeless tires for 1-ton models (Doliwa)
 19 Dec 1963     1 222 026   14" tubless tires for 3/4-ton models (Doliwa)

From the Registry, you have a VIN of of 265 010 042 and a build date
of Aug 1964.  That number is consistent with the first part of August.
This is apparently well after the change-over to 14" wheels.

On the other hand, maybe it was different for trucks as opposed to
the fully-enclosed bodies.  Not sure why that would be so but it
might account for it.  Remember, the books and lists are not always
100% accurate.
