Twice I've been off road and gotta stuck (many, many more times and not 
stuck) - neither time was I the driver. The first was with my family after 
cutting a Christmas tree (I was 10). Dad thought a big puddle was a little 
puddle. Same thing happened back in '95. Got to see my friend's brand new 
Cherokee fill up with water...

Go buy a $25 come-along and a LONG, LONG strap (to put around a tree or rock).

30 minutes of labor will extract your vehicle from most any hole or puddle 
you get yourself into. Better than the cell phone we had with us the second 
time which had a signal but I had no way to explain to the wife where 
exactly we were.

She came up anyhow with a vague idea of where we were. She drove the Westy 
of course. We got a pull out by a passing mondo 4WD before she could arrive 
and we caught her on the road. Boy was I in trouble (grin!).

Make that strap a long one. I was 10 feet short with the Cherokee. I did 
have a come-along on that trip but no handy trees.

1 towtruck bill would be more expensive than a come-along and appropriate 
gear.

Good luck to all!!!
