IIRC, a pre-fuel-injection bay tank will fit with a little judicious
denting in the middle bottom.  You can use your original gas valve
outlet, so you have the best of both worlds!  5 more gallons and a fuel
reserve/shutoff capability!

You will have to pull the engine to swap tanks, of course!
