Hi Meredith,
 The trick to drivin w/o a clutch is.... there is no
trick:) Its all practice... Best to learn when ya dont
have to. 
 You start off by killing the engine, put it in first,
when the light turns green start her up & go. Goin
thru the gears is a matter of engine rpm in relation
to vehicle speed. You ever ride in an 18 wheeler? The
driver starts in first with the clutch & doest touch
it again till the truck has to come to a complete
stop, unless ya miss a gear:( Fortunatly its easier
for us(4speed not a 13 speed w/double overdrive:) If
youll notice most 2 wheelers use the same method. I
still catch myself "powershifting" the B*g, & the '62
single cab prefers I drive her by double shifting
2,3,&4th:) When you get your cable put in, go
practice. Its like ridin a bike, the first time you do
it right, youll say to yourself "OH, so thats how!"
