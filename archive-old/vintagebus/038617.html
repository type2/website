Busses by the Sea 2001

When - September 14,15,16
Where - Frontier Town Campgrounds  Ocean City Maryland
http://www.frontiertown_dot_com/
How Much - $21 a night, reservations are NOT needed

What going on ?

Friday - Arrive early, get the good camp spot, kick back with a cool one and
greet the new arrivals
Saturday - Pot Luck Dinner followed by a 5 mile carabus to Assateaque Island
for  wild pony sightings and a  bonfire on the beach.
http://www.nps_dot_gov/asis/
Sunday - Big Breakfast and sad farewells

Things to do while your here.
Frontier town is a western themed campground.  If ever there was a family
bus event, this is it.  There is a free water park, playgrounds, canoe
rentals, nature trails, Ocean City shopping, and a great beach and ocean to
play in.

What to bring - Besides your usual arsenal of camping gear, be sure to
bring...

Firewood
Bug spray
Suntan Lotion
Bathing Suit
Firewood
Canoe or Kayak
Bicycles
Fishin' Poles
Flip Flops (nobody wears shoes around here :) )

All your tasty beverages and pets are also welcome!
