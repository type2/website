I'd say you could.
I only wanted to do the job once so I chucked up the short tubes, held a
file against the tube end/edge and sharpened it. I just possibly made a
cleaner cut.

section of the Ace Hardware.
With the cheap Chinese products coming in every day you might get a
decent set of gasket cutters for under 20$.
Nice thing about the gasket cutters is that you get a square corner
clipper and a couple of elongated holes too.
