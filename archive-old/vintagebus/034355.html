I have used an adjustable reamer
and found it a little 'risky' cos you're working
right on the bottom end of the adjustment
(adjustment is 21-23 mm on mine)...

I have used a brake master cylinder hone
(goes in yer drill) with great success and it is now
my preferred method for honing the bushings.

It takes a bit longer but you just hone to a nice tight fit!
