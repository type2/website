Hippies bought their buses cheap.

They put on colorful paint.

The paint kept rust away.

The hippies graduated from college.

They sold their buses cheap.

The buyer restored the bus.

They bought back their buses.

This time for mega bucks.

They put the bus away.

In the garage under cover.

Next to the jet skis.
