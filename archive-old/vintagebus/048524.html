Isaiah is right, I took a close look at Dan's shifter extensions last
Sunday at the KC Kombies garage tour, and I will have to say he has done a
REALLY GOOD JOB at repoing these.  In fact, of all the "original" ones that
I have, his is a much better quality.  These things are nice!!  And he
didn't cheapo out on the material, 6061 HIGH grade aircraft aluminum.  I am
surprised that he is making any money on these with as much work as what
has gone into them.

I too have picked up extensions pretty cheap at swap meets, but you won't
find a better one at the price!  (I really do hate to give Dan this much
credit)  The old shop teacher gives two thumbs up for this one.

And on a second note, the garage tour was a lot of fun, we need to do more
of these.  Dan's wonderful wife had even whipped up some great buscuits and
gravy to start the morning off.  A lot of good bus conversation.  Great to
renew old friendships and get everyone "pumped up" for the upcoming camping
and show season.  If your local bus group hasn't done one of these, you
ought to try one!
