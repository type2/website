I own a two tone kombi. It really sets off the nose-shape. Commercial
vehicles (Kombis included) almost always came in a single color. They
look great this way too. And if anyone has even a passing interest in
things VW, they don't need the two-tone scheme to alert them to the fact
that it is a split....
But for all the kids, folks on the street, and casual admirers, a
two-tone bus or truck will get more looks, and more fols will notice
that it is not just another beater bus, but "an older one" with that
funny front end and two windshields....

Craig Sauls in Modesto has one of the very nicest two-tone paint scheme
trucks around, Rosy The Rocket Sled. It has the three-area scheme, with
the stripe and nose portion only in red, and the top and bottom in white
( or do I have that backwards?) I have a picture of it somewhere, if you
want to see it.. Or I can post the pic with his permission...
