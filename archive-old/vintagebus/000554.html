"Spend a $1,000 on a computer and in five years it is worth $50.  Spend a 
$3,000 on a computer and in five years it is worth $50, maybe $60."  

This is so true.  With this in mind I set out looking for a AMD chip or a 
Celeron.  In the end I got a reconditioned 500mhz Pentium 3 unit from

has a one year warranty.  500mhz P3, 128m RAM, 16m video, 8.4g HD, 40x CD, 
56k modem, sound blaster live, speakers with subwoofer, 17" monitor, win 98, 
etc. all for $1249.  This was about the same cost as a comprably equipped AMD 
so I went with it.
