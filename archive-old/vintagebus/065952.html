Subject: RE: [VB] frozen door handles

My cure all for door locks and push buttons is to heat them up thoroughly with
a hair dryer to get them good and dry and real warm. I push a tooth pick to
hold the "key door" open so that I know the warm air got into the tumbler
area. After good and warm and dry I liberally spray down with Silicone based
TV tuner cleaner, a Radio Shack product. The silicone gets into the pores of
the metal and stays there. I then use LPS lubricant, not WD-40 which
evaporates off, and spray the button and tumbler down again. No problem after
that. I first learned that with my moms new 67 square back. M dad did all the
locks and latches and then did the family 65 bus.
