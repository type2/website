One thing I did was took a "smallish" size drill bit and drilled into the
emergrncy brake handle at the weld joint of the tube where that steel pin
goes in. Drillled until I just hit the pin. Theory was that the penetrating
oil wasn't getting into the center...... Anyway, lots of oil, more heat,
tender love with big hammer..... and it came out.

Guess I should tap that small hole for a grease fitting??? Might have been a
