I remebered to look at the English shop manual for
older buses that I mentioned a few days ago.  Here is
what I found on it- my copy is hardbound with a
plastic finish on the covers.
The title is "Workshop Manual for the Volkswagen 1200,
1500 Transporter 1954-67", by Keneth Ball, Autopress
LTD, Brighton, England, copyright 1970.  It is one of
the "Autobook" series of workshop manuals.  It is 161
pages long, including index and list of other manuals
in the series- about 150 pages or so of actual bus
related text.  The transmission chapter has a couple
of pages on non-synchro trannies, 5 pages on 3-synchro
trannies, and 6 pages on tunnel case 4 synchro
trannies.
Hope that this helps someone.  I have seen softcover
(paperback) copies of this, not xeroxes, but I can't
recall where I saw them.
According to the original receipt that I found in the 
book, it cost $7.95 in 1971.  I got it in a used book
store in the 1980's.
