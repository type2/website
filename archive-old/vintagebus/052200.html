to check furl pump remove the fuel line into the carb, or from the pump.  if 
gas drips through the pump and comes out the carb end unless gravity pressure 
it is bad.  The bus may run, but not so well as with a good pump.  I dont 
mean the gas in the filter area dripping out, you know, but coming from teh

 point it away from the coil, and either turn the engine over by hand or use 
the key for the engine to spin (a friend to help with that while you look is 
nice).  if the gas spurts out of the fuel pump it work.  No gas?  then you 
take the inlet off the fuel pump and see if gas falls out of it(put a pencil 
in it now).  if gas it supplied to the pump and does not go out, it is bad.
          Dunno if the bus died after running, or you are getting it running

collect the rust.
