than buy a drop kit. It had to be that way because the arms are right at the

You would need to pull the spindles and measure the offset from top
trailing arm eye to lower arm, and compare to VW specs. Most offset
should not be more or less than 6mm. This will tell you if you have bent
arms. Pulling on the arms after spindle is removed can help reveal
broken or loose/sloppy leaves, but not as well as removal and
inspection, and then you might as well replace them anyway.
Sounds like someone fux0red them (leaves) to lower your bus....
