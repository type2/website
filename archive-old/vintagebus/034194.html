Hi John,

all the cases I have seen do have a boss, a place to tap a hole.  This is
one of the easiest jobs you can get with a tap and die... The magnesium
alloy is very easy to tap.  Even if you take your time, a 10 minute job.

For your point of reference, just use the boss... center on it best you can
with a small drill bit then work your way up... then thread it with a proper
tap.

I covered this in the Archives ... try searching "thermostat bracket" or
"thermostat near boss".

I have photos up at http://www.i405_dot_com/vw/stand/#thermostat1
