Hi Bill,
For all stock swing axle VWs this is the norm when no load is in the VW and 
not helped when the stock interior is removed.  So install the stock interior 
or even a camper/Westie interior which is heavier, tools and spare parts will 
help lower it naturally.  If you have a SO or a compleat family adding them 
on board will definitely help.  If it turns out you are not going to do any 
of this, taking it down a notch in the rear would probably a good idea.
