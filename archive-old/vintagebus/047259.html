rubber brake hoses

replace them    the front left sounds llike it is collapsing inside - which
will cause the symptoms you descibed.   Always do both on an axle.  Heck, do
them all, rarely do people notice them and replace them.   

And flush out the brake system, if you flush it out very year or two the
hydraulic brake components will last longer.

I found out about brake hoses when one burst on me (!!!!!) causing an
immediate and  severe lack of braking ability.  good thing my hand brake was
in order.  I knew there was something wrong with the brakes, but I didn't know
that it had rubber hoses.
