My advice is to stop worrying.  Sounds like you have prepared.  do pack a 
tool box to take with you, spare oil, 3 qts, and a few spares (tune up parts, 
and a spare tire at least).   I have a little metal box that lives in my bus 
with spare tune up parts and extra light bulbs that sort of stuff in it.

The important thing in my own opinion is to use it as a daily driver for the 
next 19 days.  

This way is any kinks show up, you'll be near home, and if not, 
groovalicious!!!!   Is your speedo cable broken or disconected?   A working 
odometer is important I think so at least.  How long  have you had the bus?   
 if you haven't had it very long, and as it has sat a while, do an oil 
change/ valve adjust a day or two before you leave, and do a tea leaf reading 
of the oil.  Are you caravanning with friends?   A good idea for fun and just 
in case you need to give them a ride to the parts store :)  Have a great time 
driving your bus.
