the interior size is differnet between a split and a later bus.... but if you 
are reasonably handy at carpentry you can modify things to fit.  think the 
width is the same, but the length behind the cargo doors is shorter.   Rear 
floor mats fit the same, the holes are differnt.  late Z bed goes right in 
w/o mods, and the supports are sturdier in fact.  but the long so 42 cabinet 
froma bay must be mofied to fit a split.  Bay roof panels are not as wide, 
unfortunately, so there would be gaps, and the rear one has to be cut short.  
I put bay westy roof wood in my 65 covering the thrashed original that was 
brush painted brown, ugly it is, and falling, the bay panelling shows a gap 
of a few inches, but vastly improved the interior.
