On Wed, Nov 26, 2003 at 03:31:37PM -0500, Andrew Strom wrote:
> How does everybody clear out a blocked heat riser?  The one on my bus
> managed to get clogged at some point over the summer, and now when it gets
> cold, my intake ices up every chance it gets.  I took off my muffler to try
> to work on it, but I can't think of a way to clear out the blockage.  Any
> ideas, people?  Thanks.

keep in mind that when I did this the manifold was off the engine. I
took a piece of old clutch cable and put it on the end of a power
drill. then fed it through the riser while running it. I kept spraying
carb cleaner in there as well, and w/out much work got it cleaned out
pretty decent. HTH
