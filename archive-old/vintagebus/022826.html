I got my CE safaris today.  Took 8 days to get to Sacramento from Jolly Ol'
England.

First impression: they seem to be put together fairly well, but the welds
on the hinge is kinda sketchy - it's not spot welded - and the hinge itself
is very soft metal - I can bend it easily.  Might be an issue, we'll see.

The instructions at first blush look great - color pix even!  But they
gloss over stuff, like the dash tabs.  It's pretty much "figger out where
to put the dash tabs, then move on"

The windshield mounts are nice and sturdy, but the press-in thread collar
had some interference with the wing nut.  

I screwed up the first weld by forgetting to turn the MIG gas on - d'oh!

The seals are different than what I'd seen before; a crimp-type part for
the frame, and a curved lip toward the frame.  Hmmmm.

The only real problem is the inner dash tabs - the latch hits the screw
when you try to close it, and if I remove the screw, it hits the tab.
Hmmm.  I've fired an email off to CE to see what up.
