Hi Chris,
Your front wheel cylinders could be corroded a bit.  Best to dissassemble 
them and sand them out with 300-400 grit wet/dry sand paper.  Also make sure 
the brake shoes are evenly adjusted all around.  Lastly this could be caused 
by a slightly seeping wheel cylinder.  This causes the brakes on that side to 
grab more.
