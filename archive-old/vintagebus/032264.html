One of our members took the roof brackets from a Westy and fashioned a
padded base for them. Then he combined that base with a comealong type
strap that hooks across the roof, securing the brackets in place. He can
then set up a full Westy tent without needing to drill anything. You could
easily do the same with a Sundial-style tent which is much easier to set up
and works great for the awning aspect.
