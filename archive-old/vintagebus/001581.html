First off, the valve thing.  I'm running dual springs and I intend to keep
them on there.  I rev my engine when I drive - maybe not to the point I need
duals, but I have been told that the bigger valves require more pressure
than stock to keep them from bouncing and doing little valve no-no's while
cruising around.

As for valve geometry - I'm totally convinced that I'm off.  In my last
minute rush to get to BNNTA, I threw on stuff and didn't check it.  It's not
WAY out of whack but it ain't perfect.  With the engine in the bus, just

any tips in this area because my knowledge of what to do is ZERO.

Secondly, I'm leaking oil like crazy.  It took 4 quarts to do 350 miles and
that was traced to no oring on the dizzy but I'm still loosing it.  Some is
from the deep sump and that can be cured by putting a better gasket on
between the engine and sump.  I had to use a marginal one before because I

drips right down on the merged part of the header and you can see each blue
