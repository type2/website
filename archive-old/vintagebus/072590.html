You've got two things there.
#1) Resistor Pack, 5 black wires goung to a metal shell conatining
resistors to lower FI voltage to 8 volts.
#2) Double relay, two relays side by side, normally black, one bundle of
white wires on right side This controls the fuel pump after the AFM gives
it up.
I'd open the cover on the AFM and see if the microswitch for the fuel
pump is burnt closed.
It'll be all the way to the right and the only exposed switch.
