Hi Terry,
Yes you can fix everything in about a day and a half easily.  Our best guess
is that your torsion bars are broken (and/or the torsion bar bearing are
bad.), so you need to find a set from both the top and bottom tubes from a
wrecked bus.  Remove the wheels, the allen bolts holding the swing arms on and
pull the outer suspension off each side.  When you pull the bars out mark them
for which tube and which end.  Taking them out will give you a idea of how to
dissassemble your bus and install the new bars. Plus make sure to look for any
cracked pieces of the bars, if cracked start looking for other buses to get
whole bars from or mix and match if nessary.  We would suggest installing the
bars turned around from the way they came out of the old front suspension
(Leftside becoming rightside.) so they are given a different stress pattern
and should last longer than the other way which has already been prestressed.
