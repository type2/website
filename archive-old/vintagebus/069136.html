Hi Ed,
A kind of felt is what they seem to be.  We use folded pieces of tar paper
under the tank.  Why?  Because the two plaes that bus gas tanks rust out is:
1.  Where theses felt strips hold water like sponges under the tank and rust
through.
2.  The area near the outlet tube in the bottom of the tank traps water and
gunk in the bottom of the tank.  To prevent this cut the outlet tubing as far
down inside the tank or if using a reserve switch remove the shorter tube
completely.
