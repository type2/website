I'm a little behind in my posts because I've been driving across the SW 
desert in my '59 Westy, as Ronnie mentioned.  So, as far as I have seen with

(about 5mph), it cooled about 15 degrees immediately.  I think that you're 
right that you have to find the rpm range where your bus is most comfortable, 
but if I can cruise a small nut at 60+ mph, that's gotta be greater than 
2600-2800 rpm.
