As Clara (Claralw_at_aol_dot_com) and Kevin Guarnotta pointed out,
this isn't quite right.  The front parking lights, mounted
in the headlight shells, go out when the headlights are
turned on.  These are on terminal 57.  Therefore, the
above should read:

"When pulled out to the first click, terminals 57, 58, 
 and 58b should all be powered.  When pulled out to
 the second click, 56, 58, and 58b should all be powered."

Note: 56 is the headlight feed to dimmer switch
      57 is the front parking lights in headlight shells
      58 is the rear running lights (dim filament of rear
           stop/turn/tail lights)
      58b is the dimmable feed to the dash lights

Sorry for any confusion.
