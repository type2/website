I find the entire tire industry to be Kafkaesque so this may well be true.
Load rating spec for an early bay (splitty may be a bit less) is ~1500
pounds for comparison purposes. In 185 the Yokohamas are around 1600 in
load range C, 1850 in D (the D is an 8-ply). 185s are not nearly so easy to
get. I don't have specs for the 195s nor money for tires anyway but
whatever you get you want to be around or over 1500.

As always you are not going to pop tires like this no matter what you do to
them. Four of the 8-ply tires would give you far more capacity than you
could ever get out of second gear and if perchance you got weight like that
moving you could not stop it again with your puny brakes. 8) The important
thing is the sidewall and the 6-plies will keep you from being flung over
the center line by a crosswind and THAT is what you are after unless you
have a scrap-iron business or are a mason or something.
