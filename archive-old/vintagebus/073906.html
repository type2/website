Did you find the parts list online?

look in the electrical section for wiper parts

do you have the wiper switch and wires that run from switch to motor?

do you have a Bentley manual for the splitty?  If not *get one*.  if so turn 
to the section on wiper motor (in the electrical chapter) and *read it*.   
read the whole dern idiot book.  Not very good on electrics, but has definately a 
lot of great stuff.  

The wiper motor assembly mounts with one 10mm bolt in the middle, and at the 
pivots with a nut at each, on the outside.  Get new wiper pivot grommets 
before putting the motor in.  You will need to drop the dash tray to fit the motor 
in, well, you take it out to clean/grease/ bench test it, then you need to put 
it back in.   it has to wiggle in just right.  the radio has to be removed to 
put the wiper assembly in.  Do you have a spare battery or a battery charger? 
 for bench testing.

I know some of this is confusing, but there are resources out there.   Use 
the idiot book and the Bentley.  I love parts lists.   it has every piece of the 
bus.  wow!   check it out.  

Guess this list isn't geared to someone who just wants to take the bus to a 
mechanic.  Most of the advice is, well, learn about your bus.   .
