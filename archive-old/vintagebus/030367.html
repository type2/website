I received the following information about replacement Barndoor front shocks 
from Everett.  Maybe this will be of some help to others on the list.



Brand:             Monroe
Part #:            LE100002
Compressed Length: 8.5"
Extended length:   12.875"
Eye size:          5/8"
Color:             Black

This stock is in their "Limited Edition" line so it normally must be
ordered.  My local Kragen/Checkers warehouse parts store just happened to
have 3 sets in stock.

It is a normal eye-to-eye style and did not come with bushings to drop the
eye size of 5/8" down to 1/2" (M12).  I was unable to find these bushings at
the Hardware store but found them at my local Fly 'N' Hi jacked-up truck
parts house.  Or reuse your old metal bushings, if possible.
