you could probably find a core motor at a junkyard , out of a bug or 
whatever.  If you want specifically an H case, the H cases being originally 
1500 you may have to look longer.   Then again, really the A cases (such as 
AE, AK,) are a better alloy, and it is cheeaper to build a 1600 rather than 
1500 (1500 pc and cs being harder to find, and 1600 being very easy to get)  
so unless you and building a spiffy original showtruck, I would get a later 
1600 case with the dual oil relief and better alloy, and have a 1600.   Hope 
this helps some.
