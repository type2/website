John-
I did the swing lever bushing procedure to my bus not long ago. While
I bet it can be done with beam still on the bus, I imagine it would be
a big knuckle scraping head bashing endeavor. The beam comes off so
easy, and it is a good opportunity to clean it up, maybe paint it,
check out those grease fittings.
Buy the kit, it is worth having a shiny new pin to swivel smoothly in
your new bushings, new grease seal to keep dirt out of your new parts.
The kit is readily available, the reamer is not. At least I had a hard
time tracking one down, in the end I went to Wolfgang Int'l with the
beam and they reamed it for $20. Well worth it to me, there was a
dramatic improvement in steering once I got everything put back
together.
Good luck,
