Charles:

If you lower the rear of your Bus at all with the reduction boxes still in
place, camber will be effected. The swing axle conversion kit is the most
popular way to drop the back end.

All of the dropped spindles we have seen on the market are MUCH WEAKER than
stock spindles and are also missing the stops. That is why we only sell the
adjustable beam. As long as you don't lower it all the way down to the
snubbers, the ride is still pretty smooth.

Details can be seen here: http://www.bus-boys_dot_com/bbsusp.htm
