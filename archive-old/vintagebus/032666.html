i finally got around to putting my "new" (to me) speedo in my 61 westy. 
thanks Clay! after cleaning out the parcel tray (wow, so much stuff) i got

old, large Bosch horn from a '67 mercedes.  now i have that sweet mellow

sweet rpm's. (and no tachometer)  ....and what gas gauge?
