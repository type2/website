I have run a 6 volt motor in 2 12 volt buses for almost 5 years
now....no problems at all!!..

I buy the little ceramic voltage drops from NAPA Auto parts...I run 2 in
line....that means 

-The power from my wiper switch runs to the 'battery' terminal on the
voltage drop
-The out put side of the first voltage drop is run to the 'battery' side
of the second voltage drop...
-Then the output of the second voltage drop goes straight to the wiper
motor positive terminal...

Easy as pie....I run 2 in line since checking just one drop with a volt
meter...the meter said each v.drop only drops the current by 3 volts?
Strange but I ran 2 in line and checked again...a perfect 6 volts...
