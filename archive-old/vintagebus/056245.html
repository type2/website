It's all relative.
I guess you could even have an 'All Original Repop Parts Restored Bus'

I would venture to state:

    term               meaning
1. factory original:   unrestored... this wouldn't necessarily mean restored
2. restored original:  used original parts (note I didn't say nos)
3. restored vintage:   used vintage parts during resto (i.e. like the 59 dc)
4. restored            used what ever.

I'd say that '64 dc falls under the 'Restored' category.
