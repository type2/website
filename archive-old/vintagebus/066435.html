  Gentley tap on the back side of the drag link end. This will set it in the
hole allowing the nylock to tighten all the way. If you use castle nuts you
will have to drill a hole for a cotter key, way more work then necessary.
All of my buses have ny-lock nuts on the tie rod ends and the drag link and
have never had a failure.
