Subject: Re: [VB] constantly running on three cylinders

Thomas,
I had the same problem, it was because I installed an aftermarket air
intake on a bone stock 1600 sp. I kept upping the jet sizes but I
could not get the proper fuel air mixture so I burnt out my
heads(brand new) in about 2000 miles. Also I was going through #2
spark plugs about every 100 miles. The root problem was to lean or to
rich of a mixture (maybe you have a bad carb?) and that my valve seats
were shot (does she backfire when you come to an idle, not very loud
just a popcorn pop?) I rebuilt my heads and put the old oil bath air
intake back on and re-adjusted the carb back to stock, now no more
problems.

Also one more thing do a compression test and see what you get. They
should all be within 5psi of eachother and if any are under 90 getting
close to a rebuild any under 80 you need a rebuild NOW! When I did my
compression my #2 (the one that kept burning spark plugs) was at 75!
