non-welded bars can only be used with the torsion arms with sealing

part number for the torsion bar.  I can't find a note involving 
supercession of the torsion bar part number in the Supplements at the
beginning of the parts list.  I would assume that the part number it
gives is for the welded end bar.  Maybe somebody with an earlier copy
of the parts list can check.
