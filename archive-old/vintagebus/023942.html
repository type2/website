The 65-70 degree thermostats will open the shutters at that
temperature, Celsius. Respectively, for the 75-80 degree thermostats. 
	RMMW lists 3 thermostats and their applications:

021 119 159 A: 85 degrees C, 17-2000cc, type II, 1972-'79
043 119 159: 80-85 degrees C, 1600cc, type I, 1975-'79
111 119 159A: 65-70 degrees C, 12-1600cc, all else besides the above.
