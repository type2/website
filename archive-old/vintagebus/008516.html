Well tonight officially kicked off my first rebuild.  I started out by
watching bits and pieces of the Jerry Hahn rebuild video and then I read the
Tom Wilson book for a few minutes.  I didn't get very far on the rebuild but
I wasn't trying too hard either.  I was able to get 8 dowels in the crank
and get it bolted to the flywheel for a stand.  The bearing locator pins are
in and the bearings marked so that when I put in the crank, I'll know just
how to line up the bearings so they will fall on the dowel pins.  The ring
grooves in the pistons were dirty so I cleaned them out.  The o-ring seals
are on the 6 main engine studs and 4 lifters are in place.  Looks like now I
need some plastiguage for the rod clearance.
