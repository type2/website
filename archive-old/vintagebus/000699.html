Take off the right front kick panel, and you will find the E flasher relay 
box.  Clean ALL of those connections.  Beware of any crimp on aftermarket 
connectors.  Far better to solder these on, and heat srink on them!  If all 
else fails you might just have to open the E flasher rely box to clean any 
crud/corrosion out there.  We ran a 6 volt E flasher relay on our 1963 SC for 
several years when we had it running on 12 volt.  The E flasher relay held up 
well probably due to not using it much.
