In addition to what E&B said, I'll just say I really like my setup with a
34 PICT-3 and SVDA.  I get the response of a vacuum advance dizzy with a
mechanical advance curve similar to the 009.  I use VW# 043-905-205A.  

For your carb, I'm not too clear on what's broken.  If the body itself isn't
munged you might try getting a junk carb at a swap (they are cheeeeeeeeap)
and fixing yours with theirs.  If you go for a 30 PICT-X carb, the vacuum
signal may not be quite right for an SVDA, but it will probably work better
with a 009 than a 34 PICT-3 generally does.  HTH
