   The no hop kit consist of a tube that goes over the axle tube. They have
a grease zerk on them to keep them moving freely. From the tube a bar is
connected to the torsion to keep the axle from moving. I have gotten bugs
here in the shop where they never greased the tubes and twisted the RGB
completeley off the axle.
   There still is a company that makes these on a limited basis but I have
never found them. Most of the old timers here in town just make there own. I
have an Old School Johnson no hop kit that I got years ago
