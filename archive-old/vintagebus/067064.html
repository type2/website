In early 89 my daily driver cbecame a 59 bus with split case tranny and 1500 
sp H case motor 28 pict carb and 14" rims.  I drove it over the Santa Cruz 
mountains several times a month- and daily up and down hwy 9 on the ocean 
side. (when I wasn't mountain biking that is)  This is lots of windy curves 
while climbing, with a ravine on one side and hillside the other, lots of 
tall trees both sides.  Gus the bus was real good at zipping around those 
curves. This is when I switched from a 73 westy to a split, and loved the 
handling improvement.   A couple times I borrowed my Dad's 86 (88?)Chevy it 
was one of those early SUVs forget the name.  But the center of gravity on it 
was higher, and I drove slower in the mountains than I did with the trusty 
old bus. (and slower on the flat in the bus)
