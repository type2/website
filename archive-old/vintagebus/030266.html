Guy with a handle of 'doppelkabine' has to ask this? ;-)  Rough 
translation: Nun Sitter -- it was used by convents in the Bavarian alps 
to transport Die Schwestern ab und zu Der Bahnhof.

No!  Actually just means "nine seater" -- that's a standard bus (neither 
kombi nor deluxe, but an in-between trim level - e.g., has cargo area 
panels but no clock, etc.) with nonwalkthrough front and full-width 
middle & rear seats - thus 9 seating places.

An achtsitzer would have a bench front and 2/3rd middle seat (and cost 
$500 more ;) while a siebensitzer would have walkthrough cab.  But AFAIK 
these terms only apply to the standard bus (not deluxe or kombi)?
