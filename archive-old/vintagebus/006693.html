I might have missed this. What year bus are your RGB, if they are large nut
you should not use the seals in the kits, they are the wrong size, even
though VW superceeded these and said the seals in the kits will work. On
large nut RGB I use the 113 501 315H seal, which I have in German and always
include these seals when I sell a seal kit for the Large Nut RGB. After
30,000 miles on my DD (with large nut RGB) I have never lost a seal.
