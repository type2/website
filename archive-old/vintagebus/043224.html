Factory "re-conditioned" cases as they were called ground out a portion of
the original engine coding and replaced it with another that identified
this re-conditioned case as such.  I don't have the exact lettering the
factory used here, but could get it if someone is really interested.  I
made a post about 2 weeks ago re: this factory procedure.
The numbers under the pushrod tubes were left unchanged, and only the
engine ID letter codes were changed there at the base of the gen. stand.
