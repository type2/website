I live about 10 minutes from the winery where the SOTO meet was held.   I
was there at 8:30 AM to avoid the crowd.  I guess there was a total of about
15 to 20 busses at the peak of the event which was about 9:30 AM.  When the
members of the San Diego chapter of SVII's took off for the VW drags at
Carlsbad raceway, the bus population was cut in half at the SOTO meet.

I personally had a good time however.  Had my pick of shady parking and I
got to talk to Charlie Hammil for a while about bus pieces and parts.  It is
always a pleasure talking to someone who is an expert on a subject.  As for
the OCTO meet on the 24th of Oct., Charlie told me he is hoping to have 100+
busses attend.  I'll bet there will be even more.
