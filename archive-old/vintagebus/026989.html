I have been running the Rancho 3.88 r&p in my 64 Westy for a year and a half.I
have a 1600 sp with a header,195x70/15's and thats about it.This bus stays
pretty full most of time and sometimes with my rack.I have been averaging 20
to 23 mpg. most of my driving is on the highways around Chicago going fast
(65-70) ,but I also drive downtown Chicago on a weekly basis.However,I have
experienced an increase in gas mileage after installing a comp-u-fire ignition
three years ago.
    But I do feel the downside to this gearing is traveling up long steep
grades!! It defetinely is a strain on the motor unless you install a bigger
engine.But, for me, there isn't a steep grade for hours around here! ( my
opinions after 18,000 miles running a 3.88 rp).
