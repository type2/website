there are several varieties of backing plates and hubs.  If you mix it up 
wrong, the offset will be wrong, Ie, the gen pulley won't be straight with the 
crank pulley, making the fan belt wear funny and die a tragic death after a 
short unhappy life being made to work in unsafe conditions.  So if you are 
changing the plate over, examine it carefully to see if it is the same.

If you are planning on installing a generator, it should fit fine with its 
tin.  You do have to make sure the fan is the same as the old one, ie if the old 
one was doghouse, the new one should be too, as that means you have the 
doghouse set up already.  Make sure you put the voltage regulator in too!

You can run a generator on an alternator stand.  Though can't run an 
alternator on a generator stand (w/o mods that is).

hope that helps.
