I installed a bay flasher relay on my 59. Part # 211 953 315 C been working
fine for 6 years.

Terminal connections as follow

49: Black with white/green trace from fuse box. Thicker of the two.

49A: Black with white/green trace to TS switch. Thinner of the two.

KBL: Blue from indicator light in speedo.

31: NEW WIRE YOU WILL ADD. Make it tannish/brown and connect to a known
secure ground. The old original can picked up ground through it's case when
fastened to the mount.
