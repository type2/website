Subject: Insulation

The '64 Singlecab I just picked up had the same problem. Matted up pink 
insulation collecting moisture and rusting everything from the inside up. Try 
the self adhesive rubberized sheets sold at J.C. Whitney. The are the same 
that are sold custom/rod shops for much more money!
