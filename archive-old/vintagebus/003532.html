My 2 cents:

Why not get a surplus squirrel cage fan the plugs into
110VAC, get some 1-1/2" or 2" flexible plastic hose, attach
it to the blower, attach other end into a face mask of some
ilk, tape plastic sheet around it and duct tape the plastic
to your neck for final sealing. Cheap, YES. comfortable,
probably not. But it would work if you are very thorough
with the tape.
