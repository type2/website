e.s.:

Assuming that you will be using a SR15 starter, you will need  to use a
ZAP-91102 adapter starter bushing ($2.60).  No bushing is needed when using
a SR17 starter.

The bellhousing will need to be ground slightly in a few spots with a die
grinder or similar tool. The best way to figure out where to grind is to
shove a 200mm flywheel into the bellhousing and grind the places where it
rubs.

There are usually 4 spots that need grinding. 2 on top and 2 at the bottom.
