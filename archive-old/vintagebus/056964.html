> Hi Alex,
> Check you generator brushes to make sure they are taller than the cages they
> sit in.  Is the commutator of the generator dirty?  If so clean it with a
> cardboard emery finger nail sanding stick when the engine is running.  Clean
> and retighten all connections between the generator, regulator (regulators
> that are seperate from the generator need good contact to the body!), starter
> solinoid, battery, and all the wiring going up front through the fuse box,
> headlight switch, ignition switch and back to the starter solinoid, and coil.
>  Then take your multimeter and with it set for voltmeter check the voltage at
> the battery terminals and then check at various points of the above wiring
> system and see how much drop in voltage there is and if you can solve it.
