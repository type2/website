Subject: Re: [VB] Pulling RGB bearings

Tap the bearing back in, take a peice of emery cloth and clean-up the axle shaft and take a long
punch and lightly tap the bearing until it comes off. These bearings are not a press fit and it
shouldn't take much force to get them off.

I taken-off them off this way many times without damaging the bearing. Although chances are, if you
have metal frags in the tranny oil, your RG bearings will be damaged to some degree.
