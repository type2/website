Hi Christen,
Up to Late JULY59 the type 1 & 2 used a wire hanger that looks like a paper 
clip on steroids bolted to the upper left stud on the tranny where the nose 
cone mounts to the tranny housing.  AUG59 was the start of the use of a 
protective tube kind of like a boden tube, but much smaller diameter, part 
number 111 721 551.  The bus seems to have used the same part, but to be 
shortened to 670mm length.  To quickly fabricate the same you could use fuel 
line with the same or slightly larger inner diameter so it will slip onto the 
forward accelerator cable tubing on the body, and hose clamp on.   Eric&Barb
