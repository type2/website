That space is where the rubber center of the rear shift coupling was. The
two flat plates are the parts it was bonded to. One must remove them in
order to put the new shift coupler in. Old style coupling, the new ones
don't look the same. I have always just disconnected the forward shift
coupler and moved the rod forwards to change them, although with Arizona
buses that may be easier than in other climates. Plenty of info in the
archives on the other procedures.
