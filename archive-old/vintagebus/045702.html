Hi Wilder,
There are four bearings in each reduction box not just one.  Even if there is 
only one bad bearing it is best to just replace all four at once especially 
if you plan to do long distance trips with your bus.  If you do reuse the 
used bearing/s now or later.  You have to take the time and inspect all the 
bearings you think might be usable inside thier inner and outer races for any 
imperfections.  Even a slight mar only about a pin tip size is a sign the 
bearing is on it's way out.
You really need to buy a Bentley reprint of the VW bus shop manual!
