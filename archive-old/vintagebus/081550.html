In Arizona, you don't register as a collector vehicle.  With this new law,
you need to have collector car insurance, and whatever the mileage
limitations are with the insurance, are the limitations.  The state is
letting the insurance companies provide the guidelines as to what is a
collector car. The collector insurances we've had (there have been several
over the years) usually allow 1500-2500 miles a year.
The new law isn't perfect, but it's better than what we've had.
