I wanted to put in my two cents about paint stripper chemicals.  3M's
"Safest Stripper" is good if you have time, and the temperature is cooler.
It is nice because it only takes off a layer at a time, which can be perfect
for what I wish we had only done.  However...if you want to take off a bit
more (duh duh dun da dant!) the Citrustrip stuff is actually quite nice.  It
does the job well, doesn't dry out too fast, and doesn't send Michele inside
saying "ow ow ow" after accidentally sitting on Jasco...as a matter of fact,
all it did to my hands was to prematurely age them, but that went away.
Plus it has this nice orangey scent!
