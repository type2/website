Subject: 15" tire specs from VW

Here is specifications for tires from the Official VW Workshop Manual,
published by Bently.

These are the tires VW used and recomended for the old splits.  If not
using bias plys at least the dimmensions may help someone in searching
for close to correct radials.

By the way the Firestone 6.40-15 Bias plys I recently bought from Coker
tires are 4 ply rated, with a 27.1" diameter and 6.61" width...about as
correct size as one is going to get.
 
------------------------------------------------
3/4 ton vehicles  with 15"-4 1/2" rims require

6.40-15 Tires

Carcase strength of 4pr (ply rating)

Dimensions: 
Outside diameter (27.16" +or- .24")
Width Max. (6.42")
Effective staitc radius (12.64")
Effective dynamic radius (12.91")
------------------------------------------------
1 Ton vehicles with 14"-5" rims require

7.00-14 Tires

Carcase strength of 6pr (ply rating)

Dimensions:
Outside Diameter (26.22" +or- .24")
Operating width (7.4")
Effective staitc radius (11.88")
Effective dynamic radius (12.32")
