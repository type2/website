Well I was to leave this list and still find myself reading it.......

Anyway, I road tested a 1641 with and without the flaps about 5 years ago. 
Swap done in the same day, temperature of outside air had not varied much, 
maybe 2 degrees. With flaps I saw a 10 degree drop in oil temp. Now this is 
not a real good estimate of cooling, because oil temp is not as good as head 
temp, given that air is hitting cylinders, but after correcting for ambient 
temp of air (down 2 degrees) I would have to think that I was getting better 
airflow, and dropping temps all the way around. Like I told Clara, I would 
say a 5 to 10 degree temp drop is reasonable with flaps in.
