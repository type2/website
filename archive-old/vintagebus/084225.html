Subject: Re: [VB] Bucking.

I had a similar problem. While driving on the freeway at top speed, my 
1600sp would start bucking. I could drive on the surface streets or 
slower on the freeway with no problem. What I found was a piece of the 
rubber hose had sheared off and stuck in the hole that leads to the 
needle valve. It didn't effect the operation of the needle valve, it 
just reduced the flow of fuel. If the fuel level in the bowl is high 
enough to run at 60mph, wouldn't it be high enough at 70mph? It wouldn't 
stay at the proper level if there was a restriction in the flow of fuel. 
I would check the tank filter, line filter, fuel pump, and look for a 
clogged orifice.
