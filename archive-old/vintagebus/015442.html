Up here in this part of Northern California we seem to have alot of people 
who think "differently" than the rest of us.  Not everyplace is as hip as 
L.A.

Les Schwab Tires is currently running a tire promotion that would be the 
laughing stock in just about any region but this one.

FREE BEEF WITH A SET OF TIRES!

In their TV commercial, Les makes the anouncement while wearing a cowboy 
hat. A smiling family has just had new tires put on their pickup and the guy 
that just put them on reaches into a freezer and pulls out the beef (no 
inuendo intended).

I wonder what beef and tires have in common? Why not a free alignment or a 
pine tree shaped air freshener?  What do non-carnivorous people get?  Free 
salad?

Well, I guess next time you are driving your Bug or Bus through town and you 
get that hungry feeling, you now know were to get tires.
