We apply a very thin bead of tar inside the groove the glass seats inside of 
before installing the glass/rubber into the frame.  Trying to apply the tar 
after glass/rubber installation makes it very hard to do the top section of 
the seal properly due to gravity and the non-BD fresh air overhang.  Plus the 
seal is under pressure inside the frame which makes it hard to get the tar in 
there even more.  Better to have a bit of a tar mess to clean up now, that 
more rust later!
