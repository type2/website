Hi Isaiah,
If it only happens just after first starting your engine it is due to a 
slight tilt to the engine.  Which allows a small amount of oil to collect, 
seep past the rings, to get burnt when you start it.  Remember the cylinders 
are totally horizontal so this happens easily!   Eric&Barb
