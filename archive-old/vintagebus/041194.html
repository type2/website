    I finally picked up the two Garcia buses yesterday.The church I bought

Dad are  Vw. mechanics, and have a full shop.I have no room at all left in
my garages and he offered storage and a place to replace the clutch.He drove
the 66,with no clutch,I know it's hard on the starter but towing was not an

mph.It took us a while to make the 100 mile trip.People on the road were
freaking out over the two buses.We almost caused a few fender benders.We
also stopped a soccer game as all the kids stopped playing to checked out
the two hippy buses.We arrived in Watertown to find another good friend of
ours installing a new wiring harness in a 66 bus for one of the Holcomb's
customers.His girl friend is in love with the 66,this bus will be for
sale,though Dan K. has first dibs on her.I then decided to drive the 59 back
home to Barrington Il.in the dark.Though I had just bought this bus I felt
