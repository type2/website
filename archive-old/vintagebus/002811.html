Mechanically, this thing is potentially great...I drove home with brakes
that needed pumping before anything happened, and that thing was to pull
violently to the right....no ebrake, either.  But brakes is brakes, and I'll
fix'em.  The engine, which supposedly has about 1100 miles on it, is
supposedly an 1776, dual-port, single 34-PICT, and Monza exhaust (oooh,
rumbly!), feels pretty good.  It has an alternator, which is good, because
the fan on the full-flow oil filter and cooler sucks plenty of juice!  The
steering box is really worn, but the front end was supposedly gone through,
and besides the steering play, it feels solid.  And the tranny, which was
supposedly redone 4 years ago, right before the Prev PO blew the engine and
let the bus sit for 4 years, feels pretty good, although the shifting is
sloppy, and reverse doesn't need the push down action....I think the linkage
needs to be adjusted.

The body is missing metal in spots, and there's some nasty "I wannabe a
barndoor" paint on there, which is coming off, and there's plenty of reason
Ronnie should be feeling a tingle, 'cuz there's plenty of stuff I'm gonna
need!
