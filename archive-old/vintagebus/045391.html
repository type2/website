Hi Markus,
Well it is doubtfull if you could get brazing done with just propane, but you
could lead solder a nut onto it.  Or just drill a small hole in the center of
the plug and use a body dent puller to yank it out.  Either way make sure to
really clean the dirt and built up rust around the plug with a sharp knife or
exacto blade so the plug has a smooth exit to prevent sticking.   Eric&Barb
