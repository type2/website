Here is a tip...

Save some time by getting a turkey baster (big syringe like tube with
rubbber ball at end) and suck out the old fluid from the reservoir.  If you
are lucky you can suck some of the gunk whole you are at it.

And, oh yeah, don't reuse the turkey baster for Thanksgiving... it's ruin
the meal.....
