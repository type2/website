I just cleaned my shower doors that had years of mineral deposits on it.
Went to a glass & paint shop and on the counter was a product called
Nothin's Better.  This stuff works great.  I also got a small scrub
brush that was made for use on scratchable materials.  It is a micro
abrasive and really took the coating off.  I also used it on the aluminum
frame and it cleaned and polished that really well.

I had previously tried everything available in the supermarket/drug store
and nothing even touched it, until this product.  I even got a free
sample sized container, and it was enough to do the entire shower, so
even that size would do a whole bus.
