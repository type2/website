I can't speak for Australian buses, but VW Transporters sold in South
Africa were assembled here from CKD kits since August 1955.

I have never seen an m-code plate on SA-assembled buses. Locally assembled
VW Transporters have a VIN plate under the front passenger seat (LHS),
which includes model# and chassis# and max axle weights, but no colours or
options.

VWSA kept no records and cannot even tell you the year a vehicle was built
from the VIN#.
