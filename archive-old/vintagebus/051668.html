Rich-
You mean FRONT (not rear) main seal, right? It is behind the flywheel,
so unless the flywheel was removed it is pretty well protected. I
think the seal is more likely to ooze/leak than pour out oil. If oil
is coming from upper left I would be checking out the oil cooler and
oil cooler seals as probable culprits. A bit of a pain to get to, you
need to pull the engine in order to pull the fan shroud in order to
get at the cooler.
What you wrote about "not much activity until the motor warmed up a
bit" really makes me think the problem is oil cooler, because oil only
flows here when the engine is warm.
What would have happened if you hadn't noticed the oil leak? Hopefully
you would have noticed the low oil pressure warning light...
