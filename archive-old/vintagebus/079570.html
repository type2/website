it's a question of either/or as far as I can see.  running heater boxes (with 
air through them) cools the exhaust.   I would think this would make the 
heads run cooler (which is supported by my experience with a bad fan belt and a 
destroyed fresh air tube on a road trip, but no numbers for you)

cooling the exhaust.

  Though if someone has a head temp sensor and want to give number with the 
fresh air tube installed vs plugged off, that'd be neat.
