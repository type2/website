Hi Brad,

I'm assuming you are all VDO gauges?

Oil temp:  If you go with the dipstick sender on the oil temp, it is easy to
get working.  You didn't mention what sender you are using for oil temp,
there are at least 4 places to mount them :)  Easy gauge, just one wire to
engine.

Oil Pressure:  I'm mounting mine as we speak!  This is easy too, just a
single wire to the engine, the rest comes from front (power and gauge
light).  You have two choices of senders... one with "dual" that does the
existing Idiot light, one that is "single" that won't allow the Idiot light.
The cost isn't that much more for the "dual", but I'm told by some that the
dual may be slower to reflect Oil Pressure changes... not sure if this is
true, just passing on what others have said.  I went for the dual...

Can't help with gas gauge, I have the same problem...

Gauges are easy.  Just run you wires properly to the back.

Hope this answers some of your questions...
