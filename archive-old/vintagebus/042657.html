> Does anybody have some helpful hints on repairing a chip-up, crack, chunks 
> falling off 1966 bus steering wheel?  I was planning to get some bondo fill 
> it in and sand then paint it up.  Would this be a waste of time?  All help

how i've been told to do it:

grind the cracks into v shaped grooves, fill with epoxy putty (comes in 2
sticks of putty you cut off equal sized pieces from both sticks, knead
together) smooth it pretty much like you want it as its hard to sand.

sand and paint.

thats it,
