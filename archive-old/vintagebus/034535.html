Find a roofer and ask him for a roll of rubberized membrane material. It's
been used for the last few years as an intermediate layer on roofs, especially
flat roofs. Its used extensively here in Florida and is usually guaranteed for
at least 10 years. Its totally waterproof and is self sealing from punctures.
It comes 1/2-3/4" thick and is easy to cut to form. Many auto restoration guys
are now using this for insulation and sound deadening.
