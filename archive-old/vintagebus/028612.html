As one of the silent majority on the list, I thought I should speak up.

hope it will be useful info for some of you. I'll be adding more
bureaucratic info about the process as we jump through the hoops.

a site, so be kind. It's not quite the glamor site that Thom or Everett
have, but it gets the message across. And in the spirit of NQ, I'll be
posting more updates on it's progress to the list.
