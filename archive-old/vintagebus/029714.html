Check straps are to stop the doors from opening fully, primarily to stop
interference with the front doors, not to stop them from hitting the body.
 Rubber stoppers are to prevent body damage when the check straps are disconnected.

Deluxes have the rubber piece in the middle of the aluminum trim, when the
doors fully open the rubber pieces hit each other.
