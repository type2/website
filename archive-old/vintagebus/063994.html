Subject: RE: [VB] Heater tubes?

There should actually be two pieces in between the heater box and the tube
welded to the body... the little canisters that look like mufflers, and a
section of flexible tubing.  Both pieces are about 10 inches long.  The
flexible sections should have fabric insulation around them.  Both items
come up on thesamba and ebay.
