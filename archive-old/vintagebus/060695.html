I agree- go get a $20 rolling floor jack.  leave it in the bus in case of 
flats or emergency engine pulls.    I jack it under the U under the tranny in 
the back (put a piece of 2x4 on top of the jack) and under the beam in the 
front, just behind the swing lever (put the piece of 2x4 betwen jackand 
beam).   If these places are rusted out, the bus shouldn't be driven.   Some 
folks live in Az or So Cal where they expect jack points to be functional and 
intact, but in the PNW that is rarely the case.   If you try to use it on 
your average VW around here you end up mushing the bus as the jack point is 
smushed upwards.  Even if the jack points are intact, the stock jack is quite 
wobbley, and not terribly safe.
