  There are 4 different manufacturers of European mufflers out there (that I
have dealt with). 2 being  made in Germany and the other 2 in Denmark. I
have found that the Leitzritz (German) fits the best with the J and P
(Denmark) being second with the Dansk the most inconsistent of the 3 (people
will say the J and P and Dansk are the same, but they are not)and the Ernst,
the worst fit. I think the Ernst are made in Brazil and sold as German. As
far as import the southern country, the OEM Mexico fit excellent with the
OEM Brazil a close second, the aftermarket Mexico next and coming in a
distant last the aftermarket Brazil. The choice is yours.
