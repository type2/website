This 67 DC sounds comparable to mine; I didn't pay anywhere near $14500 
for it in 1998.  The 'Old Cars Price Guide' says the ballpark value 
should be around $15600 in 'trailer queen' condition, or $10920 in 
'superior resto/excellent original' condition.  Then down to $6240 for a 
'older resto showing wear/operable original'.
