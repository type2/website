I verified this afternoon that the light does ground through
the mounting, and the wire to it should connect to terminal
86 on the emergency light relay.  On mine, it was white or
light grey...go figure.  The wire ran from terminal 86 on the
hazard light relay (behind right kick panel, top center), to
the hazard light switch, and to the terminal on the back of
the big red light.  At the hazard light switch, the wire from
the relay and the wire from the light were crimped in the
same terminal.

The big red light flashes in-phase with the exterior turn signals
when the hazards are on.  The green arrow in the speedometer
flashes out-of-phase with the exterior turn signals with hazards
on.
