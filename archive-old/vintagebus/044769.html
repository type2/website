 Don't know where you are located but when I moved back here to Colorado 
Springs(6500 ft above sea level) I had quite a bit of tuning problems 
concerning the 34 pict3 carb. The biggest problem was also stalling at idle. 
I found the 80 air correction jet was way to small for the altitude & thin 
air. I went up to a 125 air correction. This itself cured that problem, 
though I'm still not sure its the correct size yet. I've also dropped the 
main jet size from 127.5 to a 120 and believe I still may drop a size or two. 
I've also used a 30/31 with slightly better results. The only problem I'm 
still having with both is hard starting after the engine is warm, still 
working on that one.
