according to VW, 
oil pressure, for sae 20 oils..
at idling speed with engine warm min 7.1 psi
at 70C (158F) at 2500 rpm.. min 28.5 psi
this for a 1500 original old style single relief case

my 65 with late (AE dual relief)case tends to be a bit above these marks. I 
am also using thicker oil than 20sae.   My oil sender is new a few months 
ago, think the temp is pretty accurate.    AT about 100C it idles about 10psi 
and does ~ 3-psi at 3K.   Havent had guages for these on a H case motor so I 
dunno. 

oil temp- wamed up is above 65-70C (at least 150F) and  what is too hot?   I 
prefer if it is 100C/210F or below.  235F is Berg's magic number for it's too 
hot.  Say oil degrades when it gets that hot.     My made for ACVW accessory 
style vintage VDO same face as speedo oil temp guage goes up to 120C (about 
250C) evidently anything above that is just off the chart.

tons of stuff make a difference, kind of case, kind of heads, CR is a big 
one, carb, cooling system (dog or in housing cooler), sealage, loadage, rpms 
you drive, etc etc etc.
