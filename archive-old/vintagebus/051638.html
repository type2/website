  The longest lasting boot I have found are EMPI's made in the U S A. I
started selling them a year ago and have not seen a failure yet. I have
found that the Meisterstatz last the longest in our climate, 3 years on my
DD with 1 replacement at the end of summer.
  For those of you that don't know EMPI is one of the largest axle boot (all
types, over 300 styles) manufacturere in the country. They also manufacture
CV joints and axles. Over 80% of there building is dedicated to manufacture
of drive line products. I have seen them mold the axle boots and must say it
is really interesting.
