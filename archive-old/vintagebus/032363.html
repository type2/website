I don't know if their on the web but those numbers are all part of DIN 
72552 (Bosch sells a book with them all in it)

30=Input from battery (+) terminal
56=Headlamp
57a=parking lamp 57L=parking lamp Left 57R=parking lamp Right
58= Side-marker lamps, tail lamps, license lamps & instrument lamps
58b= I haven't got a clue as my Bosch automotive handbook doesn't list it 
(I would guess one of the above)
