Not too hard of a repair.  PUll engine, remove snap ring from end of
clutch shaft, remove clutch arm from shaft with the return spring.  remove
the small bolt that lives just forward of the throwout shaft.  Slide the
bushing to the left.  Remove throwout shaft.

I recommend getting a new shaft from dunebuggy suppliers.  They are a lot
better than the VW parts.  Make sure yhou get the right one as there are
several variations.
