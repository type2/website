Accuracy is good, but just like any pressure or temp gauge if it is off a bit 
does not really matter in the long run.  It is rather the sudden change or 
even the noticable change over time from normal that can be noticed due to 
keeping track of it.

Yes, you minus the previous odometer reading from the last one and then 
divide the result by the fuel amount of the last fill up.
