See if it is stock 6 volt system or if it has been converted to 12 volts.  If it is a 12 volt system, then many of the electrical items will have to come from a 67 because 66 was the last year for 6 volt and 68 saw bigger changes all around (alot of 1 year only electricals in a 67).  I had a 66 that was converted to 12 volts and this made things such as wiper motors, turn signal switches, flasher relays, etc. very hard to find because they were one year only.
