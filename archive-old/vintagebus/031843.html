If you put on KYB's Gas-a-Just they will raise the rear of the vehical
anywhere for a quarter inch to 2 full inchs. These shocks have a heavy
Nitrogen charge and some pretty stiff valving, great shocks for a bus always
carrying a load, but may be to stiff for those that don't. KYB GR 2's are a
lighter nitrogen charged shock and will not raise a vehical and then you
also have CoFap (same as Boge and OEM to VW de Mexico), these are available
in a light nitrogen charge and also a standard oil filled configuration at a
lesser cost then the KYB's.

I had been running my bus for about 8 months with no shocks. I had
