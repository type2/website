you should not use a regular voltage drop, as radios are more picky about 
their electricity than wiper motors.  There is a device, I think it's called 
a voltage inverter or something, that you can wire in between the hot 12V and

marked on it what it is and which way you hook it up.  I have used one of 
these to power a 12V stereo in a 6V bus, but I used an old one, don't know 
where you can buy one new.  I dont doubt they are made the other direction.
