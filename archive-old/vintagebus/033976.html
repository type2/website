 Hi Jeff,
Hmm, check your tranny fluid level.  Due to the 90WT oil thickness it is easy
to push too much into the tranny at once and get a sense that it is full, when
in reality the oil has not settled down into the entire tranny.  This is
especially true if you do not fill the reduction gear boxes first, then the
tranny.  While filling the entire tranny is possible from the single fill plug
you will have to take the time to let the oil settle in everywhere.
