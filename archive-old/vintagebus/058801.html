  An easy way to tell how much air is being pushed is turn on your heater to
defrost and drive till the engine sounds like you will blow up. You will
notice a decrease in air coming out of the defroster vent. On my DD I notice
the lose of air speed at around 3900/4000 rpm. I like to run between 3400 to
3800 (blows like a champ at those rpm's. Not very scintific but it does give
you an idea.
