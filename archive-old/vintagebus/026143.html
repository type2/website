Rex,

When I put the steering box back in mine, I used 8 of the cement wall blocks.
They were laying such that the smallest dimension was vertical.  Stacking two
on the ground and two on top of those two, gave me a long platform of just shy
of 12".  I jacked the truck up one bit at a time until I was able to get the
blocks under the wheels.

Bottom line is that if the wheels are 12" off the ground, you should be able to
easily get the box in.
