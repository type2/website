Isaiah - are you running the power from the positive side of the coil to the
choke and the idle cutoff?  Make sure there are no breaks in the wiring
because the idle cutoff might not be functioning properly.  You should hear
a solid click from the carb when you put juice to the cutoff valve.  If not
then remove the cutoff valve and apply power to it and see if it moves.  If
yes, then check for an obstruction down the idle cutoff passageway.
