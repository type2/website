Those are some nice setups, though after having a few Westies with the full kit I've found I prefer a minimalist "weekender" style package -- mainly just a z-bed and table -- especially in a non-poptop westy.  My current setup includes just a 3/4 z-bed and snack table, both scrounged from bay westies:

http://www.flickr_dot_com/photos/jsclark/12415851/
http://www.flickr_dot_com/photos/jsclark/139546475/

OTOH, if I camped in inclement or non-Florida weather I guess I'd want more stuff inside the vehicle.  And if it was an original vintage camping interior, you bet I'd want it all there.  But I find myself happier with big tupperware bins that I can pull out and set on the ground, making the interior roomier.
