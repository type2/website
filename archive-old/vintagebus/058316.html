I don't know, there has always been a, IMHO, unsubstantiated opinion that
VWOA is out to get ya. Since the mid sixties there has been a growing
concern among outside manufacturers to reproduce and upgrade and manufacture
various parts for dubs. Heck, you can build a complete engine without one
OEM part. There are all sorts of other parts made too. It seems to me that
if they haven't shut down EMPI, SCAT, CB Performance, Pauter Machine,
Autocraft, Gene Berg, ect & ect, then it goes to reason that anyone who
starts up a sheet metal reproduction facility ought to enjoy just as much
success as the other biggies just mentioned. The rub lies in when and if
someone will decide to risk some venture capitol and do that. It is all in
the "demand" arena. If there are enough people willing to buy, then there
will always be enough parts available.

Unless you are a diehard OEM stock geek.

I for one wouldn't mind dropping a brand new reproduction 65 deluxe 23
window van body on my brand new reproduction Chevy Corvair van frame with
the 140hp turbo Spider motor and automatic with air conditioning. But then
