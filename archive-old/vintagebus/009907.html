It's very common to find brakes "adjusted" like the ones you found when you
pulled the drum.

For some reason "brake specialists" in commercial shops think they know what
they're doing and don't bother to check out the specs for adjusting brakes
on a particular vehicle.  They seem to assume that they all adjust the same.
I don't know if this is due to arrogance or ignorance - probably both.

An all too common result is a blown wheel cylinder and catastrophic brake
failure.  You got lucky this time around.
