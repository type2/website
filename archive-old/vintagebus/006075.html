Michael:

If you would like to update your Bus to a ball joint beam. then a 68 or 69
will bolt right in. As for disc brakes, 1971 or newer spindles and brakes
will bolt right onto the torsion arms of the 68 or 69 beam.

One note: the late Bus bolt pattern is NOT the same as the Porsche pattern.
Bus is 5 on 120mm and Porsche is 5 on 130mm.
