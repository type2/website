I haven't been following this thread closely, but this sounds a little 
like the time my flywheel gland nut came loose.  Can you affect the

road.  When I got home I borrowed a jig from a local VW mechanic and

caused the pins to wallow out their holes.  Never gave any trouble 
after that.)
