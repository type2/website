Here's how to improve the Sears allen wrench if you can't or don't want
to fool with returning it and finding the good 3/8" drive tool.

Chuck that sucker up in the vice and cut a couple of inches off the long
end with your trusty hacksaw. It may take a while, but when you're done
you'll have a nice 17mm tool that you can stick in your plugs and turn
with a socket, open end or box end wrench. I'm talking about the short
piece. Save the big L shaped piece for when you drop your other piece in
the weeds and can't find it.
