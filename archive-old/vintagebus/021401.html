Hi Paul,
We have our backing plates and other black pieces of suspension and steering 
stuff zinc primer powder coated (Marine usage = salt!) and then have these 
items semi gloss black to 80% gloss.  Which seems to be right on the money 
for both color and gloss for any and all small black VW parts/tin.  For 
powder coating best to just degrease the items and let your powder coater 
sand blast them.  Otherwise your parts sit and rust while waiting for thier 
turn.   Eric&Barb
