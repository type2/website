Subject: Re: [T2] Late model gas tank retrofit

Use a 68-71 tank.
When you find one, also get the filler neck rubber hose and about a foot of each strap, from the threaded end.
The sender in it will work on a 6V system.

Good luck finding one in good condition. Here in California, all the yards are puncturing the gas tanks. [I
believe it has to do with EPA, probably to prove it has been emptied and now what vapors are left will
evaporate. I know of one gas tank that exploded when they crushed the car.]
