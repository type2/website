Digressing somewhat: I installed the WCM complete window rubber kit in the
64 about a year ago and I've been trying to solve this since then: the
rubber between the (driver/passenger) doors and the body is amazingly
sticky. The black seal that goes around the top of the door. The doors are
alway stuck shut until you heave on them to a ridiculous degree. This is
really irritating.

Here is what we have tried:

	Wax (JC Johnson Kit Wax Paste)	(made no difference)

	Wax again			(made no difference)

	Special synthetic rubber grease
	from NAPA			(made it worse)

	Powdering with corn starch		(works for a period of days)

	Powdering with flour		(works for a period of hours)

Somehow it is just a bit much to be out in the driveway in the morning
powdering your door seals with a pastry brush. Does anybody have an idea?
