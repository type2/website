OK, figure a radius of 0.3m, which works out to a circumferance of 1.9m.
At 4500RPM, this would be 8500m/min or 8.5km/m or 510km/h.  for those
stuck in the English system, that is 315MPH.

Now, if the 4500RPM is the input speed, you'll have to divide by the
reduction box ratio to get the output speed.  4500 seems a bit fast for a
prop, unless you want to send the tips supersonic!
