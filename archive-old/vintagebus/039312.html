When I re-did my headlight buckets a few years ago, I bought two cans of
Rustoleum Stone Gray spray paint. Available just about everywhere, dries
quick and it's a *pretty good* match for the original color.

Of course, sticklers for detail will either have to find NOS buckets or go
through paint analysis using cutting edge spectro-fluxcapacitor
technologies. Results may vary. Don't be surprised if it looks a lot like
Rustoleum Stone Gray.
