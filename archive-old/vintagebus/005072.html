I had a Century brand flux core 110v welder.  Only a bad craftsman blames
his tools, but I couldn't weld with that thing for crap.  After almost the
same discussion on this list, I bit the bullet and bought a Miller 220v Mig

tackled many more projects that I would have normally been very scared to
do. I just grafted in some semaphore posts in my oval, and have plans to
actually fix that rusted out Ghia 'vert that I've been having for a while.
My confidence levels have surely increased with the quality tools I am able
to use.
