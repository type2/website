what you have is an  A case and a B case.  The B case should be a early 1600 
bay window case, dual relief but the older alloy.  The A case is a better 
version, later better alloy less likely to warp, and has the factory case 
savers with small studs.   Some of the A cases have those some don't, I think 
they are nice.   So dont worry about what exact VW they came out of, and if 
you are building a motor I recommend the A case as it is the better alloy.  
Just in case your motor gets too hot sometime, that alloy can take more abuse.
