First , have the owner take massive amounts of pictures of the bus. Detailed
pics. Pics of the sheet metal from the side ,the rockers from both sides ,
all the floors from the top and bottom, the window sills on all windows,
around all the door and hatch rubber. Pics of the rain channel, the motor
compartment , teh battery area, the rear corners from the inside,  the nose
from the inside.

 Find a reputable and or knowledgable person with a club or organisation to
go and look at it for you, of course make it worh their while.

not seem like a good idea now but if the bus is misrepresented(Im not saying
it is) for that kind of cash then oh well.
 For the price the owner is asking , they should be willing to do all this
for a serious buyer. It looks like a nice bus and would be a nice find but
only if it is as nice as they say.
