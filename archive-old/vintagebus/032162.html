>Red and Green speedo lights do not come on when the key is turned "on", but
>glow brightly when the engine is running.

Sounds like a ground is missing or reversed with a power connection.  
Sequence should be:  12V > Lamp > Oil Pressure Switch > Ground (through
switch to engine block)  Similar for Gen lamp, but ground achieved through
regulator.  But with both of them acting the same, I would look closely at
the wiring around the speedo where the lights are mounted...

>disappears above the wheel well on the left side of the bus going forward.
>Is this a Westy unique installation, and does anybody know what it does?

>Finally, there is a small brown wire running wild out of the body (not one

"Brown is ground, the world around" goes the saying -- it isn't always

the end, and should be screwed down under the mount for the relay.  I
