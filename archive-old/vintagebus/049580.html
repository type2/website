NEVER use teflon tape on ANY seal that has exposure to the oil system -
teflon migrates like CRAZY, and little bits WILL end up in the oil stream.
Only takes a tiny bit to send a rod byebye.
You will not find teflon tape on any aicraft engines in the oil system, to
my knowledge....
