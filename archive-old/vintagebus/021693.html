 where the brakes are adjusted on a 67 type 2(Singlecab).  I thought it
 should be a slot in the backing plates where a flat driver could lever the

get yourself a copy of John Muir's Idiiot book, and also a copy of the 
Bentley reprint of the 63 ed Transporter Workshop Manual (covers 63-67).   
they are useful.

Jack up wheel, take off hub cap if there, there is a hole in each drum, 
rotate wheel till you find the adjusting stars through the holes.  They are 
next to each other in the rear, and 180 degrees apart in the front.
