just a short note on cold weather running with straight 30. I used castrol
straight 30 in my engine-until it gets chilly, i think if you check either

thinner-as it didn't want to turn over too easily for me yesterday in the
cold....after all my struggles with the 6v system, i am starting to bend a
little in the direction of 12V.
