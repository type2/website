Bendix manufactured Sapphire for VWoA starting in '60 with the Sapphire I
AM radio and it featured the large channel selector.
In '62 came the Sapphire II AM/FM
'63 brought about a new appearance 3/8"x5/8" buttons
'64 Motorola and Bendix both became manufacturers for the "I" series and
the differences for this were that the PCB and tuner insides were
different.
Bendix alone made the "II" for '64 and '65
'66 Sapphire III AM was made again by both companies and the "IV" AM/FM
was a Bendix only in '66.
Sapphire V was a 12V AM made by both for '67, but the Sapphire VI, also
12V and AM/FM was a Bendix only model.
By '68 AM only radios made by both companies and Motorola was the only
manufacturer from '70 and later.
