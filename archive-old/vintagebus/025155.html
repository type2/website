Today I got my rebuilt spindles from California Import Parts, made by
Wolfgang International.

I'm really turned off.

Little things:

everything painted over, including the rubber seals
paint in the link pin holes (let the customer scrape it out)
sandblasted sealing surface for grease seal ("braindead" comes to mind)

Unforgivable:

The inside bearing seat on one spindle is excessively worn and way out
of tolerance
(yes, it looks so bad that I checked and compared it to factory spec
wear limit)

Now I will have to send that garbage back.
It seems that some rebuilders think a sandblasted part is a rebuilt part
and customers are to stupid to notice anyway.
