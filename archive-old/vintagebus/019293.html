Sounds like either your clutch cable is about to break, or you've got a broken
pressure plate.  Either that or you had some kinks that have been stretched
out of the cable, and it needs adjusting again.

One thing to be careful of when adjusting the clutch is to not let the cable
twist; it causes the cable to break.
