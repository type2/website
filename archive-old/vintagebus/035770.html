if you have the metal tang at the guide under the front of your bus, I am 
guessing your bus is before 631  071.

if this is worn and no longer keeping the rod from moving sideways, you can 
swap out for a later guide, and put the bushing on your front shift rod, 
which would help shifting.   Having new bushings really makes it nice, IMOPBO.

both the later guide and the front shift bushing are available new.
