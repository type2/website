Hi Christensen,
You can if you change everything to 12 volt exept for the starter, fuel 
gauge, and wiper motor.  The 6 volt starter only needs a 12 volt solinoid on 
it to prevent hammering of the starter gear.  With this starter setup on your 
bus you can walk your bus over a couple of blocks in first gear if the engine 
stops running!  For the wiper, and fuel gauge you can use resistors.
