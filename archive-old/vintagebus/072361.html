[quoting Berg]
> "... we continually heard and still hear from many people we talk to today
> that want a 5 speed today, is that they are doing it to get an "overdrive".

While what Mr. Berg said here is true, there is a hidden fact that he
doesn't mention.  While VW 4th gear ratios are overdrive (less than
1.0:1), VW axle ratios are quite high numerically.  Some bus axle ratios
are over 5:1 where a 4:1 is considered _really_ high for a
"conventional" vehicle.  The effect of the high axle ratio is to negate
the top gear "overdrive" to some extent.
