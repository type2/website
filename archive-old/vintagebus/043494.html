That looks suspiciously like a Bosch part number.  A Thing-specific
VW part number would probably begin with 181, but as the wiper motor
was probably something they were already using on a Bus, Bug, or Type III,
it's more likely to have something like a 113, 211, or 311 VW part number.
A lot of electrical bits made by Bosch have both Bosch and VW part
numbers.

Someone with a VW parts book or possibly a Bosch service center could
look it up for you.
