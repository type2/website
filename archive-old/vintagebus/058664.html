I never used a nibbler before, but with my vert/bus rustoraton, I used a 
sawsall carefully and a die grinder with a 1/16 steel cutting blade in tight 
spots with good success.  The sawzall is very quick, but you have to be 
careful.  I butt welded all my replacements too.  Always remember, measure 
twice, cut once.  With butt welds, filling gaps via welding or strips of 
metal is a PIA.
