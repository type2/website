 Hi, Nate:
Spray carb cleaner at the throttle butterfly shafts outside the carb(s)
and see if there are leaks which temporarily seal and the idle speed rises.
Try replacing the 009 with a vacuum advance distributor.
check the timing advance, too much will cause backfire out the intake,
and possibly a mismatched curve will cause the problem at one point
and not at another.any wobble in the timing mark indicates wear.
it also helps to have the air cleaners installed- something about the "fog"
or fuel mist above the carburetor having an effect on performance.
Carbon build-up in the cylinders can hang up valves and cause backfire.
