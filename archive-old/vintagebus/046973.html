mesh grey was used in all 65s  for the upholstery at least, the deluxes and 
standards had the paneling too.  Also was used used in 66/67 standards.  
66/67 deluxes did not come mesh grey, they got mesh platinum if they were 
painted Velvet green/white or Titian Red/white, the Blue/white 66/67s had 
aero-baleric interior- which is a creamy white and a light metallic blue.   
The 66/67 deluxes had coloured (light blue or tan) mats on the floors and 
between the front seats and the cab doors.
