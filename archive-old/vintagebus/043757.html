>25. Brake fluid.  The higher the number the higher the boiling point!

DOT-3 brake fluid is the most common and fine for VWs.  DOT-4 is not
too hard to find, has a higher boiling point, and is compatible with
DOT-3.  DOT-5 is NOT COMPATIBLE with DOT-3 or DOT-4 fluids; you have
to flush out the whole system before switching over to DOT-5.  I think
DOT-5 is silicone oil based.

>38. Axle grease.  At least enough to repack one set of front wheel bearing 
>for one side.

If your Bus has them, would a tube of CV joint grease be a good idea?
If you have a real bad one there's not much you can do but if you
discover a torn boot or something you can replace the boot and refill
the grease.  You'd also need the tool that takes out the CV bolts
(Allen or three-square) and a spare CV boot.  

For a swing-axle Bus maybe a spare axle boot would be a good idea.
