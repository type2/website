I would skip the hassle and install the original type.  If you want to put 
the 67 type in you need to alter the bus, which is a PITA, and probably hard 
to get just right, though surely it is possible I have no doubt, I just doubt 
the return on the work.   Just replace all your shift bushings and reinstall 
the shift mechansim.  I have the earlier style in my 65 and it has nice tight 
shifting.    I have not seen any improvement in the 67 style in drivability.
