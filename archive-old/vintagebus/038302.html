Thanks to everyone who responded to my odd brake shoe wear dilemma. I have
checked the operation of the e brake cables, the wheel cylinders, the return
springs and whether the shoes contact the backing plate. Everything checked
out fine.

I am therefore going to assume that the mach who did my front end rebuild,
or one of his assistants, drove my bus with the e brake up and then
readjusted the shoes to hide the fact. Shoes so far out of whack I would
have noticed as there was about five full turns more adjustment on the e
braked shoes than on the others. I would simply not have bothered adjusting
shoes out that far as I don't believe its safe as the thread of the adjuster
is then not completely supported in the star.

I'm getting a set of shoes relined to match the drums(if the drums need it
they will be trued) at the moment and as soon as they get back from the shop
I will reassemble and test them out.
