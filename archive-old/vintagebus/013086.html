The socket will probably last forever, but it will kill a lot of
flex-handles and slide-handles in its lifetime!

For the torques involved in wheel nuts and gland nuts, the 1/2 inch
drive is really marginal.  I have a collection of broken 1/2 inch
drive handles to prove it.

I've been much happier with the 3/4 inch drive socket and handles I've
been using for the last few years.  The socket isn't that much more
expensive, around $20 or so.  This is for a lifetime tool that you
will never wear out unless you make a business of VW repair.

OTOH, it might be a good thing to have around with a 3/8 drive adapter
for removing the big nut on the fan.  The 3/8 inch drive would remind
one that, despite its size, the fan nut isn't a high torque part.
