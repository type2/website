I have new 12 solenoids or just the end caps if you need, Generally it's
just the contacts in the solenoid that went bad and not the electro magnet,
The end caps are pretty straight forward to change you just have to unsolder
the 2 connector wires and apart they come (after you remove the screws),
slip the end cap on, solder the wires back and you got a new solenoid.
