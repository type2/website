I usually wipe off the old grease with a rag or paper towel before I
start, mostly so I can better see the old grease coming out as I pump
the new grease in.  If I pump a lot of old grease back out I will
wipe it off again but if it's just a little I leave it.  The Bentley is
adamant about not getting any grease on the brake hoses or tires, and
wiping it off right away if you do.

In VW's "Journeys under difficult conditions" manual, for driving in
places with bad or no roads, VW says "The collar of grease which forms
at the lubrication points should not be wiped off since it guards against
the ingress of dust and sand."
