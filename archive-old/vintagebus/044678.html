You can put either 6 or 12 volt starters in VW with just a change of the
starter bushing.  As for the flywheel you have to put the flywheel with the
right teeth for the starter you are going to use.  If you go to the bigger dia
12 volt flywheel and put it into a pre-1967 tranny you will have to grind a
little off the inside of the tranny bell housing.  As to the clutch pressure
plate, disc and the flywheel having a clutch area of 180 or 200mm these both
where used for both 12 volt and 6 volt.  We prefer using the 200mm 6 volt
flywheel for both 6 and 12 volt systems with a 9 spring pressure plate not the
weaker 6 spring or diaphram style.  On our 12 volt VWs we just change the
solinoid to 12volt on the 6 volt starter.    Eric & Barb
