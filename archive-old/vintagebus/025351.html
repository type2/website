I just took mine apart last week and it was abreeze.If you have access
to an oxy-acetylene torch you can make short work of it by heating the
coupler cherry red and attaching a vice-grip to both ends rear shift
coupler end and front shift rod(F is F) and hit the foreward vice-grip
with a hammer and all SHOULD come apart.Only took about ten minutes and
I was ready to drop the axel.Make sure you follow general safety and
common sense rules as well as take out th front seat and empty the under
seat storage area and tire well and put in some wet rags to soak up the
heat on a bench model,on a walk thru take up the rubber mat between the
seats.
