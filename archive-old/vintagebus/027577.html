If the engine was not properly prepared by the owner for storage before he 
died and if iut was not kept up during the storage the engine is suspect and 
should be dissassembled and inspected.  When we bought our 1963 SC back in 
1986 it had sat for eight years just after having a complete engine rebuild.  
One of the long 40HP early rocker studs had pulled out of one of the heads 
causing a HP loss that the owner just gave up and parked it.  Once I got the 
engine running it ate it's bearings about 50 miles later due to the rust on 
the crank.  The oil in it during the eight years sitting was dirty, acidic, 
and had water in it!
