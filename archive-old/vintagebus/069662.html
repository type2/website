Subject: Re: [VB] Window doors & popout glass

> 66 door will fit just fine on a 67.

They may fit but they will not latch correctly.
The latches are different. The catches are different. The hole in the door where the latches are are
different.
They will not be flush when you mis-match them.
Here are pics of 66 latches and catches:
http://user1.7host_dot_com/aufevervw/66_pass_door1.JPG
http://user1.7host_dot_com/aufevervw/66_pass_door2.JPG
Here are pics of 67 latches and catches:
http://user1.7host_dot_com/aufevervw/67_pass_door1.JPG
http://user1.7host_dot_com/aufevervw/67_pass_door2.JPG
The 66 catch is held on with 3 screws. 67 is 2.
The pin on the 66 catch sits in farther then the 67.
The latch on the 66 is in farther than the 67.
You can put a complete 66 door on a 67 but the door will not be flush with the B pillar. And you cannot adjust
it far enough to be flush.....BTDT.
