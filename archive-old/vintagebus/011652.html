If all you want is FM reception, you can use a through-the-glass
antenna that sticks on the inside and outside of the window.  Check
out amateur radio stores for examples.

These antennas will _not_ work for AM, as they are designed to pass
VHF signals only.

Another route is to use a gutter-mount antenna and pass the coax out
through the air vents.  Don't know if there are any convenient air
vents on your bus, though
