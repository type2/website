Hi,
Most of the time the chirping goes away once the engine warms up.  If not,
then it is a sign of needing the valves adjusted.  Did you adjust your valves
at 0 degrees?  Alot of the original pulleys do not even have a 0 degree mark
on them.  We picked up a degree pulley years ago and use it only to find where
those original pulley marks are and mark where the 180 degree mark on the
original pulley.   Eric & Barb
