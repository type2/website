I was thinking about that later, and the "aa" cylinder is the one that
goes into the later US spec buses that DID take a servo.  The adapter
thingy is to take up the space where there would have been a servo in a
later aplication.
The one for the non-servo bay bus ( US 68-70, otherplaces in the world
later still, probably still in Brazil) is the 211-611-019 T. It is not
cheap either. But the 211-611-019 AA is the one to go with for the
reasons Eric and Barb point out.
