I saw a really awesome tubular steel bus roof rack at the Volunteer Bug
Jam in Knoxville, last weekend... and this particular roof rack had a
wrinkle that not many of them do.
	The forward-most section of the roof rack was designed to serve as a
spare tire carrier, shaped accordingly in a semicircular fashion to
allow it... which produced a neat effect when "added" onto a rectangular
roof rack. You could say that it was "kewl", but practical.
	The whole rack was very professionally done, nice clean welds and the
like. The downside was that the asking price was $750.00... too steep
for me. I just thought someone might benefit from the spare tire idea if
anyone's welding one up.
