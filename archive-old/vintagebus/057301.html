Subject: The Volkswagen Protest

The Vintage Volkswagen Club of Michigan
The MVVC is attempting to confront Volkswagen on the issue of the trademark and copyright lawsuits and their alienation of the air-cooled community. To do this we need your help. The company that made the cars we love is, as the saying goes "being penny wise, and dollar foolish." In an attempt to protect it's trade marked properties VW is loosing the die hard loyalists that it worked on for decades to build. To put it another way, sadly VW seems to be concerned only with the dollars earned now from it's legal tactics. Fill out the form, and show them how many more dollars they will loose later.
