I would have to agree with Micheele here.  The two tones schemes available mar
61 through jly 64 (ch # 749 001 up to 1 328 871 ) were
 turquoise/blue white, mouse grey.pearl white, and sealing wax red/beife grey.
The turquoise is generally described as a light green rather than a blue.

THe 65  (aug 64 to july 65, ch 225 001 to 225 190 000) were
sea blue/bluw white, velvet green/blue white, and sealing wax red/beige grey.
All 65s came with mesh grey upholstery, all passenger models withmesh grey
interior panels.
