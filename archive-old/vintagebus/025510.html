here's the results of last Friday's survey on occurences
of VW Buses in pop music.  This is crossposted to
type2 and vintagebus mailing lists, and the r.a.m.v.a.
newsgroup.

Thanks to everyone who participated!


1. Bus featured in Lyrics & Video:

Men at Work, "Down Under"

Diamond Rio, "Unbelievable"

3. Bus featured in Lyrics (unambiguously):

Arlo Guthrie, "Alice's Restaurant Massacre"

Susan Werner, "Born a Little Too Late"
Alex Call, "Volkswagen Bus"


4. Bus featured in Lyrics (ambiguously):

Paul Simon, "Ace in the Hole"
The Who, "Magic Bus"


5. Bus featured on Album Cover:

Bob Dylan, "The Freewheeling Bob Dylan"


6. Unknown, Snippets:
