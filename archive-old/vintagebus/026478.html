NQ,

Rods are typically rebuilt by shaving a bit off the big end mating surface and
honing back to the standard diameter.  .060 (not .60)/.080 (not .80) *should*
be bore oversize/thrust undersize. ie: the bore is .060 larger requiring
thicker bearings and the thrust surfaces are .080 thinner, requiring thicker
thrust washers.  You *could* measure to verify...
