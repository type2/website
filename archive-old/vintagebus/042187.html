Mine rubbed on the carb fuel solenoid and came close to wearing a hole in
itself.  The plastic was "crazed" around the wear spot, indicating to me
that it could have cracked easily.  Too close for me.  It only takes one,
and I don't want to be the one!

The other disadvantage to putting a filter in that location is that the
area over the distributor is one of the busiest locations on the engine.
It makes it harder to work on the carb, distributor, coil, brake vacuum
hose, or the filter itself.
