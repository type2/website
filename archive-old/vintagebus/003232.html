Mikie,
    I think there is no better way to start as a VW mechanic than to do a
brake job. Nothing hidden or not self evident in there, Just dirty enough
and the difference when you finish is a lovely thing. Also $ saved and you
will feel good. Buy all the bits, ( I recommend NEW wheel cylinders) get the
van up, get a friend if possible and go for it. I think the VW idiot book
(Muir) is the thing to have here. Try the local library, photocopy the brake
chapter. Good luck
    Barry Wright
