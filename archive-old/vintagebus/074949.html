  All I ever use is a punch and tap out the top one, then come in from the
top and tap out the lower one. I use a body dolly to tap the bottom bushing
out as there isn't much room. I have found this faster and just as easy as
using the correct tool due to set up time. If you have a hard time tapping
the old bushings out, use a hack saw blade or die grinder and curt a groove
in them. 
  Average time to do the total job 1 to 1 1/2 hours, start to finsh.

ronnie
