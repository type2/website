This sounds like a classic throw-out bearing symptom.  You will almost
certainly have to pull the engine to take a look.  Could be a bad
bearing, the throw-out arm may be misaligned, there may be a problem
with the pressure plate, or maybe even the clutch disk.  You may want to
try making sure the clutch cable adjustment is OK before you tear into
it.
