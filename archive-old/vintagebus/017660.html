What I did was to get the cheaper bench mount VW
engine stand, and then went to the discount store and
got the cheap engine stand for "regular" engines, and
bolted the VW stand to a chunk of 6x6 wood which I
then bolted to the "regular" floor stand.  It worked
well when I had the 79  2L engine out of my 75 bus,
held the weight fine, and worked great with the 1600 I
worked on to put tin the 65 double cab.  Total cost
was $80 or so I think, and I have a stand that I can
use for AC VWs or for the engine from my old Ford 9N
tractor if necessary.
