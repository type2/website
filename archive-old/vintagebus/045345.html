in july 62 they changed the shape of the spare tire bulge behind the front 
seat.  They did this at the same time they switched the wheel wells to flare 
a tad in the rear, the later doglegs in the front, and went to the 1/3 2/3 
seat.

So, if the 62 deluxe that the paness are from is a really late 62 with a 1/3 
2/3 front seat, they will fit. If it is the full width seat bus, the bunlge 
is different, and the panels that go on it are too.
