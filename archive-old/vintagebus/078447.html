You can put a reserve tap on a 62-67 tank, and surely on the early bay ones 
too if you want th 15gallon option.  Ignore the sender thing, I mean you need a 
sender to fill the hole but you don't have to use it.   I saw a 62-67 tank on 
thesamba for $10 recently.   Where are you located?   I have some spare gas 
tanks both w/ sender hole and not.  

One REALLY nice thing about the reserve tap is when you pull the motor you 
can turn it to off, and voila, no gas dripping issues.   The motor is out of my 
61, and that was joy, I tell you, joy.   And I pulled the old fuel line off to 
put new on  as it's easy to get to, and no gas drips out w/ the tap off.   
It's the og tap that came with the bus,  I rebuildt it w/ a WW kit that comes w/ 
instructions for a bug tap which is slightly different but no big deal.
