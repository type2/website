Subject: GL4 was: Parts stores

I remember reading a thread that discussed gear oil for trannys. The tread
said something about GL5 shouldn't be used for VW trans because eats the
brass synchros or something like that.

I talked to Rick Long about this and he said this was not the case. He said
that GL5 was the only oil you could use _if_ your brass parts were moly
coated. However VW brass parts are not moly coated and therefore GL4 or GL5
is fine.

Is he right or wrong?
