  I have run 6 volt starters on 12 volt systems for years without ill
effects (this way you won't need to change flywheel or do a lot of grinding
on the trans). You can change out the the solinoid to get some more life out
of it. You should not need the relay anymore as it will turn over twice as
fast. One of the tricks? I do on high compression engines is take a 6 volt
starter and have the armature turned down to accept a 12 volt bendix,
install a 12 volt solinoid and varooom instant high torque starter, have one
of those on my DD since I put it together 6 years ago.
