Hi Mike,

Whoa!  That will be way too much play!  1/8" is .125" and 1/32 is about 
.030", making it a big differance.  You will not be able to see the endplay 
distance visably between the MC and pushrod tip.  What you have to do is to 
back off the pushrod until you can get it to wiggle up/down slightly with 
your finger tips.  Then while wiggling the pushrod with one hand pull down 
the brake pedal with the other hand and visually watch for how much movement 
it takes for the pushrod to tighten up against the MC.
