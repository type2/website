Clara et al,
 A ute (short for utility) is a particularly Australian bit of work. Smaller
than a pickup, a model based on a car but with a tray integral to the body
rather than the bit with the back seat in. Mythology abounds, country boys
make them flashy and stickers saying "shoot ferals" are placed, they appear
in the city sometimes laden with professional vomiters, do wheelies & donuts
then go home."Sound familiar? Anything small with a tray is called a ute
here.
