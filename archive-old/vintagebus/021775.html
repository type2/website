Hi Virge,
Once the engine is back in the bus you adjust the thermostat.  You want to 
remove the bolt in the bottom of the thermostat bellows out.  Then adjust the 
mounting bracket so the top of the thermostat is just below the ceiling of 
the mounting bracket. You can fine adjust it a bit more by threading the 
bellows on or off a bit on the rod, but be carefully not to loosen it too 
much!  Then just pull the bellows down, and into it's mounting bracket hole, 
and bolt it back in.

help the rubber seals seal better, and check the bottoms of these for

a exaust valve or two.
