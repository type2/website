Hi Markus,
It does not really matter where you have the drive aligned as long as #1
cylinder is at TDC when the rotor is pointing to the notch in the distributor
that marks the #1 cylinder saprak plug location.  Never time by #3 or have
your #3 cylinder plug hooked into this notched mark area of the distributor.
