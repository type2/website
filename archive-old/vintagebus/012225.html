I've done something similar.  The holes on my stock exchangers had
"egged out" somewhat, so I made a 2cm wide split ring to go into the
gap and brazed it into place.  This closed the gap and made a wide
bearing surface so that the holes wouldn't egg out again.  I didn't
think of welding the box to the pipe, as the disparate thicknesses
were beyond my abilities with ox-acet equipment.
