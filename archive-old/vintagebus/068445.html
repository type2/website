>Is the front drum held on like an old bug with the twin crappy nuts?

Yes.  You'll need new lock plates for between the nuts, too.  I am told
that you can fit a clamping nut from a bay-window Bus onto late '64 and
up splitties, but I haven't tried it myself.  I do know that the clamp
nuts for a late Bug are too small for a split.
