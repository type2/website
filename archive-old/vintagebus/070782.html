CHANGES IN THE NEW SITE

The site is completely new from the ground up, and is about as close to
state of the art as a small business (without amazon_dot_com's budget) can
get. There are too many changes to go into here. Some are outlined at
http://venus.webappcabaret_dot_net/busdepot/help.jsp#ABOUTSITE  but you'll
have to browse around it to really get a feel for it. 

One big change is that we've increased the parts database from about
15,000 parts, to roughly 200,000 - making BusDepot_dot_com literally the
largest online consumer VW parts database on the planet.  We've combined
the complete product databases from a number of importers and
wholesalers, plus nearly the whole TMI catalog, and almost every
"dealer" part as well. Some of it is not yet cataloged by application
(this will take time), and there are some vendors who didn't get us
their files yet, so it will still get better. But even now, it's
probably safe to say that you're more likely to find a part on
BusDepot_dot_com than anywhere else. 

Yes, this means that BusDepot_dot_com now lists a full line of Volkswagen
CAR parts as well (and at our usual deep discounts). While our focus is
and will remain Busses and Vans, there were some logical reasons for
expanding to VW cars. About half of you also own a VW car, and we
routinely get inquiries from list members as to whether we can get them
comparable discounts on a part for their Ghia, Jetta, etc.  Of course,
many of our suppliers also carry VW Car parts, so we always had good
availability and the opportunity to buy them cheap. So we decided to
offer them to our existing customer base.  Adding them is a win/win
situation. Since we're already dealing with these suppliers anyway, it
doesn't dilute our Bus/Van efforts. But the added volume does give us a
lot more clout with them.  Which means we can negotiate sharper prices,
and demand better availability and quality, on the Bus and Vanagon parts
that are the core of our efforts.  So if you buy your VW Original Beetle
or New Beetle parts from the Depot, you'll not only get a killer deal on
those parts, but you'll also help ensure the future of good, cheap parts
for your Bus or Vanagon. Everybody wins.
