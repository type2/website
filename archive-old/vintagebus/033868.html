water dripping through the doors is normal- that is how it was designed.  To
protect the cab door panels I take clear plastic and attach it to the door
before installing the panel.   It is important to tuck the bottom of the
plastic inside the bottomof the door.  Yes- w/o the barrier the panel will
warp.  Originally your panels would have had a waxed paper barrier, long since
gone.  I attach the plastic w/bits of duct tape.  Also it is a bad idea to use
any sort of water aborbinginsulation in the cabdoors, as it will act like a
sponge and speed rust out.  I live in the PNW too and see most every day how
important it is to try and keep the water out AMAP.  Have your heat working as
well, to help dry out the bus.
