Subject: RE: [VB] stud repair

They have the over size 8mm/6mm stud replacement. I have used them before. I
just drill out the hole and tap for the 8mm threads and insert the new stud.
On one of the blocks I just threaded it in and went from there, on the others
I cleaned the block squeaky clean and coated the threads with JB Weld before
threading it in. Didn't seem to matter either way. I got my replacement studs
from a place called Tacoma Screw which deals only in hardware related items.
Maybe you have something like that in your local area.
