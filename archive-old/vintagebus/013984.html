H Steven Dolan <dworkin_at_mtolympus.ari_dot_net> wrote:
:  there are a number of switches on the dash, none labeled.  To the left of
:  the speedo pod, there is the cigarette lighter and a switch... headlights?

On my '66, that's the wiper switch.  Hard to tell in the picture if it
is a toggle or a twist type.  Toggle's non-standard.  Should be a
twister, going clockwise for on and more on.

:  On the speedo pod, there is a little switch at the bottom of the pod...
:  for what???

Rear compartment courtesy/work light.

:  To the right of the pod, there is a switch with unknown function...

Headlights.  Pull on.  Twist for dash brightness.

:  wipers? and a bezel for a switch that appears to be missing... for what?

Emergency flashers.  Pull on.

:  also, the ignition switch is sitting on (well, hanging off) the parcel
:  shelf in the first pic.  I assume it goes in the hole in the bottom of the
:  speedo pod in the second pic?

Yes.  There's a hold screw that goes in from the bottom, through the
horizontal plate that's hidden by the plate with the padding on it.

:  a cover for under the front (this bus has one, but it is in *sad* shape!)

Often referred to as a belly pan or nose pan.  As far as I can tell,
these are pretty much interchangeable for all of the '60s.  While
you're down there, check to see how many screws managed to rust into
the captive nuts and shear off.  I haven't quite gotten to the point
of drilling mine out, but since there are only two and half useable
ones left, it's rapidly becoming a priority....

:  a cover for the windshield washer bottle.  The rubber on mine is *toast*!

Good luck.  I believe RMMW carries this for bay windows, but not for
the splits.  I'd love to be proven wrong.
