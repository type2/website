Subject: Re: [VB] heat exchangers vs J tubes

ah, here we go with the difference between turbulent and laminar flow. the 
fins sticking into the tube make turbulent flow, mixing the air up, it is better 
for passing heat to the metal from  the air flowing past.  the smooth tube 
has laminar flow w/ air moving fast in the middle of th stream, but slow at th 
edge, and th slow air acts as insulation.  

so these fins make the heat exchangers exchange heat better.     Do they make 
the exhaust flow faster/slower?   I dunno.
