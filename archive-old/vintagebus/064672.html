 yes these can be rusted and a pain to remove.   If you don't hit them 
exactly so the piecs will grab each other.  I'd take a grinder and cut along 
the small piece you are trying to pound out.  You can buy new clamps (and 
rubber bushings) fairly inexpensively.

just bag them up.  If you clean them they'll rust.  I never clean engine 
parts if they be stored for more than a week.  In fact I sometimes oil or 
grease them.

 Last question now - I disconnected the link that goes from the steering box
 to the swivel pin at the swivel pin end.   However the link has a lot of

...
not clear which bit you are talking about.  Steering bar has a pit man arm on 
it.  sometimes the shaft that the pitman arm is on can be pulled in/out (or 
side to side depending on your frame of reference) No this isn't good, 
whether it's bad enough to rebuild is your call.  I've seen it often enough.  
driven with it.  it only gets more worn.   To the pitman arm it the drag 
link- whch has ball joint ends at front, and at back attaches to the assembly 
at the swing lever.   The swing lever should not have play.  Neither end of 
the drag link should have play.
