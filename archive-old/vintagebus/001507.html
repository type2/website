Jay,

I can understand your concerns with a heater and living up north.  But that
is where the late model advantage ends.(Unless you like plastic)  I got my
first split about 4-5 yrs ago, so I am by no means an old timer, but I drive
EVERYWHERE in my bus.  Some say I am a little nutty, but what can you
do....I say drive it.  Right now I drive a 63' Panel, with a bent up rusted
roof rack from my first split I got in an accident with.  It's not the best
thing on the road, but I got style!
You would also have to be somewhat mechanically proficcient, or at least
want to be proficient.  More than once I have been stranded, but nothing I
didn't see coming.

In short, if you want a car you can kick around a bit more, than go get
yourself a watercooled.  If you like the idea of making every drive an
adventure, the split is for you.
