floor for ice fishing, cut out bulkhead with swiveling lawn chairs, fuzz and
fur everywhere (great for velcro!), pull start 1600 motor...yes! pull

stainless wire.  It looks great and he said it works nice too.  I'd like to
do it...but I dunno about the fabrication part of the bit.  He has rolled the
bus once and I think he got hit another time.  he replaced a lot of the bus's
body metal but has yet to do the drivers side which currently has about 12
gallons of bondo on it.  many affectionately call it the "Bondo Condo".  The
thing is a beast.  Woody and a few others in the DBG will vouch for this!  i
went ice fishing with the fellow a month or so back...him in his bus and me
in Stella.  He threw on his tire chains and out we went.  We sat in that bus
for hours and the woodburning stove is VERY nice to have.  man that is a nice
setup.... anyways...back to the stainless part.
