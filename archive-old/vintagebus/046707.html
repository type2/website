Yes it is no problem and if you just remove the tap often to clean out the 
shavings there will be practilly no shaving getting into the oil.  Especially 
if you use something to reach up and over to wipe anything out the sump hole.

it's length and 8mm half it's length.  Eagle hardware have these in stock.  
Also this stud with a slightly longer length can be used to install a 
doghouse cooler on any pre-doghouse engines.   Eric&Barb
