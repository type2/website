The rating point of "continuous operation" is generally
taken as "normal operation", not drag racing;<) The point
is, if you need to run more than 5,000 rpm in the normal
operation of you engine, a stock crank will not work. Up to
that point, a stock crank can handle 5,000 rpm.

Berg lists a 5,400 and a 5,800 rpm cut out rotor. They
recommend the 5,800 rpm unit for the T2 because of the lower
gearing.
