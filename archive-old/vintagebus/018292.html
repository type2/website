First off, I'll do all I can to avoid commenting on your friend's bod.  I'm
sure he's a really nice guy and all...

Anyway, a bus does NOT have a removable pan.  You *CAN* replace the frame,
but it's a lot of work.  I'm getting ready to do this on my '54 (for the
second time <*sigh*>).  What might be the trick for his bus is to replace
the rusted out pieces in situ, one by one.  You could section in the 65's
frame rails, but you have 2 long rows to hoe: one cutting them out, and one
welding them in.  These pieces are also available repro. Check out
http://www.ahschofield.co_dot_uk/
