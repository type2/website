Jerry has the right idea, but be careful to use laminated glass for the windshield(s).
The side glass need only be tempered, but the front needs to stay in one piece if it is
cracked.  I changed all the glass is my 63 with glass cut at a glass shop.  For the
windshield pieces, laminated glass normally used for shower doors was used and was just
the right thickness.  (Not DOT approved I'm sure)  If you have saved you broken pieces
they can be used as patterns and to get the thickness right.
