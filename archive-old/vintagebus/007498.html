My 2 cents.

Clean the threads real well before putting the engine into
place, also clean out the threads of the nuts. Put engine in
place. Push the bolt through the hole. Place washer onto
bolt.  Hold the nut between forefinger and thumb. Place
middle finger against bolt threads while starting nut. Once
nut is started, it will go on just fine if you put a little
side pressure on it while spinning it down. Tighten with
torque wrench or calibrated arm.  Can do this with my eyes
shut.  Got to , can't see around the fan housing anyway.
