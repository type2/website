The discussion about gearing and tire size, rpm's etc. apply to how fast
the rear wheels propel the bus. The speedo is hooked up to the front
left tire, so the only variable there to consider is tire size. If
you're running big tires in the front, your speedo will read lower than
actual speed, regardless of your tranny gearing, engine size, rear tire
size.
