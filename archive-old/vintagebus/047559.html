Hi Jim,
A lightened FW is kind of like having a hammer made from styrofoam.  You can 
move it up and down quite fast, but you will not have the mass in it to push 
as much with it.  The stock wieght FW is a mass battery to keep the engine 
turning disspite the compression of the P&Cs.  With a lightened FW you can 
RPM up the engine faster to shave off fractions of a second in a quater mile, 
but it will not have enough mass to keep the engine turning over at idle and 
low RPM.  Thusly these engines with lightened FW have to be revved up to keep 
running when stopped at a light.  Also the stock wieght FW mass helps out to 
smooth vibrations of the engine.
