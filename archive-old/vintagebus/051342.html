I have a friend that double-trapped his line, but he had a pretty
extensive network of piping in his shop and they were spaced out quite a
bit. I agree that a trap upstream of the tank would be pretty hard on
the motor. Maybe running them a few feet apart might help? Does your
tank have a spigot at the bottom of it? I used to open that and let the
water come out (stand back) before starting a project or when the
compressor had not run for a while.
