> 4. With the exception of the blinker lights - are nose skins the same from
> 60 - 67?


I would hazard a 'yes' guess on this, as the clip we used for my 67 came
from a pre -64 unit.



> 5. Is it easier to get a nose skin with a dash already attached?

NOT necessary - the welding is straightfoward enough - you can cut in many
ways. You will have to rebuild the bottoms of the windshield frames anyway
- any clip you find will be rusted there. We used double-angled-iron
pieces which were slit and bent at the corners, then filled in. The spot
welds at the frame areas can be removed easily with a bodyshop technique
that Thom could prolly tell you - I don't know it but watched it done.
