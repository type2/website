I have a 67 seat which is canted out near the cargo
door, but the legs do have the diagonals. I think
measuring the distance between the outer legs (center
of to center of tube) and putting that on the list for
comparison might help.

Another thing to check for on the fold-down part: late
in 67 they put a round release knob on the side of the
folding part. You pull it upward and it releases the
seat back to fold forward (like on most models 68 and
later VW seats)

naoither thing you can do if they have any of the
original upholstery is check the color charts on
Wolfsburg West and at least narrow it down to a few
years.
