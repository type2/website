My April 61 15 window has a red dash.    I am not sure of the exact date of 
the change, but the 61 and earlier two tone buses had the dash the lower body 
colour.   Also the insides of the doors- that area above the door panel below 
the window frame- is the lower body colour.  My 61 was hit a few years ago 
and the $&*%$* smashed the drivers door- It now has a door off a 62 (3?) with 
the area there just above the panel beige grey.   The 62 (63?) bus it came 
off was a SWR/BG 23 window (that had had the sunroof left open in the rain 
for over ten years- sorry dead) with a BG dash.

This was a topic before - and I tried to do a survey on 61/62/63 bus owners 
whether the dash was originally top or bottom colour- I think it was around 
the 62 model years beginning, I'd have to dig up the numbers- though there 
were gaps.   all the 63s had dash the upper colour I believe
