Hi Pete,
You need a special spanner wrench to loosen the chrome collar around the 
switch handle.  Or you can carefully turn/wiggle the switch body with one 
hand and work the collar loose with the other at the same time.  Once the 
chrome collar is loose remove it and then the switch handle.  Install a dash 
light bulb and reverse procedure.   Eric&Barb
