On Wed, Aug 20, 2003 at 10:07:40PM -0400, Diane wrote:
> Replacing a tranny or motor mount .. how difficult can that be ?? I
> mean, if you have someone to take out the motor .. and lets say it
> is the tranny .. For beginners, how difficult a job is it to replace
> one.  I am 99.9% sure that is my problem. [one of them.]

If the engine is out, the 2 rear ones are pretty easy (although I
suspect the real problem is the front one). you put a jack under the
tranny, then unbolt two BHB (big honkin' bolts) that hook the mounting
bracket to the bus itself. then 4 nuts on each mount and they are out,
replace in opposite order. I'm not sure how easy the front mount is
with the tranny in. I am pretty sure it can be done, but be careful
and it will be a nuckle buster.
