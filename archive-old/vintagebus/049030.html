1959 Westfalia SO23
Parked in 1976, and sat until 1998-99, rescued and sitting again during restoration

I diassembled the rear cushions so that the seamstress could make patterns from the old cushion covers in order to make the new cushion covers.  The horsehair pads LOOK almost new, no indentations from big, wide butts sitting on them, and very little hair silt from crumbling/decay.  All in all they're still very intact. I'm sure they'd look good forever if never used, but I plan on using this camper after the initial honeymoon/show tour/paranoia months pass.
