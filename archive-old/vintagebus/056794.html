Subject: Re: [VB] Things to do while yanking my engine

new fuel line
make sure your fuel line and electric wire grommets are good
check axle boots- replace if cracked or torn
change gear oil (this includes RGBoxes)
rear brake hoses if needed.  bleed rear brakes (easier with engine area empty)
starter bushing good?
grease clutch cable threads
main seal /FW oil ring
Clean engine compartment (you may want to do this before pulling motor)
if fuel sender is bad replace it. (think new ones are about $35, I put one in 
my westy)
engine to body seals
check tranny mounts.

not clear on why you want to change the exhaust valves.  Is the compression 
good?  If it ain;t broke don't fix it.   If I was getting heads rebuildt I'd 
probably buy new ones, and check the rings/ps/cs and swap in new rod 
bearings.  But if the engine doesn't smoke or have bad compression, I'd leave 
it together, just check head nut torque.
