A loose setscrew at the rear coupling will do this...even easier if there
are two loose.  It still has enough grab to slide fore and rear, but not
enough for the side to side.  Possibly.

tight you tighten, it doesnt help....we welded the area back up and
drilled a nice new hole for the set screw..

you cant get help immediately.  if it's loose, it's a problem.
