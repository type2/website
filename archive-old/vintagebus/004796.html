You can't pull the tranny and leave the reduxes on. You have to open the
reduction boxes to get the circlip off to pull the bearings and remove the
boxes from the axle, then remove the axle from the crown gears. You can run
the late tranny, it is just geared a tad taller. 14" rims, 15" rims,
whatever makes you happy. It seems kind of odd that the old fellas assurance
is free but the guarantee costs money. Pretty neat trick, that. Good luck
with it.
