sounds like you got some wires hooked up wrong.  Print out a copy of the 
wiring diagram for your year bus off Thom's webpage, and make sure all is 
hooked up right.
I bet you somehow crossed the gen light and the dash light wires somehow.  

just be methodical.  It is all colour coded, and the wiring diagram shows 
which terminal on the switch gets which colour wire to it, like it'll show a 
15 by the black wire, meaning the black wire plugs into the connection at 15, 
a 30 by the red wire, so dont plug the red wire to 57a on the headlight 
switch, plug it into the 30.

BTW, what year is your bus, the dimmer switch is totally different on 66/67 
than on earlier buses
