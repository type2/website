The new type 1 beetles cost a little over 8000.00 dollars in Mexico now. You
can go to the dealer and buy one if you have Mexican Citizenship or a
residence in Mexico. There was a time when they were 6500.00 for the City
model (carbureted engine) or 7500.00 for the Classico model, FI, alarm, AC
and chrome hubcaps. They have discontinued the City model and currently only
make 1 model, kind of half way between the City and Classico. If you go to
Nogales Mexico, you can pop in at Beetlemex's conversion shop and watch them
transform new beetles to early pans to bring in the U S. You could probably
buy your F I engine from them there also, though I haven't heard of them
selling F I engines. There is one company in the U S that imports both the
turn key carbureted and the Turn key F I engines, but then again I haven't
seen them with any F I engines as of late, only the turn key carbureted
models. Again these are Beetle engines and NOT bus and there is a
difference. I tore down a Beetlemex and a VW Mexico replacement engine and
they have different pistons. The Beetlemex were flat top and the OE VW
engine has the low compression dished pistons. The other difference was in
the valve size. The Beetlemex engine had larger valves then the OE VW
engine.
