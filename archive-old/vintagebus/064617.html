never anything wrong with looking at generator output.  however, when you say 
you cleaned all the connections, did you clean the fuse box connections?   I 
would disconnect the ground strap from battery and just unscrew the wires 
from fuse box one at a time, clean the ends of the screws and wires with fine 
sandpaer, apply dielectric grease, and reconnect. (your 60 is screw terminal, 
ya?) is it the 8 fuse fusebox or the 6 fuse fusebox?

Remove the headlights bodily from teh bus, and examine all connections 
between headlight and bus.  I did this to my 53, and the headlights got way 
brighter, even though they still don't get the full 6.5 or whatever V the 
generator puts out.   

clean connections at dimmer switch on floor.

the turn signal slowing down means it gets less voltage.  you still have an 
unclean electric path somewhere- evidently in the headlight circuit.
