Two part, or catalyzed paints are basically resins.   They chemically cure and
should be hard enough to sand and buff with in a day or so.  In fact, I've been
told that you should do your sanding and initial buffing while the paint is
relatively "soft" or the blasted stuff is almost too hard to  sand.  You can do
it, the effort is just greater.
