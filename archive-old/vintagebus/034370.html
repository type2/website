Fair enough for a 'new' box.  I think these prices refer to 'rebuilt'

As a point of interest I pulled down a 'rebuilt' US box
(don't know by whom - came in bus) and found that the
ball bearings weren't  the right size.  A little OS,
which meant 11 (instead of 12) were fitted with a little
'gap'.  I could feel the 'roughness' caused by this - no
hangups, no sticky bits, just a little bit 'graunchy'.
