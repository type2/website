any of you are not from the region and thus unfamiliar

Skyline Chili, no beans, and you generally eat it on
hot dogs (cheese coneys) or spaghetti. With the
Cincy-style chili, I can  prepare a huge pot a day in
advance and freeze it, then warm it up on Saturday so
no one has to spend the entire day cooking.  So,
everyone should bring appropriate dishes such as hot
dogs, buns, cheese, pasta, garlic bread, etc...
Of course we will be doing breakfast burritos on
Saturday morning, so bring an ingredient for that as
well.
REGARDING CANOEING:  If you think you will want to
participate in the canoe trip, please tell me ASAP so
I can give the canoe livery folks an idea of how many
will be going.
