[Backround Info]

neighborhood.  I was taking off in first gear nice and slow from a stop
sign. I left the bus in first for a few feet, because the road ahead was
bumpy and rough. 


[THE PROBLEM]

coming from the rear. Almost like if i were accellerating, breaking hard,

thought about it for a few.  This now happens in low RPM's in first gear,

of whining and other yucky noises.
