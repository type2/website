The part # is 0332 015 001. I bought it from Julianna Co, 800-334-0476.

It mounts a bit differently from the 6V equiv. The 6V piece grounds thru the
cap via a clip that mounts to a hole in a boss under the dash. The new piece
has a separate connector on the circuit board for the ground. Just run a
jumper from this lug to ground.

I think there is a schematic for it somewhere on the net. I'll poke around
and see if I can find it.
