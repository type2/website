Hi Wayne,
Here in the USA VWs from the early 50s on up could not be legally imported 
new with a radio in the dash.  Apparently USA radio manufatures did not like 
the idea of not having much of a chance to sell thier radios if the imported 
automobiles came with thier own factory radios!  So for our market all VWs 
had to have a block off plate.  The 1955-66 and the 1967 were differant.  The 
1967 one is just a 1958-67 bug without the three holes in it for the bug 
chrome strip.  For the 1964ish-67 time period the Sapphire 3 and 4 were sold 
at the dealerships.  Also Many other makes of radios of domestic and imported 
including under and in dash pull out radios were availible from VOA dealers.
