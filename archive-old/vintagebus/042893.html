Put about 1200 miles on the '57 bus this weekend, and I'd have to say that
it was pretty successful.  The girlfriend and I left friday morning and
got to Boise friday evening and went camping at "Westys in the Woods" put
on by the Bus Pilots Association.  Since it was the beginning of the
weekend, there weren't many busses out "in the woods" yet, so my bus was
one of two splittys.  It was a load of fun!  to get to the campsite, we
had to drive many, many miles on bumpy dirt roads.  I had to stop a couple
times to put my aircleaner back on!  After the camping trip we cruised
down to twin falls for a wedding, then back to Boise.  Anyway, the bus was
running pretty hot almost the whole time, and noticably hotter after the
camping trip.  I think it sucked some dust.  Anyway, I don't think it will
survive in Calif. so I'm not taking it down.  But I'll be there with the
Bus Pilots!  See ya in California!!!
