I think all the bay-window tanks will fit.

Get the filler neck and connecting hose, as well as the tank.  Using a chisel
or grinder, remove the triangular collar from the new filler neck.
You'll also need to extend the straps that hold down the tank.

I think it's 15 gallons, vs 10 gallons.
