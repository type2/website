how obtuse can you be? i remember tellen one of my local buddies how would you

the thing is... look at the part. how many ways has it got to go together? what
can the part tell you? Where are the wear markings; where did it rub off the
paint? were did the engine oil deposit on it? what was hidden from the spray?
nice and clean or dirty and worn? were did the PO rattle can spray paint it and
do a lousy mask job? what holes are ovaled and why? Why is a washer cupped a
certain way? think air flow, torque or gravity effects... do you load the dish
washer cups up or cups down? how did Fauns the junior engineer who designed
your parts at volkswagen load his dishwasher? why are there holes/openings
where there are? Do you get my drift?
