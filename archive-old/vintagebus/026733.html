Anything with "isocyinates" is dangerous.  If you are using anything with
"isos" especially in an enclosed environment you need to use a positive
pressure breathing system.  On the other hand, I have used PPG DP products for
quite some time with only a canister mask.  The key for me is that this is in
a outside environment.
I've only sprayed small parts, so the volume of mist in the air is low and I
stay up wind of the over spray.

You need to decide how much your future health is worth?  Are your lungs worth
$200 dollars or so.  I am going to build my own out of a small raft inflator
air pump and scrap vinyl tubing.  I'll purchase a over the counter hood.

Isocyinates are odorless and tasteless, so you'll never no you are breathing
them till it is too late.
