When you say b/d do you mean barndoor?

The barndoor reduction boxes are different than "normal" ones, as are the
spring plates.

The center sections (the transmission itself) are interchangeable, but the
mount from a pre-59 1/2 bus needs to be matched to the same type of
nosecone, unless you get one of those adapters from wolfgang.  To do this
conversion you need a later nose cone *without* a reverse light.  Ask me
how I know.

I don't know about the interchangability of the rear-end components.  I'm
going to guess they are not compatible.
