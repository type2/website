Here's what the "Old Cars Price Guide" has to say about ballpark values
for a mid-60s single cab:

1--mint condition (trailer queen):  $11,500
2--well-restored or excellent original: $8,100
3--older resto showing wear, or partly restored but complete:  $4,600
4--driveable, may need minor work, or deteriorated resto:  $2,300
5--needs complete resto but isn't wrecked:  $1,400
6--parts car:  $450

The prices represent data from "collector car auctions, expert opinion,
and verified private sales".
