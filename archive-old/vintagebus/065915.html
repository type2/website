Kevin, never had a problem with using 8 dowel cranks with factory 4 dowel fly
wheels when the pattern and dowel size is the same. There was 6mm and 8mm
dowels out there so make sure you get the right crank. Also make sure you are
not getting a wedge mated crank, it won't work with a factory fly wheel.
