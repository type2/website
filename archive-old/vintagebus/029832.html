My 62 has 4 of these brackets all together.  2 of them are mounted on the bulkhead for the upper portion of the seat to be adjusted...and the other 2 are mounted on the... seat stand should i call it?... so that the bottom portion of the seat can be adjusted too.  I have mine all the
way back...both of them..... and I still get cramped in there...  but I like the 'bouncy' effect of the seat...

Also...be careful with these brackets... as they like to bend...the metal between the adjusters that is.. its thin.  I had to work with mine a bit to get the seat to fit in it just right.
