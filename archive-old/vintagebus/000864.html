 Ok, this one has to be the least expensive! For starting spark plugs
without any danger of crossthreading: cut off a piece of old garden hose
just the size for holding the top of the plug while you insert it down thru
the sheetmetal and gently "feel" it start into the head (with a little
antiseize on the threads). I keep several lengths on the tool cart. Works
great.
