 I had the same prob... not the neighbors tho.... the SO...well, me too, it
was kinda loud in the shop with the doors closed. Solution... build a closet:)
used 2x4s for the frame, left about a foot around the sides & the top. Then
enclosed the frame with 1/2 plywood... then take foam insulation sheets, cut
to size, glue on the inside of the closet... It isnt silent, but you can stand
next to the closet & talk... close the doors of the shop... cant hardly hear
it at all:)
 Made a small door so ya can get in there to drain the tank.... a couple of
small holes for the cooling of the motor & compressor.... its a 60 gallon, 6
1/2 HP, two stage, 220 unit....
 Now as far keepin the impacts & air hammers quite.......well.... when ya find
out, let us know.
 Hope this helps,
