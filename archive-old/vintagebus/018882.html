There are several options.  Harbor Freight has a round oribital
sander that actualy works "ok" and it's only ~$50 or so.  If you
want the real deal, step up to a National Detroit, but that will
run you ~200-250.

The straight "spin" sanders don't work well for this kind of operation, as
they take the material off way too fast, even with fine paper.

There are "dual-action" sanders, with a locking collet to function
as a spin sander, or let the collet rotate freely and it works as
an orbital .  The HF version of this one is poor, but maybe that's cause I
have the National Detroit version of it and know better!
