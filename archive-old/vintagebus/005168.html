Well, first you need to know that it is a PRESSURE light, not a TEMP light
:))
You turn the key on and nothing, no light, correct? Leave key on. Go to the
sending unit to the left of the dist., disconnect the wire, ground the wire
to the block. Go look at light. Is it on? If yes, replace the sending unit.
Is it not on? Either bulb is dead or the wire from the bulb to the sender is
open.
