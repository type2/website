CMeanley_at_aol_dot_com wrote:
:  other is missing the swaged end. Any suggestions other then the obvious 
:  replace the cable, maybe some easy home brew way. Thanks all

Bend the end over into a half-circle.  Hook a cotter pin on it.  Slide
the cotter pin into the barrel nut and tighten down.  A PO did this on
one of my buses a while back, and it worked fine for years.  Made
engine R&R a little more interesting, because you had to keep track of
the cotter pin, but that's about it.
