VW seem to use up the old parts before starting in with the new ones.
I have a very early '64 model year with 15" rims which I believe to be
original, even though one would assume a '64 had 14".
VIN should be on both the M-code plate behind the passenger seat and
on the right side of the engine compartment close to where the apron
bolts on. I think some busses, not mine, also have a tag on the right
side of the freah air vent.
