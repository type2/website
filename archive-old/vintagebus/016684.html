If you leave the hoses off, you decress the effective volume over the 
cylinders, heads, and oil cooler.  The air isn't that much cooler
than the engine compartment, as it comes out of the engine compartment
itself, so I doubt you could measure the difference.  I think you would
do more harm than good.

What some people do is block the outlets off to increase increase the
amount of air over the cylinders; if youre heater boxes are not 
sealed perfectly, this seems like it would help.  I don't buy the 
need to cool the exhaust for 1 & 3.
