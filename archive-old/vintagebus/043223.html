I put up with the notched tranny accelarator cable syndrome for years. I
even used to throw grease in the notch! I don;t know what I was thinking
back then, but hey, I was a kid I guess. But now that I grew up, I solved
the problem years ago by just slipping a length of fuel line over the
damned cable, long enough to shield it from the tranny, and all has been
well ever since. Why I didn't think to do this twenty-five years ago will
always remain one of the mysteries of my life.
