dash is too low, but detailed with a grab handle and radio.
Small round tail lights, no front turn signals or semiphores, but push
button front door latches. Bottom says SS5403, Made in China, Mfg with
permisson of VW AG. Pull it back spring action. I paid about $4, cool toy.
