I didn't have much luck with the WW horse hair.  On the '55, I used
a layer of denim glued to the springs, then a layer of this brillo-pad
like stuff, about 3/4" thick, on top of that, then 1" (I think) 
upholstery foam on top of that.  They came out sweet!  When I did the
60, they were out of the brillo stuff, so they sold me this felt padding
instead, which I think works even better. 

It took me about 30 - 45 minutes per seat half, which doesn't include
prep work like removing the old stuff or painting the frames.
