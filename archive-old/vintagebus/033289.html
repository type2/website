Hi Andrew,
The bug and Ghia got seat belt mounting points (Threaded holes built into the 
VW) in 1963.  The bus did not have these seat blet points as a standard till 
1968.  Best you go out to your local wrecking yard and take a gander at a 
post-1967 buses for proper seat belt mounting positions.  Then it is up to 
you wether to cut these points out and weld them into your bus or just 
drawing up exact locations and drilling these holes in your bus.  If the 
latter make sure to use very large OD thick washers to prevent the belt mount 
from just pulling through the sheet metal.  Plus remember your fuel tank is 
just behind the panel/firewall behind the rear seat!   Eric&Barb
