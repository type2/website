Subject: Re: [T2] .009, There MUST be some advantages

009s are available new, and parts are readily available too.

However, check the advance curve of those 10 009s and you'll get 8 different
curves! Quality control sucks on them, they are not the same old German 009s
available 20 years ago.

009s also do not have the #3 retard that is nice on early engines
(pre-doghouse). Most of the reasons for the prevalence of the 009 is monkey
see-monkey do.

When you consider the facts, there is no reason to use the 009 since there
are other better options out there, but the 009 is available new (so it
"must" be good) and it's cheap.
