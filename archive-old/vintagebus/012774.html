See http://www.sema_dot_org/fedleg/stateredhot/ for more info on Scrappage programs in California and other states.

It looks like other states are dealing with the same issues right now.

The essence of the California bill, is that if the car is sent in as part of the Accelerated Vehicle Retirement program (voluntary, or not) it is crushed.  Parts cannot be taken from any vehicle that is to be scrapped under the program.  Despite the difficulties that it appears to be having, what I have read from other sources as well as SEMA is that there is a real likelihood that it may just pass.  Although I do not live in California, other states ARE following California's lead in this.
