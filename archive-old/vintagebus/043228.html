There was some talk about factory re-conditioned motors recently and I am
quoting from a unit number survey, supplement #6 printed in 10/75 in
Germany which states:
"With effect from October 1968 the series engine number will be ground out
during the reconditioning process and the exchange engine number will be
stamped-in in its place.  An exchange engine can be recognized by the
exchange symbol in front of the exchange engine number."
The exchange symbol pictured there is the typical VW logo surrounded by
two curved arrows.
