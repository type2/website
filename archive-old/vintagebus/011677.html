Hi there everybody
For those of you who remember, about a month ago I was wobbling between 
replacing my bus and fixing it.  Well, it's fixed.  Woo-hoo!!
I called every mobile welder in my area (about 15 guys) and one was 
actually willing to weld up my bus.  So he did.  After I did the 
grinding.  Next time I'll wear gloves.  Ouch. 
$400 later, me and my crusty, rusty, barely holding together hippy bus 
are back on the road.
I ground off the old welds and the broken shock mount, and the welder 
fixed it all up.  I'm running upside down type 3 shocks (KYBs) and it 
rides great.  I'm so impressed I'm tempted to take a long trip and 
torture you all with a description of it :-)  
One small setback: my wiring (two fuses worth) was heating up like crazy 
on her maiden voyage, plus the turn signal switch started smoking (uh 
oh) so I had to pull over and find the problem: on the back side of the 
frame where the new shock mount was welded on, the wiring harness was 
stuck to the frame a little bit.  I pulled on it and felt it pop off 
(just a very tiny bit was stuck) but I fear further problems down the 
road.  Since I can't really reach in to where the problem is (was) I'm 
not sure what to do.  Probably I'll just Mickey Mouse something to keep 
the wiring from touching the frame again.  I know, I know, BAD Veedub 
owner, but at least I didn't ignore the hot wiring and drive on until it 
caught fire.  I never thought to check for wiring back there beforehand, 
so it's my own fault and I guess eventually I'll probably have to pull 
out all those wires and fix them, but for now everything seems OK.  
Touch wood.  

I took her to the great Canadian VW show yesterday, and although I 
didn't find any parts, there were some nice campers to check out, and a 
couple of kids wanted to buy my bus then and there.  Of course I 
declined, although I was so proud somebody else actually thought my bus 
was KEWL!  They even said they would have voted for me if I had entered 
the show (people's choice) but I'm just waiting for a show with a "worst 
of" category :-)  

Diana
