The tank has a capacity of 40 litres (10.6 U.S. gals., 8.8 imp. gals.)

<picture of hand opened the gas tank cover, has the all the way around seal

<picture of fuel tap knob and its three positions>

the fuel tap is operated by a push pull type knob from the drivers seat.  
Under normal conditions the knob should be push fully home.  The tap is then 
in the open position.

If the engine begins to "stutter" as a result of lack of fuel, just pull the 
knob fully out to switch the tap to "reserve".  A fuel reserve of 5 litres 
(1.3 U.S.gals., 1.1 Imp. gals.) will then last for a further driver of about 
50 kilometers (30 miles).  It is important to push the knob fully home again 
when filling the tank, otherwise there will be danger of running out of fuel 
on the road.  With the knob pulled halfway out the fuel tap is fully closed.
