Hi Jdd,
You really need to Bentley reprint of the VW bus shop manual for 1963-67.  
The idiot manual just does not cut it.
Do not just stop with the one lower outer bearing keep taking it apart until 
you have all four per side out.  Best way to get off the outer lower for 
1955-63 RGBs is to remove the drum, then outer lower bearing cover four 
bolts, undo the brake line to the wheel cylinder and pull off the backing 
plate with all of the brakes system off as one unit.  Take a 17mm allen 
wrench or socket and make sure both the drain and fill plugs are loosened 
slightly and drain the oil out.  Remove the RGB half bolts.  Then use a 
hammer on the side of the 17mm allen wrench or socket to push the outer half 
of the RGB outward and at the same time slide off the outer lower bearing.  
We lightly polish with 400+ wet dry sandpaper the area this bearing rides on 
the stub axle to allow a little easier installation/removal later.
 
<<So, I'm hoping that I can replace the gaskets under the side covers of my

forward as I originally thought.  Will I need any special tools for this?>>
You will need a very large C-clip compresson tool (Got our from Proto), and 
remove the engine and tranny.

direction as when it is mounted in the VW.
