John,

Not sure about the tin, but it would seem any late model tin would seem to
fit nicely. As for the heat, for lack of a better term, I call them 'heater
mufflers'. I attached mine to the heater tubes on the Bus using the stock
clamps and 13mm head bolts and then used some fresh air hose, 3" ID, to
attach to the heat exchangers. To connect the fresh air hose to the heater
mufflers, I used 3"OD PVC pipe, a couple of worm drive clamps and it flows
nicely.
Good luck,
Gary
