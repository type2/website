I'm definitely no carb genius, but I have to agree with eric and barb on
this issue.  If a carb has too big of venturi no amount of re-jetting will
correct the problem.  My buddy bought a sandrail with a burned up engine
with dual webbers.  After fighting jetting and other things, he let a carb
guru look at it.  It was discovered that instead of dual webbers, it had 2
single webbers on it.  A single intake was installed and it ran perfectly.
Erin, my type 4 in my old rail had 40's on it and it ran like a striped ass
monkey!  I think I still have the intakes too.  Go with the dual webbers and
save yourself the headache!  If you decide this is what you want to do maybe
we can do a trade for something!
