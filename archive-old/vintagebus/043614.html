Both filters probably have relief/bypass valves, but the one in the
Wix filter is actually working right.  The Fram bypass valve is plastic,
while the Wix one is metal with a rubber seal.  Also, for one size of
filter, the Fram filter has 193 square inches of filter area, while
the Wix has 349 square inches.  These tidbits are courtesy of the
