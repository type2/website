Hello All,
 Last night I was in the shop lookin at the dents in
the bumpers of the S/C tryin to figure out how to fix
em...  this morning it came to me:)
 A friend of mine does mok-ups on plane parts....
pullin molds in other words... thats where the idea
came from... what I came up with was to pull a mold
off a straight section of the bumper, inside & out...
fiberglass will do the trick witha little release
agent... take the mold & cut them in half to reveal a
perfect profile of the bumper. Run down to the metal
recycle place & pick up 2 chunks of 6x6x4 mild steel.
Throw the steel on the milling machine, then we can 
build a set of dies to put in the 20 ton press to
press out the bumpers to thier original shape:) May
have to throw a little heat to em.... Im not crazy, am
I?
 Sounds doable... anyone ever tried it?
