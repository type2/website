Try contacting the Northeast Illinois VW Association (NIVA).  There are

River near Byron.  I remember an old VW place near Dundee I drove by a
few years ago.  They had a bubble window double cab on the lot.  Haven't
been back that way for a while.

find a place that can do the work.
