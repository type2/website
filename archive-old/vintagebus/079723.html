radio, etc real great, but as soon as you put a load

fixed it up good.

> Checked it again and found a nice hole burnt/blown thru
> the back of the housing..... 6V doing 12V duty maybe?

That might be part of your problem but it's not your
entire problem.  The currents in a 12 V Bus are half
of those in a 6 V Bus, so in one sense, the switches
and wires from a 6 V Bus are twice as good as they need
to be for 12 V.  (Look at the wiring diagrams for early
and late Buses...the wire cross sections didn't change
when they went to 12 V.)
