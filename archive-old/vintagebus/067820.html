Subject: RE: [VB] Engine Run On!

When I had my "baby dells" on my 1776 that I picture on my trike site I ran
into a similar problem. My fuel system was a Facet electric pump with block
off plate and a pressure regulator. I had started getting run on starting
about 150 miles and found that it was because of pressure in the fuel line
overcoming the float bowl and forcing fuel into the engine even though the
spark was missing with the key off. I bought an inline electric solenoid fuel
valve that shut the line down when power was removed. Worked like a charm
after that. I later found out that there was a better float bowl kit for the
dells but never bothered to purchase it. The engine was sold and the new owner
inherited the problem.
