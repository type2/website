I've used a huge 3-arm gear puller where I put each of the arms around the
shaft and slowly increased the pulling force while I hit the underside of
the outside ring of the wheel with my palms.  In retrospect, I think I got
extremely lucky in not cracking or chipping the wheel.  The best method
is to have a real puller with a 2-piece plate that evenly distributes force
around the whole wheel collar, IMHO.  Can these be rented from a local parts
place?
