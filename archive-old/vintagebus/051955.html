with a couple VW bus brothers and sisters and you will have enough karma to
last a year!  I am always amazed by the VW community and the sub-culture
surrounding it, in a good way of course.  

Since you are in Atlanta area definitely join the Peachstate Transporters...
I think somehow related to NEATO, but not sure.  No dues or anything, just
cool people hanging out together.  In the summer we hang out a lot.

Also if you are in the south-east or anywhere in the world actually!  The
www.fullmoonbusclub_dot_com is great for VW community information and has a
relaxed mailing list for all sorts of VW stuff that is bus related.
