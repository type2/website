Well, summer has almost slipped by us once again...

That means it is time for Peach State Transporters "End of Summer
Campout" at Duckett Mill Campground on Lake Lanier, just outside
Gainesville, GA.  All VW bus owners and enthusiasts and their
families are invited to attend.

Duckett Mill is a great Corp of Engineers campground on Lake Lanier.
The campground reaches out like fingers onto the lake and there is
easy access to the lake from almost every campsite.

*Saturday night I will grill burgers and hotdogs for everyone
*Sunday morning I will cook breakfast (bacon, eggs, & pancakes) for
everyone.

Directions from Atlanta:
Take 400 north to Exit 17, turn right on Hwy 306, turn right on Hwy 53,
turn right on Duckett Mill Rd. which ends at the campground.

The telephone # for Duckett Mill is (770) 532-9802.  Additional info
on camping at Lake Lanier can be found at:
