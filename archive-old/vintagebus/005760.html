Apply it to the cable INSIDE the sheath. You should be able to pull the
steel strand cable out of the sheath at the speedo after undoing the clip at
the left front hub cap and unscrewing the knurled connector from the back of
the speedo. It'll be greasy/dirty coming out. Wipe it as clean as you can,
then apply grease as you push it back into the sheath.
