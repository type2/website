The silicone is mostly for redundancy.  The hose clamp has a "plain"
portion that is a very tight fit over the round heat riser pipe.  Most
of the silicone is squeezed out in the process.  When I disassemble it,
the silicone has always sealed perfectly (no signs of leakage at all).

I trust the hose clamp a lot more than I trust the flanged connections
on the ends of the risers, at least until I file them flat! :)
