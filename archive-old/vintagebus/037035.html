Hi Kevin,
You just need to replace two of the upper inner head studs with longer ones.  
The DP engines from Germany used a thinner head stud that the previous SP 
heads.  Thinner studs apparently stretch better to keep from pressing the 
head and cylinders too much when the engine is heating up to operating temp..
