If there is any way he doesn't want this, please let me know!  I've put
active searches for a Double Cab out there... if he wants something else
(like a single cab, camper, panel, etc.) I would gladly help in a search.

As for value... if it indeed has little rust and no or few small dents ...
easily $1500 to $3000 is what I am finding.  Put in a new tranny and a solid
engine, get the brakes working and steering tight... suspension in order...
interior cleaned up... you are up to $5,000 to $6000 in value.  Get it
painted and looking real nice... $8,000+?

This is based on the searches I have found.  The only started producing the
Double Cab in 1958... 1962 is still pretty old (and of value).
