Subject: Re: [VB] Rear shift rod coupler replacement

what year is your bus?  

I think 60-66 1/3 you just undo put the bus in 3rd gear, undo the two screws, 
and wiggle the coupler out.  other years you have to disconnect the front 
linkage at the coupler there (which tends to be more rusty), then you can slide 
the rear shift rod froward enough the wiggle the rear coupler out.  At this 
point, you may well want to replace the forward two shift rod bushings as well.
