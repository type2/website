Hi,

I will assume that you are talking about the engine mount
bolt that is behind the fan housing. I can crawl underneath
with my 17mm craftsman and rest the open end or the 12 point
end (its been a really long time) over the nut, then slowly
turn the bolt from the other side until the wrench contacts
something that stops it from turning. Then give the engine
bolt a good push to loosen.

Good luck,
