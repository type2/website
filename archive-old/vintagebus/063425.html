I'm pretty sure you can just replace the boot.  It doesn't hold brake
fluid - you don't have to bleed it after you replace it.  I think it
is mostly there to keep junk off the piston rod so it doesn't beat up
the seal where the piston rod goes into the master cylinder.  When I
bought a replacement master cylinder it came with the boot loose in
the box.  Try West Coast Metric.
