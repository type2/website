The resistance is in the motor. The 2-speed motor has a separate winding
(sometimes) or a different tap on the winding to provide a different
amount of magnetic field for different speeds.  The best bet to make the
ex 6V wipers work where you want them to is to use a rheostat
(adjustable resistor) to set the speed and change it at will.  OK?  Not
hard, but you need a really heavy-duty one.  Otherwise, I think RMMW
sells an electronic thingy for the job.  Never used one, but they should
work.
