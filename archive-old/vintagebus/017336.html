"Busses By The Arch #16" , a.k.a., "Busses Nowhere Near the Arch"

Labor Day Weekend, Sept 1-4th, 2000

Indian Creek "Group Use" Campground, on Mark Twain Lake, 30 miles west of
Hannibal, Missouri

Join over 150 Type 2's for this end of the summer VW camping festival. 
Held over the long holiday weekend, located on a 22 mile long lake (bring
your water toys), supported by NEATO & LiMBO, this is primarily a Type 2
event that is also open to all years, makes and models of VW's that come to
camp.  Informal Bus Show with awards, door prizes, live music, informal
parts swapping, a huge (83 Busses in 1999) Saturday scenic caravan over to
historic Hannibal, MO to tour the hometown of Samuel Clemens, a.k.a. "Mark
Twain".  Decorate your Bus campsite and compete for the coveted "best
individual and Club or group Campsite" trophies.  The infamous Henry De
Kuyper is scheduled to cover BNNTA for VW Trends magazine.  Only VW's will
be admitted and allowed to stay in the campground!  All types and models of
VW are welcomed to camp.  Total event and camping fee per VW is $25 for the
entire weekend.  For event flyer, send your postal mailing address to:   
BussesByTheArch_at_compuserve_dot_com   We hope to see you around the campfire
over the upcoming Labor Day weekend!

Bill Bowman
BBTA organizer
