grinded going into second and would pop out of reverse.

The used one I put in was from a '67 bus. I was told it was good. It 
shows the same problems....grinding going into second, popping out of 
reverse....also pops out of second occasionally if not shifted in

So could this be enough to wreak havok on my shifting...maybe causing
