Diana,

You forgot the most important step in cleaning your tank.  You needed to seal
it.  There are gas tank sealing products that work great.  You first remove all
the loose stuff (you did that already) and then pour in the thick rubberized
sealer and wash it around and drain....let dry and then repeat.  This
encapsulates all of the remaining nasty stuff.

The stuff is impervious to gas and in my experience works great.   You should
be able to find it at professional auto paint suppliers or I know Eastwood
sells it as does Restomotive industries (POR 15 people) in kit form.
