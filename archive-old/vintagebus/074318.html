Subject: RE: [VB] Solex 34 PICT-3 with 009 distributor

John, I have successfully run rejetted 34 PICT-3 carbs and .009 distributors
since 71 without any problems, except maybe that defective Brosol last year
which I fixed. You can go to a good motorcycle shop and pick up various size
Mikumi jets. With the heavy stuff I haul in my truck up and down the hills
here in the PNW I have found that a 135 main and a 55 air do the trick for me.
I have also swapped a 205G for the distributor from time to time with no ill
effects other than resetting timing. On my 009 I keep my timing at 28 degrees
for the bad alcohol laden ARCO regular gas I use. Altogether, the 009 and the
34 PICT-3 are a good combination for power and fuel mileage for the 1600 DP.
