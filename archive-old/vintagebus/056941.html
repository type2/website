Subject: Bugpack & spark plugs

Hi. I've been getting the camper ready for spring travel & show season, and thought I'd tap into the list wisdom on spark plugs. I'm running a 1600 single port, 009, 30/31 carb, and a pertronix ignitor w/ high output coil, and also the Jacob's Bugpak electronic ignition module. It says to use copper core plugs, gap to (I think) .05", and then experiment with the gap to optimize.
