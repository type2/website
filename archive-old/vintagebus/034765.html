Is this the early 59 with the split case tranny?  THat has a row of bolts 
holding the halves toigether like the engine does?  

or the kind with sections from end to end?  
they are different.

DO wait and get the Bentley so you can look at the pics and read what is 
going on it there.

popular wisdom says leave the tranny to the folks who know how to do it and 
have the special tools and can dance the special jigs.  But I know someone 
who fixed a trans from popping out of 4th.

has this bus been sitting a while?   if it sat with low fluid it would have 
rust on the gears and makes noise.  That noise can be quieted done somewhat 
by just changing the fluid and driving.  Different noises can mean bad 
bearings, and obviously I didn't hear your noises.

beg borrow steal or order a bentley asap.

however, if you are dealing with a split case tranny, the 63 edition WILL NOT 
cover it, as they were used 59 and earlier. in which case you need the 55 
ediytion (would have to check, but think the 59 ed was post split case/36hp)
