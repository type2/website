Heads blowing simply means that your cylinder heads are loose.  If you 
don't fix the problem it will only get worse and can actually burn 
grooves into you heads( trashing them ).  A quick fix is to retorque 
the lower cylinder head nuts, although this *MAY* temporarily stop the 
problem the right thing is to retorque all the cylinder head nuts 
which will require pulling the engine and removing the tin. 

And YES, you are losing fuel consumption/performance/engine life!
