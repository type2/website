There's usually a little pin on the inside of the handle where it goes over
the door mechanism.  It's in that hole you see.  The pin only comes out/goes
in one way because it's bigger on one end than the other.  I've used a
little finishing nail to pop it out in the past.

Don't lose the pin, I hate when I do that.
