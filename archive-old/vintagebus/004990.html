McMaster Carr, W. W. Grainger, most industrial valve and fitting and
pneumatic suppliers all carry a wide variety of fittings like this. Kinda
scary to have a fitting designed to come off on your gas line, don't you
think? Quick couplers that have a shut-off feature on each side of the
connection are available, and this would make the procedure a lot less
frightful, but it's gonna sail way past the $5.00 mark. Can't say that it
would be an attractive proposition to me. On the single cab I have it
bulkheaded through in front of the engine, and you don't get gravity flow if
you keep it above the tank. In buses same basic premise. Bulkhead it through
fixed metal, then you can do the disconnect thing from the top, nice and
neat.
