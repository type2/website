I guess one big point I was thinking but didn't articulate... having a
professional do it has one real advantage -- they have great tools and
hopefully all the parts on hand.  I hate NOTHING MORE than starting
something like a brake job, breaking a bolt or who knows what -- then not
being able to get the part.

After doing this again... and again... I dumped a lot of money into
purchasing extra parts, just in case... a lot of this was because I could
only do work on weekends and at night -- and finding a part I needed on
Saturday really sucked.

Yes, there are shops who screw you -- say it will be 1 day when it takes 8,
charge you too much -- but a GOOD shop has the advantage of on-hand parts
and good tools that is very hard to duplicate.  It isn't just skills and
experience...

Of course, having friends (list members) like ThomF or Steven Howell who
have lots of good tools and parts also works too.  Especially something like
brakes where you can likely drive it there.  A botched engine install...
well then you ask Steven Howell to drive his motorcycle to your house (45
minutes 1 way)!

The list is the best resource, better than any shop!  It is like having 5
shops!
