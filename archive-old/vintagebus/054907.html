Subject: Re: [VB] 40 HP lives!!!

.006 should be fine, and .008 won't hurt it either (I've seen some engine
builders run em even looser during engine break-in, then set them right
after the valves 'tighten up', but I don't suggest doing this).  I would
probably look at the fuel you're using, remember that those engines were
meant for Leaded fuel.  I run some Marvel Mystery Oil in my super unleaded
gas (to help simulate LEADED fuel, because the lead lubricated the top end
of the engine), even though I have unleaded heads. The engine probably
doesn't need it, but I guarantee it doesn't hurt to keep the top end
lubricated, and may cause the engine to last longer.
