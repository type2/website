Yeh, but it'll raise the height of your bus in the front a few inches.

>71 bus beam with disc brakes
>would these spindles and brake hardware fit on a 68-69 front beam,

Yes. Might be a good idea to put the later model disc brakes (72 and on) on
there when you find some in a bone yard. One year parts are more expensive
than the multi-year and the later ones are bigger meaning a little more
braking power.

 >does anyone
>know if the 71-72 bus 5 lug is the same pattern as porsche wheels.

No...it's different.

pattern.

brakes on the front. I'll put pictures and specs on my site when I'm done.
