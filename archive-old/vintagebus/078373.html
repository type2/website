>Is there a commonly available relay for the headlights on a '67 bus?

It's not a FLAPS part, but you can use the one from a '68-up Bug.  Around
$25 new from WCM or RMMW; possibly cheaper from other vendors.

http://www.type2_dot_com/archive/vintagebus/043421.html has trouble-shooting
info.  You might also verify that all the wires are on their correct
terminals, per the wiring diagrams available at
http://www.vintagebus_dot_com/wiring .

>My hi-beams are stuck on and no amount of connection cleaning or banging a
>wrench against the relay will free it up.

If all the wires are connected as per the diagram, swap the white and
yellow wires on the relay.  This will make your low beams stuck on; you
won't be able to use the high beams but at least you can drive without
blinding people.
