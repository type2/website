I would support the reprinting of the 1955 edition, as I think it is more
important than the May 1959-62 edition.  There is VERY LITTLE info
in the May 1959-62 edition that is not already covered in the 1963-67
reprint that is already available and the 1955 edition fills in all the gaps
that the 1963-67 leaves out while giving all the valuable info for March
1955-58 buses.  In short, I think the 1955 edition reprint should be the
priority.

In saying that, I have a pristine copy of the May 1959-62 edition (sans
electrical diagrams) and a very nice condition complete 1955 edition
that I would be willing to loan RB for the project.  When I met Stan at
the Classic a couple of years ago I made this offer to him, but he
thought the market for the early reprints was too limited at that time.

Dave Easterwood
proud "old" NEATO member and bus driver
