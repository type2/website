  It all depends on the size of tubing you are trying to cut the fishmouth
in. I have a manual notched I use for 1 to 2 inch tubing. On places where I
need exact cuts I use a barrel disc sander with replaceable sleeves that
come in different grits in my die grinder. On smaller tubing I use a die
grinder (metal) bit in a drill press with a sliding vice . If I feel really
industrious I will set up the mini mill for multiple cuts using an end mill.
