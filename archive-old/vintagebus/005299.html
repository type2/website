It is Friday and once again time to shamelessly promote  EveryBus:the
official Type2_dot_campout. EveryBus will be  held on April 16,17,18 at

one of the RV camping areas as well as a picnic shelter. Lots more space is

night per site which includes hookups and use of the bathhouse with hot
showers.

Friday: arrive early and get a good spot. Some sorta jug-music (bring yer
noisemaker)
Friday night:hard-core relaxation.
Saturday afternoon: maybe visit METWIZ and see how buses are saved!*
Saturday night: bonfire and door prizes,
Sunday: Carabus to the IVWA Spring Nationals at Farmington, NC ( parts,swap,
car show Type1 drags)

*Kelly Brown (aka metwiz) has graciously offered our group a tour of his
