wait... are you getting a bare long block?   Cause not all the tin from the 
40hp will fit.

Anyway, heater boxes... if you use the better, more common, later, big 
diameter heater boxes you wil find they are NOT the same diameter as the heater 
tubes in your bus.   They enlarged the tubes in ~ 64.  So  as well as the heater 
pod mufflers, the metal flex tubes with insulation, you will need either an 
adaptor, or just duct tape them well to keep the tubes connected w/ leaks.

I would not use PVC or other plastic in there, due to heat/plastic issues.   
I won't even use the later bug/bus VW plastic tubes, since I had a fan belt 
break and killed a fresh air tube so when I swapped fan belts I had to block off 
the fresh air tube for 10 miles until I could get a new fresh air tube.   
But... it was only a few miles until this horrible  burning plastic smell entered 
the bus.  I pulled over, jumped under the bus (in rain or snow, I forget) and 
pulled off the melting tube.  It had got too hot from lack of fresh air 
cooling the heater box.   

Now I stick to metal.  Steel is real.
