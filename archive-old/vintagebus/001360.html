For what it's worth, I found out that this Porsche pulley conversion
consists of 4 parts - the inner and outer halves of the pulley, the 36mm nut
and the part I failed to order - the HUB (which I ended up welded.

I was able to get my parts through Stoddards in Ohio but calls to Porsche
warehouses will net you pretty much the same thing for the same price.

What you need is:

Inner Pulley Half
Outer Pulley Half
Hub
36 mm nut
#35015 Belt
