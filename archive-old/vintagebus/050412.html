Back in October or so of 1998, Richard, who makes reproduction round tool 
boxes (www.roundboxtools_dot_com), asked the list via Thom about interest in 
reproducting outie engine vent trim for 55 thru 3/63 deluxes.  He said: "I 
would have to make 5000 at first enuf for 275 busses. Retail would have to be 
about 80-84 dollars a full set. What do you think?".

I decided to follow up on this just to see if anything was done.  Richard 
recently wrote me back saying: "I had the tooling made and a few pieces made 
to prove up the process.  The trim looked real good.  However in my research 
for customers I found there to not be enough customers to make this project 
feasable.  If you know where I could find 275 customers willing to pay 1/2 
now and 1/2 on delivery I would do it."

There did not seem to be much interest in this back in 1998, but I told 
Richard that I would see if any interest has picked up.  The price seems okay 
for me, and I would be willing to spend even a little more for repop trim.  
It's just finding the demand that is a problem.  So is there any interest out 
there? Reply to me or the list and I will tally up the results and post them.
