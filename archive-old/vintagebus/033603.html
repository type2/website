The lead used for cars is different then wheel weight and/or fishing
weights, there are additives in it and it is a lot purer then auto grade
lead. There are a couple coach builders (2 of the best in the world, yes
here in Havasu). They build Cobra bodies and I have seen them build a couple
of 550 Porsche Spyder bodies. I have seen them take old lead and remelt it
and put the additives in it. I have learned a lot from them on leading and
was getting pretty good at it before I quite doing heavy metal resto's. A
while back Matt here in town had a DC they worked on and instead of
replacing the drivers door bottom 6 inchs and the dogleg they FABBED
complete new pieces, tiged them in, metal finished and smoothed with a
little lead absolutely beautiful.
