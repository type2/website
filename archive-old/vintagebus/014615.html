Hi Dworkin,
The best piece of salvage is to get the bell housing off a 1972(?) or later 
bus tranny.  This type of bell housing unbolts from the tranny case.  On top 
of this you end up with a bell housing with the input shaft hole through the 
front of it.  This hole can easily be used to mount the bell housing to a 
stand you build or even bolt it to a beam in your garage.  If you mount it 
loosely so the bell housing will be rotateable, you end up with a real nice 
setup for building engines on.   Eric&Barb
