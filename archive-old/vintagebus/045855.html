cut the rubber donut off. with a sharp knofe or razor blade.  The metal 
cylinder inside it is the rusted tricky part. What I found works best is 
grind a flat spot on the side of the metal tube that is stuck/rusted on 
there. (use proper ear and eye protection.  A file will work, though it is 
slow) Then, with some penetrating oil and vise grips (which now have a place 
to grab) you can twist that sucker off
