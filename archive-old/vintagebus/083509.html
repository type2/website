The easiest way to weld the tank is to pipe a hose to it off a running
vehicle and fill it with it's exhaust.

No oxygen--no BOOM !!

started mig welding a patch ----

welding it, and it scared the hell outa me---
another trip to Wal Mart for more Jockey shorts--- ~ : )

works good.

Just plug all the inlets and outlets with a cork, rag , whatever--and
