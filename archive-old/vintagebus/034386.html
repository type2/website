	Roof rust is more difficult to repair 'properly' (remove rust and
replace metal)
	than chassis related rust (outriggers, rocker panels etc).
Replacement
	sections are not available commercially and are difficult to make
'by hand'.

	However, the delimiter (I think) is whether the rust has moved 'up'
from
	the rain gutter or has moved 'down' and has gotten into the
U-section that
	the roof sits on (have a look you'll see what I mean) and 'lower'.

	If the rust has moved up and the U section is largely OK, then you
can
	save the bus (although its a lot of work). You can drill/grind the
spot welds that
	hold the whole roof onto the U section and around the overhead vent
	and replace the whole thing.....

	If rust is well into the U section - well..........
