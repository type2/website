On Sun, 13 Jul 2003, Scott Fraser wrote:

> One thing you could do is to mount the backup lights and then use a
> pushbutton or toggle switch to turn them on and off.  Not good if you
> forget to turn them off.

Mount a flashing LED from Rad Shack next to the switch. They're bright,
and you're less likely to forget to turn'em off. Since you only use the
backup lights at night, the bright flashing LED won't go unnoticed. The
flashing LED has the flashing circuit built into the LED, so it's just
the size of a standard 1/5" diameter LED. They work directly from 12
Volts.
