As far as building a bus tow bar, it really isn't that complicated, it is
built very similar to the bug tow bar, only longer.  The problem with
manufacturing one is the liability involved.  Just think what would happen
if you built one, sold it to someone who did not hook it up properly and
while towing their bus down the local interstate it came loose, crossed
over to the other side and hit a car with a mother and a bunch of children
and killed half of them!  Not enough insurance in the world could handle
that problem and patch things up.  Not to mention the guilt trip you would
have.
