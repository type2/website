Earlier buses had the dash the lower color, in about 62 two tone buses had 
the dash the upper color.  The two tone bus with red dash has the inside of the 
cab doors, over head air vent, etc also red.  
From headlight bucket  and roof paint wear through analysis, I believe the 
entire bus was painted the lower color, then the upper color was painted on top 
of it.
