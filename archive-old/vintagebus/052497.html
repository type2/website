it easier to dissassemble as much as possible while attached to the bus.  As 
it is held firmly in place.  Makes it easier to bash the offending bearing 
off.  Can always take a grinder to it (careful- dont grind into spindle, stop 
just shy of it)  and try penetrating oil.
