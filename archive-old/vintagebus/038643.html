Paul W. Smith wrote:

> early to mid 60's marantz made a 4 track tape player. the 4 track tapes had
> very few songs on them, probably only 3 to 4.

That was Marantz,(the crazy).  Craig made a 4 track player that mounted
below the dash about the same time.  Each tape held a album, 8 to 10 tunes,
just like the 8 track tapes.  I have 60 or 70 4-track albums.  8-track tapes
had the pinch roller built in which caused tape speed problems whereas the
pinch roller in 4-track was part of the player and lock the tape into place.
Also the 8 track players ran at a slower speed cramming more magnetic
information closer together.  These two differences made 8-track less
expensive so more popular among buyers.  Kind of like the VHS vs. BetaMax.
Beta is better and is now the industry standard but VHS was cheaper.
