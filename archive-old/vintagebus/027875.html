For added info here are some paraphrases from the manuals

Idiots guide 1978 :  grooved plunger (labeled 1500S) for 40hp and newer,
helps keep the oil cooler.

Bently 63-67 says :   use the 311 115 411  annular groove plunger with hot
climates, but since it lowers temp it must not be use in temperate or cold
climates.

and the
Chilton 1971 again: replaced the stock oil relief valve plain plunger with a
type 3 angular grooved type plunger to get 15deg lower oil temperature,
discouraged by the VW factory.
