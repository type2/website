Yup, they fit just fine on _my_ '67 Kombi!  The lining is some kind of
fabric or organic fiber behind the mesh.  They were good at quieting the
air stream on bug, which follows a very short path to the rear heat
outlets.  I think the muffling is mainly to cancel exhaust noise coupled
through the heat exchangers.

The original pods and metal hoses on my '67 had the worst of both coasts;
loaded up with California dust and partly eaten by Massachusetts rust,
so it was good to subsitute a plastic tube for the worst one.  Now
if I can only find the other tube parted out from that '73 bug..
