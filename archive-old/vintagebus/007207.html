Not only will a bent drag link screw up your steering but if someone ever
monkeyed with the adjustment and screwed it up, this will also cause a
problem.  Keep in mind that there are 2 little cone-head shaped bolts on
each kingpin assembly that acts as a steering stop.  If someone removed one,
ground it down, simply put a bolt in there in place of the actual
cone-headed stop bolt, you will have more play on one side than the other.
