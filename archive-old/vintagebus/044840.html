They changed them for the beginning of the 1966 model year.  The "B" version (B suffix at end of part number) was used then, don't know when the A version was intoduced , sometime after 1958, but evidently it is physically interchangable with the plain/earlier version, as the 67 ed parts list shows the B version for the 66/67 trucks, and the plain version for the 65 and earlier trucks.
   So get a pair of panels from a truck through 65, and you should be fine.
