I've had excellent luck removing bus steering wheels with heat.  A small
pinpoint torch is best.  I once didn't have a torch and I got enough
heat where needed by dropped burning pieces of mineral coal into the
center of the wheel and blowing on them through a straw to control the
heat.  This did not burn or melt the steering wheel.  It almost popped
off!
