I know the problem...same as my '67.  When I went to reupholster
mine, I spent hours in a large auto upholstery shop in Rockford,
Illinois trying to match the platinum.  I did finally find
something VERY close, both in color and thickness, to the VW
original.  Two problems with it though.  It only came in the
"thin" version so I couldn't use it for the seats.  Also, it was
$50/yard or something like that.  I passed.

One word of note, the guy said the reason it was so expensive is
that it was for a Cadillac of 50s vintage.  He didn't get
specific, but that may give someone a clue.
