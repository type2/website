Subject: Re: [VB] front wheels...link pins

I have two ideas about this
 
1) bent drag link.  the drag link does have a normal bend in   it.  When my 
61 got hit, a result was that the turn radius was much sharper  to one side 
than before, and wider on the other side.   The drag link  got bent.  I replaced 
it, and voila.. back to normal.   comparing  the old to the new there was a 
marked difference.
 
2)  the stop bolt on the spindle could be broken off.   It  is supposed to 
have a fat pointy hex shaped end that goes to a point.   these do get broken.  
see _http://failsure_dot_net/oacdp/5867part/255.png_ 
(http://failsure_dot_net/oacdp/5867part/255.png) 
it's #46 in the diagram
