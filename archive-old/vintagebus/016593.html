Emmet McGovern wrote:
> 1. What seems to be the average cost for having a bus sprayed 2 tone?

Single stage, anywhere from $150 to $800 for the *paint*, then labor 
_at_ $PrevailingRate to get it sprayed.  Figure $25-$65/hr for the rate.
And that's just *spraying* - no other costs are figured in there.
Clearcoat add approx 30%.  These are just rough numbers.  It will vary
on how much of the bus you're painting, what color (it took 8 coats of
single-stage to cover my 60)
