I agree, send the center section away for a rebuild, and do the boxes 
yourself.   You will have to get a big socket for the inner stub axle nut, 
and a way to pull the plugs.  check yr Bentley.  

Re drilling out RBox bolt holes, I never stripped one out.   I have had them 
sometimes start to cross thread.  But get a tap, hell get a whole tap and die 
set, and clean the threads out.   clean the threads on the bolts.   use 
grease on the threads when you install (or that silver stuff if it's your 
favorite)  Installed the bolts by hand, and you should be able to spin them 
in by hand. (untill you squash the spring plate down to the box. 

That said, deepening  the holes isn't bad.    

And their powder coated axle tubes and brake backing plates are extremely 
stylee.  That may seem just like extra work and money, but it is so cool.
