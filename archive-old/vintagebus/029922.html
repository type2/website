Static timing will vary with different types of distributors, but
regardless which type you have, you want your total advance to be no
more than 28-30 degrees. I made a mark on my pulley at 30 degrees, use
an inexpensive timing light (no advance adjustment), rev the engine up
while watching the mark and adjust accordingly...
