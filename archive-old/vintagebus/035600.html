 ... I recently replaced the clutch cable in my 65.   since then my clutch feels really messed up, (yes I did readjust it to compensate for stretch) not directly after but a couple weeks later I went over some really bad roads and the the clutch felt all grabby and jumpy.  
 
  Looking underneath I noticed the place where the bowden tube for the clutch cable attaches to the body has come loose from the body, so the rear end of the metal tube which is attached to the rear end of the metal throttle cable tube is hanging there, as it has not yet been rewelded.   No funny noises banging around, but I suggest you look at this area and your bowden tube.
