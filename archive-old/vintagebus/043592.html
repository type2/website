I'm guessing the cut wire was the blue one from the turn signal switch
to the hazard flasher switch.  It should be connected to terminal K
on the hazard flasher switch.  That brown wire to S- on the flasher
relay (9 prong box) should be connected to terminal S on the the
hazard flasher switch.  Also, the hazard flasher switch should be
grounded...I believe it grounds through its mounting nut - there's
not a separate wire.

If your turn signal and brake lights are all working, and everything is
hooked up right, the emergency lights *should* work.  You might want
to go through the "troubleshooting" section in my 9-prong box write up
at http://www.type2_dot_com/library/electris/vw-9prong.html .  This will
help isolate any trouble to the bulbs, wiring, turn signal switch,
hazard flasher switch, or 9-prong box.  Also, get the wiring diagram
for your Bus at http://www.vintagebus_dot_com/wiring/index.html if you
don't already have it.
