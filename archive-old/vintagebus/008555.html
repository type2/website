Steve;
I noticed the same thing last fall when I first got my '67 bus on the road.
Somebody out there correctly identified it as a resistance problem, caused
by 30+ years of oxidization and corosion.   I noticed it more at night, but
I think that was more than likely because the "glow" appeared brighter.

I spent a morning resetting and cleaning all the wire contacts under the
dash with steel wool, a pencil eraser and emory paper; I also replaced fuses
and cleaned up all the contacts on the fuse block.
The problem went away.

Hope this helps
--Rich
