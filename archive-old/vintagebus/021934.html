Hi Rob,
All four shocks on each of thier ends are mounted with a long bolt through 
both the end of the shock and suspension mounting point.  Tightened in place 
with a split lock washer and nut.  You might want to run a drill bit through 
the suspension point to clean out the bore and then apply a little grease 
over the length of the bolt to prevent futher corrosion in the future.
