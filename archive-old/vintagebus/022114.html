28 11 0 = November 28, 1960
374 is US import code
023 are you sure this is not A23, B23, C23, or D23?
    should be a designation for SO-23 Westfalia camper interior.
    my '60 SO-23 Westfalia has A23 on the plate.
168 is 88 amp/hr battery
181 is chrome hubcaps
UZ is the US delivery port
231 is LHD Kombi, cargo doors on right side
SK 654C is the special paint code for Sealing Wax Red & Beige Grey
688747 is the VIN
