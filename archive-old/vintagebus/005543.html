Dewayne-
If the white haze is inside the glass, no. Laminated
Safety glass has two panels with a plastic core.
If it is on the surface, try CLR ( Calcium, Lime, Rust )
available in grocery/hardware stores, full strength.
Otherwise, you will need to get cesium oxide from
a jeweler's supply source or Eastwood, and polish it off
with a buffing pad. polarized sunglasses will help to
make it more visible.
Dave
