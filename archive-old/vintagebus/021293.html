Remove the cylinder and piston nearest the stripped head stud hole, if the 
stud is inbetween the cylinders you will have to remove both #3 and #4 Ps&Cs. 
 Take the head stud with it's case saver on it to your VW FLAPS and have them 
take a look at it so they can tell you if they have a bigger size or the DPO 
already went too big of case saver to replace properly.  If you get a bigger 
case saver, drill and tap the hole bigger and use a little engine sealant in 
the threads to seal them up.  Make sure to only use HSS (High Speed Steel) 
drill and tap with a little oil.  Yes they are a bit more expensive, but in 
the long run better tools are worth it!   Eric&Barb
