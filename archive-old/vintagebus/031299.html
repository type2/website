> Raymond,see if you have redux boxes or not, 

If your bus is lowered then there is a good chance that they are gone. 
It's an easy check. 
Please correct me if i'm wrong here... If the redux boxes 
(sometimes labeled by listees with the nomenclature of RGBs) are gone
there are a couple of things that may be up with your trans; a) the 
ring gear may be flipped to avoid the embarrassment of a really kewell
lowered bus with four reverse gears and b) the possibility that 
your trans is not a bus trans and a type I or III with different 
ratios in the final drive and the a lower 1st gear.

Mandatory reading on RGBs & type II trannies:

http://www.type2_dot_com/library/drivetra/reductio.htm

and the "The type 2's" section of"

http://www.geocities_dot_com/Baja/Dunes/9702/transtalk.html

This last bit on the geocities site will be useful if you need 
to see if you have a early type II (three rib) or a non type II trans
