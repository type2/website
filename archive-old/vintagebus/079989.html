seat cover for my 61 DC.  The hump back style.  I was
able to secure an original pattern to help me do this.

Is the center seam a single or double stitch?

pieces together with a standard 1/4" seam and then
fold the seam over on the back side and again sew it
up.  I think you can tell this if the seam has a

material come together.
