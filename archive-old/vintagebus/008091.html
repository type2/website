> The reason I ask is because the little o-ring on the stub axle has to be
> compressed by the seal race in order to seal. If the drum isn't on and
> the nut tight, then gearlube can leak past the o-ring and run out from
> between the seal race and the axle. This isn't as much of a problem
> after things have been together a while, so it doesn't usually show up
> during a brake job. It's more of a problem with new parts.
