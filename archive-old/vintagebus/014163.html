I have Euro lights in the '55, and there really isn't any "hassle" with
bulb holders.  It's actually easier to change a bulb with a Euro light
than with American spec.  I have a set of NOS ones on it now, which are
actually *Mercedes* units that some ebay-vendors are pawning off as barndoor
lights.  They don't look any different than the real deal, other than
they don't have the VW logo on them.

I ran fluted lenses over sealed beams in the '56 for years with no problems.
