No problem you are welcome.  The thing is that buses unless driven with the
windows open/missing or sunroof open at all outside temps create there own
weater inside.  Once the differance between the warmth of the interior reaches
a certain point higher than the outside temp the water vapor begins to
condense.  So unless you live somewhere where lovers lane has no fogged
windows, you will have rust problems due to using materials that collect
moisture and holds them against the steel of your VW.
