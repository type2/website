I have noticed some of the UK importers selling Buses that they bought in CA/AZ stated as "CA/AZ Bus, rust-free, ..." and it is not always true.

For instance, my friend had a '55 Euro Single Cab (which I probably should have bought in retrospect).  It came from CA, he lived in AZ, a guy bought it and shipped it to the UK.  I saw the UK ad "AZ Single Cab, lived whole life in AZ Desert, no rust, good engine, etc..".   It was not an AZ truck, it had some bed rust (probably zero by UK standards), and don't even ask about the engine...  So buyer beware!
