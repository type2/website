As for employment, I manufacture an ICF called LUXIT.  ICF stands for
insulating concrete form.  It's basically a styro foam concrete form that
stays in place and becomes the insulation for whatever building is being
constructed.  I believe that Luxit is the first ICF ever designed.  It was
designed in Europe, the molding machines (only 3 in the world) were built in
Europe (using Bosch pneumatics, BTW), and a lot of homes were built in Europe
out of Luxit back in the early-mid '80's.  There are presently around 25
different manufacturers of ICF's in the world and ICF's are the fastest
growing new product to EVER hit the construction industry.
