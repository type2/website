Using a heater that burns it's fuel inside of an inclosed compartment that
is used for sleeping is not a good idea, but there are many heaters on the
market that can be placed inside the engine bay much like a gasoline heater
that use propane for fuel.  Here is a list of some interesting heaters, my
favorite is the Zodi.
