The Ark lives again!!  It was indeed the fuel pump that laid us low.

put an Alternator pump on since she's got an alternator.  (thought
the new pump was bolting on funny).  The difference between the 2 
seems that the lever on the pump sits lower in the base, requiring a
shorter pushrod.
