Hello All, 

Save the date - October 6, 200q for the Volkswagen
Rendezvous and "Show Off" at Firemen's Park in
Batavia, Wisconsin. a VW Gathering, Swap Meet and
"Show Off".   All VWs are welcome; Old -New, Slow -
Fast, Air - Water, Daily Drivers - Works In Progress -
Show Cars, Race Cars, etc.  If it says VW,  you are
welcome to bring it.  We want to see how many VWs we
can gather in one place.  Volkswagen Clubs are invited
to come and set up as a group and will be encouraged
to pick club  vehicles for the "Show Off". 
Individuals can also "Show Off".

Everyone who comes will be a winner - dash plaques for
all registrants.  No judging, no trophies.  Those
wishing to "Show Off" their cars will be able to enter
them into various "Show Off" Arenas for the rest of us
to admire.  This event will feature Relaxation & Good
Times. Food and berverage concessions will be provided
by the Batavia Fire Department.

VW swappers are welcome.  The more the better.  We
hope to see lots of parts, cars, toys, novelty items,
T-Shirts, etc. 

Stay tuned to your email for details.  A flyer is
being developed and will be forthcoming soon. 
Meanwhile, direct questions to Jim Ellis via email -
turtlehawk_at_yahoo_dot_com or Steve Paukner -
srpauto_at_hotmail_dot_com
 
This event is co-sponsored by The Volkswagen Club of
Sheboygan and SRP Automotive of Batavia, Wisconsin.  

Hope to see you in October.
