Subject: starter/rattle problem solved...sort of

as i stated previously in my idiot of the week award speech...as i installed
the solenoid incorrectly-hence the starting problems. i just did a little
test drive witht he solenoid installed correctly-no rattling noise.

installed-and pushing the starter constantly into the flywheel.

not sure what-it doesn't sound like the flywheel is turning.

solenoid-the symptom would be nothing happening at all, right?

on the other hand it could be the flywheel. perhaps the teeth of the
flywheel got chewed up by the starter gear.
