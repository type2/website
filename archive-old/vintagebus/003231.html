Here's the info on the Central California spring meet.

April 19th, VW Spring Fling 98

Madera County Fairgrounds, Madera

9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

On the grass, under the trees in the Madera Fairgrounds.
This is the fourth annual Spring Fling presented by N.O.S. VW
Productions and S.O.T.O. Live music
swap, games and engine blow. VW HotWheels racing, push drags.
Just off freeway 99 at Cleveland Avenue. 20 miles north of Fresno, in
the heart of the San Joaquin Valley.
Free parking, spectator admission is one can of food for the local
homeless shelter. Stock or custom show entry is $10, VW swap/vending
space is $15, 20'x'20', no reservations needed.
Please, no alchohol or loud stereos. RV hookup is available-call
(559)299-8512 for RV reservations only.
For details call Bryan (559) 251-6480.
