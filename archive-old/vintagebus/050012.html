I would advise AGAINST POR15 on brake drums.  The drums need to dissipate
heat, and that heavy coating will interfere with that, which will diminish
braking power and cook your bearings and seals.  The drums will never rust
through anyway.  I always paint drums with a light coating of flat black
spray paint.  Flat black has the best heat dissipation characteristics of
any color.
