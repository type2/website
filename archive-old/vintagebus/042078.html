You might try bullet connectors such as those used on Japanese cars.  The
male part is quite small and may fit through the hole in the body,
particularly if you use heat-shrink tubing to insulate it.  Use
heat-shrink over the whole female part and it too will be completely
insulated so that you can keep the connector inside the body.
