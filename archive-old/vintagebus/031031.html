After working at a lot of VW places here in the South Bay area, there
seems to be a strong opinion about the location of the fuel filter.  The
shop that is next door to my house even has a sign hanging up in their
lounge about it.

They all agree that having the fuel filter in the engine compartment is
a VERY BAD IDEA.  

The best place on a bug is right out of the framerail, steering clear of
the heater box.

The best place on a bus is a small loop before the clip that holds the
fuel line under the tank.

Putting the fuel filter in the engine compartment is just asking for
trouble.
