Hi Jim,
First clean all your connections up, even just taking the slip on connectors
on and off will help there.  Then replace all the relays and light bulbs to 12
volt.  The horn, fuel gauge will work fine on 12 volts as long as you do not
sit on the horn for long periods.  For the wiper motor remove clean and
regrease it's gear box with lithuim grease about 1/3 full and install a volt a
drop in front of it.  You should check with House of Ghia for thier signal box
adapter which gets rid of the $90.00+ signal relay your year and 1967 has and
allows you to use two standard three prong relays that are availble worldwide
in any parts shops.  Most of all just check everything again to make sure that
things are hooked up right.     Eric & Barb
