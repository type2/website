  I have found the "ch" sound before in a front end and turned out to be the
drum hitting against the front brake line between the wheel cylinders. On
reinstalation of the wheel cylinders I didn't get the brake line flat and
there was just enough space between it and the backing plate that it would
sometimes rub against the hub of the brake drum. Took at least 5 times
removing the drum before I discovered that irritateing "ch" sound.
