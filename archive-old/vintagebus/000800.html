I do, EVERYWHERE!! For the $.05 - $.10 a piece these things cost I think it 
would be foolish not to have them on every joint in the gas line from the 
tank all the way to the carb(s).  You may only "need" them from the pump to 
the carb, but it couldn't cost even a buck to put them everywhere.  Very 
cheap insurance.  Also make sure the brass inlet tube into the carb(s) is 
seated well.
