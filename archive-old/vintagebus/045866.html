the heater box and the hole is in the tin in front of the fan shroud
Some have a small preheat hole on the left
sme have a small preheat hole on the right
some have a small pre heat hole on each side
some have a large (same as fresh air hole size) preheat hole on the right

you can block off what you do not need
These tin can often be found at swaps for about $1.
