The later Brazilian hybrid/monster buses with baywindow fronts grafted onto
splittie rears had a double-jointed axle/reduction box set-up. RGBs were
located by trailing arms like a bay, but instead of CVs on the axles, a pair
universal joints are used on each side. Nice in theory but apparently not as
durable as the original swingaxle in practice.

I have a repair manual for one of these trannies and may be able to scan
pics if anyone's interested.
