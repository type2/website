I used outdoor carpeting (the green felt stuff) to cover the springs and
frame, attached with hog rings.  Then I used 1/2 inch stiff foam on top of
that and trimmed the edges to give the seat the proper contour.  Worked
great!

ON a previous project I used 1" foam, but it was too thick, which for a
tall guy in a bus makes a difference!  I ended up having to duck my head
to look out of the side windows!
