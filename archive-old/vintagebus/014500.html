To check that a heat exchanger has the interior fins you hold a flashlight 
against the side of your head so your eyesight is as close to alignment of 
the light beam as possible.  Then look up the rear end of the heat exchanger 
where the muffler connects.  Just about 3-4" inside on either of the insides 
you should be able to see the fins or the inside of the round tube if it is 
the earlier J-tube or cheap Dansk heat exchanger.    Eric&Barb
