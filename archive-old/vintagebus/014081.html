I used to use 20 pound freon tanks for air bottles.  These bottles had
a blow-out panel on their bottoms that would fail before the cylinder
in case of overpressurization.  They even sold valve/gauge/hose setups
in auto parts stores specifically for this conversion.  I don't know if
they have these any more, though.

Most disposable tanks tend to be rather susceptable to rust, and I
wouldn't use any tank that shows signs of rust.  Unfortunately, you
cannot see rust inside the tank, so the only way to stay safe is to
replace the tank periodically.
