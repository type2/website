Pink fibreglass insulation holds water.   

it may be okay to use if you live in the desert, but remember that water runs 
through the cab doors, and a vapor barrier keeps water off the door panel 
(which is a good thing) but not out of the door.

It is very difficult to seal perfectly, and air can carry moisture in the 
crack in the moisture barrier, or around the edge.  Cracks can occur over 
time because of driving and bouncing around teh roads.  I live in the PNW, 
and have seen the damage fibreglas does in doors and the sides of buses.   I 
think fibreglas has no place in buses.   Sprayfoam is evil.
