A stock 67 bus decklid will take a 71 bug engine latch just peachy, screws
right in no muss no fuss.

The 66 is a bit touchier, but it will fit.

66 and 67 decklids are different, a 66 decklid has a funky kind of v
shaped looking hole, a 67 decklid has a square hole with 3 screw holes
around it.
