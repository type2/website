Hi. I did this. I replaced the nonadjustable for adjustable. Is there a big 
difference in how it handles? Nope.
BUT
My alignment guy liked it alot. He showed me that the steering wheel is now 
set using the tie rods rather then the drag link. It saved him time, and 
might save you money.
And as long as that rod doesnt get bent, when you find a worn tie rod end, 
you replace it, rather then replace the whole assemby.
