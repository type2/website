I do have access to these engines, even though I have never used one
personally. As for installation in a Bus, I have no comments. These are the
same engine that is installed in the Old Beetle in Mexico. You would have to
purchase a Brain, fuel pump, install a return fuel line, etc. Last time I
checked they were $2400.00 dollars plus freight for the long block only.
These are imported in Cali, so shipping would be from there if they have any
left. If you go to the Classic the importer will be there and should have 1
on display and they could give you the name of a local dealer.
