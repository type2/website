Matthew,
I am 6'4", 300 lbs. I am very comfortable behind the wheel of my
walk-through 66, a little more cramped behind the wheel of a bench seat
model.
Speaking to reliability, you get out of a bus what you put in, I find that
if I neglect my bus it gives me problems, the upside of this is that the
problems are easily solved with tenacity, the Idiot Book and help from this
list.
rest assured that there are no black boxes or computers to inexplicably go
south on you.
I drive my bus every day, and occasionally run into trouble, but I have a
2000 Beetle and have trouble with that too, the only difference is, the
beetle is $500.00 a shot at the dealership, the bus is a couple of hours of
concentration and a few bucks for parts.
I would recommend a bus anytime over an SUV or anything else for that
matter.
