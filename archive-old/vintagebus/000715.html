The best reality check on a vehicle is "What can I buy one for in the 
condition I want?  How much will it cost to get this vehicle to that 
condition?"  Add your estimated restoration cost to your proposed purchase 
price and it will tell you if it is a good deal or not for you as opposed to 
just buying what you want in the condition you want.
