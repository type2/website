A word of warning:
 My father did this once on a deer hunting trip. Heated a rock in the fire,
wrapped it in wet cardboard and aluminum foil, then put it in the bottom of
his Coleman down bedroll (too big to be called a sleeping bag). When the
rest of his party returned (drunk and disorderly) from the wh*rehouse in
Nevada, they saw smoke pouring from the tent trailer. Not only did he burn a
hole thru his bag and the floor of the trailer, he was nearly asphyxiated by
the smoke. BE CAREFUL!
