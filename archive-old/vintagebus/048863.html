Subject: Center link pin replacement

Hi all!

Decided to tackle the center link pin and bushings on my 67 deluxe 
today.  Ordered the pin and bushing set from Wolfsburg a couple of 
months ago (on a tip from VWHoward), and waited in line to "borrow" 
their ream.  They shipped the parts and the ream together when the 
ream came back from the last borrower (I will send the ream back on 
Tuesday).

First point: I very much appreciate the customer service at Wolfsburg 
West.  Not only did the have the ream to lend, but they reminded me 
that I had a $25 credit I had forgotten about!

Second point: Muir was very helpful!  My only criticisms would be a) 
where does one get a 1-1/8" brass pin to use pounding out the old 
bushings?, and b) he says it is unpleasant work, and that should be 
in BOLD!  I ended up using a 19mm deep impact socket in lieu of the 
brass pin, and it worked well.  I also purchased a brake cylinder 
hone (recommended by Muir) at Sears, and it was almost essential to 
the task - especially in fitting the upper bushing to the new pin.

The bus drives much better than it did!  Still need to replace the 
passenger car tires, but all in all, a worthwhile evolution!

Now I'm going to go take some Alleve!
