One of the problems you might be having is that the seals you got from WCM
could be the wrong ones, they offer 2 different thick nesses of the vent
seal, 1 is actually for the flapper diverter in your airbox and is of a
thicker rubber, when they ran out of the correct? wing vent seal they
substituted the thicker seals and they won't work without a lot of cussing.
I use a workmate to change the seals. takes abbot 10 minutes per vent wing
to change, lube it up with silicon spray, set the workmate up (they have
holes in them for different pegs (fixtures) and away you go. Also works
great on pop out windows and safari's.
