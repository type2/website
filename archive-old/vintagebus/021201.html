 Brakes are an important thing that is often overlooked.  Not only would I change out that MC, but I would check and replace all lines and hoses that look the least bit questionable.  I was lucky early this year like yourself.  The PO of the Pritschenwagen had replaced aparrently every line and hose on the truck except the longest one that runs along the frame.  I was extremely lucky because it decided to pop in my alley at 5mph (it rusted through at a clamp that holds it to the frame).  The night before I had picked up a friend at O'Hare and I had him and my daughter propelled at full speed on the Kennedy Expressway, with a broken parking brake.  So in spite of my atheistic views, perhaps there was someone looking out for us.
Eric & Barb have instructions on converting the MC at http://www.type2_dot_com/library/brakes/dualmas.htm .  The topic came up a few days ago and I had bookmarked it for future reference.
