Hi Ty,
You need to remove the right kick panel.  There you will find your four prong 
relay that allows the standard three or two prong signal flasher relay in 
front of the left kick panel to operate the E-blinkers also. 
211 953 215 B  Relay-Emergency flashing indicator light 6 volt 4X18W.  Which 
should be what you have if we are reading this correctly.
211 953 215 C  Relay-Emergency flashing indicator light 12 volt 4X21W.  Is 
what you need!
