Kevin the controls on the SW heaters are pretty straight forward.  From the
factory, the control unit was a push pull cable with a turn knob switch on
the end.  You would pull the knob out a little bit, then turn the switch.
The switch is just an off/on switch to power the unit, pulling on the cable
set the thermostat so the temperature of the air coming out of the heater
would either be warm (cable pushed in) or melt your tennis shoes hot (cable
pulled out).  You could install a push pull cable, then a toggle switch
beside it and do the same thing as the original.
