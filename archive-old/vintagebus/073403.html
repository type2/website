As far as travelling games go, we play one in my '77
Bus(which has no radio) where you pick a word(i.e.
road, moon, black etc.) and you go around the car with
each person naming a song with the word in either the
title or lyrics.
As each person runs out of songs they drop out and the
last person has to come up with a final song after the
2nd last has dropped out to prove their superiority.
Being a music teacher gives me a good edge, however my
wife is also a keen muso & when we travelled with the
country band we were in last year they pulled out
tunes from artists I'd never even heard of & I knew
they weren't lying.
Basically if you're a keen listener of any
genre(classical & opera included) or have a big
general knowledge of music you have a good chance of
winning at least once a trip.
Plus, based on the key word, your preferred choice of
musical genre can give you an edge.
Give it a go.
If nothing else, you find yourself remembering all the
words to those Top40 songs you SWORE were awful & then
singing them to prove them to the other occupants of
the vehicle, fairly amusing depending on the song ;o)
