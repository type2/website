As noted in a previous post, M-Code 071 is for the second door.
The NEATO Old Bus Registry has 38 Single Cabs (type 261), back to
November 1958.  28 of them have M-Code 071 (from February 1960 to
the end of the Split run).  Of the 10 without 071, 8 of them are
before February 1960 and 2 are after that.  Maybe they just
became a "standard option" in 1960.
