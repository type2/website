Just guessing on the age of your carb, but it sounds like a vac leak, probably
at the bowl if you have a Brosol. That's not a bad thing, just dry cracked
gasket. You can buy a carb rebuild kit for $10 and that gives you a chance to
clean the throttle plate and venturi. I would also check for any play in the
throttle plate shaft. If the holes are oveld even slightly you will get vac
leak there. You can have it bushed or just replace it.
