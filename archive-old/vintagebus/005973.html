Go to a large real hardware store. Ask for a product to remove water stains
from shower doors. If you get more than one choice, pick the one that says
it is OK to use on safety glass. We have a local product here in Fresno,
Calif called Sunny Brite and Sunny Brite II. Sunny Brite is not for safety
glass, II is. It works very well. I tried another brand called 'Water Stain
Remover'. It was a very runny liquid and you had to use good rubber gloves.
It was almost 100% acid.....Did not work as good as the Sunny Brite II
product.
