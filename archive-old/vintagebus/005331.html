If it's brass, and on the lower left side looking at it from the back seat,
you've described the latch which further prevents the this seat from sliding
backwards. There should be a hole in the panel where the bolt slips into to
lock the seat in. This bolt also goes thru a side reinforcing body brace. It
is a tight fit and you may have to push this seat snugly against the back of
the drivers seat to slide the bolt in.
