Stephen,

If you are using any type of modern paint system that uses isocyinates (if you
don't know...find out from your supplier!) you should absolutely use a
supplied air breathing system that pumps fresh air to a mask or hood.  Since
you stated that you have a beard, I would suggest getting a hood.  The pump
pushes the air through a tube to the back of the hood and the positive
pressure keeps all the nasty stuff out .  Check out
http://www.autobodystore_dot_com .  They sell a few different systems.  The Hobby
Air is the least expensive and runs around $250 I think.

Steve
