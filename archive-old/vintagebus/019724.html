Just drill two new holes through the trim piece 30 mm
in from each edge.

Or, first get a pair of the endcaps for the trim piece
and use them as a guide. There is a hole in each one
of those which lines up wiht the last hole in the
trim. The rubber strip insert will cover the old
holes.

Make sure you bevel the holes like the rest of them.
You can do this by drilling a small hole just large
enough for the screw threads to pass through, then
CAREFULLY use a much larger drill bit and just kiss
the hole with the tip of the bit, making a bevel. It
works best with a drill press.

Good Luck
Jason
