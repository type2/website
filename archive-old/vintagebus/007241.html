It seems to me that what probably happens goes something like this. As our rides age, things start to tighten up in the throttle area.  The relaxation of the choke mechanism is hampered by all of this tightness. Goes to show you that all that money spent on engineers was not wasted. Keep it like they intended and well maintained and you should have few problems. Remember those engineers were paid to design things as well as to not add it if it wasn't necessary.
