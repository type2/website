I think rear mounts were standard as of 66 or 67.   my 65 westy has factory 
mounts for the front but not the back.  

When I installed front seat belts in my 61 I used some broken torsion bars 
and drilled holes, cut about 1 foot sections.   Put these under the bus where I 
bolted the seat belts on and used big washers as well.   Other  largish metal 
plates will do... take a look under the front seat belt mounts of your bus.
