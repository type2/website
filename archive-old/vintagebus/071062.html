The only things you use in that kit are the two paper gaskets, small o ring
and the seal that presses into the cover that goes over the bearings.
I have been told by a respectable trans builder to never use the metal
spacers in those kits, they are too thin and will not make a good seal on
the bearings.

torqued properly. The drum is what compacts the small o ring. Are your
spring plates in good shape?
