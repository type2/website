Subject: RE: [VB] engine, sp verses duel port

 The problem with single port heads is that they have stopped making them
now. This is just a heads up to all you single porters out there to start
saving all your single port heads as new ones are NLA. It's pretty bad when
your suppliers (mine) tell me that they have bought the last of them and are
hording them now. If anybody is thinking of using NEW single port heads, if
you find them buy them before it's to late.
  On another note. All of the new single port heaads have the same valves in
them as NEW duel port heads.
  I am also in the process of rebuilding my 1776 single port engine (blew
the thrust bearing out of it (50,000 plus miles). To help solve the mystery
of duel port verses single port power, I have made arrangements to dyno the
engine. After I run it with the single port heads I am going to put a set of
duel port on it and dyno it with them also. I have duel Kadrons on it so I
am going to use the same carbs in both test. Hopefully this will let
everyone know the difference in torque and HP through the RPM range.

ronnie
