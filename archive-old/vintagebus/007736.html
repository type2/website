I just got done asking the same questions.  I have 15" rims on my bus and
there is very little available out there regarding small truck duty tires.
If you have 14", the option are still narrowed but you have a helluva lot
more than if you are stuck with 15" rims.

Currently, the only LT (Light Truck) size available (this is in either 6 or
8 ply sidewalls) are 205's.  A 15" rim is 4.5" wide and a 14" is 5.5" wide.
I didn't like the look of the P205/15's on such a narrow rim - it looked
like an over-inflated balloon to me.  So I spent hours and days scanning the
net and finally settled on a Tranpath S/U from Toyo Tires.  It's only a 1
ply sidewall but the tire is rated for between 1300 and 1400 pounds.  It's
also listed as acceptible for use on Sport Utility vehicles (probably like a
Geo Tracker).  I know I've chosen a tire that according to the list
concensus is sub-standard but I've made the choice and will live with it.
Oh and the tire is a 195 - it looks nice on those 15" rims.

Ronnie at www.type2parts_dot_com is going to check at the SEMA show about having
some LT 195's made but I couldn't wait until November or December for my
tires.
