You can run vacuum advance with dual carbs, just make sure your signal
is pulling from two cylinders. Dual carbs seem to do a bit better than a
single carb with a centrifugel only distributor. Kad's really respond
well to running vacuum advance. The carbs have to be modified for this
though.
