Subject: RE: [VB] Which clutch & TO to use??

    It depends if you have a 200 mm clutch or a 180 mm clutch. On the 67
trans (if original) it uses the early t/o bearing, thus a p/p with the ring
on it and a t/o bearing with horseshow style clips. 
    If you need a new p/p make sure that who ever you get it from will take
it back. The new Brazilian Sachs p/p that I have seen are No Good. They
changed the design and they do not center on the flywheel properly. Don't
ask how much this cost me to find out.
