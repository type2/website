the over riders are the same -   59 through 67.  They are different front and
back, and the tubeses and guads for the back have rights and lefts.

the 59- 65s came with bumpers that were 'slash' - the edges went like the
curve of the wheel well, the 66 and 67s came with pointy ended bumpers.   

(  They are, however, just as functional and also physically interchangable,
this being a detail that may be important to some, but others dont notice.  in
fact, in the vw rags you see 'restored' buses with the wrong style and/or
mismatched bumpers- even deluxes with the wrong rear trim for the blade.    )
