Use a single stage acrylic enamel.  Don't bother with a good two part urethane
this time.  You'll save at least $100.00 bucks a gallon.  Be aware, that this
paint is much softer than a catalyzed paint.  You'll want to wait at least a
month or so if you plan on sanding and buffing.
