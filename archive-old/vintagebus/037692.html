Try hitting the spindle where the tie rod goes through with a hammer at a 
perpendicular angle to the tie rod shaft.  This will distort the tappered 
hole the tie rod is stuck in for a moment and the tie rod will suddenly be 
loose.  When reassembling remember to grease everything well!  Eric&Barb
