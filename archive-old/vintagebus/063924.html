on the side of the doors read ' Freiwillige Feuerwehr Hohne' which
translates to the Volunteer Fire Brigade of Hohne.  Hohne is a small town
near Wolfsburg.

So, if the frame is in need of major repair in this location, and the part
right behind both dog legs also needs to be replaced, that means I need
metal.  I know of a 62 15 window bus near me that is nearly stripped of all

opportunity to take it away and restore it but none have stepped forward.
Would the dog legs and other tid bits fit???  Also, I would need a new floor
section or at least on the drivers side.  Anybody have a nos piece, good
used, or a suggestion for a replacement piece that would be fairly
respectable?  I think there are a few of the outward frame supports that
could be replaced as well.   And while I'm at it, I may as well replace the
lower nose section below the seam or at least the inner section.
