My speedo is dead on the nose correct with 6:40-15"s
bias plys and is exactly 5 mph too fast with 195-75's.
 We have those neat little police set up MPH stands in
Ohio that warn you of your speed.  Cool for checking

Never had problems with any overheating...run a 1600
sp with semi-hemi cut berg heads compression is 7:0-1
and use 93-94 octane.
