I would tend to agree w/ Josh.

My westy is quite stock and i don't like hacking it up.

98% of the time the coldbox/icebox does not reside in the bus because

1. it is a major space hog
2. it makes getting in the bus a PITA because it blocks the front door
3. the insulation/weatherseal is so crummy in the bus that the ice melts very quickly

i went and bought an "extreme" coleman cooler that has double thick insulated walls and good weatherstripping snaps tight and keeps ice COLD!

best of all when you get to the campsite you can _easily_ take the coolers out to free up space inside the camper, and as an added benefit you can sit on them..

by contrast the cold box is a brute to get out, conveneiently store etc..
