  The outer framing pieces from Schofield are just square boxed pieces that
could be made in a metal brake by any body. They are not close to original,
but would work in a pinch. I have imported and tried all of his sheetmetal
and some is great and some is marginal. Kelly (aka metalwiz) makes a lot of
the pieces that are much better then Schofields as far as fit and original
shapes. 
  If you are looking for sheetmetal Kelly (aka metalwiz) would be the man to
contact as he has used about everything repoed  out there and makes the
stuff that he can't find of good quality. He would be more then happy to
sell you what he makes and refer you to whoever else has the parts he likes.

As far as the stuff being made in Michigan, it will be distributed out of

eastern distributor but politics seem to block that outlet for the time
