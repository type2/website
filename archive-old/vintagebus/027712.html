   A secret to clearing out the idle circuit on any Solex carb with a idle
cut off or idle air correction solenoid is to 1. remove the solenoid  2.
remove the air correction jet 3. blow compressed air through both openings
4. replace all parts and your idle will be back.
   I do this all the time here in the shop and have solved many an idle
problem by doing this. You would not believe how many vehicles come in the
shop with this problem after major replacement of about every tune up part
there is and all it turns out to be is a piece of crap plugging an orifice
in the idle circuit.
