In addition to Eric&Barb's excellent suggestion of cleaning the
terminals and contacts, you might check your dash lights.  There
is a variable resistor in the headlight switch that dims your
dash lights when you turn the knob.  If there's too much current
being drawn on the dash lights wire, this resistor could be
heating up.  It's possible that something "extra" (like a radio)
is being powered by the dash lights due to an incorrect hookup.
