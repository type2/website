There are plenty of posts in the archives on this subject, some written by
me.

However, your account of the seat being cut and upholstery being torn out
sets a new record, I believe, for malicious destruction.  The perpetrators
of such mayhem are usually agents of US Customs.  Canadian Customs officers
tend to be much more civil in their approach.

There is ample documentation of VW buses being disassembled by US Customs
operatives, but your account is the first I've seen of either side's people
actually destroying something.  More typically parts and pieces are removed
with minimal damage to individual components.  You're still left with a big
mess to put back together, but the parts are useable.

I don't know what the protocol would be to make a claim for damages against
Canada, but in your place I'd be burning up the phone to my Congressional
representatives, the Dept. of State, and the Dept. of Justice.  It would
also be prudent to lodge a claim with your insurance company.
