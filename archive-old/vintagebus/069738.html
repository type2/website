Not exactly vintage related, but pulley related. For hovercraft use where two
belts are needed, we have welded a second stock pulley with the center hub ct
out to a stock pulley. After balancing with a touch of arc weld here and there
we discovered that the heavier pulley acted as a vibration dampener and seemed
to add a quantity more hours to the engine. No empirical data, but of some
interest to those who are thinking of running a heavier pulley.
