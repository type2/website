Hi Terry & Sunshine,
We use a screwdriver and hammer to punch out the flashing on the cylinders.
Also you should check your heads for the same.  We just bought 4 new heads
from brazil and they all had alot of flash in the front, rear and down the
center.  For the front and rear ends we used a circular saw with a used
carbide blade for wood to clean out the flash there, took about 2 minutes for
each head.  Then files and drill bits to rid the flash in the center passage.
As for your case it sounds like it was a type 3 engine in it's previous life.
If you are not looking to drag race with this engine and it is used German and
in good condition as is it should work well.    Eric & Barb
