I know I have been flamed for mentioning this before, but its worth putting 
out there: In oly there used to be a mobile mechanic by the name of Dylan.

using some very simple tools. Anyways, he was a trained mechanic, and I have 
no reason to doubt his word.

He had a 1641 he put in, and swears he got way over 200,000 miles with that 
set up. Judging by his service calls, his trips, and just his plain attitude 
about the bus, I cant say I doubt it. It was no show bus, but he was all 
over the place. He pulled the heads off, replaced rings occasionally, and 
thats it.
