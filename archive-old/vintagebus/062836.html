  If you need the parts CUT IT. I did the same to a very straight 59 panel.
I needed a good nose clip and early doglegs. None of which I could find at a
reasonable price. I now have a beautiful nose, nice doglegs and some other
OG sheet metal that I needed for my Westy's, much more importaant to me then
a plain ol panel. Another plus is that some of the parts will eventually go
to the repair of a 55 BD and a 56 Safari bus. So 1 cut up bus is saving 4
others. 
  I did get a lot of shit when I cut up my 55 BD Deluxe and sold the 55 BD
panel. Most of the VN's said I should have sacrificed a perfect restoreable
BD panel to save the Deluxe of which I would of had to swap chassis on!!
  I have seen the Captain America bus and would love to see it refurbished,
but your needs and those of your other buses should come first. 
  I have seen some very clean kombis cut to pieces for a couple pieces of
sheet metal to save Deluxes ( a waste in my opinion), but the people doing
it make a lot more money on a pieced together Deluxe then they would of on a
nice dry Kombi!

ronnie  it's your stuff do what you need to do
