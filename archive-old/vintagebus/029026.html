Since nobody else jumped on this one, I will.  I know that Westies and
Sundials both used the crank out windows.  The way to tell the diff between
a westy and a sundial is:  Westies have a window behind the 2 cargo door
windows, and the Sundial doesn't.  The Sundials were made out of panel van,
and the windows were added by Sundial, whereas the Westies were built from
a standard bus.
