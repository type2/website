you can used wooden popsicle sticks to pry back the rubber. From the inside, 
and then push the glass out  to be caught by the person standing on the 
outside.  A bunch of popsicle sticks.  Metal screwdrivers will chip the 
glass.  with sekurit glass (which you back hatch should be) the glass can 
flex a whole bunch, but laminated/sigla/delog glass it will crack before it 
flexes.  I removed the back window this way when painting my westy, as I had 
just installed an NOS seal with nice molded corners a couple years before and 
wanted to reuse it.   It's kinda neat to see how the glass flexes.  I was 
really surprised.
