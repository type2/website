yes you must take the RGBoxes apart to remove them and the tubes and axles 
from the tranny center section.  If you are going to do this (and if you have 
changed a transaxle why not) I remcommend getting a copy of the bentley 
reprint of the transporter workshop manual 1963 edition, covers 63-67 buses 
and shows in detail this procedure.   Also get a pair of -big- snap ring 
pliers for the circlip holdiong the thrust washer and crown gear in the 
tranny.

It is fairly straightforward.
