This engine number would be approximately mid-1960, so the engine is 
about a year or so newer than the Bus.  Muir says the "bastard" 40-hp
started in May '59 at 469 506 and Doliwa says it started May 19 at
469 447.  This puts yours about a month early to originally have
the "bastard" engine, but maybe it had one and got changed out, OR
it didn't have one and was changed out anyway on the recall by a
dealer who wasn't careful about checking the chassis numbers.

As far as the Bus' condition...look at the "as bought" pictures of
Thom's '54 RHD 23-window on vintagebus_dot_com.  I would say that yours is
in better shape than that.  IMHO this bus is very save-able...if it
is beyond you, sell it to someone who can fix it up.  Again, just my
opinion.
