The thunk from a loose fastener/s is more a bone deep sounding bang/klang.  
Once you have heard it, and diagnosed it you will always know it from then 
on.  Kind of like being able to diagnos loose heads from the sound produced 
from listening to a hard acceleration from a stop.  Which releases a pounding 
popping noise.
