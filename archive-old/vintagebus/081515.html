On a simple manually switched parallel battery system, you can charge them 
both at the same time. I wouldn't flip the switch while the charger is 
attached. I also wouldn't charge it for more than 24 hours at a time. 
Overcharging a battery is as bad for it as letting it sit and discharge 
through disuse.
