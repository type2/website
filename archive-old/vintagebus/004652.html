Before you get into this any deeper, it's vital to know whether these speeds
are _indicated_ MPH or _corrected_ MPH.  [See the archives for speedo
calibration procedure.]

VW speedometers can be notoriously optimistic.  If these are _indicated_
speeds, your true MPH is probably significantly lower than these numbers.

Some folks prefer to cruise at 3,000RPM max., although experience has shown
[and VW specs verify] that 4,000 is actually pretty conservative for the
typical 1600 SP or DP engine.
