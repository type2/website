Another suggestion to the hot water in getting the rubber strips pliable is
the use of a paint stripper gun. I have used this numerous times when heat
is required to eliminate the normal tussle with "cold" parts. Check the heat
continually by using  your fingers as the temperature sensor and you can
also tell the "heat" by the color or shine and smell of the rubbber as you
work it. Smoke, of course is a bad sign! It works, have never burned my
fingers, or anything else for that matter... give it a try
