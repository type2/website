That is called "casting flash". The archives don't have much on this;
several Hoovers but he mentions it only in regard to heads, not barrels.
Brian Verbeek seems to have had a similar problem and abraded it off, which
is what I would do, especially if it were extensive.

	http://www.i405_dot_com/vwarchive/default.asp?body=casting+flash&sub=&ml=5

That last should be all one line.

Whoever made those barrels was a hack and you should measure the bore and
check everything *else* that might be screwed up before you install.
