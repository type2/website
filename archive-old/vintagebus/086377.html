For the record. 15x5.5J Mangels on a '65 Bus w/General UHP M+S  
205/70R15s. Good clearance behind the tires, very small or no

sidewall is a bit fatter than the tread.)

matter to me right now. But I'll have to either weld in the metal an  
inch higher and/or put the tires on the original rims (which are  
nasty/rusty right now).
