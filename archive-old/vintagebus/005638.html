Harbor Freight has a cheap set of bore gauges (  $10 ).
These are Tee shaped tools of various sizes that telescope to
fit and by lightly locking , then "rocking " once through the hole, they hold
the size of the bore and can be read with a quality measuring device
for a very accurate measurenent. There you go.
