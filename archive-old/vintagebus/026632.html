I agree that the project of installing the albatross beam was a bit more work

to underestimating the work involved. I have had no trouble reaching them,
and they have been very willing to answer questions on the phone. I note that

I am not sure what advice to give you about the suitability of your master
cylinder for disc brakes. I have the very expensive '67 only unit and it is a
dual circuit unit. A disc brake conversion will require the installation of
the pressure reducer from a bay window bus to the rear drum circuit and the
installation of a 2 LB check valve in the front brake circuit to keep a
little pressure on the calipers to keep them near the discs. this prevents
excessive pedal travel. The buses with discs from the factory had this built
into the master cylinder.
Check the archives for more info.
