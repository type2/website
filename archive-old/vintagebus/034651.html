1.  no idea on the top you have

2.  Dont have pics, but different camper kits I've had include the full so 42 
westy (the cabinet on door is very useful) a riveria which had copied the 
concept from so 42 westy (cabinet on door very useful)   Also have had a 
platform in the back behind the back doors for a bed  THis one was just up to 
the rear cargo door, and though many people said, isn't that bed short?,  I 
didn't have a problem., also that extended  with or without hinge to make a 
hidden storage under neath.  Or just an so 42 westy Z bed in the back.  You 
could take a Z-bed and widen it to make it full width bench/Z bed.    Also a 
homemade kit, that is really hard to explain, but makes a lot of cabinets 
including one behind the driver with a pad  behind head in case of whip lash, 
and drawers you have to lift to open, but though that was okay as a camper, 
it was difficult to move large object in there.  Anyway, you can make really 
nice panels with 1/8 inch birch veneer, just think abot which way the grain 
goes before you cut.

3. rear bumper- through 71 will fit, but the bumper blade is different.  yes 
I have put a bay window bumper on a splitty, better than nothing.  THink a 
bright white rear bumper makes you more visable at night, safety safety.  
After I painted the bumper for my westy, I was told it was so much more 
visable.
