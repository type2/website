   There really aren't any true stainless mufflers made. We started testing
one in Colorado 3 years ago and it has rusted out. Not the muffler itself,
but the top pipes going into the muffler. The upper flanges cast and flat
were made from cast iron like an original muffler has and we feel there was
a reaction between the steel and the stainless. These mufflers cost 300
dollars and were true stainless except for the parts mentioned. Supposedly
they are full stainless now (includeing the in pipes, off head)but the price
for the average none rust belt area wouldn't warrant the cost.
