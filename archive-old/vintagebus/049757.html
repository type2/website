You can get a Porsche 356 generator to fit your existing generator stand
(expensive), or change to a 12v generator stand and use a type 1/2
generator, or change to an alternator stand and use a 12v generator or
alternator.  Your bus may already have the 12v size (105mm?) generator
stand, as many 1500 buses had the large size 6v generator.  The large
6v/12v generator is about the same diameter as the generator pulley.  The
standard 6v/Porshe 12v generator is noticably smaller than the pulley.
