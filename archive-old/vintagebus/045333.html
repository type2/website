<< I am in the process of removing a sapphire V from an early 67 to my late 
67.  Is there something I am missing on getting it out, seems like there are 
4 tabs holding it in, how do I remove the faceplate.
Hi Patrick,
Pull off knobs and collars.  Undo nuts around knobs shafts.  Find and unbolt 
from the body up under the dash the support on the forward end of radio.  
Drop radio down and disconnect antenna, power, and speaker wires.  The face 
plate and the backing/stiffing plate should should then easily pop apart from 
each other.
