hi group,

I am using one of these swampy air conditioners in my work truck, and
personally, i am pleased with the results.

it wont cool of the entire inside of the bus, rather it provides a cool
breeze for you.  just point the vents at you, and it works great.  i put
it between the front seats of my bus, so it blew air at my feet and
kinda swirled up around the rest of me, and it makes it a lot more
tolerable.  you do have to leave your windows open, or else it will just
get humid.  humid = pleh!

so its not going to match the ac in your "other" car, but does help.

hope this info helps
