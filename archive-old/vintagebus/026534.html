Bosch is no longer rebuilding ( nor making new) the sr-17x "automatic"
starter, so this may not be an option for long.
Also, always keep in mind that although you dont need to match up the
correct bsuhing size, since the "auto" starter is self-supporting, it
will still ruin the flywheel of a 6-volt engine. The teeth on this
starter are still matched to the teeth on a 12-volt flywheel. I have
seen several 6-volt flywheels ruined when someone slammed in a sr-17x
starter. It worked for a while. Then it was a starter and flywheel
replacement problem.  The sr-17x is NOT a panacea.
