I'd just run what you got.
Make money decisions when you have to if your engine isn't performing
well.
When it isn't up to quota make the changes then. I find that most parts
don't fail all at once (dropped valve seats the exception).
I've seen cracked heads that ran fine but I wouldn't reuse them because I
never knew if they were just starting or just finishing on the cracking.
If your engine runs good I wouldn't pull the heads I'd just run them.
The long discussion on DP vs SP heads hasn't impressed me much, seems
they are a matter of choice.
With higher compression you can expect your engine to run hotter so more
compression might not be what you need.
