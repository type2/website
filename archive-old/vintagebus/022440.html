Das Bulli Haus is the stereotypical "mom & pop" operation.  It's just Clay
and Mary, who both have full time jobs; Clay runs an automotive repair shop
and towing biz, and Mary works at the local college.  DBH has always been a
part time gig for them.

problems.  The guy who makes the pieces (Allan Schofield) was having some
production problems in addition to some health problems.

for a while for it to be fulfilled, but since mine is a long term project,
I'm not in any significant hurry.  I've personally bought almost $2k worth
of stuff from them between the two buses, and it's only going to go up from
there.

Personally, I feel its worth the wait.  If you need something yesterday,
then you're probably stuck with the "normal" mail order houses.
