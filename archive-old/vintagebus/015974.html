I think what this person was trying to ask "Is there a
difference between a shroud and a doghouse?"  

To answer your question, a doghouse is a *style* of
shroud, correct?  I think it's a later style shroud,
just as Thom said.  I'm not sure if earlier shrouds
had names, other than "engine cooling tin."  IMHO,
stay the heck away from chrome cooling parts, even if
you chrome a perfectly good used shroud.  Chrome
reflects heat, and that seems counter-productive to
what cooling tin is supposed to do - diffuse and
exhaust heat. Even when you chrome used thicker stock
stuff, it's still chrome.
