Hi,

Shocks can be a blessing if correct and make for a very rough ride if
too stiff. If too stiff the shock doesn't collapse easily enough and
prevents the springs (read torshion bars) from flexing and taking up
the bump. If not stiff enough, the wheel will continue to bounce well
after going over a bump resulting in wierd tire wear as well as an
uncomfortable ride.

All you guys installing heavy duty shocks are just creating a rough
ride. The factory tuned units are usually the ones that supply the
most comfortable ride.

So there you have it.
