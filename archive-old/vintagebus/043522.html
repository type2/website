>On the front cover it says "EINE BILDSERIE".  My German is totally rusted
>out, but could that mean something like "First Model Year"?

Nope, it just means "A picture series."  Bild is a picture, bilder is
the plural.  Building like in construction or assembly would be another
word, like bau.  Baujahr is "build year".  "First Model Year" would
be something like "Das erste Modelljahr" or "Das erste Baujahr".

Sites like http://babelfish.altavista.digital_dot_com can help you Bondo
and rattle-can your rusty German, but if it's really gone, you have
to weld in new metal (take classes) or pay someone else to do it.  :)
