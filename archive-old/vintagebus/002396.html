It's been guessed by p-mail (Erin Lassley) and on the list (Chuck Notmeyer),
so now we can reveal what part can be removed from a 1950 Barndoor Deluxe
walk-through and installed on a 1998 Corvette...... Valve Stems... otherwise
known as Schrader valves. Strangely enough, they can be installed on a 1965
Schwinn bicycle, but not on my 1990 Raleigh Technium road bike. The skinny
tired racing bikes use a Presta valve. No spring and an external screw to
positively lock the 120 lbs. of air in.
