depends if the are laminated or tempered.  If they are tempered (Sekurit logo)
they are very sturdy-   wrap them up with a couple layers of cardboard around
them, between each one, make sure they won't slip around.    They are fine.  I
dropped an intake manifold from ten feet up on a piece of tempered vw glass
and it just bounced off.    I mailed some to someone.

But- if it is laminated- sigla or delog logo, be very very careful.  wrap them
in much bubble wrap and pray .   they can crack almost if you look at them.
I think the mailbox places suggest two inches padding (bubble wrap, or some
such)
