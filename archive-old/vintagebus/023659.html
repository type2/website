Hi Jon,
It sounds like your flexable brake lines are going bad.  Causing one of the
front brakes not to get the brake pressure until you really press hard.
Meanwhile since you have had to exert so much pressure the otherside front
brake locks up before the blocked side acuates.  When the blocked side finally
does work, it does not get all of the pressure so that side suddenly pulls
that direction.  At the least you need to replace the front two flexable brake
lines and if you can afford it replace them all to prevent more problems from
old flexable brake lines.  Eric & Barb
