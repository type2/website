The 1966 bus 6V generator is the same diameter as the 1967 & later 12V 
generator (it also uses 12V brushes), so any 12V generator stand would 
work with the 1966 bus 6V generator.

>Does a 66 bus use a squared off or rounded engine tin in front of the 
>cooling fan right above the tranny and flywheel?  I know my 71 Westy used 
>a boxed shape piece of tin.

The front firewall tin that is squared off was only used on 1968-71 buses.
