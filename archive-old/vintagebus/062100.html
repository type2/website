I did not comment on the stuff already answered by others.

Check the archives a little bit as well because last week we had a good
discussion on tranny ratios and Reduction Box ratios which might be helpful.
In your case since it seems you have a 3.88 R&P (hopefully with a .82 forth)
and you could run small nut RGBs and then maybe use any of the Beetle rear
disc kits that have e-brakes..?  Bus Boys/Wolfgang/Vintage Warehouse should
have used sets of small nut RGBs you can put on.

Also when you go full discs be sure to get an appropriate master cylinder to
match the 4 wheel discs.  For Thomas and I we will be using the 944 MC to
match our brake systems, both of us are also using the Wolfgang adapter kit
for a baywindow MC.  I think an late 70's or early 80's Audi MC would work
as well, just be sure it is a 4 wheel disc car like a Turbo.

Sounds like your engine is all set... Be sure to put in a head temp gauge
and oil temp gauge as well.
