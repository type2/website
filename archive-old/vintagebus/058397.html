Subject: RE: [VB] 1600 SP valve Q

I've had a few heads actually suck in new valve seats that were not
installed correctly. Also, if you installed heavy valve springs there may be
some wear under the springs on the head. One of the other areas was ill
fitting lifters on assembly that finally seat all the way down after some
driving. That will also change your geometry. On my blocks That get larger
cams I usually have the lifter holes faced and 100 degree chamfer so I know
they are seating correctly and are all at the same height.

Just as a note, my son put together a 1500 a few years back and used a lot
of my extra parts from the unsorted boxes. He had a heck of a time with
lifter rattle until he tore it down and found out that one of the lifters he
used was a thin crown 40hp. Live and learn, and check your parts first!
