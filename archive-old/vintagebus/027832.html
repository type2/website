I use castrol 30wt in winter, (temps usually 30-45*F, rarely down to 20* and 
20/50 in summer.  When I went to AZ last spring I went to 40wt, which is 
thicker than 20/50, both by pouring it and evidence of the oil pressure 
guage.  My oil pressure gets pretty high using 20/50  when it is cold out, so 
I change to thinner oil when the oil pressure gets close to peg (when it is 
about 70psi) upon startup  even if its a bit earlier than my usual 3K mi oil 
changes.
