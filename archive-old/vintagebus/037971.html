<< If I need replacements where do I get them?>>
So far as we know both the upper and lower axles are a used only item.  so
wrecking yards, swap meets or used parts dealers.  Also check your VW
workshop manual about having your old upper axles paddle areas redone to new
condition by having a chrome shop do some work on just the inside ends.  If
any one knows of supplies of new lower and upper axles we would love to hear
of them!
