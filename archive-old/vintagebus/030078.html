Subject: re: [VB] transmision mount replacement

Support the engine/tranny on a rolling floor jack. Support the bus on
jackstands. Disconnect the clutch cable, throttle cable, and shifter
bushing. You may also have to disconnect some engine wiring and fuel
line, if you don't have any slack to work with. Remove the 2 large bolts
holding the engine cradle to the frame horns. Remove the bolts holding
the reduction boxes to the spring plates. Pry the engine/tranny back, so
you can get to the old tranny mount. You will be working against the
e-brake cables and brake lines, so be careful not to stress them out too
much. Reassemble.

I've done this a couple of times. It is not a piece of cake.
