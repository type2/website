November 04, 2007 - Karl Custom VW Car show and swapmeet 
Mesa, Arizona 
Karls Custom 9th annual VW car show and swapmeet will take place Sunday
November 4th, 2007 in Mesa, AZ at 264 E Broadway Road. [Broadway and Mesa
drive]. Come see over 100 vintage and custom VW vehicles and a really huge
VW swapmeet. Free spectator admission at 7:00 AM. Store will be open for
business 10AM till noon during the show with special discounts . There will
be an engine blow and DJ Tony will spin the discs..............Vintage,
Custom, Dunebuggy, Rat Rod or Rail, we want to see them all out there Sunday
morning. Special ring of honor for past winners and displays. Swap opens at
6:30 am with spaces at $25.00 each. Trucks with trailer =2 spaces. No
motorhomes please. Car show entries by 9:30 AM . Car show fees are $10.00
per vehicle with lots of categories to win. Awards at 1:30pm. 

Contact Tom at Karls Mesa 480 827 8051 or email volksworks_at_cox_dot_net for more
info.

 

 

November 09, 2007 - November 11, 2007 - Volkswagens on the River 6 (Yuma
Show) 
Yuma, Arizona 
Volkswagens on the River 6 BBQ, Campout, Show and Swap! 

Welcome to our famous BBQ, Bon Fire, and Campout November 9th and 10th! New
this year is our camping location, a second night of camping, showers, and
flushing toilets! $25 per vehicle covers Friday and Saturday night camping
and the BBQ for the driver. Additional passengers pay $5/adult and $3/child
for the BBQ only. Plenty of free drinks-Keg beer, sodas, water, etc. We will
camp on a grassy area so you can camp in your bus, in a tent, on a cot, etc.
Please, no RVs. If you pull a trailer with your VW that is accepable. Starts
Friday 3PM at the Yuma County Fair Grounds located at 2520 E. 32nd Street
Yuma, Arizona 85365 

Vollkswagens on the River 6 Show and Swap! Saturday November 10th, 8AM to
3PM. West Wet Lands Park 1200 W. Water Street Yuma, Arizona 85364. Trophies,
T-shirts, Raffles-including a rebuilt long block, Engine Blow, Kids Events,
Food, Cold and Hot Drinks. New to the park this year is Arizona's largest
play ground designed by children of Yuma and built by volunteers. Spectator
entry $2, Vehicle show entry $10, Swap/Vendor space $15. 

Jeff and his friendly staff at Import Auto Supply of Yuma will hold an open
house this year. The past couple years he has opened his closed shop just to
provide parts to people that had broke down so they could get their rides
home. Stop by after the show and thank him for his sponsorship of the show.
He will have some free munchies and drinks for you. Nice thing is that they
are located right out side the West gate of the Fair Grounds! Check them out
right after the show from 3PM to 6PM at 3003 S. Pacific Avenue Yuma, Arizona
