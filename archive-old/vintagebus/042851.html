I just got back from an adventure in the foothills of Moscow, Idaho.  My
buddy informed earlier today that he had seen on the side of the road an
"old VW bus with the small back window."  I instantly thought pre-1960!

the one that Thom purchased a few months ago.  I tried talking to the
