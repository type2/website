The only differnce is that the back of the rear seat has  the metal bar on it.
Everything 65 came with salt and pepper (mesh grey) interior.   You can use
the middle and bottom of the rear seat from a 66 or 67 standard.  Both
standards and deluxes had the trim panels on the rea and middle seats.

But you don't want the seats from a commercial vehicle.  Well, the frames are
the same, I mean the upholstery is different.   Passenger vans had pleated
seats, and commercials did not have pleats.    Good original.  salt and pepper
seat covers are extremely hard to find, though.
