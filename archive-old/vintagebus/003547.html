  When I ordered my engine from Bernie Bergmann, I told him I was planning
to install a freeway flier. He recommended and supplied (for a nominal fee,
of course) a 'Porsche' generator pulley. This is smaller in diameter than
the stock pulley, resulting higher fan RPMs to compensate for the lower
engine speed. It's a simple thing and might be just what you need.
