Trying to save the seal is false economy.  Buck up and buy a new seal!

Do you have a real regulator on this compressor?  If not, get one.  The
HVLP is a little more sensitive than conventional guns to line pressure

right - two regulators.

with an integrated mask.  The paint I was using really bothered my eyes.

a few hours after shooting, and still feeling a burn in my chest!
