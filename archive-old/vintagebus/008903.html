 In defence of the 34 PICT...

I also have a 1600 DP w/ported heads and mild cam plus 009 combination. The
first 34 PICT drove me nuts and it was my own fault as I did not run an
in-line filter. Got the constant 1200 +/- rpm idle and no amount of
adjustment would fix.

Tossed it, paid $10 for another 34 to rebuild, added  in line gas filter and
followed Boston Bob's instructions w/ solid rivet, etc. Also threw in
Pertronix eliminating points. Bus starts within 2 revolutions of the
starter, idles perfectly, and no hestitation through the gears. It has on
occassion got mid 20 + mpg fully loaded at 55 mph. (larger imperial gallons,
eh!). Engine temp, plugs, exhaust color are all on the mark.
