Hi Emmet,
Rancho seems to a be a reputible place.  Best to list your area and someone 
around there maybe able to tell you of a more local shop to you.  Always nice 
to be able to drive over and be in thier face if there is a problem down the 
road.
  We would suggest using a 1960-66 tranny and just grind out the bell housing 
if you really want to use a 12 volt FW.  We just use a 6 volt 200mm FW, 
starter and a 12 volt solinoid.  Makes for a real good turning engine in even 
the coldest weather here in the NW USA.  The 1967 and later tranny have a 
very small hole for the starter bushing.  This means an even thinner 6 volt 
bushing that is almost paper thin if you decide to use a 6 volt starter.
