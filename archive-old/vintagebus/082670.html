flashers.  The US-spec hazard light system is only mentioned in the
'63-'67 Bentley, but seems to have been available before the '63 model
year.

> What is this DOLIWA that was referred to several times? I assume it's
> a production list or progressive refinements for the bus?

Yep.  It lists changes by chassis number, like Progressive Refinements,
but mortals can actually get one.  It's so OG, it's in G.  I have had
more than one person tell me that there is an English version but I
have never found it.  It may just use the same VW publicity photo on
the front (white Vanagon, silver bay-window, 1949 split-window prototype)
as some other Bus book.

It helps if you understand some German, but with a background in Buses
and the online translators you can figure it out.  It has chapters (two
to four pages each) for each year from 1949 to 1990, with a short article
and pictures in each chapter.

One thing you have to watch out for is that the "overview" charts in the
back are for the German Buses, so for example it shows the 1600 available
in a Bus all the way through '81.  It also has M-code lists for split-
window Buses, bay-window Buses, and Vanagons.

http://www.amazon_dot_de/exec/obidos/ASIN/3613020246/ says it is EUR 26,00
or about US $35.  Shipping to the US is 9-12 working days and costs
another EUR 15,50 or about US $21, for about US$56 all told.  There may
be customs on top of this; I don't know.

There is a similar book in English: "VW Transporter and Microbus
Specification Guide 1950-1967" by David Eccles.  Amazon doesn't stock it
directly but has links to purchase it new in hardback for about US $30:
http://www.amazon_dot_com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/1861265093/ .  The site
announcing the book
http://www.btinternet_dot_com/~dace.ecc/ordering_information.htm has links
to British distributors Crowood Press and VW Books.  Crowood offers a
hardback http://www.crowoodpress.co_dot_uk/2004/book_details.asp?ISBN=1+86126+509+3
at GBP 15.96 and a paperback
http://www.crowoodpress.co_dot_uk/2004/book_details.asp?ISBN=1+86126+652+9
at GBP 11.96.  Surface mail (== slow) shipping is GBP 5.50 for either
book, bringing the total to about US $42 or $34.  VW Books has the
paperback http://www.vwbooks.co_dot_uk/catalogue/covers/vwb1040.htm for
GBP 14.95 or about US $29.  They don't have a fixed shipping charge,
but do offer air mail (== fast).

If you're a true Bus nut, you might want both, as each one seems to have
changes that are not in the other.  I did a comparison of the changes
listed for 1964: http://www.type2_dot_com/archive/vintagebus/063401.html .
