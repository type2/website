likely are just barely slipping into the left side of the third & fourth 
slot.  If you replaced the hockey stick with a new one it might have the set screw

might have to ovalize the holes in the floor to allow for some adjustment that 
way.  Do you have the reverse gear lock out plate set in there correctly?
