Just wanted to update you all on the progress of the new shop.

The city codes dictated that if I built a 30 x 40 shop, I would have to do 2
things.  First off, the 8 x 12 utility shed I built would "have to go" as
you are limited to a total of 2 out-buildings with a combined total of no
more than 1200 square feet.  Second, any out-building that exceeds 1000
square feet must be double layered with sheetrock because as they put it,
"It's a torch waiting to burn."  This of course doesn't factor in that 1200
square feet between 2 buildings can actually hold more than 1 building
totaling 1200 squre feet but I digress.  The resultant beefed up foundation
and sheetrock pricing would more than double the cost I had budgeted for the
building.

So the shop space was put down to a palsy 30 x 32 which in this area and to
me is still a rather good size.  It beats the 12 x 18 I have now by a long
shot.  Even the loan officer said, "That's a pretty good size shop."  At
least I still get to keep my 8 x 12 shed and pack it full of yet more crap.

After handing in some AutoCAD drawing that my brother and I drew up, I went
down, impressed the hell out of the inspector (because us po' folk in dem
dar VDubs jist dudn't do dat torta ting.) I finally got the buidling permit
last Friday, I got the $5K loan today, and the contractor that is supposed
to drill my holes told me at work today, "Just let me know when you want
them drilled."  There will be 13 holes dug 12 inches across and 42 inches
deep.

I want to set up the webcam to film this but I have a paltry 6 foot cord.  I
need about 10 to 12 feet to reach the best view out of the window so does
anyone know if a USB extension cord exists and if this will work for USB
applications?
