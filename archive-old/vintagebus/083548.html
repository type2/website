I sold my first bus and I regretted it. I took my wife camping in it  and had 
the best of times. I sold it because I thought I could put a dollar  amount

thought. So  if you enjoy having it and plan to use it then you may regret it. If 
it's  another money pit to you then you may never think twice. I know there are 
many  people that would love to have one.
