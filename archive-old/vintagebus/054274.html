 You forgot to mention what year your bus is. If its new enougn to use the 
bug 1600
 engine or whatever, you may have a bad bypass air cut-off valve. Its item 6 
in the latest RMMW catalog. Usually, the head gets knocked off (the thingy 
that exists to the far left of item 6 in the RMMW catalog) causing the engine 
to stall instead of idle. The solution is to replace the bypass air cut-off 
valve for $18.95. It screws onto the carb.

Just thought of something - a test for whether of not your bypass air cutoff 
valvve is working. Enlist a helper. Have the helper turn the key on. listen 
for a click. If the click
occurs, the bypass cutoff valve is OK. If it does not click, the valve has 
the head knocked off and is bad. Replace it in the latter case.
