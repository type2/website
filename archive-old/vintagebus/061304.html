> 4. (bonus question) I'm just curious, what's the difference between an
> alternator and generator? What do I have? Everything 914 is
> "alternator", everything 356 seems to be "generator".

An alternator produces alternating current and uses diodes (one way
electrical valves) to convert the AC to DC to charge your battery.  A
generator uses a heavy-duty commutator to switch the current.  Alternators
are lighter and, since the current comes from the outside coils, there is
no need to pass large currents through the commutator.

Question:  Why not use a late model VW alternator?  They're readily
available, very reliable, and will produce enough amperage to meet your
needs.  It may not be completely "stock appearing", but if you aren't
going to Pebble Beach the result might be just what you are looking for.
