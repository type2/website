I am sure someone else has said this, but buses do not have separate bodys 
and pans.   so you cant swap them like you can with a bug.  Depending on the 
rust of the 21 window,   I am sure you (or your friend) can find repair 
panels    Usually bses have rust in the rockers- that is the bit of the side 
that curves in.  There are inner and outer rockers.  there are also 
outriggers, and jack point.  and often holes in the front floor.     Hope the 
main frame is still good.  Sunroof buses also have sub floor pans for 
structiral strength, which may be in doubtful condition.   You, I mean your 
friend,  will find there are a lot more pieces to a bus than a bg, especially 
a samba bus.   If you get a copy of the Transporter Workshop Manual 1963 
edition now back in print and available through Bentley, there is a nice 
section show the bits of metal folded together to make a bus.
