Actually the 411 & 412's had very little to do with the Type 3's.  The Type 4
was pretty much built from the ground up as a new idea.  Though they did use
Type 3 rear brakes, the Type 4's are wider, longer, and quite a bit faster
than the Type 3.
  The Type 4's engine has more in common with the 914 engine than the bus and
even where availiable from the factory with a very hefty 80 horse power.
As for being a disappointment for VW, this is pretty agreed upon, thou the
car did sell rather well overseas.  The good old USA just wasn't ready for a
$4,400 VW (the original sticker price of my 73')  These cars today are
actually becomming somewhat rare, but can still be picked up for next to
nothing.  I paid $800 for my 412 with a little over 60,000 miles on it, the
car is almost like new, even has killer A/C!!
