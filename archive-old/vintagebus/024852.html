I'm sure you're right, I've never come across a pre-67 12v Bus, but I've
only owned about 13 splitties (which I thought was a lot until I started
reading this list! :) 

When my 6v 2 speed wiper failed (rolling into Seattle at 4:30 AM in the
rain, no less!) I replaced it with a ~'67 or '68 Bug motor. It takes
some work, but it's been reliable. The only difference I encountered was
the distance between the drive shaft (out of the gear box) and the
attachment point on the crank arm. I had to move this pin, which I
detailed in a post to the old list (prior to the split). 

If you're into practical, not resto, this might be an option... or at
least get you by until you can find that MIB NOS '67 wiper motor for $5!
