Hello;
You may want to check out Willson Safety products (think that's their 
name). They make a disposable Tyvek / Clear plastic full face hood designed 
for people with beards. The only place I've found it is
htttp://www.labelmaster_dot_com
of course their site is written in visual basic with many problems. I would 
suggest calling them at 1-800-621-5808 and asking about product # Q2302 
(Continuos flow hood $59.40 for 1) They also sell a 10 pack of replacement 
hoods (these do not include the apparatus for holding the hood away from 
the face as the first item does) item # Q2303 ($135.85)
All this info is from their 1998 full line catalog.
I would be wary of home brew hoods as the chemicals in some of the 
paints/primers may not be compatible with the plastics used

Hope this helps
