I actually own the $80 pressurized sand blaster from Harbor Freight.  I love
it. The only thing that I would recommend is that you buy some extra valves
for the end. Because if you accidentally don't open the end valve all the
way, it will cut through it in a matter of minutes.  This thing blasts
great, and it's completely adjustable.  I blasted thin metal on my swamp
cooler (and much more), and never cut any holes through it. Oh, and you
really need to get a sand strainer (to get any big pieces out), or you will
fight with clogged nozzles.
