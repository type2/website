The problem is NOT in the ID, OD, or width. The problem is in the clearance
between the rollers and the outer race.

Let me make this clear:
The outer race is very tight in the housing.
The inner race is very tight on the stub axle.
The rollers are permanently attached around the inner race.
When you put the inner race/roller assembly inside the outer race, you CAN
move it back and forth almost an eight of an inch. This is with the bearing
laying on a flat surface and you are moving the inner race back and forth.
With the brake drum on, you can rock the drum a 1/4 of an inch at the edge!
