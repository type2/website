To your original question historically a "Samba" or "Sondermodel" in
German refers to the Special Deluxe model bus 23 windows, Sealing Wax
Red lower half and Chestnut Brown upper half, 51-58 model year only.
These are the true "sambas" as the factory has described. After 58 the
factory stopped using this term and color scheme. People have since
referred to all deluxes as such and I'm sure some will say any split bus
is a "samba."
