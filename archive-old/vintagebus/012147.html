Not sure what to do about the windows, but I have heard two different solutions for keeping your door panels from warping.

1. Paint POR-15 on the back of the panels. This should seal them, preventing them from warping

2. Line door with trash bag and duct tape, between door frame and panel, theis keeps water away from door panel.

I am not sure which method is best, I think the POR-15 method should be easier. I need to do this on my bus myself, I just haven't had time, the PO put new panels in, and didn't seal them, so they are popping all the push-button-clamp-thingys.
