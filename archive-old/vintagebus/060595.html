For the late 66/and 67 pre heat the 1" tube does go forward on the right hand 
side- so yes the front tine does have a hole in it for the tube to go 
through, and the heater box does have an spout for the hose to go on.   This 
means the heat is actually pumped by the fan through the heater box and into 
the preheat- which sounds a good system to me.

A bunch of heater boxes I found have the 1" outlets on them, both left and 
rights. Some have caps on the spouts, like that has never been used.
