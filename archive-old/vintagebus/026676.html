Hi All,
Most standard automotive batteries hate being drained flat. It has
something to do with specific gravity in the lead/acid battery fluid and
the plate's willingness to react. That's why they make deep discharge
batteries for thing like trolling motors. These batteries are more
tolerant of long charge discharge cycles but even these won't react well
to 3 months with a discharge load applied. I also have had some luck
recharging batteries in this condition but not much and most of it was
bad. And yes the radio will work without the key.
