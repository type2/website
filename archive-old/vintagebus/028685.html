Well, I'll let you all know my solution.
First off, I can't use the bay window heater boxes because they are beat to h_at_ll.  I had 2 beetle boxes in excellent shape, so I figured I would rig these to work.
I removed the lever arm and cut the short arm off with a dremel. I then made a small notch in the heater box under the pivot shaft, and drilled a small hole thru the lever and the pin. Now I'm going to spot weld the arm back on.  It's identical to the bay window box, and didn't require more than about 20 minutes to do.
So now I have two good heater boxes, i'm considering doing the other box with an arm on both sides, sort of a universal heater box. 
Why don't they make them that way in the first place?
