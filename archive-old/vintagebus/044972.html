The problem is that your engine could have been altered and/or rebuilt in such
a way to change this adjustment.  We suggest using a .006" valve lash, if this
turns out to be .002" lager than you need all it will do is run a little
noisier.  If you adjust it to tight it will burn the valves.  Eric & Barb
