The station wagon is the next SUV. Check out the
cycle: 1970's = station wagons, they got "outcooled"
by the custom van conversion in the early 80's, which
was quickly upstaged by the minivan. Pretty soon the
minivan became a dead giveaway that you had 2.3 kids
in the back and were probably headed to some park for
a kid's sporting event. Soon the SUV saved the day,
and made you look like you were going rock climbing
when you were actually headed to the frozen yogurt
shop. Now the things guzzle gas and take a grappling
hook to climb into. So, back to the old. The new.
