Just got the roof rack back - wow, it is really amazing - unbelievable -

The guy at the plating place says plating is tougher that galvanizing
but ..... who knows.

Anyway, cost me the grand total of $40.00 Cdn, so that's less than
$30.00 US, unbelievable.  I sort of have a feeling that the guy that did

huge industrial type place that zinc plates industrial stuff.  Too bad
they couldn't plate my bus :).

Great thing is that i really didn't have to do any cleaning or anything
- i think the process includes some sort of acid bath that eliminates
rust.
