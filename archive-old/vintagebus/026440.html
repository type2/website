This is also normal. Since the wheels are off, the axles have been
hanging down in a different position from their normal position. Any
fluid that was on the boots or was normally held in by a clamp that was
a little loose might be able to get out and drip. It's also normal for a
boot that was about to die to go ahead and fail due to the abnormal
position of the axles. Check your boots. Maybe you'll be lucky and they
won't have any tears. In that case tightening the clamp probably fixed
the leak.
