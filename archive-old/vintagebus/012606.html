For a low budget engine run in stand....
I recommend using a wooden pallet (skid)on the floor, and a
transmision bolted to the engine for the starter side.
I use half of a trans that i cut the started housing side out of and
put the engine on the skid as I hate the vibrations of a stand or the
concrete floor. Also, the skid may allow more airflow as the engine
is held of the floor.
