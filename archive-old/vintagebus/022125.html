At 10:03 PM 4/19/99 EDT, AvaIonR_at_aol_dot_com wrote:
>The following M-codes for what I am assuming to be MY '62 Vw bus are as
>follows...help my out and tell me who to register with:
>
>22  10  2

October 22, 1962 production (1963 model year)

>374  081  195

374 is an export code
081 is walk-thru front seats
195 I do not know

>UH 221  BGR  SR  1016654

UH is the port code for Houston/San Antonio, USA, I think
221 is Standard Microbus, Cargo Doors Right, LHD
BGR is Beige Grey upper paint
SR is Sealing Wax Red lower paint
1016654 is the VIN (confirms the bus is 1963 model year)
