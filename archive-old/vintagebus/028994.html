The large O-ring would stretch around the lower outer bearing.  Put the gasket
on behind the backing plate...then put on the backing plate... put the other
gasket on (there should be 2 of the same type...sorta square with 4 bolt holes
in them) after you put that gasket on...stretch the o-ring around the
bearing...then put on the cover with the 4 bolts to attatch it to the redux
box.  Dont forget the little o-ring that goes in between the spacers!
