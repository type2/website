Hey everybody:

Well, I went to Kool April Nights in Redding CA this past weekend and it was 
a pretty good show.

The Volkswagen turnout was slim (as always) with about a dozen cars. The VWs 
this year were high quality cars including the famous "Low Fat" oval Bug 
from the cover of the January 1981 issue of Hot VWs.

This show is mostly for the street rod crowd. It was reported that a total 
of 2,130 cars entered this year. It seemed like about a third of those cars 
were 55 to 57 Chevys.

The vendor area needs improving. For a show of this size there were only a 
handful of vendors selling automotive related products. Too many people 
selling sun glasses, cowboy hats and NWO shirts.

All things considered, it was a good deal for 5 bucks.
