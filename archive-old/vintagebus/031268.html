Let's see... what else comes up a lot:

Only use GL-4 fluid in you tranny, not GL-5, even if it says it's GL-4 compliant.
You may have to search a bit for it in your area.

Grease your front end a lot, the owner's manual says every 1200 miles for
my Bus.

Save the old grease to lube other stuff like the brake shoe adjusters.

Repack your front wheel bearings every year according to the shop manual.

If your steering box leaks, mix some front end grease in with some GL-4
fluid to thicken the mixture, it may stop the leakage.  70s VWs used a thin
grease only in the steering box, according to a recent discussion on the
Type 3 list.

If you don't know how old your rubber brakes hoses are, they need replacing.

Completely flush your brake fluid every 2 years.

I'm sure there is more...
