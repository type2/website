Your defintion of "rust free" may differ from mine.  Tell me where the rust
is, and how much.

>Finally,  Can anyone lend any advice regarding questions I should ask about
>the bus?

Get a many pix a you possibly can.  Have him write down the #'s off the
mcode tag behind the front passenger seat.

What condition are the floors?   Rockers?  Moulding? Glass?  Remaining seat
covers?  Collision damage?  Belly pans?

Isn't it fairly humid out there?  It may hide more rust than you realize.
