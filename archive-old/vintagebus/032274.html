The only problem I see is that even though we're talking about a straight
points system here, I would still imagine some people would be saying "ah
that guy never liked me anyways...that's why he scored me low on that". You
would need to have the Concours class be it's own class. And people
entering it would fully understand the ramifications.

The benefit of a points system is that a really nice panel van could beat
out a little bit less nice 54 deluxe! That might be fun to see...I think it
really evens the playing field.

I would say that the scorecards should be available before the events (is
that true for other Concours's?) I don't think there's anything proprietary
on them, right? It's essentially the ultimate "Progressive Refinements"
list. Also, what value does cleanliness play? I mean, does an original part
that's a little road-worn get less points than a bad repo that's in mint
condition?

Of course, we need to make sure we get a COMPLETE list of M-Codes before we
even think about starting this. I still haven't seen that anywhere.

I nominate Greg Noble to be a judge, by the way...he's pretty merciless on
originality.
