Thoughts from "uj" at 65 years young regarding the "New" bus--We all know that the most
popular VW out there is the VW Bus--In the VW Hobby the biggest single type shows are
always "Da Bus".
OCTO--BBB---Jerome--  just to name a few, are monster size shows basically restricted to
the Type II.
OCTO even limits it to 1967 and earlier--I cannot think of any other Type in the VW Hobby
that can even come close in the size of these shows. As much as some of you may "Dislike"?
the NB--It has brought attention to the VW Hobby--Now "Other" people are recognizing the
Vintage VW that we are driving--There some hard core Type II Titans out there that do not
consider the 1968 and newer buses-A VW Bus!  I owned a 1968 absolutely Cherry VW Bus--It
was so clean and original at 100,000+ miles that it won 1st place at a World of Wheels
show in its class against Restored American iron! One of the most notable bus Gurus, and a
friend of mine Charley Hamill--would always kid me and say-" Jerry--you Almost own a VW
Bus"!  There will always be a division amongst us with our VWs--Regarding the years or the
styling--That's good--it shows we are proud of what we own.  Perhaps VW of Germany did not
come out with a pre1967 styling clone in a VW Bus--because the lawyers of VW of America
would try to shut them down for infringement of the company logo!   It is OK
to not like the New VW Bus--But remember this--If it does come out--It will be nothing but
Good News to us that own Vintage VW Buses--I'm "down" to just 3 Vintage VW Buses--1951
microbus--
1963 23 window--& 1967 Bessie --Yet still looking for just one more.? The New VW Bus will
never change what i feel for my VW "Family"--But i do know that more people will recognize
the Early VW Buses--because of the interest in the NB--Which now could mean New Beetle or
New Bus--We can close ranks--but never close our minds--accept what is new- because it IS
new--Hug or drive your Vintage VW bus today--it is still and always be the most unique
vehicle ever to grace the roads and many times non-roads of the World--TWWM, uj
