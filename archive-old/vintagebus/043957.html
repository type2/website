I have used a length of wire with an aligator clip at one end and a blunt
probe at the other end to diagnose poor grounds at head/tail lights, etc.
I connect the clip to a good ground source at the bulb holder and then
use the probe to locate a good ground away from the holder or what ever it
is your testing.  If all of a sudden now things are working fine with this
temp. grnd, hey, guess what!
