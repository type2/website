Re the rear axles, trucks did not come with different ones.  But you have the 1500motor/1 ton suspension package.  which mean that your truck came with the option in one year only rear stub axles and drums- the stub axles are longer and the drums wider than 63 regular drums, you use the same brake pads as a 64-67, and still have the 36mm axle nut.  That is, IF you have the original axles on there.
