it really does depend on who you hook up with.  if ALL you're getting is a 
ride, bring a tent (you have those right? heh heh) bring a sleeping bag and a 
chair to sit in.  we're not going to a camp ground, it's just a big flat 
piece of dirt with porta johns and that's it.  No showers, no running water, 
no ammenities, just dirt.  it's a good idea to bring plenty of water 
something to wash up with (soap, towel).  I'm sure there'll be plenty of food 
for you and plenty to drink (beer) but you might want to bring munchies.  
Jerome isnt in the desert either, it's up in the mountains and it will 
probably rain.  It does get somewhat hot in the afternoons (90's I'd say) and 
cold enough at night for me to put on pants and a jacket so come prepared
 
 2. Do people bring toys to share?  Stuff to swap?  Or is it mainly
 communing, arrhythmic drumming, griping about what a safety nazi Thom

stuff.  I havent seen too many toys.  Jerome is dusty and dirty and if your 
toys are just for show and not for sale, I'd leave em at home.....if you want 
to sell them by all means bring them.  I'm sure we'll all sit around and talk 
about Thom.  I think he expects us to.  You'll be surprised at the # of 
people there that arent involved with the list so there's plenty of other 
stuff to keep you occupied.
