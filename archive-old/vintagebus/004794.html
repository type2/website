Hi, All!
Thanks for all the wonderful responses to my original post. It's nice
to be able to vent my frustrations and receive feedback on the problems.
Well, I believe this one has ended with a victory of sorts...
I went ahead and sent a nice letter to the city code enforcement agent
about my bus being "in the process of restoration" and explained the
circumstances involved in the restoration and what I am doing and=20
asked for some "leeway to enjoy my hobby".
It must have struck a cord, as the agent stopped by and we had a very
polite and productive conversation. He stated that the city considers a
restoration as taking no more than 45 days(where that figure came from,
I don't know, but...). He must have liked old buses as he simply said to
continue restoration and to contact him every few weeks with an update
about how the restoration is going (no time limit was given).  I was =
elated.
Maybe I just got lucky and had an understanding agent  ...no matter,=20
I am back in business!
Thanks again!
Oh, yea...for your enjoyment, here is the letter I sent to
the city code enforcement agent:
