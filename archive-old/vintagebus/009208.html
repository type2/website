The beam you have is for a 1971 and newer Bus. The mounting bolt holes will
not line up with the ones on your Bus.  Link pin torsion arms and spindles
will not fit this beam.

For people who wish to use Ball joint torsion arms and spindles in early
Buses, the 1968 or 1969 Bus beam must be used. Your link pin components will
not fit on either of these beams.
