Wow, if your heads are flycut that much that it narrowed your engine so much
you needed to bend the intake, your CR must be through the roof! A standard
set of P/S and a standard CC on your heads will come real close to the proper
CR. Lower the CC in your heads and the CR goes up. Also you valve train
geometry changes. Hope you took all that into account when you put on flycut
heads. I always use cylinder shims the thickness of the material cut into the
heads to restore correct geometry. Of course, flycut heads are weaker than
having all the meat there. Touching up the surface a couple of thousands is
not a problem to take away barrel hammer marks.
