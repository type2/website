All this talk about which is rarer and which has more value Jim hits it
on the nose. I have a Type Three Ghia factory electric sunroof. The only
one that resides in California that runs, works and is not in a junkyard
cut up. It is also one of 10 64' model year known to exist and one of 40
all production years 63-69. I can't get rid of it and it is an
incredibly cool car. It's just not desirable.

Also I believe I have seen maybe only one 55-58 SWR-CNB deluxe in the
shows and a few pics of others and heard of one more (plus mine) out of
all the countless 59-63 deluxes and the several barndoor deluxes that
I've seen out there. So I would think that those years (55-58) would be
more rare as far as Deluxes go. Or maybe they just don't get restored as
much. Just wait till mine hits the scene. And it's gonna be a driver.
With repsect of course.
