As for the screw on connectors:

Keep looking in different FLAPS and electrical supply
houses.  I found a momentary push button switch for
use as an in-engine-bay remote start switch that was a 
Euro style switch.  Large diameter install hole, threaded
connections, not push on spade connections.   Look for 
shops that cater to the flashy pop lowrider/cruising Euro look.
Look for Euro style stuff.

I found it at a Pipe & Supply shop that supplies plumbing
and electrical stuff, primarily to contractors. They had it
in a bunch of close-out stuff.

They make a paint on vinyl electrical tape, that one just paints
onto the connection.  No more hassling with tape falling off.
Comes in a can with a brush in the lid, several colors.  Check
hardware stores.  If you can't find the right connector, and if
it's gonna be a permanent connection, solder the wires together, 
then paint on tape.
