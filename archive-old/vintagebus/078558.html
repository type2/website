I use the "old man" cheapo steering wheel covers. I call 'em that 
because both my grandpa's had 'em in their trucks all the time. 
They're the "leatherette" covers with little holes all in 'em that 
you put around the wheel then patiently wrap the vinyl cord around 
the whole thing and knot it when your done. They've beeen around 
forever and...to me...are the very comfy. I insist on using 'em in 
all my drivers...VW or not. Cost $1 at the Dollar General or maybe 
$4-$5 at Wal-Mart.

You can kind of see the one on my old Kombi in this picture. Not very 
"cool" I guess, but very usable.
