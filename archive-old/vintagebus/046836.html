Hi Chris,
Depends what year bus you have!  Pre-1960 buses have a all metal guide with a 
banding strap like spring under the rod to push it up.  
1960 thru 1966 chassi # 216 028 111 uses a plastic forward shift rod bushing 
in that little tang as you describe it.
Then for later 1966 thru 1967 the forward shift rod bushing went forward of 
the shift rod socket, supporting the rod by the rod on the front of it.
