First of all, new original style tube senders are still avaiable, I have
always had these in stock, even after the sender shortage. When you use a
float style sender in the tank  (OE VW) not the VDO for VDO aftermarket
gauge one you need to install the fuel gauge vibrator ala 68 and later type
1 in order to get an accurate reading. It makes me sick when parts vendors
pass on these float gauges for our buses when they know (or should) that
they will not work without extra ad-ons.
