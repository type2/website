I haven't had much luck finding metric fasteners at Home Depot or Lowe's.
They have some basic stuff like hex head bolts but they are relatively
expensive. Each time I tackle a project on the Thing, I try to replace the
fasteners with stainless. I've had much more luck ordering on-line. My
local automotive fastener store sells a small gauge for $5 so you can
determine the right size. A good parts manual also helps but I've found
mine is not always accurate on fastener sizes. When ordering on-line it's
good to stock up on some of the more common sizes. I ordered a box of the
8mm hex heads in lengths of 16 and 20mm. You'll find these all over the
car. 4mm screws in various lengths are also common.
Try these sites.
