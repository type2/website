buy a bentley reprint of the 1963 transporter workshop manual.   Slopping 
shifting is usually the fault mainly of the rear shift coupler,  lay under 
the bus, looking just in front of the tranny, while a friend wiggles the 
shift stick side to side- the bit from the front of the bus and the bit from 
the tranny should be held firmly in the coupler, no wiggle.  use safety wire 
when installing a new one- through the holes in the screws- if a screw falls 
out you get stuck in gear.  :(
