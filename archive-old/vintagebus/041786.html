Well, my '71 uses six clamps for a 2-passenger middle seat.  Two for each
leg "loop"

If your seat has holes for the projections in the clamps, then all you
have to do is install one clamp for each hole.

I puzzled about the long and short T-bolts too, and finally came to the
conclusion that the longer bolts are for use when there is a rubber mat
between the seat and the floor.  I find the longer bolts a lot easier to
install.  Get the clamp in place, the nut threaded on the bolt, then
jiggle the seat around until all the clamps are straight and comfortable.

What I would really like to find are wing nuts to fit the T-bolts to make
removing the middle seat a no-tools exercise.
