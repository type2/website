there is a little sender screwed into your master cylinder.   go under the 
bus with you test light and find the master cylinder.  ground the test light, 
and see those two wires plugged into the sender?   one of them should be hot. 
  Your fuse is back in. right, ok  so pull one of the wires off the sender, 
and test to see if it is hot?   test the other one, it should not be hot.  
leave the hot one plugged into the sender, and test to see if the other side 
of the sender gets electricity.  It should not.  pull the brake lever down 
with you other hand, now it should be hot.

I think your sender is stuck on, and you probably need a new one.  think they 
are about $3, good thing to have a spare of around.
