I drove one in my 71 Westfailure for a couple of years and it was fine. I
used the short Mazda B2600 oil filter as the filter replacement. The only
mods I had to do was cut one little notch area in the upper tin to clear the

have put many on dune buggies and other off road apps with no problems. Even
have one for my trike.
