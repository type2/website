I use combination wrenches - 2 of 'em.  the 17mm that fits, then I add a second
wrench, whaatever's fairly long.  I fit the box end on the second wrench over
the open end of the 17mm, essentially giving me a longer 17mm. You have to try
it a couple ways before you figure it out.  It also helps to take some of the
weight with a jack or something.  I usually break all 4 free before I remove any
one nut.  Yeah it means going around twice but who's in a hurry?  With practice,
my wife and I got engine removal down to 17 minutes from setting the barely
adequate tool kit on the ground at the rear of the Tinkertoy.
