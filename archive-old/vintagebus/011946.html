I can't recall offhand the battery compartment dimensions for a splitty,
but if it'll fit get a Group 24 size battery.

Sears usually tries to sell bus and bug owners a so-called "Volkswagen
battery", partly because they don't know any better and partly because
it's usually a more expensive part.

As I've written here in the past, a "Volkswagen battery" is barely
adequate in a bus under good conditions.  A Group 24 battery, which was
the standard "American car" battery for many years, will transform your
bus's starting performance and capabilities.  

If it'll fit, get a top of the line "Diehard Gold" Group 24 battery
(they're on sale right now) and smile every time you start your bus or
turn on the headlights.
