VeeWee111_at_aol_dot_com wrote:
:  Best to get a pair of back up lights.  Paint the inside of the clear lenses 
:  with yellow tint paint and wire them into the brake/turn signal bulbs.   
:  Yellow is alot easier to other drivers to see and react to!

This is better, but still questionable legally.  In most states, it's
illegal to have white lights showing to the rear except when backing
up.  Yellow is ok most places, but might still get you in trouble with
the cops if they're in a bad mood.  For that matter, tapping the brake
lights can be enough for that, but if you do it with sufficient
distance that the person behind you can reasonably be expected to
react safely, it's ok (this catches the folks who like to cut someone
off, hit the brakes, and then sue for whiplash, etc).

:  If you want you
:  can then install a momentary switch in the pod to bypass the brake switch to
:  turn on the brake lights back there.

This, plus a third brakelight installed in the rear window, would
probably be your best bet.  You might also consider getting some of
that white/red reflective tape that they put on semis (available at
truck parts stores; just like car parts stores, but bigger).
