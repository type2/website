From the Doliwa book, the other splitty M-codes that have "time" notes 
on them:

"To 1955":
M 52  Rear flap without window
M 53  Rear flap with window
M 57  Divided front seat and "Bodenleisten lose beigelegt"

"Front, to 3/55":
M 93  Behr ventilator

"To 3/55":
M 97  Emden radio, Rear bumpers (*1)

"From 217 000 001":
M 102  Heated rear window

"From 217 148 459":
M 114  Six pop-out side windows  (*2)

"From 1 096 222":
M 161  Sliding door

"To 216 190 000":
M 168  88 amp-hour battery

"423 157 to 1 328 271":
M 199  Rotating driver's seat in camping car

"From 1.10.64 Series":
M 215  1 ton weight rating + 1.5L
M 216  1.5L

"To 215 190 000":
M 221  High roof with "sales flap" (Verkaufsklappe)

"From 1.8.66 Series":
M 620  12v system

"To 216 190 000":
M 621  12v warning light relay

My notes:

*1 I think this might be another one like 164: M 97 is rear bumpers
   until 3/55; sometime after that it means Emden radio

*2 This doesn't make any sense, as my '64 (1 203 065) has this code
