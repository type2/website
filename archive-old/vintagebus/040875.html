Yes, actually. The reservoir is made from UHMW, an industrial variation on
polyethylene, the same stuff milk jugs are made of. NOTHING sticks to it,
it's known as 'poor man's Teflon'. The only way to attach to UHMW is heat
welding. If the reservoir is not cracked, the leakage is probably due to the
seal (or lack of) between it and the M/C.
