Very true!  Plus even if one lives and drives only in a dry climate one must 
remove the bra to wash the inside of the bra off and wash and wax at least 
the body area that the bra touches/covers.  Otherwise without the extra wax

pushing it at high speed rubs the paint off.  Plus the dust/dirt/sand that

tear the paint up!  Like Cris says, use the bra only for long trips to save 
the paint from chips.    Eric&Barb
