Well, I have never been able to figure out what differnce it makes what kind of privates you have to be able to fix a VW.  I can't imagine what use they can be for that, and possibly painful to use them. 

Do you have an idiot book? (by John Muir)  get one if you dont, and sit and read the section on steering (shimmies and shakes)  tells you how to figure out what is wrong with steering, and easy to read and understand for the non mechanic.  Also it is very good to have the Bentley reprint of the 1963 ed Transporter Workshop Manual, excellent and comprehensive info on 63-67 buses (also much applies to earlier ones)   Also, friends are invaluable.   Friends with VWs are usually happy to help, and often have tools you don't have.  Some stuff really is so much easier with more than one person.

Hope this helps.  Do post what is wrong with your suspension.
