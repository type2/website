there were more than one version of the barndoor speedo, and I think yours 
would be the 36hp (54/55) version.    for several reasons, the main being  
the part number you listed is the 54 on version.    the earlier version the 
needle swings the other way.

fwiw, in canada in 1957 that  number  speedo  would cost $17.90

yes the dated stamped should be the production date.  meaning that you have a 
speedo made after they stopped making barndoors- made as a replacement parts. 

re parts lists.  you do not need a "barndoor parts list" to look up barndoor 
parts.   each parts list lists part available at that time.   meaning some 
earlier versions were superceded,  but the 67 or 70 edition I am sure would 
have that part listed.  If you have a parts list, or look at the website 
where it is scanned, you will see what I mean.
