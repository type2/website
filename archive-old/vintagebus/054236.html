You forgot to mention what year your bus is. If its new enougn to use the bug 
1600
engine or whatever, you may have a bad bypass air cut-off valve. Its item 6 
in the latest RMMW catalog. Usually, the head gets knocked off (the thingy 
that exists to the far left of item 6 in the RMMW catalog) causing the engine 
to stall instead of idle. The solution is to replace the bypass air cut-off 
valve for $18.95. It screws onto the carb.
