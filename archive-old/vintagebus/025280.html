Andrew,

I didn't say there is any rivalry.  On the contrary, people just got fed up
with paying money to a club (really only one guy) and receiving almost nothing
in return.  No news letters on a consistent basis, regular events, etc.  So as
a result, people in Southern California and mostly in Orange county decided to
try and recapture the sprit of what SOTO used to be like in the mid 80's.
While the existence of OCTO has seemed to light a fire under the collective
SOTO behind, I think its too little too late.

There will be two events on the same day...June 12.  OCTO will be in Orange
County, and I believe SOTO will be held down in Escondido at the Deer Park
Winery.  Everyone needs to make up his or her mind to what show they will
attend.  As for me I choose OCTO.
