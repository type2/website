press out?   thats a no no.  You remove the plug (weld bolt or nut to it and 
PULL)
remove nut on inside of plug area
****remove circlip****

then you can tap or press the stub axle out, and remove and replace both 
inner reduction bearings.  

I think if you try to press the stub axle out w/o doing these steps you could 
bugger up your RGbox half.

 Check out your Bentley book for pictures.
