I made a couple of adapters for tire shops back in Cali. I took early front
bug drums and machined the drum part off of it, to make a flat adapter,
kinda like spindle mounts. I them pressed in studs for lug nuts. After all
was done I took them to a place that balances torque converters and had it
balanced, with the lug nuts on it. I gave this to my godfather who owns a
tire store and to this day they still use it, as far as I know, for
balancing VW and early Porsche wheels.
