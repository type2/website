Couple of questions on Westy jalousies.  I'm talking specifically about
the ones with three adjustable panes of glass and one smaller fixed pane
at the top, although if anything from other types (eg, single-pane
EZ-camper windows) might apply, please advise.

or popout) and with three per side.  Any rhyme or reason?

into the '65 Neunsitzer at least for a while (popouts are pretty rusty
