you can use a tunnel case tranny in a split case bus if you remove the front 
tranny mount off teh tranny and get an adapter plate.  don't mess around with 
nose cones and hockey stick - that doesn't make it work.  you just use the 
tunnel case type 2 nosecone for this mod.   you do need the ring gear swapped 
to the other side , or you get 4 reverse gears and one forward.   actually a 
guy I know is wrenching on race cars, and the car in question got something 
fried in the tranny, and the driver ended up doing the last circuit in 
reverse, including a jump!  but if you don't feel like doing that stuff, the 
ring gear must be turned around.  it will the have a low/bug/.89 fourth gear, 
instead of the bus .82 4th, so freeway speeds could wind it out, but it'll 
pull the hills better in 4th.
