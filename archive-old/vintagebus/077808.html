Sulfuric acid spills are nasty business. The acid gets into the pores of
everything it touches and even though it looks good and scrubbed up, the
acid is still there. There used to be a good high alkaline content
garage floor cleaner that will balance the acid and help draw it off the
corrupted surfaces. I have had to scrub it in really good and let it sit
overnight and then rinse and scrub off in the morning. The trick is to
keep it on long enough to neutralize the acid.
