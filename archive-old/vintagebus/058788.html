  There is a ridge created at the snap ring groove from wear, a piece of
sandpaper along the snap ring groove should take care of that.
  I use an oil pump piller on the RGB plugs. I put four tack welds on each
side of each tip on the puller and tighten up the nut and out they come. Cut
a couple of the welds with a hack saw and snap the rest of the welds, clean
and reuse.
  Get a time-sert, heli coil, case saver to use in the threads of the RGB.
Each brand has a different outside diameter and you might find one that will
just thread in with-out redrilling and tapping.
