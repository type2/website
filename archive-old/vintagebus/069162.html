Ahh, the annual spring Safari window thread! 


I've mentioned it before, but here it is again...




The trunk seal from 80-90's Volvo sedans is a good replacement for Safari window 
to body seals. The profile is "UD" with the D being a hollow crush seal and the 
U having a rubber encased steel channel that holds the opening lip well. Trim 
the U at the brackets for a better fit. If you need a firmer crush, feed weather 
stripping or rope through the D with a coat hanger or spare accelerator cable. 
It looks pretty good from the inside. Cheap too.

donation to the Type2 fund.
