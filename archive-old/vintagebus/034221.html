When you go to mvd ask them to run a check on precious owners.
if they come up with a name, you need to send a certified letter to that
person asking for a copy of the title, after 3 delivery attempts (if the
person isn't there) you will get back the unopened letter.
you then take that to mvd (the unopened letter) with the bond (your
insurance agent can give you the bond) and they will issue title on the
spot.
make sure you get the previous owner search first, i have been dicking with
mvd over this, went in with a noterized bill of sale, went to a different
teller, this one ran the title search and came up with a name, so sent out
the cert letter., second time, but to the right person and address, so when
i get the letter back i will post the bond ($50.00) and get the title.
in the mean time they gave me a 90 day permit.
