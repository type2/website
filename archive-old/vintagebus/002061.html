At 55 you are not loading the engine as highly as at 65. Wind
resistance goes up with, I believe, by the cube of the speed.  That
means your primary drag (wind resistance) has increased by 1.65 times
from 55 to 65 mph. That is a large increase in engine load. That is
why you don't overheat at 55 and do at 65.
