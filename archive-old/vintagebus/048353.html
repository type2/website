A friend of mine has a standard micro w/ sunroof that not only was imported 
to seattle as per the M code tag, he is the 3rd owner and has papers for it 
from the OO.   

to find more about your bus, find the VIN # .  That will tell you when the 
bus was buildt.  AFter about ch 398xxx (around 59 model year) they got m code 
tags behind the pass seat, which gives beaucoup info.   Does the bus have 
sempahores?   50s euro spec buses came w/ semaphores.  if it has the pointy 
turn signals, not used euro spec till 61-63 buses (later than on n am spec 
buses)

does bus have tempered windsheilds, or laminated (the laminated kind get 
foggy and crack)

brake light in deck lid?
