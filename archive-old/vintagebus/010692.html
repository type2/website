switch to innie vents- 6 March 63  vin 1 080 104   (mid 63 model year)
small back hatch through   5 August   63 VIN 1 144 303  (all 63s had them)
fried eggs                    beginning of 62 model year (N American spec)
fridge handles through         18 dec 63     VIN 1 222 026  (early 64 models)

the later brakes was in the 1 ton suspension optional extra.  the 63 version 
has the little nut (36mm), but longer stub axles that take a wider drum that 
the regular 63 drum, and use the same brake shoes as 64-67.  unless it just 
had a later trans swapped in, like Taylor's bus.
