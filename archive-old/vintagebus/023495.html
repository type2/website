It is one of many radios which could have been installed either at the 
factory or the Euro/USA dealerships.  A VW could be imported into USA with a 
radio in it only if the VW was used!  Some VW dealerships installed radios

one of the Euro VW dealerships.  The new VW owner would then tour Europe and 
then return thier now used VW to one of the VW factory shipping ports.  
Saving alot on the import taxes and getting a VW to tour Europe all at once!
