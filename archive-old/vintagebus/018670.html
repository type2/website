I think it's between Izzy's (if anyone can find him.  Should we call
Robert Stack?) and Creative Engineering.  Bus Boys and Wolfgang made
too many compromises in the tradeoff between design and cost, using
a square-channel frame instead of triangular, and modified cheepie
latches instead of the original style.  CD also makes barndoor
safaris, and I think they're the only ones that do.

I have a set of CE safaris, I've had them for almost a year.  Yes,
the leak, but even NOS safaris leak.

You can get used ones, but they're one of those wallet-busting propositions
that people think they can retire on.  Then you'll have to restore them,
meaning new seals, rechroming latches, paint, etc.
