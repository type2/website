able to match it up with the correct grooved rubber. Due to the flat spots on 
the roof where the windows sit though they did go ahead and caulk the seems

rained hard. The whole cost of the job was only about 75.00 or so and was 
well worth me not having to search for the rubber and hope it would work 
after installing it.
