Hey Al,
I would think maybe you said it yourself, too rich. What's your carb
and what's the jetting?
I am running similar 1600sp with the new H30/31carb: mains 112.5,
pilot 65, aux maybe 45. Jetted like a late 30PICT carb, which I am
essentially pretending I have. I've only run about 100 miles like
this, but so far I like it a lot.  The engine is getting plenty warm
but then I think it needs to in order to run efficiently, oil dipstick
gets hot but always holdable. Haven't climbed a mountain yet, that
will be a test. If I get scared of the heat I can always slip in a 115
mains, I bought this great little $3 tool that allows me to swap mains
without removing the carb.
But if you just want to raise the temp a bit, try sticking a rag in
the fan or shedding that pesky hoover bit...
Good luck,
