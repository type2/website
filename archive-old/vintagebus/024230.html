Here's the answer, thanks to Jason for the great 
list of thread sizes.  I narowed it down (and measured)
to determine that I needed an M18x1 die.  I called
every tool distributor in Sacramento (over 15 places)
to find only one in San Francisco that had it, and 
even then would only COD it to the tune of $20.
(I didn't want to have to have SO deal with that)

I called Baum Tools to find that the price Jason
listed would actually be around $40, not $10.77.

expect to find it, and if per chance they had it,
expected to have it priced out of reach.  Surprise,
surprise, the have it for $13.  I'll get it early
next week as it had to be brought in from the 
warehouse in Carson City, NV.

Snap-On is nationwide, so give 'em a try.
I'm just glad I don't need a die for the LEFT
side using left handed threads.
