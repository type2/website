After inspecting the accessory gas gauge in the 57 hard top deluxe I came up 
with this:

The gauge is approximately the same dimensions as the later "standard" 
gauges, with a black housing and chrome ring.  The housing has printed in 
white "MADE IN WESTERN GERMANY" and on the other side some illegible #s 
ending with '52e and beneath the manufacturer "MOTEST"

The face is really a beauty showing no real signs of aging.  It has the 
MOTEST and GERMANY on it as well as Res, 1/4, a small red light at half tank, 
3/4, and Full.  All lettering is white on a dark gray face with small light 
gray rings (like on the VDO speedos.)
