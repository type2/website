  I would guess that your knobs are ABS, but without 
burning it, it is impossible to know for sure!
  ABS will disolve if exposed to acetone, gasoline, 
benzene, silicone, and ingredients of many paints and 
cleaners. That said, we have painted ABS here with some 
success.  It would help if you clean them throughly and 
rough them up before you paint.
