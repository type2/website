Something I learned:
Until I bought this Double Cab, I did not know that Double Cabs had a heater
outlet for the rear seat.  The 2 heater box tubes come up to the front like
a Single Cab or Kombi but then a little pipe comes off the big tube and
pumps heat out in front of the rear seat at the bottom of the front seat
divider.
