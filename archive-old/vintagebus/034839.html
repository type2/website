a very good question.   funny you should say that.  Yes, you are being anal,
but thats not a bad thing.    what I have seen on buses where the windows
have stayed put is that the logos are on the top, towards the rear.  This
means if you take a pop out from the left side of the bus and put it on the
other side, the logo will be in the wrong place.  Perhaps you noticed this on
my 61 when it was at Kelley Park, as I replaced the popouts (originals were
rusted) with nice ones one of a junkyard, and put them on the wrong side as
the seals were better that way.

 If you are standing on the left side of the bus, the logo should be in the
top right corner, if you are on the right side, the logo should be in the top
left corner.     I think this means that on one side the logo is on the
outside and on the other side the logo is on the inside.   (say that ten
times fast)
