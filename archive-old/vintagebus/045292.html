well, often the ends of the axles are worn, they are old and could be much
(ab)used, but usually still servicable.  Amazingly, new fulcrum plates are
available for $10 (takes 4).  Trannies are pretty sturdy.

rear axle shaft  211 501 201 is the same on all splitties.  there was some
slight changes, but they are interchangable.
 though they came in slightly different sizes marked by a paint dot blue pink
or green.  You just need to make sure the fulcrum plate clearance is within
spec and have the tranny builder okay the axle shafts.

  If you get parts out of a split case tranny DO NOT mix and match early
(early 59 and back) and late style fulcrum plates or there will be self
destruction. when you put the rounded side down on a flat surface the late
style have a slight rock to it end to end (.2 mm), the early style does not
at all.

axle shafts are only available used.
