When it rains, the humidity down in the south rises, causing the
metal to swell, distorting both the door and the door frame.  Try
filling the door cavity and door frame cavity with silica gel
to absorb the excess moisture.

Failing that, check the adjustment both on the window frame (it
can be loosened and tilted slightly) and the striker plate - your
striker plate may be excessively worn.  Also check for cracks in
the door - I've seen them crack around the latch area.
