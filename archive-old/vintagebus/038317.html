--- TIPS ON Archive Searches ---
---==========================---

    I will work on a better tutorial - how-to for the archive engine in the
next 60 days.  If you can't find what you need, please e-mail me!

    It is best to focus on keywords.  Generic things like "Brakes" are not
best for searches... focus in on "Show" or "Drum" -- unique words that are
uncommon in normal English (or German <grin>) discussions.  Also be careful
how you use multiple words.

   Sample Searches;

        brake NEAR drum NEAR turn
        tires NEAR load

   Wild cards for words like "Signal" so you also get "Signals":

        turn NEAR signal*

   Think of words like "Westy" and Campmobile that are spelled various ways.
Consider using good wildcard choices:

        west* NEAR interior
        camp* NEAR interior        


Hope this helps.
