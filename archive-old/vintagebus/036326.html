had a fabulous weekend in southern calif. myself & ronnie & john howard hooked up with the abc at the general patton museum outside indio calif. dave easterwood & 2 other guys from dallas were also in the group. 4 barndoors in a group of about 15 buses. man thats 30% pre 55 buses. picked up the few remaining items for my 1951 23 window. the grab handle at the cab divider, the correct barndoor dash ashtray & the correct horn button. lots of barndoor parts available. met several listees sold some shirts & parts. won oldest bus award at the octo show. that was a beautiful 24k gold coin commemerating the classic weekend. these coins were minted by mark adair. thanks to charlie hamill & rich kimball for doing there best to provide a fun & memorable weekend.
