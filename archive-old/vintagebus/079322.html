Hi Mike,

For any doghouse cooled engine you MUST use a 33, 34, or  better yet a 35mm 
wide (inside measurement).  You need to use the  largest diameter lower stock 
pulley which almost always has a "D" stamped on  one of the pie shaped 
corrigations of the pulley.  Using a Porsche  356/911 upper pulley increases 
fan speed 
more.  Tach your VW for a  fifteen minute drive to find out where all four 
forward 
gears hit 1,500  & 3,000 RPM at what MPH in each gear.
