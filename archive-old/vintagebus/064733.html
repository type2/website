No good reason. The engine revs freely, and while the power drops off above 
about 4,200 it keeps pulling. On flat ground it will see over 4,700, and I

this has to do with the efficiency of the fan falling off over 4,000. Another 
reason to lower the rpm for freeway use. I have the doghouse cooler, and plan 
to add a full flow filter with remote cooler when my next projects get done
