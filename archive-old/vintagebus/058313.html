I bit the bullet on mine and upgraded to the larger 24mm piston 71-79 style
master cylinder with an adapter kit from local VW store. BFY or I, whatever,
out of the VW Trends and Hot VW mags sells the adaptr kit for $39.95. I
bought the new master cylinder for $43.00 and had it all bolted in my SC in
20 minutes. The brake lines on my dual circuit 67 matched exactly with the
new master cylinder and the only other change was to lengthen the wires the
the farthest brake light switch. I even took pics, go to vintagebus_dot_com and
click on the gallery and the single cab catagory and my master cylinder mod
pictures are on section 5 about the middle of the page. Good luck.
