I would think in the pre '71 models that the bearing is always in constant
contact with the pressure plate, even if it is just the top or bottom edge --
just touching, not under any pressure.  Unless the bearing is magically
balanced, it will tilt forward and backward, bringing one edge in contact. 
This may be one reason VW decided to add the cost and complication of the tube
mount to later transmissions -- the pedal release will now draw the TO bearing
away from the PP evenly so there is no touching.
