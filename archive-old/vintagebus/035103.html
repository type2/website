Gas gauges were standard on ambulances and an option on not ambulances before 
62, not sure about barndoors.  So they get 271 part numbers.  But I have 
found dealer installed gas gauge w/sender in a 58 micro.   The gauge was 
dated 56, as was the sender, the sender was red. nifty huh.  The gauge face 
reads EMPTY to FULL.  Or actually the 61 dated guage read that, the 56 ones I 
have seen were in German, and FULL is VOLL, not R to 1/1.  And the bezel is 
slightly differnt.
