On your bus 912E engine conversion did you change out the FI camshaft when
installing the Webers?  This may explain HP loss in your bus.  The cams are
specific for carbs or FI.  The FI cams probably do not have the proper ramp
on them to create a vacuum for the carbs to give the heads a good gas
mixture.
