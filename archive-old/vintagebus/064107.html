What oil temps are you hitting in the summer time?  A thinner oil will
actually lower your oil temps by putting more oil through the oil cooler
before it hits the bearings.

> Also VW does have warnings of  running straight 5 Wt for long driving

> the temps listed for it.

I haven't seen 5w oil anywhere, and am sure it has not been avaliable for a
long, long time.  5w and 5w30 are not the same.  5w oil is what VW is
talking about with that TSB.

I know a lot of bus folk don't run the Berg dipstick because it comes on all
the time, and has been called a 'worry light'.  I think these people are
looking at it the wrong way.  When I first put my engine in my bus, my light
came on after 7-10 miles on the freeway (summer time).  Instead of pulling
the dipstick out, and being done with it, I worked on the engine and it's
cooling system until I could drive on the freeway as long as I wanted,
without tripping the light on.  If your engine does run hot enough to trip
the berg stick on, then you probably don't want to run a multi-grade oil.
But that doesn't fix the problem, ya know.
