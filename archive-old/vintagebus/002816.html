Subject: Re: [VB] 67 Front Striker plates?

Totally different...... the doors, the latches, and the strikers.
I have a 66 hardtop deluxe and a 67 standard.

door
66 = hole for latch has angle at top and bottom,
has a guide for the rubber block at the top only
67 = hole for latch has angle at top, straight on bottom,
has the guide at top and a stop at the bottom.... appears to be there to hold
pin in catch securely.
The guides are part of the latches. The 67 latch will NOT fit in the 66 door
opening.

striker plates
66 = held on with 3 small headed screws
67 = held on with 2 large headed screws

I can take pics of them if you want.
