I'm not sure I understand the logic in the paint color, much less the paint.
Why would white be any different than any other color? If you are wanting to
reflect heat away from you, ya, white would be the choice, but you are
encasing the heat, not reflecting it, or at least reflecting back into the
inside of the block because of the insulating qualities of paint itself.
There is nothing like the raw metal itself to disperse heat from the oil and
the hot cylinders.
