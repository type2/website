<<How do I tell if the drum is the wrong size?  Or if it is worn out?  I am a 
novice and have never had a "new" brake drum to look at to tell obvious 
differences.  >>
Take two pieces of wood kind of like popsicle sticks but longer.  Put in the 
drum past the unworn edge the shoes do not ride in and slide the two pieces 
of wood until they touch each side.  Mark this length and remove out of the 
drum.  Align the two pieces of wood until your mark shows and measure.  If 
the drum is not worn out do this measuring for the inner and outer side of 
the drum inside diameter to see if there is any tilt or slope there.  If 
.030" or more the drum needs to be turned.  VW drums do not have alot of 
material to remove so only turn them if they really need it!
