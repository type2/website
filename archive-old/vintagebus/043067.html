Here in WA State there are several restrictions applied to
"Classic/Historic" plate use.
The one restriction that is my favorite to abuse is "Vehicle may only be
used for occasional pleasure driving".
That pretty much opens it up for driving it all the time!
One thing I do, and others may wish to think about it too, is that I have
photo copies of the actual pages in the DMV outlining the regulations, use
and coverage.  It has saved my ass a couple of times when dealing with the
less informed Polizei.
Had been pulled once and the cop thought that because my VW wasn't a show
car it shouldn't be allowed to have "Classic" plates!  So my car had some
rust, dents, torn upholstery, discolored rims, worn out window seals and
ugly paint!  It still met all the rules they laid out.
