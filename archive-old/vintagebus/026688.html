Volks,

Many of you know that we run a classified ad page as part of the
www.type2_dot_com web site. The classified site is called "Aircooled Buy &
Sell" and I think it's probably the busiest VW classified site on the
net. That doesn't really matter, except that we know a LOT of money
changes hands over ads posted to BuySell.

We received email this past week alleging that an individual was using
BuySell to liquidate busses and parts in an unscrupulous way. Normally
we don't try to police transactions on BuySell, but the amounts of money
mentioned in the email were pretty astronomical and we felt we had to
look into it. Since the individual was alleged to be using several email
addresses and we couldn't tell which ads in BuySell might be bogus, we
pulled the BuySell site down for the time being.

Having asked around, we turned up enough corroboration that we think
there is an obligation to tell the VW community that we are no longer
accepting advertisements from this individual, and to ask for your help
in letting us know if he tries to post ads under different addresses.

The gentleman's name is Frank Leach, doing busines out of Provo, Utah,
sometimes using the name Christy Motors. We think these three email
addresses are associated with Leach:
