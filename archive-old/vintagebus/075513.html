the threads and on the smooth shaft that goes into the bore.  I was trying
to say that I didn't think there should be any goop on the teeth of the
star - the notches that you catch with a screwdriver to adjust the brakes.

to the linings, and would probably make adjusting the brakes difficult
as your screwdriver would tend to slip off the teeth.
