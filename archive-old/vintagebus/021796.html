all light bulbs should be 12V, headlights, running lights, taillights,  many 
people omit to change the bulbs in the dash/speedo.  And the dome lights.   
using 6V bulbs will melt the dome covers when run on 12V.  get 5W12V bulbs, 
10W12V bulbs will also melt covers.   

wire in a voltage drop for your wiper motor

leave the 6V horn alone.   I purposely run 6V horn with 12V, it is very loud 
and obnoxious, makes people wake up when they act like they will run into me, 
saved me from a couple accidents that would have happened from others not 
paying attention.

either your should have a 12V starter with 12V FW, or a 6V starter on a 6V FW 
w/ 12V solenoid

I wouldn't get rid of a perfectly fine generator.  But if you do, you can 
send it to me :)
