Cupholders. A magnet from a speaker holding a can will work where there 
is metal. My 69 had an interesting cupholder. A piece of plywood between 
the seats had holes cut in it. On the bottom, pieces of nylon stocking 
were stapled to the plywood. The cups, cans, bottles, would rest in the 
holes, cushioned by the nylons. This was like a shock absorber and 
didn't shake or spill the drink.
