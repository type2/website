  I still prefer the Kumho or Nanking 195/14. Got over 40k out of Nanking
guiet and not to harsh of a ride. Kumho seem stiffer. I have Uniroyal
Lorado's on another bus now 195/14, these are also guiet but with a harsher
ride. All the above listed tires cost less then 250 dollars a set mounted
and balanced. If I had the dough to spend I would have Michilen (spelling?)
Agilias. These are the guietess, smoothest tire I have ever driven a bus on,
but at approx 125 dollars a piece I can put 2 sets of tires on my other
buses for the price of 1 set of Agilias.
