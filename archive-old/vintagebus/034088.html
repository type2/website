I have taken 1 of my buses down to the local auto glass shop and for ten
dollars they sealed both my winshields with hot gun sealant, this is the
same stuff used to hold the windshields in new cars, 3 years of desert heat,
a little rain, (when it does rain it pours) over 150 days with no rain now.
They have special tips to get into the seam and run a hot bead in the seam,
when this dries it bonds the rubber to the metal. The only way to remove
glass/seal then is with a hot knife or a ton of razors.Cheap,it works and
you don't have to deal with the mess!!
