Sundial campers did start there lives as panels which makes them kind of
unique.
Most other camper conversions were Kombis.  And Sundials also had just two
windows on the passenger side on the two cargo doors.  They eliminated the
third window on that side because there is a closet that went from the
cargo doors back.  Hope that helps.
