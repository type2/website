>I have a 1960 Kombi and have a couple of electrical issues.  When the brake
>pedal is pushed, the turn signal indicator light and the generator light
>glow dimly.  When the brake is let off, the lights go out.  The second
>involves the turn signals.  When the left of right rear signal is on,
>and the brake pressed, the opposite light flashes dimly.

Look especially hard at the grounds at your rear tail lights.  Originally
they just grounded through the reflectors touching the body sheet metal -
they don't have separate ground wires.  Add ground wires from each
reflector to where the battery negative strap bolts to the body and you
will probably be amazed at how much brighter your rear lights are.
