It's been a while since I messed with my Dad's '74 Bug, but it seems
like the rotor in it did point a different direction than the earler
VWs I have worked on.  On the earlier ones (up through late 60s or
so) it seems like #1 and the notch are towards the back and right
of the car - the rotor will be pointing at you as you stand behind
the center rear of the car.  On the '74, if I remember right, #1 and
the notch were towards the front and right of the car...the rotor was
actually "pointing" at #1 cylinder (right front of the engine).

for way lots more information (including points/condenser/cap/rotor
parts numbers).  Even advance-curve information for some distributors.
