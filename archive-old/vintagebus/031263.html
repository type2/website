Steve:
I don't get it about the transaxle. It's a 71, right? Small nut and large nut
are terms for bug (001) type trans as used in pre- 68 type 2's. Your bus
doesn't have swing axles and reduction boxes does it?
63-6400 rpm at 60 mph sounds WAY too fast. (geared too low)Top speed listed for
a 1971 is 68 mph and engine hp is 60 _at_ 4400 rpm. I'd guess maybe 68mph comes at
4500 rpm or so. . I wonder if ALL your guages are just reading high?

you could independently check the oil temp to make sure you are on the right
track.
Lower compression will NEVER make it run hot, so that's not the problem.
