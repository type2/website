I have never checked the clearance on the snubbers.  There is some.   there 
started being used in late 64 or early 65.  small nut and early big nut boxes 
don't have the mounts for them.   Some times they rust off.  Dunno if it 
makes much differnce (anyone gonna test this?)    they are an anti bounce and 
jolt thing.   You can get them in various colours in pet stores as a dog toy 
called a Kong, the label on the dog chew toy says they were inspired by a VW 
bus part.

I have no idea what the original ride should be like.   I like the KYB GR-2 
ride. I have the gas a just shock on the back of my 65, and the gr-2 all 
around ride in my 61 is better- both 1 ton all around.  not too stiff, not 
too squishy.   Haven't tried new Boges.
