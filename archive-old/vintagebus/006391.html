Just to let you all know about the fiasco I had with Barrett Enterprises.  I
returned the parts along with a written list of how much I expected them to
refund me.  The parts included was the P/C set and all the cores.  They
should have received the parts sometime around the end of December.  By
January 22nd, I still hadn't heard anything from them which I thought was
odd because I had instructed them to fax me a copy of the return list, or to
mail it to me or email me - something to show that I was being credited and
they had received the parts.  January 26th was the magic date when the fax
rolled in.  The coversheet said something along the lines of "sorry for the
delay."  At least they included a copy of the actual VISA card credit.  A
day or 2 later the actual U.S. Mail version of the credit sheet arrived so
they did notify me twice - nice touch.

I just found out today that the heads I received from Barrett (keep in mind
that these are GEX rebuilts) are a mismatched set and I wasn't aware of this
before.  One is a type 1 head and the other is a type 3 head.  Isn't that
great?  This explains to some extent why there is such a difference between
the 2 heads in the CC readings.

I'm not going to stand here and force my opinion on others as to what I
think of Barrett Enterprises.  Rest assured I have formed an opinion of them
and so with this information at hand, you can decide for yourself as to
whether or not you wish to do business with them.
