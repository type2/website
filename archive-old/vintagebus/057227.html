> "While I'm at it," I am planning pressure washing it, on getting new exhaust

> checking on the existence of a Hoover bit, flushing the oil cooler, check on
> the clutch, checking the endplay, and replacing the crankshaft seals.
>
> Can anyone recommend some other common items I should check on or do "while I'm
> at it?"

Look at all the tin with a jaundiced eye.  Any cracks should be brazed up.
You will probably find cracks on the cylinder tin.  Brazed tin very seldom
requires any further repairs, as the cracking will relieve stresses and
the brazing process will tend to anneal the metal.

Also check the heater
boxes, particularly around where the pipe goes in and out.  If the housing
is loose, make some split collars out of sheet metal and braze them around
the pipe to increase the bearing surface.

Replace all the engine bay seals, of course.
