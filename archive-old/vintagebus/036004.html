Is far as I know the Dormobile roof is a patented design of the Martin Walter company.
The Dormobile conversion was applied to other vehicles such as Land Rovers and 
COMMER and Ford vans. There is a Dormobile club covering all these vehicles.
Martin Walter licensed the elevating roof design to other conversion companies, 
which is why you find them on Westies and other conversions. 

He had lots of other interesting ideas, you ought to see an early 60's Dormobile 
Split intact. The 'Dormatic' seats tilt and swivel to about a dozen different positions!
