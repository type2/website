Subject: RE: [VB] Can I put stock heads on 90.5 cylinders?

In the interime you can use standard valve heads punched to 90.5 until you get
your big valve heads fixed. I'd be looking for a NEW set of washers and clips
for your rocker arm shafts. There are several ads in the mags that have new
sets, or you can buy new rocker shaft assemblies with new hardware. I ran a
standard valve set of DP heads on two 1776 with no problems, of course I had
1.25:1 rockers to make up the difference between the standard valves and the
big valves.
