Hi Jason,
#1 Check to make sure that the reduction gear boxes are bolted tight on the
rear swing plates.  
#2  If you are going to race your engine over 4,000RPM consistantly and/or
your engine is over 1641cc then you are going to need a header muffler.  If
you want much better MPG with a 1641cc or smaller engine and longer engine
life.  Then by keeping the RPMs to between 1,500-3,000 then the stock bus
single tip muffler will do just fine and outlast the header mufflers by twice.
It sounds like a bug muffler was installed at one time thus the holes cut into
the rear bumper.  Bug mufflers will give you carbon monoxide poisioning to you
or your passengers, so stick with one of the mufflers that gets the exaust out
to one of the rear corners where the slipstream of air will carry most of it
away.
