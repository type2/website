34 carbs are notorious for having problems, either they don't idle or don't 
accelerate.  This is common if the carb has sat for a while, and is a real 
PITA to clean and get going properly. Trying using an adaptor and a cleaned 
out SP carb.

Check your wiring vs a wiring diagram to make sure the wires at the speedo 
are hooked up correctly.  You can get one from vintagebus_dot_com.  You really do 
want the idiot lights working in case something goes bad.   In the meantime, 
make sure your fan belt is good and not cracked.

Was the fuel filter full of gurk?   Has this bus sat a while?  If so, you may 
well have rust in the tank.   Using a magnet in bag on stick(wire clothes 
hanger) down the filler neck really helps.  Though people may disdain cheap 
easy solutions, it extracts rust particles from the tank without any need to 
remove engine and tank and thus is less work and expense.
