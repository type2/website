Unless I am mistaken, this can easily be tested by turning the ignition on,
removing the wire from the solenoid, and verifying that a 'click' is heard
when you touch the wire to the solenoid.

Also verify that the power wire to the solenoid is coming from the +
(ignition key) side of the coil.
