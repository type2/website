You need to block sand that down as smooth as possible with no coarser than
400 grit wet-or-dry sand paper using water. Quality paint pep is the key to
the best paint adhesion. Get all the little nooks and crannys. The bumps
will come through the finish coats and you may have a dull finish if the
paint is not applied properly.
