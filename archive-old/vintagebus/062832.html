 Actually I think there are more 67 handles out there then the earlier. In
all my handles and in most of my buses they have and/or are 67 L key
profiles. I have a harder time finding the earlier then the latter. I would
gladley trade L profile handles for any earlier ones.
  My 60 DD 2 L profile, 60 Deluxe L profile, 63 Westy (just aquired) L
profile. I haven't checked my other 5 buses yet to see what they have. 
  I think the reason for this is that after 67 that is all the VW had made
and when ever someone came in with a broken handle , it was replaced with
the later L profile. I still find 67 only used and NOS handles at the swaps
on a regular basis.
