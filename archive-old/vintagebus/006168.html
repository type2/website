You've got an exhaust leak somewhere. Look at all exhaust connections. A
leak will show as black exhaust on some part of thethe exterior of the
exhaust component. With the motor running you should be able to feel the
leak with your hand or fingertips ( of course at a safe distance, no
touching, and watching for moving parts.
