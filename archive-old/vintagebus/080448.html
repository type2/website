Per K-2 12 of the '63-'67 Bentley, the stock jets for a 1500 sp with
28 PICT 1 are 115 main, 145y air, g45 pilot.  Multiplying these by
1584/1493 gives a 122 main, 154 air and 47.7 pilot.  I think the closest
normal values are 122.5, 155, and 50 (or 55).  I have no idea how well
these will work - it's just a suggestion based on displacement.
