Happy Friday.

A funny and piognant thing happened to me on the drive
down to OCTO last Sunday.

Since it was the first time I took DARTHVW on the
freeway, I was taking it easy, going about 50-55 in
the right lane down the infamous 405.

I kept checking the rear-view for angry yuppies
(although at 6:30 am they're not even at Starbuck's
yet). Then I suddenly noticed a taxi driving behind me
(for those of you who don't live in L.A. there are no
taxis driving around waiting for fares. You have to
call them for a pick-up and a ride.) Anyway, here's
this driver with an empty taxi following me, going 50
mph for several miles.

Then I got to thinking: "Is he waiting for me to break
down?" Like he knows I'm a potential fare? Tailing me
the way vultures circle above an injured wildebeast on
the Serengetti?

Brutal.

Finally he pulled off, but the whole time I was
thinking like "oh, you're not getting me, bastard!
Today is NOT my day." Like he was the freakin' grim
reaper or something.

The kicker was, and I'm not kidding, there was another
one following me for about 4 miles on the way back
home!
Jeez. Does my bus look that bad?

So, if you find yourself being tailed by a taxi, or a
tow truck for that matter, wave the guy around and
say, "not today buddie"
