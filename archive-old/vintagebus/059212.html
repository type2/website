>Before I send somebody the wrong part: The engine lid prop for
>a '63 would be the same elbow-like thing as on my '64, right?

If you have an early '64 (made before 1 222 025, December 1963),
then yes.  It's the part made out of two pieces of stamped sheet
metal, with a pivot in the middle.  Per the parts manual, this part
(261 827 361) was used post-Barndoor till December 1963.  Barndoors
used 211 827 361 and really, really early Barndoors (before November
1950) used 111 823 315 .

>I've seen the later big spring type (on the '67)

This was used starting in December 1963 and continuing to the bay
windows.  211 827 401.
