	I use an enamel based white primer on my '67 bus.  I do not top coat
it.  The flat white looks pretty good and it washes up well.  The flat
finish also helps to hid all of the many surface irregularities.  I put
on two heavy coats of the primer and I have found that it lasts about
five years.  Next year I will paint it with white primer again for the
third time!  Gary Gibson
