these DIN standard annotations are kind of buried all over 
the Bentley manual . however, the terminal #'s to which 
you  specifically refer are not on your headlight switch, they 
refer to terminals you will install when you add a new relay.
any  properly rated relay w/ the proper configuration of inputs  
and outputs will work, whether or not these numbers are used.
some basic testing w/ a multimeter will reveal the function.
a lot of the mystery can be removed if you take the cover off
the relay and inspect it.
