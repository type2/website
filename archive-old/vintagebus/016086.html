Sounds like a good price, even if the tranny had issues.  The boxes are
a good score unto themselves.

Check the condition of the trans my manually putting it in gears and
then turning the input shaft.  Also see how much play there is by
turning one wheel (drum) and seeing how much delay there is before the
other one turns, and how smoothly.  You can also pull the plug and
look for surprises.  

The last Big Nut drum-to-drum that I sold I got $250 for, and that
was several years ago.
