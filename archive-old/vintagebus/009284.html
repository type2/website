Try:
http://search.ebay_dot_com/cgi-bin/texis/ebay/results.html?cobrandpartner=x&maxR
ecordsReturned=300&maxRecordsPerPage=200&category1=417&textonly=n&query=%28v
w%2Cvolkswagen%2Cvolkswagon%29&SortProperty=MetaEndSort&srchdesc=y

---OR---
http://search.ebay_dot_com/cgi-bin/texis/ebay/results.html?cobrandpartner=x&maxR
ecordsReturned=300&maxRecordsPerPage=200&category1=292&textonly=n&query=%28v
w%2Cvolkswagen%2Cvolkswagon%29&SortProperty=MetaEndSort&srchdesc=y
