Wrong on my bus.  I tried to fit the busboys plate.  Too thick.  I know
this plate normally works, but I sat under there quite a while, measured
this and that over and over again.  I could have cut out a big slice in

most people.  As a matter of fact I have two of them I can bring to Jerome
if anybody is interested in buying one for a good discount.:)  
I'm glad I did it by putting a stock rubber mount in now, but I would have
taken the easy way out if the solid mount had worked.
