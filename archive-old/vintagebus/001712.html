 I have about ten pair left at this point.  I will try to get more when
they're gone but am not sure if I can.  (And even if I can, it won't be
until at least February.)

Just to clarify, these heater boxes have the correct linkage for a '68-71
bus.  They will also fit a Bug or a pre-68 bus, but the linkage will be in
the wrong place.  You will have to either modify the linkage, or simply wire
them open during the winter with a piece of coat hanger.  As I recall, the
linkage for a Beetle is a bit lower on the heater box than for a Bus, so
this could be a bit more of a challenge. The present Dansk "universal" boxes
solve this with an L-shaped arm that has two places to attach the cable (one
for a bus linkage, one for a bug).  On a splittie, isn't the linkage just on
the other end, in which case spotwelding a small tab would solve the
problem?  Otherwise, how about robbing linkage parts off of the old heater
boxes you're removing?  Just some ideas. Personally, I'm much too lazy, and
I just wire them open during the winter, since there is no time between
November and0 March that I *wouldn't* want them open anyway.
