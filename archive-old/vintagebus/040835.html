This may sound a little 'too simple', but if the
paint on the semaphore is latex house paint,
place it under warm water and rub it off with
your finger.  Another great tool for removing
paint is a discarded toothbrush of the new variety
with the soft rubbery insets.  Use the rubbery 
surface to rub on the warmed paint, works great.

Careful in applying pressure to not break it!
