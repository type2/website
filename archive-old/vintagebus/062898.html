I'll second that.  the gaskets seemed to be hard to find, and five bucks a 
piece if I wanted to wait for them to be ordered.   I traced the cover on a 
paper grocery sack.  You can trace the inside with a little difficulty by 
putting the pencil through the axle hole.  Punched the holes with a punch
and 
then cut along the lines with scissors.  Voila!   gasket!
