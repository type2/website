I recently went through a similar state of mind myself, unsuccessfully. My friends could see I was severely depressed, but they had no idea how deep the hole I was in. I called Charlie Ford one night for some help, and he spent a couple of hours on the phone with me, which helped for a couple of days. Some signs to look out for are: neglecting normal household duties, neglecting needed work on the bus, withdrawal from friends, excessive alcohol use, no appetite... I could go on, but maybe you get the idea. Medication will help, I know.
