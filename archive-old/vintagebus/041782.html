My R100RT does the same thing.  When BMW first brought out the K100
4-cylinder they had a heck of a problem, since all four cylinders point to
the left.  The solution was to pin the oil control rings to prevent their
end gaps from lining up on the bottom of the cylinder.  New owners were
quite distressed that their new $13K motorcycles were smoking like a
2-stroke on startup!
