There are a lot of different processes of chromeing, the key is in the
prep.
Taiwan cheap chrome uses a single dip method, bassicaly clean and
polish the
steel and dip in the chrome vat. Quality chrome uses a few more
processes,
1. polish and clean the metal.  2. first dip in copper. 3. polish
again, if
any imperfections polish and dip in copper again. 4. dip the copper
coated
piece in nickel, this gives it a dull finish, 5. dip in the chrome
solution
then you have a finished product, this is what is so called triple
plated
chrome which in reality is the old school way of doing it, but they
had to
name it to make quality chrome sound special. If you have cheap chrome
that's rusting best to just replace the part as you will peobally
never stop
the rust.
