Pop Rivets?
Not a good idea.
Regardless how you prep the surface around them, rust will form in time
inside the 3/16's holes, and your back to square one.

I have found the absolute best way to attach any type of steel ( or
aluminum ) panel is to use 3-M #08117 Panel Bonding Adhesive.

It not only makes for a bullet proof repair, it also displaces moisture
at the repaired area.
Simple to use too--

Grind, clean, apply adhesive, and clamp.

Done.
