Let me relay a new angle on your rod knock problem. 
Along with my Splits, I have owned a 73 Super Beetle

the engine still knocks like hell, but, still running
well. Sounds like a knock, feels like a knock???
Running good and strong, so how much longer before the
explosion, time will tell.
