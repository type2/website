  I have more then 50 hours alone into the just the undercarriage of my DC.
The time included removing everything from the undercarriage, spending
countless hours wire brushing it, then taping off what I didn't want
sprayed, rust morting the under carriage, then painting it. This time does
not include any rust repair (none was needed) or straightening anything. I
now have a completely painted under carriage and figure another 100 plus
hours in rebuilding and painting the front end and trans, doing the brakes,
wiring harness etc. That puts just the undercarriage alone at $7500.00
dollars labor not counting any parts or materials. Thank god my labor is
free (or is it). Then I will add the estimated 300 hours in body work (again
no rust repair or metal replacement) that adds another $15,000 in body and
paint, again not counting materials.
  Then I have upholstery and interior stuff, that should be cheap I figure
$1000.00 dollars for that.
  So all in all I figure I will have at least $25000.00 or so in my DC, it
should be nice though and I would be lucky to sell it for $10,000.00 when
it's done.
