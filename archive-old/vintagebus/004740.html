I like the Westfailure. Best tow vehicle going. Barring the availability of
a stretched V8 Westy, get the longest wheelbase vehicle you can. A crewcab
truck or something. The longer the wheelbase, the easier the handling when
towing in crosswinds and such. Weight is also good, but secondary to
wheelbase. Third is horsepower. Don't worry about going fast if it doesn't
stay in its lane. All signs point to a stretched V8 Westy, but I may be
biased.
