>I always buy Bosch platinum plugs<
I have not had great experiences with these plugs.  I have tried them in
type 1 motors and they seemed to foul because of a little oil consumption
issue.  Also I have run them in a Mazda 626 and Honda Accord and much
preferred the NGK copper cores, the Bosch Platinum were good at high RPM but
seemed to lack low end grunt.  I think this is due to the spark size being
larger on copper cores.  I have not run a 4 tip though which may do
better..?

>When I bought a new Porsche 944 in 1983, the dealer warned me against using
synthetic oil, <
This is kind of a weird recommendation for the engine.  For the 911 trannies
901 and 915 models dino juice needs to be used though, the synthetic is too
slippery and drains off the gears too well which causes them to loose
lubrication and wear prematurely.  I would recommend Mobil1 for any Porsche
engine application no matter how old/new it is.
