Secondly, regarding the ring/collar piece: I waited to install this piece until
AFTER the main deck table was mounted on the wall. That way I was able to mark
the exact spot at which to drill the hole by swinging the table leg down. The
ring/collar piece is a flat metal disk with three small holes near the outer
edge 120 degrees apart from each other. A larger hole in the center of the flat
disk is large enough to accept the single pole-like table leg. This larger hole
has a slight cut at one point with the metal bent to create a "thread" for the
leg-pole. The set-up is simple. The disk (for the 66-67) should be painted the
same chocolate milk brown/warmgrey as the mechanisms of the Z-bed and painted
hubcaps. Expect the paint to be scuffed and removed with normal usage.
