Only if it says so in the warranty.  They have delivered to you a device
that, if installed properly and driven according to VW's instructions
(which don't mention temperature at all!), should function as advertised
for the warranty period.  The "tell-tale" is just an excuse to get out of
covering a poorly-assembled engine.  It may well be that the assembly
itself accounted for the overheating!

The only GEX engine I got had a tell-tale on it, glued to a thick layer
of paint on the side of the sump.  I removed it with my fingernail and
kept it nice and safe just in case it ever needed to be reinstalled.
Alas, the engine burned a valve at about 40K carefully driven miles in a
bug.
