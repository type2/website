Bigger physically? yes

Bigger as in more gas holding capacity? no

I have 1964, 1965, 1966 & 1967 owners manuals and they all state the 
gas tanks hold 10.6 gallons.  The later 67 tanks (they did not start with 
the 67 model year) have a hump in the top rear that is only seviceable as 
a vapor expansion area.  They can't hold more gasoline, due to the fact 
that air gets trapped there.
