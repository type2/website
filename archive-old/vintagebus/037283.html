congrats on the new bus  :)  

your VIN starts with 236, meaning 2 for type2 (bus), 3 for model kombis, and 
6 for 66 model year.  Kombis are  basic, like a panel van with windows, and 
pretty bare in the back, just grey paint with no panels and a back seat 
(middle seat optional for extra DM).  A lot got made into campers.  Westfalia 
used kombis to turn into campers.  (but westy combis got special treatment of 
paint in the cargo area and white steering wheel etc,  anyone know what the M 
code for that is if any? JC) SO did sundial  and other American companies 
(who also used panels, and cut some windows in).  and they put the paneling 
(included roof panelling) and camper kit in.  Is you panel colour extreme?  I 
thought they wre just a regular sort of finished wood.  nicer than the westy 
formica to look at anyway.
