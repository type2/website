mmmmmmm....

Thing is you have changed your bushes (which might
be part of the problem...).  My first bet would be
that whoever put em in MAY have 'expanded' up
an end (reducing ID) by overzealous pressing or drifting.

You probably need to do
some measuring to determine where the problem is
(bushes could need reaming to get back to correct ID).
Note this could be the problem with the 'tight' pin
but not the 'loose' 8-(.......

You probably need to measure your 'new' pins etc etc.

You can mix'n' match.  Clean up the old cages
and make sure that none of the bearings just 'drop out'.
If the old cages clean up OK (bearings don't fall out - no
galling or whatever)  you can mix them with the new
parts to achieve 'best' fit....
