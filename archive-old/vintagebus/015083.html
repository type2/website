Hi, I have done quite a bit of bus metal
replacement..living in the rust belt of course!

I have heard much ravings (by the person selling the
panels of course!) of Schwofields better fitting
panels.  Well I had a friend buy inner and outer
rockers, floors and doglegs from Schofield.  I must
say I was NOT impressed at all by the stampings and
fit...they were actually below the level of quality I
have seen in some other's panels.

I have found Wolfsburg West's inner and outer rockers,
outriggers and jack points to be very good...the
doglegs are so-so...nose pieces are decent.  their
corners will fit and line up decent but require some
fitting.  But their battery trays absolutey
SUCK...they need tons of re-working.

Seems to me some companies probably make some stuff
better then others.  Of course I have NEVER seen any
after market come close to fit and quality of original
VW metal...and have started replacing pieces with used
sheet metal out of donor buses with smashing results!

Good luck
