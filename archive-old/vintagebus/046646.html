Looks like VW painted it after they put on the axle boots.   But I have 
painted them before installing on the trans, just be careful of the sealing 
surfaces.  I paint a bit further towards the flange than the boots cover, 
that does not cause leaks, but dont paint the flange area.  It does have 
friction, and needs a certain clearance, painting there will mean you end up 
with paint bits in your oil, and remember it will be in oil anyway so will 
not rust.
