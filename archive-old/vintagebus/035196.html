Hey John,

Sounds like something fragged inside.  Try taking the rockers and push rods
out and see if that helps.  Check that all the valves are seated properly,
might be that a valve head is broken off and clunking around in a cylinder
(likely #3).

I have seen some oddball things break lately, so it could be a rod bolt
snapped or even the camshaft broken in half.

Any of the above will mean complications in parts reusage on the rebuild.
Each one will have to be carefully checked before refurbishing for reuse.
For instance if a rod is toast a new set will be needed and the crankshaft
checked for straightness, and of course case allignbored to straighten it.
If the valve snapped you will need rebuilt/new heads, new pistons and
cylinders, and thorough checking of the rods and crank, of course
allignbore.  I always use new lifters as standard practice, can reuse
camshaft if it is carefully checked.

I think the full autopsy will be necessary in any scenario.
