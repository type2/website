Subject: RE: stereo that fits without any cutting

 I to went this way but with a little twist. I took an Pioneer 8-track
player, took the face plate off and gutted the insides. I then installed a
small piano hinge along the bottom of the face plate and the 8 track body.
with the insides hollowed out I was able to mount a CD player in side very
nicely. I have showed the bus numerous times at local shows and only the
people I have showed how it works have ever known it was anything more than
a 8-track player.
