Subject: RE: [VB] Powdercoating

Powder coating is a electro static process... so unless the bondo is
conductive, I'm not sure how well it will coat.  After you *attract* the
powder to the part with a voltage potential, you heat it up to *melt* the
powder together and it forms a tough polymeric coating.  Chromers straighten
and then tin the bumpers before they start chroming.  I would imagine you
want to do the same.
