The only starter bushing I've done (stock 6 V replacement in my '64) fit
nice and tight.  I think there are four bushing sizes: stock 6 V, stock
12 V, 6 into 12, and 12 into 6.  A good parts guy can probably tell them
apart by looking, but the $6/hr person in the warehouse trying to get the
right bushing out of four adjacent bins probably can't.

For reference, the new bushing I installed was 0.652" or 16.56 mm OD,
measured with an inch caliper.  The ID of your bushing will be different
than what I had, but the OD should be pretty close to this, I think.
