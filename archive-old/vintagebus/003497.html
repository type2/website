You can make your own adjusting star "tongs" with steel banding available at
most lumber yards in the their dumpster.  Worse case scenario involves a
small bribe and a 12" fresh piece off of their banding spool.  I've redone
my own by drilling and tapping a small hole and bolting the adjusting star
tong do the block.  Works great on both the bug and the bus.
