	I guess Ronnie has outed me again...
	Unfortunately, I did not find a specific way to tell the difference between
the two types of rear hatches.

	I was mainly looking to answer the same question you originally had.  Are
they different.

	The only thing I can add, is pretty much what everyone else has said.
There is little to no curve to the high roof door.  Look at it from a side
profile and see if you can notice a definite arch from top to the bottom of
the door.  If you notice it straighten out near the top, then it's most
likely a high roof door.

	Pretty much, you need to try installing it on a bus to find out 100% sure.
