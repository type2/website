hello,

I have run into this before myself...

what i have tried...

if the bushing is a "split" type (has a seam in it) drive a chisel into
that seam and expand it, then the thing will come right off...

Heat it up with a torch, spray it with penetrating oil, and put vise grips
on it and try to get it moving.  Yank on vise grips to try and twist it,
or hit vise grips with a hammer to move it off...
