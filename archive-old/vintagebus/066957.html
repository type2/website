When I got mine, there was some miscellaneous dirt and trash, change,
a plastic Shell Oil thing[0], a tropical fish refrigerator magnet,
an empty pack of Marlboros[1], four matchbooks (AAMCO in San Jose CA,
Clark's Auto Parts and Machine in Cupertino CA, and two from E-Z 8

year stickers.

Now it's clean.  The cargo area is full of spare parts and tools and
stuff, though.  I have discovered that it's a good place to look when
the Bus is hungry and swallows items that I left on the front (bench)
seat.  Eventually I will probably have the jack and lug wrench back
in there.
