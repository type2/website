I have seen type3 coolers that have the same kind of finnage as the type 1 
coolers, perhaps they had a couple varieties.  FWIW, the sender on the type3 
cooler takes up the same place as the hoover bit air deflector, so either you 
leave the sender in and leave off the airdeflector, or you plug the sender 
and use the HBAD.  I found this out when I tried to add that bit around a 
type 3 cooler on a bus motor, and it wouldn't.

It would be interesting to do the math and figurae out the total cooling area 
for each kind.
