Here is a tip I learned from my good buddy: the late, great Josh Rodgers.
12volt to 110volt power inverters will run a boom box all weekend! You can
get a small one for around $50. I run a large boom box with cd changer and
can't hardly tell any battery drain at all! They also work great for hair
dryers, trouble lights, small tv's etc.
