>can I test a 6V deluxe clock in my 12V bus? Otherwise,
>how can I do it other than getting a 6V auto battery?
>

The clocks are a sort of electric self winder. When the spring runs down, a
pair of points close, actuating a coil which re-winds the spring. Very
clever and it doesn't cause a continuous drain on the battery. The 6v. coil
will take a short period of 12v. no problem (the windings are heavier than a
12v. coil) but the points may burn. I recommend either a 6v. battery
charger, a 6v. lantern battery, or 4 C or D cells in series (4 x 1.5v = 6v)
