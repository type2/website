The main reason that the airplane folks use reduction units is that auto
engines tend to produce their best power at around 4500-5000RPM while the
speed of a typical propeller is limited to less than 3000RPM because at
higher RPMs the tips of the prop go supersonic, creating noise and
reducing efficiency.  The only other solution is to go with a smaller
diameter prop, also bad for efficiency.
