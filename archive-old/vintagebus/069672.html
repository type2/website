Well Frank, I can put in my two cents from a 67 SC point of view. I'm an
engine man really and work on the body by necessity. The list has taught me a
lot and driving my SC daily has taught me about it's capability. I had a worn
out 1500 SP when I bought it. The hills were a challenge and carrying anything
was a chore. I quickly built a 1641 DP and slid it in. Now hauling and hills
are no problem. I will stick with the RGB/tranny combo because I like the
extra torque it gives me when I haul loads, which is all the time. The top
speed is around 65, although I could do better with a slightly bigger motor,
like the 1776 on my trike! I went from a 31 PICT on an adapter plate to a
rebuilt 34 PICT-3. I run 137 60 65 jets and the power vs. mileage is OK.

67 Single Cab, DP 1641cc 34 PICT-3
71 Trike 1776cc Dual T-4 34 PDSIT-3
VolksAir 1585cc 31 PICT
