I once drove my 1961 rust top (I mean rag top) standard from Vegas to Phoenix with no clutch and no generator.  I stopped half way to charge the battery after driving with the headlight on for a couple hours.  Starting it in gear with no charging system got scarier the lower the battery got, but I made it home fine.  The worse part was in Sun City (a retirement area where everyone drives golf carts) where everyone seemed to be stopped at green lights when I was trying to keep moving.  Once I made it through there the rest of town was cake.

Driving without a cluch is definitely a skill every bus owner should have.  You never know when you will need to get to work on a holiday weekend when no parts stores are open or the part isn't available.
