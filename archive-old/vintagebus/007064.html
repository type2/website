This is not that unusual. Here in the "arctic Chicagoland area" we don't see
too many splits. Over the last few years I've seen 2, maybe 3 of them (and
I'm not counting mine). But just the other day I took a long drive
south-west to drop my rims to get them sandblasted and here we go!, I
spotted a Bus sitting behind a barn. It was cold as hell but I turned
around, stopped and looked it over from a distance. Unfortunatelly it's a
bay, 1971 but still intact, just waiting to be rescued. I even left a note
for the owner and he called me. Always worth a try, the guy used to work on
ACVW's in the past and still has a stash of parts left. Looks like I'm
meeting the guy next week. They are out there, you just have to be patient.

Good luck!
