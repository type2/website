On low-current accessories like radios (<2A or so) it's no trick to
use a 6V accessory on 12V.  All you do is hook up a simple voltage
regulator chip and it'll produce a reliable 6V for your radio.  The
voltage regulator can be either external or internal, if there is a
bit of space inside your radio.

Turning 6V into 12V is a bit tougher!
