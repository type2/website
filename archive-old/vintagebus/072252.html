Hi Robert,
It will tow fine IF you first go through the suspension first.  Meaning 
complete repacking and greasing of front end and changing and filling the tranny 
and RGBs with new gear oil.  Plus a new or a good set of tires.  Lastly make 
sure the tranny is in nuetral and the E-brake is released before towing!  Though 
we would keep the towing speed to 60 MPH or less because the higher the speed 
the quicker things can get out of hand, IE getting hit by a powerfull 
sidewind.
