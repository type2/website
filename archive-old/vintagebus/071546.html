Subject: Dealer Microfische machine score!!

I went over and bought the microfische machine i mentioned to the list a while back (found in a public storage locker auction).
Turned out to be an original VW machine with logo made by Agfa, has dust jacket and original repair/cleaning literature in 4 or 5
different languages! Also came with a little wooden brush i pressume for cleaning off the slides, anyone know if the machine came
with that originally? At first glance it didnt look like he had much of the fische, and all the ones i looked at said 1974, 1975 or

(some were 2 pages)! So heres the fische it came with:
