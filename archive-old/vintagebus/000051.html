1B 052   - are you sure this is what it says?   this should be the build date
next line is M codes-
360- n Am specs, etc etc
UY- port shipped to, 211- panel van ( the vents at the upper back corners 
confirm this- and that it is a bench seat, windows put in by one of the 
conversion people who made panels into campers)
Blue- dove blue paint
948738 is the VIN, which puts it as made in May 1962, maybe that first line 
is 
18 05 2 - meaning production date 18th May 1962.

Thus the flat/ fried egg turn signals would be correct, and it would have the 
oval taillights like 62 through 71.

I believe someone recently posted a couple URLs with M code info, how to 
decipher the plate, and yes VW attached them with little screws.

Always make sure your title matches the VIN on the vehicle.
