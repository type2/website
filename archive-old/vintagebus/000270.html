Could easily be that the carb need to be cleaned again (Assuming he did when 
rebuilding it.) which is not unusual to have to clean a carb two and even 
tree times to get out all the old gunk.  Also could be the wrong size jets.  
You need to look at the stock tech info on simular sized engines for your bus 
to find possibly close jetting sizes unless you are running a completely 
correct stock engine for your year VW.  Then the jet sizes should be right on 
or very close.  Above all to really know what size jets you do have you need 
to get a jet sizing tool which is alot like a feeler gauge but has round 
sizing pieces instead of flat.  Eric&Barb
