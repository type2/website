The exact same thing happened in my '64 beetle.  It had a hack of a 12v conversion and early taillights that looked cool but didn't work well. If any of this sounds like you, you may want to look into some of these things.  This fixed it.  I'm not sure which fixed it but after that night (with a TON of help from my local VW guru) all things were well.
