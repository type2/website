Just a reminder, do not over tighten the belt if you're using an
  aftermarket (including the Porsche) pulley(s) on top and an aluminum
 bottom.
  They can explode!  I set my belt tension very loose, with no ill effects
 from slippage.

 What makes the pulley fail when overtightened is the two pulley halves do
 not get to fully seat against eachother. This causes the pulley to do the
 "tin can" effect and start bending back and forth, round and round style 
until it breakes apart near the hub.
