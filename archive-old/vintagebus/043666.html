That looks like the one on my '64.  I think the latch on the apron
changed when they changed from the church-key socket to the push button
at the very beginning of the '66 model year.

The '67 parts manual online at http://home.cwru_dot_edu/~agw4/docs.htm
has these three entries for "End cross member - Rear panel" on page 407:

211 813 175 B  up to 20-117 901
211 813 175 B  from 20-117 902 to 215 190 000
211 813 175 C  from 216 000 001 

I'm pretty sure the above numbers break down as "Barndoor" (Wolfsburg?),
"post-Barndoor to '65" (Hannover?), and "'66 and up".  I think the
original Barndoor apron would have had the starting crank hole; looks
like they had superseded it to the version without the hole by the
time this '67 manual was printed.

Anyway, what you have is probably a 211 813 175 B and what you need
is a 211 813 175 C.
