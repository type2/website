No!  The heater robs air from the fan shroud which otherwise would be
available for cooling the oil and cylinders.  This is no problem when the
heater is needed, but in the summer might be the straw that breaks the
camel's back.

In a water-cooled car, running the heater to prevent overheating is valid,
as the heater is simply a little radiator that will remvoe heat from the
coolent.
