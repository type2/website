Jim,

I can tell you from experience from last winter to stay away from those
Dansk heater boxes unless you park your bus when it gets cold. My original
rusted heater boxes put out more heat than the dansk units (which btw have
the aluminum fins inside, not the low budget stripped out versions). It has
much to do with the internal shape of the exhaust tube which is round on
the dansk units. If you look at some line art of late model (1500 and up)
engines the factory boxes have an oblong cross section that allows maximum
heat to be transferred to the interior. Eric&Barb enlightened me to this
and can probably explain it better than I can. If I had asked the list
beforehand I could have put the money towards my side scoops instead.  :)
