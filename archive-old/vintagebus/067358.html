The South Wind gasoline heaters were made by Stewart Warner who still makes
gas heaters for 18 wheelers, aircraft and the military.  Go to:

http://www.stewart-warner_dot_com/combustion.html

As far as how the VW style South Wind heaters work, what they look like and
how much "modification" you have to do to your bus, there is a partial
scanned factory manual that may answer some of your questions at:

http://vw.itgo_dot_com/heater.html

Hope this helps, if you have any more questions, feel free to pmail me.  I
will have to say this, a working gas heater in a bus does increase it's
value, unless you're selling it in S. Cal., but even the those folks like to
do a little driving in the mountains, how about that freeze your nuggies off
Mount Shasta trip!!
