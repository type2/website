The spring plates are special hacks, if you are a compotent welder you could
probably do it. The axles tubes are slightly? shortened 68 long spliners,
and the axles are just that TIII/T1 1968er, long spline/long alxes. Axle
ends appear to be stock, but the brakes are useally tIII installed swapped,
so ebrake and cyl are in TII attitude.

You cannot use the stock 68 tubes and plates, your wheels will hit stuff, I
am told.
This is based on the wolfgang kit I just removed.
