>Also, can I remove the back/closer to front engine tin
>w/out dropping the engine.  Ie: remove gas line and
>starter bolt plus 2 egine tine screws.

Remove the gas line, accelerator cable, and the cheesehead screws located on
each side and you should be able to pry it out of there. It may be bolted to
the front join between the upper and lower cylinder tin as well as the one
screw on each end. Will be more difficult if converted to doghouse. You
should not need to remove a starter bolt. It would be easier to pull the
motor out and drop it down a bit to do it, IMHO.
