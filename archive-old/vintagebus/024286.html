This tube is to keep things balanced (I've been told). I have a tube
there and my engine runs really smooth. I also T into the line with my
crancase vent. That saves me the trouble of making some sort of
connection at the air cleaner. It also lets both sides burn some of my
oil 8^). I leave the tube connected while tuning the carbs.
