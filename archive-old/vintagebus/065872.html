Steve, the two halves of the autostick oil pump are supposed to be isolated.
Eventually two pumps, one for engine and one for tranny, until the shaft
gets worn! Not a very good pump fro any real pumping power because of the
small 24mm gears. Get a Melling 30mm if you plan on running an external
cooler because it has sufficient pressure to pump that additional oil uphill
or wherever.
