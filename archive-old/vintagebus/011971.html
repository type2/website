If the bolts you referred to above were the ones that attach the
muffler to the intake manifold heat riser, be sure that you (or the
muffler shop) modify only the muffler to fit.  Don't mess with the
intake manifold side or you won't be able to fit the correct muffler
when the one you have now rusts out in a year or two.

If I were you (and I was a few years ago!) I'd count this muffler as a
lesson and buy the correct muffler right away.
