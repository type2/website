       I normally try and just brush off all the comments I have gotten from

       I beg to differ about the shape of my bus before I started it.  This
bus was bought, for $400, from Lavere's, and was minus ALL it's parts, and
very rusty, with the wrong doors, gas tank, no engine lid, nice toyota high
back seats up front, welded down!!, no side windows, no engine, had a HUGE
hole cut in the roof, and all the supports running side to side had been
hacked out, I am not mentioning all the rust that was ALL the way through
the
rain gutter, and bondo'ed over, and the 200 hundred holes drilled over the
last 40 years or so, which I refer to as home improvements.  That is a much
better description of the original shape of my bus, not a "decent honest

effort to do ANYTHING with this bus.  Please try and not diss me for SAVING
