and leaving half of your flesh hanging on the frames because of NOT
having seatbelts!!  My 58 has a pair of lap belts installed in a

be installed in older buses, but in order to anchor them sufficiently,
you have to weld a big metal plate to the inside of the door pillar to
bolt the upper part to.  I plan to do this eventually since my single
cab is an every-dayer and I live in a big city with lots of crazies.
