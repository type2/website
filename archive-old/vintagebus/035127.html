Hi Robert,
Tar paper can be used under the straps on the upper corners of the fuel tank. 
 Where we are talking about at the very least using tar paper is in the four 
areas near the hold down straps under the tank.  In these areas the tar paper 
keeps the steel tank from sitting directly on and rubbing on the steel of the 
VW bus body.  Which would cause rust and eventually holes!  As for the hole 
in the body that the bottom of the tank and it's outlet sticks through there 
should be a foam rubber seal to keep out the water and rust.  This is a good 
area to paint the body of the bus and the tank where this seal seats with tar 
to prevent more rust due to water spraying up from underneath.  This seal 
also helps to keep the engine cooler!
