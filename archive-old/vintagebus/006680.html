Hi Ronnie & lists
Sounds like the engine we built for the Burrito Express, the only difference

I can see is that we use CB Performance 044 dual port heads, mated to a 
Weber 32/36 progressive.  We went with 6.8:1 compression and the end product

was a good running engine that made lots of heat.  Last weekend we bumped 
the compression to 7.9:1 and the result is a much cooler running engine that

_likes_ to cruise at 3650 RPM (indicated 70 mph).  Ir runs much better, revs

much more willingly, and has a much better throttle response.  Now you've 
got me curious, did the swap panel run warm?  What did you use for an oil 
cooler, and what are you planning for this one?  You've just validated what 
we thought when we planned this engine - appreciate that.  good luck with 
it.
