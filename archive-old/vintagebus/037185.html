 So how do you check it or replace it?  >>
Drain tank.  On post-1961 Vws, except type 3s, you just unthread the cap nut 
off the bottom of the tank.  With pre-1962 VW you unscrew the double threaded 
nut that has both left and right handed threads to seperate the reserve 
switch from the tank.  Replace the sock with a sealing washer on it and 
thread the cap nut or reserve switch with it's double threaded nut up tight.  
Reconnect fuel line and fill tank.  check over the next couple of days that 
this area is not dripping/seeping.  If so retighten.  Eric&Barb
