Oil gets discued every other day on the 911 list.  For a while, Shell
Rotella was the "Offical" oil, with Kedall as the "unofficial"   I think
Mobile 1 is now the official oil, but that's also for the water-pumpers.
The last air-cooled 911 rolled of the line a couple years ago.

The consenus is that if you have a new engine, run synthetic.  In an older
engine, it's just going to leak out and make a mess.

Synthetic oil is a pain to clean up if you spill it.
