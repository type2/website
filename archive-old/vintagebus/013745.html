more then electrical conduit, and all of the pieces
can be found at any supply house. The couplers holding
all the pieces together are standard coundiut couplers
with one bolt to perminetly tighten to one of the
sides and a 'wing' style bolt to make for easy
tightening with no tools needed for the other side.
