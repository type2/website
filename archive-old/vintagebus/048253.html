Hi Don,
Did you make sure to measure the throw out bearing and make sure that it is
evenly placed side to side in relation to the tranny input shaft?  If not and
it is off center the TO bearing will get wiggled side to side from being out
of center to the clutch pressure plate and wear out the TO cross shaft arms
in less than 10,000 miles.
