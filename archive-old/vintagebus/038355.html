Make sure you use the right seals.There are a few different variations
depending on the case and cooler inlet/outlet size.the reason for this is
the later cases had larger(I think) ports and when fitting a later cooler
adaptor to an early engine you need a seal with two different OD's for it to
work right.Look in a new gasket kit there should be sets of different color
seals red,black,white and green if I rember right.Might be the problem.Hope
this helps.
