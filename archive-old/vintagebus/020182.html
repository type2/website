Also went to Prescott. Also ran hot in the 105F
ambient Arizona temp. Also ran guages- pressure and
temp. <understood all of the endless discussions of
inaccuracies etc>. Given that we ran at 240 F coming
out of Phoenix with 60pass Mesa cooler with fan
blowing. Big hairy dog, wife, daughter, self, under
valued Puck trailer and everything that we own bolted
inside per popular request. <sigh>

open at the top of "the" big hill aka Sunset Point
reststop. More proof that I hadn't driven a splittie
in some time. Wife just thought it was Summer time.
=)!

trip as my baby apparently partied all night. Baby and
Mom in the Punk trailer. Harry dog and self in the

the wife wishing for more sleep and less fussy baby.
The howling in the bus was just between the dog and I.
;-) woof!
Harry dog is the passive-aggressive type. Passive
until another dog comes within ten yards of the bus.
Then comes the aggression. Of course every dog owner
on VW Planet Earth was at the Prescott show. Save the
lectures, I know the REAL def'n of passive/aggressive.
Learned it from my body and paint guy. ;-)

instead. Read the same paragraph about ten times and

new shoes and cheap labor prior to the trip.

good to see everyone and have some laughs. "That other

Outside of Saturday night I had so much fun that it
reminded me that we'll have to take another VW trip
sometime next year.
