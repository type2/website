The Silicon Valley Chapter of SV2s is proud to announce it's first big show
of 2001.  Bring Your Own Bus (BYOB'01) will be happening in conjunction
with Rich Stewart's Red Barn Swap Meet at the Red Barn, about 15 miles
north of Salinas along Hwy 101 on April 21, 2001 (the day before Kelley
Park).  The event is going to be real laid back and mellow with some neat
show categories where all buses are eligible for any award.  Strictly
Vintage 2s will be hosting a fun raffle with donations from a number of
different vendors.  The swap meet is open to all types of VW and everyone's
welcome to attend!  Gates open at 7am and there's free camping Friday night
in the parking lot.
