Have you removed the carb?    Doing that tends to help.  

One way to do it is also remove the 36mm nut of the front (towards nose of 
bus) of generator.    hold pulley from turning like when you adjust fan belt. 
   Either you will loosen the nut, which SHOULD be at 80 ft lbs, or you will 
crack the hub for the generator pulley.     If it loosens the nut, carefully 
removed the nut and washers and keep them in order, you can pull the gen off 
the fan, and out of the bus.
