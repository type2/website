yes there are two versions of the stamped front bumper.  The one with the 
longer pressed bits (smaller liscence plate area) is earlier.  It was used 
until sometime in 56, but I am not sure exactly when.  AFAICFO, it is one of 
the differences between Wolfsburg buildt buses and Hannover buses.   They 
moved the factory in mid 56, and made changes about that time- about ch 178 
004.

the Wolfsburg buses have a tubes for the tranny carrier, rather than a U 
shaped channel.  The doglegs are three pieces of metal attached.  You have 
discoved the Wolfsburg bumper .  Turns out that your friends panel has the 
wrong bumper.   I would be interested if your 56 truck has the round tranny 
tube and three piece doglegs and what its vin is, and I am trying to make a 
list of buses around this change.  You probably have the original bumper on 
your truck.

they made these changes a year after barndoor production ceased.   there are 
a bout 60,000 post barndoor Wolfsberg buses.
