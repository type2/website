According to the split screen Specifications, Modifications & Optional Extras
book US port of entry codes are as follows:

UA    Los Angeles
UB    Boston or Baltimore
UC    Chicago
UD     Baltimore or Philadelphia*
UF     San Francisco
UH    Houston/San Antonio
UJ      Jacksonville*, Honolulu
UK    Unknown
UL     Unknown
UM    Unknown
UN    New York
UO     New Orleans
UP     Portland
US     Seattle
UW    Washington
UX     Houston, San Antonio*
UY     Unknown
UZ     Unknown

* Not confirmed.  Codes for Charleston, Port Everglades and Toledo are unknown.
