Kyle wrote:

> > does anybody have any info on this modification?
>
> The bay window beam into split has been discussed ad nauseum. Plenty of info
> in the archives. For a brief synopsis, it goes like this. 68-69 beam bolts
> into splitties. 71ish torsion arms fit into said beam. They have disc
> brakes. Simple bolt in except that the front of the bus will be a couple
> inches higher. For another flavor, the Westfailure has a late bay beam in it
> with vacuum assist disc brakes. Top two bolts into original holes, bottom
> had two new holes cut into beam flange to match holes on frame. Bolted right
> in, no mods to the stretched and boxed V8 Westy frame. Standard drag link as
> near as I can tell. Will need to go to dual circuit master cylinder,
> proportioning valve seems to be based on peoples tastes. If you search the
> archives you can read about disc brakes, bay beam swaps, and VeeWee's
> detailed descriptions of the dual circuit master cylinder conversion until
> your eyes bleed.
