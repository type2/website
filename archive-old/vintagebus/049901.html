Are you talking about true galvanizing (hot dipped), or so-called
'electro-galvanizing' (zinc plating)?

True galvanizing can warp stamped parts, due to the intense heat involved,
but the rust protection is outstanding.

Zinc electroplating is definitely second best, but cheaper and easier.
Most new cars with gavlanized steel body parts are only electroplated, some
on one side only.
