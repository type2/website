Speaking of which, I have a handful of N.O.S. original GERMAN Volkswagen
heat exchangers of the above description.  As Eric & Barb noted, these will
fit any fresh-air-heat bus, but the linkage will be slightly different from
that used on a Splitty.  One solution is to simply wire the box open as
noted above, although I have also heard of people spot-welding a bug or
universal style linkage onto these to make them work with the dash control
on a Splitty.

Why bother?  These are incredibly heavy duty compared to the Danish ones
sold today.  They were a lucky N.O.S. find which I very possibly will not
come across again.  Compared to a new Danish box or a rusty used one, these
will deliver much more heat and last much longer, with the above limitation.
If you are on the Type 2 ('68-79) list you have seen glowing reports of
these heat exchangers from list members who bought them.
