If you are doing the once over to catch up on delayed maintenance, here's 
some more to think about

grease suspension
check tranny boots -replace if cracked or torn
change gear oil (center and RGBoxes at same time)
inspect and repack front wheel bearings
replace rubber brake hoses if it hasn't been done in 5 years
check all wheels for leaky cylinders
check for existance of front shift bushing- install if missing or worn.
check rear shift coupler for wear/wiggling
drain and refilll steering gear box and adjust.
oil all hinges
