From the Volkswagen Parts Manual referencing M16 - the option code for a
Double Cab:
"Up to Chassis No. 397 288, as well as a further 35 vehicles between Chassis
No. 397 347 and 415 969, the Double Cabs were built by Messrs. Binz & Co.,
Lorch/Wuertt., Germany.  These vehicles have a wider cab door."
(On my Binz page)

Progressive Refinements says:
3 Nov. 58 - 404 236 - VW Pick up - Now: With Double Cab (M-16)

A couple lines down in Progressive Refinements:
3 Nov. 58 - 397 288  - VW Pickup - Now: Start of production of Double Cab

So there is a slight discrepancy.  FWIW, 404 236 would be Nov. 3rd, 1958.

So I would go with 397 288...

I once estimated chassis #397 288 to be October 26th, 1958, although such
things are approximate.
