> Isn't the "idle" screw on the arm that holds the acc
> cable and the other two relate to mix?

No, actually.  The small screw is for your idle mixture, the larger one is
for idle adjustment.  The adjustment on the throttle arm, is to be adjusted
so that it 'just' touches the fast idle cam (in the fully warmed up
position), to keep the butterfly from wedging in the barell.
