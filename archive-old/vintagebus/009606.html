The plastic is also important as a vapor barrier. It keeps the moisture
generated by all those hot, steamy bodies inside from condensing on the cold
metal, causing many of the rust problems found in areas that aren't all that
wet (like here in central CA). The plastic must be intact and completely
sealed to work properly.
