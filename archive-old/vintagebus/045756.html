I took the rubber seal, wrapped it ALL the way around the glass (yes, ALL 
four sides), STREEEEETCH, then I took a pair of vise grips to hold the rubber 
ends together on the outside.....took hot soapy water, silicone spray, ect 
ect and beat on it with a rubber mallet until it sat properly.
Undid the vise grips and soaked it in hot water letting the rubber pull back 
into the frame.
Let dry.
After a few days i rough cut the rubber, then after a few more I final cut 
the rubber and installed it.
GOOD LUCK!
