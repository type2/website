I have never found any Taiwan tin that is anywhere near as good of quality
as the original VW tin.  I've spent a lot of hours brazing up original tin
when I could have just bought new, because I didn't want to go through the
hassles of modifying the new stuff to fit.

One of the worst problems I've found with taiwan cylinder tin is that they
lack the little dents that allow the flap levers to swing.  I wonder how
many engines have been fried by folks who didn't check their flap travel?

Your experience seems to reinforce my general negative feelings about GEX.
You've done more work on this one than if you had simply bought a
longblock and parts.  Slapdash work and cheap parts seems to be the norm
for them.
