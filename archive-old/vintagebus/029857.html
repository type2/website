35psi is normal and will ride the best. 65psi is a maximum. If you are
carring a very heavy load allows you to bring them up a little. Normal
driving with anything over 40 will be a very hard ride. You will feel
every bump and road imperfection magnified. Although on the rear tires
of my old baywindow I ran 40psi because of the heavier engine and it
seemed fine. Keep it at 35psi.
