what I ended up useing for 15" tires were Cooper Achiver (?) 205/75/15, the
reason I went with these was because they are rated a SUV tire and have 2
ply side walls where as most tires of this size have 1 ply sidewalls, I had
these on the side loader going to Jerome (fully loaded) and I couldn't
notice any sway from the side walls on these going around all the curves
that we encountered on the trip up. So after all the 15 inch tire discussion
it all boiled down to a SUV rated tire which has stiffer sidewalls then most
passenger car tires, there are many brands out there like this I soon
discovered so the tire thread can END here and now, these are the facts!

ronnie

ps: I think if I recall the load rating was over 1500 lbs per tire.
