Subject: RE: [VB] Quick help need on reproduction round mirrors

First generation repo have a sharpe edge where the mirror goes in and will
cut your finger if not carefull, discontinued, these also have a brushed
finish.

Second generation have a rolled edge with a piece of rubber holding in the
mirror.

Third generation are just like OG, I have not seen these but did talk to the
manufacturer and they are being made exclussively for WW.

Izzys and Flat Fours are polished stainless. Flat Four has there name
stamped in them and IZZYS has the VW part number.

There are also a couple other companies that I have found made them (long
ago) and look like VW's but without the part number. I guess one day I can
take pictures of them all and show the differences.
