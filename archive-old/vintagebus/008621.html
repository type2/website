Just a note the SO 34 camper came from the factory without the section
behind the seats, this camper used the swivel seat body which was
manufactured without the bulkhead.  Check the M-codes on the camper and you
will see it says swivel seat-
M-code 199 (swivel seat) which comes from m-code 361 which comes from M-code
367.
