I put a fram racing filter on with about 3 feet of line. I didnt know how 
much oil to add either. What I did was put the required amount of oil in 
then about 1/2 quart more. Then I disconnected the coil and turned over the 
engine with starter. It wont start of course but will push oil. I did this 
in short bursts, checking and adding oil as I went. When no more oil was 
needed I finally hooked up the coil and fired it up.
