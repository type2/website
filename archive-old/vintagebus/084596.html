Was writ by matt:

You have a couple of options besides online ordering if the above doesn't
pan out.  You can put a lug on _both_ ends of the battery cable.  Your FLAPS
will sell a battery terminal clamp that has a stud and wing nut on it.  Put
your lug over the stud and replace the wing nut with a nyloc nut and a
washer and it's a reasonable battery terminal.  Note that some of these have
a small raised area of lead around the stud - see how much contact you're
actually getting between the lead and the lug.  If it's not much, you might
sand or file down the lead to provide a wide, flat surface for the lug to
sit on.
