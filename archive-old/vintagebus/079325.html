Subject: Re: [VB] pully wheel problems

you mean the crank pulley, right?    Eric of Eric&BarbVeewee111  gave me a 
good tip about sanding the crank pulley so it is a push fit, so you can easily 
pully it off.  So push it on part way, then pull it off and sand the scratches, 
rinse, repeat till you can push it on.   Not wiggly loose, but if you have to 
crank or whack the lower pulley on, you'll be hating life if you have to pull 
your oil pump on the side of the road.

the torque specs for the bolt are in Muir's idiot book
