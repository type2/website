The brand new engines from Mexico and Brazil the heads are torgued to 16 lbs
(according to my wrench). If you have OG studs (8mm) torque them to 18 lbs,
if you have aftermarket studs, throw them in the trash and find OG ones, the
aftermarket ones seem to stretch and/or break at 18 ft lbs.
