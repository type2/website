Tried the bug conversion and it works but makes for a real spongy pedal. The
best bet in my OPINION is to step up and buy the 67 master. CORRECT part for
a early bus. I am still not sold on putting a disc brake master on a drum
brake car. It doesn't work on dune buggys or bajas, so I still can't figure
out how it can work properly on a 4 drum brakle bus. On the buggys it locks
up the front brakes prematurely. The reason it doesn't on the bus is probaly
due to the weight, but what happens in a panic stop on a slick road?
