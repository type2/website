Is the coil you have internal ballast resister? or external???
if you do not have the ballast resister you will prematurly burn out points
and/or condenser. The Bosche blue coil being sold by most retailers are not
internally ballast resisted. The Original Blue coils are (but availability
is spotty and the prices are rising) buy them while you can!!!!! The new
Bosche (so called blue coil) are now silver with a large Blue decal these
are internal resisted and are fair priced but do not come with the coil
bracket.
