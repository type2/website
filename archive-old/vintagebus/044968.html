hm, I never but never have wheels off the ground unless I need them off.   I 
recommend putting the back ones back on while you work on the front.   have 
all 4 on the ground when you break the nts loose, and remove one rear at a 
time to do brakes, it is easier to pull the drum off with the rim attached I 
feel.   Safety.  wheels are sturdier than jackstands.

You should have a kingpin suspension.  Read your idiot book by muir. it has 
pictures and expanations.   if you dont have one get it.  the bentley is nice 
too.  read the idiot book section- it shows what  everything is.  what do you 
need to fix?  well, if it aint broke dont fix it.  un the wiggle and look 
test to see what is worn (a friend to wigggle the  steering  wheel is 
helpful)     Grease everything till new clean grease comes out, and dont 
forget 4 fittings each spindle.

how much play is there in the steering wheel?   a few inches is ok, a loose 
spot a third of hthe wheel turn is scary.  where is the play at.
