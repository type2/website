> What do you do when they're too close for comfort, especially at speed?

If you are not in the slow lane then move to the slow lane.  If you are or
if it is a two-lane road or a lane then slow gradually and pull off as

suggested is that there is a large vacuum area in the wake of a bus and
"what goes around comes around" very quickly.  Do not eject anything that
you cannot live with yourself.
