  When I got my 15 window deluxe from Kansas I had a problem with the
paperwork. MVD here needed something, I don't remember why, so I had to
write to Kansas DMV and get the history on it from them. What I got was a
list and copy of every registered owner on the bus since it was in Kansas.
It appeared to be have lived it's whole life there and one of the first
owners had bought it back at one time in it's life. It was a cleaners bus
that was sold then bought back by the same cleaners. Even though it went
through a few owners the original logos had never been painted over or
removed.
