Are you sure it's the sender?   easy test:
 un plug wire from push on connector on fuel sender (on top of tank, you are 
playing twister with th engine) and ground that wire to the body.   Secure it 
with an alligator clip.  go to the front an turn the key to on.  The guage 
should read full.  If not, the wiring and/or gauge are bad.  

I had to replace both sender and guage in my 65 to get it working.   Some say 
you need a 12V guage, but I've seen 6V ones work on 12V so I dunno.
