Bentley reprinted the 63 edition of the VW transporter workshop manual, which
covers 63-67 buses.     Thereis a 59 edition, covers 59 through 62, which
perhaps you can find in the original.      But the 63 edition covers quite a
lot for your 61-  front and rear suspension, brakes,   all kinds of neat
things like how the cab door window frames come apart.   The body section is
pretty cool.   Get it.
