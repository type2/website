"time serts" are thin wall inserts. i use them 
regularyl and have not had any problems with them. i've used them in reduction 
boxes, again with no troubles and the 24mm length insert. my catalog does not 
show a 30mm length 12X1.5, but the part number would make sense, as it would be 
the next sequential number in the series.

a "BIG-SERT" is a "heavy wall solid bushing" "will 
repair oversize holes which have been previously repaired". they install the 
same way as time-serts, but it has a locking cam pin. "back out of the insert is 
prevented by the cam lock effect of the pin" the kits are only a few $$ more 
than regular time serts, but i have not seen nor used the big-serts. seems like 
it may be a little overkill, especially in a tight area.

anyway, for those interested, time-sert company's 
phone-- 1-800-423-4070 or www.timesert_dot_com .
