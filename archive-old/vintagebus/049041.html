There's a channel down the left and right side of the windshield posts, no drilling required. 3/8" holes there already. Probably down the center too. Borrow a fish tape from any computer geek, or run a coat hanger down the hole. When you pull your current (pun intended) wire, pull a piece of string for the next set. I did this to mount some surface mount tweeters up above.  Get a couple 12v muffin fans off a dead computer and epoxy them to the plate for really cheap ambulance fans. Probably quieter too.
