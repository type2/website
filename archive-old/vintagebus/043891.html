I changed the changed the cv boots on an 86 gti the inner was still okay
so all i had to change was the outer.  All i did once i pulled the shaft
was use a ball pin hamer and a large screw driver to work the metal slip
joint loose. Once loose I picked up the shaft and let gravity help free
the joint by tapping the joint some more.  Once the axel was split i
actually used a GM universal boot( roughly 12$) to replace the factory
one.  THe only mistake I made in puutting things back together is I left
off the very thin wassher that goes on the shaft before the axel joint.  
I knew the job was going to easy and thats when I was the washer I left
off.  Without the thin washer it was ten times more difficult to get the
screw driver inserted between the joint male the lower part of the male
shaft.
