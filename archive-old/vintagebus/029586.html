The bolts are 12mm x 1.5 pitch and are 1.5" long, according to ETKA.  You would
need 8 of these to bolt the reduction boxes to the spring plates.

the studs sticking out of the front mount are 10mm and the nuts that have a 15mm
head are the easiest to use, you need 2 or 4 of those.  The 2 that go from the
mount to the nose cone were originally 14mm head, but they are nearly impossible
to find.

The bolts, nuts and washers for the STOCK cradle to the cradle mounts are 8mm x
10mm, don't forget lock washers.  It sucks to pull the motor to tighten these.

Extra 8mm nuts (13mm head size) are a must.

That would be enough to at least be able to roll it.

As far as locating the mounts, they are available at almost any FLAPS.  Where is
So Cal?
