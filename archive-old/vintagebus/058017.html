did you remove the front shift rod? if not, the 
reamer probably did not cut all the way through the top bushing. the 
reamer doesn't cut until about 20mm or so down it, so if you are hitting the rod 
when reaming it, chances are you're not getting a complete cut.

remove the rod and try reaming it 
again.
