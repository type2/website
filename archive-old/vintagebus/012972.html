Aussie busses have the vents on top because, in essence, they drive
upside-down.  Computer monitors for the southern hemisphere are also
different I found out, something to do with the magnetic alignment.

If it was an Aussie bus, the upper vents would be the same size as the lower
vents, with two "columns" of them, or one extra-wide column..

Bench panels simply had 3 or 4 small vents - less than half the size
of the standard, below-the-belt vents.

Actually the Aussie vent arrangement is so they could pull in air that was
less  dusty.
