Stock Westy config is to use special "T" bolts with
the top of the T on the floor inserted into the seat
slots. Length of this bolt is long enough to extend
from the floor up through metric equivalent of .5"
plywood plus the thickness of the vinyl flooring and
the thickness of the base of the Z-bed base cabinet.

Yes, use washers similar to fender washers with innner
diameter holes to accomodate diameter of the T bolts.
Seat belts in the back were mounted via stock mounting
holes/nuts welded as part of the body.
Use caution if thinking about drilling holes through
the rear most quasi-vertical surface by the seat back.
**Remember that there is a gas tank right on the
otherside of that surface (give or take a minor
distance)!!!**
