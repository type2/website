I just finished my lunch and almost had to re-eat it after reading this. You 
might want to trade this out for a less rare bus, rather
than begin cutting and permanently ruining the body. If you are intent on 
switching a V-8 into a bus, and cutting it up doesn't 
turn your stomach, a good switch would be to a 472 Cadillac motor. It would 
have to sit in the cargo area, and you're rear wheels
would definitely extend outboard of the wheelwells. The other thing to think 
about would be checking your link/king pins in the 
front end, and putting many hundred pounds of counter weights over the front 
axle to keep it from becoming airborn when you 
took off at a light.
