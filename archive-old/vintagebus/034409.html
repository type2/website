and
(a)sitting inside the centre chassis sections,
(b) inside the outriggers and 
(c) trapped between the outer sills, inner rockers and floor

Not just between the floor and belly pans - and then it gets
wet and your blasted and nicely painted (on the outside)
bus rots from within inside two years.

I hate sandblasting (OK for components but).  
If you do it I believe you have to be
ready to open ALL these sections to get the stuff out.
Plastic media blasting is better (but slower) and can still
sit in there and block the 'normal' water egress points
in the chassis.  I'd only blast a basket case bus BEFORE
a 'complete' metal replacement job where all these sections
will be opened and/or replaced!

Sand blasting and seat belts - both get me sorta excited!
