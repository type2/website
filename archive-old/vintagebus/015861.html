On the driver's side, the trick is to get the special nut that gets
embedded in the case, so you screw the bolt in from underneat.
If you can find a long-enough allen bolt, you're in phat city.

On the passenger side, it shouldn't be an issue.  If I can get my hands
in there, anyone can...

Also, I weld a tab onto the bolt head, so it doesn't spin.  There's
special  half-moon heads, but I find that most of the time they 
aren't large enuf.
