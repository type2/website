Hi Jon,
This material by itself will definitely create rust in your bus.  Unless you
are planning to only drive it to shows and keep it in a heated garage all
other times or giving up breathing which is how most moisture gets into the VW
interior.  Take it out now!  We got our panel with indoor/outdoor carpet glued
to the inside of the ceiling.  This the stuff that looks like felt and has a
thin foam rubber base.  Total thickness about 1/8".  The next thing we knew
this material was falling down and releasing a rain of rust on us.  Have been
fighting the rust for five years and slowly winning.  Once the rust gets
between sheet metal, into cracks and corners it is a pain to completely clean
out!  Plus the added joy of trying to work overhead, getting it into your eyes
and coated throughly.  Eric & Barb
