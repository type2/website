Actually, I really like using the 373A heads for the 15/1600 jugs.  Had a
1600 installed in a 59 Single Cab I once had when I hauled around the
window rubber and parts to all the shows that I had 373As installed.  A
real torquer engine going up the Grapevine outside of LA.  373As have a
smaller (40hp) exhaust valve verses the 311-373 head's larger one.

the same OD as 15 & 1600.  The IDs are different (77 for 1300, 83 for 1500,
85.5 for 1600).
