There were Dormobile conversions on VW vans that were "full" Dormobile
conversions.  All interior arrangements were installed by Dormobile in
England and they had the "Patented Martin Walter folding roof".

There were also Westfalia converted vans that were equipped with the Martin
Walter/Dormobile folding roof, installed by Westfalia in Germany under
license from Dormobile.

There were Westfalia conversions that had a Dormobile folding roof installed
in England, either arranged by the English dealer pre-sale or by the new
owner post-sale.

And probably an infinite number of variations on these primary themes.
