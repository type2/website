The crankstart was used all the way thru the end of
the 36hp engines on the buses which was mid `59 when
the bastard engine started.  So yes all early 36hp
engined `59s had crankstart equipment....and for the
next yet asked question...yes there was also a longer
crank start handle as with the newer style bumpers the
crank handle had to be longer to clear the wider new
style `59 bumper.

I have seen and owned a couple of these buses.  It is
also verified in my `50-`67 VW factory parts manual.
