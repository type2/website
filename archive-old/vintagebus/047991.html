Hi Bill,
Here is the info from our bus parts manual.  
Wheel Discs
211 601 023 A  Wheel disc 3.5 DX16  Up to Chassi No. 20-117 901.
211 601 027 E  Wheel disc 4.5 KX15  From Chassi No. 20-117 902 up to 1 219 510
211 601 027 F   Wheel disc 5 JKX14 Up to Chassi No. 1 219 510   M215 option  
Used from somewhere in early 1963 production, 1 ton payload and 1500cc engine 
option.  This dics size was used standard from Chassi No. 1 219 511.

So it looks like your bus came with 15" rims unless it has the M215 option 
code on it's front ID plate.  M215 parts have a "D" after the part number for 
the one year only pieces of this suspension package.  You are welcome,
