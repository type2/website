If you look on the back of the speedo you will see a stamped number
that isn't the part number and isn't the manufacture date. Mine is
0.77, which means calibrated (before I screwed it up) for 15" wheels.
There also exists a 0.8 for 14" wheels. Putting one of these 14"
calibrated speedometers on your bus with the small 15" might give you
a reading "closer" to the "truth". But knowing how to adjust the
reading would be better. Clearly it can be changed because it seems I
did it when I didn't mean to...
