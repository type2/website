Luckily,

You can replace the apron and lid fairly easily/cheaply - those are  
the parts usually people have a hard time selling, so prices are low.

worked properly. Lenses are also cheap and difficult to sell.

or so years before I took my bus off the road. I plan to straighten  
the section out with a stud welder and dolly/hammer rather than  
cutting it out. Your damage looks much less than mine was (is).
