Before I shoot myself in the foot, I'll amend this...

You are in Michigan, so I better qualify:

Little rust:  I'm thinking one replaceable patch of rust.  1) the battery
tray is gone but a square that is easily replaced (none of the side walls).
2) one of the cab floor panels (passenger or driver) is gone and is a square
patch easily replaced.  3) Surface rust in cab.  NO bubbles or other more
serious rust.

Again, only ONE of the above problems -- otherwise I would not say "little
rust".  Anything short of a full accounting of ALL RUST AREAS with COMPLETE
descriptions is almost worthless.

Also -- the values below assume this is COMPLETE and original shape.  No
cuts in roof, no cut fenders, no cut engine compartments.  No cuts in dash.
No rats nest of wires.  All gates there without dents or rust (see rust
above!).
