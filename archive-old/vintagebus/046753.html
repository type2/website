When I got my 61 it had no side view mirrors.  My boyfriend at the time found
this annoying and drilled out the drivers side round topped pin to put an
elephant ear on it.    Some years later, the door gets munched, and I replace
the drivers door, now it has a broken off mirror arm there, which ould not
budge with normal mean (penetrating oil, vice grips, rubber mallets etc)  .
So I drilled that out carefully to try not to round out the pin hole.
Switched the mirror over.    Easier than switching hinges.
