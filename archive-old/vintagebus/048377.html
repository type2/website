Best to have a 200mm 6 volt geared FW and a 9 spring pressure plate installed 
on your engine.  If you are going to run 12 volt system in your bus just have 
a 12 volt solinoid installed on the 6 volt starter.  We have run 6 volt 
starters on 12 volt without problems for many years and it really starts up 
quickly even in winter weather.  Eric&Barb
