Just happens that I used my jet-set from CB Performance (CB=Claude's Buggies) last night on my baby Dells on the DC. The guages are German, but have plastic holders for the metal guage. They are only 1/4" long and won't fit into the air jets I have for the larger Dells. The reamers are tapered so don't have size markings, that's what the guages are for. Guess I could take a picture of 'em and post on our favorite web sites. I did no comparison shopping, maybe you should. 

Karl stopped by even though he's sick, and had words of wisdom as always .  My mains were 1.28 and about 1.31, so I opened them both to 1.50. My 1835 is fine in flat Fresno, but bogs down when I escape this huge valley. 
Thinking about it now, I should have pulled the jets out of the plugs to ream from the other side so the taper went smaller towards the carb throat instead of larger.
