Eric&Barb, and the guy with the suspected ambulance fan switch,

Try http://babelfish.altavista.digital_dot_com/ for translations.

From the picture on http://www.freepichosting_dot_com/Gallery/Show.now?620
there is a Hella logo molded into the switch.  If there's a part
number too why not ask Messrs. Hella?  :)

If there are terminal designations molded into the plastic as well,
this may give some clue as to the function.  Or, map out the switching
action with an ohm-meter and see what you get.  Probably the two
"little" screw terminals are the bulb and the others are the contacts.

Just from looking at the picture of the switch and the Bentley manual,
this one almost has too many terminals to be an ambulance fan switch.
Doesn't mean it couldn't be wired and used for one, though.
