I've had this problem before, and I'm not sure there's an easy solution.

this about 5 times!  The oil was still coing out black.  I even
reached in and dug the gunk out with my fingers, which only helped a little
bit.

I don't know how effective they are - I tried a can of some stuff and it
didn't do much for me.
