<< any tips on how to determine if the system is working 'correctly?'  >>

Buy an oil temp and pressure gauges.  Install them and monitor the reading 
and check to make sure they stay between 160-210F and 10-50 PSI when the 
engine is warmed up.  Oil pressure over 80 PSI at any time will not only risk 
oil cooler blow out, but possible distortion of the cooler in such a way as 
to constrict or even block the cooling air from going through the cooler!
