It could really be installed in any bus, but if you look closer, you will
see that the body brackets have 3 holes instead of the standard one hole
found on 60 down busses.  To install this two year only seat properly you
would need to cut out these 3-hole brakets and weld them into an earlier
bus.  So, if my 61 didnt come with this seat it came with one just like it
as the paint is original near these 3 hole brackets!

Also, if you look close at the picture you can see a curved pipe behind
the seat welded to the base that the bakrest rests against.
