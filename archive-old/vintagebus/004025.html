Just got back from Pomona. Perused the general show vendor
area after I finished with the VW area. Found a booth
selling rust removers, preventers and polishes. Well, like a
fool, I purchased a quart of the rust remover and tried it
immediately when I got home. WOW. Sprayed some on a vertical
surface that had a solid but light coat of rust. 30  minutes
later I brushed and washed with water and ALL the rust was
gone. Only a clean shiny steel surface remained. Then coated
the surface with Ras-O-Nil to prevent further rusting. The
remover allows the flash rust to form when washed off.

The vendor is called Blue Lightning products, 2094 W.
Redlands Blvd, Redlands, CA  92723. Rust Remover list price
is $14, show special $13 no tax for a quart. A little goes a
long ways, sort of like Ras-O-Nil. The phone number on the
order sheet is 800-501-1562, email
blulightning_at_earthlink_dot_net. No web site listed.  The
shipping is +27 percent of order.  Thats only $3.78 on a $14
order.

I'll be using a lot of this stuff rather than blasting.

Also saw Ronnie and many others. Lots of bargains, Lots of
