Just as a comparison, the gas furnace in my house is rated at
100,000 BTU/hr "input" (not quite sure what "input" means) and has to
heat around 14,400 ft^3 (408 m^3), or about 245 BTU/hr per m^3.  A Bus
has an interior volume of around 6.5 m^3, so the 15,000-20,000 BTU/hr
heaters are putting out something like 2300 to 3100 BTU/hr per m^3 -
around ten times as much as the house furnace.  OTOH, my house doesn't
often travel at 60 MPH with leaky gaskets.  :)
