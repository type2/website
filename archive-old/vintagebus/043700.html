My first guess would be that a poor connection under the dash somewhere
downstream of the ignition switch is causing a voltage drop when the
brake lights come on.  You might measure the voltage between the + 
terminal (black wire) at the gas gauge and body ground, to see what
it does when you step on the brakes.  If it drops quite a bit when
you step on the brakes, then you probably have a bad connection
someplace.  I'd look for a bad connection at the second-from-the-right
fuse in the fuse block first and then chase it further if required.
