Hmmm, sounds like the problems I tend to have.
Check idle circuit for clogging, jet for looseness. Check jets for too big
main or incorret idle jet.
Check fuel pressure. 3-3.5psi should be good.
Check point seating and dwell.
Check plugs, fouled, gap, tight, etc.
Check for Vac leaks (especially around those DP boots and intake manifolds)
this is #1.
