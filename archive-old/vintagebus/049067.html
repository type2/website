Great show, more cars and vendors than before. Listees that I saw, Matt & Katie, AuFever (Karl), and Nate the sprinkler man. Only two T2 between us though, major case leak in Nate's camper kept it at home and M&K had the wasserpumper. Notable busses, a RHD from New Hampshire in great shape except for rockers, had a right side washer bottle too!. Also a beautiful 21 Window from San Jose, very nice stock resto.  A few busses had bay era middle seats, Karl sold one easily for $40 including hold-downs.  My '63 DC won 3rd place in the '68 and later Type 2 custom class! Not easy to do unless an organizer puts you in the wrong category. Guess how many entries the wrong class had?

He's seen my DC downtown, and we struck a deal if the measurements were the same. I measured my bed and came back to measure his and 3 people had offered $50 over his asking price. He honored our deal and another VW link started. 

The hoops are short! According to the seller there was an option for cab-high tilts, probably offered post '67. This is fine with me, I like the lower look and better aerodynamics. Now to get them blasted and powdered and some canvas.
