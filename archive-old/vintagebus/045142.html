Assuming that when you write "carb is a 009" that you mean it is the 
distributor.  Plus if you are running a stock muffler and exhuast the 
following jets should be pretty close to what you need.
Main jet 112.5
Air correction jet with emulsion tube 140z
pilot jet  65
Idling air jet drilling  135
Auxiliary fuel jet  45
Auxiliary fuel jet drilling 130
Bypass air cut-off valve  1.8 (Could this be the forward idling jet you have?)
Power fuel jet  100
