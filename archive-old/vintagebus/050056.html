Okay, this happens a lot and recourse is extensively documented (although
not well collated) so get yourself in a positive frame of mind because you
WILL prevail here if you merely have the tenacity to do so.

First, I'd go to the type2 search engine which lives here:

	http://vw.i405_dot_com/

and I would search for terms such as "totalled", along with various likely
misspellings of that word. "Insurance" is probably too broad to do you any
good. I'm getting 118 hits with "totalled" and I see some threads I
recognize that address your problem.

If that doesn't work, post to the type2 list about it and people may send
you more information. In particular the $1475 figure for a 69 Westy in
decent shape in the Northeast is preposterous, I'm sure we can come up with
any number of people with credentials who will dispute it in writing.

Another thing you can do is cruise eBay and the type2_dot_com BuySell site and
see what people are asking for vans like yours in like condition.

The insurance company is counting on the prospect that you will become
disgusted and quit trying. Don't lose your temper but don't ever stop
trying. If you don't, they eventually will.
