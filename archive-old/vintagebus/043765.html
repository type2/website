After a few responses along these lines I had the brilliant idea to
check the parts book.  The barndoor book doesn't seem to show it, but
the '58-'67 book does:

http://vintagebus_dot_com/docs/5867part/099.png   (diagram)   callout #60
http://vintagebus_dot_com/docs/5867part/105.png   (list)

The listing says: "311 127 525  Clamp-Fuel line  from engine 3 400 000"
so anything post-36hp should have it.

Even though I got my fuel line to "float" in space, it does come pretty
close to the left side of the fan shroud.  Having a clamp there makes
sense.
