Ever heard of a Preluber?  This is an electric oil pump which starts the
flow of oil to the engine, coolers and such, before the key is even cranked.

If you had a preluber installed, oil changes are a snap... open the valve
and allow the oil to drain in a container.  To fill, use the normal means,
or another hose to the preluber.

Installed one on the Hummer... don't quite know how I would do such a thing
with my bus.
