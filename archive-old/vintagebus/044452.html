Wow, I guess EB has hit the big time! For those of you just joining 
us, EveryBus is April 20-22 at Hagen Stone park in Greensboro, NC. 
The event is open to all VW Transporters. Among the general camping 
fun there will be a chilli cooking contest, door prizes, and a 
caravan to the IVWA swap meet in Farmington, NC on Sunday. Camping is 
$12 per nite per bus. Hook ups are available on a first come first 
serve basis - about 35 sites with water and electric are available.

more details visit the website at http://www.mindspring_dot_com/~ncbus/ 
or email me.

Thanks and see you there:)
