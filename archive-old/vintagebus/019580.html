More then likely you can't shim it...the end play is
set to give play between the flywheel and number 1
thrust main bearing...the extra wear-slop play folks
usually see is that number 1 main bearing loose in the
case....thus any extra shimming cannot properly
correct this.  The case needs to be machined foe
oversize thrust at number 1 main bearing...
