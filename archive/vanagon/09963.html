really dropped my city mileage waaaaaaaaay down. all the red lights didn't
help much either.

about the only thing you can do to get better city driving is to be lighter
on the throttle, get into as high a gear as is reasonable (for the traffic,
weather, and terrain), and try to judge the red lights so you don't have to
stop so often. and make as few short trips as possible.
