Hi Karen,

I strongly suggest that you do remove the fridge.  It is not hard to do, just
a little awkward, but not too bad.  I did it recently and intended to clean
up in the compartment in back.  As it turned out, it was only dusty.  I would
presume that yours may have something in the fins that might be getting
hot (hopefully not wiring).  Or there might be something getting hot just
sitting next to the fridge.  But get behind there and have a look.  Well
worth the effort IMO.
