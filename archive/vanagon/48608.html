Hi Scott,

Trakka is an Australian company and they have been doing 'up market'
conversions on a number of vehicles since the early 70s (not just
VWs)...They're main competitior in the VW was 'Sunliner' who did (think
they're out of business now?) a cheaper more traditional westie like
conversion on breadloaf vans.....

The process of trying to get a Trakka Eurovan into the US is probably too
difficult and costy for most - basically I imagine a US model would have to
be shipped to Australia, converted then imported to the US - it would cost
a fortune (the Trakka conversion is not cheap in the first place).....

The most logical thing would be for some enterprising soul to set up a
Trakka 'franchise' here in the US and build 'em.........
