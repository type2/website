  U-haul tow dolys work great.  I would leave the rear wheels on the ground
for balance.  I once towed front end down and it was a bit unstable. Remove
the rear drive shafts if needed,(auto matic or trans not supported).   Make
sure the surge brakes on the dolly are working.  They actually tow quite easy
this way.
