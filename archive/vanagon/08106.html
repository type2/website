Milky? That does not sound like water. As a pilot I routinely draw samples
of gas out of tanks to check for water, and often see it. But it appears,
in a clear container filled primarily with gas, on the bottom as a clear
"bubble", perhaps containing rust or other contaminants. Of the hundreds
of times I have seen water in gasoline, it has never appeared as "milky".
Did anyone use a sealant on your tank? An oil/water emulsion might appear
milky...I guess.
