Before you get case savers you may want to consider a heli-coil repair kit.
I don't remember how expensive they are, I know they ain't cheap. But if
you have an eight mm hole that is stripped the heli-coil tap will start
right in the stripped hole, no drilling necessary. Install the heli-coil
and you have a very perminent repair.
