As for "hesitation", "smoothness", etc., I've never driven any Vanagon
that seemed to accelerate really evenly, especially not the one I have
now.  My '89 Syncro always seems to have these slight plateaus in the
power curve as it accelerates, and on deceleration it often "oscillates"
(surges/lags), especially if I take my foot off the gas suddenly.  In
addition, it's quite hard to hold a constant speed in 1st or 2nd gear
around 3000rpm - it really seems to want to either speed up or slow
down.

Is this what they refer to as "driveability"?  An experienced VW
mechanic who's driven it pronounced it normal for a Vanagon, but this
unevenness is the one real dissatisfaction I have with the car.  I'd be
willing to go to some lengths to fix it, but I'm not sure if it can
really be improved.  Do other people experience these effects?
