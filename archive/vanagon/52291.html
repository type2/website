I've been on a lot of 4x4 trails including a lot of sandy river beds, but
haven't once ventured onto (into??) any dunes.  The dunes I'm familiar with
are very soft shifting sand and I suspect a Vanagon Syncro would fare poorly.
 The Syncro has quite a bit of weight and a relatively small tire contact
patch - a combination that I believe couldn't handle dunes.

On the other hand, I'd love to see someone else try it and report back to the
list what happens.  I'd suggest bringing along a "real" dune vehicle and a
winch!
