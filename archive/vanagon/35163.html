If you are not leaking it, you must be burning it.  Oil burners can pass smog
tests beleive it or not.  Let it break in further.  I would be concerned
about a broken piston ring or valve guides not properly fitted.  Especially
the intakes.  Make sure you are not over filling the crankcase.  Keep level
between the marks.  Never go above the top fill mark.
