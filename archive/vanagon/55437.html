> Will a GTI engine mate up to the Vanagon bellhousing w/o modification or
> adapters? Will the engine compartment hold that engine w/o major mods?

Yes, with the following from a diesel Vanagon - oil pan, engine 
mounts, bellhousing, oil filler, alternator bracket (I think). Not 
sure about exhaust, maybe custom fab something using a dual outlet 
GTI manifold, or one from a Fox. The stock diesel is single outlet.
Also you want to look for early GTI engine without knock sensor 
ignition, ie: with CIS injection, unless you want to swap over the 
wiring harness, ecu, etc.
