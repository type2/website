Here's some suggested items to bring/pack for Red Rock:

water water water!
propane
cooking stoves
D-cell et al batteries
LOTS of firewood, kindling, charcoal, skewers, starter, newspaper
matches 
stoves, pots, utensils - if in doubt, BRING IT!
sunscreen
sunglasses
light sources:  candles, lanterns, etc.
snacks:  popcorn, appetizers, nuts, smores
beer, wine - bring lots
margarita and tequila stuff, plus sangria
food food food drink drink drink
music/guitars/harmonica/etc.
Emergency parts and tools
First Aid kit
Tarps and bungee cords
Rain/cold Gear - just in case, but it could also be quite warm
Camera and film
Fire Extinguisher
Bedding
Clothes and personal items
folding chairs and stools and tables
Hiking, fishing, biking, surfing, or boating gear + games, etc.
Autoclub, health insurance, and credit cards plus emergency $$
Proof of insurance, ownership, and registration
pet proof
cash
extra gas?
