OK are you ready for this?  33 is the magic number that you can use 
with the 16' setup. BUT will they fit under the body?
Here are the formulas, from 1-87 Four Wheeler Mag. and Dick Cepek:

   MPH x GR                  RPM x D                NEW D x OLD GR
 ------------ x 336 = RPM  -----------: 336 = GR   ---------------=NGR
       D                       MPH                      OLD D
       
Where MPH = miles per hour, GR = gear ratio, D = tire diameter,
RPM = engine revolutions per min, NGR = new gear ratio  
    
Using the 2nd formula for the stock tires I get 4.8595238, that 
looks close enough to 4.86 to me. (3768 RPM).
