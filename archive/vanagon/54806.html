You are correct on most of this, but you've obviously not heard or 
driven the new TDI!!. It is much MUCH quieter than any previous VW 
diesel, due mostly to a progressive fuel injection system, and sound 
absorbing engine cover. You forgot to mention that diesels are more 
efficient by definition, and direct injection moreso than indirect, 
meaning less burnt fuel per mile, meaning potentially less pollution 
to begin with.
Also, since a diesel will stay in tune MUCH longer than a gas engine, 
it is more likely to remain in a state of low emissions than a 
typical gas engine. This is of course less true with the latest gas 
engines, though.
