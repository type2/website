===

, comma  uh  pause  wait

? question (mark)  what  huh
< less  lesser  less than  waka  left point  left arrow
  from
> more  greater  greater than  waka  right point
  right arrow  gives
` one quote  start one quote  ping
| pipe  (vertical) bar  or
\ back slash  down ramp  unzip
: colon  dotdot  eyes
; semicolon  commadot  wink
' apostrophe  one quote  end one quote  tick  one tick
" quote  two quote (start or end)  ditto  two tick  double
