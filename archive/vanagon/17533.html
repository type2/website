Err, how much you say you paid for that gas, sound expensive, does he sell 
much???  I've bought bad gas, unfortunately several times in upstate NY, 
just over the Cdn border from us. It wiped out my '85 van really well, 
hesitation on the highway, some bucking, and then a steady drop in power 
with tons of misfiring. I seriously didn't think we would finish the trip.

regular engines real well. Chevron Techron gas treatment gets good reviews,
