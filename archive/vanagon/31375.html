OK Everybody,  I told you about my 84 Westfalia lurching and
hesitating, about 10% of the time when coming off idle, and only with
the O2 sensor hooked up. Recall I couldn't find the problem so I took
it into VW for true German diagnosis.  They fixed everything up for
me and the culprit was engine management settings that were off and a
collapsed crankcase breather hose. 

I PASSED AIRCARE!

Here are the results;
