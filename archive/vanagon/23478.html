> Had the slight humming noise in '87 Syncro in all gears. Went away with an 
> oil change. I don't know why, oil change was part of my refurbishing work. 
> Currently have a rattle in 3rd, due to too close clearance between shifter 
> and housing. I did this on purpose as it cured a binding/impossible to get 
> 1st. gear. There are slots in the ball carrier that are at 45 deg. which 
> allow slight adjustment. Try loosening the two 10mm nuts under the shift 
> boot. Slide your lever to the right slightly and road test. May affect your 
> shifting, check 1st gear! To reposition 'properly' make sure the 2 holes in 
> the carrier are above the holes in the mounting plate under it, you'll see!
