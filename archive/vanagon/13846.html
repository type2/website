     Ok Kombi lovers,
     
     the way I understand it is this, 
     
     
     Model         Features
     
     Van           Plain panel van without side windows or interior panels.
     
     Kombi         Van with 3 (per side) factory fitted side windows, but   
                   no interior panels in rear.
     
     Microbus      Kombi but with rear seats and interior panels ie. bus
     
     Deluxe Micro  Microbus with deluxe extras, (extra side window, clock   
                   etc.) and options of skylight, corner windows and        
                   sunroof.
