I just picked up a set of the Sears Trailhandler 195-75-14LTS 6-ply tires 
for DaBoat (gotta have good tires for the trip next May!) but I don't have 
any opinions about how they drive yet because my baby isn't feeling well
right now (still having carb troubles).  But so far, I can tell you
that the pair came to $146 and change, including tax, mounting, and a
lifetime balance.  The tread is aggressive, and the sidewalls are very
stiff.  They are also a *lot* heavier than the tires I had on there
before.  Nearly threw my back out lifting them into the car!  (Well,
not really...)
