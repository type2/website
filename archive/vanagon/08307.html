I think I've decided to name my Brick "Rosalie" after the rotund 
German-American housewife in the film "Rosalie Goes Shopping". Rosalie 
is played by Marianne Sagebrecht who was also the lead in "Bagdad Cafe"

Rosalie did drive a conversion van (Chevy) around her adopted hometown 
of Stuttgart, Arkansas, with Arkansas plates reading "CHARGEIT".

The film is a spoof on the Savings and Loan crisis and how one woman 
strikes back. --
