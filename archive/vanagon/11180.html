	When I worked on my 84 recently I had the same problem!

	Ultimately what worked was using 2 flat-head screwdrivers
	as levers.  I put them between the drum and the wheel
	"plate" (where all the brake components are attached to) and
	just pushed/pulled HARD! - It works after a while. Just
	try different positions: 12/6 o' clock, 9/3, etc

	good luck!
