Steve,

I did this and it is tough, the books say there is a spring (there is ) 
 and that you can fish a wire into the mechanism and release it and the 
 core will come out (you cant). I ended up pulling the column mount 
 with the switch in it and thene carefully grinding away the outer 
 portion until the whole assembly could be removed. I then could reach 
 the leaf spring and remove the cylinder. I have heard that you can 
 drill a hole through the mounting bracket and reach the release spring 
 but you would have to know exactly where to drill.

Good Luck
