Answered a while ago.  The tubes are the fuel vapor recovory vents from the
gas tank.  There was a carbon canister at the top or on the side of your
engine compartment close to where the tee is.  The lines go up the sides of
the engine compartment so that fuel can not siphon in to the compartment if
the tank gets over filled.
