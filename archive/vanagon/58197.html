The 10w-30 is too thin for a Vanagon engine.  See your owners manual for the 
chart.  Extended driving at high speeds worsens the problem.  I would use at 
least a 15-40 or 20-50 for Summer.  This should take care of that light unless 
your engine is worn or is running too hot.
