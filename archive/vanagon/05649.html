Sounds like yours is a prime candidate for the smog exemption.  Just pay
up to the limit (yours is probably ~ $50.00) to fix whatevery you can
and then you get a sticker that says you are exempt.  The gotcha is that
you'll have to pay this fee every time you get it smogged which is every
other year now in California I believe.  You can mooch smog parts from
wrecking yard vehicles and attach them to yours except, your heads 
won't accept the heat pump tubing that attaches to the heads.  

To be CA smog equipped you need the EGR valves (2) that attach to the
manifolds.  A heat pump (smog pump) Various hoses and anti-backfire
valve, EGR filter and tubing.  Pretty gruesome eh?  Well, it can
be done but someone else was trying to get the EGR valves for a '73
for a while.  Don't remember the end of the story.  From the dealer?
Forget it!  ;)
