If you are going to do a full blown cost analysis, then it never pays
go to the dealer unless you make (in my area) $45*(tax rate) does it?

it - my experience has been that people really undersell themselves on
what they can and cant do - quick cure for this is to sit in the dealer
(esp VW) and listen to the knuckles scraping on the ground as the 
mechanics 'walk' by.

info (this list  for example) most anything is covered.

between the two daily drivers with an hour after dinner, taking as money
(sorry, many) days as it takes to get the job done.

Costs me about $300 extra on insurance (thanks upstate NY) which I 
usually recover in about 2 or 3 months of NOT mechanics bills.
