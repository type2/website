Sean,

Wal-Mart sells a much better oil filter wrench than the similarly-shaped
aluminum one above.  (I've bought and tried several different types, with
disappointing results, until I found this one.)  It's black and made of
steel.  There should be a display of them so that you can take your oil
filter (FRAM PH2844 for a Type IV or 912/914 engine) and try it before you
buy it to make sure that it fits.  The wrench fits a 3/8" socket extension.

When loosening or tightening the filter, you'll need to clean the outside of
the filter and the inside of the wrench with a rag.  Tap the wrench onto the
filter gently with a hammer, making sure that it seats all around.  Then
insert the socket extension and tighten or loosen.
