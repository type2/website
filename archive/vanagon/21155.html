injection kit that would inject a mist into the carb throat on

1. higher mpg
2. carbon removal
3. cooler running, smoother running
4. reduced detonation

maybe this would help you air-cooleds, it seems like a perfect 
application.  yes, its dated technology but so are some VW engines.
