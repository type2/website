Captain,

She just won't take any more!

Get blushie checked before the trip I say, far easier than renting a U-haul
& tow dolly.

Do the valve thing, including pushing up and down on the retainers so you
can see if the guides are really up to the trip.  If you get alot of
movement in the valves, think carefully and/or drive very conservatively.
Do the end play check, push foward and pull backward on the crank pulley, it
should barely noticable (.006" or less).

If you've got the basics covered you should be OK, a tune-up and oil change
couldn't hurt, unless the mechanics a clown, always a possibility.

Carry a spare fan belt and the tools to change it, a fuel pump is sometimes
nice too.

Nice kitties, don't forget water.
