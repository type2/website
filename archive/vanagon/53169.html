Thanks to all that answered my high pitched sound!

The noise made me nervous and I did not want to drive it if it was something
major.   Due to the 24 hour+? delay in messages I called the Volkscafe (Peter)
and got a prompt response.

It was a one-way check value in the diff. lock vacuum system.  Since my diff
lock is currently stuck I just turned the valve around and the noise has
stopped.

Special thanks to Joel Walker who, hearing the frustration in my note, called
me at home (San Jose Calif.) from Alabama when he was unable to get a response
on the sick server.  It was great to talk to him!

My diff lock is very stuck. When I originally bought the Van I tried the
advise of a couple of list members and worked the shaft in and out with WD-40.
 After a couple of hours I could get it to move in and out, but only by prying
it out with a bar and pounding it back with a hammer.  Also when coming out it
would take the housing with it.  I just unlocked it and removed the actuator.

I was told you had to open the trans. to replace the lock.  I was quoted $400
and advised to wait until I had to do any other tranny work and just add this
on.

Is there an easy way to replace the locking assembly in the trans?  Also,
