I had a similar situation, where it would pull, then go straight.  I was 
told that it was due to the degradation inside the brake line rubber hoses, 
i.e. a swelling would constrict the fluid getting to the brakes.  A related 
problem was that it would then pull when I released the brake pedal until 
the fluid got back past the constriction.  Replaced the rubber brake lines - 
fixed it.
