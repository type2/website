Proper Tire Inflation for Even Wear

Equipment needed: One piece of chalk

1>Draw a line across the treads of each tire.
2>Drive a few feet.
3>Look at line.
4>If line is worn in the middle,let out some air then goto step one.
5>If line is worn on edges, add air then goto step one.
6>If line is worn evenly your are done!
