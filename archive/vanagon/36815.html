Subject: Head Cracks--Zyglo
Date: Mon, 4 Mar 96 14:20:26 PST

Your local metal shop specializing in alumium fine work will be able to get 
you a bit of zyglo.  It's used to highlight incipient cracks an stress risers
in non-ferrous materials.  In ferrous materials you can use a magnetic material
similar to magnasee.
