an easy way to tell right off the bat is that Kombi's were only painted
a single color, whereas passenger wagens were painted two-tone. Also, a
Kombi has no headliner in the cargo area, and no wall paneling there
also. My friend just got a really nice '64 Kombi, and it is just like
this. Another way to tell is to check the second number of your VIN. A 2
means passenger wagen, and something else is Kombi (I forget which)
