I've made the following measurements on my two vans.  The 87 has the
weekender rear seat, but not the table/armrest walls that the 88 Wolfsburg
(and Carat & MultiVan) has.

87 GL Syncro
Width across top of rear seat back: 59"
Width across front of rear seat base: 60.75"
Width wall-to-wall across top of rear seat base: 65.5"

88 GL Wolfburg
Width across top of rear seat back: 58.25"
Width across front of rear seat base: 57.25"
Width wall-to-wall across top of rear seat base: 61.5"

So, there appears to be a significant difference in the rear seats installed
in these models.  I don't think you could install the wider non-Wolfsburg
(non-Carat, non-MultiVan) seat into a van that has the table/armrest walls.
 Sorry for the bad news.
