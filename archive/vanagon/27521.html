Don't forget the 6-cylinder version of the VAG engine! It is being used in
the LT series trucks in Europe, and also in diesel form in the mid '80s
Volvo in NA.

The 4 and 5-cylinder engines have different face plates to my best knowledge,
so you can not just bolt up a 5 in place of the 4-cyl. The 5 and 6-cylinder
engines are identical.
