I ran an electric fuel pump conversion on my 72 Westy for many K miles
after the mechanical one failed due to the engine sleeve around the pump
actuator wearing to out-of-round and causing the rod to stick.  I was also
getting lotsa fuel dilution in the oil.  It was a small unit, only about 2
x 3 x 3 inches.  A dune buggy shop in San Diego installed it, so I can't
give you details on how to disable the mechanical pump.  But, it performed
just like the stock pump.  Maybe someone else has one currently.  My 72 is
gone, but not forgot.
