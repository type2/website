>The ring and pinion can be flopped to the other side, but I would not recommend
>it. If you were to look at the way the ring & pinion mate, and you were to move
>the R&P to the other side of the tranny, you have changed surfaces of the gears
>that will carry the load. This will work, don't get me wrong, but it will
>probably be a noisy beast.Just my $.02

The gears will still be driving in the same direction though, so the faces 
will mate the same way. I don't know the specifics of putting one of these 
boxes in a bus but I do know several people who have put them in Beetles. 
There is a slight clearance problem but that probably won't occur in the bus.
