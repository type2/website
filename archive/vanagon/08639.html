>Not exactly.  The fan is centered, yes, but the cylinders are staggered.
>Think about the crankshaft - the number 3 cylinder connects furthest
>towards the front of the motor, and is therefore a few inches farther 
>away from the fan than number one.  Also, the oil cooler "uses" some of
>the cooling air for the number 3 and 4 cylinders, so I would guess they
>run hotter than the right bank.  I wonder if they designed the fan
>housing to route a little more air to the left bank to compensate for
>that... hmmm... I'll have to check into that, but I bet Dave Kautz
>already knows.  :)

Thats why they came up with the "doghouse" shroud in '71, to compensate for 
this.  They also started using a fan that was 5mm wider.  I just put one
of these setups on my beastie.  Have to wait 'til its hot to see if it works,
though!
