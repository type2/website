<H2>EDS - Electronic Diagnosis System</H2>


The main aim of this research project is to combine a diagnostic expert system
with a trouble-shooting handbook. A pathfinder module puts the hypotheses
generated
by the diagnostic module into the context of related knowledge, so that the
human
operators can better verify the proposed solutions. This results in a very
flexible man-machine
co-operation. The approach has been used to support fault management for
the "Volkswagen Digifant" motor management system and a photovoltaic inverter.
