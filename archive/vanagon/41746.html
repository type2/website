if the clip is broken, your brakes may de - adjust themselves and you may
find yourself with brakes not working right, ie pulling, or needing to be
pumped.    This wont happen if the star is well stuck, which would keep it
from turning and deadjusting.   Also keep YOU from adjusting it.     I
personally like to take the stars off and grease them well, so next time i do
brake adjustment it is easy as pie.   

my advice is to either

1)   use stuck stars so they wont de adjust   (not so good choice)

2)  make or get clips and weld them on to the backing plate, or

3)  find a backing plate with good clips and change it over.
