the range of mechanical ability represented on the list - it makes us newbies fell like we can

airheads have the advantage that, well, if we try hard enough we can do just about everything
our vehicles need. Waterboxers scare me - and they probably scare a lot of other people from
getting in there and figuring out what's going on...

That doesn't mean that when I'm sitting in my sliding door on the side of the highway waiting
for Millie to cool down I don't dream about floating down the highway in a mint '90 Westy with
the a/c on... ;)
