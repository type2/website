Mileage - ref '87 2wd Vanagon, 98k miles; I've been getting a *very*
constant 21.8 _at_ 65mph, 19.4 around town since I bought her new.  This
declined ~.5 (1/2) mpg since the introduction of 'reformulated' fuel here in
Northern California.  In the winter we are forced by the aparatchiks in D.C.
to support Archer/Daniels/Midland (supermarket to the world) to buy fuel
that has a little 90 proof added - mileage goes down by *2* (two) mpg from
November through April !!  To give y'all an idea of how I drive, I still
have the original pads/shoes on the brakes (although I have to admit I have
a new set of rotors/pads for installation this spring - pads are down to ~1/8")

your carpet, & mount at all kinds of weird angles so they can be installed
