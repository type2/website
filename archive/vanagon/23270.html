Don,

I don't know if this wil be any help to you but...
I made my own screens for the sliding side windows on my '79 bus.
The materials used were just ordinary aluminum window screen
frame material, 90 degree plastic joints to join the frame pieces
and aluminum (or steel?) screen.

I cut four pieces for each window, joined them with the 90 degree
plastic elbows and laid the screen over that.  There's a little tool that 
looks kind of like a miniature pizza slicer that presses the screen into the
grooves in the frame with a round rubber bead going all around the frame.
I fastened the screen frames to the window frames with galvanized metal screws. 

It's been more than a year now, and many miles, and the screens haven't given 
me a bit of trouble.  If you can't use this for your Westfalia, maybe someone
else out there can use it for his or her non-Westy van to keep the buggers
and no-see-ums out.
