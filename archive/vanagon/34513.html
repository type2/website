there are only two major exhaust pipes that are unique to the Synchro.  The
fron engine pipe and the pipe leading into the CAT.  Although the tailpipe is
different, a standard pipe will work fine.  All the non-synchro pieces are
available aftermarket at about 40% off dealer price.  Beware the quality and
fitt are inferior but they will work. Same with the muffler.  I can get you
these pieces if needed.  I would try to negotiate with the dealer to give you
wholesale prices ~20% on large purchase.  If you plan to keep vehicle for a
while, this is best way to go.
