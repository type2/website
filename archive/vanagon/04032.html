I think this contraption is the same thing I saw in my local marine shop.
It is an electric cooler/heater. Looks like a coleman. It isn't any heavier
than a coleman either. Will keep things cold or hot! Plugs in to a 
cigarette lighter. Uses very little power (they say).

Pretty nice. This one even came with a slidein tray that allows you to
turn it on its side to make it work like a fridge. I think it cost a
$100. I contemplated ripping out the fridge in my camper and installing
one of these in its place. Might be more practical.
