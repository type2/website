 My experience with VW of Canada rebuilds is that they take quite a few 
 liberties with replacement parts. My 1.8 had type I cam bearings which
 eventally came apart <literally>, was fitted with the camshaft with the
 raised bands that indicate a cam for an auto trans <although the engine
 had a flywheel, not a flex plate> and was built on a 2 liter case <single 
 oil pressure relief instead of dual> 

 If you take a piston off of the connecting rod, you can see whether you
 have a 2 liter or not. Connecting rods for the 66mm stroke engines have 
 a balancing "nubbin" on the small end - 2 liter connecting rods have a
 normal radius.

 1.7 liter parts <and parts that didn't change as displacement went up> will
 have an 021 part number prefix. Parts unique to the 1.8 have an 022 prefix
 while parts unique to the 2 liter have another prefix which I don't know. 
 David Schwarze can probably tell us what the 2 liter prefix is since he
 has recently had 2 liter parts spread around his kitchen <appropriate use
 of a kitchen, IMHO, not shared by the wife, however>
