Actually, I don't think we have the same problems.  Yours is probably caused
by the glow plug relay, or the same as a problem (small one) I had some time
ago.

There is a wire which runs from the glow plug relay to a temp switch in a
collant line located just below the oil pressure switch (the front of the
head).  If this connection is loose, broken, whatever, the glow plugs will
stay on as long as the ignition is on.
