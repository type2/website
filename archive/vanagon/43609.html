the front doors are the same 64-67.   as long as the door handle has the push
button rather than the handle that pulls out, youre okay.   the 66-67 front
ddors are slightly different, actually, as they have a pull handle on the
inside, but that only affects the holes for the door panels clips for the
handles, are are perfectly fine for a 65.  deluxes came with the belt trim,
so if you find a door from a not deluxe, you'll have to drill holes to mount
the trim.  
(i have some spare doors - in western washington)

use a nose from a 62-67 bus.      one with the fried eggs turn signals. 

   the dash is different between models in that the deluxes came with a
clock.  also in 65 they got emergency lights.   but if you find say, a nice
63 kombi nose, use it and measure and cut holes for the clock & emergency
light switch.
