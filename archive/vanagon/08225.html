> 
> I installed H4's on an '84 Rabid GTI I had with good results;
> 
> MUCH improved lighting-the fluting on the lens does an excellent job
> of dispersing white light where you need it the most (immediately in
> front of the car, and off to the passenger side of the road, where the
> road signs typically are), and away from where you need it the least
> (oncoming drivers' eyeballs).
> 
> A couple of caveats-check to be sure your light switch/relay can handle
> the juice (if you opt for the 80/100W bulbs); my switch sort of, uhh,...
> well,... let's just say I had to replace it...
> I would probably recommend that you stay away from plastic lens covers
> that you can get; I ended up getting cracks in both lenses (didn't
> really affect performance, but still, it allows condensation w/i the 
> housing.)
> 
> BTW, when I had 'em, they cut through the tule fog on Hwy 99 and I-5 like
> a champ; I think you'll really like 'em, Thom.
