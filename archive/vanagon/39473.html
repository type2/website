I built a 1800cc high perf. upright for my '71 westy. The late Gene Berg

in a bus and the bigger bores will crack, warp, break, etc.  I TRUST him.
I used all new internal parts, balanced and blueprinted.  It works...WELL.

and expensive dellorto carbs.  Also higher flowing heads.  But most
importantly, get a NEW case, not an alignbored one.  I have NO confidence
in them and I see no reason to screw around on a project like this to save
a few hundred bucks.
