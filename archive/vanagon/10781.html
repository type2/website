It's for the optional clock, but I intalled a tachometer instead. Rocky

>2) Just under the said gauge hole, there is a red light thingy with a "B" on 
>it.  Anyone know what this is?

That should be the brake warning light. It should go on if one of your 2
brake circuits fails. It will also light up when the ignition is turned on
and will go out after the engine has been started.
