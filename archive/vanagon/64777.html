Quality Leader in Roof Coating Products". Offices in Twinsburg OH, 44087;
Clearwater, FL, 34622; and N. Las Vegas Nevada, 89031. I buy it at
Hechinger's a Mega-hardware store. Home Depot, Lowes, Builder's Empoium, or
whatever Mega-harware store you have nearby would probably have it, or call
Kool Seal.

For those who were seeking "Waxoil", the waxy rustproofing that VW sprayed in
to all the cracks and crevices, I have found it. 3M markets this product in
an aerosol spray (which dries to a beige, water-proof waxy goo). It is called
"Rust Preventative" and comes in a white and tan can for about $13.00 a can,
which held enough to do everything I could think of on Suzy's bus. I bought
it at a body-shop-supply store called Mattos, in Annandale, VA. Other
Auto-body stores that carry 3M products could get it.
