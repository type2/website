TYPE II LITERATURE AVAILABLE

The following collection of owner's manuals and other literature related to
Transporters is available from other VANAGON list members.  Most of these
manuals are out of print and hard to come by, and therefore the holders have
offered to make copies for the benefit of others.  Some of these manuals are
quite lengthy, so PLEASE:  no idle curiousity requests.  For the larger
repair type manuals, it might be advantageous to request certain sections
rather than the entire manual.  In any case, "contributions" to cover
postage and copying fees would probably be appropriate, or offers to trade
for literature you have.  Please contact Pat Walsh
<walshp_at_ippdsgi3.nawc-ad-indy.navy_dot_mil> for additions/deletions/corrections
to this list.  Thanks!

                                GAS HEATER MANUALS
                                --- ------ -------

"South Wind Heater Installation Instructions" for Transporter
     - dated 7-63, model 8332 (the instructions say they can be used for
       model 8333 - one is 6V, the latter is 12V).  10pp with line drawings
       and black and white photos.
       Joe Clark <joeclark_at_freenet.scri.fsu_dot_edu>

South Wind Service Manual for 1956-1969 Types I, II, and III
     - 32 photos of installations in all models (bw), 85 line drawings,
       50 pp.
       Joe Clark <joeclark_at_freenet.scri.fsu_dot_edu>

"Volkswagen 1961-1965 Sedan Heater Kit Model 8345-C" (also South Wind)
     - Installation, heater checkout, parts list, graphics as above.  6pp.
       Joe Clark <joeclark_at_freenet.scri.fsu_dot_edu>

"Supplement No. 2 to Service Manual 05-1163 (Rev. 7-63)" (nope, not the 
one above)
     - 2pp typed info, apparently introducing changes to heater 8345 B for
       64/65 when it became the C.
       Joe Clark <joeclark_at_freenet.scri.fsu_dot_edu>

Misc pages from Eberspacher Heater manual, type BN2, models 20-1215 (6V)
and 20-1216 (12V); these are for 1500-1600cc type IIIs
     - Total 9pp, medium-quality non-original (xerox), with 5pp on
       description and operation, 2 pp on installation (missing much of
       this section - starts with step 21 :-(, and 2 pp on troubleshooting.
       Joe Clark <joeclark_at_freenet.scri.fsu_dot_edu>

Eberspacher Heater Manual, type BN4
     - non-original (xerox) but believed to be complete
       Ron Van Ness <rvanness_at_neuron.uchc_dot_edu>
