Mine is an air-cooled. They don't show up in the Bentley for AC engines at
all, but are called 'sealing rings' in the Haynes manual.
In contrast, they do show up in the Bentley manual for the water-cooled
engines. In essence, they are the head gaskets.
(my engine just leaked air, not water!)
