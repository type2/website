first off, having owned both types i have to admit to liking the 
looks of the rectangulars better.  and the high beams are great.  
BUT....

from an optical standpoint, you can get some outstanding 7" round
headlights.  the low beams on my rectangulars suck.  these high-end
7" rounds also have a very 'Euro' look to them which is obvious to
the trained eye (and to heck with the rest of the madding crowd, i
might add).  additionally, if your rounds get pasted by a rock,
replacement is cheap and available.

also, rock guards are readily available for the rounds and look kewl 
(that is the first time i've ever used that word, i swear) IMHO.  
maybe even look sano or bitchin' but i wouldn't be qualified on to 
make that judgement.
