FALL CAMPOUT

Peach State Transporters is sponsoring a campout for all VW bus
owners and their families at Duckett Mill Campground on Lake
Lanier, Friday through Sunday, September 5-7.  The Campground
is located off Ga. Hwy. 53 about halfway between  I-985 and
Ga. 400 near Gainsville, Ga.

A caravan of buses will be leaving Atlanta on Friday afternoon.
