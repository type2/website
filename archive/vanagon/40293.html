Unless yours is wired significantly differently from of the microbusi
that I've seen, those are your parking lights.  Try turning just those
on, and look at what comes on.  I find they can actually be more
useful than the headlights sometimes, when I'm worried more about
side-swiping something (and moving relatively slowly, of course).
