When I rebuilt a SAAB V4 engine a few years ago I had it balanced
by a speed shop.  It cost ~$200 or so and was worth every penny.

than stock.  Without the extra flywheel weight it revved real nice.

Highly recommended for anyone considering a DIY rebuild.
