ignition coil!  New Rule: Don't let the Vanagon sit a long time without 
a tune-up at regular intervals.

sucking air. Now all I need to do is replace my oil pressure switches
and I'll be back to perfect. (I suspect 11 years is a long lifetime for
a car relay.)
