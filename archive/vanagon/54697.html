Subject: Re: Blizzaks on the EV 

I strongly recommend that if you run standard load tires, such as Blizzaks, on your vans, 
inflate the tires to the maximum pressure listed on the sidewalls.

This will allow the tires to more easily support the load and to run at lower temperatures.

>short hops...<25 miles....as opposed to lots of long trips during the 
>summer. When I need to do long distance driving during the winter, the 
>Outback gets the nod.

>Based on 1 & 2 above, I felt comfortable with the Blizzaks.
