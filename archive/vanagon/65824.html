Unless it's been modified, there should be another piece of rubber hose
at the other end the plastic tube (you've got to trace the tube up near
the wheel well).  Also, there's a short piece that rubs up against the
distributor cap in the middle (not connected to the cap, but rubs up
against it).  The boot by the air box can also be a source of vacuum
leaks.  For the 87s, they came out with a modified clamp that helps hold
the boot to the afm.
