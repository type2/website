Ticking that is load related is not caused by the lifters.  When the lifters
go bad, it is usually apparent after engine startup.  Load ticking is either
caused by knock, leaky exhaust or I think in your case, heads that are coming
loose after you get the engine very hot.  The ticking is caused by combustion
gases poping through the gap between the head and cylinder. If you can change
the tick by changing the load(throttle), this is a definate possibility.
