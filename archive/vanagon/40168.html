The 215/70-14 will work fine.  The extra size will compensate for the reduced
load range of the standard tire.  Even though this is a truck labeled tire,
it is actually rated as a pasenger car tire, load range B.  Truck rated tires
have the size marked with an LT. Pasenger cars are marked with the P.  When
using the P standard tires, they must be de-rated 10% when used on a light
truck.  Considereing all this, a good set of high performance tires will
probably give a better ride and handling over the "Work horses".  Pirelli
makes a tire called the APT400 in this size.  It is called a light truck
touring tire.  May be worth checking out.  Goodyear aquatreads work well
also.  Yokohama A378 is available in this size and only costs about $60.  H
speed rating also.  Hope some of this helps.
