to be behind you too long.  Just bring some fresh tires when you are finished.
The only classification your truck would be is in SCCA Solo II.  There it
would probably be in E or H Stock.  I do know that all stock air-cooled vws
and others NOC (not otherwise classed) are in H Stock.  That also goes
for Vanagons.  I have seen fast bugs run in the D Prepared class.  Club racing
does not allow trucks unless there is a truck class I don't know about.
I know they did have racing truck series a few years ago.
