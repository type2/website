                VOLKSWAGEN

3 letters:   2 points
4 letters:   3 points
5 letters:   5 points
6 letters:   8 points
7 letters:  13 points
8 letters:  21 points
9 letters:  34 points
10 letters: 55 points

Obsolete, capitalized, hyphenated, and foreign words are all
permissible, but only count 1 point, regardless of length.
All decisions are made by Joel and are final.
