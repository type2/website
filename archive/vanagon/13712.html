Yup.  Stopped and looked at it the other day.  Picked up a nice color

worst thing about it was that I couldn't stand upright inside it, and I

of the ground.  Don't even consider taking this beast off of the pavement.
Other than that, it was real nice.  Had a really trick popout bathroom
inside.  That alone was worth stopping to see.
