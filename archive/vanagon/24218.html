Subject: Exact Formula for L82 Deltron

L82 "Silver White" is typically used only on the bumpers, wheels, and where
applicable, the poptop for the Westfalias. This L82 differs from the L87
"Pearl White." The "Silver White" has a very, very slight bluish hue to it.
The Pearl is a very slight "warm" white. Although other color combinations
were available, the Pearl White was applied to the top half of many of the
splittie buses. A buddy of mine owns a body shop (that I should have gone
to). He has restored many buses and has acquired the exact formula for the
L82 "Silver White." The formula is provided to the best of our knowledge for
the advancement of your project of general archives. Use this formula at
your own risk. Rusty VanBondo/Jim Ellis accepts no liability for use
of/application of this paint.
