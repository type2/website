	I have 2 acid baths, in 40qt tubs (with lids) one is Hydrocloric
Acid and the other Phosphoric.  I put the really rusty stuff in the
HCL first, and then in the phosphoric bath.  This last weekend I cleaned
up some wheels I bought for my SO's BMW at the junkyard, soaking them
in the phosphoric bath for about 3 hours each, and they cleaned up like
new!
