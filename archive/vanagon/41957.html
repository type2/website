Take a magnet with you when you buy 'copper' exhaust nuts.  All of the ones
I've seen were steel nuts with a thin plating of copper, about as useful as a
mammaries on a male swine.  Indeed, the ones carried at the local VW shops
here in northern San Diego County are nothing more than improperly fabricated
stop-nuts without the nylon insert -- stop nuts may be had in zinc, cadmium
or copper finish.  

Exhaust nuts are bronze, brass or stainless steel.
