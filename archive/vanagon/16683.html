Second this opinion on the Ultra Blue, the waiting 24 hrs to fill it, and
the thorough cleaning someone else mentioned.  Also, you may need to flatten
back with a hammer the parts where the nuts have previously squished the
plate, if they're really squished, it'll never seal right.
