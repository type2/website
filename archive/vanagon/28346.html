Subject: Backfiring engine

On my '71 bus ...I've been able to cure backfiring that occurs when you
let off the gas as you slow down by enriching the IDLE MIXTURE. By turning
the small idle mixture screw counter clockwise 1/2 a turn or so the problem
was solved.
