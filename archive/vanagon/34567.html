Replacing a syncro tranny is a BIG $$$$$ expense.  Locating one is some work 
also.  When I bought my 87 syncro the diff lock didn't work and the dealer 
fixed it prior to me signing the papers.  Unless you really think you'll be 
driving frequently in conditions where getting stuck is a likely hood, i'd 
leave it alone.
You can always get a second opinion also.
