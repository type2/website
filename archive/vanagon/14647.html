On Fri, 31 Mar 1995, Susan & James Kramer wrote:

> 2) After the 5 minute acceleration lead-up, at what speed can the Diesel
> Vanagon cruise comfortably on level highway?

With the 4 speed, non-turbo, non-westy diesel, around 62 mi./hr. is a 
good cruising speed, although I guess it depends on your definition of 
comfort.  The engine is pretty busy at that speed.  Of course, if you 
have a headwind, sidewind, or any kind of climbing, even that speed may 
be impossible.  Top speed is 65 mph, and I don't think the engine is 
affected by driving that speed continuously.  

> 3) What are people's actual experiences with mileage around town and on the
> highway?

I got 26 to 27.5 miles per gallon (US) in city or highway driving.  It 
never varied from that range.
