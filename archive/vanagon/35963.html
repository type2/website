There is a small vent hole at the top of the front housing.  If tranny was
sitting on floor of unheated garage, it may have water in it from
condensation.  That will cause the oil to foam.  You may also have rust in
it.  The other possibility is that the trans is over-filled.  Check level
with van on a level surface or lift.  Not with it on jack stands on one end.
