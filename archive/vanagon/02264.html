I'm sure you had something else in mind [or expected a more
technical answer] but, basically a somewhat
pressurized system with cracks pressure is lost first from the 
crack requiring the least work , once that is patched it
will take slightly more pressure to push coolant out the
next easiest place. 
  if you have a cracked radiator [say near the top] it wont leak there
until there's enough pressure to keep the radiator mostly filled.
  by the way, if the heads are not pitted, so all you need isnew
