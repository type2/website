On Sat, 28 Oct 1995, Jim Kirk wrote:

> 2. The black plastic piece that is on the bus body where the filler neck 
> and cap all come together. Anyone know how to remove this thing? It would 
> make putting the tank back on much simpler.

Try twisting it counterclockwise to remove, clockwise to tighten.  It has
these little ramps that lock into the irregular shaped hole in the body
sheetmetal.  If you pry with a screwdriver you might wreck the plastic
piece, like I did.  It's even harder to get back on.
