Don't know about a standard format, but in preparing for GNATT I spent
some time experimenting with scanning in maps.  I was able to get some
nice scans of the first page (i.e. North America) of the Rand McNally
road atlas.  I scanned it in three sections (West, Central, and East) and
then printed them and taped them together.  If you want, I could post
the scans or make them available via anonymous FTP or on the web.  I think
they are in jpeg format, but I could convert them to gif pretty easily.
They were scanned as black and white line art, so the file size wouldn't
be too big.
