A co-worker of mine had the same thing happen on his '81 Westfalia, with the
California version of L-Jetronic. The O2 sensor failed (he ignored the light
prompting him to change it) and his Catalytic converter "loaded up". The 
symptoms were a smooth running, good sounding bus with a top speed of about
50 mph.
