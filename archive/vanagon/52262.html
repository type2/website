A common yearly practice here in Canada is to inject new VW CV grease
into the CV joints. The grease comes in a tooth paste type plastic tube
to which a stiff 4 or 5 inch plastic tube of about 1/4 inch O.D. is
screwed. You can slip this stiff tube under the rubber boot and into the
center of the CV joint. By squeezing the grease tube you can deliver new
grease into the joint and expel the old grease.

This is a simple, effective and cheap job :) but a a dirty one :(
