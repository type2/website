Here's something that I did when I used to drive a '72 bus.  I used the 3 foot 
foam pipe insulation that you can get at a building supply houses.  (You can
get longer sections today.) It works well since you do not have to worry about
the gap sizes around the engine compartment and sheet metal.  It may be a
tight fit getting it it but once it's in it will stay in place.  The only
problem I had was that it may deform from the heat since the rear is so close
to the muffler.  Give that a try.  It's cheap enough.
