I think the attraction of the "Monza" exhaust over the extractor was
that it gave better ground clearance (See Speedbumps)  The Monza
exhaust is just a pair of dual pipes coming out the back that are
joined under the apron with a perpendicular pipe and routed to the
exhaust manifold/heat exchangers.
