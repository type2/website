the bastard 40 HP motor was used from 19 May 1959 (chassis # 469 447) until 1
June 1960 (chassis # 614 456) It has the bolt on generator stand, like the
later upright motors .

the likelihood of finding one of these motors is pretty slim as they tended
to self destruct quite quickly- which is why they were discontinued in just
over a year.   i think VW replaced the motor free of charge as they were so
embarassed.

convinced intohauling the ^%# thing all the way back from Alberta
