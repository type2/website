I just want to address a comment that was made about the Rialta and
the "demise" of the Westfalia....

The Rialta is a US/Canada model only. The T4 "Westy" is alive
and well in Europe in a wide range of models...from the
"Weekend" package we had here back in 1993...to the poptop
full camper model, to an extended chasis, raised roof version
called the "California".... unfortunately, we only get
the stripped Weekender and the Winnebago variations here.

As to the LT versions of the Westy...those have been available
for a long time now...almost as long as the LT has been in
existance (late 70s?)
