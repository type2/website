Two more tire to consider for van.  Pirelli P400 APT.  The APT stands for all
purpose tire.  It is available in both 205 and 215/70-14.  I thas a real nice
raised white outline letter sidewall and look like it would work very well on
Syncro.  The load rating is 96T. (1554lbs _at_ 35psi, 118mph)

Michelin MX-4 should be an excelent rain/touring tire.  205 is raised black
letters, 215 is white wall.  the 205 s rated 95T.(1490lbs), The 215 is rated
96S,(112 mph).
