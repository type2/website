I've heard of the mis-adjusted heater cable problem, so it is 
possible. However, the pressure test will NOT reveal a leaky rear 
heater core. Mine passed 3 tests before the dealer replaced the core 
and the control valve. Remove the plastic cover and look for white 
residue on the heater fins. I'd push this issue if they will cover 
the cost.
