Lets have some fun.  The pulley for the AC is located behind the fan.  The
fan has to be removed and the belt is feed behind the fan housing.  The
tension is usually adjusted with shims at the bottom of the compressor
bracket.  There should also be an idler pulley midway under the bottom of the
belt that stops the belt from whipping and vibrating.  By a very good belt
ofr this as the heat in this area will destroy standard off the shelf stuff.
 I suggest getting it from the dealer.  The correct one is made pf special
material and is expensive.  Be carefull how you use the AC as it is the kiss
of death for the air cooled engine.
