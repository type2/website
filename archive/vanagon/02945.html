> Your turn signal works because there is a heating element built into the
> signal flasher. When the turnsignal is turned on, a connection is made to
> your lights via the flasher. Inside the flasher is a heating element which
> heats up a bi-metallic strip attached to a set of points. The bimetallic
> strip heats up causing the points to open, which shuts off the heating element.
> The bimetallic strip cools, causing the points to close and the process begins
> anew.
