Nigel writes:
>Anybody ever change there windshield wiper motor from 6 volts
>to 12 volts?  I have a '66 and was wondering if an old 12 volt
>bug motor would fit, or if there's a better way.

Get a 6 to 12 volt variable speed converter. It will allow you to run a 6v
motor from a 12v source, *and* you can get a variable speed wiper out of the
deal too! This is far superior to a resistor voltage drop, *or* getting a 12v
motor to replace the 6v one (which probably doesn't work anyway)
