oh, BTW.  A nice trick in examining the sight glass on the '85 
(mounted in the drivers front wheel-well) is to use a small mirror 
and a flashlight.  Hold the mirror above the sight glass and shine 
the flashlight at the *mirror*.  The light will illuminate the sight 
glass and then you can see if the bubbles are still there when 
recharging.
