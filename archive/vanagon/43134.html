Hi Jay,

All parts numbers listed below are from chassis #211 2000 001--up (starting
with 1971 model year).

Left Rear Brake Back Plate: 211 609 425 L 

Right Rear Brake Back Plate: 211 609 426 L  

Leaf spring adjust device, rear (holds star adjusters in place): 211 609 485 B
(This is probably included on a new back plate, but I mention it for those
looking only for this piece).

I called Mofoco and Rocky Mt and neither stock the back plates for buses.  I
haven't tried the dealer yet or NOS suppliers, but they might be findable
through these sources.  Recently a lister from the UK mentioned that he
bought some new backing plates for his 70 or 71, so they are available
somewhere.  If anyone finds a source/prices please post to the list.
