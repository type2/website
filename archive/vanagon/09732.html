> Could it be worn rings? Can I afford to ignore the problem?

  Blackish smoke usually results from a rich mixture <too much gas>. Worn
  rings and/or valve guides cause oil to burn, a bluish-grey colored smoke 
  is the result.

  I would speculate that one of your injectors is leaking gasoline while the
  engine is off, and that the puddle created burns when you first start the
  engine.
