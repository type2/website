Test drove a diesel to gas conversion while in CA. In principle it is not
a difficult process of swapping the diesel for an inline 4 cyl Rabbit
engine (this seems to be the best way to go). Unfortunately the one I saw
was a mess, did not start and a had faulty clutch take up which made
driving difficult. I believe soemthing called an "idler" later broke on
the poor soul who bought the thing. DC
