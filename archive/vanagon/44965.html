It only runs hotter because you ask it to do more work.

Your story confirms what I say. Running the same load. you will produce the same 
heat, or less, with a bigger engine. You pull heaver loads (climb mountians 
faster), temps will go up. I thought the discussion was about a stock engine and 
the need for extra cooling. A proper *stock* engine has no need for additional 
coolers.

The cooling system is designed to work with the stock engine. Ask it to shed 
more heat than it was designed to, and you will have problems.
