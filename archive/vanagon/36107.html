Hello Dennis.  One thing to remember regarding tires for the Syncro 
is not only load rating, but also tire pressure.  Most tires can't 
take the 43 psi front, 53 psi rear pressures.  This is imperitive for 
proper handling and weights.  IMHO, I wouldn't touch a tire for a 
Syncro that wasn't a load range D, and couldn't handle the 
recommended tire pressures by VW.

Just my .02.
