So there I was, out on the freeway;
I drove fast, thus, I was not in my bus.
Ahead of me pulled onto the freeway;
Mr. Policeperson in his UHP Camaro.
Not that my tax money buying a Camaro is bad;
But what happens next gets me mad.

Mr. Policeperson pulls over a car.
For something or other, probably legit.

When the police officer is stopped on the side of the road!??!

Only to get stuck behind the truck in the fast lane.

Such _is_ life in the fast lane...
