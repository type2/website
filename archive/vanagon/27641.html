my solution was to get some aftermarket round 7" H4-bulbed lights. i chose
Cibie Z-Beams, but Hella and Bosch also make some pop-in replacements. you
don't have to rewire anything. just remove the four screw holding the retain-
ing ring, ease the sealed-beam bulb out, remove the electrical connector.
put the electrical connector onto the new H4-bulb (already inserted and locked
into the new non-sealed-beam unit), put the new unit back in place, and put
the retaining ring back on. if you are careful, you won't even have to
adjust the headlights. :)

cost? welllllll ... that's where they kinda getcha: about $70 for a pair of
reflectors and two H4 bulbs. :(
