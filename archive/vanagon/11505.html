for $23.50, you get a little 'urine sample' kit. fill up the sample bottle
with your oil (as it drains from the engine), fill out the form and mail it
off in the little mailer box (postage NOT included).

about two weeks later, you get back a printed sheet report, showing the
results of the analysis (as well as any previous analyses done on the same
car ... you get to choose the car's coded id). they test for various metals,
combustion byproducts, etc. for diesel, there is a separate set of tests
available; and some other more extensive tests are also available. i have
mine checked about once a year (every 10,000 miles).
