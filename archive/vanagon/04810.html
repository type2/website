>Here my question: can anybody tell me what the normal and the maximum 
>reading should be (the gauge is in Fahrenheit - unfortunately!).

I don't think there is one *correct* reading. Gauge calibration is often
poor. Moreover, there will be differences depending on where the sender is
located. Try to observe the readings after a good tune-up and under
various loads and you'll get your personal range. After that, just watch
for outliers.
