Sun Dial is an outfit that put together vw campers.  Ive seen more splitties
Sun Dials than later ones.  The set up is the same as a westy  SO42 kit  that
is the most common, but the cabinets etc are not as sturdy.  They are
finished wood, however, and have brass handles, which is attractive.  I dont
care much for the fake wood formica on the westy cabinets, though it is
practical.
