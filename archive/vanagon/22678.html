Sounds like a poor earth on the battery. The clicking is the X relay that 
cuts out the lights and stuff while you start the engine. It clicks on and 
off rapidly when the current to the starter is too much for the battery 
connections. Its definitely not (c) and probably not (a), although it could 
possibly be a duff solenoid, so don't let the dealer try and rip you off.
