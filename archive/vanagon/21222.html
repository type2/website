If you have the engine out, replace the clutch disk even if it looks like
it's new.  For the 15 or 20 bucks you'll spend it'll be well worth it.  If
you don't do it now, you will regret it soon.  I always replace my pilot
bearing anytime I have the engine out.  It costs about 5 bucks.
