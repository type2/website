temperature sender that screws into the head by cylinder #3. If that becomes
loose, or in my case the threads get stripped, the thing vibrates out the

thing running. I'm now looking into ways to secure it back into the hole or
find a new place to put it. You might want to check it. It has a wire that
runs from it into the wiring harness. Check it to see if it's loose.
