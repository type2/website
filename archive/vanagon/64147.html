Todd -

There were two main suppliers to Volkswagen were Reinz and Elring, both
out of Germany and both of which also supply Head gasket sets on the
aftermarket.  Personal preference are the Reinz made sets (they're in a
blue package) because they came with the sealing compounds mentioned in
Bentley in the appropriate tubes.  Elring's sets do not..
