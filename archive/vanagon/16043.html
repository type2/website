As I understand it, VW will pay about 85--90% of the cost of repairing your 83. 

It *does not matter* whether you are the original owner or not.

It *does not matter* how old in years your car is.

It *DOES NOT MATTER* whether you already paid to have the repair done. As I
read the FTC program, it is applicable to repairs DONE IN THE PAST. 

I wonder if we hired a lawyer, and totaled up the total bill for headgasket
repair for all the people on this list (and elsewhere, for that matter),
whether that lawyer could collect a big enough sum to justify a class action
suit.
