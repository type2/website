Anyway, there's a couple of thing to look at.

My problem was in several parts:

1) I had some rust and crud in my brake lines
2) I had 2 restricted brake flex lines (you fixed yours)
3) The master cylinder (Brazilian) albeit new, was not working properly.

I would disassemble the whole mess, piece by piece, and start over, making
sure each piece works before going on to the next.

of brake shoe, and the wheel cylinder piston's travel will exceed the
length of the bore - it pops out, and you have catastrophic pressure loss
RatBoy Johnson watched this happen to me, and it wasn't pretty!
