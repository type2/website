I've just returned from a trip to the Columbia Gorge area, (my wife
and I love to windsurf), and while there, I saw a green Syncro double
cab in Bingen WA.

This trip was a right to left coast jaunt and with our 8 month old
son, we rented a Class A motor home. (No westy rentals in the Portland
area so no snide comments are necesary :-).

We were hanging at the Bingen Marina when I looked back and saw the
Syncro. He was leaving so I couldn't catch him. Seeing this vehicle
made me realize why Joel and others are in love with it.

info to many VW owners while I was there so maybe we'll see a large 
influx of windsurfers on the list.
