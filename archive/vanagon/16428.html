They are breaking inside, a strand at a time.  Cables do not measurably
stretch.  I would check your pedal assembly to see if it is freezing up.  I
have to check the bus occasionally to see if the pedal shaft is frozen in
the bushing that it rides in.  With the cable disconnected it should be
completely loose.  The cross-shaft assembly should also be checked someday,
but that requires an engine pull.
