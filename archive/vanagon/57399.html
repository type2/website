If the adjustment between the front and back latches is off, it may 
not be fully latching, thereby allow the door to open unexpectedly.
The primary latching is done at the rear.

To correct this situation, you'll first have to remove the panel on 
the door. There is an adjustment nut at the rear of the front latch 
mechanism.  Play with that, opening and closing door to see the effect 
on the ability of the door to stay closed.
