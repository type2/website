   I'm under the impression that the only '74 busses that had EFI were
   California automatic transmission models. You may have a '75? An easy 
   way to check would be the engine code. '74s had engine code AW and '75s 
   are ED. Another difference <of no functional significance> is the controls
   for the turn signals and winshield wiper were integrated into the 
   steering column in '75, '74s still had the seperate, "clamp-on" variety.

   The fridge, if this is a Westy, is electric only <12v/110v> in '74 and
   '75. The propane is just for the stove.
