Subject: Re: Fuel Tank Overflow Hoses

I had the problem with my '89.  The main problem is the cheap plastic parts
that connect the balance and vapor recovery hoses together.  It's really no
big deal to fix, although you do have to drop the gas tank.  I think that
the problem is that he gasoline fumes weaken the plastic fittings.
