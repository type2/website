how long do you want to keep your engine?  seriously.  it will 
certainly run fine (or as fine as before) but the unmatched profiles 
*will* accelerate wear.  if you're anal-retentive like *some* people 
on this list you will think about it continually.  envisioning 
reduced cam lobe to lifter surface areas in your sleep and you can 
just FEEL those microfilings of metal between your teeth.  you have 
to balance the cost to your piece of mind...  

do this:  take the camshaft to a machinist and ask them to assess it. 
 if it is marginal, do the replacement.
