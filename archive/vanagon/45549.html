Nice work!  I've got two broken bars I leave sitting around, just to remind
myself what fun a bus is.  The cheap ratchet one looks like a really mean
medieval weapon, with it's curved, broken, splined head.

After my third bar, I ended up taking the swivel head bar (broken) to a
welder and had them put a full bead around the head with a bolt through the
middle.  Hasn't broken since, and has removed many rear axle nuts, with a 4'
long heavy square steel extension.   It also does gland nuts that are
solidly green-locktighted in place with ease, once the engine is held down
properly.
