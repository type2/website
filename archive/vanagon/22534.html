Actually, Tim, I had a '69 Bus in Colorado, with (I think) a
custom-made hitch that bolted only to the rear bumper mounts, as you
describe. I towed EVERYTHING with it, including a '72 bus that
weighed more than the '69 doing the towing. Mind you, I don't
recommend this-- I was young and foolish, and certainly exceeding
whatever ratings that thing had. (It was fun, tho!)

Point is, all the stress was falling directly on those bumper
mounts, which never creaked, shifted, or complained an iota, despite
all that abuse. These things aren't weak! At least, not on that
particular bus.

'Course, if yours are rusty, or have been tagged in an accident,
all bets are off.

Wish I still had that bolt-on hitch, tho...
