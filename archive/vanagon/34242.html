Michael,
I'd advise to just wait until it leaks.  Even then the head gasket leak
doesn't make the "whole engine toast" - just replacing the heads and gaskets
will cure the leak.  However with over 150,000 miles you MAY want to opt to
invest in a replacement engine instead of adding new heads to an old engine.
 You COULD do the heads and then have more serious trouble soon after.
