  Mike,
   You can pick up a hammer operated "impact driver" at most flea markets or
well supplied hardware stores or a good tool supply place.  Should be less
than $20.  Probably less.  They work great. The impact of the hammer serves
to keep the phillips bit in the screw and turns it a bit at the same time.
I've used them plenty on the case screws on motorcycles over the years.  It
might even save a screw that allready a little stripped.  Good luck.
