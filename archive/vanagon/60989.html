Subject: Re: ?G2 on G60 Corrado?

The G60 engine is equipped with a G-force supercharger which has 
inherently bad bearings.  That's why VW opted not to use that 
supercharger anymore, and aftermarket companies now offer a turbocharger 
replacement system which although is not as responsive, is more efficient 
in the top end.
