Hello Jim.  

With my ' 90 Syncro, I often go from LOW to 1ST with little problem.  
Just have to wait for the lever to pop up from Low to neutral, and 
then go into 1ST.  It's a balky move, but I have managed to get used 
to it by just going slow with the stick.

I usually never go into 1ST while moving... I believe the gearing is 
too low to downshift into smoothly.

You always have to push down on the lever going into reverse or LOW.

Hope this helps
