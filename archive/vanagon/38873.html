Hi!

The rapid clicking also could mean the contacts that the bulb push against
are dirty/corroded.  Replacing the bulb in this case might only solve the
problem temporarily since removing and inserting the bulb may have caused
you to make a good contact.  Make sure the old bulb is truly bad--if not,
then clean those contacts!
