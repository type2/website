Had a similar problem with my 80 Wanagon a couple a years back.  Culprit
was the Fuel Pressure Regulator.  Could be that, but check that filter
anyway.  Price on one of these are $47 (Air Cooled 83), $49.50
(Waterboxer 83).

Fuel Pumps aren't cheap.  Same one as my 84 (actually 75-91 Bus/Vanagon).
 German Pierburg is $182.58, Bosch a little less at $175.00 (I used a
Pierburg on my 84 when I replaced it, liked the fact that it has rubber
insulation surrounding it to help deaden the noise).
