> I just have two requests for the trip that I hope

> 1) Please.....PLEASE! don't embark on this trip with tires

Well of course.  I've benn running bais ply since I got my bus about 3-4

> rear view mirror.    

This is one of the things that I always hate.  I think a headlight should 
be aimed properly, both highs and lows.
