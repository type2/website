Tam,

Lee Ann & Sean writing you from the shores of beautiful lake Champlain. 
We live in Burlington Vermont and think it's pretty great here.
Mt. Mansfield ,VT is spectacular this time of 
year.  Not sure of your route but if you can take the ferry from  NY to 
Burington, then Rt. 2 over to Maine, very scenic.  You should stay at 
least on night in the Northeast Kingdom of Vermont.  Green River 
campground is wonderful I think it's is Morrisville, VT but if you want I 
can look up the address and phone number. 
Maine is amazing, Bar Harbor south will be an incredible drive and you 
will want to stop every everywhere.  But beware these are secondary 
roads and very slow going.

If you want any more info just E-mail us, and if you are in Burlington 
for any time let us know and we can show you around.
