	There seem to be quite a lot of Hi-Tops over here on all types of vans.

or they just get copied. They are all fibre glass shells, high at the back 
sloping down to the front. If I have time I'll do an  ascii picture to give you
an impression of the shape. They seem to be pretty aerodynamic and in normal 
conditions don't cause any major problems. I don't know how much they affect the 
e mpg though. Last summer on a trip to Germany I was getting an average of 25 
mpg, thats about 20 US which seems to be standard. It was mainly steady autobahn
driving so it seems a pretty decent figure. Compared to a normal car though it is 
is low, gas is pretty expensive over here. Another good reason for going unleaded
ed.
