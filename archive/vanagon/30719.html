November issue of the NEATO Old Bus Review.

and was willing to pay or trade for firewood. It turns out that he ended up
with a '61 Westy!  The same guy seems to have a pair of split oval 52 bugs.
(Ho hum, everyone has a set of these- Don't we?)  That's the cool part.
My buddy was checking with him to see if he was interested in buying his '63
Hitler Hatch Westy with the rare full width icebox that's right behind the
drivers bench and under the weird rear facing seat... The fellow had already
purchased the '61 Westy for next to nothing.  As a conversational aside, the
fellow mentioned that he had just finished cutting the rear wheel wells on
his '60 23 window.
DOH!!!!! My friend double checked to see if he was kidding and was told,
"No, I like 'em like that so I can put those big 'ol tires on them for off
road and hauling firewood."
