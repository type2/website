 Regarding the thread on non-Westy pop tops that go straight up [rather than
 hinged]: I've seen a couple of breadloaf buses with tops installed by a
 company called ASI. These tops had a mechanism that included some really
 long springs and an x-bar linkage so that the weight of the top was easily 
 raised straight up. 

 Both of the buses with these tops that I have seen were 7 passenger models 
 that had the pop-top added later.
