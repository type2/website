Gregg, there is a small black box underneath the van near the driver's wheel 
well. It is basically under the speedometer and you can see the cable coming 
out of it (when you are lying underneath the van). On the front side is a 
small button you depress into the box to reset the O2 counter. There is no 
intelligent connection to the O2 sensor or fuel/ox control system.
