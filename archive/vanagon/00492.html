A trailer for your vehicle is limited to a typical class I trailer.

Do not exceed the gross vehicle weight which is the weight of the
driver, passenger, luggage, trailer hitch and tongue weight of the
loaded trailer.

Technical Information/Data - Specifications and Capacities
