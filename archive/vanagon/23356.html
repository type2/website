One thing I haven't seen mentioned in these tire posts is the rotating
weight of the tire.  A larger tire likely weighs more and will therefore rob
a bit of power.  The 185 tire should get better mileage.  Bicyclists know
this stuff intimately.  Whether this is worth the trade in handling would be
a personal decision.
