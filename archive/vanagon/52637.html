No disrespect but by Chryslers definition the rear engined buses are not 
cab forward.  BUT...

Cab forward is the engine mushed as far forward as possible with the 
wheels moved to the corners.  

Older buses had the engine obviously not pushed forward and the wheels 
were not as far to the corners as possible.  Lets though look at the 
newer VW buses.  Well engine is mushed as far forward as possible. and 
the wheels are pegged to the corners.  SO the EV is cab forward.

Lookj at the slope of the hood on the various vans and minivans.  EV is 
the sharpest rake of any non-rear or non-mid engined bus.
