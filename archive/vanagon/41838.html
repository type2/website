I try to get the weight at my index point by standing on the bar with one
foot, with my shoe inboard of the index mark and just touching the mark with
the outside (distal) edge of the shoe.  (This is a lot easier to do than to
describe.)  By rolling the foot to the outside edge, the weight is placed on
the index mark.  This is where the rain gutter of the car is useful for
balance, as I occasionally have difficulty standing on terra firma with both
feet! ;-)

Another alternative that I haven't tried yet (I just thought of it) would be
to tie a cord/rope/baling-wire to the breaker bar at the point that you wish
to apply the weight and fashion it into a stirrup.  Step into the stirrup,
but hold on so that hoss doesn't throw you ;-)  If anyone tries this (and it
actually works) please let me know.
