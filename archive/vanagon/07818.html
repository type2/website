James Brill writes:
> 
>    So, assuming this is the way to go, these are my questions:
> 
> 1.  What type and grit of sandpaper will work best to clean up the 
> edges of the couple of holes and take off the larger areas
> of surface rust?

I would use something coarse, like 80 grit.  If you can get a power drill
to it, I have found that those wire wheels work really well compared to
sandpaper.

> 2.  These POR-15 people sell something that is supposed to clean up 
> the rusted area and prep it a little better for the application of the
> POR-15 stuff itself.  Will this really be needed since I will be 
> sanding out the rust?

I'm not sure what this POR-15 stuff is, but I once used some stuff that
sounds similar.  It was thin, milky-white liquid that turned black when
you brushed it on rusted areas.  Worked really well - 4 years later my
battery compartment is still rust-free!  I don't know if you need their
prep stuff, but if is cheap, why not?
