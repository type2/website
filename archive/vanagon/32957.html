 The US Army started sending out heaters with the MRE's a few years back 
that operate on  the same principle. You throw your maincourse in the bag
 and put in a few ounces of water and after a few minutes its hot slop!  When 
they first came out I tried one and it did improve the edibility of 
 this crap. If you 
haven't noticed I don't have a great fondness for MRE's and can proudly say
that I have brought home everyone I have received for the last three years.
Incidentally the instructions tell you to place the heater tilted 
 against a "rock or something"
and warn you against drinking the hot water from the bag, I guess they 
 had a problem
