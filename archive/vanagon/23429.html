propane needs an air/propane mix to ignite.  it will NOT travel up in 
a line and ignite the tank.  needs air.  lots of it.  someone can 
post the stoichiometric ratio but a 100% propane environment cannot 
support combustion.

also.  the quantity of propane stored in the lines with the main 
valve off is negligible.  if the line is ruptured, it will fart out a 
small quantity but causes no explosion hazard.
