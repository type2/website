Whoa...I did not know you had low oil pressure problem.  Are you sure?
Make sure the sensor and gauge are working properly.  I don't know how
you test them, but be sure you're that's correct.  After that I would
check out the pressure relief valves.  I don't remember what you drive
but you may have one or to of these valves that determine whether or
not oil will be channeled to the oil cooler or not.  If it uses a
spin-on filter, then be sure to use the right brand of filter and not
that cheapo brand. ;) For summer time, I think your temperature gauge
sounds about right.  I have to assume your engine seals/hoses are in 
good shape and "doing their thing right".  Oh, make sure the fan belt 
is at the proper tension too.

Hope this helps,
