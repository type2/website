Last year I mail-order purchased a Eureka! model for side attaching to
vans. At ~$500 it is a great tent but it doesn't really mount next to the
van the way I'd hoped, so I havn't used it camping yet. I'm considering
divising a way to connect the VW door to the tent, but it seems tricky
with the VW sliding door and all. 

Another alternative I looked into is the awnings that can be side mounted
to the VW campers.  One company has a tent package that fits with the
awning.  Although it looks really convienient and easy to put up it has a
big downside.  The company states that it cannot be left up in >15 mph
winds.  I can't imagine going to sleep knowing that if a gust comes up my
awning and tent would be ripped off the van!
