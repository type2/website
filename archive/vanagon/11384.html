The idea is to connnect the batteries together only when they are 
charging. The relay is an electric switch which connects them togther 
when it "sees" 12 volts from the ignition relay.

Most experts say connecting lead acid batteries in parallel (except during 
charging) will drain both batteries, as one always is more charged than 
the other.
