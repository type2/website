I too find it hard to believe that ANYONE could own a Westy and not KNOW what
kind of engine lid they have! I have opened the compartment just to study or
check the engine enough times to KNOW that the '91 has a plastic lid, while my
'80 had the heavy metal one. I personally thought that the metal one was safer-
in the case of a fire, etc. 

Of course my engine fire totally destroyed the plastic one - allowing much more
damage to occur to the engine!
