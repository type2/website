Jonathan,

I forgot what year your bus is but you can buy a replacement battery tray
(and maybe a lower, right-side rear corner piece, if it needs it).  I bought
these two pieces for my '79 Bus from Rocky Mountain Motor Works (The Bus
Stop). Other places carry them as well.  They're not extremely pricey.
You'll have to gas weld them in place, which screws up your paint job back
there.  I think a good, solid bus body with a little primer looks better
than a bus with an otherwise good paint job but some bad rust holes.

Concerning the water leaks: Beats the hell outta me.  Are all your engine
compartment rubber seals in good shape?  That's really important for proper
engine cooling.

Just my $20 worth.
