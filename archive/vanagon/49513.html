> Subscription Confirmation Notice
> ***
> Welcome to The Saab Network mailing list.
> The instructions below will enable you to post questions and responses and
> retrieve and perform keyword searches on past postings from email.
> Please take the time to read all of the very important instructions below.
> To subscribe to this list, if you haven't already, just send a message
> to saab_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com with the word SUBSCRIBE in the subject.
> To unsubscribe from the list, send a message with the word UNSUBSCRIBE in
> the subject.
> ***
> ADDRESSING
> saab_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com - Submissions and address change requests
> tsnreq_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com - Retrieve past postings and FAQs
> tsnfind_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com - Perform keyword searches
> ***
> POSTINGS
> You will receive each posting as a separate mailing.  If you would like
> to receive a digest of a single day's postings, please send a message
> to saab_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com with the word DIGEST in the subject.  
> If later you change your mind, send a message with the word UNDIGEST in the 
> subject and you will be returned to the regular list.
> ***
> SUBMISSIONS
> Submissions may be anything you feel that would be of interest to The Saab
> Network, but may be edited by the moderator.  Before posting a question,
> please first read the associated FAQ thread (see below) if one is
> available. Submissions on the same topic as a FAQ will be posted after all
> other pending submissions.  Send submissions to:
> saab_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com
> ***

> KEYWORD SEARCH
> The Saab Network archives can be searched as well.  Send your request to:
> tsnfind_at_network.mhs_dot_compuserve_dot_com

> message with the keyword in the subject to the address above.

> searches.  Submit one keyword per message (only one word in your subject)
