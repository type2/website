I think the heat exchange will not be sufficient considering the length 
(and surface) of the copper tube and the flow rate of cold water through 
that tube.

An friend of mine (back in Germany, and in a VW Rabbit) used approx. 3-4 
feet of plastic tubing, and tightly wraped/coiled that around the incoming 
radiator tube (the one with the really hot fluid coming from the engine). 
With this he created a kind of hot water reservoir between the real 
windshield water reservoir and the spray jets, and the water arriving on 
the windshield was actually steaming.....
