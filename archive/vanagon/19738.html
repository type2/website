Well?
Should those of us in Southern Ontario/Western New York/Eastern Michigan
get together for a camp-out?

This weekend I visited a great campground on the North side of Lake Erie
called Turkey Point (Gobble Gobble).  I camped there with my family as
a kid, and it looks like it's just as nice now as it was then.
It's two hours from Toronto and Buffalo, about three from Detroit...
you know, accessable.  Maybe 7 hours from Ottawa... And a lot more fun
than a pizza night.

They've got real crappers and showers for those of us who might care,
trailer dumping ststions for all 0 of us with Rialtas, cool trails
for walking/running/hiking/biking.

Group camping areas so that we could congregate in our own little
area away from those horrible types who might complaine about a bunch
of weirdos with rusty, broken down, old, noisy Volkswagen Vans.

There's also a playground with all sorts of fun stuff for you and/or
your children to play on.

There's a golf course right nearby... But count me out.

There's a little auto shop 10 minutes away that always has a handfull
of VWs parked out front, for those of you who don't know if your
ride is up for the round trip.

I think that it would be great fun for us Great Lakes area lenti folks
to get together.  I have lots of info on Turkey Point Provincial
Park (Gobble gobble), if you're interested let me know.

Come on folks.  Lets get it together.
