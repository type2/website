Actually, the air being discharged from the A/C vents should be pretty 
dry.  Most of the moisture will be condensed out when the air passes 
across the A/C evaporator coil.  As the air travels through the duct it 
will warm slightly, bringing the residual moisture content well below 
dew-point and avoiding any further condensation.
