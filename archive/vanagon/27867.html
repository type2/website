Unlike American vans with longitudinal ridges in the roof, Vgons can take
aftermarket sunroofs in several different spots.  I've mine over the 2nd
seat floor area, where a 6'1" driver can just reach the handle without
driving into a bridge abutment.  It makes a big difference in airflow,
so a sunroof over the 3rd seat or engine deck would probably be an alterna-
tive to the vents Fred mentions.

Some of the VW AC systems make a sunroof anywhere on the roof impossible,
I know.  Fred, is yours the upfront kind or the backtofront kind?
