Dunno about the general case, but as I noted in the Safare "tire
report" I posted recently, the tires I ended up with (205-75HR14)
had significantly stiffer sidewalls than any of the other tires I
looked at, which included several "load range C" types of 6-ply, and
even one 8-ply tire.  I am extremely happy with them!  They handle
the weight of my bus mucho better, and I have some serious weight on them
(like three batteries, two beds, lots of tools, firewood, refrigerator,
propane tank, and on and on).  They are an off brand I have never
heard of - made in Indonesia, guaranteed for 40k miles.
