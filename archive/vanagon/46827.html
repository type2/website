> When I make a turn with my '86 Vanagon camper, it whines.  Any idea what
> this is or what I need to do to fix it?
> 
Sounds like maybe a bad pump or restricted filter. Try flushing the 
system with ATF, clean or replace filter & top up. To flush, remove 
return line to PS tank, then have someone crank the engine (remove 
coil wire so it doesn't start) to pump the old fluid into a 
container. Don't let the tank go dry - top up with new fluid & repeat 
a few times until clean fluid comes out the return. Mine took about 1 
1/2 qts to flush & fill. I was able to clean the filter by rinsing in 
solvent.
