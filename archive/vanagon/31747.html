Do you have the Bentley for your van?  And is it a water-cooled
or air-cooled?  '83s apparently come in both flavors.

I have an '81 2.0L air cooled and have recently done some trouble-
shooting in the FI system, and perhaps I'm being naive but it
didn't seem too bad.  There are only about 3 pieces that I know
of that are specifically related to starting, not counting the
computer itself.  Two of those things are quite easy to get at
and test... well, now that I've taken the distributor out to
pull off the cold-start valve I consider that operation passe :)
There's another piece that's a bit harder to get to -- looks to me
like you have to pull the intake manifold stuff to get at it.
I'd recommend testing those bits first, as the Bentley gives very
clear instructions for each of them.  You only need a volt/ohm meter,
as I recall.

Steve's ideas are definitely worth checking out before diving
into the FI...
