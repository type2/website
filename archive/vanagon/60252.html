 Do you still have the box she came in?

 Seriously, we used the cardboard box provided by Ballard Hospital to take our
first infant camping.  It had a thin pad on the bottom, high sides so the kid
could not escape, and handy handles on the ends for easy transport.  We'd tuck
young Teddy in for the night and set the box on the fold up table.  First time
Mom could reach him quickly for those frequent feedings and first time Dad
could get a full nights sleep, important in those early months of child
rearing.
