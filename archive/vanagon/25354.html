I was an art student at Northern Arizona University /NAU (Flagstaff, AZ) in
the early 70's. It was almost the norm to have some folks attend classes and
actually receive advanced degrees while living in the nearby forests in
buses, vw vans, and TeePees. Do you know how to spell "Hippies"? I was lucky
to live on the back porch of a house RIGHT next to the railroad tracks. The
house was also slowly sliding down the hill. All of the doors looked like
something off of PeeWee's playhouse. No PeeWee though! Where else could you
find a house inhabited half by hippie freaks and half by police science
majors, hard core redneck cowboys, vietnam vets, and rookie cops. We all had
fun. =) Hint: I didn't get a police science degree.
