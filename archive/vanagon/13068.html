Well, it's suppose to turn on the heater that is built in under there rear
bench seat.  You have a Westy or a bus?  It's just a heater core with a
blower built in it.  When you turn on the switch (3 speeds) it activates
the blower.  From what you've said, it sounds like the blower isn't
blowing.  I'd check the fuse first and then the switch.  I've had trouble
with the switch for the main heater and had to replace it.  Kinda of a
cheapo switch.  If that doesn't do it, you'll have to check other things
like the connections, the relay at the blower end.
