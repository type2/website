Hi there.  I don't recommend scotchguarding or other treatments to 
the canvas of your poptop.  We've yet to have our canvas leak in some 
unbelievable rainstorms.  The canvas is naturally waterproof.  
There's lots of info in the archives on this subject.  The canvas 
must breathe, and by treating it is won't breathe, and you'll have 
extra condensation in your van because of it.

Hope this helps.
Harry.
