Subject: Rabbit 3 terminal regulator (for instruments)

	The Rabbit instrument voltage regulator is indeed a 3 terminal solid
state device, as Harvey describes. It just plugs into the flex circuit behind 
the instrument pannel. Yep, I replaced mine once, with one from a newer car 
from the junkyard :) No screw, but it does have a little tab sticking out of
the package opposite the three pins.
