Whatsamatter?  Never did any sewing before?  Never been
inside a blouse?  No aspersions intended.  Dart is the term
for sewing flat material to cover curves.  You fold the
material, start sewing from the edge to form a triangle of
material between the fold and the seam.  The longer the
triangle, the gentler the curve.
