ahem. :)  in 1964-65, vw made some little trucks called Fridolin which were
for the Deutsche Bundespost or Federal Post Office. they are exactly as you

for the same postal folks, that had only swing doors. it was called an EA 48
and was put out by Karmann, the convertible and Ghia builders.

a really ugly looking EA 50 was also made around then ... but the front end
looks like the butt end of a hippopotamus. really. :) now it had sliding
doors on the sides.
