The 134A is not compatible with the refrigeration oil that was in your system
 In about 6 months to a year your entire AC system will be trashed as the oil
gums up and clogs the works.  This type of situation is why there are laws
concerning the use and disrtribution of CFC products.  Also, the 134A is more
"active"  than R12. This means that your hoses will allow more to leak
through.  Different hose materials are used in 134A instalations.  This is
one of the components that adds to the cost.  

BTW, people trying things of this nature is why it will soon be neccasary to
have a license to mount tires.
