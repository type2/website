> i used to live up in the yukon territory, and the availability of parts in
> whitehorse is not good. there aren't any. everything is ordered in from
> edmonton or vancouver at astonishing prices (i had a landcruiser instead of a
> bus up there, and there was a reason for it...). as far as our
> proposed trip goes, the last places that have vw parts are prince
> george, b.c., and edmonton, alberta. and of course anchorage, but
> that would be out of our way...needless to say, there's no veedub dealers on
> the 2000 miles between edmonton and anchorage. there's one guy in whitehorse
> who will work on vw's if pushed hard enough (with those colorful canadian
> bills !). he's the local "foreign cars specialist", calls himself a
> mechanic... the town has 2-3 fairly well stocked FLAPS for general things,
> and being a frontier city in mining country, there's no shortage of welders
> and other people who will say they can fix anything. a fair number of 'em
> probably can... there's about half a dozen pretty trashed buses around town,
> and once in a while you can find one as engine donor if you need one. the
> junkyards have, like, 2 buses and half a dozen rabbits. as far as busses go,
