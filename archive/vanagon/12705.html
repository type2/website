I once rebulit my Volvo 264 engine. I basically replaced rockers and camshafts.
I decided not to go for dealer parts. I bought aftermakrket kits, made in
Germany, and saved at least 1/3 of cost. The cam and rocker lasted only
20k miles. I asked around what did I do wrong. I was told that I should
have bought OEM rockers. I believe OEM head gaskets are obviously higher
quality than aftermarket ones. I guess I would rather use the expensive
OEM parts on engine than have to tear it apart again later.

This is based on one experience on Volvo. I just thought it might be applicable
to VWs.
