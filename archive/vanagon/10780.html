>2) Just under the said gauge hole, there is a red light thingy with a "B" on
>it.  Anyone know what this is?

it's a brake circuit warning light. if one of the brake pressure sensors
(that make your taillights go on) 'fails' (whatever that means), the little
'B' light comes on ... which tells you that you have only HALF of your
normal braking power left. :(   if you push on the 'B', it should light up
... as a self-test.
