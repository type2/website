Jon -

The Fuel Pump installed on 75-79 FI Beetles, 75-79 Breadloaves and 80-91
Vanagons are all the same pump, OE VW 251-906-091.  Two different
manufacturers are available - Bosch and Pierburg (I run a Pierburg on
mine).  Don't know where the Bosch is made at this moment (Germany,
Spain, Brazil, who knows?), but the Pierburg is made in Germany.

Pierburg Price is currently $182.58 and the Bosch is $175.00.  Rebuilts
are probably available in the $100 range (exch), give or take $20.

I like the Pierburg because it is incased in rubber and helps deaden the
noise a bit.  Another Pierburg difference is that it uses "ring-type"
terminals instead of push-ons like the Bosch, but it comes with the
terminals and new "weather" boots for this type.
