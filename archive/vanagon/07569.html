> what if there is air elsewhere in the system?  Am I to assume that there

Yes, The top of the rad is the highest point in the cooling system.
The air is supposed to collect there.  But you might try checking...
Go around back(Engine end) And squeeze the rubberlike hoses that run
above the engine at the front of the engine compartment (Front is toward
seats)  If they feel hot (if a blister forms on your palm   ;)) there is
probably not any air left in the system.
