Hmmmmm?  It depends.  The natural action of the Syncro's viscous coupling
would be to lock-up and send some power to the front in this situation,
causing "some" of the "binding" you describe.  My van exhibits this behavior,
but it sounds like yours exhibits it to a greater extent.  Hard to tell
without driving them back to back.  I suspect that it might be just a matter
of perception of how much "binding" is taking place.  On the other hand, if
you have a LOT of binding, perhaps your VC is kaput an locked-up all the
time?
