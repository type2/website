I have just rebuilt a 2.0L engine and also assumed that I could rebuild it for 
$700 or $800.  
My engine is from a 78 westfalia and is fuel injected.  No real problems 
rebuilding other than parts are expensive for that engine. I also replaced my 
clutch and pressure plate while I had the engine out since they were worn.  
You probably should budget closer to $1400 to $1500 for the rebuild.  I had to 
replace a head on mine as it was damaged, so you may get by a little cheaper
