The idle speed valve is the culprit in the majority of damaged control
units. When the valve quits humming the control units works itself slowly to
death. The moral here; when the idle speed valve first starts to hang up
from carbon and/or crud clean it then now later. Or perhaps you like
spending extra money <g>. Come to think of it, it's probably a good idea to
simply clean the valve on a regular basis. Say perhaps once a year/10-15k.
