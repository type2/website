You are leaking fluid somewhere and the system may need to be bleed.
 External leaks are usually easy to find. Heater core leaks and internal
leaks more difficult.  Consider having engine leak down tested to ensure no
internal leakage of coolant into cylinders or worse, combustion gases into
colling system.
