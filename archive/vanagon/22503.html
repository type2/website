Well, not necessarily cheap, but great fit, great looking and sturdy 
enough to take your house along with:  the Paulchen rack (made in Germany) 
is beautifully crafted and well thought out.  Basically a utility rack, it 
also has attachments for carrying bikes, skis, etc.  They used to be 
handled by several local Pacific Northwest VW dealers, such as Campbell 
Nelson, Carter, but were dropped a couple of years ago.  Still, you 
may be able to find them from another source, or even, if you get lucky, 
used, somewhere.
