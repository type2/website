msanner_at_hooked_dot_net(Matt) wondered about Haynes manuals:

IMHO I think (comparing my Haynes and Bentley for transporter up to
1979) In some respects Bentley is better, but overall they are
comparable. With Haynes you have to remember all measurments are metric
(which makes it hard when you are using one on an american car) and some
tools are listed by what they are called in England (where the book
comes from), though they do provide a chart at the beginning of the book
for the US equivalent of those tools.

I feel you can get by with one or the other and Muir, although both and
the Idiot book are optimal.  Some will tell you there is no comparison
between the two, but the price difference (Haynes runs about $13US)
makes all things equal.
