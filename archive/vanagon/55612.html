Porsche 914 alloy lug bolts will fit the fronts
Audi 5000 lug bolts will fit  fronts(even thought they have a rounded taper
under the head)
these should suffice, but it would be in your best interest to go back to the

Try it and see what happens. But for safety's sake - don't do it if it looks
like it won't work. Every set of Carat's I've had, I've always had the lugs - so
I'm not totally sure how the rears will work.
