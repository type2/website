When I first got my '65 deluxe it was (still is) outfitted with a DP 1600
and the 3pct34 carberator.  Experienced similar "flat-spot", poor idle, and
numerous other annoyances.  (The PO was NOT mechanically inclined - had the
bus for ~ a year).  I removed said carberator (found one of the back nut
securing the carberator to the manifold was loose) and gave it a rebuild.
After installing I had more problems, severe icing along the throat and still
poor idle.  I removed carb and found the accelerator pump tube was gone (or
did I forget to install?)  The PPO (that's the previous previous owner) told
me I threw that baby out with the soaking solution and just didn't notice the
missing accelerator pump tube.  Somehow I felt it got lose and sucked into the
intake and is residing in one of the heads.  Replaced tube and was back on the 
road.  A week later I lost power in the #1 cylinder.  My worst fears...
