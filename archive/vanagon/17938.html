> Can someone clue me in on what a "type II" is ?

A type II in the VW world is what all the bus generations are refered to 
as.  But there are generation numbers as well.  The newest generation is 
called Eurovan in America, and T4 in Germany.  So I would take that to 
mean that the four generations of type IIs are refered to thusly:
 
T1: Split windshield bus
T2: Bay window bus (aka, breadloaf)
T3: Vanagon
T4: Eurovan
