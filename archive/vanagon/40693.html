Is the oil cooler leaking oil or water.  The "O" rings seal the oil, not the
water.  Water leaking could be either the hoses or the cooler case itself.
 Foir real fun, these coolers sometimes leak internally.  This allows the
cooling system to fill with oil making a real mess.  I personally hate thse
things.  I replaced the set-up in the FUN BUS with a 16 plate cooler mounted
in front with a 180F thermostat. Works great.
