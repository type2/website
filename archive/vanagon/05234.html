...sorry, I missed the beginning of this thread, but something similar
happened to my '78 Scirocco a while back at about 110,000 mi.  I took
the odometer apart myself and *carefully* applied some superglue to the
nylon gear (it had split so that it formed a 'c' instead of an 'o', an
thus no longer followed the odometer axle as it turned.)  That was 3
years and 40,000mi ago and no prob since.
