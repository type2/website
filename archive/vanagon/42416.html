I lost the freon (R-12) charge in my 88GL and decided to recharge it with the
R-134.
I had an old vacume pump (the old high school demo kind purchased from a flea
market) with which I evacuated the system.  I made fittings to connect to the
ports on the Vanagon system from pieces of hose that I had and common
fittings that I bought at the auto parts store.  Once evacuated, I put in 4
(48 oz) cans of the new  R-134
and 2oz of compresser oil [USE SAFETY GLASSES!].   The manual calls for 50oz
total. This was done with the engine running the air conditioner set to high.
It works great - can't feel any difference in cooling power.  Total cost =
less than $50.
