 > Earlier this week I read something about piston slap being a problem with
 > some 93 Eurovans. Based on the description of piston slap symptoms, I think
 > my van has this condition.
Better get it checked.  Unfortunately, the only true way of checking
it is removing the cylinder head, and, unless you can convince the
dealer to do it for you for free, it costs a couple of hundred bucks
to diagnose.  VWOA does have a problem number for this (I must have
lost it, I could not dig it out), so you might try to get the dealer
to listen to the engine with a stetoscope, to gather evidence.  Once
you know there's a problem, then your battle with VWOA will begin, if
the warranty has run out :-( Mine was quite under warranty, so I had
no problem.
