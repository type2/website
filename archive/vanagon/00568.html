  I have not located any leak yet. But, I have done the cooling system flush for
  4 times this year. Hopefully, I am doing it right the last time. I learned
  through these flushes that air in a Vanagon H2O cooling system can be fatal.
  Since VW shop manual does not say when I lower the front end  and level the
  vehicle during the coolant refill phase. I had to do the trail-and-error. I didn't
  do it right for several times, air stayed in afterward, coolant was pushed
  from the overflow tank to the refill tank after miles of driving, and temperature
  went up too high.
