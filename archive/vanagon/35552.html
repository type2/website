Sound like a bad sensor.  In order to get at it, you either have to remove
the tank,(you do not want to do this), or cut an access in the deck above the
tank.  I hate this approach as it damages the vehicle but the other approach
requires a lot of work;  Everything on top of the engine has to come out.
 Both these approaches should be done by someone with some experience.  Buses
burn to easy.
