I have considered what Dan Houg said and have concluded that there is a lot
of merit in his statement. Since I am interested primarily in extra ground
clearance, and know the value of a soft suspension for slow speed off-road
driving, and since I am dubious about the idea of running stiff springs up
front and soft springs in the rear, I have concluded to not order the
springs at this time, but to wait until next February or so to make this
purchase. In the meantime, there are ways to raise the van up in the front
using the stock spring.

It does seem silly to pay full price for those springs and shocks and it
does seem like, if a group of us were to band together and place an order
for many units at once, that Sekiel ought to take 20% off the price in
return for that big sale.
