I have been down this road before.  There are two VW options. The first is
to acquire a folding "theatre" style seat from a Wolfsburg model.  This
seat would go back to back with the passenger seat.  The second option is a
single seat which was sold as an option.  It is described and pictured in
the owners manual if you have it.  It attaches to the floor in grooves
found under the carpet.  

There is a multitude of information on this subject to be found in the
archives.  Let me know if you are having trouble finding them and I will
forward the messages to you.
