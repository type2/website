I sent David a copy of a set of prototype bumper sticker layouts to post up
on his homepage.  Avery stickyback laserprinter labels run about 33c per 
11x2.75inch label.  These are good for quickies.  Neither the media nor the
marking materials are weather resistant but a quick cover of clear packing
tape fixes that in the short term.

I can have examples available in the desert.

typefaces (Ariel:Block SansSerif) (Technical: scripty sansserif) (Times Roman
Serif: very uffishul looking)

fonts: main word 48pt, "vanagon_at_lenti.med.umn_dot_edu": 36pt
