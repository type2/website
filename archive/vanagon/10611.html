Nick,

I had a similiar problem with my 1987 Syncro. The door would suddenly open, well
not totally, 
whenever the temps got cold.  I didn't want to screw with adjusting the door
itself, so first
I throughly lubricated the inside of the door latch located on the left end of
the sliding door, opened and closed the door several times, and the problem was
cured.  I guess the latch was sticking inside during the cold weather and would
not close completly.  If that doesn't work he may need to re-adjust the door
itself.    

Good luck

P.S. My van is not equipped with those auto door lock gizmos,
