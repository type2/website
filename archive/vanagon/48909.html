Products (formerly Delta-Six Industries). You'll gag at the prices, but they
are much cheaper than VW dealers and the parts are not available anywhere else.

Assembly with rollers is $24

They don't sell the flip up covers separately, you must buy the entire
thing. The city water filler costs $29, I imagine the others are similarly
priced.
