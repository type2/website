Model Summary for 1987 Vanagon USA


Seven models of the Vanagon were built for model 
year 1987.  These were: Vanagon GL, Vanagon GL Syncro, Vanagon 
Camper, Vanagon Camper Syncro, Vanagon GL Camper, Vanagon 
GL Camper Syncro, and the Vanagon GL Wolfsburg Limited Edition.

Colors available: Pastel White, Mocca Brown, Savanah Beige 
Metalic, Marine Blue, Flash Silver Metalic, Dove Blue Metalic,  
Titian Red Metalic, Wolfram Grey Metalic. Not all colors available
on all models.

Distinguishing exterior features:
There are no exterior features to distinguish an '87 from the '86 
model year.  The 1987 has carryover features from 1986 including:
2 Black plastic front grills (common to 1986-1991)
Chromed steel bumpers
Rectangular primary headlights, seperate square high beam lights
(common to 1986-1991)
"Volkswagen" with VW logo on left side of rear hatch

The 1987-1/2 Wolfsburg Edition was the first Vanagon with the aero
styled urethane covered bumpers.  These bumpers are metalic grey 
and there are no matching rocker panels.  This model also has body
colored rear view mirrors and a new ride height lowered by 1.2".
