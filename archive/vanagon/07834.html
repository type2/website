Because they're meant too! You can just move the radio connection to an 
ignition  controlled terminal at the fuse panel if you don't like it. Might 
need a twinned spade lug to do it if those terminals are all full. My Rabbit 
shuts off the (factory) radio with the ignition, something I hate when 
waiting around, since all those red lights on the dash are annoying just to 
get the radio. My Honda has a radio only position on the switch, with the 
key in. I think VW wants you to enjoy the radio in your van when resting, 
without leaving any keys in the ignition. I usually rewire the radio to be 
available anytime, for convenience. The headlight thingie is for us absent 
minded professors, just like the drivers door requiring your key to lock it.
