This may sound like a silly question, but do you have an electric fuel pump
on your Bus?  A mechanical pump won't make any noise while it's working, but
an electric is noisy as hell.  I have a new electric on my '73 (to replace my
questionable old electric) and have not had one single problem with it -
except for the noise!  It makes a repeated tapping sound continuously, like
lightly rapping a hammer on metal.  At any rate, crawl under your Bus & poke
around in front of the rear wheels and a bit towards the center.  Look around
on the frame - you may see a small brass or silver-colored box with two fuel
hoses, one on each end.  That's your fuel pump (if you DO happen to have an
electric one!) and possibly the cause of your noise!  

Hope this helps!
