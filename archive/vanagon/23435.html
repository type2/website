Come on guys! You have to do an oil change and valve adjustment every
3000 miles anyway. It takes 2.6 quarts or litres fo fill the
crankcase. You are already greasy and covered with oil, so why not
clean out the oil-bath air cleaner while you are at it, and dump the
remaining .4 litre of oil in the oil bath. Where I buy oil (Kragen),
the cost is about $.89 per quart, so replacing the oil in the oil bath is 
only $.38, tax included. That's a lot cheaper than a paper element,
and guaranteed not to rip, tear, or get oil fouled.
