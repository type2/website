Sounds like upper control arm busshings.  Dealer gets big money for these.
 Available at  good FLAPS for half price.  Read bentley manual for
instalation.  Must tack welded to hold them.  Proper shims very important.
 Come in three sizes.  Buy set from dealer.  Sohld be snug fit around frame
head.  To loose with bolt pulling arm together will cause quick failure or
lots of squeking noises.
