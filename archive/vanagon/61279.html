motorhomes, a '96, and a '97 model. The '97 has the VR6 and the '96 has the
Audi inline 5.
These are the motorhomes built by Winnebago on the extended VW Eurovan
chassis. 
I looked at them out of curiosity as I've never been inside one before, and
they are quite comfortable.

a/c
full kitchen and microwave
TV
VCR
real tiolet (you don't have to carry this one out and dump it behind a
tree..)
shower
2 twin beds
and so on..
