is true, the oil must be GL4 and GL4 only, apaarently the GL rating

high performance VW know.  Some say Redline MTL, GL4 but an 80W90 I
believe is OK, parrticularly for vans but others claim horror stories.

choice is Redline MT90 but it is cautioned the Redline seems to be
out of business now days, most suppliers still have stock though.
