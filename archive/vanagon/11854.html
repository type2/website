	getting out of the way, remain in sight of the pump
	handle. once on a trip through ohio i stopped at
	a 76 station, and because i wanted to reach chicago
	before nightfall i set up that little lock lever
	on the pump handle and let her blow, while i ran
	off to the bathroom (trying to save time). when i
	came out there was a sea of gasoline spilling out
	all over the place like old faithful. the station
	owner just sat there watching, so i ran to stop
	it . the station owner blamed my car, but it
	was something with his pump, and as i was in
	a rush and had no time to argue, i had to
	leave the place paying for all that gas that
	spilled !
	moral: two things at once, maybe not better.
		and, gasoline is slippery on cement >*:
