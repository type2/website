I just had a problem with the driver side speaker of my syncro.  It ended
being a short in one of the speaker wires.  Anyway, i did buy a new set of
pioneer speakers for the fornt and they fit pretty good, sound alot better
too.  I did have to bend the window crank a bit to clear it. ( the speaker
sits out approximately 1/4").  The key is to position the speakers as far as
possible forward so that the crank handles only need a little tweaking.  So
much for OEM.
