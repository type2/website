Um, does it have gas?  :)  Seriously, sounds like maybe your fuel pump is
broken or one of the fuel lines came disconnected.  Could also be a clogged
filter.  I'd unhook it at the carb and put the end of the line in a jar and
crank it over a few times and see if any gas comes out.  (Disconnect wire
going to + side first.)  Also, I think if the fuel pump breaks, sometimes
you get gas in your oil, so check the dipstick to see if there's gas on it.
