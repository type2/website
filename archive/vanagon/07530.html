Free... Gratis... No charge.

There was an article in a local free (oh oh... there's the word free again!)
computer rag called Computer Currents entitled "MCI's Friends & Family E-Mail
Service."  I called the 800 number in the article (800-624-9862) and 
asked for more info.  So... I got lots of info, and they are mailing me 
the software.  

The free part is receiving mail.  But they said that right now you can also
send 10 messages a month for free, $0.25/msg after that.  The whole service
is free for MCI customers, and $5/month for non-MCI people.  And they also
have alot of extra services that they charge for.  The service is accessed
from another 800 number, so overall I would think that it would be good for
anyone in the country that wants to get any mailing lists.
