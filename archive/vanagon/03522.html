Try this option: cost alot less and actually looks good.

Almost from any home repair/hardware store go to the area
where they sell the indoor/outdoor carpeting.  It's clear
(mine also has raised sort of grip lines running across) and
has the small no-skid 'tits' on the bottom.  Everyone has
seen this stuff - it's usually used in buildings/homes/etc.
and strips are laid down in the high traffic areas.  Anyway, the
stuff is on a spool, about four or six feet wife.  Buy what you
need, get out the scissors, and cut to fit.
