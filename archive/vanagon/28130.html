want to wait on me. I had to actually ask, after waiting there with

gotten this sort of a deal from other parts places -- in fact, my
FLAPS seems to always quote me one price over the phone, then charge
less when I actually get there to buy it. But they don't have all 
the goodies that a VW specialty house does either.
