Don't get the ones from the Bus Boys. They had cheap, non-heat resistant
paint, which smelled awful and burned away, letting the muffler be
covered with rust within a week. 

They are not legal in California, at least not on my fuel injected, EGR
equipped, 75 bus.

It's sometimes painful to work on your engine, 'cause those 4 protruding
tips are HOT!

There is a bump in my bumper and so the muffler almost touches it: at low
idle, it vibrated against the bumper, which makes a lot of noise.
