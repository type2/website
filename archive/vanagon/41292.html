I found, what is for me, the perfect solution.  It's something called a
"hoop rack," which they hooked me up with at my LBS (local bike shop).  It
is like a big U, upside down, with the ends getting bolted on to the
bumbper with a neat U-Bolt and hook clamp type thing.  It works absolutely
wonderfully for what I need.  Mounts on the front without having to find
neat spots to weld, I think it will fit on top of a front-mounted tire.

It even (kind of) solves your locking problem:  If you lock your bike to
it, a thief would have to unbolt the sucker or beat your locking mechanism
(always a threat anyway).  Hope this helps.
