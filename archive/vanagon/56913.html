I haven't investigated the wiring to this fan, but during our two 
week trip last April in Or & Ca, with outside temps from 40 -70 f, 
running on propane & 12v the fridge worked like a charm. In fact the 
2nd or 3rd day in, after leaving the fridge on max, we awoke to 
frozen milk!
I know the fan does work as it comes on it hot weather, but I don't 
recall it ever coming on during this trip, since it was cool outside.
Also experience since then has shown the gas to be more efficent than 
110v, by maybe 25%.
