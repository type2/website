This is good input from Helmut.

My experience is limited to passenger tires.

There may be one of two reasons for the light steering seen below:

  1) Change in tire brand.

  2) Tires with higher reinforcement probably have a narrower contact patch,
     thereby reduce required steering torque (most probable).
