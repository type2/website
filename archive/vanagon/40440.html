> No grey water tank.  From the sink drain (under the car) there is a length
> of PVC pipe that diverted the flow to the driver's side of the vehicle.
> The mud puddle formed behind the driver's door.  Just put a bucket under
> there to catch the "liquids".

Not sure if it changed for the '73...  In the '72 there was a cap on the 
end of this longish pipe.  Cap off it drained.  Cap on it was a longthin 
holding tank.  We would leave it loose enough that it slow drained.  
Puddles while stopped but spread if moving at all.  With the cap tight 
you could wait and dump anywhere.

Our '72 ahd ugly tan vinyl rear seats.  greyish brown not really tan for
