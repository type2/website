On my '80 Camper there is no wire behind the dashboard on the flexible
wiring board which will supply a tach. Originally it came without tach,
clock and, of cause, without water temp indicator.

I had to run a wire from the coil #1 (green wire) to the tach,
the regulated power from the dashboard regulator and the ground wire.
Be sure to have a tach for 4 cylinder engines.
