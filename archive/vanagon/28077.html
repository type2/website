I just gotta put my two cents about The Parts Place.

In July I got a catalog from them, which got me VERY excited.  Seemed 
that they had ALL of the parts that I needed to finish off my '70 
Westy. I called them, and attempted to place my order.

The catalog showed used rear bumper brackets for my Bus for $9.95.  I 
wanted two.  "Sorry" I was told, "the price on those is $19.95."  I 
directed the salesman to the page, and catalog number of the item. "OK" 
he said, "$9.95 it is then."  Next I ordered two rear marker lights, 
and a dashboard idiot light for the brakes.  I was told that they had 
these all of these in stock.  I gave him my credit card number, and 
waited patiently

During this time we were to be off on vacation.  Upon my return I 
looked anxiously for my package, only to find a post card saying that 
NONE of the items I had ordered were available.  Needless to say, I am 
not too thrilled with "The Parts Place."
