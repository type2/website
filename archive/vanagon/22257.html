I just saw the news story about the bus wreck yesterday.

It was a mid-'60s kombi or standard, and it looks like the other car
hit it in the side, just behind the driver's door, on a path towards
the front/passenger side.

I had a "discussion" with my SO about whether they were wearing seat belts
or not.  She thinks seat belts are an automatic "get out of death free"
card.  They aren't.  They help, but when the other car parks in your lap,
there's not much you can do.

I don't know how much seat belts would have affected
the outcome of this accident - there was pretty heavy intrusion into the
cab of the bus, but the front end (nose) was relatively unscathed.

I am definitely going to see about putting belts into my bus now.
