They also make a 370 which is a passenger tire - I thought this was

Just checked mine - the sidewall says: RADIAL 370 (in large letters). 
Other ways to tell are the size itself: LT195/75R14 (where LT =light 
truck), Max PSI (65) and weight rating (1710 lbs) This tire comes in 
Load range C & D. I got mine (load range D) for ~$108 CDN from BigO 
Tire.

They're not a common tire, but worth seeking out. I would not 
recommend *any* passenger tire on a Vanagon.
