          When I bought My 1986 Vanagon in 1990, I made the 
          mistake of putting 10w-30 weight oil into it. Shortly 
          after that the oil light would come on at idle, after
          running it hard under a load.
             
          WARNING!!! DO NOT USE 10w-30 WEIGHT OIL IN YOUR VANAGON!

          I use 10w-40 all year round hear in W. PA, BUT It is
          important to use heavier oil in hotter Weather, (summer) 
          lighter oil in extreme colder weather (winter).
