A common and unfortunately expensive problem is head gasket failure.
Drive the car until warm. Park on a cardboard (pappkartong) or similar.
Watch for a while. Stop engine. Watch under for maybe 30 min. This 
catches some external leaks. (Large) front heater core, valve and hose
leaks can sometimes be seen by looking under the dash (instrumentbrada) 
on the passenger side.
