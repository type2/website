in the correct shape. This shape is dependant on tire pressure and load, or
axle weight. IE: The pressure varies for a given load. And further to this, the 
vehicle manufacturer who knows the axle loads, and the type of tire specifies 
the correct pressure for that application.

Further I assume the wieght distribution is directly related to the tire

back axle.
