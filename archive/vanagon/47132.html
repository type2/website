it's enough to get the Eurovan engines rebuilt/replaced gratis by VW!  An 
'92 was FS here with new factory engine for that reason, and I remember a 
couple of listies talking the same.  Slap is from the bearings at the little 
end of the con-rod going bad, so the piston gets thrown out and clunks 
against the bvearing at top-DC. Can also be caused by piston pins loose in 
piston, the piston is still getting slapped about.  Not good in the long 
run.  Sounds like you are running on poor grade gas with the timing way off. 
 May go away as the engine warms and oil gets up to the piston crowns, watch 
that excuse.
