Last March I posted about Bud's Bugs, a VW-only wreaking yard in Concord,
CA, that was having a "$20 Sale."  I offered to find and ship parts while
the sale was on, and several people got some parts they were needing.

I wandered back into Bud's Bugs today, and they're having their "$20
Sale" again; their flyer for the sale says "$20 For-Just-About-Anything
In-The-Yard SALE.  (excludes complete engines, cars and 1985 and newer
parts.)"

Unlike last time, I'm not going back out that way anytime soon (didn't
find anything I need) but I was told they have a web site
(www.budsbugs_dot_com) so you can email them with any requests.  (I was also
told they will ship smaller parts UPS.)

They didn't seem to have a ton of Bus and Vanagon stuff, but they had
some, including what looked to be a complete '71 camper with pop-top, so
you might get lucky.
