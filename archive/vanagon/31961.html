way,  I do not pull heads to repair small external leaks.  I use a leak down
tester to confirm there are no internal leaks.  If okay I use aradiator
sealer.  I warm the engine slightly, drain some coolant, then add sealer. I
then run the engine to circulate the sealer through the block and heads and
shut the engine down before the thermostat opens.  I then let the vehicle sit
for a few hours. Then drive normally.  I have a near 100% success with this
method.  Remember I am talking small leaks,  no gushers.
