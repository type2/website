a word of caution, however:

when importing these things into the u.s., our Friendly Federal Government,
in its unceasing effort to protect its Citizens from Themselves, and, at the
same time, protect its auto-makers from the Hordes of Volkswagen Trucks that
would descend upon these shores, will slap a twenty-five percent import tax
on that sucker! 25 percent!

point being that this fellow, trying to sell his already u.s. legal vw truck,
is also trying to recoup that expense of the tax. not to mention all the
fees and paperwork stuff that is additional to the big tax. i checked into
it a while back (wanted a double-cab non-syncro) and the price was to be
$8000 u.s., but the taxes and import duty ran it up to about $11000. :(

just be aware that if the truck is licensed in canada, and you want it in the
Unfriendly South (u.s., that is), well ... it's gonna cost ya. :)
