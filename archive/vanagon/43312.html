Just a thought:

Maybe this is an electrical problem, not in gear: no ground, in gear: ground
and the starter gets (more) juice. On my '73 I would check the groundstrap
from transmission case to body. You could also check with a jumper cable
directly between battery negative and the starter motor housing.
