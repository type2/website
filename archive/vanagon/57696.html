Check the ground connection for the headlights.  Believe it or not, they 
brought the headlight ground back to the dash. Remove the fuse box and check 
the grounds just above to the left,(those clusters).  Both headlight grounds 
are on the same connector.  You may see the insulation starting to burn off.  
I separate the headlights to two separate connections and problem won't happen 
again.  You may also want to actually ground the lights right near them.

Dennis
