     I made a few phone calls yesterday looking for good drop sides for my

     restore a REAL basket case pick-up, he suggested that I should buy

     dull and there's zero rust - it's like that.
     
     It also has all the bows and canvas and a few NOS pickup only bits.
     
     The price? 2000 ukp
     
     I weighed up the cost of doing a *complete* resto on a basket case of 
     buying this rustfree one and just washing it!
     
     I'm going for the rustfree one, of course.
