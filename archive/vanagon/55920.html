YES! There is a direct relationship to year(s) and color. Although any
specific color may be used in multiple years, you can distinguish a 
range of years that a certain color was produced. I have studied this
issue - although not in a perfect research method. We could probably
build a list, by year of colors produced - at least for the US/Can.
vehicles.

My '80 was 'pumpkin orange' although the title/reg listed it as 'brown'

	Blue (I need to look at the brochure for the exact name - med dk)
