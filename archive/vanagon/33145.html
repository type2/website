I was driving my drafty old '69 in Pennsylvania one winter day and stopped
to get Wendy's french fries (the fat, ---steaming---, fluffy kind - BTW, I
have no personal affiliation with Wendy's). This was my get-warm ritual.
Well, this particular day was about 20 degrees and 100% relative humidity.
As I sat in my beast eating the french fries, the steam rose to the
ceiling, cooled on its way down, and, I sh*t you not, fell as snow into the
speedo cup to a depth of about a millimeter!

There! can anyone else claim actual meteorlogic activity inside their bus?
I think (also hope) not.
