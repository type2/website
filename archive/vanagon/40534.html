Hi Mark.  I had the same problem when I was enquiring about this.  I 
currently have a the Syncro Special filter installed... had it for 3 
years now, and it's still clean!!  The dirt trap prior to the filter 
box does a good job of getting rid of most of the dirt before it hits 
the air filter.

Up here, a Syncro filter is $134 CDN, versus about $40 for the 
regular filter.  Both will work.  My dealer doesn't even stock the 
Syncro filters as they use the regular filter in the Syncro.  A 
Syncro filter could last up to 5 years.  The Syncro filter is useful 
if you are in extreme dust conditions.

Hope this helps
Harry.
