Hey all,
Filled up the Vanagon gas tank today (didn't realize I was so low on
gas, the tank has a dent in the bottom so the gauge stops right outside
the red zone -- It took about 13.7 gal in a 16-gal tank) and drove home.
When I got out of the van, I smelled gas, so looked down to investigate
and sure enough, right as I looked I saw a drop hit the ground, smelled
the liquid and it was gas.  

My first thought was "aw sh*t, I gotta drop the tank" but further
inspection showed that the only thing leaking was one of the rubber
cloth-covered hoses that leads to the vent tank in the driver's wheel
well.  That one hose was soaked with gas while the others were dry, and
I didn't see any leaking on the passenger side.

So, I have to replace one hose.  No big deal, it's in plain view and I'm
happy.  The question is what size is the hose?  Looks like about 7mm.
