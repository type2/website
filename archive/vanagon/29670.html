> My immediate thought was flooding, so I searched in the Bentley book but

I recently experienced a similar problem and now know it was related to 
timing, effected by my distributor shaft being incorrectly installed. 
Once I adjusted my timing (way out), the stalling when I accelerate 
ceased. You might look at the Finding TDC posts and go from there to see 
if your distributor is actually installed a notch or two off.
