   I don't know if I can contribute any words of wisdom for your idle
problem- maybe you're getting water in there someplace. I have a
problem whenever the roads get really wet- I come to a stop and she
begins to idle rough and then stalls. I gave the electrical contacts a
quick spray of silicon, but I guess I'll have to wait until it rains
again to see if it's done the trick.
   In defence of the 85, I've owned mine (a bronze Westy now named
Indica) for a year now and have made 2 trips from here (Toronto) to Halifax
(ahhh, Halifax....), a trip to North Carolina, and have driven all
over Southern/ Eastern Ontario as well as using her as a daily driver
all winter in TO while I've been on co-op. Never once, with the
exception of a frozen gas line early in the winter, has she ever let me down. 
Sure the cooling system is poorly designed and the 1.9L is
underpowered. Sure it handles like a pig in the wind. But I strongly
believe that if I take care to keep an eye on all of the known areas
of difficulty that this van will always get me wherever I'm going and
back again.... and it certainly looks much better than those new
eurotrashers... :)
