David,

All you have to fight with is the maximum allowed
axle weight for your vehicle and the loadindex of
your tires.
On my 2WD Vanagon (and all other European spec
Transporters, except those with reduces load capacity)
will carry 1100 kg front and 1300 kg rear, total
allowed weight is 2360 kg. The tires at least should
have the loadindex of 93:

205/70 R 14 93 N   (as an example)
