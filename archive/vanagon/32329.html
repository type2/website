Yup. Most companies such as Transform, install existing ring&pinion
combinations from other trannies or sources that have lower ratios. Some
Vanagon trannies from Europe have slightly lower ratios, and these R&Ps
can be transplanted. But the change in final ratio is very slight (0.82 vs. 
0.85,
for example). Only if you somehow custom-fabricate your own R&P, or have
a shop do it (anyone know of a shop that will, or even can, do this?), can 
you
get a ratio lower than this.
