   NHTSA CAMPAIGN ID Number: 92V058000
   Make: VOLKSWAGEN TRUCK
   Model: VANAGON
   Year: 1991
   Causing Fault : POOR FIT/LOOSE
   Resulting Fault : FIRE, FLUID,ELECTRICAL,GAS(LPG),GASOLINE,OTHER
   Component: FUEL:FUEL LINES FITTINGS:METALLIC
   Manufacturer: VOLKSWAGEN OF AMERICA,INC
   Year of Recall: 1992
   Type of Report: Vehicle
   Potential Number of Units Affected: 358,000
   Summary:
   THE FUEL HOSE ATTACHED TO THE FUEL DISTRIBUTION RAIL ON THE ENGINE MAY
   SHRINK BEYOND THE CLAMP FORCE OF THE FUEL HOSE CLAMPS, ALLOWING FUEL
   TO LEAK.
   
   FUEL LEAKAGE IN THE ENGINE COMPARTMENT CAN CAUSE ANUNDERHOOD FIRE IF
   THE FUEL COMES IN CONTACT WITH A SOURCE OF IGNITION.
   
   REPLACE THE FUEL HOSE ATTACHED TO THE FUEL RAIL AND INSTALL A NEW
   SPRING TYPE SELF-TIGHTENING CLAMP.
   
   SYSTEM: FUEL LINES.
   
   VEHICLE DESCRIPTION: PASSENGER CARS.
   
   NOTE: VOLKSWAGEN OF AMERICA ERRONEOUSLY INCLUDED 1985 THROUGH 1989
   VANAGONMODEL VEHICLES IN THIS RECALL. THESE VEHICLES ARE NOT PART OF
   THIS CAMPAIGN.SUPPLEMENT 92V058001 INFORMATION HAS BEEN ADDED TO THIS
   CAMPAIGN.
