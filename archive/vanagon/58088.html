That black stuff is the filter material after the evaporator.  It main 
function is to collect the water that condenses on the coil and keep it from 
blowing into the ductwork with the air.  It also collects dust and pollen 
which is then carried away with the water as it drains down.  It should be 
replaced. Without it, water will start spraying out the vents.  It is a hell 
of a job as the cabinetry has to be disassembled.  The material gets ruined 
from the heat in the Van when parked in the sun with everything closed.  Go to 
an AC supplier and get metal screening like that used on range hoods to 
replace that plastic junk.

Dennis
