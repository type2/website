I've got something called a "Kat's Volkswagen Engine Heater" on my '66 
camper and '63 bug.  It is a 150 Watt heater that bolts to the bottom of 
the engine and keeps the oil warm.  Both start and run reliably with no 
other modifications in extreme cold (The coldest I've started at is -47 F)

Anyway, Kat's makes two different models of the heater--one for upright 
engines and one for '72 and up (I'm not sure if it would fit the 
watercooled engines or not).  They cost about $20 and should be available 
in import auto part stores where ever it gets cold.  If you want one and 
can't find a source, let me know and I'll ship one out.  BTW, no 
affiliation with Kat's etc.
