Just kidding you... I'll assume that you have a Canadian Bus (with the
retractable extension cord). I have a California '75, the Deluxe version
with electrical fridge and it does NOT work, even though the electrical
stuff is all functional (i.e., the heating element does get hot). I
believe this is because the ammonia mix is all gelified (is that a word?).
Maybe by letting the fridge run for a few days in a row, it will resume
its function as a cooler.

So try to fix your electrical problem first and check whether the heating
element is working. *Then* let it run for a couple of days. (The heating
gizmo is a small copper cylinder that you'll find behind the fridge inside
a fiberglass wool insulated tube.)
