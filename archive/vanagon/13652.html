The following worked well on the rear window washer on my 82 accord.  The
washer had been plugged for eons.  Eventually I got fed up.  I disconnected
the hose leading to the nozzle from the fluid reservoir (easy to get to)
and stuffed it into the end of a high pressure bicycle pump.  At about 60
psi a wad of dirt came flying out of the nozzle and everybody lived happily
after.  Any source of compressed air would probably work.
