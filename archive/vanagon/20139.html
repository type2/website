David,
What I've always done with my cars (carbureated and FI'd) here in 
Colorado is to advance the timing 1 degree for everything 1000 ft. above 
sea level.  They don't run worth a darn at the factory specs.  I've had 
my rabbit for the last 120,000 miles and have never had a problem.  It's 
currently getting 30s to 40s mpg in mixed city/highway with this setup.  
The van (81 Westy) gets around 22 on the highway with the same setup.
Jack
