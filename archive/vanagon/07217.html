Unless I misunderstood something here, I don't think this is true for
the Vanagon.  I believe the other water-cooled VWs have slide controls
for heating/venting/ac.  But the Vanagons slide controls are only for
heating/venting and a separate pair of dial knobs control the AC.

So you'd have to manually turn it on every once in a while to get
things lubed.
