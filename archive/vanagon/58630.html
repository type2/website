We offer a USA made Turbo-Style Quiet muffler that flows better than the

installed on that year period), but I'm sure a good muffler/exhaust shop
could weld the proper flanges in place and modify it for your installation.

inquiries (most people wanna quiet their exhaust, not make it "rumble").  But
