Here's some Barndoor Rear Hatch trivia for you:

Part #         Description                        Type and Model

211 829 031    Rear panel lid without window      211-238
                 up to chassis No. 20-117 901

221 829 031    Rear panel lid with window         211-238
                 up to chassis No. 20-117 901

271 829 031    Rear panel lid                     271-274
                 up to chassis No. 20-117 901

So there were not rear hatches "officially" available for barndoor 
deluxes, only panels, microbuses, kombis, and ambulances.
