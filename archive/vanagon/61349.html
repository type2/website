Here are some of the bus related NE shows I know of:

June 21 (Sat), Litchfield, CT - Worldwide day of the Beetle; Looking Glass Hill Campground.  
Sponsored by CVA and GASS (German Air Sucker Society).  Worldwide VW celebration.  LiMBO 
sponsored potluck dinner w/chili at $1/bowl.  The campground needs reservations and 
pre-payment to guarantee spots, call (860)567-2050.

June 22 (Sun), Litchfield, CT - Litchfield Bug-Inn; Litchfield Inn, Rt. 202.  Largest all VW 
Car show & swap meet in New England.  22 classes with over 80 awards.  Reg. $6, spectators 
$2, swap space $15.  Proceeds to charity. LiMBO will be parking & judging all buses this 
year.  We need volunteers for help on this, call Stan_at_LiMBO (508)651-3502.  For show info. 
call Chuck Pisconski (203)283-5256.

June 27-29 (Fri-Sun), Hopkinton, NH - Kampers at Keyers Pond & NH VW  show; Hopkinton State 
fairground.  Miniature golf tourney for kids & adults (Sat.), need volunteers to help on 
this event.  Call John Hathaway (603)595-5957.

July 18-20 (Fri-Sun), N. Adams, MA - Transporters by the Tunnel; campout near Mt. Greylock 
and the Hoosac tunnel Historic Valley campground.  Lots of great busing fun with pancake 
breakfast, pot-luck dinner, swimming, movies for the kids, and more.  New this year, tubing 
down the Deerfield River.  Call Jim DiGennaro (413)743-1814 or e-mail limbo_at_bcn_dot_net.
