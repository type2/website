It sounds like you might have more significant problems than just the coolant
leak.  Perhaps combustion gases leaking from the cylinders into the coolant.
 Perhaps a clogged radiator.  Perhaps a stuck thermostat.

At this point it couldn't hurt to use the Barsleak.

Make sure to check out the cooling system thougoughly (primarily the
radiator) before running the new engine.  You don't want to damage the new
engine with a clogged radiator!!
