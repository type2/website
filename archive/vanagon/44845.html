As a card-carrying-hyper-paranoid-anal-retentive type I must add that I
heard somewhere the surface of the valve adjuster screw is hardened, so
filing it might yield you an adjuster that is more prone to wear.  Now I
don't know if or how long it will take for such wear to evidence itself and
I should add that I've been known to reuse old, non pitted valve adjusters
that have lost some of the curvature they originally had on their face.  My
guess is if you check the adjustment at 3,000 mile intervals anyway, even
the filed surface shouldn't let you down significantly enough to worry about.
