limbo_at_bcn_dot_net (Jim Digennaro) asked  "Just wondering if anyone knows what the
Horse power and torque specifations are for 2.0L aircooled, 1.9L and 2.1L
engines are?  "


Taken from the original 1982 dealer booklet (obtained from the dealer when I
bought my '82 diesel vanagon)describing the Vanagon and Camper vehicles,
features, colors, etc --
      
                           Std. Gas.            Optional Diesel

Bore  x stroke, in        3.70 x 2.80             3.01 x 3.4

Displacement cu in/cc       120/1970               97/1588

Compression ratio            7.3:1                  23.0:1

Hp (SAE net) _at_ rpm          67 _at_ 4200              48 _at_ 4200

Torque (SAE net _at_ rpm       101 _at_ 3000             71.5 _at_ 2000
