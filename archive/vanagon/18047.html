Thought I'd share with other Eurovan folks a windfall I stumbled onto.

While rummaging this weekend I got an urge to stop at a used tire dealer  (other than 
intuition, I can't explain why).

As soon as I drove on the property, the dealer came out and said to me, "have I got a 
deal for you!!"

As we talked, seems that through, this dealers actions, he'd purchased several lots of 
tires from somewhere.  In one of the lots were varying odd sizes but for the dealer, it 
was a "winner take all" situation ... he took the whole group.

In the lot, the dealer had a set of Goodyear G-64, 6-ply, 195-70 and had no idea about 
what they fit or how to get rid of them.

When I arrived he immediately recognized the tires on my Eurovan and "knew 
something had brought me" to him.

We inspected the tires, measured the tread and all that stuff.  While I don't think the 
tires are new, there appeared to be minimal tread-wear.

The dealer asked if I would buy the tires and he'd stand by some type of warranty or 
help to cover expenses if they went bad, anytime soon. I agreed and ...surprise of 
surprises!!!

The dealer was so happy to get rid of them ... I paid $15 per tire for the set.

I took one to a Goodyear dealer and had it checked. The dealer thinks the tire had 
been driven less than a thousand miles (questimate). We could find no defects.

While I'm still amazed at this... seems the genie of "VWdom" has smiled my way!
