The grease you're looking for is Molybdendium DiSuphide. It's black in color
and it does have it's own smell that I can't really describe. Most auto
stores have some form of it, Wolfs Head is now making a wheel bearing grease
that's moly based. Go to your local auto parts store and ask for a tube of
moly grease, it works here.
