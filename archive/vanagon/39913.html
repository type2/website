Tom,

There are some differences between '65 and '66  that are immediately 
noticeable.  The most obvious is the engine decklid and rear apron.  
The '65 has the "church-key" type while the '66 (and '67 has the 
"finger-pull" latch type.  The rear view mirror also changed between 
'65 & '66.

The other one I can think of off the top of my head is the wiper 
motor.  The '65 has a seperate wiper motor with linkages going to the 
wiper shafts.  The '66 has a one piece unit that has the motor, 
linkages, and wiper shafts all together.

go on the NEATO Technical Data Page (along with a ton of other stuff!).
