Hi Michael,

No, there is no reservation possible if your friends decide to camp on
BBTA ground. If they come days early, they could off course still stay at
this campground, but note that the daily charge will be $15. The $10 per
night charge is a BBTA special. Still I doubt that a reservation would be
necessary. 

If your friends come late, they may not get the best *spots*, but they
will definitely have no problem finding a spot at all. Camping at BBTA is
(or at least used to be) freeform.
