I'd just like to pile on in support of the lowly Jetta. I bought mine
before it was fashionable to have them, way back in 1982. My girlfriend at
the time told me it was an "old man's car". She bought one herself in 1986!

It was a great car, even if a bit under powered, but it took me faithfully
everywhere I wanted to go. I pretty much scoured the paint off the front of
the car because I took it skiing so much!

I put over 160,000 miles on it, with no engine work at all.

Just my $0.02!
