>how do I stop the leak, how do I tighten it and should I be careful of the

That's the access to the oil pressure relief valve - when you remove that
screw ( I used the widest screwdriver I could find + 'help' from a 6"
crescent wrench) you'll find a spring, and on top of the spring a simple
piston.  The piston 'should' come out with a magnet: depending on your '87's
miles & oil change intervals, varnish may hang it up.  *BUT* it's not
necessary to remove it - just unscrew the cap, goop it up with pipe thread
sealant (*NOT* tape - use pastey Teflon stuff), & you'll be home free, no
mo' leaks!
