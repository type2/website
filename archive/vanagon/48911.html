These are the problems I've seen first hand:

Oil pressure light comes on on both '87 & '88 Vanagons after long 
highway run; same thing on two '85 Jetta diesel & turbo diesels.
On both diesels I noted a difference of 5-10 psi using FRAM vs VW 
filters and 20w50 oil. (hot oil pressure). This is using a mechanical 
OP gauge mounted on cylinder head.

I presume this lower OP is similar on Vanagons, and on older engines 
with more wear (and lower OP) can be enough to trigger the OP warning light.

I feel better with the VW filters, since around here Bosch cost 
*more*.
