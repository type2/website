Hmm.  I'm pretty sure you DON'T want to take that plate/column/collar thing
apart, as it's kind of hard to get back together with the column in the
bus.  I believe, though, that in answer to your question that the plastic
collar is split longitudinally (sp?) and you can squeeze it together
slightly and then pull it up out of the plate.  But I think it's got a lip
on the inside of it that prevents it from sliding up on the column.  As I
said, it's best to avoid taking that apart.  It IS hard to get to the
connector you mentioned, but not THAT hard.  I believe that the connector is
attached to the metal plate you see inside, the one that's held to the floor
by two screws.  You should be able to put the wire on from underneath without
taking anything apart.  You need to turn the wheel slightly so that the
steering coupler (discussed some last week) is positioned such that it's
not blocking access to the male connector.  Hope this helps...
