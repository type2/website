After installing new speakers and new stereo in my 83 Westy the speakers   
still buzzed.  Knowing it wasn't the speakers or stereo I found it was   
the speaker wires that for some reason were muffling the signal and   
creating distortions.  I rewire with new wire and the problem was solved.   
 Very cheap fix.  Good luck.
