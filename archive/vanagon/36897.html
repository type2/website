Not that it is of any value, but I wired up my radio the other 
month, and whenever I turned on the windshield wipers my radio would 
go off and on!  

So I made sure last Saturday when I was installing the new CD 
player, that I wouldn't have the same problem. 
I got out the voltmeter, and with the ignition off found a live 12v.
Hooked it all up.

It worked great.  Went into the house, did whatnot and decided to go 
for a drive.  No power to the stereo when I tried it again!

I thought, oh well, and took off, thinking I'd fix it later.  That 
night on my way home, I tried it again to see if it would perchance 
work.  Yup!  Worked great.

Huh?  Turn off lights.  Off goes radio!  Turn on lights, on goes 
radio!

Seems I had the headlights on when I was testing for an always live 
line to hook into!
