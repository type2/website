My '78 ran for years with compression in the range 90-115, and ran 
reasonably well. Three years ago it started getting worse (at about 
110kmiles) and cylinder two dropped to ~75psi. It still ran ok, but I 
had trouble passing emissions (high hydrocarbons). Then last year (at 
125kmiles) it dropped to ~50psi; still ran reasonably, but there was 
no way to pass emissions. So, I retired it to chicken coop status
until I can yank and rebuild. Anyway, it was leaking a lot of oil 
from the oil cooler.
