Those Four dots on the left of lever 4 are actually six dashes (on my 
'85).  Lever 4 controls the four roof vents, AND the two door vents, ergo 
SIX dashes.  These only emit cold air, so when it's cold out, I have this 
lever permanently to the right (off).  These six vents are even 
controlled by the fan (provided it isn't siezed and the circuit works 
#*$$%*#).
To shut off outside air, I put L1 to the left, and L2&4 to the right. 
When moving down the freeway, it does get a bit loud as the air getting 
rammed into the distribution box is trying to find somewhere to go...
