I been staying out of this thread for lack of knowledge, but this post by
Steve makes a lot of sense.  I now know a bit from hooking and unhooking
the vacuum lines during my transplant.  From what I remember from the
Bentley man (sitting on my desk at home), the late FI busses get *no*
advance til (a) some outrageously high RPM as sensed by some of those
little vacuum hoses, and (b) 4th gear is engaged.  Then it gets a fair
amount all at once (if I remember correctly).  Make sure the skinny vac
hoses are all plugged in correctly.  If that doesn't help, look in the
Bentley on the distributor.
