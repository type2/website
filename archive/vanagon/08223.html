Just went to see what the Woodinville Auto auction was all about,
and saw a couple of neat things; a 1979 bug convertible, a 1974 Thing,
a couple of mid-70's bugs, and a 1976(?) westy to be sold this Saturday.

see what all's there, and I stopped at a place called Gerry's foreign
auto parts, ltd. They had quite a few busses/bugs from all eras, including
a couple of vanagons (An `84, and a 8?), a `59 micro, a `62 single cab,
(Driver's rear all smashed. :( Or I might have considered it.) A relatively
complete 1976(?) westy, amoung other things. I guess I looked like a

They seemed kind of pricey to me, but YMMV.
