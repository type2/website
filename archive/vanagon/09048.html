>What are these belly pans?

As Mr. Bozo was my first Type II, I thought they were standard issue for
several years.  Not til much later did I crawl under another bus and see
the floor!  The pans have a cross pattern pressed into them from corner to
corner (like commercial a/c ducts) and are just welded on from rocker
panels to front and rear of cargo floor cross-braces.  The entire cargo
section is sealed.
