Hi all,

Once the following happens to the mirror you can drill completely through
the mirror arm in the same axes as the original stud that is pressed into
the mirror arm.  Then buy a stainless steel bolt of appropriate size  that
you replace the stud with.  The stainless steel bolt can be countersunk
into the exterior of the mirror arm and the cavity filled with silicon or
something like that.  Now you can tighten the nut against the spring all
you want to.
