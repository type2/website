On the rear brakes of my '85 vanagon there is a spring loaded clip on 
each side shoe.  Once you remove these three or four times (don't ask) 
they aren't so hard to get back replace.  Basically, I took a pair of 
pliers half opened and pinched the little metal "shield" and spring in 
one hand.  Then reached around and held the little pin in place with the 
other hand and ....PUUUUUUUUSHED...  
The tricky part is that the catch is a kind of needle and eye thing.  You 
have to push the pin through and then somehow turn it 180o so it 
catches.  With the spring loading it isn't so easy.  I shot the damn 
"shield" across most of Florida today and killed several small innocent 
woodland creatures ; )
