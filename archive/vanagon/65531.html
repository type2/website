Dave,
Check for the proper function of your engine temperature sensor II.
This device provides  the ECU with the basic information required for it
to further determine whether cold start enrichment/modified timing map
and/or idle air bypass is required to keep your engine running.  If this
sensor is working as intended, check your idle stabilizer and its
related control unit.  Based on your description, I would suspect that
your problem is with the temp sensor.
