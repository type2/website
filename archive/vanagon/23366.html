Mark Janello writes:

>Weren't there those things (no not Things!) which were based on
>a Type 3 chassis and made for the German post office?

The Fredolin was based on a Karmann Ghia floorpan, had what appeared
to be a Typ3 front end, sliding doors on both the driver and passenger
side and had the typical upright Typ1 engine. According to the VW
museum the official name is the "Kleinlieferwagen Typ 147", making
it a Typ1 vehicle.

As to the obligitory van connection of this vehicle...the body was
built by Westfalia.
