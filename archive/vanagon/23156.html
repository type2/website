Subject:       top bunk child/pillow net

This weekend i made a 'gate' to go across the opening of the top bunk 
to keep our young 'uns in place.  i used a pickup tailgate net per a 
previous poster's suggestion... picked one up at Target for $10 for 
the small/med size pickups.  it was still too long so i cut it down 6 
inches and re-glued it together with (what else?!) Shoe-Goo.  added 
some quick release buckles (for those 2 a.m. potty calls).  overall, 
i am very pleased with it and it will allow me to sleep better 
knowing no one is going to fall out.  had that happen once when Katie 
was 4.  i still don't know why she didn't get hurt and in fact i 
think she barely even realized what happened as she was so sleepy.

anyway, thanks to whomever had this idea to begin with.
