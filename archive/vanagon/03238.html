my water pump was making a rattling noise (but faint) and was visibly leaking
(look for chaulky bird droppings around the oil filter area). i was told the
noise was 'cavetation' ...like on a boat propeller. ??? i figured it was just
the shaft wallowing around in the bushing.

one way to tell for sure is to get a short section of tubing and put one end
to your ear and move the other end around the engine. if it is the water pump
making the noise, then the loudest (and clearest through the tube) will be
right round the pump area.

>How about the possibility that it's valve lifters or valves.  Do they sound
