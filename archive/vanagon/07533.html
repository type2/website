  Howdy Alice,

  The odometer started hanging up in my '74 about 5 years ago. This was a real
  problem for me since the fuel gauge sender had died long before and I was
  using the odo to keep track of when to get gas. I took it apart and it was
  the little white gear that had cracked as we have heard mentioned here on 
  the list before. 
> 
> I went to one of those "Pick your Part" junkyards and pulled speedometer 
  units out of buses AND took them apart until I found one without a broken
  gear. They only charged me $10.00 or so for the good unit and it has
  been working well now for about 45,000 miles.

  Dealer prices for that type of item a pretty outrageous. 


  Good Luck - I thought the junkyard trip was kinda fun too.
