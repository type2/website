Well, speaking for the two of us in B.C. (Hi Alistair), we're on Vancouver
Island; a place of great beauty.  Also surfing (not that I do any).  If the
tour can make it here, we're both within less than an hour's drive of each
other.  Not that we've ever met in person.

The problem with driving across Canada and the cheap Canadian dollar, is
that even with the extremely favourable exchange rate (for American $$$),
gas is significantly cheaper on the American side.  Also, fast food and
hotel accomodations (can you say breakdown?) are significantly less in the
States.  I drove across from Ontario to B.C. in December halfway through the
States, and found that for food and accomodation, we were the same in either
country, and gas was cheaper in the States. :-(
