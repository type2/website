I have a '79 engine but the heater boxes are rotten out.  As far as I can see 
the boxes and muffler are identical to the '72 to '74 systems except for the 
attachment to the manifold.  The '72 to 74 units used a type 4 connection 
with a oval copper washer while the '79 used a flat connection with a 
rectangular port.  The problem is to get the vanagon exhaust flange onto 
the the '72 to '74 heater bax.  The muffler was never the problem.
