Actually, an ammeter will only tell you weather the battery is charging or
discharging.  It is poosible for a run down battery to take a charge at only
11 or 12 volts.  A properly wired voltmeter will actually tel you the status
of the entire system.  Overloading the alternator or wiring will result in a
lower voltage.  A slipping belt will even be detectable under a load.
 Ammeters were used long ago when the charging system was current regulated.
 Voltage regulated systemsshould the the voltage monitored.
