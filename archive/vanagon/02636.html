T4 SYNCHRO JOINS AMBULANCE FLEET

An all-wheel drive Transporter synchro, the first of its kind to be used as 
a control unit and rapid response unit, has joined the 130-strong North
Yorkshire Ambulance Service Fleet.

Based on a short wheelbase Transporter syncro, with the 2.4 litre diesel
engine, the converted vehicle replaces a trailer unit which previously
had great difficulty negotiating the rough terrain in North Yorkshire.

Coverted by Taurus of Stockport, the center part of the van is used as
an incident control area, while the rear part is used to house 
medical equipment. Standard equipment includes a 40ft telescopic mast
which carries floodlighting and an addtitional areal, 240 electricity,
a fuel-burning heater and a generator.
