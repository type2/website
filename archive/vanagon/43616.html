I've changed the generator on a doghouse cooler, single port 1600 in my '70
and '71.  It wasn't so bad, once I remembered to unscrew the thermostat from
it's shaft.  After removing the screws on either side of the fan housing and
the generator strap and belt and 4 bolts on the fan plate, I remove the
carburator for extra room.  Then it's just a matter of lifting the fan
housing so it touches the engine compartment ceiling and coaxing the
generator out.  Not so bad once you know the routine!
