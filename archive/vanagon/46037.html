>
>Here are some approaches to checking and diagnosing cracks in cylinder
>heads and blocks:
>
>1.  Cylinder compression check
>2.  Do you have coolant in your oil or do you have oil in your coolant?
>3.  Professional Approaches include:
>    a)  Hydraulic pressure
>    b)  Magnetic inspection of ferrous parts
>    c)  Dye penetrant
>
>The professional approaches to checking for cracks all require dismantling
>and thorough cleaning of these parts prior to inspection.  I don't think
>there are any easy ways to check and locate cracks when the engine is in
>the vehicle, although maybe technology of today has an approach to this.
