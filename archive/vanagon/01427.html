 I would guess the back is low, rather than the front high. To fix it you'd
need to adjust or replace the rear torsion bars. It's probably not worth it.

 If you want a balanced look you could always lower the front. Best way seems
to be to get a sway-a-way front end which I understand changes the position
of the stops for the torsion bars.
