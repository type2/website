Ball joints are a possibility, and the cheapest and easier part to 
replace on the front end. I did my Jetta for ~ $50 - alignment was 
fine before, but steering was vague on sweeping corners. New ball 
joints helped a lot. After that, tie rod ends and A arm bushings are 
next, but harder to do, and more money...
