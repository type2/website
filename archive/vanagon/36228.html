>Anybody out there Know about factory fuel injection on my 1800cc

A little bit; I have one myself.

>The car is not getting fuel.

The double relay is a (3/4" by 3/4" by 2") device in the engine compartment
on the left-hand side of the fire wall, with 2 multiple connector plugs; it
should click when the engine is being cranked. There are also two fuses
that could be at fault. Disconnect the battery ground strap before you
replace the relay.

>Also, should I just trash the injection system and buy carbs?

No, I wouldn't do that. Although FI problems can be VERY frustrating, they
are relatively rare and I still believe that FI is a superior design to
carbs. I suggest you buy or borrow a book such as "how to tune and modify
Bosch fuel injection" by Ben Watson and read the chapter on the L-jetronic
system of your '75 bus. Just an opinion, of course.
