Actually back in the early 70's we had THREE drive-ins all within 15 =

Here's a few choice memories:

Ghia backwards into a spot at the drive-in, fold down the back seat, =

over the ridge on the back seat. And then crawl into the back seat area, =

backs of the front seat while looking at the move thru the rear window. =
.... and yes it IS possible to make love in the back of a Karman Ghia =

We once found a couch by the side of the road (awaiting pick-up by the =
trash collectors) while driving my 62 Typ2 Pick-up. So we tossed it in =
the back of the pick-up, where it stayed for the summer. We did the =
drive in a bunch of times with the pick-up....three in the cab, and =

at the drive in, we would take out the couch, put it in front of the P/U =
and enjoy the movie.
