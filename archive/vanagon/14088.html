I've also noticed this especially on a locally made three wheel recumbent
bicycle, (trike). The front wheel is driven and the two rear wheels, which
are just slightly behind your butt, steer. In order to reduce this
"magnification" effect, the makers went to great lengths installing
variable rate steering (less steering effect the more you increase the
turn) aswell as installing steering dampners. Its still not a great
solution but without these modifications you tended to overcontrol and
"snake" your way along.
