  Remember that the cylinders are not directly opposite each other. They have
  to be staggered a bit so that each connecting rod can have straight line 
  access to it's own journal on the crankshaft. The result is that cylinder
  #3 is further forward than cylinder #1, and therefore further away from
  the fan which is, as you recalled correctly, smack dab in the middle on 
  the end of the crankshaft.
