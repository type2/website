Beetle ties size is 165SR15.  The lack of a "Profile" number in the size
indicates that it is actually a 165/82SR-15. This is the only size that
proerly fits the rims and maintains proper diameter.  5-1/2 inch rims can be
fitted with 185/70SR-15.
