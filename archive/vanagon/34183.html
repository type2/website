Mike, I'd recommend getting a couple of kits from RMMW.  The first is their
"shifter kit" or similar name.  It has everything for the "front" part of
the shifting mechanism, including gear shifter, spring, GUIDE PLATE, bushing,
knob, etc.  It's less than $30, closer to $20 if memory serves.  I've found
having an installed, adjusted guide plate to be a real boon.  ;-)

The second is their rear coupler kit, the rubber and metal pieces that
connect the shifter rod to the front of the transmission.  I think it's in
the $8-$11 range, and yeah it's pretty easy to install if things are about
the same between your bus and my '71.  Good luck.
