I dont know anything about the westy seals but I recently bought a full set of
Lloyds mats for my 86 GL.  They are expensive on the order of $200. for front

heavy, soil resistant, rubber backed carpet.  I actually had to get my local VW

them to do so.  So either call your local VW dealer and have them call Lloyds (I
don't have Lloyds' number) or call Metro VW Parts in DFW,TX at (214)256-1102.
They'd be glad to help.
