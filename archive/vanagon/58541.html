Katherine - this is a 12 Volt power socket that Westfalia installed for
goodies/accessories using this type of plug.  Amost every Westy 76-91 I've
seen has one.  Just finding the accessories with the plug is another
mysterious matter.  You can get the replacement socket (and the male ends) at
a place in Arizona called The Trim Shop (specializes in Type 181 Thing parts,
which also used "The Silver Socket")
