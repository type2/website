I have heard that some people put vacuum switches in line with the connection
to their compressor clutches to avoid this problem.  Whenever you are
accelerating or pulling hard, the vacuum in the intake drops.  The switch is
set to open whenever this happens, releasing the A/C clutch and removing the
load from the engine when you need the power.  Anybody here tried this?
