Russell,
     
     No, I bought a complete engine gasket set, VW sealents, and used
JB-Weld as well.
Early on in the project I read the archives about successes with JB-Weld and
wondered about substituting JB-Weld for VW head gasket sealent.  My head
surfaces looked pretty smooth until cleaning revealed pitting.  I applied
JB-Weld to pitting in both head and crankcase mating surfaces and carefully
fine sanded both.  

Still holding at 1000 miles...
