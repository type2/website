Craig,
I have used the diff lock routinely when off-roading.  If you live in an area
where you get a lot of snow / ice or plan on off-roading the diff lock could
make quite a difference.  Pickup and SUV owners that want a diff lock
typically pay $600 to $1,000 (depending on variety) to have one installed.
 I'd get a second opinion before having the transmission opened.
