I have seen this magnetic tool.  It works fine, and I think it is sold as 
a bug-pack.  Looks like a boomerang with a 17 mm hex in the middle.  I 
have used, yes really, a bit of chewing gum under the head of this bolt, 
then push it in real good.  You can also use a bit of that gooey stuff 
you use to stick posters to the wall.  Both worked for me.  

You can also put this bolt in first, then lower the jack a bit to bind 
the bolt, this works fine if you think to do it.
