I do not know about *a lot* of Vanagons, but my 84 does the
lifter noise too.  It generally does go away after a couple
of minutes.  I have noticed it to be worst when I drive,
park for a 30 minutes or so, then start driving again.  My
pseudo-VW dealer used to put a GM elixer in the oil and that
did seem to help, but the problem does come back.  I see
that Liquid Wrench suggests (on the can) adding some to the
oil to help with sticky lifters as well.  I have not tried
that.

My Vanagon has about 90K miles on the odometer, but the
lifter noise has been a problem for several years.  When I
had the head gasket problem at about 50K, my mechanic said
two of the lifters had NO fluid in them at all and replaced
them, so it can be a nuisance but may also lead to other
problems.

Anyone else?
