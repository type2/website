oh, what the heck, as an oregonian now, i'l  chirp in.  i flippin'
LOVE oregon.  living in portland for the last four months i can
honestly say i have not lived in a better city, nor have i had even
one unhappy experience!  beautiful state, tons of green places to be
and run around in, beautiful rivers everywhere (avid kayaker i am, i
admit it).  the gas thing is a little strange, and took some getting
used to but whatever.  i guess there are inconsiderate 'rednecks' in
every state, but judging a population or a group based on one days
experience or after meeting/running into 0.000001 percent of the
entire group is a little shallow i'd say.  but who knows, i seem to
have an incredible amount of luck in my life and maybe i am running
into only the 0.000001 percent of the population who are nice.  maybe
after i have been here a year i will hate it, or become assimilated
into some bizarre oregon cult thing and hate all you silly
'outsiders', but i doubt either will happen.

blow on through.  either way...
