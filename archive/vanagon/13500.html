If the flasher relay is a bi-metallic (read current->heat) electromechanical
type it will survive fine but the flash rate will be higher than the 12VDC type.

If it is a semiconductor type it may make it as the individual components
are probably rated at 150% tolerance.  Once again the voltage/time 
characteristics will be higgledypiggeldy but it should flash.
