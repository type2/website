I notice that if I shut my bus off immediately after it's been pushed
hard that the CHT skyrockets.  The temp goes up so fast I can see the
needle move.  Scary.  So, I always sit for about 30 seconds with the
engine at fast idle (2500 RPM) to let it cool off if it's been driven
hard.  Seems to keep the CHT from spiking like that.
