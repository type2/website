I once broke an antenna on my old '69 bus, and searched for a replacement
that was secured at more than one point (like '80s Toyota car antennas).
But, I was also broke, so wound up getting a cheapo from Radio Shack that
mounts both at its base, and also has a little pylon that slides onto
the antenna, and can be screwed into the sheet metal maybe 6" to a foot
above the base.

Surprisingly, the reception was excellent! Since then, I've used this
antenna on every windshield-pillar mount antenna since. Never had one
broken in a car wash yet, due I'm sure to the two-point mounting.
