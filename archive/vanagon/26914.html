I just went throught a similar bout on my '57.  The accelerator pump 
mechanism was bye-bye.  The bus wouldn't start at all if you gave it 
any gas.  After pulling off the carb and checking that the membrane 
on the acc. pump is not decayed/torn, I pulled out the float and 
noticed the little "bb" (as I call it) had fallen out from under its 
little "pellet" looking holder in the bottom of the bowl.  That cured 
my problems... well one of them anyhow!

You might, when your engine is _cold_ pull off the gas line to the 
carb, manually turn over the engine by twisting the generator pully 
(in neutral) to see if the fuel pump is squirting gas.
