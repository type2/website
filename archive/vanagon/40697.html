What 4 hole wheel are you trying to put on a Bus and why?  Do not even think
of modifying wheels that way. Wheels are designed to be "supported" where the

wheel on the drum or rotor.  When the wheel fails from being modified, and
somone gets hurt, someone will get Sued.  If you are under 21 it will
probably be your parents.
