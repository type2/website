I had something similar to this happen to me a couple of weeks back.  
After viewing a local garage sale I jumped into the Vanagon, started it 
up and the horn starts blowing.  It took me a couple of seconds to 
realize that it was my horn and quite a few seconds more to discover the 
problem -- one of the keys on my key ring had become lodged between the 
steering wheel and column (completing the horn circuit).  The blast 
lasted over 15 seconds and turned every head at the garage sale ... how 
embarassing. :-0
