  If it has a type IV motor, and I don't know if canadian buses do, and it's 
  leaking at the front of the engine then it's not the oil cooler. The type
  IV oil cooler is at the left rear of the engine, behind cylinder #4. You
  can see a bit of it from underneath. I'd be more suspicious of a leaky
  pushrod tube seal or rocker cover - Neither of which requires removing the
  engine to repair.
