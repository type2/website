Dear Jim,

Glad you got the "new" engine running. Has the clatter gone away yet? Our 87
with 111k miles on it had a lifter clatter ocassionally after starting,
would last a few to several minutes. About a month ago I pulled the valve
covers and removed all of the valve adjusting screws, three of them were
badly worn and/or damaged. After replacing all of the adjusting screws the
noise as not reoccured. 

Just as a point of interest we use Castrol Syntec 5-50 oil and VW oil
filters, the interior of the engine is extremely clean. Go figure. BTW I
recently picked up a service/collapseable push rod tube, $41.xx (list - 20%)
