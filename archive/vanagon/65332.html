My PO of recently purchased 89>syncro<GL had 4 Yakima (extended height) racks

Yakima fittings on the racks were 2 "kayak" shaped bowls on the front rack.
 2 x Bungee cords to hold the rear of the Kayaks to 2nd top rack, while the
cycles were mounted on rear top rack down to rear hatch rack....  now only if
we had graphics..
