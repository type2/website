How long has this '62 been sitting?  If you've been driving it around then
use a bigger screwdriver (or pry bar). You can also use a screwdriver to
pry the end of the shoe up and out of the adjuster  (my favorite solution).
The spring should pull it all the way back from the drum.  The drum will
come off and you can clean the crud out of the adjuster's hole and
reposition the shoe.  If the bus has been sitting around for a few years
that's a different problem requiring a different solution.  Good luck!
