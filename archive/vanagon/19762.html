Camping in DC area:

On our first night (Fri.) we drove almost too Fredricksburg VA and camped at
a very nice truck stop (pilot). We were able to shower there for 6 bucks
(they supplied the towels and soap). We drove into DC abot 10 am an found
free shaded parking near the engraving building? and wash mon. The lot was
supposed to be 3 hour parking but everbody around us stayed all day. We
didn't have sat. tickets and we were going to a wedding shower that evening
so we visited the museum of natural history. After the wedding shower (in
Reston Va) we camped on a nieghborhood street (the couple getting married
asked us to go to a party with them after the shower and said it would be ok
to park there all night). On the way home we stayed at a rest stop. 

Cool bus at the show:

There was a KILLER bus at the show, it was an early mercedees that was set
up like an early VW camper. This thing was HUGE, and had double wheels in
the back. Unfortunately I didn't get a picture or get to go inside the
thing, but it definately was an interesting looking beast!
