Old Maytag stuff is getting pretty collectible now.  I attend a lot  
of antique power equipment shows and the little maytag 2 stroke  
kick start engines that went with these washers are fetching a

nowhere but up in value.  You may want to ask around a little  
before you alter it too much. Just a thought.
