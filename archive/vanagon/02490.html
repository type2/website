i think i still have all my cardboard in the front of my 83 diesel - i
figured it was probably impt for cooling flow (same radiator and front cowl
as gas vanagon btw). I have one of the Al strips on the drip rails, the other
one is waiting for me to find a good cement to stick it back on. Havent noticed
any difference in noise right and left. I have noticed that the position
(open/close) of the front door vents (if you have them) noticeably affects
the wind noise level; closed=louder. Said noise seems to be localised at the
front of the doors - i think the design here is that at speed the aerodyn.
pull air from the cabin through the front doors - if you shut off the flow
by nixing the vents then you get a lot of turbulence (ergo noise) from the
resulting flow.
