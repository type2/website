Well, since many parts are sold by the dealer for double what he pays for
it, a discount of 15% is similar to the dealer selling these parts for 35%
over his cost. As such, 20% over cost is an additional savings of 15%. 

I get the impression, though I have not verified this, that dealers in the
Southwest pay a little less for their parts in the first place than dealers
in other parts of the country.
