Model Summary for 1989 Vanagon USA

Six models of the Vanagon were built for model year 1989.  
These were: Vanagon GL, Vanagon GL Syncro, Vanagon GL
Camper, Vanagon GL Camper Syncro, Vanagon Carat (a new model this
year) and the Vanagon GL Wolfsburg Limited Edition.

Colors available: Pastel White, Flash Silver Metalic, Dove Blue Metalic,  
Titian Red Metalic, Wolfram Grey Metalic. Not all colors available
on all models.

Distinguishing Features:
There is only one exterior features to distinguish an '89 from the '88 
model year.  The '89 has black window trim while the '88 had chrome
inserts in the window molding.
The 1989 has carryover features from 1988 including:
2 Black plastic front grills (common to 1986-1991)
Rectangular primary headlights, seperate square high beam lights
(common to 1986-1991)
Large VW logo in center of upper front grill
Large VW logo in center of rear hatch
Black plastic vents at the rear of the rear side windows
The 1989 GL Vanagons had black aero styled bumpers without matching 
rocker panels and black outside rearview mirrors. 
The Carat model included rocker panels to match the
aero styled bumpers. These bumpers and rocker panels 
are metalic grey.  Carat models came with body colored outside
rearview mirrors.

The 1989-1/2 Wolfsburg Edition continued with features and layout 
similar to 1987-1/2 and 1988-1/2 Wolfsburgs.  It featured body colored 
rocker panels and matching aero styled body colored bumpers and outside
rear view mirrors.
