>Well people, is there anyway to drain oil without using the drain plug?

ok. first you get a replacement drain plug. then you get some metal chisels
(sharp, small ones) and files ... and you slowly reshape the drain plug to
be roughly hex-shaped again (but it will be smaller than before) so you can
get a socket on it.  a little dremel motor-tool might be helpful here.
it's slow tedious work, but it can be done.
