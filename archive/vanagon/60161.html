Hi Peter, I have had a similar problem with my instruments, if you "follow"
the wiring harness under the dash,  just to the right of the steering
column, there is a connector that is specifically for all of the
instruments. There is a multiple ribbon connection which pushes on to the
connector, you may find that this has become dislodged or loose as it did
in my case. You just need to push it back into place or fiddle with it a
little to make sure that it is sitting correctly.
