I had the same problem with my 85 GL. What you can do is check the 
ignition switch because there is a white plastic part inside of it that 
maybe is worn out. When you replace it, the van should start with no 
problems. Unfortunately I don't remember the part number, but you can get 
it at any VW parts supplier.
