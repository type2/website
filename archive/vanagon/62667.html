I'm sure others can add more to this,  but be carefull with polyurethane, it
will keep expanding for some time during installation and can expand enough
to destroy your panels,  but more imporant,  in a fire it is DEADLY...  DO
NOT hang around if it catches fire!!!

Fiberglass and rockwool can add a lot of irritants in the air for you to
breathe.  The movement of the van while on the road can constantly shed tiny
fiber particles that find their way out of any crack.

Those are my only two known problems, but don't take my word on it, there are
many types of insulations and many ways to apply them.  I'm hoping others
will have more to say about this..............
