If it is a 17 digit VIN it will contain alot of information about the
vehicle but without the correct values from the manufacturer you cannot
determine just what the numbers and letters mean. The first digit is
universal 1=USA J=Japan W=Germany. There are more but I don't know what they
are. Other digits refer to seatbelt types, body style, and engine.
