You have blown you head gaskets.  Probably due to a bad engine case.  I.ve
warned many in the past about this problem.  What seems to happen is that the
case wearin the center wear it is bolted together.  This causes the case
halves to distort and the cylinders no longer sit parrellel.  The head then
can not sit flat across both cylinders on either side and the pressuers will
eat at the gasket until it burns out or comes loose.  That chirping you hear
is actually the gasket shim vibrationg leak a reed or a whistle.  I would try
to have it fixed local but make sure they check the case for straightness.
 Driving it this way will destroy the seating surfaces on the heads if this
has not happened allready.
