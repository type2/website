The type 2 list has moved to another server.  Here's the scoop.

Send a message to LISTSERV_at_HOME.EASE.LSOFT.COM.

Don't put a subject line on the message; that is, leave the subject line empty.

In the body of the message (where you usually type your note), enter the
following:

SUB TYPE2 "your name"

To send a message, send it to:

TYPE2_at_HOME.EASE.LSOFT.COM
