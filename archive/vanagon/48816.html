If your oil light flickers; you need a rebuild.

If you think a higher oil pressure switch setting will protect your engine, 
you are mistaken. Whatever causes your engine to go down, it has happened long 
before your pressure drops;>(

Bentley lists the minimum pressure on a warm engine with 20W oil as 7.1 
lbs/sq.in. The stock sender operates at 6.3 lbs/sq.in. Do you really think 0.8 
lbs/sq.in. is really going to help?
