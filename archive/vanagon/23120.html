Martha,

I think what you're talking about is a vehicle made from a brand-new German
Messerschmidt fighter plane fuselage.  Seems that there were a lot of 'em
left over after WWII, lacking wings, engine, tail, etc.  Someone apparently
decided to make use of them and turned out a number of very strange-looking
three-wheeled cars.  The passenger sits behind the driver.

The lead character in the film "Brazil" drives one (to go with all of the
other goofy-looking technological marvels in the movie).  I can't remember
what they're called but I saw a couple of them in Austin (one of them was
for sale at a gas station).  I believe that there's a club for owners of
this funny aminal.
