The Bozobus went the Heli-Coil path as well.
Did the "machining" myself.
The Heli-Coil kit was *expensive*, and I had to go to a FLAPS warehouse
supply outlet to get it (something like $54US in '91).
Try to get the "self-locking" variety of insert - this reduces the reliance
on the bent tab-thingy or simple split-ring lockwashers to keep the bolts
in place.

Be careful not to punch through *into* the oil-filled xfer case.
The gear oil is smelly and expensive, and it's disheartening to watch it
flow straight through to the floor.
I did this twice (years apart). Fixed the leak by putting a huge gobbet of
PermaTex #2 (or #3?) in the hole bottom and on the end of the bolt.
