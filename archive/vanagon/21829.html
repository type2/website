calling in a week. The ad stated that a "Tint" was for sale. Arizona is
thick with auto window tinting places and tend to be ignored due to their
commonality. My friend determined that the seller was moving and leaving

time. (No problem)! What they had was an NOS stand alone/drive away circus

red and white striped bag similar to a equipment bag for a baseball team. It
looked like it was used maybe once due to the slight mud on the underside of
the floor. The friend determined that he wanted to keep it, Cool, glad to
help a friend. End of story.
