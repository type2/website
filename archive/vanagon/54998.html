I've been curious about these guys, too.  Their prices do seem too good to 
be true.  One unusual thing is that apparantly they actually rebuild your 
motor, rather than selling you a rebuilt one.  You have to send them your 
old motor up front for them to rebuild.  A friend of mine needed a type 4 
motor quickly, and asked if they could just sell him one, charge him a core 
deposit, and then refund the core when he returned his old motor (like most 
places do). They agreed, but charged him something like $600 for a core 
deposit (this for a 1.8L, typically a $100 core).  They promised to ship 
the motor the next day via 2nd day air, but nearly a week went by and he 
never got it.  When he finally called he learned that they hadn't even 
shipped it yet(!), so he cancelled the deal and ordered from AVP.  So I 
never got a chance to check out their motor.
