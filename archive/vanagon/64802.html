> And in South Africa 'Microbus' and 'Kombi'.  Are these the original

No, like Vanagon these are South African only names. The 'Kombi' is
used in Germany for convertibles, used as transporter without rear
seats or as bus with rear seats. But there is no label 'Kombi', just
a name found in price lists.
