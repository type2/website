	The Big Sur campout was fabulous and it was wonderful to
meet everyone in person. Next time I'll have my pickup truck and 
I really WILL have to sleep in a tent! We *feasted*, and Jeff taught 
a bunch of us how to play  Hacky Sack, which I got really into. 
I was really sorry I had to go home early. But its better to have
gone and left early than to have never gone at all.

	Everyone's Vanagon, Bus or Transporter looked cool. I
particularly liked the psychedelic effect of Tobin and Krista's
yellow breadloaf with the deep-green interior pannels. SOMEBODY
inhaled something back at the color studios...

last frames and take my film down to be processed, and guess what?
NO FILM IN MY CAMERA!! OH NO :-((((((((((((((

feel foolish!

booked on a flight to Phoenix for the air races and the Blue Angels
on the weekend of the 24th-26th. So naturally I'd do better with
a closer to Bay Area site than a further away one.
