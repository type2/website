Although it probably is illegal, I have done it many times, in a rather
conspicuous rainbow split-window. Most recently, this summer, while
following a certain stigmatized rock band, I slept at a couple rest
areas, (ok, but often hasseled by cops), hotel parking lots, (ok, but
you have to leave early), and the best, truck stops. My hearty
recommendation for anyone wishing to sleep for free, in a realtively
hassle-free environment is to stay at a truck stop. Most even have
separate areas for cars, and you have access to restrooms with showers,
as well as gas, and often a cafeteria-style eatery. A big thumbs up to
Citgo, and BPtruck plazas.
