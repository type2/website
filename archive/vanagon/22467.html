Subject: AZ Bus Club Jerome Jamboree '95 info

Jerome Jamboree is not directly a Jerry Garcia event per se but is darned
near the next best thing.
Jerome, AZ is a former mining Ghost town/ tourist spot located "roughly" in
the center of Arizona. Dress for warm weather but prepare for anything.
Families welcome, yet has been known to be a little "spirited" in past
years. (Martha/busgirl would have enjoyed the magnesium fire), car/van
contest, chili cookoff, slow drags, "celebrity" item auction, misc fun
prizes, overnight camping encouraged. Come for the day, come for the weekend!

Event:Jerome Jamboree September 16 & 17, 1995
Sponsored by: Arizona Bus Club
Meeting Location: Home Depot Parking lot,
Address: Phoenix, AZ, West Thunderbird Rd. and I17 freeway, (Northwest corner).
Meeting Time: 07:00 line up early for the caravan
Departure Time/Caravan: 08:00 
Price: $5.00 per vehicle (displaying (proof of) valid id of any VW club)
       $10.00 per vehicle (not displaying any VW club id)
Distance from Phoenix: Approx 110 miles
