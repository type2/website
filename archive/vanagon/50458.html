Subject: Got new starter - strange connections

Well, after various starter problems and not being able to find a problem
with any bad connections, I decided to check out new starters.  I found a
fairly reasonable Bosch remanufactured one for $95. The thing is, comparing
it to my old one, I note it has an extra spade connection.

My old one had a double connector at the top-front of the solenoid for the
wire from the starter and the wire to the fuel injection.  This new starter
has that same double connector, but also has another spade connector at the
bottom-front of the solenoid.  Starter is identified as SR87X.  Anyone know
what the other connection is for, or if this is the correct starter for my
'80 Vanagon with manuel trans?

Also, the starter came with a new bush for the shaft to fit into.  Is this
really that important to replace?  If so, how do you get the old one out
