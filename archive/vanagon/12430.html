Some misc. things my book tells me:
Make sure the vent hole in the cap is clear of obstruction to prevent a
vacuum or pressure from forming in the fluid reservoir.

A blocked by-pass port prevents the fluid from flowing back to the reservoir
when it expands under the influence of heat (continuous braking). The fluid
will then expand towards the wheel cylinders, overcome the return spring 
pressure and cause the brake shoes to drag continually on the drums (Stop
light remains on) Hmm. Sounds like your original problem.
