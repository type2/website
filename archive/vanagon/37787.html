Your vibration is probably related to the driveshaft.  Have you checked the
universal joints at each end?  There should be no play.  Reapiring these
shafts is an iffy thing at best.  they seem to have all sorts of balance
problems after repair.  You will either need a very good drive shaft shop or
plan on buying new shaft from dealer.
