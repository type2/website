amongst the prune posts that a few people have made suggested alternative
camping sites to Red Rock.  As I am from the middle rather than
the southern end of California, and do not know the locations

to research this, suggest it, find out about it for us, and

location.

further excursions, and I for one look forward to many many

if any of you would like to visit the other nearby areas of
interest, you could always do so by taking a side excursion
from our trip, and/or by doing it a day ahead or after our
planned meet.

I guess this is to serve as my formal solid vote for sticking
with Red Rock.  Count me in:  I'll be there with Margeritas
in hand!
