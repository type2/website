I picked up a 2/3 roof rack for $50 at a VW show. It is one of the
all-metal types, and is really light, but also sturdy. Ihighly recommend
one, they are great things to put a lot of extra stuff. 
JUST BE REALLY CAREFUL as you will add at least a foot to your already
tall bus, and might very well scrape low awnings, parking decks, etc.
Mine is now at 7'4" tall, and Ihave to avoid parking decks, and be
careful in general.

As to the painting, My buses always cry out to be painted; there's so
much space, and people expect buses to be crazy-looking anyway.
definately go crazy with it. And hey, if you don't like it, do it again!
