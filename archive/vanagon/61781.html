Syncro. T get the drain plug off from the tranny, I used a short piece of a
17 mm allen wrench that I cut from a regular L shaped allen wrench. I used a
small 41/2 inch angle grinder with a metal cutting disc to cut about 1 inch
off the long end of the wrench. I placed the wrench in a vice with the bottom
submerged in water and I cut a bit at time, cooling off the metal with a wet
rag so as to not to let the metal loose it's temper. Then I used the small
