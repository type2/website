Sam,

My 84 was doing the same thing.  I noticed that my fan wasn't coming on
except when it was really hot.  I recently pulled my radiator and had it
rodded and cleaned.  Lots of junk from Barsleak.  I changed the fan switch
which is located at the lower left of the radiator.  About $15 at local
supplier.  You can test your fan (or make an override switch for that matter)
by making a small jumper wire to go between the hot wire going into the
switch and the other two wires.  One will make the fan run at low speed and
the other will make it run at high speed.  Hope this helps.
