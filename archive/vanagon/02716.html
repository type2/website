Canoes on Type 2s...or was it Tipacanoe and Youllsinktoo..

When I owned the late, lamented 67 camper, I used to carry around
a 13' Great Canadian fiberglass canoe. One of the neat things
about carrying the canoe was you could use it as an "aerodynamic"
luggage box. We would strap things into the canoe using bungee
cords and some plastic netting and then toss the canoe on
the roof rack.
