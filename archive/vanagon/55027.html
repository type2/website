The MV has two theater style, rear facing seats in the middle, the GL
has
a two seat, front facing bench in the middle.  They're both about the
same
amount of room, although I imagine the MV's seats are easier to remove
than
the GL's.

Regarding question number two; when we were shopping around for our

almost

Weekender option, or one of the Winnie EV conversions ('95 on).
