I was looking for the right replacement hose a few weeks ago. After I
settled on the Gates power brake booster vacuum hose (no wire) (1/2" id) and
had everything hooked up, the parts manager at my local vw dealership turned
me on to a better alternative:  Gates makes a wire reinforced coolant hose
with a 1/2" id nicknamed "shape to fit" hose.  It goes for around $3 a foot.
It will not collapse.  I bought some, installed it and confirmed that my
power booster leaks.  Still looking for a cheap used working booster.  The
hydraulic hose idea sounds good if it's metal reinforced---anything that
won't collapse will work.
  
Has anyone noticed that the vent tube that comes off the front (FIF) of the
power booster (by the pushrod & bellows) accomodates a 1/2" id hose, but the
tube (that passes right  behind the driver's wheel--71 Bus) on the other end
of the hose is wider.  I had to use an adapter here.  Just curious if I'm
the only one with this situation.
