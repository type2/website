All I can say about this: Read your car instructions carefully!
You will read that it is dangerous to place young kids on the
passengers seat when your car is equipped with air bags,
specially if you use baby seats which are faced backwards.
This instruction is also found (at VW's) as a sticker on the
top frame rail at the passengers door. No chance to miss this
sticker while opening this door.

BTW: In Europe we have only small size air bags which are
only useful while wearing seatbelts and having head rests.
But they also are dangerous for young kids.
