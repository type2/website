Not sure what the problem is Jeff, but here's another clue: It's _not_ the cold 
start valve or anything in that circuit. The cold start valve, which I think is 
basically just another fuel injector in the middle of your intake manifold, 
provides extra fuel when the motor is cold.

That could explain why your motor idles better cold. Hey wait a minute, maybe 
its not shutting off and the motor's running too rich when warmed up?

Anyway, food for thought...
