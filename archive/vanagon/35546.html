I've done just this.  I installed a duel sensing sending unit beneath the
left head.  I had to use teflon tape to seal the leaks.  I used plummers
5/16th piping to move the new senor away from the head about 3" and the a 90o
turn down.  The new sensor was aftermarket and if about 2" in diamer and 3"
high, thus not fitting in the same location of the original sending unit.  I
bought and electric duel sending unit so I wouldn't have to mess witht the
thin oil pressure tube running the length of the van to the guage in the
dashboard.  I have found it to be a savior to diagnose engine pressure
problems.  Especially after I rebuilt my engine, it was nice to know what the
engine was doing.
