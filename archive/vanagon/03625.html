Comparing these two catalogs, it looks like WW is using calendar 
year numbers, but Bus Stop is treating anything manufactured 
since the middle of 1956 as a '57 model year.  Your bus must have 
been manufactured in late 1956.  WW goes to the model year method
in 1962;  starting then, the two lists are consistent. 

I had thought that VW had gone to a model year concept back in 
the early 50's???
