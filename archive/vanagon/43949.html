Another good source is the Old Car Price Guide (ISSN 0194-6404), which is
published quarterly in the US. It contains prices for 130,000 cars from 1901
to 1986. Volkswagen prices start with the 1945 model, and go to 1986
waterpumper prices. The copy I have is now 2 years old, but it came in handy
for estimating a value for my bug for the insurance company.
