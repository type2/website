Depending upon how concerned and flush you are an alternative is:  A baby
monitor.  I think that there are at least two on the market, but the one I
know of is sold by (Who else) Fischer Price.  It is a wide angle Infared

little ingenuity will address that.  It transmitts in the 900 Mhz band.  Price
is about $300.  Don't know how well it would do looking at an outside area,
but works fine in the baby's room. Comes with 5" B&W "TV" receiver.
