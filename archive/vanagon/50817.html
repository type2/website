Mine leaked when I got it, although it was fairly easy to see the 
coolant once removing the box over the heater (mine's a Westy, not 
sure what passenger vans have). First the valve was replaced, but it 
still leaked. Removal of the plastic front "grill" (held on by clips) 
revealed a white crusty deposit on the fins of the core. A small leak 
may evaporate in the air flow before it starts to drip, which would 
still produce the smell.

Luckily the dealer paid for the parts - the core was around $150 Cdn 
I think.
