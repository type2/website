Hello Joerg.  A friend of mine had a '88 Westy and had one of these 
"space blankets" for the van.  It was a Westy part, and yes, it was 
expensive.  He used it in winters down to -25!  It was aluminum
foil covered.  

A suggestion.  Hardware stores sell these "blankets" by the foot.  
It's used for water heaters or garage doors for insulation.  In my 
opinion, this might be the same stuff used by Westfalia.  It sure 
looks like it anyway.  Just cut it out to size, and bungy cord it to 
the van.  A lot cheaper, and I'm sure just as effective.
