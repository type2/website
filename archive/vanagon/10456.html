 >  1) What is the best book to get to find out how to work on the FI?

I can wholeheartedly reccommend 2 FI injection books.  BOTH are 
excellent and you need BOTH if you are to become a true FI 
afficiando.

"Bosch Fuel Injection and Engine Management" by Charles Probst  AND
"How to Tune and Modify Bosch Fuel Injection" by Ben Watson

If I were to buy just one which would I buy?  Well, the one by Watson 
is more 'how to' and Probst is more 'theory', which,  if you learn, 
enables you to 'how to' better than most.  You choose.
