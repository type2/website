That was me , David Schwarze, that was (and still is) organizing
the BAA (to be named more appropriately, soon, I hope) tour.  You
have not been taken off of the "list", nor has anyone else.  Interest
has just waned lately in general, and I haven't been doing much thinking
about the trip.  The fact that my bus isn't running doesn't help my
enthusiasm either, but I'll post about that nightmare separately.

I attended a vintage vw show here in San Diego yesterday at Mission Bay
Volkswagen.  It was bigger and better than I expected.  There was the
usual assortment of bugs and type III's, and seven or eight beautiful
transporters.  Turns out there is a local type II club here, but it is
only for pre '68 busses :(   I did talk to the president about our
trip, though, and he promised to disseminate (sp?) any information
about the trip to his members that I give him.  There were two beautiful
Westys there, a 66 and a 67.  There was also a 50's vintage crew cab
restored to immaculate condition - beautiful!  

I also looked at the Eurovan Winnebago that was on display.  32K for
that puppie (ouch!) but it was, indeed nice.  Especially the little
shower nozzle thing in back.
