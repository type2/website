Todd:
I cured this problem by stopping a vacuum leak around one of the four
large rubber hoses that connect the intake manifold to the intake
runners.I noticed the intake manifold did not look centered. I slid the
rubber hoses into position by tapping them on the edge with a large
screw driver.
