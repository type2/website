If I remember right you drive syncro. Right!.  Do not rinse the trans out
with anything but what you intend to use as there is about a quart of fluid
that you cannot drain from the front.  The synchronizer hubs are hard costed
with MOLY anyway so I do not think moly aditives will hurt although I
question the need or benifit.  If you believe in the GL 4 issue, than
anything that changes the friction characteristics must be considered.  I
have a syncro transmission with over 130K using Mobil 1 SHC 75w-90 and the
syncros still work great. Even in winter.
