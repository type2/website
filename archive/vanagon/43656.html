All,

When grinding magnesium beware of steel bolts or dowels near the
spot you want to grind.

A couple of years ago I modified a T IV fanhousing and i wanted
to grind of some things, after a few minutes there was quite some
magnesium powder on the floor and workbench in my basement, the
magnesium doesn't give sparks when ground, but then I hit a steel
dowel with the disc, I reacted instantly and only a few sparks
came off but, unfortunatly, that was enough to ignite it, it gave a
flash you wouldn't believe, bright blueish white, I turned around
and saw my shadow on the opposite wall, i ran up into the kitchen
and the only thing i could grab was a pan of spinach, i ran down
again and trew it onto the bench, the magnesium allready stopped
burning, but the spinach put out the phone and some other things
that had cought fire.
