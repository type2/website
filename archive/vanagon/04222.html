Subject: Phil Spector

I remember "To Know Him Is To Love Him".  I have it on a tape made
by a fanatical friend who has all those old 45s.  I really love that
song.  I class it as one of the top 5 or so of all Doo Wop songs.

The song title "To Know Him Is To Love Him" came from the tombstone of 
Spector's father.

The female singer of the Teddy Bears was named Annette Kleinbard.  I say "was"
because she has changed her name to Carol Connors.  Under her new monicker,
she has had a successful career as a songwriter.  Mostly, I gather, she writes
movie themes.  She has been nominated for 4 Emmys, 2 Academy Awards, and 1
Grammy.  She wrote "The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia," which was a
hit for Vikki Carr (I believe) a couple of decades ago.
