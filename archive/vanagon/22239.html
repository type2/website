To solve the same problem, I looked at after market window cranks.  I found 
some that could be bent (shaped?) to clear a higher grill.  But, in fact, I 
still haven't replaced the original speakers.  I connected the new ones up 
and they didn't sound any better.  I did bend and use one of the new cranks 
though when the original came apart.

My reason for replacement was to improve the sound.  The original speakers 
appear to be glued in the baffles with permanent glue.  It looks as if the 
baffles would get destroyed removing the speakers.  Still a future project.

Mike  84 Vanagon
