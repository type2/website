For painting the pop top the manual seems to say that: 

1. No boding agent is required to paint this.
2. Prime with filler/primer
3. No elastic additive is required to make the paint flexible.

OK. not too helpful. The section on the primer says:

Prime with filler ALN 786 001 13 plus 4:1 of hardener ALZ 009 001 06 or ALZ
002 06. Adjust viscocity to 17 sec. with ALV 002 000 06. Apply 2-3 coats
(approx. 40-50um). Flash off time--until matted (dull.)
