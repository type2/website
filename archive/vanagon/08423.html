Congratulations on your success with your camper - it's nice to hear a
story where the good guy wins for a change!  As for the smell, if there
is any carpeting at all that smells, replace it.  I have found that once
carpeting is "treated" by a cat, nothing will clean it.  period.  The
only other thing I would recommend is simple green.  I have had great
success with simple green cleaning my van.  In severe situations you can
just spray it on straight even though they tell you to dilute it to something
like 10 to 1.
