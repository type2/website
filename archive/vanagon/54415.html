	My Westy did this same thing when I bought it.  I fixed it by
adjusting and re-tightening the gold rod that the door mechanism latches
onto.  The rod that is towards the rear of the door opening.All you need
is an open end wrench. Apparently mine had wiggled outward enough that the
door would bounce open quite frequently.  Of course I don't hane any young
'uns to worry about, but it still scared the hell out of me:)
