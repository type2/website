 Hi,
    I have owned my '71 passenger bus for many years now and love it.  Two
years ago I had my existing engine rebuilt by a local guy who talked me into
alignboring my existing case and regrind (3 angle) a set of used heads.
Never again!  After only 15,000 miles the engine has low oil preasure, over
heats, blows white smoke when started (leaking valve guides, I think) and
low compression (100psi).  I'm going to yank this engine and keep it for
smog checks while I put in a GOOD engine...one with a NEW case (read Gene
Bergs catalog) NEW heads, full flow oil filter and dual carbs.
