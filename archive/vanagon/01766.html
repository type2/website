regarding Recycled Inc I have certainly had good luck with them

i had to drop my tank on the diesel 83 about 2 years ago - had a hole
on the filler side just above the seam. patched with fiberglass from
a body filler kit.  Hole didnt leak but in cleaning the top of the tank
with wire brush opened a bunch of new holes .....   :-(

Now i dont fill more than one-half full (which is about three weeks of fuel
for me anyway ....) unless i am going on a road trip - it doesnt really
leak it kind of seeps a bit.  I drain the fuel filter every 3 months but
never see any water in it so doesnt seem to be a problem.

I think recycled inc sells them new for $300 (maybe $400??) figure a good
four hours to drop it and put new one in. They have recycled and remanufactures
ones too for less.
