>My instructor called the Licoln book the 'bible' of welding.  Covers

>about any type of welding.....
>
>        The other two books he said good, class room text type things.
>Personally, I didn't get to leaf thru them too much since he turned
>us loose on the Oxy-Fuel and Arc welders early..... So I can't comment
>to much on their content.  From a casual glance, all 3 seemed to be
>packed with info.  The Lincoln seemed to be "Ok you know the very basics
>now here is the rest".  The others seemed to be "here are the basics through
>intermediate stuff".
