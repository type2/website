Gaving recently sold a converter for a 86 Syncro (same cat as yours), the
aftermarket cats come with one of each.  I've let the MFR know that 86-87
models with the Original Collector Pipes used triangular gaskets on both
ends, but it remains to be seen if they'll supply them that way in the
future.  They don't like adding anything that may unnecessarily increase the
price and have anything left over for those that don't need the part.
 Suggestion:  buy the cat with the two different gaskets, buy the extra
triangular gasket as I mentioned in the earlier post and keep the doughnut
metal gasket for when the time comes to replace the Collector Pipe - you'll
need it then (VW and we get almost $12 for this doughnut - expensive, but can
only get from Germany at this time).  If you buy the VW Dealer Converter, you
don't get any gaskets and pay 4 times as much for the converter.  Converter
Part Number is VAG 025-131-701 - fits 86-91 models.
