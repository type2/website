I just put a set of Contu CT22's on my 93 EV and am pleased
-- especially with the price.  I mail ordered 'em from Tire
Rack and they were $60 apiece.  Shipping and local
mounting/balancing added a total of $61, so my net is $71.25
per tire -- not bad.  I like the performance of them, as
well.  My original Conti CS21's were shot at about 26k, and
I had a set of nonreinforced Uniroyals on for 30k -- OK for
highway driving but lackluster andling performance.
