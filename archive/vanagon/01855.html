In extreme heat it is advised to use it.  We use 20w50 in all our vehicles
without any problems.  I use Mobil 1 15w50 in my cars.

>oh, so why don't *I* use 20w40 instead of 20w50? cause i can't find it

You may be able to find 20w40 at truck or heavy equipment dealers.
Years ago when my father was doing consulting for the army loading equipment
onto ships they left behind several 5 gallon containers of 20w40 oil.
He took three containers home and we used it in our cars with no problem.
"What's good for the army is good enough for us", he said.
