Good news, I had a leak from the pop top skylight that I've
rixed by replacing it's seal. The new seal was $40, a tad bit
expensive, from Euro-American (Delta Six). It was easy to 
install, removed old one, cleaned up surface, heated up a big
pot of water and put the new seal into the water so that it
was more pliable. Then I installed it. No silicon sealer.
Works great, no more leaks, from there.
