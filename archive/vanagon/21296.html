Thanks to all who replied with advice on resurrecting my power brakes.  

For replacing the godawfully expensive VW power brake vacuum hose,  Eugene
suggested neoprene hose as a cheaper alternative and someone said they used
heater hose with no ill collapsing effect.  I looked around at my FLAPS and
found out that 'round these parts the neoprene hose is special order.  Gates
makes a "light duty" 1/2" or so inner diameter hose for $2.70/foot packaged
in 3 ft quantities.  You can buy larger quantities (over 3ft) cut to order
at only $1.75/foot.  It doesn't have the nice woven cover and wire wrapped
around it like the old VW stuff, but it is THICK and looks like it will do
the job.

A new booster from VW (211 612 103) goes for $400+!!  Someone mentioned a
place that might sell rebuilt units for $125 (but I'm still waiting for
address/phone info hint, hint).  The concensus is to get a good cheap unit
at a junkyard.

Also, I looked at the fiche at the dealership and discovered that the
protective rubber bellows that covers the vent side of the booster is no
longer available. I don't really need one, but this seems like a part that
should be reproduced somewhere (WCM?).

The check valve is still available from the dealer, as someone pointed out,
for $12.

Thanks again.  Looking forward to that feeling of power!
