Subject: Easy Rider blues/Splits in films

I remember in '68 seeing Easy Rider and coming out thinking WOW and far out
and other such adolescent stuff..at least I think I remember it...someone once
said if you remember the sixties you must have had a bum life...
Anyway it was premiered on TV the other week so I video'd it and have just
(tried) to sit through it. Omigod! Crass acting, dreadful script, appalling
stereotypes...mind you the soundtrack was still ace (but if I wanna hear it I
use me CD) and some of the scenery was good, and the bikes were OK..for five
minutes. The only bit I really enjoyed was the sight of the deluxe at the
"hippy" (i hate that word) camp...how times change ....:(
seems I can't watch a movie these days without startling the family by
shouting 'Looka that Splitty!"...and I almost got thrown out of the cinema at
the opening of "Sneakers" (interior of a Split)...do I need a cure for this
weird affliction or is this all that's worth watching on celluloid these
days...oh well...
"My people were fair and wore stars in their hair, now they've grown old and
wear stars on their brow" (bet no-one remembers that one!
