the piece around the input shaft is called the throwout bearing retaining
shaft. It is the same on all busses from 71-79, except for one year, when they
did not have them.  Mine was melted just as yours is, I got one from a 73
beetle infact, and it was made from metal.  Exact fit.  typical VWAG trying
to save a few dollars, at the cost of long life. cost me $5 from Austin VeeDub
As can be expected there were no plastic ones in the parts bin.  My clutch
was toast when I replaced it(bought it with dead clutch), and there was no
visible wear on either the flywheel, or the pressure plate.
