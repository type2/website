There is no such thing as a standard wiper relay. When a VW comes without
intermittent wipers it has a lockout lug installed in the wiper switch and
a jumper bus bar in place of the relay. Remove both and install the relay
and you have intermittent wipers. The 1HM, if it is designated by VW is
probably some type of manufacturing code. Or a redesign of the relay which
they then designated as a 1HM 955 531 C. If you have a 211 955 531 it will
work. The 955 531 designates it as a wiper relay. If you're uncertain check
the numbers on the terminals. I think you'll find them to be identical. HTH
