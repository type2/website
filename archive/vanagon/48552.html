<grin> If your wires are in good condition, they will probably handle 
the extra current. I would guess they might be in need of 
replacement, though. The insulation breaks down with age due to heat 
and oil. I'm not sure what current practice is, but we used to put solid 
core wires in our "hot rods" with the high voltage insulation (you can 
use resistor plugs to suppress ignition "noise"). In the "good old days" 
you could tell the good wiring sets because of their orange 
colored insulation.
