Don't forget about the Arizona Bus Club's Annual Jerome Jamboree that will
be held this weekend. Caravan starting on Saturday at 07:00 from I-17 and
the Greenway Rd. interchange. Meet at the HOME DEPOT parking lot. If you
choose not to caravan with the gang you can always meet in the town of
Jerome, AZ and ask the locals where the old mining camp tourist trap place

sun, fun, and prepare for anything. =)
