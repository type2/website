> Hi from Minnesota.  You can get an oil pan heater direct from your VW
> dealer.  It bolts on to the bottom of the oil pan.  We got one for our 84
> Vanagon and found that it worked great, even at -25.  The VW part # is
ZNP
> 289104.  It cost $ 66.95 on 2-1-96.
> We have since switched to an 86 Synchro which cannot use that heater
> because of the different configuration involved with the synchro.  We
> purchased an aftermarket magnetized model, which was smaller and fit in
the
> available space.  Just to be sure, we glued it on also.  (Be sure that
the
> oil pan is very clean prior to gluing it on.)  Good luck.
