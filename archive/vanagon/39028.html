The rack you saw is a Paulchen, made in Germany. They are very cool and 
will carry up to four bicycles. Very modular with many attachments.

designed especially for Vanagon (that's what's on their literature). I 
really want one of these, but can't stand the price. They are not imported 
into US as far as I know, but I found a dealer near Vancouver BC that will
