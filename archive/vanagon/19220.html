Rach,
The swap is possible but you have to mount the rear motor mount.There is
a kit that attaches to the oil pump (not the best way). If the case has
the mounting locations built in you can drill and tap these and the rear
mount will bolt right on.If these locations are there you can't use the 
adapter plate.you may also have trouble clearing the dual outlet tailpipes
with your bumper.Hope this helps.
