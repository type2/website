Had a similar problem on my 82. The light would glow. I replaced the
regulator and it went another year before I had to replace the alternator.
If you have a Bosch alternator the regulator is mounted on the back, remove
it and look at the brushes. The bosch alternators like to wear down the
brushes. You may find one kind of long and the other rather short. You
could just replace the brushes if you can find them. I used to always
replace the regulator HTH
