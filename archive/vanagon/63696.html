reason is that we have exhaust systems made of about 20 feet of thinwall tubing 
including the header.  which everyone else sees to make of cast iron.

advice from the service manager respectfully pushed aside.  he said he just doesn't 
see that many bad motor mounts, even on vanagons with 300K miles on them.  i think my

mounts.
