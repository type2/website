John,

I've got the same symptom in my 87 with 146,000 miles.   I've replaced the
upper ball joints (reduced clunks somewhat) and will soon replace the lower
ones also.   I think the better test for ball joint wear is to compress the
joint with a C-clamp and measure play , then compare with a new one (this
is test for other cars).   Unfortunately, VW doesn't specify wear limits,
that I can find, so when I replace them I will measure and report to the
group the wear measured, and whether replacing the joint fixed the clunk.
I'm pretty sure that they are the cause.

Dave
