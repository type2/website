  I found some info in a Chilton's manual last night. Valve sizes in 1.7 and
  1.8 liter engines appear to match between bus and U.S. spec 914. The 2.0
  liter engine for the 914/4 and the 912E are different than the bus 2.0. It's
  not that simple though. Valve clearances for the 1.7 and 1.8 liter 914 are
  .006" for both valves, implying solid valves (Sodium cooled valves are set
  at 008" clearance, right?). The 2.0 liter 914 and 912E have clearance specs.
  of .006" intake and .008" exhaust, implying sodium cooled valves. The 2.0
  bus engine spec. is for .006" for both intake and exhaust. So there you have
  it, more confusion than ever.
