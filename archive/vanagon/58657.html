You don't say what weight Castrol you've been using. If it's 10w30, 
you'll probably be fine with the QS. If you've been running 15w40 or 
20w50 Castrol, it is *very* likely you will encounter oil pressure 
problems with the 10w30, especially with a higher mileage engine.

The common view seems to be that the weight/grade of the oil 
(matched to ambient temp) is far more important than Castrol vs 
Quaker vs Pennzoil.

I for one have had problems with any 10w30 oil in several VWs, 
especially if used with a FRAM filter.
