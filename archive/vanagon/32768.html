of room for 'ground crew' and tools  and whatnot  Plus be nice enough to 
take whaere ever you want to when not cruising with the plane.  Gas 
mileage ought to be as good or better most anything else you'd consider 
for tow duty.  If you find and MV weekender you could even carry cool 
drinks in the special seat....
