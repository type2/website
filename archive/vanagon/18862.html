>the problem is that my sticker on my engine says
>.008 for exhaust and .006 for intake or vice versa

I would suggest that you use the clearances specified
on the engine sticker.  Use .008 for exhaust and .006
for intake.  If I remember, that's the clearances I used
on my original '73 stock motor (1.7L, dual carbs).

Of course, take this with a grain of salt because I have
recently installed a 1978 F.I. motor in my bus.  Valve
adjustments are a thing of the past with the hydraulic
lifters in my new motor.
