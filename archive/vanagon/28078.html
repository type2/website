I have done two of these seals, one on a 70 Westy and one on a 71.  I 
didn't get them from BusBoys however, but from J.C. Whitster (or 
whatever).  The main part had metal clips imbeded in the plastic, and I 
used contact cement for the seal that goes over the top of the shell.  
The main bottom seal was plenty long enough with a couple of inches to 
spare on each side.  This I easily trimmed with tin-snips.  Overall it 
was a pretty quick, simple job.  Just have to have a tall enough ladder 
:)

Hope this helps!!
