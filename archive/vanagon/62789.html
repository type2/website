yes, you can put the entire inst panel cluster from a '85-'91 vanagon with
the tach, and digital clock in a earlier one.
I have even seen a '80 with the later cluster, and they had a oil temp sender
hooked up to the coolant gauge instead, but since your van is watercooled, it
will be easier for you.
You will need to re-arrange the pins on the 14point multipin connector, but
it's not hard. You will also need to run a wire from the coil underneath the
van up to the 14 pt connector, but that's not a big deal either.
