If you want a Vanagon, go for a water cooled.  86 and up is best, mpre
options, power, better brakes.  Usually have power steering.  Camper A/C is
so so.  Air cooled Vanagons tend to blow engines, especially on long trips.
 Primary use of a camper.  Water cooled Vanagons do have head problems,  but
that can usually be avoided with proper care.
