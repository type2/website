I realize it ain't a Eurovan or even a Vanagon, but: for ye faithful 79
Bus, I bought Summit Trail Climber 205/75R14LT's.  I was going to have to
special order tires for the Bus no matter what, and went with these because
I've had excellent experience with Summits in the past.  The garage got
them and they were $68 each, mounted & balanced.  These tires greatly
improved cornering and have helped quite a bit in high crosswinds.  They do
ride a bit rougher than the old tires (awful Kelly-Springfields) but are
superior otherwise.
