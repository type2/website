>I replaced the rear brake shoes on my '84 Vanagon GL.  The Bently manual
>says they will self adjust and not to adjust the emergency brake linkage
>until they do (I think).
>A mechanic with VW said they won't self adjust, that you have to manually

  Yes the brake shoes Are self adjusting, BUT you must first adjust them
after installation!  Thru the baking plates.

  Then if your E-Brake is not up to par, adjust it.  The correct way to
adjust the e-brake is After you've adjusted the rear shoes correctly.
