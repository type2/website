I'd advise replacing them all at once.  Since the coolant is expensive and
the coolant/bleeding procedure is more than just "fill-er-up", one at a time
could be much more work.  I replaced nearly all of the engine area hoses on
my 87 Syncro a while back and the VW price was (if I remember right) closer
to $750.  I used mostly hoses from the local auto parts store and saved
hundreds of $$$.  I didn't find any hoses in the application guide, but just
walked up and down the hose aisle to find compatibly sized/shapped hoses.
