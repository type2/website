There are several early 80s Vanagons around here like that, made by 
GetAway Vans. I test drove an '82 diesel model which was a tad 
slow. I mainly wanted to look at the diesel engine bay, since I had 
never seen one up close. The ones I've seen have lots of storage 
(front & rear areas of the top), but NO sleeping area up top, which 
is a poor design IMHO. Also, the rest of the van used Westy cabinets, 
stove, fridge, etc, with the upper cabinets made to match, albeit 
of different materials.
