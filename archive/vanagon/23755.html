Gunk makes a chain lube commonly sold at hardware-ish type type ie
Walmart Kmart Target Ace etc that has "Moly" in it... this is
somewhat of a new formulation because you'll see some cans of Gunk
chain lube with it and some without, as their stock is used up.  I
use it for the linkage periodically and while it doesn't last a long
time, it is easy to apply and eases shifting.
