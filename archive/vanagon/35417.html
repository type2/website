I think you should try taking this to arbitration as the vehicle still
has relatively low miles on it.  Some friends of ours did just that and
ended up returning the van and getting a full refund.  I don't think
you could get a full refund, but as the mechanic said, you'll probably
get a partial refund on the engine work.  That seems to be the best
route to take right now for these kinds of (basket) cases.  It takes
time, but it does get resolved eventually.  Be sure to record all of
your transactions and keep your receipts!  

You can find further info. on arbitration in the archives.
