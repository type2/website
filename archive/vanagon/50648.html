Hi Mark,

My '86 Westy averaged about 20 mpg highway and my '91 Westy w/ automatic
gets around 18 to 19 mgh.  At first I was very reluctant about the
automatic  but I have been more than happy with it.  I think most list
members prefer the manual and I did for the last 25 years.   One thing
about a manual tranny is that you will have the expense for a new clutch, 
but if you change the oil in your auto it might out last the Vanagon.
