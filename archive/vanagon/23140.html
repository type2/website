The following is a list of the people who have indicated to me that they 
have some semi-serious interest in joining in on the as-yet-un-named trip 
to Inuvik, NWT, Canada (that's a few miles short of the Arctic Ocean, 
kids!) in August, 1996.  I haven't been very organized in how I keep 
track of people who tell me they're interested, so I'm sorry if I've 
missed you on this first list.  If I've missed you, contact me and I'll 
add you to the list.  

You'll see from the list below that all the people so far are from the 
west coast.  Well, hey, that's not right, so how about RENTING a westy 
here in Vancouver and taking that on the trip?  There are several places 
here in Vancouver that rent Eurovan Westies (you heard right--Westies!), 
and there's that place in Bellingham, WA that rents Vanagon Westies.  All 
you folks from elsewhere in North America (Europe?  Asia?  Oz?) might 
want to consider flying here (I'll pick you up from the airport in our 
westy--what style!) and renting a vehicle for the trip.  I can check out 
prices if there's any interest.

Still open to ideas for a tour name.  How about WATT: Western Arctic 
Transporter Tour?

The roster for the Inuvik trip (the WATT?) is open to any and all, of 
course, (providing you bring your bus!) so to all of you out there on 
the list: plead for the time off and get back to me.
