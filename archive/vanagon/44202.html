> Be sure only to keep oil above the add mark and not above the full mark. 
> More oil is better only until the crank starts to splash in the pan and 
> whisk it into a foam which neither lubricates or cools very well.

That's true in normal, modern engines, but part of the lubrication in air 
cooled VW engines is intended to be via splash. Formula V racers (who use 
type I engines) usually race with the oil level a little above the "full" 
mark.
