The following is a wonderful URL for what is basically Germany's
"Books-In-Print" catalog, plus capability of ordering.

--

Start
at a Dusseldorf bookstore (German and English)

http://www.stern-verlag_dot_de/

or go to the search engine (only German)

http://www.buchkatalog_dot_de/bin/isuche.exe?aktion=suche&caller=sternvlg

and find every book available on the German market.
