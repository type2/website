Sorry, christian, no glass here...just thought I'd chime in to tell everyone
that my 71 bus sometimes gets "too hot" even in the cold Connecticut winter
nights with my BN4 gas heater.  I haven't cracked (as in broken) any windows
yet, but I do occasionally open a vent or roll down the window.  My heater
is not ducted to the main heat tube that defrosts the front windshield,
though.  I use the stock heat exchanger configuration to provide the
windshield defrost and the gas heater to heat the interior in general.
Christian, how is your gas heater configured--in line with the main heat

know how you have it set up.

right to gloat!!
