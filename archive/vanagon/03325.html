Well, one of the books in my little "collection" is called "How to Hotrod
your air-cooled VW".  Sounds like a trash book, but it is just the opposite.
It is chocked full of good information on how the engines work and even
includes a complete history of the type I engine, along with pictures of
early prototypes.  It tells of the dangers of increased compression, all
about headers and exhaust, to name a few topics.  One thing they mention
that I want to try is high-ratio rocker arms.  Supposedly there are some
Empi 1.4-1 arms that you can put on the VW.

Some of this stuff is not applicable to late model water cooled busses, 
but some is.  The book is a great investment, IMO.  It's an HP book, yellow.
I don't remember the author offhand.
