I repaired the seal on our 74 by scraping out the rotten clips and glueing 
it back in place using Loctite Superbonder. Be careful not to use excessive 
amounts of adhesive. Just do a little at a time. I used spring clamps to 
hold it in place then peeled back and glued about 12 inches at a time. 
ALERT- Superbonder is a fast anaerobic adhesive IT WILL GLUE YOU TO THE ROOF 
OR YOURSELF IN SECONDS
