If a particular area is suspected to be leaking this starter spray is indeed
an ideal way to identify the leak. AS long as the amount of spray is small
and away from ignition and exhaust areas it is probably not going to catch
fire. Spraying water, as mentioned by Dean, is probably a good idea to trying
to identify the leak area. Starter spray is then used to narrow down to where
exactly the leak is. Humm... should have learned these net wisdom earlier.
