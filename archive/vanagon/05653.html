i think they are referring to the changeover of the model year ... vw used
to change in july-august, so any buses made in august of 1971 are actually
the 1972 models (with the type 4 engine ... which has very different heat
exchangers). you bus would have been made in november of 1970, in order to
be a 1971 model.

the price ($79) sounds about right ... i think. seems like Rocky Mountain
(Bus Stop) wanted about the same thing for theirs.  their catalog is really
worth getting, by the way. some really neat stuff in there.
