Hey Matt, that's Syncronauts to you!  :-)

In my limited experience, (Wintertime in the mountains, skiing, etc.) the
Syncro has been excellent in the snow but I haven't encountered much ice. The
line about the long wheelbase doesn't seem to make any sense to me, even if
the Syncro had a long wheelbase.  The Syncro has a relatively short
wheelbase, I remember it beeing just about 6" longer than a CJ7.  An
important key to traction in the Syncro or the friend's 4x4 is a Locking
Differential.  This was an option on the Syncro rear differential and
available aftermarket for the 4x4.  I can make all the difference in the
world!

Somehow, I don't think that any volume of Internet comments will settle this
argument.  Your friends are just going to do some head-to-head testing to see
for themselves!
