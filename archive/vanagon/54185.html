The only experience I've had with recalls is on my my '85 Jetta. I 
heard about a heater core recall, and took the car to the dealer in 
'92 asking about it. They gladly checked that it had not been done, 
and replaced it free of charge. I was the 2nd owner, the car had 
~200k kms on it, and had not seen dealer service for at least 2-3 
years, but they did not even ask.
