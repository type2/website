The following recommendations have come from the list in the past:  

Those dirty hairline cracks in the top can be cleaned with X-14 brand
mold/mildew remover. Just be sure to wash it all off the top and wash it off
painted surfaces well.

The expensive, but best way to refinish the pop-top is through gel coating.
Boating supply shops will provide info on the cost/materials involved for
this.  I remember there are a couple detailed posts on this in the 95
vanagon archive.  

Busgirl Martha refinished her top using a paint on product called Kool Seal.
As I remember, it takes a little work to spread this evenly without leaving
brush marks and it leaves a rubberized texture after drying, but I believe
she said it looked good.  How's it been holding up, Martha?
