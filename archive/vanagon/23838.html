David:
Since you mention "short block", I will assume you know about the differences in
the exhaust ports on the cylinder heads (Oval vs. rectangle). The cases are just
a bit different, but will interchange. The biggest difference yyou will notice
is the area where the oil breather is on the top of the case. On the Vanagons,
it is a tubular fitting/hole for the breather. On the older cases, it is a
rectangular, open area that a breather box is held onto with a bail clip.

I have used the older case (from a '79 2.0) on a Vanagon rebuild before, and
only had to modify the old-stlye breather to accept the Vanagon style fitting.
Worked fine, no problems. Hope this helps...
