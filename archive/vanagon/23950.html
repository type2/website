The heat exchangers work great for popcorn, too. Just pull the heater
hoses in the engine compartment and dump it in. For faster popping,
don't reconnect them-- just cap so the engine cooling air isn't lost.
Butter on the left and cheese-flavor on the right works for me.  :)

Limited capacity, tho, and a little tough to get it out. And you DON'T
want to put in too much! Turns your back seat into an ejection seat.

A side bennie: some engines supply their own cooking oil. Yum!
