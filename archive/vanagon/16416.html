The previous owner of my camper made curtains by taking hand towels and 
cutting, hemming, and sewing one end of the folded over towel (about 1 
inch).  It took quite a few towels, but makes a nice, non-see thru curtain 
that insulates heat and cold well.  I suppose they could be gotten cheap at 
some discount store - I think that there are 2 on the sliding door, 2 on the 
back hatch, and 3 on the left side.
