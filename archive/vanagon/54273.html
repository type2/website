>From the Bentley:

Torques:
Inj to head: 51 ft/lb
Fuel lines to Injector: 18ft/lb
Replace heat shields between Inj & head when installing.

A few notes about cleaning: 
Only use diesel to clean, or gas to remove carbon, but rinse 
immediately with diesel.

Leakage test for injector:
Should hold 110bar / 1595 psi for 10 seconds without leakage.
Opening pressure:
1740 - 1885psi (used)
1885 - 2001psi (new)

The shim inside the injector can be used to adjust pressure:
increase in thickness of .05mm increases opening pressure by 5 bar / 
72 psi.

Note: I have not taken injectors apart myself - this is just reading 
from the book.
