THIS IS IT!  THIS IS THE BIG ONE!  More than I've got at the moment but we
negotiate a much improved price on the phone and he keeps the porsche rims and
the safaris........

Send a $100 deposit to hold the bus until I can get there.  Pack the toolbox,
take a day off work and fly to LA intending to do a mini furball run and drive
the bus back to San Francisco............

Well, it was factory original (in the sense all the ruuning gear was out of

drive (short cos the steering was literally dangerous and brakes had to be

of the dipstick......

I couldn't get my deposit back out of the guy (I held the bus for you man I
could've sold it ten times over .... YEAH right! ) and I'm $150 down for the
(now return) airfare......

OK, I'm willing to write this one off as a learning experience.  But what do
