Hi all,

One thing often mistakenly recommended is cleaning paper air filters with
compressed air.  This loosens the dirt, and the first time the engine is
started after cleaning a huge cloud of grit enters the engine.  Want proof?
 Clean your shop-vac's filter then start it up and watch the cloud of dust
that appears.   (ok for a shop vac--not for an engine)
