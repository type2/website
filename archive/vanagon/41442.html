The mod will work on 091/1, 094 (diesel), and syncro trannys. Model years 
1980-85 use an 091 tranny with a different linkage setup. Translation: The 
mod will work on all '86 and up Vanagons and on diesels. It will not work 
on an '84 Vanagon, unless it's a diesel. You can still rebuild your linkage 
with new bushings and boots--I'm sure that would improve the shifting on 
your van.
