Marlin,

A couple of things catch my attention: 

1. If the engine's missing, you might have a shot tranny. There's a 
reason it didn't make it back in the bus.

2. "Mostly surface rust" means there's more. Is it rusted underneath?" 
are there some holes? Since I'm no body worker, rust gets terminal once 
it goes deep enough. My '71 has rust underneath and a few holes near the 
wheel wells. I wouldn't buy a bus with this much rust ever again...unless 
I learn some really good welding tricks (I may take a class one of these 
days) *and* run across the really rare bus that I want to raise from the 
dead. THAT won't be a '69.

I can't say if you've found a good deal or not. If you are sure you can 
repair the rust and you have the will to stay the course, go for it. You 
might also have to rewire, replace tranny, repair/replace CV Joints, etc. 
Just as long as you know what you're jumping into.

Just some food for thought. Joe
