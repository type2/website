>Anyone know how to adjust the windshield washer so they don't fill up the roof
> carrier instead of hit the windshield?

get a thin straight pin or safety pin ... stick the pointed end into the
hole in the washer squirter nozzle (on the "hood"). press firmly and move
the other end of the pin. just a very tiny bit of movement makes a BIG
difference in where the squirt lands on the windshield. it'll take you about
five or six tries to get it where you want it. :)
