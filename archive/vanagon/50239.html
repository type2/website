I have recently had this problem with a factry rebuilt engine I installed.
 It seems the tolerance of some of the 1.8 bar switches,(25 psi) is a bit too
high.  This is the switch near the oil pump.  it would only turn on the
buzzer after an extended highway run.  I repalced this swith with a 1.6 bar
switch and the problem went away.  I did check the oil pressure with a
mechanical gauge.  It did get up to 50 psi at 4000rpm, 35 PSI at 3000.  This
was typical of what I see on a hot engine.  The slightly lower pressure
switch took care of thre problem.
