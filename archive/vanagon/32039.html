I almost bought one of these back in '92.   This is the standard 
configuration for a Carat.  It is very much like the Eurovan MV.  But 
personally I like the Carat over the MV, it just has more character.

I don't think these came with manual transmissions.  With how many times I 
replaced the clutch in my '86 GL, I would not recommend one to anyone who 
couldn't replace them themselves.
