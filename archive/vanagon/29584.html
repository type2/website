>Joe, Joe...to find TDC remove the spark plug in #1 cylinder
turn the engine till the mark on the pull lines up with the 
crack in the crankcase AND the piston is at it's closest point
to the plug hole(use a screw driver or straw to measure).
That's TDC. 

Then turn the distributor so that the rotor is pointing to the
#1 plug wire and you've got the distributor ballparked.  Take
out your timing light and fine tune the timing to specs.
