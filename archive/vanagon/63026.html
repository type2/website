Rod,

I'm probably a li'l late but here's my 2cents:

ANY 4 shocks costing that much should be super hi-performance shocks.
You should be able to find better-than-stock dampers at most
tire/suspension stores (Monroe, Gabriel, Rancho, etc.) for a lot less.
In FourWheeler magazine, you can find ads for ProComp, Softrider,
Explorer and others that all make a very good shock.  And no, they're
not "off road only" shocks, that's just how they're marketed.  In short,
non-stock shocks from your local suspension shop or mail order.
