they fit -- no belt required.  During the Grendel Episode I lost so much
weight I had to move my belt over four holes.  Since then my levis have had
plenty of room around the waist.  

It has been nearly four months since I brought Grendel home.  She is slowly
assuming her original factory-built contours and losing weight, a few pounds
of bondo here, a few pounds of bondo there....  :-)  At the same time I've
obviously been regaining the poundage I shed in Washington.  

Saturday I picked up the last batch of photos I'd taken on the trip north,
these covering the last few repairs and the trip home.  The photos, and then
the trousers, served to rekindle memories of that unfortunate trip.

I've decided the damage to Grendel's driver's-side-rear corner is not
