I hope I didn't come off as looking like I was saying "it's your
fault the bus burned!".  There is very little that SF could be
responsible for in an engine fire when all they sold you was a
longblock, though.  The fire almost certainly started from a fuel
line, whether from the line coming loose, being rubbed or pinched

and each connection is a sourch for a leak.

were not injured.

the cheapest insurance I can't afford to be without.
