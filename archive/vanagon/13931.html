  The short answer is: Any type IV engine <'72 or later bus, any 914/4,
  411/412> will fit. Caveats are: You will need the exhaust system, since
  the '79 heat exchangers are unique to that year <I'm assuming that you'd
  have the '79 stuff>. If your '79 originated in California the Fuel Injection
  ECU will be expecting an input from an oxygen sensor. The clutches increased
  in diameter as the engine displacement grew so you will need the one that
  matches the flywheel on the engine you get.
  I'd suggest a '78 or '79 2.0 if you can find it on account of the 
  hydraulic lifters.
