Subject: Sound deadening material mentioned in Hot VWs


Hi all,

Yesterday, I received my Dec. issue of Hot VWs and read with interest
the article on page 92 about adding sound deadening material to the
interior of "Project Streetwise" their project VW sedan.

They mention three products made by "Accumat" and give a phone number for
a place called Scosche Industries in CA that is a supplier of this material.
The phone number in the article is wrong and will connect you with a
tee-shirt company! (the wrong number is (800) 621-3695)

I looked up the area code for Moorpark, CA and then directory asistance
gave me the correct phone number which is (805) 523-0687.  If you call
you will be told (by a recording) to send $3 for a catalog to:

    Scosche Industries
    P.O. Box 8099
    Moorpark, CA  93020-8099

They will send a catalog and a certificate worth $3 on your first order.

This is for anyone who is interested, I know nothing about the company
or the product mentioned above except for what I read in the Hot VW's article.
I thought some of you might like to know more about sound deadening and might
have been frustrated by the incorrect phone number in the Hot VWs article.

I'm re-doing the interior of one of my busses right now and am very interested
in making this bus as weather-tight, insulated, and quiet inside as I can.

Cheers,
