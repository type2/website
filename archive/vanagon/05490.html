Subject: Re: copper washers??

I've been heating and reusing the copper washers on oilfill plugs for 
years.  I used to be a goldsmith, did alot of model making in copper.  
Copper gets brittle when worked and requires annealing by heat to make it 
soft again.  As you continually reheat and reuse (i.e. reharden) the 
metal it will start to get flatter and wider.  After 3 or 4 times you'd 
be better off replacing the wAsher with a new one.
