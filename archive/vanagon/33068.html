Martha,

  Those heaters are likely another version of those used by the military with
MREs,(Meals Ready to Eat or Meals Refused by Ethiopians). The meals can be
had for around $3 and the heaters for about $1 where ever fine surplus is
sold(gun shows etc.).

  At the risk of sounding like a low brow, the meals are actually quite good
and include appetizer, condiments, drink,dessert, main course and toilet
paper all at the rip of a bag!  The heaters work as you described although 5
minutes seems more like it.

  Keep a couple in the camper for emergency snacking .I recommend the BBQ and
rice but that may be just a southern tendency.
