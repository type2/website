on with the headlight switch when I bought my 84' Vanagon last Dec.  I'd

14 point wire connector mates.  Then the instrument panel lighted with

I turned off the ignition key and everything worked. 

I tried disconnecting my repairs (including a disconnected wire to the right
heater blower dashboard light and the auto transmission position light), and
have no instrument panel lights, no turn signals, no windshield wipers.

key is on.
