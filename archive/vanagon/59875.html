One of the reasons I'm personally devoted to using synthetic oil is the
cleanliness of the inside of the engine that results.  My longest test to
date is a Nissan bought new in '86, & after break-in I used Red Line for
the 1st 50k miles, got smart after talking to a professional race engine
builder & switched to Mobil 1 for the next 100k, at which point (when I
sold it) the interior was literally like brand new; not a speck of varnish
or carbon visible under the valve cover or in the crankcase (pulled covers
for seal replacement).  My daughter & I rebuilt a Rabbit motor last year
(135k) & the story was the same.
