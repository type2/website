Dave Phillips writes:
> 
> On the recommendations of fine members of this list, I have added an oil
> pressure gauge to my extensive dashboard of instrumentation.  What pressure
> range is expected of a typical 1600 engine?

Not sure if the 1600 would have significantly different pressures than my 
73, but here's my data for comparison purposes:

Cold idle:		60 psi

Oil temp ~180
-------------
Warm idle:		20 psi
Warm cruise (60 mph)	50 psi

Oil temp ~210
-------------
Hot idle		10 psi
Hot cruise (60 mph)	40 psi

Oil temp > 230
-------------
Very hot idle		8 psi (flickering oil light)
Very hot cruise		30-35 psi

-David

p.s. I use Castrol 20-50
