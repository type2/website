Dan,
This summer I rebuilt the 2.0 litre in my '77.  Put it back in the bus
and proceeded for the next 2-1/2 hours to try to get the damn thing
started.  Couldn't get it started to save my life.  Checked compression
and all was fine except #2 read only 80psi.  Came out the next morning
and it cranked first try.  Ran engine for 30 minutes and checked the
compression again.  This time #2 read 130psi.  All I can figure is I
had flooded the engine and totally washed #2 with gas.  After sitting
overnight enough evaporated to start it. After running engine finally 
got enough oil into #2 to seal.  Don't know if this might also apply 
in your case.  Best of luck.
