excellent local source for most things, I use the PP catalog as a 
start and look for better prices elsewhere but would order anything 
from them again in a second.  I've ordered stuff for some pretty 
bizarre cars from them BTW with little problem, Q syncro, Corrado, 
'77 Audi Fox, etc.  Again plastic is your best protection.
