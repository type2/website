Radio Shack sells two little bitty magnets (3/16" dia., 1/16" thick) for 
about $2.  These are *really* strong, and make excellent drain plug magnets.
I super-glue mine on, ro drill a shallow hole and inset them.  A cheap bit
to add to your bus.
