I had this happen on my `74.  I took it to a shop who told me that the valve
seats were getting pounded down or somesuch (the "or somesuch" is mine, I
don't remember exactly what they said).  The upshot was that I had to replace
both heads.  The symptom I had was that I kept having to loosen some of my
valves until the adjustmo screwy things were backed out so far that I had to
bend the feeler guage funny to do it.

There could well be other causes.  I can't think of any that would be good,
though.  The one thing I would check would be to tighten my own valves so you
can keep track of what they're doing yourself.
