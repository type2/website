For what its worth: the left side of our 82 exhaust manifold needed
replacing; VW wanted 400 for it. We found  a wreckers that would take the
part off intact and sell it for 75. My point is this: if the piece of
which you speak is cast iron then it is likely that a wrecker can get you one
cheap and it will be as good as new. If anything the Vanagon exhaust
system is pretty rugged so you shouldn't have trouble finding a used one.
