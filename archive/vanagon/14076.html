Subject: Oil cooler/heater

At the weekend I visited the Motorsports show at Shefield to buy some safety 
gear and picked up a huge stack of manufacturers bumf. One of them shows a 
heater, specially for VWs that comprises an oil cooler matrix with a small 
fan attatched, an oil take off plate and a thermostatic switch. The price is 
a bit stiff at 135 UKpounds, and I've seen homemade setups done for a lot 
less, but it looks like a neat installation. It might be worth getting their 
catalogue just  to copy the design.
