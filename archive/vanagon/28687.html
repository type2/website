A very interesting trip would include the North Shore East of Quebec City
in the newly created state of Inner Quebecistan.  All kidding aside, the
trip to Tadoussac (literally "Twin Peaks" in Montignard) at the mouth of
the Saguenay and then up to Lac St. Jean and back down to Bic, and around
the Gaspe and over into NH/Vermont is a very good trip. This
includes Whale Watching, Ferry rides and some great camping in the heart
of Lower Canada. All told 10-14 days.
