I've found that there is enough air pressure in the aerosol
cans of carb cleaner to blow out anything that's in there.
With the carb soaked/covered with the carb cleaner fluid,
residue and particulate matter flows freely with little need
for pressure.

Certainly there are nozzles which fit on air compressor
lines but gas stations normally don't use the "quick
release" fittings on hoses available to the public
eventhough they use them in their shops.
