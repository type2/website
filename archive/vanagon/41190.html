I've been installing KYB shocks on all VW's that come thru our shop 
for the last5 years.  We've had nothing but great results from these 
shocks.  On 68-79 type 2 we use the white gas shocks in the rear and the 
slightly softer silver ones inthe front.  Due to the fact that the 
weight is mostly in the rear, putting a softer shock in the front makes 
the ride very pleasant.

  Kyb has a lifetime guarantee and 8 years ago I installed a set on my 2 
wd toyota p\u and they are as good today as the day I installed them.

  The Monroe and other companys shocks don't seem to hold up as well to 
the use (abuse) that the VW owners due to there cars.  I do live in an 
area were there is saltair and mountains to deal with so there is just 
reason for some of the things I see on a daily basis.

  Just my 2 cents on the shock issue.
