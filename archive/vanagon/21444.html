Christer,
In order to get my brakes (both pedal and emergency) where I like them I 
tighten them up _past_ the point that they touch the drums, where there 
is firm contact betweenten the drums and shoes.  The manual assumes that 
the automatic adjuster works and I've never been able to get mine to do 
anything.  Get the brake drums to where they lock, then back off a couple 
of star points.  I've been adjusting mine this way for the 4 years I've 
had my 81 and haven't really seen any appreciable wear on the shoes from 
this practice.  

Follow the procedure in the manual on bleeding brakes, it's pretty 
straight forward.

Jack
