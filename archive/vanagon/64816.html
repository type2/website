Here are the results of the numbering digests inquiry.

Unfortunately, ListServ doesn't support numbering digests.  What they *do*
support is MIME digests, which is an internet standard.  Unfortunately,
Eudora doesn't support this standard.  Also unfortunately, LSoft (the
company that produces ListServ) is rather customer-unfriendly and is not
willing to talk with Qualcomm to  get this problem fixed.

The good news is that there is a free mail package called Pegasus that
fully supports MIME digests.  It's a very good package, and has all of the
features of Eudora Pro.  If you're willing to switch mailers, then you can
turn on MIME digests by sending the following letter to
listserv_at_gerry.sdsc_dot_edu:

     set vanagon mime

If you *do* own Eudora, send mail to eudora-suggest_at_eudora_dot_com asking them
to support MIME email digests.
