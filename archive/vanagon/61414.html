The fan is exactly as you guess - blows across the condenser and is
thermostat-controlled.

Don't know what year Westy you have, but in mine ('90), the fan is
independent of any switch, and runs whenever the thermostat says to.     I
believe the fan only makes things more efficient (is needed when the inside
of the van gets above 80F or so), so the main effect of not having it
running is probably that the stuff in the fridge may get warm.
