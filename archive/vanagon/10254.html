  I think the way Westfalia did those pre-74 pop-top openings was to use a 
  sunroof body, without the sunroof. My '72 Westfalia <with forward hinged
  pop-top> had a "double-roof" behind the opening which formed a cavity like
  the one that a sunroof would be withdrawn into. It was a good place to carry
  skis, by the way.

  Soooo, if your present bus has a sunroof, you could go ahead with the 
  conversion to pop-top.
