Saw a '90 Syncro Camper for sale at one of the local repair shops.

Here's the stats:

'90 Syncro Camper
Titian Red
Differential Lock
47K miles

Here's the killer... I saw the "sale" sticker on the side, so I walked
in and asked what his price was.  $24,500!!!  Derek Drew, don't die 
laughing at this absurd price.  The guy told me with a straight face
that people's mouths drop when they hear the price, or they just walk 
out without another word.  I just started laughing, until he said, very
seriously, "Well, someone will pay that much."  

Sadly I'm afraid he's right.

Well, does anyone on the list want me to post the phone number of the
place?  I thought not.
