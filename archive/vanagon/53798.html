I had a similar symptom on a 79 Olds Cutlass years ago.  It turned out to be 
blockage in the exhaust system caused by the catalytic convertor freezing up 
after running a few miles in freezing temps.  When we disconnected the exhaust 
from the engine it ran fine. Since I could legally drive this way I had to 
replace the convertor.  Hope this helps in your diagnosis.
