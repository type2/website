I use vice grips to pull those 2 steeing column "special" bolts.
I lay back the whole dash, (with the glove box, inst panel, heater controls,
etc, taken out)
I disconnect the heater blower assy, (mark the cable hookup spots) and slide
it out. (Leave the hoses attached)
Bust loose those plastic welds,(split them at the seam), pull the screws and
seperate the blower box.
Pull the blower motor out.
I keep the dash supported with a 2x4, and don't for get the special clips
for the blower box plastic welds you had to split
