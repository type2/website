> 1) the EGR light is on. how can I reset this?

Congratulations on your purchase.  To reset the EGR light, follow the
speedometer cable from the left front wheel up to a black box mounted on
the frame near the spare tire carrier.  On this black box is a white
button.  Push the button until it clicks, and your EGR light will stay off
for another 15,000 miles.

> 
> 2) my vanagon was equipped with the auxiliary gas heater. Are these safe?
It works VERY well.
> 
>From what I've heard, they're safe.  If your van is air-cooled, you will
have an Eberspacher BA-6 heater.  The Eberspachers apparently have many
safeguards to keep the things from blowing up/catching fire.

> 5) Are the brake components the same as later breadloaves? (calipers,
drums, rotors, pads?)
> 
Hmm, good question.  The rear drums are probably the same, but the rotors
aren't.  Buses have rotors with studs for lug nuts while Vanagons have lug
bolts that thread into holes in the rotor.  I don't know about calipers.
