If the vehicle is equipped with an automatic transmission, empty weight
increase by 40 kg, payload decrease
by 40 kg. With the option 'extended payload' the axle weights are 1200 kg
front and 1400 kg rear.

a without/with canvas

increase by 65 kg)
c w/o rear seats

Towing loads for vehicles with regular payload:
Gasoline and Turbo Diesel: 600 kg for trailers w/o brakes
                          1500 kg for trailers with brakes
Diesel                     600 kg for trailers w/o brakes
                          1200 kg for trailers with brakes

Towing loads for vehicles with extended payload:
Gasoline and Turbo Diesel: 600 kg for trailers w/o brakes
                          1300 kg for trailers with brakes
Diesel                     600 kg for trailers w/o brakes
                          1000 kg for trailers with brakes
