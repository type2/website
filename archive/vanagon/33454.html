Hi everybody. I've been absorbing all the information from this list for 
the past few months and I must say that it has helped me quite a bit. I 
just wanted to thank everybody for their knowledge. I just finished 
reading a book called Volkswagen Blues, written by Jacques Poulin. It's a 
story that you'll all enjoy if you haven't already read it. Two friends 
travel from Quebec across the States to the West coast. They travel in a 
'68-'72? Westy, not sure exactly what year, but it's really quite a good 
book. It discusses many of the quirks of owning a VW van like gas heater 
problems, roadside repairs, mountain passes and the joy of having a home 
away from home. It was originally written in french but the version I 
read was in english. The ISBN # is 0-7710-7158-2. It is published by 
McClelland and Stewart in Toronto. Hope you guys check it out.
