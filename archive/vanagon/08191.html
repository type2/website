My manually-informed guess is that 5W30 is way too low for your climate
AND your engine type. For the aircooled engines 10W30 is not recommended
until temperatures are consistently below 10 C (about 50 F). Unless these
temps are what you are getting you are better off running 5W50 Mobil1. I
have been using 10W30 synthetic in the 82 and oil pressure does drop off
a bit when temps hover around 50, try mixing in some higher weight oil if
necessary (rather than changing all of it) even dino oil can be added if
necessary. Check the manual first......DC --
