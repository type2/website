James,

It would be worth checking the injector pump timing and the cold start
control to trouble shoot the missing when cold.  The miss can be a sign of
retarded injector timing.  If the injectors have more than 90,000 miles
on them, I would pull them and have a diesel injection shop check them
out.  The smoke could be caused by worn injectors.
