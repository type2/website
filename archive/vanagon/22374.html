FYI,

There's a mounting bracket for this. It's a four-bolt plate with a long
bolt that goes through the tire; then a plate and wing-nut secure the tire.
I think it's generic and about $10 or $15....then there are covers that run
from about $30 to more than an original, chromed emblem. Problem is, you
have to drill holes in the front of your bus to mount a tire up there.
Ouch!

Both are available through many mail-order stores and FLAPS.

BTW, one solution to emblem theft is to keep yours a little rusty. Of
course, this coming from a guy who took his off and stripped paint and rust
(then primered) within days of getting the van.
