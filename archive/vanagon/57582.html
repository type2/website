One larger wire would be stiff and increase the possibility of breakage at the 
connections.  Especially with all the movement a Vanagon engine has to offer.  
These wires though are still too small to carry the full output of the 
alternator without heating up and causing an excessive voltage drop.  I run 
another #8 wire from the alternator to the fuse box.  Makes a noticeable 
difference in light brightness. 
Dennis
