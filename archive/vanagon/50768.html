It seems that somthing is over pressurizing the system to cause repeated hose
failures.  A swollen expansion tank is another cause of concern.  Something
is wrong.  Also check cooling fan operation and have a leakdown test
performed on the engine.  It is possible to have combustion gases enter the
cooling system without a noticeable los of coolant or performance.
