Chris,

I've run Castrol (sp?) 5-50w synthetic in my last '86 westy and put 150,000
miles on it without one engine problem.  My SAAB 9000 has 250,000 miles
using synthetic and is going strong.  When I purchased my '91 westy it had
28,000 miles and I changed to synthetic.  I hope I can get 200,000+ from
this one. I change my oil every 5,000 miles and use a good filter.  I know
it cost more but I feel its worth it.
