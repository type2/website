Believe it or not, cigarette lighters come in two sizes SAE (US size), =
and DIN (European size). The SAE size (on your power cord) is slightly =
smaller than the DIN size (the lighter socket on your EV).

There are two solutions. The first is to gently bend the ground tab on =
your power cord just enough so that it makes contact in the socket. The =
other is to mount a second lighter socket of the proper diameter.

I'm going to do the second soon. I found that the little cubby hole to =
the right of the steering wheel makes a nice holder for my flip-phone, =
so I'm going to put a second socket in that cubby.
