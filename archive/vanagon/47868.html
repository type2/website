    Try this...as your driving and it's running bad, turn off the 
    ignition switch for 1 second. If it runs good for the next 15 
    seconds or so, you have a computer problem: temp. sensor, O2,
    etc.   IF there is no change check the cap, rotor, of igition
    wires.
