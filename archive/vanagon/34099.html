Recently I saw a 1970 bus that the PO had bought new in Germany,
toured around in, and then had it shipped over here to the US.

After it got here, in subsequent years it needed some brake work. The
PO told me that he discovered at that point, that the drums and shoes
were different from normal US models in 1970, and he had to get
shoes and a drum from Germany!

Check the part numbers carefully, between your bus and the US models that
US dealers have parts for. They might not match!
