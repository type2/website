Try "Zap a Gap" (or just ZAP) which is a cyanoacrylic glue designed for model 
airplane applications.  Try to get it a a good hooby shop.I knew a guy once who
 zapped his engines into the  models rather than using bolts.  Use very little 
and do not get any on your  hands or you will glue them to the work.
