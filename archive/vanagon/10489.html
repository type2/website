> 
>  2) Is the fuel pump supposed to start working when the ignition is 
>     turned on or just after the engine has been turned over?

The fuel pump works from key on to key off, *but* it gets power from
different places depending on whether you are running (key in "on" 
position) or starting (key in start position).  This changover is
accomplished by the "double relay" mounted on the left side of the plate
separating the fuel tank from the engine compartment.  I "tested" mine by
disconnecting the starter(at the motor), crawling under the car, and
having a friend do the switch while i held the pump (a cylinder over the
left rear wheel). 

> 
>  3) Should I scrap the FI and get a carb (or two)?

I recommend against it very strongly I have put 80K trouble free miles on 
my '78 with nary an FI problem.  Once working these are as bulletproof as 
any VW system (don't ask about my starter problems{grrr}).  Of course, YMMV!
