Even if you have the shaft balanced, there is a good chance it will stil
vibrate.  I think that when a joint goes bad bad, the whiping actually
distorts the shaft rendering it useless.  This is only way I can expalin that
once a joint goes bad, the shaft can not be fixed.  Also note that the
u-joints are often shimmed to one side to maintain concentricity.
