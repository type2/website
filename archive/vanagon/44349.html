It is a copper ring, on a short piece (6 inch) of stiff braided
two-conductor wire. you can mount them on one of the head bolts, or on the
spark plug. (Mine's on the plug, where it supposedly gets a slightly hotter
reading.)
It has a plastic plug on the other end where it connects to the wire coming
from the gauge.
If he did lose it, you can get them mailorder for about $10. I just picked
some place out of the back of one of the mags, don't remember the name
anymore.
