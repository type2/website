Hi All,

There have been several posts recently on AVP from other shops, etc.  I
thought I'd post my recent dealings with them as an individual since I live
nearby and dealt face to face rather than by phone, etc.  

Had my engine from my 84 pulled a couple of weeks ago as I was planning to
redo the heads.  Made the mistake of pulling the jugs off with the heads and
didn't really want to deal with the wrist pins and rings.  I went AVP before
removing the engine as I was thinking about buying my heads rebuilt from
them.  Leonard  took about an hour and showed me around the shop and what
they do to the heads.  As a layman,  their operation seems 1st rate and I
learned a lot about the waterboxer during my visit.  Since the engine was out
of the van, I decided to take the whole thing in to AVP and let them rebuild
the top end.  

We pulled the connecting rod bearings and they all looked OK so I decided to
proceed with mainly replacing the heads.  They also replaced the connecting
rod bearings, piston rings, polished the pistons, front and rear seals, pilot
bearing.  Leonard also rebuilt the lifters too for nothing.  Finally my
engine got bench tested and everything checked out nicely.  Oil pressure 68
and compression 180-180-180-185.

The whole thing cost about $900 including the rebuilt heads.  It would have
cost me about $600 doing it myself just replacing the heads so I feel it was
money well spent.  I have much more confidence in the engine  since it was
done professionally and tested too.  They promised me the engine in 2 days
and it was done right on time.  Overall it was a very positive experience.
 Erin and Leonard were very helpful and I would have no hesitation buying a
motor from them in the future.  The engine runs great too and no more
fluctuating coolant levels!!!!
