from what i gather, looking at and thru some of the junk i've got, the only
place that is still (maybe, just maybe) making the vanagon-body is South
Africa ... which drives on the, ahem, other side of the road (so the steering

head on the a-pillar getting in) had better start gathering 'em up about us.
before they start getting rare, like the old split-window buses. :(
