> Also I noted on the google search, the 356 pulleys don't have a bushing. Buy a mounting bushing, be sure it has the threaded end to fit your nut. I happened to have an aftermarket VW pulley where the bushing was not welded into the rear half of the pulley. Took it apart and the bushing fit perfectly into the 356 parts. Don't forget the shim washers.
