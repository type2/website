Thanks Conrad,
I purchased an AC Industries kit. The literature says they fit 14" stock 
and most aftermarket 15" wheels. I found a reference on The Samba that 
some material needs to ground off the caliper assembly to clear the 
stock wheels. I know that Wagens West has an extra fee to fit the Baby 
Stangs to the 15" wheels. I'm going over to a friends house to compare 
my 15's to his 14's. I'll post again and and let you know the outcome.

I don't know what calipers AC is using. I asked about the pads and they 
were a bit cagey and didn't say who's they are using but they said I 
could buy the pads at most auto parts stores.

Chris
