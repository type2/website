FYI from the factory parts fiche:

Dipstick is the same for the Bus 003 and 010 Trannies.  VW 090 321 431

Tubes are different, but they may interchange......

Early 003  "NA" Tranny  VW 011 321 405 A
Late 003  "NB" Tranny  VW 011 321 405 B

010 Tranny  VW 090 321 405 A (Has a mounting bracket the 003s do not have).
