Hi LJ, 

If it's anything like mine, it will not be in rails, but kind of leveraged into two brackets on the floor, and that center one you spotted on the highback. If you grab the front center of the bottom of the seat and lift it up, and then out, you should be able to see the brackets that hold the bottom in, towards the rear of the bottom of the seat, a few inches from the back edge of the bottom cushion. Then the top/ back attachment of the seat should then be able to be lifted out - or maybe it slides out from the bottom of that center bracket, it's been a while. I can send some pics of a seat removed if it will help. Mike
