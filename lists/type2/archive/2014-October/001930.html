      At nearly 70, I AM getting too old for this,- (see next paragraph 
for query) -got around to pulling the 75 engine from daughter's bus, as 
it was having trouble getting up hills and failed the "should stall when 
letting out the clutch when parked/blocked" test, even with cable 
maximally loosened. Another clue, even when loose, it seemed to try to 
grab right off the floor.  Got a friend to help- backed her bus up to 
the garage onto 3 2x10 boards nailed together, then jacked up the front 
to equal the incline of the driveway.  Bumper had to go with the Auch 
towhitch hanging down, but spent an hour getting one side off as it had 
rotted away around the welded nut due to a mouse nest in the box beam 
under the hatch door. Then proceeded to remove heater box connections, 
all the engine wall protrusions (electrical, cable, vacuum, and fuel- 
neded two fuel line clamps. 4 nuts off on trans housing, and used a 
motorcycle jack underneath to let it down after yanking.  Had to remove 
oil filler, coil, shrouding around the back to pull engine forward 
enough to drop.  Dropped it down on jack, and then proceeded to the 
trick of letting engine to ground- use a digging bar and a piece of 6x6 
to lift one side and stick in enough 2x4 shims to clear jack- OOPS, 
NEVER DISCONNECTED THE MPC FROM THE "BRAIN"! - was still there very 
taught, and had yanked injector plugs on right.  Now I worry if wires to 
MPC are intact )-8  Lifted other side, shims in, jack out, and slide 
engine out.  Had to lower front to lift back enough.
      Proceeded to clutch pressure plate removal, and it's trisquare 
bolts!  Could not find to one I had for CV joints, tried a 6mm hex head, 
worked on 2 but stripped third- so now the question- what size is the 
trisquare?
