and if you stay in Brunswick and want a really nice side trip for a day.
go over the potomac on rt 15 South to just North of Leesburg
turn Left onto 633 White's Ferry road,
cross the potomac on the White's Ferry get the historical tour is 
fascinating and the Civil war history
afterwards. follow White's Ferry road on Md side to
Martinsburg Road. is last original pre civil war roadway
to left on Dickerson road Rt 28
to left on Tuscaroro rd also rt 28 back to Brunswick

one of the nicest day trips in the area
