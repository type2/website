> My solution for this was to take an inexpensive gasket scraper that I
> bought off a table of Chinese tools at the Syracuse Nationals, grind the
> end flat, & then shape the corners in a "cut & try" fashion until I had an
> exact fit with the subtle (concave) curve of the slot. The square shaft of
> the scraper will accept a wrench for a little extra leverage, if necessary.
> The scraper (pre-grinding) looks like this:
> http://www.harborfreight.com/catalogsearch/result?q=gasket+scraper
