> The 78 system has a LOT of parts and gaskets to connect them.  The parts might be cheaper individually, but the 72-74 system is so much simpler:
>
> 4 copper gaskets at the head.
> 2 heater boxes.
> 2 muffler gaskets.
> 1 muffler, 1 tailpipe gasket and 1 tailpipe.
>
