Aside from Cooker's, the other name that pops up on a regular basis thru
the VW Cruisers and CV3 VW clubs is Skyline Auto restoration and repair in
Madison VA.  Paul's Auto repair in Annandale VA does good work, but I am
not sure if they do restorations.  Going a bit further south, Salem Imports
in Salem VA has a good rep with some of the southern clubs, like the FMBC.
I think there was a place in DE that I heard about but don't know it's
name.  Might search for local DE and PA clubs and see what their members
recommend.
