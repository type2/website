get ALL the parts from a FI bus and transfer to your engine. should work 
fine, maybe run a little rich until you get it diaed in.  don't forget the 
fuel tank and accel cables are different too.

might take a little tweeking but I can not think of a reason why it 
wouldn't work.  you won't have a place to put the ECU unless you cut a 
slot in the firewall but that could be put in a fabricated bracket. 
highly recomend a new wire harnes from Kyle.

good luck
Bob
