Hi Volks,

You don't HAVE to run with the dual-outlet fuel tank to make the FI 
work. I put a T in the single-line output from a 1973 fuel tank back in 
2004 and it's been running fine ever since.

I've also seen a custom made dual output made to fit the same thread 
used on the single outlet tanks, this will also work and does not 
require a tank swap.
