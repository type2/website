I find the â75 only TB (throttle body) with EGR tends to wear faster than the later style TB because of exhaust gas contamination. If you have a later TB, you should use it.

Using the â78 airbox is also a good idea. It omits the pre-heat valve and flapper assembly and allows better airflow to the AFM. It also incorporates the charcoal canister return into the emissions bypass valve.
