Hi Volks,

Just a friendly reminder that BOTC is coming up soon. August 9th, 10th
and 11th, the family-friendly buses of Southern Ontario will gather with
bus friends from far and wide to celebrate the thirteenth annual Buses
Of The Corn.

Of special note this year is the new location, Selwyn Beach Conservation
Area just 20 KM (12 miles) north of Peterborough Ontario. Our previous
location at Heber Down in Brooklin had become rather suburbanized due to
local housing developments and the Conservation Authority who regulated
the property decided to discontinue group camping on the site. So we had
to find a new home, and we think this one is a winner! Check out the
pics online at www.busesofthecorn.ca and see for yourself.

The cost structure of the event will need to change with the venue... We
will now be charging $20 per bus as an event fee and an additional
$7/person/day will be collected and remitted as camping fees. All
profits will be donated to Sick Children's Hospital in Toronto.

I will have the 2013 T-Shirt design finalized soon, and will post
details on pre-ordering at that time.

Happy Trails,

Greg Potts
Toronto, Ontario Canada
