Just to limit the "me too!"'s a bit:

confirmed, busdepot.com is broken (specifically all the content from 
cdn.busdepot.com) from some locations - looks like a (or many) broken edge 
servers from the content provider..

If you want to provide useful info, if you're seeing the problem, and you 
know how to use the telnet command (best way to get the data..), do the 
following (please send it to ron and perhaps myself directly, not the 
list..):


telnet cdn.busdepot.com 80
GET /



You'll get a result like this:

telnet cdn.busdepot.com 80
Trying 108.161.188.131...
Connected to shop.busdepot.netdna-cdn.com.
Escape character is '^]'.
GET /
You are hitting the NetDNA Dallas Datacenter<br>
<img src=netdna.gif?city=6 >

Connection closed by foreign host.


A nslookup or dig on cdn.busdepot.com from your location would be equally 
useful if less fun. :)
