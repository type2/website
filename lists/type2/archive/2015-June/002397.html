> Also Bob , even if the distributor notch correspond to the TDC, there is no 
> guaranty  that your distributor is correctly mounted. You can still have a 
> 180deg wrong rotation at the shaft level when you reintroduce the distributor 
> back to the engine. you can test it by reversing the firing order . That 
> happened to my engine a few month ago, it was not firring until I realize the 
> distributor may not have been correctly installed . I had to change the order 
> of the firing cable of the distributor, than it was working , for some 
> reason( I would like someone to explain me), my engine was not very powerful 
> but the demonstration was conclusive. After that you need to take your 
> distributor out and rotate the end shaft 180deg and put it back in. don't 
> forget to change back the firing wire order again. Be careful removing the 
> distributor by not dropping the spring inside the engine or you'll have to 
> open the case again !! (look in the manual)
>
> Good luck !
