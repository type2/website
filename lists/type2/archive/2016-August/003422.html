A quick anecdotal point after looking at the photos: I haven't had good
luck with the tiny electrode platinum plugs in general ('72 1.8 L
carbureted, '80 2.0 FI), but I also wonder if those plugs would tend to
blast off any carbon so it would be harder to tell if it's running rich,
and I think the plugs reflect what was going on most recently which might
not have been rich.  This is just a wild speculation, so take it for what
it's worth.  You'd still think you could see some buildup at the circular
part closest to the threads if it's rich, so I do wonder exactly what is
going on.  It sounds like you should be getting OK power and not damaging
the engine in any case since you had good ratio readings under the highest
power conditions.

Good Luck!
Dennis
