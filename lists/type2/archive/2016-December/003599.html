Kevin, you can unhook the EGR module entirely. Buy yourself a new speedo
cable because you'll be removing the old one along with the EGR itself, as
the speedometer cable runs though it to log the miles correctly. You can
then sell your module on thesamba.com for mabye 20 or 30 bucks since it
still works.

Source: I did this myself to both my '78 Champagne Editions.

-Patrick
