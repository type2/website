Donating to Type2.com

Since 1996, Type2.com has been kept alive by the generous donations of mailing list subscribers. In spring 2001, both lists responded wonderfully to a request for funds for purchasing the server we are now running on and for paying several months' worth of our Internet connection charges. We still have enough funds to cover the next few months, but we always gladly accept additional donations.

If you'd like to send a check, make it payable to "Aleatha Deaton" with "Type2 Donations" in the memo field and mail it to our treasurer, Leah Deaton:

Type2 Donations
1900 W Chandler Blvd
STE 15-142
Chandler, AZ 85224

If you do not wish to send a check, you may make a donation via PayPal, even if you do NOT have a PayPal account. Please use the button below to proceed.

Please email Leah with any questions.

Thanks to everyone who has donated!
