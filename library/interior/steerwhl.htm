Steering Wheels

by Karl von Salzen

Yes, the padded steering wheel was 78/79.

It will be an instant bolt on swap for the earlier hard plastic steering wheel. Not sure when the splined late column started, somewhere around 74?

The swap has a couple of plusses: The hard wheel gets VERY hot in the sun, soft ones don't. The hard wheels crack, the soft one won't.

The minus: The soft wheel for some strange reason, on humid days, gets sticky. Cleaning it helps a little......

And I have a couple of the late padded wheels if any one wants one.
