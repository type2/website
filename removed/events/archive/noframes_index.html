    Second Annual High Country Bus Festival

    July 24-26 1998, near Boone, NC

    The time has come once again - it is time to converge in a field and spend two days celebrating the gift of BUSES! The second annual HIGH COUNTRY BUS FESTIVAL has been moved into JULY to utilize the warmer weather.

    The festival will be held at THE GRANDFATHER MOUNTAIN CAMPGROUND AND RESORT, which is near BOONE NC. This campground in located at the base of one the most well known and beautiful mountain peaks in the eastern United States. Running along the other edge of the campground is the Watauga River. It really is a great place to enjoy the Blue Ridge Mountains.

    This year we are hoping to focus on a large VENDING AREA. Are you tired of paying a lot of money to go to a swap meet and having to search through a field of Bug parts to find the good bus stuff? No fees will be charged for setting up your display of parts that some busguy out there despratly needs, so bring all your spare parts and make someone's world a happy place! Tee-Shirt sales will be handled on an advance system this year. The shirts will cost $15 each and this money will be due by JUNE 12th! There will be a limited number of shirts available at the event, so please get your order in early. E-mail me for information.

    We have planned a caravan along the scenic Blue Ridge Parkway for Sunday morning before we all depart. Saturday we will simply be hanging out! There will be a large open grill lit on Saturday night so please bring some hotdogs, burgers, chicken, tofu, steak ect! Let's Feast! This area of NC is a tourist destination, there are many things you can do in your free time: lots of stuff for the kids to do! For more information, contact Michael "BUDDAH" Myers, (home) 704-236-9059, work 800-221-4201 x829, email michael.myers@gsle.gensig.com, or check out the web site at http://members.aol.com/docric/highc.html.
