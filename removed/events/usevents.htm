March

  3rd/4th    Annual Hippy Run.  The Run is a 2 day, or extended, untimed Road Rally event covering at least a few hundred miles every year.  We usually go through 4 states, Tennessee, North and South Carolina, and Georgia.  It is a great way to socialize and turns every onlooker's head, watching miles of VWs drive down the road.  This year's Run will be covered by Kathy Jacobs of VW Trends Magazine.  Volkswagens of ALL types, air and water are invited to participate in this event and the turnout is normally a few hundred vehicles. You can find more info and maps on all of the above at www.avwa.com (Appalachian VW Association, in Sevierville, TN) or www.GoodOleVolks.com
