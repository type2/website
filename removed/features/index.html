The Features Page


Howdy!

Thanks for checking out the features page. My name is Kevin and I am your host. I am fairly new VW's. I have had my 1976 Westy (aka Mr. Bananahead, named by three daughters) for a little over a year. So hopefully, if you are new to this like me, you will find something encouraging. If you're an old pro, I hope you'll find something useful.

As you can see, I have divided this page into two parts. One for useful articles/sites on maintaining, restoring, and repairing your bus. The second is for those of you who wish to share your bus with the world.

Here's the catch: There are bunches of sites for the "Look at Me!" side, however, good "Help Me!" sites are a bit hard to come by, especially since we prefer to link to non-commercial sites. So in order to be considered for "Look at Me!", you will also need to submit a "Help Me!" URL for consideration also.
