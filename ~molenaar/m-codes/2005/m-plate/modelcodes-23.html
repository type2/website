Model codes - Type 23 - Kombi

The VW Kombi is a delivery van with windows. Some models have bench seats in the cargo compartment. These bench seats can be easily removed if they need to make room for cargo. The VW Kombi with bench seats is a very economical version of the VW Microbus. It distinguishes itself by the interior side panels made of fibreboard and the headliner which is limited to the cabin only.
The VW Kombi has been used many companies as a basic for a camper conversion.
