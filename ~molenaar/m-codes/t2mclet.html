M-codes starting with a letter

Origin of the information (Or.):
A = Acquired by Alexander Prinz
B = Brochure (Volkswagen, Westfalia...)
D = VW documents
E = From Erik Meltzer's list
P = Found on Production plates of various type 2's
R = Sent in by Ron van Ness
V = VW Museum
W = Westfalia manual or brochure
S = Sent in by someone else

Note
The years placed between brackets are modelyears, not calendar years! For example "1974" would mean this code stands for all busses produced from August 1973 until July 1974. Only in some cases exact dates are mentioned.
