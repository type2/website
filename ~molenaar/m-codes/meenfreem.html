VW T2 production codes (1967-1979 models)

You probably surfed in here because you want to know more about those little plates fixed in your VW transporter. This page tries to explain all the codes and writings that are on them. Nevertheless, some codes remain a mystery, so not all the anwers are here. If have questions about the plates in your car don't hesitate to send me a message: molenari@hotmail.com
