Note

Following defects may be found despite visible sparking at plug connectors:
  • Distributor cap (damp, cracked, burnt by tracking)
  • Rotor defective
  • Loose connections on coil
  • Spark plugs or connectors defective
  • Ignition timing off (breaker points)
  • Ignition leads badly connected
  • Arcing at ignition cables on distributor (through rubber caps)
  • Voltage at terminal 15 on coil too low (minimum = 9 volts)
  • Condenser faulty
