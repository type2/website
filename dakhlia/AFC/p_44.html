Poor output / top speed too low

Test conditions (Road test):

  • Speedometer reading normal
  • Tire sizes and types of tire are to standard specifications
  • Wheels rotate freely (brakes, wheel bearings!)
  • Clutch and clutch play OK
  • No accessories which could reduce top speed (roof rack etc)
  • Break-in period completed (from approx. 3000 miles according to driving style and conditions under which car had been driven)
Faults in basic engine settings
  • Check accelerator cable adjustment (Throttle must be fully open with pedal at full throttle)
  • Check valve clearance with engine cold, adjust if necessary
  • Check ignition timing, adjust if necessary
  • Check intake air preheating (with engine warm, cold air must be drawn in)
  • Check spark plugs for:
    • dampness
    • dirt
    • burning (heat range)
    • electrode gap
    • damage
  • Check compression
