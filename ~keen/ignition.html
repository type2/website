Ignition Parts & Specifications

Presented to you on this page are the complete listings of Bosch Replacement Parts for VW Beetles, Buses and Vanagons, primarily for the US and Canadian Markets. In addition, we have also listed the Tune-Up Specifications needed for the application listed. As an added bonus, we have also listed by application the original Volkswagen Distributor Number, followed by a comma, then the corresponding Bosch Number that is usually stamped on the distributor casing. There is also a "Can Use" listing in each application. If you have questions, comments or spot an error, please contact us.

In the case of superseded distributors, they will appear like this example:

Distributor: VW 113-905-205M, Bosch 0231 115 078 > 113-905-205T, 0231 167 029

What appears first is the index, by application. Click on the application shown in the index to go directly to the desired application location on this page. Eventually, a price list of all the replacement parts will be uploaded soon.
