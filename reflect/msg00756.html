Tim,
I wanted to do the same thing in my '76. This summer I got a pair of retractable front seatbelts out of a mid 80's vanagon from a wrecking yard for about $25 each. They work great. All I had to do was get a piece of right angle steel to mount the retracting mechanism on the floor. I also had to add a few spacers under the part that goes above my shoulder as their is a 'lip' along the doorjam on my bus.
I'm very happy with how they look, feel and work. Now I can reach the glove box without having to unbuckle.
