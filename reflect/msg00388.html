On Tue, 18 Sep 2001, Brad Baker wrote:

> I want to replace my aftermarket header exhaust system
> with the stock setup.  The entire stock setup is available at
> Bus Depot for $99.

I've installed a Bus Depot setup, but it wasn't sold as a "package".  I
just asked for a complete system.

> How hard is the installation process?

Not hard.  You might want to brush up your selection of blue words for the
more challenging bits.

> Will I need any special tools?

Lots of Liquid Wrench, applied every day for several days before you take
things apart.

> Anyone out there used the Bus Depot system?
> Is it really complete?

Dunno.  When I asked them for everything, I got everything.  I don't
remember the price.

> Will the stock system be quiet (the current header setup
> is a total racket)?

It's pretty quiet.  You'll hear more engine mechanical noise.

When I bought my parts, I bought as many Liestritz brand parts as they
had.  The difference in installation ease is well worth the additional
money.  I don't know if the $99 system is Liestritz or not.
