Evening, folks...  I am currently looking for a
running Westie in the $1500 range in the North
Texas/Southern OK area, and found a 73 in OK City that
I thought I'd ask your collective advice on.  

This bus has a fresh 1800 engine (just rebuilt,
apparently).  First, is there any issue with colling
an 1800 engine in a 73's engine bay?  Second, there is
no heat... I mean the heat exchanger is rotted, and
the business end at the exhaust is completely gone. 
The end that opens into the transfer tube (the big
long one down the middle of the bus... dunno what it's
proper name is) is just hanging there. The owner says

canvas has a couple smallish rips in the sides that

long, and the other is only a couple of inches.  

Exterior paint is faded badly; it'll need a prep and
spray to look decent.

Front tires are REALLY bad, and rears really ought to
be replaced too.  Any recommendations on size, or is
stock best?  

Oooh, which reminds me... it's awfully hard to steer! 
Is this normal with a non-power steering bus?  Maybe

and front suspension looks simple enough to work on,
