I applied some heat to the hub tonight and the fan came right off. I  used a
small plumbers torch and went in circles around the hub area. It wiggled off
and then I felt how hot everything got. The fan was only warm on the back
side and little where I put the flame. I only put the torch on it for a minute.
Worked well. Thanks Art and the rest of
you for the suggestions. Now to get the oil cooler off and replace the seals.
