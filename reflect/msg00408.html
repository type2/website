You really need a puller on early bay buses. I've done it without but had to
sacrifice the steering wheel.

I got a cheapie wheel puller kit from Harbor Tools for 7.99 (on sale).

	
http://www.harborfreight.com/cpi/ctaf/Displayitem.taf?itemnumber=40966

If you don't mind spending 10 buck more go for the 3-jaw set. It's less
likely to bust the wheel.

	
http://www.harborfreight.com/cpi/ctaf/Displayitem.taf?itemnumber=40965


Spray some Liquid Wrench around the splines a couple times a day for a few
days in advance too.
