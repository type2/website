I replaced the moisture barrier in the front doors of my camper and
adhered it with 3M Super 77 spray adhesive. It's about $7 a can. When
you first put it on it's fairly tacky, so you can reposition several
times before pressing it down flat. The doors don't leak at all, so I
guess it works.
