Hi Alex,

My experience is that once you remove the split rings that hold the CV
joints onto the drive shaft then only hand pressure is necessary to slide
the CV joints off; no hammers, no hydraulic press and I'm a 10 stone
weakling.

It could be that someone (or you by hammering) has damaged the spines on the
section of shaft 'outside' of the split ring grove in which case just clean
them up with a file with a triangular cross section. The application of
excessive force could simply just increase the damage making this job
exponentially more difficult.

Good luck
