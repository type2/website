> Does anyone know of a supplier who has paints that match the original

> particular I am seeking Kansas Beige, Pastel White and that gray/black used

If the Bus runs, take it to any auto paint store, point at the thing you
want to match, and say, "that color." I think it's more accurate that way
than trying to match through color numbers; twenty-plus years of sun
exposure is going to change the color somewhat, it can't help but do so.
