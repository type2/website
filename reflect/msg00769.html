I belive AFM is going to be the Air Fuel Meter.  The Bently book says you
can test it in the car(unplugged of course) with a VOM(volt ohm meter).

Just finished testing mine and the book says that if the electrical part
of it tests out, it is supposed to work correctly.  Took about 15 minutes
once I got it out of the car.
