I am planning a two month camping trip down to Mexico (from Canada) in a 1974 Westfalia.
The problem is not in Canada or the USA - any insurance you buy from here (or USA) is not valid once you cross the border. You must have Mexican insurance to be valid in Mexico. So far the information I have found indicates that I can only purchase liability insurance for any vehicle older than 20 years. It is my understanding that Mexico goes by the Blue Book value and anything that old is deemed "worthless" regardless of appraised values etc. I gather insurance companies think they are too expensive/hard to fix.
I am very concerned about collision and theft - does anyone have any experiences with an older vehicle in Mexico and insurance and/or accidents? Any advice for what we can do?
