Canadian.  We drove the fastback up to Vancouver BC, met and swapped cars, 
and drove the grey bug  back to WA state.  I don't think she remembered to 
say anything at the border.  There was some paperwork she did have to do to 
get it a WA title, but no big deal.   You might ask the state you are going
