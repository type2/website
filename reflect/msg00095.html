Hi Gail,

If a bus has vacuum brakes or not depends not on the year but also on the market place in which the bus was originally sold and the options requested by the original purchaser.

The only way to definitively tell if there are vacuum brakes is to put your head underneath the van just behind the front driver's side wheel and look forward at the front axle to see if theres a brake booster present.
