On Sat, 22 Sep 2001, John Connolly wrote:

> it's been my experience you can't get the nut tight enough this way (fan
> nut) and more often than not, the fan comes loose and you have to do it all
> over again.


Well, the spec is 40-47 ft-lbs.  All you have to do is hold the fan nut
with a long breaker bar and tighten the pulley nut to that spec.  Both
nuts have the same spec, despite the large hex on the fan nut.  I don't
see much of a problem doing that.

It certainly beats the heck out of trying to get the shroud properly
positioned after you lift it!
